What Hairstyle Suits A Round Face Male Tips Ideas And Faq

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, you might find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right cut and styling, you can enhance your best features and create a balanced look. In this article, we’ll share some tips and ideas on what hairstyle suits a round face male. Whether you prefer a short or long cut, there’s a style that will work for you.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for a Round Face Male?

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let’s discuss the basic principles of choosing a style that suits a round face:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Choose styles that create height on top of your head to elongate your face shape.
  • Opt for haircuts that have short sides and a longer top, as they can create a more angular look.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair to create a more dynamic and interesting style.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

If you’re still unsure about what style would work best for your round face, follow these steps:

  1. Measure the width and length of your face to determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience with men's haircuts.
  4. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  5. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.

What Hairstyles Suit a Round Face Male?

Now that you know the basics of choosing a hairstyle, let’s explore some specific styles that would suit a round face:

1. Short Buzz Cut

The short buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that can work well for men with round faces. The short length on top creates height, while the short sides make your face appear more angular. This style is also very low-maintenance and easy to style.

2. Side-Parted Undercut

The side-parted undercut is another great option for a round face. The short sides and longer top create a more angular look, while the side part adds height and dimension. This style can be styled in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

3. Pompadour

The pompadour is a popular hairstyle that can work well for men with round faces. This style features a high volume of hair on top, which can elongate your face shape. The short sides and back create a more angular look, while the long top can be styled in many different ways.

4. Slicked Back Hair

For a more sophisticated look, consider slicking back your hair. This style can work well for men with round faces, as the slicked back hair creates a more elongated look. This style can be achieved with a pomade or gel and requires some styling time.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle that suits your face shape, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Visit a hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as long, curly hair or a fringe that falls straight down. These styles can make your face appear wider and rounder.

What hairstyles work well for men with thinning hair and a round face?

Men with thinning hair and a round face can opt for a short buzz cut or a side-parted undercut, as these styles can create the illusion of more volume and thickness. Avoid styles that require a lot of styling or products, as these can make your hair look even thinner.

What hair products should I use for my hairstyle?

The hair products you use will depend on your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. For most styles, you’ll need a good quality pomade, gel or wax to hold your hair in place. It’s also important to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type and needs.

Conclusion

Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape can be challenging, but with the right tips and ideas, you can find a style that works for you. Whether you prefer a short, low-maintenance cut or a longer, more textured style, there’s a hairstyle that will suit your round face. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and maintain your hair properly to keep it looking its best.
