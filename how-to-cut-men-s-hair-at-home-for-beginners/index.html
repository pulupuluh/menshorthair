How To Cut Men s Hair At Home For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're tired of paying for expensive haircuts or simply want to try your hand at cutting hair, then you've come to the right place! With the right tools and techniques, cutting men's hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of how to cut men's hair at home for beginners, as well as provide some helpful tips and FAQs to ensure you get the best results possible.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing for the Haircut
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Helpful Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Haircut

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting men's hair at home, it's important to have the right tools. Here are some essential tools you'll need:

  • Barber scissors
  • Electric clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or old towel
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Having these tools on hand will make the process of cutting men's hair at home much easier and more efficient.

Preparing for the Haircut

Once you have all the necessary tools, it's time to prepare for the haircut. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Wash the hair thoroughly and towel dry it.
  2. Put the haircutting cape or old towel around the person's neck to protect their clothing.
  3. Use the comb to part the hair and determine the length you want to cut.
  4. Turn on the electric clippers and attach the guard that corresponds to the desired length.
  5. Use the spray bottle to lightly mist the hair. This will make it easier to cut and prevent the hair from flying everywhere.

Once you have completed these steps, you're ready to start cutting the hair!

Cutting the Hair

Now that you're ready to start cutting, follow these steps:

  1. Start at the back of the head and work your way up to the crown.
  2. Use the clippers with the guard to trim the hair to the desired length.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and back by using a combination of the clippers and scissors.
  4. Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length.
  5. Blend the hair on top with the sides and back by using the scissors to create a gradual taper.
  6. Check the length and symmetry of the haircut using the mirror.

It's important to take your time and be patient when cutting men's hair at home. Don't be afraid to make small adjustments as you go along to ensure you achieve the desired result.

Finishing Touches

Once you're satisfied with the haircut, it's time to add some finishing touches:

  1. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the neck and around the ears.
  2. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.
  3. Remove the cape or old towel and brush off any loose hairs.
  4. Wash the hair again to remove any loose hairs and styling products.

With these finishing touches, your haircut is now complete!

Helpful Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you cut men's hair at home:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. You can always take more hair off, but you can't put it back on!
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a more natural look.
  • If you're using scissors, make sure they're sharp to prevent split ends.
  • Have the person sit still and look straight ahead to avoid uneven cuts.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting men's hair at home:

Q: How often should I cut men's hair?

A: It depends on the person's hair type and desired length. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Q: Can I cut men's hair without clippers?

A: Yes, you can use only scissors, but it may take longer and be more difficult to achieve an even cut.

Q: How do I fix a mistake?

A: If you make a mistake, try to even it out with the rest of the hair. If it's a major mistake, you may need to cut the hair shorter or seek professional help.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, it's important to take care of the person's hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly wash and condition the hair.
  • Use a brush or comb to style the hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat from hair dryers or styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the desired length and shape.

By following these tips, you can ensure that the person's hair looks great after the haircut!

Conclusion

Cutting men's hair at home for beginners may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you'll be on your way to giving great haircuts in no time. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to make small adjustments along the way. Good luck!
