How To Tell What Colours Suit You

Description

Choosing the right colours for your outfit can make a big difference in your appearance. Wearing the wrong colours can make you look pale, washed out, or even sickly. On the other hand, wearing the right colours can make you look vibrant, healthy, and full of life. But how do you know which colours suit you best? In this article, we will share some tips on how to tell what colours suit you.

How to

Knowing your skin tone is the first step in determining which colours suit you. Everyone's skin tone is different, and colours that may look good on one person may not look good on another. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool.

Warm skin tone

If you have a warm skin tone, you will look best in colours that have a yellow or golden undertone. Think of colours like orange, red, peach, coral, and earthy tones like brown, olive, and gold. Warm skin tones also look great in warm neutrals like beige and ivory.

Cool skin tone

If you have a cool skin tone, you will look best in colours that have a blue or pink undertone. Think of colours like blue, green, purple, pink, and cool neutrals like white, gray, and silver.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to determine your skin tone:

  1. Take a look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.
  2. Examine the jewellery you wear. Do you look better in silver or gold? If you look better in gold, you probably have a warm skin tone. If you look better in silver, you probably have a cool skin tone.
  3. Look at your natural hair colour. If you have blonde, brown, or black hair with warm undertones like red or gold, you have a warm skin tone. If you have hair with cool undertones like ash or blue, you have a cool skin tone.
  4. Take a look at your eye colour. If you have warm, golden brown or hazel eyes, you have a warm skin tone. If you have cool blue, gray or green eyes, you have a cool skin tone.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing colours:

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different colours. Sometimes, colours that you never thought would look good on you can end up being your new favourites.
  • Consider the occasion when choosing colours. Bright, bold colours may be appropriate for a night out, but may not be suitable for a job interview.
  • Think about the colours that you feel confident wearing. If you feel good in a particular colour, it's probably because it complements your skin tone.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining which colours suit you:

What if I have a neutral skin tone?

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear both warm and cool colours. Lucky you!

Can my skin tone change?

Yes, your skin tone can change over time due to factors such as sun exposure, age, and health conditions. It's important to reassess your skin tone periodically and adjust your wardrobe accordingly.

Hair Care

Choosing the right colours for your wardrobe is not the only way to enhance your appearance. Taking care of your hair is also important. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to tell what colours suit you, it's time to revamp your wardrobe and start looking and feeling your best!
