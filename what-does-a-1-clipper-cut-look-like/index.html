What Does A 1 Clipper Cut Look Like Everything You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Apr 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting a 1 clipper cut but not sure how it would look like? Or are you looking for a new hairstyle that’s low maintenance and easy to achieve? In this blog post, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about 1 clipper cuts.

Table of Contents

  • Description of 1 Clipper Cut
  • How to Achieve a 1 Clipper Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Your Hair with a 1 Clipper
  • Tips for Maintaining a 1 Clipper Cut

  • Hair Care Tips for a 1 Clipper Cut

Description of 1 Clipper Cut

A 1 clipper cut is a very short haircut that requires the use of the clipper guard number 1. This means that the hair will be cut to a length of 1/8 inch or 3 mm. It’s a popular hairstyle among men because it’s low maintenance and easy to achieve. It’s also a good option for people with thinning hair or receding hairlines.

How to Achieve a 1 Clipper Cut

If you’re thinking of getting a 1 clipper cut, you can either go to a barber or cut your hair at home. If you’re going to a barber, make sure to bring a picture of the style you want. If you’re cutting your hair at home, you’ll need a good quality clipper, a mirror, and a comb.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Your Hair with a 1 Clipper

Follow these steps to achieve a 1 clipper cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly.
  2. Attach the number 1 guard to your clipper.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up, using the clipper to cut your hair evenly.
  4. Move to the sides of your head and cut your hair in the same way.
  5. Finally, move to the top of your head and cut your hair to the desired length.

It’s important to remember that the clipper should always be moving in the opposite direction of the hair growth. This will ensure that the hair is cut evenly and doesn’t get tangled in the clipper.

Tips for Maintaining a 1 Clipper Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining a 1 clipper cut:

  • Get regular trims every 2-3 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs About 1 Clipper Cuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about 1 clipper cuts:

Is a 1 clipper cut suitable for all hair types?

A 1 clipper cut is suitable for most hair types, but it’s not recommended for people with very curly or thick hair. It’s also not recommended for people with a lot of hair loss or bald patches.

Can I style my hair with a 1 clipper cut?

A 1 clipper cut is a very short haircut, so there’s not much room for styling. However, you can apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

How often do I need to get a trim with a 1 clipper cut?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 2-3 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for a 1 Clipper Cut

Here are some hair care tips for a 1 clipper cut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Now that you know everything about 1 clipper cuts, it’s time to decide if it’s the right hairstyle for you. Remember that it’s a low maintenance and easy to achieve style that’s perfect for people who want a simple and clean look.
