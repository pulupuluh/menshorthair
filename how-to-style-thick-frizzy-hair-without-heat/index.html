How To Style Thick Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Written by Bardi May 15, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have thick frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style. Using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair and make it even more frizzy. Fortunately, there are ways to style your hair without heat that can leave you with smooth, frizz-free locks. In this article, we will guide you through how to style thick frizzy hair without heat.

  2. How to Style Thick Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Description

Thick frizzy hair is characterized by its tendency to puff up and become unmanageable. While heat styling tools can be helpful in taming frizz, they can also cause long-term damage to your hair. That's why it's important to learn how to style your hair without heat. By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, frizz-free look while keeping your hair healthy.

How to Style Thick Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Here are some ways to style thick frizzy hair without heat:

1. Blow Dry with a Diffuser

If you must use heat to dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help distribute the heat more evenly and prevent your hair from becoming too frizzy. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to protect your hair from damage.

2. Air Dry with Product

If you have time to air dry your hair, apply a smoothing product like a leave-in conditioner or serum. This will help reduce frizz and add shine to your hair. To prevent your hair from drying in a clumpy, frizzy mess, use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Once a week, use a deep conditioning hair mask to help hydrate and smooth your hair. This will make it easier to style and reduce frizz. Simply apply the mask to damp hair, comb through, and leave on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing out with warm water.

4. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair while it's damp can help create soft waves without using heat. Simply braid your hair in one or more braids, depending on how tight you want the waves to be. Leave the braids in overnight or until your hair is completely dry, then unravel them for natural-looking waves.

5. Use Rollers

If you want more structured curls, use rollers to style your hair without heat. Simply roll small sections of hair around the rollers, then secure with a clip or bobby pin. Leave the rollers in until your hair is completely dry, then remove for bouncy, voluminous curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling thick frizzy hair without heat:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup or oils. Blot your hair dry with a towel to remove excess water, but avoid rubbing it vigorously, which can cause damage and frizz.

2. Apply Product

Apply a leave-in conditioner or smoothing serum to your hair to help reduce frizz and add shine. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Braid or Roll Your Hair

If you want waves or curls, braid or roll your hair while it's still damp. Use small sections of hair for tighter waves, or larger sections for looser waves or curls.

4. Let Your Hair Dry

Leave your hair to air dry completely, or use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment on low heat to speed up the process. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz.

5. Unravel or Remove the Braids or Rollers

Once your hair is completely dry, unravel or remove the braids or rollers to reveal your waves or curls. Gently run your fingers through your hair to separate the waves or curls and add volume.

Tips for Styling Thick Frizzy Hair Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you style your thick frizzy hair without heat:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz while you sleep.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any product buildup or oils from your hair.

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without using heat by using a straightening brush or a hair straightening cream. However, keep in mind that these methods may not be as effective as using a straightener and may require more time and effort.

What is the best way to prevent frizz?

The best way to prevent frizz is to keep your hair hydrated and use the right products for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Additionally, avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying and wrap it in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Frizzy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your thick frizzy hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your thick frizzy hair without using heat and achieve a sleek, frizz-free look. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and always use the right products for your hair type.
