How To Know What Kind Of Hairstyle Suits Me Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your old hairdo and want to try something new? Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can find a style that perfectly suits your face shape, hair type, and personality. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps to finding the perfect hairstyle for you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. What is the widest part of your face? What is the shape of your jawline? Take note of these features and compare them to the six basic face shapes.

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because almost any hairstyle will suit you. For a round face shape, go for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head, like a layered bob. Square face shapes look great with hairstyles that soften the jawline, like a side-swept bangs. Heart-shaped faces suit short hairstyles that add fullness to the crown. Diamond face shapes look good with hairstyles that balance the forehead and chin, like a textured bob. Oblong face shapes look best with hairstyles that create width, such as a wavy lob.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step in choosing the right hairstyle is to consider your hair type. Is your hair thick or thin? Straight or curly? Fine or coarse? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your natural texture. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a layered haircut will help to tame your curls and add volume. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob will give your hair the illusion of more volume.

Step 3: Decide on Your Hair Length

After you’ve determined your face shape and hair type, it’s time to decide on your hair length. Do you want short hair, medium-length hair, or long hair? Short hair is great for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style. Medium-length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Long hair is great if you want to experiment with different hairstyles and updos.

Step 4: Think about Your Lifestyle

The fourth step in choosing the right hairstyle is to think about your lifestyle. Do you have a busy schedule and need a low-maintenance hairstyle? Or do you have more time to spend on styling your hair? If you have a busy schedule, go for a hairstyle that doesn’t require much maintenance, like a pixie cut or a messy bun. If you have more time to spend on styling your hair, try a more intricate hairstyle like braids or an updo.

Step 5: Consult with Your Hair Stylist

The final step in choosing the right hairstyle is to consult with your hair stylist. Your hair stylist can give you advice on which hairstyles will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also help you choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hair stylist
  • Consider getting a haircut that flatters your best features
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful
  • Remember that hair grows back, so don’t be too afraid to make a change

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered bob is a great hairstyle for a round face shape because it adds height to the crown of your head, which helps to elongate your face shape.

What is the best hairstyle for fine, straight hair?

A blunt bob is a great hairstyle for fine, straight hair because it gives the illusion of more volume and thickness.

What is the best hairstyle for thick, curly hair?

A layered haircut is a great hairstyle for thick, curly hair because it helps to tame your curls and adds volume.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair

With these tips, you’ll be able to find a hairstyle that perfectly suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Remember to consult with your hair stylist and don’t be afraid to try something new. With the right hairstyle, you’ll feel confident and beautiful every day.
