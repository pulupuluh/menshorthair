How Can You Tell What Shape Your Face Is

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're wondering what shape your face is, you're not alone! Determining your face shape is an important step in figuring out the best hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories for you. In this article, we'll go over how you can tell what shape your face is, as well as some tips for styling your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are a few different methods for determining your face shape, but the easiest way is to use a mirror and take a close look at your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Round: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a curved hairline and full cheeks.
  • Square: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and straight sides.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline.
  • Triangle: Your face is wider at the jawline than at the forehead, with a pointed chin.

Keep in mind that these are general guidelines, and some faces may be a combination of shapes. If you're having trouble determining your face shape, ask a hairstylist or makeup artist for help.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to determining your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Look at the overall shape of your face. Is it longer than it is wide, or vice versa?
  3. Look at your hairline. Is it rounded or straight?
  4. Look at your jawline. Is it rounded or angular?
  5. Look at your cheekbones. Are they prominent or more subtle?
  6. Compare your features to the general guidelines for each face shape.
  7. Determine which shape is the closest match.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your features.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair based on your face shape:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will flatter an oval face shape, but avoid styles that add too much height on top.
  • Round: Look for hairstyles with height at the crown and layers that frame the face.
  • Square: Soften the strong angles of a square face shape with curls or waves.
  • Heart: Balance a pointed chin with a hairstyle that adds volume at the jawline.
  • Diamond: Play up your cheekbones with a hairstyle that adds width at the temples.
  • Triangle: Balance a wider jawline with a hairstyle that adds volume at the crown.

Remember, these are just guidelines and you should choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable in.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining face shape:

  • Can makeup change the appearance of my face shape? Makeup can help contour your face to create the illusion of a different shape, but your natural face shape will remain the same.
  • Can weight loss or gain change my face shape? Yes, significant weight loss or gain can change the shape of your face.
  • Can face shape affect the way I age? Yes, certain face shapes may be more prone to wrinkles or sagging as you age.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

In addition to choosing a flattering hairstyle, there are some hair care tips that can help you accentuate your face shape:

  • Oval: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top, as this can elongate your face.
  • Round: Use a deep side part to create the illusion of length, and avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides.
  • Square: Soften your jawline with a side-swept fringe, and avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline.
  • Heart: Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the temples, as this can widen your forehead.
  • Diamond: Use a center part to draw attention to your cheekbones, and avoid hairstyles that add volume at the temples or chin.
  • Triangle: Add volume at the crown to balance your wider jawline, and avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline.

Remember, these tips are just suggestions and you should choose a hairstyle and hair care routine that works best for you and your lifestyle.

Conclusion

Determining your face shape is an important step in finding the perfect hairstyle and makeup look for you. By following these tips and guidelines, you can easily figure out what shape your face is and start experimenting with new styles. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and have fun with your new look!
