What To Do With Long Thick Hair Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long thick hair is a blessing, but it can also be a curse for men who don't know how to manage it. If you're one of those guys who struggles to style your long locks, don't worry. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on what to do with long thick hair guys. From hairstyles to hair care, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair Guys
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair Guys

Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair Guys

When it comes to hairstyles for long thick hair guys, the options are endless. Here are some popular hairstyles to try:

Man Bun

The man bun is a timeless hairstyle that works well for guys with long thick hair. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the top of your head and tie it into a bun. You can make it as tight or as loose as you like. The man bun is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is another great option for long thick hair guys. This style is achieved by tying the top half of your hair into a bun or ponytail while leaving the rest of your hair down. It's perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still want to show off their long locks.

Top Knot

The top knot is a more polished version of the man bun. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the top of your head and tie it into a tight knot. The top knot is great for guys who want a more sophisticated look.

Flowing Locks

If you're not into tying your hair up, you can always let your hair flow. Long thick hair looks great when it's left down and styled with a bit of product. Just make sure to keep it well-groomed to avoid looking messy.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair Guys

Managing long thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right hair care routine, you can keep your locks healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips for long thick hair guys:

Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for long thick hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, and shea butter. These ingredients will help to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Brushing

Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Trimming

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks.

Styling Products

Use styling products like hair wax or pomade to keep your hair in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs on Long Thick Hair Guys

Q: How often should I wash my long thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every two to three days.

Q: Can I dye my long thick hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your long thick hair. However, it's important to choose a dye that is specifically formulated for long hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

Q: Should I use heat styling tools on my long thick hair?

A: Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, so it's best to use them sparingly. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Q: How can I prevent my long thick hair from getting tangled?

A: Brush your hair regularly and use a leave-in conditioner to prevent tangles. Avoid wearing hats or tight hairstyles that can cause knots and tangles.

Q: Can I wear a hat with long thick hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat with long thick hair. Just make sure to choose a hat that fits well and won't cause your hair to get tangled or matted.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to manage your long thick hair like a pro. Whether you prefer to tie it up or let it flow, your long locks will look healthy and stylish.
