How To Thicken Very Fine Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Feb 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have very fine hair, you might be looking for ways to add some volume and thickness to your locks. Fortunately, there are many tips and tricks that can help you achieve a fuller look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to thicken very fine hair, from hair care to styling techniques.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Thicken Very Fine Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we get into styling techniques, it's important to start with proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for products that are specifically formulated for fine hair. These will be lighter and won't weigh down your hair as much as heavier products designed for thicker hair types.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look even thinner. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days instead.

Use a Lightweight Conditioner

Again, you want to avoid products that will weigh down your hair. Look for a lightweight conditioner that won't leave a heavy residue.

Avoid Brushing Wet Hair

Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so it's best to avoid brushing it when it's wet. If you must brush your hair after washing it, use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush.

How to Thicken Very Fine Hair

Now that you know how to take care of your fine hair, let's explore some ways to add volume and thickness.

Use a Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse can help give your hair some lift and body. Apply a small amount to your roots before blow-drying your hair.

Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help give it some lift at the roots. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow-drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then, flip your head back up and finish blow-drying your hair as usual.

Try a Root Lifter

A root lifter is a product that's designed to give your hair some lift at the roots. Apply it to your roots before blow-drying your hair for added volume.

Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush while blow-drying your hair can help add volume and body. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift your hair as you go.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to adding volume and thickness to very fine hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  3. Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  4. Flip your head back up and continue blow-drying your hair with a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a root lifter to add even more volume at the roots.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for thickening very fine hair:

  • Avoid heavy styling products, which can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider getting layers or a blunt cut to add some texture and movement to your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb oil and add some texture to your hair between washes.

FAQ

Can I use hair extensions to add thickness?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add thickness to very fine hair. Look for extensions that are specifically designed for fine hair to ensure a natural look.

What kind of hairbrush should I use?

For very fine hair, a boar bristle brush or a soft bristle brush can be a good choice. These types of brushes are gentle on your hair and won't cause unnecessary breakage.

Are there any foods that can help thicken hair?

Yes, foods that are high in protein, iron, and biotin can help promote healthy hair growth. Some examples include eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and salmon.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help thicken and add volume to very fine hair. Remember to start with proper hair care and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
