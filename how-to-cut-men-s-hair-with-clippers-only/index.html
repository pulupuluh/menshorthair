How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Only

Written by William May 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to cut men's hair using clippers only. We will provide step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQ to help you achieve a simple and clean haircut. Whether you're a beginner or a professional, this guide will help you achieve a perfect haircut with clippers. So, let's get started.

Description

Clippers are a great tool for cutting men's hair. Not only are they easy to use, but they also give you a clean and precise cut. Clippers come in different sizes, shapes, and designs. They are perfect for trimming hair, creating fades, and even shaving. Cutting men's hair with clippers only is a great way to save time and money. You can achieve a perfect haircut without having to go to a barber or hair salon. All you need is a good pair of clippers, a comb, and a mirror.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can cause the clippers to slip and make it difficult to achieve a smooth cut. Follow these steps to cut your hair with clippers only:

Step 1: Choose the right clipper guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, ranging from 1/16 inch to 1 inch. The size of the guard determines the length of the hair that will be left on the head. For a shorter cut, use a smaller guard, and for a longer cut, use a larger guard. Start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

Step 2: Begin cutting

Start cutting at the crown of the head and work your way down to the nape of the neck. Use the clipper in an upward motion to create a clean and smooth cut. Make sure to hold the clipper at a slight angle to avoid creating lines or bumps.

Step 3: Blend the sides and back

Use the clipper without a guard to blend the sides and back of the head. Use the clipper in a downward motion to create a natural-looking fade. Start at the top of the head and work your way down to the nape of the neck. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.

Step 4: Trim the edges

Use the clipper without a guard to trim the edges around the ears and neckline. Hold the clipper at a slight angle to avoid cutting into the skin. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create a natural-looking edge.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect haircut:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.
  • Hold the clipper at a slight angle to avoid creating lines or bumps.
  • Use the clipper without a guard to blend the sides and back of the head.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create a natural-looking edge.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers only:

Q: Can I achieve a professional-looking haircut with clippers?

A: Yes, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut with clippers. Just make sure to follow the instructions carefully and take your time.

Q: What type of clippers should I use?

A: There are many different types of clippers available. Choose one that is comfortable to hold and has a good motor.

Q: Do I need any other tools besides clippers?

A: You will need a comb and a mirror to achieve a clean and precise cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to clean the clippers and store them in a safe place. Use a comb to style your hair and apply a small amount of hair product if desired. Keep your hair clean and well-groomed to maintain a fresh and stylish look.

Cutting men's hair with clippers only is a simple and cost-effective way to achieve a great haircut. With these step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQ, you can achieve a perfect cut in no time. So, grab your clippers and get started!
