How To Fix A Square Haircut Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Feb 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’ve recently gotten a square haircut and it’s not looking quite right, you’re not alone. Many people struggle with this style, but there are some simple fixes that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for fixing a square haircut, including how to style it, what products to use, and how to maintain it.

How to Fix a Square Haircut

If you’re unhappy with your square haircut, there are several things you can do to fix it. Here are some tips:

1. Add Layers

If your square haircut is looking too blunt, adding layers can help soften the look. This will also add some movement to your hair and make it easier to style.

2. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can help add volume and texture to your hair. This is especially helpful if your square haircut is looking too flat or lifeless.

3. Try a Side Part

If your square haircut is looking too severe, try parting it to the side. This will help soften the look and make it more flattering.

4. Get a Trim

If your square haircut is looking too bulky, getting a trim can help remove some of the weight and make it easier to style.

5. Add Bangs

If your square haircut is looking too harsh, adding bangs can help soften the look. This is also a good option if you’re looking to change up your style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for fixing a square haircut:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will help your products work better and make it easier to achieve the look you want.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a texturizing spray or other styling product to your hair. This will help add volume and texture, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help add volume and movement to your hair.

Step 4: Style

Style your hair using a flat iron or curling wand. This will help add shape and definition to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Square Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your square haircut:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a quality shampoo and conditioner will help keep your hair healthy and looking great.

2. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your square haircut looking fresh and stylish.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a square haircut?

A: A square haircut is a blunt, straight haircut that falls at or just below the jawline. It’s a popular style for both men and women.

Q: Can I fix a square haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can fix a square haircut at home using the tips and tricks outlined in this article.

Q: How often should I get my square haircut trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get your square haircut trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

3. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and looking great.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily fix a square haircut and keep it looking great. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. And always take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
