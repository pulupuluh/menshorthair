How To Get 60 S Hair Waves A Step By Step Guide

Are you a fan of the vintage 60's look? Do you want to achieve those iconic hair waves that were popular during that era? Well, you're in luck because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to get 60's hair waves using simple techniques and tools. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get 60's Hair Waves
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect 60's Hair Waves
  • FAQs

How to Get 60's Hair Waves

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at some of the tools you'll need to achieve those 60's hair waves:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Comb
  • Rollers (preferably foam or velcro)
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start with freshly washed and dried hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent any damage to your hair from the heat styling tools. Use a comb to remove any tangles and ensure that your hair is smooth and ready for styling.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have thick hair, you may need to create more sections. Use a comb to create even sections.

Step 3: Roll your hair

Take each section of hair and wrap it around the roller. Start at the bottom and roll upwards towards your scalp. Use a clip or bobby pin to secure the roller in place. Repeat this step until all sections of your hair are rolled up.

Step 4: Apply heat

Use a hair dryer to apply heat to your hair. Make sure that the heat is evenly distributed throughout your hair. Keep the rollers in your hair until your hair has cooled down.

Step 5: Remove the rollers

Once your hair has cooled down, remove the rollers. Use a comb to gently brush out the waves and create the desired shape. Apply hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect 60's Hair Waves

  • Use foam or velcro rollers for a more natural look.
  • Don't use too much hairspray as it can weigh down your hair.
  • For a more dramatic look, create smaller sections of hair and use smaller rollers.
  • To avoid frizz, make sure that your hair is completely dry before removing the rollers.

FAQs

What if I don't have rollers?

If you don't have rollers, you can use a curling iron to create the waves. However, make sure that you use a heat protectant spray and don't apply too much heat to your hair.

Can I sleep with the rollers in my hair?

Yes, you can sleep with the rollers in your hair. However, make sure that you secure them properly and use a silk pillowcase to prevent any damage to your hair.

Hair Care for 60's Hair Waves

To maintain your 60's hair waves, make sure that you use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Lastly, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to get 60's hair waves, it's time to try it out for yourself. Follow these simple steps and you'll be rocking those iconic waves in no time!
