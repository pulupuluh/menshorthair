How To Make Your Round Face Look Oval

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Do you have a round face and wish it was more oval-shaped? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with round faces struggle to find ways to enhance their facial features. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your round face look more oval-shaped. In this article, we'll show you how to do just that.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Round Face Look Oval
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may feel that your features lack definition and look softer than you'd like. An oval face, on the other hand, is longer and narrower with more defined cheekbones and a more pointed chin. By using makeup, hairstyles, and other techniques, you can create the illusion of an oval-shaped face and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Make Your Round Face Look Oval

There are several ways to make your round face look more oval-shaped:

1. Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of shadows and depth. By applying darker shades to the areas around your cheeks and jawline, you can create the illusion of a more defined, oval-shaped face. Use a matte bronzer or contouring powder that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it in a "3" shape around your temples, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well to create a natural-looking effect.

2. Highlight Your Features

Highlighting is another makeup technique that can help create the illusion of an oval-shaped face. By applying a lighter shade of concealer or highlighter to the areas above your cheekbones, down the center of your nose, and on your forehead, you can make these features appear more prominent. This will help balance out the roundness of your face and create a more defined look.

3. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also have a big impact on the shape of your face. For a round face, it's best to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head. This will create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, as these can make your face look rounder. Instead, try a layered cut with long, face-framing layers, or a voluminous updo.

4. Wear Statement Earrings

Statement earrings can also help create the illusion of an oval-shaped face. Choose earrings that are long and angular, such as drop earrings or chandelier earrings. These will draw attention away from the roundness of your face and create a more elongated look. Avoid round or hoop earrings, as these can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your round face look more oval:

Step 1: Apply Foundation

Apply a medium-coverage foundation that matches your skin tone. Blend well to create a smooth, even base.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Using a matte bronzer or contouring powder, apply darker shades to the areas around your temples, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well to create a natural-looking effect.

Step 3: Highlight Your Features

Apply a lighter shade of concealer or highlighter to the areas above your cheekbones, down the center of your nose, and on your forehead. Blend well to create a natural-looking effect.

Step 4: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head, such as a layered cut with long, face-framing layers, or a voluminous updo.

Step 5: Wear Statement Earrings

Choose long, angular earrings such as drop earrings or chandelier earrings to draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Tips

  • Use a light hand when contouring to avoid a harsh, unnatural look.
  • Choose a concealer or highlighter that's one or two shades lighter than your skin tone.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat.
  • Choose earrings that are long and angular to create the illusion of an oval-shaped face.
  • Experiment with different makeup and hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my round face look oval without makeup?

A: Yes, you can make your round face look more oval-shaped by choosing the right hairstyle and wearing statement earrings. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, and choose earrings that are long and angular.

Q: How do I know if I have a round face?

A: A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they're all roughly the same width, you likely have a round face.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heated styling tools too often. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine, and avoid harsh chemicals. By keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained, you can enhance the natural beauty of your face.
