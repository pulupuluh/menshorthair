What Hair Cut Would Suit Me Best

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape and hair type? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing what hair cut would suit you best and how to maintain it.

Description

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you have no idea what style would suit you best. It's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when deciding on a haircut. With the right cut, you can enhance your features, balance out your face shape, and bring out your best self.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect haircut is identifying your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! You can pull off virtually any hairstyle. However, if you have a round face shape, you should avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for a haircut that adds height and length. If you have a square face shape, you can soften your features with a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe. Heart-shaped faces look best with a chin-length bob or a long, layered cut. Diamond-shaped faces can benefit from a side-swept fringe or a short, textured bob. Lastly, if you have an oblong face shape, you can add width to your face with a full fringe or a layered bob.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Look for hairstyles that flatter your features

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist

Step 4: Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle

Step 5: Maintain your haircut with regular trims

Tips

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair type as well. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look limp. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can add layers to reduce bulk and make your hair more manageable. Additionally, if you have curly hair, you should look for a haircut that enhances your curls and reduces frizz.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Q: Can I still style my hair after getting a haircut?

A: Absolutely! Your hairstylist can recommend styling products and techniques that will work best with your new haircut.

Q: What if I don't like my new haircut?

A: It's important to communicate with your hairstylist and let them know your concerns. They can make adjustments or suggest alternative styles that may better suit you.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes regular shampooing and conditioning, using heat protectant products when styling with heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals or treatments that can damage your hair. Additionally, getting regular trims will prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know what haircut would suit you best, it's time to book a consultation with a hairstylist and find your perfect style. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut, and maintain it with proper hair care. Happy hairstyling!
