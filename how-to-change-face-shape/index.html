How To Change Your Face Shape A Step By Step Guide

Are you unhappy with the shape of your face? Do you wish you could change it? Well, you're in luck! With a few simple tips and tricks, you can alter the appearance of your face shape. In this article, we'll show you how to change your face shape in easy-to-follow steps.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Change Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care and Face Shape

Description

Before we dive into the details of how to change your face shape, let's first understand what face shape is. Face shape is determined by the bone structure of your face, which is unique to each individual. However, there are certain facial features that can be altered to change the appearance of your face shape.

Changing your face shape can be done through a variety of methods, including makeup, hairstyles, and even cosmetic surgery. In this article, we'll focus on natural and non-invasive ways to alter your face shape.

How To Change Face Shape

There are several ways to change your face shape, including:

  • Using makeup to contour and highlight specific areas of your face
  • Choosing hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  • Wearing glasses that complement your face shape
  • Trying facial exercises to tone and tighten certain areas of your face

While these methods may not drastically change the bone structure of your face, they can alter the appearance of your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can change your face shape, you need to determine what your current face shape is. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Then, compare your traced outline to the six face shapes to see which one matches closest.

Step 2: Contour and Highlight

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use makeup to contour and highlight certain areas of your face to create the illusion of a different face shape.

For example, if you have a round face, you can use a bronzer to contour the sides of your face to create the illusion of a slimmer face. You can also use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, to draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step 3: Choose Flattering Hairstyles

Choosing hairstyles that flatter your face shape can also alter the appearance of your face shape. For example, if you have a square face, you can choose a hairstyle with soft layers and curls to soften the angles of your face.

Step 4: Wear Glasses That Complement Your Face Shape

Wearing glasses that complement your face shape can also change the appearance of your face shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face, you can choose glasses with rounded frames to balance out the width of your forehead.

Step 5: Try Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can also help tone and tighten certain areas of your face, which can alter the appearance of your face shape. For example, if you have a double chin, you can try exercises that target the muscles in your neck and jawline to create a more defined jawline.

Tips for Changing Face Shape

Here are a few additional tips for changing your face shape:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage your skin
  • Get plenty of sleep to reduce the appearance of dark circles and puffiness

FAQs

Can I Change the Bone Structure of My Face?

While there are cosmetic procedures that can alter the bone structure of your face, such as jaw surgery or cheek implants, these are invasive and expensive procedures.

How Long Does It Take to See Results from Facial Exercises?

The length of time it takes to see results from facial exercises varies from person to person. Some people may see results in a few weeks, while others may take several months to see a noticeable difference.

Hair Care and Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is important, but so is taking care of your hair. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Conclusion

Changing your face shape can be done through a variety of methods, from makeup to facial exercises. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can alter the appearance of your face shape and feel more confident in your own skin.
