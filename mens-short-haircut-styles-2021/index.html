Mens Short Haircut Styles 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle this year? Mens short haircut styles 2021 offer a wide range of options to choose from. Whether you want a classic look or something more modern, there's a haircut out there for you. In this guide, we'll take you through the latest styles, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your new do. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Mens short haircut styles 2021 are all about versatility. From the buzz cut to the textured crop, there's a style for every face shape and hair type. Short haircuts have always been popular among men as they are low-maintenance and easy to style. However, this year's styles bring a fresh take on some classic cuts. The key is to find a haircut that suits your personality and lifestyle.

How to

The first step in getting a mens short haircut style 2021 is to find a good barber or hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience with short haircuts and can recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type. Once you've found the right person, it's time to discuss your options. Bring in pictures of styles you like and be open to suggestions.

Face Shape

When choosing a mens short haircut style 2021, it's important to consider your face shape. Different cuts work better for different shapes. Here are some examples:

  • Round face - go for a buzz cut or a textured crop.
  • Square face - try a short fringe or a side-swept style.
  • Oval face - almost any style will work, but a messy crop is a popular choice.

Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Some styles work better with thick or curly hair, while others are better suited for fine or straight hair. It's important to choose a style that works with your natural hair texture. Your barber or hairstylist can recommend products to help you achieve the look you want.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a popular mens short haircut style 2021: the textured crop.

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as hair wax or pomade.
  3. Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair into a messy, textured look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens short haircut style 2021:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in styling products that work with your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What are the most popular mens short haircut styles 2021?

Some of the most popular mens short haircut styles 2021 include the buzz cut, textured crop, short fringe, and side-swept style.

Who can pull off a mens short haircut style 2021?

Mens short haircut styles 2021 can work for almost anyone, but it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is key to pulling off a great mens short haircut style 2021. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, too often.

With these tips and a great mens short haircut style 2021, you'll be ready to take on the world. Have fun experimenting with different styles and don't be afraid to switch things up every once in a while. Happy hairstyling!
