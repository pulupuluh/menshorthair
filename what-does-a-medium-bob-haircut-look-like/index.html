What Does A Medium Bob Haircut Look Like A Relaxed Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting a medium bob haircut but not sure what it looks like? Look no further! In this guide, we will explain what a medium bob haircut is, what it looks like, and how to style it. We will also include some tips and frequently asked questions about this trendy hairstyle. Let's get started!

Description: What is a Medium Bob Haircut?

A medium bob haircut is a shoulder-length hairstyle that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It is typically cut to the collarbone or just above it. The style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It can be worn straight, wavy, or curly and can be styled with or without bangs.

How to Get a Medium Bob Haircut

If you're interested in getting a medium bob haircut, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Research

Look for inspiration photos online or in magazines. Find a few styles that you like and bring them to your hair stylist for reference.

Step 2: Consultation

Book a consultation with your hair stylist to discuss your desired style and whether it would suit your face shape and hair type. They may also suggest some modifications to the style to better suit you.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you and your stylist have agreed on the style, they will begin cutting your hair to the desired length. They may use a razor or scissors to create texture and movement. After the cut, they will style your hair using a blow dryer, brush, and styling products.

Tips for Styling a Medium Bob Haircut

Here are some tips for styling a medium bob haircut:

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair. - Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and curl under the ends. - Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look. - Experiment with different partings to see which one suits you best. - Add some highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a medium bob haircut suit my face shape?

A: Yes, a medium bob haircut can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your hair stylist can suggest modifications to the style to better suit you.

Q: Can I wear a medium bob haircut with bangs?

A: Yes, a medium bob haircut can be styled with or without bangs. Your hair stylist can suggest the best type of bangs for your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim for a medium bob haircut?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care for a Medium Bob Haircut

To keep your medium bob haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. - Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. - Avoid using too much styling products as it can make your hair look greasy or weighed down. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Congratulations, you now know what a medium bob haircut looks like and how to style it! Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
