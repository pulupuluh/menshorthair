Best Hair Style For Round Face Over 60

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 60 and have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape, make you look younger, and be easy to maintain. In this article, we'll go over the best hair style for round face over 60, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Best Hair Style for Round Face over 60
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best hair style for round face over 60 is a layered bob with side-swept bangs. This hairstyle is perfect for women with round faces because it creates the illusion of length and draws attention to the eyes. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the side-swept bangs soften the face and add a touch of femininity.

How to Achieve the Best Hair Style for Round Face over 60

To achieve the best hair style for round face over 60, you'll need to visit a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for mature women. They will be able to recommend the best length and style for your face shape and hair type. Make sure to bring pictures of the hairstyle you want, and be open to their suggestions.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

3. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair under.

4. Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair to add shine and hold.

5. Use a comb to create a side part, and sweep your bangs to the side.

6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

- If your hair is thin or fine, consider adding some highlights to create the illusion of volume.

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.

- Invest in a good quality hair dryer and round brush to create volume and texture.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but make sure to add layers to create volume and texture, and avoid hairstyles that are too blunt.

Q: Should I go for a straight or curly hairstyle?

A: A slightly wavy or curly hairstyle can add volume and texture to your hair, while a straight hairstyle can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking great, make sure to wash it every 2-3 days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. Avoid using hot tools every day, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Finally, visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.
