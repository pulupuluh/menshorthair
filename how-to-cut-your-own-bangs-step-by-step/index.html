How To Cut Your Own Bangs Step By Step

Written by Petter Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having to go to the salon every time you want to trim your bangs? Cutting your own bangs can be a cost-effective and convenient solution. With the right tools and technique, you can easily achieve the perfect bangs at home. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of how to cut your own bangs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Bangs

How to Cut Your Own Bangs

Cutting your own bangs can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve great results. Here are the things you'll need to get started:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Water spray bottle

Once you have gathered all the necessary tools, it's time to get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to cut your own bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair to remove any excess oil or dirt. Make sure your bangs are dry before you start cutting.
  2. Section off your bangs. Use a comb to section off your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair back so that it's out of the way.
  3. Decide on the length. Decide how short you want your bangs to be. It's always better to start with a longer length and gradually work your way up.
  4. Cut the middle section. Take the middle section of your bangs and hold them between your fingers. Cut across the hair at the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much off.
  5. Cut the sides. Take the hair on one side of the middle section and hold it between your fingers. Cut across the hair at the same length as the middle section. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Check the length. After you've cut your bangs, check the length in the mirror. If they're too long, you can always go back and trim a little bit more off. If they're too short, you'll have to wait for them to grow out.
  7. Style your bangs. Once you're happy with the length, style your bangs as desired. You can blow-dry them straight, curl them, or leave them natural.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can create split ends and make your hair look frizzy.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. Start with a longer length and gradually work your way up to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a water spray bottle to keep your hair damp while cutting. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your bangs are even.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. It's always better to be safe than sorry, so start with a longer length and work your way up.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own bangs:

  • How often should I trim my bangs?
    It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • How do I know what length to cut my bangs?
    It's always better to start with a longer length and gradually work your way up. You can always cut more off, but you can't put it back on.
  • Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?
    It's better to cut your bangs when they're dry so that you can see exactly how much you're cutting off.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Bangs

After you've cut your own bangs, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut your own bangs at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have the perfect bangs in no time!
