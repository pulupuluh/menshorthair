How To Get Rid Of Brassy Tones In Blonde Hair

Written by William Feb 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Blonde hair is undoubtedly beautiful, but it can be a challenge to maintain its vibrancy. One of the most common problems that blondes face is brassy tones. Brassy tones are those unwanted warm, yellow or orange tones that can make blonde hair look dull and lifeless. Fortunately, there are several ways to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair. In this article, we will explore how to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Description

Brassy tones in blonde hair occur when the hair is exposed to too much heat, sun, or chlorine. These environmental factors can cause the hair to become dry and brittle, which can lead to the loss of its natural shine and color. The good news is that there are several ways to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair, including using toners, purple shampoos, and hair masks.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair

There are several ways to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair, including:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a must-have for blondes. It contains purple pigments that neutralize brassy tones and restore the hair's natural color. To use, wet your hair and apply the purple shampoo, leaving it on for 5-10 minutes before rinsing it out. Use it once or twice a week for best results.

2. Try a Hair Toner

A hair toner is a semi-permanent hair color that helps to neutralize brassy tones. It's perfect for blondes who want to get rid of unwanted yellow or orange tones. Hair toners come in a variety of shades, so make sure to choose the one that matches your hair color. Apply the toner to wet hair and leave it on for 10-20 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use a Hair Mask

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that helps to restore the hair's natural shine and color. Look for a hair mask that contains ingredients like keratin, argan oil, or shea butter, which help to nourish and hydrate the hair. Apply the hair mask to wet hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair:

Step 1: Identify the Problem

The first step in getting rid of brassy tones in blonde hair is to identify the problem. Look for any yellow or orange tones in your hair, and determine the cause. Is it due to sun exposure, chlorine, or heat styling? Once you know the cause, you can take steps to prevent it from happening again.

Step 2: Choose the Right Product

Choose the right product to get rid of brassy tones in your hair. Purple shampoos, hair toners, and hair masks are all great options. Make sure to choose the product that matches your hair color and the severity of the brassy tones.

Step 3: Apply the Product

Apply the product to your hair according to the instructions on the packaging. For purple shampoos, wet your hair and apply the shampoo, leaving it on for 5-10 minutes before rinsing it out. For hair toners, apply the toner to wet hair and leave it on for 10-20 minutes before rinsing it out. For hair masks, apply the mask to wet hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 4: Rinse and Style

Rinse the product out of your hair thoroughly and style as usual. Your hair should look brighter and more vibrant, with no brassy tones in sight.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Tones in Blonde Hair

Prevention is key when it comes to brassy tones in blonde hair. Here are some tips to prevent brassy tones:

  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun
  • Rinse your hair with cool water after swimming in a chlorinated pool
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

FAQ

How often should I use a purple shampoo?

You should use a purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on the severity of the brassy tones in your hair.

Can I use a hair toner and a purple shampoo together?

Yes, you can use a hair toner and a purple shampoo together. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the packaging and leave each product on for the recommended amount of time.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and using the right products, you can get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair and keep it looking beautiful and vibrant.
