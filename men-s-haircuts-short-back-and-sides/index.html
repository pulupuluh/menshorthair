Men s Haircuts Short Back And Sides A Relaxed Guide

Written by Petter Feb 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and low-maintenance hairstyle, men's haircuts short back and sides might just be the perfect choice for you. This type of haircut is versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice among men of all ages and hair types. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about men's haircuts short back and sides, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Description

Men's haircuts short back and sides are characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This creates a contrast between the top and sides, which can be styled in a variety of ways depending on your preference. The length of the hair can vary from a few millimeters to a few inches, depending on how short you want to go.

How to

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut short back and sides, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Choose your length

The first step in getting a men's haircut short back and sides is to decide how short you want to go. This will depend on your personal preference, hair type, and face shape. If you're not sure what length to choose, ask your barber for advice.

Step 2: Communicate with your barber

Once you've decided on the length, it's important to communicate your preferences to your barber. Show them a picture of the style you want or describe it in detail. Make sure you're both on the same page before they start cutting your hair.

Step 3: Cut the sides and back

The barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. This is usually done with clippers, although some barbers may use scissors or a combination of both.

Step 4: Cut the top

Once the sides and back are done, the barber will move on to the top of your hair. They will cut it to the desired length and blend it into the sides and back for a seamless look.

Step 5: Style your hair

After your haircut is complete, you can style your hair in a variety of ways depending on your preference. You can use a hair product like wax or pomade to add texture and hold, or you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a men's haircut short back and sides:

  • Choose a length that works well with your hair type and face shape.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber to ensure you get the style you want.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims to keep it looking sharp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts short back and sides:

Is a men's haircut short back and sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a men's haircut short back and sides can work for all hair types, although the length and styling may vary depending on your hair texture.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, men's haircuts short back and sides are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways depending on the occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut short back and sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a men's haircut short back and sides with confidence and ease. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this hairstyle is sure to impress.
