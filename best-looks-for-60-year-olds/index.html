Best Looks For 60 Year Olds How To Look Fabulous In Your Golden Years

Welcome to our guide on the best looks for 60 year olds. If you're in your golden years, you may be wondering what styles and trends are appropriate for your age. We've compiled expert tips and advice to help you look and feel your best. From hair care to fashion tips, we've got you covered. Read on to discover the best looks for 60 year olds.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Fashion Tips
  • Makeup Tips
  • Accessories

Hair Care

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle. However, that doesn't mean you can't have fabulous hair in your 60s. Here are some hair care tips for 60 year olds:

1. Keep it Short

Short haircuts are perfect for women in their 60s. They're easy to maintain and can make you look younger. Consider a bob or pixie cut for a chic and modern look.

2. Add Volume

If you have thinning hair, consider adding volume with a volumizing shampoo or hair spray. You can also use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.

3. Embrace Gray Hair

Gray hair is beautiful and can be very flattering. If you're tired of coloring your hair, consider embracing your gray hair. You can also add highlights or lowlights to give your hair dimension.

Fashion Tips

When it comes to fashion, there are no hard and fast rules. However, there are a few tips that can help you look and feel your best:

1. Embrace Comfortable Clothing

Gone are the days of uncomfortable clothing. In your 60s, you should prioritize comfort over fashion. Look for clothing that is comfortable and easy to wear, such as loose-fitting pants and flowy dresses.

2. Invest in Classic Pieces

Classic pieces never go out of style. Invest in timeless pieces such as a trench coat, blazer, and a little black dress. These pieces can be dressed up or down and will never go out of style.

3. Accessorize

Accessories can take an outfit from drab to fab. Consider adding a statement necklace, scarf, or hat to your outfit to add some personality and flair.

Makeup Tips

Makeup can be a fun way to enhance your natural beauty. Here are some makeup tips for 60 year olds:

1. Moisturize

As we age, our skin tends to become drier. Moisturizing your skin can help prevent wrinkles and keep your skin looking youthful.

2. Use a Light Hand

Less is more when it comes to makeup in your 60s. Use a light hand when applying foundation and avoid heavy eye makeup. A natural, fresh-faced look is always in style.

3. Experiment with Lipstick

Lipstick is a fun way to add some color to your makeup look. Experiment with different shades of lipstick to find one that flatters your skin tone.

Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit. Here are some accessory tips for 60 year olds:

1. Invest in a Good Handbag

A good handbag can elevate any outfit. Look for a high-quality handbag that is both functional and stylish.

2. Wear Statement Jewelry

Statement jewelry can add some personality and flair to your outfit. Look for bold pieces such as a chunky necklace or oversized earrings.

3. Don't Forget Your Glasses

If you wear glasses, don't forget to incorporate them into your outfit. Look for glasses that flatter your face shape and add some style to your look.

FAQ

What colors should I wear in my 60s?

There are no hard and fast rules when it comes to colors. However, some colors that tend to be flattering on 60 year olds include navy, burgundy, and emerald green.

Can I still wear jeans in my 60s?

Absolutely! Jeans can be a staple in any wardrobe. Look for high-waisted jeans that are comfortable and flattering.

How can I make my eyes look younger?

Avoid heavy eye makeup and focus on using brightening products such as concealer and highlighter. You can also try using a lash serum to make your lashes look fuller and thicker.

What type of shoes should I wear in my 60s?

Comfortable shoes should be a priority in your 60s. Look for shoes with a low heel or flats that are comfortable and stylish.

Do I have to cut my hair short in my 60s?

No, you don't have to cut your hair short in your 60s. However, short haircuts are often more flattering and easier to maintain.

Conclusion

There you have it, our guide on the best looks for 60 year olds. Remember, there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to fashion and beauty. The most important thing is to feel comfortable and confident in your own skin. Experiment with different styles and trends to find what works best for you.
