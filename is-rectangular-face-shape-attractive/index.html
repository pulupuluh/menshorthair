Is Rectangular Face Shape Attractive? - Expert Tips and Advice

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with angular features. This face shape is often described as resembling an oblong shape. Many people wonder if a rectangular face shape is attractive, as it is not as commonly seen as other face shapes. However, with the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories, a rectangular face shape can be just as beautiful as any other face shape.


How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you are unsure what your face shape is, there are a few simple steps you can follow to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror in a well-lit area.
  2. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  3. Examine the shape of your face and look for the following characteristics:
    • Forehead: Is it wider than your jawline?
    • Cheekbones: Are they the widest part of your face?
    • Jawline: Is it angular or rounded?
  4. Compare your face to the following shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and rectangular.
  5. Determine which shape your face most closely resembles.

Tips for Enhancing a Rectangular Face Shape

Hairstyles:

When it comes to hairstyles for rectangular face shapes, the goal is to create width and volume around the forehead, cheeks, and jawline. Some great hairstyles for rectangular face shapes include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Bob with volume

Makeup:

Makeup can be used to enhance the natural features of a rectangular face shape. Here are a few makeup tips for rectangular face shapes:

  • Contour the cheekbones to create more definition
  • Use blush to add color and warmth to the cheeks
  • Focus on the eyes with bold eye makeup or false lashes
  • Highlight the cupid's bow to create the illusion of a fuller, more rounded lip shape

FAQs

Is a rectangular face shape rare?

Yes, a rectangular face shape is less common than other face shapes such as oval or round. However, this does not mean that a rectangular face shape is less attractive.

What are the best accessories for a rectangular face shape?

Accessories such as statement earrings or necklaces can draw attention to the face and create balance. Avoid long, thin earrings that can elongate the face even more.

Can a rectangular face shape wear a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can work for a rectangular face shape, but it is important to add volume and texture to the hair to create balance. Consider adding layers or opting for a messy, tousled look.


Hair Care for Rectangular Face Shapes

In addition to choosing the right hairstyles, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Condition regularly to keep hair hydrated and soft
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
