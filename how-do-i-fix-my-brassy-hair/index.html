How Do I Fix My Brassy Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of looking in the mirror and seeing brassy hair staring back at you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with blonde or light-colored hair struggle with brassiness, especially after dyeing or bleaching their hair. But the good news is that fixing brassy hair is easier than you might think. In this article, we'll walk you through how to fix your brassy hair and get the bright, beautiful locks you've always wanted.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Fix Brassy Hair

Before we get started, it's important to understand what causes brassy hair in the first place. Brassy hair is caused by a buildup of warm tones in the hair, which can happen for a variety of reasons, including exposure to sunlight, hard water, and certain styling products. The good news is that there are several ways to fix brassy hair and restore your locks to their former glory.

1. Tone Your Hair

One of the most effective ways to fix brassy hair is to tone it using a purple or blue shampoo. These shampoos work by neutralizing warm tones and restoring cool tones to the hair. To use a toning shampoo, simply wet your hair, apply the shampoo, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out. You can use a toning shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your hair's color.

2. Use a Color-Depositing Conditioner

If your hair is severely brassy, you may need to use a color-depositing conditioner to restore its color. These conditioners work by depositing pigments into the hair to neutralize warm tones and restore cool tones. To use a color-depositing conditioner, simply apply it to your hair after shampooing and leave it on for the recommended amount of time before rinsing it out.

3. Get a Professional Toning Treatment

If you're not comfortable toning your hair at home, you can always visit a professional stylist for a toning treatment. A stylist can use a variety of toning products and techniques to restore your hair's color and get rid of brassy tones. This option may be more expensive than toning your hair at home, but it can also be more effective.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to fixing brassy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a purple or blue toning shampoo.
  2. Apply a color-depositing conditioner.
  3. Rinse out the conditioner after the recommended amount of time.
  4. Repeat the process once or twice a week, depending on your hair's needs.
  5. If your hair is severely brassy, visit a professional stylist for a toning treatment.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair

Preventing brassy hair is always easier than fixing it, so here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking cool and bright:

  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sunlight.
  • Use a shower filter to remove hard water minerals.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat-styling tools.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular shampoo instead of toning shampoo?

A: While regular shampoo can help clean your hair, it won't help neutralize warm tones. Toning shampoo is specifically designed to help fix brassy hair, so it's your best bet.

Q: How often should I use toning shampoo?

A: It depends on your hair's needs, but once or twice a week is usually enough to maintain your hair's color.

Q: Can I use toning shampoo on colored hair?

A: Yes, toning shampoo is safe to use on colored hair. In fact, it can help maintain your hair's color by neutralizing warm tones.

Hair Care for Brassy Hair

In addition to using toning products, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent further brassiness. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry it out.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can fix your brassy hair and keep it looking beautiful and healthy.
