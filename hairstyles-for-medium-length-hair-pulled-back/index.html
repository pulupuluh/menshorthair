Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Pulled Back

Written by Luffy Apr 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how versatile it can be. From beachy waves to sleek updos, there are so many hairstyles that you can try. One of the easiest and most popular ways to style medium length hair is by pulling it back. In this blog post, we will provide you with some of the best hairstyles for medium length hair pulled back. Follow our tips and tricks to achieve the perfect pulled back hairstyle.

Description

Medium length hair is defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length is perfect for those who want to experiment with different hairstyles. When it comes to pulling back medium length hair, you can choose from a variety of styles including ponytails, buns, and braids. The key is to find a style that works for your face shape and hair type.

How To

If you want to achieve a pulled back hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Decide which style you want to try. If you want a sleek look, try a low ponytail. If you want something more casual, try a messy bun.
  3. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.
  4. If you want to add volume, tease the crown of your hair with a comb before pulling it back.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  6. Finish with hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step By Step

Low Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. If you want to add volume, tease the crown of your hair with a comb before pulling it back.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep your style in place.

Messy Bun

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  6. Finish with hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pulled back hairstyle:

  • Use a comb to create a clean part before pulling back your hair.
  • If you have thin hair, try using a volumizing mousse to add texture.
  • If you have thick hair, try using a smoothing serum to tame any flyaways.
  • If you want to add a pop of color, try using a colorful elastic band or scrunchie.
  • If you want a more polished look, try using a hair straightener to smooth out any bumps or kinks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for medium length hair pulled back:

What is the best way to secure a ponytail?

The best way to secure a ponytail is by using an elastic band. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

How do I create a messy bun?

To create a messy bun, start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail. Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Can I still wear a hat with a pulled back hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with a pulled back hairstyle. Try wearing a baseball cap or beanie for a casual look.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles for medium length hair pulled back, it's time to try them out for yourself. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
