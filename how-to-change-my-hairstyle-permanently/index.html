How To Change Your Hairstyle Permanently A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to change your look for good? If so, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we will show you how to change your hairstyle permanently. From trendy cuts to bold colors, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Changing your hairstyle permanently means making a drastic change to your current look. It's not just a trim or a touch-up; it's a whole new style. This can be intimidating for some, but it's also exciting. With the right guidance and tools, you can achieve the look you've always wanted.

How to

The first step in changing your hairstyle permanently is to decide what you want. Do you want a shorter cut? Longer hair? A different color? Once you have a general idea, it's time to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine what will look best on you and provide guidance on how to achieve it.

Next, it's time to make the cut. This is where the permanent part comes in. A permanent hairstyle change typically involves cutting off a significant amount of hair or adding extensions. This process can take a few hours, depending on the length and complexity of the cut.

After the cut, it's time to color. Again, this will depend on the look you're going for. If you want a subtle change, highlights or lowlights might do the trick. If you want something bold, like a bright red or blue, you'll need to bleach your hair first.

Once the cut and color are complete, it's time to style. Your stylist will likely show you how to style your new look so you can maintain it at home. They may also recommend certain products to help keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Step by Step

1. Decide what type of change you want to make

2. Consult with a professional stylist

3. Make the cut

4. Color your hair

5. Style your new look

Tips

- Bring pictures to your stylist to help them understand what you're looking for

- Be open to suggestions from your stylist

- Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a permanent hairstyle change?

A: Prices vary depending on the salon and the complexity of the cut and color. Expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $500.

Q: Will a permanent hairstyle change damage my hair?

A: It's possible, but a professional stylist will take steps to minimize damage. They may use special products or techniques to protect your hair.

Hair Care

After a permanent hairstyle change, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid heat styling as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray first.

Also, be sure to schedule regular haircuts to maintain your new look. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Changing your hairstyle permanently can be a big decision, but it can also be a lot of fun. With the right guidance and care, you can achieve the look you've always wanted. Good luck!
