How to Style Long Thick Curly Hair

Long thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right styling techniques, you can achieve a look that is both effortless and chic. Here are some popular hairstyles to try:

1. Beach Waves

This is a classic style that looks great on long, thick, curly hair. To achieve beach waves, start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it up with your fingers. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair.

2. Braids

Braids are a great way to style long, thick curly hair. You can try a variety of braids, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. To create a French braid, start by sectioning your hair into three parts at the crown of your head. Begin braiding your hair, adding in more hair from each side as you go. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie. Repeat on the other side of your head.

3. Bun

A bun is a simple yet elegant style that can be achieved with long, thick, curly hair. To create a bun, start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. Add some hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some popular hairstyles to try, here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve these looks:

1. Beach Waves

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it up with your fingers.
  3. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  4. Use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

2. Braids

  1. Section your hair into three parts at the crown of your head.
  2. Begin braiding your hair, adding in more hair from each side as you go.
  3. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

3. Bun

  1. Pull your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Add hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long, thick, curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my long thick curly hair?

It is recommended to wash your long thick curly hair once or twice a week to prevent it from drying out. You can also use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair fresh.

2. How can I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy?

To prevent frizz, try to avoid touching your hair too much and use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. You can also sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for long thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long, thick, curly hair with ease. Experiment with different looks and find the style that works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
