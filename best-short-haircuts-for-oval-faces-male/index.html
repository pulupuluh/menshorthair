Best Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Male

Written by Luffy Jun 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you an oval-faced male looking for the perfect short haircut? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for oval faces, along with tips and tricks for styling and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval faces are characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, narrowing to a slightly pointed chin. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of haircuts and styles.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face, it is important to consider the balance of the face. The goal is to enhance the natural symmetry and proportions of the face, rather than exaggerate any one feature.

Step by Step

1. Determine your hair type

The first step in choosing a short haircut is to determine your hair type. This will affect the texture, volume, and overall appearance of your hair.

2. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it is important to consider your lifestyle and daily routine. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, opt for a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling.

3. Consult with a professional

If you are unsure which short haircut would best suit your face shape and hair type, consult with a professional stylist. They can offer expert advice and help you find the perfect cut for your needs.

Tips

1. Keep it simple

With an oval face, less is often more. Simple, classic haircuts are often the most flattering and versatile.

2. Experiment with texture

Adding texture to your hair can create volume and dimension, which can be especially flattering for oval faces. Consider adding layers or opting for a tousled, messy look.

3. Don't be afraid to try something new

While classic haircuts are often the most flattering for oval faces, don't be afraid to try something new and trendy. Just be sure to consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes.

FAQ

1. Can oval-faced males pull off a buzz cut?

Yes, oval-faced males can pull off a buzz cut. However, it is important to consider your hair type and personal style before opting for this look.

2. What short haircuts are most flattering for oval faces?

Short haircuts that are most flattering for oval faces include the crew cut, the buzz cut, the side part, and the textured crop.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, be sure to use high-quality hair products and follow a regular hair care routine. This may include regular trims, conditioning treatments, and styling with a quality product.

With these tips and tricks, you are sure to find the perfect short haircut for your oval face. Whether you opt for a classic crew cut or a trendy textured crop, be sure to consult with a professional stylist and follow a regular hair care routine to keep your hair looking its best.
