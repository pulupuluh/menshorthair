What Is The Best Hair Gel For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 · 9 min read

When it comes to styling thick curly hair, finding the right hair gel is crucial. The best hair gel for thick curly hair should provide hold, definition, and frizz control while keeping your curls soft and bouncy. In this article, we will explore the top hair gels for thick curly hair in 2023.

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, and it requires specific products to maintain its health and beauty. Hair gel is an essential styling product for curly hair, as it helps to define and hold curls for an extended period. The ideal hair gel for thick curly hair should provide hold without sacrificing moisture or leaving a sticky residue.

How to Choose the Right Hair Gel for Thick Curly Hair

When choosing a hair gel for thick curly hair, it is essential to consider the following factors:

Hold

The hold of the hair gel determines how well it keeps your curls in place. For thick curly hair, you need a hair gel with a strong hold to prevent your curls from falling apart.

Frizz Control

Frizz is a common problem for those with curly hair, and a good hair gel should help to control frizz. Look for hair gels that contain ingredients like argan oil or shea butter, which help to keep hair moisturized and frizz-free.

Ingredients

The ingredients in the hair gel are essential to consider, especially if you have sensitive skin. Look for hair gels that contain natural ingredients like aloe vera or chamomile, as they are gentle on the scalp and hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Hair Gel for Thick Curly Hair

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect hairstyle with hair gel:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, making it easier to style with hair gel.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel

Take a small amount of hair gel and rub it between your palms. Apply it evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Define Your Curls

Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to define your curls while the hair gel is still wet. This will help to create a more defined and polished look.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

You can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to set your curls and create a more defined look.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

To lock in your style, finish with a light hairspray. This will help to keep your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful, and voluminous curls:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Thick curly hair needs extra moisture to stay healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft, shiny, and frizz-free.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair and make it prone to breakage. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use hair gel to style them.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using brushes or fine-tooth combs on your hair, as they can cause breakage and damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

4. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz while you sleep. You can also use a silk or satin scarf to wrap your hair at night.

FAQ

1. Can I Use Hair Gel on Dry Hair?

Yes, you can use hair gel on dry hair to refresh your curls or add volume. Apply a small amount of hair gel to your fingertips and scrunch it into your hair.

2. How Often Should I Use Hair Gel on My Hair?

You can use hair gel as often as needed, but it is essential to wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup.

3. Can Hair Gel Cause Hair Loss?

Hair gel does not cause hair loss, but using too much of it or not washing it out properly can cause product buildup and scalp irritation.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Thick curly hair requires specific care to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your curls looking healthy and bouncy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to shield your hair from the sun.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair healthy and strong. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

With the right hair gel and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls that are healthy and bouncy. Experiment with different hair gels to find the one that works best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
