How To Make Short Layered Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short layered hair can be a versatile and stylish look, but it can also be a challenge to style and maintain. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are ways to make your short layered hair look good and keep it looking its best. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks for making the most of your short layered hair.

Description

Short layered hair is a popular style that can be worn by anyone. It is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, and it can be styled in many different ways. However, it can also be a challenge to maintain and style, especially if you are new to the look. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks for making your short layered hair look good and keeping it healthy.

How to

1. Use the Right Products

When it comes to short layered hair, using the right products is key. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays, pomades, and waxes. These products will help you create volume and texture, and they will also help your hair hold its shape throughout the day.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Short layered hair is versatile, and there are many different styles you can try. Experiment with different looks, such as a messy pixie cut, a sleek bob, or a tousled shag. Play around with your hair until you find a style that you love.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture. Short layered hair can look great with natural curls or waves, and it can be easier to style than straight hair. Use a curling iron or a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture.

4. Add Some Color

Adding some color to your short layered hair can be a great way to add dimension and interest to your look. Consider getting highlights or lowlights, or try a bold new color. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and is easy to maintain.

5. Use the Right Tools

When styling short layered hair, it's important to use the right tools. A good quality blow dryer, flat iron, and curling iron can all make a big difference in how your hair looks. Look for tools that are specifically designed for short hair, as they will be easier to use and will give you better results.

Step by Step

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all of the product.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Be careful not to rub your hair too vigorously, as this can cause breakage and damage.

3. Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing spray or pomade. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, focusing on the roots and ends.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to add volume and texture.

5. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired. You can create loose curls, sleek waves, or a straight and polished look.

6. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products
- Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle
- Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance
- Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
- Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as short hair is more prone to breakage

FAQ

1. How often should I wash my short layered hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people with short hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others need to wash it every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with short layered hair?

Absolutely! Short layered hair can be styled in many different ways, including updos and ponytails. Use bobby pins and hair elastics to create a variety of styles.

3. Will adding color damage my short layered hair?

Adding color to your hair can be damaging, but it doesn't have to be. Be sure to choose a professional stylist who uses high-quality products and knows how to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Keeping your short layered hair healthy is important for maintaining its style and texture. Be sure to use products that are designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance, and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your short layered hair look good and keep it looking its best. Experiment with different styles and products, and don't be afraid to try something new. With a little bit of effort and care, you can have a stylish and versatile look that you'll love.
