What Is A 4 On Top Haircut

Written by Christine Mar 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for something new? A 4 on top haircut might be just what you need! This trendy hairstyle is quite popular among men and features a short, textured cut on the top with tapered sides and back. In this article, we will discuss what a 4 on top haircut is, how to get it, and how to maintain it.

Description

The 4 on top haircut is a short and stylish cut that is versatile and easy to maintain. It features a textured top that is usually about 4 inches long, hence the name. The sides and back are tapered, which creates a seamless transition between the top and sides. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks modern and fashionable.

How to Get a 4 on Top Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 4 on top haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. It's important to bring in a photo or a clear description of the haircut you're looking for to avoid any miscommunications. Here's what you can expect during your haircut appointment:

Step 1: Consultation

Your barber or stylist will start by asking you about the style you want and any specific preferences you may have. They may also examine your hair texture, thickness, and face shape to determine the best approach for your haircut.

Step 2: Cutting the Top

The top of your hair will be cut to about 4 inches in length and texturized to create a messy, textured look. Your stylist may use scissors or clippers to achieve the desired length and texture.

Step 3: Tapering the Sides and Back

The sides and back of your hair will be tapered gradually, using clippers or scissors. The length will gradually decrease as your stylist moves towards the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Styling

Your stylist will finish off the haircut by styling your hair with a product of your choice. They may use pomade, wax, or gel to create a messy or slicked-back look, depending on your preferences.

Tips for Maintaining a 4 on Top Haircut

Once you have your 4 on top haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Try different styling products to create different looks.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a 4 on top haircut?

A 4 on top haircut can work well with most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for oval or square-shaped faces.

How often should I get a touch-up?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Can I style my hair differently with a 4 on top haircut?

Yes! A 4 on top haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can experiment with different products and styling techniques to create different looks.

Hair Care

It's essential to take good care of your hair to maintain the health and appearance of your 4 on top haircut. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance 4 on top haircut that looks great and feels great. Remember to visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups and maintain your hair care routine to keep your hair looking healthy and strong.
