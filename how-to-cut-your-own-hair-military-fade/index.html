How To Cut Your Own Hair Military Fade Step By Step Guide

How to Cut Your Own Hair Military Fade

Cutting your own hair military fade might sound challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home. Below are the steps you need to follow to achieve the military fade haircut:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather the necessary tools. You will need a clipper, scissors, comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Start with a longer guard on your clipper and cut the top of your hair. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.

Step 3: Change to a shorter guard on your clipper and create a line around the sides and back of your head. The line should be about an inch above your ear.

Step 4: Blend the line by using a longer guard and moving the clipper in an upward motion. Do this until the hair blends seamlessly from the top to the sides.

Step 5: Use scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a more natural look.

Step 6: Clean up the neck and sideburns by using a trimmer or a razor.

Step 7: Check the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Tip 1: Start with a longer guard and work your way to a shorter one. It's easier to take more hair off than to add it back.

Tip 2: Use the mirror to check the back of your head. It might be helpful to use two mirrors to see all angles.

Tip 3: Don't rush the process. Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the military fade haircut without clippers?

A: It's possible, but it might be more challenging. Clippers make it easier to create a clean and even cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's crucial to take care of it to maintain the style. Below are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the military fade haircut at home. Remember to take your time and be patient. Happy cutting!
