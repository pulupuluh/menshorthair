Is Curly Hair Fashionable In 2020

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair has always been a topic of discussion in the fashion industry. The trend for curly hair has been on the rise in recent years, and it's not slowing down in 2020. In this article, we'll discuss why curly hair is fashionable in 2020, how to care for curly hair, and tips for styling and maintaining your curls.

Why Curly Hair is Fashionable in 2020

Curly hair has always been a symbol of uniqueness and individuality. In 2020, this trend is stronger than ever. Celebrities like Zendaya, Tracee Ellis Ross, and Solange Knowles are showing off their natural curls on the red carpet, and social media influencers are embracing their natural texture. The curly hair movement is all about embracing your natural beauty.

Curly hair is also versatile. You can wear it in a variety of styles, from loose waves to tight coils. It's also easy to play with color and highlights. Curly hair is the perfect canvas for experimenting with new and trendy hair colors.

How to Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a bit of extra effort, but it's worth it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

1. Use a gentle shampoo:

Curly hair is naturally dry, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for sulfate-free shampoos that are designed for curly hair.

2. Condition, condition, condition:

Curly hair needs lots of moisture to stay healthy. Use a rich, hydrating conditioner every time you wash your hair. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

3. Avoid heat:

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage curly hair. Try to air dry your hair as much as possible, and avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis.

4. Use a diffuser:

A diffuser is a must-have tool for curly hair. It helps to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair gently and enhance your natural curls.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls every time. Here are some tips for styling your curly hair:

1. Use the right products:

Choose styling products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl creams or gels. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

2. Finger comb your hair:

Curly hair is more delicate than straight hair, so it's important to be gentle when styling. Use your fingers to comb through your hair instead of a brush or comb.

3. Experiment with different styles:

Curly hair is versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Try a messy bun, a half-up, half-down style, or a braided updo.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Straightening your hair too frequently can damage your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with curly hair can go a week without washing, while others need to wash their hair every two or three days. Listen to your hair and wash it when it feels dirty or oily.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care, but with the right tools and techniques, you can keep your curls looking beautiful and healthy. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

1. Get regular trims:

Regular trims can help to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

2. Protect your hair at night:

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.

3. Embrace your natural texture:

Don't be afraid to let your natural curls shine. Embrace your unique texture and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Conclusion

Curly hair is fashionable in 2020 and beyond. It's a trend that celebrates individuality and diversity. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can keep your curls looking healthy, beautiful, and on-trend.
