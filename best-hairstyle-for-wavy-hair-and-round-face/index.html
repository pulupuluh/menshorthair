Best Hairstyle For Wavy Hair And Round Face In 2023

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of trying to style your wavy hair to suit your round face? Do you find it difficult to decide which hairstyle will flatter your face shape and hair texture? If you answered yes, then you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best hairstyle for wavy hair and round face in 2023.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

When it comes to finding the best hairstyle for wavy hair and round face, there are a few things to consider. First, you need to determine the length of your hair. Short, medium, and long hairstyles can all work well with wavy hair and round faces, but the style you choose will depend on your personal preference and face shape.

Second, you need to consider the texture of your hair. Wavy hair can be thick or thin, and the texture will affect the way your hair falls and moves. Lastly, you need to think about your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on styling your hair. Some hairstyles require more maintenance than others.

How to

The best hairstyle for wavy hair and round face in 2023 is the layered bob. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to maintain, and looks great on almost everyone. Here’s how to achieve the layered bob:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2:

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.

Step 3:

Starting at the back, take a small section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you’ve cut the entire back section.

Step 4:

Move to the sides and repeat the same process.

Step 5:

Finally, move to the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length.

Step by Step

If you prefer to have your hair professionally cut, follow these steps:

Step 1:

Find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting wavy hair.

Step 2:

Show them pictures of the layered bob hairstyle you want.

Step 3:

Ask them to cut your hair in layers, with the shortest layer hitting just below your chin.

Step 4:

Ask for long layers around your face to frame it and create a slimming effect.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your wavy hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a layered bob?

A: Yes, you can! The layered bob is versatile, and you can wear your hair up in a ponytail, bun, or half-up style.

Q: Will the layered bob work for thin wavy hair?

A: Yes, it can! The layered bob is a great hairstyle for adding volume and movement to thin wavy hair.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. As a general rule, it’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish it and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and choosing the layered bob hairstyle, you can achieve a flattering and easy-to-maintain hairstyle for wavy hair and round face in 2023.
