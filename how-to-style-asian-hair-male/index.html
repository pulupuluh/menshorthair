How To Style Asian Hair Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Feb 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut? Do you want to change your hairstyle but don't know where to start? If you're an Asian male, you may have struggled with finding the right hairstyle that suits your features. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to style Asian hair male.

Table of Contents

  • Understanding Your Hair Type
  • Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Using the Right Styling Products
  • Hair Styling Techniques
  • Additional Tips
  • FAQ

Understanding Your Hair Type

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to understand your hair type. Asian hair is typically straight and thick, but it can also be curly or wavy. It's important to choose the right styling products that work with your hair type. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a stronger hold product. If you have thin hair, you may want to use a lighter product to avoid weighing down your hair.

Choosing the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut is crucial in styling Asian hair male. Some popular haircuts for Asian males include the undercut, side part, and messy fringe. The undercut is a versatile haircut that can be styled up or down. The side part is a classic style that works well for formal occasions. The messy fringe is a popular style that gives a casual and youthful look.

How to get the Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that involves shaving the sides and back of the head and leaving the top long. To get the undercut, you'll need to go to a barber. Ask your barber to shave the sides and back of your head with clippers. The length of the top can vary, but it's typically around 2-5 inches. To style the undercut, use a strong hold pomade and comb your hair to the side or back.

How to get the Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. To get the side part, you'll need to have medium to long hair on top. Ask your barber to cut your hair with scissors and create a side part. To style the side part, use a medium hold pomade and comb your hair to the side.

How to get the Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular style that can be achieved with shorter or longer hair. To get the messy fringe, you'll need to have longer hair on top. Ask your barber to cut your hair with scissors and create a messy fringe. To style the messy fringe, use a light hold pomade and mess up your hair with your fingers.

Using the Right Styling Products

Using the right styling products is crucial in achieving the perfect hairstyle. There are many different types of styling products, including pomade, wax, gel, and clay. It's important to choose the right product for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Pomade

Pomade is a versatile styling product that can be used to achieve a wide range of styles. It's typically used for slicked-back hairstyles, but it can also be used for messy hairstyles. Pomade comes in different holds, including light, medium, and strong. It's important to choose the right hold for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Wax

Wax is a popular styling product for creating messy hairstyles. It provides a natural look and a flexible hold. Wax comes in different holds, including light, medium, and strong. It's important to choose the right hold for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Gel

Gel is a strong hold styling product that's typically used for slicked-back hairstyles. It provides a shiny finish, but it can also make your hair look greasy if you use too much. Gel is best for thick and coarse hair.

Clay

Clay is a popular styling product for creating textured hairstyles. It provides a matte finish and a strong hold. Clay is best for thick and coarse hair.

Hair Styling Techniques

Now that you've chosen the right haircut and styling product, it's time to style your hair. Here are some hair styling techniques that you can use to achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Slicked-Back Hairstyle

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic style that's perfect for formal occasions. To achieve the slicked-back hairstyle, apply pomade to your hair and comb your hair back. Use a hair dryer to set the style.

Messy Hairstyle

The messy hairstyle is a popular style that gives a casual and youthful look. To achieve the messy hairstyle, apply wax to your hair and mess up your hair with your fingers. Use a hair dryer to set the style.

Additional Tips

Here are some additional tips that you can use to style your Asian hair male:

  • Use a hair dryer to set your hairstyle.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I style my hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without using any products, but it may not hold the style as well.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: To prevent your hair from getting frizzy, use a hair serum or oil to tame your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips for Asian hair male:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curlers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your Asian hair male. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the one that suits you best.
