Older Mens Hairstyles Medium Length A Guide To Looking Dapper

Written by Bardi Apr 20, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re an older gentleman, you might be looking for a new hairstyle that suits your age and personality. Medium-length hair is a great option that can work for all hair types and face shapes. In this article, we’ll discuss some of the best older mens hairstyles medium length and give you tips on how to achieve the look you want.

Description

Medium-length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many ways. This length allows for more creativity than short hair but is easier to manage than long hair. It can be worn straight, wavy, or curly and can be styled with a side part, slicked back, or messy.

How to

To achieve older mens hairstyles medium length, there are a few things you need to do. Firstly, choose the right length for your face shape and hair type. A good rule of thumb is to keep the hair a few inches above the shoulders. Secondly, decide on the style you want – do you want it slicked back, messy or something else?

Step-by-Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving an older mens hairstyle medium length:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go
  4. Use a comb to create a side part or slick it back
  5. Finish with a hairspray to keep the style in place

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect older mens hairstyle medium length:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape
  • Invest in a good quality styling product
  • Don’t overuse hair products – less is more
  • Try different styles until you find one that you like
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles

FAQ

What are some popular older mens hairstyles medium length?

Popular older mens hairstyles medium length include the slicked-back look, side part, messy look, and curly hair.

What styling products should I use for older mens hairstyles medium length?

You can use a variety of styling products for older mens hairstyles medium length, including pomade, wax, gel, and hairspray. Choose the product that works best for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It’s best to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve a dapper older mens hairstyle medium length that suits your personality and style. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!
