What Bangs Suit My Face Shape

Written by Christine Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure which style would suit your face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing what bangs suit different face shapes, how to style them, and tips on how to take care of your new fringe. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Right Bangs
  • Tips on Styling Your Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Bangs

Description

Bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a drastic change. However, choosing the right style for your face shape is crucial to ensure your new fringe flatters your features. The right bangs can accentuate your eyes, cheekbones, and jawline while also balancing out your facial proportions.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different types of bangs, it's essential to determine your face shape. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror. Then, step back and compare your outline to the four face shape descriptions above.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Right Bangs

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's discuss what bangs would suit you best:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, opt for long, side-swept bangs that elongate your face. Avoid straight-cut bangs as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, go for a soft, rounded fringe that's longer on the sides. This will help soften your jawline and balance out your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any type of bangs will suit you. However, if you have a high forehead, consider getting a thick, straight-cut fringe to balance out your features.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, opt for a wispy, side-swept fringe that's shorter in the middle and longer on the sides. This will help balance out your forehead and chin. Avoid heavy, blunt-cut bangs as they can overpower your features.

Tips on Styling Your Bangs

Now that you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape, here are some tips on how to style them:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to achieve a smooth, polished look.
  • Use a hair straightener to smooth out any flyaways or frizz.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.
  • Experiment with different partings to switch up your look.

FAQ

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long and heavy.

Can I still wear a ponytail with bangs?

Absolutely! In fact, wearing a high ponytail with side-swept bangs can create a chic, effortless look.

What should I do if I don't like my new bangs?

Don't panic! Wait a week or two before making any drastic changes as it can take some time to get used to the new style. If you're still not happy, speak to your stylist and see if they can adjust the length or shape to better suit your preferences.

Hair Care Tips for Your Bangs

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to keep your new bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs as they can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Blot your bangs with a microfiber towel to remove excess water after washing.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from getting oily.

And there you have it! With these tips, you'll be able to choose the right bangs for your face shape and keep them looking stylish and healthy. Remember to communicate with your stylist and experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.
