How To Do A Guys Fade Haircut

Written by Oliver Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this article, we will guide you on how to do a guys fade haircut. The fade haircut is a popular hairstyle for men, and it involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving it longer on top. This hairstyle has been around for decades and has evolved into different variations. We will show you the steps, tips, and hair care for this style.

Description

The fade haircut is a short hairstyle that is tapered on the sides and back. It is called a "fade" because the hair gradually fades from short to longer as it goes up the head. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it is usually longer than the sides and back. The fade can be done with clippers or scissors, and it can be blended with different techniques to create different variations.

How to

To do a guys fade haircut, you will need a few tools: clippers, scissors, comb, clipper guards, and a mirror. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, and dry it with a towel. Make sure the hair is completely dry before cutting.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length of the hair on top and the sides. Use clipper guards to set the length on the sides and back. The hair should be shorter on the sides and gradually longer as it goes up the head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair on the sides and back with the clippers. Use a higher guard for the bottom and gradually switch to a lower guard as you move up. Blend the hair by using different guards or by using scissors to create a natural look.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Cut the hair on top using scissors. You can use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Blend the top with the sides by cutting the corners and edges.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Use scissors to trim any stray hairs and clean up the edges. Use a mirror to check the back and sides for any missed spots.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you do a guys fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard on the sides and gradually work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Blend the hair by using different guards or by using scissors to create a natural look.
  • Use a mirror to check the back and sides for any missed spots.
  • Experiment with different variations of the fade, such as a high fade or a low fade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a guys fade haircut:

Q: How long does a fade last?

A: A fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I do a fade haircut on my own?

A: It is possible to do a fade haircut on your own, but it is recommended to have someone help you, especially if you are not experienced.

Q: What is the difference between a fade and a taper?

A: A fade is a shorter version of a taper. A fade starts with a shorter length and gradually fades into a longer length. A taper starts with a longer length and gradually tapers into a shorter length.

Hair Care

To maintain a guys fade haircut, you should wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner. Use a comb to style the hair on top and use a trimmer to clean up the edges. You can also use hair gel or pomade to add texture and hold to the hair.

Now that you know how to do a guys fade haircut, you can try it out for yourself or help someone else achieve this popular hairstyle. Remember to follow the steps, tips, and hair care for the best results.
