How To Take Care Of Permanent Straightening Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have recently undergone permanent straightening hair treatment, you may be wondering how to maintain your sleek and smooth locks. Permanent straightening hair is a chemical process that alters the structure of your hair, leaving it straight and shiny. However, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain its health. In this article, we will discuss how to take care of permanent straightening hair.

How to Take Care of Permanent Straightening Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is designed for chemically straightened hair. These shampoos are formulated to protect your hair from damage and keep it healthy. Avoid using shampoos that contain sulphates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair Regularly

Use a conditioner that is designed for chemically straightened hair. These conditioners are formulated to nourish and hydrate your hair, leaving it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and blow dryers. These tools can cause damage to your hair and make it prone to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Step 4: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf. The sun can cause damage to your hair and make it dry and brittle.

Step 5: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and breakage. Trimming your hair regularly will keep it healthy and promote growth.

Tips for Taking Care of Permanent Straightening Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of permanent straightening hair:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Do not wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ: Taking Care of Permanent Straightening Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about taking care of permanent straightening hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Can I color my hair after permanent straightening?

Yes, you can color your hair after permanent straightening. However, it is important to wait at least two weeks after the treatment before coloring your hair.

Can I swim with permanent straightening hair?

Yes, you can swim with permanent straightening hair. However, it is important to wear a swim cap or tie your hair up to prevent it from getting wet.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Taking care of permanent straightening hair requires a bit of effort, but the results are worth it. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and maintain its sleek and smooth appearance. Remember to use the right products, avoid heat styling, protect your hair from the sun, and trim it regularly. With proper care, your permanent straightening hair will look beautiful and healthy for years to come.
