What Is A Messy Pixie Haircut

Written by Christine Sep 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A messy pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that has become increasingly popular among women in recent years. This style features short, choppy layers with a lot of texture and volume. It is a low maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a stylish, edgy look without spending a lot of time styling their hair.

Description

The messy pixie haircut is characterized by its short length and choppy layers. The hair is typically cut to just above the ears, with longer layers on top. The layers are cut in a way that creates a lot of texture and movement, giving the hair a messy, tousled look.

This style is perfect for those with naturally wavy or curly hair, as it allows for the hair to be styled in a way that enhances its natural texture. However, it can also be achieved with straight hair by using a curling iron or texturizing spray.

How to

If you are interested in getting a messy pixie haircut, it is important to find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting this style. The hairstylist will begin by cutting the hair to the desired length, typically just above the ears.

Next, they will add layers to the hair, cutting them in a way that creates texture and movement. The layers will be shorter on the sides and longer on top, allowing for the hair to be styled in a messy, tousled look.

To style the hair, simply apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to the hair and use your fingers to tousle it into place. This style is meant to look messy and undone, so don't worry about making it look perfect.

Step by Step

1. Find an experienced hairstylist who is familiar with cutting a messy pixie haircut.

2. Discuss with the hairstylist the length and texture you want for your hair.

3. Allow the hairstylist to cut your hair to the desired length, typically just above the ears.

4. The hairstylist will then add layers to your hair to create texture and movement.

5. Style your hair with a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade, using your fingers to tousle it into place.

Tips

- Use a texturizing spray or pomade to enhance the texture of your hair.

- Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

- Embrace the messy, undone look of this style.

FAQ

Q: Will a messy pixie haircut work for all hair types?

A: While this style is often associated with wavy or curly hair, it can be achieved with straight hair as well. Talk to your hairstylist about what will work best for your hair type.

Q: How often will I need to get my hair cut with this style?

A: This style requires regular trims to maintain its shape, typically every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for special occasions?

A: Absolutely! The beauty of this style is its versatility. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is crucial when maintaining a messy pixie haircut. Be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. If you must use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Regular trims are also important to maintain the shape and texture of your hair. Be sure to schedule appointments with your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy the edgy, stylish look of a messy pixie haircut for years to come.
