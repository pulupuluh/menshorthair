Best Hairstyles For Oval Face Shape Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 14, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you an oval-faced male looking for the best hairstyle that complements your facial structure? You're not alone. Many men struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters their face shape. Luckily, we've got you covered. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the best hairstyles for oval face shape male and give you the tips and tricks to maintain them. So, without further ado, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape Male
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Men with Oval Face Shape

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and balanced of all face shapes. It's characterized by a forehead that's slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. Men with oval face shapes can rock almost any hairstyle, from short to long. The key is to choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features and balances out your proportions.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape Male

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for oval face shape male is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and from ear to ear. If the measurements are roughly the same, then you have an oval face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to consider your hair type and texture. This will help you narrow down your hairstyle options. For example, if you have thick, wavy hair, you might want to consider a side-swept undercut or a messy fringe. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, a short, textured crop might work best.

Another factor to consider is your personal style and lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can quickly style in the morning, or are you willing to put in more effort to achieve a more polished look? Keep these factors in mind when choosing your hairstyle.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape Male

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape male:

1. Short Textured Crop

This hairstyle is perfect for men with fine hair. It's a low-maintenance look that's easy to style and maintain. The hair is cut short on the sides and back and left longer on top. The top is styled with a messy, textured look.

2. Undercut with Side-Swept Fringe

This hairstyle is ideal for men with thick, wavy hair. The sides and back are cut short, and the top is left longer. The hair is styled with a side-swept fringe that adds volume and texture.

3. Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on almost any face shape. This style is perfect for men with medium to thick hair. The hair is parted on one side and combed over to the other side.

4. Messy Fringe

This hairstyle is perfect for men with thick hair. The hair is cut in a messy, textured style with a longer fringe that falls over the forehead. This look is effortless and easy to style.

5. Long and Layered

Men with oval face shapes can rock longer hairstyles. This look is perfect for men with thick hair. The hair is cut in layers and left long. The layers add texture and movement to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Styling your hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and products, you can achieve the perfect look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that works with your hair type and texture.
  3. Use a comb or brush to style your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Maintaining your hairstyle is essential to keep it looking fresh and polished. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use high-quality hair products that work with your hair type and texture.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

FAQs

1. Can oval-faced men rock a buzz cut?

Yes, oval-faced men can rock a buzz cut. However, it's essential to choose a buzz cut that complements your facial features. A shorter buzz cut might work best for men with smaller facial features, while a longer buzz cut might work better for men with larger features.

2. Can oval-faced men wear long hairstyles?

Yes, oval-faced men can wear long hairstyles. Long hairstyles can add texture and movement to the hair, and they're perfect for men with thick hair. However, it's important to keep the hair well-maintained to prevent it from looking unkempt.

3. Can oval-faced men wear a middle part?

Yes, oval-faced men can wear a middle part. However, it's important to choose a middle part that complements your facial features. A middle part that's too severe might not work well for oval-faced men.

Hair Care for Men with Oval Face Shape

Proper hair care is essential for men with oval face shapes. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a high-quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, men with oval face shapes can look their best. Use this guide to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
