Mens Hairstyles 2020 High Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, then the mens hairstyles 2020 high fade is worth considering. This hairstyle has been popular for years and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we will explore the different variations of the high fade, how to style it, and share some tips on taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get the High Fade
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling the High Fade
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The high fade is a classic men's hairstyle that emphasizes the contrast between the longer hair on top and the short sides and back. The fade is a gradual taper that starts at the temples and fades into the skin. The high fade is characterized by the fade starting higher up on the head, usually around the temples or even higher. This creates a more dramatic contrast between the longer hair on top and the short sides and back.

How to Get the High Fade

To get the high fade, you'll need to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. They will use clippers to gradually taper the hair on the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer. The fade can be as high or low as you like, depending on your personal preference. It's important to choose a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with fades, as a poorly executed fade can ruin the entire look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style the high fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb it in the direction you want it to lay.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair.
  4. Using a hairdryer, blow dry your hair on low heat while using a brush to shape it into the desired style.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of product to define the style and hold it in place.

Tips for Styling the High Fade

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when styling your high fade:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type. If you have thick hair, a strong hold product may be necessary, while those with fine hair may need a lighter product.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. The high fade is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape of your fade and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs

What's the difference between a high fade and a low fade?

The main difference between a high fade and a low fade is where the fade starts. A high fade starts higher up on the head, usually around the temples or even higher, while a low fade starts lower down on the head, around the ear.

Is the high fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the high fade can work for all hair types, although the styling may vary depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How often do I need to visit the barber to maintain my high fade?

It's recommended to visit the barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your fade and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your high fade looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the mens hairstyles 2020 high fade with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a clean and classic look or a more edgy and trendy style, the high fade is a versatile and timeless haircut that will never go out of fashion.
