How To Style Long Naturally Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles for your long, naturally curly hair? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to style your long, naturally curly hair with ease. From braids to updos, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Long Naturally Curly Hair
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Long naturally curly hair is beautiful, but it can also be challenging to style. It’s essential to find the right techniques and products that work best for your hair type. With the right care and styling, you can achieve a variety of looks that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to Style Long Naturally Curly Hair

The following are some popular hairstyles for long naturally curly hair:

Braids

Braids are a great way to style long naturally curly hair. You can create a variety of braids, from Dutch braids to French braids, depending on your preference. Braids are also an excellent protective style for your hair, keeping it safe from damage while still looking stylish.

Updos

Updos are another popular hairstyle for long naturally curly hair. They can be worn for formal or casual events, and there are endless options when it comes to updos. From messy buns to sleek chignons, updos are a versatile and elegant way to style your hair.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their long, naturally curly hair while keeping it out of their face. This style is effortless to achieve and can be done in a matter of minutes. Plus, it looks great with any outfit.

Loose Waves

If you want to keep your hair down, loose waves are a great option. This style is effortless to achieve, and it looks beautiful with long naturally curly hair. You can use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural curls.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve loose waves:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections.
  5. Use a curling wand or iron to curl small sections of your hair.
  6. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it.
  7. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.
  8. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long naturally curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long naturally curly hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, washing your hair every two to three days is recommended for long naturally curly hair.

What products should I use for my hair?

Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy long naturally curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long naturally curly hair with ease. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for you. Embrace your curls and rock your beautiful hair with confidence!
