How to Style Guys Hair Messy

Messy hair has been a popular trend for guys in recent years. It's a great way to add some texture and personality to your look. The key to achieving messy hair is to make it look effortless. Here are some tips to help you style your hair in a messy way:

  • Start with clean hair
  • Use a lightweight styling product
  • Mess up your hair with your hands
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a messy hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a small amount of lightweight styling product, such as a texturizing cream, to your hair.
  4. Mess up your hair with your hands to create a tousled look.
  5. Use a blow dryer on low heat to add volume to your hair.
  6. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Messy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling messy hair:

  • Don't overdo it with the styling product. A little goes a long way.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures.
  • If you have longer hair, try tying it up in a messy bun or ponytail.
  • If you're in a rush, use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy hair:

Q: Can I achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a messy look with short hair. Use a texturizing cream to add some volume and mess up your hair with your hands.

Q: Is messy hair appropriate for work?

A: It depends on your workplace. If you work in a more conservative environment, it's best to stick to a more polished look. However, if your workplace allows for more creative expression, go for it!

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I want to achieve the messy look?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too oily. However, you can use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while achieving a messy look:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a low heat setting on your blow dryer and limit your use of hot tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a messy hairstyle that looks effortless and stylish. Remember to experiment with different styles and textures to find what works best for you. Have fun with it!
