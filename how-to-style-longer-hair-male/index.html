How To Style Longer Hair For Men A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for men in recent years. Whether you’re growing out your locks or have been sporting long hair for a while, it can be challenging to know how to style it. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to style longer hair male. From hair care tips to step-by-step instructions, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Longer Hair for Men
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Longer Hair Male
  • FAQs

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to style longer hair male, it’s essential to take care of your hair. Longer hair requires more maintenance than shorter hair, so it’s crucial to develop a hair care routine that works for you. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dye

How to Style Longer Hair for Men

Styling longer hair for men can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it’s easy to achieve a variety of looks. Here are a few popular ways to style longer hair male:

  • Man Bun
  • Ponytail
  • Half-Up Man Bun
  • Slicked Back
  • Loose Waves
  • Curly Hair
  • Surfer Hair

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s how to achieve three popular long hair styles for men:

Man Bun

  1. Brush your hair back into a ponytail at the top of your head
  2. Wrap the ponytail around itself to create a bun
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins
  4. Use a small amount of hair product to smooth down any flyaways

Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair back into a high ponytail at the top of your head
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie
  3. Use a small amount of hair product to smooth down any flyaways

Slicked Back

  1. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair gel to your hair
  2. Brush your hair back away from your face
  3. Use a comb to create a defined part if desired

Tips for Styling Longer Hair Male

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when styling longer hair male:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products that work for your hair type
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to style your hair
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQs

How often should I wash my long hair?

It’s best to wash your long hair 2-3 times a week with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Can I still use hot tools on my long hair?

Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

What hair products should I use for long hair?

Invest in high-quality hair products that work for your hair type. A good shampoo and conditioner, as well as a leave-in conditioner, can do wonders for long hair. You may also want to try a hair oil or serum to keep your hair smooth and shiny.

How can I prevent split ends?

Regular trims are essential to prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed.

What if I don’t like my long hair?

If you’re not happy with your long hair, consider cutting it. It’s just hair, and it will grow back.

Styling longer hair male can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it’s easy to achieve a variety of looks. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your long hair healthy and stylish.
