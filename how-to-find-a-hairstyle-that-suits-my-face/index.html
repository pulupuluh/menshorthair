How To Find A Hairstyle That Suits Your Face A Complete Guide

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles but not getting the desired look? Well, the problem may not be with the hairstyle itself, but rather with the fact that it doesn't suit your face shape. Yes, finding a hairstyle that complements your face shape is crucial to achieving the perfect look. In this article, we will guide you on how to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape.

How to Find a Hairstyle That Suits Your Face

Before we dive into the specifics, it's essential to understand the importance of face shape when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will suit you. Here are the basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Now that you know the different face shapes let's look at how to find a hairstyle that suits your face.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding a hairstyle that suits your face is to determine your face shape. One way to do this is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil. Alternatively, you can take a photo of yourself and trace the outline on the photo.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once you know your face shape, the next step is to research hairstyles that suit your face shape. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by searching online. Look for celebrities or models with a similar face shape to yours and see which hairstyles they wear.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will suit you. For example, if you have curly hair, a short bob may not be the best option. Similarly, if you have fine hair, a heavy fringe may not be suitable. Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is an expert in hair and can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist and show them photos of the hairstyles you like. They will be able to advise you on which hairstyles will suit you best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find a hairstyle that suits your face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles for your face shape
  3. Consider your hair texture
  4. Consult with your hairstylist

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality
  • Choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features
  • Try out different hairstyles before committing to one

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyles for a round face are those that add height and length to the face. This includes long layers, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile, and most hairstyles will suit this face shape. However, a center parting and long layers are particularly flattering for an oval face.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyles for a square face are those that soften the angles of the face. This includes long layers, side-swept bangs, and soft waves.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have found a hairstyle that suits your face, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after every wash
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and maintain the look of your new hairstyle.

Conclusion

Finding a hairstyle that suits your face shape is crucial to achieving the perfect look. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture. Remember to consider your lifestyle, personality, and best features when choosing a hairstyle. Consult with your hairstylist for expert advice, and take care of your hair to maintain the look. With these tips, you can finally find a hairstyle that suits your face and makes you feel confident.
