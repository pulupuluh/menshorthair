How To Make Your Own Clip In Hair Extensions At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to add length or volume to your hair without going to the salon? Clip in hair extensions are a great way to achieve this. But instead of buying expensive ones, why not make your own at home? In this article, we will guide you on how to make your own clip in hair extensions at home.

Description

Clip in hair extensions are a popular hair accessory that can be easily clipped onto natural hair to add volume, length, and color. They come in different lengths, textures, and colors. However, buying clip in hair extensions can be expensive, especially if you want to use them often. Making your own clip in hair extensions at home is a cost-effective way to achieve the same results.

How To

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair

The first step in making your own clip in hair extensions at home is to choose the right hair. You can either use synthetic or human hair. However, human hair is the best option as it looks more natural and can be styled like your own hair. You can buy human hair online or from a beauty supply store.

Step 2: Measure and Cut the Hair

Measure the hair extensions against your head to determine the length you want. Cut the hair to that length using scissors. Make sure to cut in a straight line to avoid uneven extensions.

Step 3: Attach the Clips

Attach the clips to the hair extensions. You can buy clips online or from a craft store. To attach the clips, sew them onto the hair extensions using a needle and thread. Make sure to sew them tightly to prevent them from falling off.

Step 4: Install the Hair Extensions

Now that your clip in hair extensions are ready, it’s time to install them. Start by parting your hair where you want to attach the extensions. Clip the rest of your hair up to make it easier to work with. Take one of the clip in extensions and attach it to your natural hair by snapping the clip onto your hair. Repeat this process with the other extensions until you have achieved the desired volume and length.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to ensure that the clip in hair extensions are evenly distributed.
  • Choose the right color that matches your natural hair to achieve a natural look.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. Start with a small amount and cut more if needed.
  • Take care of your clip in hair extensions by washing and conditioning them regularly.

FAQ

Q: Can I use synthetic hair for clip in hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can. However, synthetic hair does not look as natural as human hair and cannot be styled with heat.

Q: How long do clip in hair extensions last?

A: Clip in hair extensions can last up to a year if taken care of properly.

Q: Can I sleep with clip in hair extensions?

A: It is not recommended to sleep with clip in hair extensions as they can cause discomfort and damage to your natural hair.

Hair Care

To take care of your clip in hair extensions, wash and condition them regularly using sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Gently comb them using a wide-tooth comb and avoid using heat styling tools on them. Store them in a cool, dry place to prevent tangling.

By following this guide, you can make your own clip in hair extensions at home and achieve a natural look without breaking the bank. Have fun experimenting with different lengths and styles!
