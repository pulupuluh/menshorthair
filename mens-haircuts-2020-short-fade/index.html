Mens Haircuts 2020 Short Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 26, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on mens haircuts 2020 short fade. In this post, we’ll be covering everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle trend, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice. So, whether you’re looking for a fresh new look or just want to stay up-to-date with the latest hairstyles, this guide is for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The mens haircuts 2020 short fade is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that has been trending for a few years now. It is a short haircut that is tapered on the sides and back, gradually getting shorter towards the bottom. The top is left longer, usually with a few inches of length, which can be styled in various ways depending on your preference.

The short fade is a great option for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain. It works well for all hair types and face shapes, making it a versatile option for everyone.

How to

To get a mens haircuts 2020 short fade, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in short haircuts. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by deciding on the length of the fade you want. The shorter the fade, the more dramatic the contrast will be between the top and sides.
  2. Ask your barber to use clippers to gradually taper the sides and back, starting with a longer guard and working their way down to a shorter guard as they get closer to the bottom.
  3. Leave the top longer, usually around 2-3 inches, and ask your barber to add texture or layers if desired.
  4. Style the top as desired, using a pomade, wax, or other styling product to add texture and hold.

Step by step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide for getting a mens haircuts 2020 short fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and leave your hair clean and healthy.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Decide on the length of the fade you want, and communicate this clearly to your barber or hairstylist.
  4. Ask your barber to use clippers to gradually taper the sides and back, starting with a longer guard and working their way down to a shorter guard as they get closer to the bottom.
  5. Use scissors or clippers to trim the top to your desired length, leaving it longer than the sides and back.
  6. If desired, ask your barber to add texture or layers to the top using scissors or a razor.
  7. Style the top as desired, using a pomade, wax, or other styling product to add texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your mens haircuts 2020 short fade:

  • Choose a fade length that works well with your face shape and hair type. A shorter fade will create more contrast and draw attention to the top, while a longer fade will create a more gradual transition.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and maintenance to keep your fade looking fresh and sharp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts 2020 short fade:

Q: Is the short fade suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the short fade works well for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

Q: How often should I visit the barber for maintenance?

A: It’s recommended to visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups and maintenance.

Q: Can I style the top of my hair in different ways?

A: Yes, the top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, textured, or messy.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your mens haircuts 2020 short fade:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a styling product that provides hold and texture without weighing down your hair or leaving it greasy.
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and maintenance to keep your fade looking fresh and sharp.

That concludes our ultimate guide on mens haircuts 2020 short fade. We hope you found this post helpful and informative. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to leave them below.
