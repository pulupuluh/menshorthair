How To Cut Your Bangs Side Swept A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and looking for a change? Cutting your bangs side swept can be a great way to update your look. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your bangs side swept in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. By following our step-by-step guide, you can achieve a chic and stylish look in no time. Let's get started!

How to Cut Your Bangs Side Swept

Cutting your bangs side swept is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering style. Here's how to cut your bangs side swept:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your bangs, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections, one for your bangs and the other for the rest of your hair. Use a comb to create a straight line from the top of your head to your ear on both sides. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. To do this, brush your bangs straight down and hold them between your fingers at the desired length. It's best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using your scissors, cut your bangs at an angle. Start at the center and work your way outwards. This will create a soft and natural look. Avoid cutting straight across, as this can create a harsh and blunt appearance.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After cutting one side, check your progress in the mirror. Make sure both sides are even and adjust as needed. It's better to cut less than more, so take your time and be patient.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Once you're happy with the length, style your bangs by sweeping them to the side. Use a comb or your fingers to create a natural-looking part. You can also use a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Cutting Your Bangs Side Swept

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect side swept bangs:

  • Start with dry, clean hair
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Don't cut your bangs too short
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated
  • Ask a friend or professional stylist for help if needed

FAQ

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your bangs yourself as long as you follow the proper technique and use the right tools.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim your bangs a little shorter or style them differently to hide the mistake. If it's a major mistake, seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Now that you have your side swept bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent oil buildup
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and length

Cutting your bangs side swept can be a fun and easy way to update your look. By following our step-by-step guide and tips, you can achieve the perfect side swept bangs in no time. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed. Good luck!
