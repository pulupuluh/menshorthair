How To Figure Out What Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Bardi Apr 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle or looking for a change? Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with a little help, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personality. In this article, we will discuss how to figure out what hairstyle suits you, step by step.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the right hairstyle for you. The four common face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width, you have an oval face shape. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your face is almost as wide as it is long, you have a round face shape. If your face is angular with a strong jawline, you have a square face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type also plays a significant role in determining the right hairstyle for you. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle that is easier to manage. If you have fine, straight hair, longer hairstyles may work better for you. Consider your hair texture, density, and length when choosing a hairstyle.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be a factor in choosing the right hairstyle for you. Do you have a busy schedule and little time to style your hair? A low-maintenance hairstyle may be the best choice for you. Do you work in a professional setting? A classic, polished hairstyle may be more appropriate.

Step 4: Look for Inspiration

Before making a drastic change to your hairstyle, look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles for ideas. Consider the styles that catch your eye and whether they would work for your face shape and hair type.

Step 5: Consult with a Hairstylist

Finally, consult with a hairstylist. They can provide expert advice on what hairstyles would look best on you. Bring pictures of styles you like and discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also recommend products and techniques to maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Look for inspiration in magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for expert advice.
  • Be open to trying new styles, but also consider your comfort level.
  • Remember that hair grows back, so don't be afraid to make changes.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs and chin-length cuts. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles with layers that frame the face.

What hairstyles work best for fine, straight hair?

Longer hairstyles with layers and texture work best for fine, straight hair. Adding volume to the roots with a volumizing product can also create the illusion of thicker hair.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hairstyle and hair growth rate. Generally, a trim every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

  • Use hair products recommended by your hairstylist for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in high-quality hair tools, such as a good brush and blow dryer.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, looking for inspiration, and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. Remember to also take care of your hair with proper maintenance and products to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
