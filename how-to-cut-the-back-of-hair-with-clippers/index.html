How To Cut The Back Of Hair With Clippers

Written by Oliver May 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon for a simple haircut? If so, investing in a good pair of clippers can save you a lot of money in the long run. However, cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to the back. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting the back of your hair with clippers in relaxed English language, so you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting the back of your hair with clippers is a simple process that can be done by anyone. It involves using clippers to trim the hair on the back of your head to a desired length. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a neat and clean-cut look that rivals any professional haircut.

How to Cut the Back of Hair with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the right tools on hand. You will need a good-quality pair of clippers, a comb, a mirror and a towel or cape to catch the hair. Follow these simple steps to cut the back of your hair with clippers:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. This will remove any dirt, oil or product buildup and make it easier to cut. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Set the Clippers to the Desired Length

Choose the length guard that you want to use and attach it to the clippers. If you are unsure about the length, start with a longer guard and work your way down. Turn on the clippers and test them on a small section of hair to make sure that they are working properly.

Step 3: Start at the Base of the Neck

Begin by trimming the hair at the base of your neck. Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Work your way up the back of your head, using the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you have trimmed the hair at the back of your head, move on to the sides. Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion, starting from the base of the neck and working towards the top of the head. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Check for Any Missed Spots

After you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any missed spots. If you notice any uneven areas, go back and trim them with the clippers. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting the back of your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check for any missed spots.
  • Clean your clippers after each use to maintain their performance.

FAQ

Q. Can I cut the back of my hair with scissors?

A. While it is possible to cut the back of your hair with scissors, using clippers is a more efficient and precise method.

Q. What if I make a mistake?

A. Don't worry if you make a mistake. It's better to go slow and be cautious than to rush and make a mess. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them with the clippers.

Q. Do I need to be a professional to cut my own hair?

A. No, anyone can cut their own hair with the right tools and techniques. It just takes a little practice and patience.

Hair Care

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil and product buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
