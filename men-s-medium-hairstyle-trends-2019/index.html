Men s Medium Hairstyle Trends 2019 A Guide To Relaxed Style

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

The medium hairstyle trend for men in 2019 is all about relaxed, effortless style. This look is characterized by longer hair on top that is styled in a messy, textured way, while the sides and back are kept shorter and clean-cut. This versatile hairstyle can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and tousled, making it perfect for any occasion.

To achieve this look, you'll need to grow out your hair to a medium length and work with a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the perfect cut and style. You'll also need to be willing to put in a little extra effort to maintain your hair and keep it looking its best.

How to Achieve the Look

The key to achieving the men's medium hairstyle trend for 2019 is to start with the right cut. This style requires longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways, so it's important to find a stylist who has experience working with this type of look.

Once you have the right cut, you can experiment with different styling techniques to achieve the perfect look for you. Some popular styling options for medium length hair include:

  • Slicked back with a high shine finish
  • Messy and tousled for a relaxed, casual look
  • Textured and layered for added volume and movement
  • Swept to the side for a classic, sophisticated look

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's medium hairstyle trend for 2019:

  1. Grow out your hair to a medium length.
  2. Find a skilled hairstylist who has experience working with medium length hair.
  3. Discuss your desired look with your stylist and work together to come up with the perfect cut and style.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.
  5. Maintain your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's medium hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect one for your hair type and desired look.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools to make it easier to achieve your desired look.

FAQs About Men's Medium Hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's medium hairstyles:

Q: What face shapes are best suited for the men's medium hairstyle trend?

A: This hairstyle works well with a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I achieve this look if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can work well for men with thinning hair. A skilled hairstylist can help you achieve a look that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry and damaged.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Hairstyles

Here are some tips for caring for your men's medium hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, the men's medium hairstyle trend for 2019 is all about relaxed, effortless style. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a look that is both versatile and fashionable. By following these tips for maintaining and caring for your hair, you can keep your medium hairstyle looking its best for years to come.
