How To Get 40S Style Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 20, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to channel your inner vintage goddess, then 40s style waves are the perfect hairstyle for you. Not only are they glamorous and elegant, but they're also incredibly easy to achieve. In this guide, we'll show you how to get 40s style waves in just a few simple steps. So, grab your curling iron and let's get started!

Description

40s style waves are a classic hairstyle that was popular during the 1940s. These waves are characterized by their soft, loose curls that frame the face and create a romantic, vintage look. They're perfect for special occasions, such as weddings, proms, or any other event where you want to look your best.

How to Get 40s Style Waves

To get 40s style waves, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will give your hair some lift and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back of your head. Use a clip to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a curling iron and wrap each section of hair around the barrel, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, gently brush out your curls with a boar bristle brush. This will create soft, loose waves that resemble the 40s style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your waves in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add some shine and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 40s style waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create soft, loose curls.
  • Brush out your curls with a boar bristle brush to create a more natural look.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some lift.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your waves in place.

FAQ

What length of hair is best for 40s style waves?

40s style waves work best on medium to long hair. If you have short hair, you can still achieve this look by using a curling iron with a small barrel to create tighter curls.

How long will 40s style waves last?

If you use a light mist of hairspray to finish, your 40s style waves should last all day or night.

Can I achieve 40s style waves without a curling iron?

Yes, you can achieve 40s style waves without a curling iron by using hot rollers or braiding your hair overnight. This will create a similar effect to using a curling iron.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant before styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals or treatments that can damage your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve beautiful, 40s style waves that will turn heads wherever you go.
