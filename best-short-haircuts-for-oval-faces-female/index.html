Best Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Female

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you are considered lucky as almost all short haircuts look good on you. With that being said, it is still important to find the best short haircut that flatters your facial features and highlights your best features. In this article, we will discuss the top short haircuts for oval faces female.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Oval Faces Female

When choosing a short haircut for oval faces female, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to guide you in choosing the best short haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that emphasizes your best facial features such as eyes, cheekbones, and lips.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face as it can make your face look rounder.
  • Consider your hair texture as it can affect how your haircut will look. For example, curly hair may need more layers to add volume while straight hair may need blunt cuts to add dimension.
  • Choose a haircut that complements your personal style and lifestyle. A low-maintenance haircut may be the best option if you have a busy schedule.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular short haircut that looks great on oval faces female. It is a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled in different ways to add variety to your look. The pixie cut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head with longer hair on top. It can be styled with a side-swept bangs or pushed back for a more edgy look.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short haircut that flatters oval faces female. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways to suit your personal style. The bob cut is characterized by hair that falls just above the shoulders with sharp, blunt edges. It can be styled with layers, bangs, or curls to add dimension to your look.

Lob Cut

The lob cut, also known as a long bob, is a popular short haircut that looks great on oval faces female. It is a longer version of the bob cut that falls just below the shoulders. The lob cut is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways to add variety to your look. It can be styled with waves, curls, or straight hair.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy short haircut that looks great on oval faces female. It is a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. The shag cut is characterized by choppy layers and bangs that frame the face. It can be styled with waves or curls to add dimension to your look.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for oval faces female with curly hair?

The best short haircut for oval faces female with curly hair is a layered haircut that adds volume and dimension to your hair. The pixie cut, bob cut, and shag cut are great options for curly hair.

What is the best short haircut for oval faces female with straight hair?

The best short haircut for oval faces female with straight hair is a blunt cut that adds dimension to your hair. The bob cut and lob cut are great options for straight hair.

How often should I trim my short haircut?

You should trim your short haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance but it is still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down. Use a small amount of product to add texture and hold.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for oval faces female, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best!
