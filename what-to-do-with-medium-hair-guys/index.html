What To Do With Medium Hair Guys A Guide To Styling And Care

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium hair length for guys can be a bit tricky to style and maintain. It's not too short to go for a classic buzz cut, but it's also not long enough to rock a man bun. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on what to do with medium hair guys. From hairstyles to hair care, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles for Medium Hair Guys
  • Hair Care for Medium Hair Guys
  • Tips for Styling Medium Hair Guys
  • FAQs about Medium Hair Guys

Hairstyles for Medium Hair Guys

There are plenty of hairstyles that medium hair guys can try. One of the most popular ones is the messy quiff. This style involves keeping the hair on top of the head longer than the sides and back. To achieve this look, blow dry your hair upwards and use a styling product like wax or pomade to hold it in place.

If you're looking for a more classic look, you can try the side part. This style involves combing your hair to one side and keeping it sleek and polished. You can also experiment with different variations of this style, such as the side-swept fringe or the disconnected undercut.

For those who want to embrace their natural texture, the messy curly hair look is perfect. This style involves keeping the hair medium length and allowing the curls to do their thing. Use a curl-defining cream or mousse to enhance your natural texture and add some volume.

Hair Care for Medium Hair Guys

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. For medium hair guys, this means using a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing conditioner to hydrate your strands.

In addition to using the right products, it's also important to avoid over-washing your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

When it comes to styling your hair, be gentle. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush when it's wet. Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so be extra careful when handling it.

Tips for Styling Medium Hair Guys

Here are some tips to help you style your medium hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for better control.
  • Use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and switch things up.

FAQs about Medium Hair Guys

Q: Can I still get a fade with medium hair?

A: Yes, you can. The fade is a versatile hairstyle that can work with different hair lengths, including medium hair. Talk to your barber about the specific fade you want and how it will work with your hair type.

Q: Do I need to use heat protectant when styling my hair?

A: Yes, using a heat protectant spray or cream can help prevent damage from heat styling tools like hairdryers and straighteners. Look for a product that's specifically designed for men's hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want to keep your hair. As a general rule, aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

Medium hair guys have plenty of options when it comes to styling their hair. Whether you prefer a classic look or something more experimental, there's a hairstyle that's right for you. Just remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding over-washing or over-styling. With these tips, you'll be able to rock your medium hair with confidence.
