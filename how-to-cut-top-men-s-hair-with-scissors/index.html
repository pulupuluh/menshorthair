How To Cut Top Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting men's hair with scissors can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're new to it. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut that will make any man feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to cut top men's hair with scissors in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. So, let's get started!

  • How to Cut Top Men's Hair with Scissors
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Top Men's Hair with Scissors

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need to cut men's hair with scissors. Here's what you'll need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Once you have all the necessary tools, you can start cutting men's hair with scissors. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry the hair: Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut as it tends to stick together.
  2. Comb the hair: Use a comb to remove any tangles and ensure the hair is smooth and even.
  3. Section the hair: Starting from the front, use a comb to section the hair into manageable pieces. Clip the sections you're not working on to keep them out of the way.
  4. Start cutting: Hold the hair section between your fingers and cut with scissors. Start with small snips to avoid cutting too much at once. Continue cutting until you reach the desired length.
  5. Blend the hair: To create a natural look, blend the cut hair by using scissors to cut at an angle.
  6. Repeat: Work your way through each section of hair, cutting and blending until you've finished the entire head.
  7. Clean up: Use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs and ensure a neat finish.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great-looking haircut:

  • Start with small snips: It's better to cut too little rather than too much. Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Blend the hair: To create a natural look, blend the cut hair by using scissors to cut at an angle.
  • Use a mirror: Make sure you can see what you're doing by using a mirror. This will help you avoid any mistakes and ensure a neat finish.
  • Take your time: Cutting hair with scissors can take a bit of practice. Don't rush and take your time to ensure a great-looking haircut.

FAQs

Here are some common questions people have about cutting men's hair with scissors:

Can I use regular scissors?

No, you should always use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can be too blunt and damage the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on the individual and their hair. As a general rule, it's recommended to cut hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a neat appearance.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Mistakes can happen, but they can usually be fixed. If you're not happy with the way the hair looks, take a break and come back to it later. You can also blend any mistakes by cutting at an angle.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting men's hair with scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain its appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition regularly: Keep the hair clean and healthy by washing and conditioning it regularly.
  • Use hair products: Use hair products such as gel or wax to style the hair and keep it in place.
  • Protect from heat: Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners or curling irons as they can damage the hair.

Cutting top men's hair with scissors can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut. Follow these tips and steps to ensure a neat and stylish finish every time.
