How To Make Your Hair Curly For The Next Morning

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools on your hair every day to achieve those perfect curls? Heat damage can leave your hair dry and brittle, making it prone to breakage. But don't worry, there are ways to achieve those beautiful curls without causing any damage to your hair. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair curly for the next morning, without using any heat tools.

Description

Many people love having curly hair, but not everyone is blessed with natural curls. Fortunately, there are several ways to achieve those luscious curls without causing any damage to your hair. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair curly for the next morning, using some simple techniques and products that you probably already have at home.

How To

1. Use Hair Rollers

Hair rollers are a great way to achieve curly hair without using any heat. You can use them on wet or dry hair, depending on your preference. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, section your hair and roll each section around the roller, securing it with a clip. Leave the rollers in overnight and remove them in the morning for beautiful, bouncy curls.

2. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another great way to achieve curly hair without using heat. Simply divide your hair into sections and braid each section before you go to bed. You can either create one big braid or multiple small braids, depending on how tight you want your curls to be. In the morning, remove the braids and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

3. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are another great option for achieving curly hair without using heat. They are flexible, foam rods that you can wrap your hair around. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, section your hair and wrap each section around a flexi rod, securing it in place. Leave the flexi rods in overnight and remove them in the morning for beautiful, defined curls.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections.

Step 3: Choose your preferred method for achieving curls (hair rollers, braids, or flexi rods).

Step 4: Wrap each section of hair around the roller, braid, or flexi rod.

Step 5: Secure each section with a clip or tie.

Step 6: Leave the rollers, braids, or flexi rods in overnight.

Step 7: Remove the clips or ties in the morning.

Step 8: Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

Tips

- For best results, start with clean, dry hair.

- Use a styling product, such as mousse or curl cream, to help hold the curls.

- If you're using hair rollers, make sure to choose the right size for your desired curl size.

- If you want tighter curls, leave the rollers, braids, or flexi rods in for longer.

FAQ

Q: Can I use heat to achieve curly hair?

A: While heat tools can be used to achieve curly hair, they can also cause damage to your hair. It's best to avoid heat as much as possible and opt for heat-free methods instead.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls depends on several factors, such as your hair type and the method you used to achieve the curls. On average, your curls should last for a day or two.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using heat tools on a regular basis. To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoid using heat tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat tools.

So, there you have it, a simple and easy way to achieve beautiful curls without using any heat tools. Try out these methods and see which one works best for you. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve those perfect curls every time.
