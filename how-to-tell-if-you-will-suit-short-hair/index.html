How To Tell If You Will Suit Short Hair

Written by Eveline May 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read
How to Tell If You Will Suit Short Hair | Tips & Hairstyles

Short hair has always been a statement of boldness and confidence. However, not all of us are brave enough to take the plunge and chop off our locks. One of the main concerns when considering a short haircut is whether or not it will suit our face shape and features. In this article, we will guide you through the process of determining if short hair is right for you.

Determining Your Face Shape

The shape of your face plays a crucial role in determining if short hair will suit you or not. There are several face shapes, including round, square, heart, oval, and diamond. Each face shape has its own ideal haircut, and it's essential to choose a haircut that complements your face shape.

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to pull your hair back and assess the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you have a circular shape with fuller cheeks. A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, while a heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and narrow jawline. An oval face is balanced and symmetrical, while a diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and chin with wider cheekbones.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair is right for you. If you have thick and curly hair, a short haircut may not be the best choice as it can make your hair look even fuller. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, a short haircut can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have straight hair, a short haircut can give you a sleek and polished look.

Matching Your Personality

Your personality also plays a vital role in determining if short hair is right for you. If you are a confident and bold person who loves to stand out, a short haircut can be an excellent choice. However, if you are more reserved and prefer a low-key look, a short haircut may not be the best choice for you. It's essential to choose a haircut that matches your personality and makes you feel comfortable.

Hairstyles for Short Hair

There are several hairstyles for short hair that you can choose from. The classic bob is a timeless option that can work for any face shape and hair texture. The pixie cut is another popular choice that is perfect for those with an oval or heart-shaped face. The lob or long bob is a great option for those who want a longer hairstyle but still want to keep it short. The shag cut is a layered haircut that works well with thick and curly hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Invest in styling products, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to create different looks
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually make your face appear slimmer and more angular.

Q: Can I still style short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can actually make you look younger and more vibrant. It all depends on the haircut and how it complements your face shape and features.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different approach to hair care than longer hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too greasy or dry
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair hydrated without weighing it down
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to avoid breakage and tangles

Now that you know how to tell if you will suit short hair, it's time to take the plunge and try out a new look. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
