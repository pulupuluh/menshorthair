How To Tell If You Have A Round Or Square Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to tell if you have a round or square face. Knowing your face shape is essential when choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and even accessories that will flatter your features. In this article, we’ll discuss the differences between a round and square face, and how you can determine which one you have. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • How to Tell If You Have a Round or Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Flattering Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round or Square Faces

Introduction

Before we dive into the differences between round and square faces, let’s first define what these two face shapes are. A round face has soft curves and a circular shape, while a square face has strong angles and a prominent jawline.

Knowing your face shape is important because it can help you enhance your natural features and balance out any asymmetry in your face. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to add some angles to your hairstyle to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you may want to soften your features with rounded hairstyles and makeup.

How to Tell If You Have a Round or Square Face

So, how can you tell if you have a round or square face? Here are some key differences to look for:

Round Face

A round face typically has the following characteristics:

  • A circular shape
  • Soft, rounded features (such as a rounded chin and forehead)
  • A lack of strong angles or prominent bone structure

If you’re still not sure, try measuring your face with a tape measure. Divide the length of your face by the width. If the result is close to 1, then you likely have a round face.

Square Face

A square face typically has the following characteristics:

  • A strong, angular jawline
  • Straight sides and a flat forehead
  • Prominent cheekbones

If you’re still not sure, try measuring your face with a tape measure. Divide the length of your face by the width. If the result is greater than 1, then you likely have a square face.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re still having trouble determining your face shape, here is a step-by-step guide that can help:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using lipstick or an eyeliner pencil.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you’ve traced.
  4. Compare the shape to the images of round and square faces above.
  5. Determine which shape your traced outline matches most closely.

Tips for Flattering Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips for flattering your features:

Round Face

  • Add angles to your hairstyle with layers or a side-swept fringe.
  • Use contouring makeup to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

Square Face

  • Softened your features with rounded hairstyles, such as waves or curls.
  • Use blush to add some roundness to your cheeks.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your angular jawline, such as straight bobs.

FAQs

What other face shapes are there?

Other common face shapes include oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped faces.

Can face shape change over time?

While your bone structure won’t change, your face shape can appear to change over time due to factors such as weight gain or loss, aging, and changes in hairstyle.

Hair Care for Round or Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for round or square faces, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some tips:

Round Face

  • Opt for hairstyles with angles or layers to add some definition to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe to draw attention to your eyes and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Square Face

  • Choose hairstyles that soften your features, such as waves or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your angular jawline, such as straight bobs.
  • Consider a side part to create the illusion of a more rounded face shape.

Now that you know how to tell if you have a round or square face, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles, makeup, and accessories to flatter your features. Remember, the key is to enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin!
