Mid Back Length Hair Braids A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this year 2023, mid back length hair braids have become a popular trend. Whether you are looking for a protective hairstyle or just want to switch up your look, this style is versatile and easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to achieve the perfect mid back length hair braids.

Description

Mid back length hair braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves braiding your hair from the roots to the ends. This style can be achieved using various types of braids, such as box braids, cornrows, or twists. It is a great way to protect your hair from damage and promote hair growth.

How to

Before you start braiding your hair, make sure it is clean and moisturized. You can use a deep conditioner or hot oil treatment to prepare your hair for braiding. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect mid back length hair braids:

Step 1:

Start by sectioning your hair into small parts using a rat-tail comb. The size of the sections will depend on the type of braid you want to achieve. For box braids, the sections should be about an inch wide. For cornrows, the sections should be smaller.

Step 2:

Apply a hair gel or edge control to the roots of each section to make it easier to braid. Divide the section into three strands and start braiding from the root. Make sure to braid tightly to prevent the braids from unraveling.

Step 3:

Continue braiding each section until you reach the ends. Use a small rubber band or a hair tie to secure the ends of the braids. Repeat this process until you have braided all sections of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding your mid back length hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before braiding to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid braiding your hair too tightly to prevent hair loss.
  • Take breaks between braiding sessions to give your hair time to rest.
  • Use a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep.

FAQ

How long does it take to braid mid back length hair?

The time it takes to braid mid back length hair will depend on the type of braid you choose and your braiding skills. It can take anywhere from 4 to 12 hours to complete the braiding process.

How long can I keep my mid back length hair braids in?

You can keep your mid back length hair braids in for 4 to 6 weeks. After that, you should take them out to prevent hair damage and to give your hair a break.

How do I maintain my mid back length hair braids?

To maintain your mid back length hair braids, you should keep your scalp clean and moisturized. You can use a scalp oil or a spray to keep your scalp hydrated. Avoid scratching your scalp or pulling on your braids, as this can cause breakage.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially when you are wearing protective styles like mid back length hair braids. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hot oil treatment to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy hair while wearing mid back length hair braids. Remember to take breaks between braiding sessions to give your hair time to rest and recover.
