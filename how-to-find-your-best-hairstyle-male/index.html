How To Find Your Best Hairstyle Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 26, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut you've been rocking for years? Do you want to switch things up and find a style that suits you better? Finding your best hairstyle as a male can be a daunting task, but fear not – we're here to help. In this post, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to find your perfect hairstyle, along with some frequently asked questions and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find Your Best Hairstyle Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Find Your Best Hairstyle Male

The first step in finding your best hairstyle is to consider your face shape. Different styles suit different face shapes, so it's important to know yours before choosing a haircut. Here are the main face shapes and the hairstyles that suit them:

  • Round: Shorter sides and longer top, with texture and height on top.
  • Square: Short sides and back, with length on top and a textured fringe.
  • Oval: Most hairstyles suit oval faces, but shorter sides and a textured top work well.
  • Heart: Shorter sides and longer top, with a textured fringe or quiff.

Once you've determined your face shape, think about your hair type. Do you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair? Different hair types suit different styles, so it's important to consider this when choosing a haircut.

Finally, think about your personal style and the image you want to project. Do you want a classic, timeless look or something more modern and edgy? Consider your lifestyle, too – if you have a job that requires a more conservative look, a wild and crazy haircut might not be the best choice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find your best hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair type
  3. Think about your personal style and the image you want to project
  4. Browse hairstyles online or in magazines
  5. Take inspiration to your hairstylist

Remember, communication with your hairstylist is key. They can give you advice on what styles will suit you best and can make adjustments to a style to make it work for you.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for finding your best hairstyle:

  • Consider your hairline – if you have a receding hairline, a shorter style might be more flattering
  • Experiment with different products to achieve the look you want
  • Don't be afraid to try something new – hair grows back, after all
  • Take care of your hair with regular trims and quality hair products

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding your best hairstyle:

What if I don't know my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. There are also online quizzes and tools that can help you determine your face shape.

What if I have thinning hair?

There are plenty of hairstyles that work well for thinning hair, such as a buzz cut or a short, textured style. You can also try using volumizing products to give your hair more body.

Do I need to bring a picture to my hairstylist?

It can be helpful to bring a picture of a style you like to your hairstylist, but it's not necessary. They can also give you advice on what styles will suit you best based on your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found your best hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Don't overuse styling products, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding your best hairstyle as a male. Remember to have fun with it and don't be afraid to try something new – you might just discover your new signature style.
