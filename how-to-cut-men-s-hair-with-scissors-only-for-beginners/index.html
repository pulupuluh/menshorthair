How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Only For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're a beginner looking to cut men's hair with scissors only, this guide is for you. Cutting hair can seem like a daunting task, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a great haircut. In this article, we'll go through the process of cutting men's hair with scissors only for beginners.

Description

Cutting hair with scissors only is a great way to get started with hair cutting. It's a simple technique that requires only a pair of scissors and a comb. This method is perfect for beginners who are just starting out and don't want to invest in expensive hair clippers. Cutting hair with scissors only is also great for those who want to achieve a more natural look, as it allows for more texture and movement in the hair.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Only for Beginners

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin cutting hair, you'll need to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Prep the Hair

Next, you'll need to prep the hair. Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly. Then, comb the hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Once the hair is prepped, you'll need to section it. Use the comb to create a part down the middle of the head, from the forehead to the nape of the neck. Then, create another part from ear to ear. You should now have four sections of hair.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Begin with one of the back sections. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your fingers. Then, use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut evenly and to blend the hair as you go.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting the hair, it's important to check your work. Use the comb to check for any uneven spots or missed areas. If you find any, go back and trim them until the hair is even.

Tips

- Always start with longer lengths and work your way down to shorter lengths. - Be patient and take your time. Rushing can result in uneven haircuts. - Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as you cut. This will make it easier to cut and help prevent split ends. - Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair than it is to add it back.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors only. However, it may be easier to have someone else do it for you, especially if you're a beginner.

Do I need to invest in expensive scissors?

No, you don't need to invest in expensive scissors. A good pair of scissors can be found at a reasonable price. Just make sure they're sharp and comfortable to hold.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back, and mistakes can be fixed. If you're unsure of how to fix a mistake, seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair. In conclusion, cutting men's hair with scissors only for beginners is a simple and effective technique. With the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a great haircut. Remember to take your time, start with longer lengths, and check your work as you go. Happy cutting!
