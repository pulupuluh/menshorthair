Is It Good To Blow Dry Curly Hair

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a beautiful and unique feature, but it can also be difficult to manage. One of the most common questions that people with curly hair ask is whether it is good to blow dry their hair. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of blow drying curly hair and provide tips on how to do it safely.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Blow Dry Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Blow drying curly hair can have both positive and negative effects. On the one hand, blow drying can help to smooth out frizz and add volume to the hair. On the other hand, too much heat can damage the hair and cause breakage.

It is important to use the right tools and techniques when blow drying curly hair to minimize damage and achieve the best results.

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair

The first step in blow drying curly hair is to prepare the hair for heat styling. This can be done by washing the hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and using a heat protectant spray or serum.

Next, use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently remove any tangles or knots from the hair. It is important to be gentle when brushing curly hair to avoid causing breakage or frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to blow dry curly hair:

  1. Section the hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  2. Starting with the bottom section, use a round brush to gently pull the hair away from the scalp and blow dry on a low heat setting.
  3. Move up to the next section and repeat until all of the hair is dry.
  4. Once the hair is dry, use a cool shot setting on the blow dryer to help set the style and reduce frizz.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray or serum to keep the hair in place.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when blow drying curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Blow dry on a low heat setting to minimize damage to the hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the hair with the brush, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a moisturizing hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid blow drying your hair too often, as frequent heat styling can cause damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying curly hair:

  • Can blow drying curly hair cause damage?
  • What is the best heat setting to use when blow drying curly hair?
  • How often should I blow dry my curly hair?
  • What is the best type of brush to use when blow drying curly hair?

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to blow drying, there are many other things you can do to take care of your curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

In conclusion, blow drying curly hair can be a great way to smooth out frizz and add volume, but it is important to use the right tools and techniques to minimize damage. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls without causing harm to your hair.
