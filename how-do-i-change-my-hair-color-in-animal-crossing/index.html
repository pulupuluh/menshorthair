How Do I Change My Hair Color In Animal Crossing

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to the world of Animal Crossing, where you can create your own avatar and live in a virtual world filled with cute animals. One of the many things you can do in Animal Crossing is change your hair color. If you're wondering how to do this, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you step by step how to change your hair color in Animal Crossing.

How to Change Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing is a simple process. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Go to the Mirror

The first thing you need to do is find a mirror. You can find mirrors in the Able Sisters' Shop or in your house. Once you find a mirror, stand in front of it.

Step 2: Choose the "Change Look" Option

When you're standing in front of the mirror, press the A button on your controller. This will give you the option to "Change Look." Select this option.

Step 3: Choose the "Hair Color" Option

After you select "Change Look," you'll see several options. Choose the "Hair Color" option.

Step 4: Choose Your New Hair Color

Once you've selected the "Hair Color" option, you'll see a list of different hair colors. Choose the one you want and press A to confirm.

Step 5: Pay for Your New Hair Color

After you've chosen your new hair color, you'll need to pay for it. The cost of changing your hair color varies depending on the color you choose. Once you've paid, your hair color will be changed.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Here are some tips that will make changing your hair color in Animal Crossing easier:

  • Before you change your hair color, make sure you have enough money to pay for it.
  • If you're not happy with your new hair color, you can always change it again.
  • Changing your hair color is a great way to switch up your look and make your avatar feel new and fresh.

FAQs

How many hair colors can I choose from in Animal Crossing?

There are several different hair colors to choose from in Animal Crossing. You can choose from natural colors like brown and blonde, or you can choose more unique colors like pink and blue.

Can I change my hair color more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair color as many times as you want. However, you will need to pay for each change.

Can I change my hair style in Animal Crossing?

Yes, you can change your hair style in Animal Crossing. You can choose from several different styles, including short, long, and curly.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Now that you've changed your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy in Animal Crossing:

  • Visit the hair salon regularly to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Use hair care products like shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid overusing hair accessories, as they can weigh your hair down and cause damage.

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing is a fun and easy way to switch up your look. Whether you're going for a natural look or something more unique, there's a hair color for you. Follow these steps and tips to change your hair color and keep your hair healthy in Animal Crossing.
