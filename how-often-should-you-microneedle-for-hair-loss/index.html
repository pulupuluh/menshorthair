How Often Should You Microneedle For Hair Loss

Written by Christine Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're dealing with hair loss, microneedling might be a solution to consider. This treatment involves using tiny needles to puncture the scalp, which stimulates hair growth. However, it's important to know how often you should microneedle for hair loss to avoid damaging your scalp or overstimulating hair follicles. In this article, we'll go over the recommended frequency for microneedling, how to do it safely, and tips for optimizing your results.

Table of Contents

  • Recommended Frequency
  • How to Microneedle for Hair Loss
  • Tips for Optimizing Results
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Recommended Frequency

When it comes to microneedling for hair loss, less is more. It's recommended to microneedle once a week or every other week, depending on your scalp's sensitivity. Overstimulating hair follicles can lead to inflammation and even more hair loss, so it's important to give your scalp time to rest and heal.

It's also important to note that microneedling is not a quick fix. You may not see results for several months, and it may take a series of treatments to achieve the desired outcome. Consistency is key.

How to Microneedle for Hair Loss

Before microneedling, it's important to prepare your scalp by washing it with a gentle shampoo and drying it thoroughly. You also want to make sure your microneedling tool is clean and sanitized.

When microneedling, use gentle pressure and roll the tool in different directions over the areas of hair loss. Avoid applying too much pressure or overlapping areas, as this can cause damage to the scalp.

After microneedling, apply a soothing serum or oil to the scalp to help with healing and encourage hair growth. Avoid using any harsh chemicals or treatments on your scalp for a few days after microneedling.

Tips for Optimizing Results

While microneedling can be an effective treatment for hair loss, there are a few things you can do to optimize your results:

  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals that support hair growth
  • Reduce stress, as stress can contribute to hair loss
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair and scalp
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling and harsh chemicals

FAQ

Is microneedling for hair loss safe?

Yes, microneedling for hair loss is generally safe when done correctly. However, it's important to follow proper hygiene and technique to avoid infection or scalp damage.

Can microneedling cause more hair loss?

If done too frequently or aggressively, microneedling can cause inflammation and hair loss. It's important to stick to the recommended frequency and technique.

How long does it take to see results from microneedling for hair loss?

It can take several months to see results from microneedling for hair loss. Consistency is key.

Hair Care

In addition to microneedling, taking care of your hair and scalp can help prevent further hair loss and promote growth. This includes eating a healthy diet, reducing stress, avoiding tight hairstyles, and using gentle hair products.

If you're dealing with hair loss, microneedling can be an effective treatment to consider. Just be sure to follow proper frequency and technique, and take care of your hair and scalp overall.
