How To Test If A Hair Colour Suits You

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair colour but unsure if it will suit you? It can be a daunting process but don’t worry, we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on how to test if a hair colour suits you. We will provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, FAQ and hair care advice.

Table of Content:

  • How to Test if a Hair Colour Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice

Step-by-Step Guide:

The first step is to determine your skin tone. There are two types of skin tones; warm and cool. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they are blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they are green, you have a warm skin tone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair colour that complements it. For warm skin tones, choose hair colours with warm undertones such as golden browns, copper reds or honey blondes. For cool skin tones, choose hair colours with cool undertones such as ashy browns, platinum blondes or burgundy reds.

Before you commit to a hair colour, try a wig or a temporary hair dye. This will give you a good idea of how the hair colour will look on you without the long-term commitment.

Another way to test if a hair colour suits you is to use an app that allows you to try on different hair colours virtually. This will give you a good idea of how the hair colour will look on you without actually dyeing your hair.

Consult with a hair stylist. They will be able to advise you on which hair colour will suit you best based on your skin tone, eye colour and natural hair colour. They will also be able to guide you on how to maintain your new hair colour.

Tips:

Always choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.

Try a temporary hair dye or a wig before committing to a hair colour.

Use a virtual hair colour app to try on different hair colours virtually.

Consult with a hair stylist before dyeing your hair.

FAQ:

Q: How do I determine my skin tone?

A: Look at the veins on your wrist. If they are blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they are green, you have a warm skin tone.

Q: What hair colours complement warm skin tones?

A: Hair colours with warm undertones such as golden browns, copper reds or honey blondes.

Q: What hair colours complement cool skin tones?

A: Hair colours with cool undertones such as ashy browns, platinum blondes or burgundy reds.

Hair Care Advice:

After dyeing your hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for coloured hair. This will help to maintain the vibrancy of your hair colour.

Avoid washing your hair too frequently as this can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause the colour to fade. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. This will help to maintain the overall health of your hair.

Now that you know how to test if a hair colour suits you, you can confidently choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone. Remember to take good care of your hair after dyeing it to maintain the vibrancy of your new hair colour.
