How To Find The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Robby Mar 07, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't seem to flatter your face shape? It's time to stop the guesswork and find the perfect haircut for your unique features. In this article, we'll go over how to determine your face shape, what haircuts suit each shape, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Haircuts for Different Face Shapes
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Look
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes: round, square, heart, oval, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down these measurements and compare them to the following:

  • Round: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, with a strong jawline
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline, with a pointed chin
  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, with gently rounded hairline and chin
  • Diamond: Forehead and jawline are narrower than the cheekbones, with a pointed chin
  • Oblong: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, with a longer face shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will complement your features.

Haircuts for Different Face Shapes

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds angles and lengthens your face. A long bob, layered cuts, or hairstyles with bangs that sweep to the side are great options. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape looks best with haircuts that soften the angles of the jawline. Medium-length hair with layers or a side-swept bang can help achieve this. Avoid straight bobs or blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make your face appear even more square.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want a haircut that adds width to the jawline and balances out the forehead. Long layers or a side-swept fringe can help achieve this. Avoid heavy bangs or short haircuts that emphasize the forehead.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile, as most haircuts will suit this face shape. You can experiment with different lengths and styles, from a bob to long layers.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want a haircut that narrows the width of the cheekbones. A layered haircut that falls below the shoulders or a wispy bob can help achieve this. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts or short haircuts that emphasize the width of the cheekbones.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape benefits from haircuts that add width and volume to the sides of the face. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a blunt bob with bangs can help achieve this. Avoid long, straight haircuts that make your face appear longer.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools
  • Consider investing in a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep

FAQ

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

If you have a combination of face shapes, focus on the features that you want to highlight. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face with a prominent forehead, choose a haircut that adds width to the jawline and balances out the forehead.

Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can pull off a short haircut if you have a round face. However, it's important to choose a style that adds angles and lengthens your face, such as a layered pixie cut or a bob with longer layers.

Hair Care

Finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is just the first step in achieving your dream hair. It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent heat damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and finding the perfect haircut for your face shape, you'll be well on your way to achieving your dream hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
