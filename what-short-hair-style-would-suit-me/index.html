What Short Hair Style Would Suit Me

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but not sure what would suit you? Well, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll be discussing what short hair style would suit you and how to achieve it.

Description

Short hair can be a great way to refresh your look and feel more confident. It’s also low maintenance and versatile. However, not all short hair styles suit everyone. Your face shape, hair texture, and personal style will all play a role in determining what short hair style would suit you.

How to

The first step in determining what short hair style would suit you is to identify your face shape. The four main face shapes are oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Once you know your face shape, you can then look for hairstyles that will complement it.

For example, if you have an oval face shape, you can pretty much pull off any short hair style. If you have a round face shape, a pixie cut or a bob with layers would work well to elongate your face. If you have a square face shape, a textured bob or a layered pixie cut would soften your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, a chin-length bob or a layered pixie cut with side-swept bangs would be perfect.

Step by Step

Once you’ve identified what short hair style would suit you, it’s time to take the plunge and get it cut. Here are some steps to ensure a successful haircut:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short hair styles.
  2. Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want, so your hairstylist knows exactly what you’re looking for.
  3. Be honest with your hairstylist about your hair texture, lifestyle, and styling habits. This will help them recommend the best short hair style for you.
  4. Listen to your hairstylist’s advice on how to maintain your new short hair style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your short hair style:

  • Invest in good quality styling products, such as a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse.
  • Get regular trims to keep your short hair style looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as using a curling iron or flat iron, to create different looks.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new and different. Short hair is all about being bold and confident.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair styles:

Q: Will a short hair style suit me if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, a short hair style can work well with thick hair. However, you may need to get regular trims to prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a short hair style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a short hair style. However, you may need to use hair accessories, such as bobby pins or hair ties, to secure your hair.

Q: Will a short hair style suit me if I have a small face?

A: Yes, a short hair style can work well with a small face. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and doesn’t overwhelm your features.

Hair Care

When it comes to short hair styles, hair care is just as important as the cut itself. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates or alcohol.

Now that you know what short hair style would suit you, it’s time to book that hair appointment and embrace your new look!
