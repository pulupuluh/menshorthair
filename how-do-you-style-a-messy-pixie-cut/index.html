How To Style A Messy Pixie Cut

Written by Robby Jun 15, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have recently chopped off your hair into a pixie cut, congratulations! Pixie cuts are trendy, low-maintenance, and perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look. However, when it comes to styling your pixie cut, things can get a little tricky. In this article, we will show you how to style a messy pixie cut in the most effortless and chic way possible. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Start with clean hair
  • Step 2: Apply a texturizing spray
  • Step 3: Use a hairdryer
  • Step 4: Add some hair wax or pomade
  • Step 5: Finish with hairspray
  • Tips for styling a messy pixie cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The key to styling a messy pixie cut is to start with clean, freshly washed hair. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, whether it's dry, oily, or normal. Once you have washed your hair, gently towel dry it and comb out any tangles.

Step 2: Apply a texturizing spray

Once your hair is clean and dry, apply a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your pixie cut. A texturizing spray will help to create a messy, undone look that is perfect for a pixie cut. Spray the product evenly throughout your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step 3: Use a hairdryer

Next, use a hairdryer to add some volume and lift to your hair. Use your fingers to lift your hair at the roots and direct the airflow upwards. This will help to create more volume and texture. As you dry your hair, continue to scrunch and tousle your hair with your fingers.

Step 4: Add some hair wax or pomade

Once your hair is dry, add a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair. This will help to define your layers and add some texture to your hair. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish your messy pixie cut with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat. Remember to use a light hold hairspray, as you want your hair to look natural and not too stiff or crunchy.

Tips for styling a messy pixie cut

  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays and hair waxes until you find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to create more volume and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to create a messy, undone look.
  • If you have bangs, try sweeping them to the side for a more relaxed, effortless look.

FAQs

Q. Can you style a messy pixie cut without using heat?

A. Yes, you can style a messy pixie cut without using heat. Instead of using a hairdryer, let your hair dry naturally and use your fingers to create volume and texture. You can also use a texturizing spray and hair wax to add some definition and hold to your hair.

Q. How often should I wash my pixie cut?

A. It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair less frequently, such as every three days. However, if you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Q. Can I wear a messy pixie cut to a formal event?

A. Absolutely! A messy pixie cut can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. To make your pixie cut more formal, you can add some sparkly hair accessories or sweep your hair to one side for a sleeker look.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to style a messy pixie cut, it's time to rock your new look with confidence! Whether you're going to work, running errands, or going out with friends, your messy pixie cut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
