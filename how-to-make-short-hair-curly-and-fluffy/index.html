How To Make Short Hair Curly And Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some bounce and volume to your locks? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make short hair curly and fluffy. From the tools you'll need to the techniques you should use, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Curling Your Hair
  • Fluffing Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curly, Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Tools You'll Need:

Before we get started, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools on hand:

  • Curling iron or wand (preferably with a small barrel)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Comb or brush

Prepping Your Hair:

Once you have your tools ready, it's time to prep your hair. Here's what you should do:

  1. Wash your hair and make sure it's completely dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.

Curling Your Hair:

Now it's time to start curling your hair:

  1. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold it for 5-10 seconds (depending on your hair's thickness) and release.
  2. Repeat this process with each section of hair until your entire head is curled.
  3. Once you've curled all of your hair, use a comb or brush to gently loosen the curls and create more volume.

Fluffing Your Hair:

The final step is to fluff up your curls to create that fluffy, voluminous look:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to give it more volume and texture.
  2. Flip your hair upside-down and gently shake it out with your fingers to loosen the curls even more.
  3. Flip your hair back up and use your fingers to separate and fluff up the curls.
  4. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly, Fluffy Hair:

Now that you've achieved the curly, fluffy look you've been dreaming of, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate and fluff up your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs:

Q: Can I achieve this look without using heat?

A: While heat is the most effective way to create curls, you can try using foam rollers or braiding your hair overnight to achieve a similar look.

Q: How long will this style last?

A: With the right hair care and maintenance, this style can last for several days.

Q: Will this work on all hair types?

A: This style can work on most hair types, but it may require some modifications depending on your hair's texture and thickness.


Hair Care:

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage. Instead, use a scrunchie or hair clip to keep your hair in place.

There you have it â a step-by-step guide on how to make short hair curly and fluffy! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, voluminous look that will turn heads. Just remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Happy styling!
