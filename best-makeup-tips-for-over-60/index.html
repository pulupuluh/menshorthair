Best Makeup Tips For Over 60

Written by Eveline Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our skin changes and so should our makeup routine. It can be challenging to navigate the world of makeup as we get older, but fear not! With the right techniques, products, and tools, you can look fabulous at any age. In this article, we will share the best makeup tips for over 60 to help you achieve a youthful and radiant look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prep Your Skin
  • Best Makeup Products for Over 60
  • How to Apply Makeup for Over 60
  • Tips for Makeup on Mature Skin
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Prep Your Skin

Before applying any makeup, it's important to prep your skin properly. As we age, our skin tends to become drier and loses its elasticity. To combat this, make sure to moisturize your skin daily with a hydrating moisturizer.

You can also use a primer to create a smooth canvas for your makeup. Look for a primer that has a blurring effect to minimize the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Finally, don't forget to use sunscreen. Sun damage can cause premature aging, so it's important to protect your skin from harmful UV rays. Look for a sunscreen with at least SPF 30 and apply it daily before leaving the house.

Best Makeup Products for Over 60

When it comes to makeup products for mature skin, there are a few things to keep in mind. Look for products that are hydrating, lightweight, and won't settle into fine lines and wrinkles. Here are some of the best makeup products for over 60:

  • Hydrating foundation or tinted moisturizer
  • Cream blush
  • Neutral eyeshadows
  • Lengthening mascara
  • Moisturizing lipstick or lip gloss

How to Apply Makeup for Over 60

Now that you have your products ready, it's time to apply them. Here's how to apply makeup for over 60:

  1. Start with a hydrating foundation or tinted moisturizer. Apply it evenly with a sponge or brush, blending it into your skin.
  2. Add a pop of color to your cheeks with a cream blush. Smile and apply it to the apples of your cheeks, blending it upwards towards your temples.
  3. Apply a neutral eyeshadow to your eyelids. Use a fluffy brush to blend it outwards towards your brow bone.
  4. Lengthen your lashes with mascara. Apply it to your top and bottom lashes, wiggling the wand back and forth to separate your lashes.
  5. Finish off with a moisturizing lipstick or lip gloss. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Tips for Makeup on Mature Skin

Here are some additional tips for makeup on mature skin:

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup. Too much product can settle into fine lines and wrinkles, making them more noticeable.
  • Avoid matte or powder products, as they can make your skin look dry and dull.
  • Use a setting spray to help your makeup last longer and stay in place.
  • Invest in good quality brushes and tools. They can make a big difference in how your makeup looks and applies.

FAQs

What kind of foundation should I use?

Look for a hydrating foundation or tinted moisturizer that will provide coverage without settling into fine lines and wrinkles.

Should I use powder products?

Avoid using matte or powder products, as they can make your skin look dry and dull. Opt for cream or liquid products instead.

What color lipstick should I wear?

Choose a lipstick or lip gloss that complements your skin tone. Neutral shades like pink, peach, and mauve are great options.

Hair Care

Don't forget about your hair! As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle. To keep your hair looking healthy and full, make sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.

You can also add volume to your hair with a volumizing spray or mousse. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and cuts to find what works best for you.

With these makeup tips for over 60 and hair care advice, you'll be looking and feeling your best in no time. Remember, age is just a number and you can rock any look you want!
