How To Cut Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and low-maintenance, a short sides long top cut could be just what you need. This stylish look is versatile enough to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types, and it's easy to maintain with regular trims.

In this post, we'll walk you through the steps necessary to get a perfect short sides long top haircut. We'll cover everything from preparation and tools to cutting techniques and hair care tips, so you'll be ready to tackle this style with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Tools Needed
  • Cutting Techniques
  • Tips for a Great Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Preparation

Before you start cutting, it's important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve. Look for pictures of short sides long top haircuts that you like and bring them with you to the salon or barber. This will help your stylist understand exactly what you're looking for.

You should also make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of your hair, which will help you achieve a more even cut.

Tools Needed

To cut a short sides long top style, you'll need a few basic tools:

  • Scissors: A good pair of scissors is essential for any haircut. Look for scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair, as they will be sharper and more precise.
  • Clippers: Clippers are used to create the short sides of the haircut. Look for clippers with adjustable blades, so you can vary the length of the cut.
  • Comb: A comb is used to separate sections of hair and create a clean, even part.
  • Hair Gel or Wax: These products help to define the hair and keep it in place.

Cutting Techniques

The key to a successful short sides long top cut is to create a sharp contrast between the short sides and the long top. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by using the clippers to create a short, even cut on the sides and back of the head. You can vary the length depending on your preference, but a #1 or #2 guard is a good starting point.
  2. Next, use the scissors to cut the hair on top of the head. This should be done in sections, starting at the front and working your way back. Use the comb to create a clean, even part and then cut the hair to your desired length.
  3. Blend the sides and top together by using the scissors to create a gradual transition between the two lengths. This should be done with short, quick snips, taking care not to cut too much hair at once.
  4. Finally, use hair gel or wax to define the style and keep it in place. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through the hair, starting at the front and working your way back.

Tips for a Great Cut

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short sides long top haircut:

  • Be patient: Cutting hair takes time, so don't rush the process. Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Communicate with your stylist: Make sure you're clear about the style you want to achieve and ask your stylist for their advice on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Practice good hair care: Regular trims and proper hair care will help to keep your short sides long top cut looking sharp and fresh. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner and avoid over-styling with heat tools.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about short sides long top haircuts:

1. Is a short sides long top haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and shape to flatter your features.

2. How often should I get a trim?

It's a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking sharp and well-maintained.

3. Can I style my short sides long top cut in different ways?

Yes, this style is very versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different hair products and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short sides long top cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a perfect short sides long top haircut that looks great and is easy to maintain. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
