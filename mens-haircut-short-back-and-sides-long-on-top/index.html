Mens Haircut Short Back And Sides Long On Top A Guide To The Relaxed Style

Written by Luffy Sep 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut that's easy to wear, then the mens haircut short back and sides long on top might be just what you need. This popular style has been around for decades and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect short back and sides long on top haircut, the best products to use, and how to take care of your hair.

Description: What is a Short Back and Sides Long on Top Haircut?

A short back and sides long on top haircut is a style that features a close-cropped cut on the sides and back of the head, while the hair on top is left longer. The hair is usually cut at an angle, with the longest hair at the front and gradually getting shorter towards the back. This style is versatile and can be worn in various ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get the Perfect Short Back and Sides Long on Top Haircut:

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

The first step in getting a great haircut is finding a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience with this style and can give you the exact look you want. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family, or check online reviews to find a reputable barber in your area.

Step 2: Discuss Your Haircut with Your Barber

When you're at the barber shop, make sure to discuss your haircut with your barber. Show them pictures of the style you want and talk to them about the length and texture you prefer. Be open to their suggestions and ask for their professional opinion.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

Once you and your barber have agreed on the style, it's time to get the haircut. Your barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, usually with clippers. They will then use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length and shape. The hair at the front will be left longer than the hair at the back, creating the angled look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to style your hair. You can use a variety of products to achieve different looks, from a slicked-back style to a messy, textured look. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Back and Sides Long on Top Haircut:

Now that you have your new haircut, it's essential to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your short back and sides long on top haircut:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep the style looking fresh and clean.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice on how to maintain your haircut.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How often should I get my short back and sides long on top haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and clean.

Q: Can I style my short back and sides long on top haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, this style is versatile and can be styled in different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

Q: What products should I use to style my short back and sides long on top haircut?

A: You can use a variety of products to achieve different looks, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Hair Care for Short Back and Sides Long on Top Haircut:

Proper hair care is essential to keep your short back and sides long on top haircut looking great. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or flat irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the short back and sides long on top haircut with ease. Remember to find a good barber, discuss your haircut with them, and take care of your hair to keep your style looking great.
