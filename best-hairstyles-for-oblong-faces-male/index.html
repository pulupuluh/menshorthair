Best Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Male

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oblong faces male. An oblong face is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all of similar width. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider the shape of your face to enhance your features and create a balanced look. Here are some hairstyles that work best for oblong faces.

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a strong jawline and high forehead. It's often referred to as a rectangular face shape. The key to finding the best hairstyle for this face shape is to add volume to the sides and avoid styles that would elongate the face further.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, keep in mind that you want to create the illusion of width. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the face. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make the face look even longer.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face. 2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a side-swept fringe or textured layers. 3. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate the face further. 4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension to your hairstyle.

Tips

1. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. 2. Use hair products such as mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. 3. Consider consulting with a hairstylist who specializes in men's hair to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a buzz cut if I have an oblong face? A: Yes, but it's important to keep the sides short and leave more length on top to add volume and width to the face. Q: What is the best length for a hairstyle for an oblong face? A: The best length is usually mid-length, as it adds volume to the sides of the face without elongating it further. Q: What are some hairstyles to avoid if I have an oblong face? A: Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as well as styles that are slicked back or flat to the head, as they can elongate the face further.

Hair Care

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type, such as shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for fine or thick hair. Regular trims and styling can also help maintain the shape of your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that work well for oblong faces male. The key is to add volume to the sides of the face to create the illusion of width. Experiment with different hairstyles and consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
