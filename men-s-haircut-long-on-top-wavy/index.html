Men s Haircut Long On Top Wavy Rocking The Relaxed Look

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

In recent years, men's hairstyles have evolved and become more diverse. From buzz cuts to man buns, there is no shortage of options for men to choose from. One classic hairstyle that has stood the test of time is the men's haircut long on top wavy. This hairstyle is perfect for men with wavy or curly hair who want to showcase their natural texture while keeping their hair long on top. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect men's haircut long on top wavy and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Achieve the Perfect Men's Haircut Long on Top Wavy

If you're looking to get a men's haircut long on top wavy, you'll need to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting men's hair. Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect men's haircut long on top wavy:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step to getting a men's haircut long on top wavy is to choose the right length. This will depend on your personal preference, but generally, the hair on top should be at least 3 inches long. This will give you enough length to showcase your natural texture while still having a manageable hairstyle.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back Shorter

To achieve the men's haircut long on top wavy, the sides and back of your hair should be cut shorter than the hair on top. This will create a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back, which is essential for this hairstyle.

Step 3: Add Texture

Once the sides and back are cut shorter, it's time to add texture to the hair on top. This can be done by using thinning shears or razors to create choppy layers. The layers will help to enhance your hair's natural texture, making it easier to style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut and textured, it's time to style it. To style the men's haircut long on top wavy, apply a styling product to damp hair, such as a mousse or sea salt spray. Then, use your fingers to scrunch your hair, creating waves and enhancing your natural texture. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your waves and add volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Haircut Long on Top Wavy

Now that you have the perfect men's haircut long on top wavy, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to the ends of your hair to keep them from becoming dry and frizzy.

FAQs About Men's Haircut Long on Top Wavy

What face shape is best suited for the men's haircut long on top wavy?

The men's haircut long on top wavy is suitable for most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with square or oval faces. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the sides and back shorter to avoid adding too much volume to your hair.

Can I still get a men's haircut long on top wavy if I don't have wavy hair?

Yes, you can still get a men's haircut long on top wavy even if you don't have naturally wavy hair. You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves in your hair, or you can apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers to enhance your natural texture.

What styling products are best for the men's haircut long on top wavy?

The best styling products for the men's haircut long on top wavy are those that enhance your hair's natural texture. Mousse, sea salt spray, and texturizing spray are all great options. Avoid using heavy products, such as gel or pomade, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Long on Top Wavy

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the men's haircut long on top wavy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's haircut long on top wavy with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
