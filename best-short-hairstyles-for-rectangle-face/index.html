Best Short Hairstyles For Rectangle Face

Written by Daniel Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but don't know which one suits your rectangular face? Worry no more! We have got you covered with some of the best short hairstyles for a rectangle face. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right hairstyle, step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care for a rectangle face shape.

Description

A rectangular face shape has a longer forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The length and width of the face are almost equal, making it appear angular. The best short hairstyles for rectangle face shape are the ones that add volume and width to the face to balance out the angular features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

Choosing the right hairstyle for a rectangle face shape can be a daunting task. Here are some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle:

  • Short hairstyles that add volume and width to the face are ideal for a rectangle face shape.
  • Side-swept bangs and layered hairstyles can soften the angular features of a rectangular face.
  • Avoid straight and long hairstyles that can make the face appear longer.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide for Best Short Hairstyles for Rectangle Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best short hairstyles for a rectangle face:

1. Bob Hairstyle

Bob hairstyle is one of the best short hairstyles for a rectangle face. Here is how to achieve it:

  1. Cut your hair into a chin-length bob.
  2. Add layers to the sides to create volume and width.
  3. Style the hair with a side parting to balance out the angular features.

2. Pixie Cut

Pixie cut is a trendy and stylish short hairstyle for a rectangle face. Here is how to achieve it:

  1. Cut your hair into a short pixie cut.
  2. Add layers and texture to the sides to create volume and width.
  3. Style the hair with a side-swept fringe to soften the angular features.

3. Layered Bob

Layered bob is a versatile short hairstyle that can suit a rectangle face shape. Here is how to achieve it:

  1. Cut your hair into a layered bob.
  2. Add layers to the sides to create volume and width.
  3. Style the hair with a side-swept fringe to soften the angular features.

Tips for Best Short Hairstyles for Rectangle Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind while styling your short hair for a rectangle face shape:

  • Use hair products that add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that require too much maintenance.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume to the hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQ

1. Can I have a long hairstyle if I have a rectangle face shape?

Long hairstyles can make the face appear longer, which is not ideal for a rectangle face shape. It is best to stick with short hairstyles that add volume and width to the face.

2. Can I have bangs if I have a rectangle face shape?

Side-swept bangs can soften the angular features of a rectangle face shape. It is best to avoid blunt bangs that can make the face appear longer.

Hair Care for Best Short Hairstyles for Rectangle Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind while maintaining short hair for a rectangle face shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourishment to the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and volume.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling that can damage the hair.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for a rectangle face shape, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you the best. Remember to consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair. Happy styling!
