How To Shape Your Face A Step By Step Guide To Flattering Your Features

Written by Christine Apr 28, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you looking to enhance your natural beauty by shaping your face? Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, there are simple techniques you can use to accentuate your best features and create a more balanced look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for shaping your face, including hair styles, makeup tips, and skincare advice. So let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start shaping your face, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose the right hairstyle, makeup, and skincare products to flatter your features. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

Using a tape measure, measure the distance from your hairline to the highest point of your forehead.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Measure the distance across your cheekbones, starting from the top of one cheekbone to the top of the other.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Measure the distance from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone begins to angle upwards.

Step 4: Measure Your Face Length

Measure the distance from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Now that you have your measurements, you can determine your face shape using the following guidelines:

  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape.
  • If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your face tapers towards your chin, you have a heart-shaped face.
  • If your face is longer than it is wide, with a pointed chin, you have an oval face.
  • If your face is widest at the cheekbones and tapers towards the forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape.
  • If your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline and forehead, you have a pear-shaped face.
  • If your face is roughly the same width at the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, but has soft curves, you have a round face shape.

How to Shape Your Face with Hair

Your hairstyle can have a big impact on the overall shape of your face. Here are some tips for using hair to shape your face:

  • If you have a round face, try a long, layered cut that falls below your chin. This will help elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, try a soft, layered cut with side-swept bangs. This will help soften your features.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, try a chin-length bob with soft layers. This will help balance your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle!

How to Shape Your Face with Makeup

Makeup can also be a powerful tool for shaping your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Contouring can help give your face more definition. Use a contouring powder or cream that's a few shades darker than your skin tone and apply it under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and on the sides of your forehead.
  • Highlighting can help bring out your best features. Use a highlighting powder or cream that's a few shades lighter than your skin tone and apply it to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and the center of your forehead.
  • Use a blush that complements your skin tone to add a pop of color to your cheeks.
  • Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in your eyebrows and create a more defined shape.
  • Choose eye makeup that flatters your eye shape. For example, if you have round eyes, apply eyeliner in a winged shape to elongate your eyes. If you have almond-shaped eyes, you can pretty much pull off any eye makeup look!

How to Shape Your Face with Skincare

Finally, taking care of your skin can also help shape your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and using a moisturizer that's appropriate for your skin type.
  • Use a gentle exfoliator once or twice a week to remove dead skin cells and reveal brighter, smoother skin.
  • Protect your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen with at least SPF 30 and avoiding prolonged exposure to the sun.
  • If you have acne-prone skin, use products that contain salicylic acid or benzoyl peroxide to help prevent breakouts.

Tips for Shaping Your Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when shaping your face:

  • Choose clothing and accessories that flatter your face shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face, try wearing earrings that are wider at the bottom than at the top.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles, makeup looks, and skincare products until you find what works best for you.
  • If you're unsure about which face shape you have or how to shape your face, consider consulting with a hairstylist, makeup artist, or skincare professional.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my face shape?

A: While you can't change your bone structure, you can use hair, makeup, and skincare to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q: What's the best way to contour my face?

A: Use a contouring powder or cream that's a few shades darker than your skin tone and apply it under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and on the sides of your forehead. Blend well for a natural look.

Q: How can I make my round face look more angular?

A: Try a long, layered haircut that falls below your chin and use contouring makeup to add definition to your cheekbones and jawline.

Hair Care for Shaped Face

Whether you're using hair, makeup, or skincare to shape your face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Shaping your face can be a fun and rewarding process. With the right techniques and products, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel more confident in your own skin. So go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles, makeup looks, and skincare products until you find what works best for you!
