How To Make My Hair Look Messy Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's low-maintenance and effortlessly cool, then the messy look is perfect for you. This style is great for guys who want to look relaxed and laid-back, but still put-together. In this guide, we'll show you how to make your hair look messy in just a few simple steps.

How to Make My Hair Look Messy Guys

The messy look is all about creating a style that looks effortless and natural. It's perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance style that still looks cool and put-together. Here's how to make your hair look messy:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help the product you use to hold better and last longer.

2. Choose the right product.

The key to achieving the messy look is using the right product. We recommend using a texturizing spray, wax, or pomade. These products will help create texture and hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

3. Apply the product.

Start by applying a small amount of product to your fingertips. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Then, work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and the ends.

4. Mess it up.

Once you've applied the product, it's time to start creating the messy look. Use your fingers to tousle your hair, pulling it in different directions to create texture and volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair look messy:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help the product you use to hold better and last longer.

Step 2: Choose the right product.

Tips for Maintaining the Messy Look

Here are some tips for maintaining the messy look:

1. Use the right product.

Choosing the right product is key to maintaining the messy look. Stick to texturizing sprays, waxes, or pomades that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

2. Don't overdo it.

Less is more when it comes to the messy look. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair gradually, adding more as needed.

3. Use your fingers.

Instead of using a comb or brush, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture. This will help maintain the natural, effortless look.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the messy look with short hair?

A: Yes! The messy look works well with all hair lengths, including short hair. Just adjust the amount of product you use accordingly.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain the messy look?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain the messy look. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.

Q: Can I use hairspray to maintain the messy look?

A: We don't recommend using hairspray to maintain the messy look. Hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural. Stick to texturizing sprays, waxes, or pomades instead.

Hair Care for the Messy Look

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo.

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's sulfate-free and contains natural ingredients.

2. Condition regularly.

Conditioning your hair regularly will help keep it soft and manageable. Use a conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.

3. Trim your hair regularly.

Regular trims will help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy look in no time. Remember to choose the right product, use your fingers to create texture, and don't overdo it. Happy styling!
