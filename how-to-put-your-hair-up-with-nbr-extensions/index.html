How To Put Your Hair Up With Nbr Extensions

Written by Eveline Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Putting your hair up with NBR extensions can be a bit of a challenge, but it's definitely doable with the right techniques. In this article, we'll share with you some easy steps to follow to help you put your hair up with NBR extensions.

Description

Natural Beaded Rows (NBR) extensions are a type of hair extension that are popular among women who want to add length and volume to their hair. These extensions are made from high-quality human hair and are attached to your natural hair using a beaded track method. One of the biggest advantages of NBR extensions is that they look and feel incredibly natural, making them a great choice for everyday wear.

How to Put Your Hair Up with NBR Extensions

Putting your hair up with NBR extensions can be a bit tricky, especially if you're used to styling your hair without extensions. But with a little practice and the right techniques, you'll be able to create a variety of updos that look great with your extensions.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to put your hair up with NBR extensions:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Decide on the type of updo you want to create. Some popular options include a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo.
  3. If you're creating a ponytail or bun, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure to leave out a few sections of hair to create a more natural, tousled look.
  4. If you're creating a braided updo, start by braiding a small section of hair at the front of your head. Continue adding sections of hair to the braid until you reach the nape of your neck, then secure the braid with an elastic band. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. If you're creating a messy bun, gently tug on the sections of hair that you left out of the elastic band to create a more disheveled look.
  6. If you're creating a braided updo, gently tug on the sections of the braid to create a more voluminous look.
  7. If you have any loose strands of hair, use bobby pins to secure them in place.
  8. If you want to add some extra volume to your updo, tease the crown of your head using a teasing comb.
  9. Spray your hair with hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting your hair up with NBR extensions:

  • Use a gentle touch when handling your extensions to avoid damaging them.
  • Don't pull your hair too tight when creating an updo to avoid putting unnecessary stress on your scalp.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the ones that work best for you and your extensions.
  • Use high-quality hair products to help protect your extensions and keep them looking their best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting your hair up with NBR extensions:

  • Q: Can I put my hair up with NBR extensions?
  • A: Yes, you can put your hair up with NBR extensions. Just be gentle when handling your extensions and avoid pulling your hair too tight.
  • Q: What updo styles work best with NBR extensions?
  • A: Messy buns, sleek ponytails, and braided updos are all great options for women with NBR extensions.
  • Q: How do I protect my NBR extensions when putting my hair up?
  • A: Use high-quality hair products and be gentle when handling your extensions to avoid damaging them.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your NBR extensions looking their best. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your extensions of their natural oils.
  • Don't wash your hair too often to avoid drying out your extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions.
  • Brush your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristled brush.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to help keep your extensions looking shiny and healthy.

Putting your hair up with NBR extensions can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right techniques, you can create a variety of updos that look great with your extensions. Follow these steps and tips to help you put your hair up with NBR extensions and keep them looking their best!
