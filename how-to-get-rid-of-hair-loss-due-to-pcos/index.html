How To Get Rid Of Hair Loss Due To Pcos

Written by Daniel Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, you are not alone. Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. One of the symptoms of PCOS is hair loss, which can be distressing for many women. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of hair loss due to PCOS.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Rid of Hair Loss Due to PCOS
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

PCOS is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It is caused by an imbalance of hormones, including insulin, estrogen, and testosterone. This can lead to a variety of symptoms, including irregular periods, acne, weight gain, and hair loss.

Hair loss due to PCOS is caused by an excess of androgens, which are male hormones that are present in both men and women. Androgens can cause hair follicles to shrink, leading to hair loss.

If you are experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, it is important to seek medical advice. Your doctor may recommend medication or other treatments to help manage your symptoms and prevent further hair loss.

How to Get Rid of Hair Loss Due to PCOS

Here are some steps you can take to help get rid of hair loss due to PCOS:

1. Manage Your Insulin Levels

PCOS is often associated with insulin resistance, which can lead to high insulin levels in the body. This can cause an increase in androgens, which can lead to hair loss. To manage your insulin levels, you can:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is low in sugar and refined carbohydrates
  • Exercise regularly
  • Take medication to help manage your insulin levels, if recommended by your doctor

2. Take Medication

Your doctor may recommend medication to help manage your PCOS symptoms and prevent hair loss. Some medications that may be prescribed include:

  • Birth control pills
  • Anti-androgen medication
  • Metformin, which helps to manage insulin levels

3. Use Topical Treatments

There are a variety of topical treatments that may help to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Some of these include:

  • Minoxidil, which is a topical treatment that is applied to the scalp
  • Finasteride, which is a medication that is taken orally

Step by Step

Here are the steps you can take to get rid of hair loss due to PCOS:

  1. Manage your insulin levels through diet, exercise, and medication
  2. Take medication to help manage your PCOS symptoms
  3. Use topical treatments to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help manage hair loss due to PCOS:

  • Avoid hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as tight braids or ponytails
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as perms or coloring
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals

FAQ

What is PCOS?

PCOS is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It is caused by an imbalance of hormones, including insulin, estrogen, and testosterone.

What are the symptoms of PCOS?

The symptoms of PCOS can vary, but may include irregular periods, acne, weight gain, and hair loss.

How is PCOS diagnosed?

PCOS is typically diagnosed through a physical exam, blood tests, and an ultrasound.

Hair Care

If you are experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as perms or coloring
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as tight braids or ponytails
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons

By taking these steps, you can help manage your PCOS symptoms and prevent further hair loss. If you are experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, it is important to seek medical advice and treatment.
