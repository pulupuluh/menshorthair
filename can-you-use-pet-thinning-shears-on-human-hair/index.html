Can You Use Pet Thinning Shears On Human Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a pet owner, you may have noticed that your pet's hair tends to grow quickly and can become tangled easily. To keep your pet looking neat and tidy, you may have invested in a pair of pet thinning shears. But can you use these shears on your own hair as well? In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide tips for using pet thinning shears on human hair.

Description

Pet thinning shears are designed to thin out thick fur on animals. They are made with a specific number of teeth on the blade and are used to remove small amounts of hair at a time, creating a more natural look. These shears are often cheaper and more readily available than human hair thinning shears, which is why some people may be tempted to use them on their own hair.

How to Use Pet Thinning Shears on Human Hair

While it is possible to use pet thinning shears on human hair, it is not recommended. The blades on pet shears are typically wider and the teeth are spaced further apart than those on human hair shears. This means that they are more likely to create uneven and choppy results, which can be difficult to fix.

If you do decide to use pet thinning shears on your own hair, it is important to take extra precautions to avoid damaging your hair. Here are some tips for using pet thinning shears on human hair:

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may result in uneven results.

2. Section your hair into small, manageable sections. This will help you to see what you are cutting and avoid accidentally cutting too much hair at once.

3. Hold the shears at a slight angle and use small, gentle snips to thin out the hair. Avoid using long, sweeping motions as this can create uneven results.

4. Work slowly and carefully, checking your progress frequently. It is better to remove too little hair than too much.

Tips for Using Pet Thinning Shears on Human Hair

While we do not recommend using pet thinning shears on human hair, if you do decide to give it a try, here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

- Use a comb to help guide the shears and ensure even results.

- Invest in a high-quality pair of human hair thinning shears if you plan to thin out your hair regularly.

- Consider consulting a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about using pet shears on your hair.

FAQ

1. Can pet thinning shears be used on human hair extensions?

It is not recommended to use pet shears on human hair extensions as they can damage the hair and cause it to become tangled or break off.

2. Can pet thinning shears be sharpened for use on human hair?

While it is technically possible to sharpen pet thinning shears for use on human hair, it is not recommended. The blades and teeth are designed for a different purpose and may not create the desired results.

Hair Care

Whether you are using pet thinning shears or human hair thinning shears, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.

- Get regular trims to remove split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

While it is possible to use pet thinning shears on human hair, it is not recommended. The blades and teeth are designed for a different purpose and may not create the desired results. If you are unsure about using pet shears on your hair, it is best to consult a professional hairstylist for advice.
