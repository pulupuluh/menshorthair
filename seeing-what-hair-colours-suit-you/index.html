Seeing What Hair Colours Suit You A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Apr 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering a new hair colour but don't know which shade to choose? Seeing what hair colours suit you can be a daunting task, but fear not! We have put together a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect match for your skin tone and personality. Read on to discover our tips and tricks for choosing the best hair colour for you in 2023.

How to: Seeing What Hair Colours Suit You

The first step in finding the right hair colour for you is to consider your skin tone. This will help you determine whether you're better suited to warm or cool tones. If you have warm undertones, you may want to consider shades such as golden blonde, copper, or warm brown. If you have cool undertones, you may want to opt for shades like ashy blonde, cool brown, or black.

Another important factor to consider is your natural hair colour. If you're looking for a subtle change, you may want to choose a shade that is close to your natural hair. If you're looking for a dramatic transformation, you may want to go for a completely different shade.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle and personality. If you're someone who likes to stand out, you may want to consider a bold shade such as pink, purple, or blue. If you prefer a more natural look, you may want to stick to shades such as blonde, brown, or red.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Determine your skin tone

2. Consider your natural hair colour

3. Think about your lifestyle and personality

4. Research different hair colours and shades

5. Consult with a professional hairstylist

Tips:

- Don't be afraid to try something new

- Consider the maintenance required for different hair colours

- Take inspiration from celebrities and social media

- Use temporary hair colour products to test out different shades before committing

FAQ:

Q: Will a certain hair colour suit me based on my ethnicity?

A: No, hair colour suitability is based on skin tone and personal preference, not ethnicity.

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

A: It's best to avoid dyeing damaged hair, as it can lead to further damage. Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice on how to repair your hair before dyeing it.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hair colour?

A: It depends on the hair colour and the rate of hair growth. Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice on how often to touch up your hair colour.

Hair Care:

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure the colour lasts as long as possible. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat and sunlight.

Now that you know how to see what hair colours suit you, it's time to book a consultation with a professional hairstylist and start experimenting! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a shade that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
