What To Do For Frizzy Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Feb 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Frizzy curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can have beautiful, defined curls that are free of frizz. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for managing frizzy curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Manage Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Managing Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

How to Manage Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step to managing frizzy curly hair is to identify the cause of the frizz. Frizz can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, dryness, and damage. Once you identify the cause, you can take steps to manage the frizz.

One of the best things you can do for frizzy curly hair is to keep it moisturized. Dry hair is more prone to frizz, so it’s important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

Another way to manage frizzy curly hair is to avoid heat styling. Heat can damage the hair and make it more prone to frizz, so try to air dry your hair whenever possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Managing Frizzy Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to managing frizzy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair.
  3. Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel to remove excess water.
  5. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  6. Diffuse your hair with a blow dryer on low heat, or air dry your hair.
  7. Scrunch your hair again to release any crunchiness from the product.
  8. Apply a hair oil or serum to your ends to seal in moisture.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt your curls and cause frizz.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my frizzy curly hair?

A: It’s best to use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly or frizzy hair, as these products are formulated to hydrate and smooth the hair.

Q: How often should I wash my frizzy curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Some people with frizzy curly hair can go several days between washes, while others need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my frizzy curly hair?

A: While you can use a hair straightener on your frizzy curly hair, it’s best to avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage and frizz. If you do use a hair straightener, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid using products with sulfates and alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Consider getting a silk or satin hair cap to wear at night to protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can make your hair more prone to frizz.

By following these tips and tricks, you can manage frizzy curly hair and enjoy beautiful, defined curls that are free of frizz.
