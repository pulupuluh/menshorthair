How To Do Messy Hair Look A Step By Step Guide

Messy hair is a popular trend that has been around for years. It is a hairstyle that looks effortless yet stylish. If you want to achieve this look, but you don't know where to start, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to do messy hair look in a relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from how to create the perfect messy bun to how to add volume to your hair. So, let's get started!

How to Do Messy Hair Look

To achieve the messy hair look, you need to start with the right haircut. A layered cut works best for this style as it adds texture and volume. You can also add highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

Next, you need to choose the right products. You'll need a texturizing spray, dry shampoo, and a hairspray. These products will help you create a messy, tousled look.

Once you have your haircut and products, it's time to start styling. You can do messy hair look with various hairstyles like a messy bun, a messy braid, or a messy ponytail. Here are the steps to create a messy bun:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. If your hair is greasy, use dry shampoo to absorb the oil.

Step 2: Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to create volume and texture.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.

Step 4: Twist your hair and wrap it around the elastic band to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 5: Pull out some strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hair look:

  • Don't be too precise â messy hair look is supposed to be messy, so don't worry about making it perfect.
  • Use your fingers to tousle your hair â this will help create a natural, effortless look.
  • Use a sea salt spray to add texture to your hair.
  • Try different hairstyles â messy hair look can be achieved with a messy bun, braid, or ponytail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I do messy hair look with short hair?

A: Yes, you can. Messy hair look can be achieved with any hair length. Just make sure you have enough layers to create texture and volume.

Q: Do I need to use heat tools to achieve messy hair look?

A: No, you don't. Messy hair look can be achieved without heat tools. Just use your fingers to create texture and volume.

Hair Care for Messy Hair Look

To maintain your messy hair look, you need to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to do messy hair look, it's time to try it out. Remember, messy hair look is all about being effortless and natural. So, don't overthink it, and have fun with it!
