Introduction

The messy short hair look has been a trend for quite some time now. This hairstyle gives off a carefree and effortless vibe that a lot of women want to achieve. However, getting the perfect messy short hair look can be a bit challenging. It requires some technique and the right products to achieve the desired result. In this guide, we will show you how to get the messy short hair look girl in a relaxed and easy-to-follow way.

How To Get Messy Short Hair Look Girl

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to achieving the perfect messy short hair look girl is to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Towel-dry your hair, then use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely.

Step 2: Use a texturizing spray or pomade

Next, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some grip to your hair. This will help create the messy look you want. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair.

Step 3: Create messy waves

Now, it's time to create some messy waves in your hair. You can use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves. Start at the roots, then work your way down to the ends of your hair. Don't worry about creating perfect waves - the messier, the better!

Step 4: Add volume with a teasing comb

To add some volume to your hair, use a teasing comb. Tease the roots of your hair by backcombing them to create a lift. You can also tease the crown of your hair to add some height. Be careful not to over-tease your hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

The final step in getting the perfect messy short hair look girl is to finish with a good-quality hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat. Apply the hairspray to your hair, then use your fingers to tousle your hair for a messy finish.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Messy Short Hair Look

  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Choose a texturizing spray or pomade that works well with your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different curling techniques to create the perfect messy look.
  • Use a teasing comb to add volume to your hair, but be gentle to avoid damage.
  • Finish with a good-quality hairspray to hold your hair in place.

FAQs About Messy Short Hair Look Girl

Q: Can I achieve the messy short hair look girl with naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can! Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves in your hair, then follow the steps above to achieve the perfect messy look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain the messy short hair look?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every two days should be enough to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Q: Can I still achieve the messy short hair look girl if my hair is thin?

A: Yes, you can! Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some body to your hair, then follow the steps above to create the messy look.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Short Hair Look

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a good-quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

Getting the perfect messy short hair look girl is easier than you think! With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a carefree and effortless vibe that will make you feel confident and stylish. Follow the steps above, experiment with different curling techniques, and don't be afraid to have fun with your hair!
