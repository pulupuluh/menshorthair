Best Hairstyles For Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 07, 2023 Â· 10 min read
Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Don't worry, you're not alone! Round faces can be tricky to style, but with the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and look fabulous. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for round faces, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your new 'do. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • What is a Round Face?
  • What Hairstyles Look Good with Round Faces?
  • How to Achieve These Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

  • Hair Care for Round Faces

What is a Round Face?

A round face has soft, curved lines with a width and length that are roughly equal. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the chin is rounded. If you have a round face, you may also have fuller cheeks and a softer jawline. Some celebrities with round faces include Selena Gomez, Chrissy Teigen, and Emma Stone.

What Hairstyles Look Good with Round Faces?

When it comes to hairstyles for round faces, the goal is to add some length to your face and create angles. Here are some hairstyles that look great on round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces because they create movement and add length to the face. Ask your hairstylist for long layers that start at the chin or below to draw attention away from the cheeks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for round faces because they create angles and draw attention to the eyes. Ask your hairstylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the eyebrows and angle down towards the cheekbones.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great choice for round faces because it creates the illusion of length. Ask your hairstylist for a chin-length bob with some volume at the crown to elongate your face.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great choice for round faces. Shorter haircuts can help to elongate the face and create angles. Ask your hairstylist for a choppy, textured pixie cut that adds volume at the top.

How to Achieve These Hairstyles

Now that you know which hairstyles look great on round faces, it's time to learn how to achieve them. Here are some tips:

1. Long Layers

To achieve long layers, ask your hairstylist for layers that start at the chin or below. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and movement.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

To achieve side-swept bangs, ask your hairstylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the eyebrows and angle down towards the cheekbones. Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and texture.

3. Bob with Volume

To achieve a bob with volume, ask your hairstylist for a chin-length bob with some volume at the crown. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and lift.

4. Pixie Cut

To achieve a pixie cut, ask your hairstylist for a choppy, textured cut that adds volume at the top. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've achieved your perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Regular Trims

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh, schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and style. For example, if you have thin hair, use a volumizing spray to add lift and texture.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools. You can also protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear a middle part with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a round face, but it's important to add some volume and texture to your hair to create angles. Try adding some waves or curls to your hair to add dimension.

2. Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts if you have a round face. Shorter haircuts can actually be very flattering on round faces, as long as they create angles and add length.

Hair Care for Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

2. Protect your hair from the elements

Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf in cold weather, and by using a UV protectant spray in the sun.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients can help to keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

4. Stay hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Aim for at least 8 glasses of water per day.

Conclusion

We hope this guide has helped you find the perfect hairstyle for your round face. Remember, the key is to create angles and add length to your face. With the right haircut and some simple hair care tips, you can look and feel your best. Good luck!
