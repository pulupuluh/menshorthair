Do Front Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Frank Mar 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this year 2023, front bangs are still a popular hairstyle choice. But the question remains, do front bangs look good on round faces? The answer is yes! Front bangs can actually complement and enhance the features of a round face, if done correctly. In this article, we'll go over how to achieve the perfect front bangs for a round face, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care for maintaining this hairstyle.

How To Achieve The Perfect Front Bangs for a Round Face

When it comes to achieving the perfect front bangs for a round face, it's all about the length and shape of the bangs. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before getting bangs, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you in deciding what kind of bangs will suit you best. For round faces, the goal is to elongate the face and create angles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your front bangs is crucial in achieving the perfect look. It's recommended to go for longer bangs that graze the eyebrows or shorter bangs that end above the eyebrows. This will help elongate the face and create angles. Avoid bangs that are too short or too blunt, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

Step 3: Consider the Shape

The shape of your front bangs is also important. Opt for side-swept bangs that are angled towards the cheekbones. This will create the illusion of angles and elongate the face. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, as they can make the face appear wider.

Tips for Styling Front Bangs on a Round Face

Once you've achieved the perfect front bangs for your round face, here are some tips for styling:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Add some texture to your bangs by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  • Part your bangs slightly off-center to create the illusion of angles.

FAQs About Front Bangs on a Round Face

Q: Can I still get front bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, absolutely! With the right length and shape, front bangs can complement and enhance a round face.

Q: Can I pull off short, blunt bangs with a round face?

A: It's not recommended, as short, blunt bangs can make the face appear even rounder. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs instead.

Q: Will front bangs require a lot of maintenance?

A: Yes, front bangs do require regular trimming to maintain their shape and length. It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Front Bangs on a Round Face

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining front bangs:

  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to avoid over-washing.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.
  • When blow drying your bangs, use a heat protectant to avoid damage.
  • Regularly trim your bangs to maintain their shape and length.

Conclusion

So, do front bangs look good on round faces? Yes, with the right length and shape, front bangs can complement and enhance a round face. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to achieve the perfect front bangs for your round face shape. And don't forget to regularly maintain and care for your bangs to keep them looking their best!
