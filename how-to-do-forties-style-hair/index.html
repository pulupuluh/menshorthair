How To Do Forties Style Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Forties style hair is a classic look that has been popular since the 1940s. It's characterized by soft waves and curls, as well as a deep side part. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this timeless look in just a few easy steps.

How to

To get started, you'll need the following items:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron
  • Hair clips
  • Hair spray

Here's how to do forties style hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start by parting your hair on the side, and then divide your hair into two sections - one on each side of your head.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it for a few seconds, and then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Set Your Curls

Once your hair is curled, let it cool for a few minutes. Then, remove the hair clips and gently brush out your curls with a soft-bristled brush.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your curls stay in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect forties style hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel for tighter curls.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends - leave the last inch or so straight for a more natural look.
  • Use hair clips to hold your curls in place as they cool.
  • Experiment with different parts to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about forties style hair:

How long does it take to do forties style hair?

It typically takes about 30-45 minutes to curl your hair in a forties style.

Can I do forties style hair on short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a forties style look with shorter hair. Just use a smaller curling iron to create tighter curls.

How often should I wash my hair if I want to do forties style hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you want to maintain the forties style look.

Hair Care

To keep your forties style hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair - limit your use of curling irons and flat irons.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect forties style hair in no time. Whether you're going for a retro look or just want to add some vintage-inspired glamour to your everyday style, this classic look is sure to turn heads.
