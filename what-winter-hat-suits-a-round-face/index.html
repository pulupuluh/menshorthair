What Winter Hat Suits A Round Face

Written by Bardi Mar 21, 2023 · 8 min read

Winter is the season of cold winds, snow, and fashion. Everyone wants to look their best while staying warm in the winter season. However, it can be a little tricky to choose the right winter hat that suits your face shape. In this blog post, we will discuss what winter hat suits a round face.

  • How to Choose the Right Winter Hat for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Winter Hat for a Round Face

Description

A round face is characterized by soft curves and a lack of sharp angles. If you have a round face, you should avoid hats that add extra volume to your head, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, you should opt for hats that elongate your face and add some height to your head.

How to Choose the Right Winter Hat for a Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right winter hat for a round face, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, you should look for hats that have a vertical element, such as a high crown or a tall pom-pom. This will help elongate your face and make it appear less round.

Second, you should avoid hats that have a round shape, such as berets or beanies. These hats will only add extra volume to your head and make your face appear rounder. Instead, you should opt for hats that have a more angular shape, such as fedoras or newsboy caps.

Third, you should pay attention to the material of the hat. Hats that are made of thick, heavy material can add extra bulk to your head and make your face appear rounder. Instead, you should opt for hats that are made of lightweight, breathable material, such as wool or cashmere.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Winter Hat for a Round Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right winter hat for a round face:

  1. Look for hats that have a vertical element, such as a high crown or a tall pom-pom.
  2. Avoid hats that have a round shape, such as berets or beanies.
  3. Opt for hats that have a more angular shape, such as fedoras or newsboy caps.
  4. Pay attention to the material of the hat. Opt for hats that are made of lightweight, breathable material, such as wool or cashmere.

Tips for Choosing the Right Winter Hat for a Round Face

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right winter hat for a round face:

  • Choose a hat that complements your outfit and personal style.
  • Consider the color of the hat. Darker colors can make your face appear slimmer, while lighter colors can make it appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different styles and shapes of hats to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hat with a scarf or gloves to complete your winter look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about what winter hat suits a round face:

Q: Can I wear a beanie if I have a round face?

A: While beanies can add extra volume to your head, you can still wear them if you choose the right style. Look for beanies that have a slouchy, relaxed fit and avoid ones that fit tightly around your head.

Q: What color hat should I choose if I have a round face?

A: Darker colors can make your face appear slimmer, so you may want to opt for a hat in a darker shade. However, you can still wear lighter colors if you choose the right style and shape of hat.

Q: Can I wear a hat with ear flaps if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat with ear flaps if you choose the right style. Look for hats with ear flaps that are angled downward, as this will help elongate your face.

Winter Hair Care Tips

Winter can be harsh on your hair, so it's important to take care of it during the colder months. Here are some winter hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair less frequently to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons.
  • Wear a hat to protect your hair from the cold and wind.

Choosing the right winter hat for a round face can be a little tricky, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hat for your face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and shapes to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to accessorize your hat with a scarf or gloves to complete your winter look.
