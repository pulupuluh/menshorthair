How To Cut Perfect Side Swept Bangs At Home A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to change up your hairstyle, cutting side swept bangs at home can be a great option. Not only is it a simple way to update your look, but it can also help frame your face and accentuate your features. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut perfect side swept bangs at home, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your new style looking fresh and fabulous.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs at Home

Before you start cutting your bangs, it's important to have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, here's how to cut perfect side swept bangs at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair where you normally do.
  3. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  4. Use a comb to brush the hair forward and down over your forehead.
  5. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers where you want to make the cut.
  6. Cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to only cut a small amount at a time. You can always trim more if needed, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  7. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you reach the desired length.
  8. Once you've cut the bangs, use the comb to blend the hair into the rest of your hair.
  9. Style your new side swept bangs as desired using a blow dryer or flat iron.

Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you cut perfect side swept bangs at home:

  • Start with longer hair than you think you need. You can always trim more, but you can't add hair back.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Instead, cut them at a slight angle to create a more natural look.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • If you have curly hair, straighten your bangs before cutting them to ensure a more accurate cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side swept bangs at home:

How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

You should trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and well-maintained.

Can I cut side swept bangs on my own?

Yes, you can cut side swept bangs on your own at home. Just make sure to follow the steps outlined above and take your time.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake while cutting your bangs, don't panic. You can always trim more hair to fix the mistake. If the mistake is severe, you can visit a hairstylist to have them fix it for you.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Once you've cut your side swept bangs at home, it's important to take care of them to ensure they stay looking great. Here are some hair care tips for side swept bangs:

  • Wash your bangs daily to keep them clean and oil-free.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to help them maintain their shape.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as it can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • If you have oily skin, use a headband or clips to keep your bangs off your forehead to prevent them from becoming greasy.

By following these tips and taking care of your side swept bangs, you can enjoy a fresh and stylish look all year round.
