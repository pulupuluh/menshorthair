How To Style Men s Curly Hair Up Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Luffy Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is unique and requires a specific approach to styling. Men with curly hair often struggle to find the right style that suits them. Styling curly hair can be challenging, but once you learn how to work with it, you can create a great look that will make you stand out. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style men's curly hair up. We will also give you some tips on how to care for your curly hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. Preparation
  3. Products
  4. Styling
  5. Tips
  6. FAQ

Hair Care

Before styling your hair, it is essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils, which can make it frizzy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners. Heat can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.

Preparation

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. If you have just washed your hair, towel dry it gently. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot the excess water.

Products

The right products can make a huge difference in how your curly hair looks. Here are some products that you can use:

  • A leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.
  • A styling cream or gel to define your curls and hold your hair in place.
  • A hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

Styling

Here's how to style men's curly hair up:

  1. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to moisturize it.
  2. Use a styling cream or gel to define your curls. Apply it evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots to the tips.
  3. Part your hair on one side or in the middle, depending on your preference.
  4. Use your fingers to create volume and lift your hair up. You can also use a comb to create a more polished look.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you get the most out of your curly hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be dry, and washing it too often can strip it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally, you should wash your curly hair once or twice a week. Washing it too often can strip it of natural oils, which can make it dry and frizzy.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's not recommended. Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it frizzy. If you want to straighten your hair, use a heat protectant and a low-heat setting.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, use a leave-in conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools. You can also use a hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.

Q: What's the best haircut for curly hair?

A: The best haircut for curly hair is one that suits your face shape and hair type. Some popular haircuts for curly hair include the undercut, the curly fringe, and the curly quiff.

Q: Can I use the same products for curly and straight hair?

A: No, you should use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Curly hair requires more moisture and hold than straight hair, and using the wrong products can make it frizzy and unmanageable.

Styling men's curly hair up can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can create a great look that suits your style. Remember to take care of your hair properly, experiment with different styles, and have fun with it!
