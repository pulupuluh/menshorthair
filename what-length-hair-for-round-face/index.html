What Length Hair For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 01, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but none of them seem to suit your round face? Well, you're not alone. Many women struggle with finding the right hair length for their round face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about what length hair for round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with soft angles and a width that is almost equal to its length. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. The right length of hair is crucial when it comes to achieving this.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what length hair for round face, let's first determine your face shape. To do this, you'll need to do the following:

  1. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror.
  3. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  4. Step back and observe the shape that you traced.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to finding the right length of hair for your round face.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on what length hair for round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Texture

The first step in finding the right hair length for your round face is to determine your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the thickness and coarseness of your hair. If you have fine hair, you'll want to stick to shorter haircuts that will add volume and body to your hair. If you have thick hair, longer hairstyles will work better for you as they will help to weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in the length of hair you choose. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style your hair, a shorter haircut will be more manageable. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on styling your hair, a longer haircut will offer more versatility.

Step 3: Choose the Right Length

Now that you know your hair texture and lifestyle, it's time to choose the right length of hair for your round face. Generally, hairstyles that fall below the chin work best for round faces. This length will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. You can also opt for a layered haircut to add more volume and movement to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair length for your round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that end at your chin as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle. Side-swept bangs will help to create the illusion of a more angular face.
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers to add more volume and movement to your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that end at your chin as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too short as they will make your face appear wider.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, make sure to keep the ponytail low and loose to avoid accentuating the roundness of your face.

What is the best length of hair for a round face?

Generally, hairstyles that fall below the chin work best for round faces. This length will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Hair Care

Regardless of the length of hair you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.

Now that you know what length hair for round face, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong.
