How To Use Clippers To Cut Hair On Top Of Head

Written by Petter Apr 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Clippers are a great tool for cutting hair, especially for those who prefer a shorter style. They are easy to use and can save you time and money on trips to the salon. In this article, we will discuss how to use clippers to cut hair on top of the head.

Description

Using clippers to cut hair on top of the head is a simple process that can be done at home. Clippers are typically electric and come with different guard sizes to help achieve the desired length. Cutting hair with clippers is a great option for those who prefer a shorter style, as it can be done quickly and easily.

How to

Before using clippers to cut hair, it is important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. It is also a good idea to have a towel or cape to catch any hair that falls during the process.

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the clippers can glide smoothly through your hair.

Step 2: Choose the guard size that you want to use. The larger the guard, the longer the hair will be. It is a good idea to start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Begin by using the clippers at the base of the hairline, moving the clippers up and away from the head. Continue to move around the head, using the clippers to create an even length on top of the head.

Step 4: Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer pieces. This will help to create an even length and ensure that the hair is not too bulky.

Step 5: Once you have achieved the desired length, use the clippers without a guard to trim around the edges and create a clean line.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair
2. Choose the guard size
3. Begin at the base of the hairline
4. Use the clippers to create an even length
5. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer pieces
6. Use the clippers without a guard to trim around the edges

Tips

- Start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length
- Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer pieces
- Use the clippers without a guard to trim around the edges and create a clean line
- Use a towel or cape to catch any hair that falls during the process

FAQ

Q: How often should I use clippers to cut my hair?
A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame to keep your hair trimmed with clippers.

Q: Can I use clippers on wet hair?
A: It is not recommended to use clippers on wet hair, as it can cause the clippers to become dull and damage the hair.

Hair Care

After using clippers to cut your hair, it is important to maintain it properly. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a leave-in conditioner to keep it moisturized, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. It is also a good idea to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
