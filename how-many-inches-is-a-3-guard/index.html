How Many Inches Is A 3 Guard

Written by Oliver Apr 05, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are someone who is new to cutting hair, it can be quite confusing when it comes to the different clipper guard sizes. One of the most common questions asked is, “How many inches is a 3 guard?” In this article, we will delve into everything you need to know about a 3 guard and its measurement.

Description

A clipper guard is an attachment that is used to determine the length of hair that is cut. It fits onto the blade of the clipper and can be adjusted to different sizes. Each guard is numbered, indicating the length of hair that will be left after the cut. A 3 guard is one of the most commonly used guards and is used for a medium length haircut.

How to use a 3 guard

Using a 3 guard is relatively simple. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  • Attach the 3 guard to the clipper blade
  • Turn on the clipper and run it through the hair, starting at the back of the head
  • Move the clipper in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth
  • Repeat the process until the desired length is achieved

It is important to note that the length of hair left after the cut will depend on the type of hair and the direction in which the clipper is moved.

Step by step guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a medium length haircut using a 3 guard:

  1. Start at the back of the head and run the clipper through the hair in an upward motion
  2. Move the clipper to the side of the head and run it through the hair in an upward motion
  3. Repeat the process on the other side of the head
  4. Move to the top of the head and run the clipper through the hair in an upward motion
  5. Blend the sides and the top of the head by running the clipper over the area where they meet
  6. Trim the hair around the ears and the neckline with a smaller guard
  7. Style the hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a 3 guard:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Clean and oil your clipper regularly

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about a 3 guard:

1. How long is a 3 guard?

A 3 guard leaves 3/8 of an inch of hair after the cut.

2. What is a 3.5 guard?

A 3.5 guard is the next size up from a 3 guard and leaves 7/16 of an inch of hair after the cut.

3. Can I use a 3 guard for a buzz cut?

No, a 3 guard is not suitable for a buzz cut as it leaves too much hair behind.

Hair care

After the haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Now that you know how many inches is a 3 guard, you can confidently use it to achieve a medium length haircut. Just remember to take your time, use a comb to lift the hair, and clean and oil your clipper regularly to ensure it lasts a long time.
