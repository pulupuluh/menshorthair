Pull Off Baby Bangs A How To Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and add a little bit of edge to your look, baby bangs may be the perfect choice for you. Baby bangs, also known as micro bangs, are a short fringe that sits above the eyebrows. They can be worn blunt or textured, and can complement a variety of hair lengths and textures. In this article, we'll show you how to pull off baby bangs and provide tips for hair care and styling.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Pull Off Baby Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling and Maintenance
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Baby bangs have been a popular trend in the hair world for a few years now, and they show no signs of slowing down. They can add a touch of edge to any hairstyle and can be worn with a variety of lengths and textures. They're also perfect for those who want to disguise a larger forehead or who are looking to switch up their look without making a drastic change.

How To Pull Off Baby Bangs

Pulling off baby bangs is all about confidence and finding the right style for your face shape and hair texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there's a baby bang style that will work for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect baby bangs:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. This can be done by using a comb to create a triangle shape at the front of your hairline.
  2. Use sharp scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. It's best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair off as needed.
  3. When cutting, make sure to hold the hair at a 45-degree angle to achieve a textured look.
  4. Once you've achieved the desired length, use a razor to texturize the bangs. This will give them a softer, more natural look.
  5. Style your baby bangs using a small round brush to add volume and shape.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Here are a few tips for styling and maintaining your baby bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to style your bangs.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum to add shine and smooth any flyaways.
  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to maintain their shape.
  • When washing your hair, be sure to rinse your bangs thoroughly to avoid product buildup.
  • Use dry shampoo in between washings to keep your bangs looking fresh.

FAQs

Can anyone pull off baby bangs?

Yes! Baby bangs can be worn by anyone, no matter their face shape or hair texture. It's all about finding the right style for you.

How do I know if baby bangs will suit me?

If you're unsure if baby bangs will suit you, try using a hair simulation app to get an idea of what you'll look like with them. Alternatively, you can talk to your stylist and they'll be able to advise you on the best style for your face shape and hair texture.

Do baby bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Like any hairstyle, baby bangs do require maintenance to keep them looking fresh. You'll need to trim them every few weeks and use styling products to keep them in place. However, they're relatively low maintenance compared to other hairstyles.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to pull off baby bangs like a pro. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a bold new look, baby bangs are a great choice for anyone looking to switch up their hairstyle.
