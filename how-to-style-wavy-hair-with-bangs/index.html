How To Style Wavy Hair With Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 06, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of struggling to style your wavy hair with bangs? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style wavy hair with bangs, along with tips and tricks to ensure your hair looks flawless.

How to Style Wavy Hair with Bangs

Wavy hair with bangs can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a flawless look. Here are the steps you need to follow to style your wavy hair with bangs:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before styling your wavy hair with bangs, you need to make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for wavy hair to ensure your locks are hydrated and healthy. Also, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. This will help set them in place and make them easier to style.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts – the top and the bottom. Use a clip to secure the top section so you can work on the bottom section first. This will make it easier to style your bangs and prevent them from getting in the way.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl the bottom section of your hair. Start from the back and work your way to the front, making sure to curl your hair away from your face. This will create a natural-looking wave.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Using a flat iron, straighten your bangs and style them in the direction you want them to fall. You can also use a round brush to curl your bangs if you prefer a more textured look.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Spray

To ensure your hairstyle stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your waves and bangs in place without making them stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your wavy hair with bangs:

  1. Prep your hair with shampoo, conditioner, and heat protectant spray.
  2. Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  3. Section your hair into two parts – the top and the bottom.
  4. Curl the bottom section of your hair using a curling iron.
  5. Straighten and style your bangs using a flat iron or round brush.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle with bangs:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel if you want looser waves.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to ensure your bangs are straight and smooth.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair to prevent frizz and add shine.
  • Don't overuse hairspray – a light mist is all you need to hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Can I style my wavy hair with bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your wavy hair with bangs without using heat. You can braid your hair overnight to create natural-looking waves, or use a texturizing spray to add texture and volume to your hair.

How often should I wash my wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you should wash your wavy hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

What type of products should I use on my wavy hair with bangs?

You should use products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as shampoo and conditioner, mousse, and texturizing spray. Make sure to choose products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair with Bangs

To keep your wavy hair with bangs looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your wavy hair with bangs like a pro. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle every time!
