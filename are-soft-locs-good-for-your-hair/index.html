Are Soft Locs Good For Your Hair

Written by Eveline Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Soft locs, as the name suggests, are a type of dreadlocks that are relatively softer and lighter than traditional locs. They are created by using a special technique that involves wrapping the hair around itself without any twisting or backcombing. But the question is, are soft locs good for your hair? In this article, we will explore the benefits and drawbacks of soft locs and give you some tips on how to care for them.

Description of Soft Locs

Soft locs are a relatively new trend in the natural hair community, and they are becoming increasingly popular due to their versatility, ease of maintenance, and overall aesthetic appeal. Unlike traditional locs, soft locs are created using a wrapping technique that involves wrapping the hair around itself in a continuous spiral. This method creates a softer, more lightweight loc that is easier to style and maintain.

How to Create Soft Locs

Creating soft locs requires a bit of skill and patience, but it can be done at home with the right tools and techniques. Here are the basic steps to create soft locs:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start the loc process, it's important to make sure your hair is clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and condition your hair, and then towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using a comb or your fingers. The sections should be about the size of a pencil or smaller, depending on how thick you want your locs to be.

Step 3: Start Wrapping

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around itself in a continuous spiral. Use a small amount of styling gel or cream to help hold the hair in place, and then continue wrapping until you reach the end of the section.

Step 4: Seal the Ends

Once you reach the end of the section, use a small amount of hair glue or a hot tool to seal the ends. This will help the locs stay in place and prevent them from unraveling.

Step 5: Repeat Until Complete

Repeat the wrapping and sealing process for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in soft locs.

Tips for Maintaining Soft Locs

Maintaining soft locs is relatively easy, but there are a few things you can do to keep them looking their best:

  • Moisturize regularly with a light leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh your locs down
  • Cleanse your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo or apple cider vinegar rinse
  • Avoid excessive manipulation, as this can cause breakage and damage to your locs

FAQs About Soft Locs

Q: How long do soft locs last?

A: Soft locs can last anywhere from 6 months to 2 years, depending on how well you care for them and how quickly your hair grows.

Q: Can you color soft locs?

A: Yes, you can color soft locs just like you can with any other type of hair. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye to prevent damaging your locs.

Q: Can you swim with soft locs?

A: Yes, you can swim with soft locs, but it's important to protect them from chlorine and salt water. Wear a swim cap or cover your locs with a protective oil or gel before swimming.

Hair Care for Soft Locs

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, vibrant soft locs. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after washing to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your locs
  • Trim your locs regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your locs and cause damage

Overall, soft locs can be a great option for those who want the look of traditional locs without the weight and stiffness. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy soft, healthy, and beautiful locs for years to come.
