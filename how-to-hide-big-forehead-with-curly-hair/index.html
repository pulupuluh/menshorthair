How To Hide Big Forehead With Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Mar 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a big forehead and curly hair? Do you feel like your forehead is the first thing people notice about you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue, but there are ways to hide your big forehead with curly hair. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to do just that.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Big Forehead with Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Hide Big Forehead with Curly Hair

The first step in hiding your big forehead with curly hair is choosing the right hairstyle. There are a few different styles that work well for this, including:

  • Bangs: Bangs are a great way to cover up a big forehead. You can go for longer bangs that sweep to the side or shorter bangs that sit just above your eyebrows.
  • Layers: Layers can also help to hide a big forehead. By adding layers to your curly hair, you can create volume and movement that distracts from your forehead.
  • Updos: Updos can be a great way to pull your hair away from your face and create a more polished look. Try a messy bun or a braided updo to keep your hair out of your face.

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, you can start experimenting with different ways to style it to further hide your forehead. Here are a few tips:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your curly hair. Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to keep your locks looking healthy and defined.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to help define your curls and add volume.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your curls and create volume.
  4. If you're going for bangs, cut them to the desired length. You can do this yourself or visit a hair salon.
  5. Style your hair in a way that hides your forehead. Try adding layers or pulling your hair back into an updo.
  6. Finish off your look with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks to help you hide your big forehead with curly hair:

  • Use a headband or scarf to pull your hair away from your face.
  • Add highlights to your hair to create dimension and distract from your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you.

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair down if I have a big forehead?

Yes, you can still wear your hair down if you have a big forehead. Just make sure to style your hair in a way that creates volume and distracts from your forehead.

Do I have to cut bangs to hide my forehead?

No, you don't have to cut bangs to hide your forehead. There are plenty of other hairstyles that work well for this, such as layers and updos.

What type of products should I use for my curly hair?

You should use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as mousse, gel, and leave-in conditioner. These products will help to define your curls and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your locks.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide your big forehead with curly hair. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and styling techniques until you find the perfect look for you.
