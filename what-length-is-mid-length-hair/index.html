What Length Is Mid Length Hair

Description

Mid length hair is a popular hair length that falls between shoulder length and long hair. It is a versatile length that can be styled in many ways and is suitable for most face shapes and hair types. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, mid length hair is a great option if you want a change from your current hairstyle.

How to Determine Mid Length Hair

The length of mid length hair can vary depending on personal preference and hair type, but generally it falls between 10-14 inches long. To determine if your hair is mid length, measure the length from the top of your shoulder to the ends of your hair. If it falls within this range, you have mid length hair.

Factors to Consider

It's important to keep in mind that the length of mid length hair can vary depending on a few factors:

  • Your height - If you're taller, mid length hair may appear shorter on you than someone who is shorter.
  • Your face shape - Certain face shapes may require longer or shorter hair lengths to flatter your features.
  • Your hair type - Curly hair may appear shorter when stretched out than straight hair.

Step by Step Guide

If you're unsure how to style your mid length hair, here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. If you want to add texture, use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.
  5. Finish off your look with hairspray or styling products to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your mid length hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • If you have fine hair, avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I still wear my hair up with mid length hair?

Absolutely! Mid length hair is a great length for wearing your hair up in a ponytail, bun, or half-up half-down style.

2. Is mid length hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, mid length hair is a versatile length that can be tailored to flatter any face shape.

3. How often should I wash my mid length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is recommended to prevent your hair from becoming too dry or oily.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy mid length hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you plan on swimming.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know what length is mid length hair, you can decide if it's the right length for you. With the versatility and ease of styling, mid length hair is a great option for anyone looking for a change.
