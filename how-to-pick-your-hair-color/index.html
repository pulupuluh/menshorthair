How To Pick Your Hair Color A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, whether you're looking to switch up your style or simply enhance your natural shade. With so many options available, it's important to take your time and consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal preferences before making a decision. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step and share some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hue.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Pick Your Hair Color
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Color
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Pick Your Hair Color

The first step in picking your hair color is to consider your skin tone. Generally, warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, and honey, while cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown shades. Another factor to consider is your eye color. If you have blue eyes, cooler shades like ash blonde or cool brown will make them stand out, while warmer shades like golden blonde or chestnut will complement brown eyes.

Next, think about the level of commitment you're willing to make. If you're not ready for a drastic change, consider a semi-permanent or temporary hair color that will fade over time. If you're ready for a more permanent change, a permanent hair color may be the way to go.

Finally, consider your personal style and preferences. Do you want a bold and dramatic change or a subtle enhancement? Do you want to stand out or blend in? Think about your lifestyle, career, and personal taste when making your decision.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect hair color:

  1. Identify your skin tone and eye color
  2. Consider the level of commitment you're willing to make
  3. Think about your personal style and preferences
  4. Choose a few shades that you like
  5. Do a strand test to see how the color will look on your hair
  6. Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure
  7. Make your decision and enjoy your new look!

Tips for Choosing the Right Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair color:

  • Consider your natural hair color as a starting point
  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and eye color
  • Think about the season and the latest hair color trends
  • Don't be afraid to ask for advice from friends or a professional stylist
  • Remember that hair color can fade over time, so choose a shade that will age well

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?
A: It's best to avoid coloring damaged hair, as the chemicals can further weaken and damage the hair. If you're looking to switch up your color, consider using a hair mask or treatment to strengthen and nourish your hair before coloring. Q: How often should I touch up my roots?
A: This depends on the type of hair color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color will need to be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent and temporary hair color can last up to 8 weeks. Q: Will my hair color fade over time?
A: Yes, hair color will fade over time due to exposure to the sun, washing, and styling. To maintain your color, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid excessive heat styling.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've picked your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask or treatment to nourish your hair

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect hair color for you. Remember to take your time, consider your options, and consult with a professional if you're unsure. Happy coloring!
