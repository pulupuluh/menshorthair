Best Hairstyles For Round Faces In 2023

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Round faces can be tricky to style, but with the right haircut and hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and create a flattering look. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for round faces in 2023, including how to style them and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft, curved features with a width and length that are roughly equal. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and add height to the crown of the head. This can be achieved through layers, volume, and texture. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Consult with a hairstylist to find a cut and style that complements your face shape and features. To style your hair, use products that add volume and texture, such as mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the crown of the head. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a layered, textured hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the crown to create volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends for added shine.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Consider a side part or asymmetrical cut to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that add width to the face.
  • Add layers and texture to create movement and volume.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the crown.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls for added texture.
  • Avoid styles that are too short or close to the head, as they can make the face appear wider.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds length and height to the crown of the head. This can be achieved through layers, volume, and texture.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs. Also, steer clear of styles that are too short or close to the head, as they can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, the best hairstyles for round faces in 2023 are those that create the illusion of length and add height to the crown of the head. Consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a cut and style, and consult with a hairstylist for expert advice. Follow these tips for styling and hair care to keep your hair looking its best.
