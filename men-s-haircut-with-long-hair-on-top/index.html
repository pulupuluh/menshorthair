Men s Haircut With Long Hair On Top

Written by William Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, a men's haircut with long hair on top may be the perfect choice for you. This trendy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It's a great way to show off your personality while still looking polished and put together. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting a men's haircut with long hair on top, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips for maintaining your hairstyle, and more.

Description

A men's haircut with long hair on top typically features longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides, which helps to highlight the length and texture of the hair on top. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and tousled, depending on your personal preference and the occasion.

How to Get the Look

Getting a men's haircut with long hair on top is relatively simple, but it's important to find a skilled stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Here's what to expect during your haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they'll sit down with you to discuss your desired look. This is a great opportunity to bring in pictures of hairstyles you like, or to talk about specific features you want to highlight, like your natural hair texture or face shape.

Step 2: Haircut

Once you and your stylist have agreed on the look you want, they'll start cutting your hair. This typically involves cutting the sides and back of your hair to a shorter length, while leaving the hair on top longer. Your stylist may use scissors or clippers, depending on your hair type and preference.

Step 3: Styling

After your haircut is complete, your stylist will style your hair to achieve the desired look. This may involve blow-drying your hair, applying styling products like gel or pomade, or using a straightening iron or curling wand to create texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've gotten your men's haircut with long hair on top, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts with long hair on top:

Q: What face shapes work best with this hairstyle?

A: Men's haircuts with long hair on top can work well with a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist to find the best hairstyle for your unique features.

Q: Is this hairstyle easy to maintain?

A: Men's haircuts with long hair on top can be relatively easy to maintain, but it's important to invest in high-quality hair care products and to get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I style this haircut in different ways?

A: Yes! Men's haircuts with long hair on top are highly versatile, and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal preference and the occasion. Try slicking your hair back for a formal event, or tousling it for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take care of it with a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and investing in high-quality hair care products, you can keep your men's haircut with long hair on top looking great for weeks to come.
