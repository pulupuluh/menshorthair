What Hairstyle Makes My Face Look Thinner

Written by Petter Aug 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of looking at your chubby cheeks and wondering what hairstyle could make your face look thinner? Well, you're in luck because we have the answers you've been searching for. In this article, we'll discuss hairstyles that can make your face appear slimmer and more defined. So, without further ado, let's get started!

Description

The right hairstyle can do wonders for your face shape. It can highlight your best features and draw attention away from areas you're less confident about. If you have a round or chubby face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of a slimmer face.

How to

Here are some tips on how to choose a hairstyle that will make your face look thinner:

1. Side Part

A deep side part can create the illusion of a slimmer face. By parting your hair to the side, you draw attention away from the center of your face, making it appear narrower.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for those with round faces. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, which can make the face appear longer and more oval-shaped.

3. Long Layers

If you have long hair, adding layers can help to create the appearance of a slimmer face. The layers should be cut to frame your face and draw attention away from the cheeks.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold choice, but it can work wonders for those with round faces. The short length draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create a hairstyle that makes your face look thinner:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Is it round, square, oval, or heart-shaped? Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

2. Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that will make your face look thinner. They can also recommend styling products and techniques that will enhance your new look.

3. Choose the Right Cut

Once you've consulted with a hairstylist, it's time to choose the right cut. Remember, the goal is to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Opt for a cut that draws attention away from the center of your face and towards your best features.

4. Experiment with Styling

Experiment with different styling techniques to enhance your new haircut. For example, a side part or loose waves can make your face look slimmer. Play around with different products and techniques until you find a look that works for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look thinner:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face look shorter and wider. Opt for a side-swept fringe instead.

2. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use volumizing products and styling techniques to add lift and movement to your hair.

3. Use Highlights

Highlights can help to create dimension and depth in your hair, which can make your face look slimmer. Consult with a hairstylist to choose the right shade for your skin tone.

FAQ

Q: Can hair color make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, hair color can create the illusion of a slimmer face. Highlights and lowlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, which can make your face appear more defined.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob. These styles can make your face look wider and shorter.

Hair Care

Remember, the key to a great hairstyle is healthy hair. Make sure you're taking care of your hair by using high-quality products and avoiding heat styling whenever possible. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose a hairstyle that makes your face look thinner and more defined. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect look for you.
