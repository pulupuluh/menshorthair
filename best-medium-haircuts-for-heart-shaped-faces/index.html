Best Medium Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 · 10 min read

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, you’re in luck because you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, when it comes to medium haircuts, there are a few styles that can accentuate your features even more. In this article, we’ll go over the best medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces, as well as how to style them, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Choose the Best Medium Haircut for Heart Shaped Faces

The first step in choosing the best medium haircut for heart-shaped faces is to determine the length of your face. Heart-shaped faces are longer than they are wide, so you want to choose a haircut that will balance out your features. The best medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume and width to the bottom of your face, while minimizing the width of your forehead.

The Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that looks great on heart-shaped faces. It has a length that falls between the chin and the shoulders, which is perfect for balancing out the face. The bob adds volume to the bottom of the face, while the blunt cut minimizes the width of the forehead. If you have thick hair, you can opt for a blunt bob. If you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and movement to your hair.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is another great medium haircut for heart-shaped faces. It’s longer than the bob, falling just above or below the shoulders. It adds volume to the bottom of the face, while the length elongates the face. If you have thick hair, a blunt lob can look great. If you have fine hair, a layered lob can add volume and movement to your hair.

Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are great for heart-shaped faces because they add volume and movement to the hair. They can be paired with a bob or a lob, or they can be worn on their own. The layers should be cut so that they fall at the jawline or below, to balance out the face. If you have thick hair, you can opt for shorter layers. If you have fine hair, longer layers can add volume and movement to your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces. They draw attention to the eyes and balance out the face. The bangs should be cut so that they fall just above the eyebrows, and they should be swept to the side. If you have thick hair, you can opt for blunt bangs. If you have fine hair, wispy bangs can add volume and movement to your hair.

Updos

Updos are great for heart-shaped faces because they elongate the face and draw attention to the neck and shoulders. The updo can be a bun, a chignon, or a braided updo. The key is to keep the hair away from the forehead and to add volume to the bottom of the face.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair is a great medium haircut for heart-shaped faces because it adds volume and movement to the hair. The waves should start at the jawline or below, to balance out the face. You can achieve waves by using a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight. If you have fine hair, you can use volumizing products to add volume to your waves.

Tips for Styling Medium Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for styling medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces:

  • Use volumizing products to add volume to the bottom of your face
  • Part your hair in the middle or on the side, but avoid parting it down the middle
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as they can make the forehead look wider
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair, to add volume and movement
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, or clips to draw attention to the bottom of your face

FAQs

What’s the best length for medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces?

The best length for medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces is between the chin and the shoulders. This length adds volume to the bottom of the face, while minimizing the width of the forehead.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off bangs?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off bangs. Side-swept bangs are a great addition to medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces, as they draw attention to the eyes and balance out the face.

What’s the best way to style layered haircuts for heart-shaped faces?

The best way to style layered haircuts for heart-shaped faces is to use volumizing products to add volume and movement to the hair. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair, to add volume and movement. You can also use a curling iron or braids to add waves to your hair.

Hair Care for Heart-Shaped Faces

Here are some hair care tips for heart-shaped faces:

  • Wash your hair every other day, to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and to prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as it can cause breakage

Now that you know the best medium haircuts for heart-shaped faces, you can choose the one that works best for you. Remember to use the tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
