Hairstyles That Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Petter Apr 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help balance out the proportions of your face and showcase your natural beauty. In this post, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for round faces and provide tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles That Look Good on Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that flatters your features is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, from one hairline to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across the widest part of your cheekbones, just below the outer corners of your eyes.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point just below your ear where your jawbone angles upward. Then double this measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape:

  • Round: Your face length and cheekbone measurements are similar, and your jawline is soft and rounded.
  • Oval: Your face length is greater than your cheekbone measurement, and your jawline is slightly rounded.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbone, and jawline measurements are similar, and your jawline is sharp and angular.
  • Heart: Your forehead measurement is greater than your cheekbone measurement, and your jawline tapers to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbone measurement is greater than your forehead and jawline measurements, and your chin is pointed.

Hairstyles That Look Good on Round Faces

Now that you know your face shape, let's take a look at some hairstyles that flatter round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the width. Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear rounder.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out the proportions of your face and create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt bangs, which can make your face appear shorter and wider.

3. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves can help soften the angles of your face and add volume to the sides, creating the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for round faces, as it can help create the illusion of a longer face. Just be sure to add volume to the top and avoid flat, sleek styles.

5. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers can help add volume to the top and sides of your hair, creating the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear rounder.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Regardless of your face shape, healthy hair is always in style. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove dirt, oil, and product buildup, which can weigh your hair down and make it look dull.

2. Use Conditioner

Conditioner can help moisturize your hair and prevent breakage. Be sure to apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, as this is where it tends to be the driest.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. To prevent this, use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Round Faces

Q: Can short hair work for round faces?

A: Yes, short hair can work for round faces. Just be sure to add volume to the top and avoid flat, sleek styles.

Q: Are bangs a good option for round faces?

A: Yes, bangs can be a good option for round faces. Side-swept bangs can help balance out the proportions of your face and create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just be sure to add volume to the top and avoid flat, sleek styles.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. Just be sure to add volume to the top and avoid pulling your hair back too tightly.

In conclusion, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter round faces. Whether you prefer long layers, side-swept bangs, or a bob with layers, there's a style out there that's perfect for you. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using conditioner, protecting it from heat, and getting regular trims. With these tips, you'll be able to showcase your natural beauty and feel confident in any hairstyle you choose.
