How To Style Men s Slicked Back Undercut

Written by Frank Mar 01, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both classic and trendy, then the slicked back undercut is the perfect option for you. This style has been making waves in the men's grooming world for quite some time now and is still going strong. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and the occasion. In this article, we'll be discussing how to style men's slicked back undercut, hair care tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Slicked Back Undercut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Slicked Back Undercut
  • FAQ

How to Style Slicked Back Undercut

The slicked back undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. It's a great option for guys with medium to long hair who want a classic yet trendy look. Here's how you can style your slicked back undercut:

1. Prep Your Hair

The first step to styling your slicked back undercut is to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product build-up and make your hair easier to style. Once you've washed your hair, towel dry it and apply a heat protectant spray.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a low to medium heat setting and a round brush to create volume and texture. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.

3. Apply Product

Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair product to your hair. A pomade or wax works best for slicked back undercut. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly.

4. Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to push your hair back from your forehead. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps as you go. You can also use your fingers to create texture and volume. Once you've styled your hair, use a hairspray to hold it in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your slicked back undercut:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  4. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume and texture.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair.
  6. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair using a comb.
  7. Use a comb or your fingers to push your hair back from your forehead.
  8. Smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  9. Create texture and volume using your fingers.
  10. Use a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Slicked Back Undercut

Here are some tips for styling your slicked back undercut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a pomade or wax for a more natural look, or a gel for a more polished look.
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to push your hair back from your forehead.
  • Smooth out any bumps or lumps as you go.
  • Create texture and volume using your fingers.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to keep it healthy and shiny. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Q: Can I style my slicked back undercut with a beard?

A: Yes, you can definitely style your slicked back undercut with a beard. In fact, it can add a rugged and masculine look to your style.

Q: How do I know if slicked back undercut suits me?

A: The slicked back undercut is a versatile hairstyle that suits most face shapes and hair types. However, it's always best to consult with your hairstylist to see if it works for you.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your slicked back undercut looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the slicked back undercut like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that suits you best. Happy styling!
