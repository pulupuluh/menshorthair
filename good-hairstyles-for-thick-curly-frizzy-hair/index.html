Good Hairstyles For Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thick curly frizzy hair, you know the struggle of finding the perfect hairstyle that works for you. It can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you don't know what you're doing. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some good hairstyles for thick curly frizzy hair that will make your life a lot easier.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Thick curly frizzy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of volume and texture to work with. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. The key is to find hairstyles that work with your hair type rather than against it. You want to enhance your natural texture and volume rather than trying to tame it.

How To

The first step to finding a good hairstyle for your thick curly frizzy hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down your options and find a style that flatters your features. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for inspiration.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for styles that add height and length. A layered bob or a long layered cut with side-swept bangs are great options.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you have a lot of versatility when it comes to hairstyles. You can go for a long layered cut, a blunt bob, or even a pixie cut. The key is to avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of your head.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you want to soften your features with a layered cut. A long layered cut with face-framing layers or a shoulder-length cut with soft waves are great options.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your forehead with a hairstyle that adds volume to the bottom of your face. A long layered cut with side-swept bangs or a shoulder-length cut with loose waves are great options.

Step By Step

Here are some step by step instructions for a few good hairstyles for thick curly frizzy hair:

Low Ponytail

  1. Start by applying a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic.

Side Braid

  1. Start by applying a smoothing serum to your damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to smooth out any frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your thick curly frizzy hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to enhance your natural texture.
  • Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick curly frizzy hair?

The best haircut for thick curly frizzy hair depends on your face shape. A layered bob, a long layered cut with side-swept bangs, or a shoulder-length cut with soft waves are great options.

How do you manage thick curly frizzy hair?

You can manage thick curly frizzy hair by using a curl-enhancing product, blow drying your hair with a diffuser attachment, avoiding using a brush on your hair when it's dry, and misting your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for thick curly frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.

With these good hairstyles for thick curly frizzy hair and hair care tips, you'll be able to manage and style your hair with ease. Remember to embrace your natural texture and volume, and don't be afraid to try something new!
