Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Curly Top A Trendy Men s Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Luffy Feb 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that's perfect for men, the "mens haircut shaved sides curly top" is definitely worth considering. This hairstyle is all about having shaved sides and a curly top, creating a unique and eye-catching look that's sure to turn heads. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, as well as some tips and hair care advice to keep it looking great.

Description

The mens haircut shaved sides curly top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The shaved sides give a clean and edgy look, while the curly top adds volume and texture to the hair. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

How to achieve the mens haircut shaved sides curly top

The mens haircut shaved sides curly top can be achieved in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

Start by choosing the right haircut. You'll need to have a short buzz cut or a fade on the sides, while the top should be left long enough to create curls. You can ask your barber to create a fade or a buzz cut on the sides, leaving around 2-3 inches of hair on the top.

Step 2: Create the curls

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to create the curls on the top. You can use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose curls or waves. Alternatively, you can use a hair product such as a curling cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers to scrunch and shape the curls.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once you've created the curls, it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to style the curls, shaping them to the desired look. You can use a hair spray or a hair gel to hold the curls in place and add shine to your hair.

Tips for maintaining the mens haircut shaved sides curly top

To keep your mens haircut shaved sides curly top looking great, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair and make the curls frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Can I still achieve this hairstyle if I don't have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve this hairstyle even if you don't have curly hair. You can use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create curls or waves on your hair. Alternatively, you can use a hair product such as a curling cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this hairstyle?

A: You'll need to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shaved sides and keep the top looking neat and tidy.

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: The mens haircut shaved sides curly top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes. However, it may not be suitable for men with a round face as the shaved sides can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mens haircut shaved sides curly top requires a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Overall, the mens haircut shaved sides curly top is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for men who want to make a statement. By following our step-by-step guide and hair care tips, you can achieve this hairstyle and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
