How To Make Thin Hair Into A Big Bun Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Bardi Mar 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin hair, you might think that creating a big bun is impossible. But with the right tips, steps, and hair care, you can achieve a voluminous bun that can make you stand out in any occasion. In this article, we’ll show you how to make thin hair into a big bun in a relaxed English language, plus some frequently asked questions and tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Thin Hair into a Big Bun
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making a Big Bun with Thin Hair
  • FAQs

How to Make Thin Hair into a Big Bun

Making a big bun with thin hair is possible with these steps:

1. Use volumizing products

Before styling, apply volumizing products to your hair. This can add texture and thickness to your hair, making it easier to style. Some of the best volumizing products include mousse, root lifters, and volumizing sprays.

2. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair can add volume and texture to make a big bun. Take a fine-toothed comb and tease the crown area of your hair. This can create a cushion for the bun to sit on.

3. Use a hair donut

A hair donut is a tool that can help you create a perfect bun. You can buy a hair donut at a beauty supply store or online. Place the donut at the base of your ponytail and wrap your hair around it. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

4. Loosen the bun

After securing the bun, gently loosen it to create a more relaxed and natural look. You can also pull out a few strands to frame your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make thin hair into a big bun:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply volumizing products

Apply volumizing products to your damp hair. Work the products through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This can add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Take a fine-toothed comb and tease the crown area of your hair. This can create a cushion for the bun to sit on.

Step 5: Create a ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the base of your neck.

Step 6: Use a hair donut

Place the hair donut at the base of your ponytail and wrap your hair around it. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 7: Loosen the bun

After securing the bun, gently loosen it to create a more relaxed and natural look. You can also pull out a few strands to frame your face.

Tips for Making a Big Bun with Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you make a big bun with thin hair:

  • Use a hair donut to create a perfect bun.
  • Tease your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Apply volumizing products to your hair before styling.
  • Loosen the bun to create a more relaxed and natural look.
  • Pull out a few strands to frame your face.

FAQs

Q: Can I make a big bun with short hair?

A: It’s possible to create a big bun with short hair, but you might need to use hair extensions or a hairpiece to achieve the desired look.

Q: How long will the bun last?

A: The bun can last for several hours, but it might start to loosen over time. You can use hairspray to help hold the bun in place.

Q: Can I make a big bun without a hair donut?

A: You can make a big bun without a hair donut, but it might be harder to achieve a perfect shape. You can use bobby pins and hairspray to help hold the bun in place.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help care for your thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Making a big bun with thin hair is possible with the right steps and hair care. By using volumizing products, teasing your hair, and using a hair donut, you can achieve a voluminous bun that can make you stand out in any occasion. Don’t forget to care for your hair to maintain its health and thickness.
