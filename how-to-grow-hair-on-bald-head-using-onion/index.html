How To Grow Hair On Bald Head Using Onion

Written by Christine Jun 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are someone who is experiencing hair loss or baldness, you may be looking for natural remedies that can help you grow your hair back. One such remedy is using onion juice on your scalp. In this article, we will discuss how to grow hair on bald head using onion, as well as some tips and hair care practices to help you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Onion Juice on Bald Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Onion Juice on Bald Head
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Practices for Bald Head

How to Use Onion Juice on Bald Head

Onion juice is believed to be beneficial for hair growth due to its high sulfur content, which can improve blood circulation and promote the production of collagen. Here is how you can use onion juice on your bald head:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Peel and chop one large onion into small pieces.
  2. Blend the onion pieces in a blender or food processor until they become a paste.
  3. Strain the onion paste using a cheesecloth or fine mesh strainer to extract the juice.
  4. Apply the onion juice directly to your bald head using a cotton ball or your fingertips.
  5. Massage your scalp with the onion juice for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Leave the onion juice on your scalp for at least 30 minutes or overnight.
  7. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water and shampoo as usual.

Repeat this process 2-3 times a week for best results.

Tips for Using Onion Juice on Bald Head

  • Use fresh onions to make the juice, as they contain more nutrients than stored onions.
  • You can add a few drops of essential oils like lavender or rosemary to the onion juice to mask the smell.
  • Do a patch test before applying the onion juice to your entire scalp to check for any allergic reactions.
  • Be patient and consistent with the treatment, as it may take several weeks to see results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will onion juice make my hair grow faster?

A: While onion juice can promote hair growth, it does not necessarily make your hair grow faster. Hair growth rate is determined by genetics and other factors like diet and lifestyle.

Q: Can onion juice cause hair damage?

A: Onion juice is generally safe to use on hair, but it can cause some scalp irritation or dryness in some people. It is important to do a patch test and use the juice in moderation.

Hair Care Practices for Bald Head

In addition to using onion juice, there are other hair care practices that can help you maintain healthy hair and promote hair growth:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals that support hair health.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles or hair products that can pull or damage your hair.
  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized to prevent dandruff or other scalp conditions.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a sunscreen spray designed for hair.

By following these tips and using onion juice on your bald head, you can promote hair growth and achieve a fuller, healthier head of hair.
