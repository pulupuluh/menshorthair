What To Do To Get Thick Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Mar 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Do you dream of having thick, long hair that cascades down your back? While genetics play a significant role in determining the thickness and length of your hair, there are several things you can do to encourage healthy hair growth. In this guide, we will explore the steps you need to take to achieve thick, luscious locks.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Thick Long Hair

The first step to getting thick long hair is to assess your current hair care routine. Are you using the right products? Are you taking care of your scalp? Are you eating a healthy diet? These are all factors that can impact the health and growth of your hair.

Here are some things you can do to encourage healthy hair growth:

1. Use the Right Products

When it comes to hair care, it's essential to use products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thin hair, look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that will help add body to your locks. If you have dry or damaged hair, opt for moisturizing products that will help restore your hair's natural oils.

2. Take Care of Your Scalp

Your scalp plays a crucial role in the health and growth of your hair. Make sure you're keeping your scalp clean and free from buildup by washing your hair regularly. You can also use a scalp scrub or massage your scalp to help stimulate hair growth.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet can also impact the health of your hair. Make sure you're eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods like salmon, eggs, spinach, and sweet potatoes are all great for promoting healthy hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to get thick long hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your hair goals:

Step 1: Assess Your Current Hair Care Routine

Take a look at the products you're currently using and evaluate whether they're meeting your hair's needs. If not, consider switching to products that are better suited for your hair type.

Step 2: Cleanse Your Scalp

Make sure you're washing your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and free from buildup. You can also use a scalp scrub or massage your scalp to help stimulate hair growth.

Step 3: Condition Your Hair

After you shampoo your hair, make sure you're using a conditioner to help hydrate and nourish your locks. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out thoroughly.

Step 4: Protect Your Hair

Use heat protectant sprays and avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and lead to split ends and breakage.

Step 5: Get Regular Trims

Even if you're trying to grow your hair out, it's essential to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining thick long hair:

1. Use Hair Masks

Using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your locks from the sun.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Pulling your hair back into tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can cause tension on your scalp and lead to breakage. Opt for looser styles that won't pull on your hair.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow thick long hair?

The rate at which your hair grows depends on several factors, including your age, genetics, and overall health. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months or even years to grow long, thick hair.

Can I make my hair grow faster?

While you can't make your hair grow faster, there are things you can do to encourage healthy hair growth. Eating a healthy diet, using the right products, and taking care of your scalp can all help promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Long Hair

If you're looking to grow thick long hair, here's a sample hair care routine to get you started:

Morning:

1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo.

2. Apply a moisturizing conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or heat protectant spray.

4. Style your hair as desired.

Evening:

1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.

2. Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to help prevent split ends.

3. Put your hair up in a loose bun or braid to protect it while you sleep.

By following these tips and incorporating them into your hair care routine, you can achieve the thick, long hair of your dreams.
