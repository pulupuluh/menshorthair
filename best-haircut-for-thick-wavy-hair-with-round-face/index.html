Best Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair With Round Face

Written by Eveline Sep 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair and a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and showcases your gorgeous hair. In this post, we will discuss the best haircut for thick wavy hair with a round face, and provide you with some tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair with Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Best Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Choose a Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair with Round Face

The first step to finding the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair and round face is to identify your face shape. If you have a round face, you want a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a slimmer profile.

When it comes to thick wavy hair, you want a style that works with your natural texture. A layered cut will help to lighten up your hair and make it more manageable, while still showcasing your curls. Opt for longer layers around the face to create movement and frame your features.

Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair with Round Face

  • Long Layers
  • Shoulder-Length Bob
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Layered Pixie Cut

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Best Haircut

Once you have identified the best haircut for your thick wavy hair with a round face, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist. Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Bring in pictures of the style you want to show your stylist.
  2. Discuss your hair type and face shape with your stylist.
  3. Have your hair washed and conditioned before your cut.
  4. Ask your stylist to cut your hair dry, so they can see the natural texture and movement of your hair.
  5. Be clear about the length and layers you want.
  6. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your new haircut at home.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Invest in a good detangling brush to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face with thick wavy hair?

The best haircut for a round face with thick wavy hair is a layered cut with longer layers around the face. This will create movement and frame your features, while still showcasing your curls.

How often should I get a haircut for thick wavy hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut for thick wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain healthy ends.

In conclusion, finding the right haircut for thick wavy hair with a round face can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By following the tips and suggestions in this post, you can find the perfect haircut for your hair type and face shape, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
