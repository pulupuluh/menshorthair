What Haircut Suits Heart Shaped Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut for your heart shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for heart shaped faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

Heart shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrow chin, giving them a distinct shape. The best haircuts for heart shaped faces work to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to consider the length and layers. A long, layered cut with waves or curls can soften the jawline and add volume to the hair. A shoulder-length bob with layers can also be flattering, especially if it's styled with bangs that hit just above the brows.

For shorter haircuts, a pixie cut or a bob that's shorter in the back and longer in the front can work well. This style adds volume to the top of the head and draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step

When getting a haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to communicate with your stylist about the look you're going for. Follow these steps to ensure you get the perfect haircut:

  • Bring in pictures of haircuts you like that work well with heart shaped faces
  • Discuss with your stylist the length and layers you want
  • Consider adding bangs to balance out your forehead
  • Ask your stylist for recommendations on how to style your new haircut

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape
  • Consider using a straightener or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or make your forehead look wider, such as slicked back ponytails or high buns.

Do I need to have bangs with a heart shaped face?

No, bangs aren't necessary with a heart shaped face, but they can help balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain the health and shape of your new haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect haircut for your heart shaped face. Remember to communicate with your stylist and take care of your hair to maintain your new look!
