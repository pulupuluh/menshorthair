How To Change Hair Color In Pokemon Let s Go

Written by Eveline May 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a fan of Pokemon Let's Go, you may have noticed that your character's appearance can be customized. One way to change up your look is by changing your hair color. In this article, we'll go over how to change hair color in Pokemon Let's Go and provide some tips for keeping your new 'do looking fresh.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Change Hair Color

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the basics. In Pokemon Let's Go, you can change your hair color by visiting a hair salon. There are several hair salons located throughout Kanto, so you should be able to find one relatively easily. Each hair salon has a different selection of hair colors to choose from, so you may need to visit a few different salons to find the color you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to changing your hair color in Pokemon Let's Go:

  1. Find a hair salon in Kanto.
  2. Talk to the stylist and select "Change Hair Color."
  3. Select a color from the menu.
  4. Confirm your selection and pay the fee.
  5. Your hair color will immediately change.

It's as simple as that! Just keep in mind that some hair colors may be more expensive than others, so be sure to check the price before confirming your selection.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've changed your hair color, you'll want to make sure it stays looking fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining your new 'do:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

By following these tips, you can help your new hair color last as long as possible.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing hair color in Pokemon Let's Go:

  • Can I change my hair color back to its original color? Yes, you can visit a hair salon and select "Change Hair Color" again. This time, select your character's original hair color from the menu.
  • Can I change my hair color to any color? No, the selection of hair colors available at each hair salon is limited.
  • How much does it cost to change my hair color? The cost varies depending on the salon and the color you choose.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you've changed your hair color or not, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too frequently.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips, you can help keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.

Changing your hair color in Pokemon Let's Go is a fun way to switch up your character's appearance. By following the steps we've outlined in this article and taking care of your new hair color, you can enjoy your fresh 'do for as long as possible. Happy gaming!
