How To Get Rid Of Yellow Orange Brassy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have dyed your hair blonde or lightened it, you may have noticed that it can turn yellow or orange over time. This is due to the natural pigments in your hair being exposed after the dye fades. But don't worry, there are ways to get rid of this brassiness and restore your blonde locks to their former glory. In this post, we will cover everything you need to know about how to get rid of yellow orange brassy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Brassiness in blonde hair is a common problem that can be caused by a variety of factors, including exposure to sunlight, chlorine, and hard water. It can also be caused by using the wrong hair care products or leaving hair dye on for too long. Brassiness can make your hair appear dull and lifeless, and it can be difficult to get rid of without the right techniques.

How To Get Rid of Yellow Orange Brassy Hair

There are a few different methods you can use to get rid of brassiness in your hair, including:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is specifically designed to neutralize yellow and orange tones in blonde hair. It contains purple pigments that counteract the warm tones in your hair, making it appear cooler and brighter. To use purple shampoo, simply wet your hair and massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes before rinsing it out. Use it once or twice a week.

2. Try a Toning Treatment

A toning treatment can also help to neutralize brassy tones in your hair. There are a variety of toning treatments available, including toning conditioners, masks, and sprays. These products usually contain purple or blue pigments that neutralize the warm tones in your hair. Follow the instructions on the product for best results.

3. Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help to remove buildup and brassiness from your hair. To use apple cider vinegar, mix equal parts water and vinegar in a spray bottle. Spray it onto your hair and let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out. You can use this treatment once a week.

4. Get a Gloss Treatment

A gloss treatment is a semi-permanent hair color that adds shine and tone to your hair. It can also help to neutralize brassy tones. Gloss treatments are available at most salons and can be done in as little as 30 minutes.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting rid of yellow orange brassy hair:

Step 1: Use a Purple Shampoo

  1. Wet your hair in the shower
  2. Apply a small amount of purple shampoo to your scalp and massage it in
  3. Leave the shampoo on for 5-10 minutes
  4. Rinse the shampoo out thoroughly

Step 2: Try a Toning Treatment

  1. Choose a toning product that is right for your hair type
  2. Follow the instructions on the product for best results

Step 3: Use Apple Cider Vinegar

  1. Mix equal parts water and apple cider vinegar in a spray bottle
  2. Spray the mixture onto your hair
  3. Leave it on for a few minutes
  4. Rinse it out thoroughly

Step 4: Get a Gloss Treatment

  1. Visit a salon and ask for a gloss treatment
  2. Sit back and relax while the treatment is applied to your hair
  3. Rinse the treatment out thoroughly

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get rid of yellow orange brassy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and exacerbate brassiness
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water
  • Wear a hat when you're outside to protect your hair from the sun
  • Be patient â it may take a few treatments to completely get rid of brassiness

FAQ

What causes brassy hair?

Brassy hair is caused by exposure to sunlight, chlorine, and hard water. It can also be caused by using the wrong hair care products or leaving hair dye on for too long.

Can I get rid of brassy hair without using products?

While there are natural remedies you can use, such as apple cider vinegar, it's difficult to get rid of brassiness without using products specifically designed for this purpose.

How often should I use a purple shampoo?

You should use purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how frequently you wash your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent brassiness from occurring in the first place. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, brass-free hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Avoid leaving hair dye on for too long

By following these tips and using the methods outlined in this post, you can get rid of yellow orange brassy hair and maintain healthy, beautiful blonde locks.
