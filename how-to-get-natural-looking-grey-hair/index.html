How To Get Natural Looking Grey Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Apr 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking to embrace your natural grey hair? Whether you're tired of coloring your hair or just want to try something new, going grey can be a chic and stylish look. But how do you get natural looking grey hair? In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect shade of grey.

How to Get Natural Looking Grey Hair

If you want to achieve natural looking grey hair, it's important to find the right shade of grey for your skin tone. While some people look great with silver or white hair, others may prefer a more muted grey. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Embrace the Transition

If you're currently coloring your hair, it may take some time to fully transition to grey. Instead of going cold turkey, consider gradually lightening your hair over time. This will help you avoid the harsh contrast between your natural color and grey.

2. Use a Toner

If you have blonde or light brown hair, you may be able to achieve grey hair with a toner. A toner is a semi-permanent hair dye that can help neutralize any yellow or brassy tones in your hair. Look for a toner with cool undertones to achieve a natural looking grey.

3. Try a Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

If you have darker hair, you may need to use a semi-permanent hair dye to achieve grey hair. Look for a dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair, as these will typically have cool undertones that mimic natural grey hair.

4. Consider Highlights

If you're not ready to fully commit to grey hair, consider adding highlights to your hair. This will add dimension and depth to your hair, while also helping to blend in any grey hairs you may have.

5. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining healthy, natural looking grey hair. This will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

Step by Step: How to Get Natural Looking Grey Hair

Ready to try natural looking grey hair for yourself? Follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on Your Shade of Grey

As we mentioned earlier, it's important to choose a shade of grey that complements your skin tone. Take some time to research different shades of grey and find one that you love.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

If you're currently coloring your hair, you may need to wait a few weeks before attempting to go grey. This will give your hair time to recover from any damage caused by coloring. You should also deep condition your hair to keep it healthy and moisturized.

Step 3: Use a Toner or Semi-Permanent Dye

Depending on your natural hair color, you may need to use a toner or semi-permanent dye to achieve grey hair. Follow the instructions on the packaging carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Maintain Your Hair

Once you've achieved your desired shade of grey, it's important to maintain your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments. You should also avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals, as these can damage your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Natural Looking Grey Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your natural looking grey hair:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo can help neutralize any yellow or brassy tones in your hair, keeping your grey hair looking fresh and vibrant.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized, preventing breakage and split ends.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, causing it to look dull and lifeless. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer.

FAQ: Natural Looking Grey Hair

Q: Will going grey damage my hair?

A: Going grey shouldn't damage your hair, as long as you use the right products and take care of your hair properly.

Q: Can I go grey if I have dark hair?

A: Yes, you can go grey if you have dark hair. You may need to use a semi-permanent dye to achieve grey hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy, natural looking grey hair.

Hair Care: Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth, so make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Excessive sun exposure can damage your hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're outside.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help prevent breakage and split ends, keeping your hair looking healthy and shiny.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve natural looking grey hair that is stylish and chic. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and vibrant.
