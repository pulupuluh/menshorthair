Easy Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Thin Hair

Written by Aprilia May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium length thin hair, you might think that updo hairstyles are out of reach. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will show you some easy updo hairstyles that are perfect for medium length thin hair.

Description

Medium length thin hair can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can create some beautiful updos. Updos are a great way to keep your hair off your face and neck, especially during the warmer months. They are also perfect for special occasions or just adding a touch of elegance to your everyday look.

How to

Before we get started, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use hair accessories like bobby pins, hair elastics, and hair clips.

Step by Step

Now let's get into some easy updo hairstyles for medium length thin hair:

1. Low Bun

This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. Start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail and securing it with an elastic. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to soften the look.

2. Half-Up Bun

This is a great hairstyle for when you want your hair off your face but still want to show off your length. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with an elastic. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Let the rest of your hair flow freely.

3. French Twist

This elegant hairstyle is perfect for a special occasion. Start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail and twisting it upwards. Secure the twist with bobby pins and tuck the ends under. Use more bobby pins to secure any loose ends.

4. Braided Updo

This hairstyle is perfect for adding some texture to your hair. Start by braiding a section of your hair and securing it with an elastic. Repeat on the other side. Pull the braids up and secure them with bobby pins, creating a bun shape. Tuck any loose ends under and secure them with more bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling updo hairstyles for medium length thin hair:

  • Use a hairspray to hold your updo in place.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair to create more volume.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add some flair to your updo.
  • Practice your updo before the day of the event to avoid any last-minute stress.

FAQ

Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

Absolutely! With the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful hairstyles that work with your thin hair.

What products should I use for thin hair?

Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners, as well as texturizing sprays and dry shampoos. These products can help add fullness and grip to your hair.

What if I have trouble getting my updo to stay in place?

Try using more bobby pins or a stronger hairspray to hold your updo in place. You can also try teasing your hair for more grip.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products, avoiding heat styling as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. With healthy hair, you'll have an easier time styling it and creating beautiful updos.

Now that you have some easy updo hairstyles for medium length thin hair, it's time to start experimenting! With a little practice, you'll be able to create beautiful updos that work with your hair type and personal style. Remember to have fun and enjoy the process!
