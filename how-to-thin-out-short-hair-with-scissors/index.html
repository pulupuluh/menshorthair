How To Thin Out Short Hair With Scissors

Written by Eveline Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair feeling heavy and thick? Thinning out your hair can help create a more manageable and stylish look. While going to a salon is an option, it can also be expensive. But did you know that you can thin out short hair with scissors yourself? In this article, we'll go through the steps on how to thin out short hair with scissors and provide some helpful tips and hair care advice.

How to

Before starting, make sure you have a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors. Using regular scissors can cause damage to your hair and create split ends. It's also important to have a comb on hand to help separate sections of your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help get rid of any tangles and make it easier to work with. Once your hair is dry, comb it out so it's smooth and easy to section.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into four sections. Two on the top and two on the bottom. You can use hair clips to keep each section separate while you work.

Step 3: Begin Thinning

Starting with one of the bottom sections, take a small subsection of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, moving the scissors up and down to create a feathered effect. Repeat this process on each subsection of hair until you have thinned out the entire section.

Repeat this process on the other three sections of your hair. Take your time and be patient, as this process can take a while.

Tips

Thinning out your hair can make it more prone to breakage and split ends. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Don't overdo it - it's better to thin out your hair gradually over time
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help strengthen your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

FAQ

Can I thin out my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can still thin out curly hair. However, it's important to be careful and not thin out too much, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. In general, it's recommended to thin out your hair every 2-3 months.

Can I thin out my hair if it's already damaged?

It's best to avoid thinning out damaged hair, as it can cause further breakage and split ends. Focus on nourishing and repairing your hair before attempting to thin it out.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to change up your look and make it more manageable. However, it's important to take care of your hair before and after the process to keep it healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you're unsure about thinning out your hair, it's always best to consult with a professional stylist.
