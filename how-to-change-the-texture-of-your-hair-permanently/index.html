How To Change The Texture Of Your Hair Permanently

Written by Eveline Aug 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair texture? Do you want to change it permanently? Well, you're in luck because we have the solution for you! In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to change the texture of your hair permanently. Let's get started!

Description

Changing the texture of your hair permanently is a big decision. It requires time, effort, and a bit of investment. However, it can also be a life-changing experience that will boost your confidence and give you a whole new look. There are different methods to permanently change your hair texture, and we'll discuss them below.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Method

Before you start, you need to choose the method that suits you best. There are two main methods for permanently changing your hair texture: chemical treatments and heat styling. Chemical treatments involve using a relaxer or a perm to alter the hair structure. Heat styling, on the other hand, uses heat tools to straighten or curl the hair.

Step 2: Consult a Professional

It's essential to consult a professional hairstylist before you proceed with any method. They will analyze your hair type, texture, and condition and recommend the best method for you. They will also advise you on the maintenance required after the treatment.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you undergo any hair treatment, it's essential to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair thoroughly and avoiding any hair products that contain oils or silicone. You should also avoid using any heat tools or chemical treatments for at least two weeks before the procedure.

Step 4: Undergo the Treatment

Once you have chosen the method, consulted a professional, and prepared your hair, it's time to undergo the treatment. This step will depend on the method you have chosen. If you have opted for a chemical treatment, the hairstylist will apply the relaxer or perm solution to your hair. If you have chosen heat styling, the hairstylist will use a flat iron or curling iron to straighten or curl your hair.

Step 5: Maintenance

After the treatment, it's essential to maintain your hair to keep it healthy and prolong the results. This involves using hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. You should also avoid using heat tools or chemical treatments for at least six months after the procedure.

Tips

  • Choose a reputable hairstylist.
  • Invest in good quality hair products.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat or sunlight.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by using a silk or satin pillowcase.

FAQ

Is it safe to permanently change the texture of my hair?

Yes, it's safe as long as you choose a reputable hairstylist and follow the maintenance guidelines. However, there is always a risk of damaging your hair if the procedure is not done correctly.

How long will the results last?

The results can last anywhere from six months to a year, depending on the method and maintenance. However, the new growth will have your natural texture, so you may need to repeat the treatment.

Can I color my hair after the treatment?

It's best to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after any chemical treatment or heat styling. This will give your hair time to recover and prevent further damage.

Hair Care

After permanently changing the texture of your hair, it's crucial to take care of it to keep it healthy and maintain the results. This involves using hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. You should also avoid using heat tools or chemical treatments for at least six months after the procedure. Finally, get regular trims to prevent split ends and protect your hair while sleeping by using a silk or satin pillowcase.

Conclusion

Permanently changing the texture of your hair can be a life-changing experience. Whether you choose a chemical treatment or heat styling, it's essential to consult a professional hairstylist, prepare your hair, and maintain it after the procedure. With proper care, you can enjoy your new hair texture and boost your confidence!
