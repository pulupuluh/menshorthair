Best Hairstyles For Long Faces And Big Foreheads

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face and a big forehead, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of options to choose from that can flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

A long face and a big forehead can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements your features. The key is to choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look. You'll also want to avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate your forehead.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle, focus on adding volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with layers, curls, or waves. You can also try a side-swept fringe to draw attention away from your forehead.

If you have long hair, try adding layers that frame your face. This will create a more balanced look and add movement to your hair. You can also try curling your hair to add volume and texture.

If you prefer shorter hair, a bob or a pixie cut can be a great option. A bob that ends just below your chin will accentuate your jawline and balance out your face. A pixie cut can also be flattering, especially if you add volume to the sides of your hair.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape. If you have a long face and a big forehead, you'll want to focus on adding volume to the sides of your face.

2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Look for styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, curls, or waves.

3. Consider adding a side-swept fringe to draw attention away from your forehead.

4. If you have long hair, try adding layers that frame your face or curling your hair to add volume.

5. If you prefer shorter hair, consider a bob or a pixie cut. A bob that ends just below your chin will accentuate your jawline, while a pixie cut can be flattering if you add volume to the sides of your hair.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate your forehead.

2. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

3. Consider using hair products that add volume and texture to your hair.

4. If you have thin hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

FAQ

Q: What are the best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads?

A: The best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads are those that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, curls, or waves. You can also try a side-swept fringe to draw attention away from your forehead.

Q: Should I avoid hairstyles that pull my hair back tightly?

A: Yes, you should avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate your forehead.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair, it's important to use high-quality products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thin hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. You should also use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. You should also avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and beautiful every day.
