What Size Are Clipper Guards

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Clipper guards are essential tools in maintaining short haircuts. These attachments are used with clippers to cut hair at different lengths, allowing you to achieve a variety of styles. Knowing what size are clipper guards is crucial in achieving your desired look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about clipper guards, including their sizes, how to use them, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Clipper Guards Sizes
  • How to Use Clipper Guards
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Clipper Guards
  • Clipper Guards FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Clipper Guards Sizes

Clipper guards come in different sizes, usually numbered from 0 to 8. The smaller the number, the shorter the hair will be cut. For instance, a #0 guard will cut hair very close to the scalp, while a #8 guard will leave hair relatively long.

However, it's important to note that sizes may differ slightly across brands. Always check the size of the guard before using it to avoid any surprises.

How to Use Clipper Guards

Using clipper guards is relatively easy, even for beginners. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the right size of guard based on the length you want to achieve.

Step 2: Attach the guard to your clipper. Make sure it's securely in place.

Step 3: Start at the bottom of your head and work your way up. Use long, sweeping motions to avoid creating uneven lines.

Step 4: Use a smaller size of guard to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head.

Step 5: Clean your clippers and guards after use.

Tips for Using Clipper Guards

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your clipper guards:

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to lift and guide the hair while clipping.
  • Don't push the clipper too hard against your scalp as it may cause skin irritation.
  • If you're unsure about the guard size to use, always go for a larger size to be on the safe side.

Clipper Guards FAQ

Q: Can clipper guards be used on wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to use clipper guards on wet hair as it may cause the blades to rust or dull faster.

Q: How often should I replace my clipper guards?

A: Clipper guards can last for several years if properly maintained. However, if you notice any signs of wear or tear, it's best to replace them.

Q: Can I use clipper guards on pets?

A: Yes, clipper guards can be used on pets such as dogs and cats. However, make sure to use the right size and type of clippers for your pet's fur.

Hair Care Tips

When using clipper guards, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid any damage or breakage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know what size are clipper guards and how to use them, you can achieve a variety of short haircuts with ease. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and sharp.
