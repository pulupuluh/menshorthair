Best Way To Sleep With Tape Hair Extensions A Complete Guide

If you are a tape hair extensions wearer, you must know how important it is to take care of them. One of the most important things you need to consider is how you sleep with them. Sleeping with tape hair extensions can be quite challenging, especially if you're new to hair extensions. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on the best way to sleep with tape hair extensions.

How to Sleep with Tape Hair Extensions

Sleeping with tape hair extensions can be challenging as you don't want to damage them or cause any tangles. The best way to sleep with tape hair extensions is to keep them secure and prevent them from getting tangled. Here are some tips on how to sleep with tape hair extensions:

1. Brush Your Hair

Before going to bed, make sure to brush your hair to remove any tangles. Use a soft-bristled brush to gently detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

2. Tie Your Hair

Tie your hair into a low ponytail or braid it to prevent tangling. Make sure to use a soft elastic band to secure your hair, as a tight one can cause breakage and damage to your hair extensions.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can reduce friction and prevent your hair from getting tangled. Silk pillowcases are also gentle on your skin and help prevent wrinkles.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to sleep with tape hair extensions:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Use a soft-bristled brush to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair

Tie your hair into a low ponytail or braid it to prevent tangling. Use a soft elastic band to secure your hair.

Step 3: Secure Your Extensions

Use a clip or bobby pin to secure your hair extensions to your head. This will prevent them from getting tangled while you sleep.

Step 4: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent your hair from getting tangled.

Tips to Follow

Here are some additional tips that you should follow to ensure that your tape hair extensions stay in good condition while you sleep:

1. Don't Sleep with Wet Hair

Make sure your hair is completely dry before going to bed. Sleeping with wet hair can cause tangling and damage to your hair extensions.

2. Avoid Sleeping on Your Back

Avoid sleeping on your back as much as possible. Sleeping on your back can cause your hair to tangle and become matted.

3. Avoid Using Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers on your hair extensions. Heat can damage the adhesive and weaken the bond of the tape.

FAQs

Q. Can I sleep with my tape hair extensions?

A. Yes, you can sleep with your tape hair extensions, but you need to take care of them to prevent tangling and damage.

Q. How long can I wear tape hair extensions?

A. Tape hair extensions can last for 6-8 weeks with proper care.

Q. Can I wash my tape hair extensions before sleeping?

A. Yes, you can wash your tape hair extensions before sleeping, but make sure they are completely dry before going to bed.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips that you should follow to keep your tape hair extensions in good condition:

1. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangling and matting. Use a soft-bristled brush to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair extensions. Avoid using shampoos that contain sulfates, as they can strip the hair of its natural oils.

3. Avoid Using Conditioner on the Tape Bonds

Avoid using conditioner on the tape bonds as it can weaken the adhesive and cause the extensions to fall out.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your tape hair extensions stay in good condition while you sleep. Remember to take care of your hair extensions, and they will last for a long time.
