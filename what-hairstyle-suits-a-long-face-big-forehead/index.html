What Hairstyle Suits A Long Face Big Forehead

Written by Luffy Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you someone with a long face and a big forehead wondering what hairstyle suits you best? Look no further as we have got you covered! In this article, we will provide you with tips, tricks and a step-by-step guide on finding the perfect hairstyle that will accentuate your features and make you look your best.

Description

Having a long face and a big forehead can be tricky when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle that suits you. You want to accentuate your features, but also want to make sure that the hairstyle doesn't make your face look even longer. It can be overwhelming and confusing to decide on which hairstyle to choose. However, with our helpful tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for a long face big forehead is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If the length of your face is longer than the width, then you have a long face.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can now start looking at hairstyles that suit a long face big forehead. The key is to choose a hairstyle that will balance out the length of your face and make your forehead appear smaller. Here are a few hairstyles that are perfect for a long face big forehead:

1. Bangs

Bangs are a great way to draw attention away from your forehead and create a balanced look. You can opt for side-swept bangs, blunt bangs or wispy bangs depending on your preference. Just make sure that the bangs are not too short as they can make your face look longer.

2. Layers

Layers are perfect for adding volume and texture to your hair. They also create an illusion of a shorter face. You can choose to add layers to the front of your hair or all over depending on your preference.

3. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on a long face big forehead. It creates a balanced look and draws attention away from your forehead. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob depending on your preference.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a hairstyle that suits a long face big forehead:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If the length of your face is longer than the width, then you have a long face.

Step 2: Choose a hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that will balance out the length of your face and make your forehead appear smaller. Opt for bangs, layers or a bob.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

Consult with a stylist to determine which hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair type best. They can also give you advice on how to maintain the hairstyle.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face big forehead:

  • Opt for bangs to draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Consult with a stylist to determine which hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair type best.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a long face big forehead?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a long face big forehead. However, make sure to add layers to create volume and texture and avoid hairstyles that are too long.

Q: What kind of bangs should I opt for if I have a long face big forehead?

A: You can opt for side-swept bangs, blunt bangs or wispy bangs depending on your preference. Just make sure that the bangs are not too short as they can make your face look longer.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle for your long face big forehead, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you have all the tips, tricks and a step-by-step guide, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face big forehead. Remember to consult with a stylist and take care of your hair to maintain the look. Good luck!
