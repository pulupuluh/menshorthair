Inverted Triangle Face Shape Makeup How To Enhance Your Features

Written by Christine Apr 16, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have an inverted triangle face shape, you might find it challenging to apply makeup that enhances your features. This face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a balanced and flattering look that complements your unique facial structure. In this article, we'll share tips and tricks for inverted triangle face shape makeup, as well as hair care and FAQ.

Description

The inverted triangle face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. This shape is also known as the heart-shaped face, and it's a common facial structure among women. The key to applying makeup for this face shape is to balance out the wider top and narrower bottom. You can create the illusion of a more proportional face by using contouring techniques and playing up your best features.

How to Apply Inverted Triangle Face Shape Makeup

When it comes to applying makeup for an inverted triangle face shape, the goal is to create balance and proportion. Here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Use contouring to create a more defined jawline and balance out the wider forehead. Apply a darker shade of foundation or contour powder along the jawline and blend upwards towards the ears.
  • Avoid applying too much blush to the apples of your cheeks, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, apply blush slightly higher up towards your temples to create a more lifted look.
  • Play up your eyes with eyeshadow and mascara to draw attention upwards towards the wider part of your face. Avoid applying heavy eyeliner to your lower lash line, as this can make your chin appear even narrower.
  • Choose lip colors that complement your skin tone and enhance your natural lip shape. Avoid bold or dark lip colors that can draw attention away from your eyes and cheeks.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to apply makeup for an inverted triangle face shape:

  1. Start by applying a primer to your face to create a smooth base for your makeup.
  2. Apply foundation evenly across your face, blending outwards towards your hairline and jawline.
  3. Use a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to define your jawline. Apply the product along your jawline and blend upwards towards your ears.
  4. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards towards your temples. Be sure to use a light hand and build up the color gradually.
  5. Use a highlighter along the tops of your cheekbones and brow bone to create a lifted effect.
  6. Apply eyeshadow to your lids, using a lighter color on the inner corners and a darker color on the outer corners. Use mascara to lengthen and volumize your lashes.
  7. Choose a lip color that complements your skin tone and natural lip shape. Apply the color evenly and define your lips with a lip liner if desired.
  8. Finish your makeup look with a setting spray to keep your makeup in place all day.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for applying makeup for an inverted triangle face shape:

  • Use a lighter shade of foundation or concealer on your chin to create the illusion of a wider chin.
  • Avoid applying too much highlighter to your forehead, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Experiment with different lip colors and finishes to find the shades that work best for your unique face shape.
  • Invest in good quality makeup brushes and tools to ensure a flawless application.
  • Take care of your skin by cleansing, moisturizing, and using sunscreen daily. Healthy skin is the foundation for a flawless makeup look.

FAQs

What hairstyles work best for an inverted triangle face shape?

Hairstyles that work well for an inverted triangle face shape include long layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.

What are some common makeup mistakes to avoid for an inverted triangle face shape?

Common makeup mistakes to avoid for an inverted triangle face shape include applying too much blush to the apples of your cheeks, using heavy eyeliner on your lower lash line, and using bronzer on your forehead. These mistakes can make your face appear wider and less balanced.

What is the best way to apply contour for an inverted triangle face shape?

The best way to apply contour for an inverted triangle face shape is to use a darker shade of foundation or contour powder along your jawline and blend upwards towards your ears. This creates a more defined jawline and balances out the wider forehead.

Hair Care for Inverted Triangle Face Shapes

In addition to makeup, hair care is also important for enhancing your features and creating a balanced look. Here are some tips for hair care for inverted triangle face shapes:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the lower part of your face, such as chin-length bobs or layered hairstyles.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.
  • Experiment with different hair colors and highlights to find the shades that complement your skin tone and enhance your features.
  • Take care of your hair by using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, as well as regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free from split ends.

In conclusion, applying makeup for an inverted triangle face shape can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a balanced and flattering look. Remember to focus on creating a defined jawline and balancing out the wider forehead, and experiment with different shades and finishes to find the colors that work best for your unique facial structure. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to enhance your features and feel confident in your own skin.
