How To Look Good With Bangs And Glasses

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you someone who wears glasses and has recently gotten bangs? Or are you considering getting bangs but unsure if they will look good with your glasses? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with finding the right hairstyle to complement their glasses. In this article, we will discuss how to look good with bangs and glasses in easy-to-understand language.

How to Look Good with Bangs and Glasses

The key to looking good with bangs and glasses is finding the right balance. You want your hairstyle to complement your glasses and not overpower them. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose the Right Bangs

When choosing bangs, consider your face shape and the type of glasses you wear. For example, if you have a round face, go for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides. If you have a square face, consider soft, wispy bangs that are shorter in the middle and longer on the sides. If you wear glasses with thick frames, avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can overpower your face.

2. Balance Your Hairstyle

When you have bangs and wear glasses, it's important to balance your hairstyle. If you have short hair, consider longer bangs that can be swept to the side. If you have long hair, consider shorter, blunt bangs that can frame your face. The goal is to create a harmonious look that complements both your glasses and your hairstyle.

3. Highlight Your Best Features

When you have bangs and glasses, it's important to highlight your best features. If you have beautiful eyes, consider wearing contacts or choosing glasses that draw attention to your eyes. If you have a great smile, consider wearing bright lipstick that can draw attention to your mouth. The key is to find the right balance that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure how to make bangs and glasses work, here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right bangs for your glasses is to determine your face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and examining your face. Is your face round, square, oval, or heart-shaped?

Step 2: Choose the Right Glasses

Once you know your face shape, you can choose glasses that complement your features. If you have a round face, consider glasses with angular frames that can add definition to your face. If you have a square face, consider glasses with round frames that can soften your features.

Step 3: Choose the Right Bangs

Once you have your glasses, you can choose bangs that complement your face shape and glasses. Side-swept bangs are great for round faces, while blunt bangs are great for square faces. If you have an oval face, you can try any type of bangs.

Step 4: Balance Your Hairstyle

When you have bangs and glasses, it's important to balance your hairstyle. If you have short hair, consider longer bangs that can be swept to the side. If you have long hair, consider shorter, blunt bangs that can frame your face.

Step 5: Highlight Your Best Features

Finally, highlight your best features. If you have beautiful eyes, wear glasses that draw attention to your eyes. If you have a great smile, wear bright lipstick that can draw attention to your mouth.

Tips for Making Bangs and Glasses Work

Here are some additional tips for making bangs and glasses work:

1. Keep Your Glasses Clean

Dirty glasses can detract from your overall look. Make sure to clean your glasses regularly to keep them looking their best.

2. Use a Styling Product

Use a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place. This will prevent them from falling in your eyes and detracting from your glasses.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Try wearing your hair up or down, or parting your bangs in different ways. The more you experiment, the more you'll learn what works for you.

4. Embrace Your Individuality

Most importantly, embrace your individuality. Your glasses and bangs are a part of who you are, so don't be afraid to show them off. Be confident and proud of your unique style.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear bangs if I have a high prescription?

Yes, you can wear bangs regardless of your prescription. However, if you have a high prescription, it's important to choose glasses that are lightweight and comfortable. Heavy glasses can put pressure on your nose and ears, making them uncomfortable to wear for long periods of time.

2. Can I wear glasses with bold frames and heavy bangs?

Yes, you can wear glasses with bold frames and heavy bangs, but it's important to find the right balance. If your glasses are bold and heavy, consider softer, wispy bangs that can complement your glasses without overpowering them.

3. How often should I trim my bangs?

It's important to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking their best. Depending on how fast your hair grows, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs and Glasses

Here are some additional hair care tips for bangs and glasses:

1. Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. This will ensure that your bangs look their best.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair after washing it to keep your bangs in place. Use a round brush to create volume and sweep your bangs to the side.

3. Use Heat-Protectant Products

If you use a flat iron, curling iron, or other heat-styling tool, make sure to use a heat-protectant product. This will prevent your hair from becoming damaged and keep it looking healthy.

In conclusion, wearing bangs and glasses can be a great way to express your individual style. By following these tips, you can create a balanced, harmonious look that complements both your glasses and your hairstyle. Remember to experiment and have fun with your unique style!
