How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Bob Step By Step Tutorial

Written by William Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without breaking the bank, cutting your own hair into a short bob is a great option. Not only is it cost-effective, but it also gives you the freedom to style your hair the way you want it. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to cut your own hair short bob in relaxed English language, step-by-step. So grab your scissors and let's get started!

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Bob

Before we dive into the step-by-step process of cutting your own hair short bob, here's a quick list of the tools you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to cutting your own hair short bob is to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to see the natural fall of your hair and ensure that your haircut is even. Once your hair is dry, comb it straight down.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start with the Back

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Remember to cut less hair than you think you need, as you can always go back and cut more.

Step 4: Move to the Front

Once you've finished the back section, move to the front. Take a small section of hair from one side and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair to your desired length, following the angle of your jawline. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all four sections, it's time to blend the layers. Comb your hair straight down and snip any uneven pieces. Use your fingers to blend the layers together, making sure that there are no harsh lines.

Step 6: Check the Length

Once you've finished blending the layers, check the length of your hair. If you need to make any final adjustments, do so now.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your own hair short bob, it's time to style it. You can blow-dry your hair straight or add some curls with a curling iron. Whatever style you choose, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Short Bob

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair short bob:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • If you have curly hair, cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises once it dries.
  • Take breaks if you get tired or need to step away. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and it's important to take breaks to avoid mistakes.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair short bob?

Yes, you can cut your own hair short bob. It's important to have the right tools and follow the step-by-step process to ensure a clean cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix the mistake or wait for your hair to grow out. Remember, hair always grows back!

How often should I cut my own hair short bob?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short bob haircut.

Hair Care for Your Short Bob

Now that you've cut your own hair short bob, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your short bob:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your short bob.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut your own hair short bob at home. Remember to take your time and follow the step-by-step process to ensure a clean and even cut. Happy cutting!
