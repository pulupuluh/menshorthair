Best Men s Hair Paste For Thick Hair In 2023

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 · 9 min read

When it comes to styling thick hair, it can be a challenge to find the right product that will provide the hold and texture you need without weighing your hair down. Hair paste is a popular choice among men with thick hair because it provides a strong hold and allows for easy styling. In this article, we'll take a look at the best men's hair paste for thick hair in 2023, how to use it, and some tips for maintaining your style.

How to Choose the Best Men's Hair Paste for Thick Hair

There are a few things to consider when choosing the best hair paste for thick hair. Here are some factors to keep in mind:

Hold

The hold you need will depend on the style you're trying to achieve. If you want a messy, textured look, a medium hold paste will work well. If you're looking for a slicked back style, you'll want a stronger hold paste.

Texture

Thick hair can be difficult to style, so you'll want a paste that provides texture and separation. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair.

Ingredients

Look for hair pastes that are made with natural ingredients and are free of harsh chemicals. Ingredients like beeswax, clay, and shea butter can provide a strong hold without damaging your hair.

Best Men's Hair Paste for Thick Hair

Here are some of the best hair pastes for men with thick hair:

1. Baxter of California Clay Pomade

This clay pomade provides a strong hold and a matte finish. It's made with natural ingredients like clay and beeswax, which help to add texture and volume to your hair.

2. American Crew Fiber

American Crew Fiber is a popular choice among men with thick hair. It provides a strong hold and a matte finish, and is made with natural ingredients like beeswax and lanolin.

3. Jack Black Wax Pomade

Jack Black Wax Pomade is a versatile product that can be used to create a variety of styles. It provides a strong hold and a natural shine, and is made with natural ingredients like beeswax and tea tree oil.

How to Use Hair Paste

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use hair paste:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel, but leave it slightly damp.

Step 2: Scoop Out a Small Amount of Paste

Depending on the length of your hair, you'll need to use a different amount of paste. Start with a small amount, and add more if necessary.

Step 3: Rub the Paste Between Your Hands

Rub the paste between your hands to warm it up and make it easier to apply.

Step 4: Apply the Paste to Your Hair

Apply the paste to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray (Optional)

If you want extra hold, you can finish with a light spray of hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your style throughout the day:

1. Don't Touch Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair can cause it to lose its hold and texture. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

If your hair starts to look greasy, use a dry shampoo to absorb oil and refresh your style.

3. Use a Hair Comb

A hair comb can help you to keep your style looking polished and neat throughout the day.

FAQs

What is the difference between hair paste and hair gel?

Hair paste is a thicker, more matte product that provides a strong hold and texture. Hair gel is a thinner, more sticky product that provides a shiny, wet look.

Can I use hair paste on wet hair?

It's best to use hair paste on slightly damp hair. This will help the product to spread evenly and provide a better hold.

How often should I wash my hair if I use hair paste?

It's best to wash your hair every other day if you use hair paste. This will help to prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care

Using the right product is important, but taking care of your hair is equally as important. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, finding the best men's hair paste for thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right product and some styling tips, you can achieve the look you want. Remember to choose a hair paste that provides a strong hold and texture, and use it on slightly damp hair for best results.
