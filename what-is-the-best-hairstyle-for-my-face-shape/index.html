What Is The Best Hairstyle For My Face Shape

Written by Oliver Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're looking for a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape before making any decisions. The right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look your best, while the wrong one can have the opposite effect. In this article, we'll go over how to determine your face shape and what hairstyles work best for each shape.

Table of Contents

  • What is Face Shape?
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Each Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle
  • FAQ

What is Face Shape?

Your face shape is determined by the proportions and angles of your face. There are several different face shapes, including:
  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Pear
Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and proportions, which can help determine which hairstyles work best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

To determine your face shape, start by pulling your hair back and taking a look at your face in the mirror. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the following:
  • Forehead width
  • Cheekbone width
  • Jawline width
  • Face length
Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape. There are also online face shape calculators that can help you determine your face shape if you're still unsure.

Best Hairstyles for Each Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the best hairstyle for you. Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, but some of the best options include:
  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is roughly equal in width and length, with a rounded jawline and cheeks. The best hairstyles for round faces include:
  • Long, layered cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a forehead, cheekbones, and jaw that are roughly equal in width. The best hairstyles for square faces include:
  • Soft, layered cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long waves

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline. The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces include:
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Pixie cuts

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is widest at the cheekbones and has a pointed chin and forehead. The best hairstyles for diamond faces include:
  • Short, layered cuts
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Side-swept bangs

Pear Face Shape

A pear face shape is wider at the jawline than at the forehead. The best hairstyles for pear-shaped faces include:
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Long layers

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

In addition to considering your face shape, there are a few other things to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:
  • Your hair texture
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personal style
  • Your hair care routine
It's important to choose a hairstyle that works with your natural hair texture and that you can maintain with your daily routine. You should also consider your personal style and choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQ

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of face shapes, in which case you should consider the guidelines for both shapes and choose a hairstyle that works for both.

Can I still wear a hairstyle that isn't recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions, and ultimately it's up to you to decide what hairstyle makes you feel your best.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
Remember, the best hairstyle for your face shape is one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Use these guidelines as a starting point, but don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles.
