Which Hairstyle Suits Me Male Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what hairstyle suits your round face? As a man with a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. You don't want a style that accentuates your roundness, but you also don't want a style that makes your face appear longer. In this article, we will discuss which hairstyle suits you as a male with a round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by its circular shape and lacks sharp angles. The cheekbones and jawline are usually the widest points of the face, and the forehead is typically rounded. Men with round faces tend to have softer features and fuller cheeks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into which hairstyle suits a male round face, it's essential to determine your face shape. You can do this by standing in front of the mirror and using a tape measure. Measure the distance between your hairline and your chin, and the distance across your cheekbones. If both measurements are relatively similar, you have a round face.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have determined your face shape, let's take a look at which hairstyle suits you best.

1. Short Sides and Long Top

This hairstyle is perfect for round faces as it adds length to the face, making it appear slimmer. The short sides create the illusion of a more defined jawline, while the long top adds height to the face. This style is versatile and can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular style for men with round faces. This style features a short cut on the sides and back and a textured, choppy top. The texture on the top adds volume and height to the face, while the short sides create a more defined jawline. This style is low maintenance and easy to style.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with round faces. This style features a deep part on one side of the head, with the hair combed over towards the other side. The side part creates the illusion of a longer face, making it appear slimmer. This style can be worn with short or long hair and is perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a male with a round face:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face.
  • Choose styles that add height to the face, making it appear slimmer.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut on the sides to create a more defined jawline.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which hairstyle suits a male round face:

What are some hairstyles to avoid for a male with a round face?

Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as a bowl cut or a mushroom cut. These styles can make the face appear even rounder.

Can a male with a round face wear a buzz cut?

Yes, a buzz cut can work well for a male with a round face. However, consider getting a fade on the sides to create a more defined jawline.

Should a male with a round face grow a beard?

A beard can help to balance out a round face, but it's not necessary. Consider growing a short, well-groomed beard to add definition to the jawline.

Hair Care

Regardless of which hairstyle you choose, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, there are several hairstyles that suit a male with a round face. Consider styles that add length and height to the face and avoid styles that add volume to the sides. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
