How To Cut Your Own Hair High Fade A Step By Step Guide

In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair high fade.

How to Cut Your Own Hair High Fade

A high fade haircut is a popular style that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The hair near the top of the head is left longer, while the sides and back are cut very short or shaved. Here are the basic steps to follow:

1. Gather Your Tools

You'll need the following tools to cut your own hair high fade:

  • Clippers with different guard sizes (1, 2, 3, 4, and 5)
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A towel or cape to cover your shoulders

2. Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the length of your hair. You can use different guard sizes to achieve different lengths. For a high fade, the hair near the top of the head should be left longer, while the sides and back should be cut very short or shaved.

3. Start with the Sides

Start by using the clippers with the highest guard size (5) to cut the sides and back of your hair. Use long, even strokes and move the clippers upward towards the top of your head. Be sure to keep the clippers level with your head, and don't push too hard to avoid cutting too much hair.

4. Gradually Decrease the Guard Size

As you move upwards, gradually decrease the guard size to achieve a fade effect. Use the clippers with guard sizes 4, 3, 2, and 1 to achieve different lengths. Be sure to blend the hair between each guard size to achieve a smooth, even transition.

5. Cut the Top of Your Hair

After you've finished the sides and back, it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and use the comb to guide your scissors. You can also use the clippers without a guard to trim the hair near the top of your head.

6. Blend the Top and Sides

Use the scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair. Use the comb to lift the hair on top of your head and cut the hair near the sides to achieve a smooth, even blend.

7. Check Your Work

After you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is level and even.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your own hair high fade:

  1. Gather your tools.
  2. Decide on the length of your hair.
  3. Start with the sides.
  4. Gradually decrease the guard size.
  5. Cut the top of your hair.
  6. Blend the top and sides.
  7. Check your work.

Tips for a Perfect High Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect high fade haircut:

  • Start with a higher guard size and gradually decrease the guard size to achieve a smooth, even fade.
  • Use the scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair.
  • Don't push too hard with the clippers to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the mirror to check your work and make any necessary adjustments.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair high fade:

Can I cut my own hair high fade?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can cut your own hair high fade.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair high fade?

You'll need clippers with different guard sizes, scissors, a comb, a mirror, a spray bottle filled with water, and a towel or cape to cover your shoulders.

What length should I cut my hair for a high fade?

The hair near the top of the head should be left longer, while the sides and back should be cut very short or shaved.

Hair Care Tips

After you've cut your own hair high fade, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair high fade, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps and tips provided in this article. Good luck!
