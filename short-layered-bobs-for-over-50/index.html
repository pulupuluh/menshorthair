Short Layered Bobs For Over 50 A Perfect Hairstyle For Mature Women

Written by Petter Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle that is chic, trendy, and easy to maintain? Look no further than the short layered bob! This versatile cut can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we'll show you how to rock a short layered bob at any age.

Description

The short layered bob is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It features multiple layers of hair that are cut at different lengths, creating a textured and voluminous look. This style is perfect for women over 50 who want to add some dimension and movement to their hair without sacrificing length.

How to Rock a Short Layered Bob

If you're considering a short layered bob, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The length of your short layered bob will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. Some women prefer a chin-length bob, while others go for a shorter pixie cut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for you.

Step 2: Decide on the layers

The beauty of a short layered bob is that it can be customized to your liking. You can opt for subtle layers that blend seamlessly into your hair, or go for more dramatic layers that create a choppy, edgy look.

Step 3: Choose the right styling products

To maintain your short layered bob, you'll need to invest in some quality styling products. Look for products that add volume, texture, and hold, such as mousse, texturizing spray, and hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Layered Bob

Now that you have your short layered bob, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

TIP #1: Get regular trims

To keep your short layered bob looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims. Aim for a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your layers looking sharp.

TIP #2: Experiment with different styling techniques

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. You can try blow-drying your hair with a round brush for volume, using a flat iron for sleekness, or scrunching your hair with a texturizing product for a beachy look.

TIP #3: Protect your hair from heat damage

If you're using heat styling tools on your short layered bob, make sure to protect your hair from damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about short layered bobs for over 50:

Q: Will a short layered bob make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! A short layered bob can actually make you look more youthful and vibrant, especially if you add some volume and texture.

Q: Do I need to have thick hair to pull off a short layered bob?

A: No, you don't need thick hair to rock a short layered bob. Your hairstylist can customize the layers to suit your hair texture and thickness.

Q: Can I still wear my short layered bob in an updo?

A: Yes, you can! While a short layered bob may not be as versatile as longer hairstyles when it comes to updos, you can still experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a braided updo.

Hair Care for Short Layered Bobs

Here are some additional tips for caring for your short layered bob:

TIP #1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair, especially if you have color-treated hair. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that will gently cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

TIP #2: Deep condition regularly

To keep your short layered bob looking healthy and shiny, it's important to deep condition your hair regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

TIP #3: Protect your hair from the sun

The sun's UV rays can be damaging to your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Protect your short layered bob from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

In conclusion, a short layered bob is a stylish and practical hairstyle for women over 50. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can rock this look with confidence and ease.
