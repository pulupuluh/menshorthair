How To Cut Men s Hair Without Clippers At Home

Written by Bardi Jul 19, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money and cut your hair at home, you might be wondering how to cut men's hair without clippers. While clippers can make the process easier, they're not always necessary. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut without ever turning on a clipper. In this blog post, we'll show you how.

Description

Cutting men's hair without clippers is a great way to save money and achieve a great look. While it may seem intimidating at first, it's actually quite simple with the right tools and techniques. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror to help you see what you're doing. With these tools in hand, you'll be able to cut your hair with confidence and achieve a great look.

How to Cut Men's Hair Without Clippers at Home

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll want to wash and dry it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt or oil that could make it harder to cut. Once your hair is clean, use a towel to dry it completely. You may also want to comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll want to section your hair so that you can cut it more easily. Use a comb to create a part down the center of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then create two more parts, one on each side of your head. This will create four sections of hair.

Step 3: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Starting with the top section of your hair, comb it straight up and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can repeat this process over the entire top section of your hair until you're happy with the length.

Step 4: Cut the Sides of Your Hair

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Use the same technique as before, combing the hair straight up and cutting it to your desired length. You may want to use your fingers to hold the hair in place as you cut.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've cut the top and side sections of your hair, you'll want to blend them together for a seamless look. Use the scissors to cut at an angle, blending the two sections together. You may need to use your fingers to hold the hair in place as you blend.

Step 6: Trim Your Bangs

If you have bangs, you'll want to trim them separately. Comb your bangs straight down and cut them to your desired length. You can use your fingers to hold the hair in place as you cut.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any additional cuts as necessary, and trim any stray hairs that you may have missed. You may also want to have someone else check your work to make sure everything looks even.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Without Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to make cutting easier
  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes – hair grows back!
  • Take your time and work in small sections for the best results

FAQ

Can I cut my hair without scissors?

No, scissors are necessary for cutting hair. You can't achieve a good cut with other tools.

How long does it take to cut men's hair?

It depends on how long and thick your hair is, as well as the style you're going for. Generally, it takes around 30 to 45 minutes to cut men's hair at home.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry – mistakes happen. If you make a mistake, simply trim the hair to the same length as the rest of your hair.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money, but it's important to take care of your hair afterwards. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and looking great. You may also want to use a styling product to help you achieve your desired look.

Cutting men's hair without clippers is easier than you might think. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Follow these steps, and you'll be well on your way to a fresh new look.
