What Length Is Number 4 Haircut In Mm

Written by William Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a haircut that's not too short but not too long, a number 4 haircut might be just what you need. But what length is number 4 haircut in mm? In this blog post, we'll answer that question and provide you with some tips on how to style and care for your hair after getting a number 4 haircut.

Description

A number 4 haircut is a medium-length haircut that leaves about 1/2 inch (12.5mm) of hair on the scalp. It's typically achieved using clippers with a number 4 guard attached. This haircut is great for those who want a low-maintenance style that's not too short or too long.

How to

If you want to get a number 4 haircut, you can either go to a barber or do it yourself at home. Here's how to do it yourself:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Attach a number 4 guard to your clippers.
  3. Begin cutting your hair, starting at the nape of your neck and moving upward.
  4. Be sure to cut evenly and use a mirror to check your progress.
  5. If you want to blend the hair on the top of your head with the hair on the sides, use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Be sure to blend it evenly with the sides.
  6. Once you're finished cutting, use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  7. Finish by washing your hair again and styling it as desired.

Step by Step

If you're still not sure how to get a number 4 haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Attach a number 4 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the nape of your neck and move upward, using the clippers to cut the hair evenly.
  4. Move to the sides and continue cutting, being sure to keep the guard flush against your scalp.
  5. Blend the hair on the top of your head with the hair on the sides using scissors. Be sure to blend it evenly.
  6. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  7. Wash your hair again and style as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a number 4 haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure you're cutting evenly.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you're not sure how short you want to go.
  • If you're not confident in your ability to cut your own hair, go to a barber instead.
  • Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Use styling products like hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place and add texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 4 haircuts:

What other haircuts are similar to a number 4?

Other haircuts that are similar to a number 4 include a crew cut, a buzz cut, and a high and tight haircut.

How often should I get a number 4 haircut?

You should get a number 4 haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and keep it looking fresh.

What hairstyles can I do with a number 4 haircut?

You can do a variety of hairstyles with a number 4 haircut, including a messy top, a slick back, a side part, and a textured crop.

Hair Care

After getting a number 4 haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause breakage.

Now that you know what length is number 4 haircut in mm, you can decide if it's the right haircut for you. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.
