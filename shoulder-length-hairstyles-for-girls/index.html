Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Girls A Guide To Trendy And Easy Hairdos

Written by Luffy May 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Shoulder length hairstyles are versatile and easy to maintain, making them an ideal choice for girls who want to rock a chic and trendy look without putting in too much effort. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there's a shoulder length hairstyle that will suit your style and personality. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for girls, including how to create them and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and wavy. Here are some popular shoulder length hairstyles for girls:

  • Blunt Bob: This classic hairstyle is perfect for girls who want a chic and sophisticated look. The blunt cut adds volume and texture to your hair, while the length frames your face beautifully.
  • Beach Waves: For a more relaxed and casual look, try beach waves. This hairstyle is perfect for girls with naturally wavy or curly hair, but can also be achieved with a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Layered Lob: The layered lob is a favorite among celebrities and fashionistas. This hairstyle features long layers that add movement and dimension to your hair, while the length hits just above the shoulders.
  • Straight and Sleek: If you prefer a more polished and sophisticated look, try straightening your shoulder length hair with a flat iron. This hairstyle is perfect for special occasions or a day at the office.

Step-by-Step Guide for Creating Shoulder Length Hairstyles

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating some of the most popular shoulder length hairstyles:

Blunt Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair straight, using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Finish with a shine serum or spray to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Beach Waves

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture.
  3. Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Gently tousle your hair with your fingers to create a more natural look.
  5. Finish with a sea salt spray to add even more texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your shoulder length hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and shiny.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Girls

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hairstyles:

Q: Will shoulder length hair suit my face shape?

A: Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can suit almost any face shape. Talk to your hairstylist about which shoulder length hairstyle will best flatter your features.

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and your lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If your hair is dry, you can get away with washing it every other day or even less frequently.

Q: How can I add volume to my shoulder length hair?

A: There are many ways to add volume to your shoulder length hair, including using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a root lifting spray.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for keeping your shoulder length hair looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as these can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Be gentle with your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on it too roughly.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of trendy and easy shoulder length hairstyles that will keep you looking stylish and chic. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.
