How Short Is 1 8 Haircut Everything You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're thinking about getting a 1/8 haircut, you might be wondering just how short it is. This popular buzz cut is a favorite for men and women alike, but it's not for everyone. In this post, we'll break down everything you need to know about the 1/8 haircut, including how short it is, how to get it, and how to care for it.

  • How to Get a 1/8 Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a 1/8 Haircut

  • Hair Care for a 1/8 Haircut

How Short is a 1/8 Haircut?

A 1/8 haircut is one of the shortest haircuts you can get. It involves cutting the hair to a length of 1/8 of an inch all over the head. That's just 3mm of hair!

This type of haircut is also known as a #1 clipper cut. It's a popular choice for men who want a low-maintenance, military-inspired look, but it's also becoming more common among women who want a super short, edgy style.

How to Get a 1/8 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 1/8 haircut, the best way to achieve the look is to go to a professional stylist or barber. They will have the tools and expertise needed to cut your hair to the correct length and shape your hairline.

However, if you're feeling brave and want to try cutting your own hair at home, you can use a set of clippers with a #1 guard attachment. Make sure to go slowly and carefully, as it's easy to make mistakes when cutting hair this short.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a 1/8 Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your own 1/8 haircut at home:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Attach a #1 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, using the clippers to cut your hair to a length of 1/8 inch.
  4. Be careful around the hairline and ears, as these areas can be tricky to cut.
  5. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas.

Tips for Maintaining a 1/8 Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your 1/8 haircut looking sharp and stylish:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair at the same length.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Consider using a styling product, like a wax or gel, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using a sunscreen with a high SPF.

FAQs About the 1/8 Haircut

Q: Is a 1/8 haircut the same as a bald fade?

A: No, a bald fade involves cutting the hair to a very short length at the bottom and gradually blending it into longer hair at the top. A 1/8 haircut is the same length all over.

Q: Will a 1/8 haircut work for all hair types?

A: It can work for most hair types, but it may not be the best choice for very curly or wavy hair.

Q: Will a 1/8 haircut make my hair grow back thicker?

A: No, cutting your hair short does not affect the thickness or texture of your hair as it grows back.

Hair Care for a 1/8 Haircut

Even though a 1/8 haircut is very short, it still requires some hair care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy scalp and hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and moisturized.
  • Use a scalp treatment to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

With the right care and maintenance, a 1/8 haircut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for anyone looking for a super short style.
