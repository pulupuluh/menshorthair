Does Short Hair Lengthen Your Face

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering getting a short haircut but worried it will make your face look longer? Many people believe that short hair lengthens the face, but is it true? In this blog post, we will explore this topic and provide you with valuable information to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts have been in style for many years, and they can be a great way to update your look. However, some people are hesitant to get a short haircut because they believe it will make their face look longer. This belief is not entirely true, and we will explain why in this article.

How to

To determine if short hair will lengthen your face, you need to consider your face shape. Face shape plays a significant role in how a hairstyle will look on you. If you have a round or square face shape, a short haircut can actually make your face appear shorter. On the other hand, if you have an oblong or rectangular face shape, a short haircut may make your face look longer.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Research short hairstyles that will complement your face shape

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for you

Tips

  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. This will help balance out a longer face shape.
  • Bangs can also be a great way to balance out a longer face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A: It depends on your face shape. If you have an oblong or rectangular face shape, a short haircut may make your face look longer. However, if you have a round or square face shape, a short haircut can actually make your face look shorter.

Q: Can I still wear a short haircut if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can still wear a short haircut if you have an oblong face shape. Look for styles that add volume to the top of your head and avoid styles that are too short.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Make sure to schedule regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, short hair does not necessarily lengthen your face. It all depends on your face shape and the hairstyle you choose. By considering your face shape and following our tips, you can find a short hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident and stylish.
