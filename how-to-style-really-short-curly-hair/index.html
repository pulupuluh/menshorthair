How To Style Really Short Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with a few tips and tricks, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls that turn heads. In this article, we'll show you how to style really short curly hair, step by step. Whether you have natural curls or you're using a curling iron, these tips will help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prep Your Hair
  • How to Style Really Short Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Styling Short Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

How to Prep Your Hair

Before you style your short curly hair, it's important to prep it properly. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid products that contain sulfates or alcohol, as they can dry out your curls.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

If you have natural curls, apply a curl enhancing product to define your curls and add volume. If you're using a curling iron, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

How to Style Really Short Curly Hair

Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Curling Iron

If you have straight or wavy hair, use a curling iron to create curls. Start by sectioning your hair and curling each section away from your face. Hold the curling iron for about 10 seconds, then release the curl.

2. Finger Comb Your Curls

After curling your hair, use your fingers to comb through your curls. This will give them a more natural, tousled look.

3. Use a Texturizing Spray

To add volume and texture to your curls, use a texturizing spray. Spray it onto your roots and scrunch your hair to distribute the product.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hair:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase will help reduce frizz and breakage, as it's gentler on your hair than cotton.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel

Drying your hair with a microfiber towel will help reduce frizz and breakage, as it's gentler on your hair than a regular towel.

FAQs About Styling Short Curly Hair

1. How do I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?

To prevent frizz, use a leave-in conditioner and a curl enhancing product, and avoid touching your curls too much.

2. Can I use a diffuser to dry my hair?

Yes, a diffuser can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Just make sure to use it on a low heat setting and avoid over-drying your hair.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week will help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Try to limit your use of curling irons and blow dryers.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray can help protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short curly hair with confidence. Whether you're going for a natural look or a more glamorous style, these tips will help you achieve the perfect curls.
