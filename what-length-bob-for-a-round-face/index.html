What Length Bob Is Best For A Round Face

Written by Frank Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what length bob is best for you. A bob is a timeless hairstyle that can be worn by anyone, but finding the right length can be tricky. In this blog post, we will discuss what length bob is best for a round face, as well as tips on how to style and care for your bob.

Description

A bob is a short to medium-length haircut that is typically cut straight around the head at about jaw-level. It can be worn straight, curly, or wavy, and can be styled in a variety of ways. For round faces, it's important to choose a length that will elongate the face and create the illusion of more length and less width.

How to Choose the Right Length

When choosing the right length bob for a round face, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A bob that is too short can make your face look even rounder, while a bob that is too long can drag down your features and make your face look wider.

The best length for a round face is a bob that falls just below the chin. This length will elongate your face and create the illusion of more length. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a longer bob that falls just above the shoulders. This will help to balance out your features and prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose the right length bob for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length for your face shape.
  3. Decide on a style for your bob, such as straight, curly, or wavy.
  4. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your features.
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your bob.

Tips for Styling and Care

Once you have the perfect bob for your round face, it's important to know how to style and care for it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your bob.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a side part or bangs, to switch up your look.

FAQ

What other hairstyles complement a round face?

Other hairstyles that complement a round face include long layers, a side-swept fringe, and a pixie cut with volume on top.

Can I wear a bob if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! A curly bob can be a great option for those with curly hair. Just make sure to choose a length that works with your curls and consult with your stylist on how to best style it.

How often should I wash my bob?

It's recommended to wash your bob every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference.

Can I still wear a bob if I have thin hair?

Yes! A bob can actually be a great style for those with thin hair, as it can create the illusion of more volume and body.

Hair Care

Taking care of your bob is essential to keeping it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting to prevent damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can cause damage and dryness.

In conclusion, finding the right length bob for a round face can be a game-changer. By choosing a length that flatters your features and complements your hair texture, you can create a chic and timeless style that's perfect for any occasion.
