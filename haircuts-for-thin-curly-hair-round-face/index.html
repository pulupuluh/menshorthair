Haircuts For Thin Curly Hair Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Eveline Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a round face and thin curly hair can be challenging when it comes to finding the perfect haircut. But, with the right tips and ideas, you can have a hairstyle that flatters your features and boosts your confidence. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions about haircuts for thin curly hair round face.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Thin Curly Hair Round Face

How to Choose a Haircut for Thin Curly Hair Round Face

The key to finding a haircut that suits your thin curly hair and round face is to create an illusion of volume and length. Here are some tips to help you choose a haircut:

  • Opt for layers: Layers create movement and add volume to thin curly hair. They also elongate the face and balance out roundness.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs make the face appear narrower and draw attention away from the roundness of the face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make thin curly hair look flat and lifeless.
  • Long hair: Long hair is a great option for thin curly hair round face. It adds length and volume to the hair, making the face appear slimmer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Thin Curly Hair Round Face

If you are looking to cut your thin curly hair round face at home, here is a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  2. Section your hair into four sections.
  3. Start cutting the hair at the back, working your way up to the crown.
  4. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut your hair into long layers. Always cut at an angle to create movement and volume.
  5. Once you have finished the back, move on to the sides. Cut the hair at an angle to create a soft frame around the face.
  6. Finish by cutting the bangs. Side-swept bangs are the best option for a round face.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Now that you have your perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Air dry your hair whenever possible to avoid frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs about Haircuts for Thin Curly Hair Round Face

What is the best haircut for thin curly hair round face?

The best haircut for thin curly hair round face is one that adds volume and length to the hair. Layers and side-swept bangs are great options.

Can I cut my thin curly hair round face at home?

Yes, you can cut your thin curly hair round face at home. However, it's important to follow a step-by-step guide and use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I trim my thin curly hair round face?

You should trim your thin curly hair round face every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin curly hair round face:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and ideas, you can find a haircut that flatters your thin curly hair round face and boosts your confidence.
