Does Facial Hair Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Christine Jun 09, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Facial hair has become a popular trend in recent years, and many men are sporting different styles of beards and mustaches. While some men grow facial hair for fashion, some do it to cover up a round or chubby face. But does facial hair make your face look thinner? In this blog post, we will explore this question and provide you with tips on how to style your facial hair to achieve a slimmer look.

Description

Having a round or chubby face is not necessarily a bad thing, but some men may feel self-conscious about it. If you are one of them, you may have heard that growing a beard or mustache can make your face look thinner. However, the truth is that facial hair can either make your face look thinner or fuller, depending on how you style it.

How to Style Your Facial Hair

The key to achieving a slimmer look with facial hair is to create an illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some tips on how to style your facial hair:

1. Keep it Short

If you want to create the illusion of a thinner face, keeping your facial hair short is the way to go. A short beard or mustache can help define your jawline and make your face look more angular. Avoid growing your facial hair too long, as it can make your face look fuller.

2. Shape Your Beard or Mustache

Shaping your beard or mustache can also help create a more defined jawline. A well-groomed beard or mustache can make your face look slimmer and more proportionate. Consider going to a professional barber to get your facial hair trimmed and shaped.

3. Avoid Full Beards

If you have a round or chubby face, avoid growing a full beard. A full beard can make your face look fuller and rounder. Instead, go for a goatee or a chinstrap beard. These styles can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

4. Use Hair Products

Using hair products can also help create a slimmer look. If you have a long beard, consider using beard oil or balm to keep it well-groomed and shiny. If you have a mustache, use wax to shape it and keep it in place.

Step by Step Guide

If you are new to styling your facial hair, here is a step by step guide on how to achieve a slimmer look:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you have a round or chubby face, go for a short beard or mustache. If you have an oval or square face, you can experiment with different styles.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style. Go for a goatee or a chinstrap beard if you have a round face, and experiment with different styles if you have an oval or square face.

Step 3: Trim and Shape Your Facial Hair

Trim and shape your facial hair regularly to maintain a well-groomed look. Use clippers or scissors to trim your beard or mustache, and use a razor to create clean lines.

Step 4: Use Hair Products

Use hair products such as beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair well-groomed and shiny. Use wax to shape your mustache and keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

Maintaining your facial hair is essential to achieve a slimmer look. Here are some tips for maintaining your facial hair:

1. Wash Your Facial Hair Regularly

Wash your facial hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and itchiness.

2. Moisturize Your Skin

Moisturize your skin regularly to prevent dryness and flakiness. Use a moisturizer that is suitable for your skin type.

3. Trim Your Facial Hair Regularly

Trim your facial hair regularly to maintain a well-groomed look. Use clippers or scissors to trim your beard or mustache, and use a razor to create clean lines.

FAQ

Q: Does facial hair make your face look thinner?

A: Facial hair can either make your face look thinner or fuller, depending on how you style it. Keeping your facial hair short and shaping it can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Q: What facial hair styles are best for a thin face?

A: If you have a thin face, you can experiment with different facial hair styles, such as a full beard or a mustache. However, avoid growing your facial hair too long, as it can make your face look longer and thinner.

Q: How often should I trim my facial hair?

A: You should trim your facial hair regularly to maintain a well-groomed look. If you have a short beard or mustache, trim it every few days. If you have a longer beard, trim it every few weeks.

Hair Care

Facial hair requires proper care to look its best. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your facial hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for facial hair, such as beard oil or balm. These products can help moisturize your facial hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

2. Brush Your Facial Hair

Brush your facial hair regularly to keep it detangled and looking neat. Use a brush that is suitable for your facial hair type.

3. Trim Your Facial Hair

Trim your facial hair regularly to maintain a well-groomed look. Use clippers or scissors to trim your beard or mustache, and use a razor to create clean lines.

In conclusion, facial hair can make your face look thinner or fuller, depending on how you style it. Keeping your facial hair short and shaping it can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline. With the tips and tricks outlined in this blog post, you can style your facial hair to achieve a slimmer look.
