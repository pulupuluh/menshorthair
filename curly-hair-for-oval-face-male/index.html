Curly Hair For Oval Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a male with an oval face and curly hair? Are you struggling to find the right hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide for curly hair for oval face male. In this article, we will cover everything from how to style your curls to hair care tips to maintain healthy and luscious curls.

Description

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be challenging to maintain and style, especially if you have an oval face. An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly rounded jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. The key to finding the right hairstyle for oval face male with curly hair is to balance the volume and texture of your hair with your face shape.

How to Style Curly Hair for Oval Face Male

When it comes to styling curly hair for oval face male, it's essential to find a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. Here are some tips to help you style your curls:

1. Embrace Your Natural Curls

The best way to style curly hair is to embrace your natural texture. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair and scrunch your curls to enhance their shape. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls gently and avoid frizz.

2. Opt for Shorter Haircuts

If you have tight curls, consider getting a shorter haircut that complements your oval face shape. A short haircut will help balance the volume of your hair with your face shape and make styling your curls more manageable. You can opt for a textured crop, a curly fringe, or a curly undercut.

3. Try a Side Part

A side part is a great way to balance the width of your forehead with your chin. You can part your hair on the side and let your curls fall naturally on both sides of your face. This will create a more symmetrical look and enhance your facial features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair for Oval Face Male

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out the excess water.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. Scrunch your curls to enhance their shape.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

You can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls gently. To avoid frizz, use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair while drying.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it according to your preference. You can use a hair wax, pomade, or gel to define your curls and add texture to your hair. You can also use a hair spray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Curly Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and luscious curls:

1. Use Sulfate-Free Shampoo and Conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help nourish your curls and prevent breakage. You can use a hair mask or a homemade deep conditioner made of natural ingredients such as avocado, honey, and coconut oil.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling wands can damage your curls and cause frizz. Avoid using them as much as possible and opt for natural styling methods such as air drying and diffusing.

4. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your curls while sleeping. This will prevent your hair from rubbing against cotton sheets and causing friction and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Is it possible to straighten curly hair for oval face male?

A: Yes, it is possible to straighten curly hair using a flat iron or a chemical treatment such as a keratin treatment. However, straightening your hair can cause damage and breakage, so it's essential to use heat protectant products and deep condition your hair regularly.

Q: Can oval face male with curly hair have a beard?

A: Yes, oval face male with curly hair can have a beard. A well-groomed beard can complement your facial features and add definition to your jawline.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry and coarse hair, you can wash your hair once or twice a week. If you have fine and oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Conclusion

Curly hair for oval face male can be a challenge, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your facial features and embrace your natural texture. Remember to take care of your curls by using sulfate-free products and deep conditioning your hair regularly. With these tips and tricks, you can rock your curls and feel confident in your own skin.
