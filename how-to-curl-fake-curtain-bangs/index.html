How To Curl Fake Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 08, 2023 · 7 min read
Table of Content: 1. Introduction 2. How to Curl Fake Curtain Bangs 3. Step-by-Step Guide 4. Tips for Curling Fake Curtain Bangs 5. Frequently Asked Questions 6. Hair Care Tips for Fake Curtain Bangs 7. Final Thoughts

If you’re looking to change up your hairstyle without committing to a drastic cut, fake curtain bangs may be the way to go. These faux bangs can add dimension and movement to your hair, giving you a fresh new look. However, achieving the perfect curl can be tricky. In this article, we’ll show you how to curl fake curtain bangs in a few easy steps.

How to Curl Fake Curtain Bangs

Curling fake curtain bangs is all about creating natural-looking waves that flow seamlessly with the rest of your hair. The key is to use a curling iron or wand that is the same size as your natural curl pattern. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Clips
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl your fake curtain bangs:

  1. Start by applying heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  2. Section off your fake curtain bangs from the rest of your hair and clip them up.
  3. Take a small section of hair from your fake curtain bangs and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting from the middle of the section.
  4. Hold for a few seconds and release the curl.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 with the remaining sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curls.
  6. Once you’ve curled all of the sections, lightly brush through your fake curtain bangs with your fingers to loosen the curls.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Fake Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when curling your fake curtain bangs:

  • Use a curling iron or wand that is the same size as your natural curl pattern to create a seamless look.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls to create more volume and movement.
  • Don’t curl your fake curtain bangs too tightly – you want them to look natural and effortless.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about curling fake curtain bangs:

What type of curling iron or wand should I use?

Use a curling iron or wand that is the same size as your natural curl pattern. This will help create a seamless look.

How tightly should I curl my fake curtain bangs?

Don’t curl your fake curtain bangs too tightly – you want them to look natural and effortless. Use a light touch when wrapping your hair around the curling iron or wand.

How do I make my fake curtain bangs look more voluminous?

Alternate the direction of your curls to create more volume and movement. You can also tease the roots of your fake curtain bangs for extra lift.

Hair Care Tips for Fake Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your fake curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your fake curtain bangs looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your fake curtain bangs to prevent damage.
  • Trim your fake curtain bangs every 6-8 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Final Thoughts

Curling fake curtain bangs is a great way to change up your hairstyle without committing to a drastic cut. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve natural-looking waves that flow seamlessly with the rest of your hair. Remember to take good care of your fake curtain bangs to keep them looking their best.
