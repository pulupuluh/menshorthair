How To Manage Mid Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have mid length hair, you know that it can be challenging to manage. It's not quite short enough to be low maintenance, but not long enough to easily style. In this post, we'll cover some tips and tricks for managing mid length hair, from how to style it to how to care for it. Read on for our expert advice!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Mid Length Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair
  • FAQ about Managing Mid Length Hair

How to Style Mid Length Hair

If you're looking for ways to style your mid length hair, you have plenty of options! Here are a few of our favorites:

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for mid length hair. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to your hair. Then, use a curling wand to create loose waves. Finally, run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural look.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you're looking for a simple and easy hairstyle, try a half-up, half-down look. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or hair tie. This style is perfect for a casual day out or for keeping your hair out of your face while you work.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add some interest to your mid length hair. Try a simple three-strand braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid for a more intricate look.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

In addition to styling your mid length hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Don't Overwash

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dry and damaged. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or every two to three days if possible.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Apply the treatment to your hair, leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse it out thoroughly.

FAQ about Managing Mid Length Hair

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: How can I make my hair look thicker?

A: To make your hair look thicker, try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider getting layers to add some texture.

Q: What should I do if my hair is frizzy?

A: To combat frizz, use a smoothing serum or oil to tame flyaways and keep your hair looking smooth.

Managing mid length hair doesn't have to be difficult! With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style and care for your hair with ease.
