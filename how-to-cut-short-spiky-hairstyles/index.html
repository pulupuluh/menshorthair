How To Cut Short Spiky Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, edgy hairstyle, a short spiky haircut might be the perfect choice for you. Not only is it easy to style, but it also adds a lot of personality to your overall look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut short spiky hairstyles in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. We'll cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. Tools You Need
  2. How to Cut Short Spiky Hairstyles
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Cutting Short Spiky Hairstyles
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Short Spiky Hairstyles

Tools You Need:

Before you begin cutting your hair, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools on hand. Here's a list of what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle
  • Styling product (gel, wax, or pomade)

Make sure your clippers and scissors are sharp and clean before you start cutting. Dull blades can result in uneven cuts and can damage your hair.

How to Cut Short Spiky Hairstyles:

Cutting short spiky hairstyles can be tricky, but with a little practice, you'll be able to master it. Here's an overview of the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.
  3. Starting with the sides, use your clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. Use a shorter guard for a closer cut, and a longer guard for a more textured look.
  4. Repeat the same process for the back of your head, making sure to blend the hair with the sides.
  5. Move on to the top section. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. For a spiky look, cut the hair shorter in the front and longer towards the back.
  6. Using your scissors, create texture by cutting small sections of hair at different angles. This will help create the spiky effect.
  7. Comb your hair forward, and use your scissors to trim any uneven pieces.
  8. Apply your styling product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  9. Use your fingers to style your hair into spikes.
  10. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Cutting Short Spiky Hairstyles:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short spiky hairstyle:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter. It's easier to cut more hair than to try to fix a cut that's too short.
  • Use thinning shears to create texture and a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.
  • When using clippers, start at the bottom and work your way up.
  • Always use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid damage to your hair.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short spiky hairstyles:

1. Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can. However, it's recommended that you seek the help of a professional hairstylist, especially if you've never cut your own hair before.

2. What's the best styling product to use for short spiky hairstyles?

Gel, wax, and pomade are all great options. It's best to choose a product that matches your hair type and desired level of hold.

3. How often do I need to cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. Generally, short spiky hairstyles require more frequent cuts to maintain their shape.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Hairstyles:

Here are some tips to keep your short spiky hairstyle looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your own short spiky hairstyle like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles. Happy cutting!
