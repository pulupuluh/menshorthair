How To Stop Hair Fall Naturally At Home In Tamil

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of seeing a bunch of hair strands on your comb or pillow every morning? Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as stress, hormonal imbalance, genetics, and poor nutrition. However, there are natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

Table of Content

  1. How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Tamil
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  4. Frequently Asked Questions

Step by Step Guide

Here are some natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall:

Egg Mask

Egg is a rich source of protein which is essential for hair growth. It also contains biotin and other nutrients that help to strengthen hair strands.

  1. Take one egg and beat it in a bowl.
  2. Add one tablespoon of olive oil and honey to the egg mixture.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair and scalp.
  4. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

Amla or Indian Gooseberry

Amla is a rich source of vitamin C which helps to strengthen hair follicles and prevent hair fall. It also has anti-inflammatory properties that help to soothe the scalp.

  1. Take a few pieces of amla and boil them in coconut oil.
  2. Let the oil cool down and then apply it to your hair and scalp.
  3. Massage your scalp gently for a few minutes.
  4. Leave it on for an hour and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in vitamin C, iron, and potassium which are essential for hair growth. It also contains lecithin which strengthens hair strands and prevents hair fall.

  1. Soak two tablespoons of fenugreek seeds in water overnight.
  2. Grind the soaked seeds into a fine paste.
  3. Apply the paste to your hair and scalp.
  4. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Along with these natural remedies, here are some tips that can help to prevent hair fall:

  • Follow a balanced diet that is rich in protein, iron, and vitamins.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Do not wash your hair with hot water as it can damage hair follicles.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hair dryers and other heat styling tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I use these natural remedies?

You can use these natural remedies once or twice a week depending on your hair type and the severity of your hair fall.

2. Can I use these remedies if I have colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can use these remedies even if you have colored or chemically treated hair. However, it is best to consult with a hair specialist before trying any new hair care routine.

3. How long will it take to see results?

It may take a few weeks or months to see visible results. Consistency is key when it comes to natural remedies.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a simple hair care routine that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo twice a week.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.

Try these natural remedies and tips to prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be consistent and patient as natural remedies take time to show results.
