How Do I Style My Short Thick Hair

Written by Luffy May 11, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have short and thick hair, you might find it challenging to style your hair every day. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can easily transform your hair into stunning hairstyles. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style your short thick hair. We will cover everything from how to curl your hair to how to create an updo. So, let’s get started!

Description

If you have short thick hair, you might think that you are limited in terms of styling options. However, this couldn’t be further from the truth. Short thick hair is incredibly versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you want to create a sleek and sophisticated look or a messy and textured style, there are plenty of hairstyles that you can try out.

How To Style Short Thick Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to styling your short thick hair is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have thick hair, look for a conditioner that is designed to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start styling your hair, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

If you want to create curls, use a curling iron to curl your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and then curl each section one at a time. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the hair. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

If you want to create a sleek and straight look, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and then straighten each section one at a time. Hold the straightening iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the hair. Repeat this process until all of your hair is straightened.

Step 6: Create an Updo

If you want to create an updo, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. You can also create a bun by twisting your hair and then securing it in place with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short thick hair:

  • Use products that are designed for your hair type
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Don’t be afraid to use accessories such as headbands and hair clips to add interest to your hairstyles

FAQs

What products should I use on my short thick hair?

You should use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. You can also use styling products such as mousse and hairspray to help hold your hairstyle in place.

Can I create an updo with short thick hair?

Yes, you can create an updo with short thick hair. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and then use bobby pins to secure it in place. You can also create a bun by twisting your hair and then securing it with bobby pins.

Hair Care

To keep your short thick hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can ensure that your short thick hair looks healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

In conclusion, styling short thick hair is easier than you might think. By following the steps outlined in this blog post, you can create a variety of stunning hairstyles. Remember to use the right products and tools, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair, and you’ll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
