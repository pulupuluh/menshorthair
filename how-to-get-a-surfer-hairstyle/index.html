How To Get A Surfer Hairstyle The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you want to achieve that cool and effortless surfer look, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll show you step by step how to get a surfer hairstyle that will make you look like you just came out of the water.

Description

Surfer hairstyles are all about a natural, tousled look that is easy to maintain. This hairstyle is perfect for those who love the beach and the ocean, and want a hairstyle that reflects their lifestyle.

The surfer hairstyle is characterized by long, layered hair that is styled in a messy, beachy way. The hair is usually worn in a side part and has a lot of texture and volume.

How to Get a Surfer Hairstyle

If you want to get a surfer hairstyle, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

The first step to getting a surfer hairstyle is to grow your hair out. You'll need at least 6-8 inches of hair to achieve this look. If your hair is already long, you can skip this step.

Step 2: Get a Haircut

Once your hair is long enough, you'll need to get a haircut. Ask your hairstylist for long, layered hair with plenty of texture. Make sure to tell them that you want a surfer hairstyle, as they'll know exactly what to do.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now that you have the right haircut, it's time to style your hair. Start by applying a sea salt spray to your damp hair. This will give your hair that beachy texture and volume that is characteristic of a surfer hairstyle.

Next, blow-dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add more texture and definition to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your surfer hairstyle with a light hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place while still allowing it to move naturally.

Tips for Maintaining Your Surfer Hairstyle

Here are some tips that will help you maintain your surfer hairstyle:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look less natural.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

FAQ

What kind of hair is best for a surfer hairstyle?

A surfer hairstyle works best on thick, wavy or curly hair. If you have straight hair, you can still achieve this look with the right haircut and styling products.

How often do I need to wash my hair to maintain a surfer hairstyle?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain a surfer hairstyle. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look dull and lifeless.

Can I still get a surfer hairstyle if my hair is short?

If your hair is too short for a surfer hairstyle, you can still achieve a similar look with a shorter, textured haircut and styling products.

Hair Care for Surfer Hairstyles

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for surfer hairstyles:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look less natural.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Getting a surfer hairstyle is easy if you follow these steps and take care of your hair properly. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a natural, beachy look that will make you feel like a true surfer.
