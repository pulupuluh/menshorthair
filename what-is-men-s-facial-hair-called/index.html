What Is Men s Facial Hair Called A Relaxed Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a man who has ever grown out some scruff, a mustache or a full-on beard, you may have wondered what exactly that hair on your face is called. In this relaxed guide, we'll explore what men's facial hair is called, how to maintain it, and some tips and tricks for keeping it healthy and looking its best.

What is Men's Facial Hair Called?

The hair on a man's face can be called a few different things, depending on the specific type and style. Here are some common terms you might hear:

  • Beard: This refers to the hair that grows on a man's chin, cheeks, and neck, typically longer and fuller than other facial hair.
  • Mustache: This is the hair above a man's upper lip, often styled in various ways.
  • Goatee: A goatee is a small beard that only covers the chin, often accompanied by a mustache.
  • Soul patch: This is a small patch of hair under the lower lip, often worn alone or with a mustache.
  • Sideburns: These are the strips of hair that grow down the sides of the face, often connecting to a beard or hair on the head.

How to Grow Facial Hair

If you're looking to grow out your facial hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Be patient: Growing facial hair can take time, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't come in right away.
  • Take care of your skin: Healthy skin can promote healthy hair growth, so make sure to cleanse and moisturize regularly.
  • Eat a balanced diet: Good nutrition can help promote hair growth, so be sure to eat plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

How to Maintain Facial Hair

Once you've grown out your facial hair, you'll need to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim regularly: Use a beard trimmer or scissors to keep your facial hair at the desired length.
  • Cleanse and moisturize: Just like the skin on your face, your facial hair can benefit from regular cleansing and moisturizing.
  • Comb or brush: Use a comb or brush to keep your facial hair looking neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do men grow facial hair?

Facial hair can serve a variety of purposes, from protecting the skin to signaling social status. Some men simply like the way it looks.

Can all men grow facial hair?

Most men are capable of growing some degree of facial hair, though the amount and thickness can vary.

How do I know what style of facial hair is right for me?

Experiment with different styles and lengths to see what looks best on you. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Facial Hair Care Products

If you're looking to keep your facial hair healthy and looking its best, there are a number of products available:

  • Beard oil: A moisturizing oil that can help keep your beard soft and shiny.
  • Beard balm: A styling product that can help tame flyaways and add volume.
  • Beard comb or brush: A tool for keeping your facial hair neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Facial hair can be a fun and stylish way for men to express themselves. By knowing what it's called, how to grow and maintain it, and what products to use, you can keep your facial hair looking its best. Experiment with different styles and have fun with it!
