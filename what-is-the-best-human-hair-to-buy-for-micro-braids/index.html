Best Human Hair For Micro Braids A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Micro braids are a popular protective hairstyle that requires a lot of patience, time, and effort. One of the most crucial aspects of achieving a flawless micro braid hairstyle is choosing the best human hair. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about what is the best human hair to buy for micro braids.

What is Micro Braiding?

Micro braids are tiny braids that are braided tightly to the scalp. This hairstyle is popular among women with natural hair or those who want to protect their hair from damage. Micro braids can last for up to three months, and they require minimal maintenance.

What is the Best Human Hair for Micro Braids?

The best human hair for micro braids is virgin human hair. Virgin human hair is hair that has never been chemically processed, bleached, or dyed. This hair is soft, durable, and has a natural luster that blends seamlessly with natural hair.

Types of Human Hair for Micro Braids

There are different types of human hair that you can use for micro braids. They include:

  • Brazilian Human Hair
  • Peruvian Human Hair
  • Malaysian Human Hair
  • Indian Human Hair

How to Choose the Best Human Hair for Micro Braids

When choosing the best human hair for micro braids, there are several factors that you need to consider. These factors include:

Quality

The quality of the human hair is essential. You want to choose hair that is soft, tangle-free, and has a natural luster. Low-quality hair will not last long, and it will not look natural.

Length

The length of the hair is also crucial. You want to choose hair that is long enough to achieve your desired hairstyle.

Texture

The texture of the hair is also an important factor. You want to choose hair that has a texture that matches your natural hair.

Color

The color of the hair is also crucial. You want to choose hair that matches your natural hair color.

How to Install Micro Braids with Human Hair

Installing micro braids with human hair requires a lot of patience, time, and effort. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to install micro braids with human hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before installing micro braids, you need to wash and condition your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and tangle-free.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair Extensions

Divide your hair extensions into small sections that are easy to braid. Make sure that you have enough hair extensions to achieve your desired hairstyle.

Step 3: Create Small Partings

Create small partings in your hair using a rat-tail comb. The partings should be small enough to create tiny braids.

Step 4: Braid Your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and add a hair extension to it. Braid the hair extension and your natural hair tightly to the scalp. Repeat this process until you have braided all of your hair.

Step 5: Seal the Ends

Seal the ends of your micro braids with hot water. This will prevent the braids from unraveling.

Tips for Maintaining Micro Braids

Maintaining micro braids requires a lot of care and attention. Here are some tips to help you maintain your micro braids:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a mild shampoo
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products that can weigh down your hair
  • Sleep with a satin bonnet or pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your micro braids

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long do micro braids last?

A: Micro braids can last for up to three months with proper maintenance.

Q: Can I swim with micro braids?

A: Yes, you can swim with micro braids. However, you need to protect your hair by wearing a swim cap or wrapping your hair in a towel.

Q: How do I remove micro braids?

A: To remove micro braids, you need to carefully cut the ends of the braids and unravel them from your hair.

Hair Care for Micro Braids

Taking care of your hair while it is in micro braids is essential. You need to keep your hair clean, moisturized, and protected. Here are some hair care tips for micro braids:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a mild shampoo
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products that can weigh down your hair
  • Sleep with a satin bonnet or pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your micro braids

In conclusion, the best human hair for micro braids is virgin human hair. When choosing human hair for micro braids, you need to consider factors such as quality, length, texture, and color. Installing micro braids with human hair requires patience, time, and effort. Maintaining micro braids requires a lot of care and attention. By following the tips and hair care techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve a flawless micro braid hairstyle that will last for up to three months.
