How To Know If You Would Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a bold move that can transform your appearance, but it can also be a bit scary to take the plunge. If you're considering chopping off your locks, you're probably wondering whether you would look good with short hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of figuring out whether short hair is the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair is a versatile look that can suit a variety of face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. If you're considering making the switch to short hair, it's important to take a few factors into account to ensure that the new style will flatter your features and complement your overall look.

How to

The first step in figuring out whether short hair is right for you is to examine your face shape. Some face shapes, such as oval and heart-shaped faces, tend to look good with shorter haircuts. On the other hand, if you have a round or square face shape, you may want to stick with longer locks to balance out your features.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick, coarse hair, a shorter haircut can help to tame your locks and make them more manageable. On the other hand, if you have fine, thin hair, a shorter haircut may make your hair look even thinner and more limp.

It's also important to think about your personal style and how a shorter haircut would fit into your wardrobe and overall aesthetic. If you're someone who loves to experiment with different looks and isn't afraid to take risks, a shorter haircut may be just the change you need. If you prefer a more classic or conservative look, you may want to stick with longer hair that you can style in a variety of ways.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to figure out if you would look good with short hair:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type.
  3. Think about your personal style.
  4. Look for inspiration on social media or in magazines.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when deciding whether short hair is right for you:

  • Be open to trying new things. You never know how a shorter haircut will look on you until you try it!
  • Look for examples of short haircuts on people with similar face shapes and hair types to yours.
  • Consider getting a wig or trying on a shorter hairstyle virtually before committing to the cut.
  • Remember that hair grows back! If you don't love your new short haircut, you can always let it grow out.

FAQ

What face shapes look good with short hair?

Oval and heart-shaped faces tend to look good with shorter haircuts.

What hair types are best suited for short hair?

Thick, coarse hair is well-suited for short haircuts, while fine, thin hair may look better with longer locks.

How can I determine my face shape?

There are several ways to determine your face shape, including measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and examining the proportions of your face in a mirror.

Hair Care

If you do decide to go for a shorter haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it in shape and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality styling products, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips and taking the time to consider whether short hair is right for you, you can make an informed decision about your new hairstyle and feel confident and stylish with your new look.
