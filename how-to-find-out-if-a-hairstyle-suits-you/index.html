How To Find Out If A Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Robby May 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn't suit your face shape or style? It can be frustrating to spend time and money on a new hairstyle, only to feel disappointed with the results. Luckily, there are a few ways to determine if a hairstyle will suit you before you take the plunge. In this article, we'll explore how to find out if a hairstyle suits you, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Consider Your Hair Type

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a haircut that enhances your best features and balances out any asymmetry. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart-shaped, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. There are also online tools and quizzes that can help you determine your face shape if you're unsure.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Is your hair straight, curly, wavy, or somewhere in between? Do you have thick, thin, or medium hair? These factors will affect how your hair falls and how much maintenance is required to keep it looking its best.

For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid short, blunt hairstyles that require a lot of styling to keep them looking neat. Instead, you may want to opt for a longer, layered cut that enhances your natural texture.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Do you have a low-maintenance routine or do you enjoy spending time styling your hair? Do you work in a professional setting that requires a more conservative hairstyle?

If you have a busy schedule or prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle, you may want to opt for a wash-and-go cut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and have more time to dedicate to your hair, you may want to choose a more intricate cut that allows for more styling options.

Assess Haircut Suitability

Once you've determined your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, it's time to assess the suitability of different haircuts. There are a few key factors to consider when choosing a haircut:

  • Length: Do you want a short, medium, or long haircut?
  • Layers: Do you want a layered or blunt cut?
  • Bangs: Do you want bangs or a fringe?
  • Texture: Do you want a textured or smooth cut?

It's important to keep in mind that not all haircuts will suit everyone. A certain haircut may look great on a celebrity or friend, but it may not be the best choice for your face shape or hair type. It's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist before making a final decision.

Tips for Choosing a Flattering Hairstyle

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a flattering hairstyle:

  • Consider your natural features: Choose a haircut that enhances your best features, such as your eyes or cheekbones.
  • Avoid extremes: Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can be difficult to style and may not suit your face shape.
  • Take your time: Don't rush into a new haircut. Take your time to research different styles and consult with a hairstylist.
  • Be open to change: Don't be afraid to try something new. A fresh haircut can be a great way to switch up your look and boost your confidence.

FAQs

What haircut suits my face shape?

The best haircut for your face shape depends on your individual features. Generally, a haircut that balances out your face shape and enhances your best features is the most flattering choice.

How can I tell if a haircut will suit me?

You can tell if a haircut will suit you by considering your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and haircut suitability. Consulting with a hairstylist can also help you determine the best haircut for you.

How often should I get a haircut?

The frequency of your haircuts depends on your hair type and personal preference. On average, most people get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain their style and keep their ends healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant when necessary.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to find out if a hairstyle suits you, it's time to start experimenting with different cuts and styles. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure. With these tips, you'll be well on your way to finding a flattering hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
