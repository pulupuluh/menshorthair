How To Cut Layers In Hair Youtube Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 29, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, then layers are the way to go. Cutting layers in your hair can give it a fresh and stylish look. However, going to a salon can be expensive, so why not try cutting your hair at home? Thanks to YouTube, you can find a vast array of tutorials that can guide you every step of the way. In this article, we'll show you how to cut layers in hair YouTube style - easy, convenient, and affordable.

Description

Layering is a haircutting technique that involves cutting different lengths of hair to create volume, texture, and movement. Layers can be added to any hair length or texture, and can also help to soften sharp features or angles on your face.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, there are a few things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Once you've gathered all the necessary tools, you can start cutting your hair by following these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry naturally or using a hairdryer. It's essential to have a clean and dry base to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair from the bottom and hold it between your fingers. Using your scissors, cut the hair at a slight angle. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair in the back section.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Once you've finished cutting the back section, move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from one side and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, using the hair you've already cut in the back section as a guide. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, using the hair you've already cut as a guide. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all the hair in the top section.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect layered haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair, and cut less than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more later.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while you're cutting.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect look for you.
  • Use hair clips to hold the hair you're not cutting out of the way.

FAQ

Can I cut layers in my hair if it's curly?

Absolutely! Layering can actually enhance the natural curl in your hair, giving it more volume and bounce.

Can I cut layers in my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can. Layering can work on any hair length, although the technique may vary depending on the length of your hair.

Do I need to use professional scissors?

While professional scissors can give you a cleaner cut, you can still achieve great results with regular household scissors as long as they're sharp.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every few weeks to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Cutting layers in your hair can be an easy and affordable way to change up your look. With the help of YouTube tutorials, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, start with small sections of hair, and have fun experimenting with different lengths and angles. Happy cutting!
