How To Wear Curly Hair To Bed When Wet Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of waking up with frizzy, tangled curls? Do you struggle to maintain your curls overnight? If so, you're not alone. Many people with curly hair struggle with keeping their curls intact and looking good while they sleep. Fortunately, there are some simple tips and tricks you can use to wear your curly hair to bed when wet. In this article, we'll share some of our favorite techniques for keeping your curls looking fabulous overnight.

How to Wear Curly Hair to Bed When Wet

Wearing curly hair to bed when wet can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. Here are some simple steps to follow:

Step 1: Apply Your Styling Products

Before you go to bed, make sure your hair is properly moisturized and styled. Apply your favorite styling products to damp hair, making sure to distribute them evenly throughout your curls.

Step 2: Choose Your Sleeping Position

The way you sleep can have a big impact on your curls. Avoid sleeping on your stomach or with your head down, as this can cause your curls to flatten and lose their shape. Instead, try sleeping on your back or on your side.

Step 3: Use a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Regular pillowcases can cause friction and damage to your curls, especially when your hair is wet. To prevent this, use a silk or satin pillowcase instead. These materials are much gentler on your hair and will help to maintain your curls overnight.

Step 4: Keep Your Hair Loose

Avoid tying your hair up or putting it in a tight bun or ponytail when you go to bed. This can cause your curls to become flattened and lose their shape. Instead, keep your hair loose and free.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for wearing curly hair to bed when wet:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Apply Your Styling Products

While your hair is still damp, apply your favorite styling products. This might include curl cream, gel, or mousse. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Microfiber Towel to Dry Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel to gently dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 4: Choose Your Sleeping Position

Decide on a comfortable sleeping position that won't disturb your curls. This might mean sleeping on your back or on your side.

Step 5: Use a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Switch to a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and damage to your curls.

Step 6: Keep Your Hair Loose

Avoid tying your hair up or putting it in a tight bun or ponytail. Instead, let your hair hang loose and free.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair Overnight

Here are some additional tips for keeping your curly hair looking great while you sleep:

  • Use a silk or satin hair bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep
  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases, as these can cause friction and damage to your hair
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while you sleep, as this can cause frizz and damage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair before bed, as this can cause damage and dryness

FAQs

Q: Should I brush my hair before bed?

A: It's generally not a good idea to brush curly hair when it's wet, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

Wearing curly hair to bed when wet can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, it's definitely possible. Follow the tips in this article to keep your curls looking fabulous overnight, and don't forget to take good care of your hair with regular washing, conditioning, and styling.
