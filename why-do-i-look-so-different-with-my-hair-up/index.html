Why Do I Look So Different With My Hair Up

Written by Oliver Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Have you ever wondered why you look so different with your hair up? It's a common question that many people ask themselves, and the answer lies in the way our hair frames our face. When we wear our hair down, it can create a certain look or style that we may be used to seeing. However, when we put our hair up, it can completely change the way we look and feel. In this blog post, we'll explore why this happens and provide tips on how to make the most of your hair up look.

Description

It's no secret that our hair can completely transform our appearance. Whether we have short or long hair, curly or straight, the way we wear our hair can make a huge difference in how we look and feel. When we wear our hair up, we expose more of our face and neck, which can create a different look entirely. This can be both exciting and daunting, especially if you're used to wearing your hair down most of the time.

How to

If you're looking to experiment with wearing your hair up more often, there are a few things you can do to make the most of your new look. First, start by experimenting with different hairstyles. Try a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo to see what works best for you. Second, consider playing around with your makeup to complement your new hairstyle. A bold lip or a smoky eye can help draw attention to your face and create a cohesive look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to create a simple yet chic updo:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Loosen a few strands of hair around your face to create a softer look.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to play with your makeup to complement your new look.
  • Accessorize your hair with barrettes, headbands, or scarves to add some extra flair.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Invest in some high-quality hair ties and bobby pins to ensure your updo stays in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair up:

Q: Why do I look so different with my hair up?

A: When you wear your hair up, you expose more of your face and neck, which can create a different look entirely.

Q: What are some easy updo hairstyles I can try?

A: Some easy updo hairstyles include a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo.

Q: How do I keep my updo in place?

A: Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair, and invest in some high-quality hair ties to ensure your updo stays in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're wearing it up more often. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can create a beautiful and unique look with your hair up. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
