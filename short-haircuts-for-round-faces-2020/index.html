Short Haircuts For Round Faces 2020 Perfect Styles For Your Face Shape

Written by William Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, it can be tough to find a haircut that flatters your features. However, there are plenty of short haircuts for round faces 2020 that can help to elongate your face and make your features stand out. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short haircuts for round faces 2020, and provide tips on how to style and care for your new look.

Description

A round face is defined by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that helps to elongate your features and create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Short haircuts are a great option for round faces, as they can help to highlight your cheekbones and draw attention away from your chin.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Round Face

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, you'll want to choose a style that complements your face shape. This means opting for haircuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head, and avoiding styles that add too much width to your face.

Second, consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have fine hair, you'll want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a haircut that thins out your hair and creates a more streamlined look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Now that you know what to look for in a short haircut for a round face, let's take a look at some of the best styles for 2020:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can work well for round faces. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, and leave longer layers on top. You can style your hair with a side part, or sweep it back for a more dramatic look.

2. Bob Cut

The bob is another classic short haircut that can flatter round faces. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin length, with layers that frame your face. You can style your hair with a side part, or add volume to the crown of your head for a more dramatic look.

3. Shag Cut

The shag is a trendy short haircut that works well for round faces. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut layers into your hair, starting at chin level. You can style your hair with a center part, or add volume to the crown of your head for a more dramatic look.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Once you've chosen the right short haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the crown of your head
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs, which can make your face look rounder
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that flatters your face shape

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, there are plenty of short haircuts that can flatter a round face. Just be sure to choose a style that adds height and volume to your hair, and avoid styles that add too much width to your face.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Avoid blunt, heavy bangs and styles that add too much width to your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create height and elongate your features.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It's best to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, which can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

With the right short haircut and styling tips, you can enhance your features and create a look that flatters your round face. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, or shag, these short haircuts for round faces 2020 are sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
