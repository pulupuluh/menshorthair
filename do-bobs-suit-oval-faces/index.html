Do Bobs Suit Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide To Finding Your Perfect Bob

Written by Bardi Mar 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Bobs are a great hairstyle choice for those with oval faces. But, finding the right bob for your face shape can be a challenge. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about bobs and how to find the perfect one for your oval face.

Why Do Bobs Suit Oval Faces?

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape. This means that almost any hairstyle can suit an oval face. However, bobs are particularly flattering for those with oval faces. The reason for this is that the bob frames the face, highlighting your best features.

How to Choose the Right Bob for Your Oval Face

When choosing a bob for your oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Length

The length of your bob will depend on a few factors. If you have a long neck and want to show it off, you can go for a shorter bob. If you have a shorter neck, a longer bob may be a better choice.

Texture

If you have fine hair, a blunt bob may not be the best choice as it can make your hair look too thin. Instead, opt for a textured bob that will add some volume and movement to your hair.

Face Shape

While oval faces can suit almost any bob, it's important to consider your specific face shape. If you have a longer face, a shorter bob may be a better choice. If you have a wider face, a longer bob may be more flattering.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Bob

Ready to get a bob? Follow these steps:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, find some inspiration for your bob. Look for pictures of bobs that you like and bring them with you to your appointment.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the best bob for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you've decided on the perfect bob, your stylist will cut and style your hair. They may also recommend products to help you maintain your new style at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob

Now that you have a bob, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Regular Trims

To keep your bob looking fresh, it's important to get regular trims. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style of your bob. For example, if you have a textured bob, use a texturizing spray to enhance the movement in your hair.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.

FAQs

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and a face that is longer than it is wide.

What is the most flattering bob for an oval face?

The most flattering bob for an oval face will depend on your specific face shape and hair type. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect bob for you.

Can bobs be styled in different ways?

Yes, bobs can be styled in many different ways. You can wear your bob straight, curly, or with waves.

Hair Care for Bobs

When it comes to hair care for bobs, it's important to use products that will help maintain your style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style of your bob. If you have a textured bob, use a texturizing spray to enhance the movement in your hair. And always protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.

With this guide, you now know that bobs do suit oval faces and how to find the perfect bob for your face shape and hair type. Follow our tips for maintaining your bob and you'll have a stylish and flattering hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.
