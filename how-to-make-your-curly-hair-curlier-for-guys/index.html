How To Make Your Curly Hair Curlier For Guys

Written by Robby Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. While it can give you a unique look, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking to enhance your natural curls and make them curlier, this guide will help you achieve the desired result. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and steps to follow to make your curly hair curlier.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Make Your Curly Hair Curlier for Guys
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips to Follow
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair is unique and can vary in texture, thickness, and curl pattern. However, with the right techniques and products, you can enhance your natural curls and make them curlier. This guide is designed to help guys with curly hair achieve the desired result.

How to Make Your Curly Hair Curlier for Guys

The following steps will help you make your curly hair curlier:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness, which can lead to frizz. Wash your hair every 2-3 days and condition it every time you shampoo.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancer

After washing your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to damp hair. This will help to define your curls and add volume. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. This will help to create more defined, bouncy curls.

Step 4: Air Dry or Diffuse Your Hair

Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out any excess water with a t-shirt or microfiber towel. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Tips to Follow

Here are some additional tips to help you make your curly hair curlier:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and maintain healthy hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my straight hair curly?

A: While it is possible to create curls with styling tools, such as a curling iron or perm, it is not possible to permanently change the texture of your hair. If you have naturally straight hair, you may need to consider using a perm or other styling tools to create curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my curly hair?

A: It is recommended to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to the steps and tips mentioned above, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine for your curly hair. This includes:

  • Using a deep conditioner once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoiding heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Using a hair mask once a month to nourish your hair and enhance your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair curlier and enhance your natural curls. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, as it may take some time to see the desired results.
