Mens Short Sides Long Top Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, then the mens short sides long top haircut might be just what you need. This haircut is a popular choice among men of all ages, and it can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. In this article, we'll take a closer look at how to get the mens short sides long top haircut and provide some tips for styling and maintaining it.

Description

The mens short sides long top haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it's typically at least a few inches long. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut.

How to Get the Mens Short Sides Long Top Haircut

If you're interested in getting the mens short sides long top haircut, then the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist. Make sure that the person you choose has experience with this type of haircut and can give you the look that you want.

When you're at the salon, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style that you want. You can bring in pictures or examples of the haircut that you like to make sure that you're both on the same page.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens short sides long top haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Next, use clippers to trim the hair on the sides of your head to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a #1 to a #4 guard, depending on how short you want the hair to be.
  3. Once the sides are trimmed, use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a few inches to several inches, depending on how long you want the hair to be.
  4. Finally, blend the hair on the sides and the top using scissors or clippers, depending on the look that you want.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining the Mens Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Once you have the mens short sides long top haircut, you'll need to know how to style and maintain it. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Use a pomade or wax to style the hair on top. This will help to keep it in place and add texture and volume.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep the haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type to keep it healthy and nourished.

FAQ

Q: Is the mens short sides long top haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the mens short sides long top haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and style to enhance your features.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim with this haircut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how tidy you want the haircut to look. Most men will need a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the haircut looking fresh.

Q: Can I style the hair on top differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, the mens short sides long top haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find a look that suits you.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is key to making the mens short sides long top haircut look great. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair growth and health.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, as this can also help to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get the mens short sides long top haircut and style it to perfection. Whether you want a classic, slicked-back look or a modern, textured style, this haircut is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
