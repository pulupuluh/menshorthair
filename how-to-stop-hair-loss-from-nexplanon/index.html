How To Stop Hair Loss From Nexplanon

Written by Oliver Mar 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're experiencing hair loss while using Nexplanon, don't worry. You're not alone. Many women have reported hair loss as a side effect of this birth control implant. In this article, we'll be discussing how to stop hair loss from Nexplanon.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Nexplanon is a small, thin implant that is inserted under the skin in your upper arm. It contains the hormone progestin, which prevents pregnancy by thickening cervical mucus and thinning the lining of the uterus. While Nexplanon is a highly effective form of birth control, it can also have some side effects, including hair loss.

Many women have reported hair loss while using Nexplanon. The exact cause of this is unknown, but it is believed to be related to the hormonal changes that occur in the body as a result of using the implant.

How to

If you're experiencing hair loss from Nexplanon, there are a few things you can do to try and stop it:

1. Talk to your doctor

The first thing you should do if you're experiencing hair loss from Nexplanon is to talk to your doctor. They may be able to recommend a different form of birth control that won't cause hair loss, or they may be able to prescribe medications or supplements that can help.

2. Take biotin supplements

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth. Taking biotin supplements may help to prevent hair loss and promote new hair growth. You can find biotin supplements at most health food stores.

3. Use hair growth products

There are many hair growth products on the market that can help to promote new hair growth and prevent hair loss. Look for products that contain ingredients like minoxidil, which is a topical medication that is FDA-approved for hair loss treatment.

Step by Step

If you're experiencing hair loss from Nexplanon, here are the steps you can take to try and stop it:

Step 1: Talk to your doctor

Schedule an appointment with your doctor to discuss your hair loss and see if they can recommend any solutions.

Step 2: Take biotin supplements

Start taking biotin supplements daily to promote healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Use hair growth products

Incorporate hair growth products into your daily routine to help promote new hair growth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair loss from Nexplanon:

  • Be gentle with your hair. Avoid pulling or tugging on it, and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle it.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth, such as vitamin D, iron, and zinc.
  • Manage your stress levels, as stress can contribute to hair loss.

FAQ

How long does hair loss from Nexplanon last?

The length of time that hair loss from Nexplanon lasts can vary from person to person. In some cases, it may resolve on its own after a few months. In other cases, it may persist for the duration of time that the implant is in place.

Is hair loss from Nexplanon permanent?

No, hair loss from Nexplanon is not permanent. In most cases, hair will grow back once the implant is removed.

Hair Care

In addition to taking the steps outlined above, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent further damage and promote healthy growth. Here are some tips for healthy hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails, as these can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and taking the steps outlined above, you can help to prevent hair loss from Nexplanon and promote healthy hair growth.
