Easy To Manage Short Hairstyles For Curly Hair

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, especially when it comes to styling. But with the right haircut, you can have a low-maintenance look that still looks great. Short haircuts for curly hair are a popular choice for many women because they require less time and effort to style. In this article, we will discuss some easy to manage short hairstyles for curly hair that you can try out.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles for curly hair are great for those who want to cut down on styling time. A good short haircut can enhance your natural curls and make them easier to manage. The best part is that there are many different styles to choose from, so you can find one that suits your face shape and personal style.

How to

To achieve easy to manage short hairstyles for curly hair, you will need to get a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. A good stylist will be able to recommend a style that will work for you. When choosing a style, keep in mind that the shorter the hair, the less time it will take to style.

Step by Step

Here are some easy to manage short hairstyles for curly hair:

1. The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that works well for curly hair. It is short and easy to manage, and it enhances your natural curls. To style, all you need to do is apply some curl-enhancing gel or mousse and let your hair air dry.

2. The Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for curly hair. It is short enough to be low-maintenance, but long enough to give you some styling options. You can leave it natural or blow-dry it straight for a sleek look.

3. The Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered style that works well for curly hair. It adds texture and volume to your hair, which makes it easy to style. To style, simply apply some curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair with your hands.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for curly hair:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Keep it short to make it easy to manage.
  • Use curl-enhancing products to enhance your natural curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair if I have a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair if you have a short haircut. However, keep in mind that heat styling can damage your hair, so it's best to use a heat protectant and limit the amount of heat you apply to your hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my short curly hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style.

With these easy to manage short hairstyles for curly hair, you can have a low-maintenance look that still looks great. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob cut, or shag cut, you can enhance your natural curls and make them easier to manage. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking great.
