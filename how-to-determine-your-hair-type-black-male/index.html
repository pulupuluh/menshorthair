How To Determine Your Hair Type Black Male

Written by Eveline Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a black male, determining your hair type is essential in taking care of your hair. Understanding your hair type will help you choose the right products and methods to maintain and style your hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine your hair type and provide tips on how to take care of your hair.

Table of Content

  • Step by Step Process
  • Tips on Taking Care of Your Hair

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Black male hair types can range from straight to curly, and the texture can vary from fine to coarse. To determine your hair type, you need to examine your hair's curl pattern, density, and texture.

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. After washing, let your hair dry naturally without using any products.

Curl Pattern

The curl pattern of your hair will help you identify whether your hair is straight, wavy, curly, or kinky. Here's how you can identify each curl pattern:

  • Straight hair has no curl pattern and is completely straight.
  • Wavy hair has a slight curl pattern and tends to form an S-shaped wave.
  • Curly hair has a defined curl pattern and forms a spiral shape.
  • Kinky hair has a tight curl pattern and forms a zigzag shape.

Once you have identified your curl pattern, move on to the next step.

Density

The density of your hair refers to the amount of hair on your head. To determine your hair's density, you need to examine how much hair you have on your scalp.

If you can see your scalp easily, you have low-density hair. If you can see some scalp but not all of it, you have medium-density hair. If you cannot see your scalp at all, you have high-density hair.

Texture

The texture of your hair refers to how thick or thin each strand of your hair is. To determine your hair's texture, you need to examine each strand of your hair.

If your hair feels fine and fragile, you have fine hair texture. If your hair feels thick and sturdy, you have a coarse hair texture. If your hair falls in between, you have medium hair texture.

Step by Step Process

Here's a step-by-step process to determine your hair type:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo.
  2. Let your hair dry naturally without using any products.
  3. Identify your hair's curl pattern by examining its shape.
  4. Determine your hair's density by examining how much hair you have on your scalp.
  5. Determine your hair's texture by examining each strand of your hair.

Tips on Taking Care of Your Hair

Now that you have determined your hair type, here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Use products that are specific to your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product as this can weigh your hair down.

FAQs

Can I have more than one hair type?

Yes, it is common to have a combination of hair types. For example, you may have wavy hair with a coarse texture.

Can my hair type change over time?

Yes, your hair type can change due to various factors such as aging, hormonal changes, and lifestyle changes.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a simple hair care routine for black males:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo once a week.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle it.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain healthy and stylish hair. Remember to always choose products and methods that work best for your hair type.
