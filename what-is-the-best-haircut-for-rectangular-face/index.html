What Is The Best Haircut For Rectangular Face

Written by Oliver Aug 30, 2023 Â· 10 min read

In this article, we will be discussing what is the best haircut for rectangular face. A rectangular face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a straight hairline. If you have a rectangular face shape, you might find it challenging to choose a haircut that complements your features. However, with the right styling and haircut, you can enhance your facial features and look stunning. Hereâs everything you need to know about the best haircut for rectangular face shape.

Description

A rectangular face shape is often referred to as an oblong face shape. This face shape is longer than it is wide, with a square or angular jawline. People with rectangular face shapes often have a prominent forehead, a straight hairline, and a sharp chin. Choosing the right haircut for this face shape can help balance out the features and create a more flattering look.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Rectangular Face?

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for rectangular face shape, you need to look for styles that soften the features and add volume to the sides of your face. Here are some tips on how to choose the best haircut for rectangular face shape:

1. Look for Soft Layers

Soft layers can help soften the angles of your face and add volume to the sides of your face. Layers can also help create movement and texture, making your hair look thicker and fuller. You can opt for long layers, choppy layers, or feathered layers, depending on your hair type and texture.

2. Try a Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out your forehead and create a more flattering look. You can opt for long side-swept bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair or short choppy bangs that add texture and dimension to your hair.

3. Avoid Straight Haircuts

Straight haircuts can make your face look even longer and accentuate your sharp features. Instead, opt for haircuts with waves, curls, or layers that add volume and movement to your hair. You can also try asymmetrical haircuts that create an illusion of width.

4. Consider Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can help balance out your face and create a more proportional look. You can opt for a pixie cut, a bob haircut, or a lob haircut, depending on your hair texture and face shape. Short haircuts can also be low-maintenance and easy to style.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Best Haircut for Rectangular Face

Hereâs a step-by-step guide to the best haircut for rectangular face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a square or angular jawline, you have a rectangular face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

Next, choose the right haircut that complements your face shape. Soft layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical haircuts are all great options for rectangular face shape.

Step 3: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and texture. Your hairstylist can also give you tips on how to style your hair at home and recommend products that work best for your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Haircut for Rectangular Face

Here are some tips for maintaining the best haircut for rectangular face shape:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This can help keep your hair looking healthy, shiny, and voluminous.

2. Schedule Regular Haircuts

Schedule regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your haircut. This can also prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair looking healthy and full.

3. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Experiment with different hairstyles to find the best look for your face shape. You can try different updos, braids, and ponytails to switch up your look and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for rectangular face with curly hair?

A: The best haircut for rectangular face with curly hair is a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. You can also opt for long side-swept bangs that blend seamlessly with your curls.

Q: Can I wear a center part with a rectangular face?

A: Yes, you can wear a center part with a rectangular face. However, a side part can help balance out your forehead and create a more flattering look.

Q: Can I wear a bob haircut with a rectangular face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob haircut with a rectangular face. A long bob or a lob that falls just below your shoulders can help balance out your features and create a more proportional look.

Hair Care for Rectangular Face Shape

Caring for your hair is essential to maintaining a healthy and vibrant look. Here are some hair care tips for rectangular face shape:

1. Shampoo and Condition Your Hair Regularly

Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant sprays and avoiding excessive heat styling. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to prevent frizz and breakage.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Foods such as salmon, spinach, and avocados are excellent for promoting hair health.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for rectangular face shape can seem daunting, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect haircut that complements your features. Remember to choose soft layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical haircuts that add volume and texture to your hair. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and texture. With these tips and tricks, you can create a stunning look that enhances your facial features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
