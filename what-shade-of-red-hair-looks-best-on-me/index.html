What Shade Of Red Hair Looks Best On Me

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering dying your hair red but not sure which shade would look best on you? Look no further! In this post, we will discuss the different shades of red hair and provide tips on how to choose the best one for your skin tone and features.

Description

Red hair is a bold and vibrant choice that can make you stand out in a crowd. There are many shades of red hair to choose from, ranging from bright and fiery to subtle and muted. Some popular shades include:

  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Burgundy
  • Cherry
  • Strawberry blonde

Choosing the right shade of red hair can enhance your natural beauty and complement your features. It's important to consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal style when selecting a shade.

How to Choose the Right Shade of Red Hair

When choosing a shade of red hair, it's important to consider your skin tone. Cooler skin tones tend to look best with cooler reds, such as burgundy or cherry, while warmer skin tones can rock warmer reds, such as copper or auburn.

You should also consider your eye color. Green or hazel eyes can be complemented by a copper or strawberry blonde shade, while blue eyes look stunning with a deeper, richer red like burgundy.

Finally, think about your personal style. If you prefer a more natural look, a subtle auburn or strawberry blonde shade may be the best fit. If you want to make a bold statement, a bright cherry or fiery copper shade may be more your style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dying Your Hair Red

If you've decided on a shade of red hair, here's a step-by-step guide to dying your hair at home:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye in your desired shade of red.
  2. Read the instructions carefully before starting. Some dyes may require pre-lightening or a strand test.
  3. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it thoroughly.
  4. Apply petroleum jelly or conditioner around your hairline and ears to prevent staining.
  5. Wear gloves and mix the dye according to the instructions.
  6. Apply the dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  7. Leave the dye on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  8. Condition your hair and style as desired.

Remember to always follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Tips for Maintaining Red Hair

Red hair requires special care to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your red locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as heat can fade the color.
  • Use a color-protecting hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can also fade the color.

FAQs

Can anyone pull off red hair?

Yes, anyone can pull off red hair! It's all about finding the right shade to complement your skin tone and features.

Will red hair look good with my natural hair color?

Red hair can look good with any natural hair color, but it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and features.

How often will I need to touch up my red hair?

This depends on the shade of red and how quickly your hair grows. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair red at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair red at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first.

Hair Care Products for Red Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for maintaining vibrant red hair:

  • Color Wow Color Security Shampoo
  • Redken Color Extend Magnetics Conditioner
  • Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector
  • Joico Color Infuse Red Shampoo
  • Matrix Biolage ColorLast Deep Treatment Pack

With the right shade of red hair and proper maintenance, you can rock this bold and vibrant look with confidence!
