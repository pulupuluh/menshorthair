How To Bring Curly Wig Back To Life A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a curly wig that has lost its bounce and shine? Don't worry, you can easily bring it back to life with a few simple steps. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to revive your curly wig.

How to Bring Curly Wig Back to Life

Before we start, let's list down the things you will need to revive your curly wig:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Wig detangler or leave-in conditioner
  • Water spray bottle
  • Wig stand or mannequin head
  • Hot water (not boiling)
  • Microfiber towel or t-shirt

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

The first step is to detangle the wig using a wide-tooth comb. This will help to remove any knots or tangles that may have formed over time. Be gentle while combing the wig to avoid damaging the curls.

Step 2: Apply Wig Detangler or Leave-in Conditioner

After detangling, apply a wig detangler or leave-in conditioner to the wig. This will help to soften the curls and make them more manageable. Spray the detangler or conditioner evenly throughout the wig, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Scrunch the Curls

Scrunch the curls using your fingers to encourage them to reform. Do this gently to avoid damaging the wig.

Step 4: Wet the Wig

Use a water spray bottle to wet the wig evenly. This will help to reactivate the curls. Be careful not to oversaturate the wig as it may take longer to dry.

Step 5: Set the Wig

Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head and let it air dry. If you want to speed up the drying process, you can use a hairdryer on a low, cool setting. Be sure to keep the hairdryer at least 6 inches away from the wig to avoid damaging the fibers.

Step 6: Dip the Wig in Hot Water

If your wig is still not as curly as you would like it to be, you can dip it in hot water (not boiling). This will help to set the curls. Dip the wig in the hot water for a few seconds, then remove it and gently scrunch the curls with your fingers.

Step 7: Dry the Wig

After dipping the wig in hot water, dry it using a microfiber towel or t-shirt. Avoid using a regular towel as it can cause frizz.

Tips

  • Always use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig as they can damage the fibers.
  • Do not use regular shampoo and conditioner on your wig as they can strip the fibers of their natural oils.
  • Store your wig on a mannequin head or wig stand to help maintain its shape.

FAQ

How often should I wash my wig?

It is recommended to wash your wig every 6-8 wears or when it starts to look dull or dirty.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, regular shampoo and conditioner can strip the fibers of their natural oils. Use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner instead.

Can I use hot tools on my wig?

No, hot tools can damage the fibers of the wig. Avoid using them altogether.

Hair Care

To keep your wig looking its best, it is important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Store the wig on a mannequin head or wig stand when not in use.
  • Avoid exposing the wig to direct sunlight for prolonged periods.
  • Use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner to wash the wig.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the wig.

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this article, you can easily bring your curly wig back to life. With proper care, your wig can look as good as new for years to come.
