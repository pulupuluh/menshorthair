How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Long Length

If you are looking to give a long haircut to a man, using scissors is the way to go. In this guide, we will cover step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's hair with scissors long length.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Long Length

Before we begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle

Now, let's get started!

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Wash and dry the hair thoroughly. Comb the hair to ensure there are no tangles or knots. Section the hair into smaller parts and clip them out of the way.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Starting at the front of the head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight out from the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the desired length with the scissors. Continue this process for the rest of the hair.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

After cutting all the hair to the desired length, blend the hair by combing it out and cutting any uneven strands.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Check the hair for evenness by combing it out and looking for any areas that may need to be trimmed further.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Style the hair using a blow dryer or other tools as desired.

Tips

  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure evenness.
  • Use the comb to guide your scissors and ensure a straight cut.
  • If you are unsure about cutting the hair to the desired length, start by cutting a little bit at a time and work your way up.

FAQ

Can I cut men's hair with regular scissors?

While it is possible to cut men's hair with regular scissors, it is recommended to use hair-cutting scissors for a more precise cut.

How often should I cut men's long hair?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length.

Hair Care

To keep men's long hair healthy, it is important to wash and condition regularly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Avoid using hot tools too often and use a heat protectant when necessary. Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and steps, you can successfully cut men's hair with scissors long length. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and always focus on achieving an even cut.
