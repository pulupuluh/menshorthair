What Hair Colour Suits Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by William Aug 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with the same width and height. It is often associated with youthfulness and softness. However, finding the right hair colour to complement a round face can be a bit of a challenge. The good news is that with the right hair colour, you can create the illusion of a more elongated face. In this article, we will discuss what hair colour suits round face and provide tips and ideas to help you choose the perfect shade for your face shape.

What Hair Colour Suits Round Face?

The answer is not straightforward. It largely depends on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preference. However, there are some general guidelines you can follow to find the perfect hair colour for a round face:

  • Choose hair colours that create an elongating effect
  • Avoid hair colours that make your face look wider
  • Consider warm hair colours that add depth and dimension to your face
  • Stay away from hair colours that are too light or too dark for your skin tone

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour?

Choosing the right hair colour for a round face can be overwhelming. Here are some steps to help you narrow down your options:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Knowing your skin tone is crucial in finding the right hair colour. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear blue-green, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also help you choose the right hair colour. Warm hair colours like golden blonde, copper, and auburn complement brown and hazel eyes, while cool hair colours like platinum blonde, ash brown, and black complement blue and green eyes.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you have determined your skin tone and eye colour, it's time to look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, or consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect hair colour for your round face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Opt for highlights or balayage to create depth and dimension
  • Avoid hair colours that are too light or too dark for your skin tone
  • Consider warm hair colours like honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut
  • Avoid hair colours that make your face look wider, like all-over platinum blonde

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Once you have found the perfect hair colour for your round face, it's essential to take care of your coloured hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair colour
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair colour suits round face:

What hair colour makes a round face look slimmer?

Warm hair colours like honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut can create an elongating effect and make a round face look slimmer.

Can I dye my hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Can I dye my hair blonde if I have a round face?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a warm blonde shade that complements your skin tone.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour for a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and ideas, you can find a shade that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preference when choosing a hair colour. And don't forget to take care of your coloured hair with proper hair care techniques and products.
