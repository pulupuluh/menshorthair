Shoulder Length Haircuts For Semi Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Mar 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that complements your semi curly hair? Shoulder length haircuts are a great option for those with semi curly hair as they provide the perfect balance between length and manageability. In this article, we will guide you through the best shoulder length haircuts for semi curly hair, how to style them, and how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length haircuts for semi curly hair are a great option for those who want to keep their hair at a manageable length while still maintaining their natural curls. These haircuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose beachy waves to defined curls. They are also great for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

How to

Before getting a shoulder-length haircut for your semi curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, a layered haircut can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a blunt cut with soft layers around the face can help to soften your features.

When it comes to styling your shoulder-length haircut, there are a few options. You can opt for a messy, tousled look by using a texturizing spray and scrunching your hair with your hands. For a sleeker look, you can straighten your hair with a flat iron or use a curling iron to define your curls.

Step by Step

Here's how to style your shoulder length haircut for semi curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for semi curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help detangle it and prevent frizz.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while maintaining your curls.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair for added volume and texture.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create loose, tousled waves.
  6. For a sleeker look, straighten your hair with a flat iron or use a curling iron to define your curls.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a shoulder length haircut for your semi curly hair:

  • Communicate with your stylist about your hair texture and what you want to achieve with your haircut.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for semi curly hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Is shoulder length hair good for semi curly hair?

Yes, shoulder length haircuts are great for semi curly hair as they provide the perfect balance between length and manageability. They are also versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

What face shape is best for a shoulder length haircut?

A layered haircut can help to elongate a round face, while a blunt cut with soft layers around the face can help to soften a square face.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your semi curly hair healthy and shiny, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair texture. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. It's also important to deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and prevent breakage.

When styling your hair, use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help detangle your hair and prevent frizz. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before using them. Finally, protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy a beautiful and healthy shoulder length haircut for your semi curly hair.
