How To Curl Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that curling it is impossible. But we're here to tell you that it's not! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls in no time. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to curl short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: What You'll Need
  • Section 2: How to Curl Short Hair Step by Step
  • Section 3: Tips for Curling Short Hair
  • Section 4: FAQ
  • Section 5: Hair Care for Curly Hair

Section 1: What You'll Need

Before you start curling your short hair, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips or bobby pins
  • Hairspray

Make sure your curling iron or wand is the right size for your hair length. For short hair, you'll want to use a smaller barrel size, around 1/2 inch to 1 inch.

Section 2: How to Curl Short Hair Step by Step

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to start curling! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to curl short hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips or bobby pins.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold for 10-15 seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat on all sections of hair until you've curled all of your hair.
  5. Once you've finished curling, spray your hair with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

And that's it! With these simple steps, you can achieve beautiful, voluminous curls in no time.

Section 3: Tips for Curling Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you curl short hair:

  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand for better control over the curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly or it will look unnatural.
  • Use hairspray to hold the curls in place, but don't overdo it or your hair will look stiff.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting the hair before curling or curling in different directions, to achieve different looks.

Section 4: FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling short hair:

Q: Can you curl short hair with a straightener?

A: Yes, you can curl short hair with a straightener. Simply clamp the straightener down on a small section of hair, then twist the straightener 180 degrees and pull it through the hair.

Q: How do you keep short hair curls from falling out?

A: To keep short hair curls from falling out, use a strong hold hairspray and avoid touching or brushing your hair too much.

Q: How long does it take to curl short hair?

A: The time it takes to curl short hair depends on the thickness of your hair and the size of your curling iron or wand. Generally, it takes around 20-30 minutes to curl short hair.

Section 5: Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and combat frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can break up your curls.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Curling short hair might seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's actually quite easy. By following the steps and tips in this guide, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls in no time. Remember to take care of your hair properly and experiment with different curling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
