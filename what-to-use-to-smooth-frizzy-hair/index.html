What To Use To Smooth Frizzy Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Mar 14, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re someone who struggles with frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and tame your locks. No matter what you do, it always seems like your hair has a mind of its own. Fortunately, there are products out there that can help you smooth frizzy hair and achieve a sleek, polished look. In this article, we’ll explore what to use to smooth frizzy hair and provide you with some tips and tricks for managing unruly hair.

How to Smooth Frizzy Hair

Before we dive into what products to use, it’s important to understand what causes frizzy hair in the first place. Frizz occurs when your hair is dry and lacks moisture. This can be due to a variety of factors, including genetics, humidity, and heat damage from styling tools. To smooth frizzy hair, you need to restore moisture and seal the hair cuticle. Here’s how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with Moisturizing Shampoo

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo. Look for a shampoo that contains ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, or shea butter, which can help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of natural oils and make frizz worse.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

After shampooing, apply a conditioner to your hair. Focus on the ends, which tend to be the driest part of your hair. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water. Cool water can help seal the hair cuticle, which can reduce frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Once you’ve towel-dried your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair throughout the day and provide an extra layer of protection against frizz. Look for a leave-in conditioner that contains ingredients like glycerin, which can help attract moisture to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Smoothing Serum or Oil

To further smooth frizzy hair, apply a smoothing serum or oil. These products can help to seal the hair cuticle and prevent moisture from escaping. Look for products that contain ingredients like silicone, which can provide a barrier against humidity. However, be careful not to overdo it with these products, as too much can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair using a blow dryer or straightening iron. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before applying heat to your hair, as this can help to prevent further damage. Use a brush or comb to smooth your hair as you style it, and finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Hair

In addition to using the right products, there are a few other tips and tricks that can help you manage frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and make frizz worse.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of natural oils and make frizz worse.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make frizz worse.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

FAQ

Q: What causes frizzy hair?

A: Frizzy hair is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. This can be due to genetics, humidity, or heat damage from styling tools.

Q: How can I prevent frizzy hair?

A: To prevent frizzy hair, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner and smoothing serum or oil. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and avoid products that contain alcohol.

Hair Care for Smooth, Frizz-Free Hair

Now that you know what to use to smooth frizzy hair, it’s time to start incorporating these products into your hair care routine. By following the steps outlined in this article, and using the right products, you can achieve smooth, frizz-free hair that looks and feels healthy. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time to find the right products and routine for your hair type. With a little effort and care, you can have the smooth, shiny hair you’ve always wanted.
