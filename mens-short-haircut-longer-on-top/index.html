Mens Short Haircut Longer On Top A Relaxed And Trendy Look

Are you looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut? Then, a mens short haircut longer on top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been trending for years and has become a popular choice among fashion-conscious men. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get this look, hair care tips, and frequently asked questions.

How to Get Mens Short Haircut Longer on Top?

The mens short haircut longer on top is a versatile and trendy look that can suit all face shapes and hair types. To achieve this look, you need to visit a professional hairstylist who has experience in creating this style. You can also show them pictures of the hairstyle you want to get a better idea.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a mens short haircut longer on top:

  1. First, decide on the length of your hair on the sides and back. Generally, a faded or tapered cut is preferred with shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on top.
  2. Next, ask your hairstylist to cut the hair on the sides and back using clippers or scissors. The length can be gradually increased as it goes up to the top.
  3. For the top, your hairstylist will use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. The length can vary, but typically it is longer than the sides and back.
  4. You can also ask your hairstylist to add texture and layers to the hair on top to make it more voluminous and stylish.
  5. Finally, apply some hair styling product to the hair on top to keep it in place.

Tips to Maintain Short Haircut Longer on Top

Here are some tips to keep your mens short haircut longer on top looking fresh and stylish:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and to maintain the length of the hair.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Apply a small amount of hair styling product to the hair on top to keep it in place and add texture.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hairdryer to style your hair and add volume.

FAQ About Mens Short Haircut Longer on Top

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens short haircut longer on top:

1. Is a mens short haircut longer on top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can suit all face shapes. However, the length of the hair on top can be adjusted to suit your face shape and facial features.

2. What type of hair works best for a mens short haircut longer on top?

This hairstyle can work for all hair types, but it looks best on thick and wavy hair. Fine hair can also work, but it may need more styling product to add volume.

3. Does a mens short haircut longer on top require a lot of maintenance?

No, this hairstyle is low-maintenance and only requires regular trims to maintain the length of the hair.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Short Haircut Longer on Top

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens short haircut longer on top looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage the hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a hairdryer or styling tools.

Now you know everything about the mens short haircut longer on top. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance look that can suit any occasion. Follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.
