Cute Hairstyles For Medium Length Straight Hair 10 Ideas For 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 19, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on cute hairstyles for medium length straight hair! If you're blessed with this hair type, you're in luck – there are plenty of stylish, easy-to-do hairstyles that you can try out. In this post, we'll be sharing 10 ideas for cute hairstyles that you can rock in 2023. Whether you're going to a party, a date, or just want to switch up your everyday look, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Medium length straight hair is a versatile hair type that can be styled in many different ways. It's not too short or too long, which makes it easy to manage and style. Whether you have thick or thin hair, medium length straight hair can work for you. It's also a great length for trying out different hairstyles without committing to a drastic cut or color change.

How to

The key to achieving cute hairstyles for medium length straight hair is to have the right tools and products on hand. You'll need a good quality hairbrush, a flat iron or curling iron, and some hair accessories such as bobby pins, hair ties, and headbands. You'll also need some styling products such as hairspray, mousse, or serum, depending on the look you're going for.

Step by step

1. Sleek and Straight:

Start with clean, dry hair. Brush out any tangles and apply a heat protectant. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, section by section. Finish with a shine serum.

2. Half-Up Half-Down:

Part your hair down the middle. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail or bun. Leave the rest of your hair down.

3. Low Ponytail:

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it into a low ponytail. Use a comb to tease the crown of your head for extra volume.

4. Top Knot:

Gather your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure with bobby pins.

5. Braided Crown:

Part your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from the front and braid it. Repeat on the other side. Cross the braids over the top of your head and secure with bobby pins.

6. Beach Waves:

Apply mousse to damp hair. Use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish with hairspray.

7. Side Braid:

Part your hair on one side. Braid the hair on the opposite side and secure with a hair tie. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a softer look.

8. Messy Bun:

Gather your hair into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a messy bun. Pull out some pieces of hair for a more relaxed look.

9. Sleek Ponytail:

Part your hair down the middle. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Tie your hair into a high ponytail and wrap a piece of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

10. Side Swept Bangs:

If you have side swept bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them. Sweep them to one side and secure with a bobby pin.

Tips

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

- Don't be afraid to use hair accessories to add some extra flair to your look.

- Use heat protectant to prevent damage from styling tools.

- Use a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's not perfectly straight?

A: Absolutely! Many of these hairstyles can work with slightly wavy or curly hair as well.

Q: What if my hair is too thick or too thin?

A: You can adjust the hairstyles to fit your hair type. For example, if your hair is too thick for a sleek ponytail, try a messy bun instead. If your hair is too thin for a voluminous half-up half-down style, try teasing it at the roots for some extra lift.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length straight hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair. Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and avoid using them too often. Get regular trims to prevent split ends, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

We hope you found these cute hairstyles for medium length straight hair helpful and inspiring. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles – you never know what you might discover!
