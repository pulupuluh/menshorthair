How Can I See What Colour Hair Suits Me Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 29, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're thinking of changing your hair colour, you might be wondering how to choose the right one for you. With so many options out there, it can be tough to figure out which shade will work best with your skin tone and features. But don't worry – we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for figuring out how to see what colour hair suits you best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine the Best Hair Colour for Your Skin Tone
  2. Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  3. Top Tips for Finding the Perfect Hair Colour
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your New Hair Colour Looking Great

How to Determine the Best Hair Colour for Your Skin Tone

Before you start browsing hair colour swatches, it's important to understand how your skin tone affects which shades will look best on you. Generally, there are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Here's how to tell which category you fall into:

  • Warm skin tone: If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow, peachy, or golden hue. You might also have green veins on the underside of your wrist.
  • Cool skin tone: If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink, red, or blue-ish hue. You might also have blue veins on the underside of your wrist.
  • Neutral skin tone: If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool hues, or a more muted tone. You might also have both green and blue veins on the underside of your wrist.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start thinking about which hair colours will complement it best.

Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here's a step-by-step guide for figuring out which hair colour will suit you best:

  1. Determine your skin tone: Use the tips above to figure out whether you have a warm, cool, or neutral skin tone.
  2. Consider your eye colour: Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you best. For example, if you have blue eyes, you might look great with a warm, golden blonde hair colour.
  3. Think about your natural hair colour: If you're not sure what hair colour will suit you best, consider sticking with a shade that's close to your natural colour. This will help ensure that the colour looks natural and flattering on you.
  4. Look at your wardrobe: The colours you wear most often can also give you clues about which hair colour will suit you best. For example, if you tend to wear a lot of warm, earthy tones, you might look great with a warm, caramel brown hair colour.
  5. Consult a professional: If you're still not sure which hair colour will suit you best, consider consulting a professional stylist. They can help you choose a shade that will complement your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

Following these steps can help you choose the perfect hair colour for your individual features and complexion.

Top Tips for Finding the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you lead an active lifestyle, you might want to choose a low-maintenance hair colour that won't require frequent touch-ups.
  • Be realistic: If you're considering a dramatic hair colour change, be realistic about whether you'll be able to maintain the new colour. For example, if you're used to having dark hair, it might be tough to keep up with a platinum blonde shade.
  • Choose a shade that makes you feel confident: Ultimately, the most important thing is to choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour:

  • What hair colour looks best on fair skin? Generally, warm, golden tones like honey blonde, copper, and strawberry blonde look great on fair skin.
  • What hair colour looks best on dark skin? Darker skin tones can look great with rich, warm colours like caramel, chestnut, and auburn.
  • Can I dye my hair at home? While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting a professional stylist for the best results.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your New Hair Colour Looking Great

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use colour-safe shampoo and conditioner: This will help prevent your colour from fading prematurely.
  • Avoid over-washing: Washing your hair too frequently can cause your colour to fade faster. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.
  • Avoid hot tools: Heat can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade. Try to avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can also cause your colour to fade, so be sure to wear a hat or use a protective hair product when spending time in the sun.

By following these tips, you can help ensure that your new hair colour looks great for as long as possible.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting process, but it's important to take your individual features and skin tone into account. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can find a shade that truly complements your complexion and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
