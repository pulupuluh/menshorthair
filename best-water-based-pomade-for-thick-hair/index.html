As someone with thick hair, finding the right hair product can be a challenge. Luckily, water based pomades are a great option for those with thick hair. In this guide, we'll cover the best water based pomades for thick hair, how to apply them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Best Water Based Pomades for Thick Hair

When it comes to pomade for thick hair, there are a few things to look for. You want a pomade that provides strong hold, but also allows for flexibility and movement. Here are some of the best water based pomades for thick hair:

1. Suavecito Firme Hold Pomade

Suavecito Firme Hold Pomade is one of the most popular water based pomades on the market. It provides a strong hold that can last all day, and works well with thick hair. It also has a pleasant scent and is easy to wash out.

2. Layrite Superhold Pomade

Layrite Superhold Pomade is another great option for those with thick hair. It provides a strong hold, but also allows for some flexibility and movement. It's easy to apply and wash out, and has a pleasant scent.

3. Uppercut Deluxe Pomade

Uppercut Deluxe Pomade is a high-quality water based pomade that provides a strong hold and matte finish. It works well with thick hair and is easy to apply and wash out. It also has a pleasant scent.

How to Apply Water Based Pomade

Applying water based pomade is easy, but it's important to do it correctly to get the best results. Here's how to apply water based pomade:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Make sure your hair is clean and towel-dried before applying pomade. This will help the pomade spread evenly and provide a better hold.

2. Scoop out a small amount of pomade

Start with a small amount of pomade, about the size of a dime. You can always add more if you need it.

3. Rub the pomade between your palms

Rub the pomade between your palms until it's warm and evenly distributed.

4. Apply the pomade to your hair

Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Make sure to evenly distribute the pomade throughout your hair.

5. Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair the way you want it. You can add more pomade if needed.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

While water based pomade can be a great option for styling thick hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help prevent buildup and keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a conditioner

Using a conditioner after washing your hair can help keep it soft and healthy.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair, so it's best to avoid it if possible.

4. Get regular haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is water based pomade?

Water based pomade is a hair styling product that provides a strong hold and washes out easily with water.

Can water based pomade be used on thick hair?

Yes, water based pomade can be used on thick hair. Look for a pomade that provides strong hold and allows for flexibility and movement.

How do I wash out water based pomade?

Water based pomade can be washed out easily with water and shampoo.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires some extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for thick hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent tangles and breakage.

2. Don't over-wash your hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of natural oils, so it's best to wash it every other day or every few days.

3. Use a hair mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair soft and healthy.

4. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers. These can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

By following these tips and using one of the best water based pomades for thick hair, you can achieve a great hairstyle while keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.
