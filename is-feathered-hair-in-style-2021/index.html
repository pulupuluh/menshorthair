Is Feathered Hair In Style 2021

Written by William Mar 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Feathered hair is a classic haircut that has been around since the 1970s. It was a popular hairstyle in the 80s and 90s, and it seems to be making a comeback in 2021. If you're wondering if feathered hair is still in style, the answer is yes!

Description

Feathered hair is a layered hairstyle that features long, feather-like layers. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from a sleek and straight look to a tousled and messy style.

How To

If you want to try out feathered hair for yourself, here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Hairstylist

If you're not sure if feathered hair is right for you, it's best to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine if the style will work with your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Ask for Feathered Layers

To achieve the feathered look, ask your hairstylist to cut long, feather-like layers throughout your hair. The layers should be longer in the front and shorter in the back.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, you can style your hair in many different ways. You can blow-dry your hair straight for a sleek and polished look, or you can tousle your hair with some texturizing spray for a messy and beachy vibe.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your feathered hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the feathered layers.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: Will feathered hair work with my hair type?

A: Feathered hair can work with many different hair types, but it's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if the style will work for you.

Q: How often should I get my feathered hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the feathered layers.

Q: Can I style my feathered hair in different ways?

A: Yes, feathered hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways.

Hair Care

To keep your feathered hair looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.

In conclusion, feathered hair is definitely in style in 2021. It's a classic haircut that has stood the test of time, and it's making a comeback this year. If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try, consider feathered hair!
