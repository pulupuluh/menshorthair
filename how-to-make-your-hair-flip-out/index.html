How To Make Your Hair Flip Out Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 · 7 min read
How to Make Your Hair Flip Out: Step by Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

Do you want to add some bounce and movement to your hair? One easy way to do that is by making your hair flip out. This style is perfect for anyone who wants to add some fun and flirty vibes to their hair. In this step-by-step guide, we will teach you how to make your hair flip out and provide some tips and hair care advice along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Flip Out
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Flipping Out Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Make Your Hair Flip Out

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand what it means to flip out your hair. Flipping out your hair refers to adding a slight curl at the ends of your hair, creating a flipping motion. You can achieve this style using a variety of tools, including a curling iron, straightener, or even a blow dryer.

The key to making your hair flip out is to focus on the ends of your hair. By adding a curl at the ends, you'll create a subtle flip that adds movement and volume to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you understand the basics of flipping out your hair, let's get into the step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Towel-dry your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy. Spray the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron

Take a small section of your hair (about 1-2 inches wide) and clamp it with a curling iron. Hold for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 4: Flip Out Your Hair

Once you've curled your hair, it's time to flip out the ends. Take a small section of your hair and use a comb or your fingers to gently flip the ends out. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've flipped out your hair, finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place. Be sure to use a hairspray that won't weigh down your hair or make it feel stiff.

Tips for Flipping Out Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect flip-out hairstyle:

  • Use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls that flip out more dramatically.
  • Use a larger curling iron for looser curls that create a more subtle flip.
  • Don't curl all the way to the roots – focus on the ends for a more natural look.
  • For extra volume, tease your hair at the roots before flipping out the ends.

FAQs

Can I achieve this style with a straightener?

Yes! You can achieve a flipped-out look with a straightener by twisting the straightener away from your face as you glide it down your hair.

How can I make my hair flip out without heat?

If you want to avoid using heat tools, try braiding your hair and leaving it in overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have natural waves that can be flipped out at the ends.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to make your hair flip out and rock this fun and flirty style!
