How To Pick Your Best Hair Color A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. But fear not! We've created a step-by-step guide to help you pick your best hair color. In this article, we'll go over everything from how to determine your skin tone to tips for maintaining your new hair color. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Consider Your Lifestyle
  • Hair Color Options
  • Try on Wigs
  • Consult a Professional
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking your best hair color is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. A warm skin tone has yellow or golden undertones, while a cool skin tone has pink or blue undertones. A neutral skin tone has a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you likely have a cool skin tone. If you can't tell whether they're blue or green, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewelry you wear. If you look best in gold jewelry, you likely have a warm skin tone. If you look best in silver jewelry, you likely have a cool skin tone. If you look good in both gold and silver jewelry, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When picking your hair color, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Do you work in a professional setting where a more natural hair color is preferred? Or do you have a more creative job where you can experiment with bold colors? Do you have the time and budget to maintain a high-maintenance hair color?

It's important to pick a hair color that fits your lifestyle and won't cause any unnecessary stress or inconvenience.

Hair Color Options

There are many hair color options to choose from, including:

  • Blonde
  • Brunette
  • Red
  • Black
  • Highlights
  • Ombre
  • Balayage

When considering hair color options, it's important to pick a color that complements your skin tone and fits your lifestyle.

Try on Wigs

If you're unsure about which hair color to choose, try on wigs in different colors. This will give you a better idea of how the color will look on you without the commitment.

Consult a Professional

If you're still unsure about which hair color to choose, consult a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine your skin tone and suggest hair colors that will complement it. They can also give you tips for maintaining your new hair color.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair every day
  • Avoid hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your hair color

FAQ

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's best to consult a professional hairstylist. They can ensure that the hair color is applied evenly and that the color complements your skin tone.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the hair color and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips, you can find a hair color that complements your skin tone and fits your lifestyle. And with proper maintenance and care, you can enjoy your new hair color for months to come!
