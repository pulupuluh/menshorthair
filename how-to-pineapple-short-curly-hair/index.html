How To Pineapple Short Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short curly hair is beautiful, but it can be tricky to maintain. One popular technique for preserving curls overnight is called "pineappling". This involves loosely gathering your curls at the top of your head so that they resemble a pineapple. In this article, we'll teach you how to pineapple short curly hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pineapple Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Pineappling Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

How to Pineapple Short Curly Hair

Pineappling short curly hair is a great way to preserve your curls overnight and reduce frizz. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Start by gathering all of your hair at the top of your head. You can use a scrunchie or a hair tie to secure it in place. Make sure the ponytail is loose and doesn't pull on your curls.

Step 2: Adjust Your Hair

Adjust your hair so that it's comfortable to sleep in. You can pull your ponytail to the front or the side, or leave it at the back of your head. Make sure it's loose enough that it won't cause any tension on your scalp or damage your curls.

Step 3: Protect Your Curls

Protect your curls by using a silk or satin pillowcase. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage. Silk or satin will help keep your curls smooth and intact.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to pineapple short curly hair:

  1. Start with dry, detangled hair.
  2. Flip your head over and gather all of your hair at the top of your head.
  3. Use a scrunchie or hair tie to secure your hair in place. Make sure it's loose and comfortable.
  4. Adjust your hair so that it's comfortable to sleep in. You can pull your ponytail to the front or the side, or leave it at the back of your head.
  5. Protect your curls by using a silk or satin pillowcase.
  6. In the morning, gently take down your hair and fluff your curls with your fingers.

Tips for Pineappling Short Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your pineappled curls:

  • Use a silk or satin scrunchie to avoid pulling on your hair and causing breakage.
  • For extra protection, you can wrap your hair in a silk scarf before going to bed.
  • If you have shorter hair, you can use a soft headband to pineapple your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream before pineappling to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Can I pineapple my hair if it's wet?

No, it's best to pineapple your hair when it's dry. Wet hair is more prone to breakage and can cause your curls to lose their shape.

How long can I leave my hair pineappled?

You can leave your hair pineappled overnight, but it's best to take it down in the morning so your curls can breathe.

Can I pineapple my hair every night?

Yes, you can pineapple your hair every night, but make sure to use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your curls.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Pineappling is just one part of a healthy hair care routine for short curly hair. Here are a few more tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips and pineappling your hair at night, you can keep your short curly hair looking beautiful and healthy.
