How To Make Your High Ponytail Look Better

Written by Aprilia Mar 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of your high ponytail looking plain and boring, don't worry! There are plenty of ways to spruce up your look and make your ponytail pop. In this article, we'll walk you through some tips and tricks to make your high ponytail look better than ever.

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for a Better High Ponytail

How to Make Your High Ponytail Look Better

The first thing you need to do is decide on the height of your ponytail. A high ponytail is a classic look that never goes out of style, but you can also experiment with a mid-height or low ponytail for a different vibe.

Once you've decided on the height, it's time to gather your hair into a ponytail. Use a fine-tooth comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways, and secure your hair with a hair tie. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort or headaches.

Now that your ponytail is in place, it's time to add some volume and texture. You can do this by using a teasing brush to backcomb the hair at the crown of your head. This will create a more voluminous and dramatic ponytail.

Another option is to add some curls or waves to your ponytail. You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves or tight curls. This will add some dimension and movement to your ponytail.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your high ponytail look better:

  1. Decide on the height of your ponytail.
  2. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with a fine-tooth comb.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  4. Backcomb the hair at the crown of your head with a teasing brush to create volume.
  5. Add curls or waves to your ponytail with a curling iron or hot rollers.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Tips for a Better High Ponytail

Here are some additional tips to make your high ponytail look better:

  • Use a hair tie that matches the color of your hair to create a more seamless look.
  • Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it and create a more polished look.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a scrunchie or barrette, to add some flair to your ponytail.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as sleek and straight or tousled and messy, to find your perfect ponytail look.
  • Use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add grip and hold to your hair before creating your ponytail.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of hair works best for a high ponytail?

A high ponytail can work for all hair types, but it works best for medium to long hair. If you have short hair, you can still create a high ponytail by using hair extensions.

How do I prevent my high ponytail from causing headaches?

To prevent headaches, make sure the hair tie is not too tight. You can also try using a scrunchie or a softer hair tie to reduce tension on your scalp.

Can I wear a high ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a high ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To make it more formal, add some curls or waves and use a hair accessory to add some glamour.

Hair Care for High Ponytails

Creating a high ponytail can put strain on your hair, so it's important to take care of your locks before and after styling. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • When removing your hair tie, be gentle to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a high ponytail that looks better than ever. Whether you're headed to the gym or a formal event, a high ponytail is a versatile and stylish option that never goes out of style.
