How To Fix Frizzy Curly Hair In The Morning

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of waking up to frizzy, unruly curls in the morning? It can be frustrating trying to tame your hair each day, especially when you’re short on time. Fortunately, there are ways to fix frizzy curly hair in the morning and achieve a beautiful, defined look. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you get the perfect curls every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Frizzy Curly Hair in the Morning
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Fix Frizzy Curly Hair in the Morning

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to understand why curly hair can become frizzy in the first place. Curly hair is naturally drier than straight hair, which means it’s more prone to frizz. Additionally, humidity, heat, and styling products can all contribute to frizz. With that in mind, here are some steps you can take to fix frizzy curly hair in the morning:

Step 1: Start with a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

The foundation of any good hair care routine is a quality shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will help to moisturize and define your curls. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and contribute to frizz.

Step 2: Use a Microfiber Towel

When drying your hair, use a microfiber towel instead of a regular cotton towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and help to minimize frizz. Simply wrap your hair in the towel and gently squeeze out excess water.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After towel-drying your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and defined. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for styling curly hair, as it helps to distribute heat evenly and minimize frizz. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and gently scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair oil to help smooth down any remaining frizz. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed guide on how to fix frizzy curly hair in the morning:

  1. Wash your hair with a curly hair shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Wrap your hair in a microfiber towel and gently squeeze out excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, working it through with your fingers.
  4. Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Scrunch your hair as you dry it.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to smooth down any remaining frizz.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair and help to reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and contribute to frizz.
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, and use a microfiber towel to dry your hair.

What is the best way to style curly hair?

The best way to style curly hair is to use a diffuser on your hair dryer and scrunch your hair as you dry it. Finish with a small amount of hair oil to smooth down any remaining frizz.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair is naturally drier than straight hair, so it’s best to wash it no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and contribute to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and contribute to frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix frizzy curly hair in the morning and achieve a beautiful, defined look. Remember to be gentle with your hair and use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.
