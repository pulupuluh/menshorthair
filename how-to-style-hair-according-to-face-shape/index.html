How To Style Hair According To Face Shape Tips And Techniques In 2023

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that just don't seem to work for you? It's possible that you haven't considered your face shape when choosing a hairstyle. Different face shapes require different types of haircuts and styling techniques to bring out the best in your features. In this blog post, we'll explore how to style hair according to face shape, including tips and techniques to help you find the perfect look for you.

Description

Before we dive into how to style hair according to face shape, let's take a moment to understand the different face shapes. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own unique features, and certain hairstyles can complement or enhance those features.

How to Style Hair According to Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval face: Oval faces have balanced features and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, long layers and loose waves can accentuate your features.
  • Round face: Round faces have soft, curved features. To elongate the face, try a long bob or layers that hit below the chin. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs.
  • Square face: Square faces have strong, angular features. Soften the face with layers or a side-swept fringe. Avoid harsh, straight cuts.
  • Heart face: Heart faces have a wider forehead and narrower chin. A chin-length bob or long layers can help balance the face. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts.
  • Diamond face: Diamond faces have wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. Try a layered cut or a side-swept fringe to soften the face. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts.
  • Oblong face: Oblong faces have a long, narrow shape. A long bob or shoulder-length cut can help balance the face. Avoid super-long styles and heavy, blunt cuts.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair according to your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape.
  4. When styling your hair, consider your face shape and use techniques that enhance your features.
  5. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips and Techniques

Here are some additional tips and techniques to help you style your hair according to your face shape:

  • Use hair accessories to enhance your hairstyle. For example, a headband can help balance a heart-shaped face.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle. Thick, curly hair may require a different cut than fine, straight hair.
  • Try different parting styles to enhance your features. A side part can help balance a square face, while a middle part can complement an oval face.
  • Use hair products to enhance your hairstyle. For example, a volumizing mousse can help add volume to fine hair, while a smoothing serum can help tame frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair according to face shape:

  • Can I wear any hairstyle regardless of my face shape? While you can technically wear any hairstyle you want, certain styles may not be as flattering as others. By choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape, you can enhance your features and feel more confident.
  • How do I determine my face shape? Look in the mirror and identify the widest part of your face (forehead, cheekbones, or jawline). Then, measure the distance between these points. Oval faces have balanced features and are longer than they are wide, while round faces are almost as wide as they are long.
  • Can I change my face shape with a haircut? While a haircut can enhance your features, it can't change the shape of your face. Focus on choosing a hairstyle that complements your natural features instead of trying to alter your face shape.

Hair Care

No matter what your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know how to style your hair according to your face shape, it's time to experiment and find the perfect look for you. Remember, the key is to choose a hairstyle that enhances your natural features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
