Guys Hairstyles In Nigeria A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to hair, Nigerian men are known for their unique and stylish hairstyles. Whether you're looking for a classic cut or something more trendy, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the most popular guys hairstyles in Nigeria, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Popular Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Guys Hairstyle in Nigeria
  • FAQs About Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria
  • Hair Care Tips for Nigerian Men

How to Achieve Popular Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria

There are many popular guys hairstyles in Nigeria, including:

  • Dreadlocks
  • Low cut
  • High top fade
  • Cornrows
  • Twists

To achieve these hairstyles, you'll need to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut and style to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria

If you want to achieve a guys hairstyle in Nigeria at home, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as hair gel or wax.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to set the style.

Remember, it's always best to visit a professional stylist if you want to achieve the perfect guys hairstyle in Nigeria.

Tips for Maintaining Your Guys Hairstyle in Nigeria

Once you have achieved your desired guys hairstyle in Nigeria, you'll need to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs About Guys Hairstyles in Nigeria

What is the most popular guys hairstyle in Nigeria?

The most popular guys hairstyle in Nigeria is the low cut.

How much does it cost to get a guys hairstyle in Nigeria?

The cost of a guys hairstyle in Nigeria can vary depending on the salon or stylist you visit, but you can expect to pay between 1,500 and 5,000 naira.

How often should I visit the barber or stylist to maintain my guys hairstyle in Nigeria?

You should visit the barber or stylist every 2-4 weeks to maintain your guys hairstyle in Nigeria.

Hair Care Tips for Nigerian Men

Here are some general hair care tips for Nigerian men:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can ensure that your guys hairstyle in Nigeria always looks its best.

So, there you have it - a comprehensive guide to guys hairstyles in Nigeria. Whether you're looking for a classic cut or something more trendy, there's a hairstyle to suit every taste and style. Remember to visit a professional stylist for the best results, and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
