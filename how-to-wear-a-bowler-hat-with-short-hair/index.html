How To Wear A Bowler Hat With Short Hair

Written by Petter Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair and want to add some extra flair to your style, consider wearing a bowler hat. This classic accessory can add some personality to any outfit, but it can be tricky to pull off with shorter locks. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of how to wear a bowler hat with short hair, so you can look your best and feel confident.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear a Bowler Hat with Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing a Bowler Hat with Short Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair and Bowler Hats

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to wear a bowler hat with short hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hat

Make sure the hat fits your head properly and complements your face shape. You can choose a classic black or brown bowler hat or opt for a more colorful or patterned design.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Short hair can look great with a bowler hat, but you'll need to style it first. Consider using a bit of pomade or hair wax to add some texture and volume. You can also blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots.

Step 3: Place the Hat on Your Head

Place the hat on your head at a slight angle, with the brim sitting just above your eyebrows. Make sure the hat is snug but not too tight.

Step 4: Adjust the Brim

You can adjust the brim of the hat to suit your style. You can wear it straight across your forehead or tilt it slightly to one side. You can also fold the brim up slightly to create a more vintage look.

Step 5: Own Your Look

Finally, wear your bowler hat with confidence. It's a bold accessory, so make sure you own your look and feel comfortable in it.

Tips for Wearing a Bowler Hat with Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a bowler hat with short hair:

  • Consider pairing your bowler hat with a simple, classic outfit to let the hat stand out.
  • Experiment with different styles of bowler hats to find the one that suits you best.
  • Try wearing your hair in a side part or slicked back style to create more volume at the crown.
  • Avoid wearing your bowler hat too far back on your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and well-groomed before wearing a bowler hat.

FAQs

Can I wear a bowler hat with short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can look great with a bowler hat, as long as you style it properly.

What should I wear with a bowler hat?

You can pair a bowler hat with a variety of outfits, from casual to dressy. Consider wearing it with a simple suit or jeans and a t-shirt.

How do I clean a bowler hat?

You can use a soft brush or cloth to remove any dirt or dust from your bowler hat. Avoid getting it wet, as this can damage the material.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair and Bowler Hats

If you're wearing a bowler hat with short hair, it's important to take care of your locks. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Consider getting regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh and neat.

By following these steps and tips, you can wear a bowler hat with short hair and look great. It's all about finding the right fit, styling your hair properly, and owning your look with confidence. So go ahead and give it a try!
