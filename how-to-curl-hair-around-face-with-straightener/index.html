How To Curl Hair Around Face With Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to add a little bit of flair to your look? One way to spice things up is by curling your hair around your face with a straightener. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll take you through the process of curling your hair using a straightener.

Description

Curling your hair with a straightener is a great way to achieve a natural-looking curl. It’s also a lot easier than it looks! All you need is a straightener, some heat protectant, and a little bit of patience.

How to

Here’s how to curl your hair around your face using a straightener:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the back of your head. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener near the roots. Twist the straightener away from your face and glide it down to the end of your hair.

For a tighter curl, twist the straightener more. For a looser curl, twist the straightener less.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat steps 3 for each section of hair, working your way towards the front of your head.

Step 5: Finish

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look. Finish off with a little bit of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curl:

  • Use a high-quality straightener with ceramic plates.
  • Don’t curl too much hair at once.
  • Twist the straightener away from your face to create a more natural-looking curl.
  • Experiment with different twisting techniques to achieve different types of curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair with a straightener:

Can I curl my hair with a flat iron?

Yes, a flat iron can be used to curl hair.

How long does the curl last?

The duration of the curl depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, the curl should last for a few hours to a day.

Can I curl my hair if it’s wet?

No, you should never use a straightener on wet hair.

Hair Care

Curling your hair can be damaging, so it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream before curling your hair.
  • Don’t curl your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair.

Now that you know how to curl your hair around your face with a straightener, go ahead and give it a try! With a little bit of practice, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect curl.
