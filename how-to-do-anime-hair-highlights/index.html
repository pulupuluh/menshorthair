How To Do Anime Hair Highlights A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're a fan of anime and want to add a touch of the style to your hair, anime hair highlights may be just the thing for you. Anime hair highlights are a popular trend that can add a pop of color to your hair and give you a fun and unique look. In this article, we'll go over how to do anime hair highlights in a step-by-step guide, as well as some tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking healthy.

Description

Anime hair highlights are a fun and creative way to add some color to your hair. They are inspired by the bold and bright hair colors often seen in anime and manga. Anime hair highlights can be done in a variety of colors, from pastels to bright neons, and can be placed in different areas of the hair to create different effects.

How to Do Anime Hair Highlights

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do anime hair highlights:

Step 1: Choose your color

Choose the color you want to use for your anime hair highlights. You can go for a bold and bright color, or a more subtle pastel shade.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section off the area of your hair where you want to add the highlights. You can do this by separating your hair into small sections with clips.

Step 3: Apply the color

Apply the color to your hair using a brush or applicator. Make sure to apply the color evenly and saturate the hair thoroughly.

Step 4: Process the color

Let the color process for the recommended amount of time. This will vary depending on the brand and color you are using, so be sure to follow the instructions carefully.

Step 5: Rinse and condition your hair

Once the color has processed, rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. Follow up with a deep conditioning treatment to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing anime hair highlights: - Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the vibrancy of your highlights. - Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can cause damage to your hair and fade the color. - Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong. - If you're not sure about doing the highlights yourself, consider going to a professional stylist for the best results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about anime hair highlights:

Q: How long do anime hair highlights last?

A: The longevity of anime hair highlights will depend on a variety of factors, including the color used, how often you wash your hair, and how well you take care of it. Generally, you can expect the color to last for a few weeks to a few months.

Q: Can I do anime hair highlights on dark hair?

A: Yes, you can still do anime hair highlights on dark hair. However, you may need to bleach your hair first to make the color more visible.

Q: Can I do anime hair highlights on short hair?

A: Absolutely! Anime hair highlights can be done on any length of hair, from short pixie cuts to long flowing locks.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair after doing anime hair highlights. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week. With proper care and maintenance, your anime hair highlights can last for weeks or even months. In conclusion, anime hair highlights are a fun and creative way to add some color to your hair. By following these step-by-step instructions, you can achieve the look you want and keep your hair healthy and strong. Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and placement to create a unique and personalized style.
