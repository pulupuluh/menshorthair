Mid Length Hairstyles For Oval Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This versatile face shape can pull off a variety of hairstyles, including mid length hairstyles. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best mid length hairstyles for oval faces, as well as how to style them and maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Your Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When choosing a mid length hairstyle for your oval face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. Here are some of the best mid length hairstyles for oval faces:

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a great option for oval faces because it frames the face and accentuates your features. This style works well with straight or wavy hair and can be customized with layers or bangs.

2. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add volume and dimension to mid length hair while still maintaining a flattering shape for oval faces. This style works well with all hair types and can be styled straight or wavy.

3. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to add some extra dimension to your mid length hairstyle. They also work well with oval faces because they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair

Now that you've chosen the perfect mid length hairstyle for your oval face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The first step in styling mid length hair is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your hair hold its shape and prevent frizz.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Use a Curling Iron or Straightener

Depending on the style you've chosen, you may want to use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair. Be sure to use the right heat setting for your hair type and take breaks to prevent damage.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right mid length hairstyle and styling it properly, it's important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Regularly washing and conditioning your hair is important for maintaining healthy hair, but it's also important to choose products that are gentle and won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

While heat styling tools can be great for creating different looks, using them too often can damage your hair. Try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week and use a heat protectant every time.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is important for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

1. Can oval faces pull off shorter or longer hairstyles?

Yes! Oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, including shorter or longer styles. It's all about finding the right shape and cut for your face shape and personal style.

2. What if I have curly hair? Can I still wear mid length hairstyles?

Absolutely! Mid length hairstyles can work well with curly hair, but it's important to find a style and cut that works with your natural texture. Talk to your stylist about the best options for you.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

This can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference, but most people can benefit from washing their hair every 2-3 days. If you have very dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a mid length hairstyle that flatters your oval face shape. Remember to choose a style that works with your hair texture and personal style, and always take steps to maintain healthy hair.
