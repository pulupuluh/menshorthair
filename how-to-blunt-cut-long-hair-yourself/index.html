How To Blunt Cut Long Hair Yourself

Written by Frank Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your long hair and give it a fresh, new look, a blunt cut may be just what you need. And while going to a hairdresser can be expensive, you can do a blunt cut at home. In this article, we'll show you how to blunt cut long hair yourself.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Blunt Cut Long Hair Yourself
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A blunt cut is a type of haircut that involves cutting the hair straight across, creating a blunt edge. This type of cut can work well on long hair, as it can help to add volume and thickness to the hair. To achieve a blunt cut at home, you'll need a few basic tools, such as hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to have a friend or family member help you, especially if you're new to cutting your own hair.

How to Blunt Cut Long Hair Yourself

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also want to have all of your tools on hand, and a mirror that allows you to see the back of your head. To begin, follow these steps:

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Using your comb, separate your hair into four equal sections, two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the first section

Starting with one of the front sections, comb your hair straight down and clip the ends with a hair clip. Use your hair cutting scissors to cut straight across the ends. Make sure to cut the hair in a straight line, and not to cut off too much hair at once.

Step 3: Cut the second section

Repeat the same process with the other front section of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the back sections

Release the back sections of your hair and bring them forward. Comb your hair straight down and clip the ends with a hair clip. Use your hair cutting scissors to cut straight across the ends. Make sure to cut the hair in a straight line and not to cut off too much hair at once.

Step 5: Check your work

Once you've cut all four sections, check your work in the mirror. If you notice any unevenness, use your scissors to trim any areas that need to be fixed.

Tips

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you're new to it. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small cuts, and cut off a little bit at a time
  • Use sharp, high-quality hair cutting scissors
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before cutting it
  • Use hair clips to keep sections of hair separated
  • Use a mirror to check your work

FAQ

Can I blunt cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can blunt cut curly hair, but it may be more difficult to achieve a straight line. If you're having trouble, consider asking a friend or family member to help you.

How often should I blunt cut my hair?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows. On average, most people need to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Get regular hair trims to keep your hair looking its best
  • Eat a healthy diet, as this can help to promote hair growth and health

Now that you know how to blunt cut long hair yourself, you can give your hair a fresh, new look without spending a lot of money. Remember to take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you can become a pro at cutting your own hair.
