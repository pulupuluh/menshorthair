Hairstyles That Don t Suit Round Faces A Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying out hairstyles that don't seem to complement your round face shape? Don't worry, you're not alone. Round faces have a unique structure that requires certain hairstyles to enhance their features. In this article, we will discuss some hairstyles that don't suit round faces and provide tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

Before we dive into the hairstyles that don't suit round faces, let's discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape. Round faces have a soft, circular shape with equal width and length. The goal is to create the illusion of length and angles to balance out the face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for hairstyles with volume on top to elongate your face.
  • Choose hairstyles with side-swept bangs to create angles and length.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at the chin.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and lift.

Hairstyles That Don't Suit Round Faces

Now, let's talk about some hairstyles that don't suit round faces. These hairstyles can make your face appear wider and rounder, which is not the ideal look for a round face shape.

Bowl Cut

The bowl cut is a popular hairstyle that has made a comeback in recent years. However, it is not the best choice for round faces. The straight, blunt lines of the bowl cut can make your face appear wider and more circular.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, but it can also accentuate the roundness of your face. The short layers and cropped length can make your face appear wider and less defined.

Straight Bob

The straight bob that ends at the chin can be a challenging hairstyle for round faces. The blunt ends can make your face appear wider and less angular. If you love the bob, consider adding layers or length to create a more flattering look.

Center Part

The center part is a popular hairstyle, but it can make a round face appear wider. The symmetry of the center part can accentuate the circular shape of your face. Consider opting for a side part to create angles and length.

Tips for Styling a Round Face

Now that you know which hairstyles to avoid, here are some tips for styling a round face:

  • Add volume on top to elongate your face.
  • Choose side-swept bangs to create angles and length.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and lift.
  • Opt for hairstyles that frame your face, such as a long, layered cut or a side-swept bob.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but you should consider adding layers or length to create a more flattering look. A bob that ends at the chin can make your face appear wider and less defined.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that are perfect for round faces?

A: Yes, there are many hairstyles that complement round faces. Consider a long, layered cut or a side-swept bob with volume on top.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, round faces require specific hairstyles to enhance their features. Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear wider and less defined, such as the bowl cut, pixie cut, straight bob, and center part. Instead, opt for hairstyles with volume on top, side-swept bangs, and layers. Experiment with different parting styles and find what works best for you. And don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, beautiful locks.
