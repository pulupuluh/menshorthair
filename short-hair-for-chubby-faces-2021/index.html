Short Hair For Chubby Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you someone with a chubby face and looking for the perfect short hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short hair for chubby faces 2021. From how to choose the right hairstyle to tips and tricks for maintenance, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Short hair is a great way to flatter a chubby face, and it has been a popular trend for many years. In 2021, we have seen a lot of new hairstyles that suit different face shapes, including chubby ones. Short hair for chubby faces 2021 is all about creating a balance between the face and the hair. It is about finding a hairstyle that draws attention away from the cheeks and highlights the best features of your face.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle?

Choosing the right short hairstyle for a chubby face can be challenging, but with the following tips, you can make the process a lot simpler:

Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing any hairstyle, it is essential to consider your face shape. Chubby faces come in different shapes, such as round, square, heart, and oval. Each face shape requires a different hairstyle. For example, if you have a round face, you should go for a hairstyle that adds height to your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you should opt for a hairstyle that softens your jawline.

Look for Inspiration

Looking for inspiration is an excellent way to find the perfect short hairstyle. Browse through magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles to get an idea of what you like. You can also use hairstyle apps to see how different hairstyles would look on you.

Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is the best person to guide you on the right hairstyle for your face shape. They can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also give you advice on maintenance and styling.

Step by Step Guide for Short Hair for Chubby Faces 2021

Here are some step-by-step guides to help you achieve the perfect short hairstyle for a chubby face:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply styling products, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Make sure to lift your hair at the roots to add volume. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. You can go for a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob. The key is to add volume to the crown of your head and keep the sides and back sleek. Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your hair and add texture.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Chubby Faces 2021

It is essential to take care of your short hair to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips:

Regular Trims

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape. Visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks for a trim.

Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant sprays before using styling tools.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for chubby faces?

The best short hairstyles for chubby faces are pixie cuts, bobs, and lobs. These hairstyles add volume to the crown of the head and keep the sides and back sleek.

Can I dye my short hair if I have a chubby face?

Yes, you can dye your short hair if you have a chubby face. However, it is essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair for chubby faces 2021 can be a great style choice for anyone looking for a low-maintenance and trendy hairstyle. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can achieve the perfect short hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.
