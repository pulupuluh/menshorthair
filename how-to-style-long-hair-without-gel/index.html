How To Style Long Hair Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair, you may be used to using gel to help tame and style it. However, not everyone likes the feeling of gel in their hair, and some people may even be allergic to certain ingredients found in gel. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to style long hair without using gel. In this article, we'll go over some of the best methods for creating stylish, gel-free hairstyles that will keep your hair looking great all day long.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Hair Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair Without Gel
  • FAQs

How to Style Long Hair Without Gel

Before we dive into the specifics of how to style long hair without gel, it's important to understand what you're working with. Long hair can be heavy and difficult to manage, so it's important to use the right techniques and tools to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it harder to style.
  • Invest in some good quality hair styling tools, such as a hairbrush and a hair dryer, to help you get the look you want.

Now that you know what to keep in mind, let's get into some specific methods for styling long hair without gel.

1. Braids

Braids are a great option for long hair, as they can help keep it in place and prevent it from getting tangled. There are many different types of braids to choose from, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

2. Updos

Updos are another great option for long hair, as they can help keep it off your face and out of the way. There are many different types of updos to choose from, including buns, chignons, and twists. Again, experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

3. Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. You can wear a high ponytail, a low ponytail, or a side ponytail, depending on the look you're going for. You can also add accessories, such as ribbons or hair clips, to make your ponytail more interesting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the best methods for styling long hair without gel, let's go through a step-by-step guide to help you create your perfect hairstyle.

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.

2. Choose your hairstyle.

Decide on the hairstyle you want to create. Braids, updos, and ponytails are all great options.

3. Use hair accessories.

Add hair accessories, such as ribbons or hair clips, to your hairstyle to make it more interesting.

4. Use a hairbrush.

Use a hairbrush to help smooth out your hair and create the style you want.

5. Use a hair dryer.

If you need to dry your hair, use a hair dryer on a low heat setting. Don't use too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Without Gel

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long hair without using gel:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories, such as ribbons or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it harder to style.

FAQs

Can I style long hair without using any products?

Yes, there are many ways to style long hair without using any products. Braids, updos, and ponytails are all great options.

How can I keep my long hair from getting tangled?

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a hairbrush to help smooth out your hair, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your long hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it harder to style.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to style your long hair without using gel. Remember to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you, and always take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
