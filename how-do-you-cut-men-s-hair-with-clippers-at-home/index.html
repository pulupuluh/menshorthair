How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon? With the right tools and technique, you can easily cut men's hair with clippers at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting men's hair with clippers step-by-step, and provide tips for a successful haircut. Let's get started!

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you will need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a good quality pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also helpful to have a cape or towel to protect the person you are cutting from hair clippings.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut and can lead to uneven results. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for the person's hair type, and dry the hair completely with a towel or blow dryer.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Clippers come with different guard sizes, which determine the length of the hair that will be cut. Choose the right guard size based on the desired length of the haircut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start at the Sides

Begin cutting at the sides of the head, using the clippers in an upward motion towards the top of the head. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut. Work your way around the head, being careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Move to the Top

Once the sides are complete, move to the top of the head. Use the same technique as before, using the comb to guide the clippers in an upward motion. Cut small sections of hair at a time, and check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much off.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

After the top and sides are complete, use scissors to blend the hair and remove any uneven or longer pieces. Comb the hair straight up and cut across the top to create a uniform length. Blend the hair where the top and sides meet to create a seamless transition.

Step 7: Clean up the Neckline

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up the neckline and remove any stray hairs. Use a mirror to ensure the neckline is straight and even.

Step 8: Finish with Styling

Once the haircut is complete, use a styling product to add texture and hold to the hair. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair as desired.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair off at once.
  • Check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much hair off.
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the hair where the top and sides meet to create a seamless transition.
  • Use a mirror to ensure the neckline is straight and even.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers with the right technique and tools.

What guard size should I use for a short haircut?

For a short haircut, start with a #2 guard and work your way down gradually to achieve the desired length.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on the hair. Regular trims can also help to maintain the health and appearance of the hair.
