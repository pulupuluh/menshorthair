How To Thin Out Thick Shoulder Length Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick shoulder length hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. It can be challenging to style, and it can take a long time to dry. However, there is a solution to this problem: thinning out your hair. Thinning out your hair can make it more manageable, easier to style, and quicker to dry. In this article, we will show you how to thin out thick shoulder length hair and provide you with some tips on how to care for your hair.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Thin Hair

How to Thin Out Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Thinning out thick shoulder length hair can be done in several ways. You can use scissors or a razor, or you can use a thinning shears. Whatever method you choose, make sure you have the right tools and know what you are doing.

Scissors or Razor

If you want to use scissors or a razor to thin out your hair, you should go to a professional stylist. A stylist will be able to thin out your hair evenly and give you the look you desire. However, if you want to do it yourself, you can follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Clip one side of your hair up to keep it out of the way.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the other side.
  5. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and gently pull it away from your head.
  6. Cut the hair at an angle, about halfway down the section.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 until you have thinned out all the hair on one side of your head.
  8. Repeat steps 3-7 on the other side of your head.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Thinning Shears

Thinning shears are a great tool for thinning out thick shoulder length hair. Thinning shears have teeth on one blade, which helps to remove some of the hair while leaving the rest intact. To use thinning shears, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair from one side.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and gently pull it away from your head.
  5. Using the thinning shears, cut the hair about halfway down the section.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until you have thinned out all the hair on one side of your head.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 on the other side of your head.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Thin Hair

Once you have thinned out your hair, you will want to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

FAQ

How often should I thin out my hair?

You should only thin out your hair when you feel that it is too thick. You do not need to thin out your hair regularly.

Can I thin out my hair at home?

Yes, you can thin out your hair at home using scissors, a razor, or thinning shears. However, it is recommended that you go to a professional stylist if you are not confident in your ability to thin out your hair.

Will thinning out my hair make it grow back thicker?

No, thinning out your hair will not make it grow back thicker. However, it can make your hair look healthier and more voluminous.

Hair Care Tips

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes and relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily thin out your thick shoulder length hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair properly, and it will reward you with a luscious, voluminous look.
