Best Product For Slicking My Hair Back A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Feb 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly hair that just won't stay in place? Slicking your hair back is a classic hairstyle that can give you a sleek and polished look. But what's the best product to use for this style? In this guide, we'll explore the top products for slicking your hair back and share some tips for achieving the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Your Hair Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back
  • FAQ

Description

Slicking your hair back is a timeless hairstyle that can work for any occasion, from a formal event to a casual day out. This style involves using a product to smooth your hair back and keep it in place. The right product can give you a sleek and polished look that will last all day.

There are many products on the market that can be used for slicking your hair back, including gels, pomades, and waxes. Each product has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to choose the right one for your hair type and desired look.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Slicking your hair back may seem like a simple style, but there are a few things to keep in mind to achieve the perfect look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Choose the right product for your hair type
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair
  • Use a comb to smooth your hair back
  • Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to keep your hair in place

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Choose the right product for your hair type
  3. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and rub them together
  4. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  5. Use a comb to smooth your hair back, starting at the hairline and working your way back
  6. Continue combing until your hair is sleek and in place
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to keep your hair in place

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to get a sleeker look
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray or other finishing products to keep your hair in place
  • Try slicking your hair back when it's wet for a different look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking your hair back:

What's the best product for slicking my hair back?

There are many products on the market that can be used for slicking your hair back, including gels, pomades, and waxes. Each product has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to choose the right one for your hair type and desired look.

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and spend more time smoothing your hair to achieve the desired look.

How long will my slicked-back hair last?

Your slicked-back hair should last all day if you use the right product and finishing products to keep it in place.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from blow dryers and straighteners
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know the best product for slicking your hair back and how to achieve the perfect look, you can rock this classic style with confidence. Whether you're heading to a job interview or a night out on the town, slicked-back hair will give you a polished and put-together look that's sure to turn heads.
