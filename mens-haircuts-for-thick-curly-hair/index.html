Mens Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair How To Nail The Look

If you have thick, curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll take you through the best mens haircuts for thick curly hair, as well as some tips for hair care and styling. So whether you're looking for a short, medium, or long haircut, read on to discover how to nail the look.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to mens haircuts for thick curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want a haircut that will work with your natural curl pattern. Second, you want a haircut that will complement your face shape. Here are a few of the best haircuts for thick curly hair:

Short Haircuts

If you want a low-maintenance haircut that's easy to style, a short haircut is a great option. One of the most popular short haircuts for thick curly hair is the buzz cut. This haircut is short all over, and it works particularly well for men with a round face shape. Another option is the crop haircut, which is a bit longer than the buzz cut but still short and easy to style.

Medium Haircuts

If you want a bit more length, a medium-length haircut is a great option for thick curly hair. One of the most popular medium haircuts is the messy quiff. This haircut is longer on top and shorter on the sides, and it's styled with a messy, textured look. Another option is the curly fringe, which is a longer haircut that features a fringe that's styled forward.

Long Haircuts

If you want to embrace your curls and go for a longer haircut, there are a few options to consider. One of the most popular long haircuts for thick curly hair is the man bun. This haircut features longer hair that's pulled back into a bun at the back of the head. Another option is the long curly afro, which is a longer haircut that features a full head of curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your thick curly hair, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, but don't rub it too vigorously. Instead, gently pat it dry.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product, such as a cream or gel, to your hair from roots to tips.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a hair pick to gently lift and separate your curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut and style it, here are a few tips for maintaining your look:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: While it's possible to straighten thick curly hair, it's not recommended as it can damage your hair. Instead, embrace your natural curls and choose a haircut and styling products that work with your curl pattern.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week. This will help to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle.

Q: What's the best haircut for a square face shape?

A: If you have a square face shape, a medium-length haircut with texture on top, such as a messy quiff, is a great option.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to hair care for thick curly hair, it's important to use products that will keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Here are a few of the best products for thick curly hair:

  • A moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • A curl-defining cream or gel
  • A deep conditioning treatment
  • A hair pick or wide-tooth comb

By choosing the right haircut and styling products, and following a consistent hair care routine, you can have healthy, stylish hair that makes you look and feel your best.
