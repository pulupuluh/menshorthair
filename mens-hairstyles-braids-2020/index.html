Mens Hairstyles Braids 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to update your hairstyle this year, one trend that's worth considering is mens hairstyles braids 2020. Braids are a versatile and stylish way to add some texture and interest to your hair, and they're also a great option for guys who want to keep their hair out of their face. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at some of the best mens hairstyles braids 2020, as well as how to achieve these looks, tips for maintaining your braids, and more.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Braids 2020
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Braids
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Mens Hairstyles Braids 2020
  • Hair Care Tips for Braided Hair

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Braids 2020

The first step to achieving mens hairstyles braids 2020 is to decide on the type of braid you want. There are many different types of braids to choose from, including box braids, cornrows, and French braids. Once you've picked the braid that you want, you'll need to prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Next, you'll need to section your hair into small, even sections. This will help to ensure that your braids are neat and uniform. You can use clips or hair ties to keep the sections in place as you work.

Once your hair is sectioned, you can begin braiding. If you're new to braiding, it's a good idea to start with a simple braid and work your way up to more complicated styles. You can find tutorials online or ask a friend or stylist for help.

Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding Your Hair

Step 1: Start by sectioning your hair into small, even sections.

Step 2: Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.

Step 3: Cross the right section of hair over the center section, then cross the left section over the new center section.

Step 4: Continue crossing the right and left sections over the center section until you reach the end of the hair.

Step 5: Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or clip.

Tips for Maintaining Your Braids

Once you've achieved your mens hairstyles braids 2020, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that your braids last as long as possible. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your braids regularly to keep them clean and free of buildup.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause them to loosen or fall out.

Frequently Asked Questions About Mens Hairstyles Braids 2020

Q: Are mens hairstyles braids 2020 suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, there are braids that work well for all hair types, including straight, curly, and kinky hair.

Q: How long do mens hairstyles braids 2020 last?

A: The duration of your braids will depend on a variety of factors, such as the type of braid, the length of your hair, and how well you take care of them. On average, braids can last anywhere from two to eight weeks.

Q: Can I still exercise with braids?

A: Yes, you can still exercise with braids, but it's important to protect your hair by wearing a sweatband or headscarf and washing your hair regularly to remove sweat and dirt buildup.

Hair Care Tips for Braided Hair

When you have braids, it's important to take extra care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause it to break.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • When it's time to remove your braids, be gentle and take your time to avoid damaging your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve mens hairstyles braids 2020 with ease and keep your hair looking healthy and strong. So why not give braids a try and switch up your look this year?
