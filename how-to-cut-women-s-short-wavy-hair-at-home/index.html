How To Cut Women s Short Wavy Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon every time you need a haircut? Do you have short wavy hair that needs a trim? Cutting your own hair at home can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting women's short wavy hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short wavy hair can be a challenge to cut at home, especially if you are not familiar with the right techniques. It is important to keep in mind that cutting hair is a skill that requires practice and patience. With the right tools, such as sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it is important to have a clear idea of the style you want. You can browse online for inspiration or consult with a hairstylist. Make sure you have the right tools and a clean workspace.

Tools you will need:

  • Sharp scissors: Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors.
  • Comb: Choose a wide-toothed comb for detangling and a fine-toothed comb for precision cutting.
  • Hair clips: Use hair clips to section off your hair.
  • Spray bottle: Fill a spray bottle with water to keep your hair damp while cutting.

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to cut your short wavy hair at home:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Divide your hair into four sections: front, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Trim the back

Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and comb it through. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process for the rest of the back section.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and comb it through. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process for the rest of the side sections.

Step 5: Trim the front

Finally, move on to the front section. Take a small section of hair and comb it through. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Make sure the front section is even with the sides and back.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your short wavy hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair and trim gradually.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry, as wet hair tends to look longer.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can result in an uneven cut.
  • If you have curly hair, avoid cutting it too short, as it tends to shrink when dry.

FAQ

Q. Can I cut my wavy hair when it is wet?

A. It is best to cut your wavy hair when it is dry, as wet hair tends to look longer.

Q. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A. It is recommended to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and more precise.

Q. How often should I trim my short wavy hair?

A. It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair straighteners or curling irons.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Cutting your short wavy hair at home can be a rewarding experience, but it requires practice and patience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home.
