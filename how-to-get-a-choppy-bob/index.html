How To Get A Choppy Bob A Relaxed Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut, a choppy bob might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to style, and suits almost every face shape. In this post, we'll guide you through the steps of getting a choppy bob and offer some tips and tricks to make the most out of your new haircut.

How to Get a Choppy Bob

A choppy bob is a short haircut that is typically shoulder-length or above. This hairstyle features layers of different lengths and textures, resulting in a choppy, edgy look. To get a choppy bob, follow these steps:

1. Find a Good Stylist

Look for a stylist who has experience cutting choppy bobs. Ask for recommendations from friends or family, or do some research online. Check the stylist's portfolio to see if they have worked with similar hair types and styles before.

2. Bring Inspiration Photos

Before your appointment, gather some inspiration photos of choppy bobs you like. This will help your stylist understand what you're looking for and give them a better idea of how to cut your hair.

3. Communicate Your Preferences

During your appointment, communicate your preferences to your stylist. Let them know how short you want your hair to be, where you want the layers to start, and how much texture you're comfortable with. Be open to your stylist's suggestions, but also be clear about what you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a choppy bob:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four equal parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

3. Cut the Back

Start at the back of your head and work your way up. Use scissors to cut your hair in a straight line, starting at the nape of your neck. Make sure to cut small sections at a time.

4. Cut the Sides

Move on to the sides of your head. Again, cut small sections at a time, and use your fingers to hold the hair in place. Cut at an upward angle to create layers.

5. Cut the Top

Finally, cut the top of your head. Use scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating layers that frame your face.

6. Add Texture

Use texturizing shears to add texture to your hair. This will create a choppy, edgy look. Use the shears to cut into the ends of your hair, creating a jagged effect.

7. Style Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining a Choppy Bob

Here are some tips for maintaining your choppy bob:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks.

FAQs

Is a choppy bob easy to maintain?

Yes, a choppy bob is a low-maintenance hairstyle. However, you'll need to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

What face shape is best for a choppy bob?

A choppy bob suits almost every face shape. However, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for longer layers to create a more elongated look.

Can I style a choppy bob in different ways?

Yes, a choppy bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for a Choppy Bob

Here are some hair care tips for a choppy bob:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage.

Getting a choppy bob is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and try something new. Whether you opt for a short or shoulder-length cut, a choppy bob is a versatile and edgy look that will suit almost any face shape. Follow our guide and tips to get the most out of your new haircut.
