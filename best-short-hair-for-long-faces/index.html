Best Short Hair For Long Faces Tips And Styles

Description

Long faces can be tricky to style, but with the right haircut, you can make your features stand out in all the right ways. Short haircuts are a great option for long faces because they can help balance out your features and add volume to your hair. In this article, we'll explore the best short haircuts for long faces and provide tips on how to maintain your new look.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair for Long Faces

When choosing a short haircut for a long face, you want to look for styles that will help balance out your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, rather than on top.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider bangs to help shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Try a layered haircut to add texture and volume to your hair.

Best Short Haircuts for Long Faces

Here are some of the best short haircuts for long faces:

  • Pixie Cut: This classic short haircut is perfect for long faces because it adds volume to the sides of your face and can be styled in a variety of ways.
  • Bob Cut: A bob cut that grazes the chin can help balance out your features and add volume to your hair.
  • Layered Cut: A layered haircut can help add texture and volume to your hair, which can help balance out a long face.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of your face and add volume to the sides of your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Short Haircut

Getting a short haircut can be nerve-wracking, but with the right stylist and a clear idea of what you want, you can get the perfect short haircut for your long face. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research different short haircuts that would work for your long face.
  2. Find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  3. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  4. Discuss your hair type and lifestyle with your stylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  5. Let your stylist work their magic!

Tips to Maintain Your Short Hair for Long Faces

Short haircuts require maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair products designed for short haircuts.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about short haircuts for long faces:

What is the best short haircut for a long face?

The best short haircut for a long face is one that adds volume to the sides of your face and balances out your features. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts are all great options.

Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually help balance out your features and make your face appear shorter.

Can I still style my short haircut?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Long Faces

Short haircuts require different care than longer haircuts. Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

With the right short haircut and proper hair care, you can make your long face look its best. Use these tips and styles to find the perfect short haircut for your features and lifestyle.
