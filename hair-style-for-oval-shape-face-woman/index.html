Hair Style For Oval Shape Face Woman How To Choose The Perfect Look

Written by Robby Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As a woman with an oval-shaped face, you have the luxury of being able to pull off a variety of hair styles. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll go over some tips for choosing the perfect hair style for oval shape face woman, as well as some popular styles to consider.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hair styles, it's important to make sure you know your face shape. To determine if you have an oval face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are relatively similar and your face is slightly longer than it is wide, you likely have an oval-shaped face.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Style

When choosing a hair style for oval shape face woman, keep these tips in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in which styles will work best for you. If you have curly or thick hair, a shorter cut can help lighten the weight of your hair and make it more manageable. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, longer styles can help create the illusion of more volume.

Emphasize Your Best Features

Oval faces are often considered the most versatile because they have balanced proportions. That being said, you can still emphasize your best features with your hair style. For example, if you have a nice jawline, consider a chin-length bob to draw attention to it.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your hair style should also fit your lifestyle. If you're constantly on the go and don't have time for frequent salon visits, a low-maintenance style like a lob (long bob) or layered cut can be a great option.

Popular Hair Styles for Oval Shape Face Woman

Now that you have some tips for choosing the perfect hair style, let's take a look at some popular options:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for oval faces because they add movement and dimension to your hair. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair textures.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a longer forehead and draw attention to your eyes. They can be worn with a variety of hair lengths and styles.

Pixie Cut

For those who want a low-maintenance option, a pixie cut can be a great choice. This short style can help accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob, also known as a lob, is a classic style that works well with oval faces. This style can be worn straight or with loose waves.

FAQ

Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes, oval faces are well-suited for short hair styles like pixie cuts or bobs.

What if I have a rounder oval face?

If you have a rounder oval face, opt for hair styles that create angles and lengthen your features. Side-swept bangs and longer hair styles can also help balance out a rounder face.

Can I still wear bangs with an oval face?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and draw attention to your best features.

Hair Care Tips

No matter which hair style you choose, it's important to take care of your locks. Here are some tips:

Use Heat Protectant

If you frequently use hot tools like flat irons or curling wands, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use dry shampoo in between washes if needed.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Choosing the perfect hair style for oval shape face woman doesn't have to be difficult. Keep these tips in mind, try out different styles, and don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect look for you.
