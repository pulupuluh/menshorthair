Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Eveline May 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to do easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair that will give you a fresh new look. Whether you're going to work or a night out with friends, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways. It's not too short that you can't do anything with it, and it's not too long that it becomes difficult to manage. With the right tools and products, you can create easy hairstyles that will take your look to the next level.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to know the basic steps for styling shoulder length hair. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it first.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make styling easier.
  • Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to keep your hair in place.

Step by Step

1. Beach Waves

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that are perfect for summer or any time of year. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Divide your hair into sections.
  2. Take a curling iron or wand and curl each section away from your face.
  3. Once you've curled all of your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the waves.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to give your hair that beachy look.

2. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic look that's easy to do. Here's how:

  1. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie.
  2. Fluff up the ponytail to give it volume.
  3. Finish with a hairspray to keep it in place.

3. Braided Updo

If you're looking for a more formal hairstyle, the braided updo is a great option. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a section of hair from one side of your head and braid it.
  2. Repeat on the other side.
  3. Take the two braids and twist them together, securing with bobby pins.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

  • Invest in good quality styling tools for best results.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

What are some easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair include beach waves, half up half down, and braided updos.

What tools do I need to style shoulder length hair?

You will need a curling iron or wand, a hair tie, bobby pins, and a hairspray or other styling product.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you can switch up your look in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun!
