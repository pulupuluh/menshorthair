How To Make Your Face Look Wider Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Feb 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a narrow or long face, you may want to try some techniques to make it look wider. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks to help you achieve a wider face shape.

Description

A wide face is considered to be a desirable facial feature in many cultures. A wider face is often associated with youthfulness, health, and attractiveness. However, not everyone is born with a wide face. If you have a narrow or long face, you may feel self-conscious about it. Fortunately, there are some techniques that you can use to make your face look wider.

How to Make Your Face Look Wider – Step by Step

1. Use Contouring Makeup

Contouring makeup is a great way to make your face look wider. Use a matte bronzer or contour powder to shade the sides of your face. Apply the contour powder along your jawline, temples, and the side of your forehead. Blend the powder well to create a natural-looking shadow.

2. Get a Haircut

Your hairstyle can make a big difference in the shape of your face. If you have a narrow face, try getting a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your head. A layered haircut or a bob with side-swept bangs can make your face look wider.

3. Wear Earrings

Wearing earrings can help to create the illusion of a wider face. Choose earrings that are wider at the bottom and narrower at the top. Hoop earrings, chandelier earrings, and teardrop earrings are great options.

4. Wear Glasses

Glasses can also help to make your face look wider. Choose glasses with wide frames that sit high on your nose. The frames should be wider than your cheeks to create the illusion of a wider face.

5. Smile

When you smile, the muscles in your cheeks and jawline become more prominent. This can help to make your face look wider. Practice smiling in front of a mirror to see how it affects the shape of your face.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Wider

1. Use Light-Colored Lipstick

Dark lipstick can make your lips look smaller and your face look narrower. Use light-colored lipstick to make your lips look fuller and your face look wider.

2. Avoid Long Hair

Long hair can make your face look longer and narrower. Try a shorter haircut or a medium-length hairstyle to create the illusion of a wider face.

3. Use Highlighter

Highlighter can help to create the illusion of a wider face. Apply highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. This will help to create a glowy, wide-eyed look.

FAQ About Making Your Face Look Wider

Q. Can I make my face look wider without makeup?

A. Yes, there are several ways to make your face look wider without makeup. You can try a new hairstyle, wear glasses with wide frames, or smile more often.

Q. Can losing weight make my face look narrower?

A. Yes, losing weight can make your face look narrower. When you lose weight, you may lose fat from your cheeks and jawline, which can make your face look thinner.

Q. How can I make my face look wider permanently?

A. There are some surgical procedures that can make your face look wider permanently. However, these procedures are invasive and expensive, and should only be considered as a last resort.

Hair Care for a Wider Face

In addition to the techniques mentioned above, taking good care of your hair can also help to make your face look wider. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look wider and feel more confident in your appearance.
