How To Style Short Pixie Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short pixie hairstyles are one of the most versatile hairstyles out there. They can be edgy, playful, or elegant, depending on how you style them. But figuring out how to style short pixie hairstyles can be challenging, especially if you're new to the cut. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about styling short pixie hairstyles, from how to do it to some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Description

Short pixie hairstyles are a popular choice for women who want to make a bold statement with their hair. This cut is characterized by short layers that are cut close to the scalp, giving the hair a textured, choppy look. Pixie cuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked-back strands to messy, tousled locks. However, the key to styling a short pixie cut is to work with your hair's natural texture and shape.

How to Style Short Pixie Hairstyles

If you're new to styling short pixie hairstyles, don't worry. The process is simple and straightforward. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil buildup and leave your hair feeling soft and hydrated. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Once you've washed your hair, gently towel dry it to remove any excess water. Don't rub your hair too hard, as this can cause breakage and damage. Instead, use a soft, microfiber towel to blot your hair dry.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This will help add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style. Choose a product that works with your hair's natural texture and thickness. For example, if you have fine hair, opt for a lightweight mousse or gel. If you have thick, curly hair, go for a heavier styling cream or pomade.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've applied your styling product, it's time to style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create the look you want. You can slick your hair back for a sleek, polished look, or tousle it for a more playful, messy vibe. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any flyaways. Just be sure not to overdo it, as too much hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair, as this will help prevent breakage.
  • Don't be afraid to play with your hair's natural texture and shape.
  • Try adding a pop of color to your hair for a fun, playful look.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short pixie hairstyles:

Q: Can short pixie hairstyles work for all hair types?

A: Yes, short pixie hairstyles can work for all hair types. However, the styling process may vary depending on your hair's natural texture and thickness.

Q: Do I need to use heat styling tools to style my short pixie cut?

A: No, you don't need to use heat styling tools to style your short pixie cut. In fact, using too much heat can damage your hair and cause breakage. Instead, work with your hair's natural texture and shape.

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie cut?

A: This will depend on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, if you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Hairstyles

To keep your short pixie cut looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short pixie cut with confidence and ease. Remember to have fun with your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
