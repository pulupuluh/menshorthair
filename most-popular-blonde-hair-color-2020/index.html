The Most Popular Blonde Hair Color Of 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post about the most popular blonde hair color of 2020! In this article, we’ll provide you with all the information you need to know about this hair color trend. From how to achieve it to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Most Popular Blonde Hair Color of 2020
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair Color
  • FAQs about the Most Popular Blonde Hair Color of 2020
  • Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

Description

The most popular blonde hair color of 2020 is the “icy blonde” look. This hair color trend involves platinum blonde hair with cool undertones. It’s a fresh and modern take on the classic blonde look, and it’s perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

Icy blonde hair is achieved by using a high-lift hair color or bleach to lighten the hair to a pale yellow color. Then, a toner is applied to neutralize any unwanted warm tones and create a cool, ashy finish. It’s important to note that this hair color trend is high maintenance and requires regular salon visits to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.

How to Achieve the Most Popular Blonde Hair Color of 2020

If you’re interested in achieving the most popular blonde hair color of 2020, it’s important to go to a professional stylist who specializes in blonde hair color. They will be able to assess your hair and recommend the best course of action to achieve the desired look while keeping your hair healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the most popular blonde hair color of 2020:

  • Step 1: Lighten your hair to a pale yellow color using a high-lift hair color or bleach. This step is crucial to achieving the desired icy blonde look.
  • Step 2: Apply a toner to neutralize any unwanted warm tones and create a cool, ashy finish.
  • Step 3: Maintain the color by using color-safe shampoo and conditioner and visiting your stylist regularly for touch-ups.

Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you maintain your blonde hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from fading and becoming brassy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as heat can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups to keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant.

FAQs about the Most Popular Blonde Hair Color of 2020

Here are some frequently asked questions about the most popular blonde hair color of 2020:

Q: Can I achieve this hair color trend at home?

A: It’s not recommended to try to achieve this hair color trend at home. It’s a high maintenance hair color that requires the expertise of a professional stylist.

Q: Will this hair color trend work for all skin tones?

A: This hair color trend works best on those with cool or neutral skin tones. If you have warm undertones, a different blonde hair color trend may be more suitable for you.

Q: How often do I need to visit my stylist to maintain this hair color trend?

A: You’ll need to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain this hair color trend and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

Here are some hair care tips specifically for blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming brassy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as heat can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

We hope you found this comprehensive guide to the most popular blonde hair color of 2020 helpful. Remember, it’s important to go to a professional stylist who specializes in blonde hair color to achieve the desired look while keeping your hair healthy.
