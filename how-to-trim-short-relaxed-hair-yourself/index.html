How To Trim Short Relaxed Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short relaxed hair, you know how important it is to keep it trimmed and healthy. Going to the salon can be expensive, but with a little practice, you can learn to trim your own hair at home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to trim short relaxed hair yourself, along with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Trimming short relaxed hair is important to maintain a healthy look and feel. It is also a great way to save money on salon visits. In this guide, we will show you how to trim short relaxed hair yourself, so you can keep your hair looking its best.

How To

To trim short relaxed hair yourself, you will need a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to hold hair in place

It's important to use sharp scissors so you don't end up with split ends or uneven cuts. A comb will help you section your hair, and clips will keep the sections in place.

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to trim your short relaxed hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then let it air dry or blow dry it.

  2. Use the comb to part your hair down the middle, then comb each side forward.

  3. Clip the hair at the crown of your head out of the way.

  4. Starting at the back of your head, divide your hair into small sections.

  5. Comb each section straight down, then trim off the ends using the sharp scissors.

  6. Continue working your way around your head, taking small sections and trimming the ends.

  7. Once you've finished trimming the back, unclip the hair at the crown of your head and trim the ends.

  8. Check for any uneven spots or longer strands, and trim as needed.

  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and trim gradually. You can always trim more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head, or ask a friend to help you.
  • Don't trim your hair when it's wet, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming short relaxed hair:

How often should I trim my short relaxed hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

No, you should always trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair will look shorter when it dries.

Do I need special scissors to trim my hair?

Yes, it's important to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Using dull scissors can lead to split ends and uneven cuts.

Hair Care

After trimming your short relaxed hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can easily trim your short relaxed hair yourself and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
