Ways To Tie Hair Back For Guys A Guide For A Trendy Look

Written by William Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you know how challenging it can be to keep it out of your face. Whether you're hitting the gym, going out with friends, or just lounging at home, you need to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Luckily, there are many ways to tie hair back for guys. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to tie your hair back and keep it looking stylish.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Tie Hair Back for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Tying Your Hair Back
  • Tips for Tying Hair Back for Guys
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Guys with Long Hair

How to Tie Hair Back for Guys

There are many ways to tie hair back for guys, but some of the most popular include:

  • Ponytail
  • Man Bun
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Braid
  • Top Knot

Each of these styles has its unique look and requires different techniques to accomplish. You can choose the one that best suits your hair length and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Tying Your Hair Back

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to tie hair back for guys:

Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic or scrunchie.
  4. Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness.


Man Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it forms a tight coil.
  3. Wrap the coil around its base to form a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair elastic or pins.


Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic or scrunchie.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.


Braid

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Divide the ponytail into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair elastic.


Top Knot

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it forms a tight coil.
  3. Wrap the coil around its base to form a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair elastic or pins.


Tips for Tying Hair Back for Guys

Here are some tips to keep in mind when tying your hair back:

  • Use a hair elastic or scrunchie that matches your hair color.
  • Avoid tying your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider using styling products like hair gel or hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're struggling to tie your hair back.


Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about tying hair back for guys:

Q: Can I tie my hair back if it's short?

A: Yes, you can still tie your hair back if it's short. Try a half-up half-down style or a small ponytail at the back of your head.

Q: Can I tie my hair back if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can tie your hair back if it's curly. Just be sure to use a hair elastic or scrunchie that won't damage your curls.

Q: How often should I tie my hair back?

A: It's best to avoid tying your hair back every day, as this can cause breakage and damage. Instead, try leaving your hair loose every other day.


Hair Care Tips for Guys with Long Hair

If you have long hair, it's essential to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for guys:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.


With these tips and techniques, you can tie your hair back in a variety of ways and keep it looking stylish and trendy. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
