How Fast Can Hair Grow With Rice Water The Secret To Long And Healthy Hair

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of trying various hair growth products and treatments that promise to give you long and healthy hair but fail to deliver results? If yes, then you need to try rice water. Yes, you heard it right – rice water, the secret to long and healthy hair. In this article, we will discuss how fast can hair grow with rice water and how you can use it to achieve your dream hair.

Description

Rice water is the starchy water left over after soaking or boiling rice. It is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that nourish and strengthen hair, making it grow faster and healthier. Rice water has been used for centuries by women in China, Japan, and Southeast Asia to maintain long and healthy hair.

Using rice water for hair growth is a natural and inexpensive way to achieve long and healthy hair. It is suitable for all hair types, including curly, straight, thick, and thin hair. Rice water contains inositol, a carbohydrate that repairs damaged hair and strengthens hair roots, reducing hair fall and promoting hair growth. Regular use of rice water can make your hair stronger, smoother, and shinier.

How to Use Rice Water for Hair Growth

There are several ways to use rice water for hair growth. You can use it as a hair rinse, hair mask, or hair spray. The most common method is to use rice water as a hair rinse. Here’s how to do it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water

Directions:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly to remove any dirt or impurities.
  2. Add water to the rice and let it soak for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Strain the rice and collect the rice water in a bowl.
  4. Use the rice water as a final rinse after shampooing your hair.
  5. Massage your scalp and hair with the rice water for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.

You can also add a few drops of your favorite essential oil to the rice water for a pleasant fragrance.

Step by Step Guide to Use Rice Water for Hair Growth

If you want to use rice water as a hair mask or hair spray, here’s a step by step guide:

As a Hair Mask:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and towel dry.
  2. Apply the rice water to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.
  3. Massage your scalp and hair for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Cover your hair with a shower cap and leave the rice water on for 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water and condition as usual.

As a Hair Spray:

  1. Add the rice water to a spray bottle.
  2. Spray the rice water on your hair and scalp.
  3. Massage your scalp and hair for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Leave the rice water on for 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water and condition as usual.

Tips for Using Rice Water for Hair Growth

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from using rice water for hair growth:

  • Use organic rice to avoid harmful chemicals and pesticides.
  • Do not use hot water to rinse your hair after using rice water, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use rice water once or twice a week to avoid overloading your hair with protein.
  • Combine rice water with other natural ingredients like honey, aloe vera, or coconut oil for added benefits.
  • Be patient and consistent. It may take a few weeks or months to see significant results.

FAQs

How often should I use rice water for hair growth?

You can use rice water once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and condition. Overuse of rice water can cause protein overload, leading to hair breakage and damage.

Can I use rice water on color-treated hair?

Yes, you can use rice water on color-treated hair. It is safe and gentle on all hair types.

How long does it take to see results from using rice water for hair growth?

You may see results within a few weeks or months, depending on your hair type and condition. Consistency is key to achieving long and healthy hair.

Hair Care Tips for Long and Healthy Hair

Using rice water for hair growth is just one part of your hair care routine. Here are some additional tips to help you achieve long and healthy hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to reduce stress and improve blood circulation to the scalp.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.

So, how fast can hair grow with rice water? The answer is – it depends on your hair type and condition. However, regular use of rice water can make your hair grow faster and healthier, giving you the long and beautiful hair you’ve always dreamed of.
