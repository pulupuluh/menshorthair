How To Pick The Best Hair Color For Your Face

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many hues to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. In this article, we will guide you through the process of picking the best hair color for your face. From determining your skin tone to understanding your undertones, we will cover everything you need to know to find the perfect shade for you.

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Understanding Your Undertones
  • Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking the right hair color is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone refers to the color of your skin, and it can be categorized into three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral.

If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have a yellow or golden undertone. If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have a pink or blue undertone. If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear both green and blue, you have a neutral skin tone.

Understanding Your Undertones

Once you have determined your skin tone, it is important to understand your undertones. Undertones are the colors that come through your skin and affect the overall look of your complexion.

If you have a warm skin tone, your undertones will be yellow, peach, or gold. If you have a cool skin tone, your undertones will be pink, red, or blue. If you have a neutral skin tone, your undertones will be a mix of warm and cool colors.

Understanding your undertones is crucial in picking the right hair color, as it will help you choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty.

Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you have determined your skin tone and undertones, it is time to choose the right hair color. There are several factors to consider when picking a hair color, including:

  • The level of contrast between your hair color and skin tone
  • Your eye color
  • Your natural hair color
  • Your lifestyle

If you have a warm skin tone, consider shades like golden blonde, honey blonde, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, consider shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or blue-black. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with a variety of shades.

When picking a hair color, it is important to consider your eye color as well. If you have blue eyes, consider shades like platinum blonde or ash brown. If you have green eyes, consider shades like copper or golden blonde. If you have brown eyes, consider shades like chocolate brown or auburn.

Your natural hair color can also play a role in picking the right hair color. If you have naturally dark hair, consider going a few shades lighter to add dimension. If you have naturally light hair, consider going a few shades darker to add depth.

Finally, your lifestyle should also be considered when picking a hair color. If you have a busy lifestyle, consider a low-maintenance color that will require minimal upkeep. If you have more time to dedicate to your hair, consider a high-maintenance color that will require more frequent touch-ups.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen the right hair color, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips for keeping your colored hair looking its best:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Avoid washing your hair every day
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can help extend the life of your hair color and keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a drastically different color than my natural hair color?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a drastically different color than your natural hair color. However, it is important to consider the maintenance and upkeep required for the new color. You may need to touch up your roots more frequently or use special products to maintain the color.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. However, it is important to do a patch test before applying the dye to your hair to ensure that you do not have an allergic reaction.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: The frequency of root touch-ups will depend on the color of your hair and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, root touch-ups should be done every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair while pregnant?

A: It is generally safe to dye your hair while pregnant, but it is important to consult with your doctor before doing so. Some doctors may recommend waiting until after the first trimester to dye your hair.

Picking the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By understanding your skin tone, undertones, and other factors, you can choose a shade that enhances your natural beauty and complements your lifestyle. With proper care and maintenance, your colored hair can look healthy and vibrant for weeks to come.
