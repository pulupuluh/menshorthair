How To Get Curly Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a boy with straight hair, you may be looking for ways to add some curls to your locks. Whether you want tighter curls or looser waves, there are several methods you can try to achieve the look you want. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get curly boy hair, from styling tips to hair care advice.

Table of Content

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Curly Boy Hair

There are a few different ways you can get curly boy hair, depending on your hair type and the style you're going for. Here are some methods to try:

  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Using hot rollers
  • Getting a perm
  • Using a texturizing product

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, and the right choice for you will depend on your hair type, the amount of time you have to style your hair, and the look you're trying to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curly boy hair using a curling iron or wand:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as normal.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron or wand.
  4. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  5. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting at the base of the hair and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Hold the hair on the curling iron or wand for a few seconds, then release.
  7. Repeat with each section of hair until you've curled all of your hair.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to help your curls hold their shape.

Remember, the key to getting curly boy hair with a curling iron or wand is to take your time and work in small sections. You may need to experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair type and the look you're going for.

Tips for Curly Hair

Once you've got your curly boy hair, here are a few tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to help keep your curls looking their best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly boy hair:

Can I get curly hair without using heat?

Yes, there are natural methods for getting curly hair, such as using bantu knots, twist outs, or flexi rods. However, these methods may take longer to achieve the desired look and may not work for all hair types.

How long will my curls last?

This will depend on the method you use and how well you care for your hair. Curls achieved with a curling iron or wand may last until your next wash, while curls from a perm can last for several months.

Can I still straighten my hair if I have curly boy hair?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have curly boy hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat tools too frequently to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly boy hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly locks:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to help keep your curls looking their best.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant when you do.

By following these tips and using the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve and maintain curly boy hair that looks great and feels healthy.
