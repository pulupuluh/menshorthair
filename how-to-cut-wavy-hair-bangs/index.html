How To Cut Wavy Hair Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Wavy hair can be tricky to manage, especially when it comes to cutting bangs. But don't worry! With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a beautiful and effortless look. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting wavy hair bangs from start to finish. Follow our tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look!

How to Cut Wavy Hair Bangs

Cutting bangs can be intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. Follow these steps to cut wavy hair bangs:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Clips to section off your hair

Step 2: Choose Your Bang Style

There are different styles of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Straight across bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Curtain bangs
  • Micro bangs

Choose the style that best suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Use clips to section off the hair you want to cut. Make sure you only cut the hair you want to cut and not any other hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Use your comb to comb your hair straight down. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure you cut the hair slowly and carefully.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your bangs are even and straight.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to cutting wavy hair bangs:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Choose Your Bang Style

Choose the style of bangs you want to cut. Look for inspiration online or talk to your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Use clips to section off the hair you want to cut. Make sure you only cut the hair you want to cut and not any other hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Use your comb to comb your hair straight down. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure you cut the hair slowly and carefully.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your bangs are even and straight.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you cut wavy hair bangs:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more later.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't push yourself too hard.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you if you're feeling nervous.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different bang styles.

FAQ

Can I cut my own bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

How often should I cut my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs become too long. On average, most people cut their bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?

It's best to cut your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may result in uneven bangs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your wavy hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut wavy hair bangs like a pro. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and have fun with your new look!
