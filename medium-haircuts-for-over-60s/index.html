Medium Haircuts For Over 60S A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Bardi Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness, and we may feel like we are limited in our choices for hairstyles. However, there are plenty of medium haircuts for over 60s that are both stylish and flattering. In this guide, we will cover how to choose the right haircut, step-by-step instructions for getting the perfect cut, tips for styling and caring for your hair, and frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When considering a new haircut, it's important to take into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For over 60s, medium haircuts are a great choice as they offer versatility and ease of styling without sacrificing length. Some popular medium haircuts for over 60s include:

  • Layered bob
  • Shoulder-length shag
  • Long bob (lob)
  • Blunt cut with side-swept bangs

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will work best for you based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Getting the Perfect Cut

When getting a haircut, it's important to communicate clearly with your hairstylist to ensure you get the desired result. Follow these steps to get the perfect medium haircut:

  1. Bring in pictures of the haircut you want.
  2. Communicate your desired length and style.
  3. Ask for layers to add movement and texture.
  4. Request side-swept bangs for a youthful look.
  5. Ensure your hairstylist uses sharp scissors for a clean cut.

Remember, communication is key to getting the perfect haircut.

Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Hair

Once you have your new medium haircut, it's important to style and care for it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to thinning hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush for added volume and lift.
  • Use a root-lifting spray to add even more volume.
  • Experiment with accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be beautiful at any age. Consider a shoulder-length shag or layered bob to show off your curls. Be sure to use products designed for curly hair to keep your curls defined and moisturized.

What if I have thinning hair?

Many women experience thinning hair as they age. A layered bob or long bob can add volume and movement to thinning hair. Consider using a volumizing spray or root-lifting spray to add even more volume.

What if I want to keep my hair long?

Long hair can be beautiful at any age, but it's important to keep it healthy and well-maintained. Consider adding layers for movement and texture, and side-swept bangs for a youthful touch.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important at any age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools when possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful at any age.
