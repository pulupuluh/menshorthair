How To Style Thin Frizzy Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Jun 02, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin, frizzy, wavy hair, it can be a challenge to find the right styling techniques to give your hair the volume and definition it needs. But with the right tips and techniques, you can transform your hair into a stylish and manageable look that you’ll love.

How to Style Thin Frizzy Wavy Hair

To style thin frizzy wavy hair, you need to follow a few simple steps:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it’s about 80% dry.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a volumizing mousse or gel to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and control frizz.

3. Blow dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, lifting the hair as you go to add volume.

4. Add waves

Using a curling iron or wand, add loose waves to your hair. Start at the mid-lengths and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light-hold hairspray, focusing on the roots to add extra volume and hold.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style thin frizzy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a volumizing mousse or gel to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and control frizz.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair in sections using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, lifting the hair as you go to add volume.

Step 4: Add waves

Using a curling iron or wand, add loose waves to your hair. Start at the mid-lengths and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray a light-hold hairspray all over your hair, focusing on the roots to add extra volume and hold.

Tips for Managing Thin Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you manage your thin frizzy wavy hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and minimize frizz.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools every day

Using heat styling tools every day can damage your hair, making it more prone to frizz and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools to 2-3 times a week.

3. Use a heat protectant

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to minimize damage and keep your hair healthier.

4. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair, so it won’t cause as much damage as a cotton pillowcase.

FAQs

Q. Can I use a straightener instead of a curling iron?

A. Yes, you can use a straightener to add waves to your hair. Simply clamp the straightener around a section of hair and twist it as you glide the straightener down the hair.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying and damage.

Q. What type of shampoo and conditioner should I use?

A. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume and control frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your thin frizzy wavy hair looking its best:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair healthier and more manageable.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid over-brushing

Brushing your hair too much can cause damage and breakage, so try to limit your brushing to once or twice a day.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to help protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

By following these tips and techniques, you can transform your thin frizzy wavy hair into a stylish and manageable look that you’ll love.
