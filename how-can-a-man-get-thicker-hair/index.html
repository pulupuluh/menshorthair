How Can A Man Get Thicker Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a man who's been struggling with thinning hair or hair loss? Don't worry, you're not alone. In fact, studies show that over 50% of men will experience some form of hair loss by the age of 50. But don't lose hope yet. There are plenty of ways to get thicker hair and boost your confidence. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve a fuller, thicker head of hair.

  • How to Get Thicker Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Get Thicker Hair

There are several ways to get thicker hair, some of which include:

  • Eating a balanced diet
  • Using hair thickening products
  • Stimulating hair growth with scalp massages
  • Reducing stress
  • Taking hair growth supplements

Let's dive deeper into each of these methods and how they can help you get thicker hair.

Eating a Balanced Diet

One of the easiest ways to get thicker hair is by eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals. Some of the most important nutrients for hair growth include:

  • Protein
  • Vitamin A
  • Vitamin C
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin E
  • Vitamin B6
  • Vitamin B12
  • Zinc
  • Iron

You can find these nutrients in foods such as eggs, fish, nuts, spinach, sweet potatoes, and berries.

Using Hair Thickening Products

Another way to get thicker hair is by using hair thickening products such as shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products work by coating the hair shaft and adding volume to your hair.

Look for products that contain ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen, as these can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

Stimulating Hair Growth with Scalp Massages

Did you know that massaging your scalp can actually help stimulate hair growth? This is because scalp massages increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth.

To give yourself a scalp massage, use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

Reducing Stress

Stress can have a negative impact on your overall health, including your hair. In fact, studies show that stress can cause hair loss and thinning.

To reduce stress, try practicing relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.

Taking Hair Growth Supplements

Finally, you can also take hair growth supplements to help get thicker hair. Some of the most popular supplements for hair growth include biotin, collagen, and saw palmetto.

However, it's important to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements, as they can interact with certain medications and have side effects.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you get thicker hair:

  1. Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals
  2. Use hair thickening products such as shampoos and conditioners
  3. Massage your scalp daily to stimulate hair growth
  4. Reduce stress through relaxation techniques
  5. Talk to your doctor about taking hair growth supplements

Useful Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get thicker hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair
  • Wash your hair every other day instead of every day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids, as these can pull on your hair and cause damage
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Can hair loss be reversed?

While there's no cure for hair loss, it can be reversed in some cases with the right treatment. Talk to your doctor or a hair loss specialist to determine the best course of treatment for you.

Can stress cause hair loss?

Yes, stress can cause hair loss and thinning. This is because stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to hair shedding.

What should I look for in hair thickening products?

Look for products that contain ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen, as these can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Once you've achieved thicker hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and volume. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to prevent hair breakage
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, healthier hair and boost your confidence. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll see results in no time.
