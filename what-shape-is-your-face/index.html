What Shape Is Your Face A Comprehensive Guide To Determine Your Facial Shape

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Knowing your facial shape is important to determine the most flattering hairstyles, makeup looks, and even eyeglasses that will complement your features. In this guide, we will help you determine what shape is your face and provide tips and tricks on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Facial Shape
  • Different Types of Facial Shapes
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Facial Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Determine Your Facial Shape

The first step in determining your facial shape is to tie your hair back and stand in front of a mirror with good lighting. Look straight ahead and take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.

Measure your face with a tape measure to get accurate measurements. You can also trace the outline of your face on a piece of paper and compare it to the different facial shapes to determine which one matches yours.

Step by Step Guide:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with good lighting and tie your hair back.
  2. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.
  3. Measure your face with a tape measure to get accurate measurements or trace the outline of your face on a piece of paper.
  4. Compare your measurements or traced outline to the different facial shapes to determine which one matches yours.

Different Types of Facial Shapes

There are six different types of facial shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Below are the characteristics of each shape:

  • Oval: The length of the face is greater than the width with a rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Round: The width and length of the face are almost equal with a round hairline and jawline.
  • Square: The width and length of the face are almost equal with a square hairline and jawline.
  • Heart: The forehead is wider than the cheeks and the jawline is pointed.
  • Diamond: The face is widest at the cheekbones with a pointed hairline and jawline.
  • Oblong: The face is longer than it is wide with a straight hairline and jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Your Facial Shape

Once you have determined your facial shape, you can now choose hairstyles that will flatter your features. Here are some hair care tips for each facial shape:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle will look great on an oval-shaped face.
  • Round: Long, layered hairstyles will elongate the face while short hairstyles should be avoided.
  • Square: Soft, wavy hairstyles will soften the angles of a square-shaped face.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and chin-length hairstyles will balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: Short hairstyles and voluminous hairstyles will complement a diamond-shaped face.
  • Oblong: Long hairstyles with layers will add width to an oblong-shaped face.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I measure my face shape?

To measure your face shape, use a tape measure to measure the length and width of your face. You can also trace the outline of your face on a piece of paper and compare it to the different facial shapes to determine which one matches yours.

What is the most common facial shape?

The most common facial shape is oval, which is considered to be the most versatile and flattering shape.

Can I change my facial shape?

No, you cannot change your facial shape. However, you can enhance your natural beauty by choosing hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your features.

What kind of glasses should I wear for my facial shape?

For an oval-shaped face, any type of glasses will look great. For other facial shapes, choose glasses that complement your features. Round glasses will soften the angles of a square-shaped face while angular glasses will complement a round-shaped face.

Conclusion

Knowing your facial shape is important to determine the most flattering hairstyles, makeup looks, and even eyeglasses that will complement your features. By following the steps in this guide, you can easily determine what shape is your face and enhance your natural beauty with the right hair care tips.
