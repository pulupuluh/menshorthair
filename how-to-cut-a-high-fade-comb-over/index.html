How To Cut A High Fade Comb Over Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting hair can be quite a challenging task, especially if you are trying to achieve a specific hairstyle. One such hairstyle is the high fade comb over, which has been gaining popularity in recent years. It is a versatile and stylish look that suits all face shapes and hair types. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a high fade comb over in relaxed English language. This guide is perfect for beginners and anyone who wants to learn the basics of cutting hair.

Description

A high fade comb over is a haircut that features short sides and back that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. The hair on the top is then styled to one side, creating a sleek and sophisticated look. It is a popular hairstyle among men of all ages and is perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

How to Cut a High Fade Comb Over: Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to achieve a high fade comb over:

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

Before you start cutting hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, clippers, comb, spray bottle, and cape.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner and dry it with a towel. Use a spray bottle to wet the hair and comb it thoroughly.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Use the clippers to cut the sides and back of the hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually switch to a shorter one as you move up. Blend the hair using an upward motion to achieve a smooth transition to the longer hair on top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top. Start from the front and work your way to the back. Leave enough length to create the comb-over look. Use a razor or texturizing shears to add texture and volume to the hair.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Apply a pomade or wax to the hair and comb it to one side. Use a blow dryer to set the style and add volume to the hair. Finish with a hairspray to hold the hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high fade comb over:

  • Start with a longer guard and gradually switch to a shorter one to achieve a smooth blend
  • Use scissors to cut the hair on the top for more control and precision
  • Add texture and volume to the hair using a razor or texturizing shears
  • Use a pomade or wax to style the hair and a hairspray to hold it in place

FAQ

How long should the hair be for a high fade comb over?

The hair on the top should be at least 2 to 3 inches long, while the sides and back should be gradually faded to a shorter length.

Can anyone get a high fade comb over?

Yes, anyone can get a high fade comb over. It is a versatile haircut that suits all face shapes and hair types.

How often should I get a high fade comb over?

You should get a high fade comb over every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the style and keep the hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

Maintaining a high fade comb over requires regular washing and styling. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on the hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair. Trim the hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

A high fade comb over is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be achieved with a few simple steps. Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect high fade comb over. Remember to use the right tools and products and maintain the hair regularly to keep it looking fresh and stylish.
