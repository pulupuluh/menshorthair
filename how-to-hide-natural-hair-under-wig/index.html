How To Hide Natural Hair Under Wig

Description

If you are someone who loves to wear wigs or someone who needs to wear them for medical reasons, you may be wondering how to hide your natural hair under a wig. Hiding natural hair under a wig can be a tricky task, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done easily. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to hide your natural hair under a wig, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Hide Natural Hair Under Wig

The first step in hiding your natural hair under a wig is to make sure that your hair is as flat as possible. This can be done by braiding your hair or using a wig cap. Once your hair is flat, you can then put on the wig. To ensure that the wig looks natural, it is important to make sure that the wig is the right size and that it is placed correctly on your head. You can also use bobby pins or wig clips to secure the wig in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to hide your natural hair under a wig:

  1. Braid your hair or use a wig cap to flatten your hair as much as possible.
  2. Put on the wig and adjust it so that it is the right size and is placed correctly on your head.
  3. Use bobby pins or wig clips to secure the wig in place.
  4. If necessary, use a wig grip or wig tape to keep the wig in place.
  5. Style the wig as desired.

Tips for Hiding Natural Hair Under Wig

Here are some tips for hiding your natural hair under a wig:

  • Choose a wig that is the right size and is made from a material that is comfortable for you.
  • Use a wig cap or braid your hair to keep it as flat as possible.
  • Use bobby pins or wig clips to secure the wig in place.
  • Use a wig grip or wig tape if necessary.
  • Style the wig as desired.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding natural hair under a wig:

Q: Can I wear a wig if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a wig if you have short hair. You may need to use a wig cap or braid your hair to keep it as flat as possible.

Q: How do I choose the right size wig?

A: To choose the right size wig, measure the circumference of your head and compare it to the sizing chart provided by the wig manufacturer.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It is recommended that you wash your wig after 6-8 wears or when it becomes dirty or sweaty.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for wearing wigs:

  • Wear a wig cap to protect your natural hair from any potential damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or wig brush to detangle the wig.
  • Wash the wig using a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the wig as it can damage the fibers.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or in a hair net to prevent tangling and damage.

Now that you know how to hide your natural hair under a wig, you can confidently wear your wigs without worrying about anyone noticing your natural hair. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a natural-looking wig that will enhance your beauty and style.
