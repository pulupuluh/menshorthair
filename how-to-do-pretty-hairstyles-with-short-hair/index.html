How To Do Pretty Hairstyles With Short Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Short hair can sometimes feel limiting when it comes to styling, but there are actually plenty of cute and pretty hairstyles you can create with shorter locks. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are a variety of ways to switch up your look and add some flair to your hair. Here are some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions for achieving pretty hairstyles with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before diving into specific hairstyles, it's important to know some general tips for styling short hair. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Invest in some styling products: Short hair often requires some product to keep it in place and add texture. Look for a good pomade, wax, or texturizing spray.
  • Don't be afraid to use heat tools: Even if your hair is short, you can still use a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.
  • Accessorize: Hair accessories like headbands, barrettes, and clips can add some interest to your style.

Step by Step

Now, onto the hairstyles! Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving different pretty looks:

1. Braided Crown

This style works best on hair that is shoulder-length or longer. Here's how to do it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of one side and braid it.
  3. Wrap the braid over the top of your head and secure it with a bobby pin behind your ear.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. If you have longer hair, you can continue braiding and wrapping until you reach the back of your head.
  6. Secure any loose pieces with bobby pins.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

2. Messy Bun

This is a quick and easy style that works well for both casual and dressy occasions. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Wrap the coiled ponytail around the base to form a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces around your face to create a messy, undone look.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold everything in place.

3. Half-Up Twist

This style is a cute and simple way to add some interest to your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a small section of hair from the front of one side.
  2. Twist the section until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Pin the twisted section to the back of your head with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. If you want to add some more texture, use a curling iron to curl the remaining hair.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating pretty hairstyles with short hair:

  • Experiment with different partings: Changing up your part can create a completely different look.
  • Try a half-up/half-down style: This is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your locks.
  • Use a headband: Headbands can add some interest and texture to your hair, and they're especially great for those with bangs.
  • Accessorize with clips and barrettes: A well-placed clip or barrette can take your hairstyle to the next level.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

1. Can you still use heat tools on short hair?

Yes! Even if your hair is short, you can still use a curling iron, straightener, or other heat tools to create different looks.

2. What products should I use for styling short hair?

You'll want to invest in some texturizing products like pomade, wax, or spray to help add volume and hold to your hair.

3. How often should I wash my short hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference, but generally speaking, you can wash short hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your short hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

There you have it! With these tips and tricks, you can create a variety of pretty hairstyles with short hair. Whether you're heading to a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday look, there's a style for every occasion.
