How To Keep Hair Healthy After Bleaching Tips And Tricks

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Bleaching your hair can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look, but it can also be damaging to your hair. If you're not careful, you can end up with dry, brittle, and damaged hair. That's why it's important to take good care of your hair after bleaching to keep it healthy and strong. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to keep your hair healthy after bleaching.

How to Keep Hair Healthy After Bleaching

Bleaching can damage your hair by stripping it of its natural oils and moisture. To keep your hair healthy after bleaching, you need to take extra care of it. Here are some things you can do to keep your hair healthy after bleaching:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

One of the best ways to keep your hair healthy after bleaching is to use a deep conditioning treatment. This will help to restore moisture and nourishment to your hair. You can use a store-bought deep conditioner or make your own using natural ingredients like honey, avocado, and olive oil.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can further damage your hair after bleaching. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, and opt for air-drying your hair instead. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help you get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. After bleaching, your hair may be more prone to breakage, so getting regular trims can help prevent further damage.

4. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and moisture, which can be especially damaging after bleaching. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to minimize damage and keep your hair healthy.

5. Limit Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can also strip it of its natural oils and moisture. Limit the number of times you wash your hair each week, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep your hair healthy after bleaching:

Step 1: Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to restore moisture and nourishment.

Step 2: Avoid heat styling tools.

Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, and opt for air-drying your hair instead.

Step 3: Trim your hair regularly.

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and further damage.

Step 4: Use a sulfate-free shampoo.

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to minimize damage to your hair.

Step 5: Limit washing your hair.

Don't wash your hair too often, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy hair after bleaching:

1. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated throughout the day.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF protection.

3. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein-rich foods like eggs and nuts.

4. Don't brush wet hair

Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so wait until your hair is dry before brushing or combing it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How often should I deep condition my hair after bleaching?

A: You should deep condition your hair at least once a week after bleaching.

Q: Can I still dye my hair after bleaching?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair after bleaching, but you should wait at least a week and use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye.

Q: How long will it take for my hair to recover after bleaching?

A: It depends on the condition of your hair and how well you take care of it. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months for your hair to fully recover after bleaching.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a sample hair care routine for keeping your hair healthy after bleaching:

Day 1:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment and leave it in for 10-15 minutes.
  • Rinse with cool water.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner.

Day 2:

  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair.

Day 3:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment and leave it in for 10-15 minutes.
  • Rinse with cool water.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and strong after bleaching. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid heat styling, and use nourishing products to keep your hair looking its best.
