Best Hairstyle For Double Chin Fat Face

Written by Daniel May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a double chin or a fat face can be quite challenging when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. You want to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and minimizes the appearance of your double chin. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for double chin fat face, along with tips and tricks to help you look and feel your best.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the best hairstyle for double chin fat face, it's important to understand your face shape. A fat face can have different shapes such as round, square, heart-shaped, or oval. The best hairstyle for double chin fat face will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. The goal is to create an illusion of a slimmer face and neck, and draw attention away from the double chin.

How to

The best hairstyle for double chin fat face is a layered haircut. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help create an illusion of a slimmer face. The layers should be long and soft, not short and choppy. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your face look wider.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve the best hairstyle for double chin fat face:

  1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting layered haircuts.
  2. Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want, and discuss it with your hairstylist.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to create long, soft layers that start at your chin and go down to the ends of your hair.
  4. Choose a hair part that flatters your face shape. A side part can help create a slimmer face.
  5. Add some volume to the crown of your head to create height, which can elongate your face.
  6. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for double chin fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Choose a hairstyle that frames your face, such as a bob or a lob.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Accessorize with earrings or a necklace to draw attention away from your double chin.
  • Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims to keep the layers looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for double chin fat face:

What is the best hair length for a double chin?

The best hair length for a double chin is chin-length or longer. Short hair can make your face look wider, while long hair can add weight to your face.

Can bangs help hide a double chin?

Yes, bangs can help hide a double chin by drawing attention to your eyes and forehead. However, make sure the bangs are soft and wispy, not blunt and heavy.

Should I avoid hairstyles with volume?

No, you can still have volume in your hair, as long as it's in the right places. Adding volume to the crown of your head can elongate your face, while volume on the sides can make your face look wider.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying and flat ironing.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for double chin fat face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember, the key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from your double chin. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best!
