Short Haircut For Round Face Female A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Daniel May 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But fear not! With the right style and technique, you can rock a short haircut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. In this article, we'll share our top tips for finding the perfect short haircut for round face females.

Description: What is a Round Face?

Before we dive into haircuts, let's define what a round face is. A round face is characterized by a circular shape, with similar measurements for the width and length of the face. The cheeks are often fuller, and the jawline is rounded rather than angular. Some famous examples of round-faced celebrities include Chrissy Teigen, Emma Stone, and Selena Gomez.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Face Female

The key to finding the right short haircut for a round face is to create balance and elongate the face shape. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider the Length

Short haircuts can be incredibly flattering for round faces, but it's essential to find the right length. A haircut that's too short can make your face appear rounder, while a cut that's too long can overwhelm your features. Aim for a length that falls between your chin and your shoulders.

Try Angled Bangs

Angled bangs can help to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. Consider getting side-swept bangs that angle down towards your cheekbone, or try a longer curtain bang that frames your face.

Opt for Layers

Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can be especially flattering for round faces. Ask your stylist to add subtle layers throughout your haircut, or try a choppy, layered pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Face Female

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a short haircut, follow these steps:

Step 1: Do Your Research

Before heading to the salon, do some research and find inspiration photos of short haircuts for round faces. Consider what length, style, and color you want, and bring these photos to your stylist as a reference.

Step 2: Find a Skilled Stylist

When it comes to short haircuts, it's crucial to find a stylist who has experience with your hair type and face shape. Look for reviews and recommendations online, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their credentials and experience level.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Stylist

During your consultation, be sure to communicate your wants and needs with your stylist. Let them know what you like and don't like, and be open to their suggestions and advice.

Step 4: Enjoy Your New Look!

Once your haircut is complete, take some time to enjoy your new style. Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help you care for your new 'do:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking sharp, you'll need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.

Invest in Quality Products

Invest in high-quality styling products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This will help you to achieve your desired style and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Additionally, avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face is one that creates balance and elongates the face shape. Consider a layered bob, a choppy pixie cut, or a long bob with angled bangs.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a round face?

Yes! A pixie cut can be incredibly flattering for round faces, as long as it's styled correctly. Opt for a choppy, layered pixie cut that adds volume and texture to your hair.

What should I avoid when getting a short haircut for a round face?

Avoid haircuts that are too short, as these can make your face appear rounder. Additionally, avoid blunt bangs that cut across your forehead, as these can also make your face appear wider.

Final Thoughts

With the right haircut and styling techniques, anyone with a round face can rock a short haircut with confidence. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect style for you, and don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your new look!
