What To Wear If You Have A Double Chin Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Feb 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're self-conscious about your double chin, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue, but there are ways to dress to flatter your features and feel confident in your skin. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for what to wear if you have a double chin.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dress to Flatter a Double Chin
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Dressing for a Double Chin
  • Tips for Dressing for a Double Chin
  • FAQs About Dressing for a Double Chin
  • Hair Care Tips for a Double Chin

How to Dress to Flatter a Double Chin

When dressing to flatter a double chin, the goal is to draw attention away from the chin and towards other features. Here are some general tips:

  • Choose tops with interesting necklines, like scoop necks or v-necks, to draw attention upwards.
  • Avoid turtlenecks or high-necked tops, which can emphasize the chin area.
  • Wear statement jewelry or scarves to draw attention upwards.
  • Choose tops that fit well and don't cling to the chin area.
  • Avoid tops with horizontal stripes or patterns, which can make the chin area look wider.

Choose the Right Neckline

When it comes to necklines, there are a few options that can flatter a double chin:

  • V-Necks: V-necks create a natural line that draws the eye upwards, away from the chin area. They also elongate the neck, which can make the chin area look smaller.
  • Scoop Necks: Scoop necks have a similar effect to v-necks, drawing the eye upwards and elongating the neck.
  • Off-the-Shoulder Tops: Off-the-shoulder tops can be a great option for those with a double chin. They draw attention to the shoulders and collarbone, rather than the chin area.

Avoid High-Necked Tops

High-necked tops, like turtlenecks, can emphasize the chin area and make it look larger. It's best to avoid these types of tops if you're self-conscious about your chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dressing for a Double Chin

If you're still unsure about how to dress to flatter your double chin, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a top with a flattering neckline, like a v-neck or scoop neck.
  2. Pair the top with statement jewelry or a scarf to draw attention upwards.
  3. Avoid tops that cling to the chin area.
  4. Avoid tops with horizontal stripes or patterns.
  5. Choose bottoms that flatter your body type.
  6. Consider wearing shapewear to smooth out any lumps or bumps.
  7. Pair the outfit with shoes that elongate the legs, like heels or platform shoes.
  8. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from the chin area.
  9. Apply makeup to highlight your best features and draw attention away from the chin area.
  10. Finish the look with confidence!

Tips for Dressing for a Double Chin

Here are some additional tips for dressing for a double chin:

  • Choose tops with ruching or draping, which can help camouflage any lumps or bumps.
  • Avoid wearing chokers or other necklaces that sit right on the chin area.
  • Consider wearing a blazer or jacket that hits at the hip, which can help balance out the proportions of the body.
  • Choose accessories, like handbags or shoes, that draw attention away from the chin area.
  • Avoid wearing anything that makes you feel uncomfortable or self-conscious.

FAQs About Dressing for a Double Chin

Q: Can I still wear turtlenecks if I have a double chin?

A: While it's generally best to avoid high-necked tops if you're self-conscious about your double chin, you can still wear turtlenecks if you love them! Just make sure to pair them with statement jewelry or a scarf to draw attention upwards.

Q: What types of jewelry should I wear to draw attention upwards?

A: Statement necklaces, long necklaces, and scarves can all help draw attention upwards and away from the chin area.

Q: Should I wear shapewear if I have a double chin?

A: It's up to you! If you feel more comfortable wearing shapewear to smooth out any lumps or bumps, go for it. But if you prefer not to wear shapewear, that's perfectly fine too.

Hair Care Tips for a Double Chin

Your hairstyle can also play a role in how your double chin looks. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and draws attention away from the chin area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can emphasize the chin area.
  • Consider getting face-framing layers or a side-swept fringe, which can help draw attention upwards.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the chin area, as these can make the chin area look larger.

Remember, the key to dressing for a double chin is to draw attention away from the chin area and towards other features. With these tips and tricks, you can feel confident and stylish, no matter what you wear!
