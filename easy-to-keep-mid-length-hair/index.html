Easy To Keep Mid Length Hair A Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's not too short and not too long, mid length hair may be the perfect option for you. But with our busy lives, we don't always have the time to spend hours styling our hair every day. That's why we've put together this guide to help you achieve easy to keep mid length hair.

Description

Mid length hair falls between your shoulders and your collarbone, making it a versatile and popular choice for many women. It's long enough to style in a variety of ways but short enough to be easy to maintain. With the right care and styling techniques, you can have beautiful mid length hair without spending too much time on it.

How to Achieve Easy to Keep Mid Length Hair

Follow these steps to achieve easy to keep mid length hair:

Step 1: Find the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving easy to keep mid length hair is to find the right haircut. Look for a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. A layered cut can add volume and movement to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair Regularly

Washing and conditioning your hair regularly is important for maintaining healthy mid length hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Step 3: Use Heat Protectant Products

If you're using heat styling tools like a hair dryer or straightener, it's important to use a heat protectant product to prevent damage to your hair. Apply the product to your hair before using any heat styling tools to protect it from heat damage.

Step 4: Choose Easy to Maintain Styles

When it comes to styling your mid length hair, choose styles that are easy to maintain. A simple ponytail or messy bun can be a quick and easy option for busy days. You can also try braids or twists for a more polished look.

Tips for Easy to Keep Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving easy to keep mid length hair:

Tip 1: Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can be a lifesaver when you're short on time. It can help absorb excess oil and refresh your hair between washes. Just spray it on your roots and brush it through your hair for a quick pick-me-up.

Tip 2: Embrace Your Natural Hair Texture

Your natural hair texture can work to your advantage when it comes to easy to keep mid length hair. Embrace your natural waves or curls by using a styling cream or mousse to enhance them.

Tip 3: Invest in Quality Hair Tools

Investing in quality hair tools like a good hair dryer or straightener can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your mid length hair. Look for tools that have multiple heat settings and are gentle on your hair.

FAQ

How often should I wash my mid length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Can I still style my mid length hair if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! You can add volume and texture to fine hair with the right styling products and techniques. Try using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to give your hair some oomph.

What are some easy styles for mid length hair?

Some easy styles for mid length hair include a messy bun, a simple ponytail, braids, and twists. You can also try half-up, half-down styles or accessorize with a headband or scarf.

Hair Care for Easy to Keep Mid Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and easy to keep mid length hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair. Use heat protectant products before using any heat styling tools, and choose easy to maintain styles that work for your hair type and lifestyle.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful and easy to keep mid length hair that fits your busy lifestyle.
