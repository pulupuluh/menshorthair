How To Wear Your Hair While Playing Sports Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 21, 2023 · 9 min read

Sports can be a lot of fun, but they can also be pretty tough on your hair. Between the sweat, the wind, and the constant movement, it's easy for your hair to get tangled, frizzy, or just plain messy. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to keep your hair looking great while you're playing sports. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for how to wear your hair while playing sports.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Your Hair While Playing Sports
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Your Hair While Playing Sports
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips for Active Women

How to Wear Your Hair While Playing Sports

There are plenty of ways to wear your hair while playing sports, and the best style for you will depend on your hair type, the sport you're playing, and your personal preferences. Here are some of the most popular styles:

Ponytail

A simple ponytail is a classic choice for sports. It keeps your hair out of your face and off your neck, and it's pretty easy to do. To make a ponytail, just gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can wear your ponytail high or low, depending on your preference.

Braids

Braids are another great option for sports. They keep your hair neat and tidy, and they're less likely to come undone than a ponytail. You can do a simple three-strand braid, a French braid, or a Dutch braid, depending on your skill level and the look you're going for.

Bun

A bun is a good choice if you have long hair that tends to get in your way. It keeps your hair up and out of your face, and it's less likely to come undone than a ponytail. To make a bun, just gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a coil. Then, wrap the coil around itself and secure it with bobby pins.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to wear your hair while playing sports:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be more likely to tangle and frizz.
  2. Decide on a style that works for you. Ponytails, braids, and buns are all good choices.
  3. If you're doing a ponytail or a bun, brush your hair back and gather it at the back of your head.
  4. If you're doing a braid, start by dividing your hair into three sections.
  5. If you're doing a French braid or a Dutch braid, start by gathering a small section of hair at the top of your head and dividing it into three sections.
  6. Begin braiding or twisting your hair, taking care to keep it neat and tidy.
  7. Secure your hair with an elastic band or bobby pins.
  8. If necessary, use hairspray or a headband to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Wearing Your Hair While Playing Sports

Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great while you're playing sports:

  • Use a hair tie that won't slip or break. Look for elastic bands that are designed for sports or that are made from materials like silicone or fabric.
  • Wear a headband to keep your hair out of your face. Look for headbands that are made from moisture-wicking materials like polyester or spandex.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place. Look for a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh your hair down or make it feel sticky.
  • If you have long hair, consider using a hair net to keep it contained. Hair nets are often used in ballet and other dance styles, but they can be helpful for sports as well.
  • If you're playing a sport that involves a helmet, be sure to adjust your hairstyle accordingly. A bun or a low ponytail may be the best choice.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I'm playing sports regularly?

A: It's a good idea to wash your hair after every workout if you're playing sports regularly. Sweat and oil can build up on your scalp and lead to dandruff or other scalp issues.

Q: Is it bad for my hair to wear it in a tight ponytail or bun?

A: Wearing your hair in a tight ponytail or bun can cause hair breakage and damage over time. To minimize damage, try to avoid wearing your hair in the same style every day, and use gentle hair ties that won't pull on your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Active Women

If you're an active woman, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great while you're playing sports. Whether you prefer a ponytail, a braid, or a bun, there are plenty of ways to keep your hair under control while you're active. So go ahead and get out there and enjoy your favorite sports!
