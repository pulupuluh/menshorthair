Best Short Haircut For Oval Faces

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new short haircut that flatters your oval face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for oval faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval face shapes are considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. They have balanced proportions and a slightly longer face length, which makes them perfect for short haircuts. The best short haircuts for oval faces are those that accentuate the natural shape of the face and add volume to the crown.

How To

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face, it is important to consider your hair texture and personal style. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture. For thick hair, a blunt bob or lob can help to control the volume and add structure. The key is to find a haircut that complements your face shape and works with your hair texture.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve the best short haircut for oval faces:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suited for your hair type.
  3. Towel dry your hair gently.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and texture.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  6. Use a straightener or curling iron to add additional texture and style.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist for regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use heat protectant products when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some variety to your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for oval faces:

Q: What is the best short haircut for fine hair and an oval face?

A: A layered bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture to fine hair.

Q: What is the best short haircut for thick hair and an oval face?

A: A blunt bob or lob can help to control the volume and add structure to thick hair.

Q: Can short haircuts be styled differently?

A: Yes, short haircuts can be styled in many different ways, such as straight, curly, or wavy.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it is important to use products that are suited for your hair type and to visit your hairstylist for regular trims. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also contribute to healthy hair. Remember to protect your hair from heat styling tools and to experiment with different styling products and accessories to add some variety to your look.

Now that you know the best short haircut for oval faces, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and get ready to rock your new look!
