How To Cut Even Layers In Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give your hair a fresh new look, cutting even layers is a great option. Not only does it add volume and texture to your hair, but it also looks great on all hair types and lengths. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut even layers in hair at home, without having to visit a salon. Let's get started!

How to Cut Even Layers in Hair

Cutting even layers in hair is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look at home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure you get an even cut. Use a water spray bottle to dampen your hair if needed.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle, creating two sections. Clip one section to the side to keep it out of the way while you work on the other section.

Step 3: Cut the First Layer

Starting at the front of your hair, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the ends of the hair in a straight line. This will be your guide for the rest of your haircut.

Step 4: Cut the Remaining Layers

Using the first layer as your guide, take another small section of hair and cut it to the same length. Continue this process, working your way to the back of your head. Be sure to check that each layer is even before moving on to the next one.

Step 5: Cut the Other Section

Once you've finished cutting one side of your hair, unclip the other section and repeat the same process.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've cut all the layers, check your hair for evenness. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them until they're even.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut even layers in your hair, it's time to style it. You can leave it down for a natural look or create curls or waves for added texture.

Tips for Cutting Even Layers in Hair

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take small sections of hair to ensure evenness.
  • Check your hair for evenness after each layer.
  • If you're unsure, start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut even layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut even layers in curly hair. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink up to 50% of its length. Take smaller sections of hair and cut it while it's in its natural state to ensure even layers.

How often should I cut my hair to maintain even layers?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain even layers and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've cut even layers in your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting even layers in hair is a great way to switch up your look and add some texture and volume to your hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for best results. Happy cutting!
