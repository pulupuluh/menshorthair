How To Cut Top Hair With Clippers Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of paying your hairdresser for simple haircuts? Want to try cutting your own hair at home with clippers? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the top hair with clippers yourself.

How to Cut Top Hair with Clippers Yourself

Before we get started on the step-by-step guide, it's important to have the right tools. To cut your hair with clippers, you will need:

  • Clippers with different guard sizes
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror
  • Clean towel or cape to keep the hair off your clothes

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have all the necessary tools, let's start the cutting process:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it completely before starting.
  2. Decide on the length of your hair and choose the appropriate guard size for your clippers. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if necessary.
  3. Clip the top section of your hair using the chosen guard size. This will create an even length across the top of your head.
  4. Blend the sides of your hair with the clippers. Use a shorter guard size to create a fade effect, or use the same guard size for an even cut.
  5. Use scissors to trim any long hair on the top that was missed with the clippers. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to create texture.
  6. Use a comb to blend the top and sides of your hair. Comb the hair up and out from the head and use scissors to trim any longer pieces that stick out.
  7. Use the clippers to clean up the hairline around your neck and ears.
  8. Use a mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas.
  9. Rinse your hair to remove any loose hair and style as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a professional-looking haircut:

  • Work in a well-lit area with a large mirror.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to prevent cutting your hair too short.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and create an even cut.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first few haircuts don't turn out perfectly.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting hair with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and technique, cutting your own hair with clippers is possible.

What guard size should I use?

The guard size you use will depend on the length of your hair and the style you want to achieve. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if necessary.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to maintain. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and styled:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut top hair with clippers yourself, you can save money and achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
