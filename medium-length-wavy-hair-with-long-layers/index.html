Medium Length Wavy Hair With Long Layers A Stylish Look For 2023

Written by Luffy Mar 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle for 2023, medium length wavy hair with long layers is the way to go. This look is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair, while still keeping it manageable and stylish. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, step by step, and give you some tips and tricks for maintaining it. Let's get started!

Description:

Medium length wavy hair with long layers is a hairstyle that features loose waves and layers that fall below the shoulders. This look is perfect for those with naturally wavy or curly hair, but can also be achieved with a curling iron or wand. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, while the medium length keeps it from becoming too heavy or difficult to manage.

How to:

Creating medium length wavy hair with long layers is easier than you might think. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, bottom, and two sides.
  3. Starting with the bottom section, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Be sure to curl away from your face for a more natural look.
  4. Repeat this process with the other sections of your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  5. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the waves and create more volume.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Step by Step:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's clean and completely dry. This will help your waves hold their shape and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Section your hair into four parts.

Using a comb, divide your hair into four equal sections: top, bottom, and two sides. Clip each section up and out of the way.

Step 3: Curl the bottom section.

Starting with the bottom section, take a small section of hair and wrap it around your curling iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process with the rest of the bottom section.

Step 4: Curl the other sections.

Repeat step 3 with the other sections of your hair, working from the back to the front. Be sure to curl the mid-lengths and ends, rather than the roots.

Step 5: Separate the waves.

Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the waves and create more volume. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can make your waves look flat and lifeless.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray.

To keep your waves in place, finish with a light-hold hairspray. Hold the can about 12 inches away from your head and spray lightly.

Tips:

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining your medium length wavy hair with long layers:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

FAQ:

Q: Will this hairstyle work on all hair types?

A: While medium length wavy hair with long layers looks great on most hair types, it may not work as well on very fine or straight hair. If you have fine or straight hair, you may need to use more product to create volume and texture.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: To maintain the shape and style of your long layered hair, it's recommended that you get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your hair up with this style. Try a messy bun or a loose braid for a casual, effortless look.

Hair Care:

To keep your medium length wavy hair with long layers looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the perfect medium length wavy hair with long layers. Whether you're going to work or out for a night on the town, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
