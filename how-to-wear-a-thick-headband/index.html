How To Wear A Thick Headband A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Headbands have been in trend for a long time, and they are not going away anytime soon. They are versatile, easy to wear, and can upgrade any hairstyle in seconds. However, wearing a thick headband can be a bit tricky, especially if you are not used to it. In this guide, we will show you how to wear a thick headband like a pro, with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Wear a Thick Headband

Wearing a thick headband may seem daunting at first, but it's actually quite simple. All you need is a bit of practice and some styling tips. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Choose a headband that fits your head comfortably but doesn't slide off easily.
  • Make sure your hair is dry and styled the way you want it to be.
  • Place the headband on your head, about an inch behind your hairline.
  • Adjust the headband so that it fits snugly around your head, without causing any discomfort.
  • Style your hair around the headband, using bobby pins or hair ties to secure any loose strands.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a thick headband like a pro:

  1. Start by choosing a headband that matches your style and outfit. Thick headbands come in different materials, colors, and patterns, so choose one that complements your hair and skin tone.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. If your hair is frizzy or oily, use a dry shampoo or hairspray to tame it.
  3. Place the headband on your head, starting from the back and working your way forward. Make sure the headband is snug but not too tight, as it can cause headaches or discomfort.
  4. If you have bangs, tuck them under the headband or leave them out, depending on your preference. You can also pull out some strands of hair to frame your face.
  5. Style your hair around the headband, using bobby pins, hair ties, or clips to secure any loose strands. You can create a braid, a bun, a ponytail, or any other hairstyle that complements the headband.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hair serum to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Wearing a Thick Headband

Here are some tips to make your thick headband look even better:

  • Choose a headband that matches your hair color or contrasts it for a bold look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as messy buns, low ponytails, or half-up half-down dos.
  • Add accessories, such as earrings, necklaces, or bracelets, that complement your headband.
  • Wear a headband that has a statement design or pattern, such as florals, animal prints, or geometrics.
  • Try different materials, such as velvet, satin, or leather, to add texture and dimension to your outfit.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a thick headband:

  • Can I wear a thick headband with short hair? Yes, you can. Choose a headband that is not too wide or bulky, and style your hair in a pixie cut, a bob, or a layered style.
  • How do I keep my headband from slipping? Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure the headband to your hair. You can also spray some hairspray on the headband before wearing it.
  • Can I wear a thick headband to a formal event? Yes, you can. Choose a headband that is elegant and sophisticated, and pair it with a chic dress or a gown.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that matches your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins, which are essential for hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution, by wearing a hat or a scarf when outdoors.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can wear a thick headband with confidence and style. Whether you are going to work, school, or a party, a headband can add a touch of glamour and sophistication to your look. Try different styles, experiment with colors and patterns, and have fun with your hair accessories.
