How To Cut Stacked Bob Yourself Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

A stacked bob is a trendy and stylish haircut that's perfect for any occasion. If you're looking to switch things up and want to try cutting your hair at home, then a stacked bob is a great choice. In this article, we'll teach you how to cut stacked bob yourself in relaxed English language. You'll learn how to do it step by step, tips to make the process easier, and how to take care of your new haircut.

Description

A stacked bob is a haircut that's shorter in the back and longer in the front. It's usually cut at an angle to create a stacked effect, hence the name. This type of haircut is suitable for any hair type, and it's easy to maintain. You can wear it straight or with waves, and it looks great with bangs or without.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Here's how to cut stacked bob yourself:

Step 1:

Wash your hair and let it air dry. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2:

Part your hair down the middle and clip the top section away.

Step 3:

Take a section of hair from the bottom layer and comb it straight down. Hold it between your middle and index fingers and cut it straight across.

Step 4:

Take another section of hair and cut it at an angle, starting from the back and going towards the front. Make sure to follow the angle of the hair.

Step 5:

Continue cutting sections of hair at an angle until you've reached the top layer.

Step 6:

Unclip the top layer and repeat the process.

Step 7:

Once you've finished cutting all the layers, check for any uneven spots and trim them if necessary.

Tips

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area so you can see what you're doing.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Don't rush the process.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting stacked bobs yourself:

Can I cut a stacked bob on curly hair?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to cut each section of hair individually and follow the shape of your hair.

How often do I need to trim my stacked bob?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should trim it every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.

Is it better to cut my hair wet or dry?

It's better to cut your hair dry so you can see the shape better.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

Cutting stacked bob yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following our step-by-step guide and tips, you'll be able to achieve a stylish haircut that's sure to turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair properly, and you'll have a fabulous look that lasts.
