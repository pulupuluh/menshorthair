Best Hairstyles For Round Faces 2022 A Complete Guide

Description

Whether you're looking to try something new or just want to update your current look, finding the best hairstyle for your face shape is key. For those with round faces, it can be especially challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But fear not! We've put together a list of the best hairstyles for round faces in 2022, along with tips on how to style and maintain your new 'do.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, it's important to determine if you do indeed have a round face shape. Here's how:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face.
  • Take a close look at the outline of your face. Is it widest at the cheeks and tapering at the jawline and forehead?
  • If the answer is yes, you likely have a round face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's take a look at the best hairstyles for round faces in 2022:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate a round face shape and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Keep the layers soft and subtle, and avoid blunt cuts that can make the face appear wider.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice for those with round faces, but when done correctly, it can be incredibly flattering. Opt for a longer, textured pixie with side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

3. Messy Bob

A messy bob is a great option for round faces, as it can add volume and texture without overwhelming the face. Keep the length just below the chin and add subtle layers for movement.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

For those with round faces, side-swept bangs can help to create a more angular look. Keep the bangs long and wispy, and avoid blunt cuts that can make the face appear wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape, here's how to style it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from hot styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired style.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim your hair to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs and chin-length bobs.

Can I still wear a ponytail if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just be sure to keep the ponytail low and loose to elongate your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips for those with round faces:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals and frequent coloring to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Invest in a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent hair damage while you sleep.

With these tips and best hairstyles for round faces in 2022, you'll be sure to find the perfect look to flatter your features and show off your personality.
