Best Haircuts For Square Faces Female A Comprehensive Guide

Welcome to our guide on finding the best haircuts for square faces female. If you have a square face shape, you may struggle to find the right hairstyle that compliments your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Best Haircuts for Square Faces Female

Description

Square face shapes have a strong jawline and angular features. The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal. To soften the angles of the face, it is essential to choose a haircut that adds volume to the crown and creates a softness around the edges.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is the first step in finding the right haircut. To determine if you have a square face shape, measure the length and width of your face. If the measurements are almost equal and your jawline is angular, you have a square face shape.

Best Haircuts for Square Faces Female

When it comes to haircuts for square faces female, the aim is to create a softness around the edges and add volume to the crown. Here are some haircuts that work best:

Long Layers

Long layers soften the angles of the face and create movement. The layers should start from the chin and continue till the end of the hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs add softness to the face and draw attention away from the angular jawline. The bangs should be long and swept to the side.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is perfect for square face shapes. The bob should be chin-length or longer and should have layers. The layers should be soft and create movement around the edges.

Wavy Bob

A wavy bob is perfect for square faces as it creates softness around the edges. The waves should be created using a curling iron or a flat iron and should be loose.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have found the perfect haircut, it is essential to know how to style it. Here is a step by step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the crown.
  3. Use a round brush to create soft curls.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to create movement.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for a trim.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

Q. Can I wear a short haircut with a square face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a short haircut with a square face shape. However, it is essential to choose a haircut that creates softness around the edges.

Q. Can I wear a middle part with a square face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a middle part with a square face shape. However, it is essential to add volume to the crown to balance out the angular jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Female

Here are some hair care tips for square faces female:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the crown.
  • Avoid using too much heat as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

We hope this guide on finding the best haircuts for square faces female has been helpful. Remember, the key is to create softness around the edges and add volume to the crown. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can accentuate your features and look your best.
