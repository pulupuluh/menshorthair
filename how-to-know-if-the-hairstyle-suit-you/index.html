How To Know If The Hairstyle Suits You A Complete Guide

Choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure how it will look on you. With so many options available, it's easy to get lost in the sea of hairstyles, and it can be difficult to find one that suits you. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll show you how to know if the hairstyle suits you, and give you some tips on how to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Know if the Hairstyle Suits You

There are a few things to consider when choosing a hairstyle that will suit you. These include your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are generally six face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has different features that you'll want to highlight or downplay with your hairstyle.

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile, and you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob. If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your angles with a layered hairstyle or soft, side-swept bangs. If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones with a layered, chin-length bob or long, face-framing layers. If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin, such as a layered bob or long, side-swept bangs. If you have an oblong face, you'll want to add width to your face with a chin-length bob or layered hairstyle.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are generally three hair textures: fine, medium, and thick. Each hair texture has different needs and will look best with certain hairstyles.

If you have fine hair, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture, such as a layered bob or pixie cut. If you have medium hair, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle, but you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add too much weight to your hair, such as blunt bangs or a heavy, one-length cut. If you have thick hair, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that thins out your hair and adds movement, such as a layered cut or long, face-framing layers.

Personal Style

Your personal style is the final factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. You'll want to choose a hairstyle that reflects your personality and makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Some people prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a simple ponytail. Others prefer more elaborate hairstyles, such as a braided updo or a sleek, straight bob. It's important to choose a hairstyle that you'll be happy with, and that won't require too much upkeep or styling.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Determine your hair texture.
  3. Consider your personal style.
  4. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and other sources to find hairstyles that you like.
  5. Consult with a stylist. A professional stylist can help you choose a hairstyle that suits you and your needs.
  6. Try it out. If you're not sure about a hairstyle, try it out with a wig or hair extensions before committing to a cut or color.
  7. Maintain your hairstyle. Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, make sure to take care of it with regular salon visits, haircuts, and styling.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule or don't have much time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. If you're feeling adventurous, try out a new hairstyle or hair color.
  • Take care of your hair. Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle, so make sure to use quality hair care products and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Consider your skin tone. Some hair colors will look better with certain skin tones, so choose a color that complements your complexion.

FAQs

What if I'm not sure what face shape I have?

You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a measuring tape, and then comparing the measurements to the standard face shapes.

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture, such as a layered bob or pixie cut. You can also use volumizing products and hair extensions to add volume to your hair.

Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can be flattering on many face shapes and hair textures. Consult with a stylist to find a short hairstyle that suits you and your needs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to choose the perfect hairstyle, it's time to find the one that suits you best. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with new hairstyles and hair colors. With a little bit of patience and some expert advice, you'll find the perfect hairstyle in no time!
