How To Dye Asian Hair Blonde A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 17, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

If you're an Asian with black or dark brown hair, you may have wondered what it would be like to go blonde. While it may seem like a daunting task, it's definitely possible with the right products and techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to dye Asian hair blonde in a relaxed English language, from start to finish. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Dye Asian Hair Blonde
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Dyeing Asian Hair Blonde
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Blonde Asian Hair

Description

Dyeing Asian hair blonde can be challenging, as the hair is naturally dark and may require multiple bleaching sessions to achieve the desired shade. However, with the right products and techniques, it's possible to get the blonde hair you've always wanted.

How to Dye Asian Hair Blonde

The first step in dyeing Asian hair blonde is to choose the right shade of blonde. If you have warm-toned skin, you may want to opt for a honey or golden blonde, while those with cool-toned skin may prefer a platinum or ash blonde.

Once you've chosen your shade, it's time to gather your supplies. You'll need:

  • Bleach
  • Developer
  • Toner
  • Gloves
  • Applicator brush
  • Bowl
  • Shampoo and conditioner for blonde hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your supplies, here's how to dye Asian hair blonde:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours before dyeing it.
  2. Mix the bleach and developer according to the instructions on the packaging. Wear gloves to protect your hands.
  3. Apply the bleach to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Use an applicator brush to ensure even coverage.
  4. Leave the bleach on for the recommended amount of time, checking your hair every 10 minutes to ensure it's not over-processing.
  5. Rinse out the bleach with cool water and shampoo your hair. Avoid using conditioner at this stage.
  6. Apply the toner to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. This will help remove any brassy tones and give your hair a more natural-looking blonde shade.
  7. Rinse out the toner with cool water and shampoo and condition your hair as usual.

Tips for Dyeing Asian Hair Blonde

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when dyeing Asian hair blonde:

  • Don't bleach your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can further damage your hair.
  • Consider getting your hair done by a professional stylist for the best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about dyeing Asian hair blonde:

  • How long does it take to dye Asian hair blonde? The process can take several hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.
  • Can I dye my hair blonde without bleach? While it's technically possible, it's not recommended, as bleach is necessary to lift the dark pigment in Asian hair.
  • Will dyeing my hair blonde damage it? Yes, dyeing your hair can cause damage, especially if you bleach it multiple times. It's important to take care of your hair before and after dyeing it to minimize damage.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Asian Hair

Now that you have blonde hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for blonde Asian hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for blonde hair to keep it from turning brassy.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive sunlight, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as chlorine in swimming pools.

With these tips and techniques, you can successfully dye Asian hair blonde and enjoy your new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
