How To Cut Men s Long Hair With Scissors Step By Step

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to cut your long hair but don't want to go to a salon? Cutting men's long hair with scissors can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great haircut. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting men's long hair with scissors.

How to Cut Men's Long Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash and dry the hair before you start cutting. Wet hair is easier to cut, so make sure the hair is damp.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair. Start from the top of the head and work your way down. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start cutting the hair from the back of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it with the scissors. Make sure to cut small sections at a time. Repeat this process for each section.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After you have cut all the sections, blend the hair by combing it and cutting any uneven parts.

Step 6: Check the Length

Check the length of the hair and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired. You can use hair products to add volume or texture.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Blend the hair by combing it to ensure a natural look.
  • Check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Style the hair as desired using hair products.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home. However, it is important to have the right tools and technique to avoid damaging the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy and stylish look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know how to cut men's long hair with scissors, you can give yourself or someone else a great haircut at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully for the best results. Happy cutting!
