How To Get A Square Face Shape Tips Tricks And Hair Care

If you're looking to achieve a square face shape, there are a few things you can do to help achieve that look. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to get a square face shape, as well as hair care tips to help maintain it. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Square Face Shape
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Maintaining a Square Face Shape
  • FAQs

How to Get a Square Face Shape

While genetics plays a large role in determining your face shape, there are things you can do to help emphasize certain features and achieve a square face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones, which are key features of a square face shape. Use a contour powder or cream to shade just below your cheekbones and along your jawline.

2. Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also help emphasize a square face shape. Opt for a haircut with blunt, straight lines that frames your face, such as a blunt bob or long layers.

3. Facial Hair

For men, a well-groomed beard or stubble can help create the appearance of a more defined jawline and square face shape.

4. Exercise

Exercises that target your jaw and cheek muscles can help strengthen and define those areas, which can help create a square face shape. Try exercises like chewing gum or using a jaw exerciser.

Step by Step

If you're looking for a more defined square face shape, here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start trying to change your face shape, it's important to determine what your natural face shape is. Take a look at yourself in the mirror and identify the shape of your face.

2. Contour Your Face

Using a contour powder or cream, apply shading just below your cheekbones and along your jawline. Blend well for a natural look.

3. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Opt for a haircut with blunt, straight lines that frames your face, such as a blunt bob or long layers.

4. Maintain Facial Hair

For men, maintain a well-groomed beard or stubble to help create the appearance of a more defined jawline and square face shape.

Tips for Maintaining a Square Face Shape

Once you've achieved a square face shape, you'll want to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Continue Contouring

Continue contouring regularly to maintain the illusion of a defined jawline and cheekbones.

2. Stick to Your Hairstyle

Stick to your blunt, straight hairstyle to help emphasize your square face shape.

3. Stay Active

Regular exercise can help maintain the muscle definition in your jaw and cheek areas.

FAQs

Can I achieve a square face shape without makeup?

Yes, by choosing the right hairstyle and maintaining facial hair, you can create the illusion of a square face shape without makeup.

How long will it take to achieve a square face shape?

Depending on your starting face shape, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to achieve a more defined square face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Square Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips to help maintain your square face shape:

1. Use Heat Protectant

When using heated styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

In conclusion, achieving a square face shape can be done through a combination of makeup techniques, hairstyles, facial hair, exercise, and more. Remember to take care of your hair as well to maintain your new look. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the square face shape you desire!
