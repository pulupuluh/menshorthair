How To Curl Short Hair With A Straightener

Written by Frank Jun 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that you can't achieve beautiful curls with a straightener. But the truth is, with the right technique and tools, you can create stunning curls that will last all day. In this article, we'll show you how to curl your short hair with a straightener in a few easy steps.

Description

Curling your short hair with a straightener is an easy and versatile way to change up your look. Whether you want loose, beachy waves or tight, defined curls, a straightener can help you achieve the style you want. Plus, it's a great way to add volume and dimension to short hair.

How to Curl Short Hair with a Straightener

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the straightener. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. The size of the sections will depend on the length of your hair and how tight you want your curls to be. For shorter hair, smaller sections will create tighter curls.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root. Twist the straightener away from your face and glide it down the length of the hair. The slower you move the straightener, the tighter the curl will be. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until you have curled your entire head.

Step 4: Finish Your Style

Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create the desired shape. You can also use a flexible hold hairspray to keep your curls in place and add shine to your hair.

Tips for Curling Short Hair with a Straightener

  • Use a high-quality straightener with ceramic plates for even heat distribution and less damage to your hair.
  • Don't clamp the straightener too tightly on your hair to avoid creating creases or kinks in your curls.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes and directions to create a more natural, textured look.
  • Use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to refresh your curls and add volume between washes.

FAQ

Can I curl my short hair with a flat iron?

Yes, you can curl your short hair with a flat iron or straightener using the technique described in this article.

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curls created with a straightener can last up to 24 hours with the right care and maintenance.

Can I curl my hair with a straightener if it's wet?

No, you should never use a straightener or any heat styling tool on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Short Hair

To keep your short hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too many styling products or heat tools that can cause damage. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can also help keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy.

Curling your short hair with a straightener is an easy and versatile way to change up your look. With the right technique and tools, you can create stunning curls that will last all day. Follow our tips and tricks, and you'll be a curling pro in no time!
