How To Grow Dreads With Sponge A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Dreadlocks, or simply dreads, are a popular hairstyle that has been around for centuries. They are often associated with Rastafarianism, but many cultures around the world have embraced this unique hairstyle. Dreads are a great way to express yourself and show off your individuality. In recent years, a new method of growing dreads has emerged, which involves using a sponge. In this article, we will discuss how to grow dreads with sponge and provide you with some useful tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. What are dreadlocks?
  2. How to grow dreads with sponge
  3. Step-by-step guide
  4. Tips for growing dreads with sponge

  6. Hair care tips for dreads

What are dreadlocks?

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that involves matted hair that is twisted into ropes or cords. The hair is not combed or brushed, but instead, it is allowed to form clumps and tangles. Dreadlocks can be created by various methods, including the natural method, backcombing, and interlocking. In recent years, a new method of growing dreads has emerged, which involves using a sponge.

How to grow dreads with sponge

The sponge method is a simple and easy way to grow dreads. It involves using a sponge to twist and lock your hair. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-step guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or locking cream to your hair.
  3. Take a sponge with small holes and rub it in a circular motion over your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Repeat this process until your hair starts to twist and lock.
  5. Use a hair tie or rubber band to secure the twisted section of hair.
  6. Repeat the process on the rest of your hair, working in small sections.
  7. Leave the hair ties or rubber bands in place for a few days to allow the dreads to set.
  8. Remove the hair ties or rubber bands and use a crochet hook to tidy up any loose hairs.

Tips for growing dreads with sponge

Here are some useful tips to help you grow dreads with a sponge:

  • Start with short hair. The sponge method works best on hair that is less than 4 inches long.
  • Use a sponge with small holes. A sponge with larger holes will not work as well.
  • Use a good quality locking cream or hair gel to help your hair lock faster.
  • Be patient. It can take several months for your dreads to fully form.
  • Don't wash your hair too often. Washing your hair too often can cause your dreads to unravel.

Frequently asked questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about growing dreads with a sponge:

  • Q: Can I use a regular sponge?
  • A: No, you need to use a sponge with small holes to get the best results.
  • Q: How often do I need to sponge my hair?
  • A: You can sponge your hair as often as you like, but it's best to wait a few days between sponging sessions.
  • Q: Can I use a sponge on long hair?
  • A: Yes, but it may take longer for your dreads to form.

Hair care tips for dreads

Here are some tips to help you care for your dreads:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can build up in your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your dreads from getting frizzy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your dreads looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to grow dreads with a sponge and enjoy this unique and stylish hairstyle. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you'll soon have a head full of beautiful, healthy dreads!
