Do Short Haircuts Suit Oval Faces

Written by Luffy May 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you an oval-faced beauty wondering if short haircuts will suit you? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the answer to the question "do short haircuts suit oval faces?" and give you tips on how to choose the perfect short haircut that flatters your face shape. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape. They have balanced proportions and a slightly rounded jawline, which means they can rock almost any hairstyle. Short haircuts can be an excellent option for oval faces as they emphasize your facial features and highlight your natural beauty. However, not all short haircuts are created equal, and some may not suit your face shape.

How to

Before you chop off your locks, consider these factors to ensure that your short haircut flatters your oval face:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair plays a significant role in determining the best short haircut for you. If you have thick hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut with choppy layers will add dimension and texture to your hair. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a short shag will add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Think About Your Face Shape

While oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, some short haircuts may not suit your face shape. For example, a short bob that ends at your chin may make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a shorter bob that ends above your chin or a pixie cut that accentuates your cheekbones.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also influence your short haircut choice. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, a low-maintenance pixie cut or a messy bob may be the way to go. If you're looking for a more polished look, a sleek bob or a layered lob may suit you better.

Step by Step

Here's how to choose the perfect short haircut for your oval face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure to measure the widest point of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair's thickness, density, and texture. Determine which short haircuts will flatter your hair texture.

Step 3: Research Short Haircuts

Look for short haircuts that will flatter your face shape and hair texture. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Bring pictures of short haircuts you like to your stylist and ask for their opinion. They can recommend short haircuts that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.

Step 5: Experiment with Styling

Once you've chosen a short haircut, experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. Try using a curling iron, straightener, or texturizing spray to add volume and dimension to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves to add interest to your short haircut.

FAQ

1. What short haircuts suit oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off almost any short haircut. Some popular options include bobs, pixie cuts, and lobs.

2. Will a short bob make my face appear wider?

A short bob that ends at your chin may make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a shorter bob that ends above your chin or a pixie cut that accentuates your cheekbones.

3. How do I know if I have an oval face shape?

Use a tape measure to measure the widest point of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance compared to longer hairstyles, but it's still essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts:

  • Wash your hair every other day or as needed.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

In conclusion, short haircuts can look stunning on oval faces, as long as you choose the right haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short haircut with confidence and style!
