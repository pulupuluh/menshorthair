How To See What Haircut Suits You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 08, 2023 · 8 min read

Have you ever wondered what haircut would suit you the best? It's a common question that many people ask themselves. It can be challenging to decide on a haircut that will look good on you, especially if you're not sure what to look for. In this article, we'll discuss how to see what haircut suits you and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to See What Haircut Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to See What Haircut Suits You

When choosing a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you best. It's also important to consider your hair texture because certain hairstyles work better with specific hair types. Lastly, think about your lifestyle because some haircuts require more maintenance than others.

There are a few different ways to determine which haircut suits you best:

  • Look at pictures of hairstyles you like and see if they suit your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a haircut based on your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a virtual hairstyle app or website that allows you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to see what haircut suits you:

  1. Determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Look in the mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape. Look for pictures of hairstyles that you like and see if they suit your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to avoid haircuts that require a lot of styling and maintenance.
  4. Think about your lifestyle. If you're someone who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair every day, you may want to choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist. A hairstylist can recommend a haircut that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. They can also provide you with tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.
  6. Try on different hairstyles using a virtual hairstyle app or website. This can give you a better idea of what different hairstyles will look like on you.
  7. Take the plunge and get a new haircut!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect haircut:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new. A new haircut can be a great way to freshen up your look.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist. This can help them better understand what you're looking for.
  • Consider getting a haircut that complements your natural hair texture. This can make styling your hair much easier.
  • Think about your hair color when choosing a haircut. Certain haircuts may work better with certain hair colors.
  • Remember that hair grows back. If you're not happy with your new haircut, it will eventually grow out.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to see what haircut suits you:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircuts for a round face are ones that add length to the face. This can be achieved with haircuts that have layers, angles, or a side-swept fringe.

What is the best haircut for an oval face?

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. Most haircuts will suit an oval face shape, so it's best to choose a haircut that complements your hair texture and lifestyle.

What is the best haircut for a square face?

The best haircuts for a square face are ones that soften the angles of the face. This can be achieved with haircuts that have layers, curls, or a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in protein and vitamins.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge, but by considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find a haircut that suits you best. Don't be afraid to try something new and consult with a hairstylist if you need some guidance. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best!
