Can You Curl Short Layered Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair never goes out of style. It’s an easy way to add volume and dimension to your hair, no matter the length or texture. But what about short layered hair? Can you curl it? The answer is yes! In this article, we’ll show you how to curl your short layered hair and provide some tips and tricks along the way.

Description

Short layered hair is a popular style that’s easy to maintain and perfect for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair. However, some people may think that short hair can’t be curled. That’s not true! You can still achieve a curly look with short layered hair, and it’s easier than you think.

How To Curl Short Layered Hair

Follow these steps to curl your short layered hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. You can also apply a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some extra hold and texture.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any sections. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Finish Your Look

Once you’ve curled your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume and texture to your hair.

Tips For Curling Short Layered Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when curling short layered hair:

  • Use a smaller curling iron for shorter hair to create tighter curls.
  • Don’t hold the curling iron for too long to avoid heat damage.
  • Wrap your hair around the curling iron in different directions to create a more natural look.
  • Use a hairspray to set your curls in place.

FAQ

Can you curl short hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can curl short hair without a curling iron. You can use hot rollers, a curling wand, or even bobby pins to create curls.

How long do curls last in short hair?

The length of time that curls last in short hair depends on the type of curl and how well you set them. Generally, curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can you curl short hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl short hair with a straightener. Simply twist your hair around the straightener and pull it through to create a curl.

Hair Care Tips

When curling your short layered hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to curl short layered hair, it’s time to give it a try! Follow these steps and tips to achieve a curly look that’s sure to turn heads.
