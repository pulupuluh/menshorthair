Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Small Forehead

Written by Luffy Apr 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you an oval-faced beauty with a small forehead? Lucky you, because you have the most versatile face shape that suits almost any hairstyle. However, finding the perfect haircut that flatters your small forehead can be a bit tricky. Fret not, because we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll guide you on how to choose and style the best hairstyle for oval face small forehead.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It’s considered the most balanced face shape and can pull off various hairstyles. However, if you have a small forehead, you need to avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer or emphasize your forehead. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your face.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Small Forehead

When choosing the best hairstyle for oval face small forehead, you should consider the following:

  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your face shape and features
  • Your lifestyle and personality

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for oval face small forehead:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Identify your forehead size. If it’s smaller than your cheekbones and jawline, you have a small forehead.
  3. Look for hairstyles that add volume and width to your face, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and loose waves.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer or emphasize your forehead, such as slicked-back hair, center parts, and blunt bangs.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips to Style the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Small Forehead

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, here are some tips to style it:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and bounce.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that weigh your hair down.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for oval face small forehead:

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a small forehead?

A: Yes, but avoid blunt bangs that emphasize your forehead. Opt for side-swept bangs that add volume and width to your face.

Q: What haircut should I avoid if I have a small forehead?

A: Avoid haircuts that make your face look longer or emphasize your forehead, such as slicked-back hair, center parts, and blunt bangs.

Q: What hairstyle adds volume to my face?

A: Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and loose waves add volume and width to your face.

Hair Care for the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Small Forehead

To maintain the best hairstyle for oval face small forehead, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

So, there you have it – the best hairstyle for oval face small forehead. Follow our tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and features. Remember, confidence is the key to rock any hairstyle!
