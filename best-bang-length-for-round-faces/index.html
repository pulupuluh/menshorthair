Best Bang Length For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Feb 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you have a round face, you may have wondered what the best bang length is for your face shape. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some definition to your face, but it's important to choose the right length to flatter your features. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about finding the best bang length for round faces.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing bangs for this face shape is to add some length and balance out the roundness. The right bang length can help elongate the face and draw attention to your best features.

How to

To find the best bang length for your round face, follow these steps: 1. Determine your face shape: The first step is to make sure you actually have a round face. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you likely have a round face. 2. Consider your hair type: The thickness and texture of your hair can also play a role in choosing the right bang length. Thin, fine hair may look best with shorter bangs, while thicker hair can handle longer bangs. 3. Look at celebrity inspiration: Look for celebrities with round faces and see what types of bangs they wear. This can give you an idea of what styles work well with your face shape. 4. Consult with a stylist: If you're still unsure, talk to your stylist. They can help you choose the best length and style for your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

1. Start with a consultation: Talk to your stylist about your face shape and hair type. 2. Consider your options: There are a variety of bang styles to choose from, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. 3. Choose the right length: For round faces, longer bangs that hit just below the eyebrows or chin-length bangs that graze the jawline are typically the most flattering. 4. Style your bangs: Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs, or use a straightener to create a sleek look.

Tips

- Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as they can make the face look wider. - Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of the face. - Consider adding layers to your hair to add more shape and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?
A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose the right length and style to flatter your features. Q: What's the best way to style my bangs?
A: Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume, or use a straightener for a sleeker look. Q: Can I wear blunt bangs if I have a round face?
A: While it's not the most flattering option, you can still wear blunt bangs if you love the look. Just make sure to keep them longer and side-swept.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, make sure to trim them regularly to avoid split ends. Use a dry shampoo to keep them from getting oily, and avoid touching them too much throughout the day to prevent them from losing their shape. In conclusion, the best bang length for round faces is typically longer, chin-length bangs or side-swept bangs that graze the eyebrows. Consider your hair type and consult with a stylist to find the perfect length and style for your face shape. With the right bangs, you can add some definition to your features and change up your look in a fun and flattering way.
