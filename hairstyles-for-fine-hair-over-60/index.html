Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 31, 2023 · 8 min read

If you are over 60 and have fine hair, you may be struggling to find a hairstyle that suits you. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks for choosing the perfect hairstyle for fine hair over 60. We’ll also be sharing some step-by-step guides for creating some popular hairstyles, as well as answering some frequently asked questions about hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guides for Popular Hairstyles

Tips for Choosing Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and finer. This can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that looks good and is easy to manage. Here are some tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle for fine hair over 60:

1. Keep it short

Short hairstyles are perfect for fine hair over 60. Not only do they add volume to your hair, but they are also easy to manage and maintain. A short bob or pixie cut can be a great option.

2. Add layers

Adding layers to your hair can also help to add volume and texture. Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair to give it more body and movement.

3. Embrace your natural texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it! Don’t try to straighten your hair as this can make it look even thinner. Instead, work with your natural texture and embrace your curls or waves.

4. Avoid heavy products

Heavy products can weigh down fine hair and make it look even thinner. Avoid using heavy gels or mousses and opt for lightweight products instead.

5. Get regular trims

Regular trims are important for maintaining healthy hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Step by Step Guides for Popular Hairstyles

Now that you know some tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle for fine hair over 60, let’s take a look at some step-by-step guides for popular hairstyles.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on fine hair over 60. Here’s how to create the perfect pixie cut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section off the top section of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Using a pair of scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Unclip the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length.
  5. Style your hair with some lightweight mousse or styling cream.

The Bob

The bob is another great hairstyle for fine hair over 60. Here’s how to create the perfect bob:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section off the bottom section of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Using a pair of scissors, cut the top section of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Unclip the bottom section of your hair and cut it to your desired length.
  5. Style your hair with some lightweight mousse or styling cream.

FAQs About Hair Care for Fine Hair Over 60

Q. What is the best hairstyle for fine hair over 60?

A. Short hairstyles such as a pixie cut or bob are great options for fine hair over 60.

Q. What products should I avoid using on fine hair?

A. Heavy products such as gels and mousses should be avoided as they can weigh down fine hair and make it look even thinner.

Q. How often should I get a trim?

A. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Q. Can I still color my hair if it’s fine?

A. Yes, you can still color your hair if it’s fine. However, be sure to use a gentle color and avoid over-processing your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair Over 60

Here are some additional hair care tips for fine hair over 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair as this can cause breakage
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your fine hair over 60. Remember to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained by following a good hair care routine.
