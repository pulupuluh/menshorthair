Best Hair Length For Heart Shaped Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 04, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin, which can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right hair length, you can enhance your natural beauty and make your heart-shaped face look even more stunning. In this article, we'll guide you through the best hair lengths for heart-shaped faces, how to achieve them, and some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hair length for your heart-shaped face is to determine your face shape. Here are some steps to help you determine if you have a heart-shaped face:

  • Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a tape measure
  • If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and your chin is pointed, you likely have a heart-shaped face
  • If your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline, you might have an oval face shape
  • If your forehead and jawline are both wide and your face has a square appearance, you might have a square face shape
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width and your face is longer than it is wide, you might have a rectangular face shape

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the best hair length for your heart-shaped face.

The Best Hair Lengths for Heart Shaped Faces

The best hair lengths for heart-shaped faces are those that balance out the width at the forehead and the narrowness at the chin. Here are some of the best hair lengths for heart-shaped faces:

Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Short haircuts work well for heart-shaped faces as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the width of the forehead. Some of the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered bob
  • Side-swept bangs

Short haircuts can be a great way to show off your heart-shaped face and create a bold, confident look.

Medium Length Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Medium length haircuts are also a great option for heart-shaped faces as they add volume to the chin area and create balance between the forehead and jawline. Some of the best medium length haircuts for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Shoulder-length cut
  • Layered cut with side-swept bangs
  • Wavy bob
  • Long bob with layers

Medium length haircuts can be versatile and easy to manage, making them a popular choice for heart-shaped faces.

Long Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

Long haircuts can also work well for heart-shaped faces as they add volume to the chin area and create balance between the forehead and jawline. Some of the best long haircuts for heart-shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Beach waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Half-up, half-down hairstyle

Long haircuts can be elegant and romantic, making them a great choice for special occasions.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Length

Once you've found the best hair length for your heart-shaped face, it's important to maintain it to keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair length:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as they can damage your hair

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous, no matter what hair length you choose.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair length for heart-shaped faces:

What if I don't like the recommended hair lengths for heart-shaped faces?

Ultimately, the best hair length is the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. If you don't like the recommended hair lengths for heart-shaped faces, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Can I still have bangs with a heart-shaped face?

Absolutely! Bangs can work well with heart-shaped faces, especially side-swept bangs that draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Do I have to follow these recommendations for hair length?

No, you don't have to follow these recommendations for hair length. However, they are a great starting point to help you find the best hairstyle for your heart-shaped face.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy, shiny, and fabulous.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair length for heart-shaped faces can be a bit of a challenge, but with these tips and recommendations, you can find the perfect hairstyle to enhance your natural beauty. Whether you choose a short haircut, medium length haircut, or long haircut, the key is to create balance between the width at the forehead and the narrowness at the chin. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can look and feel your best every day.
