How To Maintain Curly Hair Without Washing Everyday

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be difficult to maintain. One of the biggest challenges is figuring out how often to wash it. Washing everyday can dry out your curls and strip them of their natural oils, causing frizz and damage. But going too long without washing can leave your hair feeling greasy and weighed down. So, what's the solution? In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for maintaining your curly hair without washing everyday.

How to Maintain Curly Hair Without Washing Everyday

The first step to maintaining your curly hair without washing everyday is to understand your hair type. Curly hair is naturally drier than straight hair, so it requires more moisture. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage. Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

1. Use a Co-Wash

A co-wash is a conditioner that is designed to cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils. Look for a co-wash that is specifically formulated for curly hair. Massage the co-wash into your scalp and through your hair, then rinse it out thoroughly. This will help to remove dirt and product buildup without drying out your curls.

2. Refresh Your Curls

If your curls are looking a little limp or frizzy, you don't need to wash them to revive them. Instead, use a curl refresher spray or mist to perk them up. Look for a product that contains moisturizing ingredients like aloe vera or glycerin. Spray it onto your hair and scrunch your curls to reactivate them.

3. Use a Dry Shampoo

If your hair is feeling greasy, you don't need to wash it. Instead, use a dry shampoo to absorb the excess oil. Look for a dry shampoo that is specifically formulated for curly hair, as some can leave a white residue or make your curls feel stiff. Apply the dry shampoo to the roots of your hair and massage it in. Then, use a wide-tooth comb to distribute it through your hair.

4. Protect Your Curls at Night

Your curls can get flattened and tangled while you sleep, so it's important to protect them. Use a silk or satin pillowcase, or wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet. This will help to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to maintaining your curly hair without washing everyday:

  1. Wet your hair with warm water.
  2. Massage a co-wash into your scalp and through your hair.
  3. Rinse the co-wash out thoroughly.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  5. Scrunch your curls to help them retain their shape.
  6. If your curls are looking limp, use a curl refresher spray or mist to revive them.
  7. If your hair is feeling greasy, use a dry shampoo to absorb the excess oil.
  8. Protect your curls at night by using a silk or satin pillowcase, or wrapping your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.
  9. Style your curls as desired using a curl cream or gel.
  10. Repeat this routine every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt instead.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about maintaining curly hair without washing everyday:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair should be washed every 2-3 days, or as needed. Washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and prone to breakage.

What is a co-wash?

A co-wash is a conditioner that is designed to cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils. It's a great alternative to shampoo for curly hair.

What is a curl refresher?

A curl refresher is a spray or mist that is designed to revive your curls between washes. It can help to reduce frizz and give your curls a boost of moisture.
