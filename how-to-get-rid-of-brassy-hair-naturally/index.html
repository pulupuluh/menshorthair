How To Get Rid Of Brassy Hair Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have blonde or light-colored hair, you may have experienced the frustration of brassy tones. Brassy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including exposure to sun, chlorine, and hard water. If you're tired of dealing with brassy hair, don't worry - there are plenty of natural remedies that can help. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to get rid of brassy hair naturally.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Brassy Hair

How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Naturally

Here are some of the best ways to naturally get rid of brassy hair:

1. Use Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a great option for those with blonde or light-colored hair. It works by neutralizing the yellow and orange tones in your hair, which can make it look less brassy. Use it once or twice a week in place of your regular shampoo.

2. Try a Vinegar Rinse

Vinegar is a natural clarifier and can help remove buildup from your hair. Mix 1 part apple cider vinegar with 2 parts water and use it as a rinse after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Apply a Hair Mask

A hair mask can help nourish and hydrate your hair, which can make it look less brassy. Mix equal parts of honey, coconut oil, and yogurt and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

4. Use Tea as a Rinse

Tea can help darken your hair and make it look less brassy. Brew a strong pot of black tea and let it cool. Use it as a rinse after shampooing, leaving it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting rid of brassy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a purple shampoo
  2. Rinse your hair with an apple cider vinegar rinse
  3. Apply a hair mask
  4. Rinse your hair with black tea

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for getting rid of brassy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and make your hair look more brassy
  • Wear a hat or headscarf when spending time in the sun
  • Use a water filter in your shower to remove hard water minerals
  • Avoid using harsh hair products, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of brassy hair:

1. What causes brassy hair?

Brassy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including exposure to sun, chlorine, and hard water. It can also be caused by using harsh hair products or hot tools on your hair.

2. Can I use lemon juice to get rid of brassy hair?

Lemon juice can help lighten your hair, but it can also be drying and damaging. It's best to avoid using lemon juice on your hair.

Hair Care for Brassy Hair

If you have brassy hair, it's important to take good care of it to prevent further damage. Here are some tips for caring for brassy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Wear a hat or headscarf when spending time in the sun

By following these tips and tricks, you can naturally get rid of brassy hair and keep your locks looking beautiful and healthy.
