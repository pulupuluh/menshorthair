Best Haircuts For Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Eveline May 10, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you in your 50s and looking for the perfect haircut for your wavy hair? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for wavy hair over 50, including how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Description

Wavy hair is known for its effortless and natural look, making it a popular choice for women over 50. However, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that complements your features and hair texture. Here are some of the best haircuts for wavy hair over 50 that you can try:

1. Long Layers

If you have long wavy hair, adding layers can give it more volume and movement. Long layers also help frame your face and bring attention to your best features. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to create long layers that start at your chin and gradually blend into the rest of your hair.

2. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic haircut that looks great on all hair types, including wavy hair over 50. This cut is easy to style and maintain, making it a perfect option for busy women. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to create a blunt cut that falls just above your shoulders.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy haircut that can give you a youthful and modern look. This cut is perfect for women who want to embrace their natural texture and rock a low-maintenance style. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to create short layers that frame your face and add volume to your crown.

How to

Now that you know the best haircuts for wavy hair over 50, it's time to learn how to achieve them. Here are some steps you can follow:

1. Consultation

Before getting a haircut, it's essential to have a consultation with your stylist. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style to determine the best haircut for you. You can also bring pictures of haircuts that you like to show your stylist.

2. Preparation

Before getting your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Avoid using any styling products or oils that can weigh down your hair and make it difficult for your stylist to cut.

3. Cut

Once your stylist knows what haircut you want, they will start cutting your hair. Make sure to communicate with your stylist throughout the process and let them know if you like the length and shape of your hair.

4. Styling

After getting your haircut, your stylist will style your hair to help you achieve the look you want. They may use styling products, heat tools, or other techniques to enhance your hair's texture and volume.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the long layers haircut:

1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Blow-dry your hair until it's 80% dry.

3. Divide your hair into four sections using clips.

4. Take a 1-inch section of hair and hold it straight up. Cut the ends of the hair in a straight line, starting from the chin and working your way down.

5. Repeat this process on all sections of hair until you achieve the desired length and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your wavy haircut:

1. Use the right products

Use products that are designed for wavy hair, such as a curl-defining cream or mousse. These products can help enhance your hair's natural texture and reduce frizz.

2. Protect your hair

Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

FAQ

1. What is the best haircut for wavy hair over 50?

The best haircuts for wavy hair over 50 are long layers, shoulder-length bobs, and pixie cuts. These haircuts can enhance your natural texture and frame your face.

2. How do I style my wavy hair?

You can style your wavy hair by using a curl-defining cream or mousse and scrunching your hair with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture.

3. Can I color my wavy hair?

Yes, you can color your wavy hair. However, it's essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and doesn't damage your hair. It's also crucial to use color-safe shampoos and conditioners to maintain your hair color.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

1. Wash your hair less often

Wavy hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to avoid washing it too often. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Leave the conditioner on your hair for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays. Too much sun exposure can make your hair look dull and dry.

In conclusion, there are many great haircuts for wavy hair over 50. Whether you prefer a long layers haircut, a shoulder-length bob, or a pixie cut, there is a perfect style for you. Follow our tips and care instructions to keep your wavy hair healthy and beautiful.
