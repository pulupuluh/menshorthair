What Colours Suit You Quiz Discover Your Perfect Shade

Written by William Jul 04, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’ve ever wondered what colours suit you best, you’re not alone. With so many shades and tones to choose from, it can be tough to know which ones will flatter your complexion and enhance your natural beauty. But fear not – by taking our what colours suit you quiz, you can find out which hues will make you look and feel your best.

Description

Our what colours suit you quiz is a fun and easy way to discover your perfect shade. By answering a few simple questions about your skin tone, hair colour, and personal style, you’ll receive a personalised colour palette that’s tailored to your unique features and preferences.

How to Take the Quiz

To take our what colours suit you quiz, simply click the “Start Quiz” button below. You’ll be taken through a series of questions that will help us determine which colours will suit you best. Be sure to answer honestly and don’t overthink your responses – the more accurate your answers are, the more accurate your results will be.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start the Quiz

Click the “Start Quiz” button to begin.

Step 2: Answer the Questions

Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability.

Step 3: Get Your Results

Once you’ve answered all of the questions, you’ll receive a personalised colour palette that’s tailored to your unique features and preferences.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Shade

Here are a few tips to help you find the colours that suit you best:

  • Consider your skin tone: If you have warm undertones, look for shades like peach, coral, and gold. If you have cool undertones, try colours like blue, green, and purple.
  • Think about your hair colour: If you have dark hair, vibrant jewel tones like emerald and ruby will look stunning on you. If you have blonde hair, pastel shades like lavender and baby blue will be more flattering.
  • Find your signature colour: Everyone has a colour that they feel confident and comfortable in. Try to identify yours and incorporate it into your wardrobe and makeup routine.

FAQ

What if I don’t like my results?

Remember, our what colours suit you quiz is just a guide – you should always wear colours that make you feel confident and comfortable.

Can I take the quiz more than once?

Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like to see if your results change.

Do I need to buy new clothes and makeup in my recommended colours?

No, you don’t need to buy anything new. Our quiz is simply a tool to help you identify the colours that will flatter you most.

Hair Care Tips for Your Perfect Shade

Once you’ve found your perfect shade, you’ll want to make sure your hair looks its best too. Here are a few hair care tips to help you maintain your colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools with a hat or heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting a colour touch-up every 4-6 weeks to keep your shade looking fresh.

Now that you know which colours suit you best, it’s time to start incorporating them into your wardrobe and beauty routine. With a little experimentation and creativity, you’ll soon discover your signature style – and feel confident and beautiful every day.
