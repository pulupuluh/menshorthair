How To Make Thick Hair Look Good Short Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thick hair that always seems to get in the way? Maybe you're ready for a change and want to try a shorter hairstyle. But how do you make thick hair look good short? In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you rock a short hairstyle with your thick locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Thick Hair Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Thick Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Thick Hair
  • FAQs about Short Hair for Thick Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

How to Cut Thick Hair Short

Before you can style your thick hair in a short cut, you need to make sure you get the right cut. Here are some tips for cutting thick hair short:

  • Choose a style that works with your hair's natural texture. If you have curly hair, for example, choose a style that embraces your curls rather than trying to straighten them out.
  • Ask your stylist to thin out your hair to reduce bulk. This will make it easier to style and can prevent your hair from looking too heavy or boxy.
  • Consider a layered cut to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try bangs if you like them, but keep them on the longer side to avoid adding too much bulk to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Thick Hair

Now that you have the right cut, it's time to style your short thick hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add lift and texture.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair, if desired.
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Thick Hair

Once you've styled your short thick hair, you'll want to maintain it. Here are some tips to help:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time. Use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes.

FAQs about Short Hair for Thick Hair

Can I still wear my hair up with a short cut?

Absolutely! You can still wear your hair up with a short cut. Try a messy bun or a half-up style for a cute and easy look.

What if I don't like my short cut?

If you're not happy with your short cut, talk to your stylist about making some tweaks or growing it out. It's important to love your hair, so don't be afraid to speak up.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's often envied for its volume and body, it can also be difficult to manage. Here are some hair care tips for thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair to help control frizz and add moisture.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim for every other day or every few days instead.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Opt for lightweight products instead.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your thick hair look good short. Whether you're going for a bob, a pixie cut, or something in between, embrace your natural texture and have fun with your new style!
