How To Do Slicked Back Hairstyle Female A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Feb 27, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a chic and polished hairstyle that’s easy to achieve, the slicked back hairstyle for females is a great option. This hairstyle is perfect for both formal and casual occasions and can be worn with any hair length. In this post, we’ll be sharing a step-by-step guide on how to do the slicked back hairstyle for females, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Do Slicked Back Hairstyle Female
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicked Back Hairstyle Female
  • FAQs

How to Do Slicked Back Hairstyle Female

The slicked back hairstyle is all about creating a clean and polished look, so start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean and damp, apply a styling gel or pomade to your hair. You can use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from the roots to the tips.

Next, brush your hair straight back, starting from the front of your hairline. Use a fine-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Once your hair is slicked back, you can leave it as is or create a low bun or ponytail. To create a bun, twist your hair into a coil and secure it with a hair tie. To create a ponytail, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash and condition your hair.

2. Apply a styling gel or pomade to your hair.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Brush your hair straight back, starting from the front of your hairline.

5. Use a fine-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

6. Create a low bun or ponytail, if desired.

Tips for Slicked Back Hairstyle Female

- Use a styling gel or pomade that’s specifically designed for slicked back hairstyles.

- Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

- Use a fine-tooth comb or boar bristle brush to create a smooth and polished look.

- If you have short hair, you may need to use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

FAQs

Q: Can I do the slicked back hairstyle if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve the slicked back hairstyle if you have curly hair. However, you may want to use a stronger styling product to keep your curls in place.

Q: Can I wear the slicked back hairstyle to a formal event?

A: Yes, the slicked back hairstyle is a great option for formal events, as it looks polished and sophisticated.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it’s important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair.

In addition, it’s important to invest in high-quality hair styling products, such as gels, pomades, and hair sprays, that are specifically designed for your hair type and style. These products can help you achieve your desired look while also protecting your hair from damage.

Overall, the slicked back hairstyle for females is a chic and versatile look that’s easy to achieve with the right products and techniques. Whether you’re getting ready for a formal event or just want to look polished and put together for a night out, this hairstyle is a great option for any occasion.
