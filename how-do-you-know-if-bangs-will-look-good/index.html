How Do You Know If Bangs Will Look Good

Written by Daniel Mar 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are thinking about getting bangs, you might be wondering how to know if they will look good on you. Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look, but they can also be a big commitment. Here are some tips to help you determine if bangs are right for you.

Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if bangs will look good on you is to consider your face shape. Bangs can be a great way to highlight your best features and balance out your face. If you have a round face, for example, bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, bangs can help balance out your forehead and chin.

It's important to talk to your stylist about your face shape and what kind of bangs will work best for you. They can help you determine if blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or another style will look best with your face shape.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if bangs will look good on you is your hair texture. If you have thick, coarse hair, blunt bangs can be a great option. If you have fine hair, side-swept bangs can help add volume and texture.

It's important to talk to your stylist about your hair texture and what kind of bangs will work best for you. They can help you determine if bangs will be easy to style and maintain with your hair texture.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When deciding if bangs will look good on you, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Bangs can be high maintenance, so if you have a busy schedule or don't like to spend a lot of time styling your hair, bangs might not be the best option for you.

It's also important to consider your activities and hobbies. If you like to work out or swim, bangs might not be practical for you. They can also be a hassle if you wear glasses or sunglasses regularly.

Think About Haircut Maintenance

Bangs require regular maintenance, so it's important to think about how often you are willing to visit the salon for trims. Depending on the style of bangs you choose, you may need to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

It's also important to consider the cost of regular haircuts. If you are on a budget, bangs might not be the best option for you.

Think About Your Styling Ability

When deciding if bangs will look good on you, it's important to think about your styling ability. Bangs can require a lot of styling to keep them looking their best, so if you aren't comfortable using a blow dryer or flat iron regularly, bangs might not be the best option for you.

It's also important to think about the products you will need to style your bangs. Depending on the style of bangs you choose, you may need to use hairspray, mousse, or other styling products to keep them in place.

Consider Face-Framing Bangs

If you are unsure if bangs will look good on you, you might want to consider face-framing bangs. These are shorter pieces of hair that frame your face, but are not a full-on bang. Face-framing bangs can be a great way to switch up your look without committing to a full-on bang.

Try Before You Buy

If you are unsure if bangs will look good on you, you might want to try clip-in bangs before committing to a real haircut. Clip-in bangs can give you an idea of what you will look like with bangs without the commitment.

FAQ

Q: How do I know what kind of bangs will look good on me?

A: The best way to determine what kind of bangs will look good on you is to talk to your stylist. They can help you determine what style will work best with your face shape and hair texture.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: Depending on the style of bangs you choose, you may need to trim them every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Q: Are bangs high maintenance?

A: Yes, bangs can be high maintenance. They require regular trims and styling to keep them looking their best.

Hair Care

If you do decide to get bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Wash your bangs regularly and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a blow dryer or flat iron to style your bangs, and use a hairspray or other styling product to keep them in place.

It's also important to protect your bangs from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

Now that you know how to determine if bangs will look good on you, you can confidently make a decision about whether or not to get them. Remember, it's important to talk to your stylist and consider your lifestyle and hair care routine before making a decision.
