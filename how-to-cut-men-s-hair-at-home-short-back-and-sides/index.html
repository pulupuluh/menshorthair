How To Cut Men s Hair At Home Short Back And Sides

Written by Oliver Mar 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut, you can easily do it at home with some basic tools and techniques. In this article, we'll be focusing on how to cut men's hair at home short back and sides. It's a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style and is easy to maintain. Let's get started!

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a timeless style that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn with a variety of lengths on top and styled in different ways. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and put-together.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of quality hair clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel-dry the hair until it's slightly damp. This will make it easier to cut and will also help the hair lay flat.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

Using your clippers and a guard size that matches the desired length, start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up to the top of the sides and back. Keep the clippers level with the head and move them in an upward motion. Repeat this process with different guard sizes until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can use a comb to help guide the scissors and ensure an even cut. If you want a textured look, you can point-cut the hair by cutting into it vertically instead of horizontally.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use the clippers without a guard to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a flicking motion to blend the hair. You can also use the scissors to blend any harsh lines.

Step 5: Clean up the Neckline and Sideburns

Using your clippers or scissors, clean up the hairline at the back of the neck and the sideburns. You can use a comb to help guide your cutting and ensure a clean, straight line.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once
- Use a mirror to check the back of the head
- Take your time and don't rush the process
- If you're not sure how short to cut, start with a longer length and cut more if needed
- Practice makes perfect!

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I cut my own hair if I have long hair?

Cutting long hair can be more challenging, but it's still possible to do it at home. You'll need to use scissors instead of clippers and be more careful with the cutting process. It's recommended to start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once.

How do I clean my clippers?

After each use, remove any excess hair from the clippers with a brush or cloth. You can also use a disinfectant spray or wipe to clean the blades. Make sure to oil the blades regularly to keep them sharp and functioning properly.

Hair Care

To maintain your short back and sides haircut, it's important to keep your hair clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much product. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair at home short back and sides, you can give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut anytime. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and practice your technique. Happy cutting!
