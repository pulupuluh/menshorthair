How To Tell Which Hair Color Is Best For You

Written by Luffy Apr 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing a hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are unsure of what shade will suit you best. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to determine which hair color will enhance your natural features and complement your skin tone. In this article, we will guide you on how to tell which hair color is best for you, providing step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  2. Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  3. Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  4. Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Color
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair color is best for you is to identify your skin tone. Your skin tone can either be warm or cool, which will determine the hair colors that will suit you best. Here are some simple steps to help you determine your skin tone:

Step 1: Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Check Your Skin’s Reaction to Sun Exposure

How does your skin react when exposed to the sun? If you burn easily and don’t tan, you have a cool skin tone. If you tan easily, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 3: Check Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help determine your skin tone. If you have blue, green, or grey eyes, you have a cool skin tone. If you have brown, amber, or hazel eyes, you have a warm skin tone.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you will look best in hair colors that have warm undertones. Here are some hair colors that will suit you best:

  • Golden blonde
  • Honey blonde
  • Caramel
  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Warm brown

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you will look best in hair colors that have cool undertones. Here are some hair colors that will suit you best:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Icy blonde
  • Jet black
  • Cool brown
  • Burgundy

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair color that requires minimal upkeep.
  • Think about your wardrobe: Consider your clothing colors when choosing a hair color. Your hair color should complement your wardrobe.
  • Consult a professional: If you are unsure about which hair color to choose, consult a professional stylist who can provide expert advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the perfect hair color:

What hair color suits fair skin?

People with fair skin look best in hair colors with cool undertones, such as platinum blonde, ash blonde, and cool brown.

What hair color suits olive skin?

People with olive skin can pull off a wide range of hair colors, but look best in warm hair colors, such as caramel, honey blonde, and copper.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it is important to use hair dye specifically designed for sensitive skin. Always do a patch test before applying hair dye to your entire head.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools to prevent your hair color from fading.

Choosing the perfect hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these simple steps, tips, and frequently asked questions, you can determine which hair color will suit you best and maintain your gorgeous new look.
