How To Get Curls In Your Hair Boy A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Feb 16, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a boy who wants to add some curls to your hair? You're not alone! Many guys are looking for ways to get curly hair without having to go to a salon. Luckily, there are a few simple steps you can follow to get those curls you've been dreaming of. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get curls in your hair boy, including how to do it, step by step, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curls in Your Hair Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting and Maintaining Curls
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curls in Your Hair Boy

There are a few methods you can try to get curls in your hair as a boy. Some of these include:

  • Using curling irons or hot rollers
  • Using a perm kit
  • Using natural hair products and techniques

Which method you choose will depend on your hair type, texture, and personal preferences. Keep reading to learn more about each method and how to get the best results.

Using Curling Irons or Hot Rollers

If you want to add curls to your hair temporarily, using curling irons or hot rollers is a good option. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Take one section at a time and wrap it around the curling iron or hot roller, starting from the end and rolling towards your scalp.
  5. Hold the curl in place for a few seconds, then release it.
  6. Repeat with the rest of your hair.
  7. Once all your hair is curled, use a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Note: Be sure to use a curling iron or hot roller with a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel; for looser curls, use a larger barrel.

Using a Perm Kit

If you want to add curls to your hair permanently, using a perm kit is an option. However, this method should only be done by a professional, as it can be damaging to your hair if done incorrectly. Here's how a perm works:

  1. Your hair is washed and wrapped around perm rods.
  2. A perm solution is applied to your hair, which breaks down the bonds that give your hair its shape.
  3. After a set amount of time, a neutralizer is applied to your hair to reform the bonds in the new shape (curly).
  4. Your hair is rinsed and styled.

Again, we recommend going to a salon to get a perm, as it can be difficult to do at home.

Using Natural Hair Products and Techniques

If you don't want to use heat or chemicals to get curls in your hair, you can try using natural hair products and techniques. Here are a few things to try:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a curl cream or mousse to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing the curls.
  • Twist your hair into sections while it's damp and let it air dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curls in your hair as a boy:

  1. Decide which method you want to use (curling irons, perm kit, or natural hair products).
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it.
  3. Apply any necessary products, such as heat protectant spray or curl cream.
  4. Follow the instructions for your chosen method (see above).
  5. Once your hair is curled, use a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Getting and Maintaining Curls

Here are a few tips for getting and maintaining curls in your hair:

  • Use the right products for your hair type (curly, wavy, or straight).
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Refresh your curls in the morning with a spritz of water and a bit of curl cream.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions about getting curls in your hair as a boy:

Can boys use curling irons and hot rollers?

Yes, anyone can use curling irons and hot rollers to add curls to their hair.

Is it safe to use a perm kit on your hair?

Perms can be damaging to your hair if done incorrectly, so we recommend going to a professional salon to get a perm.

How often should you wash curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair only once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically for curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can get curls in your hair as a boy and maintain them for a stylish and trendy look. Whether you choose to use heat styling tools or natural methods, be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
