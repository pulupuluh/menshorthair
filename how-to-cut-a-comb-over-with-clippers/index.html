How To Cut A Comb Over With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic, stylish haircut that's easy to maintain, a comb over might be just what you need. And with the right clippers, you can easily achieve the look at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a comb over with clippers, step by step.

How to Cut a Comb Over with Clippers

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

To start, you'll need a good pair of clippers. Look for a set that comes with different guards, so you can customize the length of your hair. You may also want to invest in a pair of scissors, for any touch-ups or trimming around the edges.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with, and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Decide on Your Part

Next, decide where you want your part to be. This will determine how you cut your hair, and where you focus your attention.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Using the clippers and a guard, start cutting the sides of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, moving the clippers against the grain of your hair. Use a smaller guard for the bottom, and gradually increase the length as you move up.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you've finished the sides, it's time to blend them with the top of your hair. Start by removing the guard from your clippers, and using just the blade. Use short, upward strokes to blend the two sections together.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of your hair. Using scissors or the clippers with a guard, trim your hair to your desired length. Be sure to cut straight across, and take your time to ensure an even cut.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to style your hair. Apply some pomade or styling cream, and use a comb to create your part. Use your fingers to style your hair into place, and add some more product if needed.

Tips for Cutting a Comb Over with Clippers

Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve a great-looking comb over every time:

  • Start with a longer guard, and work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress, and make sure you're cutting evenly on both sides.
  • Take your time, and don't rush the process. It's better to take a little longer and get a good cut, than to rush and end up with a botched job.

FAQ

Can I Cut a Comb Over with Scissors?

Yes, you can cut a comb over with scissors. However, clippers are usually faster and more efficient, especially if you're cutting your own hair.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows, and how quickly you want to maintain your style. As a general rule, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water, to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

With these tips, you'll be able to maintain a great-looking comb over, and keep your hair healthy and strong.
