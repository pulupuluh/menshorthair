How To Sleep With Short Hair Extensions A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Feb 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair extensions can be a great way to add volume, length, and style to your hair. However, sleeping with them can be quite challenging. Tangled, frizzy hair in the morning can ruin your entire look, and nobody wants that. In this blog post, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to sleep with short hair extensions without damaging them, so you can wake up with perfect hair every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Sleep with Short Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Sleeping with Short Hair Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair Extensions

How to Sleep with Short Hair Extensions

Sleeping with short hair extensions can be tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some things you should keep in mind:

  • Brush your hair before going to bed.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase.
  • Tie your hair up in a loose bun or braid.
  • Don't sleep with wet hair.

Following these simple steps can help you keep your short hair extensions looking great even after a night's sleep.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to sleep with short hair extensions:

  1. Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, and braid each section loosely.
  3. Tie the two braids together at the nape of your neck.
  4. Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or use a silk or satin pillowcase.
  5. Go to sleep and wake up with beautiful, tangle-free hair.

Tips for Sleeping with Short Hair Extensions

Here are some additional tips that can help you sleep with short hair extensions:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner before going to bed to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.
  • Don't sleep with damp hair, as it can cause your hair to tangle and break.
  • Don't use hair ties or clips that are too tight, as they can cause tension and damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair net to keep your hair in place while you sleep.
  • Avoid sleeping on your back or stomach, as this can cause your hair to flatten and tangle.

FAQs

Can I sleep with wet short hair extensions?

No, it's not recommended to sleep with wet short hair extensions. Wet hair is more prone to tangling and breakage, and sleeping with wet hair can cause damage to both your natural hair and your extensions.

Will sleeping with short hair extensions damage them?

It depends on how you sleep with them. Sleeping with short hair extensions without taking proper precautions can cause tangling, frizz, and damage to your extensions. However, if you follow the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can sleep with short hair extensions without damaging them.

How long can I wear short hair extensions?

Short hair extensions can last anywhere from 6 to 12 weeks, depending on the quality of the hair and how well you take care of them.

Hair Care for Short Hair Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the longevity of your short hair extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Brush your hair gently and regularly with a soft-bristled brush or a wide-tooth comb.
  • Wash your hair with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and flat irons, as they can damage your extensions.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can sleep with short hair extensions without damaging them and maintain beautiful, healthy hair.

Now that you know how to sleep with short hair extensions, go ahead and try these tips tonight to wake up with perfect hair every day.
