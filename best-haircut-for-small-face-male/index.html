Best Haircut For Small Face Male A Complete Guide In 2023

If you have a small face, finding the right haircut can be a bit challenging. A good haircut can help to balance out your features and make your face look more proportionate. In this guide, we will discuss the best haircut for small face male and provide some tips on how to maintain it.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Small Face Male

Before choosing a haircut, it is important to understand your face shape. If you have a small face, you should opt for a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and makes your face look wider. You should also avoid haircuts that make your face look longer.

When choosing a haircut, it is important to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have fine hair, a layered haircut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a shorter haircut can help to manage it better.

Best Haircuts for Small Face Male

Here are some of the best haircuts for small face male:

1. Short Side Part

A short side part is a classic haircut that can work well for small face male. The short sides and back help to balance out the proportions of the face, while the longer hair on top adds volume and texture.

2. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a low-maintenance haircut that can work well for small face male. It can help to make the face look wider and more proportionate. It is also a great option if you have fine hair.

3. Crew Cut

A crew cut is a popular haircut that can work well for small face male. It is a short haircut that is tapered on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. It can help to add volume and texture to the hair.

4. Quiff

A quiff is a stylish haircut that can work well for small face male. It is characterized by a longer hair on top that is styled upwards and back. It can help to add height and dimension to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Get the Best Haircut for Small Face Male

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the best haircut for small face male:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your barber or stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Step 3: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to add volume and texture. Use a product that is suitable for your hair type and desired style.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a comb, brush, or your fingers. Work the product evenly through your hair and style it according to your desired look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep it in place. Use a hairspray that is suitable for your hair type and desired hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a styling product that is suitable for your hair type and desired style.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a small face male?

The best haircut for a small face male is a haircut that adds volume to the top of the head and makes the face look wider. Short side part, buzz cut, crew cut, and quiff are some of the best haircuts for small face male.

Can I style my hair differently after getting a haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair differently after getting a haircut. Consult with your barber or stylist on how to style your hair and what products to use.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Small Face Male

Here are some hair care tips for small face male:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing the best haircut for small face male can be a daunting task, but with the right information and guidance, you can find a haircut that complements your features and makes you look your best. Remember to consult with your barber or stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. Follow the tips and guidelines provided in this guide to maintain your haircut and promote healthy hair growth.
