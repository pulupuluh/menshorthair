How To Style Short Undercut Pixie A Relaxed English Guide

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that's both stylish and edgy, the short undercut pixie might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle features a longer top and shorter sides, with a shaved undercut that adds a touch of attitude. To help you achieve the perfect look, we've put together a step-by-step guide on how to style short undercut pixie. Read on for tips, tricks, and answers to common questions.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Short Undercut Pixie

The short undercut pixie is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. Here are a few popular options:

1. Messy Textured Look

This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks chic. To achieve the messy textured look, simply apply some texturizing spray or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to tousle it. You can also use a small amount of wax to create definition and hold.

2. Sleek and Polished

If you're looking for a more polished look, try straightening your hair with a flat iron and applying some hair serum for shine. You can also use a small amount of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

3. Edgy and Punk

The short undercut pixie is already edgy, but you can take it to the next level by adding some colorful highlights or shaving designs into the undercut. You can also use a strong-hold gel or wax to style your hair into spikes or a faux hawk.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of the different styles you can achieve with a short undercut pixie, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style it:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. This will help your hair hold its shape better.

2. Apply styling product

Apply your preferred styling product to your hair. This can be texturizing spray, pomade, wax, gel, or hairspray depending on the look you're going for.

3. Style your hair

Use your fingers, a brush, or a comb to style your hair. You can tousle it for a messy look, straighten it for a sleek look, or create spikes for an edgy look.

4. Finish with hairspray

Apply a small amount of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks for styling your short undercut pixie:

  • Use a small amount of product at a time. You can always add more if you need it.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different looks.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Don't overdo it with the hairspray. Too much can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my short undercut pixie without using any product?

A: Yes, you can. However, using a styling product can help your hair hold its shape better and make it easier to style.

Q: How often should I wash my short undercut pixie?

A: This depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people can go a few days between washes, while others need to wash their hair every day.

Q: Can I dye my short undercut pixie?

A: Yes, you can. However, keep in mind that bleaching and coloring can damage your hair, so it's important to use a good quality dye and take good care of your hair.

Hair Care for Short Undercut Pixie

Here are a few tips for keeping your short undercut pixie looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and peroxide on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short undercut pixie like a pro in no time. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
