Short Haircut For Curly Hair With Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of long curly hair that always ends up in a messy bun? Do you have a round face and struggle to find the perfect short haircut for your curls? Look no further, because in this article we will guide you through the process of finding the best short haircut for curly hair with a round face.

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it is unique and beautiful, it can also be challenging to style and maintain. This is especially true for those with a round face as the wrong haircut can make their face appear even rounder. However, with the right short haircut, you can accentuate your curls and flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Curly Hair with a Round Face

Choosing the right short haircut for curly hair with a round face can be a daunting task, but it is not impossible. Here are some things to consider:

1. Face Shape

The first thing to consider when choosing a short haircut is your face shape. For those with a round face, it is best to opt for a haircut that creates the illusion of length and angles.

2. Hair Texture

The second thing to consider when choosing a short haircut is your hair texture. Curly hair has different textures, and it is essential to choose a haircut that works well with your hair type.

3. Lifestyle

The third thing to consider when choosing a short haircut is your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Curly Hair with a Round Face

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short haircut, here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short haircut for your curly hair with a round face:

1. Research Hairstyles

Start by researching different hairstyles that work well with curly hair and a round face. Look for inspiration from celebrities or ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

2. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist and bring pictures of the hairstyles you like. Discuss your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best short haircut for you.

3. Prepare Your Hair

Before your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and detangled. Your hairstylist may also recommend a deep conditioning treatment to prepare your hair for the cut.

4. Get the Haircut

Once you and your hairstylist have decided on the perfect short haircut, it's time to get the cut. Sit back, relax, and let your hairstylist work their magic.

5. Style Your Hair

After your haircut, ask your hairstylist for tips on styling your new cut. They may recommend products or techniques to enhance your curls and flatter your face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Curly Hair with a Round Face

Now that you have the perfect short haircut, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your haircut looking fresh:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for curly hair to enhance your curls and keep them defined.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your haircut looking fresh, schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even rounder?

A: It depends on the haircut. Opt for a short haircut that creates angles and lengthens your face to avoid making it look rounder.

Q: What products are best for curly hair?

A: Look for products that are designed for curly hair and provide moisture and definition.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: Schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Conclusion

Choosing the right short haircut for curly hair with a round face can be challenging, but with the right considerations and a great hairstylist, you can find the perfect haircut. Remember to maintain your haircut with the right products and regular trims to keep it looking fresh. With these tips, you'll be rocking your new short haircut in no time!
