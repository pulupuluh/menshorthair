Medium Long Hairstyles Boy A Guide To Styling And Maintenance

Written by Robby Mar 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that's not too short or too long? Medium long hairstyles for boys might be the perfect solution. This hairstyle falls somewhere between a short and long haircut and can be versatile enough to suit any occasion. In this article, we'll guide you through how to style and maintain medium long hairstyles for boys, as well as share some tips and frequently asked questions.

Description

Medium long hairstyles for boys typically fall between the chin and shoulders. This length is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of the way but still want some length to style. The great thing about medium long hairstyles is that they can be styled in many different ways, whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style.

How to Style Medium Long Hairstyles for Boys

Styling medium long hairstyles for boys can be easy with the right products and tools. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while styling it with your fingers or a brush. You can create volume by blow drying your hair upside down or use a round brush to create a sleeker look.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Long Hair for Boys

If you're new to styling medium long hair for boys, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your desired look:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while styling it with your fingers or a brush.
  4. If you want a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create volume and texture by scrunching your hair as you blow dry it.
  5. If you prefer a sleeker look, use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow dry it.
  6. Finish off with a styling cream or hairspray to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Long Hairstyles for Boys

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium long hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools to prevent heat damage.

FAQs

What face shape is best suited for medium long hairstyles for boys?

Medium long hairstyles for boys can suit any face shape, but they tend to look best on oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I wash my hair if I have a medium long hairstyle?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have a medium long hairstyle. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and brittle.

What styling products should I use for a messy, textured look?

You can use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to create a messy, textured look. These products add volume and texture to your hair without making it look greasy.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Long Hairstyles for Boys

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your medium long hairstyle looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often. These can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. This is gentler on your hair and prevents breakage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause hair loss and damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and keep your hair looking healthy.

Now that you know how to style and maintain medium long hairstyles for boys, it's time to try out some new looks. Whether you prefer a messy, textured style or a sleek, polished look, there's a medium long hairstyle that's perfect for you. Just remember to take good care of your hair and use the right products and tools to keep it looking its best.
