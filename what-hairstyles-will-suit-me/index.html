What Hairstyles Will Suit Me

Written by Daniel Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to change up your look but don't know what will suit you? In this blog post, we will discuss various hairstyles that will suit different face shapes and hair types. Read on to find out what hairstyles will suit you!

Description

Choosing a hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially when there are so many options available. However, selecting a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type can make all the difference in enhancing your features and making you feel confident. In this article, we will provide you with tips and recommendations on what hairstyles will suit you based on your face shape and hair type.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face straight on. Then, use a ruler or measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Compare the measurements and determine which face shape you have.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky as most hairstyles will suit you. Consider trying a layered haircut, a lob (long bob), or a side parting with loose waves. These hairstyles will enhance your features and make your face appear more balanced.

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face. Consider trying a long layered haircut, a side-swept fringe, or a high ponytail. These styles will create the illusion of a longer face shape and make your features appear more defined.

Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose a hairstyle that will soften your features. Consider trying a long layered haircut with soft waves, a side-swept fringe, or a messy updo. These styles will create a more feminine look and soften your jawline.

Hairstyles for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, choose a hairstyle that will balance out your chin. Consider trying a long layered haircut with beachy waves, a side-swept fringe, or a messy bun. These styles will draw attention away from your chin and enhance your cheekbones.

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose a hairstyle that will emphasize your cheekbones. Consider trying a short bob, a pixie cut, or a side-swept fringe. These styles will enhance your features and make your face appear more symmetrical.

Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, choose a hairstyle that will add width to your face. Consider trying a layered haircut with curls, a side-swept fringe, or a messy bun. These styles will create the illusion of a wider face shape and enhance your features.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, consider the following tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair
  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your stylist to ensure they understand your vision
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I still try a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! These recommendations are just a guide, and ultimately, it's up to you to decide what hairstyle you want to try. However, choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type can make all the difference in enhancing your features and making you feel confident.

Hair Care

Remember, taking care of your hair is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful locks. Regularly wash and condition your hair, use heat protectant before styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends. By taking care of your hair, you will be able to experiment with different hairstyles and feel confident in your new look.
