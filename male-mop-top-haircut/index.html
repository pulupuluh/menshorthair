Male Mop Top Haircut The Classic Style That Never Goes Out Of Fashion

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 · 7 min read

The male mop top haircut is a classic hairstyle that has been around since the 1960s. The style is characterized by long, shaggy hair that falls over the forehead and ears. It was made famous by the Beatles, who sported the look during their early years. Today, the style is still popular and has been adapted to suit modern tastes. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the male mop top haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to care for it.

Description

The male mop top haircut is a low-maintenance style that is perfect for men who want to look stylish without spending too much time on their hair. The hair is cut in layers, with the longest layers falling over the forehead and ears. The style is typically worn with a side parting, but can also be worn with a center parting or no parting at all.

How to Get the Male Mop Top Haircut

To get the male mop top haircut, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. The stylist will start by washing your hair and then cutting it in layers. They will leave the longest layers at the top and trim the hair at the back and sides. The length of the hair will depend on your preference, but it is usually kept between 3 to 6 inches long.

Tips:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to your hairstylist
  • Make sure your stylist understands the length and texture of your hair
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Male Mop Top Haircut

Styling the male mop top haircut is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax.
  4. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, shaggy look.
  5. Finish off by using a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • Try using a hair straightener to create a sleeker look
  • Use a hairdryer to create volume and texture

FAQs

Q: Is the male mop top haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the style can be adapted to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a male mop top haircut?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: Can the male mop top haircut be worn with a beard?

A: Yes, the style can be worn with a beard to create a rugged, masculine look.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your male mop top haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style

By following these tips, you can keep your male mop top haircut looking its best for years to come.

Conclusion

The male mop top haircut is a classic style that has stood the test of time. It is a low-maintenance style that can be adapted to suit modern tastes. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can get the male mop top haircut, style it to perfection, and keep it looking great for years to come.
