Best Hairstyles For Thinning Hair On Crown

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are experiencing thinning hair on your crown, don't worry, you are not alone. Many people have this issue and there are ways to style your hair to make it look fuller and thicker. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for thinning hair on crown.

Description

Thinning hair on crown is a common issue that affects both men and women. As we age, our hair starts to thin out due to various reasons such as genetics, hormonal changes, and even our lifestyle. Thinning hair on crown can be frustrating, but there are ways to style your hair to make it look fuller and thicker. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for thinning hair on crown.

How To

The first step to styling thinning hair on crown is to choose the right haircut. A good haircut can make a huge difference in the way your hair looks. Here are some of the best hairstyles for thinning hair on crown:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for thinning hair on crown. A short haircut can make your hair look fuller and thicker. A pixie cut, a bob, or a layered cut can be a great option for thinning hair on crown. Short haircuts are low maintenance and easy to style, which makes them a popular choice for people with thinning hair.

Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts are another great option for thinning hair on crown. A medium length haircut can add volume and texture to your hair. A layered haircut or a shaggy haircut can be a great option for thinning hair on crown. A medium length haircut can be styled in various ways, which makes it a versatile option for people with thinning hair.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can also be a great option for thinning hair on crown. Long hair can add volume and texture to your hair. A layered haircut or a wavy haircut can be a great option for thinning hair on crown. Long hair can be styled in various ways, which makes it a versatile option for people with thinning hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions on how to style your hair for thinning hair on crown:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Use a volumizing spray

Use a volumizing spray on your hair. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or a straightening iron. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your hair for thinning hair on crown:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  • Use a volumizing spray
  • Style your hair using a curling iron or a straightening iron
  • Avoid using heavy styling products
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have thinning hair on crown?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have thinning hair on crown. Just make sure to add some volume and texture to your hair before you put it up.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have thinning hair on crown?

A: Yes, you should avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly. These hairstyles can make your hair look thinner.

Hair Care

If you have thinning hair on crown, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Take supplements
  • Avoid smoking and excessive drinking
  • Get enough sleep

Remember, thinning hair on crown is a common issue and there are ways to style your hair to make it look fuller and thicker. Use the tips and hairstyles mentioned in this article to help you style your hair for thinning hair on crown.
