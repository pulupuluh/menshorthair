How To Straighten Very Short Curly Hair A Simple Guide

Written by Eveline Feb 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have very short curly hair, you may find it difficult to straighten it. Straightening curly hair requires a lot of time, patience, and effort, but it's not impossible. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to straighten very short curly hair. We'll also provide you with tips on how to care for your hair after straightening it.

How to Straighten Very Short Curly Hair

Straightening very short curly hair can be tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some things you'll need:

  • A good quality flat iron
  • Heat protectant spray or serum
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair clips or elastics

Before you start straightening your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wet hair is more prone to damage, so wait until your hair is completely dry before you start straightening it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to straighten very short curly hair:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics. This will make it easier to straighten your hair.
  3. Take a small section of hair and comb it through. Make sure there are no tangles.
  4. Place the flat iron at the base of the section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Don't hold the flat iron in one place for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of hair until all of your hair is straightened.

And that's it! Straightening very short curly hair can be time-consuming, but the results are worth it.

Tips for Straightening Very Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you straighten your very short curly hair:

  • Use a good quality flat iron with ceramic plates. This will help distribute heat evenly and reduce damage to your hair.
  • Don't straighten your hair too often. Frequent straightening can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don't use too much heat. Use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair.
  • Use a comb or brush to help straighten your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening very short curly hair:

Can I straighten my hair if it's wet?

No, you should never straighten your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is more prone to damage, so wait until your hair is completely dry before you start straightening it.

How often should I straighten my hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair too often. Frequent straightening can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit straightening to once a week.

What should I do if my hair gets damaged from straightening?

If your hair gets damaged from straightening, you should give your hair a break from heat styling. Use a deep conditioning treatment to help repair your hair.

Hair Care After Straightening

After straightening your very short curly hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

Straightening very short curly hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look. Just remember to take care of your hair after straightening it to maintain its health and prevent damage.
