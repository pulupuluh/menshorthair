How To Cut Thick Short Hair At Home

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending time and money at the hair salon? Do you want to learn how to cut your thick short hair at home? Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have thick hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to cut thick short hair at home.

How to

The first step to cutting your own hair is to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help wet your hair and make it easier to cut. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut. Use a towel to dry your hair and make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into four parts: the front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section. Use your comb to comb your hair straight down and cut it straight across. Make sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut. Use the mirror to check the back of your head to make sure it is even.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move on to the side sections. Comb your hair straight down and cut it straight across. Make sure to cut at an angle so that the hair is shorter towards the back of your head and longer towards the front.

Step 5: Cut the Front Section

Finally, move on to the front section. Comb your hair straight down and cut it straight across. Make sure to cut at an angle so that the hair is longer towards the front of your head and shorter towards the back.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger sections
  • Use a comb to comb your hair straight down before cutting
  • Cut at an angle to achieve a layered look
  • Use the mirror to check the back of your head
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick short hair:

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it is not recommended to use regular scissors to cut your hair. You should use professional hair cutting scissors to achieve the best results.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a healthy and even look.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional hair stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and always use professional hair cutting scissors. Happy cutting!
