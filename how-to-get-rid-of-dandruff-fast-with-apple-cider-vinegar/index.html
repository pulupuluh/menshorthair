How To Get Rid Of Dandruff Fast With Apple Cider Vinegar

Written by Petter Mar 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Dandruff is a common scalp condition that causes the skin to become flaky and itchy. It can be caused by various factors such as dry skin, oily skin, fungal infections, and more. While there are many treatments available to get rid of dandruff, one natural remedy that has gained popularity is apple cider vinegar. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of dandruff fast with apple cider vinegar.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff
  • Tips for Using Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff

  • Hair Care Tips to Prevent Dandruff

How to Use Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff

Apple cider vinegar has natural antifungal and antibacterial properties that make it an effective treatment for dandruff. It also helps to balance the pH level of the scalp, which can reduce the growth of yeast that can cause dandruff. Here are some ways to use apple cider vinegar for dandruff:

Vinegar Rinse

One of the easiest ways to use apple cider vinegar for dandruff is to use it as a rinse. Mix equal parts of apple cider vinegar and water and apply it to your scalp after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes and rinse it off with water. Repeat this once or twice a week.

Vinegar and Tea Tree Oil

Tea tree oil is a natural antifungal and antiseptic agent that can help to treat dandruff. Mix a few drops of tea tree oil with apple cider vinegar and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 15-20 minutes and rinse it off with water. Repeat this once a week.

Vinegar and Baking Soda

Baking soda helps to exfoliate the scalp and remove dead skin cells, which can contribute to dandruff. Mix equal parts of apple cider vinegar and water and add a teaspoon of baking soda. Apply it to your scalp and massage it gently. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes and rinse it off with water. Repeat this once a week.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff

Now that you know how to use apple cider vinegar for dandruff, here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and rinse it off with water.
  2. Mix equal parts of apple cider vinegar and water.
  3. Apply the mixture to your scalp and massage it gently.
  4. Leave it on for a few minutes and rinse it off with water.
  5. Condition your hair as usual.
  6. Repeat this once or twice a week.

Tips for Using Apple Cider Vinegar for Dandruff

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your apple cider vinegar treatment:

  • Use raw, unfiltered apple cider vinegar as it contains the "mother" which has beneficial enzymes and bacteria.
  • Do a patch test before using apple cider vinegar on your scalp to check for any allergic reactions.
  • Don't use apple cider vinegar on broken or irritated skin.
  • Don't use apple cider vinegar on colored or chemically treated hair as it can strip the color.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp and hair healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is apple cider vinegar safe to use on the scalp?

Yes, apple cider vinegar is safe to use on the scalp. However, you should dilute it with water before using it and avoid using it on broken or irritated skin.

How often should I use apple cider vinegar for dandruff?

You can use apple cider vinegar for dandruff once or twice a week.

How long does it take to see results?

You may see results after the first use, but it may take a few weeks to see significant improvement.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Dandruff

Here are some hair care tips that can help to prevent dandruff:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the scalp of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair styling products that contain alcohol as it can dry out the scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

Now that you know how to get rid of dandruff fast with apple cider vinegar, you can try this natural remedy and enjoy a healthy, flake-free scalp.
