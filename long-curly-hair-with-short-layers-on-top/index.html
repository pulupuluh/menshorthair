Long Curly Hair With Short Layers On Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Feb 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain, you might want to consider long curly hair with short layers on top. This look is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their curls without sacrificing length. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips on how to take care of your curly locks.

Description

The long curly hair with short layers on top is a popular hairstyle among women with naturally curly hair. This look involves cutting short layers on top of the head while leaving the rest of the hair long and curly. The layers help to add volume and texture to the hair, making it look more dynamic and playful. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their curls long but still want to have a little bit of fun with their hair.

How to Achieve the Look

So, how do you achieve the long curly hair with short layers on top? Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step to achieving this look is to decide how long you want your hair to be. Ideally, your hair should be long enough to form curls but not too long that it becomes unmanageable. You can consult with your hairstylist to help you decide on the right length for your hair.

Step 2: Cut the Short Layers on Top

The next step is to cut the short layers on top of your head. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair. The length of the layers will depend on your personal preference and the length of your hair. Your hairstylist will be able to guide you on the right length for your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Curls

Once you've cut the short layers, it's time to style your curls. You can use a curling iron or a diffuser to enhance your natural curls. You can also use a hair serum to add shine and definition to your curls. Remember to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Hair with Short Layers on Top

Now that you've achieved the perfect long curly hair with short layers on top, it's important to take care of your locks to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing, such as leave-in conditioners and hair oils. Avoid products that contain sulfates and alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important not to overwash your hair. Wash your hair only when it's necessary, and use a gentle shampoo that's designed for curly hair. You can also use a co-wash, which is a conditioner that cleanses your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Protect your hair while you sleep by using a silk or satin pillowcase. These materials are gentle on your hair and won't cause friction, which can lead to breakage and frizz. You can also tie your hair up in a loose bun or braid to prevent it from tangling while you sleep.

FAQ

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length and style of the layers might need to be adjusted to suit your face shape.

Can I achieve this look on my own?

We recommend going to a professional hairstylist to achieve this look. They will be able to cut the layers in the right places and give you tips on how to style and maintain your hair.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the layers and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Long curly hair with short layers on top is a fun and playful hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their curls. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect look and maintain your curly locks. Remember to use the right products, avoid overwashing your hair, and protect your hair while sleeping. With the right care, you can keep your hair looking healthy, shiny, and beautiful.
