How To Haircut Baby Boy Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Babies grow up so fast, and before you know it, they already need a haircut. It can be daunting to cut your baby boy's hair, but with a step-by-step guide, it can be a breeze. In this article, we will show you how to haircut baby boy in relaxed English language.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to Haircut Baby Boy
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we start, let us first discuss some things you need to know before you haircut baby boy. Babies can be fidgety during haircuts, so it's best to have a helper to hold them still. You also need to choose the right time to do it. Pick a time when your baby is full, rested, and in a good mood.

How to Haircut Baby Boy

When it comes to haircutting baby boy, there are different styles to choose from. You can go for a buzz cut, a crew cut, or even a Mohawk. But before you decide on a style, you first need to gather the necessary tools:

  • Scissors or clippers
  • Comb
  • Hairbrush
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Bib or towel

Once you have all the tools, you can now start your baby boy's haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to haircut baby boy:

  1. Wet your baby's hair by spraying it with water.
  2. Comb the hair, and decide on the length you want to cut.
  3. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it with scissors or clippers.
  4. Move around your baby's head, cutting hair in sections.
  5. Trim around the ears and the neck carefully.
  6. Brush the hair and inspect if there are uneven parts or missed spots.
  7. Make final touches and clean up any hair on your baby's clothes.

Remember to take your time and be patient. You can also reward your baby with a small toy or a treat after the haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips you can follow when you haircut baby boy:

  • Use a high-quality scissor or clipper to avoid accidents.
  • Start with a longer length, and gradually cut it shorter.
  • Use distractions like toys or videos to keep your baby calm.
  • Keep a wet towel or bib on your baby's neck to catch falling hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircutting baby boy:

  • At what age can I start haircutting my baby boy? You can start as early as three months, but it's best to wait until six months when their hair is thicker.
  • How often should I haircut my baby boy? It depends on the hair growth, but it's recommended to do it every six to eight weeks.
  • What if my baby moves a lot during the haircut? You can ask someone to hold them still or use a special hair cutting cape to restrain their movements.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's essential to take care of your baby boy's hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hair styling products. You can also use a soft-bristled brush to detangle their hair gently.

That's it! Now you know how to haircut baby boy in relaxed English language. Remember to enjoy the process and make it a positive experience for your baby.
