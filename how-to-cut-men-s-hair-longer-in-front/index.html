How To Cut Men s Hair Longer In Front A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to try a new hairstyle or give yourself a fresh new look? One of the trendiest styles for men is longer hair in the front. This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, from a messy tousled look to a sleek and polished style. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's hair longer in front, step by step.

Description

Longer hair in the front is a popular hairstyle for men that can be achieved by cutting the hair shorter in the back and longer in the front. This style can be worn with long or short hair and can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you're going for a casual, messy look or a sleek, polished style, cutting your hair longer in the front can give you the trendy look you desire.

How to Cut Men's Hair Longer in Front

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider visiting a professional stylist. Follow these steps to cut your hair longer in the front:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to comb and cut.
  2. Comb your hair straight back and clip the hair at the crown of your head to keep it out of the way.
  3. Using your scissors, cut the hair in the back of your head to the desired length. This will create a baseline for your haircut.
  4. Unclip the hair at the crown of your head and comb it forward.
  5. Using your scissors, cut the hair in the front to the desired length. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create a gradual lengthening effect.
  6. Blend the hair in the front with the hair in the back by cutting at an angle.
  7. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair straight and even.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional stylist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair:

  • How often should I cut my hair?

    • You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

  • Should I use scissors or clippers?

    • For longer hair, scissors are the best tool to use. Clippers are better for shorter haircuts.

  • Can I cut my own hair?

    • Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's important to be patient and take your time. Consider getting professional help if you're not confident in your skills.

Hair Care for Men

After cutting your hair longer in the front, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Cutting men's hair longer in the front is a trendy and versatile style that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the look you desire, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
