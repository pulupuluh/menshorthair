Hairstyles For Wide Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your wide face? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll be sharing some of the best hairstyles for wide faces, as well as tips on how to choose the perfect one for you.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Wide Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for Wide Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle for Wide Faces
  • FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles for Wide Faces
  • Hair Care for Wide Faces: Dos and Don’ts

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Wide Faces

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for wide faces, the key is to create balance. You want to minimize the width of your face and draw attention to your best features. Here are some tips:

  • Choose hairstyles with layers and volume on top to add height and length to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or straight bobs.
  • Consider a side part or asymmetrical cut to create the illusion of a narrower face.
  • Opt for hairstyles that frame your face, such as soft waves or curls.

Best Hairstyles for Wide Faces:

Now that you know what to look for, here are some of the best hairstyles for wide faces:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Shoulder-length waves or curls
  • Pixie cut with longer layers on top
  • Side-parted lob
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Half-up half-down hairstyle with soft waves

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for Wide Faces

Ready to try out one of these hairstyles? Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for wide faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  3. If you have long hair, create loose waves with a curling iron or wand.
  4. If you have short hair, use a texturizing product to add volume and dimension.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle for Wide Faces

Now that you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent drying it out.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles for Wide Faces

Q: Can I still wear a blunt bob if I have a wide face?

A: While blunt bobs can be tricky for those with wide faces, it’s not impossible to pull off. To make it work, try adding layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a narrower face.

Q: Should I avoid center parts if I have a wide face?

A: Not necessarily. While a center part can accentuate the width of your face, it can also create a symmetrical look that can be flattering. If you want to try a center part, opt for a longer hairstyle with layers.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a wide face?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to balance out the width of your face. You can also try a side-swept bang to create angles and draw attention away from the width of your face.

Hair Care for Wide Faces: Dos and Don’ts

When it comes to hair care for wide faces, here are some dos and don’ts to keep in mind:

Dos:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Don’ts:

  • Don’t over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Don’t use hot tools on high heat settings, as this can damage your hair.
  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Don’t forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can flatter your wide face and feel confident and beautiful every day. Happy styling!
