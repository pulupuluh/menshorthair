Hair Cut Short In Back Long In Front The Trendy Hairstyle Of 2023

Written by Robby Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new and trendy hairstyle, the "hair cut short in back long in front" might just be the perfect option for you. This chic and edgy haircut has been making waves in the fashion industry and is loved by many celebrities and fashion icons. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

The hair cut short in back long in front, also known as the "mullet," is a hairstyle that features short hair on the back and sides of the head, while the top and front are left long. The length of the front hair can vary, from just above the ears to shoulder-length. This hairstyle is characterized by its sharp contrast between the short and long hair, creating an edgy and modern look.

How to Get It

The "hair cut short in back long in front" is a versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair textures. To get this haircut, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Consultation

During your consultation, your hairstylist will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best version of the "mullet" for you.

Step 2: Cutting

After the consultation, your hairstylist will start cutting your hair. They will first trim the back and sides short, usually using clippers. Then, they'll move on to the top and front, leaving them longer. The length of the front hair will depend on your preference and face shape.

Step 3: Styling

To style your new haircut, you can experiment with different techniques and products. You can blow-dry the front hair for volume, use a flat iron for a sleek look, or tousle it for a messy and edgy vibe. The possibilities are endless!

Tips

If you're considering getting the "hair cut short in back long in front," here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstylist who specializes in this type of haircut to ensure the best results.
  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist, as they know best what will suit you.
  • Invest in good hair care products to maintain your new haircut.

FAQ

Is the "hair cut short in back long in front" suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit different hair types, from straight to curly.

Will the "mullet" make my face look longer?

The "mullet" can elongate the face, but it can also create the illusion of a more balanced face shape, depending on how it's styled.

Can I wear the "mullet" to formal events?

Yes, the "mullet" can be styled in a sleek and sophisticated way for formal events, or in a messy and edgy way for casual occasions.

Hair Care

To maintain your "hair cut short in back long in front," you'll need to invest in good hair care products. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and neat.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock the "hair cut short in back long in front" and embrace this trendy hairstyle. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or a subtle update, this haircut is a great option to consider.
