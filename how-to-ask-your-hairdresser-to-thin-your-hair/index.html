How To Ask Your Hairdresser To Thin Your Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thick, voluminous hair, you may find it difficult to style and manage on a daily basis. Thinning your hair can help to reduce the amount of hair you have, making it easier to style and maintain. However, asking your hairdresser to thin your hair can be daunting if you don't know the right terminology to use. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to ask your hairdresser to thin your hair.

How to

Step 1: Research

Before you visit your hairdresser, it's important to do some research on the different techniques used to thin hair. This will help you to communicate with your hairdresser and understand the process. Some common techniques include razor cutting, point cutting, and texturizing.

Step 2: Speak to Your Hairdresser

When you arrive at your hair appointment, speak to your hairdresser about your concerns regarding your hair's thickness. Explain that you would like your hair thinned out and ask for their professional opinion on which technique would work best for your hair type.

Step 3: Use the Right Terminology

When asking your hairdresser to thin your hair, it's important to use the right terminology. Some phrases you can use include:

  • "Can you thin out my hair?"
  • "I'd like my hair to be texturized."
  • "Can you use a razor to thin my hair?"

Using these phrases will help your hairdresser to understand exactly what you want.

Step 4: Trust Your Hairdresser

Your hairdresser is a trained professional and knows how to thin hair properly. Trust their judgement and let them do their job. Don't be afraid to ask questions if you're unsure about anything.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when asking your hairdresser to thin your hair:

  • Bring in a picture of the style you're looking for to give your hairdresser a better idea of what you want.
  • Be honest about your hair's history, such as whether you've recently had it colored or chemically treated.
  • Don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the results.

FAQ

How much should I expect to pay for a hair thinning service?

Prices for hair thinning services can vary depending on the salon and location. Generally, you can expect to pay between $30 and $70 for a hair thinning service.

Will thinning my hair make it look thinner overall?

No, thinning your hair will not make it look thinner overall. It will simply remove some of the bulk and make it easier to style.

Will thinning my hair damage it?

No, when done properly, thinning your hair should not cause any damage. However, it's important to make sure you're going to a reputable salon and that your hairdresser is experienced in thinning hair.

Hair Care

After getting your hair thinned, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great after getting it thinned.
