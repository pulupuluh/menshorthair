How To Slick Your Hair Back Woman Bun

Written by Petter Aug 23, 2023 · 7 min read

The sleek, slicked back bun is a timeless hairstyle that is both elegant and sophisticated. It's perfect for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or just a day at the office. In this article, we will show you how to slick your hair back into a woman bun step by step. We will also provide you with tips and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back Woman Bun
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to slick your hair back into a woman bun:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly with a towel. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. This will help to keep your hair in place and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Using a brush, start brushing your hair back towards the crown of your head. Make sure to brush your hair evenly so that there are no bumps or tangles. If you have curly hair, use a comb instead of a brush to smooth out your hair.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Once you have brushed your hair back, tie it into a tight ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid any visible distractions. Make sure the ponytail is secure and tight so that it doesn't come loose throughout the day.

Step 4: Twist and Wrap

Take the ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun. Keep twisting until the bun is tight and secure. Then, wrap the hair tie around the bun to hold it in place.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Apply a small amount of hairspray to the bun to keep it in place throughout the day. Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair around the bun. Voila! You now have a sleek and sophisticated slicked-back bun.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid any visible distractions.
  • Apply a small amount of hairspray to the bun to keep it in place throughout the day.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair around the bun.
  • If you have curly hair, use a comb instead of a brush to smooth out your hair.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off a slicked-back bun?

Yes, anyone can pull off a slicked-back bun! It's a versatile hairstyle that works for any hair length, texture, or face shape.

How do I keep my bun from coming loose throughout the day?

Make sure your ponytail is tight and secure before twisting it into a bun. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid any visible distractions. Apply a small amount of hairspray to the bun to keep it in place throughout the day.

Can I slick my hair back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still slick your hair back if you have short hair! Instead of tying your hair into a ponytail, use a comb to slick your hair back and apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to keep it in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-washing.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy growth.

Now that you know how to slick your hair back into a woman bun, you can rock this sleek and sophisticated hairstyle anytime, anywhere. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Happy styling!
