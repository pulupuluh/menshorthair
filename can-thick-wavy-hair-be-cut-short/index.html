Can Thick Wavy Hair Be Cut Short

Written by Luffy Mar 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look beautiful when styled correctly, it can also be difficult to manage and maintain. One question that many people with thick wavy hair ask is whether or not it can be cut short. The answer is yes, but there are some things to consider before making the chop.

Description

Thick wavy hair is characterized by its volume and texture. It can be difficult to manage and style, but it can also look stunning when it is well taken care of. When considering cutting your hair short, it is important to think about how your hair will look and feel with less length.

How to Cut Thick Wavy Hair Short

If you decide that you want to cut your thick wavy hair short, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it is important to find a hairstylist who has experience working with this hair type. They will be able to help you find a cut that will work well with your hair's natural texture and volume.

When cutting thick wavy hair short, it is also important to consider the shape of your face. A shorter cut may not be flattering on everyone, so it is important to find a style that will complement your facial features.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when cutting thick wavy hair short:

  1. Find a hairstylist who has experience working with thick wavy hair.
  2. Discuss your options with your stylist and find a cut that will work well with your hair's texture and volume.
  3. Consider the shape of your face when choosing a shorter cut.
  4. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it.
  5. Dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to enhance your hair's natural waves and texture.
  6. Section your hair using hair clips.
  7. Cut your hair in small sections, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  8. Blend the layers together using scissors or a razor.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting thick wavy hair short:

  • Use a hairstyling product, such as mousse or gel, to help enhance your hair's natural texture.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush when styling your hair. Instead, use your fingers to shape your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your hair's shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick wavy hair short:

Can I cut my thick wavy hair short if I have a round face?

Yes, but it is important to find a style that will complement your facial features. A shorter cut with layers can help to elongate your face.

Will cutting my thick wavy hair short make it easier to manage?

Yes, cutting your hair short can make it easier to manage, but it is important to find a style that will work well with your hair's natural texture and volume.

Hair Care

Whether you decide to cut your thick wavy hair short or keep it long, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often. These can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.

In conclusion, yes, thick wavy hair can be cut short. However, it is important to find a style that will work well with your hair's natural texture and volume, and to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and manageable.
