How To Cut A Man s Hair Short On Sides Longer On Top

Written by Aprilia Apr 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

A short on the sides, longer on top haircut has been a popular style for men for years. It's a versatile look that can be adapted to suit a range of face shapes and hair types. In this blog post, we'll be showing you how to cut a man's hair short on the sides and longer on top, including tips and tricks to help you achieve a professional-looking cut.

Description

A short on the sides, longer on top cut involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head to a shorter length, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a contrast in length that is both stylish and easy to maintain. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance cut that still looks great.

How to Cut a Man's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather some tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Haircutting scissors
  • Water spray bottle

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Start by wetting the hair with the water spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and will help you achieve a more even cut. Use the comb to comb the hair straight back.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using the clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length. Use the comb to keep the hair straight and even as you cut.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Using scissors, blend the hair on the sides with the longer hair on top. Use the comb to hold the hair straight up and cut across to blend the two lengths together.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on top to the desired length. Use the comb to hold the hair straight up and cut across to achieve an even cut.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting, use the clippers to trim around the edges and the back of the neck. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take your time and work in small sections.
  • Make sure you're cutting the hair evenly on both sides.
  • Blend the hair on the sides with the longer hair on top for a seamless look.
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs for a neat finish.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's best to leave it to a professional if you're not confident in your abilities.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, a short on the sides, longer on top cut will need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your short on the sides, longer on top cut looking its best, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh the hair down and make it look greasy. Regular trims will help keep the style looking neat and tidy.
