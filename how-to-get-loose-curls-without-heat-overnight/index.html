Here are some tips that can help you get the perfect loose curls:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and breakage. It will also help your hair retain its moisture, which is essential for achieving healthy-looking curls.

2. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate the curls.

3. Apply Hair Oil
