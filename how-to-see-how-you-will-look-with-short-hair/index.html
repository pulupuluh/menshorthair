How To See How You Will Look With Short Hair

Written by William Sep 04, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short but unsure if it will suit you? With the help of technology, you can now see how you will look with short hair before making the big chop. In this article, we will guide you on how to see how you will look with short hair and provide tips on hair care for short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to See How You Will Look with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Short Hair Care
  • FAQs

How to See How You Will Look with Short Hair

There are several ways to see how you will look with short hair.

1. Virtual Makeover Apps

Virtual makeover apps such as YouCam Makeup, Perfect365, and Taaz allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including short hair. These apps use augmented reality technology to superimpose the hairstyle onto your photo, giving you a realistic preview of how you will look with short hair.

When using these apps, make sure to choose a photo where your face is visible and your hair is pulled back or tied up. This will make it easier for the app to detect your facial features and apply the hairstyle accurately.

2. Hair Styling Websites

Some hair styling websites like TheHairStyler.com and VirtualHairstyler.com offer virtual hair try-on services. These websites have a database of different hairstyles, including short hair, that you can try on by uploading your photo or using their virtual model.

These websites also provide information on the best short hairstyles for different face shapes, hair types, and ages, helping you choose a hairstyle that suits you best.

3. Photoshop or Photo Editing Software

If you have basic Photoshop or photo editing skills, you can try cutting your hair short in a photo editing software. This method requires more effort than using virtual makeover apps or hair styling websites, but it gives you more control over the final result.

Make sure to use a high-resolution photo, and practice cutting your hair short by using the crop tool, eraser tool, and clone stamp tool. You can also use the liquify tool to adjust the shape and volume of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to see how you will look with short hair using virtual makeover apps:

Step 1: Download a Virtual Makeover App

Download a virtual makeover app such as YouCam Makeup, Perfect365, or Taaz from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Choose a Photo

Choose a photo of yourself where your face is visible and your hair is pulled back or tied up. You can also take a new photo using the app's camera feature.

Step 3: Try on Different Short Hairstyles

Try on different short hairstyles by selecting the "Hairstyles" or "Haircuts" feature. Browse through the different hairstyles and select the ones you want to try on.

Step 4: Adjust the Hairstyle

Adjust the hairstyle by using the app's editing features. You can adjust the length, volume, and color of the hair to make it look more realistic.

Step 5: Save and Share

Save the photo and share it with your friends and family to get their feedback.

Tips for Short Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some tips for short hair care:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

2. Use a hair serum

Use a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount of serum to your hair after washing and leave it in.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. This will also help your hair grow faster and healthier.

4. Style your hair with care

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners. Avoid pulling or tugging your hair when styling, as it can cause breakage and damage.

FAQs

1. Will short hair suit me?

Short hair suits most face shapes and hair types. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist before cutting your hair short to get a professional opinion.

2. How often should I wash my short hair?

You should wash your short hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

3. Can I style my short hair in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short hair in different ways, such as by adding waves or curls, using hair accessories, or creating a pompadour or quiff.

Now that you know how to see how you will look with short hair, you can confidently make the decision to cut your hair short. Remember to take care of your short hair by washing it regularly, using a hair serum, trimming it regularly, and styling it with care.
