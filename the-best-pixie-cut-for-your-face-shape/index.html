The Best Pixie Cut For Your Face Shape A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Apr 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're considering a pixie cut, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape. A good cut can enhance your features and highlight your best qualities, while a bad cut can leave you feeling self-conscious and unflattered. In this guide, we'll help you find the best pixie cut for your face shape, with tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Face Shapes

Before we dive into the best pixie cuts for each face shape, let's quickly review the five main types:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and proportions, and the goal of a good pixie cut is to balance and flatter those features. Keep this in mind as we move on to the best cuts for each face shape.

Best Pixie Cuts

Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to add some height and volume to elongate your features. A layered pixie cut with volume on top and shorter sides can work well. Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the roundness of your face.

Oval Face

Lucky you! Oval faces can wear almost any pixie cut with ease. Consider a classic pixie with side-swept bangs, or a longer layered cut with face-framing layers.

Square Face

A square face usually has a strong jawline, so you'll want to soften that with a pixie cut that has volume on top and softer, longer layers on the sides. A textured, choppy cut with side-swept bangs can also work well.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is wider at the forehead and tapers down to a pointy chin. To balance out this shape, consider a layered pixie cut with volume at the crown and side-swept bangs to soften the forehead. Avoid cuts that are too short on the sides, as they can make the chin appear even more pointy.

Diamond Face

A diamond face has wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. A pixie cut with volume on top and shorter sides can work well, as can a layered cut with side-swept bangs to balance out the wide cheekbones. Avoid cuts that are too short on top, as they can make your forehead appear even narrower.

How to Style

Once you've chosen the best pixie cut for your face shape, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a dime-sized amount of styling cream or mousse
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots for volume
  • Use a flat iron to smooth any flyaways or frizz
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Apply styling cream or mousse
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots for volume
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth any flyaways or frizz
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock your new pixie cut:

  • Invest in a good quality flat iron and round brush
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find what works best for you
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, and statement earrings
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for maintenance

FAQ

Can I wear a pixie cut with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, a curly pixie cut can be very flattering. Just make sure to choose a cut that works with your natural curl pattern, and use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.

Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

It depends on the cut. A blunt, straight-across cut can emphasize the roundness of your face, while a layered cut with volume on top can elongate your features.

Do I need to use special styling products for my pixie cut?

Not necessarily. A good quality styling cream or mousse can help add volume and texture to your hair, but you can use whatever products work best for your hair type.

Hair Care

Maintaining a pixie cut is relatively low-maintenance, but there are still some things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind with a hat or scarf

With these tips and tricks, you're now ready to find the best pixie cut for your face shape and rock your new look with confidence!
