Best Men s Hair Styling Product For Slicked Back Look

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the best men's hair styling product for slicked back look? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the top products that can help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle. We will also provide tips on how to style your hair and take care of it.

Description

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for men for many years. It is a classic look that can be worn for any occasion. The style involves combing the hair back and keeping it in place with a styling product. The key to achieving this look is finding the right product that will hold your hair in place without making it look greasy or crunchy.

How to Achieve the Slicked Back Look

Before we dive into the best products for the slicked back look, let's go over how to achieve the style. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and rub them together.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair, distributing the product evenly.
  4. Using a comb, comb your hair back, starting from the front and working your way to the back.
  5. Style your hair to your desired look.

Top Products for Slicked Back Hair

1. Pomade

Pomade is a popular hair styling product for men. It provides a strong hold and a shiny finish. Pomade can be used on all hair types and is great for achieving the slicked back look. It is also easy to apply and can be re-styled throughout the day.

2. Gel

Gel is another popular hair styling product that can be used to achieve the slicked back look. It provides a strong hold and a wet, shiny finish. However, it can make your hair look crunchy and stiff if you use too much. Gel is best for those with thick, coarse hair.

3. Wax

Hair wax is a versatile product that can be used to achieve a variety of hairstyles, including the slicked back look. It provides a strong hold and a natural-looking finish. Wax is also great for adding texture and definition to your hair.

Tips for Slicked Back Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb to evenly distribute the product through your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy or crunchy.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

What hair type is best for the slicked back look?

The slicked back look can be achieved on all hair types, but it works best on medium to long hair that is straight or wavy.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know the best men's hair styling product for slicked back look, you can achieve this classic hairstyle with ease. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to style your hair like a pro!
