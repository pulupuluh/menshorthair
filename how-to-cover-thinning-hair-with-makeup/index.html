How To Cover Thinning Hair With Makeup A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem that many men and women face as they age. While there are several options available to treat hair loss, makeup is an effective way to cover up thinning hair and create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair. In this post, we will guide you through the process of covering thinning hair with makeup, step-by-step. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Thinning Hair with Makeup
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Thinning Hair with Makeup
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

How to Cover Thinning Hair with Makeup

The first step in covering thinning hair with makeup is to choose the right products. You will need a root touch-up spray or powder, a hair fiber product, and a hairspray. These products are specifically designed to cover up thinning hair and create the illusion of fuller hair.

Next, you will need to choose the right shade. Look for a shade that matches your natural hair color or is a shade lighter. Avoid using a shade darker than your natural hair color as it will look unnatural.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cover thinning hair with makeup:

  1. Begin by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before applying any makeup.
  2. Apply the root touch-up spray or powder to your roots. This will cover up any visible scalp and create the illusion of thicker hair.
  3. Apply the hair fiber product to your hair. These products are made from tiny fibers that attach to your hair and create the illusion of fuller hair.
  4. Use a brush or your fingers to distribute the hair fibers evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Apply a hairspray to your hair to set the hair fibers in place.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Covering Thinning Hair with Makeup

Here are some tips to help you cover up thinning hair with makeup:

  • Use a light hand when applying the root touch-up spray or powder. Build up the coverage slowly to avoid a heavy or unnatural look.
  • Use a brush or your fingers to distribute the hair fibers evenly throughout your hair. This will create a more natural-looking result.
  • Choose a hairspray that is specifically designed for use with hair fiber products. This will help to set the fibers in place and prevent them from falling out.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy or oily.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering thinning hair with makeup:

Can I use regular makeup to cover up thinning hair?

While regular makeup can be used to cover up thinning hair, it is not designed for this purpose and may not work as well as products specifically designed for this purpose.

How long does the makeup last?

The makeup can last for several hours, depending on the product and how it is applied. However, it may need to be reapplied throughout the day to maintain the desired coverage.

Can I use hair fiber products on wet hair?

No, hair fiber products should only be applied to dry hair. Wet hair will not hold the fibers in place and may cause them to clump together.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for thinning hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Take care of your scalp by keeping it clean and moisturized.
  • Consider using a hair growth serum or supplement to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can help to prevent further hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cover thinning hair with makeup, you can feel confident and beautiful every day. Remember to choose the right products and follow the steps carefully for the best results. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at covering up thinning hair in no time!
