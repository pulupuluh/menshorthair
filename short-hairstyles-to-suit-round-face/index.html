Short Hairstyles To Suit Round Face

Written by Bardi Sep 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles to suit a round face shape. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there is a short hairstyle that will suit your round face perfectly. Here are our top picks:

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, wide cheeks, and a round chin. The goal of any hairstyle is to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces because they help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the widest part of the face, which is the cheeks.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle to suit a round face is to determine your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or pixie cut will add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a short shag will help to reduce bulk and create a sleeker look.

Next, consider the shape of your face. A round face looks best with a hairstyle that has a lot of volume on top and some length on the sides. This will help to elongate the face and create a more oval shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your hair texture.

Step 2: Consider the shape of your face.

Step 3: Choose a short hairstyle that has volume on top and length on the sides.

Tips

When styling your short hair, consider adding waves or curls to create more volume and texture. You can also use hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

Another tip is to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a straight bob. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a layered bob that will add dimension to your hair without adding width to your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. Just make sure that the cut has a lot of volume on top and some length on the sides to create a more elongated look.

Q: What is the best hair color for a round face?

A: The best hair color for a round face is one that creates depth and dimension. Highlights and lowlights can help to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it is important to keep your hair healthy and well-nourished. Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Regular trims are also important to keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, there are many short hairstyles that will suit a round face shape. By considering your hair texture and face shape, you can find a hairstyle that will flatter your features and create a more elongated look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep your short hairstyle looking its best!
