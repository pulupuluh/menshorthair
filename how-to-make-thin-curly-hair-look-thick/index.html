How To Make Thin Curly Hair Look Thick

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thin curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to style and make it look voluminous. However, with a few simple tricks and hair care tips, you can achieve a fuller and thicker look. In this article, we will be discussing how to make thin curly hair look thick and provide you with some helpful tips and advice.

How to Make Thin Curly Hair Look Thick

There are several ways to make thin curly hair look thick. The key is to add volume and texture to your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

The right products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are specifically designed for thin hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners. Use a lightweight mousse or styling cream to add volume and texture to your curls.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a great way to add volume and lift to your roots. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and scrunch your hair as you dry it. This will help to create more defined curls and add volume.

3. Use Hot Rollers

Hot rollers are a great tool for adding volume and curl to your hair. Use them to create loose waves or tight curls, depending on your preference. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an easy way to add volume and texture. Use a rat-tail comb to backcomb your roots, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased area. This will create a fuller, thicker look.

5. Get a Haircut

A layered haircut can add volume and movement to your hair. Ask your stylist for layers that frame your face and add texture to your curls.

Step by Step: How to Make Thin Curly Hair Look Thick

Here is a step-by-step guide to making thin curly hair look thick:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Lightweight Mousse or Styling Cream

Apply a small amount of mousse or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and scrunch your hair as you dry it to add volume and lift to your roots.

Step 4: Use Hot Rollers

Use hot rollers to create loose waves or tight curls, depending on your preference.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Backcomb your roots with a rat-tail comb, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased area to create a fuller, thicker look.

Tips for Making Thin Curly Hair Look Thick

Here are some additional tips for making thin curly hair look thick:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and product residue.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I make thin curly hair look thicker without using hot tools?

A: Yes, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and/or use hot rollers to achieve a thicker look without using hot tools.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to your hair. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for thin curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than three times a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thin curly hair look thicker and fuller. Remember to use the right products, blow dry your hair upside down, use hot rollers, tease your hair, and get a layered haircut for added volume and movement. With a little bit of effort and care, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle.
