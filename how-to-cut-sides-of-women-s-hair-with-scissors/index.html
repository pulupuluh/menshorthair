How To Cut Sides Of Women s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to haircuts, the sides of women's hair can be tricky to cut. However, with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and efficiently. In this article, we will show you how to cut sides of women's hair with scissors, from start to finish.

How to Cut Sides of Women's Hair with Scissors

Before we begin, let's go over the tools you will need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Comb
  • Spray bottle with water

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started!

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair. Take a section of hair from the crown of the head and clip or tie it out of the way. This will make it easier to work on the sides of the head.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Use the spray bottle to wet the hair on the sides. This will make it easier to see the natural fall of the hair and ensure a more even cut.

Step 3: Comb the Hair

Use a comb to comb the hair on the sides downwards. This will help you see the length of the hair and the areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Using your sharp hairdressing scissors, start cutting the hair on the sides. Work in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb as a guide to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting, use a mirror to check for evenness. Make sure both sides are the same length and adjust any areas that need trimming.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

To ensure a seamless blend between the hair on the sides and the hair on top, use the scissors to blend the hair. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Once you are happy with the length and blend of the hair on the sides, you can style it as desired. Use a hair dryer, straightener or curling iron to create your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Sides of Women's Hair with Scissors

  • Always start with a small section of hair and work your way up. This will prevent you from cutting off too much hair.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Use the comb as a guide to ensure an even cut.
  • Take breaks if necessary. Cutting hair can be tiring on the arms and hands.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair?

It is possible to cut your own hair, but it can be difficult to achieve an even cut. We recommend going to a professional hairdresser for the best results.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. Take a step back and assess the situation. You can always adjust the hair and try again. If it's a major mistake, we recommend going to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

How often should I cut the sides of my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your hairstyle. We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy head of hair.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut sides of women's hair with scissors, you can achieve the perfect haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow our tips for the best results. Happy cutting!
