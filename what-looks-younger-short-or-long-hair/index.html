What Looks Younger Short Or Long Hair

Written by Christine Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to freshen up your appearance and look younger? One easy and effective way to do this is by changing your hairstyle. It's a common question that people ask themselves: what looks younger short or long hair? In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide some tips on how to achieve a youthful look with your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Short Hair vs. Long Hair
  • How to Achieve a Youthful Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide to a Youthful Hairstyle

Short Hair vs. Long Hair

The answer to this question actually depends on a few different factors, such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Generally speaking, short hair tends to make people look younger because it creates an illusion of fullness and volume. Long hair, on the other hand, can sometimes weigh you down and make you look older.

However, this isn't always the case. If you have a round face shape, for example, long hair can actually help to elongate your features and make you appear slimmer. And if you have naturally curly or wavy hair, long hair can be a great way to show off your texture and add some youthful bounce to your locks.

How to Achieve a Youthful Look

No matter what length of hair you choose, there are a few key things you can do to ensure that your hairstyle looks youthful and fresh:

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and features.
  • Add layers to create volume and movement.
  • Opt for soft, face-framing layers instead of harsh choppy ones.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add fullness to your roots.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide to a Youthful Hairstyle

If you're looking for a specific hairstyle that will make you look younger, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a youthful bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair to one side.
  3. Section off the front of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  4. Take a section of hair from the back of your head and cut it to chin length.
  5. Continue cutting the back of your hair in this way, working from the bottom up.
  6. Unclip the front section of your hair and cut it to chin length as well.
  7. Add layers throughout your hair to create volume and movement.
  8. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add even more volume.
  9. Use a texturizing spray to finish off the look.

Tips for Maintaining a Youthful Appearance

Once you've achieved your youthful hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to ensure that it stays looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Wash your hair every other day instead of every day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make me look too young?

A: Not necessarily. It all depends on the cut and styling. If you want to avoid looking too young, opt for a more mature style with soft layers and minimal volume.

Q: Can long hair make me look older?

A: In some cases, yes. Long, heavy hair can sometimes weigh you down and make you look older. To avoid this, consider adding layers and volume to your locks.

Hair Care for a Youthful Look

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure that it stays looking youthful and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining a youthful appearance:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your hair and scalp moisturized.

So, what looks younger short or long hair? The answer isn't so simple, as it depends on individual factors like face shape and hair texture. However, by following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can achieve a youthful and fresh hairstyle no matter what length you choose.
