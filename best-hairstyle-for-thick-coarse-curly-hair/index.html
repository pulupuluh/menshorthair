Best Hairstyle For Thick Coarse Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Thick coarse curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s voluminous and can create stunning looks. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. But don’t worry! In this article, we’ll guide you through the best hairstyles for thick coarse curly hair, and how to care for it.

Description

Thick coarse curly hair is a hair type that is characterized by its voluminous, thick, and curly texture. It can be challenging to manage and style due to its thickness and coarseness. But with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create stunning looks that enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Choosing the right hairstyle for thick coarse curly hair can be challenging. Here are some tips to help you choose the best one:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will suit you the best. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a layered haircut that frames your face. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different hairstyles.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be considered when choosing a hairstyle. If you’re always on-the-go, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn’t require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, you can opt for a more intricate hairstyle.

Consider the Thickness and Coarseness of Your Hair

Thick coarse curly hair needs a hairstyle that can accommodate its volume and texture. You should opt for a haircut that complements your hair type, such as layers or a bob. These haircuts can help to reduce the weight of your hair and make it more manageable.

Best Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Now that you know how to choose the best hairstyle for thick coarse curly hair, let’s take a look at some of the best hairstyles for this hair type:

Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is an excellent choice for thick coarse curly hair. It can help to reduce the weight of your hair and create a more manageable look.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for thick coarse curly hair. It can help to frame your face and create a chic and stylish look.

Braids

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. They’re an excellent choice for thick coarse curly hair because they can help to keep your hair in place and reduce frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Styling thick coarse curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques, you can create stunning looks. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to styling your hair is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair to help reduce frizz and enhance your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around in circular motions.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create the look you want.

Tips for Caring for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your thick coarse curly hair:

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will help to reduce breakage and damage to your hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it more susceptible to breakage and frizz.

Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair and reduce frizz. Look for a hair mask that is designed for curly or coarse hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my thick coarse curly hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your thick coarse curly hair. However, it’s important to choose a hair dye that is designed for curly hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

Q: How often should I wash my thick coarse curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to wash your thick coarse curly hair once or twice a week. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and make it more susceptible to frizz.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick coarse curly hair?

A: To reduce frizz in your thick coarse curly hair, use a leave-in conditioner, avoid heat styling, and use a hair mask once a week.

Conclusion

Thick coarse curly hair can be challenging to manage and style, but with the right techniques and hairstyles, you can create stunning looks that enhance your natural beauty. Follow our guide to choose the best hairstyle for your hair type and care for it properly to achieve healthy and beautiful hair.
