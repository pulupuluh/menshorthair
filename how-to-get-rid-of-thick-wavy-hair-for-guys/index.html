How To Get Rid Of Thick Wavy Hair For Guys

Written by Robby May 28, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thick, wavy hair that just won't cooperate? You're not alone. Many men struggle with this hair type, but there are ways to manage it. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get rid of thick wavy hair for guys. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Rid of Thick Wavy Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Process
  • Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Description

Thick, wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can look great when styled properly. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking to get rid of your thick, wavy hair, there are a few options available to you. You can choose to cut your hair short, use hair products to tame your waves, or get a chemical treatment to straighten your hair.

How to Get Rid of Thick Wavy Hair for Guys

If you're looking to get rid of your thick wavy hair, there are a few options available to you:

Cut Your Hair Short

If you're tired of dealing with your thick, wavy hair, one option is to cut it short. Short hair is easier to manage and requires less styling. You can choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair's natural texture.

Use Hair Products

If you don't want to cut your hair short, you can use hair products to tame your waves. Look for products that are designed for thick, wavy hair, such as curl creams and gels. These products can help to define your waves and keep them under control.

Straighten Your Hair

If you want to get rid of your waves altogether, you can get a chemical treatment to straighten your hair. This process involves applying a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its natural texture. The process can take several hours, but the results can last for several months.

Step-by-Step Process

If you're planning to cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step process to follow:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair where you want it.
  3. Use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Blend the sides and back of your hair with a shorter clipper guard.
  5. Use scissors to texture the top of your hair.
  6. Style your hair using a product that works with your hair type.

If you're planning to use hair products to tame your waves, here's a step-by-step process to follow:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a curl cream or gel to your hair.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to work the product through your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  5. Style your hair using a product that works with your hair type.

If you're planning to get a chemical treatment to straighten your hair, you'll need to visit a salon. The process can take several hours, and you'll need to follow the stylist's instructions carefully.

Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair

Here are a few tips to help you manage your thick wavy hair:

  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.

FAQs

Is it possible to permanently straighten thick wavy hair?

Yes, it's possible to permanently straighten thick wavy hair using a chemical treatment. This process involves breaking down the bonds in your hair that give it its natural texture.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my thick wavy hair?

It's best to use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Look for products that are labeled as "thickening" or "curl enhancing."

How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

It's best to wash your thick wavy hair every other day or every third day. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

If you have thick wavy hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can get rid of your thick wavy hair and enjoy a hairstyle that looks great and is easy to manage.
