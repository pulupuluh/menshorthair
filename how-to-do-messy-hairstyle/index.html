How To Do Messy Hairstyle A Fun And Easy Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's effortlessly chic and low-maintenance, then a messy hairstyle is your answer. This look is perfect for those who want to add some texture and dimension to their hair without spending too much time styling it. In this guide, we'll show you how to do messy hairstyle in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • What is a messy hairstyle?
  • How to do messy hairstyle
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for achieving the perfect messy hairstyle
  • FAQs about messy hairstyles
  • Hair care tips for maintaining your messy hairstyle

What is a messy hairstyle?

A messy hairstyle is a look that's tousled, textured, and slightly undone. It's a popular style that can be achieved by using various techniques, including teasing, backcombing, and scrunching. The aim is to create a natural-looking, effortless hairstyle that appears as though you haven't spent hours styling it.

How to do messy hairstyle

Messy hairstyles are perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair. Here's a list of the things you'll need to achieve this look:

  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Bobby pins
  • Teasing comb
  • Elastic hair ties

Step-by-step guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can enhance the texture by using a texturizing spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and tease the crown of your head using a teasing comb. This will add volume and texture to your hair.
  3. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail, leaving a few strands out around your face.
  4. Using your fingers, gently pull at the ponytail to create a messy, undone look. You can also wrap a few strands of hair around the elastic hair tie to hide it.
  5. Take a few bobby pins and secure any loose strands of hair around your face.
  6. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for achieving the perfect messy hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating your messy hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to create texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to use bobby pins to secure loose strands of hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to enhance the natural texture of your hair.
  • Tease the crown of your head to add volume to your hairstyle.
  • Use your fingers to create a messy, undone look.

FAQs about messy hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy hairstyles:

1. Can I achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture, and use your fingers to create an undone look.

2. How do I prevent my messy hairstyle from looking too messy?

The key to achieving the perfect messy hairstyle is to strike a balance between messy and polished. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair, and finish off with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. How often should I wash my hair when I have a messy hairstyle?

It's recommended to wash your hair every two to three days when you have a messy hairstyle. This will help maintain the natural texture of your hair and prevent it from becoming too oily.

Hair care tips for maintaining your messy hairstyle

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining your messy hairstyle:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the natural texture.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.

With these simple tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle in no time. Remember, the key is to embrace the natural texture of your hair and have fun with it!
