Is It Haram To Put Black Henna On Your Hair

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Black henna is a popular hair dye that is commonly used by many people around the world. It is made from a plant called Lawsonia inermis, which is also known as henna. However, there have been concerns about the use of black henna, especially when it comes to religious beliefs. In this article, we will explore whether it is haram to put black henna on your hair and answer some of the most frequently asked questions related to this topic.

What does Haram mean?

Haram is an Arabic word that means prohibited or forbidden. In Islam, there are certain things that are considered haram, and Muslims are not allowed to engage in those activities. These prohibitions are based on religious beliefs, and they are meant to protect the individual and the community from harm.

Is it Haram to Put Black Henna on Your Hair?

The use of black henna on hair is a controversial topic, and there is no clear consensus among scholars regarding its permissibility. Some believe that it is haram because it contains a substance called paraphenylenediamine (PPD), which is a synthetic dye that can cause skin allergies and other health problems. Others argue that it is permissible as long as it does not harm the individual or the community.

However, it is worth noting that some Islamic scholars have issued fatwas (religious rulings) stating that the use of black henna is haram. They argue that it is a form of imitating non-Muslims and that it may lead to health problems, which can be seen as a violation of the Islamic principle of protecting one's health.

How to Use Black Henna on Your Hair

If you decide to use black henna on your hair, it is important to follow some basic guidelines to ensure that you do not experience any adverse effects. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose a High-Quality Product

Make sure that you buy a high-quality black henna product that is free from harmful chemicals and additives. Look for products that are made from natural ingredients and have been tested for safety.

Step 2: Test for Allergies

Before using black henna on your hair, it is important to test for allergies. Apply a small amount of the product on your skin and wait for 24 hours to see if there is any reaction. If you experience any itching, redness, or swelling, do not use the product.

Step 3: Prepare the Henna Mixture

Follow the instructions on the product package to prepare the henna mixture. You can also add some essential oils or other natural ingredients to enhance the color and fragrance of the henna.

Step 4: Apply the Henna on Your Hair

Apply the henna to your hair, making sure to cover all the strands. Leave it on for the recommended time, usually 1-2 hours, and then rinse it off with water. Do not use shampoo or conditioner for at least 24 hours after applying the henna.

Tips for Using Black Henna on Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using black henna on your hair:

  • Do not use black henna on damaged or sensitive skin.
  • Do not apply black henna near your eyes or mouth.
  • Do not use black henna if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • Do not leave black henna on your hair for too long as it can cause damage.
  • Do not use black henna if you have a history of skin allergies or reactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is black henna safe for hair?

Black henna is generally safe for hair as long as it is used properly and in moderation. However, it is important to choose a high-quality product that does not contain harmful chemicals or additives and to test for allergies before use.

How long does black henna last on hair?

Black henna can last on hair for several weeks to a few months, depending on the frequency of washing and exposure to sunlight and other environmental factors.

Can black henna cause hair loss?

Black henna is not known to cause hair loss. However, if it is used improperly or too frequently, it can cause damage to the hair and scalp, which can lead to hair loss.

How to take care of hair after using black henna?

After using black henna on your hair, it is important to wait for at least 24 hours before washing it with shampoo or conditioner. You can use natural oils or hair masks to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Conclusion

So, is it haram to put black henna on your hair? The answer is not clear-cut, and it depends on your personal beliefs and the opinions of your religious scholars. However, if you decide to use black henna on your hair, make sure to follow the guidelines and tips mentioned in this article to ensure that you do not experience any adverse effects.
