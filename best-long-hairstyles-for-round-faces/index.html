Best Long Hairstyles For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Feb 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right long hairstyle, you can enhance your features and create a flattering look. In this guide, we'll explore the best long hairstyles for round faces, offering tips, ideas, and step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to long hairstyles for round faces, it's all about creating length and angles. Long, layered cuts can help elongate the face, while side-swept bangs can create a slimming effect. Additionally, styling techniques like curls and waves can add volume and texture, creating a more flattering look.

How to

To create the perfect long hairstyle for your round face, start by selecting a cut that flatters your face shape. Look for styles that add length and angles, such as long layers or a graduated cut. Additionally, consider adding side-swept bangs to create a slimming effect.

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces, as they help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin and blend into the ends of your hair. To create volume and texture, consider adding loose waves or curls using a curling iron or wand.

Graduated Cut

A graduated cut, also known as an A-line cut, is another great option for round faces. This style features shorter layers at the back of the head that gradually get longer towards the front. This creates a slimming effect and helps to elongate the face. To style, use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, then add loose waves using a curling iron.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any long hairstyle for round faces. These bangs create a diagonal line across the face, creating a slimming effect. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the temples and blend into the rest of your hair. To style, use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, then add loose waves or curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a long hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect long hairstyle for your round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can create a rounder look.
  • When adding curls or waves, focus on the ends of your hair to create length.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  • For a sleek, straight look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a slimming effect.

FAQ

What are some other hairstyles for round faces?

Other hairstyles that flatter round faces include bob cuts, pixie cuts, and long hairstyles with layers and bangs.

What should I avoid when choosing a long hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear rounder. Additionally, avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can create a rounder look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a long hairstyle for a round face. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use products that are designed for your hair type. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a long hairstyle for a round face that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
