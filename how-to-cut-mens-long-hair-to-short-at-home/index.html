How To Cut Men s Long Hair To Short At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and ready for a shorter, fresher look? Going to a salon can be expensive and time-consuming, but cutting your hair at home doesn't have to be scary. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish short haircut in the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut men's long hair to short at home.

Table of Contents

  • Tools you'll need
  • Prepping your hair
  • Cutting your hair
  • Fading your hair
  • Tips for a successful haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair care after your haircut

Tools you'll need

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Cape or towel to protect your clothing
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Prepping your hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and part it the way you normally wear it. Use hair clips to separate the top and bottom sections of your hair.

Cutting your hair

Begin by using the clippers to trim the back and sides of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach your desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it.

Next, move on to the top section of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Start by cutting small sections at a time and don't cut too much off at once. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to trim it. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Fading your hair

If you want a fade, use the clippers to fade the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. Blend the hair where the longer guard meets the shorter guard to create a smooth transition. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to fade it.

Tips for a successful haircut

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Cut hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors or clippers to cut it.
  • Don't rush and take your time to ensure a good cut.

FAQs

How do I know what length guard to use?

Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you reach your desired length. It's better to start longer and work your way down than to cut too much off at once.

How do I fade my hair?

Use the clippers to fade the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. Blend the hair where the longer guard meets the shorter guard to create a smooth transition.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a salon to have it fixed or try cutting it again at home.

Hair care after your haircut

After your haircut, make sure to wash your hair and style it as desired. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short haircut.

Congratulations, you've successfully learned how to cut men's long hair to short at home. With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience.
