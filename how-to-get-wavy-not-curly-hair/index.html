How To Get Wavy Not Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having curly hair and wanting to switch it up to wavy hair? Achieving wavy hair can be a challenge, especially if you have naturally curly hair. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect wavy hair that you have always wanted. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get wavy not curly hair.

How to

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in achieving wavy hair is to wash your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. After shampooing, use a conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it moisturized. Rinse your hair thoroughly to make sure there is no product left in your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

To achieve wavy hair, you need to keep your hair moisturized. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Apply a Wave-Enhancing Product

Apply a wave-enhancing product, such as a mousse, to your hair. This will help to define your waves and hold them in place. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to the ends.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair to help define your waves. Use your hands to scrunch your hair from the bottom up. Do not use a towel or anything else that could cause frizz.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry. Do not use a hairdryer, as this can cause frizz and damage your hair. If you need to leave the house before your hair is dry, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair on low heat.

Step 6: Touch Up Your Waves

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair again to enhance your waves. You can also use a curling iron or a wand to touch up your waves if needed.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve wavy not curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • When scrunching your hair, use a light touch to avoid causing frizz.

FAQ

Can I achieve wavy hair if I have naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve wavy hair even if you have naturally curly hair. Follow the steps outlined in this article to achieve the perfect wavy hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every other day.

What should I do if my hair becomes frizzy?

If your hair becomes frizzy, try using a smoothing serum or a hair oil to help tame the frizz. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and maintain your wavy hair, it is essential to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect wavy hair that you have always wanted. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
