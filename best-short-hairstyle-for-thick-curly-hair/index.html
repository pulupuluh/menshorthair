Best Short Hairstyle For Thick Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a new short hairstyle for your thick curly hair? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing the best short hairstyles for those with thick curly hair. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips. So, let's get started!

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to style, especially when it comes to short hairstyles. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a stunning look that complements your natural curls. In this guide, we will be sharing the best short hairstyles for those with thick curly hair, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

How To

Before we dive into the different short hairstyles, let's discuss some general tips for styling your thick curly hair. First, make sure to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and minimize frizz. Second, avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can rough up the cuticle and cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

Now, let's move on to the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on those with thick curly hair. This cut features short layers all around the head, creating a voluminous look that works well with curls. To style, apply a curl-defining product to damp hair and scrunch your curls with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural curls.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great short hairstyle for those with thick curly hair. This cut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, opt for a longer bob that falls just below your chin. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls. You can also use a curling iron to define any loose curls.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered hairstyle that works well with thick curly hair. This cut features choppy layers all around the head, creating a textured look that adds volume to your curls. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and scrunch your curls with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls.

Step By Step

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your curls hydrated and minimize frizz.

Step 2: Apply product

Apply a curl-defining or curl-enhancing product to damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them looking bouncy and voluminous.

Step 3: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural curls and minimize frizz.

Step 4: Define your curls

Use a curling iron to define any loose curls or add extra volume to your hair. You can also use a hairspray or hair oil to set your style and keep your curls looking great all day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling and caring for your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and hair breakage while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and nourished.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thick curly hair:

Q: Will a short haircut make my hair look thinner?

A: No, a short haircut can actually make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. This is because the weight of your hair is distributed evenly across your head, creating a fuller look.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair with a short haircut. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Are there any short haircuts that are not recommended for thick curly hair?

A: Yes, avoid getting a too-short haircut that doesn't leave enough length to showcase your curls. Also, be cautious of getting a haircut that is too layered, as this can make your hair look frizzy and unmanageable.

Hair Care

To keep your thick curly hair looking its best, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat tools too often. It's also important to get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.

Overall, there are many great short hairstyles for those with thick curly hair. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob cut, or shag cut, make sure to follow the tips and techniques we discussed in this guide to ensure your hair looks its best. Happy styling!
