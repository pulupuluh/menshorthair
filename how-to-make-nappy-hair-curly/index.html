How To Make Nappy Hair Curly

Written by Luffy Apr 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some bounce and texture to your nappy hair? Well, you’re in the right place! In this article, we’ll show you how to make nappy hair curly using some simple techniques and products. So let’s get started!

Table of Content

  • How to Make Nappy Hair Curly
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Best Results
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Before we get started, it’s important to understand that making nappy hair curly is a process. You can’t expect to see results overnight. With that said, let’s dive into the steps:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

The first step is to cleanse your hair using a shampoo specifically designed for nappy hair. You want to make sure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After you’ve towel-dried your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and provide moisture. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product such as a curl defining cream or gel. This will help define your curls and provide hold. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Now it’s time to dry your hair. Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Make sure to scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it to help define your curls.

Step 5: Finish with an Oil

Finally, finish off with a lightweight oil to add shine and moisture to your hair. Apply a small amount to your palms and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Best Results

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Don't overuse products, as this can weigh your hair down
  • Try using a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair

FAQs

Can you make nappy hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can make nappy hair curly without heat by using styling products and techniques such as twist outs, braid outs, or flexi rods.

How often should I wash my nappy hair?

You should wash your nappy hair once a week or every two weeks, depending on your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help keep your nappy hair healthy:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as relaxers or dyes
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage

Now you know how to make nappy hair curly! With these tips and techniques, you can achieve bouncy and defined curls that will make heads turn. Remember to be patient and consistent, and you’ll see results in no time.
