How To Do Messy Bun With Short Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short and thin hair, you might think that doing a messy bun is impossible. But the truth is, you can still achieve that effortless and chic look with just a few simple steps. In this blog post, we will teach you how to do a messy bun with short thin hair. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Messy Bun with Short Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Messy Bun Look Great
  • FAQs About Messy Buns with Short Thin Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin and Short Hair

How to Do Messy Bun with Short Thin Hair

The first step in creating a messy bun with short thin hair is to make sure that your hair is dry and free from tangles. You can use a comb to detangle your hair and a dry shampoo to add texture to it. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 2: Twist and Pin

Take the ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun. Pin it in place with bobby pins. You can leave some pieces of hair out to create a messy look.

Step 3: Pull Out Strands

Use your fingers to gently pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create a messier look. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to do a messy bun with short thin hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Apply a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 2: Create a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Loosen the Ponytail

Use your fingers to loosen the ponytail to create a more relaxed look.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 5: Pull Out Some Strands

Use your fingers to gently pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create a more messy look. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Making Your Messy Bun Look Great

Here are some tips to help you make your messy bun look great:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.
  • Leave some pieces of hair out to create a more relaxed and casual look.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • Don't worry about making it perfect. Messy buns are meant to look effortless.

FAQs About Messy Buns with Short Thin Hair

Can I still do a messy bun with short thin hair?

Yes, you can still do a messy bun with short thin hair. Just follow the steps we outlined in this blog post.

Do I need to use any products to make my messy bun look great?

You can use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair. You can also use bobby pins to secure your bun in place.

Hair Care Tips for Thin and Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your thin and short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and flat irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Now that you know how to do a messy bun with short thin hair, you can easily achieve that effortless and chic look. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips to make your messy bun look great. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using the right hair care products and following our hair care tips.
