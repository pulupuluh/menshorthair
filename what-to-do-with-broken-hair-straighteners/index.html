What To Do With Broken Hair Straighteners Tips And Faqs

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re reading this, chances are you’ve encountered a broken hair straightener. It can be frustrating to invest in a tool that suddenly stops working, but don’t worry, there are things you can do to salvage your hair straightener. Here are some tips and FAQs to help you deal with a broken hair straightener.

How to Determine if Your Hair Straightener is Broken

The first thing you need to do is determine if your hair straightener is really broken. Here are some signs to look out for:

  • The straightener won’t turn on
  • The plates don’t heat up
  • The plates don’t close properly
  • The cord is frayed or damaged

If you’ve noticed any of these signs, chances are your hair straightener is indeed broken.

What to Do with Broken Hair Straighteners

Once you’ve confirmed that your hair straightener is broken, the next thing to do is decide what to do with it. Here are some options:

Get it Repaired

If your hair straightener is still under warranty, you may be able to get it repaired or replaced for free. Check the manufacturer’s website for more information on warranty coverage. If your hair straightener is not under warranty, you can still take it to a repair shop to get it fixed. Just make sure to get a quote before agreeing to any repairs.

Recycle it

If your hair straightener is beyond repair, don’t throw it in the trash. Hair straighteners contain materials that can be recycled, such as metal and plastic. Check with your local recycling center to see if they accept hair straighteners.

Donate it

If your hair straightener is still in good condition but you no longer use it, consider donating it to a local charity or shelter. Many organizations accept donations of gently used beauty tools.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Repair a Broken Hair Straightener

If you’ve decided to repair your broken hair straightener, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Identify the Problem

Before you start repairing your hair straightener, you need to identify the problem. Is it the cord that’s frayed? Are the plates not heating up? Once you’ve identified the problem, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Purchase the Necessary Parts

Once you know what’s wrong with your hair straightener, you need to purchase the necessary parts. This may include a new cord, heating element, or plate. Make sure to purchase parts that are compatible with your hair straightener model.

Step 3: Disassemble the Hair Straightener

Before you can replace any parts, you need to disassemble your hair straightener. This may involve removing screws or prying apart plastic pieces. Make sure to keep track of any screws or small parts so you can put them back in the right place later.

Step 4: Replace the Broken Part

Once you’ve disassembled your hair straightener, you can replace the broken part. Follow the instructions that came with your replacement part, or consult a repair guide online. Make sure to reassemble your hair straightener in the reverse order that you took it apart.

Step 5: Test Your Hair Straightener

After you’ve reassembled your hair straightener, test it out to make sure it’s working properly. If everything is in order, you can start using your hair straightener again!

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Straightener

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair straightener:

  • Clean the plates regularly with a damp cloth
  • Store your hair straightener in a cool, dry place
  • Don’t wrap the cord tightly around the hair straightener
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using your hair straightener

FAQs

Can I fix a hair straightener with a frayed cord?

Yes, you can replace the cord on your hair straightener. Just make sure to purchase a cord that is compatible with your hair straightener model.

Can I use a broken hair straightener?

No, it’s not safe to use a broken hair straightener. A broken hair straightener can cause damage to your hair or even start a fire.

How long do hair straighteners last?

The lifespan of a hair straightener depends on how often it’s used and how well it’s maintained. On average, a hair straightener can last anywhere from 2-5 years.

Dealing with a broken hair straightener can be frustrating, but with these tips and FAQs, you can find a solution that works for you. Whether you choose to repair, recycle, or donate your hair straightener, make sure to take care of it properly so it lasts as long as possible.
