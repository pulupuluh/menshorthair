How To Style Very Fine Long Hair

Written by Frank Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have very fine long hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. It can be difficult to add volume and texture without causing damage or weighing your hair down. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks for styling very fine long hair, including how to add volume, create beachy waves, and more.

Description

Very fine long hair is delicate and prone to breakage, which means you need to be gentle when styling it. You also need to be careful not to weigh your hair down with heavy products or too much heat styling. With the right techniques and products, however, you can create a variety of stylish looks that will make your fine hair look thicker and more voluminous.

How to Style Very Fine Long Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean, Conditioned Hair

The first step to styling very fine long hair is to start with clean, conditioned locks. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down or strip it of its natural oils. This will help make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Product

Next, apply a volumizing product to your hair. This could be a mousse, a root lifter, or a volumizing spray. Apply it to the roots of your hair, then work it through to the ends. This will help add lift and volume to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair in sections, lifting it at the roots as you go. This will help add even more volume and lift to your hair. Use a round brush to create smooth, bouncy curls or waves, or simply use your fingers to tousle your hair for a more relaxed look.

Step 4: Create Beachy Waves

To create beachy waves, start by applying a heat protectant to your hair. Then, using a curling wand or a flat iron, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds. Release the curl, then repeat on the rest of your hair. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to tousle it and create loose, beachy waves.

Tips for Styling Very Fine Long Hair

Here are a few additional tips for styling very fine long hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to avoid breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage when using hot tools.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer that won't overheat or damage your hair.
  • Try using dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair between washes.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for very fine long hair?

Some of the best hairstyles for very fine long hair include layered cuts, long bobs, and tousled waves. These styles help add volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous.

How often should I wash my very fine long hair?

It's best to wash very fine long hair every other day or every two days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to breakage and damage.

How can I add volume to my very fine long hair?

You can add volume to your very fine long hair by using a volumizing product, blow drying your hair with a round brush, or creating beachy waves with a curling wand or flat iron. You can also try teasing your hair at the roots to add even more lift and volume.

Hair Care for Very Fine Long Hair

To keep your very fine long hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling whenever possible, and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. You can also try using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your very fine long hair in a variety of ways that will make it look thicker, more voluminous, and more stylish than ever before.
