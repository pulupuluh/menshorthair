How To Manage Thick Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick short hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage it. It can be tough to style, and it can become unruly and difficult to maintain. But fear not, with the right techniques and hair care routine, you can get your hair looking its best. In this article, we will be providing you with a complete guide on how to manage thick short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Thick Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips on Managing Thick Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Thick Short Hair

How to Manage Thick Short Hair

When it comes to managing thick short hair, the key is to find a style that works for your hair texture and face shape. Short haircuts like bobs, pixies, and layered cuts can be great for thick hair as they add volume and texture. If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to reduce the weight of your hair and make it more manageable.

It’s also important to invest in the right hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for thick hair to help control frizz and add moisture. Additionally, a good styling product like a hair gel or mousse can help tame your hair and keep it in place throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to manage thick short hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a styling product like a hair gel or mousse to your hair while it’s still damp.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips on Managing Thick Short Hair

Here are some tips on managing thick short hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to help detangle your hair and distribute natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about managing thick short hair:

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if it’s short and thick?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up if it’s short and thick. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it’s best to wash your hair every other day or every two days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Q: What should I do if my hair becomes frizzy?

A: Use a smoothing serum or oil to help control frizz and add shine to your hair.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Short Hair

Here’s a hair care routine for managing thick short hair:

  1. Wash your hair every other day or every two days with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
  3. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair and distribute natural oils.
  4. Apply a styling product like a hair gel or mousse to your hair while it’s still damp.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  6. Style your hair as desired using a flat iron or curling iron.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Managing thick short hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking its best. Follow the tips and steps outlined in this article to achieve healthy, manageable hair.
