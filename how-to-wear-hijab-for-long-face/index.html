How to Wear Hijab for Long Face - Tips, FAQs & Hair Care

Are you looking for tips on how to wear hijab for long face? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs and hair care tips to help you style your hijab perfectly for your long face shape. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Hijab for Long Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Hijab for Long Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to wear hijab for long face:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hijab

The first step to wearing hijab for long face is choosing the right hijab. Opt for a hijab that is long and wide enough to cover your face, neck and chest adequately. Choose a fabric that is comfortable and breathable, such as cotton, chiffon or silk.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before wearing hijab, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can tie your hair in a bun or a ponytail to keep it neat and tidy. Use a headband or a hair clip to keep any flyaways in place.

Step 3: Put on the Hijab

Place the hijab on your head with one side longer than the other. Take the longer side and wrap it around your head, bringing it to the other side of your neck. Pin it in place with a hijab pin.

Step 4: Adjust the Hijab

Adjust the hijab so that it covers your forehead, ears, neck and chest adequately. Make sure the hijab is not too tight or too loose. You can use hijab pins to adjust the hijab as needed.

Tips for Wearing Hijab for Long Face

Here are some tips to help you wear hijab for long face:

  • Choose a hijab style that suits your face shape. For a long face, opt for a hijab style that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  • Experiment with different hijab styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Choose hijabs in different colors and patterns to add variety to your wardrobe.
  • Wear a necklace or earrings to draw attention away from your long face.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a turban-style hijab if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear a turban-style hijab if you have a long face. However, make sure the hijab adds volume to the sides of your face to balance out your face shape.

Q: How do I keep my hijab from slipping?

A: You can use hijab pins to keep your hijab in place. You can also wear an underscarf or a hijab cap to keep your hijab from slipping.

Q: Can I wear a hijab with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear a hijab with glasses. Choose a hijab style that does not cover your glasses and adjust the hijab accordingly.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing hijab:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before wearing hijab to prevent scalp infections.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause hair breakage and hair loss.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf underneath your hijab to prevent hair damage.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear hijab for long face like a pro. Remember to choose a hijab style that suits your face shape, experiment with different styles and colors, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
