How To Tell My Face Shape Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man looking to determine your face shape, you've come to the right place. Knowing your face shape is crucial when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle or beard style. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to tell your face shape male. We'll also give you some tips on how to maintain your hair according to your face shape. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Measure Your Face
  • Step 2: Analyze Your Features
  • Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape
  • FAQs

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step in determining your face shape is to measure your face. You'll need a ruler or a measuring tape for this. Here's how to do it:

  1. Measure your forehead from the widest point.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from the widest point.
  3. Measure your jawline from the widest point.
  4. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Write down the measurements as you go along.

Step 2: Analyze Your Features

Now that you have your measurements, it's time to analyze your features. Look at the following features:

  • Forehead
  • Cheekbones
  • Jawline
  • Chin

Are any of these features more prominent than the others? Take note of which ones stand out the most.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

Based on your measurements and feature analysis, you can now determine your face shape. Here are the different face shapes:

  • Oval: The length of your face is greater than the width, and your forehead and jawline are slightly curved.
  • Round: The length and width of your face are about the same, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Square: The length and width of your face are about the same, and your jawline is the most prominent feature.
  • Heart: Your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrow.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is the widest part of your face, and your forehead is narrow.

Based on your measurements and feature analysis, which face shape do you think you have?

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle or beard style that complements it. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will suit an oval face shape.
  • Round: Go for hairstyles that add height to your face, such as a quiff or a pompadour.
  • Square: Choose hairstyles that soften your features, such as a side-swept fringe or a textured crop.
  • Heart: A side-parted hairstyle or a messy fringe will suit a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: A short back and sides with a textured top will complement a diamond-shaped face.
  • Triangle: Go for a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of your head, such as a messy fringe or a textured crop.

FAQs

1. What if my face shape doesn't match any of the above?

If your face shape doesn't match any of the above, it's possible that you have a combination of two face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that complements the dominant face shape.

2. Can I still choose a hairstyle that doesn't match my face shape?

Of course! These tips are just a guide. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that suits your personality.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

4. How can I prevent hair damage?

To prevent hair damage, avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoid using them too frequently. Also, make sure to use a conditioner regularly to keep your hair hydrated.

5. Can I grow a beard if I have a round face?

A beard can actually complement a round face, as it adds definition to the jawline. Just make sure to choose a beard style that suits your face shape.

That's it for our guide on how to tell your face shape male. We hope you found it helpful! Remember, choosing the right hairstyle or beard style can make all the difference in your appearance. Good luck!
