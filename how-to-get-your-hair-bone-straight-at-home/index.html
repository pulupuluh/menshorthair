How To Get Your Hair Bone Straight At Home

Written by Bardi Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a sleek, chic hairdo, then getting your hair bone straight is the way to go. However, going to the salon every time can be expensive and time-consuming. That's why we're going to show you how to get your hair bone straight at home. In this article, you'll learn how to do it, step by step, and also some tips and tricks to make it easier. So, let's get started!

How to Get Your Hair Bone Straight at Home

The first step is to wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or product residue. Then, condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner to keep it hydrated and healthy. After rinsing out the conditioner, towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. Then, section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work on each section and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Now it's time to blow-dry your hair using a round brush to straighten it out. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go. Use a nozzle attachment on your blow dryer to direct the air flow and help smooth out your hair.

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it further. Work on small sections at a time, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to help guide the hair through the flat iron and keep it straight.

Finally, apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and keep your hair looking sleek and straight all day long.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup.

2. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner.

3. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

4. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

5. Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.

6. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

7. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair further.

8. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine.

Tips

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates for the best results.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that you don't miss any spots.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to help straighten it out.
  • Finish with a hair serum or oil to add shine and keep your hair looking sleek and straight all day long.

FAQ

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to limit the amount of heat you apply to your hair. Try to straighten your hair no more than once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use a straightening brush instead of a flat iron?

A: Yes, a straightening brush can be a great alternative to a flat iron. However, it may not be as effective on very curly hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to get your hair bone straight at home, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Just remember to take care of your hair and use heat protectant spray to prevent damage. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a sleek, straight hairdo in no time!
