Mens Haircuts Longer On Top Shorter On Sides The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and low-maintenance, mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides could be the perfect option for you. This style is versatile enough to suit any face shape, hair type, and personal style, and it's been popular for years. In this post, we're going to break down everything you need to know about mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides, from how to get the look to how to take care of your hair.

Description

Mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides are a versatile and popular hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type, and it's perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. Mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides are often paired with fades, undercuts, or tapered cuts on the sides and back, which creates a seamless transition between the longer and shorter sections of hair.

How To Get Mens Haircuts Longer on Top Shorter on Sides

The first step in getting mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides is to find a good hairstylist who specializes in men's hair. You'll want to bring in a photo of the style you want to achieve and discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style with your stylist. From there, your stylist will determine the best length and shape for your hair and create a customized cut that suits your needs.

Step-by-Step Guide

To achieve mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it, leaving it slightly damp.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax, to help hold the style in place.
  3. Use a comb to create a part on the side of your head, if desired.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to the longer top section of hair.
  5. Use a clipper or trimmer to trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length, creating a fade or undercut if desired.
  6. Style the longer top section of hair using your fingers or a comb, shaping it into the desired style.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips For Styling Mens Haircuts Longer on Top Shorter on Sides

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that's appropriate for your hair type and desired style.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace the texture and use a curl-enhancing product to enhance your natural waves.
  • If you're not sure how to style your hair, ask your stylist for advice or watch tutorials online.

FAQs

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides can be customized to suit any hair type, from straight to curly.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Q: Can I style this haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides are versatile and can be styled in many different ways depending on your personal style and the occasion.

Hair Care Tips For Mens Haircuts Longer on Top Shorter on Sides

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or flat-ironing, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • If you have dandruff or a dry scalp, use a specialized shampoo or treatment to alleviate the symptoms.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain mens haircuts longer on top shorter on sides with ease. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance everyday style or a more polished look for a special occasion, this hairstyle is sure to impress. Remember to consult with your stylist and experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.
