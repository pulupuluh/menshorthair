Men s Wavy Hairstyle Medium How To Achieve The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with wavy hair, you're in luck. The medium length is perfect for showcasing your natural texture, and there are plenty of styling options to choose from. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about men's wavy hairstyle medium, including how to achieve the perfect look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A men's wavy hairstyle medium is a versatile and popular choice that can work for a variety of face shapes and hair types. This style typically features hair that is longer on top and shorter on the sides, with natural waves and texture throughout the hair.

There are many different ways to style a men's wavy hairstyle medium, from messy and textured to slick and polished. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look that still makes a statement.

How to Achieve the Look

The key to achieving a men's wavy hairstyle medium is to embrace your natural texture and work with your hair, rather than against it. Start by growing your hair out to medium length, keeping the sides shorter than the top.

Next, choose a styling product that will help enhance your waves and hold your style in place. A lightweight mousse or sea salt spray can work well for this style, or you can opt for a pomade or wax for a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's how to achieve a men's wavy hairstyle medium in just a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.
  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create natural waves and texture.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your waves.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair into your desired style.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray or more styling product, if needed.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your men's wavy hairstyle medium looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Visit your barber regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking neat and polished.

FAQs

Can I achieve a men's wavy hairstyle medium with straight hair?

Yes, it is possible to achieve a men's wavy hairstyle medium with straight hair. You may need to use a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves and texture in your hair, and you may need to use more styling product to hold your style in place.

What face shape does a men's wavy hairstyle medium work best for?

A men's wavy hairstyle medium can work for a variety of face shapes, but it tends to look best on those with oval or square faces. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a shorter or longer hairstyle instead.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers on your hair, as they can damage and weaken it.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect men's wavy hairstyle medium and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish for years to come.
