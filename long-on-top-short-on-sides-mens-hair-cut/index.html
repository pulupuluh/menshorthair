Long On Top Short On Sides Mens Hair Cut

Are you looking for a trendy and versatile haircut? Look no further than the long on top short on sides mens hair cut. This style has been popular for years and continues to be a go-to for men who want a stylish and low-maintenance cut. In this post, we'll go over everything you need to know about the long on top short on sides mens hair cut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

How to Get the Long on Top Short on Sides Mens Hair Cut

The long on top short on sides mens hair cut is easy to get at any barbershop or salon. Simply tell your stylist that you want a long top with shorter sides and back. You can bring in a picture for reference, but most stylists are familiar with this popular style.

One thing to keep in mind is that the length of "long" on the top can vary. Some men opt for a longer top that can be styled in a variety of ways, while others prefer a shorter top that is easier to manage. It's important to communicate with your stylist about your preferences so they can give you the best cut possible.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cut the Long on Top Short on Sides Mens Hair Cut

If you're feeling adventurous and want to try cutting your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  3. Section off the top of your hair with clips.
  4. Starting at the front, cut the top of your hair to your desired length. For a longer top, cut less off. For a shorter top, cut more off.
  5. Blend the top into the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Keep in mind that cutting your own hair can be tricky, especially if you're not experienced. It's always best to go to a professional if you're not confident in your skills.

Tips for Styling the Long on Top Short on Sides Mens Hair Cut

The long on top short on sides mens hair cut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips for styling:

  • For a sleek look, use a pomade or wax and comb your hair back.
  • For a messy, textured look, use a matte paste and tousle your hair with your fingers.
  • If you have a longer top, you can experiment with different styles like a slicked-back look, a messy quiff, or a side part.
  • For a more casual look, leave your hair natural and tousled.

FAQs About the Long on Top Short on Sides Mens Hair Cut

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your long on top short on sides mens hair cut.

Can I still wear a hat with this haircut?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this haircut. Just be mindful of how you style your hair underneath the hat.

Is this haircut good for all hair types?

The long on top short on sides mens hair cut can work for most hair types, but it's best for hair that is straight or has a slight wave. It may not work as well for very curly or thick hair.

Hair Care for the Long on Top Short on Sides Mens Hair Cut

To keep your long on top short on sides mens hair cut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

The long on top short on sides mens hair cut is a classic and versatile style that can work for any man. Whether you're looking for a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured style, this cut has got you covered. Just remember to communicate with your stylist about your preferences and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
