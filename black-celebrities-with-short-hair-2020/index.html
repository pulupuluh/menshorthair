Black Celebrities With Short Hair 2020

Written by Christine Sep 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for some inspiration for your next haircut? Look no further than the black celebrities who rocked short hair in 2020. From the red carpet to the streets, these stars prove that short hair can be just as stylish and versatile as long locks. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how to achieve and maintain the perfect short haircut.

Description

In 2020, we saw many black celebrities embracing short haircuts. From buzz cuts to pixie cuts, these stars showed us that short hair can be just as beautiful and versatile as long hair. Short haircuts can also be low maintenance and easy to style, making them perfect for those who are always on the go.

How to

If you're ready to take the plunge and try a short haircut, here's how to do it:

1. Find the Right Cut

Before you chop off your locks, it's important to find the right short haircut for your face shape and hair type. Consult with a stylist who specializes in short haircuts to find the perfect style for you.

2. Maintain Your Cut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. Visit your stylist every four to six weeks for a trim to keep your cut in shape.

3. Style Your Hair

Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. Experiment with different products and styling tools to find the perfect look for you.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a classic pixie cut:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before beginning. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.

3. Cut the Top

Starting with the top section, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut in small sections, and take your time to ensure that your hair is even.

4. Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. Comb your hair downward and cut it to match the length of the top section.

5. Cut the Back

Finally, move on to the back section. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head, and cut your hair to match the length of the top and side sections.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when rocking a short haircut:

1. Experiment with Products

Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, so don't be afraid to experiment with different products to achieve the perfect look.

2. Protect Your Hair

Short haircuts can be prone to breakage, so make sure to protect your hair with a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

3. Embrace Your Texture

If you have naturally curly or kinky hair, embrace your texture by using products that enhance your curls or coils.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut work for my face shape?

A: Short haircuts can work for any face shape, as long as you find the right cut for you. Consult with a stylist to find the perfect style for your face shape and hair type.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. Visit your stylist every four to six weeks for a trim.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is key to rocking a short haircut. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Deep Condition Weekly

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent breakage and frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short haircut like the black celebrities who inspired us in 2020. So go ahead, chop off those locks and embrace your new look!
