How To Get Wavy Hair For Thick Hair A Guide To Effortlessly Beautiful Waves

Written by Robby Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair always looking the same? Want to switch things up and achieve some beautiful waves? Look no further, as we've got the ultimate guide on how to get wavy hair for thick hair. From the right products to use to the techniques to try, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While you've got plenty of volume to work with, it can be difficult to achieve certain styles without spending hours on your hair. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful waves that will add some dimension and texture to your locks.

How to Get Wavy Hair for Thick Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving beautiful waves is to start with clean hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and make sure your hair is completely clean. This will help the waves hold better and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a Wave-Enhancing Product

Next, apply a wave-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a mousse, a sea salt spray, or a curl enhancer. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from the roots to the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help to enhance the natural wave pattern in your hair and create texture and volume.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add more definition to your waves, you can use a curling iron to touch up certain sections of your hair. Use a medium-sized barrel and wrap small sections of your hair around the iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 5: Finish with a Texturizing Spray

To set your waves and add some extra texture, finish with a texturizing spray. This will also help to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Getting Wavy Hair for Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect waves:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling
  • Avoid brushing your hair after you've styled it, as this can disrupt the wave pattern
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braids to help maintain the wave pattern overnight
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ About Wavy Hair for Thick Hair

Can I achieve wavy hair without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve waves without using heat. Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or letting your hair air dry while scrunching it with your hands.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain my waves?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain your waves. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to achieve and maintain your desired style.

What if my hair is too thick to hold waves?

If your hair is extremely thick, you may need to use a stronger hold product or try using smaller sections when curling with a curling iron. You can also talk to your stylist about thinning out your hair to make it more manageable.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you've achieved beautiful waves, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll have beautiful, wavy hair in no time. Whether you're going for a casual, beachy look or something more formal, waves are a versatile and stylish option for thick hair. So, have fun experimenting with different products and techniques, and rock those waves with confidence!
