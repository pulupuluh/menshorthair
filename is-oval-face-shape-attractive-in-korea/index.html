Is Oval Face Shape Attractive In Korea

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are interested in Korean beauty standards, you may be wondering if having an oval face shape is considered attractive in Korea. In this article, we will explore this topic and provide you with some tips on how to enhance your oval face shape.

Description

Korean beauty standards have been gaining popularity all over the world. Many people are fascinated by the Korean beauty industry and are curious about what makes Korean beauty standards unique. One aspect that stands out is the emphasis on having a small, oval-shaped face.

An oval face shape is defined as having a longer length than width, with a wider forehead and a narrower jawline. This face shape is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical, which is why it is highly sought after in Korea.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive deeper into the topic, it is essential to know your face shape. Here are some steps to help you determine your face shape:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down these measurements.

Step 2: Calculate Your Face Shape

Using the measurements, calculate your face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a pear-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have an oval face shape.

Tips for Enhancing Your Oval Face Shape

While having an oval face shape is considered attractive in Korea, there are still ways to enhance your features. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can enhance your facial features and make your face look more symmetrical. For oval face shapes, a side-swept bang or a layered haircut can help to add volume to your hair and balance out your features.

Tip 2: Use Contouring Techniques

Contouring can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Use a contour powder or cream to shade the areas underneath your cheekbones and jawline.

Tip 3: Wear the Right Accessories

Accessories can help to draw attention to your best features. For oval face shapes, earrings that are wider at the bottom can help to balance out your features. Statement necklaces can also draw attention to your collarbone and create the illusion of a longer neck.

FAQ

Q: Is having an oval face shape the only beauty standard in Korea?

A: No, there are many other beauty standards in Korea, such as having fair skin, double eyelids, and a V-shaped jawline. However, having an oval face shape is still highly valued.

Q: Can I still be considered attractive in Korea if I don't have an oval face shape?

A: Absolutely! Beauty standards are subjective, and everyone has their preferences. While having an oval face shape may be desirable in Korea, it does not mean that other face shapes are not considered attractive.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your face shape, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful locks. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing conditioner to add hydration.

Tip 2: Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools Too Often

Heat styling tools can damage your hair over time. Try to limit the use of blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy.

Conclusion

In conclusion, having an oval face shape is considered attractive in Korea. However, it is important to remember that beauty standards are subjective, and everyone has their preferences. Regardless of your face shape, taking care of your hair and enhancing your features can help you feel confident and beautiful.
