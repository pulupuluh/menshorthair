Cute Half Up Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have medium length hair, you’re in luck – there are so many cute half up hairstyles you can try! Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there’s a half up hairstyle that will work for you. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best cute half up hairstyles for medium length hair, as well as some tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Half Up Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Step by Step Guide to Creating Cute Half Up Hairstyles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Half Up Hairstyle
  • FAQ about Half Up Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Half Up Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Before we get into specific hairstyles, let’s go over some general tips for styling half up hairstyles for medium length hair. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling. You can add some texturizing spray or mousse to give your hair some grip and hold. You may also want to use a heat protectant if you’re going to be using hot tools.

When it comes to creating a half up hairstyle, there are a few different methods you can try. You can simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or hair tie. Or, you can create a more elaborate style by braiding, twisting, or knotting the top section of your hair. Don’t be afraid to experiment and try different techniques!

Step by Step Guide to Creating Cute Half Up Hairstyles

Now, let’s take a look at some specific cute half up hairstyles for medium length hair.

1. Half-Up Twisted Crown

Start by taking a small section of hair from one side of your head, near your temple. Twist the section of hair and bring it over to the other side of your head, securing it in place with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side of your head. Once both sections of hair are twisted and pinned, gather the remaining hair at the back of your head and tie it into a low ponytail.

2. Braided Half Up

Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three sections. Begin braiding the hair, incorporating small sections of hair as you go. Once you reach the back of your head, tie off the braid with a clear elastic. Gather the remaining hair into a low ponytail.

3. Half-Up Space Buns

Part your hair down the middle and create two high ponytails on either side of your head. Twist each ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins. Pull a small section of hair from the front of each side and wrap it around the base of the bun to hide the elastic.

Tips for Maintaining Your Half Up Hairstyle

Once you’ve created your cute half up hairstyle, you’ll want to make sure it stays in place all day. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Carry extra bobby pins and hair ties with you in case you need to make any adjustments throughout the day.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the style to come undone.

FAQ about Half Up Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray before styling your hair. You can also tease the top section of your hair to create more volume.

Can I create a half up hairstyle with bangs?

Yes! You can either leave your bangs down and style the rest of your hair in a half up style, or you can incorporate your bangs into the style by twisting or braiding them along with the rest of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

To keep your medium length hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these cute half up hairstyles for medium length hair, you’ll be able to switch up your look and keep your hair looking stylish and healthy!
