Can You Have A Fringe With Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you someone who loves experimenting with their hair? Do you have wavy hair and are wondering if you can pull off a fringe? You’re not alone. Many people with wavy hair are curious if they can sport a fringe without looking like a poodle. The good news is that you definitely can! In this article, we will discuss how to style a fringe with wavy hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A fringe is a great way to change up your look without going for a full haircut. It can frame your face, highlight your eyes, and add a touch of whimsy to your style. However, it can be a bit tricky to style a fringe when you have wavy hair. The key is to find the right length and shape that flatters your face and works with your natural waves.

How to

To style a fringe with wavy hair, you will need a few essential tools: a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a hair dryer. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by step

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the length of your fringe. It’s best to start with a longer length and gradually trim it shorter if needed.
  3. Section off the hair that you want to be your fringe.
  4. Using the comb, comb your hair forward and cut the fringe to the desired length.
  5. Once you’ve cut your fringe, use the hair dryer to style it. Use a round brush to shape the fringe and blow-dry it in the direction you want it to go.
  6. If you want a more tousled look, you can use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves in your fringe.
  7. Finish with a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling a fringe with wavy hair:

  • Choose a length that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider the thickness of your hair when deciding on the length of your fringe.
  • Use a hair dryer to blow-dry your fringe in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a round brush to shape your fringe while blow-drying it.
  • If you want a more natural look, try using a flat iron to create loose waves in your fringe.
  • Trim your fringe gradually to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for help if you’re unsure about how to style your fringe.

FAQ

1. Can I have a fringe with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it’s important to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair to avoid any mishaps.

2. How often should I trim my fringe?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how fast your fringe starts to look unruly. As a general rule, you should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

3. What kind of products should I use on my wavy hair?

It’s best to use products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as curl-enhancing creams, mousses, and gels. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

Hair Care

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so it’s important to keep it moisturized. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

In conclusion, you can definitely have a fringe with wavy hair. Just remember to find the right length and shape that flatters your face and works with your natural waves. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article, and you’ll have a stylish and manageable fringe in no time.
