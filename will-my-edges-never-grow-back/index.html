Will My Edges Never Grow Back Understanding Hair Loss And How To Regrow Your Edges

Written by Luffy Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss around your hairline or edges? If so, you're not alone. Many people, particularly women of color, struggle with hair loss due to a variety of reasons such as tight hairstyles, chemical treatments, genetics, and hormonal imbalances. However, the good news is that hair loss doesn't have to be permanent. In this article, we'll discuss how to regrow your edges and answer the question, "will my edges never grow back?"

Understanding Hair Loss

Hair loss can be caused by several factors, including genetics, stress, medication, and certain medical conditions. However, when it comes to hair loss around the edges, there are a few common culprits:

Tight Hairstyles

Wearing tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, and extensions can cause tension on your hairline, leading to hair loss. This is known as traction alopecia.

Chemical Treatments

Relaxers, perms, and other chemical treatments can weaken your hair and cause it to break off around the edges.

Genetics

If your parents or grandparents experienced hair loss, you may be more prone to it as well.

How to Regrow Your Edges

While hair loss can be frustrating, there are several things you can do to regrow your edges.

Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles and be gentle when styling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle, and avoid using heat tools such as flat irons and curling irons.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Use Hair Growth Products

There are several hair growth products on the market that can help stimulate hair growth, such as serums, oils, and shampoos. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, castor oil, and peppermint oil.

FAQ

Will my edges never grow back?

No, hair loss is not always permanent. With the right care and treatment, you can regrow your edges.

How long does it take for hair to grow back?

Everyone's hair grows at a different rate, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. It may take several months to a year to see significant growth around your edges.

What can I do to prevent hair loss?

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, be gentle when styling your hair, and avoid using harsh chemicals. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, and consider taking a hair growth supplement.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the tips mentioned above, there are a few things you can do to keep your hair healthy and promote growth:

Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair from breakage while you sleep.

Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it moisturized and healthy.

Trim Your Ends

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to hair loss.

Conclusion

If you're experiencing hair loss around your edges, don't panic. With the right care and treatment, you can regrow your hair and restore your confidence. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and consider using hair growth products to help stimulate growth.
