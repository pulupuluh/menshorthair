How To Tell What Hairstyle Will Suit You In 2023

Written by Oliver Apr 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up but don't know where to start? It can be overwhelming to choose a new hairstyle, especially with so many options available. In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks on how to tell what hairstyle will suit you in 2023.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining what hairstyle will suit you is to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles flatter different face shapes, so it's important to identify your face shape first. The main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you've identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. It's also important to consider your hair texture, thickness, and length when choosing a new hairstyle.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to determine what hairstyle will suit you:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture, thickness, and length
  3. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape and hair type
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for you

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to see how different hairstyles will look on you
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that suits you
  • Ask for recommendations from friends or family members with similar face shapes or hair types

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new hairstyle:

What hairstyles suit round faces?

Hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head, such as a high ponytail or short layered bob, can help elongate a round face.

What hairstyles suit heart-shaped faces?

Hairstyles that balance out a heart-shaped face, such as a chin-length bob or long layers, can help soften the forehead and jawline.

What hairstyles suit oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, from short pixie cuts to long beachy waves. Consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a new hairstyle.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine what hairstyle will suit you in 2023 and feel confident in your new look.
