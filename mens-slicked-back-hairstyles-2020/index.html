Slicked back hairstyles have been a classic look for men for decades. In 2020, this hairstyle became even more popular, with many variations and styles to choose from. Here, we will discuss everything there is to know about mens slicked back hairstyles 2020, including how to style it, tips, and hair care.

How to Style Mens Slicked Back Hairstyles 2020

To achieve a slicked back hairstyle, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or gel, to your hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a part in your hair where you want it to be slicked back.
  3. Comb your hair back and away from your face, using a gentle yet firm motion. Ensure that all of your hair is slicked back.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

There are also different variations of the slicked back hairstyle, such as the undercut slick back, the side-part slick back, and the messy slick back.

Tips for Mens Slicked Back Hairstyles 2020

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling mens slicked back hairstyles 2020:

  • Choose the right product for your hair type. If you have thin hair, use a lightweight product to avoid weighing down your hair. If you have thicker hair, use a stronger product to help hold your hair in place.
  • Don't overuse product. Too much product can create a greasy and unnatural look.
  • Experiment with different styles and variations to find the one that works best for you.
  • Maintain your slicked back hairstyle by getting regular haircuts and trims to keep your hair in shape.

FAQs about Mens Slicked Back Hairstyles 2020

Q: Can I achieve a slicked back hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back hairstyle with short hair. Use a lightweight product and comb your hair back firmly to create the desired look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair when styling a slicked back hairstyle?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to avoid drying out your scalp and hair.

Q: Is the slicked back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the slicked back hairstyle can work for all face shapes. However, it's essential to experiment with different variations to find the one that suits your face shape best.

Hair Care for Mens Slicked Back Hairstyles 2020

Maintaining a slicked back hairstyle requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as this can cause breakage and damage.

By following these hair care tips and following the steps for styling a slicked back hairstyle, you'll be able to maintain a stylish and healthy look.
