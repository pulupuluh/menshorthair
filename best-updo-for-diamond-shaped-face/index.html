Best Updo For Diamond Shaped Face A Complete Guide

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. The diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow hairline, a pointed chin, and wide cheekbones. The key to finding the best updo for diamond-shaped faces is to create balance by softening the angles and elongating the face. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the best updo for diamond-shaped faces, step-by-step guide to achieving the best updo, tips for maintaining the style, and hair care tips for diamond-shaped faces.

How to Choose the Best Updo for Diamond Shaped Face?

The best updo for a diamond-shaped face is one that elongates the face and softens the angles. Here are some tips to help you choose the best updo:

  • Opt for updos that create volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Avoid updos that are too tight as they can accentuate the angles of your face.
  • Choose updos that have soft, face-framing tendrils to soften the angles of your face.
  • Consider updos that have a side-swept fringe to create a more balanced look.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Updo for Diamond Shaped Face

Now that you know how to choose the best updo for your diamond-shaped face, let's take a look at the step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect updo:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose curls throughout your hair.
  5. Tease the crown of your hair to create volume.
  6. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  7. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  8. Use a comb to create soft, face-framing tendrils on either side of your face.
  9. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your updo in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Updo for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some tips to help you maintain the best updo for diamond-shaped faces:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz and disrupt the style.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear an updo if I have a diamond-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear an updo if you have a diamond-shaped face. The key is to choose an updo that elongates your face and softens the angles.

Q: What updos should I avoid if I have a diamond-shaped face?

A: You should avoid updos that are too tight or slicked back as they can accentuate the angles of your face.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips for diamond-shaped faces:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips, you can achieve the best updo for diamond-shaped faces and maintain a healthy, beautiful head of hair.
