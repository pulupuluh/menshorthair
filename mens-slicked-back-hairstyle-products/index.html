Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle Products The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia May 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to rock a slicked back hairstyle? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about mens slicked back hairstyle products, from how to achieve the look to hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and stylish. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that never goes out of style. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. To achieve the look, you’ll need the right mens slicked back hairstyle products.

How to Achieve the Look

The first step in achieving the slicked back hairstyle is to choose the right haircut. A shorter cut with tapered sides and a longer top works best for this look. Once you have the right haircut, you’ll need to invest in the right mens slicked back hairstyle products.

Products

There are a few key products you’ll need to achieve the slicked back look:

  • Pomade or wax: This will give your hair the hold it needs to stay in place.
  • Comb: You’ll need a comb to evenly distribute the product and style your hair.
  • Hairspray: This will help set your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Now that you have your products, let’s dive into the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips and rub it evenly into your hair.
  3. Comb your hair back, starting from the front of your head and working your way back.
  4. Use your comb to create a part in your hair, if desired.
  5. Smooth down any flyaways with hairspray.
  6. Finish with another light layer of pomade or wax to give your hair extra hold.

And voila! You now have a slicked back hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Now that you’ve achieved the slicked back look, you’ll want to maintain it throughout the day. Here are some tips:

  • Carry a small container of pomade or wax with you for touch-ups throughout the day.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt the style.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup.
  • Consider investing in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and hair breakage while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens slicked back hairstyle products:

What type of pomade or wax should I use?

It depends on your hair type and the level of hold you’re looking for. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to opt for a stronger hold pomade. If you have fine hair, a lighter hold wax may work better. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils. However, if you’re using a lot of product in your hair, you may need to wash it more frequently to prevent buildup.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips, you’ll be well on your way to achieving and maintaining a stylish slicked back hairstyle.

So, there you have it – everything you need to know about mens slicked back hairstyle products. With the right products and hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect look and keep your hair healthy and stylish.
