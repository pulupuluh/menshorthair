What Is The Best Bob For A Round Face

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. For those with a round face, a bob can be a flattering and stylish option. However, not all bobs are created equal. In this article, we'll explore the best bobs for a round face and provide tips on how to care for your new style.

What is a Round Face?

Before we dive into the best bobs for a round face, let's define what a round face is. A round face typically has a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The length and width of the face are usually similar, with no prominent angles or corners.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have a round face, there are a few ways to determine your face shape. One method is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Another method is to measure the width and length of your face and compare the ratios. A round face will have a similar width and length, while other face shapes will have different proportions.

The Best Bobs for a Round Face

The Classic Bob

The classic bob is a timeless and versatile choice for those with a round face. This style features a straight cut that falls just below the chin, with or without bangs. The length of the bob can be adjusted to flatter your face shape, with longer bobs elongating the face and shorter bobs emphasizing the roundness.

The Inverted Bob

The inverted bob, also known as a graduated bob, is a modern twist on the classic bob. This style features shorter layers in the back that gradually get longer towards the front. This creates a subtle angle that can help to slim and elongate the face.

The Textured Bob

The textured bob is a great option for those with finer hair or a round face. This style features choppy layers and tousled waves, which add volume and texture to the hair. The uneven layers also create the illusion of length and angles, which can balance out a round face.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get a Bob for a Round Face

Now that you know which bobs are best for a round face, it's time to get the cut. Follow these steps to achieve your new look:

  1. Choose a bob style that flatters your face shape.
  2. Find a reputable stylist who has experience cutting bobs.
  3. Bring pictures of the style you want to show your stylist.
  4. Discuss any concerns or modifications you want to make to the style.
  5. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  6. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic!

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob

Once you have your new bob, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your bob:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to create beachy waves.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Bobs for Round Faces

Can I get bangs with a bob for a round face?

Yes, you can definitely get bangs with a bob for a round face. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can help to elongate the face, while blunt bangs can emphasize the roundness.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can look great in a bob, but it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture. A layered bob can help to define your curls and create volume, while a blunt cut can weigh down the hair and make it look flat. Ask your stylist for recommendations based on your hair type and texture.

Can I still wear my hair up with a bob?

Yes, you can definitely still wear your hair up with a bob. A low ponytail, messy bun, or half-up style can all look great with a bob. Just be sure to use bobby pins or hair ties that match your hair color to avoid any unsightly bumps or lumps.

Conclusion

A bob can be a great choice for those with a round face, as long as you choose the right style and take care of your hair. Whether you opt for a classic bob, inverted bob, or textured bob, you're sure to look stylish and chic with your new 'do.
