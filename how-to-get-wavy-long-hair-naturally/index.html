How To Get Wavy Long Hair Naturally

Written by Oliver Apr 14, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools and chemicals to achieve wavy long hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing how to get wavy long hair naturally. From hair care to simple styling tips, we’ve got you covered. Read on to find out more.

How to

To achieve wavy long hair naturally, you need to start with healthy hair. This means taking care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a good conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals. Once you have healthy hair, you can start incorporating these simple tips to achieve natural waves:

1. Braid your hair

Braiding your hair is the easiest way to achieve natural waves. All you have to do is braid your hair before going to bed and undo it in the morning. You can create loose or tight braids depending on the type of waves you want. If you want more defined waves, you can apply some mousse or sea salt spray before braiding.

2. Use a diffuser

If you have naturally curly hair, using a diffuser is a great way to enhance your curls and create natural waves. Apply some curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair and use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair to create waves.

3. Twist and pin

Twist and pin is another simple technique to achieve natural waves. All you have to do is twist small sections of your hair and pin them with bobby pins. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight and undo the twists to reveal natural waves.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve natural waves:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using harsh chemicals and opt for natural products instead.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair to enhance your natural curls and create waves.

Step 3: Braid, twist, or pin your hair

Use one of the techniques mentioned above to create waves in your hair. You can experiment with different techniques to achieve different types of waves.

Step 4: Leave it in

Leave your hair in braids, twists, or pins for a few hours or overnight to allow the waves to set.

Step 5: Undo the braids, twists, or pins

Undo the braids, twists, or pins to reveal natural waves.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve natural waves:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid causing damage to your hair.

2. Avoid heat tools

Avoid using heat tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can cause damage to your hair.

3. Use natural products

Use natural products such as mousse, sea salt spray, and curl-enhancing creams to enhance your curls and create waves.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve natural waves without using any products?

A: Yes, you can achieve natural waves without using any products. You can use the braiding, twisting, or pinning technique to create waves in your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: How do I avoid frizz?

A: To avoid frizz, use a good conditioner, avoid using hot water to wash your hair, and use a microfiber towel to dry your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and hair dye as they can cause damage to your hair.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these simple tips, you can achieve wavy long hair naturally. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using harsh chemicals and heat tools. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
