Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile hairstyle, look no further than the mens haircut short sides long top! This popular haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, and longer hair on top. It's a great option for men of all ages and hair types, and can be customized to suit your individual style. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens haircut short sides long top, along with tips for styling and hair care.

Description

The mens haircut short sides long top is a modern and trendy hairstyle that has been popular for several years. It's a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your individual style, whether you prefer a slick and polished look or a more relaxed and messy style. This haircut is also known as the "undercut," as the short sides create a contrast with the longer hair on top. It's a great option for men who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top

The mens haircut short sides long top can be achieved by visiting a professional hairstylist or barber. During your appointment, you can discuss your desired style and length with your stylist. They will then use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to a short length, usually around a #2 or #3 guard. The top of your hair will be left longer, typically around 2-4 inches. Your stylist may also use scissors to add texture and movement to the top of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving the mens haircut short sides long top:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist or barber.
  2. Discuss your desired style and length with your stylist.
  3. Have your stylist use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to a short length, around a #2 or #3 guard.
  4. Leave the top of your hair longer, around 2-4 inches.
  5. Have your stylist use scissors to add texture and movement to the top of your hair.
  6. Style your hair as desired using hair products.

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your mens haircut short sides long top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as slicking your hair back or creating a messy and tousled look.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your haircut.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

FAQs

Q: Is the mens haircut short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the mens haircut short sides long top is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How should I style my mens haircut short sides long top?

A: You can style your mens haircut short sides long top in a variety of ways, depending on your personal style and preferences. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my mens haircut short sides long top?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens haircut short sides long top.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and hydrated hair with your mens haircut short sides long top:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner every other day.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a nourishing hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens haircut short sides long top and maintain healthy and hydrated hair. Whether you prefer a polished and sleek look or a more relaxed and messy style, the mens haircut short sides long top is a versatile and trendy haircut that is sure to turn heads.
