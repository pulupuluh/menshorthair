Korean Hairstyle For Long Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long face shapes can be tricky to style, but Korean hairstyles offer a wide range of options for women with this face shape. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about finding the perfect Korean hairstyle for long face females. From how to choose the right cut to styling tips and hair care, read on for our comprehensive guide.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Cut
  • Popular Korean Hairstyles for Long Face Females
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right Korean hairstyle for long face females, the key is to find a cut that adds width to the face. This can be achieved through layers, bangs, and volume. It's also important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a cut.

One of the most popular haircuts for long face females is the long bob, or "lob." This cut typically falls just above the shoulders and features layers for added volume. Bangs can also be added to frame the face and add width.

Another option is the "shag" haircut, which features choppy layers and a lot of texture. This cut works well for those with thick, straight hair.

Popular Korean Hairstyles for Long Face Females

There are many popular Korean hairstyles for long face females, including:

1. Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are a popular trend in Korean hairstyles and work well for long face shapes. These bangs are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, creating a curtain-like effect. They help to frame the face and add width.

2. Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic Korean haircut that works well for long face shapes. This cut features layers that add volume and texture to the hair, which can help to balance out a long face shape.

3. Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are a great option for those who want to add some dimension to their hair without committing to a full fringe. These bangs are light and airy, and can be styled to frame the face and add width.

4. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a popular Korean hairstyle that works well for long face shapes. This style is easy to achieve and can be worn high or low on the head. It helps to add volume and texture to the hair, which can balance out a long face shape.

Styling Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect Korean hairstyle for your long face shape, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • Use a round brush to create a slight curl at the ends of your hair, which can help to add width.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still rock a Korean hairstyle for long face females. Consider a layered cut that adds volume and texture to your curls.

How do I know if a certain Korean hairstyle will work for my face shape?

Consult with your hairstylist to find out which Korean hairstyles will work best for your face shape. They can help you choose a cut that will enhance your features and balance out your face shape.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect Korean hairstyle for your long face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
