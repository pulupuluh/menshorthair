Men s Haircuts With Longer Hair On Top A Guide To The Latest Trend

Written by Daniel Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man looking for a new hairstyle, you might want to consider one of the latest trends: haircuts with longer hair on top. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. Not only is it versatile and easy to maintain, but it also allows you to express your personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to get this look and provide tips on how to care for it.

How to Get a Haircut with Longer Hair on Top

The first step in getting a haircut with longer hair on top is to find a good hairstylist who understands your hair type and personal style. Once you've found someone you trust, you can discuss the look you want to achieve. Some popular styles include the pompadour, quiff, messy fringe, and slicked-back undercut.

During the haircut, your hairstylist will typically use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. They may also use clippers to create a fade on the sides and back of the head. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on the style you choose, but it's usually at least two inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Haircuts with Longer Hair on Top

Styling men's haircuts with longer hair on top is relatively easy, and there are many different ways to do it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not soaking wet
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade, wax, or gel, to your hair
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape
  5. Finish with hairspray or a similar product to hold the style in place

Tips for Maintaining Men's Haircuts with Longer Hair on Top

To keep your haircut looking great, there are a few things you can do:

  1. Get regular trims to keep the hair on top from getting too long
  2. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  3. Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  4. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you

FAQs about Men's Haircuts with Longer Hair on Top

What hair types are best suited to haircuts with longer hair on top?

Men with straight or wavy hair are the best candidates for haircuts with longer hair on top. However, men with curly hair can also achieve this look with the right styling products and techniques.

What face shapes work well with haircuts with longer hair on top?

Men with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces are the best candidates for haircuts with longer hair on top. However, men with round faces can also achieve this look with the right styling techniques.

Can men with thinning hair get haircuts with longer hair on top?

Yes, men with thinning hair can still achieve a haircut with longer hair on top. However, it's important to choose a style that works with your hair type and avoid styles that require a lot of volume.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Longer Hair on Top

Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  2. Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair
  3. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  4. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage

By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can achieve a great look with a men's haircut with longer hair on top. So why not give it a try?
