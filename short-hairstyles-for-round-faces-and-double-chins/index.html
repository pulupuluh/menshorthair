Short Hairstyles For Round Faces And Double Chins

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face and a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You need a style that will flatter your features and draw attention away from your chin. Fortunately, there are many short hairstyles that can work for you. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and double chins, along with tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Description

Having a round face and double chin can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that suits you. You need a style that will balance out the proportions of your face and draw attention away from your chin. Short hairstyles can be a great option, as they can make your face look slimmer and more balanced. However, not all short hairstyles will work for you. You need to choose a style that flatters your features and complements your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face and double chin, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length in your face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the top of your head or by creating asymmetrical layers. You also want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider. Finally, you want to choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some step by step instructions for styling some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and double chins:

Pixie Cut

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's slightly damp.

2. Apply a small amount of hair mousse to your roots and work it through your hair.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Bob Cut

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's slightly damp.

2. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your ends to add shine and prevent frizz.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, flipping the ends outwards for a flirty look.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or bumps.

5. Finish with a medium-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be a challenge, but there are some tips that can make it easier:

- Use the right products for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight mousse or spray to add volume. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing serum to tame frizz.

- Invest in a good blow dryer and flat iron. These tools can help you achieve a salon-quality look at home.

- Experiment with different partings and styles. A center part can make your face look rounder, while a side part can create the illusion of length.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face and double chin?

A: Yes, you can! Side-swept bangs can be a great option, as they can draw attention away from your chin and create a flattering frame for your face.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: It depends on the style of your haircut and how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it brittle.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best with a round face and double chin. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you!
