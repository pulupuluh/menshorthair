How To Make Easy Hairstyles For Medium Hair

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have medium hair and you're looking for some easy hairstyles to try out, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some simple and stylish hairstyles that you can create in no time. Whether you're getting ready for work, school, or a night out, these hairstyles will help you look your best without spending a lot of time or effort.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get started, it's important to note that you should always start with clean, dry hair. If you're using any styling products, apply them to your hair before you begin. Here are some easy hairstyles for medium hair:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for those days when you don't have a lot of time or energy to spend on your hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Don't worry about making it perfect - the messier, the better. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your medium-length hair while keeping it out of your face. To create this look, take the top section of your hair and gather it into a ponytail. Secure it with a hair tie and then pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

3. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for weddings or other special occasions. To create this look, part your hair down the middle and then braid each side. Wrap the braids around your head like a crown and secure them with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to soften the look.

4. Side Braid

The side braid is a cute and casual hairstyle that's perfect for everyday wear. To create this look, gather all of your hair to one side and then braid it. Secure the end with a hair tie and then pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the messy bun:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the half-up half-down hairstyle:

  1. Take the top section of your hair and gather it into a ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the braided crown:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Braid each side of your hair.
  3. Wrap the braids around your head like a crown.
  4. Secure the braids with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to soften the look.

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the side braid:

  1. Gather all of your hair to one side.
  2. Braid your hair.
  3. Secure the end with a hair tie.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating easy hairstyles for medium hair:

  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Don't worry about making it perfect - messy hairstyles are in!
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use styling products like hairspray or mousse to help hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for medium hair:

Q: Are there any easy hairstyles for medium hair that don't require any tools?

A: Yes! The messy bun and the half-up half-down hairstyle can both be created without any tools.

Q: How can I make my hair look fuller?

A: You can use volumizing products like mousse or dry shampoo to help give your hair more body.

Q: How can I make my hairstyle last all day?

A: Use hairspray or other styling products to help hold your hairstyle in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these easy hairstyles for medium hair and some basic hair care tips, you'll be able to keep your hair looking stylish and healthy. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles are sure to impress.
