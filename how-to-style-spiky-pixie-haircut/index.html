How To Style Spiky Pixie Haircut

Written by Frank Mar 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut, a spiky pixie might be the perfect choice for you. This edgy style is perfect for those who want to add some personality to their hair without spending hours in front of the mirror. In this article, we will show you how to style spiky pixie haircut in a few simple steps.

Table of Content

  • How to Style Spiky Pixie Haircut
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Here are the steps to style spiky pixie haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will give your hair some texture and make it easier to style.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.
  3. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to your fingertips and run them through your hair. This will help to create definition and hold.
  4. Using your fingertips, lift and tousle your hair to create a spiky texture. You can also use a comb or brush to create more defined spikes.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

That's it! With just a few simple steps, you can create a stylish and edgy spiky pixie haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your spiky pixie haircut:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair.
  • If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, use a straightening iron to smooth it out before styling.
  • Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with your hair and create different looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about spiky pixie haircuts:

  • Will a spiky pixie haircut work for all face shapes? Yes, a spiky pixie haircut can be adapted to suit any face shape.
  • Do I need to have short hair to get a spiky pixie haircut? Yes, a spiky pixie haircut is a short hairstyle.
  • How often do I need to get my spiky pixie haircut trimmed? It depends on how fast your hair grows, but you should aim to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your spiky pixie haircut looking its best:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style and maintain a spiky pixie haircut. Remember to be creative and have fun with your hair, and don't be afraid to try new things!
