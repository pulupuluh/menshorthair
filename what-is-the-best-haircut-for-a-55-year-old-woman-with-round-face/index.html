What Is The Best Haircut For A 55 Year Old Woman With Round Face

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a 55-year-old woman with a round face and wondering what the best haircut for you is? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for your face shape, as well as tips and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and looking their best.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, rounded cheekbones, and a rounded chin. The best haircut for a round face should help elongate the face and create angles, making it appear slimmer and more defined. A good haircut should also highlight your best features while minimizing any areas you may want to downplay.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a round face, keep in mind that a layered haircut is the best option. Layers will help add volume and dimension to your hair, creating angles and softening your features. A long bob, medium-length cut, or a shoulder-length cut with layers will work best for you.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by consulting with your hairstylist. They will help you choose the best haircut that will flatter your face shape.

Step 2: Decide on the length of your hair. A long bob, medium-length cut, or a shoulder-length cut with layers will work best for you.

Step 3: Opt for a layered haircut. Layers will help add volume and dimension to your hair, creating angles and softening your features.

Step 4: Style your hair to add volume and texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and create waves or curls.

Tips

When getting a haircut, avoid blunt cuts that fall at the chin level. This will only emphasize the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a layered cut that will create movement and volume.

Keep your hair healthy by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.

Accessorize your hair with headbands, hair clips, or scarves to create different looks and add some personality to your style.

FAQ

Q: Can I have bangs with a round face?

A: Yes, you can have bangs with a round face. However, make sure they are side-swept or long bangs that will create angles and soften your features.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid blunt cuts that fall at the chin level, as this will emphasize the roundness of your face. Also, avoid hairstyles that have too much volume on the sides, such as a bouffant or a mushroom cut.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any haircut. Keep your hair hydrated by drinking plenty of water and eating a balanced diet. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to brush your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to your strands.

Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to minimize friction and prevent breakage. This will also help your hair retain its moisture, keeping it healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, when choosing a haircut for a 55-year-old woman with a round face, opt for a layered cut that will create movement and volume. Keep your hair healthy and hydrated by drinking plenty of water and using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. And don't forget to accessorize your hair with headbands, hair clips, or scarves to add some personality to your style.
