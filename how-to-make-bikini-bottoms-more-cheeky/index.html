How To Make Bikini Bottoms More Cheeky A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your boring, full-coverage bikini bottoms? Do you want to show off a little bit more skin this summer? If so, then you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky. From simple alterations to DIY hacks, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Make Bikini Bottoms More Cheeky

There are several ways to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky. The easiest way is to buy a bikini bottom that is already cheeky. However, if you already have a bikini bottom that you love, you can make it more cheeky by altering it. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Cut the Back

The simplest way to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky is to cut the back. You can do this by using scissors to trim the fabric along the seam that runs down the center of the back of the bikini bottom. Be sure to only cut a little bit at a time, and try on the bikini bottom frequently to make sure you don't cut too much.

Tip #2: Ruching

Ruching is a technique where you gather fabric together to create a ruffled effect. You can use this technique to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky by gathering the fabric in the center of the back of the bikini bottom. To do this, you'll need a needle and thread. Simply sew a few stitches in the center of the back of the bikini bottom, and then pull the thread tight to gather the fabric.

Tip #3: Add Some Fringe

If you want to add a little bit of flair to your bikini bottoms, consider adding some fringe. You can buy fringe at your local craft store, and then sew it onto the back of your bikini bottoms. This will not only make your bikini bottoms more cheeky, but it will also add some fun movement when you walk or swim.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some tips for making your bikini bottoms more cheeky, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

Step 1: Choose Your Bikini Bottom

The first step is to choose the bikini bottom that you want to make more cheeky. Keep in mind that not all bikini bottoms are created equal, so you may need to try on a few different styles before you find one that you like.

Step 2: Decide How Cheeky You Want to Go

Next, decide how cheeky you want to make your bikini bottoms. Do you want just a little bit of cheek, or do you want to go all out? This will determine which technique you choose.

Step 3: Prep Your Bikini Bottom

Before you start altering your bikini bottom, make sure it's clean and dry. You don't want to accidentally cut or sew through dirt or sand. Also, try on your bikini bottom to make sure it fits properly before you start making alterations.

Step 4: Cut or Sew

Depending on which technique you choose, you'll either need to cut the back of your bikini bottom or sew some fabric together. Follow the tips above for guidance.

Step 5: Try It On

Once you've made your alterations, try on your bikini bottom to make sure it fits properly and looks how you want it to. If you need to make any additional alterations, now is the time to do it.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your bikini bottoms more cheeky:

- Start small and work your way up. You can always make your bikini bottoms more cheeky, but you can't make them less cheeky. - Be careful when cutting or sewing your bikini bottoms. You don't want to accidentally cut or sew through the wrong part of the fabric. - Consider the occasion. Cheeky bikini bottoms may not be appropriate for all occasions, so make sure you're wearing them in an appropriate setting.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making bikini bottoms more cheeky:

How much should I cut off my bikini bottoms?

Start by cutting off a little bit at a time. You don't want to cut too much and end up with a bikini bottom that is too cheeky for your liking.

Can I make my bikini bottoms more cheeky without cutting them?

Yes! You can use the ruching or fringe technique mentioned above to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky without cutting them.

Can I alter any type of bikini bottom?

It depends on the bikini bottom. Some bikini bottoms may not be able to be altered, so make sure you choose one that is easy to work with.

Hair Care

Don't forget about hair care when you're wearing cheeky bikini bottoms! Make sure to tie your hair up or wear a swim cap to keep it out of the way while you're swimming. Also, rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove any salt or chlorine.

Now that you know how to make your bikini bottoms more cheeky, it's time to hit the beach or pool and show off your new look! Remember to be confident and have fun.
