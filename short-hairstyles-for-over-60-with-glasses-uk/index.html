Short Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses Uk

Written by Christine Mar 31, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're over 60 and wear glasses, you might be wondering what kind of short hairstyles would look great on you. There are plenty of options to choose from, and we've gathered some of the best ones here in this article. Whether you're looking for something low-maintenance or a style that will turn heads, we've got you covered.

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 who want to look stylish and feel comfortable at the same time. They can be low-maintenance, easy to style, and versatile enough to suit any occasion. When you add glasses into the mix, you need to make sure your hair is not getting in the way of your frames. That's why choosing the right short hairstyle is so important.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. You want a hairstyle that will flatter your features and make you feel confident. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist to show them what you're looking for.

Some popular short hairstyles for women over 60 with glasses include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts. Pixie cuts are great for women with oval or heart-shaped faces, while bobs work well on round or square faces. Layered cuts can add volume and texture to thin or fine hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it gently.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.

3. Use a blow dryer to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.

4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture or waves to your hair if desired.

5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

- Use a volumizing shampoo to add body to your hair.

- Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

- Keep your glasses in mind when choosing a hairstyle. You don't want your hair to be in the way of your frames.

FAQ

Q: Can women over 60 wear short hairstyles?

A: Absolutely! Short hairstyles can be flattering and stylish at any age.

Q: What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A: Pixie cuts look great on women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style loses its shape. Generally, getting a trim every 6-8 weeks is recommended.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, make sure you take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat styling. If you have color-treated hair, use products specifically designed for colored hair to keep it looking vibrant. And don't forget to schedule regular haircuts to maintain your style.

Short hairstyles for women over 60 with glasses can be both stylish and practical. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can look and feel your best at any age. So go ahead and try out some of these short hairstyles – you might just be surprised at how great you look!
