Who Has The Best Hair In Bts In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 10, 2023 · 7 min read

As a K-pop fan, we know how important hair is not only to BTS members but also to their fans. BTS has some of the best hairstyles in the K-pop industry, and we're always excited to see what they come up with next. In this blog post, we'll discuss who has the best hair in BTS, how to achieve their hairstyles, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy.

Table of Contents

  1. Who Has the Best Hair in BTS?
  2. How to Achieve BTS Hairstyles
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to BTS Hairstyles
  4. BTS Hair Care Tips
  5. FAQs

Who Has the Best Hair in BTS?

It's tough to pick just one member who has the best hair in BTS because they all have unique and stunning hairstyles. However, if we had to choose, we'd say that Jungkook has the best hair in BTS. He's experimented with various hairstyles, from long hair to short hair, and he always manages to pull it off. His hair is always styled perfectly, and he's not afraid to try something new.

However, other members also have excellent hairstyles. For example, V's purple hair during the "Dynamite" era was a fan favorite, while Jimin's pink hair during the "Blood Sweat & Tears" era was iconic. Suga's mint hair during the "Spring Day" era also deserves an honorable mention.

How to Achieve BTS Hairstyles

If you want to achieve a BTS hairstyle, the first step is to find a style that suits you. You can look through photos of the members or watch their music videos to get inspiration. Once you've found a style, you'll need to visit a hairstylist who is experienced in K-pop hairstyles.

When you visit the hairstylist, make sure to bring a photo of the hairstyle you want. This will help the hairstylist understand what you're looking for and give you the best results. It's also important to communicate with the hairstylist and let them know if you have any hair concerns.

Step-by-Step Guide to BTS Hairstyles

If you want to achieve a BTS hairstyle at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as hair wax or pomade, to your hair.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair according to the BTS member's hairstyle you want to achieve.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

BTS Hair Care Tips

While achieving a BTS hairstyle is exciting, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some BTS hair care tips:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. It can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair to achieve a BTS hairstyle?

A: It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to prevent dryness and damage.

Q: Can I achieve a BTS hairstyle on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve a BTS hairstyle on short hair. Just make sure to find a style that suits your hair length and texture.

Q: What should I do if my hair is damaged from heat styling?

A: If your hair is damaged from heat styling, you can use a hair mask or oil treatment to restore moisture. It's also best to avoid heat styling for a while and let your hair rest.

Overall, BTS has some of the best hairstyles in the K-pop industry, and it's no wonder why fans love them. Whether you want to achieve a BTS hairstyle or simply admire their hair, we hope this blog post has been helpful. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.
