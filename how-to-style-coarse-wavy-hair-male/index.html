How To Style Coarse Wavy Hair Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Mar 14, 2023 · 9 min read

Coarse wavy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style coarse wavy hair male, including how to choose the right haircut, what products to use, and some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Coarse Wavy Hair Male
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Coarse Wavy Hair

How to Style Coarse Wavy Hair Male

The first step in styling coarse wavy hair is to choose the right haircut. A good haircut can make a world of difference in how your hair looks and feels. For coarse wavy hair, we recommend a medium length cut with layers. This will help to reduce bulk and create movement in your hair.

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few key products that you’ll need. These include a good quality shampoo and conditioner, a styling cream or pomade, and a hairspray or gel. Look for products that are specifically designed for coarse or wavy hair, as these will provide the best results.

Choosing the Right Products

When choosing a shampoo and conditioner for coarse wavy hair, look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients such as shea butter or coconut oil. These will help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Avoid products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even drier.

For styling your hair, a cream or pomade is ideal for creating texture and definition. Look for products that are lightweight and non-greasy, as these will not weigh your hair down. Apply the cream or pomade to damp hair and work it through with your fingers, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths of your hair.

To finish off your style, use a hairspray or gel to hold your hair in place. Look for products that provide a strong hold without making your hair feel stiff or crunchy. Apply the hairspray or gel to dry hair, holding the can about 12 inches away from your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have the right products and haircut, it’s time to style your hair. Follow these steps for a stylish and manageable look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently pat your hair dry with a towel.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to damp hair, working it through with your fingers.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  4. If you have naturally curly hair, use a curling iron to define your curls and create more volume.
  5. Apply a small amount of hairspray or gel to your hair, holding the can about 12 inches away from your head.
  6. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a more natural look.
  7. If necessary, use a flat iron to straighten any stubborn curls or waves.
  8. Finish off your look with a final spritz of hairspray or gel.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks for styling coarse wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or flat irons too often, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • If you have long hair, try wearing it in a messy bun or ponytail for a more casual look.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for coarse wavy hair?

A medium length cut with layers is ideal for coarse wavy hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and create movement in your hair.

How often should I wash my coarse wavy hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even drier.

What products should I use to style coarse wavy hair?

Look for products that are specifically designed for coarse or wavy hair, such as a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a styling cream or pomade, and a hairspray or gel.

Hair Care for Coarse Wavy Hair

To keep your coarse wavy hair looking and feeling its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or flat irons too often, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even drier.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look for your coarse wavy hair.
