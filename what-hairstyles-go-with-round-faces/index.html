What Hairstyles Go with Round Faces?

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what hairstyles and haircuts will best complement your features. The good news is that there are plenty of options to choose from! In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for round faces, as well as tips for maintaining your new 'do.

How to Determine If You Have a Round Face

Before we dive into hairstyles, let's first discuss how to determine if you have a round face. This face shape is characterized by a wide hairline, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you're still unsure, try measuring the width of your face at the cheeks, forehead, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you likely have a round face.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of length. Opt for layers that start at or below the chin, and avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheeks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a round face by covering some of the forehead and drawing attention to the eyes. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs, which can make the face appear even rounder.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for those with round faces, especially if you have fine hair. Keep the cut short and textured to add volume and create the illusion of height.

4. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can help to slim down a round face and create a more defined jawline. Opt for layers that start at the chin and angle downward, and avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheeks.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've found a hairstyle that complements your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about hairstyles for round faces:

What hairstyles should you avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides, such as wide curls or blunt cuts that end at the cheeks.

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to keep the hair at the crown of your head lifted to elongate the face.

Do all short hairstyles work with round faces?

No, it's important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape. The pixie cut and bob with angled layers are two great options for those with round faces.

Hair Care for Round Faces

In addition to styling, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and protein to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut that complements your round face and enhances your natural beauty.
