Mens Short Military Haircut Styles

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, clean-cut hairstyle, then a short military haircut might be just what you need. This style is popular among men of all ages and is perfect for those who want a no-fuss look that still looks stylish. In this article, we'll go over some popular men's short military haircut styles, how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Achieve a Short Military Haircut
  • Popular Styles
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

How to Achieve a Short Military Haircut

The first step to achieving a short military haircut is to choose the right length. A typical military haircut is usually around 1-2 inches long on the top and tapered on the sides and back. You can either go to a professional stylist or use clippers at home to achieve this look.

Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  3. Next, use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the same length.
  4. Blend the sides and top together using clippers or scissors.
  5. Finally, clean up the neckline and sideburns using clippers.

Keep in mind that the length and style of your military haircut will depend on your face shape and personal preferences. Some men prefer a more faded look, while others like a blunt cut. It's important to communicate with your stylist to get the look that suits you best.

Popular Styles

There are many different styles of military haircuts, from buzz cuts to crew cuts. Here are a few popular styles to consider:

High and Tight

The high and tight is a classic military haircut that is short on the sides and back and slightly longer on the top. The top is usually brushed forward and styled with a little bit of product for a clean, polished look.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is another popular military haircut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides and back. It's a versatile style that can be worn with or without product, and it's easy to maintain.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is the shortest military haircut, with hair shaved down to a uniform length all over. It's a no-fuss style that is perfect for active men who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've achieved your desired military haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a light conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

FAQ

Q: Will a military haircut look good on me?

A: Military haircuts are a versatile style that can look good on men of all ages and face shapes. However, it's important to choose a style that suits your personal preferences and lifestyle.

Q: How often should I get a military haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a military haircut every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking sharp.

Q: Can I style my military haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your military haircut in different ways depending on the length and texture of your hair. However, keep in mind that military haircuts are designed to be low-maintenance and easy to style.

In conclusion, a short military haircut is a classic and stylish choice for men who want a low-maintenance look. With the right length, style, and maintenance, you can achieve a sharp and polished look that will never go out of style.
