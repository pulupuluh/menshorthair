Youtube How To Cut A Flat Top Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Flat top haircuts have been a popular hairstyle for men for years. It’s a versatile cut that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. However, cutting a flat top haircut can be a bit tricky, especially if you’re doing it for the first time. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut a flat top haircut using Youtube videos. We’ll also share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect flat top haircut.

Description

A flat top haircut is a style where the hair is cut short on the sides and back, while the top is cut flat and square. This style is achieved by using clippers to create a straight line across the top of the head, and then using scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. The flat top haircut is a classic style that has been popular for decades, and it’s still a go-to style for many men today.

How to Cut a Flat Top Haircut

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a clipper, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before you start cutting. It’s easier to cut hair when it’s dry, and you can see the natural texture of the hair better.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using your clippers, start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a #2 or #3 guard to create a short, even cut. Make sure you cut the hair in the same direction as it grows.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using your clippers or scissors, create a straight line across the top of your head. Make sure the line is level and square. Then, use your scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Make sure you cut the hair in the direction it grows.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Using your clippers, blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Use a #1 or #2 guard to create a gradual taper from the top to the sides and back.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Using your clippers or scissors, clean up the edges around your ears and neck. Make sure the edges are clean and straight.

Tips for Cutting a Flat Top Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect flat top haircut:

  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way shorter.
  • Use scissors for the top of the head to create a more natural look.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

What length should I cut the top of my hair?

The length of the top of your hair depends on your personal preference. However, a typical length for a flat top haircut is around 1-2 inches.

Can I cut a flat top haircut on my own?

Yes, you can cut a flat top haircut on your own, but it’s best to have someone help you with the back of your head.

How often should I get a flat top haircut?

A flat top haircut should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care

After you’ve cut your flat top haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a light pomade or styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.

Cutting a flat top haircut may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and a little practice, you can achieve the perfect flat top haircut. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks, and you’ll be rocking a classic flat top haircut in no time!
