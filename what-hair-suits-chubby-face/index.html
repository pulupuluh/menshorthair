What Hair Suits Chubby Face A Guide For 2023

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to flatter your chubby face? Well, fret not, because we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll be sharing some tips on what hair suits chubby face and how you can style your hair to accentuate your features. Read on to find out more.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face with a bar of soap or a washable marker. Make sure to include your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline in the outline.
  3. Step back and look at the outline. Does it resemble any of the six basic face shapes?

If you're having trouble determining your face shape, you can also ask your hairstylist for help. They should be able to give you a professional opinion on what hair suits chubby face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that create height at the crown. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.
  2. If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften your jawline. Soft curls or waves can help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or angular.
  3. If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and jawline. Side-swept bangs or a layered bob can help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too top-heavy.
  4. If you have a diamond-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add width at the temples and chin. A chin-length bob or a pixie cut can help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the crown.
  5. If you have an oblong face, opt for hairstyles that add width to your face. Soft waves or curls can help to achieve this. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight.

Remember, these are just guidelines. At the end of the day, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tips on Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to style your hair to suit your chubby face. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat. These can make your face look wider.
  • Go for hairstyles that add volume at the crown. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to create a diagonal line that slims your face.
  • Layers can help to soften your features and create movement in your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. These can make your face look wider.

FAQs on Hairstyles for Chubby Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to add volume at the crown to elongate your face.

Q: Can I wear a bob if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to add layers and volume to create movement in your hair.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to add volume at the crown to elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips for Chubby Faces

Lastly, here are some hair care tips for chubby faces:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

And there you have it - a guide on what hair suits chubby face. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
