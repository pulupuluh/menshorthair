Layered Haircuts With Bangs Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Apr 05, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some volume and movement to your curls? If so, a layered haircut with bangs could be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we will provide a complete guide on how to achieve the perfect layered haircut with bangs for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve a Layered Haircut with Bangs for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut with Bangs Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layered Haircuts with Bangs Curly Hair

Description

A layered haircut with bangs can add movement and volume to your curly hair. Layers can help reduce bulk and add definition to your curls. A fringe or bangs can also help to frame your face and add a trendy touch to your look.

How to Achieve a Layered Haircut with Bangs for Curly Hair

Before getting started, it is important to find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Curly hair can be more complex to cut than straight hair, and a skilled stylist can ensure that your haircut looks great and is easy to maintain.

When it comes to achieving a layered haircut with bangs for curly hair, there are several factors to consider. These include the length of your hair, the shape of your face, and the type of curls you have.

Length of Hair

The length of your hair can impact the look of your layered haircut with bangs. Shorter hair can create a more voluminous look, while longer hair can provide more weight and definition to your curls.

Shape of Your Face

The shape of your face can also impact the type of layered haircut with bangs that will suit you best. A round face may benefit from longer layers that create a slimming effect, while a square-shaped face may look better with shorter, choppy layers.

Type of Curls

The type of curls you have can also play a role in the type of layered haircut with bangs that will work for you. Tighter curls may benefit from shorter layers to help define the curls, while looser waves may benefit from longer layers to create more movement.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a layered haircut with bangs for curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining product to help enhance your natural curls.
  3. Part your hair where you want your bangs to fall, and section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut your bangs to the desired length.
  5. Next, section off the rest of your hair and divide it into layers.
  6. Starting at the back of your head, use a pair of scissors to cut the first layer.
  7. Continue cutting each layer, working your way up to the top of your head.
  8. Once all the layers have been cut, use your fingers to separate and define your curls.
  9. Style your bangs and hair as desired.
  10. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold the look in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut with Bangs Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your layered haircut with bangs curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your natural curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to help detangle your curls without causing damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your layered haircut with bangs looking fresh.

FAQs

What type of bangs are best for curly hair?

Side-swept bangs or fringe that start from the crown of your head can be a good option for curly hair. These types of bangs can help to frame your face and create a trendy look.

How often should I get my layered haircut with bangs trimmed?

It is recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your layered haircut with bangs looking fresh and healthy.

Can I still wear my hair up with a layered haircut with bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a layered haircut with bangs. You may need to use bobby pins or other hair accessories to keep your bangs in place.

Hair Care for Layered Haircuts with Bangs Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for your layered haircut with bangs curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage to your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, a layered haircut with bangs can be a great way to add volume and movement to your curly hair. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a trendy and flattering look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
