Bangs For Round Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 13, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to flatter your round face shape? Bangs could be the answer! In this article, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about bangs for round faces in 2021. From how to choose the right style to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. Let’s jump in!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Round Face
  • Types of Bangs for Round Faces
  • How to Cut Bangs at Home
  • Tips for Styling Bangs with a Round Face
  • FAQ About Bangs for Round Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Round Face

Before you dive into a new hairstyle, it’s important to consider your face shape. Round faces have soft, curved lines without any sharp angles. To balance out the roundness, you’ll want to choose bangs that create angles and draw attention to other features.

When choosing bangs for a round face, look for styles that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This creates a diagonal line that slims down the face. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across, as they can make the face appear wider.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Thin or fine hair might not be able to support heavy, full bangs. On the other hand, thick or curly hair might require more effort to style and maintain.

Types of Bangs for Round Faces

Now that you know what to look for, let’s explore some of the best bangs styles for round faces:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a classic choice for round faces. They create a diagonal line that slims down the face and draws attention to the eyes. This style works well with most hair textures and lengths, from short bobs to long layers.

Asymmetrical Bangs

Asymmetrical bangs are another option for adding angles to a round face. This style features longer bangs on one side and shorter on the other, creating an off-center part. Asymmetrical bangs work well with medium to long hair lengths and can be styled straight or curly.

Curly Bangs

If you have naturally curly hair, don’t be afraid to embrace it with curly bangs. This style adds volume and texture to a round face, and the curls can be customized to suit your face shape. Keep in mind that curly bangs require regular maintenance to prevent frizz and maintain their shape.

How to Cut Bangs at Home

If you’re feeling brave and want to cut your bangs at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  3. Hold the hair straight up and comb it forward.
  4. Cut the hair at the desired length, starting longer than you think you need.
  5. Gradually trim the hair shorter, checking the length as you go.
  6. Repeat on the other side, making sure to keep the length and angle consistent.
  7. Style your new bangs as desired.

Remember, it’s always best to seek professional help if you’re unsure or nervous about cutting your own hair.

Tips for Styling Bangs with a Round Face

Once you have the perfect bangs for your face shape, it’s important to style them correctly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.
  • If your bangs are too long, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to tuck them behind your ears.
  • For a more dramatic look, try adding a slight curl or wave to your bangs.

FAQ About Bangs for Round Faces

What type of bangs should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across, as they can make the face appear wider. You should also avoid very short bangs that sit above the eyebrows, as they can make the forehead look too short.

Can I wear bangs if I have a double chin?

Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a double chin. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and doesn’t draw attention to the chin. Side-swept bangs or asymmetrical bangs are good options.

Do bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Yes, bangs require regular trimming to maintain their shape and length. You’ll also need to wash and style them every day to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs every day to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or oils near your bangs, as they can weigh them down.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • If your bangs get oily throughout the day, use a dry shampoo to refresh them.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking the perfect bangs for your round face in no time. Whether you opt for side-swept bangs or curly bangs, the key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
