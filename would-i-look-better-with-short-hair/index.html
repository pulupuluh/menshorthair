Would I Look Better With Short Hair

Written by Bardi May 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Short hair is a bold and stunning look that can transform your appearance. But before you take the plunge, you might be wondering, “Would I look better with short hair?”

Description

The answer to this question isn’t a simple yes or no. It depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we’ll explore some tips and tricks to help you decide if short hair is right for you.

How to

When considering a shorter hairstyle, it’s essential to do your research. Look online for pictures of celebrities or models with similar face shapes and hair textures to your own. This will give you an idea of how a shorter cut might look on you.

Another option is to try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different styles will look on you without committing to a haircut. This can help you narrow down your options and find a style that suits you.

Step by Step

1. Research different short hairstyles online and find pictures of people with similar face shapes and hair textures to your own.

2. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to get a better idea of how different styles will look on you.

3. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what style will work best for you.

4. Take into account your personal style and how much time and effort you want to put into styling your hair.

Tips

- If you have a round face, opt for a shorter cut with layers to add height and lengthen your face.

- If you have a square face, consider a bob or a pixie cut with soft, wispy bangs to soften your features.

- If you have a heart-shaped face, try a chin-length bob or a layered cut that falls just above your shoulders.

- If you have an oval face, you’re in luck! Nearly any short hairstyle will look great on you.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut be easier to maintain?

A: In general, short hair is easier to maintain than longer hair. However, there are some short styles that require more upkeep, such as pixie cuts with longer bangs or textured, layered cuts that need to be styled daily.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look too round?

A: Not necessarily. There are plenty of short styles that can flatter a round face, such as layered bobs or asymmetrical cuts. The key is to add texture and volume to the hair to create the illusion of length and height.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Be sure to schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Invest in high-quality styling products, such as a good hairspray or texturizing spray, to help hold your style in place and add volume and texture to your hair.

Finally, don’t forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

So, would you look better with short hair? It all depends on your individual features and preferences. Use these tips and tricks to help you make the best decision for you and your hair.
