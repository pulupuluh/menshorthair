How To Get More Defined Curls After A Perm

Written by Christine May 10, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have recently gotten a perm, but you feel like your curls aren't as defined as you'd like them to be, don't worry! There are several things you can do to get more defined curls after a perm. In this blog post, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get More Defined Curls After a Perm
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Get More Defined Curls After a Perm

If you're looking to get more defined curls after a perm, there are a few things you can do. The first thing to keep in mind is that it may take some time for your hair to adjust to the perm. Your hair may be a little bit frizzy or undefined at first, but don't worry – this is normal.

The following are some steps you can take to get more defined curls:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key when it comes to getting more defined curls after a perm. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid anything that contains sulfates or alcohol. These ingredients can dry out your hair and make your curls look frizzy and undefined.

2. Style Your Hair While It's Wet

If you want more defined curls, it's best to style your hair while it's wet. This is because wet hair is more pliable and easier to work with. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and then apply your styling products.

3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is an attachment that you can add to your hair dryer to help distribute the heat more evenly. This can help to reduce frizz and define your curls. To use a diffuser, simply attach it to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get more defined curls after a perm:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply your styling products while your hair is still wet. Start with a curl-defining cream or gel, and then use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting. Start by drying the roots of your hair and then work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair up towards your scalp. This will help to define your curls and give them more volume.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you get more defined curls after a perm:

  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying – this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to help keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it normal for my curls to be undefined after a perm?

Yes, it's normal for your curls to be a little bit undefined after a perm. Your hair may need some time to adjust to the chemicals used in the perm.

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. However, as a general rule, it's best to wash your hair no more than every other day to prevent your curls from becoming dry and frizzy.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your curls look frizzy.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get more defined curls after a perm and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
