How To Put A Messy Bun In Your Hair A Relaxed Guide

Description

Do you have long, unruly hair that always seems to be in the way? A messy bun is the perfect hairstyle for you! It's easy to do and looks effortlessly chic. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to put a messy bun in your hair.

How To

The first step in putting a messy bun in your hair is to gather all of your hair into a ponytail. You can choose to have your ponytail high or low, depending on your preference. Once you have your hair in a ponytail, twist it around itself until it forms a bun. You can secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie. Voila! You now have a messy bun.

Step by Step

1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.

2. Gather your hair into a ponytail with your hands.

3. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun.

4. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.

5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Tips

- Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.

- Pull out strands of hair around your face to create a more relaxed look.

- Apply hairspray to your bun to help it hold its shape.

- Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I put a messy bun in my hair if it's not long?

A: Yes! You can put a messy bun in your hair no matter what length it is. You may need to use bobby pins to secure any shorter pieces of hair.

Q: How long does it take to put a messy bun in my hair?

A: It should take no more than a few minutes to put a messy bun in your hair. The more you practice, the faster you'll become!

Hair Care

Putting your hair in a messy bun too often can cause damage to your hair. It's important to take care of your hair by using a hair mask once a week and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, make sure to use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.

Now that you know how to put a messy bun in your hair, you can enjoy this easy and relaxed hairstyle anytime you want!
