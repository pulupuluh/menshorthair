Guys Hair Long Top Short Sides A Trendy Style For Modern Men

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern hairstyle, then the guys hair long top short sides is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a combination of a longer top with shorter sides, creating a neat and edgy look. This article will provide you with a how-to guide, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice on this trendy hairstyle for men.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The guys hair long top short sides is a popular hairstyle among modern men. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in several ways, depending on your personal preference. The longer top provides volume and texture, while the shorter sides create a clean and sharp look. This style can be achieved with any hair type, whether you have thick or thin hair, straight or curly hair.

How To

To achieve the guys hair long top short sides, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to create a personalized cut that suits your face shape and hair type. You can bring in a photo of the style you want to show your hairstylist, so they can tailor the cut to your preference. Once you have the haircut, you can style it at home using hair products such as pomade or wax.

Step By Step

1. Visit a professional hairstylist and show them a photo of the guys hair long top short sides style you want.


2. Have your hair cut with a longer top and shorter sides. The length of the top can vary depending on your preference, but it is typically longer than the sides.


3. Use hair products such as pomade or wax to style the longer top. You can create a messy or slicked-back look depending on your personal preference.


4. Use a hair clipper to trim the sides to the desired length. The shorter sides should be clean and sharp.

Tips

- Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.


- Regularly trim the sides to maintain the clean and sharp look of the style.


- Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the guys hair long top short sides with curly hair?


A: Yes, the style can be achieved with any hair type, including curly hair. Your hairstylist can tailor the cut to suit your hair type and face shape.


Q: How often should I trim the sides?


A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. It is recommended to trim the sides every 2-3 weeks to maintain the clean and sharp look of the style.

Hair Care

To keep your guys hair long top short sides looking its best, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes using quality hair products, such as shampoo and conditioner, and regularly trimming the sides. You can also use hair products such as pomade or wax to style the longer top. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy this trendy hairstyle for men.
