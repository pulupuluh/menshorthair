How To Make Big Hair Bows Out Of Ribbon Step By Step

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair accessories? Do you want to add some flair to your hair? Try making big hair bows out of ribbon! Not only are they a fun and easy DIY project, but they can also be a statement piece for any outfit. In this article, we will show you how to make big hair bows out of ribbon step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Materials Needed
  • How to Make Big Hair Bows Out of Ribbon: Step by Step
  • Tips for Making Big Hair Bows
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Materials Needed

  • 3-inch wide ribbon (at least 2 yards)
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread or hot glue gun
  • Barrette or hair elastic

How to Make Big Hair Bows Out of Ribbon: Step by Step

Step 1: Cut the Ribbon

Start by cutting a piece of ribbon that is at least 2 yards long. This will ensure that you have enough ribbon to create a big bow. Cut another small piece of ribbon (about 6 inches long) that will be used to wrap around the center of the bow.

Step 2: Fold the Ribbon

Fold the ribbon back and forth like an accordion, making sure that each fold is the same size. Use your fingers to hold the folds in place.

Step 3: Tie the Ribbon

Take the small piece of ribbon you cut earlier and wrap it around the center of the folded ribbon. Tie a knot to secure it in place.

Step 4: Fluff the Bow

Gently pull each fold of the ribbon to the center of the bow, fluffing it up as you go. Make sure that the bow is symmetrical and adjust the folds as needed.

Step 5: Attach the Barrette or Hair Elastic

Turn the bow over and sew or glue a barrette or hair elastic to the back. Make sure that it is securely attached so that it doesn't fall out of your hair.

Tips for Making Big Hair Bows

  • Use a high-quality ribbon that will hold its shape.
  • Experiment with different widths and textures of ribbon to create unique bows.
  • Try adding embellishments like beads or rhinestones to the center of the bow.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't get discouraged if your first few bows don't turn out the way you want.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a different type of ribbon?

A: Yes! You can use any type of ribbon you like, but make sure that it is at least 3 inches wide so that you can create a big bow.

Q: How do I attach the bow to my hair?

A: You can use a barrette or hair elastic to attach the bow to your hair. Sew or glue it to the back of the bow to make sure it stays in place.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a big hair bow, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet, as it is more fragile at this time.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and steps, you're now ready to make big hair bows out of ribbon. Have fun creating different bows to match your style and outfits!
