How To Make Your Hair Look Boy Short A Guide For Women

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a woman who wants to try a new look, going for a boy short hairstyle can be a bold move. The boy short cut is a classic and timeless style that can make you look chic and edgy. In this guide, we will discuss how to make your hair look boy short with some simple steps and tips.

Description

The boy short hair cut is a popular hairstyle for women who want to have short hair without looking too feminine. It's a versatile style that can suit different face shapes and hair textures. The boy short cut usually involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer. This creates a contrast between the short and long parts of the hair, giving it a unique and stylish look.

How to Make Your Hair Look Boy Short

Step 1: Find Inspiration

The first step in making your hair look boy short is to find inspiration. Look for pictures of women with boy short hairstyles that you like and show them to your hairstylist. This will give your hairstylist an idea of what you want and help them create the look you're going for.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstylist

Choosing the right hairstylist is crucial in achieving the perfect boy short haircut. Look for a hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair and who understands the boy short style. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have had a similar haircut.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Hairstylist

Communicating with your hairstylist is important in ensuring that you get the haircut you want. Be clear about what you want and don't be afraid to ask questions. Your hairstylist can also give you advice on what will work best for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair Short

The next step is to cut your hair short. Your hairstylist will use scissors or clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving the top longer. The length of the top can vary depending on your preference.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Styling your hair is the final step in making your hair look boy short. There are different ways to style a boy short haircut, such as adding texture, using hair products, or creating a messy look. Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect boy short hairstyle:

  • Use a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and create a messy look.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some extra style to your haircut.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut to add some edge to your boy short haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles until you find one that works for you.

FAQ

Is the boy short haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the boy short haircut can suit different face shapes, including round, square, and oval. However, it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine whether this style will work for you.

How often do I need to get my hair cut to maintain a boy short style?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how you want to maintain your style. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain a boy short style.

Can I still style my hair if it's too short for a boy short haircut?

Yes, you can still style your hair even if it's too short for a boy short haircut. Experiment with different hair products, such as mousse or gel, to add some texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is important in achieving a boy short haircut. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular hair treatments, such as deep conditioning or hair masks, to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and steps, you can easily make your hair look boy short and achieve a stylish and edgy look. Go ahead and rock that boy short haircut!
