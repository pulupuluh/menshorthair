What is 90's style of dress?

The 90's style of dress was characterized by a mix of grunge, minimalism, and bold statement pieces. Some of the key fashion trends of this decade included:

  • Denim jackets
  • Flannel shirts
  • Leather jackets
  • Chokers
  • Platform shoes
  • Bomber jackets
  • Overalls
  • High-waisted jeans
  • Crop tops
  • Tracksuits

The 90's style was all about mixing and matching different pieces to create your own unique look. It was also a time when fashion became more casual and relaxed, with oversized clothing and comfortable fabrics.

How to achieve the 90's style of dress

If you want to achieve the 90's style of dress, there are a few key pieces that you should invest in:

  • Denim jacket: A classic denim jacket is a must-have for any 90's-inspired outfit. Look for one that's oversized and has a slightly faded wash.
  • Flannel shirt: A flannel shirt is another staple of the 90's style. Wear it buttoned up or tied around your waist for a grunge-inspired look.
  • High-waisted jeans: High-waisted jeans were a popular trend in the 90's and are still a great choice today. Look for a pair that's straight-legged and slightly loose-fitting.
  • Chokers: Chokers were a popular accessory in the 90's and can add a touch of edginess to any outfit. Look for one that's simple and black.
  • Platform shoes: Platform shoes are a fun way to add some height and a 90's-inspired touch to your outfit. Look for a pair that's chunky and has a thick sole.

Remember, the key to achieving the 90's style is to mix and match different pieces to create your own unique look. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your fashion choices!

Step-by-step tips for 90's style of dress

Here are some step-by-step tips for achieving the 90's style of dress:

  1. Start with a basic outfit, such as high-waisted jeans and a white t-shirt.
  2. Add an oversized denim jacket or flannel shirt to the outfit.
  3. Accessorize with a choker and platform shoes.
  4. Finish the look with bold makeup and messy hair.

Remember, the 90's style is all about being relaxed and comfortable, so don't worry too much about looking perfect!

FAQ about 90's style of dress

Q: Can I wear 90's style clothing to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your office is more traditional, you may want to stick to more classic pieces with a subtle 90's twist, such as a denim blazer or high-waisted pants. If your workplace is more casual, you can have more fun with your outfit choices.

Q: Can I wear 90's style clothing if I'm not a teenager?

A: Absolutely! The 90's style of dress is all about being comfortable and expressing your individuality. It's a great style for anyone, regardless of age.

Q: Where can I find 90's style clothing?

A: There are many retailers that sell 90's-inspired clothing, including Urban Outfitters, Forever 21, and ASOS. You can also find vintage pieces at thrift stores or online marketplaces like Etsy.

Hair care for 90's style of dress

The 90's style of dress is often accompanied by messy, undone hair. Here are some tips for achieving the perfect 90's-inspired hairstyle:

  • Try a messy bun or topknot
  • Add some texture with a sea salt spray
  • Leave your hair natural and tousled
  • Experiment with fun hair accessories, such as scrunchies or butterfly clips

Remember, the key to the 90's style of dress is to have fun and express your individuality. Don't be afraid to experiment and make the look your own!
