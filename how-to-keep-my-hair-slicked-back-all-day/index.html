How To Keep My Hair Slicked Back All Day A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of constantly having to fix your hair throughout the day? Do you want a sleek and stylish look that lasts from morning to night? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to keep your hair slicked back all day.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Slicked back hair is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can work for both men and women. It involves combing the hair back and smoothing it down with product to create a sleek and polished look. However, keeping this style in place all day can be a challenge, especially if you have fine or curly hair.

In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to keep your hair slicked back all day, no matter your hair type or length.

How To

The key to keeping your hair slicked back all day is to use the right products and techniques. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Begin by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up or residue. This will help the product adhere better to your hair and prevent it from looking greasy or weighed down.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your damp hair. This can be a gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your hair type and desired level of hold. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, using a comb or your fingers to ensure all strands are coated.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back in the direction you want it to lay. Make sure the product is evenly distributed and your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair

Using a blow-dryer, dry your hair while combing it back with your fingers. This will help set the product and keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to lock everything in place. Be sure to hold the can at least 6 inches away from your head to avoid any clumping or buildup.

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed breakdown of the steps outlined above:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to remove any product buildup or residue.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a quarter-sized amount of styling product to your damp hair. Rub the product between your hands to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back in the direction you want it to lay. Make sure the product is evenly distributed and your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair

Using a blow-dryer, dry your hair while combing it back with your fingers. Be sure to hold the dryer at least 6 inches away from your head to avoid overheating your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to lock everything in place. Be sure to hold the can at least 6 inches away from your head to avoid any clumping or buildup.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your hair slicked back all day:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is designed for your hair type
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for you
  • Blow-dry your hair on the cool setting to avoid overheating and damaging your hair
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to ensure all strands are evenly coated with product
  • Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly after washing to remove any product buildup or residue

FAQ

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can still slick back curly hair, but you may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I want to keep it slicked back?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup and maintain a healthy scalp.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

A: Yes, you can still slick back short hair, but you may need to use less product and a lighter hold hairspray to avoid weighing it down.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair slicked back all day requires more than just the right products and techniques. It also requires proper hair care to keep your locks healthy and strong. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep your hair slicked back all day and achieve a sleek and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go!
