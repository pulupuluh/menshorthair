Face Frame Haircut With Long Layers How To Achieve This Look In 2023

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that is stylish, versatile, and easy to maintain, then you might want to consider getting a face frame haircut with long layers. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair without sacrificing length. In this blog post, we'll discuss what a face frame haircut with long layers is, how to achieve this look, and some tips on how to care for your hair afterwards.

Description

A face frame haircut with long layers is a haircut that is designed to frame your face and highlight your facial features. This haircut is achieved by cutting layers into your hair that are longer in the front and shorter in the back. The layers are also cut at an angle, which creates a tapered effect that adds volume and movement to your hair. The face framing layers are cut around your face, which draws attention to your facial features and adds dimension to your haircut.

How to Achieve This Look

If you're interested in getting a face frame haircut with long layers, then here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Find a Good Stylist

The first step to achieving this look is to find a good stylist who has experience cutting face frame haircuts with long layers. Look for a stylist who has a portfolio of their work, and make sure they have good reviews from previous clients.

Step 2: Consultation

Once you've found a good stylist, schedule a consultation with them. During the consultation, you can discuss the look you're going for and ask any questions you might have. Your stylist will also be able to provide recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

Step 3: Cut and Style

After the consultation, your stylist will begin cutting your hair. They will start by cutting the layers into your hair, starting from the front and working their way back. They will also cut the face framing layers around your face. Once the cut is complete, your stylist will style your hair to show off the layers and the face framing.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your hair after getting a face frame haircut with long layers:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the ends of your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face frame haircuts with long layers:

Q: Will this haircut work for my hair type?

A: This haircut can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist will be able to recommend modifications to the cut based on your hair type.

Q: How often should I get this haircut?

A: You should get this haircut every 8-12 weeks to maintain the layers and prevent split ends.

Q: How long does it take to style this haircut?

A: This haircut is easy to style and can be done in 10-15 minutes. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add some waves or straighten your hair for a sleeker look.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after getting a face frame haircut with long layers. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain your face frame haircut with long layers and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
