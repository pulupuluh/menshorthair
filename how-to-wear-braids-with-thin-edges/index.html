How To Wear Braids With Thin Edges Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Are you worried about wearing braids because of thin edges? Don't worry, we got you covered. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to wear braids with thin edges and how to take care of your hair while wearing them.

How to Wear Braids with Thin Edges

Wearing braids with thin edges can be a bit tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you out:

  • Choose the right size and type of braid. Small braids can put a lot of tension on your edges, so it's best to go for larger braids. Also, consider using softer and gentler braiding hair, such as kanekalon or marley hair.
  • Don't braid your edges too tightly. This can cause breakage and damage to your edges. Ask your stylist to be gentle and avoid pulling too hard on your hair.
  • Consider leaving out your edges. Leaving out some of your hair around the hairline can help protect your edges and give you a more natural look.
  • Use edge control or gel. Apply a little bit of edge control or gel to your edges before braiding to help smooth them down and prevent breakage.

Step by Step Process

Here's a step by step process on how to wear braids with thin edges:

  1. Choose the right size and type of braid.
  2. Wash and condition your hair.
  3. Apply edge control or gel to your edges.
  4. Section your hair and start braiding from the back.
  5. Be gentle and avoid pulling too hard on your hair.
  6. Continue braiding until you reach your desired length.
  7. Trim the ends of your braids.
  8. Style your braids as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you wear braids with thin edges:

  • Protect your edges at night by wearing a satin scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, like high ponytails, which can put stress on your edges.
  • Massage your edges regularly to promote hair growth.
  • Take breaks between braiding styles to give your hair a rest.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing braids with thin edges:

Can I wear braids if I have thin edges?

Yes, you can wear braids if you have thin edges. Just be sure to choose the right size and type of braid, and be gentle when braiding your hair.

How can I protect my edges while wearing braids?

You can protect your edges while wearing braids by using edge control or gel, leaving out some of your hair around the hairline, and avoiding tight hairstyles.

How long can I wear braids with thin edges?

You can wear braids with thin edges for 4-6 weeks, but it's best to take a break between braiding styles to give your hair time to rest.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair while wearing braids:

  • Wash your braids regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Moisturize your hair and scalp with a light oil, like jojoba or almond oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to wear braids with thin edges, you can rock this protective style with confidence. Just remember to be gentle with your hair, choose the right size and type of braid, and take care of your hair while wearing braids. Happy braiding!
