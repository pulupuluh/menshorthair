Best Cornrow Styles For Round Faces

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and want to try something new? Cornrows are a great option and can be styled in many different ways. However, if you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the right style that flatters your features. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best cornrow styles for round faces, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.

How to Cornrow

Cornrowing is a popular protective hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It involves braiding the hair close to the scalp in a continuous, intricate pattern. Here are the steps to achieve this style:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into small, even parts using a comb.
  2. Take one section and divide it into three equal strands.
  3. Begin braiding by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the new middle strand.
  4. As you braid, add more hair to each strand from the section next to it.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section, then secure the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.
  6. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are braided.
  7. To finish, you can either leave the ends loose or tuck them under and secure with bobby pins.

Cornrowing can take some practice, so don't worry if your first attempt doesn't turn out perfectly. With time and patience, you'll be able to master this style.

Best Cornrow Styles for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing a cornrow style for a round face, it's all about creating angles and height to elongate the face. Here are some of the best cornrow styles for round faces:

1. Jumbo Cornrows

Jumbo cornrows are perfect for round faces because they create the illusion of length and height. To achieve this style, braid your hair into large, thick cornrows that start at the front of your head and go straight back. You can add extensions or accessories to make the style more unique.

2. Zigzag Cornrows

Zigzag cornrows are another great option for round faces because they create a diagonal line that elongates the face. To achieve this style, braid your hair in a zigzag pattern from the front of your head to the back. You can also add beads or other accessories for a more personalized look.

3. Halo Cornrows

Halo cornrows are a beautiful and elegant style that can be worn by anyone, regardless of face shape. To achieve this style, braid your hair in a circular pattern around your head, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. You can also add extensions or accessories for a more dramatic look.

4. Mohawk Cornrows

Mohawk cornrows are a bold and edgy style that can work well on round faces. To achieve this style, braid your hair in a row of cornrows down the center of your head, leaving the sides loose. You can also add extensions or accessories to make the style more unique.

Tips for Maintaining and Caring for Your Cornrows

Cornrows can last for several weeks with proper maintenance and care. Here are some tips to keep your cornrows looking fresh:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Wash your scalp and hair with a sulfate-free shampoo every two weeks to prevent buildup and maintain scalp health.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.
  • Don't leave your cornrows in for too long, as this can cause tension and damage to your hair and scalp.

FAQ: Cornrows and Round Faces

Q: Can anyone with a round face wear cornrows?

A: Yes! Anyone can wear cornrows, regardless of face shape. The key is to choose a style that flatters your features and elongates your face.

Q: How long do cornrows last?

A: Cornrows can last for several weeks, depending on how well you care for them and how quickly your hair grows. It's important to remove them before they become too tight or begin to cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Q: Can I add extensions to my cornrows?

A: Yes, you can add extensions to your cornrows to make them longer or thicker. Just make sure to choose extensions that are made from high-quality hair and won't cause damage to your natural hair.

In conclusion, cornrows are a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any face shape, including round faces. By choosing the right style and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy this protective style for several weeks and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
