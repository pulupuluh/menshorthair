Do Bangs Make Me Look Older Or Younger

Written by Petter Aug 29, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried about how they will affect your appearance? One of the most common questions people have is, "do bangs make me look older or younger?" The answer is not straightforward and depends on several factors such as face shape, hair texture, and styling preferences. In this article, we will explore this topic in detail and provide you with tips on how to make the most of your bangs.

Description

Bangs or fringe are a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair at the front of the head and letting it fall over the forehead. They can transform your look and add a touch of elegance and sophistication. However, the question of whether bangs make you look older or younger has been a subject of debate for several years. The answer is not black and white, and it depends on several factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and styling preferences.

How to

If you're considering getting bangs, there are a few things you should consider before taking the plunge. Here are some tips on how to get the perfect bangs:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The shape of your face plays a crucial role in determining the type of bangs that will suit you. For instance, if you have a round face, you should opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that create an illusion of length. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you should go for soft, rounded bangs that soften your features.

2. Choose the Right Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is another factor that determines the type of bangs that will suit you. If you have curly or wavy hair, you should avoid heavy bangs that require frequent styling. Instead, opt for light, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly with your natural hair texture.

3. Take Your Lifestyle into Account

Your lifestyle and daily routine should also influence your choice of bangs. If you have a busy schedule and don't have time to style your hair every day, you should opt for low-maintenance bangs that require minimal styling. However, if you have more time on your hands, you can experiment with different types of bangs.

Step by Step

Getting bangs can be a game-changer, but it's essential to get the right cut and style to avoid any regrets. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect bangs:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before getting your bangs, you should browse through magazines, social media, and other sources to find inspiration. Look for pictures of people with similar face shapes and hair textures to get an idea of what will suit you.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Once you've found your inspiration, book an appointment with your stylist and discuss your options. Your stylist will assess your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle to recommend the best type of bangs for you.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, your stylist will cut them to the desired length. Make sure you communicate your preferences clearly to avoid any miscommunication.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Styling your bangs is crucial to achieve the desired look. You can use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume or a straightener for a sleek look. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your bangs:

1. Trim Them Regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and neat, you should trim them every four to six weeks. This will prevent them from getting too long and falling into your eyes.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a significant difference in the appearance of your bangs. Use a volumizing mousse or a heat protectant spray to add volume and protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You can try pinning your bangs to the side or creating a side part for a different look.

FAQ

Q: Do bangs make you look older or younger?

A: It depends on several factors such as face shape, hair texture, and styling preferences. Bangs can make you look younger if you choose the right style that suits your features.

Q: What type of bangs should I get if I have a round face?

A: If you have a round face, you should opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that create an illusion of length.

Q: Can I style my bangs differently every day?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You can pin them to the side or create a side part for a different look.

Hair Care

Getting bangs requires extra care and attention to keep them looking fresh and neat. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your bangs:

1. Wash Them Regularly

You should wash your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming greasy and dirty. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Blow Dry Them

Blow-drying your bangs is essential to achieve the desired look. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume or a straightener for a sleek look.

3. Trim Them Regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and neat, you should trim them every four to six weeks. This will prevent them from getting too long and falling into your eyes.

In conclusion, the question of whether bangs make you look older or younger is subjective and depends on several factors. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose the right type of bangs that suit you. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. With proper care and attention, your bangs can transform your look and add a touch of elegance and sophistication.
