How To Stop Dht Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss or thinning? One of the main culprits could be DHT, a hormone that can cause hair follicles to shrink and eventually stop producing hair. Fortunately, there are natural ways to combat DHT and promote hair growth. In this guide, we’ll walk you through everything you need to know about how to stop DHT naturally.

Table of Contents

  • What is DHT?
  • How DHT Causes Hair Loss
  • Foods That Stop DHT Naturally
  • Supplements That Stop DHT Naturally
  • Essential Oils That Stop DHT Naturally
  • Lifestyle Changes to Stop DHT Naturally
  • Hair Care Tips to Stop DHT Naturally
  • FAQ

What is DHT?

DHT, or dihydrotestosterone, is a hormone that’s derived from testosterone. It’s responsible for the development of male sex characteristics, such as facial hair, deepening of the voice, and muscle mass. However, DHT can also have negative effects, such as hair loss.

How DHT Causes Hair Loss

DHT can bind to hair follicles and cause them to shrink. This can eventually lead to the follicles stopping hair production altogether. The hair that is produced can also become thinner and weaker, which can contribute to hair loss.

Foods That Stop DHT Naturally

There are certain foods that can help block DHT production. These include:

  • Saw palmetto
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Green tea
  • Soy
  • Tomatoes

Supplements That Stop DHT Naturally

In addition to foods, there are also supplements that can help block DHT production. These include:

  • Saw palmetto
  • Pumpkin seed oil
  • Biotin
  • Zinc
  • Vitamin D

Essential Oils That Stop DHT Naturally

Essential oils can also be effective in blocking DHT. Some of the best oils for hair loss include:

  • Lavender oil
  • Rosemary oil
  • Peppermint oil
  • Tea tree oil

Lifestyle Changes to Stop DHT Naturally

There are certain lifestyle changes that can help reduce DHT production. These include:

  • Reducing stress
  • Getting regular exercise
  • Getting enough sleep
  • Avoiding smoking and excessive drinking

Hair Care Tips to Stop DHT Naturally

There are also hair care tips that can help reduce the impact of DHT on your hair. These include:

  • Avoiding tight hairstyles
  • Using a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling
  • Using a hair mask once a week

FAQ

Can DHT be completely eliminated?

No, DHT is a natural hormone that’s important for male development. However, it can be reduced to prevent negative effects like hair loss.

How long does it take to see results from natural DHT blockers?

Results can vary, but it usually takes several weeks to several months before you see a noticeable difference.

Are there any side effects to using natural DHT blockers?

Most natural DHT blockers are safe, but you should always check with your doctor before starting any new supplements or treatments.

Can women use DHT blockers?

Yes, women can also experience hair loss due to DHT and can use natural blockers to combat it.

By following these tips and making simple lifestyle changes, you can help combat DHT and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent, as results may take time to appear. Good luck!
