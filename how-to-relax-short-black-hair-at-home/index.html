How To Relax Short Black Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to relax your short black hair? Relaxing your hair at home can be a cost-effective and convenient option. In this guide, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to relax short black hair at home. We will also give tips on how to care for your relaxed hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Relax Short Black Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Relaxing Short Black Hair at Home
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

How to Relax Short Black Hair at Home

Before we get started, it is important to have all the necessary tools and products. You will need:

  • Relaxer kit (make sure to choose a kit that is specifically for short hair)
  • Protective gloves
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Plastic cap
  • Neutralizing shampoo
  • Deep conditioner
  • Towel

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by reading the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully. Follow the instructions on how to mix the relaxer, and put on the protective gloves.

2. Use the wide-tooth comb to divide your hair into small sections. Apply the relaxer to each section, starting from the back of your head and working your way to the front.

3. Once you have applied the relaxer to all sections of your hair, use the wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

4. Put on the plastic cap and leave the relaxer on your hair for the amount of time specified in the instructions. Make sure to set a timer to ensure you do not leave the relaxer on for too long.

5. After the specified amount of time has passed, rinse out the relaxer with warm water. Make sure to rinse out all of the relaxer.

6. Apply the neutralizing shampoo to your hair and massage it into your scalp. Rinse out the shampoo thoroughly.

7. Apply the deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for the amount of time specified in the instructions. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water.

8. Pat your hair dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause breakage.

Tips for Relaxing Short Black Hair at Home

- Always follow the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully.

- Use a relaxer kit that is specifically for short hair.

- Protect your scalp by applying petroleum jelly or a protective cream around your hairline and ears.

- Avoid using a relaxer kit if your hair is damaged or if you have a sensitive scalp.

- Do not relax your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I relax my hair?

You should wait at least six to eight weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I relax my hair if it is already damaged?

No, it is not recommended to relax damaged hair. Instead, focus on repairing your hair with deep conditioning treatments and avoiding heat styling.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

- Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.

Relaxing short black hair at home can be a cost-effective and convenient option. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a sleek and smooth look. Remember to take care of your relaxed hair by deep conditioning regularly and avoiding heat styling tools too often.
