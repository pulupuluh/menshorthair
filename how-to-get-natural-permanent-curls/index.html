How To Get Natural Permanent Curls A Complete Guide

Are you tired of using heat tools and chemicals to achieve curls that don't last? Do you want to embrace your natural texture and have permanent curls? Well, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to get natural permanent curls without damaging your hair. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Natural Permanent Curls
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Natural Permanent Curls

How to Get Natural Permanent Curls

The first step to achieving natural permanent curls is to understand your hair type. Curls are determined by the shape of your hair follicles, which can range from straight to wavy to curly. If you have naturally straight hair, don't worry! You can still get permanent curls.

There are two main methods for getting natural permanent curls:

Method 1: Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the shape of your hair follicles to create curls. This method is best for those with straight or slightly wavy hair. It's important to note that perming can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly, so it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Method 2: Natural Hair Care

If you don't want to use chemicals, you can embrace your natural texture and encourage curls with natural hair care. This method is best for those with wavy or curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid using heat tools and chemical treatments that can damage your hair
  • Use hair products that enhance your natural curl pattern, such as curl creams or gels
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to go the natural hair care route, here's a step-by-step guide for getting natural permanent curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to damp hair
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a small bun
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat
  5. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the buns and shake out your curls
  6. Apply a lightweight oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz

Tips for Maintaining Natural Permanent Curls

Now that you have natural permanent curls, it's important to take care of them so they last. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to prevent heat damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind with a hat or scarf

FAQs

Can I get natural permanent curls if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get natural permanent curls with a perm. However, it's important to go to a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

How long do natural permanent curls last?

With proper care, natural permanent curls can last for several months to a year.

What products should I use to enhance my natural curl pattern?

You can use curl creams, gels, or mousses that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that contain natural ingredients and avoid products with sulfates or silicones.

Hair Care for Natural Permanent Curls

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your natural permanent curls:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid using heat tools and chemical treatments that can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater with a swim cap or leave-in conditioner

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve natural permanent curls that are healthy and long-lasting. Embrace your natural texture and rock those curls!
