How To Tie A Handkerchief As A Headband

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring headbands? Try a handkerchief instead! Not only is it a cute accessory, but it's also practical for keeping your hair out of your face. In this article, we'll teach you how to tie a handkerchief as a headband in just a few easy steps.

How to

First, choose a handkerchief that you love. It can be any size or pattern, as long as it's big enough to tie around your head.

Step 1: Fold the handkerchief in half to form a triangle.

Step 2: Place the folded edge of the triangle at the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Bring the two corners of the triangle up to the top of your head.

Step 4: Tie the two corners together into a knot at the top of your head.

Step 5: Tuck any loose edges or corners into the knot to create a clean and polished look.

And voila! You now have a stylish and functional handkerchief headband.

Tips

- Experiment with different folding techniques to create different looks.

- Use bobby pins to secure any loose edges or corners.

- Choose a handkerchief that matches your outfit or adds a pop of color.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a handkerchief headband with any hairstyle?

A: Absolutely! It works great with straight, curly, or wavy hair.

Q: Can I wear a handkerchief headband to a formal event?

A: It depends on the handkerchief and the event. A more formal handkerchief (think silk or lace) could work, but it's not typically considered formal attire.

Hair Care

When tying a handkerchief as a headband, it's important to make sure your hair is clean and free of tangles. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any knots before tying the headband.

Additionally, avoid tying the headband too tightly, as this can cause headaches and damage to your hair. Instead, opt for a looser fit that's comfortable and stylish.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock a handkerchief headband with any outfit. Happy styling!
