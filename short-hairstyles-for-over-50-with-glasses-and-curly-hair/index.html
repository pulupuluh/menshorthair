Short Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses And Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Are you over 50 and struggling to find the perfect short hairstyle that suits your curly hair and glasses? Look no further! In this guide, we will be sharing some of the best short hairstyles for over 50 with glasses and curly hair, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the perfect short hairstyle for over 50 with glasses and curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a short hairstyle. For example, if you have a round face, go for a short hairstyle that adds height to the crown and lengthens the face. If you have a square face, opt for a short hairstyle that softens the angles of your face.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture is also an important factor to consider. If you have thin hair, go for a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume at the crown. If you have thick hair, consider a short shaggy cut or a curly bob.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in choosing the right short hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, go for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can opt for a short hairstyle that requires more styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your curly hair and glasses, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and keep your hair healthy.

2. Apply Styling Products

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

3. Blow-Dry

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

4. Style

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any curls that need a little extra help. You can also use a straightener to create loose waves.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hair:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it will be gentler on your curls and prevent breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my short curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your short curly hair. However, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as curly hair is more prone to dryness and damage.

2. How often should I shampoo my short curly hair?

It's recommended to shampoo your short curly hair once or twice a week, as curly hair is more prone to dryness and over-shampooing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your short curly hair:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage, as it's gentler on your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

With these tips and short hairstyles for over 50 with glasses and curly hair, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance look that suits your unique style and personality. Remember to always choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
