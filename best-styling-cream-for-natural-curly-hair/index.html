Best Styling Cream for Natural Curly Hair - 2023 Guide

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It looks amazing when it's well-styled, but it can also be difficult to manage. That's where styling creams come in. They can help define your curls, keep them moisturized, and prevent frizz. If you're looking for the best styling cream for natural curly hair, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best options on the market in 2023.

Description

If you have natural curly hair, you know that finding the right styling products is crucial. The wrong product can leave your curls looking dull, frizzy, or even greasy. That's why it's important to choose a styling cream that's specifically designed for curly hair. These creams are formulated to enhance your curls, control frizz, and provide moisture without weighing your hair down.

How to Choose the Best Styling Cream for Natural Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a styling cream for natural curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

1. Look for Moisturizing Ingredients

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to choose a styling cream that contains moisturizing ingredients. Look for products that contain shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These ingredients will help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Not all styling creams are created equal. Some are better suited for fine hair, while others work best on thick, coarse hair. Consider your hair type when choosing a styling cream. If you have fine hair, look for a lightweight cream that won't weigh your curls down. If you have thick hair, opt for a cream with more hold.

3. Check the Ingredients List

Make sure to check the ingredients list before purchasing a styling cream. Avoid products that contain alcohol, sulfates, or silicones. These ingredients can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage over time.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Styling Cream on Natural Curly Hair

Now that you know how to choose the best styling cream for natural curly hair, it's time to learn how to use it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Wet Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

2. Apply the Styling Cream

Squeeze a quarter-sized amount of styling cream into the palm of your hand. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Then, apply the cream to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

3. Define Your Curls

Use your fingers to define your curls. Twirl individual curls around your finger to enhance their shape. If you have frizz-prone areas, smooth them down with your hands.

4. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Allow your hair to air dry. Don't use a blow dryer, as this can cause frizz. Once your hair is dry, you can fluff your curls with your fingers to add volume.

Tips for Styling Natural Curly Hair

Styling natural curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your curls:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Curly hair doesn't need to be washed every day. In fact, overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb. This will help prevent breakage and damage to your curls.

3. Sleep with a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage. Invest in a silk pillowcase to help protect your curls while you sleep.

4. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your curls healthy and moisturized. Aim to deep condition your hair once a week, or as needed.

FAQ

What is the Best Styling Cream for Natural Curly Hair?

There are many great styling creams on the market for natural curly hair. Some of the best options include DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler, Ouidad Curl Quencher Moisturizing Styling Gel, and Moroccanoil Curl Defining Cream.

How Often Should I Use Styling Cream on My Natural Curly Hair?

It's best to use styling cream on your natural curly hair every time you wash it. This will help define your curls and control frizz.

Can I Use Styling Cream on Dry Hair?

While it's best to use styling cream on damp hair, you can also use it on dry hair to refresh your curls. Simply apply a small amount of cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Hair Care for Natural Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips to help you care for your natural curly hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that's gentle and sulfate-free. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage over time.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your curls moisturized and healthy. Look for a conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your curls. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your curls from the sun.

5. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks.
