How To Curl Hair On The Right Side A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of always having your hair styled the same way? Do you want to add some curls to your look but don't know where to start? Look no further! This guide will teach you how to curl your hair on the right side, from start to finish.

Description

Curling your hair can be a fun and easy way to switch up your style. Whether you have straight or wavy hair, adding some curls can add volume and texture to your look. In this guide, we will focus on how to curl your hair on the right side, using a curling iron or wand.

How to Curl Hair on the Right Side

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from damage. Divide your hair into sections, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Clip each section up using hair clips.

Step 2: Choose your curling tool

There are many different types of curling irons and wands to choose from. Consider the size of the barrel and the type of curl you want to achieve. For tighter curls, choose a smaller barrel size. For looser waves, choose a larger barrel size.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of your curling iron or wand. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way from the back to the front.

Step 4: Repeat on the other side

Once you have finished curling the right side of your hair, repeat the same process on the left side.

Tips for Curling Hair on the Right Side

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a lower heat setting to avoid damaging your hair
  • Use a comb or brush to separate the curls and create a more natural look
  • Use a hairspray or other styling product to help hold the curls in place
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes and curling techniques to find the look that works for you

FAQ

How long will the curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type, the products you use, and the curling technique you use. On average, curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I curl my hair if it is already curly?

Yes, you can curl your hair even if it is already curly. However, keep in mind that adding more curls may make your hair look too voluminous.

How often can I curl my hair?

It is best to avoid curling your hair every day, as this can cause damage to your hair. Limit curling to once or twice a week, and use a heat protectant spray or cream each time.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a deep conditioner regularly and avoid excessive heat styling. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when brushing.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls on the right side of your hair. Happy styling!
