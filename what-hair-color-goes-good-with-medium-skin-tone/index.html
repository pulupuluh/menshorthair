What Hair Color Goes Good With Medium Skin Tone

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be a daunting task, especially if you have a medium skin tone. The key to finding the perfect hair color for your skin tone is to choose a shade that complements your natural complexion. In this article, we will discuss what hair color goes good with medium skin tone and provide you with some tips on how to choose the perfect hair color.

How to

When it comes to choosing a hair color for medium skin tone, there are a few factors that you need to consider:

  • The undertones of your skin
  • Your natural hair color
  • Your personal style

It is important to choose a hair color that complements the undertones of your skin. If you have warm undertones, choose a hair color that has warm tones like golden blonde or caramel. If you have cool undertones, choose a hair color that has cool tones like ash blonde or platinum.

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the perfect hair color for your skin tone. If you have dark hair, choosing a hair color that is too light can look unnatural. On the other hand, if you have light hair, choosing a hair color that is too dark can also look unnatural.

Your personal style is another important factor to consider. If you are someone who likes to stand out, you might want to choose a bold hair color like red or purple. If you prefer a more natural look, you might want to choose a hair color that is closer to your natural hair color.

Step by step

Here are some steps that you can follow to choose the perfect hair color for your medium skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin undertones
  2. Consider your natural hair color
  3. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see how the hair color looks on you
  5. Consult a professional hair colorist for advice

Tips

  • Choose a hair color that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color
  • Avoid hair colors that are too brassy or orange-toned
  • Avoid hair colors that are too light or too dark for your skin tone
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair for a more natural look
  • Take care of your hair by using products that are specifically designed for colored hair

FAQ

Q: What hair color goes good with medium skin tone?

A: Hair colors that have warm tones like golden blonde or caramel complement medium skin tones with warm undertones. Hair colors that have cool tones like ash blonde or platinum complement medium skin tones with cool undertones.

Q: Can I dye my hair a lighter color if I have dark hair?

A: It is possible to dye your hair a lighter color if you have dark hair, but it is important to do it gradually to avoid damaging your hair. It is best to consult a professional hair colorist for advice.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its color and health. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Now that you know what hair color goes good with medium skin tone, it's time to choose the perfect hair color for you. Remember to consider your skin undertones, natural hair color, and personal style when choosing a hair color. Consult a professional hair colorist for advice and take care of your hair to maintain its color and health. Happy coloring!
