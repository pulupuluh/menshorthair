Women s Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the women's haircut short sides long top! This versatile haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. With short sides and a longer top, this style is perfect for those who want to experiment with different textures and lengths. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the women's haircut short sides long top, from how to get it to how to care for it.

Description

The women's haircut short sides long top is a daring and trendy hairstyle that features shaved or closely-cropped sides and a longer, often textured top. This style is often worn as an undercut, where the hair on the top is left longer and the sides are buzzed or shaved. The haircut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, and it's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance but stylish look.

How To

Getting a women's haircut short sides long top is relatively easy, but it's important to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Here are the steps involved:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in getting a women's haircut short sides long top is to decide how short you want the sides to be. You can opt for a buzz cut or a close crop, or you can leave a bit of length for a softer look. The length of the top can also be customized to suit your preferences.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once you've decided on your length, your stylist will section your hair and clip the top portion out of the way. They'll then use clippers or scissors to cut the sides to the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

With the sides done, your stylist will move on to the top portion of your hair. They'll use scissors or a razor to texturize and layer the hair, creating a longer, more voluminous look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, you can style your hair in a variety of ways. You can leave it down for a messy, tousled look, or you can slick it back for a more polished style. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a women's haircut short sides long top:

  • Choose a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing your length.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find a look that works for you.
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance to keep your sides looking sharp.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the women's haircut short sides long top:

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the women's haircut short sides long top can be customized to suit any hair type. Your stylist will be able to advise you on the best length and texture for your hair.

Do I need to have a certain face shape to pull off this style?

No, the women's haircut short sides long top can be adapted to suit any face shape. Talk to your stylist about how to customize the cut to flatter your features.

Is this haircut high maintenance?

The women's haircut short sides long top does require regular maintenance to keep the sides looking sharp. You'll need to visit your stylist every few weeks for touch-ups.

Hair Care

To keep your women's haircut short sides long top looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the women's haircut short sides long top with confidence and style!
