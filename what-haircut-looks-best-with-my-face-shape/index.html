What Haircut Looks Best With My Face Shape

Written by Christine May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you're unsure of what style will suit your face shape. The good news is that there is a haircut that will flatter any face shape, and it's all about finding the right one for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

  • Hair Care for Your New Haircut

Description

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own set of characteristics, and certain haircuts will flatter them better than others.

For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as bangs or a blunt bob. Instead, opt for a longer, layered haircut that will elongate your face. If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a haircut that has soft layers or waves.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape

Now that you know what your face shape is, it's time to choose the right haircut. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Round face: opt for longer, layered haircuts that elongate your face.
  • Square face: soften your features with a haircut that has soft layers or waves.
  • Oval face: lucky you! You can pretty much pull off any haircut.
  • Heart face: balance out your features with a haircut that has layers around your chin.
  • Diamond face: soften your angular features with a haircut that has layers and volume.
  • Oblong face: add width to your face with a haircut that has fullness at the sides.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing the right haircut for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research haircuts that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will suit your lifestyle and hair type.
  4. Show your hairstylist pictures of haircuts you like.
  5. Ask your hairstylist for his or her opinion on what will suit you best.
  6. Consider your face shape when choosing a hair color as well.
  7. Try out different hairstyles before committing to a new haircut.
  8. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling.
  9. Experiment with different styling products to achieve the look you want.
  10. Enjoy your new haircut!

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use styling products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Invest in good quality hair tools, such as a high-quality hair dryer or flat iron.
  • Take care of your hair by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioning regularly.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure that the haircut has layers and volume to balance out your features.

Q: What haircut should I avoid if I have a square face?

A: Avoid haircuts that are too blunt or too short, as they can accentuate your jawline.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure that the bangs are longer and wispy, rather than blunt.

Hair Care for Your New Haircut

Now that you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for your face shape can be a fun and exciting process. By following these guidelines and consulting with your hairstylist, you can find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and good hair care practices to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
