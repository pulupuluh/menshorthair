How To Know If Your Face Suits Short Hair

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and looking to switch up your hairstyle? Short hair can be a great option, but it's important to consider if it will suit your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss how to know if your face suits short hair and provide some tips for choosing the perfect cut.

How to Know if Your Face Suits Short Hair

Short hair can look great on anyone, but certain face shapes may be better suited for this style than others. Here are a few tips to help you determine if short hair is right for you:

Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hairstyle is your face shape. Some face shapes, such as oval, heart, and square, tend to look better with short haircuts. Others, such as round and diamond, may not be as well-suited for this style.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. Short haircuts can work well for those with straight or wavy hair, but may be more difficult to style for those with curly or thick hair. If you have naturally curly or thick hair, you may want to consider getting layers or a longer bob instead of a short pixie cut.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If you're someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, then a short haircut may be a good option for you. However, if you prefer low-maintenance styles, then a longer cut may be a better choice.

Step by Step Guide to Determine if Short Hair is Right for You

If you're still unsure if short hair is the right choice for you, follow these steps to help you make a decision:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a close look at your face and determine what shape it is. Is it oval, heart-shaped, square, round, or diamond-shaped?

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair texture. Is it straight, wavy, curly, or thick?

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle. Do you prefer low-maintenance styles, or do you enjoy spending time styling your hair?

Step 4: Look at Pictures

Look at pictures of short haircuts that you like and see if they would work well with your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Short Haircut

Once you've determined that short hair is right for you, here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect cut:

Consider Your Face Shape

Choose a haircut that complements your face shape. For example, if you have an oval face, a pixie cut or bob would look great. If you have a heart-shaped face, a short shag or layered cut would be a good choice.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Choose a haircut that works well with your hair texture. If you have straight or wavy hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut would be a good choice. If you have curly hair, a layered cut or longer bob would work better.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle. If you prefer low-maintenance styles, a longer bob or layered cut would be a good choice. If you enjoy spending time styling your hair, a shorter pixie cut would be a great option.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the cut. If you have a round face, a blunt bob or a pixie cut with longer layers on top can help elongate your face and make it appear less round.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! You can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a half-up half-down style or a small bun at the nape of your neck.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different type of care than longer locks. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. Look for styling products such as gels and waxes that can help you create different looks.

Experiment with Styling

Short hair is very versatile! Experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.

Protect Your Hair

Use heat protectant products when styling with hot tools, and try to avoid using them too often.

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can determine if short hair is right for you. With the right cut and care, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance look!
