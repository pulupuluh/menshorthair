How To Cut Hair To Feather On Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your dull, lifeless hair? Feathering your hair on the sides is an easy way to add movement and texture to your locks. In this article, we will show you how to cut hair to feather on sides in a relaxed English language. Read on for our step-by-step guide!

How to Cut Hair to Feather on Sides

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a sharp pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into two sections, using a comb to create a clean part down the middle of your head. Clip one section out of the way so you can work on the other side first.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to make small, diagonal cuts into the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up towards the scalp. This will create a feathered effect.

Repeat this process with small sections of hair, making sure to blend the cuts together for a seamless look.

Step 4: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you have finished one side, unclip the other section of hair and repeat the process on the other side of your head. Make sure to keep both sides even for a balanced look.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

After you have feathered both sides of your hair, use your scissors to blend the layers together for a more natural look. Take small sections of hair and cut into the ends to create a subtle graduation.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a straightener, curling iron, or simply leave it natural. Your feathered layers will add movement and texture to any hairstyle.

Tips for Cutting Hair to Feather on Sides

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair to feather on sides:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections to ensure even layers.
  • Make sure to cut at a slight angle for a feathered effect.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any unevenness.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair to maintain a feathered look?

It really depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. As a general rule, aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your feathered look.

Can I feather my hair if it's very short?

Feathering works best on hair that is at least shoulder length. If your hair is very short, you may not be able to achieve a feathered look.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, don't forget to take care of your feathered locks! Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and try to let it air dry whenever possible.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your hair to feather on sides like a pro. Have fun experimenting with different styles and enjoy your new, textured locks!
