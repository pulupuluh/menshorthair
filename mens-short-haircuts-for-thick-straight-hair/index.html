Mens Short Haircuts For Thick Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Jun 06, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a man with thick straight hair looking for a new short haircut? Look no further! In this guide, we will discuss the best mens short haircuts for thick straight hair in 2023. From the classic crew cut to the trendy textured crop, we’ve got you covered. Here’s what we’ll cover:

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thick Straight Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair for Men with Thick Straight Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Men with Thick Straight Hair
  5. FAQs about Short Haircuts for Men with Thick Straight Hair
  6. Hair Care Tips for Men with Thick Straight Hair

Description

Thick straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s easy to style and looks great with minimal effort. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and can easily become unruly if not properly cared for.

Short haircuts are a great option for men with thick straight hair. Not only do they require less maintenance than longer styles, but they also help to control thickness and add structure to your face. Plus, short haircuts are always in style and can be customized to fit your individual preferences.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thick Straight Hair

Before choosing a short haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Men with round faces should opt for styles that add height and volume, while men with square faces should choose styles that soften their features. If you have thick straight hair, you’ll want to choose a cut that adds texture and dimension.

Some popular short haircuts for men with thick straight hair include:

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short haircut that is perfect for men with thick straight hair. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. The crew cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a great choice for any occasion.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy short haircut that has been popular in recent years. This style is characterized by short, choppy layers and a textured, messy finish. The textured crop is perfect for men with thick straight hair, as it adds movement and dimension to your locks.

Short Quiff

The short quiff is a stylish and modern short haircut that is perfect for men with thick straight hair. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top styled into a quiff. The short quiff adds height and volume to your hair, making it a great choice for men with round faces.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair for Men with Thick Straight Hair

If you’re planning on cutting your hair at home, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect short haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it’s slightly damp.
  2. Using a comb, divide your hair into sections.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, use clippers to trim the hair on the back and sides of your head. Use a guard that is shorter on the sides than on top.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top, using scissors or clippers with a longer guard.
  5. Using scissors, texturize the hair on top by cutting into it at a diagonal angle.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred product.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Men with Thick Straight Hair

Short haircuts for men with thick straight hair require minimal maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Style your hair with a matte finish product for a natural look.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.

FAQs about Short Haircuts for Men with Thick Straight Hair

What is the best short haircut for men with thick straight hair?

The best short haircut for men with thick straight hair depends on your face shape and personal preferences. Some popular options include the crew cut, textured crop, and short quiff.

How often do I need to get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Thick Straight Hair

To keep your thick straight hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a lightweight conditioner to your hair after shampooing to keep it hydrated and soft.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect mens short haircut for your thick straight hair in 2023. Whether you prefer a classic crew cut or a trendy textured crop, there’s a style out there that will suit your individual style and preferences. Happy styling!
