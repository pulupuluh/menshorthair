Best Hair Color For 30S A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Oliver Mar 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're in your 30s and looking to switch up your hair color, you're not alone. Many women in their 30s are looking for a change, whether it's to cover up grays or simply to try something new. But with so many hair colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this guide, we'll walk you through the best hair colors for 30s and give you tips on how to find the perfect shade for you.

Description: What Are the Best Hair Colors for 30s?

As you enter your 30s, you'll want to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some of the best hair colors for 30s:

  • Warm browns
  • Dark blondes
  • Caramel highlights
  • Copper reds

These hair colors are flattering on a variety of skin tones and can help brighten up your overall look. However, it's important to note that everyone's hair is different, so what looks good on one person may not look good on another.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color for 30s

If you're not sure where to start when it comes to choosing a hair color, here are some tips to help you find the perfect shade:

  1. Consider your skin tone. Are you warm-toned or cool-toned? If you're not sure, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you're cool-toned. If they appear green, you're warm-toned.
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Are you low-maintenance or do you enjoy spending time on your hair? If you're low-maintenance, you may want to choose a hair color that requires minimal upkeep.
  3. Look at your wardrobe. What colors do you wear most often? You'll want to choose a hair color that complements your clothing.
  4. Consider your natural hair color. If you're looking for a subtle change, you may want to choose a hair color that's similar to your natural shade.

By taking these factors into consideration, you'll be able to narrow down your options and find a hair color that works for you.

Step by Step: How to Color Your Hair at Home

If you decide to color your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Choose a hair color that's right for you.
  2. Read the instructions carefully before beginning.
  3. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the dye.
  4. Apply the dye to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  7. Apply the conditioner that comes with the dye to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

It's important to do a patch test before coloring your hair to make sure you're not allergic to the dye. You should also do a strand test to make sure you like the color before applying it to your entire head.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've colored your hair, you'll want to take steps to maintain your new shade. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can help your hair color last longer and stay vibrant.

FAQs

What is the most flattering hair color?

The most flattering hair color depends on a variety of factors, including your skin tone and personal style. However, warm browns, dark blondes, caramel highlights, and copper reds are generally flattering on most people.

Can I color my hair if I have grays?

Absolutely! Many women in their 30s start to see grays and choose to color their hair to cover them up. Just make sure to choose a hair color that covers grays and follow the instructions carefully.

How often should I color my hair?

How often you color your hair depends on the color and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks and recolor your entire head every 8-10 weeks.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips, you can help your colored hair stay healthy and vibrant for longer.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair color for your 30s can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, lifestyle, and natural hair color, you can find a shade that complements your overall look. Whether you decide to color your hair at home or visit a salon, make sure to take steps to maintain your new shade and keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
