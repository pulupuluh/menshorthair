Which Hairstyle Suits My Face Online The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Apr 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon and not knowing which hairstyle suits your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to determine which hairstyle suits your face shape online. We’ll also give you tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle and answer some frequently asked questions. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Which Hairstyle Suits Your Face Shape Online
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Which Hairstyle Suits Your Face Shape Online

Knowing your face shape is key to finding the perfect hairstyle. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart

To determine your face shape online, take a selfie and upload it to a face shape analyzer tool. These tools use facial recognition technology to determine your face shape. Some popular face shape analyzer tools include:

  • SheKnows Face Shape Quiz
  • InStyle Virtual Makeover Tool
  • Total Beauty Face Shape Quiz

Once you determine your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it’s time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles that complement your face shape. Look for inspiration online or in magazines.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional can provide you with personalized recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.
  3. Consider your lifestyle. Are you looking for a low-maintenance style or do you have the time and resources to maintain a more complex style?
  4. Take into account your hair texture. Some styles may work better with straight hair, while others may require curly or wavy hair.
  5. Think about your personal style. Do you prefer a classic, edgy, or bohemian look?

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use the right hair products for your hair type and style.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don’t like my new hairstyle?

If you’re not happy with your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different style that complements your face shape.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it doesn’t complement my face shape?

Of course! At the end of the day, you should wear whatever hairstyle makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh shampoos.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tools, it’s easier than ever to find a style that complements your features. Remember to take care of your hair and consult with a professional if you need help. Happy hair styling!
