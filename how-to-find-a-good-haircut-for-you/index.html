How To Find A Good Haircut For You Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Getting a good haircut is a great way to transform your look and feel confident. However, finding the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you’re unsure about what suits you best. In this guide, we’ll share tips on how to find a good haircut for you, from determining your face shape to choosing the right salon.

  • Research Haircuts

Determine Your Face Shape

One of the first things to consider when choosing a haircut is your face shape. Your face shape can determine which haircuts will flatter you the most. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, tie your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following:

  • Round: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in width
  • Oval: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face is longer than it is wide
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in width, and the face is angular
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face tapers to a point at the chin
  • Diamond: cheekbones are wider than the forehead and jawline, and the face is angular
  • Oblong: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in width, and the face is longer than it is wide

Knowing your face shape can help you choose haircuts that will enhance your best features and balance your face shape.

Research Haircuts

Once you know your face shape, it’s time to research haircuts that will suit you. Look for inspiration online, in magazines, or on social media. Consider the length, layers, and texture of the haircuts you like. Keep in mind that not all haircuts will work for every face shape, so choose haircuts that flatter your face shape.

Choose a Salon

Choosing the right salon is crucial for getting a good haircut. Look for salons that specialize in the type of haircut you want. Read online reviews from previous customers to make sure the salon has a good reputation. Consider the salon’s location, hours, and pricing. Don’t be afraid to call and ask questions before booking an appointment.

Bring Inspiration

When you go to the salon, bring pictures of the haircuts you like. This will help your stylist understand what you’re looking for and provide suggestions based on your face shape and hair type. Be open to your stylist’s suggestions, but don’t be afraid to speak up if you don’t like something.

Talk to Your Stylist

Communication is key when getting a haircut. Talk to your stylist about your hair type, lifestyle, and maintenance routine. Be honest about your hair goals and any concerns you have. Your stylist can provide valuable advice on how to care for your hair and maintain your haircut.

Try the Haircut

Before committing to a haircut, ask your stylist to show you the cut in different styles. This will give you an idea of how the haircut will look when styled differently. Don’t be afraid to ask for tweaks or adjustments to the cut if it’s not exactly what you’re looking for.

Maintain Your Haircut

Maintaining your haircut is essential for keeping it looking good. Follow your stylist’s advice on styling and hair care. Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Q: How can I make my haircut last longer?

A: Use high-quality hair products, avoid heat styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking good:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips, you can find a good haircut that works for you and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
