How To Make Hair Look Short Like A Boy

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle? Want to try something different and edgy? Making your hair look short like a boy is a great way to switch things up. In this article, we’ll show you how to achieve this look in easy steps. From choosing the right haircut to styling, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description

The short boyish hairstyle has been popular for years. It’s a low-maintenance haircut that’s perfect for those who want to look stylish without spending too much time on their hair. This hairstyle can work for any hair type or texture, and it’s suitable for both men and women. The key to making your hair look short like a boy is to choose the right cut and style it properly.

How to

Follow these easy steps to make your hair look short like a boy:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving the short boyish look is to choose the right haircut. You can go for a classic buzz cut, a textured crop, or a short bob. The key is to keep it short and low-maintenance. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash it first. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair. Avoid using hot tools or blow-drying as this can damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair. You can use a styling cream, gel, or wax. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers, style your hair in the direction you want it to go. You can use a comb or brush to help shape your hair. Keep in mind that the short boyish look is all about texture and volume. Don’t be afraid to play around with your hair to achieve the desired look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’re happy with your hairstyle, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and add shine to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair look short like a boy:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the best one for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to try different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Can I make my hair look short like a boy if I have long hair?

Yes, you can. However, you’ll need to get a shorter haircut first before styling your hair to make it look shorter.

Will the short boyish look suit my face shape?

This hairstyle can work for any face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that will complement your features.

Do I need to use hot tools to style my hair?

No, you don’t. You can achieve the short boyish look without using hot tools.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

With these tips and steps, you can easily make your hair look short like a boy. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to find the best one for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Try out this edgy and stylish look today and rock it with confidence!
