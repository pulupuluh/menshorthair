What Hairstyle Looks Good On A Round Face

Written by Daniel Sep 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Round faces have a certain charm to them, but finding the right hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. You don't want to make your face look even rounder than it already is, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for round faces, along with tips on how to style them and keep your hair healthy.

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face
  3. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair with a Round Face
  4. Frequently Asked Questions About Hairstyles for Round Faces
  5. Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. This means avoiding hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length.

Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add length and movement to your hair without adding volume to the sides of your face. This style works well for both straight and wavy hair, and can be styled with a center or side part.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces. They help to create angles and draw attention to your eyes, while also adding a touch of softness to your face. Make sure to keep your bangs long enough to sweep to the side, rather than straight across your forehead.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice, but it can also be incredibly flattering for round faces. The key is to keep the cut short on the sides and back, while leaving a bit more length on top. This creates the illusion of height and angles, while still maintaining a soft and feminine look.

4. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple and easy hairstyle that can work wonders for a round face. By pulling your hair up and away from your face, you create the illusion of length and height. Make sure to tease your hair at the crown to add even more height.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for round faces, it's time to learn how to style them. Here's a step by step guide:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect it from heat damage.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go. This will help to create volume and movement.

3. Style Your Hair

Depending on the hairstyle you've chosen, you may need to style your hair further. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, or a curling iron to add loose waves. Finish with a bit of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair with a Round Face

Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle. Here are some tips to help keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

2. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining healthy hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair a boost of hydration. This will help to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hairstyles for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part with a round face. Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid short hairstyles if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Short hairstyles can be flattering for round faces, as long as they create the illusion of length and angles.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and damaged. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or even every few days, to keep it healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, just like it can be damaging to your skin. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, there are plenty of hairstyles that look great on round faces. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and taking proper care of your hair, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that flatters your features.
