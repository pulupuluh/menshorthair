How To Curl Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're rocking a trendy curtain bangs hairstyle, you may be wondering how to style them in different ways. One popular look is curly curtain bangs, which can add some volume and texture to your hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to curl curtain bangs and give you some tips to make the process easier. Let's get started!

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that features long, wispy bangs that frame the face. These bangs are often styled with a center part, creating a "curtain" effect. To add some volume and texture to your curtain bangs, you can curl them using a curling iron or hot rollers. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a fun and flirty look that complements your face shape.

How to Curl Curtain Bangs

Here's a step-by-step guide to curling your curtain bangs:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron or hot rollers. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some extra volume to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Separate your curtain bangs from the rest of your hair. Use a clip to keep the other sections of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Choose Your Tool

You can curl your curtain bangs using a curling iron or hot rollers. Both tools can give you a similar result, so choose the one you're most comfortable using.

Step 4: Curl Your Bangs

Take a small section of your curtain bangs and wrap it around the barrel of your curling iron or hot roller. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your curtain bangs are curled.

Step 5: Comb Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your curtain bangs, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will help to separate the curls and create a more natural look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To help your curls last all day, spritz your curtain bangs with a light hold hairspray. This will help to set the curls and prevent them from falling flat.

Tips for Curling Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when curling your curtain bangs:

  • Choose a curling iron or hot roller with a small barrel size to create tighter curls.
  • Curl your bangs away from your face for a more flattering look.
  • If you're using a curling iron, wrap your bangs around the barrel starting from the middle to create a more natural-looking curl.
  • Be careful not to over-curl your bangs, as this can make them look too voluminous and unnatural.
  • If you have thick hair, you may need to use a higher heat setting on your curling iron or leave the hot rollers in for a longer period of time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling curtain bangs:

Can I curl my curtain bangs with a straightening iron?

While it's possible to curl your curtain bangs with a straightening iron, it can be more difficult to achieve the desired look. It's best to use a curling iron or hot rollers to get the best results.

How long will my curled curtain bangs last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, you can expect your curls to last for 4-6 hours, but you can extend the life of your curls by using a strong hold hairspray.

Can I curl my curtain bangs if they're wet?

No, it's not recommended to curl your curtain bangs when they're wet. Wet hair is more prone to damage from heat styling tools, so make sure your hair is completely dry before curling.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, make sure to take good care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to curl your curtain bangs, it's time to give it a try! Experiment with different curling techniques and products to find the look that works best for you.
