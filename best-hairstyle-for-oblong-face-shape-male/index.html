Best Hairstyle For Oblong Face Shape Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oblong or rectangular face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a straight hairline and jawline. However, with the right cut and style, you can enhance your facial features and look your best. In this guide, we'll explore the best hairstyle for oblong face shape male, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape Male
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyle for oblong face shape male, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a measuring tape and measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

For an oblong face shape, the measurements will be relatively similar, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being almost the same width.

Best Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape Male

The best hairstyle for oblong face shape male is one that adds width and volume to the sides while keeping the top flat. Here are some popular hairstyles to consider:

1. The Side Parting

The side parting is a classic and versatile hairstyle that suits most face shapes, including oblong. The side parting adds volume to the sides, making the face appear wider, and the top is kept flat. This look is achieved by keeping the hair short on the sides and longer on top.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a low-maintenance and stylish haircut that flatters oblong face shape male. This haircut features short hair on the sides and back, with the top being slightly longer. The crew cut adds volume to the top, making the face appear less elongated.

3. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a popular and timeless hairstyle that can work well with oblong face shape male. This haircut features short hair all around, making the face appear more proportionate. The buzz cut is also low-maintenance and easy to style.

4. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and trendy hairstyle that can suit oblong face shape male. This look features short hair on the sides and back and slightly longer hair on top. The top is styled with texture and volume, adding width to the face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for oblong face shape male:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make the face appear longer.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat on top, as they can elongate the face.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and features.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, flat on top, or have too much volume on the crown. These hairstyles can make your face appear longer.

Can I wear a fringe with an oblong face shape?

Yes, a fringe can work well with oblong face shape male. However, avoid fringes that are too long or heavy, as they can elongate the face.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Shape Male

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a hair styling product that adds volume and texture to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and use a hat or sunscreen when outdoors.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing the best hairstyle for oblong face shape male can be challenging, but with the right cut and style, you can enhance your features and look your best. Use these tips and ideas to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish.
