Can You Cut The Top Of Your Hair With Clippers

Written by Bardi May 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Clippers are a fantastic tool to cut hair at home. They're easy to use, affordable, and versatile. But can you cut the top of your hair with clippers? The answer is yes, you can. In this article, we'll discuss how to cut the top of your hair with clippers, step by step, and provide you with some tips to make your DIY haircut a success. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and give you some hair care advice. Let's get started!

How to Cut the Top of Your Hair with Clippers

Cutting the top of your hair with clippers is not as difficult as it seems. You just need to follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

First, you need to choose the right clippers. You should look for clippers with adjustable blades and guard combs. The adjustable blades will allow you to change the length of the cut, while the guard combs will help you achieve an even cut.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you should wash it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup and make your hair easier to cut.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard Comb

Next, you need to choose the right guard comb. The guard comb will determine the length of the cut. The higher the number of the guard comb, the longer the cut will be. For the top of your hair, you should use a guard comb with a higher number, such as 4 or 5.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Start at the front of your head and work your way to the back. Use slow, even strokes and keep the clippers level with your head. Remember to use the guard comb to achieve an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've finished cutting the top of your hair, it's time to blend the sides and back. Use a lower guard comb to blend the sides and back with the top. Use slow, even strokes to achieve a natural look.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a higher guard comb and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make small, even strokes.
  • If you're unsure about the length, start with a higher guard comb and work your way down.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even before cutting.

FAQ

Can You Cut Your Hair with Clippers Without a Guard Comb?

You can cut your hair without a guard comb, but it's not recommended. Clippers without a guard comb will cut your hair very short, and it's difficult to achieve an even cut without a guard comb.

What Should You Do if You Make a Mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and fix it. Use a higher guard comb and work your way down to blend the mistake. If the mistake is too severe, you might have to cut your hair shorter than you intended.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Cutting the top of your hair with clippers is easy if you follow the right steps and use the right tools. Remember to choose the right clippers, wash your hair, choose the right guard comb, start cutting, and blend the sides and back. Take your time, use a mirror, and don't rush the process. With these tips, you can achieve a great DIY haircut at home.
