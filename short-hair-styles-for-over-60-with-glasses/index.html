Short Hair Styles For Over 60 With Glasses A Guide

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Do you want to try a new short hair style that will complement your glasses and enhance your features? Look no further, as we have compiled a guide for short hair styles for over 60 with glasses. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

How to Choose a Short Hair Style for Over 60 with Glasses

Choosing a short hair style can be a daunting task, especially if you wear glasses. Here are some tips to help you choose a short hair style that will complement your glasses:

  • Consider your face shape. Short hair styles that are layered or textured work best for round or square faces, while short bob hair styles work well for oval faces.
  • Take your glasses into account. Your glasses should complement your hair style, not clash with it. If you have bold or colorful glasses, consider a simple and classic hair style. If you have simple glasses, you can try a bolder and edgier hair style.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you are active and on-the-go, choose a low-maintenance hair style that requires little styling. If you prefer to spend more time on your hair, choose a hair style that allows for versatility and experimentation.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Achieve a Short Hair Style for Over 60 with Glasses

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a short hair style for over 60 with glasses:

  1. Choose a hair style that complements your glasses and face shape.
  2. Show your hair stylist a picture of the hair style you want to achieve.
  3. Ask your hair stylist to cut your hair while it is dry, as this allows for more precision.
  4. Style your hair using a hair dryer and round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Use a hair product, such as styling cream or mousse, to enhance your hair style and hold it in place.
  6. Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add a personal touch to your hair style.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Over 60 with Glasses

Maintaining a short hair style requires some effort and care. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair style:

  • Visit your hair stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair style looking fresh.
  • Use a hair product, such as dry shampoo or hairspray, to refresh your hair style between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair dryer, straightener, or curling iron.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF, and by rinsing your hair after swimming.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially if it is wet. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair styles for over 60 with glasses:

What hair styles are best for over 60 with glasses?

Short hair styles that are layered, textured, or bobbed work well for over 60 with glasses. These hair styles add volume and movement to the hair, and complement the face shape and glasses.

Can I still wear bangs with glasses?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with glasses. However, make sure to choose a hair style and bangs that complement your glasses and face shape. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs work well with glasses.

Can I dye my hair if I wear glasses?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you wear glasses. However, consider the color of your glasses when choosing a hair color. If you have bold or colorful glasses, consider a neutral hair color. If you have simple glasses, you can try a bolder hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Styles for Over 60 with Glasses

Here are some hair care tips for short hair styles for over 60 with glasses:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, wash it every 2-3 days.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, as this can improve the overall health of your hair.

With these tips and advice, you can confidently choose and maintain a short hair style that will enhance your features and complement your glasses. Experiment with different hair styles and accessories, and have fun with your new look!
