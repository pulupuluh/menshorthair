How To Flatten Thick Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Feb 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a guy with thick hair, you may be wondering how to flatten it. Thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a curse if you don't know how to manage it. Fortunately, there are several ways to flatten thick hair, from using the right products to getting a haircut that suits your hair type. In this article, we'll show you how to flatten thick hair for guys, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Flatten Thick Hair for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Flattening Thick Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Thick Hair

How to Flatten Thick Hair for Guys

Flattening thick hair requires a combination of the right products and techniques. Here are some of the most effective ways to flatten thick hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to help smooth and soften your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to help straighten and flatten it.
  • Use a flat iron or straightener to further flatten your hair.
  • Get a haircut that suits your hair type and helps flatten your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to flattening thick hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair. This will help cleanse your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Hair Oil or Serum

Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair while it's still damp. This will help smooth and soften your hair, making it easier to flatten.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will help straighten and flatten your hair. Use the brush to pull your hair straight as you blow-dry it.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron or Straightener

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or straightener to further flatten your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Spray

Finish by applying a light hairspray to hold your hair in place and prevent it from becoming frizzy.

Tips for Flattening Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you flatten your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before blow-drying it.
  • Blow-dry your hair in sections to ensure that it's evenly dry.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth and flatten your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I flatten my thick hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can try using a hair straightening brush or a wet brush to help flatten your hair without using heat.

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair healthy and manageable, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your thick hair looking its best and flatten it when necessary. Remember, the key to flattening thick hair is to use the right products and techniques, and to be patient and gentle when styling your hair.
