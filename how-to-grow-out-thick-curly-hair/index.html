How to Grow Out Thick Curly Hair: A Comprehensive Guide for 2023

If you have thick curly hair, you may be wondering how to grow it out. Growing out curly hair can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve long, luscious locks. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about growing out thick curly hair, including how to, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

How to Grow Out Thick Curly Hair

Growing out thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques, you can achieve long, healthy locks.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to grow out thick curly hair:

  1. Start with a healthy scalp. A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to keep your scalp clean and free from buildup.
  2. Trim your ends regularly. Regular trims will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down hair growth.
  3. Be gentle with your hair. Curly hair is delicate and can easily become damaged. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, and be gentle when detangling your hair.
  4. Protect your hair while you sleep. Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.
  5. Eat a healthy diet. A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can promote healthy hair growth.
  6. Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water can also promote healthy hair growth.

Following these steps can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage and damage.

Tips for Growing Out Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for growing out thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics, which can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Try protective hairstyles, such as braids or twists, to help protect your hair and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs About Growing Out Thick Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out thick curly hair:

How long does it take to grow out thick curly hair?

Everyone's hair grows at a different rate, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. It may take several months or even years to grow out thick curly hair, but with the right care, you can achieve long, healthy locks.

Can I use heat styling tools on my thick curly hair?

Heat styling tools can be damaging to curly hair, so it's best to avoid them if possible. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low.

How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

Everyone's hair is different, but in general, it's best to wash curly hair once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water, which can also strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using alcohol-based styling products, which can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can grow out thick curly hair and achieve long, healthy locks.
