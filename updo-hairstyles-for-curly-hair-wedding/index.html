Updo Hairstyles For Curly Hair Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 08, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you might be wondering what kind of updo hairstyle will work for your wedding day. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss some of the best updo hairstyles for curly hair weddings. Whether you're the bride, bridesmaid or just attending as a guest, these hairstyles will make you look stunning on your special day. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to style, but that doesn't mean you have to compromise on your wedding day. Updo hairstyles are a great option for curly hair weddings as they keep the hair off the face and showcase the natural curls. There are several updo hairstyles that work well for curly hair, such as braids, buns, and twists. These hairstyles can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion, making them perfect for weddings.

How to

The first step in creating an updo hairstyle for curly hair is to make sure your hair is properly moisturized. Curly hair tends to be dry, so using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner is essential. Once your hair is clean and conditioned, you can start styling it. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Choose your hairstyle

Decide on the type of updo hairstyle you want to create. You can choose from braids, buns, twists, or a combination of these. Look for inspiration online or in bridal magazines to find a style that you love.

2. Prep your hair

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls and keep them in place. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse depending on your hair type. Comb your hair to distribute the product evenly.

3. Section your hair

Section your hair into smaller sections to make it easier to work with. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use clips to hold each section in place.

4. Create your updo

Start creating your updo by twisting, braiding, or pinning each section of hair. Make sure to leave some curls loose to frame your face. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure each section in place.

5. Finish with hairspray

Once your updo is complete, finish with a light hairspray to hold it in place. Don't use too much hairspray as it can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating a simple braided updo for curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to clean and condition your curly hair. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all the product.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a curl cream or mousse to your hair to help define your curls and keep them in place. Comb your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into smaller sections using clips. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

Step 4: Braid your hair

Start braiding each section of your hair. You can choose to do a regular braid or a French braid depending on your preference. Secure each braid with an elastic band.

Step 5: Pin the braids

Take each braid and wrap it around the base of your head, securing it with bobby pins. Make sure to tuck the ends of the braids neatly under the updo.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Spray a light hairspray over your updo to hold it in place. Your braided updo is now complete!

Tips

  • Don't wash your hair on the day of the wedding. Clean hair is harder to style and tends to be more slippery, making it difficult to hold an updo.
  • If you're doing your hair yourself, practice the hairstyle before the wedding day to make sure you're comfortable with the process.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine to your curls and tame any frizz.
  • Accessorize your updo with hairpins, flowers, or a veil to add some extra flair.

FAQ

Q. Can I create an updo hairstyle for curly hair without using heat?

A. Yes, you can create an updo hairstyle for curly hair without using heat. In fact, using heat can damage your curls and make them look frizzy. Instead, try using styling products and braids to create your updo.

Q. How can I make my curly hair stay in an updo all day?

A. To make your curly hair stay in an updo all day, use bobby pins and hair ties to secure each section of hair. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz.

Now that you have a complete guide to updo hairstyles for curly hair weddings, you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your special day. Remember to moisturize your hair, use styling products, and follow our tips for healthy curly hair. Happy wedding day!
