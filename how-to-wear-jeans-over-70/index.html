How To Wear Jeans Over 70 Tips Tricks And Trends

Written by Oliver Aug 13, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Jeans are a timeless classic that never goes out of style. But as you age, you may wonder if you can still wear jeans and look stylish. The answer is yes! In this article, we will share some tips, tricks, and trends on how to wear jeans over 70. We will cover everything you need to know from how to choose the right fit, style, and color to pairing them with the right top, shoes, and accessories. So, let's get started!

Description

As you age, your body shape and size may change, and you may also have to deal with health conditions that affect your mobility and comfort. This can make it challenging to find jeans that fit well, look good, and feel comfortable. But with the right knowledge and approach, you can find jeans that flatter your figure, suit your style, and match your personality.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Fit

The first step in wearing jeans over 70 is to choose the right fit. You want to look for jeans that are comfortable, flattering, and easy to move in. The best fit for you will depend on your body shape, size, and personal preference. Here are some tips to help you find the right fit:

  • Look for jeans with a high waistband that sits at or above your natural waistline. This will help to smooth and shape your tummy and hips.
  • Choose jeans with a straight or bootcut leg that flares slightly at the ankle. This will balance out your figure and create a longer, leaner look.
  • Avoid skinny or tight-fitting jeans that can be uncomfortable and unflattering. These styles are better suited for younger women with slimmer figures.
  • Consider jeans with stretchy material or elastic waistbands that provide extra comfort and flexibility.

Step 2: Pick the Right Style

Once you have found the right fit, it's time to choose the right style of jeans. There are various styles of jeans available, and you want to pick one that suits your personality and lifestyle. Here are some popular styles to consider:

  • Straight leg jeans: This classic style is versatile and flattering for most body shapes.
  • Bootcut jeans: This style flares slightly at the ankle, making it ideal for wearing with boots or heels.
  • Wide-leg jeans: This trendier style is roomy and comfortable, perfect for casual or dressy occasions.
  • Boyfriend jeans: This relaxed style is loose-fitting and comfy, ideal for a laid-back look.

Step 3: Choose the Right Color

The color of your jeans can also make a difference in how you look and feel. While classic blue jeans are always a safe choice, you can also experiment with other colors and washes. Here are some options to consider:

  • Dark wash: This color is slimming and sophisticated, perfect for dressy occasions.
  • Light wash: This color is casual and relaxed, ideal for weekend wear.
  • Black: This color is chic and versatile, suitable for both casual and dressy occasions.
  • White: This color is fresh and crisp, perfect for summer or spring wear.

Step 4: Pair with the Right Top

The key to wearing jeans over 70 is to pair them with the right top. You want to choose a top that flatters your figure, complements your jeans, and suits the occasion. Here are some ideas to inspire you:

  • A blouse or tunic: This pairing is dressy and elegant, perfect for a night out or a special occasion.
  • A shirt or sweater: This pairing is casual and comfortable, ideal for everyday wear or running errands.
  • A jacket or blazer: This pairing is stylish and versatile, suitable for both casual and dressy occasions.
  • A tank top or tee: This pairing is simple and easy, perfect for a casual day out or lounging at home.

Step 5: Add the Right Accessories

The final step in wearing jeans over 70 is to add the right accessories. Accessories can elevate your look, add personality, and make you feel confident and stylish. Here are some ideas to consider:

  • A scarf or necklace: This accessory can add color, texture, and visual interest to your outfit.
  • A belt or bag: This accessory can define your waistline, add structure, and complete your look.
  • A hat or sunglasses: This accessory can protect you from the sun, add flair, and make a statement.
  • A pair of shoes: This accessory can tie your outfit together, add height, and provide comfort.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear jeans over 70:

  • Choose jeans with a darker wash or pattern to create a slimming effect.
  • Wear a longer top or tunic to cover your hips and thighs if you are self-conscious about those areas.
  • Experiment with different styles, fits, and colors to find what works best for you.
  • Invest in high-quality jeans that will last longer and look better over time.
  • Accessorize with items that reflect your personality and style, such as a statement necklace or a colorful scarf.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing jeans over 70:

  • Q: Can I wear skinny jeans over 70?
    A: While skinny jeans can be trendy and stylish, they are better suited for younger women with slimmer figures. You can still wear skinny jeans if you feel comfortable and confident in them, but you may want to pair them with a longer top or tunic to balance out your figure.
  • Q: Can I wear jeans with sneakers over 70?
    A: Yes! Wearing jeans with sneakers is a comfortable and casual look that can be suitable for running errands, walking, or traveling. Just make sure to choose sneakers that are comfortable, supportive, and stylish.
  • Q: Can I wear jeans to a formal event over 70?
    A: It depends on the dress code and the occasion. While jeans can be dressy and elegant, they are not always appropriate for formal events such as weddings, galas, or black-tie affairs. If you want to wear jeans to a formal event, make sure to choose a dark wash, pair them with a dressy blouse or blazer, and accessorize with elegant jewelry and shoes.

Hair Care

While wearing jeans over 70 is easy and stylish, you also want to make sure your hair looks great. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo that suits your hair type and condition.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing to keep it soft, smooth, and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products that can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to trim your hair, maintain your style, and get advice on how to care for your hair.

Conclusion

Wearing jeans over 70 is easy, stylish, and comfortable. By following these tips, tricks, and trends, you can find jeans that flatter your figure, suit your style, and match your personality. Whether you prefer a classic or trendy look, there is a pair of jeans out there that is perfect for you. So, go ahead and rock those jeans with confidence and style!
