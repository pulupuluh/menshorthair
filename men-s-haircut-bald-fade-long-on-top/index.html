Men s Haircut Bald Fade Long On Top How To Get The Look

Written by Christine May 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a modern and stylish haircut, the men's haircut bald fade long on top is a great option. This hairstyle combines the clean look of a bald fade with the length and volume of longer hair on top. In this blog post, we'll go over everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get the Look

The men's haircut bald fade long on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to your preferences. Here's what you need to tell your barber:

  • Ask for a high skin fade on the sides and back
  • Leave about 2-3 inches of hair on top
  • Make sure the top is layered to add volume and texture
  • Ask for a textured fringe or side-swept bangs, depending on your preference

It's important to bring reference photos to your barber to ensure you get the exact look you want. You can search for "men's haircut bald fade long on top" on Google or Pinterest to find inspiration.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your men's haircut bald fade long on top:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body.
  2. Blow-dry your hair on low heat, using your fingers to lift the hair at the roots for maximum volume.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips.
  4. Rub the product into your palms and apply to the hair on top, working from the roots to the ends.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle and style your hair, creating a messy and textured look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your men's haircut bald fade long on top looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and add volume.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.

FAQ

Is the men's haircut bald fade long on top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. Your barber can adjust the length and shape of the top to flatter your features.

How do I know if this haircut is right for me?

If you like the look of longer hair on top and want to keep the sides and back clean and short, then this haircut may be right for you. Consult with your barber to see if it would suit your hair type and face shape.

Can I style my men's haircut bald fade long on top in different ways?

Yes, this haircut is highly versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your preferences. Experiment with a side part, slicked back look, or messy texture to find a style that suits you.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using heat styling tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's haircut bald fade long on top with confidence and style.
