Cute Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Girl How To Style Your Hair Like A Pro

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to add some variety to your medium-length hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share some cute hairstyles for medium length hair girl that are easy to do and will make you look and feel amazing. From braids to buns, we've got you covered!

How to Style Your Medium Length Hair

Before we jump into the cute hairstyles, let's talk about how to style your medium length hair. First, start with clean and dry hair. You can either air dry or blow dry your hair, but make sure it's completely dry before styling. Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage. Then, use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to your hair. Finally, finish off with hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture and interest to your medium length hair. There are many different types of braids that you can try, such as:

  • Fishtail braid
  • French braid
  • Dutch braid
  • Waterfall braid
  • Crown braid

To create a braid, start by separating a section of hair and dividing it into three strands. Then, cross the right strand over the middle strand, followed by the left strand over the middle strand. Continue braiding in this pattern until you reach the end of your hair. Secure with a hair tie and you're done!

Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Here are some cute bun hairstyles for medium length hair:

  • Messy bun
  • Low bun
  • Top knot
  • Braided bun

To create a bun, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins. For a messy bun, pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Cute Hairstyles

Fishtail Braid

Step 1: Divide your hair into two equal sections.

Step 2: Take a small strand of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section.

Step 3: Take a small strand of hair from the outside of the right section and cross it over to the left section.

Step 4: Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 5: Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Messy Bun

Step 1: Pull your hair back into a ponytail.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail into a bun.

Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 4: Pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your cute hairstyle looking great all day long, follow these tips:

  • Use a good quality hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs About Cute Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Girl

Q: Can I wear a cute hairstyle for medium length hair to work?

A: Absolutely! There are many cute hairstyles for medium length hair that are appropriate for the workplace, such as a low bun or a side braid.

Q: Will braids work on all types of medium length hair?

A: Yes, braids can work on all types of medium length hair. However, if you have very fine hair, you may need to use a texturizing spray to add some grip to your hair before braiding.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy and Shiny Hair

Finally, to keep your medium length hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these cute hairstyles for medium length hair girl and hair care tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect hairstyle every time. Whether you're going to work or a special event, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful!
