Best Hair Tongs For Short Hair Top Picks For 2023

If you have short hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right styling tools. Hair tongs, also known as curling irons, are an essential tool for adding volume and texture to short hair. In this article, we'll take a look at the best hair tongs for short hair in 2023.

How to Choose the Right Hair Tongs for Short Hair

Before we dive into our top picks, let's talk about how to choose the right hair tongs for your short hair. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Barrel size: For short hair, you'll want to choose a hair tong with a smaller barrel size. This will allow you to create tighter curls and more defined waves.
  • Heat settings: Look for a hair tong with adjustable heat settings, so you can customize the temperature to your hair type and styling needs.
  • Material: Ceramic and tourmaline are popular materials for hair tongs, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage to your hair.

Top Picks for Best Hair Tongs for Short Hair

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Spring Curling Iron

The BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Spring Curling Iron is a popular choice for short hair. It features a 1-inch barrel size, adjustable heat settings, and a titanium-ceramic coating for even heat distribution. Plus, its slim design makes it easy to use on short hair.

2. T3 SinglePass Compact Iron

The T3 SinglePass Compact Iron is a great option for short hair, thanks to its 0.8-inch barrel size. It also features adjustable heat settings and a ceramic coating for smooth, frizz-free results.

3. Remington Pro 1/2-1 Inch Curling Wand

The Remington Pro 1/2-1 Inch Curling Wand is a versatile option for short hair. Its tapered barrel allows you to create both tight curls and loose waves, while its ceramic coating reduces heat damage. Plus, it heats up quickly and has multiple heat settings.

How to Use Hair Tongs on Short Hair

Using hair tongs on short hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  2. Section your hair into small pieces, using clips to keep them in place.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the hair tong, starting at the root.
  4. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat on the remaining sections of hair, working your way around your head.
  6. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create volume.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Using Hair Tongs on Short Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you get the most out of your hair tongs:

  • Don't wrap too much hair around the barrel at once, as this can prevent the heat from reaching all of the hair.
  • Use a comb to help guide the hair onto the barrel, especially if you're working with very short hair.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes and directions to find the look that works best for your hair.
  • Don't forget to let the curls cool before touching them, as this will help them set and last longer.

FAQ: Hair Tongs for Short Hair

What's the best barrel size for short hair?

For short hair, a barrel size of 1 inch or smaller is recommended. This will allow you to create tighter curls and more defined waves.

What's the best material for hair tongs?

Ceramic and tourmaline are popular materials for hair tongs, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage to your hair.

Can I use hair tongs on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use hair tongs on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a hair tong, and always use a heat protectant spray.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Finally, here are a few hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

With the right hair tongs and proper technique, you can create beautiful, bouncy curls on your short hair. Just remember to use a heat protectant spray, work in small sections, and experiment with different curl sizes and directions. Happy styling!
