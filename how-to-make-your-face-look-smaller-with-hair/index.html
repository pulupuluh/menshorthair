How To Make Your Face Look Smaller With Hair

Written by Bardi Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you ever feel self-conscious about the shape of your face? While there's nothing wrong with having a round or square face, some people prefer a more sculpted look. The good news is that you can make your face look smaller with the right hairstyle. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Smaller with Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Face Look Smaller
  • FAQs

How to Make Your Face Look Smaller with Hair

The key to making your face appear smaller with hair is to add volume to the top of your head. This helps balance out the proportions of your face, making it look more narrow. Here are some styles to consider:

1. Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider getting layers that start at your chin or below. By adding volume to the bottom of your hair, you'll draw attention away from your cheeks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face, but they can also make it look wider. To avoid this, try side-swept bangs that start at your temple and angle down towards your cheekbone. This will draw attention to your eyes and away from the width of your face.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, consider a pixie cut. This short style can work wonders for making your face look smaller. By keeping the hair close to your head, you'll create a sleek, streamlined look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to achieve a smaller-looking face with your hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you select a style that complements your features. Common face shapes include round, square, oval, and heart-shaped.

Step 2: Add Volume

The key to making your face look smaller is to add volume at the top of your head. You can achieve this by blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing mousse or spray. Avoid flat styles that cling to your head.

Step 3: Consider Layers and Bangs

If you have long hair, consider layers that start at your chin or below. Side-swept bangs can also help balance out your features. If you have short hair, a pixie cut can work wonders.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen a style, it's time to style your hair. Use a round brush to add volume while blow-drying, and finish with a volumizing hairspray. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Smaller

Here are some additional tips for making your face look smaller with your hair:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Avoid heavy, flat styles
  • Use volumizing products to add height at the crown of your head
  • Consider side-swept bangs to draw attention to your eyes
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Can I make my face look smaller without cutting my hair?

Absolutely! You can use makeup to contour and highlight your features, which can create the illusion of a slimmer face. You can also experiment with different hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Will a short hairstyle make my face look wider?

Not necessarily. A short hairstyle can actually make your face appear longer and more streamlined. Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Condition regularly to keep your hair hydrated and shiny
  • Avoid heat styling whenever possible
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

With these tips and tricks, you can make your face look smaller with your hair. Whether you choose a long layered style, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut, remember to add volume at the crown of your head for the best results. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair for optimal health and shine.
