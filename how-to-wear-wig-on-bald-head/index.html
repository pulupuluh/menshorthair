Yes, you can wear a wig even if you have a sensitive scalp. Choose a wig with a breathable cap and avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that can irritate your scalp.

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig after 8-10 wears or when it starts to look dull or dirty. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and gently wash the wig by soaking it in water and then rinsing it thoroughly. Avoid rubbing or twisting the wig as it can damage the hair.

  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat as it can damage the hair fibers.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair and style it gently.
  • Store your wig in a cool and dry place away from moisture and dust.
