How To Make A Homemade Clipper Guard A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly buying new clipper guards every time they wear out? Or maybe you just can't seem to find the right size for your needs? Whatever the reason, making your own clipper guard can be a cost-effective and convenient solution. In this guide, we'll show you how to make a homemade clipper guard in just a few easy steps.

What You'll Need

To make your own clipper guard, you'll need a few basic supplies:

  • A pair of scissors
  • A ruler
  • A piece of cardboard or plastic
  • A pen or marker
  • A pair of clippers

Once you have these supplies, you're ready to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Measure the Clipper Blade

The first step in making a homemade clipper guard is to measure the blade of your clippers. Use a ruler to measure the width and length of the blade, and write down the measurements on a piece of paper.

Step 2: Cut the Cardboard or Plastic

Next, cut a piece of cardboard or plastic to the same size as the blade of your clippers. Use your scissors to trim the edges until the cardboard or plastic fits snugly over the blade.

Step 3: Trace the Guard

Once you have the cardboard or plastic cut to size, use a pen or marker to trace the shape of the guard onto the material. You can use an existing guard as a template, or create your own design.

Step 4: Cut Out the Guard

Using your scissors, carefully cut out the guard along the traced lines. Be sure to make clean, straight cuts to ensure that the guard fits properly on your clippers.

Step 5: Test the Guard

Once you have the guard cut out, test it on your clippers to make sure it fits properly. If necessary, make any adjustments to the size or shape until it fits snugly over the blade.

Tips for Making a Homemade Clipper Guard

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making your own clipper guard:

  • Use sturdy cardboard or plastic that will hold up to regular use
  • Make sure the guard fits snugly over the blade to prevent slipping
  • Be careful when cutting out the guard to avoid jagged or uneven edges
  • Test the guard on your clippers before using it to trim your hair

FAQ

Can I reuse the same homemade clipper guard?

Yes, you can reuse the same homemade clipper guard as long as it remains in good condition. Just be sure to clean it after each use to prevent buildup of hair and debris.

What materials can I use to make a homemade clipper guard?

You can use a variety of materials to make a homemade clipper guard, including cardboard, plastic, and even metal. Just make sure the material is sturdy enough to hold up to regular use.

Can I make different sized guards for my clippers?

Yes, you can make different sized guards for your clippers by adjusting the size and shape of the guard. Use a ruler to measure the size of the blade, and cut the cardboard or plastic to fit.

Hair Care

Using a homemade clipper guard can help you achieve the perfect haircut without breaking the bank. However, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a quality hairbrush or comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make a homemade clipper guard in no time. Happy trimming!
