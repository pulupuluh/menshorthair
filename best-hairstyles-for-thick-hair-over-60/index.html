Best Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 60

Written by Robby Feb 18, 2023 · 9 min read

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and less dense, but for some lucky individuals, their hair remains thick and voluminous. If you’re over 60 and blessed with thick hair, you might be wondering what the best hairstyles are that will suit your hair type and age. In this article, we’ll share with you some of the best hairstyles for thick hair over 60.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Thick Hair Over 60

When choosing the best hairstyle for thick hair over 60, there are a few things that you need to consider:

  • Face Shape – Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Hair Texture – Make sure the hairstyle you choose works with your hair texture.
  • Lifestyle – Consider how much time and effort you’re willing to put into styling your hair.
  • Personal Style – Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personality and personal style.

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let’s take a look at some of the best hairstyles for thick hair over 60.

Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair Over 60

Short hairstyles are a great option for those with thick hair over 60. They’re easy to maintain and can make you look years younger. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Pixie Cut – A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that’s cut close to the head. It’s a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Bob – A bob is a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for those with thick hair. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways.
  • Layered Cut – A layered cut is a great option for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair.

Medium Hairstyles for Thick Hair Over 60

Medium hairstyles are another great option for those with thick hair over 60. They’re easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. Here are some medium hairstyles to consider:

  • Shoulder-Length Cut – A shoulder-length cut is a great option for those who want a medium-length hairstyle. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways.
  • Layered Cut – A layered cut is a great option for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair.
  • Blunt Cut – A blunt cut is a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for those with thick hair. It’s a simple hairstyle that’s easy to maintain.

Long Hairstyles for Thick Hair Over 60

Long hairstyles can be a bit more challenging to maintain, but they can be a great option for those with thick hair over 60. Here are some long hairstyles to consider:

  • Layered Cut – A layered cut is a great option for those who want to add some volume and movement to their hair. It’s also a great option for those who want to keep their hair long.
  • Beachy Waves – Beachy waves are a great option for those who want a more relaxed hairstyle. They’re easy to achieve and can be styled in many different ways.
  • Braids – Braids are a great option for those who want a more bohemian hairstyle. They’re easy to achieve and can be styled in many different ways.

Tips for Styling Thick Hair Over 60

Styling thick hair over 60 can be a bit challenging, but with the right tips, it can be easy. Here are some tips for styling thick hair over 60:

  • Use the Right Products – Use products that are specifically designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid Heavy Products – Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-Dry Upside Down – Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume to your hair.
  • Use Rollers – Using rollers can add volume and curl to your hair.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for thick hair over 60?

A: The best haircut for thick hair over 60 depends on your personal style, face shape, and hair texture. Short haircuts like a pixie cut or bob are great options for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. Medium haircuts like a shoulder-length cut or blunt cut are great options for those who want a versatile hairstyle. Long haircuts like a layered cut or beachy waves are great options for those who want a more relaxed hairstyle.

Q: How do I style thick hair over 60?

A: You can style thick hair over 60 by using the right products, avoiding heavy products, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using rollers.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair Over 60

Thick hair can be challenging to maintain, but with the right hair care tips, it can be easy. Here are some hair care tips for thick hair over 60:

  • Use a Deep Conditioner – Use a deep conditioner at least once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid Heat Styling – Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and flat irons as much as possible.
  • Protect Your Hair from the Sun – Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Trim Your Hair Regularly – Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles, you’ll be able to rock your thick hair over 60 with confidence and style.
