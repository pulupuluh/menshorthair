What Hair Part Is Best For Square Face

Written by Aprilia Feb 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what hair part is best for square face? If you have a square-shaped face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features. Your hair part plays a significant role in framing your face, and finding the right one can make a difference in how you look. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair part for square face and other tips to help you achieve your desired hairstyle.

Description

Square-shaped faces have strong jawlines, forehead, and cheekbones that are almost equal in width. To soften the angles and balance the features, the ideal hair part for square face is a deep side part or a middle part. These hair parts create an illusion of length that elongates the face and makes it appear more oval-shaped.

How to Find Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair part for square face, let's first determine if you have a square-shaped face. Here's how to find out:

  1. Tie your hair back and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the mirror using a washable marker or lipstick.
  3. Step back and observe the shape you traced.

If the outline looks like a square with sharp angles, then you have a square-shaped face. If your jawline is more rounded, you might have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is narrower than your forehead and cheekbones, you might have an oval-shaped face. And if your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width, then you have a square-shaped face.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Part Your Hair

Now that you know your face shape let's move on to the step-by-step guide on how to part your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it.
  2. Using a comb or your fingers, create a part on either side of your head. If you're going for a deep side part, aim for a part that's about an inch away from the center of your head.
  3. If you're going for a middle part, use the arch of your eyebrows as a guide. Part your hair from the center of your hairline to the back of your head, dividing your hair into two equal sections.
  4. Run your fingers or a comb through your hair to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  5. If you have baby hairs or flyaways, use a small amount of hair gel or cream to tame them.
  6. Finish off with hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips to Consider

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair part for square face:

  • Consider your hair type and texture. Thick hair may require a different part than fine hair.
  • Experiment with different parts to see what works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hair with clips, headbands, or scarves to add some flair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about which part looks best on you.

FAQs

Can I change my hair part every day?

Yes, you can change your hair part every day. It's a great way to switch up your look and add some variety to your hairstyle.

Will a side part or middle part work on any hair length?

Yes, a side part or middle part works on any hair length. However, some hair lengths may require more styling to keep the part in place.

Can I still wear bangs with a square-shaped face?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with a square-shaped face. Opt for side-swept bangs that soften the angles of your face.

Hair Care for Square-Shaped Faces

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

In conclusion, the best hair part for square face is a deep side part or middle part. Experiment with different parts to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to accessorize your hair to add some personality. Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
