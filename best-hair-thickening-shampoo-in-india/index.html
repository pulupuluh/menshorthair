Best Hair Thickening Shampoo In India Top Products For 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 17, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thin and lifeless hair? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with hair that lacks volume and thickness, which can make it difficult to achieve the hairstyles you want. Fortunately, there are a variety of hair thickening shampoos available on the market that can help. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the best hair thickening shampoos in India for 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hair Thickening Shampoo
  • Top Hair Thickening Shampoos in India
  • Tips for Using Hair Thickening Shampoo
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Thickening Hair

How to Choose the Best Hair Thickening Shampoo

Choosing the best hair thickening shampoo can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. Here are a few things to keep in mind when looking for a hair thickening shampoo:

1. Look for Ingredients That Promote Hair Growth

The best hair thickening shampoos contain ingredients that promote hair growth. Some of the most effective ingredients include biotin, caffeine, and niacin. These ingredients work by stimulating the hair follicles and promoting blood flow to the scalp, which can help to thicken the hair.

2. Avoid Products with Harsh Chemicals

Harsh chemicals can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause damage, which can lead to thinning hair. Look for hair thickening shampoos that are free from sulfates and parabens, which can be damaging to the hair.

3. Consider Your Hair Type

Not all hair thickening shampoos are created equal. Consider your hair type when choosing a product. If you have oily hair, look for a shampoo that is lightweight and won't weigh down your hair. If you have dry hair, look for a product that is moisturizing and nourishing.

Top Hair Thickening Shampoos in India

Now that you know what to look for in a hair thickening shampoo, let's take a look at some of the best products available in India:

1. TRESemmé Collagen+ Fullness Shampoo

The TRESemmé Collagen+ Fullness Shampoo is a great option for those looking to add volume and thickness to their hair. This shampoo contains collagen and glycerin, which work together to strengthen the hair and add volume. It is also free from sulfates and parabens, making it a gentle option for all hair types.

2. L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo

The L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo is another great option for those looking to thicken their hair. This shampoo contains filloxane, which penetrates the hair shaft to thicken and strengthen the hair. It is also free from sulfates and parabens, making it a gentle option for all hair types.

3. Dove Advanced Hair Series Rejuvenated Volume Shampoo

The Dove Advanced Hair Series Rejuvenated Volume Shampoo is a great option for those with fine hair. This shampoo contains a blend of ingredients that work together to add volume and thickness to the hair. It is also gentle enough to use on a daily basis.

Tips for Using Hair Thickening Shampoo

Here are a few tips for using hair thickening shampoo:

1. Use a Small Amount

A little goes a long way when it comes to hair thickening shampoo. Use a small amount, about the size of a quarter, and work it into your scalp and hair.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp while shampooing to help stimulate blood flow to the hair follicles.

3. Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all of the shampoo. Leaving shampoo in your hair can cause buildup, which can lead to thinning hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hair thickening shampoo for men?

There are many hair thickening shampoos available for men. Some of the best options include Nizoral A-D Anti-Dandruff Shampoo, OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo, and Jack Black True Volume Thickening Shampoo.

Can hair thickening shampoo cause hair loss?

No, hair thickening shampoo should not cause hair loss. In fact, many hair thickening shampoos contain ingredients that promote hair growth.

How often should I use hair thickening shampoo?

You can use hair thickening shampoo on a daily basis. However, if you have dry hair, you may want to limit your use to every other day to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thickening Hair

In addition to using a hair thickening shampoo, there are a few other things you can do to thicken your hair:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help to promote healthy hair growth. Some of the best foods for healthy hair include salmon, eggs, spinach, and sweet potatoes.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage the hair and cause thinning. Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, or use them sparingly.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends, which can lead to thinning hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

In conclusion, the best hair thickening shampoos in India for 2023 are those that contain ingredients that promote hair growth, are free from harsh chemicals, and are suitable for your hair type. By using a hair thickening shampoo and following these hair care tips, you can achieve thicker, fuller hair in no time!
