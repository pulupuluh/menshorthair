How To Shave The Back Of Your Neck With Clippers

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts just to maintain the hair on the back of your neck? With the right tools and technique, you can easily shave the back of your neck with clippers at home. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions to help you achieve a clean and professional-looking cut.

Description

The back of your neck can be a difficult area to shave, especially if you don't have a lot of experience or the right tools. Clippers are a great alternative to using a razor, as they are less likely to cause irritation or cuts. With clippers, you can also easily adjust the length of the hair to achieve the desired look.

How to Shave the Back of Your Neck with Clippers

Before you start, make sure you have a set of clippers with guards, a mirror, and a towel. Here's how to shave the back of your neck with clippers:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Skin

Start by washing your hair and neck with warm water and shampoo. This will help to soften the hair and make it easier to cut. Dry your hair and neck with a towel.

Step 2: Set Up Your Clippers

Attach the appropriate guard to your clippers, depending on the length of hair you want to leave. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if needed.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Stand in front of a mirror with your back facing the mirror. Use your non-dominant hand to hold the mirror and position it so you can see the back of your neck. Use your dominant hand to hold the clippers and start cutting from the bottom of your hairline, moving upwards. Use short, gentle strokes and move the clippers in a straight line. Repeat this process until you've cut the hair on the back of your neck to the desired length.

Step 4: Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting, use a towel to wipe away any loose hair. Check your work in the mirror to make sure you haven't missed any spots.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when shaving the back of your neck with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Use short, gentle strokes to avoid irritation or cuts.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member to help you with hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving the back of your neck with clippers:

Can I use a razor instead of clippers?

You can use a razor to shave the back of your neck, but it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut and you may be more prone to cuts or irritation.

How often should I shave the back of my neck?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and the look you're going for. As a general rule, you should shave the back of your neck every 2-4 weeks to maintain a clean look.

How do I clean my clippers?

After each use, remove the guard and wipe the clippers with a damp cloth. You can also use a small brush to remove any hair that's stuck in the blades.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash and condition it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. If you're unsure about how to care for your hair, consider consulting a professional stylist.

Shaving the back of your neck with clippers can be a quick and easy way to maintain your hair without breaking the bank. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a clean and professional-looking cut in no time.
