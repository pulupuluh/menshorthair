Boy Braids Hairstyles 2020 A Guide To Trendy Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Braids have been a popular hairstyle for centuries, but in 2020, we saw a surge in boys embracing this trend. From cornrows to box braids, there were numerous styles that boys could choose from to elevate their look. In this article, we'll be exploring the different boy braids hairstyles that were popular in 2020 and how to achieve them. We'll also be sharing some tips on how to care for your braids and answering some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Boy Braids Hairstyles 2020
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Boy Braids Hairstyles 2020
  • Tips for Maintaining Boy Braids Hairstyles
  • Frequently Asked Questions about Boy Braids Hairstyles
  • Hair Care for Boy Braids Hairstyles

How to Achieve Boy Braids Hairstyles 2020

The first step in achieving a boy braids hairstyle is to decide which style you want to go for. The most popular boy braids hairstyles in 2020 were:

  • Cornrows
  • Box Braids
  • French Braids
  • Dreadlocks

Once you've decided on your style, you'll need to find a skilled braider who can create the look for you. It's important to choose someone who has experience in creating boy braids hairstyles, as it requires a different technique than braiding women's hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Boy Braids Hairstyles 2020

Here's a general step-by-step guide to achieving boy braids hairstyles:

  1. Wash and condition your hair. This ensures that your hair is clean and moisturized before braiding.
  2. Section your hair. Depending on the style you've chosen, your hair may need to be sectioned into small or large sections.
  3. Braid your hair. Your braider will use a technique specific to the style you've chosen to create the braids.
  4. Style your braids. You can leave your braids as is, or you can add accessories such as beads or hair cuffs to give them a unique look.

Tips for Maintaining Boy Braids Hairstyles

Here are some tips for maintaining your boy braids hairstyles:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your scalp from drying out.
  • Wash your braids. It's important to keep your braids clean to prevent buildup and odor. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid pulling your braids too tightly. This can cause tension on your scalp and lead to hair loss.

Frequently Asked Questions about Boy Braids Hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about boy braids hairstyles:

Q: How long do boy braids hairstyles last?

A: Boy braids hairstyles can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Q: Can I swim with boy braids hairstyles?

A: Yes, you can swim with boy braids hairstyles, but it's important to rinse your hair with clean water after swimming to remove any chlorine or saltwater.

Q: Can I style my boy braids hairstyles differently?

A: Yes, you can style your boy braids hairstyles in different ways, such as tying them up in a bun or ponytail.

Hair Care for Boy Braids Hairstyles

Here are some general hair care tips for boy braids hairstyles:

  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as this can weigh down your braids and cause buildup.
  • Protect your braids while sleeping by wearing a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.
  • Be gentle with your braids when styling, as rough handling can cause them to unravel or break.

Boy braids hairstyles were a popular trend in 2020, and it looks like they're here to stay. Whether you opt for cornrows, box braids, French braids, or dreadlocks, there's a style that can suit your personality and lifestyle. With proper care and maintenance, your boy braids can last for weeks and give you a stylish, low-maintenance look.
