How To Get Curly Hair Without A Perm Or Heat

Written by Petter Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you want to have curly hair without damaging your strands? Are you tired of using heat styling tools and chemical treatments that can cause long-term damage to your hair? Well, you're in luck because there are ways to achieve curly hair without a perm or heat. In this article, we will share with you some tips and steps on how to get curly hair without a perm or heat.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Curly Hair Without a Perm or Heat
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve curly hair without a perm or heat:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prepare it for styling.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product such as a curl cream or mousse to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

3. Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections and twist them into small coils. Secure each coil with a clip or bobby pin. This will help to create defined curls without using heat or a perm.

4. Let your hair air dry

Let your hair air dry completely before removing the clips or bobby pins. This will help to set the curls and prevent frizz.

5. Finger comb your curls

Once your hair is completely dry, gently finger comb your curls to separate them and create volume.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curls
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated

FAQs

Can I use a diffuser to dry my hair?

Yes, you can use a diffuser to dry your hair and enhance your curls. However, make sure to use it on a low heat setting and don't over-dry your hair to prevent frizz.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Can I use hair rollers to create curls?

Yes, you can use hair rollers to create curls without heat. Apply a styling product to your damp hair, roll it into the rollers, and let it air dry.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt instead
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to get curly hair without a perm or heat, you can try these tips and steps at home. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair. With proper care and maintenance, you can have beautiful, healthy curls without damaging your hair.
