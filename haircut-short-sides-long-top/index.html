Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, then the "short sides long top" is the perfect choice. This haircut has been in trend for years and continues to be popular among men of all ages. In this ultimate guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this iconic haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To Get It
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

The "short sides long top" is a haircut that features short hair on the sides of the head and longer hair on the top. The length of the top hair can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on your personal preference. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice among men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut.

How To Get It:

The "short sides long top" can be achieved by visiting a professional hairstylist or barber. It's important to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want. You can bring in a photo or describe the haircut in detail to make sure you get the look you want. If you're feeling adventurous, you can even try cutting your hair at home using clippers and scissors, but be sure to do your research and proceed with caution.

Step By Step Guide:

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the "short sides long top" haircut:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Using a comb or your fingers, part your hair where you want it to be.
  3. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a gradual fade.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style the top hair using a comb or your fingers. You can add product for extra hold and texture.

Tips for Styling:

Here are some tips for styling your "short sides long top" haircut:

  • Use a matte or low-shine product for a natural look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look.
  • Invest in a good quality comb and brush to help you style your hair effectively.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on styling your hair.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "short sides long top" haircut:

Q: Will this haircut suit me?

A: The "short sides long top" is a versatile haircut that can suit many different face shapes and hair types. It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if this haircut is right for you.

Q: How often should I get this haircut?

A: It's recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I style this haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, the "short sides long top" can be styled in many different ways, including a slicked-back look, a messy, textured look, or a classic side part.

Hair Care:

Here are some tips for taking care of your "short sides long top" haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the "short sides long top" haircut with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a classic look or a modern twist, this haircut is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
