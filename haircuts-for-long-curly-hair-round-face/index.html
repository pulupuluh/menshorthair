Haircuts For Long Curly Hair Round Face

Written by Frank Apr 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut for your long curly hair round face? Look no further! We have put together a guide to help you find the perfect haircut to flatter your face shape and enhance your natural curls. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a soft jawline. Long curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut, it can look stunning. The key is to find a style that elongates your face and adds volume to your curls. The right haircut can also help balance out your proportions and highlight your best features.

How to

When choosing a haircut for your long curly hair round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for styles that add height to the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face and balance out your proportions. You should also look for haircuts that add volume to your curls. This will help give your hair more body and movement.

Another important factor to consider is the length of your hair. Long hair can be stunning on a round face, but it's important to choose the right length. Hair that is too long can overwhelm your face and make it look rounder. On the other hand, hair that is too short can accentuate your round features.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a haircut for your long curly hair round face:

  1. Identify your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width and length of your face and comparing the two.
  2. Look for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  3. Choose a haircut that adds volume to your curls.
  4. Consider the length of your hair. Aim for a length that falls between your chin and your collarbone.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with long curly hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your long curly hair round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or too layered. Blunt cuts can make your face look rounder, while too many layers can make your hair look too thin.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut. Bangs can help balance out your proportions and highlight your eyes.
  • Experiment with different parting styles. A deep side part can help elongate your face, while a middle part can help balance out your features.
  • Use a diffuser when drying your hair to enhance your curls and add volume.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long curly hair round face?

The best haircut for a long curly hair round face is one that adds height to the crown of your head and volume to your curls. Aim for a length that falls between your chin and your collarbone.

Should I add layers to my long curly hair?

Yes, but be careful not to add too many layers. Too many layers can make your hair look too thin and accentuate your round features.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

Absolutely! A ponytail can be a great way to show off your curls and keep your hair out of your face. Just be sure to position the ponytail high on your head to add height.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your long curly hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips in mind, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your long curly hair round face. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with long curly hair to get the best results. Good luck!
