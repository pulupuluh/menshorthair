How To Cut Layers In Wavy Hair At Home

Written by William Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy hair is beautiful, but sometimes it can be challenging to manage. Adding layers to your wavy hair can help make it more manageable and enhance its natural texture. Instead of going to a hair salon, you can save money and cut your hair at home. In this article, we will share with you how to cut layers in wavy hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Layers in Wavy Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Layers in Wavy Hair at Home
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Cutting Layers in Wavy Hair

How to Cut Layers in Wavy Hair at Home

The first thing you need to do is gather all the tools you need. You will need sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, a spray bottle, and a mirror. Once you have all the tools, you can start cutting your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for wavy hair. Once you have washed your hair, dry it with a towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by dividing your hair into two sections, one at the back and one at the front. Then, divide each of these sections into two more sections, one on each side of your head.

Step 3: Cut the First Layer

Start by cutting the first layer. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of your hairline and hold it between your fingers. Then, point-cut the hair with your scissors in an upward motion. This will create a soft and seamless layer.

Step 4: Continue Cutting the Layers

Continue cutting the layers using the same technique. Take another small section of hair, hold it between your fingers, and point-cut it in an upward motion. Make sure the length of the layers is consistent throughout your hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you have finished cutting all the layers, use your scissors to blend them together. Take a small section of hair from the top layer and cut it at an angle to blend it with the lower layers.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut layers in wavy hair at home:

  1. Gather all the tools you need.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Cut the first layer.
  5. Continue cutting the layers.
  6. Blend the layers.

Tips for Cutting Layers in Wavy Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you cut layers in wavy hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it gets too dry.
  • Always cut in an upward motion to avoid creating harsh lines.
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair when it is wet or dry?

You can cut your hair when it is wet or dry, but it is easier to cut when it is wet.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors because they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Layers in Wavy Hair

After cutting layers in your wavy hair, you should take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for wavy hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Cutting layers in wavy hair at home is easy with the right tools and techniques. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks to give your hair a fresh and effortless look.
