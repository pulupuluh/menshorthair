How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors For Beginners

Written by Robby Apr 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you want to save some money and give your male family members or friends a new haircut, then this article is for you. Cutting men's hair with scissors is not rocket science, but it does require some practice, patience, and basic knowledge. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut men's hair with scissors for beginners, step by step.

How to

The first thing you need to do is prepare your tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Before starting, make sure the person you are cutting the hair for is wearing a shirt that you don't mind getting hair on.

Next, you need to decide on the length and style of the haircut. You can ask the person what he wants or show him some pictures of haircuts that you can do. Then, you need to wet the hair with the spray bottle and comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Start cutting the hair from the back of the neck, and work your way up to the top of the head. Use the comb to hold the hair in place, and use the scissors to cut off the desired length.

Step by Step

1. Prepare your tools.

2. Decide on the length and style of the haircut.

3. Wet the hair and comb it thoroughly.

4. Start cutting the hair from the back of the neck.

5. Use the comb to hold the hair in place.

6. Use the scissors to cut off the desired length.

7. Continue cutting the hair from the sides and the top of the head.

8. Check the length as you go along to ensure that it is even.

9. Trim the hair around the ears and the neckline.

10. Finish off the haircut by blending the hair on the top and the sides.

Tips

- Don't rush the haircut, take your time.

- Use a comb to hold the hair in place, especially when cutting around the ears and the neckline.

- Take breaks if necessary, cutting hair can be tiring.

- Clean your scissors after use and store them in a safe place.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut men's hair with regular household scissors?

A: No, it is not recommended to use regular household scissors as they are not sharp enough and can damage the hair.

Q: How often should men get a haircut?

A: It depends on the length and style of their hair, but on average, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it is important to take care of the hair by washing it with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water and excessive heat styling, as it can damage the hair. Regular trims can help maintain the shape of the haircut and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, cutting men's hair with scissors for beginners is a simple process, but it does require some practice and knowledge. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can give your male family members or friends a new haircut and save some money in the process. Happy cutting!
