Short Bob Hairstyles For Fine Straight Hair Over 60

Written by Oliver Mar 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, a short bob could be the perfect choice. Not only is it a classic style that never goes out of fashion, but it can also be tailored to suit your fine, straight hair. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best short bob hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60, as well as how to style and care for your new 'do. Read on for our top tips!

Description

Short bob hairstyles are a great choice for fine, straight hair as they add volume and texture to your locks. The cut typically sits just above the jawline and is angled slightly downwards, creating a flattering shape that frames the face. This style can be customized with layers, bangs, or asymmetrical cuts to suit your personal style.

How to

The first step in getting a short bob hairstyle is to find a trusted hair stylist who has experience working with fine, straight hair. They can help you choose a cut that will flatter your face shape and hair texture. Once you've got your new cut, here's how to style it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for fine, straight hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short bob hairstyle:

  1. Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Book an appointment with a trusted hair stylist.
  3. Bring pictures of the style you want to show your stylist.
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions you have about the cut.
  5. Get your hair cut and styled by your stylist.
  6. Learn how to style your new cut at home.
  7. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short bob hairstyle:

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on how to style your new cut at home.
  • Invest in quality hair care products designed for fine, straight hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short bob hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60:

What face shape suits a short bob hairstyle?

A short bob hairstyle can suit a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can a short bob hairstyle be customized?

Yes, a short bob hairstyle can be customized with layers, bangs, or an asymmetrical cut to suit your personal style.

How do I care for my new short bob hairstyle?

Use hair care products designed for fine, straight hair, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair. Regular trims can also help to maintain your new cut.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your fine, straight hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for fine hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short bob hairstyle that flatters your fine, straight hair over 60. Whether you opt for a classic cut or a customized style, your new 'do is sure to turn heads!
