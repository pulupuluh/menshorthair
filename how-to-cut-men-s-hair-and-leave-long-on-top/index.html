How To Cut Men s Hair And Leave Long On Top

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and leave it long on top? This popular men's cut is trendy and versatile, but it's important to know how to cut it properly to achieve the desired look. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair and leave long on top, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Men's Hair and Leave Long on Top

The first step is to decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will determine how much hair you need to leave during the cutting process. Once you've decided on the length, follow these steps:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair so that you can see the natural texture and shape of the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Section the hair

Use a comb to section the hair on top and clip it up. This will make it easier to focus on the sides and back first.

3. Cut the sides and back

Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line. You can also use a fade technique to blend the hair into the longer hair on top.

4. Blend the sides and top

Use scissors to blend the sides into the longer hair on top. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to create a seamless transition.

5. Cut the hair on top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Start at the back and work your way forward, taking small sections of hair and cutting at an angle to create texture and movement.

6. Style the hair

Use a styling product like pomade or wax to style the hair on top. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy and tousled.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to cutting men's hair and leaving it long on top:

1. Gather your tools

You'll need a comb, scissors, clippers, and a styling product like pomade or wax.

2. Wash and dry the hair

Start with clean, dry hair so that you can see the natural texture and shape of the hair.

3. Section the hair

Use a comb to section the hair on top and clip it up. This will make it easier to focus on the sides and back first.

4. Cut the sides and back

Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line. You can also use a fade technique to blend the hair into the longer hair on top.

5. Blend the sides and top

Use scissors to blend the sides into the longer hair on top. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to create a seamless transition.

6. Cut the hair on top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Start at the back and work your way forward, taking small sections of hair and cutting at an angle to create texture and movement.

7. Style the hair

Use a styling product like pomade or wax to style the hair on top. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy and tousled.

Tips for a Great Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair and leaving it long on top:

  • Start with clean, dry hair so that you can see the natural texture and shape of the hair.
  • Use scissors to create texture and movement on top.
  • Blend the sides into the longer hair on top for a seamless transition.
  • Experiment with different styling products to achieve the desired look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair and leaving it long on top:

Q. Can I cut my own hair?

A. It's possible to cut your own hair, but it's recommended to go to a professional for the best results.

Q. How often should I get my hair cut?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the style. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Q. What if I don't like the way it looks?

A. If you're not happy with the way your hair looks, you can always go back to the stylist for adjustments or try a different style.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut men's hair and leave it long on top like a pro. Whether you're looking for a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured style, this versatile cut is perfect for any occasion.
