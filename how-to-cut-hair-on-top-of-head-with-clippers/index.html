How To Cut Hair On Top Of Head With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for an easy way to cut your hair on top of your head, using clippers is a great option. Not only is it quick and efficient, but it also allows you to achieve a more professional-looking haircut. In this blog post, we'll go over how to cut hair on top of head with clippers in a relaxed English language. We'll cover everything you need to know, including how to prepare your hair, what tools you'll need, and step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect haircut. Let's get started!

Description

Cutting your hair on top of your head with clippers is a great way to achieve a neat and tidy haircut. It's especially useful if you want to maintain a short hairstyle, but it can also be used for longer styles. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to get started with cutting your own hair at home.

How to Cut Hair on Top of Head with Clippers

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step in cutting your hair with clippers is to prepare it for cutting. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to dry completely. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut. If your hair is particularly thick or curly, you may want to consider using a conditioner to help soften it.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

The next step is to gather all the tools you'll need to cut your hair. You'll need clippers, a comb, and scissors. Make sure your clippers are fully charged and have a sharp blade. You may also want to consider using a mirror to help you see the back of your head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting your hair. Begin by using the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Use the longest guard attachment to start, and then work your way down to a shorter length. Remember to use a comb to guide the clippers and keep your hair in place.

Once you've finished trimming the sides and back, move on to the top of your head. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed. Start with small snips and work your way up to larger sections if necessary.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to check your work. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure everything looks even. If you notice any uneven spots, use the clippers or scissors to make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Hair on Top of Head with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter length
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and keep your hair in place
  • Take your time and make small snips with the scissors
  • Check your work in a mirror to ensure everything looks even

FAQ

Can I cut my hair on top of my head with clippers if I have long hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut longer hair on top of your head. However, you may need to use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

What guard attachment should I use to cut my hair on top of my head?

Start with a longer guard attachment, such as a #6 or #8, and then work your way down to a shorter length if necessary.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you have any concerns about your hair, consult with a hair care professional.

Conclusion

Cutting your hair on top of your head with clippers is a great way to achieve a neat and tidy haircut. By following these simple steps, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut right in the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and check your work as you go. Good luck!
