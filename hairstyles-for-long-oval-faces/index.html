Best Hairstyles For Long Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Sep 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This shape is considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. However, if you have a long oval face, you might find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. In this guide, we've rounded up the best hairstyles for long oval faces, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

What is a Long Oval Face Shape?

A long oval face shape is characterized by a longer shape than the typical oval face shape. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, and the face has a slight taper towards the chin. If you're not sure if you have a long oval face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they're all similar, and your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long oval face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few ways to determine it. One way is to take a selfie and draw an outline of your face shape using a photo editing tool. Another way is to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, as we mentioned earlier. You can also use a mirror to trace the outline of your face with a dry erase marker and see what shape it resembles the most.

Best Hairstyles for Long Oval Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long oval faces because they add volume and dimension to the hair, which can balance out the length of the face. They're also versatile and work well with both straight and curly hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the face and draw attention to the eyes. They work well with both long and short hair and can be styled straight or wavy.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut that falls just below the chin can help balance out the length of a long oval face. It's a classic and timeless look that works well with straight and wavy hair.

4. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for long oval faces because they add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the length of the face. They're also low-maintenance and easy to style.

5. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for long oval faces. It can help shorten the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. It's also low-maintenance and easy to style.

How to Style Your Hair for a Long Oval Face

When styling your hair for a long oval face, it's important to focus on adding volume and texture to the hair. This can help balance out the length of the face and make it appear shorter. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add volume to the roots.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add waves or curls to the hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume and body to the hair.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make the face appear even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as they can make the face appear even longer.

Can I wear a middle part with a long oval face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a long oval face, but it's best to add some volume to the hair to balance out the length of the face.

Should I get bangs if I have a long oval face?

Side-swept bangs are a great option for long oval faces because they can help shorten the face and draw attention to the eyes. However, if you prefer not to have bangs, that's perfectly fine too!

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a long oval face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that can flatter a long oval face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, a bob haircut, beachy waves, and a pixie cut are all great options to consider. Remember to focus on adding volume and texture to the hair to balance out the length of the face. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face shape!
