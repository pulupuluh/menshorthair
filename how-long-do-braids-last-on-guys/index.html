How Long Do Braids Last On Guys

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting braids but wondering how long they will last on guys? Braids are a popular hairstyle for both men and women, but the longevity of the style can vary depending on several factors. In this article, we will discuss how long braids typically last on guys and provide tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Get Braids on Guys
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Braids on Guys
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care for Braids on Guys

How to Get Braids on Guys

Before discussing how long braids last on guys, it's essential to know how to get them. If you're new to braiding, it's best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist who specializes in braids. They can help you choose the type of braids that suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

When getting braids, ensure that your hair is clean and free of any products. Your hairstylist will then section your hair and start braiding from the root. The braids can be styled in various ways, from simple cornrows to intricate designs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding Hair

If you want to try braiding your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean hair and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb.
  2. Section your hair into small parts and secure them with hair ties.
  3. Take one section and divide it into three equal parts.
  4. Cross the right strand over the middle strand, then cross the left strand over the new middle strand.
  5. Repeat step four until you reach the end of the section.
  6. Secure the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.
  7. Repeat the process on the remaining sections.

Tips for Maintaining Braids on Guys

The longevity of braids on guys can vary depending on how well they are maintained. Here are some tips for keeping your braids looking fresh:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your scalp clean.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your braids from frizzing.
  • Wear a hat or scarf when going outside to protect your hair from the sun and wind.
  • Avoid excessive tugging, pulling, or twisting of your braids, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do braids typically last on guys?

The length of time braids last on guys can vary depending on several factors, such as hair texture, the type of braid, and how well they are maintained. Generally, braids can last anywhere from two to eight weeks.

Can you wash your hair with braids?

Yes, you can wash your hair with braids. It's essential to keep your scalp clean to prevent buildup and maintain healthy hair. When washing your hair, be gentle and avoid scrubbing your scalp too hard, as this can cause your braids to unravel.

Can guys with short hair get braids?

Yes, guys with short hair can get braids. However, it's essential to have enough hair length to braid. Your hairstylist can help you determine if your hair is long enough for braids and recommend the best type of braid for your hair length and texture.

Hair Care for Braids on Guys

Braids on guys require proper hair care to maintain their longevity and keep your hair healthy. Here are some hair care tips for braids on guys:

  • Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids and cause buildup.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Take breaks between braiding to allow your hair to breathe and prevent damage.

Now that you know how long braids typically last on guys and how to maintain them, you can confidently rock this trendy hairstyle. Whether you prefer simple cornrows or intricate designs, braids are a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can elevate your look.
