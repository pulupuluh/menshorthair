Easy To Maintain Medium Length Haircuts For Mildly Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and complements your mildly wavy hair? You’re in luck because we’ve got you covered with some of the best medium length haircuts that are perfect for those with mildly wavy hair. These haircuts are not only easy to maintain but also versatile and stylish, making them perfect for any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length haircuts are a popular choice for many women as they offer the perfect balance between long and short hair. They are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions. Mildly wavy hair is also a great texture as it adds volume and movement to the hair, making it look more lively and dynamic. With the right haircut, you can enhance your wavy hair’s natural beauty and create a look that is both effortless and chic.

How to

When choosing a medium length haircut for mildly wavy hair, it’s important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Some haircuts may look better on certain face shapes than others, and some haircuts may require more maintenance than others. It’s also important to communicate your preferences and lifestyle with your hairstylist so they can recommend a haircut that suits your needs.

Step by Step

Here are some easy to maintain medium length haircuts for mildly wavy hair:

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a popular haircut that is both stylish and easy to maintain. It’s a versatile haircut that can be worn straight or wavy, and it looks great on both thin and thick hair. To achieve the lob, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder length with a few face-framing layers. This will add movement to your hair and make it look more voluminous.

The Shag

The shag is another popular haircut that is perfect for those with mildly wavy hair. It’s a layered haircut that adds texture and volume to the hair, making it look more lively and dynamic. To achieve the shag, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers starting from your chin and working their way down. This will create a choppy, textured look that is perfect for those who want a more edgy and modern hairstyle.

The Blunt Cut

The blunt cut is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. It’s a simple yet elegant haircut that looks great on everyone. To achieve the blunt cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair straight across at shoulder length. This will create a clean and polished look that is perfect for those who want a more sophisticated hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length haircut:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip the natural oils from your hair
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

What face shape is best for a lob?

A lob looks great on most face shapes, but it’s particularly flattering on those with a heart-shaped face, as it draws attention to the jawline and cheekbones.

What is the best styling product for mildly wavy hair?

The best styling product for mildly wavy hair depends on your preference and the look you’re trying to achieve. Some popular styling products include mousse, texturizing spray, and sea salt spray.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length haircut looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. This includes using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and protecting your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right medium length haircut, you can enhance your mildly wavy hair’s natural beauty and create a look that is both effortless and chic. Whether you opt for the lob, shag, or blunt cut, these haircuts are easy to maintain and perfect for any occasion.
