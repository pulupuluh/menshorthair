Why Do I Have Less Hair On The Sides Of My Head

Written by Petter Sep 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people experience hair loss or thinning on the sides of their head, which can be frustrating and difficult to deal with. This type of hair loss is often referred to as “male pattern baldness” or “female pattern baldness,” and it is a common condition that affects both men and women. While it can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, and hormonal imbalances, there are several steps you can take to help prevent and treat hair loss on the sides of your head.

How to

One of the most effective ways to prevent hair loss on the sides of your head is to maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle. This means eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, and getting plenty of exercise and sleep. You should also avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these habits can contribute to hair loss and thinning.

Another way to prevent hair loss on the sides of your head is to avoid wearing tight hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as cornrows and braids. These styles can cause tension and damage to the hair follicles, which can lead to hair loss over time.

If you are already experiencing hair loss on the sides of your head, there are several treatments that may be effective. These include medications like minoxidil and finasteride, which can help to stimulate hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Hair transplant surgery is also an option for some people, although it is expensive and may not be covered by insurance.

Step by step

If you are experiencing hair loss on the sides of your head, here are some steps you can take to help prevent and treat it:

  1. Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  2. Get plenty of exercise and sleep
  3. Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  4. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  5. Consider using medications like minoxidil and finasteride
  6. Consider hair transplant surgery

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help prevent and treat hair loss on the sides of your head:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage the hair
  • Consider using a hair growth supplement or vitamin

FAQ

Q: Is hair loss on the sides of the head more common in men or women?

A: Both men and women can experience hair loss on the sides of their head, although it is more common in men.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss on the sides of the head?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss and thinning on the sides of the head. Managing stress through relaxation techniques and self-care can help to prevent hair loss.

Q: Are there any home remedies that can help prevent hair loss on the sides of the head?

A: While there are many home remedies that claim to help prevent hair loss, there is limited scientific evidence to support their effectiveness. However, some people find that using essential oils like rosemary and peppermint oil can help to stimulate hair growth.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for preventing and treating hair loss on the sides of the head. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get plenty of exercise and sleep
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help to prevent and treat hair loss on the sides of your head.
