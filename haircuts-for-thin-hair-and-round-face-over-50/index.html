Haircuts For Thin Hair And Round Face Over 50

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut to flatter your thin hair and round face? If you're over 50, you may feel like your options are limited, but that's simply not true! Here are some great haircuts that will work wonders for those with thin hair and a round face.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose volume. This can be especially true for those with a round face, as it can accentuate the roundness. However, with the right haircut, you can still look and feel fabulous at any age.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect haircut is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to focus on styles that elongate the face and add volume to the crown. A layered cut with long layers can be a great option, as it adds movement and volume without making the face appear wider.

Another option is a bob, but make sure it's a longer bob that hits just below the chin. This will help elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness. A side-swept fringe can also be a great addition, as it can help create the illusion of a longer face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape: Round

2. Choose a layered cut with long layers or a longer bob

3. Add a side-swept fringe for extra dimension

4. Use volumizing products to add body and movement to your hair

5. Style your hair with a round brush to create volume at the crown

Tips

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, use volumizing products like mousse or root lifter to add body and movement. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the crown, and use a flat iron to add subtle waves or curls.

Another tip is to avoid heavy bangs or blunt cuts, as these can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for soft layers or a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of length.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a round face and thin hair?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to add layers and volume to the crown to create movement and texture.

Q: What styling products should I use?

A: Volumizing products like mousse or root lifter can be great for adding body and movement. You can also use a flat iron to add subtle waves or curls.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your thin hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, be gentle when brushing or styling your hair to avoid breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles, and avoid pulling or tugging at your hair.

Another tip is to avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can also lead to breakage. If you do use heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Finally, make sure to keep your hair healthy by getting regular trims and using high-quality products. A good shampoo and conditioner can make all the difference in keeping your hair looking and feeling its best.

Conclusion

With the right haircut and styling tips, you can still look and feel fabulous with thin hair and a round face over 50. Remember to focus on styles that elongate the face and add volume to the crown, and use volumizing products and gentle styling techniques to keep your hair healthy and full of life.
