How Do You Know If Bangs Look Good On You

Description

Thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Bangs can be a big change and it's important to make sure they will look good on you before taking the plunge. In this article, we will provide tips and advice on how to know if bangs look good on you.

How To

The best way to determine if bangs will look good on you is to consider your face shape. Bangs generally look great on people with oval, heart, and square-shaped faces, but may not be as flattering on those with round or long faces.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. You can also take a photo of yourself and trace the outline of your face on paper.

Step 2: Research different types of bangs that suit your face shape. For example, side-swept bangs are great for heart and oval-shaped faces, while blunt bangs work well on square-shaped faces.

Step 3: Consider your hair texture and thickness. Bangs can be difficult to style if you have thick or curly hair, so make sure to consult with your stylist before making a decision.

Step 4: Try on different types of wigs or use virtual try-on apps to see how bangs will look on you before committing to a cut.

Tips

1. Consider the length of your forehead. If you have a short forehead, avoid thick blunt bangs that will make your forehead appear even shorter.

2. Choose the right length. Shorter bangs work well on those with smaller foreheads, while longer bangs are better for those with larger foreheads.

3. Consult with your stylist before making a decision. They can provide expert advice on what type of bangs will work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will bangs make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the type of bangs and your face shape. Blunt bangs can make a round face appear wider, while side-swept bangs can elongate the face.

Q: Can I still wear hairstyles without bangs if I get them cut?

A: Yes, you can still wear hairstyles without bangs. However, keep in mind that bangs will require more maintenance and styling.

Hair Care

Once you have decided to get bangs, it's important to take good care of them. Here are some tips:

1. Trim them regularly to keep them looking fresh.

2. Use a small amount of styling product to keep them in place.

3. Wash them regularly to prevent oil buildup.

4. Blow dry them straight or use a straightener to keep them looking sleek.

5. Consider getting a fringe trimmer to maintain your bangs at home.

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine whether bangs will look good on you.
