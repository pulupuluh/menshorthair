How To Get Straight Hair Curly For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a guy with straight hair who wants to try out a curly hairstyle? You're not alone! Many men with straight hair dream of having bouncy, voluminous curls. Fortunately, with a few tricks and some styling products, you can easily transform your straight hair into a curly masterpiece.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Straight Hair Curly for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting and Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Straight Hair Curly for Guys

Before we dive into the specifics of how to get curly hair, it's important to understand that there are different ways to achieve curly hair depending on your hair type and desired level of curliness. Here are a few methods:

Using Hair Rollers or Curling Irons

One of the easiest ways to get curly hair is to use hair rollers or a curling iron. Hair rollers come in various sizes, so choose the size that will give you the curls you want. Curling irons also come in different sizes, and they are heat-based tools that can give you more defined curls. However, using heat regularly can also damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron.

Perming Your Hair

If you want a more permanent solution for curly hair, you can get a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly. However, perms can be expensive and damaging to your hair if not done correctly, so make sure to go to a professional stylist.

Using Hair Products

Another way to get curly hair is to use styling products specifically designed for curly hair. These products can enhance your natural curl pattern or create curls if you don't have any. Look for products like mousse, curl enhancers, or curl defining creams.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of the different methods for getting curly hair, here is a step-by-step guide on how to get curly hair using hair rollers:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the hair rollers.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section onto a hair roller, starting from the ends and rolling up to the roots.
  4. Leave the hair rollers in your hair for at least an hour, or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Remove the hair rollers gently and use your fingers to loosen the curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Getting and Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get and maintain curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your curls as it can damage them. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I get curly hair naturally?

A: Yes, some people have naturally curly hair. However, if you have straight hair, you can use the methods mentioned above to get curly hair.

Q: How long do hair rollers need to be in my hair to get curly hair?

A: Hair rollers need to be in your hair for at least an hour, or until your hair is completely dry.

Q: Will using heat to curl my hair damage it?

A: Using heat regularly can damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or other heat-based tool.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are a few hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can cause dryness and frizz. Use lukewarm or cool water instead.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your curls.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily get curly hair and keep it looking healthy and bouncy. Remember to choose the method that works best for you and your hair type, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
