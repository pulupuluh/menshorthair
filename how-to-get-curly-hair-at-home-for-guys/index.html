How To Get Curly Hair At Home For Guys In 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to switch to a curly hair look? Don't worry! In this article, we will guide you on how to get curly hair at home for guys. Whether you have short hair or long hair, we have got you covered. So, let's get started with this step-by-step guide.

Table of Content

  • How to Get Curly Hair at Home for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair at Home for Guys

Before we start with the process, let's understand the two methods of getting curly hair at home for guys.

Method 1: Heat Treatment

This method involves using a curling iron or a flat iron to create curls. It is a quick and easy way to get curly hair at home. However, it may damage your hair if not done properly.

Method 2: Natural Treatment

This method involves using natural ingredients to create curls. It is a safer way to get curly hair at home, but it may take some time to see the results.

Step-by-Step Guide

Method 1: Heat Treatment

Follow these steps to get curly hair at home using heat treatment:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Take a curling iron and wrap a section of hair around it, holding it for 5-10 seconds.
  5. Repeat the process for all sections of hair.
  6. Apply a hair spray to set the curls.

Method 2: Natural Treatment

Follow these steps to get curly hair at home using natural treatment:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product such as mousse or gel to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair and secure it with a clip.
  5. Leave the clips on for a few hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the clips and gently shake your hair to loosen the curls.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to get the best results while getting curly hair at home:

  • Choose the right curling iron size depending on the length of your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron.
  • Don't use a curling iron on wet hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Don't wash your hair too often as it may strip your hair of its natural oils.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair at home for guys:

Q. How long will the curls last?

A. The curls will last for a day or two depending on your hair type and the method you have used.

Q. Is it safe to use heat treatment to get curly hair?

A. Heat treatment is safe as long as you use a heat protectant spray and don't overuse it.

Q. Can I get curly hair at home if I have short hair?

A. Yes, you can get curly hair at home even if you have short hair. Use a small curling iron or twist small sections of hair and secure them with clips.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Once you have got curly hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Don't use a brush on your curly hair as it may cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to avoid frizz.

Now that you know how to get curly hair at home for guys, try out these methods and see which one works best for you. Remember to take care of your curly hair to maintain its health and shine. Happy styling!
