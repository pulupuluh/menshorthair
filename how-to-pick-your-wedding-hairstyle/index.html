How To Pick Your Wedding Hairstyle Tips Faq And Hair Care

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task. Do you want to wear your hair up or down? Do you want to add some accessories or leave it simple? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we will provide you with all the information you need to pick your wedding hairstyle. We'll cover tips on how to choose the right style, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to ensure your locks look their best on your big day.

Table of Contents

  • Tips on How to Choose the Right Style
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Tips on How to Choose the Right Style

Before you start browsing Pinterest for inspiration, there are a few things you should consider when picking your wedding hairstyle.

Consider Your Dress

Your wedding dress will set the tone for your entire bridal look. If your dress has a lot of intricate details, you may want to opt for a simpler hairstyle to avoid clashing. Alternatively, if your dress is simple and elegant, you can get away with a more elaborate hairstyle.

Think About Your Venue

The location and theme of your wedding can also play a role in determining your hairstyle. For example, if you're having a beach wedding, you may want to consider a hairstyle that can withstand the wind and humidity. If you're having a formal indoor wedding, an updo may be more appropriate.

Consider Your Hair Length and Texture

If you have short hair, you may want to consider adding hair extensions to achieve the style you want. Similarly, if you have curly hair, you may want to straighten it for a sleeker look.

Don't Forget Your Accessories

Your wedding hairstyle can be enhanced with the right accessories. Consider adding a headband, tiara, or veil to complete your look. Just be sure to choose accessories that complement your dress and don't overpower your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Far in Advance Should I Choose My Wedding Hairstyle?

We recommend choosing your wedding hairstyle at least three months before your wedding. This will give you enough time to schedule a trial run with your hairstylist and make any necessary adjustments.

Should I Wash My Hair on My Wedding Day?

It's best to wash your hair the day before your wedding. This will give your hair enough time to dry and settle into its natural texture. Avoid washing your hair on the day of your wedding, as freshly washed hair can be harder to style.

How Do I Choose the Right Hairstylist?

Start by asking for referrals from friends and family who have had positive experiences with hairstylists. Research potential hairstylists online and read reviews from past clients. Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss your vision and ensure that they understand your needs.

Hair Care Tips

Healthy hair is crucial for achieving a beautiful wedding hairstyle. Here are some tips to ensure your locks are in top shape on your big day.

Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to achieve the style you want. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on the lower side.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

A deep conditioning treatment can help nourish your hair and make it more manageable. Consider using a treatment once a week leading up to your wedding day.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep split ends at bay and ensure that your hair looks healthy and full. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks leading up to your wedding day.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp hydrated, which can improve overall hair health. Aim to drink at least eight glasses of water a day.

With these tips, FAQ, and hair care advice, you'll be well on your way to picking the perfect wedding hairstyle. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and you'll shine on your big day.
