Youtube How To Cut Your Own Hair Medium Length

Written by Petter Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for haircuts every few months? Or maybe you just want to try something new and cut your hair yourself. Whatever your reason may be, cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have medium-length hair. But fear not, with the help of Youtube, you can learn how to cut your own hair medium length and achieve the perfect look you desire.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it can also be overwhelming if you don't know where to start. Luckily, Youtube has a plethora of videos that can guide you through the process of cutting your own hair medium length. These videos range from simple tutorials to more in-depth guides, depending on your skill level and desired outcome.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Medium Length

Before you begin cutting your hair, it's important to have the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you begin cutting. Wet hair can be harder to cut, and you want to make sure your hair is free of any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Using your comb, section off your hair into smaller, manageable sections. Clip each section out of the way as you go.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section, use your scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way up, cutting each section to the same length until you reach the top of your head.

Step 4: Trim the Ends

Once you've cut your hair to the desired length, use your scissors to trim the ends. This will give your hair a polished look and help prevent split ends.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can blow dry, straighten, or curl your hair to achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make your hair cutting experience go smoothly:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to larger ones. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's been cut.
  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break if you need one.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair medium length:

Is it hard to cut your own hair?

It can be challenging to cut your own hair, especially if you're not familiar with the process. However, with practice and the right tools, anyone can learn how to cut their own hair.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always trim the hair to even it out, or you can visit a salon to have it fixed.

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's curly. Just make sure you cut it when it's dry, as wet curly hair can be harder to cut.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.

With the help of Youtube and these tips, you can learn how to cut your own hair medium length and achieve the look you desire. Happy cutting!
