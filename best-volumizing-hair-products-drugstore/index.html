Best Volumizing Hair Products From The Drugstore Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide to the best volumizing hair products from the drugstore. In this post, we’ll be sharing our top picks for products that will give your hair that coveted volume and bounce, without breaking the bank. From shampoos and conditioners to styling products, we’ve got you covered.

Why Choose Volumizing Hair Products?

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? If you’re looking for a way to add some volume and bounce to your locks, volumizing hair products are a great option. Not only do they add volume, but they can also make your hair look fuller and thicker. Plus, many volumizing products are designed to be lightweight, so you don’t have to worry about weighing down your hair.

How to Choose the Right Volumizing Hair Products

When it comes to choosing the right volumizing hair products, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your hair type. If you have fine hair, you’ll want to look for lightweight products that won’t weigh down your strands. If you have thick hair, you may be able to handle heavier products.

Next, think about your styling routine. If you like to blow dry your hair, look for products that provide heat protection. If you prefer to air dry, look for products that enhance your natural texture.

Finally, consider your budget. While there are plenty of high-end volumizing hair products out there, there are also plenty of affordable options at the drugstore.

Top Volumizing Shampoos and Conditioners

L'Oreal Paris Elvive Volume Filler Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner

This shampoo and conditioner duo is designed to add volume and thickness to fine, flat hair. The formula is enriched with Filloxane, which penetrates deep into the hair fiber to create a thicker, fuller look. Plus, it’s safe for color-treated hair.

OGX Bamboo Fiber-Full Shampoo and Conditioner

This shampoo and conditioner set contains bamboo fibers and soy proteins to help add volume and body to your hair. The formula is also sulfate-free, so it won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.

Top Volumizing Styling Products

TRESemme TRES Two Extra Hold Hair Spray

This hair spray is designed to provide extra hold and control, while also adding volume to your hair. The formula is humidity-resistant, so your style will stay in place all day.

Not Your Mother's Beach Babe Texturizing Sea Salt Spray

This sea salt spray is perfect for adding texture and volume to your hair. The formula contains sea salt and sea kelp to help create beachy waves and a tousled look.

Volumizing Hair Product Tips

When using volumizing hair products, there are a few tips to keep in mind for best results:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Apply volumizing products to damp hair
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume
  • Use a round brush to create lift at the roots

Volumizing Hair Product FAQs

Can volumizing hair products damage my hair?

Most volumizing hair products are designed to be gentle and safe for daily use. However, if you have particularly sensitive hair, it’s always a good idea to patch test a product before using it all over your hair.

Do I need to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner together?

While using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner together can provide the best results, it’s not always necessary. If you have a favorite shampoo or conditioner that you love, you can always pair it with a volumizing styling product for added volume.

Can volumizing products work on curly hair?

Absolutely! Volumizing products can work on all hair types, including curly hair. Just be sure to choose products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Conclusion

There you have it – our ultimate guide to the best volumizing hair products from the drugstore. Whether you’re looking for a volumizing shampoo and conditioner or a styling product to add some lift to your locks, there are plenty of affordable options out there. Just remember to choose products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and follow our tips for best results.
