How To Know What Haircut Suits You A Comprehensive Guide

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it can also be overwhelming. With so many options to choose from, it can be difficult to know what haircut suits you. That's why we've put together this comprehensive guide to help you figure out which hairstyle will look best on you. From face shape to hair texture, we'll cover everything you need to know to make an informed decision.

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors in choosing a new haircut is your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's important to determine which category you fall into. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and identify the widest part of your face. Then, measure the length of your face from forehead to chin. Finally, compare the two measurements to determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut is your hair texture. Different hair textures suit different hairstyles, so it's important to understand what type of hair you have. There are four basic hair textures:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

If you're not sure what your hair texture is, try running your fingers through your hair when it's dry. If your hair feels smooth and straight, you have straight hair. If it has a slight wave or bend, you have wavy hair. If it forms loose or tight curls, you have curly hair. If it forms tight coils or ringlets, you have kinky hair.

Choosing Your Hair Length

Once you know your face shape and hair texture, you can start thinking about what length of hair will suit you best. There are a few general guidelines to keep in mind:

  • Oval faces can pull off almost any length of hair
  • Round faces look best with longer hair that has layers and volume
  • Square faces look best with hair that falls just below the jawline
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with chin-length or longer hair
  • Diamond-shaped faces look best with hair that falls just above or below the shoulders
  • Triangle-shaped faces look best with hair that falls just above or below the shoulders

Picking the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can also make a big difference in how your haircut looks. Here are a few things to consider when picking a hair color:

  • Your skin tone: Warmer skin tones look best with warm hair colors, while cooler skin tones look best with cool hair colors.
  • Your natural hair color: If you're not looking for a dramatic change, try sticking close to your natural hair color.
  • Your lifestyle: If you're someone who spends a lot of time outdoors, a low-maintenance hair color might be the way to go.

Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you've determined your face shape, hair texture, and ideal hair length and color, it's time to start looking for your perfect hairstyle. Here are some popular hairstyles to consider:

  • Bob
  • Layered
  • Pixie
  • Bangs
  • Shag
  • Curls
  • Braids
  • Ponytail

Remember, the key to finding the perfect hairstyle is to choose one that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. It's also important to choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable wearing.

Expert Tips for Choosing a Haircut

Here are a few expert tips to keep in mind when choosing a new haircut:

  • Bring in pictures: If you're not sure how to describe the haircut you want, bring in pictures to show your stylist.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're someone who doesn't like to spend a lot of time styling your hair, choose a low-maintenance haircut.
  • Be open to change: Sometimes, the best haircut is one that's completely different from what you're used to.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new haircut:

  • What if I don't like my new haircut?
  • Can I change my haircut if it doesn't suit me?
  • How often should I get a haircut?
  • What if I have thinning hair?
  • What if I want to try a bold new hair color?

Hair Care Tips for Your New Haircut

Once you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can ensure that your new haircut looks great for weeks to come.
