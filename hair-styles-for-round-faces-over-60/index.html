Best Hair Styles For Round Faces Over 60

Written by Aprilia Sep 01, 2023 · 9 min read

As we age, our hair changes in many ways, from thinning to graying. However, one thing that remains constant is the importance of finding a hairstyle that flatters our face shape. If you have a round face and are over 60, this guide is for you. We'll cover everything you need to know about choosing the right hair style, including tips for maintaining healthy hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Style

Choosing the right hair style can make all the difference when it comes to looking and feeling your best. Here are some tips for selecting a style that flatters your round face:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The first thing to think about when choosing a hair style is your hair texture. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume, as they can make your face look even rounder. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to help distribute the weight of your hair.

Think About Your Face Shape

When it comes to hair styles for round faces, you want to avoid anything that emphasizes the roundness of your face. Instead, look for styles that create the illusion of length and angles. For example, a layered cut that falls just below your chin can help elongate your face.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in your hair style choice. If you're someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance, you may want to opt for a wash-and-go style that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different looks, you may want to choose a more versatile cut that you can style in different ways.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for in a hair style, here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect cut:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right hair style, you need to know your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a look at the shape of your face – is it round, oval, square, or heart-shaped?

Step 2: Research Hair Styles

Once you know your face shape, it's time to start researching hair styles that flatter your features. Look for styles that create angles and length, such as layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts with longer layers on top.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

When you've found a few styles you like, bring them to your stylist and ask for their opinion. They may have suggestions for tweaks that can make a style work better for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Try it Out

Once you've settled on a style, it's time to give it a try. Keep in mind that it may take a few weeks for your hair to adjust to a new cut, so be patient and give yourself time to get used to the change.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what hair style you choose, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products for your hair type can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are specifically formulated for mature hair, which tends to be drier and more brittle.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools, and when you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, even if you're growing your hair out.

FAQ

What is the best hair style for a round face over 60?

The best hair style for a round face over 60 is one that creates the illusion of length and angles. This might include a layered cut, a long bob, or a pixie cut with longer layers on top.

What haircuts should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid haircuts that emphasize the roundness of the face. This might include blunt cuts that fall at the chin or shorter, choppy styles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, there are a few things you can do to keep your locks looking their best:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps keep your scalp clean and healthy. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type.

Invest in a Good Brush

A good brush can make all the difference when it comes to detangling and styling your hair. Look for a brush that's gentle on your hair and scalp, such as a boar bristle brush.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To protect your locks, wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when you're spending time outdoors.

With the right hair style and proper care, you can keep your locks looking vibrant and healthy well into your golden years. Use these tips to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair texture, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
