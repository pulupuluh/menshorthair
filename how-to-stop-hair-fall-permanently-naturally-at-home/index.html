How To Stop Hair Fall Permanently Naturally At Home

Written by William Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of seeing your hair fall out every time you brush or wash it? Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. While there are many products available in the market that claim to stop hair fall, they often come with harmful chemicals that can damage your hair in the long run. In this article, we will explore some natural remedies that can help you stop hair fall permanently at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Stop Hair Fall Permanently Naturally at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Massage your scalp with warm oil: Massaging your scalp with warm oil can help improve blood circulation in the scalp, thereby promoting hair growth. You can use coconut, olive, or almond oil for this purpose. Warm the oil slightly and massage it into your scalp in circular motions. Leave it on for an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Use onion juice: Onion juice has been found to be effective in promoting hair growth. It contains sulfur, which helps improve blood circulation in the scalp, and promotes the production of collagen, which is essential for hair growth. To use onion juice, grate an onion and squeeze out its juice. Apply it to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Apply aloe vera gel: Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help promote hair growth. It also has anti-inflammatory properties that can help soothe an irritated scalp. Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Eat a balanced diet: Your diet plays a crucial role in maintaining healthy hair. Make sure you are eating a diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

1. Avoid using hot styling tools: Hot styling tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Avoid using these tools as much as possible.

2. Use a mild shampoo: Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Use a mild shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens to keep your hair healthy.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, can pull on your hair and cause it to fall out. Avoid wearing these hairstyles for long periods of time.

FAQs

Q. How long will it take to see results?

A. It may take several weeks or even months to see results, as natural remedies take time to work.

Q. Are these remedies safe?

A. Yes, these remedies are safe and natural. However, if you have any allergies or medical conditions, it is best to consult a doctor before trying them.

Hair Care Routine

1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a mild shampoo.

2. Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair soft and manageable.

3. Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause it to fall out.

4. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it is less likely to cause hair fall.

5. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

By following these natural remedies and tips, you can stop hair fall permanently at home. Remember to be patient, as natural remedies take time to work. With a little bit of effort and consistency, you can have healthy, luscious locks that you can be proud of.
