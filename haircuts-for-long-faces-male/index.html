Haircuts For Long Faces Male Finding The Perfect Style

Are you a man with a long face looking for a new haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long faces male, as well as some tips and tricks for hair care.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces can be tricky when it comes to haircuts. The goal is to find a style that will balance out the length of the face while accentuating your best features. A good haircut can also make you appear more youthful and stylish. So, let's get started!

How To Choose the Right Haircut?

When choosing a haircut, keep in mind your face shape, hair type, and personal style. For long faces, aim for styles that add width and volume to the sides of your face, while avoiding styles that elongate your face even more.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for long faces, as long as they don't add too much height on top. A short, textured crop can add volume to the sides of your face, while a sleek buzz cut can make your features stand out.

Medium-Length Haircuts

For medium-length haircuts, try a layered cut or a side-swept fringe. These styles will add volume to the sides of your face, while drawing attention away from the length of your face.

Long Haircuts

Long hair can be tricky for long faces, but with the right cut, it can look great. Try a layered cut with side-swept bangs or a messy, textured style. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face look even longer.

Step by Step: How to Get the Perfect Haircut

Follow these steps to ensure you get the perfect haircut for your long face:

  1. Research hairstyles that work well for long faces, and choose a few that you like.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist and show them the styles you have in mind.
  3. Consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a style.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to add volume to the sides of your face, while avoiding styles that add height on top.
  5. Take care of your hair between cuts to ensure it looks its best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid haircuts that add height on top of your head, such as slicked-back styles or high fades. Blunt, straight cuts can also make your face look even longer.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

What products should I use to style my hair?

Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type. Some popular options include pomade, wax, and gel.

Hair Care for Long Faces Male

Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the health of your hair.

Now that you know the best haircuts for long faces male and how to take care of your hair, it's time to book your next haircut appointment. With the right style and care, you'll be looking and feeling your best in no time!
