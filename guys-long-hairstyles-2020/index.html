Guys Long Hairstyles 2020 How To Keep Up With The Trend

Written by Daniel Apr 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair has always been a popular trend among guys, and 2020 was no exception. With the pandemic forcing many salons to close their doors, more men than ever decided to grow out their locks. In this blog post, we'll discuss some of the top guys long hairstyles of 2020 and give you some tips on how to maintain them in 2023.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The long hair trend for guys in 2020 was all about embracing natural texture and movement. From messy, beachy waves to sleek, polished styles, there was a long hairstyle to suit every guy's personality and lifestyle. Some of the most popular styles included:

  • Long, layered hair with a side part
  • Curly or wavy hair with a messy, tousled look
  • Slicked-back hair with a low, tight bun or ponytail
  • Braided hair with a half-up, half-down style

How to

If you're looking to rock a long hairstyle in 2023, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First and foremost, be patient. Growing out your hair can take time, and there will be awkward stages along the way. But with a little patience and perseverance, you'll eventually achieve your desired length.

Next, consider your hair type and face shape when choosing a long hairstyle. If you have thick, curly hair, a messy, textured style might work best. If you have fine, straight hair, a sleek, polished look might be more your speed. And if you have a round face, a side part can help create the illusion of length.

Step by step

Here are some general steps to follow when styling your long hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to help create texture and hold.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool, such as a curling iron or straightener, to create your desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to help hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your long hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools and products to help achieve your desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider consulting with a professional stylist for personalized advice and recommendations.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys long hairstyles:

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most guys can get away with washing their hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I still wear hats with long hair?

A: Yes! Just make sure to adjust your hat size accordingly to accommodate your longer locks.

Q: Will long hair make me look unprofessional?

A: Not necessarily. As long as your hair is clean and well-groomed, you can still look professional with long hair. Just be mindful of any dress codes or workplace policies that may apply.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your long hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you can keep up with the guys long hairstyles trend in 2023 and beyond!
