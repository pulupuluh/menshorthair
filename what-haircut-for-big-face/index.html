What Haircut Is Best For A Big Face

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 · 9 min read

Do you have a big face and you’re unsure of what haircut to get? Choosing the right haircut can make all the difference in enhancing your facial features, so it’s important to pick a style that flatters your face shape. In this article, we’ll explore what haircut for big face is best, and provide you with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Having a big face doesn’t mean you have to compromise on style. In fact, there are a variety of haircuts that can help balance out your facial features and create a flattering look. The key is to choose a cut that adds volume and texture to the top of your head while keeping the sides of your hair shorter.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Big Face

So, what haircut for big face is best? Here are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a style:

Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, it’s essential to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or bobs that end at the jawline. Instead, opt for styles that add height to your hair, such as layered cuts or long, side-swept bangs.

Choose a Cut that Adds Volume

Adding volume to the top of your head can help balance out a big face. Consider a layered cut or a shaggy style that adds texture and movement to your hair. Avoid flat, straight styles that can make your face appear wider.

Keep the Sides Short

Shorter hair on the sides can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Consider a pixie cut or a bob with shorter layers on the sides. Avoid styles that have a lot of volume on the sides or curls that add width.

Experiment with Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and add dimension to your hair. If you have a big forehead, consider long, side-swept bangs that help balance out your facial features. Avoid blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the right haircut for your big face, it’s important to know how to style it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse, gel, or hair spray. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a hair dryer to add volume to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, lifting your hair as you go.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired. Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you’ve styled your hair, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

FAQ

What haircut is best for a big face?

A layered cut or a shaggy style that adds texture and movement to your hair is best for a big face. Keep the sides shorter to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for a big face?

Avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or bobs that end at the jawline. Avoid styles that have a lot of volume on the sides or curls that add width.

How do I style my hair after getting a haircut?

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse, gel, or hair spray. Blow-dry your hair and style it using a flat iron or curling iron.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for a big face is all about creating balance and enhancing your facial features. Keep these tips in mind when choosing a style, and be sure to maintain your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and shiny. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can feel confident and stylish no matter what your face shape.
