How To Make Short Straight Hair Wavy For Guys

Written by Luffy Mar 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Having short, straight hair can be limiting when it comes to styling options, but with these simple steps, any guy can achieve wavy hair in no time. Whether you're looking for a casual, beachy look or a more refined, polished style, this guide will teach you how to make short straight hair wavy for guys.

Description

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for men, but achieving this look can be difficult for those with short, straight hair. Luckily, there are a few easy steps you can follow to create waves in your hair without using any heat tools or damaging products. Whether you want loose, natural waves or more defined curls, this guide will show you how to make short straight hair wavy for guys.

How To

To make short straight hair wavy for guys, you'll need the following:

  • Sea salt spray
  • Mousse or styling cream
  • A comb or brush
  • A blow dryer (optional)

Now, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. If your hair is too dry, the waves won't hold.
  2. Apply sea salt spray to your hair. This will add texture and create waves.
  3. Add mousse or styling cream to your hair. This will help the waves hold their shape.
  4. Use a comb or brush to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.
  5. If you have time, let your hair air dry. If you're in a hurry, use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create waves.
  7. If you want more defined waves, use a curling iron or wand to curl sections of your hair.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Step By Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of each step:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply sea salt spray

Spray sea salt spray evenly onto your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create texture.

Step 3: Add mousse or styling cream

Add a small amount of mousse or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Use a comb or brush to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Use a comb or brush

Use a comb or brush to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer

If you have time, let your hair air dry. If you're in a hurry, use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Step 6: Scrunch your hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create waves.

Step 7: Use a curling iron or wand (optional)

If you want more defined waves, use a curling iron or wand to curl sections of your hair.

Step 8: Finish with hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, damp hair for best results.
  • Use a sea salt spray to add texture and create waves.
  • Apply a small amount of mousse or styling cream to help the waves hold their shape.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create more defined waves if desired.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place without making your hair look stiff or crunchy.

FAQ

Can I make my hair wavy without using heat tools?

Yes, using a sea salt spray and scrunching your hair with your fingers can create natural-looking waves without using any heat tools.

How long will the waves last?

The waves should last all day, but you may need to touch up with hairspray throughout the day to keep them in place.

What if my hair is too short to create waves?

If your hair is too short to create waves, try using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Now that you know how to make short straight hair wavy for guys, you can experiment with different styles and create the perfect look for any occasion. With a little bit of product and some scrunching, you can achieve natural-looking waves that will last all day.
