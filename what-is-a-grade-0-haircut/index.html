What Is A Grade 0 Haircut

Written by Oliver Mar 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A Grade 0 haircut, also known as a bald fade, is a very short cut that is achieved by using clippers with no guard attached. This haircut is commonly seen among men, but women can also rock this look. The Grade 0 haircut gives a clean and sharp appearance, and it is perfect for those who want a low maintenance haircut.

How to Get a Grade 0 Haircut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a Grade 0 haircut, here's how to do it:

1. Wash Your Hair

Before you go to the barber, make sure your hair is clean and free of any products. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair.

2. Communicate with Your Barber

When you get to the barber, communicate clearly what you want. Use pictures or a visual reference if you have one to make sure you and the barber are on the same page.

3. Start with a Longer Guard

Your barber will likely start with a longer guard, such as a #2 or #3, to create a fade. This will leave some hair on the top of your head and gradually fade to a shorter length on the sides and back.

4. Switch to the Grade 0 Guard

Once the barber has created the fade, they will switch to the Grade 0 guard to create the bald look. The barber will use the clippers to cut the hair as close to the scalp as possible.

5. Clean Up the Edges

The barber will then clean up the edges around your hairline and ears to create a neat finish.

Tips for Maintaining a Grade 0 Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your Grade 0 haircut:

1. Keep Your Scalp Moisturized

A bald head can become dry and itchy, so make sure to keep your scalp moisturized with a good quality moisturizer or oil.

2. Wear Sunscreen

Your scalp is now exposed to the sun, so make sure to wear sunscreen to protect it from sunburn.

3. Get Regular Touch-Ups

To maintain the sharp look of your Grade 0 haircut, you will need to get regular touch-ups every two to three weeks.

FAQs

1. Can women get a Grade 0 haircut?

Yes, women can also rock a Grade 0 haircut. It's a bold and edgy look that can be both feminine and masculine.

2. Is a Grade 0 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a Grade 0 haircut can work for all face shapes. However, if you have a round face, you may want to keep some hair on top to balance out your features.

3. Will a Grade 0 haircut suit my hair type?

A Grade 0 haircut can work for all hair types, but it may look different depending on your hair texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, the bald fade may not be as noticeable as it would be on straight hair.

Hair Care for Grade 0 Haircut

Even though a Grade 0 haircut is low maintenance, you still need to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Scalp Regularly

Even though you have no hair on your head, you still need to wash your scalp regularly to remove any dirt and oil.

2. Moisturize Your Scalp

As mentioned earlier, it's important to keep your scalp moisturized to prevent it from becoming dry and itchy.

3. Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a sunscreen specifically designed for the scalp to protect it from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Get Regular Touch-Ups

Regular touch-ups will keep your Grade 0 haircut looking sharp and clean.

In conclusion, a Grade 0 haircut is a very short cut that gives a clean and sharp appearance. It's perfect for those who want a low maintenance haircut. If you're ready to take the plunge and get a Grade 0 haircut, make sure to communicate clearly with your barber and follow the tips for maintaining and caring for your hair and scalp.
