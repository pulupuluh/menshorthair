How Much Does A Curly Hair Perm Cost

Written by Eveline Apr 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting a curly hair perm? One of the biggest questions you may have is, "how much does it cost?" The answer isn't always straightforward, as the price can vary depending on several factors. In this article, we'll break down the cost of a curly hair perm and provide helpful tips on how to prepare for your appointment.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Prepare for Your Curly Hair Perm
  2. Factors That Affect the Cost of a Curly Hair Perm
  3. Typical Cost of a Curly Hair Perm
  4. Tips for Saving Money on a Curly Hair Perm
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips After Your Curly Hair Perm

How to Prepare for Your Curly Hair Perm

Before getting a curly hair perm, it's important to properly prepare your hair to ensure the best results. Here are a few steps to take:

  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours before your appointment.
  • Avoid using any hair products before your appointment, such as hairspray, gel, or mousse.
  • Make sure your hair is free of tangles and knots.
  • Bring pictures of the type of curls you want to achieve to show your stylist.

By following these steps, you'll give your stylist the best canvas to work with and increase the chances of achieving the curls you desire.

Factors That Affect the Cost of a Curly Hair Perm

The cost of a curly hair perm can vary depending on several factors, including:

  • The length of your hair
  • The thickness of your hair
  • Your location
  • The salon you choose
  • The experience level of your stylist
  • The type of perm solution used
  • The additional services you request, such as a haircut or deep conditioning treatment

Keep in mind that some salons may offer package deals or discounts if you bundle multiple services together.

Typical Cost of a Curly Hair Perm

The cost of a curly hair perm can range from $75 to $250, depending on the factors mentioned above. In general, the longer and thicker your hair, the more you can expect to pay. Additionally, if you live in a major city or choose a high-end salon, you may pay more than if you live in a smaller town or choose a budget-friendly salon.

Tips for Saving Money on a Curly Hair Perm

If you're looking to save money on a curly hair perm, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a salon that offers package deals or discounts for bundling services together.
  • Consider getting a perm during a salon's slow season, when they may offer lower prices to attract customers.
  • Look for coupons or promotions on the salon's website or social media pages.
  • Consider getting a partial perm instead of a full perm to save money.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a curly hair perm last?

A curly hair perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can you wash your hair after getting a curly hair perm?

You should wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a curly hair perm. After that, it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair to help maintain the curls and prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips After Your Curly Hair Perm

After getting a curly hair perm, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the curls and prevent damage. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the curls to loosen and become frizzy.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair to help maintain the curls and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause damage to the curls.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or styling product to help define and enhance the curls.

By following these tips, you can help your curly hair perm last as long as possible and maintain healthy, beautiful curls.
