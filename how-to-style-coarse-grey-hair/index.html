How To Style Coarse Grey Hair

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your coarse grey hair? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to style your hair to look its best. From braids to updos, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair becomes coarser and more difficult to manage. Grey hair, in particular, can be very challenging to style. It's important to find the right products and techniques to help tame your hair and create a look that you love. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your coarse grey hair.

How to

The first step in styling your coarse grey hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are designed specifically for coarse hair, such as thickening sprays, volumizing mousses, and hydrating oils. These products will help to make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

Once you have the right products, you can begin to experiment with different hairstyles. Here are a few ideas:

Braids

Braids are a great option for coarse grey hair. They can be worn in a variety of styles, from simple braids to more intricate designs. To create a braid, start by dividing your hair into three sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair, then secure the braid with a hair tie.

Updos

Updos are another great option for coarse grey hair. They can be worn in a variety of styles, from simple buns to more elaborate updos. To create an updo, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun, then secure it with bobby pins.

Curls

Curls are a classic hairstyle that can be worn by anyone, regardless of hair type. To create curls in your coarse grey hair, start by applying a curl-enhancing product. Then, use a curling iron to create loose or tight curls, depending on your preference.

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to create a simple braid:

  1. Divide your hair into three sections.
  2. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  3. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to create a simple bun:

  1. Pull your hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you style your coarse grey hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about styling coarse grey hair:

Q: Can I dye my coarse grey hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your coarse grey hair. However, it's important to use a dye that is designed specifically for grey hair, as regular hair dye may not work as well.

Q: What types of hairstyles are best for coarse grey hair?

A: Braids, updos, and curls are all great options for coarse grey hair. These styles help to tame your hair and make it more manageable.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your coarse grey hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can create beautiful hairstyles that showcase your coarse grey hair. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new styles - you may just discover a look that you love!
