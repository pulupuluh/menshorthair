Is Curtain Bangs Good For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your round face and looking for ways to make it look more angular? Curtain bangs might just be the solution you need. This trendy hairstyle has taken the fashion world by storm and is loved by many. But the question is, is curtain bangs good for round face?

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted in the middle, framing the face on both sides. They are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, giving the illusion of a more angular face. Curtain bangs are suitable for all face shapes, including round faces. They draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, making the face look slimmer.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you are thinking of getting curtain bangs, it is best to get them done by a professional hairstylist. They will help you determine the right length and thickness of the bangs based on your face shape and hair texture. The hairstylist will also show you how to style them for the best results.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Curtain Bangs for Round Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curtain bangs for round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry it gently.
  2. Comb your hair and create a center parting.
  3. Take the section of hair in the front and comb it forward.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut the hair in a straight line, following your eyebrows.
  5. Trim the hair slowly, checking the length after every cut.
  6. Once you have the desired length, use a round brush to blow-dry the bangs.
  7. Twist the ends of the bangs away from your face and use a hair clip to hold them in place.
  8. Blow-dry the rest of your hair as usual.
  9. Release the hair clip and style your bangs using a hair straightener or curling iron.
  10. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting oily.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush for a smoother finish.
  • Use a hair straightener or curling iron to style your bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

  • Can curtain bangs be styled differently? Yes, you can style your curtain bangs in different ways, such as curling them, straightening them or twisting them to the side.
  • Do curtain bangs suit all face shapes? Yes, curtain bangs suit all face shapes, including round faces.
  • Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance? Curtain bangs require regular trimming to maintain the length, but other than that, they are low maintenance.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for healthy curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair and bangs moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, curtain bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can suit all face shapes, including round faces. They are low maintenance and easy to style, making them a popular choice among women. With the right length and thickness, curtain bangs can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. So, if you're thinking of getting curtain bangs, go ahead and give them a try!
