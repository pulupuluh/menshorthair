Mens Cuts Longer On Top A Popular Trend In 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, then the mens cuts longer on top trend might be the perfect option for you. This style features longer hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are trimmed shorter. The longer top hair can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types. In this article, we'll discuss how to get this haircut, offer some styling tips, and answer some common questions about mens cuts longer on top.

How to Get a Mens Cut Longer on Top

The first step in getting a mens cut longer on top is to find a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. Once you've found a professional you trust, you'll need to communicate your desired length and style. It's important to be specific about how long you want the top hair to be and what kind of shape you want it to have. You can bring in pictures or examples of other men's haircuts that you like to help your stylist understand your vision.

During the haircut, your stylist will typically use scissors to trim the top hair to your desired length, while using clippers to trim the sides and back. The stylist may also use a razor or texturizing shears to add texture and movement to the hair. Once the haircut is complete, your stylist may recommend some styling products to help you achieve your desired look.

Styling Tips for Mens Cuts Longer on Top

One of the best things about mens cuts longer on top is that they offer a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new haircut:

  • Use a quality styling product: Whether you prefer a pomade, wax, or gel, using the right product can help you achieve your desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find one that suits you best.
  • Use a blow dryer: Blow drying your hair can help add volume and texture, making it easier to style.
  • Don't forget about the sides and back: While the top of your hair may be longer, it's still important to keep the sides and back well-groomed.

FAQs About Mens Cuts Longer on Top

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, mens cuts longer on top can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist can help you choose the best length and shape for your hair type.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. On average, most men with this style will need a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Can I still wear hats with this style?

Absolutely! Mens cuts longer on top can be easily styled to accommodate hats and other accessories.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Cuts Longer on Top

To keep your mens cut longer on top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Keeping your hair clean and free of buildup can help it look and feel its best.
  • Use a conditioner: Conditioner can help keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid heat styling: Using heat tools like flat irons or curling irons can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

Mens cuts longer on top are a stylish and versatile option for men looking for a new haircut. By following the tips in this article and working with a skilled stylist, you can achieve a look that suits your personality and lifestyle. With a little care and attention, your new haircut will look great for weeks to come.
