How To Hide Facial Hair Without Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Facial hair can be a source of embarrassment for many people, especially for women. While makeup can be an effective way to cover up facial hair, not everyone wants to wear makeup or knows how to apply it properly. The good news is that there are other ways to hide facial hair without makeup. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to hide facial hair without makeup.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Hide Facial Hair Without Makeup
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Hide Facial Hair Without Makeup

There are several ways to hide facial hair without makeup, including:

  • Waxing or threading
  • Shaving
  • Using hair removal creams
  • Using dermaplaning tools

While these methods won't completely remove facial hair, they can make it less noticeable.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying one of these methods, here's a step-by-step guide:

Waxing or Threading

Waxing and threading are both methods of removing hair from the root. Here's how to do it:

  1. Make sure your skin is clean and dry.
  2. Apply wax or thread to the area with facial hair.
  3. Hold the skin taut and quickly pull the wax or thread off in the opposite direction of hair growth.
  4. Repeat until all the hair is removed.
  5. Apply a soothing lotion to the area to reduce redness and irritation.

Shaving

Shaving is a quick and easy way to remove facial hair, but it can cause irritation and razor burn. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wet your skin with warm water.
  2. Apply shaving cream or gel to the area with facial hair.
  3. Use a sharp razor to shave in the direction of hair growth.
  4. Rinse your skin with cool water and pat dry.
  5. Apply a soothing lotion to the area to reduce redness and irritation.

Using Hair Removal Creams

Hair removal creams dissolve the protein in hair, making it easy to wipe away. Here's how to use them:

  1. Apply the cream to the area with facial hair.
  2. Wait the recommended amount of time (usually 5-10 minutes).
  3. Wipe away the cream and hair with a damp cloth.
  4. Rinse your skin with cool water and pat dry.
  5. Apply a soothing lotion to the area to reduce redness and irritation.

Using Dermaplaning Tools

Dermaplaning tools are small, handheld devices that use a blade to remove hair and dead skin cells. Here's how to use them:

  1. Hold the tool at a 45-degree angle to your skin.
  2. Gently glide the blade over the area with facial hair.
  3. Rinse your skin with cool water and pat dry.
  4. Apply a soothing lotion to the area to reduce redness and irritation.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for hiding facial hair:

  • Avoid touching your face too much, as this can stimulate hair growth.
  • Wear clothing with patterns or prints to draw attention away from your face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that flatters your face shape.
  • Try using a color corrector or concealer on any areas of your face that have a shadow from facial hair.

FAQs

Is it safe to use hair removal creams on your face?

Yes, but you should always do a patch test first to make sure you're not allergic to the cream. Follow the instructions carefully and don't leave the cream on for longer than recommended.

Will shaving make my facial hair grow back thicker?

No, this is a myth. Shaving only cuts the hair, it doesn't affect the follicle or the rate of hair growth.

How often do I need to remove facial hair?

This depends on your hair growth rate and personal preference. Some people may need to remove facial hair every few days, while others may only need to do it once a week.

Hair Care for Facial Hair

If you decide to remove facial hair, it's important to take care of the skin and hair in that area. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize the area daily to prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Avoid sun exposure, as it can cause hyperpigmentation.
  • Exfoliate the area once a week to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Consider using a growth inhibitor to slow down hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can hide facial hair without makeup and feel more confident in your own skin.
