How To Cut Shaggy Hair Style A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that oozes effortless style, the shaggy hairstyle might just be the perfect option for you. This haircut has been popular for decades and remains a favorite among both men and women. In this article, we'll show you how to cut shaggy hair style in a simple, easy-to-follow guide.

Table of Contents

Description of Shaggy Haircut

The shaggy haircut is a layered hairstyle with choppy ends that create a messy, disheveled look. It's a versatile cut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to Cut Shaggy Hair Style

The shaggy haircut is best achieved with scissors and a comb. It's important to work with dry hair to get an accurate idea of how the hair will fall after the cut. Here are the basic steps to cutting a shaggy haircut:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back.
  2. Begin with the back section and hold the hair at a 45-degree angle away from the head. Cut the hair in a straight line using scissors, working from the bottom up.
  3. Move on to the side sections and repeat the same process, cutting the hair at a 45-degree angle away from the head.
  4. For the top section, hold the hair straight up and cut the ends in a choppy, uneven manner. This will create texture and volume.
  5. Blend the layers by combing the hair and snipping away any uneven ends.
  6. Finish the haircut by adding layers around the face and creating fringe if desired.

Tips for Cutting Shaggy Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting shaggy hair:

  • Start with small snips and gradually work your way up to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a razor to create wispy ends for a more textured look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and layers to find the perfect shaggy haircut for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaggy haircuts:

  • What face shapes suit shaggy haircuts? Shaggy haircuts work well with oval and heart-shaped faces.
  • Can shaggy haircuts be styled? Yes, shaggy haircuts can be styled in many ways, including messy, beachy waves, and sleek, straight looks.
  • How often should I get a shaggy haircut? It depends on how fast your hair grows, but it's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of the cut.

Hair Care for Shaggy Hair Style

To keep your shaggy haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance the texture and volume of your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your shaggy haircut.

With these simple tips, you can easily achieve a shaggy haircut that looks effortless and stylish. Remember to take your time, start with small snips, and experiment with different lengths and layers to create a customized look that suits you.
