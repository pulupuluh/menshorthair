How To Cut Men s Long Curly Hair With Scissors

Written by Oliver May 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is a blessing and a curse. It can be beautiful and unique, but it can also be difficult to manage. Long curly hair, in particular, can be a challenge to cut. But with the right tools and techniques, you can give your client the perfect haircut. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's long curly hair with scissors.

Description

Cutting men's long curly hair with scissors requires skill and patience. You need to take into account the natural movement and texture of the hair to create a flattering shape. It's important to work with the curls, not against them, to avoid frizz and uneven layers. This technique works best on hair that is at least shoulder-length and has a defined curl pattern.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can shrink up to 25%, which can result in a shorter haircut than intended. If the hair is tangled, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle it without pulling too hard.

Next, section the hair into smaller sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any areas. Start at the back and work your way forward, cutting in small sections at a time. Use a comb to guide the hair and cut with the scissors at a slight angle to create layers.

When cutting the sides, be careful not to cut too much off. You want to maintain the length and shape of the curls. Use the same technique, cutting at an angle and following the natural movement of the hair.

Once you've cut all the sections, comb through the hair to make sure there are no uneven spots or stray hairs. Trim any areas that need it and make sure both sides are even.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Section the hair into smaller sections

3. Cut in small sections at a time, following the natural movement of the hair

4. Use a comb to guide the hair and cut at a slight angle

5. Be careful not to cut too much off the sides

6. Comb through the hair to check for uneven spots or stray hairs

7. Trim any areas that need it and make sure both sides are even

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to avoid tearing or damaging the hair

- Cut in small sections to avoid cutting too much off at once

- Follow the natural movement of the hair to create a flattering shape

- Don't be afraid to ask your client for feedback during the process

- Take breaks if you need to, cutting curly hair can be tiring

FAQ

Q. Can you cut curly hair with clippers?

A. It's possible, but scissors are better for creating a natural shape and avoiding uneven spots.

Q. How often should men with long curly hair get a haircut?

A. It depends on the individual's hair growth and style. On average, every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and shape. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance the curls and reduce frizz. Avoid using heat styling tools and opt for air-drying or diffusing instead. Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape.

Now that you know how to cut men's long curly hair with scissors, you can give your clients the perfect haircut. Remember to take your time and work with the natural movement of the hair to create a flattering shape. With practice, you'll become a pro at cutting curly hair.
