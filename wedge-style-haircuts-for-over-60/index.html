Wedge Style Haircuts For Over 60 A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are over 60 and looking for a stylish haircut, the wedge style might just be what you need. This classic cut has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about wedge style haircuts for over 60, including how to get the perfect cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The wedge style haircut is a short, layered haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It is a low-maintenance cut that is perfect for women over 60 who want a stylish and youthful look. The wedge style can be customized to fit your face shape and hair type, making it a versatile cut that can work for almost anyone.

How To

To get the perfect wedge style haircut, you should start by finding a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting this style. Bring in pictures of wedge style haircuts that you like and discuss your hair type and face shape with your stylist. They will be able to recommend the best length and layers for your hair.

Step By Step

Here are the basic steps to getting a wedge style haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Sit in the stylist's chair and discuss the cut you want.
  3. The stylist will section your hair and start cutting the back first.
  4. They will then move to the sides and front, cutting at an angle to create the wedge shape.
  5. Once the cut is complete, the stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer and styling products.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the perfect wedge style haircut:

  • Choose a stylist who has experience cutting wedge style haircuts.
  • Bring in pictures of wedge style haircuts that you like to show your stylist.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing the length and layers of your cut.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is the wedge style haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: The wedge style can work for most hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. Your stylist can customize the cut to fit your hair type and texture.

Q: How often should I get a wedge style haircut?

A: The wedge style is a low-maintenance haircut, but you should still get regular trims to keep the shape and layers looking fresh. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I style my wedge haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, the wedge style can be styled in different ways depending on your mood and the occasion. You can add volume with a blow dryer and round brush, or use a straightening iron for a sleeker look.

Hair Care

To keep your wedge style haircut looking its best, you should follow a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep the shape and layers looking fresh.

In conclusion, the wedge style haircut is a classic and stylish choice for women over 60. With the right stylist and some basic hair care tips, you can rock this low-maintenance cut and look fabulous at any age.
