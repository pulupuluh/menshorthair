What Kind Of Hairstyle Makes Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Bardi Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, you may be wondering what kind of hairstyle makes your face look thinner. The good news is that there are plenty of hairstyles that can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this blog post, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for making your face look thinner, as well as tips for styling and caring for your hair.

  • Hair Care Tips for a Healthier, Thinner-Looking Mane

How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can go a long way in creating the illusion of a thinner face shape. Here are some of the best hairstyles for a slimmer-looking face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs, which can make your face look rounder.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create a diagonal line across your face, which can make it look slimmer. Avoid full bangs, which can make your face look rounder.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help create the illusion of a longer neck, which can make your face look slimmer. Avoid cuts that are too short or too choppy, which can make your face look wider.

4. Bob

A bob can help create the illusion of a more angular jawline, which can make your face look slimmer. Avoid bobs that are too short or too blunt, which can make your face look rounder.

5. Updo

An updo can help create the illusion of a longer face shape, which can make your face look slimmer. Avoid updos that are too tight or too high, which can make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Slimmer Look

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that can help make your face look thinner, it's important to style it correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a slimmer look:

1. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the roots and elongate your face shape.

2. Use Hair Products

Use hair products like mousse or volumizing spray to add texture and volume to your hair.

3. Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair at the crown to create height and elongate your face shape.

4. Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, style them to create a diagonal line across your face.

5. Pin Your Hair

If you're styling an updo, pin your hair loosely to create a relaxed, elongated look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Thinner-Looking Hairstyle

Once you've achieved the perfect hairstyle for making your face look thinner, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay and maintain your style.

2. Use Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling your hair to prevent damage.

3. Wash Your Hair Properly

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQs About Hairstyles for a Thinner Face

1. Can a short haircut make my face look thinner?

Yes, a short haircut like a pixie cut can help create the illusion of a longer neck, which can make your face look slimmer.

2. What kind of bangs should I avoid?

Avoid full bangs, which can make your face look rounder.

3. Can an updo make my face look wider?

Yes, an updo that's too tight or too high can make your face look wider. Opt for a looser, relaxed updo instead.

Hair Care Tips for a Healthier, Thinner-Looking Mane

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for maintaining a healthy, thinner-looking mane:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

4. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and maintain your hairstyle overnight.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle can go a long way in creating the illusion of a slimmer face. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, a bob, or an updo, make sure to style it correctly and maintain it with proper hair care. With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to a thinner-looking mane.
