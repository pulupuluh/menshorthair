How To Cut Hair Evenly With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to cut your hair at home, using clippers is a great option. Not only is it more cost-effective than going to a salon, but it's also a lot more convenient. However, cutting your own hair can be daunting, especially if you're new to it. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your hair evenly with clippers, step-by-step.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut. Clippers are a popular choice for at-home haircuts because they're easy to use, affordable, and versatile. With clippers, you can create a variety of hairstyles, from buzz cuts to fades. However, using clippers requires some skill and practice. In this article, we'll cover the basics of how to use clippers to cut your hair evenly.

How to Cut Hair Evenly with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather your tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape

Make sure your clippers are clean and oiled before you start using them. You can use a small brush to remove any hair or debris from the blades. Apply a few drops of clipper oil to the blades to keep them lubricated.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Length

The guard length determines how short your hair will be. Clippers come with different guard lengths, which are usually numbered. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. For example, a #1 guard will leave 1/8 inch of hair, while a #4 guard will leave 1/2 inch of hair. Choose the guard length that you want to use for your haircut.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by cutting the sides of your hair. Attach the guard to your clippers and start at the bottom of your hairline. Use an upward motion to cut the hair, moving the clippers in the direction of your hair growth. Continue cutting the sides of your hair, moving the clippers up in small sections until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and moving the clippers upward. Be sure to check the mirror regularly to make sure you're cutting your hair evenly.

Step 5: Trim the Top

After you've finished the sides and back, it's time to trim the top of your hair. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head, pulling the hair up and cutting it to the desired length. You can also use the clippers without a guard to trim any stray hairs on the top of your head.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Top

To create a seamless look, you'll need to blend the sides and top of your hair. Use the clippers without a guard to create a fade, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use a comb to blend the hair between the sides and top of your head.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

Finally, use the clippers to clean up the edges around your ears and neck. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer guard length and work your way down if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head regularly.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to cut a little bit at a time than to cut too much.
  • Use a towel or cape to catch any hair clippings.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend or family member to help.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with clippers without a guard?

Yes, you can use clippers without a guard to create a shorter haircut or to trim stray hairs. However, be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

How often should I oil my clippers?

You should oil your clippers before every use to keep the blades lubricated. Apply a few drops of clipper oil to the blades and turn on the clippers for a few seconds to distribute the oil.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim your hair shorter or ask a professional to fix it. It's better to cut a little bit at a time and be cautious than to cut too much at once.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your hair evenly with clippers and achieve a great-looking haircut at home. Remember to be patient, take your time, and practice regularly to improve your skills.
