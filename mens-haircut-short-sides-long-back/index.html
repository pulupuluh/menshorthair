Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Back

If you are looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then the mens haircut short sides long back is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is also known as the "mullet" and has been popularized by celebrities like Brad Pitt and David Beckham. In this article, we will guide you on how to get the perfect mens haircut short sides long back.

Description

The mens haircut short sides long back is a hairstyle where the sides and back are clipped short, while the hair on the top is left long. This creates a contrast between the short and long hair, giving the hairstyle a unique and edgy look. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference.

How to

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

The first step in getting a mens haircut short sides long back is finding a good barber who is experienced in this type of haircut. Look for someone who has a good reputation and has experience cutting different hair types and textures.

Step 2: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of your hair on the top. You can go for a longer or shorter length, depending on your preference. Make sure to communicate this clearly to your barber.

Step 3: Clip the Sides and Back

The barber will clip the sides and back of your hair short, using clippers. The length can vary depending on your preference, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it shorter than the hair on the top.

Step 4: Layer the Top

The barber will layer the hair on the top, creating texture and movement. This is important to achieve the right look for the mens haircut short sides long back.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair in different ways, depending on your preference. You can comb it back for a sleek look, or use a hairdryer and some styling products to create a messy and edgy look.

Tips

  • Bring a picture of the mens haircut short sides long back to show your barber what you want.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Is the mens haircut short sides long back suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens haircut short sides long back can be styled for all hair types and textures. Your barber can adjust the length and layering to suit your hair type.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut short sides long back looking fresh and stylish, you need to take good care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use styling products sparingly, and always wash them out thoroughly to prevent build-up.
