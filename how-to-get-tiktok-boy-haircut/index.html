How To Get Tiktok Boy Haircut A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to achieve the popular TikTok boy haircut, you're in the right place. This trendy haircut has become a hit among young men around the world, thanks to its edgy and stylish look. In this article, we'll show you how to get this haircut in a step-by-step guide. We'll also provide you with tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Get TikTok Boy Haircut

The TikTok boy haircut is a mix of a fade and messy, textured hair on top. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. To get this haircut, you need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is familiar with this style.

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you head to the barber, decide on the style you want. There are different variations of the TikTok boy haircut, such as the low fade, mid fade, and high fade. You can also choose the length and texture of the hair on top. Look for inspiration on TikTok or Instagram to find a style that suits you.

Step 2: Find a Barber

Once you've decided on your style, find a barber who is experienced in cutting this type of haircut. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family, or look for a barber online. Make sure to book an appointment in advance to avoid waiting in line.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber

When you arrive at the barbershop, communicate with your barber about the style you want. Show them a picture or video of the TikTok boy haircut you like, and explain the details, such as the length and texture of the hair on top, and the type of fade you prefer. It's important to be clear and specific to avoid any misunderstandings.

Step 4: Sit Back and Relax

Once your barber starts cutting your hair, sit back and relax. The TikTok boy haircut involves a fade on the sides and back, and texturized hair on top. Your barber will use clippers and scissors to achieve the desired length and texture. They may also use a razor to create a clean hairline.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After your haircut is finished, your barber will style your hair to your liking. They may apply hair products, such as wax or pomade, to add texture and hold. You can also ask your barber for styling tips to help you recreate the look at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to get the TikTok boy haircut:

  1. Choose your style
  2. Find a barber
  3. Communicate with your barber
  4. Sit back and relax
  5. Style your hair

Tips for Getting the Perfect TikTok Boy Haircut

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect TikTok boy haircut:

  • Bring a picture or video of the style you want to show your barber.
  • Be specific about the length and texture of the hair on top.
  • Choose the type of fade that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the TikTok boy haircut:

How long does the TikTok boy haircut take?

The haircut itself usually takes around 30-45 minutes, but it may take longer depending on the complexity of the style and your hair type.

How much does the TikTok boy haircut cost?

The cost of the haircut varies depending on the location and the experience of the barber. It can range from $20 to $50 or more.

Can I style my TikTok boy haircut differently?

Yes, the TikTok boy haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. You can use hair products to add texture and hold, or you can slick it back for a more formal look.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your TikTok Boy Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your TikTok boy haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use hair products, such as wax or pomade, to add texture and hold.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect TikTok boy haircut. Remember to communicate with your barber and be specific about the style you want. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock this trendy look with confidence.
