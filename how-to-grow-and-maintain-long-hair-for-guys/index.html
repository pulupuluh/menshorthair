How To Grow And Maintain Long Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair has always been associated with women. But in recent years, more and more men have been embracing long hair. If you’re one of those men who wants to grow and maintain long hair, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of growing and maintaining long hair for guys. We’ll cover everything from how to grow your hair to how to keep it healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Long Hair
  • How to Maintain Long Hair
  • Tips for Long Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Grow Long Hair

Growing long hair takes time and patience. Here are some tips on how to grow long hair:

1. Be Patient

It takes time to grow long hair. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. So, if you want to grow your hair from a buzz cut to shoulder length, it could take you up to 2 years. Be patient and don’t expect overnight results.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you’re getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Eat foods like fish, eggs, nuts, and leafy greens.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons can damage your hair and make it harder to grow long. Try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow longer. Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks can help prevent split ends from traveling up the hair shaft and causing more damage.

How to Maintain Long Hair

Once you’ve grown your hair out, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips on how to maintain long hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps keep it clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb helps prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and buns can cause tension and breakage. Try to wear your hair down or in loose styles as much as possible.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to keep your hair protected.

Tips for Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your long hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

2. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases help prevent breakage and damage to your hair while you sleep.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims helps prevent split ends and keeps your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

1. How long does it take to grow long hair?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. It could take up to 2 years to grow your hair from a buzz cut to shoulder length.

2. How often should I wash my long hair?

Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.

3. Can I still use hair products with long hair?

Yes, you can still use hair products with long hair. Just make sure to use products that are designed for your hair type and that won’t cause buildup.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and looking great takes time and effort. Follow these tips and tricks to grow and maintain long hair for guys. And remember, patience is key!
