Women s Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 How To Choose The Right One

If you have a round face, you might find it challenging to choose the right haircut that flatters your features. But with the right knowledge, you can select a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your beauty. In this blog post, we'll discuss women's haircuts for round faces 2021, including how to choose the right one, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Round Face

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Round Face

The key to choosing the right haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. You want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face and draw attention to your cheeks. Instead, you should opt for haircuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head and elongate your face.

Some of the best haircuts for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut with volume on top
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Chin-length bob with layers

When choosing a haircut, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Follow these steps to get the perfect haircut for your round face:

  1. Research hairstyles that flatter round faces and choose one that you like.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine if the hairstyle will work with your hair type and face shape.
  3. Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your appointment.
  4. Communicate with your hairstylist about your preferences and concerns.
  5. Trust your hairstylist's expertise and let them work their magic.
  6. Ask for styling tips and product recommendations to maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer to add volume
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products that weigh it down

FAQs about Women's Haircuts for Round Faces

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut with a round face?

A: Yes, you can. Short haircuts like pixie cuts and bobs can be flattering for round faces if done correctly. Opt for a style with volume on top and avoid blunt cuts that add width to your face.

Q: What kind of bangs should I get with a round face?

A: Side-swept bangs are the best option for round faces. They create angles and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Q: How can I make my face look slimmer with my haircut?

A: You can create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding volume to the crown of your head and elongating your face with layers. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy and Beautiful Hair

In addition to getting the right haircut, you should also take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, which can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine with a hat or swim cap

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right haircut for your round face, you can have beautiful and healthy hair that flatters your features.
