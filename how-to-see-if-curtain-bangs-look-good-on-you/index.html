How To See If Curtain Bangs Look Good On You

Written by Bardi Apr 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but not sure if it’ll suit your face shape or hair type? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll show you how to see if curtain bangs look good on you and provide some helpful tips and hair care advice. Keep reading to find out more!

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if curtain bangs will look good on you is to consider your face shape. Curtain bangs suit most face shapes, but some may benefit more than others. Here are some suggestions:

  • Oval face shape: Almost any style, including curtain bangs, will suit an oval face shape.
  • Round face shape: Curtain bangs can help elongate a round face shape.
  • Square face shape: Soft, curtain-like bangs can help soften a square face shape.
  • Heart face shape: Curtain bangs can balance out a heart-shaped face and draw attention to the eyes.

Remember, these are just suggestions. The most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Step 2: Look at Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Curtain bangs can work with most hair types, but some may require more maintenance than others. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Straight hair: Curtain bangs can be easily styled with straight hair and require minimal maintenance.
  • Wavy hair: Curtain bangs can add texture and volume to wavy hair, but may require more upkeep to prevent frizz.
  • Curly hair: Curtain bangs can frame curly hair beautifully but may require more maintenance to prevent them from becoming unruly.
  • Thick hair: Curtain bangs can work well with thick hair but may require more frequent trims to prevent them from looking too heavy.

Again, these are just suggestions. Talk to your hairstylist about your hair type and how to maintain your curtain bangs.

Step 3: Try a Virtual Makeover

If you’re still unsure about getting curtain bangs, try a virtual makeover. There are many apps and websites that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. This can give you a better idea of how they’ll look on you without committing to a haircut.

Tips for Rocking Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for rocking curtain bangs:

  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your curtain bangs.
  • Part your bangs in the middle for an effortless, boho look.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or updos, to show off your bangs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Will curtain bangs suit my hair length?

Yes, curtain bangs can be styled with any hair length.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs require some maintenance, such as regular trims and styling, but they’re generally low-maintenance compared to other bang styles.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs to prevent damage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming greasy.

Now that you know how to see if curtain bangs look good on you, you can confidently rock this trendy hairstyle. Remember to talk to your hairstylist about your hair type and maintenance needs. Happy styling!
