How To Make Thin Grey Hair Look Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having thin grey hair? Do you want to know how to make it look thicker and fuller? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks for making your thin grey hair look thicker and more voluminous. Keep reading to find out more.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and grey. This is because the production of melanin (which gives color to our hair) decreases with age. This makes our hair look dull and lifeless. However, there are ways to make thin grey hair look thicker and more voluminous.

How to Make Thin Grey Hair Look Thicker

1. Use Thicker Hair Products

One of the easiest ways to make thin grey hair look thicker is to use thicker hair products. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. You can also use a hair mousse or hair spray to add more volume and hold to your hair.

2. Get a Haircut

Getting a haircut can also help make your thin grey hair look thicker. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair. This will create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

3. Use Hair Extensions

If you want an instant solution for thin grey hair, you can use hair extensions. Hair extensions can add length and volume to your hair. You can use clip-in hair extensions, tape-in hair extensions, or sew-in hair extensions.

4. Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a great way to make your thin grey hair look thicker. Hair fibers are small fibers that you sprinkle onto your hair. They cling to your hair and create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair. Clean hair is easier to style and will hold the style better.

2. Apply Hair Mousse

Apply a small amount of hair mousse to your hair. This will add volume and hold to your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the round brush to lift your hair and create volume. Finish with a blast of cold air to set the style.

4. Use Hair Fibers

Sprinkle hair fibers onto your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the fibers evenly throughout your hair. The fibers will cling to your hair and create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

5. Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair. Finish with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Get a haircut with layers to create the illusion of more volume and thickness.
  • Use hair extensions or hair fibers for an instant solution for thin grey hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. This can damage your hair and make it look thin and lifeless.

FAQ

Q. Can I use hair fibers on colored hair?

A. Yes, hair fibers can be used on colored hair. They will cling to your hair and create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

Q. Can I use hair extensions on thin hair?

A. Yes, you can use hair extensions on thin hair. However, make sure to use extensions that are lightweight and do not pull on your hair.

Q. Can I use a hair dryer on thin hair?

A. Yes, you can use a hair dryer on thin hair. However, make sure to use a low heat setting and hold the dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and thick hair. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated. And finally, get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
