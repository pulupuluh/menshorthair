How Many Inches Is Mid Back Length Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Description

Mid back length hair is a popular hair length that falls between the shoulder blades and the mid-back. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways and suits almost every face shape. However, the exact length of mid back length hair in inches can vary from person to person depending on their height, body type, and hair texture.

So, how many inches is mid back length hair? The answer is not straightforward as it can range from 14 to 18 inches. In general, mid back length hair falls between the 7th and 12th thoracic vertebrae, which are located at the bottom of the shoulder blades.

How to Measure Mid Back Length Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide to measure mid back length hair, let's take a look at some of the essential tools you will need:

  • A tail comb
  • A hair tie
  • A ruler or measuring tape

Step by Step Guide to Measure Mid Back Length Hair

Follow these steps to measure your mid back length hair:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into two equal parts by parting it down the middle of your head.
  2. Take a tail comb and measure the distance from the nape of your neck to the bottom of your shoulder blades. This distance will give you an idea of where your mid back length hair will fall.
  3. Divide the hair at the back into two equal parts and bring them to the front over your shoulders.
  4. Take a hair tie and tie the two sections of hair together at the front.
  5. Use a ruler or measuring tape to measure the length of the tied hair. The length from the hair tie to the ends of the hair will give you the exact measurement of your mid back length hair.

Tips to Maintain Mid Back Length Hair

Mid back length hair requires proper care and maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you maintain your mid back length hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly, as it can cause hair breakage and damage.

FAQs

1. How long does it take to grow mid back length hair?

It can take anywhere from 1 to 3 years to grow mid back length hair, depending on your hair growth rate and how well you take care of your hair.

2. Can you achieve mid back length hair with extensions?

Yes, you can achieve mid back length hair with extensions, but it is essential to choose high-quality extensions that match your hair texture and color.

3. What face shapes suit mid back length hair?

Mid back length hair suits almost every face shape, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Mid Back Length Hair

Here are some additional tips for hair care for mid back length hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

So, there you have it - a comprehensive guide on how many inches mid back length hair is and how to measure it. With the right care and maintenance, you can achieve healthy and shiny mid back length hair that looks great on any occasion.
