How to Make Your Forehead Smaller in a Ponytail - Tips and Tricks

If you have a larger forehead, you may feel self-conscious when wearing your hair in a ponytail. The good news is that there are simple ways to make your forehead appear smaller in a ponytail. This article will provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look for any occasion.

How to Make Your Forehead Smaller in a Ponytail

One of the easiest ways to make your forehead appear smaller in a ponytail is to create volume at the crown of your head. This can be accomplished by teasing the hair at the crown or using a volumizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Volume

1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.

2. Take a section of hair at the crown of your head and backcomb it using a teasing brush.

3. Spray the backcombed section with hairspray to hold the volume.

4. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section to create a polished look.

5. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head, making sure to include the teased section to create volume.

6. Secure the ponytail with an elastic band.

Another way to make your forehead appear smaller is to create a side-swept fringe. This can be done by parting your hair to the side and sweeping the hair across your forehead. This will create a diagonal line that will draw the eye away from your forehead.

Tips for Creating a Side-Swept Fringe

1. Start by parting your hair to the side.

2. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and twist it away from your face.

3. Pin the twisted section of hair at the back of your head using bobby pins.

4. Sweep the remaining hair across your forehead, creating a diagonal line.

5. Secure the side-swept fringe with hairspray.

FAQ About Making Your Forehead Smaller in a Ponytail

Q: Will wearing a high ponytail make my forehead appear larger?

A: Yes, wearing a high ponytail can make your forehead appear larger. To make your forehead appear smaller, try wearing a low ponytail or a side ponytail instead.

Q: How can I prevent my ponytail from slipping down?

A: To prevent your ponytail from slipping down, try using a hair tie that is designed to grip the hair. You can also try twisting the hair before securing it with a hair tie to create more grip.

Hair Care Tips for Making Your Forehead Smaller in a Ponytail

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume at the crown of your head.

2. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can make your forehead appear larger.

3. Use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair to prevent breakage.

4. Take breaks from wearing your hair in a ponytail to prevent damage to your hairline.

5. Use a hairspray or gel to keep your ponytail in place throughout the day.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your forehead appear smaller in a ponytail and achieve the perfect look for any occasion.
