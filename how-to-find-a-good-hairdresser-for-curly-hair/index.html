How To Find A Good Hairdresser For Curly Hair

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, especially when it comes to finding the right hairdresser. It's important to find a hairdresser who understands the unique needs of curly hair and can provide the right cuts and styles. In this guide, we'll share some tips on how to find a good hairdresser for curly hair.

How to Find a Good Hairdresser for Curly Hair

When searching for a hairdresser for curly hair, it's important to look for someone who has experience working with textured hair. Here are some tips to help you find the right hairdresser:

1. Ask for Referrals

Ask friends, family, or co-workers with curly hair for recommendations. If you see someone with a great curly hairstyle, don't be afraid to ask where they got it done. Referrals can be a great way to find a hairdresser who understands how to work with curly hair.

2. Research Online

Do some research online to find hairdressers in your area who specialize in curly hair. Look for reviews and check out their social media pages to see examples of their work. You can also check out forums and online communities for people with curly hair to see if there are any recommended hairdressers in your area.

3. Look for Certifications

Some hairdressers have certifications specifically for working with curly hair, such as DevaCurl or Ouidad. Look for hairdressers who have these certifications, as they will have received specialized training in working with curly hair.

Step by Step Guide to Finding a Good Hairdresser for Curly Hair

Here's a step by step guide to help you find a hairdresser who can work with your curly hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you start your search, it's important to determine your hair type. This will help you find a hairdresser who specializes in your specific hair texture.

Step 2: Ask for Referrals

Ask friends, family, or co-workers with similar hair types for recommendations.

Step 3: Research Online

Use search engines and social media to find hairdressers in your area who specialize in curly hair.

Step 4: Read Reviews

Read reviews from previous clients to get an idea of the hairdresser's skill level and how they work with curly hair.

Step 5: Schedule a Consultation

Before booking an appointment, schedule a consultation with the hairdresser to discuss your hair type, concerns, and desired style.

Step 6: Look at Examples of Their Work

Ask to see examples of the hairdresser's work on clients with similar hair types to yours.

Step 7: Book Your Appointment

If you feel comfortable and confident in the hairdresser's abilities, book your appointment.

Tips for Finding a Good Hairdresser for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you find a hairdresser who can work with your curly hair:

1. Don't Be Afraid to Ask Questions

Ask the hairdresser about their experience working with curly hair and what techniques they use.

2. Look for a Hairdresser Who Listens

Find a hairdresser who listens to your concerns and takes the time to understand your hair type and desired style.

3. Consider the Salon Environment

Look for a salon that has a welcoming and comfortable environment. A relaxed atmosphere can help you feel more at ease during your appointment.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my curly hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a trim every 2-3 months to maintain healthy hair.

Q: How do I know if a hairdresser is experienced with curly hair?

A: Look for hairdressers who have specialized training or certifications for working with curly hair. You can also ask to see examples of their work on clients with similar hair types to yours.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that will keep your hair moisturized.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage curly hair and cause frizz. Instead, opt for air-drying or using a diffuser.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on curly hair and won't cause breakage.

By following these tips and finding a good hairdresser for curly hair, you can achieve healthy and beautiful curls.
