How To Pick The Best Hair Color For Yourself

Written by William Jun 30, 2023 · 9 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task – with so many shades to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. But fear not! In this article, we will guide you through the process of picking the best hair color for yourself. From determining your skin tone to considering your lifestyle, we’ve got you covered. Read on to discover our tips and tricks for finding your perfect hair color!

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Step 4: Consult with a Professional
  • Tips for Picking the Best Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Coloring

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in picking the best hair color for yourself is to determine your skin tone. This will help you choose a shade that complements your natural complexion. Here are a few tips to help you figure out your skin tone:

  • If your skin has warm undertones, you will have veins that appear greenish and your skin may have a yellow or golden hue.
  • If your skin has cool undertones, your veins will appear bluish and your skin may have a pink or rosy hue.
  • If your skin has neutral undertones, your veins will appear blue-green and your skin will have a balance of warm and cool tones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can start looking for hair colors that will complement it. If you have warm undertones, look for hair colors with golden or honey tones. If you have cool undertones, look for hair colors with ashy or cool tones. If you have neutral undertones, you can choose hair colors with warm or cool tones.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

When picking a new hair color, it’s important to consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it may be difficult to achieve a very light hair color without damaging your hair. On the other hand, if you have light hair, you may not be able to go too dark without it looking unnatural.

It’s also important to consider the undertones of your natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, you may want to stick with warm hair colors. If you have cool undertones, you may want to stick with cool hair colors.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider when picking a hair color is your lifestyle. Do you have a job that requires a more conservative look? If so, you may want to stick with natural hair colors. Do you have a more creative job that allows for more experimentation? If so, you may want to try a more unconventional hair color.

You should also consider how often you are willing to touch up your hair color. If you don’t have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you may want to stick with a low-maintenance hair color.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you’re still unsure about what hair color is best for you, it’s always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine your skin tone, recommend hair colors that will work well with your natural hair color, and give you advice on how to maintain your new hair color.

Tips for Picking the Best Hair Color

Here are a few more tips to help you pick the best hair color:

  • Consider your eye color – if you have blue eyes, a warm hair color can make them pop. If you have brown eyes, a cool hair color can make them stand out.
  • Look at pictures of hair colors you like and bring them to your stylist.
  • Consider your age – as you get older, a lighter hair color can be more flattering.
  • Don’t forget about your eyebrows – if you dye your hair a drastically different color than your eyebrows, it can look unnatural.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the best hair color:

Q: Can I dye my hair a lighter color without bleaching it?

A: It depends on your natural hair color. If you have very dark hair, it may be difficult to achieve a very light hair color without bleaching. However, if you have light to medium hair, you may be able to dye your hair a few shades lighter without bleaching.

Q: Can I dye my hair a darker color without damaging it?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a darker color without damaging it. However, if you have previously dyed your hair or if you have damaged hair, it’s important to be cautious when dyeing your hair.

Hair Care After Coloring

After you have dyed your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain your new color. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often – this can cause your hair color to fade more quickly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often – this can also cause your hair color to fade.
  • Consider using a color-depositing conditioner to maintain your hair color between touch-ups.

By following these tips and considering your skin tone, natural hair color, lifestyle, and consulting with a professional, you can pick the best hair color for yourself. Good luck!
