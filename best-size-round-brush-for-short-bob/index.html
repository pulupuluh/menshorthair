Best Size Round Brush For Short Bob The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Mar 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you struggling to achieve the perfect blowout for your short bob hairstyle? One key tool that can make all the difference is a round brush. But with so many sizes and materials to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the best one for your hair type and length. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the best size round brush for your short bob and provide tips for achieving salon-worthy results.

Description

A round brush is a hair styling tool with bristles that wrap around a cylindrical barrel. It is designed to create volume, smoothness, and curl in the hair when used with a blow dryer. The size of the brush barrel determines the size of the curl or wave that is created. For short bob hairstyles, a smaller barrel size is generally recommended to create more defined curls and volume.

How to Choose the Best Size Round Brush for Short Bob

Choosing the best size round brush for your short bob depends on your hair length, texture, and desired style. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

1. Consider Hair Length

For short bob hairstyles, a barrel diameter of 1 inch or smaller is recommended. This size is ideal for creating defined curls and waves that won't overpower the shorter length of your hair.

2. Choose Bristle Type

The type of bristle on your round brush can also affect the outcome of your styling. Natural boar bristle brushes are great for adding shine and reducing frizz, while synthetic bristles are more durable and can withstand heat from a blow dryer.

3. Look for a Lightweight Brush

Since you will be using the brush with a blow dryer, it's important to choose a lightweight option that won't strain your arm or hand during the styling process.

4. Consider Your Desired Style

If you want to create loose waves or curls, a larger barrel size may be more appropriate. However, if you want to add volume and lift to your roots, a smaller barrel size will be more effective.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you've chosen the best size round brush for your short bob, it's time to put it to use. Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving the perfect blowout:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before using your round brush, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work through your hair and ensure that each section is evenly dried and styled.

Step 3: Begin at the Roots

Starting at the roots, use your round brush and blow dryer to lift and curl your hair. Hold the brush at the roots and use the blow dryer to heat the hair while rolling the brush away from your face.

Step 4: Work Through Each Section

Continue to work through each section of your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Use the round brush to curl the ends of your hair under for a polished look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your entire head, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Using a Round Brush on Short Bob Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving the best results with your round brush:

1. Use a High-Quality Blow Dryer

A high-quality blow dryer with multiple heat and speed settings will make the styling process easier and more effective.

2. Don't Overheat Your Hair

Be careful not to overheat your hair with the blow dryer. Use a low to medium heat setting and take breaks if your hair feels too hot.

3. Experiment with Angles

Experiment with different angles when using your round brush to achieve different styles and volume.

4. Practice Makes Perfect

Like any new hair styling technique, using a round brush takes practice. Don't get discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.

FAQs

1. Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use a round brush on wet hair. Wet hair is more susceptible to damage and breakage, and using a round brush on wet hair can cause tangling and pulling.

2. How often should I clean my round brush?

You should clean your round brush at least once a month to remove any buildup of product or hair. To clean, use a comb to remove any hair and then soak the brush in warm water with a gentle shampoo. Rinse and allow to air dry.

3. What if I have thick or curly hair?

If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to use a larger barrel size to achieve your desired style. It's important to choose a round brush with sturdy bristles that can handle thicker hair without breaking or tangling.

Hair Care for Short Bob Hairstyles

Using a round brush is just one aspect of achieving a perfect short bob hairstyle. Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain the shape and style of your short bob, it's important to schedule regular haircuts every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Short hair can be prone to dryness and breakage, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive heat styling can help prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right size round brush, you can achieve a beautiful, salon-worthy blowout for your short bob hairstyle. Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques and products until you find the perfect routine for your hair type and desired style.
