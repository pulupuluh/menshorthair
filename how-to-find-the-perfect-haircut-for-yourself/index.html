How To Find The Perfect Haircut For Yourself

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you or have you been wanting to change up your hairdo for a while but don't know where to start? Finding the perfect haircut for yourself can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personality. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find your perfect haircut:

How to:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for yourself is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Knowing your face shape is essential because it will help you choose the right haircut that will complement your features and enhance your best features while minimizing any flaws.

Tips: If you're not sure what your face shape is, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or a washable marker. Then, compare it to the six common face shapes to determine which one matches your features the most.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. Do you have thick, fine, curly, or straight hair? Knowing your hair type is crucial because it will help you choose a haircut that works with your hair texture and thickness. For example, if you have thin hair, you'll want to avoid haircuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless, like a blunt bob. Instead, you'll want to opt for haircuts that add volume and texture, like a layered bob.

Tips: If you're not sure what your hair type is, talk to your hairstylist, who can help you determine your hair type and give you recommendations for haircuts that work with your hair texture.

Step 3: Find Inspiration

The next step is to find inspiration for your perfect haircut. Look for pictures of celebrities or models with a similar face shape and hair type as yours. You can also browse through hairstyle magazines or social media platforms like Instagram or Pinterest to get ideas for your perfect haircut.

Tips: When looking for inspiration, make sure to choose pictures of people with a similar face shape and hair type as yours. This will help you get a better idea of how the haircut will look on you.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

The final step is to consult with your hairstylist. Bring in pictures of your inspiration and discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist will be able to give you recommendations for haircuts that will suit your features and lifestyle. They can also provide guidance on hair care and styling tips to help you maintain your new haircut.

Tips: Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist questions and voice your concerns. Communication is key in achieving the perfect haircut for yourself.

Tips:

- Keep in mind that haircuts require maintenance, so consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling and upkeep before choosing a haircut.

- Don't be afraid to try something new, but also don't stray too far from your comfort zone.

- Be realistic about your hair type and what haircuts will work with your natural texture and thickness.

FAQ:

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: This depends on your hair type and the haircut you choose. Generally, haircuts should be done every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I still get a trendy haircut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, but make sure to choose a haircut that works with your hair type and adds volume and texture. Talk to your hairstylist for recommendations.

Hair Care:

After getting your perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that works with your hair type.

- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use hair masks or treatments to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and steps, you can find the perfect haircut for yourself that suits your features and personality. Remember to communicate with your hairstylist, consider your face shape and hair type, and be open to trying something new. Happy haircut hunting!
