Best Short Hairstyles For Oval Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! This is considered to be the most versatile face shape, meaning you can rock a wide variety of hairstyles. However, finding the perfect short hairstyle for an oval face can still be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for oval face shapes. Read on to find your perfect match!

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and cheekbones that are high and prominent. This face shape is considered to be the most proportionate, which means you can experiment with a range of short hairstyles that will complement your features.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for an oval face, it’s important to consider your hair texture, personal style, and overall lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best short hairstyle for your oval face:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in determining the best short hairstyle for your oval face. If you have thick hair, a choppy or layered bob can help to add volume and movement. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can create the illusion of fuller hair.

Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration when choosing a short hairstyle. If you prefer a more edgy look, a short undercut or shaggy pixie cut may be perfect for you. If you prefer a more classic look, a sleek bob or blunt cut may be a better fit.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be taken into consideration when choosing a short hairstyle. If you’re busy and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut like a messy bob or textured pixie may be the best option for you.

Step by Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Short Hairstyle for Oval Face

Follow these steps to find your perfect short hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it’s important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

As mentioned earlier, your hair texture will play a big role in determining the best short hairstyle for your oval face shape.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also important when choosing a short hairstyle. Consider the clothes you wear, your makeup style, and your overall personality.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be taken into consideration when choosing a short hairstyle. Think about how much time you have to style your hair each day, and how much maintenance you’re willing to do.

Step 5: Consult with a Hairstylist

Finally, it’s always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist before making any major hair changes. They can help you determine the best short hairstyle for your oval face shape and give you tips on how to maintain it.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Once you’ve found your perfect short hairstyle, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQs

What is the best short hairstyle for an oval face?

The best short hairstyle for an oval face depends on your personal style, hair texture, and lifestyle. However, some popular options include a bob, pixie cut, or shaggy cut.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it’s important to choose a bang style that complements your face shape. A soft, side-swept bang is a great option for oval faces.

What hair color looks best on oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off a wide range of hair colors. However, some popular options include warm, golden blondes, rich brunettes, and vibrant reds.

Conclusion

Choosing the best short hairstyle for an oval face can be overwhelming, but with the right guidance, it can be a fun and exciting process. Consider your hair texture, personal style, and lifestyle when making your decision, and don’t forget to consult with a hairstylist for expert advice. With these tips, you’ll be sure to find your perfect match!
