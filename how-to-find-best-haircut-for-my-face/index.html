How To Find The Best Haircut For Your Face

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't flatter your face shape? Finding the right haircut can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect hairstyle for you. In this article, we'll show you how to find the best haircut for your face.

  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Choosing the Right Length
  • Highlighting Your Features
  • Finding the Right Stylist

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your face is determining your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, triangular, and diamond.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead.
  • Round: Your face is almost as wide as it is long, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones.
  • Square: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong, square jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and tapers to a point at the chin.
  • Triangular: Your face is wider at the jawline and narrows at the forehead.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline.

Knowing your face shape will help you choose a haircut that flatters your features.

Consider Your Hair Type

In addition to your face shape, you should also consider your hair type when choosing a haircut. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a layered cut can help control frizz and define your curls.

Consider the texture, thickness, and natural movement of your hair when choosing a haircut. Your stylist can also provide recommendations based on your hair type.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your haircut can also play a role in how it flatters your face shape. Generally, shorter haircuts are more flattering for round faces, while longer haircuts can elongate square faces. Heart-shaped faces can pull off a variety of lengths, while oval faces can rock just about any length.

When choosing a length, consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Your stylist can help you find the perfect length for you.

Highlighting Your Features

The right haircut can help highlight your best features. For example, if you have high cheekbones, a layered haircut can draw attention to them. If you have a strong jawline, a bob can accentuate it.

Consider the features you want to highlight and choose a haircut that flatters them. Your stylist can also provide recommendations based on your features.

Finding the Right Stylist

When it comes to finding the best haircut for your face, it's important to find the right stylist. Look for a stylist who has experience working with your hair type and face shape. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or read online reviews.

When you book your appointment, be sure to communicate your hair goals and concerns with your stylist. They can provide recommendations and work with you to find the perfect haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. This includes regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. You should also use the right hair products for your hair type and style.

Your stylist can provide recommendations for hair products and styling tips to help you maintain your new haircut.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

Shorter haircuts, such as a bob or pixie cut, are generally more flattering for round faces.

What is the best haircut for a square face?

Longer haircuts, such as a layered cut, can help elongate square faces.

Can I pull off bangs?

Bangs can be flattering on a variety of face shapes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type. Your stylist can provide recommendations.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

What hair products should I use?

The right hair products depend on your hair type and style. Your stylist can provide recommendations for shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

Conclusion

Finding the best haircut for your face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you can easily find a haircut that flatters your features and suits your style. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences when choosing a haircut, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for recommendations.
