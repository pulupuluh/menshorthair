How To Tell If Hair Is Fine Or Medium

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you unsure if your hair is fine or medium? Knowing your hair type is essential when it comes to choosing the right products and styling techniques. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of determining if your hair is fine or medium.

Description

Hair thickness refers to the width of each individual strand of hair. Hair can be categorized into three types: fine, medium, and thick. Fine hair has the smallest diameter, while thick hair has the largest. Medium hair falls in between these two.

How to Tell if Hair is Fine or Medium

The easiest way to determine your hair type is to take a single strand of hair and hold it up to the light. Fine hair will be barely visible, while medium hair will be visible but not as thick as a sewing thread. Thick hair will be easily visible and feel like a sewing thread.

Another way to tell is by feeling the texture of your hair. Fine hair will feel lightweight and delicate, while medium hair will have more texture and feel thicker to the touch. Thick hair will feel heavy and dense.

Step by Step

Step 1: Take a single strand of hair and hold it up to the light.

Step 2: Determine if the hair is barely visible (fine), visible but not as thick as a sewing thread (medium), or easily visible and feels like a sewing thread (thick).

Step 3: Feel the texture of your hair. Fine hair will feel lightweight and delicate, while medium hair will have more texture and feel thicker to the touch. Thick hair will feel heavy and dense.

Tips

• If you have trouble determining your hair type, consult with a hairstylist. They can help you identify your hair type and recommend the best products and styling techniques for your hair.

• Avoid using products that are too heavy or too light for your hair type. This can lead to greasy or limp hair.

• When styling your hair, use products that are specifically formulated for your hair type. This will help you achieve the best results.

FAQ

Q: What is the difference between fine and thin hair?

A: Fine hair refers to the thickness of each individual strand, while thin hair refers to the overall density of hair on the scalp.

Q: Is it possible to have a mix of fine and medium hair?

A: Yes, it is possible to have a mix of fine and medium hair. This is known as having a “fine-medium” hair type.

Hair Care

Knowing your hair type is essential when it comes to hair care. Fine hair requires lightweight products that won’t weigh it down, while medium hair can handle slightly heavier products. Here are some tips for caring for fine and medium hair:

• Fine hair: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and thickness to your hair. Avoid heavy styling products and instead opt for lightweight mousses and sprays.

• Medium hair: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Use a leave-in conditioner to add extra moisture and protection. Use a medium hold styling product for best results.

Remember, taking care of your hair starts with knowing your hair type. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best!
