How to Tell if Your Crown is Balding

The crown is the area on the top of your head towards the back, and it can be difficult to tell if it's balding. However, there are a few signs to look out for:

  • Thinning hair
  • Bald spots
  • Hair that falls out easily
  • A receding hairline
