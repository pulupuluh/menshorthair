What Swimsuit Bottom Is Best For My Body

Written by Oliver Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right swimsuit bottom for your body can be a daunting task. With so many styles and cuts available, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect one that flatters your body type. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the best swimsuit bottom for your body shape. Let's get started!

  • High-Waisted Bottoms

Determining Your Body Shape

Before we dive into the different swimsuit bottom styles, it's important to determine your body shape. Knowing your body shape will help you choose a swimsuit bottom that accentuates your best features and flatters your figure.

The four main body shapes are:

  • Pear-shaped
  • Apple-shaped
  • Straight-shaped
  • Hourglass-shaped

Take a look at yourself in the mirror and determine which shape best describes your body. Once you've identified your body shape, you can start exploring the different swimsuit bottom styles that work best for you.

High-Waisted Bottoms

High-waisted bottoms are a great option for pear-shaped and apple-shaped body types. These bottoms sit above your hips, creating a flattering silhouette that elongates your legs and accentuates your waistline. High-waisted bottoms are also ideal if you're looking for a bit more coverage and support in the tummy area. Pair these bottoms with a crop top or a bikini top that has a lower cut to balance out the look.

Bikini Bottoms

Bikini bottoms are a classic style that work well for all body types. If you have an hourglass figure, choose a bikini bottom with a high-cut leg to elongate your legs and show off your curves. For those with a straight figure, a bikini bottom with ruching or embellishments can add some dimension and create the illusion of curves. Apple-shaped and pear-shaped body types can opt for a bikini bottom with a higher waistline to create a more balanced look.

Boyshorts

Boyshorts are a great option if you're looking for more coverage and comfort. This style works well for all body types, but particularly for those with a pear-shaped figure. Boyshorts create a flattering silhouette that emphasizes your hips and thighs, while providing ample coverage in the tummy area. Pair boyshorts with a tankini top or a crop top for a sporty and chic look.

String Bottoms

String bottoms are a popular choice for those looking for minimal tan lines and maximum style. This style works well for those with an hourglass or straight-shaped figure, as it accentuates your curves and elongates your legs. String bottoms can also work for apple-shaped and pear-shaped body types, but be sure to choose a style with thicker strings and more coverage to balance out your figure.

Tips for Choosing the Right Swimsuit Bottom

  • Try on different styles and cuts to see what works best for your body shape.
  • Consider the level of coverage and support you want.
  • Choose a swimsuit bottom that complements your bikini top or tankini top.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles and colors to create a unique look.
  • Remember to always choose a swimsuit bottom that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a thong swimsuit bottom?

A: While thong swimsuit bottoms are available, they may not be appropriate for all occasions or settings. Consider the dress code and your personal comfort level before choosing a thong bottom.

Q: How do I prevent my swimsuit bottom from riding up?

A: Look for swimsuit bottoms with a secure fit and proper sizing. Applying sunscreen or body oil on the back of your thighs can also help prevent the swimsuit bottom from riding up.

Swimsuit Bottom Hair Care

After a day at the beach or pool, it's important to take care of your hair. Saltwater and chlorine can be damaging to your locks, so be sure to rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect and hydrate your hair. Avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals on your hair to prevent further damage.

Now that you know what swimsuit bottom is best for your body, it's time to hit the beach or pool with confidence. Remember to have fun and embrace your unique style!
