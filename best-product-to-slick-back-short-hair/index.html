Description

Slicking back short hair is a popular hairstyle for men who want a classic, polished look. However, finding the right product to achieve this style can be challenging. In this article, we will explore the best product to slick back short hair in 2023. We will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look and tips to maintain it.

How to Slick Back Short Hair

Before we dive into the best product to slick back short hair, let's first discuss the basics of achieving this look. Slicking back short hair involves brushing the hair back and using a product to keep it in place. The key is to find a product that provides hold without making the hair too stiff or greasy.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to achieve the slicked back short hair look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips.
  3. Spread the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the crown and working your way back.
  4. Use a comb to brush your hair back, starting at the hairline and working your way back.
  5. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingers.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Now that you know the steps, let's talk about the best product to use for this look.

Tips for Slicking Back Short Hair

Choosing the right product is key to achieving a slicked back short hair look. Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a product:

  • Look for a product that provides a strong hold without making your hair stiff or greasy.
  • Consider your hair type when selecting a product. If you have fine hair, a lighter product may work better for you. If you have thicker hair, you may need a stronger product.
  • Experiment with different products to find the right one for you. What works for one person may not work for another.
  • Don't overdo it with the product. A little goes a long way when it comes to slicking back short hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back short hair:

What is the best product to slick back short hair?

The best product to slick back short hair is a pomade, gel, or wax that provides a strong hold without making your hair stiff or greasy.

How do I know if a product is too heavy for my hair?

If a product makes your hair look greasy or weighed down, it may be too heavy for your hair. Try using a lighter product or using less of the product.

Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use a stronger product to keep the curls in place.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Short Hair

Now that you know how to slick back short hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo your hair regularly to remove any buildup from the product.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can cause buildup and damage to your hair.
  • Use a light conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and polished.

Now that you have all the information you need, go ahead and try out the slicked back short hair look with the best product for 2023. With the right product and some styling tips, you'll be able to achieve a classic, polished look in no time.
