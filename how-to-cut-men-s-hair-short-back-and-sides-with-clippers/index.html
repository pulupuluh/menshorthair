How To Cut Men s Hair Short Back And Sides With Clippers

Written by Frank Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barber shop every time you need a haircut? Learning how to cut men's hair short back and sides with clippers can save you both time and money. In this article, we will take you through an easy step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic and timeless look that has been around for decades. It is a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back undercut to a messy textured crop. The key to achieving this look is to have a good pair of clippers and some basic cutting skills.

How to

To cut men's hair short back and sides with clippers, you will need the following:

  • A good pair of clippers
  • A comb
  • A pair of scissors
  • A mirror

Once you have these tools, follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by selecting the appropriate clipper guard size for the length you want to achieve. For a short back and sides look, a grade 2 or 3 guard is recommended.
  2. Begin by trimming the sides and back of the head with the clippers, starting at the nape of the neck and working your way up towards the crown. Use the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut.
  3. Next, use the scissors to trim any longer hairs and create texture. This will give the hair a more natural and lived-in look.
  4. Move on to the top of the head and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. This will depend on the style you are going for, but generally, the hair should be shorter on the sides and longer on top.
  5. Blend the sides and top together using the clippers and scissors to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.
  6. Finally, use the clippers to trim the hairline around the ears and neckline.
  7. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers:

  • Start with longer guard sizes and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and create tension, which will make it easier to cut.
  • Invest in a good pair of clippers and scissors to ensure a clean and precise cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair short back and sides with clippers:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers, but it can be tricky to get the back of your head and neckline even. It's best to have a friend or family member help you or seek the help of a professional.

What is the best clipper guard size for a short back and sides haircut?

A grade 2 or 3 guard is recommended for a short back and sides haircut, but it ultimately depends on the length you want to achieve.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides haircut looking sharp, it's important to maintain good hair care habits. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy. Use a comb to style your hair and avoid using a brush, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short back and sides with clippers, you can achieve this classic look at home. With a little practice and patience, you'll be a pro in no time!
