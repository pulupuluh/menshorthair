What Not To Wear With Grey Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are someone who is blessed with grey hair, you may be wondering what clothes and accessories you should wear to complement your hair color. While grey hair is often associated with wisdom and experience, it can also make you look older if you don't know how to dress up properly. In this article, we will discuss what not to wear with grey hair and some tips and tricks on how to style it to perfection.

Table of Contents

  • Say No to Neon
  • Skip the All-Black Outfit
  • Don't Overdo the Jewelry
  • Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Invest in Quality Clothing
  • FAQs
  • Conclusion

Avoid Wearing Washed-out Colors

One of the biggest mistakes you can make when dressing up with grey hair is wearing washed-out colors. These colors can make you look pale and washed-out, which can add years to your appearance. Instead, opt for bold and vibrant colors that complement your hair color. Some great colors to wear with grey hair include jewel tones like emerald green, sapphire blue, and ruby red.

Say No to Neon

While bright and bold colors can look great with grey hair, neon colors should be avoided at all costs. Neon colors can clash with your hair color and make you look like you are trying too hard to be trendy. Stick to more subdued colors that complement your hair color and skin tone.

Skip the All-Black Outfit

While black is a classic color that can look great on anyone, it can be too harsh with grey hair. All-black outfits can make you look older and more tired than you actually are. Instead, try wearing black with other bold colors to add some contrast and dimension to your outfit.

Don't Overdo the Jewelry

While jewelry can add some sparkle and shine to your outfit, it is important not to overdo it. Too much jewelry can distract from your hair and make you look cluttered and overwhelmed. Stick to simple and elegant pieces that complement your outfit without overpowering it.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can make a huge difference in how you look with grey hair. If you have long hair, consider getting a shorter haircut that flatters your face shape and complements your hair color. If you have short hair, try experimenting with different styles and textures to add some dimension and interest to your look.

Invest in Quality Clothing

Investing in quality clothing is key when it comes to dressing up with grey hair. High-quality clothing will not only last longer but will also look more polished and put-together. Look for clothing that is well-made and fits your body shape perfectly.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear white with grey hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear white with grey hair. Just make sure to pair it with other bold colors to add some contrast and interest to your outfit.

Q: What type of jewelry should I wear with grey hair?

A: Simple and elegant jewelry is best when it comes to wearing jewelry with grey hair. Avoid chunky and oversized pieces that can distract from your hair color.

Conclusion

Dressing up with grey hair can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. By avoiding washed-out colors, neon colors, all-black outfits, and overdoing the jewelry, you can create a look that complements your hair color and makes you feel confident and comfortable. Remember to choose the right hairstyle and invest in quality clothing to complete your look. With these tips and tricks, you can dress up with grey hair like a pro.
