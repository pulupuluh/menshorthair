Korean Curtain Bangs For Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face and are looking for a hairstyle that will flatter your features, it's time to consider Korean curtain bangs. This trendy hairstyle has been taking the world by storm and it's not hard to see why. Not only is it chic and stylish, but it's also versatile and easy to style. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve Korean curtain bangs for round face, tips on how to maintain them, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Korean curtain bangs are a type of fringe that frames the face and falls below the eyebrows. The style is characterized by its parted center, creating a curtain-like effect that frames the face. This hairstyle was popularized by Korean celebrities and has since become a global trend. Korean curtain bangs are particularly flattering for those with round faces as they help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

How to Achieve Korean Curtain Bangs for Round Face

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the length of your hair. Here's how to achieve Korean curtain bangs for round face:

Step 1: Determine the Length of Your Bangs

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. For round faces, it's best to keep them long and sweeping as they help to elongate the face. If you have long hair, you can opt for longer curtain bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair. If you have shorter hair, you can go for shorter curtain bangs that stop at or just above your cheekbones.

Step 2: Part Your Hair in the Center

Using a comb, create a clean center part in your hair. This will help to create the curtain effect and frame your face.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Using sharp hair cutting scissors, cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the center of your forehead and moving towards the sides. Be sure to cut them slightly longer than your desired length as you can always go back and trim them later. Keep your cuts soft and feathered to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, you can style them according to your preference. You can blow-dry them using a round brush to create volume and movement, or you can air-dry them for a more relaxed look. You can also use a straightening iron to create a sleeker look.

Tips for Maintaining Korean Curtain Bangs for Round Face

Now that you've achieved the perfect Korean curtain bangs for round face, it's important to maintain them to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are a few tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at your desired length.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from looking greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too often as this can lead to greasy hair and frizz.

FAQ

What face shape is best for Korean curtain bangs?

Korean curtain bangs are particularly flattering for those with round faces as they help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. However, they can also look great on other face shapes such as oval and heart-shaped faces.

Can I achieve Korean curtain bangs if I have short hair?

Yes, you can achieve Korean curtain bangs even if you have short hair. However, it's best to keep them shorter than if you had longer hair to avoid overwhelming your face.

How do I style Korean curtain bangs?

You can style Korean curtain bangs using a round brush and a hairdryer to create volume and movement. You can also use a straightening iron for a sleeker look. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as this can lead to heat damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Now that you know everything there is to know about Korean curtain bangs for round face, it's time to take the plunge and try them out for yourself. With the right cut and styling, you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
