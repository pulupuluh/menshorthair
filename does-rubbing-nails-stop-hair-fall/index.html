Rubbing your nails together is a centuries-old technique that has been passed down from generation to generation as a way to prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. The practice is said to stimulate hair follicles, improve blood circulation, and balance the body's energy levels. But, does rubbing nails stop hair fall? Let's find out!

How to Rub Your Nails?

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand how to rub your nails properly. To do this, simply touch the fingernails of both hands together and rub them back and forth. You can do this in any position, whether sitting, standing, or even lying down.

It's important to note that you should rub the nails of both hands together and not the fingers. Also, make sure your nails are clean and free from any nail polish or dirt. You can do this technique for 5-10 minutes every day or as often as you'd like.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to rub your nails to prevent hair fall:

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable place where you can sit down and relax.
  2. Sit with your back straight and your shoulders relaxed.
  3. Place your hands on your lap with your palms facing up.
  4. Touch the fingernails of both hands together.
  5. Start rubbing your nails back and forth as fast as you can.
  6. Continue rubbing for 5-10 minutes or longer if you'd like.
  7. Take deep breaths while doing the technique to help you relax.
  8. Repeat this practice every day or as often as you'd like.

It's important to note that while this technique is safe for everyone, it may not work for everyone. Some people may see improvements in their hair growth, while others may not notice any changes.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of this hair fall prevention technique:

  • Be patient. It may take some time before you start seeing results.
  • Try to do this technique every day or as often as you can.
  • Combine this technique with a healthy diet and lifestyle.
  • Use natural hair care products that are free from harsh chemicals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

While rubbing nails together may help prevent hair fall, it's important to take care of your hair in other ways as well. Here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair fall:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling on your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners and curling irons.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle.
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels.

In conclusion, while rubbing nails together may not work for everyone, it's a safe and easy technique to try if you're experiencing hair fall. Remember to be patient and consistent with the practice, and combine it with a healthy diet and lifestyle for best results.
