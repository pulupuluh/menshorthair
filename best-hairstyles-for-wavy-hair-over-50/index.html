Best Hairstyles For Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 · 7 min read

As we age, our hair changes too. For those of us with wavy hair, it can become more difficult to find a hairstyle that suits our changing texture and face shape. However, there are plenty of options available for wavy-haired women over 50. In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyles for wavy hair over 50, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy and beautiful locks.

Table of Contents

  2. Best Hairstyles for Wavy Hair Over 50
  3. Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Wavy Hair Over 50
  4. Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair Over 50
  5. FAQs About Wavy Hair Over 50
  6. Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Over 50

How To Choose The Best Hairstyle For Wavy Hair Over 50

Choosing the right hairstyle for wavy hair over 50 can be challenging. Here are a few tips to help you find the perfect look:

  • Consider your face shape. Some hairstyles are better suited to round faces, while others work better for square or oval faces.
  • Think about your hair texture. Wavy hair can be thick, thin, or somewhere in between, so choose a style that works with your natural texture.
  • Look for inspiration. Browse magazines or online galleries to find styles that you like, and bring pictures with you to your stylist.
  • Consult a stylist. A good stylist can help you find a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type and texture.

Best Hairstyles for Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some of the best hairstyles for wavy hair over 50:

1. Shoulder-Length Waves

Shoulder-length waves are a classic look that works well with wavy hair. This style is versatile and can be worn with or without bangs.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great way to add volume and texture to wavy hair. This style is particularly flattering for women with round faces.

3. Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut is a bold and edgy look that works well with wavy hair. This style is particularly flattering for women with square or heart-shaped faces.

4. Long Layers

Long layers can add movement and volume to wavy hair. This style is particularly flattering for women with oval faces.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Wavy Hair Over 50

Here’s a step-by-step guide for styling wavy hair over 50:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful wavy hair over 50:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Wavy Hair Over 50

Q: Can I dye my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your wavy hair. However, be sure to use a dye that is gentle on your hair, and avoid over-processing your hair with frequent color treatments.

Q: How can I add volume to my wavy hair?

A: You can add volume to your wavy hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow-drying your hair upside down, or using a round brush to lift your roots.

Q: Should I brush my wavy hair?

A: It’s best to avoid brushing wavy hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, comb your hair with a wide-toothed comb when it’s wet, and use your fingers to detangle any knots.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some additional hair care tips for wavy hair over 50:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Try air-drying your hair instead of blow-drying it to minimize heat damage.
  • Get regular scalp massages to improve circulation and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can embrace your wavy hair over 50 and look and feel your best.
