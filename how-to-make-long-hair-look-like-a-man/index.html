How To Make Long Hair Look Like A Man

Written by Eveline Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you may want to change up your look and make it appear more masculine. Here are some tips and tricks to help you make your long hair look like a man.

Description

Long hair has been a popular hairstyle for men for centuries. However, not all men want their long hair to look feminine. To achieve a more masculine look, there are a variety of ways to style and cut your long hair.

How to

There are many ways to make long hair look like a man, including:

1. Get a Haircut

Getting a haircut can make a big difference in making your long hair look more masculine. Consider asking for a tapered cut, where hair is shorter on the sides and longer on top, or a layered cut that adds texture and volume to your hair.

2. Style Your Hair

Styling your hair can also help make it look more masculine. Consider using hair gel or wax to create a messy, textured look. You can also slick back your hair for a more polished look.

3. Add Facial Hair

Adding facial hair, such as a beard or mustache, can also help make your long hair look more masculine. Be sure to keep your facial hair well-groomed and trimmed to avoid looking unkempt.

4. Wear Masculine Clothing

Wearing masculine clothing, such as leather jackets or flannel shirts, can also help make your long hair look more masculine. Avoid wearing clothing that is too feminine, such as floral prints or tight-fitting clothing.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your long hair look like a man:

Step 1: Get a Haircut

Visit a barber or stylist to get a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consider a tapered or layered cut to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Use hair gel or wax to create a messy, textured look. Slick back your hair for a more polished look.

Step 3: Add Facial Hair

Grow a beard or mustache to add a more masculine look to your appearance. Keep your facial hair well-groomed and trimmed.

Step 4: Wear Masculine Clothing

Choose clothing that is masculine and suits your personal style. Avoid clothing that is too feminine or tight-fitting.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your long hair look like a man:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

Try out different hairstyles and facial hair styles to find what works best for you.

2. Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are designed for men's hair, such as hair gel or wax.

3. Embrace Your Personal Style

Don't be afraid to embrace your personal style and express yourself through your hair and clothing choices.

FAQ

1. Can I make my long hair look like a man without cutting it?

Yes, you can style your long hair in a way that looks more masculine without getting a haircut. Consider using hair gel or wax to create a messy, textured look, or slick back your hair for a more polished appearance.

2. Should I grow a beard or mustache to make my long hair look more masculine?

Adding facial hair, such as a beard or mustache, can help make your long hair look more masculine. However, it's important to keep your facial hair well-groomed and trimmed to avoid looking unkempt.

Hair Care

To maintain your long hair and keep it looking healthy, be sure to:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair at least every other day to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your long hair look like a man and achieve the masculine look you desire.
