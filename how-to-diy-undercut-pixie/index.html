How To Diy Undercut Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up with an edgier look? Then you might want to try the DIY undercut pixie cut! This bold and daring hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It's low maintenance, versatile, and adds a touch of personality to any outfit. But before you grab your scissors, let's dive into how to achieve this look at home without any mishaps.

Description

The undercut pixie cut is a trendy hairstyle that features shaved or closely cropped sides and a longer top. It's a great way to showcase your facial features and draw attention to your eyes, cheekbones, and jawline. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some edge and spunk to their look.

How to

Ready to try out the undercut pixie cut for yourself? Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Gather your supplies

Before you start snipping away, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a razor, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Start by sectioning off the top of your hair with clips, leaving the sides and back exposed. This will help you focus on the undercut portion of the cut.

Step 3: Trim the sides

Using your scissors or razor, carefully trim the sides and back of your hair, leaving a short length of hair. If you're unsure about how short to go, start with a longer length and gradually work your way down until you're satisfied with the result.

Step 4: Cut the top

Once you've finished trimming the sides, unclip the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Use your scissors to cut the top to your desired length. You can opt for a choppy, textured look or a sleek, smooth style.

Step 5: Blend the sides and top

Using your scissors or razor, blend the sides and top together to create a seamless transition. Make sure to take your time and blend the hair evenly to avoid any choppy or uneven lines.

Step 6: Style your hair

Now that you've achieved your undercut pixie cut, it's time to style it! You can use a hair wax, gel, or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Play around with different styles and see what works best for you.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when attempting a DIY undercut pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way down to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a razor for a more precise cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about anything.
  • Take breaks if you need them to avoid making any errors.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the DIY undercut pixie cut:

Is it easy to maintain?

Yes, the undercut pixie cut is low maintenance and easy to maintain. You can simply style it with some hair wax or gel and you're good to go.

Will it suit my face shape?

The undercut pixie cut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it's best for those with oval or heart-shaped faces. It can also complement high cheekbones and a strong jawline.

Can I dye my hair after getting an undercut pixie cut?

Yes, you can dye your hair after getting an undercut pixie cut. Just make sure to wait a few days before doing so to allow your hair to recover from any potential damage caused by the cut.

Hair Care

To maintain your undercut pixie cut, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. You can use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and a hair oil to add shine. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style. Avoid using heat tools too often and protect your hair from the sun to prevent any damage.

Now that you know how to achieve a DIY undercut pixie cut, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take your time and be patient with the process. Good luck and have fun experimenting with your new edgy look.
