How to Get Mens Haircuts Short Sides Long Top Straight Hair

The first step in getting this haircut is to find a good barber or stylist who has experience with this style. You can also bring a photo of the style to show your stylist exactly what you want. The haircut typically involves cutting the sides and back of the hair short, while leaving the top longer. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference.

The mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways. You can leave the hair on top flat or add some volume by using a hair dryer and styling products. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and show them a photo of the style you want.
  3. The barber will cut the sides and back of your hair short, using clippers or scissors.
  4. The hair on top will be left longer and can be trimmed or layered depending on your preference.
  5. The barber will style your hair and show you how to maintain the style at home.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling the mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair:

  • Use a hair dryer to add volume to the hair on top.
  • Apply a styling product, such as a pomade, to the hair on top to keep it in place.
  • Try different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape of the haircut.

FAQs

What face shape suits this haircut?

The mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair suits most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on those with oval or square faces.

What hair type suits this haircut?

The mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair suits those with straight hair, but it can also work for those with wavy or curly hair.

How often should I get this haircut?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for those with mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid damaging your hair when styling it.

In conclusion, the mens haircuts short sides long top straight hair is a stylish and versatile haircut that is perfect for those who want to try something new. With the right styling products and a good barber, you can achieve a look that is both trendy and timeless.
