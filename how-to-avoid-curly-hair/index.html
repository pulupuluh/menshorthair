How To Avoid Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your hair always curling up after a fresh blowout or relaxer? Do you want to achieve straight, sleek locks without the added hassle of curls? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss various tips and tricks on how to avoid curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Avoid Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips on Avoiding Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care and Maintenance

How to Avoid Curly Hair

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it is important to understand why hair curls in the first place. Hair curls when the cuticles on the hair strands are lifted, causing the hair to expand and take on a curly shape. Therefore, to avoid curly hair, we need to focus on keeping the cuticles flat and smooth.

The following are some ways to avoid curly hair:

1. Use a Quality Flat Iron or Straightening Brush

Invest in a good flat iron or straightening brush that can effectively straighten your hair without causing damage. Look for one that has adjustable heat settings and ceramic plates that can distribute heat evenly. Using the right tool can make a big difference in preventing curls.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair in Sections

When blow drying your hair, divide it into sections and use a round brush to pull each section straight while blow drying. This will help to keep the cuticles flat and smooth, while also giving your hair a sleek look.

3. Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Look for a heat protectant that also has smoothing properties to help keep your hair straight.

4. Avoid Humidity

Humidity can cause your hair to curl or frizz, so try to avoid it as much as possible. If you live in a humid area, consider using a dehumidifier in your home or car to help keep the air dry.

5. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in preventing curls. Look for products that are designed for straight hair, such as smoothing shampoos and conditioners. Avoid products that are designed for curly hair, as they can actually enhance curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve straight, sleek hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair straight and smooth.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and use a round brush to pull each section straight while blow drying. This will help to keep the cuticles flat and smooth, while also giving your hair a sleek look.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron or Straightening Brush

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or straightening brush to further straighten your hair. Make sure to use a quality tool that can effectively straighten your hair without causing damage.

Step 5: Apply a Smoothing Serum

Finish off by applying a smoothing serum to your hair. This will help to keep your hair straight and smooth throughout the day.

Tips on Avoiding Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you avoid curly hair:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help to reduce friction on your hair, which can prevent curls and tangles. Plus, they feel luxurious and can help to prevent wrinkles on your face!

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Touching your hair can cause the cuticles to lift, which can lead to curls and frizz. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair healthy and smooth, which can prevent curls. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week for best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. However, keep in mind that curly hair is more prone to damage, so be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using too much heat.

Q: Can I prevent curly hair without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can prevent curly hair by using the right hair products and avoiding humidity. Look for products that are designed for straight hair and avoid products that are designed for curly hair.

Hair Care and Maintenance

To keep your hair straight and healthy, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to keep it straight and smooth.

2. Avoid Using Too Much Heat

Using too much heat can cause damage to your hair, so be sure to use heat styling tools sparingly.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, so be sure to protect it by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve straight, sleek hair without the added hassle of curls. Remember to use the right hair products, protect your hair from heat and humidity, and follow a good hair care routine for best results.
