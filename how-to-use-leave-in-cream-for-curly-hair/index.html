How To Use Leave In Cream For Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing or a curse depending on how you manage it. One of the best ways to keep your curls moisturized and defined is by using a leave-in cream. However, not everyone knows how to use leave-in cream for curly hair. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about how to use leave-in cream for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Leave In Cream for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Leave In Cream for Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Leave-in creams are a type of hair product that is designed to be left in your hair after washing. They can help to moisturize your hair, define your curls, and reduce frizz. Leave-in creams come in different types, including lightweight, medium hold, and heavy hold. It's important to choose a leave-in cream that matches your hair type and curl pattern.

How to Use Leave In Cream for Curly Hair

Using leave-in cream for curly hair is easy, but there are a few things to keep in mind. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use leave-in cream for curly hair:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. After washing your hair, gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz.
  3. Take a dime-sized amount of leave-in cream and rub it between your palms.
  4. Apply the leave-in cream to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  5. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form.
  6. If you have thicker or longer hair, you may need to apply more leave-in cream. Avoid applying too much as this can weigh down your curls.
  7. Once you've applied the leave-in cream, you can air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Tips for Using Leave In Cream for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of using leave-in cream for curly hair:

  • Don't apply leave-in cream to your scalp as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • If you have fine hair, use a lightweight leave-in cream to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • If you have thick or coarse hair, use a heavier leave-in cream to moisturize your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying leave-in cream.
  • Try different techniques, such as raking or praying hands, to apply leave-in cream and find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using leave-in cream for curly hair:

  • Can I use leave-in cream every day? Yes, you can use leave-in cream every day. However, be mindful of how much you're using as too much can weigh down your curls.
  • Should I apply leave-in cream to wet or dry hair? It's best to apply leave-in cream to wet hair as this allows it to penetrate your hair shaft and moisturize your curls.
  • Can I use leave-in cream on straight hair? Yes, you can use leave-in cream on straight hair to moisturize and protect it.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Using leave-in cream for curly hair is just one part of a good hair care routine. Here are some other tips to keep your curls healthy and defined:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your curls.
  • Protect your curls at night by using a satin or silk pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet.

By following these tips and using leave-in cream for curly hair, you can keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

Now that you know how to use leave-in cream for curly hair, go ahead and try it out for yourself. With the right products and techniques, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls every day.
