Best Round Brush For Curly Hair Achieving Perfect Curls

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you know the importance of finding the perfect round brush to tame your locks. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we will explore the best round brushes for curly hair, how to use them, and some tips for achieving perfect curls.

Description

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you want to achieve a smooth, polished look. A round brush is a great tool to help you achieve this style. However, not all round brushes are created equal, and finding the right one for your hair type is key.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Curly Hair

When choosing a round brush for curly hair, there are a few key factors to consider:

Bristles

The bristles on your round brush are important because they determine how well the brush will grip your hair. For curly hair, look for a brush with natural bristles, like boar or horsehair. These bristles are gentler on your hair and will help to minimize breakage and frizz.

Size

The size of your round brush will depend on the length of your hair. For shorter hair, choose a smaller brush with a diameter of 1-1.5 inches. For longer hair, a larger brush with a diameter of 2-2.5 inches will work best.

Shape

The shape of your round brush is also important. Look for a brush with a barrel that is slightly curved, which will help to create a smooth, polished look without flattening your curls.

Step by Step: Using a Round Brush on Curly Hair

Now that you have chosen the best round brush for your curly hair, it's time to put it to use. Follow these steps for the perfect curls:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from your blow dryer. For best results, use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into 2-3 inch sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that each section is evenly styled.

Step 3: Begin Styling

Starting at the back of your head, take a section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of your round brush. Use your blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry the hair as you brush it. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are styled.

Step 4: Finish with a Cool Shot

Once your hair is dry, use the cool shot on your blow dryer to set your curls. This will help to keep them in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve perfect curls:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Choosing the right hair products for curly hair is important. Look for products that are designed to enhance curls, like curl defining creams or mousses.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It

When using a round brush on curly hair, it's important not to overdo it. Too much brushing can cause frizz and breakage. Use a light touch and be gentle with your curls.

Tip 3: Embrace Your Natural Texture

Remember, curly hair is beautiful in its natural state. Don't feel like you need to straighten or smooth out your curls completely. Embrace your natural texture and work with it to create a style that you love.

FAQ

What is the best round brush for curly hair?

The best round brush for curly hair is one with natural bristles, a slightly curved barrel, and the right size for your hair length.

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

No, it's best to use a round brush on dry hair. Wet hair is more fragile and can be easily damaged by heat styling tools.

How often should I use a round brush on my curly hair?

It's best to use a round brush on curly hair no more than once a week to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Curly hair is more prone to dryness, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your curls moisturized and healthy. Try using a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can be damaging to curly hair. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, and when you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

With the right round brush and proper care, you can achieve perfect curls that will turn heads wherever you go. Remember to embrace your natural texture and work with it to create a style that you love.
