Best Mid Length Hairstyles For Over 50

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a fresh new hairstyle, mid length hairstyles are a great option to consider. Not only are they versatile and easy to maintain, they're also perfect for a wide range of face shapes and hair textures. In this article, we'll be taking a look at the best mid length hairstyles for over 50, and giving you some tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Mid Length Hairstyle for You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Mid Length Hairstyle
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Length Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Mid Length Hairstyles for Over 50
  • Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

How to Choose the Best Mid Length Hairstyle for You

When it comes to choosing the best mid length hairstyle for you, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, think about your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a style with layers to create more dimension. If you have a long face, a blunt cut may work better to add volume.

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can help create the illusion of thickness. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help tame unruly locks.

Finally, think about your personal style. Do you prefer a classic or edgy look? Are you willing to spend time styling your hair, or do you need a low maintenance option? These factors can all influence your choice of mid length hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Mid Length Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen your mid length hairstyle, it's time to achieve the perfect look. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a center part or a side part, depending on your preference.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on the style you've chosen.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Length Hairstyle

Maintaining your mid length hairstyle is key to keeping it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in quality styling tools to make the process easier and more effective.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep split ends at bay.

FAQs About Mid Length Hairstyles for Over 50

Here are some common questions about mid length hairstyles for over 50:

Q: What are the best mid length hairstyles for fine hair?

A: Layered cuts can help create the illusion of thickness, while blunt cuts can help add volume.

Q: Can mid length hairstyles work for curly hair?

A: Yes! Mid length hairstyles can work for a wide range of hair textures, including curly hair. Just be sure to choose a style that complements your natural curls.

Q: Are bangs a good option for mid length hairstyles?

A: Bangs can be a great option for mid length hairstyles, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your mid length hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving the perfect mid length hairstyle for over 50. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one.
