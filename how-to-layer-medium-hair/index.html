How To Layer Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to add some dimension and depth to your medium-length hair? One way to achieve this is by getting layers. Layered hair can add volume, texture, and movement to your locks, making it a popular choice for many women.

How to Layer Medium Hair

Layering medium hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create movement and texture. It can be done on all hair types, from straight to curly, and is a great way to switch up your look without losing too much length.

Before getting started, it's important to find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting layers. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape to create a customized layering technique that suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to layer medium hair:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will give your hairstylist a better idea of how your hair falls naturally and what areas need more layering.

Step 2: Section your hair

Your hairstylist will likely start by sectioning your hair into manageable sections. This will help them to see and cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Determine the length of your layers

Your hairstylist will determine the length of your layers based on your face shape and the look you're going for. For a subtle look, they may choose to cut longer layers. For a more dramatic look, shorter layers may be used.

Step 4: Cut the layers

Using shears, your hairstylist will start cutting your hair at different lengths to create the layered effect. They may use a variety of techniques, such as point cutting or slicing, to create texture.

Step 5: Blend the layers

Once all the layers have been cut, your hairstylist will blend them together to create a seamless look. This is done by feathering the edges of each layer with scissors or a razor.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once your hair has been cut and styled, your hairstylist may recommend products that can help enhance your layered look. These may include texturizing sprays or volumizing mousses.

Tips for Layering Medium Hair

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your layered medium hair:

  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience cutting layers.
  • Be clear about the look you're going for.
  • Consider your face shape when deciding on the length of your layers.
  • Use products that enhance texture and volume.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layered look.

FAQ

How often should I get my layered medium hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Will layering my hair make it appear thinner?

No, layering can actually make your hair appear thicker and fuller, as it adds dimension and movement to your locks.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail after getting layers?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail after getting layers. However, the layers may create a more textured and tousled look.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your layered hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layered look.

Now that you know how to layer medium hair, it's time to book your appointment with a trusted hairstylist. With the right technique and care, your layered locks will look healthy, stylish, and full of volume.
