How To Style Men s Medium Length Curly Hair

Men's medium length curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls work for you. In this article, we will discuss how to style men's medium length curly hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your curls healthy and looking their best.

How to Style Men's Medium Length Curly Hair

The key to styling men's medium length curly hair is to embrace your natural curls and work with them, rather than against them. Here are some tips to help you style your curls:

  • Start with clean, damp hair
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair
  • Scrunch your hair while drying to enhance your curls
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls
  • Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your curls in place

Now that you know the basics, let's dive into a step-by-step guide for styling men's medium length curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your medium length curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair. This will help keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a curl cream, gel, or mousse.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Set the dryer to a low heat and low speed setting.
  5. Scrunch your hair while drying to enhance your curls. Use your hands to lift your hair at the roots and scrunch your curls upwards towards your scalp.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls. Gently separate your curls and arrange them into the desired style.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your curls in place.

With these steps, you should have a defined and stylish look that showcases your curls. But there are some additional tips that can help you achieve the perfect style.

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your medium length curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair. This can help you achieve the best possible style and make it easier to manage your curls.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, on your hair. These can damage your curls and make them lose their shape.

With these tips, you should be well on your way to achieving the perfect style for your medium length curly hair. But there are still some common questions that people have about styling curly hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's medium length curly hair:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should aim to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.

What products should I use to style my curly hair?

You should use a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse, to style your curly hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair.

How can I avoid frizz in my curly hair?

To avoid frizz, you should avoid over-washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using hot tools on your hair.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Length Curly Hair

To keep your medium length curly hair looking its best, you should follow a hair care routine that is specifically designed for curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, on your hair. These can damage your curls and make them lose their shape.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair. This can help you achieve the best possible style and make it easier to manage your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to style your men's medium length curly hair with confidence and ease. Remember to embrace your natural curls and work with them, rather than against them, to achieve the best possible style.
