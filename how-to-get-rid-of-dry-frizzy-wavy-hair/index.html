How To Get Rid Of Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Mar 18, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Dry frizzy wavy hair can be a real hassle to deal with. It can make your hair look dull, lifeless, and unkempt. However, there are several ways to get rid of dry frizzy wavy hair and restore your hair's natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore how to get rid of dry frizzy wavy hair and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Description

Dry frizzy wavy hair is a common hair problem that can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, climate, and hair care practices. When hair is dry, it lacks moisture, which can make it prone to breakage and damage. Frizz occurs when the hair cuticle becomes raised, allowing moisture to enter the hair shaft and causing it to swell. Wavy hair can exacerbate the problem of dryness and frizz, as the natural texture of wavy hair tends to be more porous and prone to frizz.

How to Get Rid of Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

If you're struggling with dry frizzy wavy hair, there are several things you can do to get rid of it:

1. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner

Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed to hydrate and moisturize dry hair. These products should contain ingredients like glycerin, shea butter, and coconut oil, which can help to nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair an extra dose of moisture. Apply the treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends, and leave it in for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it even more prone to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, or even less frequently if your hair is particularly dry.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Look for a product that is specifically designed for wavy or curly hair, as these products will be more effective at taming frizz.

5. Use a hair oil

Applying a hair oil to your hair can help to smooth down the hair cuticle and prevent frizz. Look for a lightweight hair oil that won't weigh your hair down, and apply it sparingly to the ends of your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting rid of dry frizzy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for dry hair. Apply the shampoo to your hair and massage your scalp, then rinse it out thoroughly. Follow up with the conditioner, focusing on the ends of your hair.

Step 2: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to give it an extra dose of moisture. Leave the treatment in for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Don't over-wash your hair.

Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, or even less frequently if your hair is particularly dry.

Step 4: Apply a leave-in conditioner.

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day.

Step 5: Use a hair oil.

Apply a lightweight hair oil to the ends of your hair to help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Tips to Prevent Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help prevent dry frizzy wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach and hair dye, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will be gentler on your hair than a brush.

FAQs

A: Yes, there are several things you can do to get rid of dry frizzy wavy hair, including using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment, and using a leave-in conditioner and hair oil.

Q: What causes dry frizzy wavy hair?

Frizz occurs when the hair cuticle becomes raised, allowing moisture to enter the hair shaft and causing it to swell. Wavy hair can exacerbate the problem of dryness and frizz, as the natural texture of wavy hair tends to be more porous and prone to frizz.

A: Yes, you can prevent dry frizzy wavy hair by using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment, and avoiding over-washing your hair. You can also protect your hair from the sun and avoid using harsh chemicals and hot tools on your hair.

Hair Care Routine for Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here's a hair care routine that's ideal for dry frizzy wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than every other day, using a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra dose of moisture.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it to help keep it moisturized throughout the day.
  • Apply a lightweight hair oil to the ends of your hair to help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools and harsh chemicals on your hair, and protect your hair from the sun.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of dry frizzy wavy hair and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
