How To Style Unmanageable Wavy Hair Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Eveline Feb 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unmanageable, frizzy, and unruly wavy hair? If so, you're not alone. Many people with wavy hair struggle to style it in a way that looks polished and put-together. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful, defined wave that will turn heads. In this article, we'll share our top tips and techniques for styling unmanageable wavy hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Unmanageable Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Style Unmanageable Wavy Hair

The key to styling unmanageable wavy hair is to use products that are specifically designed for wavy hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and glycerin, which can help to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for wavy hair, and avoid anything that contains sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, paying extra attention to the ends.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product that is designed for wavy hair, like a curl cream or mousse. Use a quarter-sized amount for shorter hair, and a golf ball-sized amount for longer hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching as you go to help define your waves.

Step 4: Diffuse Dry Your Hair

Instead of using a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting, and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, finish with a few drops of hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz. Apply the oil to the ends of your hair, and avoid applying it to your roots, which can make your hair look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for styling unmanageable wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for wavy hair, and avoid anything that contains sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess moisture. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, paying extra attention to the ends.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that is designed for wavy hair, like a curl cream or mousse. Use a quarter-sized amount for shorter hair, and a golf ball-sized amount for longer hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching as you go to help define your waves.

Step 5: Diffuse Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting, and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Oil

Finish with a few drops of hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz. Apply the oil to the ends of your hair, and avoid applying it to your roots, which can make your hair look greasy.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling unmanageable wavy hair:

  • Try using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz while you sleep.
  • If you're short on time, try using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture, but most people with wavy hair can get away with washing their hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: What should I do if my waves fall flat?

A: Try refreshing your waves with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. You can also try using a curling wand to add definition to your waves.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

In addition to using the right styling products, it's important to take care of your wavy hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can finally tame your unmanageable wavy hair and achieve beautiful, defined waves that will turn heads.
