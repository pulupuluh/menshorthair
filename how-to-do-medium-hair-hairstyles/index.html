How To Do Medium Hair Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have medium-length hair, there are plenty of hairstyles you can try. From braids to buns, there are endless possibilities. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for creating beautiful medium hair hairstyles. Read on for our guide on how to do medium hair hairstyles.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in creating medium hair hairstyles is to determine what style you want to achieve. Do you want to wear your hair up or down? Do you want to create a braided style or a bun? Once you have an idea of the style you want, you can start gathering the tools you'll need.

Some of the tools you may need include:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair ties or bobby pins
  • Hair spray or other styling products
  • Straightening or curling iron (optional)

Step-by-Step

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in creating any hairstyle is to start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and then let it air dry or blow dry it. If you want to add some texture to your hair, you can use a curling or straightening iron to create waves or curls.

2. Choose Your Style

Once your hair is dry, it's time to choose the style you want to create. Some popular options for medium-length hair include:

  • Half up, half down
  • Ponytail
  • Braids
  • Bun
  • Top knot

3. Start Styling

Once you've chosen your style, it's time to start styling. Follow these steps to create some popular medium hair hairstyles:

Half Up, Half Down

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and pull it back. You can use your fingers or a comb to create a smooth line.
  3. Secure the section of hair with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a curling or straightening iron to add some texture to the bottom half of your hair.

Ponytail

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather all your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Use a curling or straightening iron to add some texture to the ends of your hair.
  4. Apply some hair spray or other styling product to keep your ponytail in place.

Braids

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Choose the type of braid you want to create (e.g. French braid, fishtail braid, etc.) and start braiding your hair.
  3. Secure the end of your braid with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a curling or straightening iron to add some texture to the ends of your hair.

Bun

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather all your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a curling or straightening iron to add some texture to the ends of your hair.

Top Knot

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather all your hair at the top of your head and twist it into a knot.
  3. Secure the knot with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a curling or straightening iron to add some texture to the ends of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating medium hair hairstyles:

  • Use hair ties and bobby pins that match the color of your hair to create a seamless look.
  • Apply styling products sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • If you have fine hair, use volumizing products to add some lift.
  • If you have thick hair, use smoothing products to tame frizz.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What are some popular medium hair hairstyles?

Some popular medium hair hairstyles include half up, half down, ponytail, braids, bun, and top knot.

What tools do I need to create medium hair hairstyles?

You may need a brush or comb, hair ties or bobby pins, hair spray or other styling products, and a straightening or curling iron (optional).

How do I keep my hairstyles in place?

You can use hair spray or other styling products to keep your hairstyles in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create beautiful medium hair hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going to work, school, or a special event, these hairstyles will help you look and feel your best.
