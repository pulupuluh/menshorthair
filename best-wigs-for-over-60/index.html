Best Wigs For Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the best wig for over 60? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the top wigs that are perfect for women over 60. We will cover everything from how to choose the right wig to how to care for it. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Wig

Choosing the right wig is essential to ensure that you look and feel your best. Here are some tips to help you choose the right wig:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a wig, it's essential to consider your face shape. A wig that complements your face shape will enhance your overall appearance. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round faces should choose wigs with layers that frame the face.
  • Square faces should choose wigs with soft layers that soften the jawline.
  • Oval faces can wear almost any wig style.

Choose the Right Color

The right color can make all the difference when it comes to choosing a wig. If you're not sure which color to choose, consider your skin tone:

  • Warm skin tones look best in shades of golden blonde, copper, and auburn.
  • Cool skin tones look best in shades of ash blonde, black, and cool brown.
  • Neutral skin tones look great in almost any color.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a wig, it's essential to consider your lifestyle. If you're active, you may want a wig that's easy to maintain and won't get in the way. If you're more low-key, you may prefer a wig that's more natural-looking.

The Best Wigs for Over 60

Now that you know how to choose the right wig, let's take a look at some of the best wigs for women over 60:

1. Short Wavy Wig

A short wavy wig is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance, natural-looking style. This wig is easy to style and can be worn for almost any occasion.

2. Bob Wig

A bob wig is a classic style that never goes out of fashion. This wig is perfect for women who want a chic and sophisticated look.

3. Curly Wig

A curly wig is perfect for women who want a fun and flirty look. This wig is easy to maintain and can be worn for almost any occasion.

4. Pixie Cut Wig

A pixie cut wig is perfect for women who want a bold and edgy look. This wig is easy to style and can be worn for almost any occasion.

Tips for Wearing a Wig

Wearing a wig can take some getting used to, but with these tips, you'll be a pro in no time:

Wear a Wig Cap

A wig cap can help keep your wig in place and prevent it from slipping. It can also protect your scalp from irritation.

Style Your Wig

Styling your wig can help it look more natural. Use a wig brush or comb to style your wig and avoid using heat tools.

Take Care of Your Wig

Taking care of your wig is essential to ensure it looks great and lasts a long time. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for washing and conditioning your wig.

FAQ

1. How do I know what size wig to buy?

Measure your head before buying a wig. Use a tape measure to measure around your head, just above your ears and across your forehead.

2. Can I wear a wig if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have sensitive skin. Look for wigs made from hypoallergenic materials.

Hair Care

Even if you're wearing a wig, it's essential to take care of your natural hair. Here are some tips:

Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoid using hot water.

Protect Your Hair

Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat tools.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein to promote hair growth.

With these tips and recommendations, you're sure to find the perfect wig for you. Remember to take care of your wig and natural hair to keep them looking great. Happy wig shopping!
