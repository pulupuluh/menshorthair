What Do Guys Think Of Armpit Hair

Armpit hair has always been a taboo topic, especially for women. It’s something that we’re taught to shave or wax from a young age, but have you ever wondered what guys actually think of armpit hair? In this article, we’ll dive into the world of armpit hair and explore what guys really think about it.

Description

Armpit hair is a natural part of our body, but it’s often seen as unattractive or unfeminine. However, there are a growing number of women who are embracing their armpit hair and choosing not to shave or wax it. So, what do guys think of armpit hair?

The answer is not as straightforward as you might think. Some guys find armpit hair attractive, while others find it a turn-off. It really depends on the individual and their personal preferences.

How to

If you’re thinking of growing out your armpit hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Firstly, it’s important to remember that everyone’s hair grows at a different rate, so it may take longer for some people to see noticeable growth.

You may also experience some discomfort or itching as your hair grows, especially if you’re used to shaving or waxing. To minimize this, try using a gentle exfoliating scrub and moisturizer to keep your skin soft and smooth.

Step by Step

1. Stop shaving or waxing your armpits.

2. Be patient and wait for your hair to grow.

3. Use a gentle exfoliating scrub and moisturizer to minimize discomfort and itching.

4. Embrace your armpit hair and rock it with confidence!

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you feel confident and comfortable with your armpit hair:

  • Wear sleeveless tops and dresses to show off your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as braiding or dyeing your hair.
  • Find a supportive community of people who also embrace their body hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about armpit hair:

Q: Will my armpit hair grow back thicker if I stop shaving?

A: No, this is a common myth. Your hair may appear thicker if you’ve been shaving regularly, but this is just because the hair has been cut at a blunt angle.

Q: Is it unhygienic to have armpit hair?

A: No, armpit hair is not unhygienic. In fact, it can actually help to trap bacteria and prevent it from spreading.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your armpit hair, just like you would any other hair on your body. Here are a few tips for keeping your armpit hair healthy:

  • Wash your armpits regularly to keep them clean.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

In conclusion, whether or not you choose to grow out your armpit hair is a personal decision. While some guys may find it attractive, others may not. The most important thing is to do what makes you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
