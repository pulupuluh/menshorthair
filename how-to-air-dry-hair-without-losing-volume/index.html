How To Air Dry Hair Without Losing Volume

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of blow drying your hair after every wash just to maintain its volume? Or do you simply want to avoid damaging your hair with heat styling tools? Air drying your hair is a great way to achieve a natural and effortless look, but it can often result in flat and lifeless hair. In this blog post, we will share with you the best tips and tricks to air dry your hair without losing volume.

How to Air Dry Hair Without Losing Volume

Air drying your hair can seem like a simple and easy process, but there are a few things you can do to ensure that your hair maintains its volume and shape. Here are some steps you can follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to air drying your hair without losing volume is to start with clean hair. Make sure to wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra lift to your roots.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Product

Before you start air drying your hair, apply a volumizing product to your roots. This will help to add some extra lift and volume to your hair. You can use a volumizing mousse, spray or powder depending on your hair type and preference.

Step 3: Use a Microfiber Towel

Instead of using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and help to prevent frizz and breakage.

Step 4: Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you have dried your hair with a microfiber towel, avoid touching it as much as possible. Touching your hair can cause it to lose its volume and shape, so let it air dry naturally.

Step 5: Use a Diffuser

If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your natural texture. This will help to maintain the volume and shape of your curls or waves while still air drying your hair.

Step 6: Finish with a Volumizing Spray

Once your hair is completely dry, finish off with a volumizing spray. This will help to add some extra lift and hold to your hair, without weighing it down.

Tips for Air Drying Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you air dry your hair without losing volume:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before air drying
  • Avoid brushing your hair after it has dried, as this can cause it to lose its shape
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain its shape
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQ

Can I air dry my hair if I have fine hair?

Yes, you can air dry your hair if you have fine hair. Just make sure to use a volumizing product and avoid touching your hair too much while it dries.

How long does it take for hair to air dry?

The time it takes for hair to air dry depends on your hair type and length. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to 2 hours for hair to air dry completely.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to maintain its health and volume. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times a week
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils

By following these tips and tricks, you can air dry your hair without losing volume and achieve a natural and effortless look. Give it a try and see the difference it makes!
