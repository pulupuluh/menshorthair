What Do Numbers Mean Barber A Guide To Understanding Barber Shop Number System

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In a barber shop, you may have heard the barber ask you what number you want for your haircut. This may leave you confused, especially if it's your first time getting a haircut in a barber shop. In this article, we will discuss what do numbers mean barber and how to understand the barber shop number system.

What Do Numbers Mean Barber?

The numbers in a barber shop refer to the length of the hair that will be left after the haircut. Each number corresponds to a specific clipper guard. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be left. For example, a number 1 guard will leave hair that is approximately 1/8 inch long, while a number 4 guard will leave hair that is approximately 1/2 inch long.

How to Understand the Barber Shop Number System

To understand the barber shop number system, you need to know the different clipper guard sizes and their corresponding numbers. Here are the most common clipper guard sizes:

Number 0

This is the shortest clipper guard size and will leave the hair almost bald.

Number 1

This is the second shortest clipper guard size and will leave the hair approximately 1/8 inch long.

Number 2

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 1/4 inch long.

Number 3

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 3/8 inch long.

Number 4

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 1/2 inch long.

Number 5

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 5/8 inch long.

Number 6

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 3/4 inch long.

Number 7

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 7/8 inch long.

Number 8

This clipper guard size will leave the hair approximately 1 inch long.

Tips for Choosing the Right Clipper Guard Size

Choosing the right clipper guard size can be tricky, especially if it's your first time getting a haircut in a barber shop. Here are some tips to help you choose the right clipper guard size:

  • If you want a very short haircut, choose a number 0 or number 1 clipper guard.
  • If you want a longer haircut, choose a number 2, 3, or 4 clipper guard.
  • If you're not sure what clipper guard size to choose, ask your barber for recommendations.

FAQs

What if I don't want a clipper cut?

If you don't want a clipper cut, you can ask your barber to use scissors instead. Scissors can be used to create a variety of lengths and styles, depending on your preference.

What if I want a specific length?

If you want a specific length, you can ask your barber to use a ruler or measuring tape to ensure that the hair is cut to the desired length. This is especially important if you're getting a haircut for a special occasion or event.

Can I bring a picture of the hairstyle I want?

Yes, you can bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your barber. This can be helpful in ensuring that you and your barber are on the same page about the length and style of the haircut.

Hair Care Tips

After getting a haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a hair dryer or towel to dry your hair gently. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can damage the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often. These tools can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Understanding what do numbers mean barber can help you communicate effectively with your barber and ensure that you get the haircut you want. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain a healthy and stylish look.
