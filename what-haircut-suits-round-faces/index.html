What Haircut Suits Round Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a proud owner of a round face? Do you often feel like your haircuts don't flatter your face shape as much as you'd like? Fear not, because in this article, we'll be discussing what haircut suits round faces. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved lines and lack of angles. They typically have wider cheekbones and a rounded jawline. When it comes to haircuts, the goal is to create the illusion of a more angular face shape by adding height and volume to the crown and keeping the sides close to the head. This will help elongate the face and balance out the roundness.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid styles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or a full, voluminous bob. Instead, opt for styles that create height at the crown, such as layered cuts or a side-swept fringe. Secondly, consider the length of your hair. Shorter haircuts can be very flattering on round faces, as they help to elongate the neck and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different textures, such as curls or waves, as they can add dimension and movement to the hair.

Step By Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Step 2: Choose a haircut that adds height and volume to the crown and keeps the sides close to the head. Step 3: Consider the length of your hair and opt for shorter styles that elongate the neck and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Step 4: Experiment with different textures, such as curls or waves, to add dimension and movement to the hair.

Tips

- Avoid styles that add width to the sides of the face - Opt for styles that create height at the crown - Experiment with different textures, such as curls or waves - Consider the length of your hair

FAQ

What kind of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs or full, voluminous bangs that add width to the sides of the face. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across the face.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add layers and volume at the crown to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for round faces, the goal is to keep the hair healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims are important to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut. Additionally, consider using volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add height and volume to the crown. Finally, be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

In conclusion, finding the right haircut for a round face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to a flattering and stylish look. Remember to experiment with different styles and textures, and most importantly, have fun with your hair!
