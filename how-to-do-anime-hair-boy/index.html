How To Do Anime Hair Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you want to achieve the perfect anime hair boy look? Look no further! In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this popular hairstyle. From choosing the right style to taking care of your hair, we'll cover everything you need to know.

Description

Anime hair boy is a popular hairstyle among anime and manga fans. It's characterized by its spiky and wild appearance, often in bright and unusual colors. This hairstyle is inspired by the exaggerated and bold hairstyles of anime characters, making it a favorite among cosplayers and anime enthusiasts.

How to

Before starting, you'll need to gather some essential tools and products. You'll need a comb, hair gel or wax, hair spray, and hair dye if you want to add color to your hair. Once you have everything ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your style

There are various anime hair boy styles to choose from, so you should pick one that suits your face shape and personal preferences. Some popular styles include spiky, messy, slicked-back, and layered. You can also add bangs or highlights to make your hair stand out.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free from any dirt or oil. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Apply hair gel or wax

Using a comb, apply hair gel or wax to your hair. This will help hold your hair in place and make it easier to style. Apply a small amount of product, and don't use too much, or your hair will look greasy.

Step 4: Style your hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair according to your chosen style. For a spiky look, spike your hair upwards or outwards. For a messy look, use your fingers to create a tousled appearance. For a slicked-back look, comb your hair back and use hair spray to hold it in place.

Step 5: Add color (optional)

If you want to add color to your hair, you can use hair dye or temporary color spray. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair type. Apply the color evenly and follow the instructions on the product.

Step 6: Apply hair spray

To keep your hair in place, apply hair spray evenly. This will help hold your style in place and prevent frizz or flyaways.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you.
  • Use high-quality hair products to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take good care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, or your hair will look greasy.
  • Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add volume or create curls.

FAQ

What hair products should I use?

You should use hair gel or wax to help hold your hair in place. Use hair spray to keep it in place and prevent frizz. Choose high-quality products to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I add color to my hair?

Yes, you can add color to your hair using hair dye or temporary color spray. Make sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair type.

How do I take care of my hair?

You should wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it healthy. Avoid using too much heat or product, as this can damage your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, you should take good care of it. This includes washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat or product use.

Additionally, you should avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair. If you're unsure about what products to use, consult a hairstylist or dermatologist for advice.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the perfect anime hair boy look and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
