How To Apply Gel To Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long hair, you might find that it can be challenging to style it the way you want. One of the easiest ways to tame your long locks is by using gel. Gel can help keep your hair in place, reduce frizz, and add shine. However, applying gel to long hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll show you how to apply gel to long hair in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Apply Gel to Long Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Applying Gel to Long Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Apply Gel to Long Hair

Before applying gel to your hair, it's essential to choose the right type of gel. There are many different types of gel available, including firm hold, medium hold, and light hold. If you have long hair, you'll want to choose a gel with a firm hold so that it can keep your hair in place all day.

Once you've chosen the right gel, you're ready to apply it to your hair. Here's how:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before applying gel to your hair, you should wash and condition your hair. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may be on your hair and make it easier to style. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing and conditioning your hair, gently towel dry it. Be careful not to rub your hair too hard with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, pat your hair dry with the towel.

Step 3: Apply Gel to Your Hair

Next, take a small amount of gel and rub it between your palms. Then, apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've applied the gel, you can style your hair as desired. You can use a comb or your fingers to create the look you want. If you want a sleek, polished look, you can use a comb to smooth your hair back. If you want a more tousled, natural look, you can use your fingers to scrunch your hair.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Once you've styled your hair, let it dry naturally. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, allow your hair to air dry. This may take some time, so be patient.

Tips for Applying Gel to Long Hair

Here are a few tips to help you apply gel to long hair:

  • Start with a small amount of gel and add more if necessary.
  • Avoid applying gel to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Be gentle when applying gel to your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying gel.

FAQs

Q: Can I use gel on wet or dry hair?

A: You can use gel on either wet or dry hair. However, it's best to use gel on damp hair as it will help distribute the gel more evenly.

Q: How much gel should I use?

A: Start with a small amount of gel and add more if necessary. You don't want to use too much gel as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are a few tips to help you take care of your long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into a ponytail or bun as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and using gel to style your hair, you can have beautiful, healthy-looking long hair that's easy to manage.
