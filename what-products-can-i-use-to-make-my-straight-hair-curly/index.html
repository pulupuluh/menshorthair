What Products Can I Use To Make My Straight Hair Curly

Written by Aprilia May 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you have naturally straight hair but want to switch things up with some curls? There are plenty of products on the market that can help you achieve luscious, bouncy curls without the need for a perm or hot rollers. In this article, we'll explore some of the best products to make your straight hair curly.

Table of Contents

  • Curling Mousses
  • Curling Sprays
  • Curling Creams
  • Curling Gels
  • Curling Irons
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Curling Mousses

Curling mousses are lightweight products that add volume and hold to your hair. They work by coating your strands with a layer of polymer that helps to define curls and prevent frizz. Simply apply a golf ball-sized amount to damp hair and scrunch your hair to enhance your curls. Our top picks for curling mousses are:

  • OGX Locking + Coconut Curls Air Dry Cream
  • TRESemmé Flawless Curls Mousse
  • Cantu Coconut Curling Cream

Curling Sprays

Curling sprays are a quick and easy way to add texture and volume to your hair. They work by providing a light hold that can help to enhance your natural curls and waves. Simply mist evenly over your hair, scrunch, and let dry. Our top picks for curling sprays are:

  • Herbal Essences Totally Twisted Curl-Boosting Hair Spray
  • John Frieda Frizz Ease Dream Curls Daily Styling Spray
  • Aussie Sprunch Mousse + Leave-In Conditioner

Curling Creams

Curling creams are a great option if you're looking for a product that can provide both hold and hydration. They work by moisturizing your hair and defining your curls at the same time. Simply apply a small amount to damp hair and scrunch your hair to enhance your curls. Our top picks for curling creams are:

  • Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Coconut Curling Cream
  • Mizani True Textures Curl Enhancing Lotion
  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

Curling Gels

Curling gels are a great option if you're looking for a product that can provide a strong hold. They work by coating your strands with a layer of polymer that helps to define curls and prevent frizz. Simply apply a small amount to damp hair and scrunch your hair to enhance your curls. Our top picks for curling gels are:

  • DevaCurl Light Defining Gel
  • Kinky-Curly Original Curling Custard
  • Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Moisturizing Curl Activator Cream

Curling Irons

If you're looking for a more dramatic curl, a curling iron might be the way to go. Curling irons come in a variety of sizes, so you can choose the one that works best for your hair length and desired curl size. Before using a curling iron, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage. Our top picks for curling irons are:

  • Conair Double Ceramic Curling Iron
  • BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Spring Curling Iron
  • T3 SinglePass Curl 1.25-Inch Professional Curling Iron

Hair Care Tips

When using products to make your hair curly, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent damage and keep your curls looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

FAQ

Can I make my straight hair curly without heat?

Yes! You can use a variety of products like curling mousses, sprays, creams, and gels to enhance your natural curls without heat.

What's the difference between a curling mousse and a curling cream?

Curling mousses are lightweight products that add volume and hold, while curling creams are thicker products that provide both hold and hydration.

How often can I use a curling iron on my hair?

It's best to limit your use of hot tools like curling irons to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know what products can make your straight hair curly, it's time to experiment and find the ones that work best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep your curls looking their best!
