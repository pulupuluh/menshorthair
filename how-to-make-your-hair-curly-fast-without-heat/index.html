How To Make Your Hair Curly Fast Without Heat

Written by Aprilia Apr 12, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to achieve curly hair? Not only can they damage your hair, but they can also take a lot of time and effort. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair curly fast without heat. In this article, we’ll share tips and tricks to help you achieve beautiful curls without using any heat.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Your Hair Curly Fast Without Heat
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Achieving Curly Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Curly Fast Without Heat

There are many ways to make your hair curly without using heat. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Twist and pin

Twist small sections of your hair and pin them to your scalp. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight for best results.

2. Braid

Braid your hair into several sections and leave them in for a few hours or overnight.

3. Rollers

Use rollers to curl your hair. You can either use traditional rollers or velcro rollers. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight.

4. Flexi rods

Flexi rods are a great alternative to traditional rollers. They’re made of foam and can be twisted around your hair to create curls. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight.

5. Curling cream

Curling cream is a great product to use if you want to make your hair curly without using heat. Apply it to damp hair and scrunch your hair to create curls.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair curly fast without heat:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to define your curls and make them more manageable.

Step 2: Apply curling cream

Apply curling cream to damp hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Twist or braid your hair

Twist or braid small sections of your hair, depending on the method you choose. Secure them with a hair tie or bobby pins.

Step 4: Leave them in

Leave the twists or braids in for a few hours or overnight for best results.

Step 5: Take them out

Remove the twists or braids and gently shake your hair to loosen the curls.

Tips for Achieving Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve beautiful curls:

1. Use the right products

Use curl-enhancing products such as shampoo, conditioner, and curling cream to help define your curls.

2. Don’t brush your hair

Brushing your hair can cause it to become frizzy and lose its shape. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate your curls.

3. Use a diffuser

If you’re using a hair dryer, use a diffuser to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

4. Avoid touching your hair

Touching your hair can cause it to become frizzy and lose its shape. Try to avoid touching it as much as possible.

FAQ

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The length of time your curls will last depends on your hair type and the method you used to create them. Generally, they will last anywhere from a day to a week.

Q: Can I use these methods on all hair types?

A: Yes, these methods can be used on all hair types. However, some methods may work better on certain hair types than others.

Q: Will these methods damage my hair?

A: No, these methods will not damage your hair. In fact, they’re a great alternative to using heat styling tools, which can cause damage over time.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

4. Protect your hair at night

Protect your curls at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair curly fast without using any heat. Not only will this save you time and effort, but it will also help to keep your hair healthy and damage-free. Try out these methods and see which one works best for you!
