How Do I Style My Short Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi May 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's low-maintenance and fun to play around with. On the other hand, it can be tricky to style and maintain its structure. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to style your short curly hair to perfection.

Description

Before we dive into the styling process, let's talk about what short curly hair actually is. Short curly hair refers to hair that is cut above the shoulders and has a natural curl pattern. This hair type can range from loose waves to tight coils and requires a specific set of products and techniques to style and maintain.

How To Style Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short curly hair:

Step 1: Start With Clean Hair

The first step to styling your short curly hair is to start with clean hair. This means washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause your curls to become dry and brittle.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Once your hair is clean, apply a styling product that's designed for curly hair. This can be a gel, mousse, or cream. The product you choose will depend on your hair type and the look you're going for. Apply the product to your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front.

Step 3: Define Your Curls

After applying your styling product, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to define your curls. This will help enhance your natural curl pattern and give your hair more structure. If you have tighter curls, you can also use a curl defining cream or gel to help define your curls even more.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Once you've defined your curls, it's time to dry your hair. To avoid causing frizz, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help distribute the heat evenly and give your curls more volume. Start by drying the roots of your hair, then work your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish With A Serum

Once your hair is dry, finish off your look with a serum or oil that's designed for curly hair. This will help add shine and reduce frizz. Apply a small amount of product to your palms, then run your hands through your hair to distribute it evenly.

Tips For Styling Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can make it work. Here are some tips for styling short curly hair:

Tip 1: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause your curls to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.

Tip 2: Use A Microfiber Towel

Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Tip 3: Experiment With Different Products

Not all curly hair products are created equal. Experiment with different gels, mousses, and creams to find the products that work best for your hair type and texture.

Tip 4: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair if you want to switch up your look. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my short curly hair?

A: Frizz can be a common problem for curly hair. To prevent frizz, avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, and finish your look with a serum or oil.

Hair Care For Short Curly Hair

As with any hair type, it's important to take care of your short curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short curly hair:

Tip 1: Use A Deep Conditioner

Curly hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Sleep On A Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage, as it's gentler on your hair than a regular cotton pillowcase.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair From The Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF.


Styling short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, defined look. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture. And don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments. With a little bit of effort, you can have the perfect short curly hair look.
