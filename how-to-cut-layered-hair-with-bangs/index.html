How To Cut Layered Hair With Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 · 8 min read

Layered hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle that can give you a chic and trendy look. However, getting this style at a salon can be expensive. So why not learn how to cut layered hair with bangs at home? In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step. Plus, we’ll provide some tips for maintaining your new haircut and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Layered Hair with Bangs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Layered Hair with Bangs
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair with Bangs

How to Cut Layered Hair with Bangs

The first step in cutting layered hair with bangs is to decide on the length and style of your bangs. You can choose between side-swept bangs, blunt bangs, or choppy bangs. Once you have decided on the style, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair if it is dry.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into three sections: the top, middle, and bottom. Clip the top section out of the way and focus on the bottom section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process for the entire bottom layer of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the top section of your hair and focus on the middle layer. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle. This will create a layered effect. Repeat this process for the entire middle layer of your hair.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, release the top layer of your hair and cut it at a slight angle. This will blend the layers together and create a seamless look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide that you can follow:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Decide on the length and style of your bangs.
  3. Divide your hair into three sections: top, middle, and bottom.
  4. Clip the top section out of the way and focus on the bottom layer.
  5. Take a small section of hair and cut it straight across.
  6. Release the top section of your hair and focus on the middle layer.
  7. Take a small section of hair and cut it at a slight angle.
  8. Finally, release the top layer of your hair and cut it at a slight angle.
  9. Trim any stray hairs and make any final adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Layered Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layered haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. Cut small sections at a time for a more precise cut.
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t add it back.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure your bangs are even.
  • If you’re not confident in your cutting skills, start with small trims and work your way up.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always visit a salon to have your hair fixed or wait for it to grow out.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

That’s it! You now know how to cut layered hair with bangs. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a salon-quality haircut at home. Just remember to follow the steps carefully and take your time. Good luck!
