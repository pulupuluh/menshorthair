Men s Medium Hair Short Sides How To Style And Maintain

Written by Frank May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile haircut that can work for both casual and formal occasions, then the men's medium hair with short sides is the perfect choice. This hairstyle has become a popular trend in recent years, and for good reason. It is easy to maintain, looks great, and can be customized to suit your individual style. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about men's medium hair short sides, including how to style it, maintain it, and more.

Description

The men's medium hair short sides hairstyle is characterized by a medium-length top that is styled in various ways, such as slicked-back, messy, or textured. The sides and back are usually cut short, either with clippers or scissors, and can be styled with a fade or undercut for a sharper look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair length moderately long while keeping the sides and back short for a cleaner and more polished appearance.

How to Style

Styling men's medium hair with short sides is relatively easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style this haircut:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup, leaving your hair clean and ready for styling.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply hair product

Next, apply a hair product of your choice, such as a pomade, wax, or gel, to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair to ensure a uniform hold.

Step 4: Style your hair

Using a comb or brush, style your hair to your desired look. You can slick it back, create a side part, or add texture for a more relaxed style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's medium hair short sides hairstyle:

  • Choose a hair product that suits your hair type and desired hold. For example, if you have thick hair, you may need a stronger hold product than someone with thin hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that suits you best.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's medium hair short sides:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for most face shapes, including oval, square, and round.

How often should I get my haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

What hair products should I use?

The hair product you use will depend on your hair type and desired hold. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a great-looking hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips for men's medium hair with short sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create and maintain the perfect men's medium hair with short sides hairstyle. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this haircut is versatile enough to suit any occasion.
