How Do You Change Your Hair Color In Animal Crossing New Horizons

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're an Animal Crossing fan, you know that customizing your character's appearance is a big part of the game. One thing you might be wondering about is how to change your hair color. In this post, we'll go over how to do just that in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

How to Change Your Hair Color

Before we get into the steps, it's important to note that you'll need to have unlocked access to the Able Sisters shop on your island. Once you've done that, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to the Able Sisters Shop

Head over to the Able Sisters shop on your island. If you haven't unlocked it yet, you'll need to do so by spending a certain amount of money at the Nook Stop and waiting a few days for it to be built.

Step 2: Sit Down in the Chair

When you're inside the Able Sisters shop, look for the chair in the corner. Sit down in it to access the customization menu.

Step 3: Choose "Change My Look"

Once you're in the customization menu, select "Change My Look" to bring up more options.

Step 4: Choose "Hair Color"

In the "Change My Look" menu, select "Hair Color" to see a list of all the available hair colors.

Step 5: Choose Your New Hair Color

Scroll through the list of hair colors and select the one you want. You'll see a preview of what your character will look like with the new hair color.

Step 6: Confirm Your Choice

Once you've chosen your new hair color, confirm your choice and the Able Sisters will make the change for you.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Color in Animal Crossing New Horizons

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair color:

  • Some hair colors are only available during certain seasons.
  • You can change your hair color as often as you'd like, but it will cost you 3,000 Bells each time.
  • Changing your hair color will not affect your character's name or any other aspects of their appearance.
  • If you change your mind about your new hair color, you can always go back to the Able Sisters and change it again.

FAQs About Changing Your Hair Color

Here are a few common questions people have about changing their hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

Can you change your hair style?

Yes, you can change your hair style as well as your hair color. Just select "Hair Style" instead of "Hair Color" in the "Change My Look" menu.

Can you change your character's eye color?

No, you cannot change your character's eye color in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

Can you change your character's skin color?

Yes, you can change your character's skin color in the "Change My Look" menu.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing New Horizons

Now that you know how to change your hair color, you might be wondering about hair care in general. Unfortunately, there's not much you can do to take care of your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons. You can't shampoo or condition it, and there's no way to trim split ends. But hey, at least you can change the color whenever you want!

Conclusion

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a fun way to customize your character's appearance. By following the steps outlined in this post, you'll be able to do it in no time. Just remember to visit the Able Sisters and have 3,000 Bells on hand!
