Best Haircuts For Round Faces Boy A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you know that finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. Your face shape can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your overall look. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for round faces boy. Whether you want a short or long hairstyle, we've got something for everyone. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Having a round face means that your cheekbones and jawline have a similar width, and your face length is shorter than your face width. The best haircuts for round faces boy are those that create the illusion of a longer face shape. This means that you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for haircuts that add length to your face and create more definition.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle that will be easier to manage. If you have thin hair, a longer hairstyle may be a better option to create the illusion of more volume.

Second, think about the length of the haircut. The best haircuts for round faces boy are those that are longer on top and shorter on the sides. This will create the illusion of a longer face shape and draw attention away from the width of your face.

Finally, consider the style of the haircut. A messy, textured look can add more definition to your face and create the illusion of more length. A side-swept fringe can also be a great option, as it will draw attention away from the width of your face and create a more angular look.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces boy:

1. The Classic Crew Cut

This timeless style is perfect for boys with round faces. It features short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. This style works well for all hair types and is easy to maintain.

2. The Side Part

The side part is a classic look that never goes out of style. This style features a deep part on one side of the head, with hair combed over to one side. This style works well for boys with round faces, as it creates the illusion of more length.

3. The Quiff

The quiff is a stylish and versatile haircut that can work well for boys with round faces. This style features longer hair on top that is styled upwards and backwards. This style works well for boys with thick hair, as it adds volume and texture.

Tips

When getting a haircut for a round face, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Opt for haircuts that add length to your face and create more definition.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a haircut that works well with it.
  • Choose a style that is easy to maintain and suits your lifestyle.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face boy?

The best haircut for a round face boy is one that creates the illusion of a longer face shape. This means avoiding hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face and opting for haircuts that add length and definition.

What hairstyles should round-faced boys avoid?

Round-faced boys should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as the mushroom cut or the bowl cut. These styles will only make the face appear rounder.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and the best haircuts for round faces boy, you'll be sure to look your best no matter what the occasion.
