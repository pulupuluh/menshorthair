How To Train Your Hair Backwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, training your hair to lay backwards can be a great option. This style can create a sleek and polished look, perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps on how to train your hair backwards, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice along the way. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Train Your Hair Backwards
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Train Your Hair Backwards

Training your hair to lay backwards is all about creating a new habit for your hair. You'll need to train your hair to lay in a new direction until it becomes natural. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair until it is damp. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to your hair. Use a small amount and work it evenly through your hair.

Step 4: Comb your hair backwards

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair backwards from the front of your head. Start at the hairline and work your way back. Repeat until your hair is laying in the desired direction.

Step 5: Train your hair

Repeat steps 3 and 4 every time you wash your hair. Over time, your hair will begin to lay backwards naturally.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide to training your hair backwards:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair until it is damp. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to your hair. Work it evenly through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 4: Comb your hair backwards

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair backwards from the front of your head. Start at the hairline and work your way back. Make sure to comb your hair evenly and avoid creating any partings.

Step 5: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a medium heat setting. Use your fingers to comb your hair backwards as you dry it. This will help to set the style and create volume.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat steps 3-5 every time you wash your hair. Over time, your hair will begin to lay backwards naturally.

Tips for Training Your Hair Backwards

Here are some tips to keep in mind when training your hair to lay backwards:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it harder to train.
  • Be patient! It can take several weeks for your hair to start laying backwards naturally.

FAQs

How long will it take for my hair to start laying backwards naturally?

It can take several weeks for your hair to start laying backwards naturally. Be patient and stick to your training routine.

Can I still part my hair when training it backwards?

Avoid creating any partings when training your hair backwards. This will help to create a smooth and polished look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to train your hair backwards, you can switch up your look and create a sleek and polished style. Remember to be patient and stick to your training routine, and your hair will start laying backwards naturally over time.
