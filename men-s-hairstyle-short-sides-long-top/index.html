Men s Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that's easy to maintain, then the men's hairstyle short sides long top might be the perfect choice for you. This versatile haircut has been around for a while and has become increasingly popular in recent years. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to style it, tips, and hair care.

Description

The men's hairstyle short sides long top is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. The length of the hair on the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's generally longer than the hair on the sides. This haircut can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men who want a versatile and low-maintenance haircut.

How to Style Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

The men's hairstyle short sides long top can be styled in many different ways, depending on personal preference and the occasion. Here are some of the most popular styles:

  • The Classic Pompadour: This style is achieved by slicking back the hair on the top and creating a high-volume pomp with a side part. This is a classic and timeless look that works well for formal occasions.
  • The Messy Top: This style is achieved by leaving the hair on the top messy and tousled while keeping the sides short. This is a more casual and relaxed look that works well for everyday wear.
  • The Side Part: This style is achieved by creating a side part and slicking back the hair on the top. This is a versatile look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.
  • The Textured Top: This style is achieved by adding texture to the hair on the top using a styling product like pomade or wax. This is a modern and edgy look that works well for younger men.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style the men's hairstyle short sides long top:

  1. Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to the hair on the top.
  3. Style your hair using your fingers or a comb, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  4. Use a hair dryer to set the style and add volume to the hair on the top.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hairstyle short sides long top:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides short and the top looking neat.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hairstyle short sides long top:

Is the men's hairstyle short sides long top suitable for all hair types?
Yes, this haircut can work well for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.
How often should I get a trim for this haircut?
You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides short and the top looking neat.
Can I style the men's hairstyle short sides long top in different ways?
Yes, this haircut can be styled in many different ways, depending on personal preference and the occasion.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

Here are some hair care tips for men's hairstyle short sides long top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look frizzy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily maintain and style the men's hairstyle short sides long top to achieve a trendy and stylish look that works for you. So go ahead and try out this versatile haircut â you won't be disappointed!
