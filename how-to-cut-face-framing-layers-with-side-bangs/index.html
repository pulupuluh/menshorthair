How To Cut Face Framing Layers With Side Bangs

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh and youthful look? Consider cutting face framing layers with side bangs! This hairstyle is versatile, suits all face shapes, and is easy to maintain. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting face framing layers with side bangs.

Description

Face framing layers with side bangs is a haircut that involves cutting layers around the face to create a frame. The layers can be short or long, depending on your preference. The side bangs are cut diagonally across the forehead, creating a flattering and stylish look. This haircut is perfect for people who want to add dimension and texture to their hair while keeping it manageable.

How to Cut Face Framing Layers with Side Bangs

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to prepare it. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly, and comb it to remove any tangles. Make sure you have all the necessary tools, including scissors, clips, and a comb.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Clip the top part of your hair away and focus on the bottom three sections.

Step 3: Cut the Layers

Take a small section of hair from the left side and cut it at a slight angle towards the face. Repeat this on the right side. Continue cutting small sections of hair around the face until you have created the desired number of layers. Make sure the layers are blended well.

Step 4: Cut the Side Bangs

Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. Cut the hair at a diagonal angle towards the opposite ear. Repeat this on the other side. Make sure the bangs are even and complement your face shape.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use your scissors to blend the layers together. Make sure there are no harsh lines and the layers look natural.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can blow-dry it straight, curl it, or leave it natural. Apply some hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more later.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair tends to be longer, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't rush it.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's recommended to seek professional help if you're not confident in your skills.

What face shape suits face framing layers with side bangs?

Face framing layers with side bangs suit all face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
