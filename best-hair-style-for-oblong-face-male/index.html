Best Hair Style For Oblong Face Male How To Choose The Perfect Cut

Written by Eveline Jun 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a male with an oblong face shape? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your facial features? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best hair style for oblong face male and how to choose the perfect cut.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for oblong face male is to find a cut that adds width to the face and balances out the length.

How to Choose the Perfect Cut

When choosing the perfect cut, keep in mind that the goal is to add width to the face. This can be achieved by adding volume and layers to the hair. Avoid haircuts that are too short or flat as they can make the face appear even longer.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Research hairstyles that suit an oblong face shape

Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist

Step 4: Try out different styles before committing to a cut

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid hairstyles that are too short or flat

Tip 2: Consider adding layers and volume to the hair

Tip 3: Experiment with different styles before committing to a cut

Tip 4: Keep facial hair well-groomed to balance out the face

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face male?

The best hairstyle for an oblong face male is one that adds width to the face. This can be achieved by adding layers and volume to the hair.

Can an oblong face male have short hair?

An oblong face male can have short hair, but it is important to choose a style that adds width to the face. Avoid haircuts that are too short or flat as they can make the face appear even longer.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on the hair as this can damage it and cause split ends.

In conclusion, finding the best hair style for oblong face male is all about adding width to the face. Consider adding layers and volume to the hair to balance out the length of the face. Consult with your hairstylist and try out different styles before committing to a cut. With the right care and maintenance, your hair will look great and complement your facial features perfectly.
