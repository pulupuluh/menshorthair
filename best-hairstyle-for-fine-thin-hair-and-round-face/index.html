The Best Hairstyles For Fine Thin Hair And Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have fine thin hair and a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes your hair look fuller. But fear not, as we have compiled a complete guide to help you find the best hairstyle for your hair type and face shape. In this article, we will cover everything from how to choose the right hairstyle, step by step instructions for styling, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Instructions for Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Fine Thin Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for fine thin hair and a round face, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to choose a style that adds volume and movement to your hair. Layers are a great option as they create the illusion of thickness and add texture to your hair. Secondly, you want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt cuts or bobs that end at your chin. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles that elongate your face. Lastly, consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair. Choose a style that is low maintenance if you don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair.

Best Hairstyles for Fine Thin Hair and Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best options for fine thin hair and a round face:

  • Long Layers: Long layers add movement and volume to your hair, making it appear thicker. They also elongate your face, making it look slimmer.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs add dimension to your hair and cover your forehead, creating the illusion of a longer face.
  • Wavy Lob: A wavy lob (long bob) is a low maintenance option that adds texture and volume to your hair. It also frames your face, making it look slimmer.
  • Asymmetrical Bob: An asymmetrical bob is a great option if you want a shorter hairstyle. It adds texture and movement to your hair and elongates your face.

Step by Step Instructions for Styling

Now that you have chosen your hairstyle, here are step by step instructions for styling:

  • For long layers, wash and condition your hair with volumizing products. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume. Finish with a texturizing spray.
  • For side-swept bangs, wash and condition your hair with smoothing products. Blow dry your hair with a paddle brush, directing your bangs to the side. Finish with a smoothing serum.
  • For a wavy lob, wash and condition your hair with texturizing products. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat. Use a curling iron to add loose waves. Finish with a sea salt spray.
  • For an asymmetrical bob, wash and condition your hair with volumizing products. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, directing your hair away from your face. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use the right products for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still have short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have short hair if you have a round face. Opt for a shorter hairstyle with layers and volume to add dimension and elongate your face.

What products should I use on my fine thin hair?

Use volumizing products, such as mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo. Avoid heavy products that weigh down your hair.

Can I still have bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Thin Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for fine thin hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove build-up.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for fine thin hair and a round face may seem daunting, but with the right information and tools, it can be easy. Choose a style that adds volume and movement to your hair, elongates your face, and is low maintenance. Follow our step by step instructions for styling and use the right products for your hair type. With these tips and tricks, you'll have a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes your hair look fuller and more voluminous.
