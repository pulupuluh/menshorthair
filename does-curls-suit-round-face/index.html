Does Curls Suit Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering whether curls suit round face or not? If you have a round face shape and want to try out curly hair, you might be hesitant because you're not sure if it will look good on you. In this guide, we'll answer your questions and give you tips on how to rock curly hair with a round face shape. Read on to find out!

Description

A round face shape is characterized by having a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. It's a beautiful face shape, but it can be a bit tricky to find the right hairstyle that complements it. When it comes to curls, you might be wondering if they'll make your face look even rounder or if they'll enhance your features. The truth is, it all depends on the type of curls and how you style them.

How To

If you're new to curly hair or want to switch up your current style, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Curl Type

There are different types of curls, from loose waves to tight coils. Knowing your curl type will help you choose the right products and styling techniques. You can determine your curl type by looking at a curl chart or consulting a hairstylist.

Step 2: Choose The Right Cut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in how your curls look on a round face. A layered cut with longer pieces framing the face can help elongate the face and add dimension. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face look wider.

Step 3: Use The Right Products

Curly hair needs moisture and definition, so make sure to use products that cater to those needs. Look for leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and gels that will enhance your curls without weighing them down. Avoid products that contain sulfates and silicones, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Curls

There are different ways to style curly hair, from air-drying to diffusing. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for your curl type. You can also add accessories like headbands and scarves to elevate your look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for looser curls or waves instead of tight coils, as they can make the face look wider.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept bangs instead.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair and round face shape:

Q: Will tight curls make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, tight curls can make the face look wider, so opt for looser curls or waves instead.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or bun. Just make sure to add some volume to the crown of your head to elongate the face.

Q: Do I need to use a diffuser to dry my hair?

A: It depends on your curl type and preference. Some people prefer air-drying, while others find that diffusing gives them more definition and volume.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz.

In conclusion, curls can definitely suit round face shape if you choose the right cut, products, and styling techniques. Experiment with different looks and have fun with your curly hair!
