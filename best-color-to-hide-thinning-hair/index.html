Best Color To Hide Thinning Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning hair can be a frustrating issue to deal with, but thankfully, there are ways to conceal it. One of the most effective ways is to choose the right hair color. In this article, we will discuss the best color to hide thinning hair and provide tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Color to Hide Thinning Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Thinning Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Colored Thinning Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

How to Choose the Best Color to Hide Thinning Hair

The key to finding the best color to hide thinning hair is to choose a shade that closely matches your natural hair color. This will help to create a seamless look and make your hair appear thicker. Additionally, darker colors tend to make hair look fuller, so you may want to consider going a shade or two darker than your natural color.

Another option is to go for highlights or lowlights. This technique involves adding lighter or darker strands of color to your hair to create depth and dimension. This can help to distract from thinning areas and make your hair appear fuller.

Ultimately, the best color to hide thinning hair will depend on your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal preferences. It may be helpful to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best color for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Thinning Hair

Before coloring your hair, it is important to prepare it properly. This will help to ensure that the color is even and long-lasting. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and comb through any tangles.
  3. Apply a protective barrier cream around your hairline and ears to prevent staining.
  4. Mix your chosen hair color according to the instructions on the box.
  5. Section your hair and apply the color from roots to tips, making sure to saturate each strand.
  6. Leave the color on for the recommended amount of time.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  8. Apply the included conditioner and leave it on for several minutes before rinsing.

It is important to note that coloring your hair can be damaging, especially if you have thinning hair. Be sure to use a color treatment that is specifically designed for thinning hair and follow up with a nourishing hair mask to help repair and strengthen your strands.

Tips for Maintaining Colored Thinning Hair

Once you have colored your hair, it is important to take steps to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the vibrancy of your color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, which can further damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use hot tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQs

Q. Will coloring my hair make it look thicker?

A. Yes, darker colors tend to make hair look fuller, so coloring your hair can help to conceal thinning areas.

Q. Can I color my hair at home?

A. Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it is important to follow the instructions carefully and use a color treatment that is specifically designed for thinning hair.

Q. How often should I color my hair?

A. It is generally recommended to wait at least six to eight weeks between color treatments to allow your hair time to recover.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

In addition to coloring your hair, there are other steps you can take to care for thinning hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products like mousses or gels, which can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using hair growth supplements or treatments to help stimulate hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the best color to hide thinning hair, you can achieve a fuller, more voluminous look that will boost your confidence and help you feel great about your appearance.
