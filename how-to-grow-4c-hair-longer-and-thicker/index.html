How To Grow 4C Hair Longer And Thicker

Written by Luffy Jul 19, 2023 · 8 min read

A lot of people with 4C hair struggle with growing their hair longer and thicker. It can be frustrating, especially when you’ve tried everything and nothing seems to work. However, there are a few things that you can do to help your hair grow longer and thicker. In this article, we’ll explore some tips and tricks to grow 4C hair longer and thicker.

How to Grow 4C Hair Longer and Thicker

Before we get started, it’s important to understand what type of hair 4C is. 4C hair is the most fragile hair type and requires a lot of care to prevent breakage. Growing 4C hair longer and thicker requires patience, consistency, and dedication. Here are a few things you can do to help your hair grow longer and thicker:

1. Keep your hair moisturized

4C hair tends to be very dry and brittle, which can lead to breakage. To prevent breakage, it’s important to keep your hair moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner, oil, or butter to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Protect your hair at night

Wrapping your hair at night can help protect it from breakage. Use a satin or silk scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

4C hair is very fragile and can easily break. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

4. Avoid heat

Heat can cause damage to your hair and lead to breakage. Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low.

5. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help promote hair growth. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits and vegetables.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to grow 4C hair longer and thicker:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can be harsh on your hair and strip it of its natural oils.

Step 2: Deep condition

After washing your hair, apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour.

Step 3: Moisturize

Apply a leave-in conditioner, oil, or butter to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Step 4: Detangle

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Protect

Protect your hair at night by wrapping it with a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.

Step 6: Avoid heat

Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low.

Tips for Growing 4C Hair Longer and Thicker

Here are a few additional tips to help grow 4C hair longer and thicker:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage.

2. Drink water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

3. Use a protein treatment

Protein treatments can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

FAQs

How often should I wash my 4C hair?

It’s recommended that you wash your hair once a week.

How often should I deep condition my 4C hair?

Deep conditioning once every two weeks is recommended.

Do I need to use a heat protectant if I’m not using high heat?

Yes, it’s always a good idea to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for 4C Hair

Here are a few additional tips for caring for 4C hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

2. Keep your hair moisturized

Use a leave-in conditioner, oil, or butter to keep your hair moisturized.

3. Protect your hair at night

Use a satin or silk scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.

4. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

In conclusion, growing 4C hair longer and thicker requires patience, consistency, and dedication. By following these tips and tricks, you can help promote hair growth and prevent breakage. Remember to keep your hair moisturized, protect it at night, use a wide-tooth comb, avoid heat, and eat a healthy diet.
