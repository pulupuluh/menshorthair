Does Blonde Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Blonde hair is a popular hair color choice for many women. However, some people are hesitant to dye their hair blonde because they fear it will make their face look fatter. In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with some tips on how to look your best with blonde hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that blonde hair can make your face look fatter. However, this is not necessarily true. The way your hair color affects your face shape depends on your skin tone, hair length, and the type of hairstyle you choose.

How to

If you are considering going blonde but are worried about your face shape, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your hair color flatters your features. Here are some tips:

Choose the Right Shade

The key to finding the right blonde shade for your face shape is to choose a color that complements your skin tone. If you have warm-toned skin, you should opt for a warmer, honey blonde shade. If you have cooler-toned skin, a cool, ashy blonde will look best on you.

Consider Your Hair Length

The length of your hair can also affect how your face shape appears. If you have a round face, you should consider a longer hairstyle that can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shorter bob or pixie cut can soften your features.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

The type of hairstyle you choose can also affect how your face shape looks. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the sides, as this can make your face look wider. Instead, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to elongate your face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some simple steps you can follow to ensure that your blonde hair looks flattering on your face shape:

  1. Choose the right blonde shade for your skin tone
  2. Consider your hair length
  3. Choose the right hairstyle
  4. Add volume to the top of your head to elongate your face shape
  5. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the sides if you have a round face

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when going blonde:

  • Consult with a professional hair stylist to determine the best shade and cut for your face shape
  • Use hair products that are designed for color-treated hair to maintain the vibrancy of your blonde color
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Wear makeup that complements your blonde hair color to enhance your features

FAQ

Q: Does blonde hair make your face look fatter?

A: Blonde hair does not necessarily make your face look fatter. The way your hair color affects your face shape depends on your skin tone, hair length, and the type of hairstyle you choose.

Q: What is the best blonde shade for a round face?

A: If you have a round face, a warmer, honey blonde shade will look best on you. Avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the sides, as this can make your face look wider.

Hair Care

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your blonde locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a UV-protectant spray to prevent your hair from becoming brassy or yellow in the sun
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

In conclusion, whether or not blonde hair makes your face look fatter depends on a variety of factors. By choosing the right shade, length, and hairstyle, you can ensure that your blonde hair flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
