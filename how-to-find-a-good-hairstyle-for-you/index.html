How To Find A Good Hairstyle For You Tips And Tricks In 2023

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and ready for a change? Finding a new hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered with these tips and tricks on how to find a good hairstyle for you. Let's dive in!

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a good hairstyle is determining your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which hairstyles will be most flattering for you. The main face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in a mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, your jawline, and your forehead. Once you have identified these features, match them to the corresponding face shape.

For example, if your face is widest at the cheekbones and tapers to a narrow chin, you likely have a heart-shaped face. If your face is as wide as it is long, you have a round face. Knowing your face shape will help you find a hairstyle that will balance and flatter your features.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Another important factor in finding a good hairstyle is understanding your hair type. Your hair type will affect how your hair behaves and what hairstyles will work best for you. The main hair types are straight, wavy, curly, and coily.

Take note of how your hair reacts to different products and styling techniques. If you have straight hair, you may find that it is easy to manage but lacks volume. If you have curly hair, you may find that it is prone to frizz and needs extra moisture.

Understanding your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your hair's natural texture and behavior.

Considering Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be easily maintained. If you work in a professional environment, you may want to choose a more conservative hairstyle.

Think about your daily routine and what kind of hairstyle will fit into it. If you're an athlete or enjoy outdoor activities, you may want a hairstyle that can withstand sweat and wind.

Hairstyle Ideas Based on Your Features

Once you have determined your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, it's time to start looking for hairstyle ideas. Here are some ideas based on different features:

Oval Face Shape

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off most hairstyles. Consider a bob, long layers, or a pixie cut.

Round Face Shape

Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and length. Consider a long bob, layered cut, or a side-swept bang.

Square Face Shape

Square faces benefit from softening hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. Consider a layered cut, a side-swept bang, or loose waves.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that balance the face shape. Consider a chin-length bob, side-swept bangs, or long layers.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond faces look best with hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin. Consider a layered cut, a side-swept bang, or loose waves.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Style

Once you have found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often

- Use a heat protectant when using hot tools

- Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle

- Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how fast your hair grows. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back and there are always options for styling and modifying your hair. Talk to your stylist about your concerns and they can help you find a solution.

Can I dye my hair after getting a new hairstyle?

Yes, you can dye your hair after getting a new hairstyle. However, it's recommended to wait a few weeks after getting a haircut before coloring your hair to allow your hair to recover.

Finding a good hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and trying out different styles, you can find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Happy hairstyling!
