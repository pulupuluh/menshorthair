Do Bangs Make A Round Face Look Rounder Find Out Here

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worried that they might make your round face look even rounder? If so, you're not alone. Many people with round faces are hesitant to try bangs because they fear that they will only accentuate the fullness of their face shape. But do bangs really make a round face look rounder? Let's find out.

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. If you have a round face, you may be looking for ways to balance out your features and make your face appear longer and slimmer. While bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle, they can also be tricky to pull off if you have a round face.

How To

If you want to try bangs but are worried about making your face look rounder, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your new style flatters your face shape:

1. Choose the Right Type of Bangs

Not all bangs are created equal when it comes to round faces. To avoid making your face look fuller, opt for longer, side-swept bangs that angle down towards the sides of your face. This will help to create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

2. Avoid Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can be cute, but they're not the best choice for round faces. This type of bangs can make your face look even fuller, so it's best to steer clear if you're trying to create a more slimming effect.

3. Consider Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs that are shorter in the middle and feather out towards the sides can be a great choice for round faces. This type of bangs will help to soften the angles of your face and make your features appear more delicate and feminine.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and try bangs, here's what you need to do:

1. Consult With Your Stylist

Your stylist will be able to help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and recommend a length and style that will flatter your features.

2. Cut Your Bangs

Once you've decided on the perfect style, it's time to cut your bangs. Make sure to cut them when your hair is dry, and take your time to ensure that they're even and the right length.

3. Style Your Bangs

Now that your bangs are cut, it's time to style them. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to smooth them out and create the desired shape. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture and movement to your bangs.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your bangs work for your round face:

1. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

If you're not sure which type of bangs will work best for you, don't be afraid to experiment. Try out different lengths and styles until you find the perfect look for your face shape.

2. Keep Your Hair Long

If you have a round face, keeping your hair long can help to balance out your features and make your face appear more slender. Consider adding layers to create more movement and texture.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don't try to fight your natural texture when styling your bangs. Instead, work with your hair's natural wave or curl to create a look that's effortless and flattering.

FAQ

Q: Will bangs make my round face look rounder?

A: It depends on the type of bangs you choose. Side-swept or wispy bangs that angle down towards the sides of your face can help to create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Q: What type of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Blunt bangs can make your face look even fuller, so it's best to avoid them if you're trying to create a more slimming effect.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! With the right type of bangs and some styling know-how, you can rock this look no matter what your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, make sure to trim them every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.

Now that you know how to make bangs work for your round face, why not give this look a try? With a little bit of confidence and some styling tips, you can rock this trend and feel great about your new look.
