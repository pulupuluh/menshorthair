Best Haircut For Heart Shaped Face Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart shaped face and curly hair, finding the perfect haircut can be a little challenging. You want a cut that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural curls. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair, and provide you with tips on how to care for your curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart shaped face typically has a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. When it comes to curly hair, you want a cut that highlights your best features and balances out any asymmetry in your face. The best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair is one that adds volume to the lower half of your face, while keeping the top half sleek and smooth.

How to

When deciding on the best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair, you want to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right cut:

  • Opt for layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your curls, while also highlighting your cheekbones.
  • Keep length in the lower half of your face: This will help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Avoid heavy bangs: While bangs can look great on heart shaped faces, heavy bangs can make your face appear top-heavy.
  • Consider a side part: A side part can help add symmetry to your face and draw attention to your cheekbones.
  • Try a bob: A chin-length bob can be a great option for heart shaped face curly hair, as it adds volume to the lower half of your face.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieving the best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you choose a cut that flatters your face shape and complements your curls.
  2. Choose a length: Decide on the length you want your hair to be, keeping in mind that you want to add volume to the lower half of your face.
  3. Add layers: Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair, which will help add volume and movement to your curls.
  4. Consider a side part: A side part can help add symmetry to your face and draw attention to your cheekbones.
  5. Care for your curls: Once you have your new cut, it's important to care for your curls properly to keep them looking their best.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for heart shaped face curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner: Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause your curls to become dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can cause damage to your curls, so it's best to avoid it whenever possible.
  • Use a diffuser: When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner: A leave-in conditioner can help keep your curls hydrated and defined throughout the day.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can cause your curls to look frizzy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair:

What is the best length for heart shaped face curly hair?

The best length for heart shaped face curly hair is one that adds volume to the lower half of your face, while keeping the top half sleek and smooth. A chin-length bob can be a great option, as it adds volume to the lower half of your face.

What type of bangs should I avoid?

You should avoid heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear top-heavy. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a center part.

How can I care for my curls?

To care for your curls, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling whenever possible, and use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Now that you know the best haircut for heart shaped face curly hair, it's important to care for your curls properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling whenever possible, and use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Remember, the key to a great haircut is choosing one that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural curls. With the right cut and proper care, you can have beautiful, healthy curls that make you feel confident and beautiful.
