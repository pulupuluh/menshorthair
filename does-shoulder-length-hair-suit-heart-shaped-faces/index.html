Does Shoulder Length Hair Suit Heart Shaped Faces

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you're probably wondering if shoulder length hair is a good choice for you. You may have heard that certain hairstyles can highlight your best features and downplay your less flattering ones. In this article, we'll explore the question, "does shoulder length hair suit heart shaped faces?" and provide tips to help you make the best decision for your hair.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broader forehead and narrower chin, often with high cheekbones. Many celebrities, such as Reese Witherspoon and Jennifer Aniston, have heart-shaped faces. If you have this face shape, you may be looking for a hairstyle that balances your features and highlights your best assets.

How To

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for heart-shaped faces, there are a few key things to consider. Shoulder length hair can be a good choice because it helps to balance out the wider forehead and narrower chin. However, you'll want to avoid styles that add too much volume at the crown or around the ears, as this can make your face appear top-heavy.

Step By Step

Follow these steps to find the right shoulder length hairstyle for your heart-shaped face:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Choose a shoulder length hairstyle that adds volume to the lower half of your face. This can help to balance out your features and create a more proportionate look.
  3. Avoid styles that add too much volume at the crown or around the ears. This can make your face appear top-heavy and throw off your balance.
  4. Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture. This can help to soften your features and create a more feminine look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle for your heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as these can emphasize the width of your forehead or the narrowness of your chin.
  • Consider a side part or soft bangs to help balance out your features.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair for heart-shaped faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a heart-shaped face. Just be sure to avoid styles that add too much volume at the crown or around the ears, as this can make your face appear top-heavy.

Q: What other hairstyles are good for heart-shaped faces?

A: Other hairstyles that are good for heart-shaped faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, or use them sparingly. Heat can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair can be a good choice for heart-shaped faces, as long as you choose a style that adds volume to the lower half of your face and avoids adding too much volume at the crown or around the ears. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
