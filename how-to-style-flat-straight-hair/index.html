How To Style Flat Straight Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Jun 21, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your flat, straight hair always looking lifeless and dull? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. Flat straight hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy locks in no time. In this article, we’ll cover how to style flat straight hair, step-by-step instructions, and hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Flat Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Flat Straight Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Flat Straight Hair

How to Style Flat Straight Hair

The key to styling flat straight hair is to create volume and texture. Here are some styling techniques you can use:

1. Blow-Dry with a Round Brush

Blow-drying your hair with a round brush can add volume and movement to your hair. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to your roots and blow-dry your hair in sections, using the round brush to lift the hair at the roots.

2. Use Hot Rollers

Hot rollers can add volume and texture to flat straight hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair and wrap small sections around the rollers. Leave the rollers in for about 15-20 minutes, then remove and style as desired.

3. Try a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can add texture and grip to your hair, making it easier to style. Apply the spray to your roots and tousle your hair with your fingers to create volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style flat straight hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots, working it through to the ends of your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume and movement.

Step 4: Use Hot Rollers

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair and wrap small sections around hot rollers. Leave the rollers in for about 15-20 minutes, then remove and style as desired.

Step 5: Apply a Texturizing Spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your roots and tousle your hair with your fingers to create volume and texture.

Tips for Styling Flat Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling flat straight hair:

1. Use a Root-Lifting Spray

A root-lifting spray can add volume and lift to your hair at the roots. Apply the spray to your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Apply dry shampoo to your roots and tousle your hair with your fingers.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my flat straight hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I use a straightener on my flat straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener on your flat straight hair to create a sleek, polished look. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Flat Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your flat straight hair looking healthy and vibrant:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Flat straight hair can be prone to dryness, so it’s important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

When using hot tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your flat straight hair with ease and keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for your hair type and style.
