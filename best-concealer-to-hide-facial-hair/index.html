The Best Concealer To Hide Facial Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you struggle with facial hair that just won't seem to go away? Are you tired of constantly shaving or waxing? Enter: the best concealer to hide facial hair. Not only can it cover up any unwanted hair, but it can also give you a flawless complexion. In this article, we'll go over the top concealers on the market and provide you with tips and tricks for achieving a hair-free look.

What is a Concealer?

A concealer is a type of makeup that is used to cover up imperfections in the skin, such as dark circles, blemishes, and yes, even facial hair. It typically comes in a liquid or cream formula and is applied with a brush or sponge. When choosing a concealer to hide facial hair, you'll want to look for one that is full-coverage and long-wearing.

How to Choose the Best Concealer for Facial Hair

When selecting a concealer, it's important to consider your skin type and the color of your facial hair. Here are some tips for choosing the best concealer to hide facial hair:

  • If you have fair skin and light facial hair, opt for a concealer with pink undertones.
  • If you have medium skin and dark facial hair, look for a concealer with orange undertones.
  • If you have dark skin and dark facial hair, choose a concealer with red or purple undertones.
  • For oily skin, select a matte concealer that won't slide off throughout the day.
  • If you have dry skin, choose a hydrating concealer to prevent flakiness.

How to Apply Concealer to Hide Facial Hair

Now that you've selected your perfect concealer, it's time to apply it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Cleanse and moisturize your face before applying any makeup.
  2. Apply a color-correcting primer if necessary.
  3. Using a small brush or sponge, apply the concealer directly to the areas with facial hair.
  4. Blend the concealer outwards towards the edges of your face.
  5. Set the concealer with a translucent powder to prevent creasing and ensure long wear.
  6. Apply the rest of your makeup as usual.

Tips for Achieving a Flawless Look

Here are some additional tips for achieving a flawless and hair-free look:

  • Exfoliate your skin regularly to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Use a primer to help the concealer adhere better to the skin.
  • Set your makeup with a setting spray for added staying power.
  • Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone for a natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using concealer to hide facial hair:

  1. Can concealer cause breakouts? Yes, if you don't cleanse your skin properly before applying it. Make sure to remove all dirt and oil before applying any makeup.
  2. Can I use concealer to cover up other imperfections? Absolutely! Concealer can be used to cover up dark circles, blemishes, and other imperfections in the skin.
  3. How long does concealer last? It depends on the formula and your skin type, but most concealer can last up to 8 hours without touch-ups.

Hair Care Tips

While using concealer can hide facial hair, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy facial hair:

  • Use a gentle cleanser to prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Apply a beard oil or balm to keep your hair soft and conditioned.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage the follicles.

With these tips and the best concealer to hide facial hair, you can achieve a flawless and hair-free look. Remember to take care of your skin and hair to maintain a healthy and radiant appearance.
