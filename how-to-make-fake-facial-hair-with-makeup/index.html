How To Make Fake Facial Hair With Makeup

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to create fake facial hair with makeup? Whether you need it for a costume party, a theatrical performance, or just for fun, there's an easy way to achieve the look you want. Keep reading to learn how to make fake facial hair with makeup, step by step.

Description

Fake facial hair is a fun way to change up your look without committing to a permanent change. There are many different types of fake facial hair, from mustaches to beards to sideburns. In this article, we'll focus on how to create a mustache and a beard with makeup.

How To

Mustache

To create a mustache with makeup, you will need the following:

  • Brown or black eyeliner pencil
  • Concealer
  • Small angled brush

Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by drawing a thin line above your upper lip with the eyeliner pencil. Make sure the line is thin and even.
  2. Use the angled brush to smudge the line slightly, blending it into your skin.
  3. Apply a small amount of concealer on top of the line to set it in place and make it look more natural.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your upper lip.

Beard

To create a beard with makeup, you will need the following:

  • Brown or black eyeliner pencil
  • Brown or black eyeshadow
  • Concealer
  • Small angled brush

Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by drawing a thin line along your jawline with the eyeliner pencil. Make sure the line is even on both sides.
  2. Use the angled brush to smudge the line slightly, blending it into your skin.
  3. Dip the brush into the eyeshadow and use it to fill in the area between the line and your chin. Make sure to blend the eyeshadow into your skin to create a natural look.
  4. Apply a small amount of concealer on top of the line to set it in place and make it look more natural.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating fake facial hair with makeup:

  1. Choose the type of facial hair you want to create (mustache, beard, sideburns, etc.)
  2. Gather your supplies (eyeliner pencil, eyeshadow, concealer, angled brush)
  3. Draw a thin line with the eyeliner pencil where you want the facial hair to be
  4. Use the angled brush to smudge the line slightly
  5. Fill in the area with eyeshadow (if creating a beard)
  6. Apply a small amount of concealer on top of the line to set it in place and make it look more natural
  7. Repeat on the other side of your face (if necessary)

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect fake facial hair:

  • Choose a color that matches your natural hair color
  • Use a light hand when applying the makeup to avoid a heavy, unnatural look
  • Practice before the event to make sure you get the look you want
  • Take pictures to make sure the facial hair looks good from all angles

FAQ

Here are some common questions about creating fake facial hair with makeup:

  • Can I use any type of makeup?
  • Yes, you can use any type of makeup as long as it is safe for your skin and doesn't cause an allergic reaction.
  • How long will the makeup last?
  • The makeup should last for several hours, but you may need to touch it up throughout the day.
  • How do I remove the makeup?
  • You can remove the makeup with a gentle cleanser or makeup remover.

Hair Care

Creating fake facial hair with makeup is a fun way to change up your look, but it's important to take care of your natural hair as well. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

Now that you know how to make fake facial hair with makeup, you can experiment with different looks and have fun with your appearance. Don't be afraid to try new things and express yourself!
