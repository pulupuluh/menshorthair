How To Know What Hair Color Suits Your Skin Tone

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair color, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. Not sure how to determine what hair color suits your skin tone? We've got you covered! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to find the perfect hair color for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Color
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color for your skin tone is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural coloring of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. Here's how to determine your skin tone:

Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell if they are blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Assess Your Jewelry

Think about which type of jewelry looks best on you. If you tend to look better in gold jewelry, you have a warm skin tone. If you look better in silver jewelry, you have a cool skin tone. If you look good in both, you have a neutral skin tone.

Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also give you a clue about your skin tone. If you have black, deep brown, or ash blonde hair, you have a cool skin tone. If you have golden blonde, red, or auburn hair, you have a warm skin tone. If your hair is a mix of warm and cool tones, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Color

Now that you know your skin tone, it's time to choose your hair color. Here's how:

Step 1: Determine Your Undertones

Once you know your skin tone, it's important to determine your undertones. Undertones are the cool or warm tones that are present in your skin. If you have a cool skin tone, you'll have pink, red, or bluish undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, your undertones will be a mix of cool and warm tones.

Step 2: Choose a Hair Color

Now that you know your undertones, it's time to choose a hair color. If you have a cool skin tone, choose a hair color that has cool undertones, such as ash brown or platinum blonde. If you have a warm skin tone, choose a hair color with warm undertones, such as golden blonde or copper brown. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose a hair color with either warm or cool undertones.

Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have blue or green eyes, choose a hair color with cool undertones. If you have brown or hazel eyes, choose a hair color with warm undertones.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color for your skin tone:

  • Consider your natural hair color. If you're not sure what color to choose, try a shade that's close to your natural hair color.
  • Ask for advice. If you're unsure about what hair color to choose, ask your stylist for advice.
  • Take care of your hair. Once you've chosen your new hair color, take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

FAQs

What hair color suits cool skin tones?

For cool skin tones, choose hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash brown, platinum blonde, or cool black.

What hair color suits warm skin tones?

For warm skin tones, choose hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, copper brown, or warm black.

What hair color suits neutral skin tones?

For neutral skin tones, you can choose hair colors with either warm or cool undertones. Some good options include caramel brown, honey blonde, or auburn.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to heat, such as from blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know how to choose a hair color that suits your skin tone, it's time to book that appointment with your stylist! Remember to take care of your new hair color to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
