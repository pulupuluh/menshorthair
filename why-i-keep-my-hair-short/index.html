Why I Keep My Hair Short A Hair Care Guide

Written by Bardi May 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're wondering why I keep my hair short, you're not alone. Short hair has been my go-to style for years, and there are many reasons why I prefer it over longer locks. In this blog post, I'll share my personal experiences and tips for keeping short hair healthy and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

First things first, let me explain why I keep my hair short. For starters, it's low maintenance. Short hair requires less time and effort to style and maintain than longer hair. It's also easier to keep clean and healthy, as there's less hair to shampoo and condition.

Another reason why I keep my hair short is that it suits my face shape and features. I have a round face, and short hair helps to balance it out and make it appear slimmer. Plus, I love how a short haircut can highlight my eyes and cheekbones.

Lastly, I enjoy the versatility of short hair. You can experiment with different styles, from pixie cuts to bobs to shags, and change up your look without committing to a long-term hairstyle.

How To

If you're thinking of trying a short haircut, here are some tips for getting started:

  • Research different styles and find inspiration from celebrities, Pinterest, and Instagram.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in your chosen style and whether it fits your lifestyle.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as a good shampoo and conditioner, styling cream, and hairspray.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling cream or mousse.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots for volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls, if desired.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for short hair:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

Short hair can be flattering for most face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Does short hair require less maintenance than long hair?

Yes, short hair typically requires less time and effort to style and maintain than longer hair. However, it still requires regular trims and proper care to keep it healthy and stylish.

Can I still experiment with different styles with short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair offers plenty of versatility, from edgy pixie cuts to chic bobs to tousled shags. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hair length, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking healthy, stylish, and easy to maintain. Whether you're a busy mom, a working professional, or a trendsetter, short hair offers endless possibilities for expressing your personal style.
