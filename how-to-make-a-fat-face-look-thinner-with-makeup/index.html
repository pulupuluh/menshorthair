How To Make A Fat Face Look Thinner With Makeup

Written by William Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may feel self-conscious about its appearance. However, with the right makeup techniques, you can make your face look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we'll show you how to make a fat face look thinner with makeup.

Description

A round or chubby face can be caused by genetics, weight gain, or other factors. While there's nothing wrong with having a fuller face, some people may prefer a more angular or defined look. Makeup can be a powerful tool to achieve this.

How to

To make your face look slimmer with makeup, follow these steps:

Step 1: Contouring

Contouring is the key to creating a more defined and slimming effect on your face. Use a contouring powder or cream that's two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the areas of your face that you want to slim down, such as your cheekbones, temples, and jawline.

Step 2: Highlighting

After contouring, use a highlighting powder or cream that's two shades lighter than your skin tone. Apply it to the areas of your face that you want to bring forward, such as the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the tops of your cheekbones.

Step 3: Blending

Blend the contouring and highlighting products well using a makeup brush or sponge. This will create a seamless and natural-looking effect.

Step 4: Eye Makeup

Your eye makeup can also play a role in making your face look slimmer. Apply darker eyeshadow shades to the outer corners of your eyes and blend well. This will create a more elongated and defined eye shape.

Step 5: Lip Makeup

Your lip makeup can also help balance out your face shape. Use a lip liner to define your lips and create a more defined shape. Choose a lipstick shade that complements your skin tone and apply it evenly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your face look slimmer with makeup:

  • Use a matte foundation to avoid adding shine to your face, which can make it look fuller.
  • Avoid using too much blush, which can make your cheeks look rounder.
  • Use a brow pencil or powder to define your eyebrows and create a more angular shape.
  • Use a setting powder to keep your makeup in place and prevent it from smudging or fading throughout the day.
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. A layered haircut or side-swept bangs can help create a more elongated look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a fat face look thinner with makeup:

Q: Can makeup really make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, makeup can create the illusion of a slimmer and more defined face shape. By using contouring and highlighting techniques, you can create shadows and highlights that help sculpt your face.

Q: Do I need to use expensive makeup products to make my face look slimmer?

A: No, you don't need to use expensive makeup products to achieve a slimmer face look. There are many affordable contouring and highlighting products available that work just as well as high-end ones.

Q: How long will it take to make my face look slimmer with makeup?

A: The time it takes to make your face look slimmer with makeup will depend on your skill level and the products you use. With practice, you can achieve a slimmer face look in just a few minutes.

Hair Care

While makeup can help make your face look slimmer, it's also important to take care of your hair. A flattering haircut can help balance out your face shape and create a more elongated look. Consult with a hairstylist to find a style that works best for you.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your face look slimmer and more defined with makeup. Remember to practice and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
