How To Go Natural With Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 31, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you're considering going natural with short hair, you're not alone. Many women are embracing their natural hair texture and opting for shorter styles that require less maintenance. Whether you're transitioning from relaxed hair or just looking for a change, going natural with short hair can be a liberating experience. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of going natural with short hair, from the initial decision to the final chop.

Table of Contents

  • Making the Decision
  • Transitioning from Relaxed Hair
  • The Big Chop
  • Styling Your Short Natural Hair
  • Choosing Natural Hair Products
  • Maintenance Tips for Short Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Natural Hair Care

Making the Decision

The first step in going natural with short hair is making the decision to do so. This can be a big decision, especially if you've been relaxing your hair for years. Take some time to think about why you want to go natural, and what your expectations are for your hair. It's important to have a clear understanding of what you're getting into before you make the leap.

Some common reasons for going natural include wanting to embrace your natural hair texture, reducing the amount of time and money spent on hair maintenance, and wanting to avoid the chemicals in relaxers. Whatever your reason, make sure it's something that you're committed to, as going natural can be a challenging process.

Transitioning from Relaxed Hair

If you currently have relaxed hair, you may want to consider transitioning to natural hair before making the big chop. This can be a gradual process that allows you to grow out your natural hair while still maintaining some length. Some tips for transitioning to natural hair include:

  • Protective styling, such as braids, twists, and bantu knots
  • Using natural hair products that promote growth and strength
  • Avoiding heat styling and chemical treatments
  • Trimming your relaxed ends regularly to prevent breakage

Transitioning to natural hair can take several months to a year or more, depending on how quickly your hair grows. Be patient and take good care of your hair during this time to ensure that it remains healthy and strong.

The Big Chop

If you're ready to go natural with short hair, the next step is the big chop. This involves cutting off all of your relaxed hair, leaving only your natural hair. The big chop can be a scary experience, but it can also be incredibly freeing. Some tips for the big chop include:

  • Choose a reputable stylist who has experience with natural hair
  • Bring pictures of the style you want to help your stylist understand your vision
  • Be prepared for a dramatic change in your appearance
  • Wear your hair with confidence and pride

The big chop can be a liberating experience that allows you to start fresh with your natural hair texture. Embrace the change and enjoy the journey!

Styling Your Short Natural Hair

Styling short natural hair can be fun and versatile. There are many different styles that you can try, from wash-and-go's to twist outs to bantu knot outs. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair texture and face shape. Some tips for styling short natural hair include:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair
  • Try protective styles, such as braids and twists, to give your hair a break
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, and hair clips to add some flair to your style

Styling short natural hair can be a fun and creative process. Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your hair!

Choosing Natural Hair Products

Choosing the right natural hair products can be a daunting task, as there are so many options available. Some tips for choosing natural hair products include:

  • Look for products that are free of sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals
  • Read reviews and do research to find products that work well for your hair type
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair
  • Use a clarifying shampoo occasionally to remove buildup from your hair and scalp

Choosing the right natural hair products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Take some time to find products that work well for you.

Maintenance Tips for Short Natural Hair

Maintaining short natural hair requires some effort, but it doesn't have to be difficult. Some tips for maintaining short natural hair include:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and chemical treatments

Maintaining short natural hair requires some effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

FAQs

Q: Is it possible to go natural with short hair?

A: Yes, it is possible to go natural with short hair. The process may be different than for longer hair, but it can still be done.

Q: How long does it take to transition to natural hair?

A: The length of time it takes to transition to natural hair can vary depending on your hair type and how quickly your hair grows. It can take several months to a year or more.

Q: Will my natural hair be difficult to manage?

A: Natural hair can be more challenging to manage than relaxed hair, but with the right products and techniques, it can be just as manageable.

Natural Hair Care

Caring for your natural hair is essential to keeping it healthy and strong. Some tips for natural hair care include:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and chemical treatments

Caring for your natural hair can take some effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

Conclusion

Going natural with short hair can be a liberating experience that allows you to embrace your natural hair texture and reduce the amount of time and money spent on hair maintenance. Whether you choose to transition from relaxed hair or make the big chop, there are many different styles and techniques to try. With the right products and maintenance routine, your short natural hair can be healthy, strong, and beautiful.
