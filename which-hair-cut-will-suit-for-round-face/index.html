Which Hair Cut Will Suit For Round Face

Written by Robby Jun 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you might be struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing which hair cut will suit for round face and the best hairstyles to complement your facial shape.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to elongate the face and create the illusion of more angular features. This can be achieved through a variety of different hairstyles and haircuts.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and angles of the cut. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Length

Longer hair can elongate the face and create a slimming effect. However, avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face appear wider. Opt for layers or a side-swept fringe to add dimension and movement.

Layers

Layers can also help to elongate the face by adding volume and texture. Avoid heavy, blunt layers that add bulk to the sides of the face. Instead, opt for light, wispy layers that frame the face and create a more angular appearance.

Angles

Hairstyles with angles, such as bobs or asymmetrical cuts, can also create the illusion of a slimmer face. A bob with longer layers in the front and shorter layers in the back can elongate the face, while an asymmetrical cut can add visual interest and dimension.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect haircut for a round face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are relatively similar, you likely have a round face.

Step 2: Consider your hair type

Your hair type can also influence the best haircut for your face shape. For example, if you have thin hair, a blunt bob may not be the best option. Instead, opt for a layered cut that adds volume and movement.

Step 3: Choose your length

Determine the length that flatters your face shape the most. Longer hair can elongate the face, while shorter hair can create the illusion of more angular features.

Step 4: Add layers and angles

To add dimension and create a more angular appearance, consider adding layers or angles to your haircut. A side-swept fringe, asymmetrical cut, or layered bob can all be great options for a round face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that shorten the face
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face
  • Consider a deep side part to create the illusion of a longer face
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs or heavy bangs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate the face and create the illusion of more angular features.

What length of hair is best for a round face?

Longer hair can elongate the face and create a slimming effect. However, avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face appear wider.

Do layers work well for a round face?

Yes, layers can help to elongate the face by adding volume and texture. Opt for light, wispy layers that frame the face and create a more angular appearance.

Hair Care

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing the perfect haircut for a round face, it's all about creating the illusion of more angular features. Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a haircut, and don't be afraid to experiment with layers and angles. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you'll be able to rock any hairstyle with confidence!
