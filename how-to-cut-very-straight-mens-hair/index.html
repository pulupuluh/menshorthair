How to Cut Very Straight Men's Hair | Step-by-Step Guide

Cutting men's hair can be tricky, especially when dealing with very straight hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to cut very straight men's hair.

How to Cut Very Straight Men's Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with different guard sizes.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair thoroughly and dry it completely. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, which can lead to mistakes when cutting.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Use a comb to separate the hair into sections and use hair clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a higher guard size for the top and a lower guard size for the sides and back.

Step 4: Create a Guide Cut

Use hair cutting scissors to create a guide cut. Take a small section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Use this section as a guide to cut the rest of the hair.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Start by cutting the top section of the hair. Use hair cutting scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a straight line.

Step 6: Cut the Sides and Back

Use hair clippers with a lower guard size to cut the sides and back of the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the same guard size throughout.

Step 7: Blend the Hair

Use a higher guard size on the clippers to blend the sides and back into the top section of the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, blending the hair as you go.

Step 8: Check for Unevenness

Check the hair for any unevenness. If necessary, make small adjustments using hair cutting scissors.

Step 9: Clean Up the Hairline

Use hair clippers with a shorter guard size to clean up the hairline around the ears and neck. Use hair cutting scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 10: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired using hair products such as gel or pomade.

Tips for Cutting Very Straight Men's Hair

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid making mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the hair from different angles.
  • Ask the person whose hair you are cutting for feedback throughout the process.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on the individual's hair growth rate and desired hairstyle. On average, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a professional-looking haircut without the help of a professional.

Hair Care for Very Straight Men's Hair

Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for straight hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on the hair and use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as a blow dryer or straightener.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a great haircut for very straight men's hair at home. Remember to take your time and work carefully to avoid making mistakes. Good luck!
