Half Up Braided Hairstyles For Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 01, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have long hair, then half up braided hairstyles are perfect for you. They are versatile, easy to do, and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to create different half up braided hairstyles for long hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Create Half Up Braided Hairstyles for Long Hair

Before you start creating your half up braided hairstyle, you need to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. If you have long hair, you may need to use hair clips to section your hair. Here are the basic steps to create a half up braided hairstyle:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. If you have curly hair, you can use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and divide it into three equal sections. This will be the starting point of your braid.

Step 3: Create the Braid

Start braiding your hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section. Continue braiding until you have reached the desired length. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Step 4: Secure the Half Up Portion

Take the top section of your hair and gather it into a half-up ponytail. Use a hair elastic to secure it in place.

Step 5: Combine the Braid and Half Up Ponytail

Take the braided section and wrap it around the base of the half-up ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure the braid in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to create different half up braided hairstyles for long hair:

Half Up Fishtail Braid

To create a half up fishtail braid, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and divide it into two equal sections.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the right section and cross it over to the inside of the left section.
  4. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the inside of the right section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have reached the desired length.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  7. Take the top section of your hair and gather it into a half-up ponytail. Use a hair elastic to secure it in place.
  8. Take the braided section and wrap it around the base of the half-up ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure the braid in place.

Half Up Dutch Braid

To create a half up Dutch braid, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and divide it into three equal sections.
  3. Start braiding your hair by crossing the right section under the middle section, then the left section under the middle section.
  4. As you continue braiding, add small sections of hair to the braid from the sides.
  5. When you have reached the desired length, secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  6. Take the top section of your hair and gather it into a half-up ponytail. Use a hair elastic to secure it in place.
  7. Take the braided section and wrap it around the base of the half-up ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure the braid in place.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Half Up Braided Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect half up braided hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use hair clips to section your hair.
  • Make sure that your braids are tight and secure.
  • Use bobby pins to pin any loose strands of hair in place.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to create a half up braided hairstyle?

A: It depends on the complexity of the hairstyle and your level of experience. Simple half up braided hairstyles can be done in less than 10 minutes, while more complex hairstyles may take up to 30 minutes.

Q: Can I create a half up braided hairstyle on curly hair?

A: Yes, you can create a half up braided hairstyle on curly hair. You may need to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before braiding it.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it is wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Creating half up braided hairstyles for long hair is easy and fun. With these step-by-step instructions and tips, you can create different half up braided hairstyles for any occasion. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
