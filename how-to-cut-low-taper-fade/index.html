How To Cut Low Taper Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, a low taper fade is a great option. This haircut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back that gradually gets longer towards the top. It's a versatile cut that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a low taper fade.

Description

A low taper fade is a popular haircut that involves gradually tapering the hair on the sides and back of the head. The hair is usually short at the bottom and gradually gets longer towards the top, creating a fade effect. This style is perfect for men who want a stylish and modern look that is easy to maintain.

How to Cut Low Taper Fade

Step 1: Cut the Top

Start by cutting the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair evenly.

Step 2: Create the Fade

Use clippers to create a fade on the sides and back of the head. Start with a higher guard and gradually switch to a lower guard as you move towards the bottom. Blend the hair evenly to create a smooth transition.

Step 3: Define the Hairline

Use clippers to define the hairline around the ears and the back of the neck. Use a higher guard to create a clean line.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use scissors or clippers to blend the hair on the sides and back of the head. Make sure the hair is evenly blended and there are no harsh lines.

Step 5: Clean Up

Clean up any stray hairs and make sure the haircut is even. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that may be sticking out.

Tips

- Use a good quality pair of clippers and scissors for the best results. - Start with a higher guard and gradually switch to a lower guard for a smooth fade. - Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair takes practice and skill. - Use a comb to guide your clippers for a more precise cut.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut a low taper fade at home?

A: Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut a low taper fade at home. However, if you're not confident in your skills, it's best to go to a professional barber.

Q: What type of hair is best for a low taper fade?

A: A low taper fade works well on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a low taper fade?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

To keep your low taper fade looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dry and brittle.

In conclusion, a low taper fade is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut a low taper fade at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for the best results. Remember to take care of your hair to keep your style looking fresh and stylish.
