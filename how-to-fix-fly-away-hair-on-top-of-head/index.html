How To Fix Fly Away Hair On Top Of Head

Written by Eveline Apr 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with fly away hair on top of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this hair problem, especially during the winter months when the air is dry. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to fix fly away hair on top of your head, so you can look and feel your best.

Description

Fly away hair is caused by static electricity, which is more prevalent during the winter months. The dry air robs your hair of moisture, causing it to become dry and brittle. When you brush your hair, the friction causes static electricity, which makes your hair stand up and fly away. This can be frustrating, especially when you're trying to style your hair.

How to Fix Fly Away Hair on Top of Head

There are several ways to fix fly away hair on top of your head. Some of the most effective methods include:

1. Use a Humidifier

Using a humidifier can help add moisture back into the air, which can help prevent fly away hair. This is especially helpful during the winter months when the air is dry. Place a humidifier in your bedroom or other areas where you spend a lot of time.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help add moisture to your hair, which can help prevent fly away hair. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing and conditioning it. This will help keep your hair hydrated and less prone to static electricity.

3. Use a Hair Oil

Using a hair oil can help add moisture to your hair and prevent fly away hair. Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair after washing and conditioning it. This will help keep your hair hydrated and less prone to static electricity.

4. Use a Static Guard Spray

Using a static guard spray can help prevent fly away hair. Spray a small amount of static guard spray onto your hairbrush before brushing your hair. This will help reduce the amount of static electricity in your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide on how to fix fly away hair on top of your head:

Step 1: Use a Humidifier

Place a humidifier in your bedroom or other areas where you spend a lot of time. This will help add moisture back into the air, which can help prevent fly away hair.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing and conditioning it. This will help keep your hair hydrated and less prone to static electricity.

Step 3: Use a Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair after washing and conditioning it. This will help keep your hair hydrated and less prone to static electricity.

Step 4: Use a Static Guard Spray

Spray a small amount of static guard spray onto your hairbrush before brushing your hair. This will help reduce the amount of static electricity in your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help fix fly away hair on top of your head:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause more static electricity.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, as these can cause more damage and make fly away hair worse.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the dry winter air.

FAQ

Q: Can fly away hair be fixed permanently?

A: Unfortunately, fly away hair cannot be fixed permanently. However, using the methods mentioned in this article can help reduce the amount of fly away hair.

Q: Can I use hairspray to fix fly away hair?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to fix fly away hair. Spray a small amount onto your hairbrush before brushing your hair.

Q: Can I use a home remedy to fix fly away hair?

A: Yes, there are several home remedies that can help fix fly away hair. Some of the most effective include using apple cider vinegar, coconut oil, or aloe vera gel on your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to preventing fly away hair. Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help fix fly away hair on top of your head and keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.
