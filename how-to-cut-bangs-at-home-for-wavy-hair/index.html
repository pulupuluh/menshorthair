How To Cut Bangs At Home For Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have wavy hair and want to switch up your hairstyle, cutting bangs can be a great option. Not only will bangs add some depth and dimension to your hair, they can also help frame your face and accentuate your features. However, it can be intimidating to cut your own bangs at home, especially if you're not a professional hairdresser. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to cut bangs at home for wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Cut Bangs at Home for Wavy Hair
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Bangs at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Bangs

How To Cut Bangs at Home for Wavy Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to decide what type of bangs you want. Do you want blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or something in between? Once you've made your decision, you'll need a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle of water
  • A mirror

Step-By-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut bangs at home for wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb through your hair and make sure it's free of tangles.
  2. Decide on the length and style of your bangs. Use your fingers to separate the section of your hair that will become your bangs.
  3. Use the comb to make a straight part in your hair where you want your bangs to start.
  4. Use the spray bottle to dampen your bangs. This will make them easier to cut.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  6. Move your fingers to the side and cut at an angle for side-swept bangs.
  7. Repeat on the other side, making sure your bangs are even.
  8. Use the scissors to make any necessary adjustments to the length or shape of your bangs.

Tips for Cutting Bangs at Home

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting bangs at home:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's been cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean, even cut.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make small adjustments as you go.
  • Consider watching a few YouTube tutorials before you start cutting. There are plenty of tutorials available that will show you different cutting techniques and styles.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own bangs, consider asking a friend or family member to help you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs at home for wavy hair:

1. How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking fresh.

2. Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?

It's best to cut your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair can be deceiving, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

3. Should I cut my bangs straight or at an angle?

This depends on the look you're going for. Straight across bangs are more classic, while side-swept bangs are more modern and versatile.

Hair Care After Cutting Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can dry them out and make them brittle.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as wax or pomade, to keep your bangs in place.
  • Wash your bangs regularly, but avoid using too much shampoo or conditioner, as this can weigh them down.
  • If you're experiencing any problems with your bangs, such as split ends or breakage, consider booking an appointment with a professional hairstylist.

Now that you know how to cut bangs at home for wavy hair, you're ready to try it out for yourself. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to make small adjustments as you go. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect set of bangs.
