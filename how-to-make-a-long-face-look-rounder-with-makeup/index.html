How To Make A Long Face Look Rounder With Makeup A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 28, 2023 · 9 min read

Do you have a long face and are looking to make it look rounder? If so, you're in luck! With the right makeup techniques, you can create the illusion of a rounder face. In this article, we'll discuss the steps you can take to achieve a rounder face shape using makeup. We'll also provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice to complement your new look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make a Long Face Look Rounder with Makeup
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Make a Long Face Look Rounder with Makeup

The key to making a long face look rounder is to create the illusion of width and fullness. This can be achieved through contouring, highlighting, and choosing the right makeup products. Here are some tips to get you started:

Contouring

Contouring is a technique that involves using a darker shade of makeup to create the illusion of shadows on the face. By applying this darker shade in the right places, you can create the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some tips for contouring:

  • Use a contouring powder or cream that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Apply the contouring product in the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and at the temples.
  • Blend the product well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Highlighting

Highlighting is a technique that involves using a lighter shade of makeup to bring forward the areas of the face that you want to emphasize. By applying this lighter shade in the right places, you can create the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some tips for highlighting:

  • Use a highlighting powder or cream that is one to two shades lighter than your skin tone.
  • Apply the highlighting product to the tops of your cheekbones, the center of your forehead, and the bridge of your nose.
  • Blend the product well to create a natural-looking glow.

Choosing the Right Products

Choosing the right makeup products is important when trying to create the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some tips for choosing the right products:

  • Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and has a natural finish.
  • Choose a blush that is a warm, rosy shade.
  • Avoid using shimmery or glittery products, as these can emphasize the wrong areas of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to make a long face look rounder with makeup, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
  2. Apply your foundation as you normally would.
  3. Apply your contouring product to the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and at the temples.
  4. Blend the contouring product well to create a natural-looking shadow.
  5. Apply your highlighting product to the tops of your cheekbones, the center of your forehead, and the bridge of your nose.
  6. Blend the highlighting product well to create a natural-looking glow.
  7. Apply a warm, rosy blush to the apples of your cheeks.
  8. Use a fluffy brush to blend everything together for a seamless finish.
  9. Apply your favorite eye makeup and lip products to complete the look.
  10. Set your makeup with a setting spray to ensure it lasts all day.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve a rounder face shape:

  • Use a lighter shade of foundation in the center of your face to create the illusion of fullness.
  • Use a fluffy brush to apply your contouring and highlighting products for a seamless finish.
  • Avoid applying your contouring product too close to your mouth, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Use a cream blush for a more natural-looking flush.

FAQs

What if I don't want to contour?

If you don't want to contour, you can still achieve a rounder face shape by using highlighting and blush. Apply your highlighting product to the tops of your cheekbones, the center of your forehead, and the bridge of your nose, and apply a warm, rosy blush to the apples of your cheeks.

What if I have oily skin?

If you have oily skin, choose a matte foundation and setting spray to keep your skin looking matte throughout the day. You can still use highlighting and blush, but choose powder products instead of cream to avoid adding extra shine to your face.

Hair Care Tips

Complement your new rounder face shape with the right hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Choose a hairstyle with layers and volume at the sides to create the illusion of width.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as these can emphasize the length of your face.
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe or bangs to break up the length of your face.
  • Use volumizing products to add fullness to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can make a long face look rounder with makeup. Remember to choose the right products, use the right techniques, and complement your new look with the right hairstyle. Have fun experimenting with different makeup looks and hairstyles!
