How To Cut Men s Hair On Top With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barbershop every time you need a haircut? Well, why not learn how to cut men's hair on top with clippers yourself? You can save time and money while achieving the look you want. In this guide, we will take you through the steps on how to cut men's hair on top with clippers.

Description

Cutting men's hair on top with clippers can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, it can be easy and fun. This guide will help you learn how to cut men's hair on top with clippers, from choosing the right clippers to trimming the hair.

How To

To start, you will need a few tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a mirror, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Once you have all your tools, you can start by choosing the right clippers.

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

When choosing clippers, you should consider the brand, the motor, the blade, and the size. The most popular brands are Andis, Wahl, and Oster. The motor should be powerful enough to cut through thick hair, and the blade should be adjustable for different lengths. The size of the clippers should be comfortable for you to hold and maneuver.

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, you should prepare the hair by washing and drying it. This will make it easier to cut and style. You should also comb the hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 3: Choose the Length

Once the hair is prepared, you should choose the length you want to cut. You can do this by using the adjustable blade on the clippers. Start with a longer length and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

To start cutting, hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and start at the back of the head. Move the clippers up and down, using a comb to guide the hair. Continue this process until you have cut all the hair on top of the head.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

To trim the edges, use a pair of scissors to cut the hair around the ears and the neckline. Be careful not to cut too much, as this can ruin the overall look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers
  • Trim the edges carefully
  • Practice makes perfect

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair on top with clippers:

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good timeframe.

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It is best to cut hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be harder to cut evenly.

Q: Can I use the same clippers for my beard and my hair?

A: Yes, you can use the same clippers, but you should clean them thoroughly before and after use.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take good care of it. This includes washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo, conditioning it, and using a hairbrush to keep it tangle-free. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.

Cutting men's hair on top with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect haircut every time. So, what are you waiting for? Grab your clippers and start cutting!
