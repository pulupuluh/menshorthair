How To Tone Orange Hair With Purple Shampoo

Written by Oliver Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have recently dyed your hair blonde or lightened your natural hair color, you may have noticed that your hair has turned orange. This is a common problem that can be caused by a variety of factors, including the presence of minerals in your water or the use of a low-quality hair dye. Fortunately, there is a simple solution to this problem: purple shampoo. In this article, we will explain how to tone orange hair with purple shampoo.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Tone Orange Hair with Purple Shampoo
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Purple Shampoo
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Tone Orange Hair with Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a special type of shampoo that is designed to neutralize brassy tones in blonde and lightened hair. It works by depositing purple pigment into the hair, which cancels out the orange and yellow tones that can make your hair look dull and unattractive. Here's how to use purple shampoo to tone orange hair:

Step 1: Wet your hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure that all of your hair is saturated, from root to tip.

Step 2: Apply the purple shampoo

Next, apply a generous amount of purple shampoo to your hair. Work the shampoo into a rich lather, making sure that you cover all of your hair evenly.

Step 3: Leave the shampoo on your hair for a few minutes

Once you have applied the shampoo, leave it on your hair for a few minutes. The exact amount of time will depend on the brand of shampoo you are using and the level of brassiness in your hair. Check the instructions on the bottle for guidance.

Step 4: Rinse your hair

After a few minutes, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure that you remove all of the shampoo from your hair.

Step 5: Condition your hair

Finally, condition your hair as you normally would. This will help to keep your hair soft and shiny, and will prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Tips for Using Purple Shampoo

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using purple shampoo:

  • Use purple shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your hair's color.
  • Be careful not to leave the shampoo on your hair for too long, as this can cause your hair to become overly purple.
  • If you have very light hair, you may need to dilute the shampoo with water to prevent your hair from turning purple.
  • Follow up with a good conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: Can I use purple shampoo on brown or black hair?

A: Purple shampoo is designed for use on blonde and lightened hair, and may not be effective on darker hair colors. If you have brown or black hair, you may want to try a different type of color-correcting shampoo.

Q: Can I use purple shampoo every day?

A: It is not necessary to use purple shampoo every day, and doing so may actually cause your hair to become too purple. Instead, use the shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your hair's color.

Q: Will purple shampoo damage my hair?

A: Purple shampoo is formulated to be gentle on your hair, and should not cause any damage. However, it is important to follow up with a good conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using purple shampoo, there are a few other things you can do to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use hot tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using purple shampoo, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and vibrant, even if it has turned orange due to brassiness.
