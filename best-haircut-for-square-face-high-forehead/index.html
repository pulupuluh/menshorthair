Best Haircut For Square Face High Forehead

Written by Petter Mar 20, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a square face shape and a high forehead, you may be wondering what the best haircut is to flatter your features. With the right cut, you can balance out your forehead and enhance your square jawline. In this article, we’ll show you the best haircut for square face high forehead and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is nearly as wide as the cheekbones. If you also have a high forehead, it can create a boxy appearance that you may want to soften. The best haircut for square face high forehead is one that adds volume to the crown of the head and creates length at the sides.

How to

The best way to achieve this look is to opt for a layered haircut that falls at or below the chin. This will create movement and texture in your hair, drawing attention away from your forehead. You can also add soft, side-swept bangs to further balance out your face shape.

Step by Step

Here’s how to get the best haircut for square face high forehead:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Ask for long layers that begin at the chin and frame your face.
  3. Add side-swept bangs that end just below the eyebrows.
  4. Use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying to add height at the crown of the head.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting the best haircut for square face high forehead:

  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face appear boxy.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what flatters your face shape.
  • Invest in a quality flat iron or curling wand to create versatile styles.
  • Keep your hair healthy by using a nourishing shampoo and conditioner and avoiding excessive heat styling.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for square face high forehead:

What hairstyles should I avoid with a square face and high forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or pixie cuts. Additionally, steer clear of center parts, as they can highlight your forehead.

Can I still wear a short haircut with a square face and high forehead?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add layers and texture to create movement and balance out your face shape. Soft, side-swept bangs can also help to soften your forehead.

What if I have curly or thick hair?

Curly and thick hair can still look great with the best haircut for square face high forehead. Just make sure to work with your hairstylist to find the right length and layering to suit your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best after getting the best haircut for square face high forehead, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, and use a heat protectant spray before applying heat.
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush and comb to prevent breakage and tangles.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and shine to your hair.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your square face shape and high forehead. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, or highlights, make sure to work with a skilled hairstylist to achieve the best results.
