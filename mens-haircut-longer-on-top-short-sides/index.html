Mens Haircut Longer On Top Short Sides A Trending Style In 2023

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that's perfect for 2023, then the mens haircut longer on top short sides is the way to go. This haircut is a popular style that many men of all ages are rocking. It's a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways, and it's easy to maintain. This article will guide you on how to get this haircut, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The mens haircut longer on top short sides is a haircut that's exactly what it sounds like. The hair on the top of the head is longer, while the sides are shorter. This style is very versatile and gives you the opportunity to play around with different lengths and textures. The longer hair on top can be styled in many different ways, while the shorter sides give the haircut a clean and polished look. This style is perfect for men who want a modern and trendy haircut that's easy to style and maintain.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides

If you're interested in getting the mens haircut longer on top short sides, then you need to find a good barber or stylist. It's important to choose someone who has experience with this style and can give you the look you want. When you're at the barbershop, tell your barber or stylist that you want the mens haircut longer on top short sides. They will then cut the hair on the sides short, and leave the hair on the top longer. The length of the hair on top is up to you, but it's important to keep in mind that the longer the hair, the more maintenance it will require.

Step by Step Guide:

  1. Find a good barber or stylist
  2. Tell them you want the mens haircut longer on top short sides
  3. Decide on the length of the hair on top
  4. Sit back and let your barber or stylist do their magic
  5. Enjoy your new haircut

Tips for Styling Your Mens Haircut Longer on Top Short Sides

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to know how to style it. The mens haircut longer on top short sides is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some tips for styling your new haircut:

  • Use a good quality hair product to style your hair
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and movement in your hair

FAQ

What face shape does the mens haircut longer on top short sides suit?

The mens haircut longer on top short sides suits most face shapes. It's a versatile style that can be adapted to suit your face shape and features.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended that you get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.

What hair products should I use?

It's important to use a good quality hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some popular hair products for this style include pomade, wax, and clay.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut longer on top short sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a good quality hair product to style your hair

The mens haircut longer on top short sides is a stylish and trendy haircut that's perfect for 2023. It's a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways, and it's easy to maintain. Whether you're looking for a new haircut for work or play, the mens haircut longer on top short sides is a great choice. So, find a good barber or stylist, get your new haircut, and enjoy your stylish new look!
