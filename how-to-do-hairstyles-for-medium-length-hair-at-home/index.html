How To Do Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair At Home

Written by William Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to style. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will show you how to do hairstyles for medium length hair at home. From simple updos to beachy waves, we've got you covered.

Description

Medium length hair is hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a popular length because it's easy to manage and style. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going to work or a night out with friends, there's a hairstyle for every occasion.

How to

Here's how to do hairstyles for medium length hair at home:

1. Straight and Sleek

To achieve this look, start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Then, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair in sections. Finish with a shine serum to add some extra shine to your hair. This is a great look for a professional setting or a night out.

2. Beachy Waves

To get this look, start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Then, use a curling wand to curl your hair in sections. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Finish with a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your hair.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

This is a great look for a casual day out. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and pulling it back into a ponytail. Then, use a curling iron to curl the bottom half of your hair. Finish with a hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Step by Step

1. Straight and Sleek

Step 1: Wash and blow-dry your hair.
Step 2: Section your hair and use a straightening iron to straighten it.
Step 3: Finish with a shine serum.

2. Beachy Waves

Step 1: Wash your hair and let it air dry.
Step 2: Section your hair and use a curling wand to curl it.
Step 3: Use your fingers to loosen the curls.
Step 4: Finish with a texturizing spray.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

Step 1: Section off the top half of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail.
Step 2: Use a curling iron to curl the bottom half of your hair.
Step 3: Finish with a hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

- Use the right tools for the job.
- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.
- Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
- Use a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Q: How do I know which hairstyle is right for me?
A: Consider your face shape and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles for medium length hair at home. Whether you're going for a sleek and professional look or a casual beachy vibe, there's a hairstyle for every occasion. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
