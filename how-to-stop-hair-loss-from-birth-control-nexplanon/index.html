How To Stop Hair Loss From Birth Control Nexplanon

If you are experiencing hair loss while using the birth control Nexplanon, you are not alone. Hair loss is a common side effect of this form of birth control. However, there are steps you can take to prevent it. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair loss from birth control Nexplanon, including tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

What Causes Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon?

The birth control Nexplanon contains the hormone progestin, which can interfere with the natural hair growth cycle. Progestin can cause hair to enter the resting phase of the growth cycle prematurely, leading to increased shedding and hair loss. This can be especially true for those with a genetic predisposition to hair loss or those who are already experiencing hair loss due to other factors.

Hair Care Tips to Stop Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon

The following hair care tips can help minimize hair loss while using Nexplanon:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and ponytails, which can pull on the hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid chemical treatments, such as coloring, perming, or relaxing, which can damage the hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing wet hair, which is more fragile and prone to breakage.

Additionally, consider using a hair growth shampoo or serum that contains ingredients such as biotin, keratin, or caffeine, which can stimulate hair growth.

Supplements to Stop Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon

In addition to hair care, supplements can also help prevent hair loss while using the birth control Nexplanon. The following supplements may be helpful:

  • Biotin: Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Vitamin D: Vitamin D is important for hair growth and can help prevent hair loss.
  • Zinc: Zinc is a mineral that is important for hair growth and can help prevent hair loss.
  • Iron: Iron is important for healthy hair growth and can help prevent hair loss.

Before taking any supplements, be sure to consult with your healthcare provider to ensure they are safe for you to take.

Dietary Changes to Stop Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon

Your diet can also play a role in preventing hair loss while using the birth control Nexplanon. The following dietary changes may be helpful:

  • Eat a diet rich in protein, which is important for healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits and vegetables.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, which can help prevent hair loss.

Managing Stress to Stop Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon

Stress can also contribute to hair loss, so it is important to manage stress levels while using the birth control Nexplanon. The following tips may be helpful:

  • Practice relaxation techniques, such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.
  • Get regular exercise to help reduce stress.
  • Get enough sleep to help reduce stress levels.

Medications to Stop Hair Loss from Birth Control Nexplanon

If hair loss is severe or persistent, your healthcare provider may recommend medications to help stop hair loss. The following medications may be helpful:

  • Minoxidil: Minoxidil is a topical treatment that can help stimulate hair growth.
  • Finasteride: Finasteride is an oral medication that can help prevent hair loss.

Before taking any medications, be sure to consult with your healthcare provider to ensure they are safe for you to take.

FAQs

Can I still use Nexplanon if I am experiencing hair loss?

Yes, you can still use Nexplanon if you are experiencing hair loss. However, you may want to speak with your healthcare provider about other birth control options if hair loss is a concern for you.

Will my hair grow back after I stop using Nexplanon?

It is possible for hair to grow back after stopping Nexplanon. However, it may take several months or more for hair growth to return to normal.

Can I use hair growth products while using Nexplanon?

Yes, you can use hair growth products while using Nexplanon. However, be sure to speak with your healthcare provider before using any new products to ensure they are safe for you to use.

Can stress cause hair loss while using Nexplanon?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss while using Nexplanon. It is important to manage stress levels to help prevent hair loss.

How long does it take for hair loss to stop after stopping Nexplanon?

It may take several months or more for hair loss to stop after stopping Nexplanon. However, hair growth should gradually return to normal over time.

Conclusion

Hair loss can be a frustrating side effect of the birth control Nexplanon. However, by following these tips and tricks, you can help prevent hair loss and maintain healthy hair. If hair loss persists or is severe, be sure to speak with your healthcare provider for further evaluation and treatment options.
