How To Do Drop Fade Low Fade Haircut

Written by Bardi Apr 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our blog where we will be discussing the step-by-step process on how to do a drop fade low fade haircut. This haircut has been trending for a while now, and it’s a great way to give your hair a fresh look. We will be discussing everything you need to know about this haircut, from the tools you need to the aftercare.

Table of Content

  • Tools You Will Need
  • How to Do Drop Fade Low Fade Haircut
  • Tips for a Perfect Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Haircut

Tools You Will Need

Before we begin, let's discuss the tools you will need for this haircut:

  • Clipper
  • Guard sizes 1 to 4
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Towel

How to Do Drop Fade Low Fade Haircut

Now that you have gathered all the necessary tools, let's get started on the haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before the haircut, it’s important to wash and dry your hair. This will remove any dirt or product buildup and make it easier to cut. After washing, towel dry your hair until it’s slightly damp.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want the top of your hair to be. Using the clipper with a size 4 guard, cut your hair to the desired length. Use the scissors to trim any uneven strands.

Step 3: Create the Fade

Attach a size 3 guard to the clipper and begin cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start from the bottom and work your way up, making sure to blend the hair as you go. Repeat the process with the size 2 guard and then the size 1 guard until you reach the desired fade.

Step 4: Create the Drop Fade

To create the drop fade, use the clipper with no guard and shave a straight line around the head, starting from the temple and going down to the back of the neck. Make sure the line is straight and even on both sides.

Then, use the clipper with a size 1 guard and create another straight line about an inch above the first line, going around the head. Make sure the line is straight and even on both sides.

Using the clipper with a size 2 guard, blend the hair between the two lines to create the drop fade effect.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Apply some hair gel or pomade to your hair and style it as desired. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a messy or sleek look.

Tips for a Perfect Haircut

  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing the haircut can result in uneven hair and an unsatisfactory look.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the haircut is even.
  • If you’re not confident in doing the haircut yourself, ask a friend or visit a professional barber.

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I do the haircut on curly hair?

Yes, the drop fade low fade haircut is suitable for all hair types, including curly hair.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to do a drop fade low fade haircut, go ahead and give it a try. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a fresh and stylish look.
