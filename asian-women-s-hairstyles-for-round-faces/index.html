Asian Women s Hairstyles For Round Faces Tips And Ideas

Written by Aprilia Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Round faces are beautiful, but they can be challenging to style when it comes to hair. Luckily, there are plenty of Asian women's hairstyles that complement round faces. Here are some ideas, tips, and tricks to help you find the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Description

Round faces have soft features and a rounded chin, and they are widest at the cheeks. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. This can be achieved through haircuts that add height and volume at the crown of the head or by creating layers that frame the face.

How To

The best way to find the right hairstyle for your round face is to consult with a hairstylist who can recommend cuts that work with your unique features. However, there are some general guidelines to follow. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. Instead, opt for haircuts that add height and volume, such as layered cuts, long bobs, or pixie cuts with lots of texture.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your hair based on your face shape:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. If you have a round face, avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.
  3. Instead, opt for haircuts that add height and volume, such as layered cuts, long bobs, or pixie cuts with lots of texture.
  4. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  6. If you have long hair, create layers that frame your face.
  7. If you have short hair, use a texturizing spray to add volume and definition.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair if you have a round face:

  • Avoid center parts, which can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and create layers that frame your face.
  • If you have straight hair, create volume at the crown of your head with a volumizing mousse or spray.
  • If you have bangs, make sure they are side-swept and not blunt.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. To elongate your face, create volume at the crown of your head and avoid pulling your hair back too tightly.

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

A: The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is one that adds layers and creates volume at the crown of the head. This will elongate the face and showcase your natural texture.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. If you have curly hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated. If you have straight hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.

Additionally, make sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect Asian women's hairstyle for your round face. Whether you opt for a long bob, layered cut, or textured pixie cut, you're sure to look stunning.
