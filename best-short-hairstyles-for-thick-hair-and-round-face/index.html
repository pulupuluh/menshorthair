Best Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair And Round Face

Written by Luffy Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face and thick hair, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be challenging. However, with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your features and create a stunning look. In this article, we will share the best short hairstyles for thick hair and round face, along with tips and tricks to maintain your hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles are a great option for those with thick hair, as they can help to reduce the weight and volume of your hair, making it easier to style and manage. When it comes to a round face shape, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. This can be achieved by adding layers and texture to your hair, as well as keeping the length above the chin.

How to

The first step in choosing the perfect short hairstyle for your thick hair and round face is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture. When choosing a hairstyle, consider your personal style, as well as your lifestyle and maintenance requirements.

Step by step

1. Consult with a professional hairstylist
2. Determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture
3. Consider your personal style, lifestyle, and maintenance requirements
4. Choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape
5. Add layers and texture to your hair to reduce the weight and volume
6. Keep the length above the chin for the best results

Tips

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and depth
- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture to your hair
- Invest in a quality hair dryer and round brush for styling
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
- Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face shape?
A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can help to elongate your face and create a more oval shape.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face shape?
A: Yes, but it's important to keep the length slightly longer on top and shorter on the sides to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle for thick hair and round face, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair every other day with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant before styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently choose the best short hairstyle for your thick hair and round face. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.
