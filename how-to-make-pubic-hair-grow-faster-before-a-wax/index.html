How To Make Pubic Hair Grow Faster Before A Wax

Are you planning to have a Brazilian wax but worried that your pubic hair is not long enough? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people face this problem, and the good news is that there are ways to make your pubic hair grow faster before a wax. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you achieve the desired hair length for your waxing appointment.

How to Make Pubic Hair Grow Faster

Before we jump into the tips and tricks, let's understand the basics of hair growth. Hair growth is influenced by various factors, including genetics, hormones, and diet. While you cannot control your genetics, you can take care of your hair and ensure that you are providing it with the right nutrients to promote growth.

Here are some ways to make your pubic hair grow faster:

  1. Take hair growth supplements: Biotin, vitamin D, iron, and zinc are some of the nutrients that are essential for hair growth. You can take supplements or include these nutrients in your diet to promote hair growth.
  2. Massage your scalp: Massaging your pubic area can improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use a gentle circular motion to massage your pubic area for a few minutes every day.
  3. Use a hair growth oil: There are several hair growth oils available in the market that claim to promote hair growth. Look for oils that contain ingredients like castor oil, coconut oil, and peppermint oil.
  4. Eat a balanced diet: Your diet plays a crucial role in promoting hair growth. Make sure you are eating a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  5. Avoid tight clothing: Wearing tight clothing can restrict blood flow to your pubic area, which can slow down hair growth. Wear loose-fitting clothes that allow your skin to breathe.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the ways to make your pubic hair grow faster, let's look at a step-by-step guide to help you prepare for your waxing appointment:

  1. Stop shaving at least two weeks before your waxing appointment. This will give your hair enough time to grow to the desired length.
  2. Exfoliate your pubic area a day before your appointment. This will help remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.
  3. Apply a numbing cream to your pubic area 30 minutes before your appointment. This will help reduce pain during the waxing process.
  4. Take a painkiller like ibuprofen an hour before your appointment. This will help reduce pain and inflammation.
  5. Wear loose-fitting clothes to your appointment. This will prevent irritation and discomfort after the waxing process.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you prepare for your waxing appointment:

  1. Communicate with your esthetician. Let them know if you have any concerns or if this is your first time getting a Brazilian wax.
  2. Take a warm shower before your appointment. This will help open up your pores and make the waxing process less painful.
  3. Don't schedule your appointment during your period. Your skin is more sensitive during this time, and waxing can be more painful.
  4. Don't apply any lotions or creams to your pubic area before your appointment. This can make it difficult for the wax to adhere to your hair.
  5. Avoid sun exposure for at least 24 hours after your appointment. Your skin is more sensitive after waxing, and sun exposure can cause irritation.

FAQs

Does shaving make hair grow faster?

No, shaving does not make hair grow faster. It may appear so because shaving cuts the hair at the surface of the skin, making it appear thicker and darker. However, shaving does not affect the rate of hair growth.

How long should my hair be before a wax?

Your hair should be at least a quarter of an inch long before a wax. This is approximately two to three weeks of hair growth after shaving or four to six weeks after your last wax.

Is waxing painful?

Waxing can be painful, especially if it's your first time. However, the pain is usually tolerable and lasts only a few seconds. The pain also reduces with subsequent waxing appointments as the hair becomes thinner.

Hair Care

After your waxing appointment, it's essential to take care of your pubic area to prevent ingrown hairs and irritation. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  1. Exfoliate your pubic area regularly to prevent ingrown hairs.
  2. Avoid tight clothing that can cause irritation.
  3. Apply a soothing cream or lotion to your pubic area to reduce inflammation.
  4. Avoid sun exposure for at least 24 hours after your appointment.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your pubic hair grow faster before a wax and prepare yourself for a smooth and painless waxing experience.
