How To Make Your Hair More Curly In The Shower

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who loves curly hair but struggles to get those defined curls, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to make your hair more curly in the shower. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair to the products you should use. So let's get started!

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but getting those defined curls can be a challenge. Many factors come into play, including genetics, hair type, and styling. However, one of the easiest ways to enhance your natural curls is by making a few changes to your shower routine. By taking the right steps and using the right products, you can make your hair more curly in the shower.

How To

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step to making your hair more curly in the shower is to prep it. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. A good sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner will help retain moisture in your hair, making it easier to curl.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Before getting in the shower, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for curly hair. This will help prevent breakage and ensure that your curls are defined and bouncy.

Step 3: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

While your hair is still wet, apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel. This will help define your curls and keep them in place. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from roots to ends.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once you've applied the product, scrunch your hair with your fingers. This will help encourage your curls to form and give them more definition. Continue scrunching until your hair is no longer dripping wet.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, dry your hair on low heat. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around your head, scrunching your hair as you go. This will help set your curls and add volume.

Tips

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. These materials are gentler on your hair than regular towels, which can cause frizz.
  • If you don't have a diffuser attachment, you can still make your hair more curly in the shower. After applying the curl-enhancing product, twist small sections of your hair around your finger. This will help define your curls and encourage them to form.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair after you've applied the curl-enhancing product. This can cause your curls to separate and lose definition.
  • If you have naturally straight hair, you can still make your hair more curly in the shower. Follow the same steps, but use a stronger curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair more vigorously.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair more curly in the shower without using any products?

A: While it's possible to enhance your natural curls without using any products, using a curl-enhancing product will make the process much easier and more effective. The product will help define your curls and keep them in place, ensuring that you have beautiful, bouncy curls.

Q: Can I make my hair more curly in the shower if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still make your hair more curly in the shower if you have straight hair. Follow the same steps, but use a stronger curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair more vigorously.

Q: How often should I make my hair more curly in the shower?

A: You can make your hair more curly in the shower as often as you like. However, it's important to give your hair a break between styling sessions to prevent damage.

Hair Care

Making your hair more curly in the shower can be a great way to enhance your natural curls and add volume to your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, detangle your hair before getting in the shower, and avoid brushing or combing your hair after applying the curl-enhancing product.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make your hair more curly in the shower and enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls.
