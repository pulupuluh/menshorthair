Best Short Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Mar 30, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face looking for a new short hairstyle? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for round faces over 50 that will have you looking fabulous in no time. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to choose the perfect style to hair care tips to keep your new 'do looking its best.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Choosing the right short hairstyle for your round face can be a daunting task. Here are a few things to keep in mind when selecting a style:

Consider Your Face Shape

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wider forehead. The goal of any hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and definition. Look for styles that add height to the crown of your head and length to your face.

Think About Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in which hairstyles will work for you. If you have fine hair, consider a short, layered cut that adds volume and movement. If your hair is thick and curly, a short pixie cut can help keep your curls under control.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're a busy woman who doesn't have a lot of time to spend styling your hair, opt for a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled with a bit of product and a blow dryer.

Best Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Now that you know what to keep in mind when selecting a short hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best styles for round faces over 50.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For a round face, opt for a pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs that create the illusion of length. This style is perfect for women with fine hair and a busy lifestyle.

The Bob

A bob is another classic short hairstyle that works well for round faces. To create the illusion of length, opt for a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back. This style works well for women with thick, straight hair.

The Shag

The shag is a layered hairstyle that works well for round faces. To create the illusion of length, opt for a shag with long, choppy layers that frame your face. This style works well for women with thick, wavy hair.

The Asymmetrical Cut

The asymmetrical cut is a trendy short hairstyle that works well for round faces. To create the illusion of length, opt for an asymmetrical cut that is longer on one side than the other. This style works well for women with fine hair and a bold sense of style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair

Now that you've selected the perfect short hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair, creating volume at the crown and length around your face.

Step 5: Add Finishing Touches

Add any finishing touches, such as hairspray or a shine serum, to keep your hair looking its best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Now that you've styled your short hair, it's important to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

Get Regular Trims

Short hairstyles require regular trims to keep them looking their best. Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron.

FAQs

Can women over 50 wear short hairstyles?

Absolutely! Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 50, as they can be low-maintenance and flattering.

What should I look for in a short hairstyle for a round face?

Look for styles that add height to the crown of your head and length to your face. Avoid styles that are too blunt or too short, as they can make your face look rounder.

What products should I use on my short hair?

Use products that are formulated for your hair type, such as mousse or gel for fine hair and a smoothing serum for thick, curly hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair can become greasy more quickly than longer hair, so wash it regularly to keep it looking fresh.

Avoid Over-Brushing

Over-brushing can cause breakage and frizz, so be gentle when brushing your short hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, there are many great short hairstyles for round faces over 50. When selecting a style, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. To keep your new 'do looking its best, use the right products and follow a few simple hair care tips. With the right style and care, you'll be rocking your new short hair in no time!
