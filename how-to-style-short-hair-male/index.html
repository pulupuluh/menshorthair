How To Style Short Hair For Men Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Mar 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with short hair, you may feel limited in your styling options. But fear not! There are plenty of ways to switch up your look, whether you're going for a classic or trendy style. In this article, we'll cover how to style short hair male, step by step, and provide some helpful tips and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Style Short Hair Male: Step by Step

Before we get into the details, here are some general steps you can follow to style your short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice (e.g. wax, pomade, gel)
  3. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape
  5. Finish with hairspray (optional)

Classic Styles for Short Hair

If you're looking for a timeless look, consider trying one of these classic styles:

  • The Side Part: Use a comb to create a clean, deep part on one side of your head. Comb the rest of your hair to the opposite side.
  • The Buzz Cut: Keep your hair short and uniform all over your head. This low-maintenance style is perfect if you're looking for something simple and easy to maintain.
  • The Crew Cut: This style is similar to the buzz cut, but slightly longer on top. Use a bit of product to add texture and volume.

Trendy Styles for Short Hair

If you're feeling more adventurous, try one of these trendy styles:

  • The Textured Crop: This style features short, choppy layers on top with a faded undercut on the sides. Use a bit of product to add texture and definition.
  • The Quiff: This style features longer hair on top that is swept upward and back. Use a bit of product to add volume and hold.
  • The French Crop: This style features a short, textured fringe on top with a faded undercut on the sides. Use a bit of product to add texture and definition.

Tips for Styling Short Hair Male

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short hair:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type (e.g. wax for thick hair, gel for thin hair)
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you
  • Visit a barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh

FAQ: How to Style Short Hair Male

What is the best product to use for short hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired style. For thick hair, a wax or pomade may work well. For thin hair, a lighter product like a gel or mousse may be better. Experiment with different products to find what works for you.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair looking healthy.

Can I style my short hair without using product?

Yes! While product can help add texture and hold, it's not necessary for every style. You can use a comb or your fingers to create your desired shape.

Hair Care for Short Hair Male

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short hair male in no time. Whether you're going for a classic or trendy look, remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!
