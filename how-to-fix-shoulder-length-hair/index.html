How To Fix Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have shoulder length hair, you know that sometimes it can be difficult to style. Whether you want to try a new look or just need to fix your hair for the day, this guide will help you achieve the perfect style. Here are some tips and tricks for fixing shoulder length hair.

Description: Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair is typically around 12 to 16 inches long, depending on how you measure. It can be straight, wavy, or curly and can be styled in a variety of ways. However, it can be challenging to style because it's not long enough for some styles and too long for others. With the right tools and techniques, you can fix your shoulder length hair and look great every day.

How to Fix Shoulder Length Hair

Here are the steps to follow to fix your shoulder length hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to fixing your hair is to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo and a lightweight conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any residue.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you use any heated styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the heat. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a round brush to add volume and lift to your hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair without causing frizz.

Step 4: Straighten or Curl Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can straighten or curl it. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron or wand to create curls. Make sure to apply a heat protectant before using any heated styling tools.

Step 5: Add Texture

If you want to add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. Apply it to the roots and work it through your hair with your fingers. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place. Hold the hairspray about 8 inches away from your hair and spray evenly all over.

Tips for Fixing Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your shoulder length hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ: How to Fix Shoulder Length Hair

What is the best way to fix shoulder length hair?

The best way to fix shoulder length hair is to start with clean, dry hair and use the right tools and products. Use a heat protectant before using any heated styling tools, and finish your look with a light hairspray.

What are some easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair include a messy bun, a half-up half-down style, and loose waves. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

How often you should wash your shoulder length hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your shoulder length hair looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your shoulder length hair and look great every day. Whether you want to try a new style or just need to fix your hair for the day, these steps will help you achieve the perfect look.
