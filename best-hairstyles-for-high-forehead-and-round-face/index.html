Best Hairstyles For High Forehead And Round Face

Written by William Apr 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a high forehead and a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances out these features and flatters your face shape. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for high forehead and round face, including how to style them and tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Individuals with high foreheads and round faces can appear unbalanced, and their face can seem wider than it is. A good hairstyle can help to balance out the features and provide a more symmetrical look. The best hairstyles for this face shape will usually have some form of fringe or bangs to help conceal the high forehead.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face shape is to identify the features that you want to balance out. In this case, it's the high forehead and the roundness of the face. The next step is to choose a hairstyle that will achieve this balance.

One of the best hairstyles for this face shape is a long, layered haircut with side-swept bangs. The layers help to add volume and texture to the hair, while the bangs help to conceal the high forehead. Another great option is a bob haircut with side-swept bangs. This style is perfect for those who prefer shorter hair and still want to conceal their high forehead.

Step by Step

If you're looking to achieve the long, layered haircut with side-swept bangs, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Part your hair to the side and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add some loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and definition.
  6. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and cut it into side-swept bangs.
  7. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  8. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray.

If you're looking to achieve the bob haircut with side-swept bangs, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Part your hair to the side and blow-dry it with a round brush.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten it.
  5. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and cut it into side-swept bangs.
  6. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  7. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for a high forehead and round face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Opt for a hairstyle that has some form of bangs or fringe to conceal the high forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and volume.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a high forehead and a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to leave some hair out to frame your face and add some volume to the top of your head.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a high forehead and a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can achieve a balanced and flattering look that highlights your best features. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
