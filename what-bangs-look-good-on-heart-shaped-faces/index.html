Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Bangs can help balance out the proportions of your face and draw attention to your eyes. However, not all bangs are created equal. The goal is to choose bangs that donât make your forehead appear wider than it already is and that donât accentuate your narrow chin.

How to Choose Bangs for Heart Shaped Faces

The best bangs for heart shaped faces are those that are either side-swept or curtain bangs. Side-swept bangs are angled and fall to one side of your forehead, while curtain bangs are parted in the middle and frame your face on both sides. Both styles help draw attention away from your forehead and balance out your face.

When choosing the length of your bangs, consider your forehead size. If you have a small forehead, opt for shorter bangs. If you have a larger forehead, longer bangs can help make it appear smaller.

  3. Prepare for regular trimming to keep your bangs looking fresh.

Tips for Styling Bangs on Heart Shaped Faces

Styling your bangs can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Here are some tips for styling bangs on heart shaped faces:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. This will help create volume and shape.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your bangs.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Maintaining bangs requires regular trimming and styling. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and round brush to achieve the perfect blowout.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your bangs.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your bangs to keep them looking healthy and shiny.
