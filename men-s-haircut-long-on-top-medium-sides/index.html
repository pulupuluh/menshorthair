Men s Haircut Long On Top Medium Sides A Relaxed Style Guide

Written by Christine Apr 19, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and relaxed haircut, the men's haircut long on top medium sides is a great choice. This versatile style can be adapted to suit any face shape or hair type, and is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks put together. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this popular style, including how to get the cut, how to style it, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Description

The men's haircut long on top medium sides is a classic style that has been popular for many years. It involves keeping the hair longer on the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter. The length of the hair on the top can vary, but it's usually between 2-4 inches. The sides and back are usually cut to a length of around 1-2 inches. This gives the style a neat and tidy appearance, while still allowing for some length and volume on the top.

How to Get the Cut

If you're interested in getting the men's haircut long on top medium sides, you should start by finding a good hairstylist who has experience with this style. You can bring in a picture of the style you want, or you can ask your stylist to recommend a variation of the style that will suit your face shape and hair type. Once you've decided on the length and style of your hair, your stylist will use clippers and scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the men's haircut long on top medium sides:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Part your hair on the side or in the middle, depending on your preference.
  3. Section off the hair on the top of your head using hair clips or rubber bands.
  4. Using clippers, your stylist will trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Your stylist will then use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length.
  6. Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it using a blow dryer and styling products.
  7. Finally, your stylist will trim any stray hairs and make sure your hair looks neat and tidy.

Tips

If you're thinking of getting the men's haircut long on top medium sides, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a length and style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider adding some texture or layers to your hair to give it more volume and movement.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top medium sides:

1. Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top medium sides can be adapted to suit any hair type, whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as often as you need to keep your hair clean and fresh.

3. Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top medium sides is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways, depending on your preference. You can slick it back, part it to the side, or add some texture with a styling product.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting the men's haircut long on top medium sides:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Overall, the men's haircut long on top medium sides is a classic and versatile style that is easy to maintain and looks great on almost anyone. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style for everyday wear, or something a little more formal for special occasions, this style is sure to impress. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
