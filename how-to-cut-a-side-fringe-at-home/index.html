How To Cut A Side Fringe At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to switch things up? A side fringe is a perfect way to add a new look to your hairstyle. Not to mention, it's easy to achieve and can be done at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a side fringe at home.

How to Cut a Side Fringe at Home

Before we start, it's important to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have these, follow these simple steps to cut your own side fringe:

Step 1: Determine the Length

The first step is to determine the length of your side fringe. Use the comb to separate a section of hair from the rest. Decide how long you want your side fringe to be and mark the spot with your fingers.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, part your hair to create a section for your fringe. Make sure it's in line with your eyebrows and angled towards the side you want your fringe to be on.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Now it's time to cut the hair. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut it straight across. Remember, it's better to start with a longer length and then cut more if needed. You can always trim it later.

Step 4: Check the Length

Check the length of your fringe by letting it fall naturally. If you're happy with the length, move on to the next step. If not, trim some more until you're satisfied.

Step 5: Style Your Fringe

Now that you've cut your side fringe, it's time to style it. Use a hairdryer and a round brush to blow dry your fringe in the direction you want it to go.

Step 6: Finish with Products

To finish off your new side fringe, use a small amount of hair wax or gel to style it and hold it in place.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Side Fringe

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own side fringe:

  • Start with a longer length and trim more if needed. It's easier to cut more than to try and fix a fringe that's too short.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your fringe when it's wet. It will look shorter when it dries.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to be patient and get it right than to make a mistake.

FAQ

Can I cut my own side fringe if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own side fringe if you have curly hair. Just be sure to cut it when it's dry so you can see the curl pattern and how it will fall.

How often should I trim my side fringe?

You should trim your side fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your side fringe looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Now that you know how to cut a side fringe at home, it's time to give it a try. Follow these simple steps and you'll have a new look in no time!
