How to Grow Out a Pixie Cut in Lockdown - Tips and Tricks

Are you tired of your pixie cut and looking for ways to grow it out during lockdown? Well, you are not alone. Many women have been experimenting with short haircuts during the pandemic, but now they are ready to switch things up. In this blog post, we will provide you with some essential tips and tricks on how to grow out a pixie cut in lockdown.

How to Grow Out a Pixie Cut in Lockdown: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Be Patient

The first and most crucial step in growing out a pixie cut is to be patient. It takes time to grow out short hair, and you need to be willing to wait. During lockdown, you have the perfect opportunity to let your hair grow without worrying about social events or work.

Step 2: Get Regular Trims

While you are waiting for your hair to grow, it is important to get regular trims. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks will help remove split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Styles

As your hair grows, it can be tempting to chop it all off again. However, instead of going back to a pixie cut, try experimenting with different styles. You can try a bob, lob, or even layers to give your hair some shape and volume.

Step 4: Use Hair Accessories

During the awkward growing-out phase, hair accessories can be your best friend. Headbands, clips, and scrunchies can help keep your hair out of your face and add a touch of style to your look.

Tips for Growing Out a Pixie Cut in Lockdown

Tip 1: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Short hair is fragile, so you need to be gentle when styling and washing it. Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Instead, opt for natural hair care products that are gentle and nourishing.

Tip 2: Eat a Healthy Diet

Your diet can have a significant impact on your hair's health and growth. Make sure you are eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. Foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens are excellent for promoting healthy hair growth.

Tip 3: Stay Active

Exercise is not only good for your overall health, but it can also help promote hair growth. When you exercise, you increase blood flow to your scalp, which can stimulate hair follicles and promote growth.

Tip 4: Be Open to Haircuts

If you are struggling with growing out your pixie cut, don't be afraid to get a haircut. A professional stylist can help shape your hair as it grows and give you tips on how to style it during the awkward phase.

FAQs About Growing Out a Pixie Cut in Lockdown

Q: How long does it take to grow out a pixie cut?

A: It can take anywhere from six months to a year to grow out a pixie cut, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I color my hair while growing out a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can color your hair while growing out a pixie cut. However, it is essential to choose gentle hair dyes and avoid harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair when growing out a pixie cut?

A: It is best to wash your hair every two to three days when growing out a pixie cut. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can slow down hair growth.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out a Pixie Cut in Lockdown

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

When washing your hair, choose a gentle shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals. Look for products that are specifically designed for growing out short hair.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair is essential when growing out a pixie cut. Use a nourishing conditioner every time you wash your hair to keep it soft and healthy.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, so it is essential to protect it when using them. Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using a curling iron to prevent damage.

Tip 4: Get Plenty of Rest

Getting enough rest is essential for promoting healthy hair growth. Make sure you are getting at least seven to eight hours of sleep every night to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, growing out a pixie cut during lockdown can be a breeze. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair with gentle products and a healthy lifestyle.
