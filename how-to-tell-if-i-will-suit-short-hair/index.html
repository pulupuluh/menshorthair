How To Tell If You Will Suit Short Hair

How to Tell If You Will Suit Short Hair | Tips & FAQs

Are you considering chopping off your long locks and going for a short hairstyle? Short hair is trendy, stylish, and low maintenance. But, before you make the big chop, you may be wondering if you will suit short hair. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to tell if you will suit short hair, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Tip 1: Consider Your Face Shape

The first tip on how to tell if you will suit short hair is to consider your face shape. Short hair can be flattering for any face shape, but some styles may suit certain face shapes better than others.

If you have a round face, a short bob or pixie cut can elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. If you have a square face, a short haircut with layers can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you are lucky as most short hairstyles will suit you. If you have a heart-shaped face, a short haircut with side-swept bangs can balance out your features.

Tip 2: Look at Your Hair Texture

Another tip on how to tell if you will suit short hair is to look at your hair texture. Short hair can be styled in various ways, but some styles may require more maintenance than others.

If you have thin hair, a short haircut with layers can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can be flattering. If you have curly hair, a short haircut with layers can enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, a short haircut with bangs can add dimension to your hair.

Tip 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

The last tip on how to tell if you will suit short hair is to think about your lifestyle. Short hair may require less maintenance, but it may not be suitable for everyone's lifestyle.

If you are a busy person who doesn't have much time to style your hair, a short haircut may be perfect for you. If you are an active person who loves to exercise, a short haircut may be more practical. If you work in a conservative environment, a short haircut may be more professional.

Step 1: Research Short Hairstyles

The first step in determining if you will suit short hair is to research short hairstyles. Look for inspiration on social media platforms such as Pinterest, Instagram, or YouTube. Save pictures of hairstyles that you like and show them to your hairstylist.

When researching short hairstyles, pay attention to the length, texture, and layers. Short hair can be anything from a pixie cut to a bob, and it can be styled in countless ways.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

The second step in determining if you will suit short hair is to consult with your hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can provide you with expert advice on which short hairstyles will suit you based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Your hairstylist can also help you choose a short hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. They can also provide you with tips on how to style and maintain your short hair.

Step 3: Try on Wigs or Use Virtual Hairstyling Tools

The third step in determining if you will suit short hair is to try on wigs or use virtual hairstyling tools. This step can help you visualize how you will look with a short hairstyle before making the big chop.

You can try on wigs at a wig store or use virtual hairstyling tools available online. Virtual hairstyling tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and virtually try on different short hairstyles.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, short hair can actually be flattering for those with a double chin. A short bob or pixie cut can draw attention to your face and away from your neck.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Yes, short hair can still be styled in various ways. You can use hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some flair to your short hair.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: No, short hair can actually make you look younger and more modern. A short hairstyle can also enhance your facial features and highlight your best assets.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Now that you have decided to go for a short hairstyle, here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent your scalp from drying out.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup on your scalp and hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and use it to style your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your short haircut.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your short hair looking healthy and stylish.


In conclusion, there are various tips and steps to determine if you will suit short hair. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, researching short hairstyles, consulting with your hairstylist, trying on wigs or using virtual hairstyling tools, and following hair care tips, you can make an informed decision on whether short hair is right for you.
