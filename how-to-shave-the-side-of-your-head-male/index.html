How To Shave The Side Of Your Head Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Shaving the side of your head has become a popular trend among men over the years. It's a bold statement that can make you stand out from the crowd. However, if you're new to this trend, you might be wondering how to shave the side of your head male. In this article, we will guide you through the process with easy steps and tips to ensure that you get the perfect shave.

How to Shave the Side of Your Head Male: Step by Step Guide

Before we start, here are some things you will need:

  • Electric trimmer or clippers
  • Scissors
  • Mirror
  • Towel
  • Shaving cream
  • Razor
  • A friend or family member to help you

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

Start by trimming your hair with an electric trimmer or clippers. Use a shorter guard to trim the sides of your head. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down until you get the desired length. Remember, you can always trim more, but you can't add hair back.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length. You can leave it long, or you can go for a shorter style if you prefer. This step is optional, but it will give you a cleaner look.

Step 3: Apply Shaving Cream

Apply shaving cream to the area you want to shave. Make sure the cream covers the entire area you want to shave. This will help to prevent razor burn and irritation.

Step 4: Shave the Side of Your Head

Use a razor to shave the side of your head in the direction of hair growth. Be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this can cause razor burn or cuts. Rinse the razor frequently to remove hair and shaving cream.

Step 5: Rinse and Dry

Once you've finished shaving, rinse the area with water to remove any remaining shaving cream. Then, pat the area dry with a towel.

Tips for Shaving the Side of Your Head Male

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect shave:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.
  • Use a mirror to help you see what you're doing.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • If you're not sure how short to go, start with a longer guard and work your way down.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you if you're having trouble with the back of your head.
  • Be careful not to apply too much pressure with the razor, as this can cause razor burn or cuts.

FAQs

Is it easy to shave the side of your head male?

Shaving the side of your head male can be easy if you follow the proper steps and use the right tools. It may take some practice, but with patience and care, you can achieve the perfect shave.

Will shaving the side of my head male damage my hair?

If done correctly, shaving the side of your head male should not damage your hair. However, if you're not careful, you could cause razor burn or cuts, which can damage your skin.

How often should I shave the side of my head male?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. If you prefer a shorter style, you may need to shave the side of your head every few days. If you want to keep it longer, you can wait a week or two between shaves.

Hair Care after Shaving the Side of Your Head Male

After shaving the side of your head male, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and promote growth. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp with a scalp oil or serum.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.

Now that you know how to shave the side of your head male, you can rock this bold and trendy look with confidence. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the proper steps to achieve the perfect shave.
