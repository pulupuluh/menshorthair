How To Shorten A Long Face With Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 04, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have a long face and want to know how to make it appear shorter? One of the easiest ways to do so is by choosing the right hairstyle. Here are some tips and tricks on how to shorten a long face with hair:

Table of Contents

  • How to Shorten a Long Face with Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Shorten a Long Face with Hair

The key to shortening a long face with hair is by creating width around the face. This can be achieved through adding layers, bangs, or waves to your hair. Here are some hairstyles that can help:

1. Bob Haircut

A chin-length bob with layers can help create width and volume around the face. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that is still stylish.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can also help shorten the appearance of a long face. This style is great for those who want a bold and edgy look.

3. Shoulder-Length Hair

Hair that falls just below the shoulders can also help create width around the face. Adding layers and waves can further enhance this effect.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a hairstyle that shortens the appearance of a long face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it’s important to determine your face shape. A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow width.

Step 2: Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and features. They can also offer advice on the best products and tools to use.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face such as a bob, pixie cut, or shoulder-length hair. Adding layers, waves, or bangs can further enhance this effect.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using the right tools and products such as a curling iron, hair dryer, or mousse. Follow the instructions carefully and don’t be afraid to experiment.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you shorten the appearance of a long face:

  • Add volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your features
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, straight, or flat as they can make your face appear even longer. Instead, choose a hairstyle that adds width and volume around the face.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A high ponytail can make your face appear longer, but a low ponytail or messy bun can help create width around the face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too many heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily shorten the appearance of a long face with hair. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and use the right products and tools to keep your hair looking its best.
