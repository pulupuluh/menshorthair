How To Trim Curly Hair With Scissors

Written by Oliver Mar 12, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right techniques and tools, you can keep your curls looking healthy and vibrant. One of the most important aspects of curly hair care is trimming. In this article, we’ll show you how to trim curly hair with scissors, step by step.

Description

Trimming curly hair is essential to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends. Regular trims can also help to maintain your curls’ shape and prevent frizz. However, cutting curly hair can be tricky, especially if you’re doing it yourself. Using the wrong technique or tools can lead to uneven or damaged hair.

How to Trim Curly Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start trimming your curly hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry completely. This will make it easier to see the shape of your curls and ensure that you’re cutting the right amount.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up, gently pulling apart any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and prevent snagging.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using clips or hair ties, divide your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair in Small Sections

Using sharp scissors, cut your hair in small sections. Start with the ends and work your way up, cutting off 1/4 to 1/2 inch at a time. This will prevent you from cutting off too much hair at once and ensure that your curls are even.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After you’ve cut each section, check for evenness by running your fingers through your hair. Make sure that the length is consistent and that there are no uneven sections.

Step 6: Trim Any Flyaways

If you notice any flyaway hairs or split ends, trim them off using small scissors. Make sure to only cut off the damaged hair and not any healthy hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trimming curly hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent snagging and uneven cuts
  • Never cut your hair when it’s wet, as this can lead to uneven cuts when it dries
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once – it’s better to cut small sections and check for evenness as you go
  • If you’re not sure how much to cut, start with a small amount and work your way up
  • When in doubt, seek the advice of a professional stylist

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming curly hair with scissors:

Do I have to trim my curly hair?

Yes, trimming curly hair is essential to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends. Regular trims can also help to maintain your curls’ shape and prevent frizz.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I trim my curly hair myself?

Yes, you can trim your curly hair yourself, but it’s important to use the right technique and tools. If you’re not confident in your abilities, it’s best to seek the advice of a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Trimming your curly hair with scissors is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to prevent frizz and maintain your curls’ shape
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and maintain your curls’ shape

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and vibrant. Remember to trim your hair regularly, and don’t be afraid to seek the advice of a professional stylist if you need it.
