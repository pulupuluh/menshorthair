Pixie Haircuts Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 60

Written by Bardi May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 60 and have thick hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Pixie haircuts have become increasingly popular among women of all ages, but they're especially great for those with thick hair who want a low-maintenance, stylish look. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about pixie haircuts short hairstyles for thick hair over 60, including how to style them, care for them, and more.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Pixie haircuts are a short, cropped hairstyle that is usually around 2 to 3 inches in length. They're versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to textured and messy. For women over 60 with thick hair, a pixie cut can help to reduce the weight and volume of the hair, making it more manageable and easier to style.

How to

To get the perfect pixie haircut for your thick hair, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced with short hairstyles. You'll also want to bring in pictures of the style you're looking for to ensure that you and your stylist are on the same page. Once you have your pixie cut, you can experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie haircut short hairstyle for thick hair over 60:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using your hands to shape and style it as you go.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Apply a finishing product, such as hairspray, to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your pixie haircut short hairstyle for thick hair over 60:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as finger-styling or using a round brush, to create different looks.
  • Add some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth to your style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie haircuts short hairstyles for thick hair over 60:

Q: Will a pixie cut look good on me if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, a pixie cut is a great option for women with thick hair. It can help to reduce the weight and volume of your hair, making it more manageable and easier to style.

Q: How do I know if a pixie cut is right for me?

A: A pixie cut can work for women of all ages and face shapes, but it's important to find a style that suits your personal style and preferences. You can talk to your stylist to get their opinion on what will work best for you.

Q: How do I care for my pixie cut?

A: To care for your pixie cut, you'll want to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for your pixie haircut short hairstyle for thick hair over 60:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance pixie haircut short hairstyle for thick hair over 60. Remember to experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try new things!
