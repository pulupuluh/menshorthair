How To Layer Your Own Medium Hair At Home

Written by Eveline Mar 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layering your hair is an excellent way to add volume and depth to your look. However, going to a professional stylist can be expensive. With a little bit of practice and patience, you can layer your own medium hair at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process step-by-step.

How to Layer Your Own Medium Hair at Home

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin layering your hair, you need to prepare it. Start by washing your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, and then brush it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into four parts. Divide your hair down the middle, from your forehead to the nape of your neck, and then divide each half into two equal sections.

Step 3: Choose the Length of Your Layers

The next step is to decide on the length of your layers. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of each section and hold it between your fingers. Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Rest of Your Hair

After you have cut the first section of hair, use it as a guide to cut the rest of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the next section, and use the cut hair as a guide to cut it to the same length. Repeat this process for all four sections of hair.

Step 5: Blend Your Layers

Once you have cut all four sections of hair, it's time to blend your layers. Comb your hair straight down, and then use the scissors to cut into the ends of your hair. This will blend your layers and create a soft, natural look.

Tips for Layering Your Own Medium Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when layering your own medium hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Brush your hair thoroughly before cutting to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ About Layering Your Own Medium Hair at Home

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering your own medium hair at home:

Can I layer my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry, so you can see the shape of the curls and avoid cutting too much.

Can I layer my own hair if it's thin?

Yes, you can. Layering your hair can actually make it appear thicker and more voluminous.

How often should I layer my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should layer your hair every 6-12 weeks.

Hair Care After Layering Your Own Medium Hair at Home

After you have layered your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to maintain your layers and avoid split ends.

Layering your own medium hair at home is a great way to save money and achieve a fresh, new look. With these tips and instructions, you can do it yourself and feel confident in the results. Happy styling!
