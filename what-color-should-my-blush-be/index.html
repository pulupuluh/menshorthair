What Color Should My Blush Be

Written by Oliver Jun 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the right blush color can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to makeup. With so many different shades available, it can be difficult to know which one will look best on your skin. In this blog post, we'll explore what color your blush should be and give you some tips on how to choose the perfect shade.


Description

Blush is a makeup product that adds color to your cheeks, giving you a healthy, youthful glow. It's an essential part of any makeup routine and can be used to contour and highlight your face. Choosing the right shade of blush is important because it can either enhance your natural features or make you look overly made-up.


How to Choose the Right Blush Color

Choosing the right blush color depends on your skin tone and the look you're going for. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect blush shade:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right blush color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear both blue and green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Choose a Blush Color

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a blush color that complements it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Cool skin tones look best with pink or berry shades.
  • Warm skin tones look best with peach or coral shades.
  • Neutral skin tones can wear a wide range of shades, including pink, peach, and coral.

Step 3: Consider Your Makeup Look

In addition to your skin tone, you should also consider the makeup look you're going for. If you're going for a natural, no-makeup look, choose a blush color that's close to your natural flush. If you're going for a more dramatic look, you can choose a brighter or darker shade.

Tips for Applying Blush

Now that you've chosen the perfect blush color, here are some tips for applying it:

  • Use a light hand and build up the color gradually.
  • Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend upwards towards your temples.
  • Use a clean brush or sponge to blend the blush for a natural look.

FAQ

What if I have oily skin?

If you have oily skin, look for a powder blush instead of a cream or liquid formula. Powder blushes are less likely to slide off your skin and will last longer.

Can I wear blush if I have acne?

Yes, you can still wear blush if you have acne. Just be sure to apply it lightly and avoid any areas with active breakouts.

Hair Care

When choosing a blush color, it's important to consider your hair color as well. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have blonde hair, choose a light pink or peach blush.
  • If you have red hair, choose a coral or berry blush.
  • If you have dark hair, choose a deeper shade of pink or peach.

By following these tips, you can choose the perfect blush color for your skin tone and hair color. Remember to apply it lightly and blend well for a natural, youthful glow.
