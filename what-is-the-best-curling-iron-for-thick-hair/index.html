Best Curling Iron For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Mar 14, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right styling tools that can handle your locks. Curling irons, in particular, can be a minefield, with so many options out there. But fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing what is the best curling iron for thick hair, and provide you with tips and tricks on how to get perfect, long-lasting curls.

Why You Need a Curling Iron for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be challenging to style, especially if you’re aiming for curls. A curling iron can be a lifesaver, as it can help you achieve the curls you desire without having to spend hours styling your hair. It’s also an excellent option if you want to switch up your look and add some volume and texture to your locks.

How to Choose the Best Curling Iron for Thick Hair

When it comes to choosing the best curling iron for thick hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to look for a curling iron with a high heat setting, as thick hair requires higher temperatures to ensure that the curls hold. Secondly, consider the barrel size. For thick hair, you’ll want a slightly larger barrel size, as this will help create looser, more natural-looking curls. Finally, look for a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline coating, as these materials help distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for, here’s a step by step guide on how to use a curling iron for thick hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, you want to ensure that your hair is prepped and ready. This means washing and conditioning your hair, and then applying a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. You can also add a volumizing mousse to add some extra body.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you want to section your hair. Divide your hair into two-inch sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it in place for a few seconds. Release the curl and move on to the next section, working your way around your head.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve curled your entire head, finish off with a light mist of hairspray to ensure that your curls hold all day.

Tips for Curling Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for curling thick hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Don’t curl your hair when it’s wet
  • Wrap your hair around the curling iron, rather than twisting it
  • Use a slightly larger barrel for looser, more natural-looking curls
  • Work in small sections for better results

FAQ

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling out?

A: Use a high heat setting and a hairspray to ensure that your curls hold. You can also try curling your hair in smaller sections.

Q: Can I curl my hair when it’s wet?

A: No, you should never curl your hair when it’s wet. This can damage your hair and cause it to break.

Q: Should I use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline coating?

A: Yes, a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline coating will help distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

When it comes to caring for thick hair, here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to add moisture
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair

Now that you know what is the best curling iron for thick hair, and how to use it, it’s time to get styling! Remember to take care of your hair, and it will reward you with beautiful, healthy locks.
