Best Swimwear For Big Busts Uk Tips How To And Top Picks

Written by Daniel May 05, 2023 · 10 min read

Finding the best swimwear for big busts can be a daunting task, especially in the UK where options are limited. But worry not, as we’ve got you covered with this guide to help you find the perfect swimsuit that fits and flatters your curves. In this article, we’ll give you tips on how to choose the right swimwear, step-by-step guide on how to measure yourself, and the best swimwear options for big busts in the UK.

How to Choose the Right Swimwear

Before we dive into the best swimwear options for big busts, let’s talk about how to choose the right swimwear that suits your body type. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip #1: Look for Support

When shopping for swimwear, look for styles that offer support to your bust. This means choosing swimsuits with underwire, thick straps, or built-in cups. Avoid styles with thin straps or no support as they won’t provide enough support for your bust.

Tip #2: Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric of your swimsuit can also affect how well it supports your bust. Opt for fabrics with more structure and thickness such as neoprene, scuba, or textured materials. These fabrics can help hold your bust in place and prevent any unwanted movement.

Tip #3: Find the Right Fit

Finding the right fit is crucial when it comes to choosing swimwear for big busts. Make sure the swimsuit fits properly and doesn’t dig into your skin or cause any spillage. If you’re unsure about your size, take your measurements and refer to the size chart of the brand you’re purchasing from.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Measure Yourself

Measuring yourself is essential to finding the right swimwear size. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you measure yourself properly:

Step 1: Measure Your Band Size

Take a measuring tape and wrap it around your ribcage, just below your bust. Make sure the tape is snug but not too tight. This measurement is your band size.

Step 2: Measure Your Bust Size

Wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your bust. Make sure the tape is parallel to the ground and not too tight or too loose. This measurement is your bust size.

Step 3: Calculate Your Cup Size

Subtract your band size from your bust size. The difference between the two measurements will give you your cup size. For example, if your band size is 34 inches and your bust size is 40 inches, the difference is 6 inches. This means your cup size is a D.

Best Swimwear Options for Big Busts in the UK

Now that you know how to choose the right swimwear and how to measure yourself, it’s time to explore the best swimwear options for big busts in the UK. Here are our top picks:

Option #1: Underwire Swimsuits

Underwire swimsuits are a great option for big busts as they provide support and lift. Look for styles with adjustable straps and thick underwire for maximum support.

Option #2: Halterneck Swimsuits

Halterneck swimsuits are another great option for big busts as they provide support and lift to your bust. Look for styles with thicker straps and adjustable ties at the neck and back for a customized fit.

Option #3: High-Neck Swimsuits

High-neck swimsuits are a trendy option for big busts as they provide coverage and support. Look for styles with built-in cups or underwire for added support.

Option #4: Bikinis with Wide Straps

Bikinis with wide straps are a great option for big busts as they provide support and distribute the weight of your bust evenly. Look for styles with thicker straps and adjustable ties for a customized fit.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Swimwear

Taking care of your swimwear is important to ensure it lasts longer and stays in good condition. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip #1: Rinse Your Swimwear

After wearing your swimwear, rinse it with cold water to remove any chlorine or saltwater. This will help prevent discoloration and damage to the fabric.

Tip #2: Hand-Wash Your Swimwear

Hand-washing your swimwear is the best way to ensure it stays in good condition. Use a mild detergent and gently wash your swimsuit in cold water. Avoid wringing or twisting your swimwear as it can damage the fabric.

Tip #3: Air-Dry Your Swimwear

Avoid using a dryer to dry your swimwear as it can damage the fabric and cause shrinkage. Instead, lay your swimwear flat on a towel and let it air-dry in a shaded area.

FAQ

Q: What swimwear styles should I avoid if I have a big bust?

A: Avoid styles with thin straps, no support, or low-cut tops as they won’t provide enough support for your bust.

Q: Can I wear a bikini if I have a big bust?

A: Yes, you can wear a bikini if you have a big bust. Look for styles with wide straps, built-in cups, or underwire for support.

Q: How do I know if my swimwear fits properly?

A: Your swimwear should fit snugly but not too tight. It should cover your bust properly and not cause any spillage.

Q: Can I wear a strapless swimsuit if I have a big bust?

A: It’s not recommended to wear a strapless swimsuit if you have a big bust as it won’t provide enough support.

Finding the best swimwear for big busts in the UK doesn’t have to be a struggle. Follow these tips, measure yourself properly, and choose from our top picks to find the perfect swimsuit that fits and flatters your curves.
