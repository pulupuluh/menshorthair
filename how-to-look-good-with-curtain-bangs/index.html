How To Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Christine Aug 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle trend for a while now, and for good reason. They can frame your face beautifully, add volume to your hair, and give you a chic and effortless look. However, not everyone knows how to style curtain bangs to make them look good. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks to make sure you rock your curtain bangs and look your best.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle and hang down on either side of your face, framing it like a curtain. They can be long or short, depending on your preference, and can be styled in different ways to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Styling curtain bangs can be tricky, but with a few simple steps, you can achieve a look that suits you:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your curtain bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can blow-dry it or let it air-dry, depending on your preference. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it before styling your bangs.

Step 2: Part your hair in the middle

The key to getting the perfect curtain bangs is to part your hair in the middle. Use a comb to create a straight line from the middle of your forehead to the back of your head, and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Trim your bangs

If you're starting with long bangs, you may need to trim them to get the right length. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle and working your way to the sides. Be careful not to cut them too short.

Step 4: Style your bangs

Once your bangs are the right length, you can style them in different ways depending on your preference. You can blow-dry them with a round brush to add volume, or use a straightener to make them sleek and straight. You can also curl them with a curling iron for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Looking Good with Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you look good with curtain bangs:

Tip 1: Find the right length

The length of your curtain bangs can make a huge difference in how they look. If you have a small forehead, you may want to go for shorter bangs that start just above your eyebrows. If you have a larger forehead, longer bangs may be more flattering.

Tip 2: Consider your face shape

The shape of your face can also play a role in how your curtain bangs look. If you have a round face, longer bangs that are parted in the middle can help elongate your face. If you have a square jawline, shorter bangs that are swept to the side can soften your features.

Tip 3: Use the right products

To keep your curtain bangs looking good, you may need to use some hair products. A volumizing mousse can help add volume to your bangs, while a hairspray can keep them in place. You may also want to use a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh between washes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. On average, you may need to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs without using heat?

A: Yes, you can air-dry your bangs and use a hairbrush to style them. You can also use hair rollers to add volume without using heat.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking good, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat too often

Using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too often can damage your hair. Try to limit their use and use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Make sure to trim your curtain bangs regularly to keep them looking good.

Conclusion

Curtain bangs can be a great hairstyle choice if you know how to style them right. By following the tips and tricks in this article, you can achieve a chic and effortless look that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
