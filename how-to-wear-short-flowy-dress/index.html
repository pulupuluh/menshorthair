How To Wear A Short Flowy Dress Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Short flowy dresses are a summer staple for many, but they can be tricky to style. Whether you’re going to a picnic in the park or a night out with friends, there are plenty of ways to wear a short flowy dress and look your best. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks for styling a short flowy dress, answering frequently asked questions, and even touching on hair care. So, let’s get started!

How to Wear a Short Flowy Dress

Choosing the right short flowy dress for your body type is crucial. Opt for a dress that flatters your figure and highlights your best features. Here are some tips on how to wear a short flowy dress:

1. Accessorize

Accessorizing is key when it comes to styling a short flowy dress. You can add a belt to cinch in your waist, wear statement earrings or a necklace, and even add a hat or sunglasses to complete your look. Just be sure not to overdo it – you don’t want to take away from the dress itself.

2. Choose the Right Shoes

When it comes to shoes, you have a lot of options. You can opt for sandals, sneakers, or even heels. The key is to choose a pair of shoes that complements the dress and makes you feel comfortable.

3. Layer Up

If you’re not comfortable showing too much skin, you can layer up with a denim jacket or cardigan. This is a great way to add some dimension to your outfit and make it more versatile.

4. Play with Patterns

Don’t be afraid to play with patterns. Floral, polka dots, and stripes are all great options when it comes to short flowy dresses. Just be sure to choose a pattern that flatters your body type.

5. Mix and Match

One of the great things about short flowy dresses is that they can be mixed and matched with other pieces in your wardrobe. Try wearing a dress with a denim skirt or shorts over it, or even layering a t-shirt underneath for a more casual look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to wear a short flowy dress:

Step 1: Choose the Right Dress

Choose a dress that flatters your body type and highlights your best features.

Step 2: Accessorize

Add accessories like a belt, statement jewelry, and sunglasses to complete your look.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shoes

Choose a pair of shoes that complement the dress and make you feel comfortable.

Step 4: Layer Up

If you’re not comfortable showing too much skin, layer up with a denim jacket or cardigan.

Step 5: Mix and Match

Get creative and mix and match your short flowy dress with other pieces in your wardrobe for a more versatile look.

Tips for Wearing a Short Flowy Dress

Here are some additional tips for wearing a short flowy dress:

1. Choose the Right Undergarments

Wear undergarments that won’t show through your dress, like seamless underwear or a slip.

2. Watch the Length

Make sure your dress isn’t too short, as this can be inappropriate for certain occasions.

3. Consider the Occasion

Think about where you’ll be wearing your dress and dress accordingly. A short flowy dress might not be appropriate for a formal event.

4. Be Confident

Confidence is key when it comes to wearing a short flowy dress. Wear what makes you feel comfortable and confident.

FAQs

What is a Short Flowy Dress?

A short flowy dress is a dress that is short in length and features a loose, flowing silhouette.

What Shoes Should I Wear with a Short Flowy Dress?

You can wear sandals, sneakers, or even heels with a short flowy dress. The key is to choose a pair of shoes that complements the dress and makes you feel comfortable.

Can I Wear a Short Flowy Dress to a Formal Event?

A short flowy dress might not be appropriate for a formal event. It’s best to opt for a longer, more formal dress for these occasions.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a short flowy dress, your hair can make or break your look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

1. Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking clean and healthy.

2. Use Hair Products

Use hair products like hairspray or mousse to keep your hair in place.

3. Accessorize Your Hair

Accessorize your hair with headbands, hair clips, or even a hat to complete your look.

Now that you know how to wear a short flowy dress, it’s time to start experimenting with different styles and accessories. Remember to choose a dress that flatters your body type, accessorize your look, and have fun with your outfit. Whether you’re going to a casual brunch or a night out on the town, a short flowy dress is the perfect addition to any summer wardrobe.
