What Are Medium Sizes A Guide To Understanding Hair Length

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Medium sized hair is a popular choice for those looking for a versatile and manageable length. But what exactly does medium size mean when it comes to hair? In this article, we will break down what medium size hair is, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

What are Medium Sizes?

Medium size hair typically falls between the chin and shoulders, with the length varying depending on personal preference. It is a popular choice because it is not too short or too long, making it easy to style and maintain.

Achieving medium size hair can be done through a variety of methods, including growing out shorter hair, cutting longer hair, or using hair extensions. It is important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best method for achieving your desired length.

How to Achieve Medium Size Hair

There are a few key methods for achieving medium size hair:

  • Growing out shorter hair: If you currently have shorter hair, you can gradually grow it out to achieve medium size hair.
  • Cutting longer hair: If you have longer hair, you can cut it to the desired length to achieve medium size hair.
  • Using hair extensions: If you are unable to grow or cut your hair to the desired length, you can use hair extensions to achieve medium size hair.

It is important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best method for achieving your desired length.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Size Hair

Maintaining medium size hair can be done with a few key tips:

  • Regular trims: It is important to get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Proper hair care: Use products that are suited for your hair type and avoid over-styling with heat tools.
  • Experiment with styles: Medium size hair is versatile, so have fun experimenting with different styles and looks.

FAQ about Medium Size Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium size hair:

How long does medium size hair take to grow?

The rate at which hair grows varies from person to person, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month.

Can I style medium size hair in different ways?

Yes, medium size hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos, braids, and curls.

Is medium size hair easy to maintain?

Yes, medium size hair is generally easy to maintain and style. However, it is important to use proper hair care and get regular trims to keep it healthy and manageable.

Hair Care for Medium Size Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and manageable medium size hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools, and use a heat protectant spray when using them.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and looks to keep your hair fresh and fun.

In conclusion, medium size hair is a versatile and manageable length that can be achieved through a variety of methods. With proper hair care and maintenance, you can keep your medium size hair healthy and stylish.
