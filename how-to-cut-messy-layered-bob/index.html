How To Cut Messy Layered Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and looking for a change? A messy layered bob could be the perfect solution for you. This hairstyle is chic, trendy, and easy to style. However, getting the perfect messy layered bob cut can be tricky. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to cut messy layered bob in relaxed English language. We will cover everything from how to prepare your hair to how to style it.

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's essential to prepare it properly to ensure that you get the best results. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Your hair should be damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Next, comb your hair to remove any tangles. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by dividing it into four equal parts: top, sides, and back. You can use hair clips to secure each section.

How to Cut Messy Layered Bob

Now that you have prepared your hair, it's time to cut your messy layered bob. Here's how:

Step 1: Cut the Back

Start by cutting the back of your hair. Take a section of hair from the nape of your neck and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest point and gradually getting longer. Repeat this process for each section until you have cut the entire back of your hair.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of your hair. Take a section of hair from the front and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest point and gradually getting longer. Repeat this process for each section until you have cut both sides of your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Finally, cut the top of your hair. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest point and gradually getting longer. Repeat this process for each section until you have cut the entire top of your hair.

Tips for Cutting Messy Layered Bob

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your messy layered bob:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and gradually work your way up to larger sections.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry to get a more accurate length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Make sure you cut both sides of your hair evenly to avoid any asymmetry.

FAQ

How often should I cut my messy layered bob?

You should cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain your messy layered bob's shape and length.

Can I cut my hair myself or should I go to a salon?

You can cut your hair yourself if you feel confident and have experience cutting hair. However, if you're unsure, it's best to go to a salon and get it done by a professional.

How should I style my messy layered bob?

You can style your messy layered bob by using a curling iron or a straightener to create loose waves or straighten your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add texture and volume.

Hair Care

After cutting your messy layered bob, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect messy layered bob cut at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to get the best results.
