How To Brush Long Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful, it can be a nightmare to brush and maintain. Brushing your hair regularly is important to keep it healthy and shiny. However, if you have long thick hair, you may find it challenging to brush it properly. In this article, we have shared some tips and tricks on how to brush long thick hair easily.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Brush Long Thick Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Brushing Long Thick Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair

How to Brush Long Thick Hair

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let's start with the basics. Here's how you should be brushing your long thick hair:

  1. Start by detangling your hair with a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush.
  2. Divide your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Hold the section you're working on with one hand and brush it with the other hand.
  4. Brush your hair from the ends to the roots to avoid breakage.
  5. Repeat with each section until you've brushed your entire head.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let's dive into a step-by-step guide on how to brush long thick hair:

  1. Start with dry hair. Brushing wet hair can cause breakage and damage.
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.
  3. Divide your hair into sections. Use hair clips or hair ties to keep them in place.
  4. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. Hold the section you're working on with one hand and brush it with the other hand.
  5. Brush your hair from the ends to the roots. This will prevent breakage and damage.
  6. Repeat with each section until you've brushed your entire head.
  7. Finish with a boar bristle brush. This will distribute your scalp's natural oils and make your hair shiny.

Tips for Brushing Long Thick Hair

Brushing long thick hair can be a challenge, but these tips will make it easier:

  • Use a detangling brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before brushing.
  • Divide your hair into manageable sections to make brushing easier.
  • Hold each section you're working on with one hand and brush it with the other hand.
  • Brush your hair from the ends to the roots to prevent breakage.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute your scalp's natural oils and make your hair shiny.
  • Don't brush your hair too often. Overbrushing can cause breakage and damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about brushing long thick hair:

How often should I brush my long thick hair?

You should brush your hair every day, but avoid overbrushing, as it can cause breakage and damage.

What kind of brush should I use for long thick hair?

Use a boar bristle brush to distribute your scalp's natural oils and make your hair shiny.

Should I brush my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to brush your hair when it's dry, as brushing wet hair can cause breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep your long thick hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Brushing long thick hair can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you can make it easier and keep your hair healthy and shiny. Remember to use a detangling brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before brushing, divide your hair into manageable sections, and brush your hair from the ends to the roots to prevent breakage. Happy brushing!
