How To Know Your Hairstyle Male A Guide For Men In 2023

Knowing your hairstyle as a man is crucial in defining your personal style and creating a lasting impression. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to determine which style is right for you. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to discovering your perfect hairstyle as a male.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Ideal Style
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding your perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. The shape of your face will play a significant role in which styles will suit you best. Here are the five main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face straight on. Next, trace the outline of your face onto the photo and compare it to the above shapes. You can also ask a hairstylist for their opinion.

Face Shape Hairstyle Recommendations

  • Oval: Most hairstyles will suit an oval face, but avoid styles that cover your forehead.
  • Square: Short, textured styles or longer, layered styles work well for square faces. Avoid styles that are too short or too long.
  • Round: Longer, textured styles or styles with height on top are best for round faces. Avoid styles that are too short or too round.
  • Heart: Short, textured styles or longer, layered styles with bangs work well for heart-shaped faces. Avoid styles that are too full on top.
  • Diamond: Short, textured styles or styles with height on top are best for diamond faces. Avoid styles that are too full on top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Ideal Style

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find your ideal style.

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky/coily. Determine your hair type by looking at the natural shape of your hair when it's dry.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your hairstyle should fit your lifestyle. If you have a job that requires a more professional look, consider a shorter, more polished style. If you have an active lifestyle, shorter, low-maintenance styles may be better suited for you.

Step 3: Browse Hairstyle Options

Browse through hairstyle options online or in magazines to get an idea of what styles you like. Consider the length, texture, and shape of the style.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can give you expert advice on which styles will suit you best based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Once you've found your ideal style, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your style.
  • Use styling products, such as pomade or gel, to keep your hair in place.

FAQs

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist about making changes. They may be able to adjust the style to better suit your preferences.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your style.

Hair Care Tips for Men

In addition to maintaining your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair overall. Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, which is essential for healthy hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to determine your hairstyle as a male, it's time to experiment and find your perfect look. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and maintain your hair properly to keep your style looking fresh and polished.
