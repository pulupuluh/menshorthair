Best Braid Styles For Big Foreheads A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 04, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a big forehead, you may feel self-conscious about it. However, there are ways to style your hair to make it look more proportional. One of the best ways to do this is by getting braids. Braids not only look chic and stylish, but they also help to frame your face and draw attention away from your forehead. In this article, we will be discussing the best braid styles for big foreheads.

How to Choose the Right Braid Style for Your Big Forehead

When it comes to choosing the right braid style for your big forehead, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, you want to choose a style that will flatter your face shape. Secondly, you want to choose a style that will suit your hair type and texture. Lastly, you want to choose a style that you are comfortable with and that you can maintain.

Face Shape

If you have a big forehead, you may want to choose a braid style that will help to balance out your face shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to choose a style that has braids that start at the crown of your head and cascade down the sides of your face. This will help to create balance and make your forehead look smaller.

Hair Type and Texture

When it comes to choosing the right braid style for your big forehead, you also want to consider your hair type and texture. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a style that has smaller braids so that your hair looks fuller. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that has larger braids so that your hair doesn't look too bulky.

Best Braid Styles for Big Foreheads

Box Braids

Box braids are a classic braid style that looks great on everyone. They are versatile, low-maintenance, and can be styled in many different ways. Box braids are also great for big foreheads because they help to frame the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

Cornrows

Cornrows are another classic braid style that looks great on big foreheads. They are easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. Cornrows are also great for big foreheads because they help to frame the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

Fulani Braids

Fulani braids are a popular braid style that originated in West Africa. They are characterized by their thin braids and unique parting style. Fulani braids are great for big foreheads because they help to frame the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

Halo Braid

The halo braid is a beautiful braid style that wraps around the head like a halo. It's a great style for big foreheads because it helps to frame the face and draw attention away from the forehead. It's also a great style for formal occasions.

How to Style and Maintain Your Braids

Once you have chosen the right braid style for your big forehead, it's important to know how to style and maintain your braids. Here are some tips to help you:

Styling

You can style your braids in many different ways. You can wear them down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. You can also add accessories like beads or rings to your braids to make them look more stylish.

Maintenance

To maintain your braids, you should wash your hair regularly and keep your scalp moisturized. You should also avoid sleeping with wet hair and pulling your braids too tight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get braids if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can get braids if you have thin hair. However, you may want to choose a style that has smaller braids so that your hair looks fuller.

How long do braids last?

Braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks depending on how well you maintain them.

Can I swim with braids?

Yes, you can swim with braids. However, you should avoid exposing your braids to chlorine and saltwater for extended periods of time.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy while wearing braids, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Keep your scalp moisturized
  • Avoid pulling your braids too tight
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many different braid styles that look great on big foreheads. When choosing the right style for you, consider your face shape, hair type and texture, and personal preferences. With the right style and proper maintenance, your braids will look beautiful and stylish.
