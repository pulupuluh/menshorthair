Do Bangs Make Your Face Smaller

Description

Many people wonder if bangs can make their face look smaller. The answer is yes, bangs can help to make your face appear smaller by covering up your forehead and creating a more balanced look. However, not all types of bangs will work for every face shape.

How To

If you want to try bangs to make your face look smaller, here are some steps to follow:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing the type of bangs to get, it's important to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Each face shape requires a different type of bangs to create a balanced look.

2. Choose the Right Type of Bangs

Depending on your face shape, you may want to choose one of the following types of bangs:

  • Round face: Side-swept bangs or long, layered bangs can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.
  • Square face: Soft, wispy bangs can help to soften the angles of a square face and create a more feminine look.
  • Oval face: Almost any type of bangs can work with an oval face, but blunt bangs or side-swept bangs can help to highlight your facial features.
  • Heart-shaped face: Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a heart-shaped face by drawing attention away from the forehead and creating a more balanced look.

3. Consult with a Hairstylist

Before getting bangs, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and provide advice on how to style and maintain your new look.

Step By Step

If you've decided to get bangs to make your face look smaller, here are some steps to follow:

1. Schedule an Appointment with a Hairstylist

Find a hairstylist that you trust and schedule an appointment for a consultation. They can help you determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and provide advice on how to style and maintain your new look.

2. Choose the Right Type of Bangs

Based on your face shape, choose the type of bangs that will work best for you. Be sure to consider the length, thickness, and texture of your hair when making your decision.

3. Get Your Bangs Cut

Once you've chosen the type of bangs you want, it's time to get them cut. Work with your hairstylist to create the perfect length and shape for your face shape.

4. Style Your Bangs

Once your bangs have been cut, you'll need to style them on a daily basis. Depending on the type of bangs you have, you may need to blow-dry them, straighten them, or use a curling iron to create the perfect look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting bangs to make your face look smaller:

  • Choose the right type of bangs for your face shape
  • Consult with a hairstylist before getting bangs
  • Be prepared to style your bangs on a daily basis
  • Consider the length, thickness, and texture of your hair when choosing bangs
  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to maintain their shape

FAQ

Q: Will bangs work for every face shape?

A: No, not all types of bangs will work for every face shape. It's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape to create a balanced look.

Q: Do bangs require a lot of maintenance?

A: Yes, bangs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. You'll need to style them on a daily basis and keep them trimmed regularly to maintain their shape.

Hair Care

When getting bangs to make your face look smaller, it's important to take good care of your hair. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly and use a heat protectant when styling your bangs with a blow dryer or other heat styling tool. Also, be sure to schedule regular haircuts to keep your bangs looking their best.
