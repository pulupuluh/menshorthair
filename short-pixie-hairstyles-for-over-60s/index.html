Short Pixie Hairstyles For Over 60S A Guide

Written by Christine Mar 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, easy to maintain, and suits your age? Look no further than short pixie hairstyles! Pixie cuts are versatile and can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair texture. In this article, we will guide you through some of the best short pixie hairstyles for over 60s, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

Why Choose Short Pixie Hairstyles?

Short pixie hairstyles are a popular choice for over 60s because they are low maintenance, easy to style, and can make you look younger. They are also versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. With a pixie cut, you can embrace your natural hair texture or add some volume and texture with products and styling tools.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut for You

Choosing the right pixie cut can be overwhelming, but don't worry, we've got you covered. Here are some factors to consider:

Face Shape:

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the most flattering pixie cut for you. If you have a round face, go for a longer pixie cut that elongates your face. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different pixie cuts. For a square face, a short textured pixie cut will soften your features.

Hair Texture:

Your hair texture also plays a role in determining the right pixie cut for you. If you have fine hair, go for a layered pixie cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a short textured pixie cut will help manage your hair.

How to Style Short Pixie Hairstyles

Styling short pixie hairstyles is easy and quick. Here's how:

Blow-dry:

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Add Texture:

Add texture to your hair using a sea salt spray or a texturizing spray. Apply the product to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a messy look.

Accessorize:

Add some accessories to your hair to make it look more stylish. You can use hairpins, headbands, or scarfs to add some personality to your look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Pixie Hairstyles

Short pixie hairstyles require minimal maintenance, but here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and stylish:

Trim Regularly:

Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Heat Styling:

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent heat damage to your hair.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair with a short pixie cut?

Yes, you can dye your hair with a short pixie cut. Consult a professional stylist to choose the right hair color for you.

Can I style my short pixie cut differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your short pixie cut differently for different occasions. Experiment with different accessories and styling products to create different looks.

How often should I wash my hair with a short pixie cut?

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Hairstyles

Short pixie hairstyles require minimal hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Use a Gentle Shampoo:

Use a gentle shampoo that is sulfate-free to prevent your hair from drying out.

Avoid Brushing Wet Hair:

Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Wrap Your Hair at Night:

Wrap your hair with a silk scarf at night to prevent your hair from tangling and breaking.

In conclusion, short pixie hairstyles are a trendy and low-maintenance option for over 60s. With the right cut and styling products, you can create a look that suits your face shape and hair texture. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and stylish.
