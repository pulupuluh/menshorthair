How To Wear Head Wraps With Short Hair

Written by Christine Apr 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Head wraps are a great accessory that can add a stylish touch to any outfit. They are versatile, easy to wear, and perfect for all hair types, including short hair. However, if you have short hair, you may find it challenging to wear a head wrap. In this article, we will discuss how to wear head wraps with short hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Head Wraps with Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Head Wraps with Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Wear Head Wraps with Short Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the types of head wraps that are suitable for short hair. For short hair, it's best to choose head wraps that are not too bulky or too long. Look for head wraps that are made of lightweight materials like cotton, silk or rayon. These materials will not weigh your hair down, and they are gentle on your hair and scalp.

Now, let's get started with the step-by-step guide to wearing head wraps with short hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before wearing a head wrap, make sure your hair is clean and free of tangles. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to clean and condition your hair. If your hair is prone to tangles, use a detangling spray or cream to make it easier to comb through.

Step 2: Choose the Right Head Wrap

Choose a head wrap that is the right size for your head. You don't want a head wrap that is too tight or too loose. If the head wrap is too tight, it will be uncomfortable to wear, and if it's too loose, it will fall off your head. Look for head wraps that have some elasticity to them so that they will fit snugly on your head.

Step 3: Fold the Head Wrap

Fold the head wrap in half, so it's a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be facing down, and the two corners should be at the back of your head. The center of the head wrap should be at the front of your head.

Step 4: Place the Head Wrap on Your Head

Place the center of the head wrap on your forehead, and bring the two corners to the back of your head. Tie the corners into a knot at the back of your head. Make sure the knot is not too tight, as this could be uncomfortable.

Step 5: Style the Head Wrap

You can style the head wrap in different ways, depending on your preference. You can leave the knot at the back of your head, or you can tuck it under the head wrap for a neater look. You can also pull the head wrap forward, so it covers your ears, or you can leave your ears exposed.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing head wraps with short hair:

  1. Start with clean hair.
  2. Choose the right head wrap.
  3. Fold the head wrap.
  4. Place the head wrap on your head.
  5. Style the head wrap.

Tips for Wearing Head Wraps with Short Hair

Here are some tips for wearing head wraps with short hair:

  • Choose head wraps that are made of lightweight materials.
  • Look for head wraps that have some elasticity to them.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the head wrap in place.
  • Accessorize your head wrap with earrings or a necklace.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a head wrap with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a head wrap with short hair. Just choose a head wrap that is the right size and made of lightweight materials.

Q: How do I keep the head wrap in place?

A: Use bobby pins to secure the head wrap in place. You can also tie the head wrap into a knot at the back of your head.

Q: Can I wear a head wrap to bed?

A: Yes, you can wear a head wrap to bed. A head wrap will protect your hair from breakage and keep it moisturized.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for wearing head wraps with short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to clean and condition your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair before wearing a head wrap.
  • Avoid wearing head wraps that are too tight, as this could cause hair breakage.
  • Take breaks from wearing head wraps to give your hair a break.

Wearing head wraps with short hair is a great way to add style and flair to your look. By following these simple steps and tips, you can wear head wraps with confidence and ease.
