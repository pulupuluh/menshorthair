How To See What You Would Look Like With Bangs Without Cutting Your Hair

Written by Petter Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but are hesitant to take the plunge and cut your hair? Don't worry, there's a way to see what you would look like with bangs without actually cutting your hair. In this article, we'll go over the steps you can take to see yourself with bangs and even provide some hair care tips if you do decide to go for the chop.

How to

The best way to see what you would look like with bangs is by using a virtual hairstyle tool. These tools allow you to upload a picture of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including different types of bangs. Here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Find a Virtual Hairstyle Tool

There are plenty of virtual hairstyle tools available online, so find one that you like. Some popular options include:

  • Virtual Hairstyles by TheHairStyler
  • Virtual Makeover by InStyle
  • Hairstyle Mirror by Modiface

Whichever tool you choose, make sure it allows you to try on different types of bangs.

Step 2: Upload a Picture of Yourself

Once you've found a virtual hairstyle tool, upload a clear picture of yourself. Make sure the picture is taken straight on and that your hair is pulled back so you can see your entire face.

Step 3: Try on Different Types of Bangs

Now it's time to try on different types of bangs. Most virtual hairstyle tools will have a section specifically for bangs. You can try on different lengths, thicknesses, and styles to see which bangs suit your face shape and features best.

Step 4: Save and Share

Once you've found a bangs style that you like, save the picture and share it with friends or family for their opinions. You can also take the picture to your hairstylist to show them exactly what you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying on virtual bangs:

  • Choose a virtual hairstyle tool that allows you to try on different types of bangs
  • Upload a clear picture of yourself with your hair pulled back
  • Try on different lengths, thicknesses, and styles to find the right bangs for your face shape
  • Save and share your favorite looks with others for their opinions
  • Show your hairstylist the picture of your favorite bangs style to ensure they understand exactly what you want

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trying on virtual bangs:

  • Do virtual hairstyle tools cost money? Most virtual hairstyle tools are free, but some may require a subscription or payment.
  • Can I use a virtual hairstyle tool on my phone? Yes, many virtual hairstyle tools have mobile apps that you can download.
  • Will the virtual bangs look exactly like real bangs? While virtual hairstyle tools can provide a good idea of what you would look like with bangs, they may not be 100% accurate.
  • Can I undo virtual bangs? Yes, most virtual hairstyle tools have an undo button so you can easily try on different styles.

Hair Care

If you do decide to get bangs, there are some hair care tips that you should keep in mind:

  • Wash and blow-dry your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to create volume and shape
  • Consider using dry shampoo to extend the time between washes
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long and obstructing your vision
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners on your bangs to prevent damage

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your bangs looking great and avoid any hair disasters.

So, now you know how to see what you would look like with bangs without actually cutting your hair. Use a virtual hairstyle tool to try on different styles, find the perfect bangs for your face shape, and follow our hair care tips to keep your bangs looking fabulous.
