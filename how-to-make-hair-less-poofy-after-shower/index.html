How To Make Hair Less Poofy After Shower

Written by Petter Aug 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with poofy hair after every shower? Don't worry, you're not alone. Poofy hair can be a real struggle to manage, but there are ways to tame it. In this article, we'll walk you through some tips and tricks to make your hair less poofy after a shower.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in taming poofy hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add moisture and calm frizz. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more poofy.

Use a leave-in conditioner

After you've washed your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair. This will help to add moisture to your hair and prevent it from becoming too dry and frizzy.

Use a wide-tooth comb

When you're ready to style your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and make your hair easier to manage.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can make your hair even more poofy and frizzy. Try to avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use a hot tool, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair less poofy after a shower:

  1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  4. Avoid heat styling
  5. Towel dry your hair gently
  6. Apply a smoothing serum
  7. Use a boar bristle brush to smooth your hair
  8. Finish with a light hairspray

Tips

  • Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and can help to reduce frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to add moisture to your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from hair products and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

How can I prevent my hair from becoming poofy in the first place?

To prevent poofy hair, try to avoid using hot tools as much as possible. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair. Towel dry your hair gently and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

Can I use a hair dryer to make my hair less poofy?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer to make your hair less poofy. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy, manageable, and less poofy after a shower.
