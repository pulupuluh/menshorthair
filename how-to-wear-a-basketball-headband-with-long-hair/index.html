How To Wear A Basketball Headband With Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a basketball player with long hair, you know how frustrating it can be to keep your hair out of your face during a game. That's where a basketball headband comes in handy. Not only does it keep your hair back, but it also helps to absorb sweat and keep it out of your eyes. However, wearing a headband with long hair can be tricky. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to wear a basketball headband with long hair.

How to Wear a Basketball Headband with Long Hair

There are a few different ways you can wear a basketball headband with long hair, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you put on your headband, it's important to make sure your hair is prepped properly. You don't want any loose strands or bumps that could get in the way. Here's what you can do:

  • Brush your hair to remove any tangles
  • Apply a bit of hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair in place
  • If you have very long hair, consider putting it up in a ponytail or bun

Step 2: Put on the Headband

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to put on the headband. Here's how:

  • Hold the headband at the back of your head, with the elastic band facing downwards
  • Pull it over your head and position it so that it sits snugly on your forehead
  • If you have bangs, you can tuck them under the headband or leave them out, depending on your preference
  • If your headband has a silicone strip on the inside, make sure it's facing towards your forehead for maximum grip

Step 3: Adjust as Needed

Once your headband is on, you can make any necessary adjustments. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • If the headband feels too tight, you can adjust it by pulling it down slightly
  • If the headband feels too loose, you can adjust it by pulling it up slightly
  • If you have any stray hairs that are getting in your face, you can use bobby pins or hair clips to keep them in place

Tips for Wearing a Basketball Headband with Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing a basketball headband with long hair:

  • Choose a headband that's made of moisture-wicking material to keep sweat out of your eyes
  • If you have very thick hair, look for a headband that's wider or has a stronger grip
  • Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you
  • Wash your headband regularly to prevent bacteria buildup

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a basketball headband with long hair:

Q: Can I wear a basketball headband with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a basketball headband with curly hair. Just make sure to prep your hair properly and adjust the headband as needed.

Q: How do I keep my headband from slipping during a game?

A: Look for a headband with a silicone strip on the inside, which will help it grip to your forehead. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to keep it in place.

Q: Can I wear a basketball headband with a hat or helmet?

A: It depends on the hat or helmet. Some may be too tight to wear with a headband, while others may work just fine. Experiment to see what works best for you.

Hair Care

Wearing a headband can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face during a game, but it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove sweat and dirt buildup
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great while wearing a basketball headband.
