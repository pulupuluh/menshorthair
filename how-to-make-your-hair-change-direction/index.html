How To Make Your Hair Change Direction

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In this year 2023, we all want to change things up a bit, especially when it comes to our hair. One way to do this is by making your hair change direction. Whether you want a side part, a middle part or just want your hair to go in a different direction, this guide will help you achieve the look you want.

Description

Changing the direction of your hair can give you a whole new look without having to cut or color your hair. It can also make your hair appear thicker and fuller. This guide will show you how to change the direction of your hair in a few simple steps.

How To

The first step to changing the direction of your hair is to start with clean and dry hair. This will make it easier to style and will help your hair hold its shape. Next, you will need to decide which direction you want your hair to go in. If you want a side part, then you will need to part your hair on the side. If you want your hair to go in a different direction, then you will need to decide which way you want to comb your hair.

Once you have decided which direction you want your hair to go in, you will need to use a comb or brush to comb your hair in that direction. You may also want to use a styling product such as a gel or mousse to help your hair hold its shape.

If you have long hair, you may also want to consider using a hair clip or hair tie to keep your hair in place while you style it. This will make it easier to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go in.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to changing the direction of your hair is to start with clean and dry hair. This will make it easier to style and will help your hair hold its shape.

Step 2: Decide on the Direction

Decide which direction you want your hair to go in. If you want a side part, then you will need to part your hair on the side. If you want your hair to go in a different direction, then you will need to decide which way you want to comb your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair in That Direction

Use a comb or brush to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go in. You may also want to use a styling product such as a gel or mousse to help your hair hold its shape.

Step 4: Use a Hair Clip or Hair Tie (Optional)

If you have long hair, you may also want to consider using a hair clip or hair tie to keep your hair in place while you style it. This will make it easier to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go in.

Tips

- Start with clean and dry hair to make it easier to style.
- Use a comb or brush to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go in.
- Consider using a styling product such as a gel or mousse to help your hair hold its shape.
- If you have long hair, use a hair clip or hair tie to keep your hair in place while you style it.

FAQ

Q: Will changing the direction of my hair damage my hair?

A: No, changing the direction of your hair will not damage your hair. However, using heat styling tools such as a straightener or curling iron can damage your hair if not used properly.

Q: Can I change the direction of my hair if it is curly?

A: Yes, you can still change the direction of your hair if it is curly. However, it may be more difficult to get your hair to stay in place.

Q: How often should I change the direction of my hair?

A: There is no set time frame for changing the direction of your hair. You can change the direction of your hair as often as you like.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while changing the direction of your hair, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You may also want to use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

It is also important to avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

By following these tips, you can change the direction of your hair without damaging it and achieve the look you want.
