How To Care For Relaxed Hair After Working Out

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Exercising is essential for maintaining good health and a fit body. But, for those with relaxed hair, working out can be a bit tricky. Sweat, humidity, and other factors can cause damage to relaxed hair, leading to dryness, breakage, and split ends. In this article, we will discuss how to care for your relaxed hair after working out and keep it healthy and beautiful.

Description

Relaxed hair is hair that has been chemically straightened using a relaxer. This process breaks down the hair's natural curl pattern, making it easier to manage and style. However, relaxed hair is also more fragile than natural hair and requires special care to keep it healthy.

Working out can be particularly challenging for those with relaxed hair. Sweat, heat, and humidity can cause the hair to become dry and brittle, leading to breakage and split ends. To avoid these problems, it is essential to take proper care of your hair after working out.

How to Care for Relaxed Hair After Working Out

Here are some steps you can take to care for your relaxed hair after working out:

1. Rinse Your Hair

After working out, rinse your hair with lukewarm water to remove any sweat and dirt. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

2. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Choose a moisturizing shampoo that is specifically designed for relaxed hair. These shampoos contain ingredients that help to hydrate and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and manageable. Be sure to massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, and rinse thoroughly.

3. Apply a Deep Conditioner

After shampooing, apply a deep conditioner to your hair. Leave it on for at least 15 minutes, or as directed on the product label. Deep conditioners help to restore moisture to your hair, making it soft and supple.

4. Detangle Your Hair

Gently detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to tug or pull on your hair.

5. Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair after working out. Instead, allow your hair to air dry naturally. If you must blow dry your hair, use a low heat setting and a heat protectant spray.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to caring for your relaxed hair after working out:

  1. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water
  2. Apply a moisturizing shampoo, and massage it into your scalp and hair
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly
  4. Apply a deep conditioner, and leave it on for at least 15 minutes
  5. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly
  7. Squeeze out excess water from your hair
  8. Wrap your hair in a microfiber towel or a T-shirt to absorb excess water
  9. Allow your hair to air dry naturally
  10. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Caring for Relaxed Hair After Working Out

Here are some additional tips to keep your relaxed hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair
  • Wear a protective style, such as a bun or a braid, to minimize manipulation
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or a scarf
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can be drying to your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about caring for relaxed hair after working out:

Q: Can I wash my hair after working out?

A: Yes, it is essential to wash your hair after working out to remove sweat and dirt.

Q: What type of shampoo should I use on my relaxed hair after working out?

A: Use a moisturizing shampoo that is specifically designed for relaxed hair. These shampoos contain ingredients that help to hydrate and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and manageable.

Q: How often should I deep condition my relaxed hair after working out?

A: It depends on your hair's condition and needs. If your hair is dry and brittle, deep condition it once a week. If your hair is healthy, deep condition it once a month.

Hair Care Products for Relaxed Hair After Working Out

Here are some hair care products that are ideal for caring for your relaxed hair after working out:

  • Moisturizing shampoo
  • Deep conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Microfiber towel or T-shirt

Caring for your relaxed hair after working out requires a bit of extra effort, but it is well worth it. With the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful, even after a sweaty workout.
