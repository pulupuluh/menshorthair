Face Framing Haircuts For Medium Hair With Bangs

Written by Aprilia May 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and enhance your features, consider a face framing haircut with bangs. This style is perfect for medium length hair and can be customized to suit your personal preferences and lifestyle. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about face framing haircuts for medium hair with bangs, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Face framing haircuts are designed to highlight your best features and create an overall flattering effect. These haircuts typically involve longer layers around the face that draw attention to your cheekbones, jawline, and eyes. Bangs can also be added to further accentuate your features and add a touch of style and personality to your look. For medium length hair, face framing haircuts are a great way to add shape and movement without sacrificing length or versatility.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in trying a face framing haircut with bangs, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like and be clear about your preferences and lifestyle. Your hairstylist will work with you to create a customized haircut that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

When it comes to styling your new haircut, there are a few key tips to keep in mind. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and gently towel dry. Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron. To create volume and movement, use a round brush to blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on the layers around your face. Finish with a light-hold hairspray or styling cream.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a face framing haircut with bangs:

Step 1: Consultation

Meet with your hairstylist to discuss your preferences and options for a face framing haircut with bangs.

Step 2: Wash and Condition

Wash and condition your hair with products that are suited to your hair type.

Step 3: Cut the Layers

Your hairstylist will cut longer layers around your face to create a face framing effect.

Step 4: Cut the Bangs

If you've opted for bangs, your hairstylist will cut them to your desired length and shape.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use heat styling tools and products to create volume and movement in your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a face framing haircut with bangs:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Use products that are suited to your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Stay up to date with regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face framing haircuts for medium hair with bangs:

What face shape is best suited for a face framing haircut with bangs?

A face framing haircut with bangs can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's especially flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

What hair texture is best suited for a face framing haircut with bangs?

A face framing haircut with bangs can work for a variety of hair textures, but it's especially flattering for those with wavy or curly hair.

How often should I get a haircut to maintain my face framing style?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your face framing haircut with bangs.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to use products that are suited to your hair type. This includes using a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair texture and using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Additionally, it's recommended to get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your face framing haircut with bangs.

Overall, a face framing haircut with bangs is a great way to add shape, movement, and personality to your medium length hair. By working with a skilled hairstylist and following the right hair care routine, you can achieve a look that flatters your face shape and enhances your features.
