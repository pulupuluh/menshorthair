Best Short Hairstyles For Over Sixties

Written by Oliver Jul 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're over sixty and looking for a new hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll share the best short hairstyles for over sixties. From pixie cuts to bob hairstyles, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair texture and thickness change. That's why it's essential to choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type and face shape. The best short hairstyles for over sixties are low maintenance, easy to style, and make you feel confident and beautiful.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it's essential to do some research and find the hairstyle that works best for you. Look for inspiration photos, and bring them to your stylist. They'll be able to help you choose the best cut and style for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Research the best short hairstyles for over sixties.

Step 2: Bring inspiration photos to your stylist.

Step 3: Discuss the best cut and style for your hair type and face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best short hairstyles for over sixties:

  • Choose a style that works with your hair type.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a haircut.
  • Choose a low maintenance style to avoid frequent salon visits.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to find the perfect shade for you.
  • Consider adding layers to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short hairstyles for over sixties:

What are the best short hairstyles for over sixties?

The best short hairstyles for over sixties are pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and layered cuts. These styles are low maintenance, easy to style, and make you look youthful and vibrant.

What should I consider before choosing a short hairstyle?

Before choosing a short hairstyle, consider your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Choose a style that works with your hair texture and thickness, and consider how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for over sixties, it's time to book your salon appointment. Remember to bring inspiration photos and discuss the best cut and style for your hair type and face shape. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you'll look and feel your best.
