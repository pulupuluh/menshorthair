How To Make Thick Wavy Hair Curly For Guys

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you want to switch up your hairstyle and transform your thick wavy hair into curly locks? Many guys with thick wavy hair often struggle to achieve the curly look that they desire. Luckily, there are several methods that you can use to make your hair curly. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make thick wavy hair curly for guys.

  • How to Make Thick Wavy Hair Curly for Guys

Description

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to style, and many guys wish they had curly hair instead. Fortunately, there are several methods to make your thick wavy hair curly. You can achieve this look with the right products, tools, and techniques. With a little effort and patience, you can transform your hair into bouncy, curly locks.

How to Make Thick Wavy Hair Curly for Guys

To make thick wavy hair curly for guys, you can use a variety of techniques, including using heat styling tools, using hair rollers, or getting a perm. However, the most popular and effective method is to use hair products specifically designed to enhance curls.

1. Use Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Before styling, start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products are formulated to add moisture and definition to curls, making them more manageable and easier to style.

2. Apply a Curl Enhancing Cream or Gel

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently and apply a curl enhancing cream or gel. These products work to define your curls and reduce frizz, making it easier to style your hair in the desired shape.

3. Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Blow Dryer

Next, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while enhancing your curls. The diffuser helps to spread the heat evenly and gently, reducing the risk of damage to your hair. Dry your hair by cupping small sections of hair in the diffuser and moving it around your head.

4. Use a Curling Iron or Wand

If you want more defined curls, you can use a curling iron or wand to create tighter curls. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Be sure to use heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make thick wavy hair curly for guys:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while enhancing your curls.
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to create tighter curls if desired.
  6. Use hair spray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your thick wavy hair curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply your hair products when your hair is damp, not wet, to avoid diluting the product.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair when it is dry, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Be patient and give your hair time to dry before styling or touching it.

FAQ

Can I make my thick wavy hair curly without using heat styling tools?

Yes, you can use hair rollers or get a perm to make your thick wavy hair curly without using heat styling tools. However, these methods require more time and effort.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. Over-washing can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy, curly hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your thick wavy hair curly and achieve the hairstyle you desire. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
