How To Reduce Hair Fall Problem Naturally

Written by Oliver Feb 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are experiencing hair fall problem, you are not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that affects millions of people around the world. While there are many hair care products available in the market that claim to reduce hair fall, most of them contain harmful chemicals that can damage your hair and scalp in the long run. In this article, we will discuss natural and effective ways to reduce hair fall problem without using any harmful chemicals.

How to Reduce Hair Fall Problem Naturally

There are many natural ways to reduce hair fall problem. Here are some of the most effective ones:

1. Oil Massage

Massaging your scalp with warm oil is a great way to reduce hair fall problem. You can use coconut oil, almond oil, or any other oil of your choice. Massage your scalp gently for 5-10 minutes and leave it on for at least an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural hair fall remedy that can also promote hair growth. Apply aloe vera gel directly to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is an effective natural remedy for hair fall. Apply onion juice to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Green Tea

Green tea is rich in antioxidants that can promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. Brew a cup of green tea and let it cool down. Apply it to your scalp and hair and leave it on for an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

5. Egg Mask

Egg mask is a great natural remedy for hair fall. Beat an egg and apply it to your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to reduce hair fall problem naturally:

Step 1: Choose a natural remedy that suits your hair type.

There are many natural remedies for hair fall, but not all of them may suit your hair type. Choose a remedy that suits your hair type and scalp.

Step 2: Apply the remedy to your scalp and hair.

Apply the remedy to your scalp and hair and massage gently for 5-10 minutes.

Step 3: Leave it on for a specific time.

Leave the remedy on your scalp and hair for a specific time. The time may vary depending on the remedy you choose.

Step 4: Wash it off with a mild shampoo.

Wash off the remedy with a mild shampoo and rinse your hair thoroughly with water.

Tips to Reduce Hair Fall Problem Naturally

Here are some tips to reduce hair fall problem naturally:

1. Eat a healthy diet.

Eating a healthy and balanced diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Include foods that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins in your diet.

2. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

Avoid using hair care products that contain harsh chemicals like sulfates, parabens, and silicones. These chemicals can damage your hair and scalp in the long run.

3. Avoid using hot styling tools.

Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and hair dryers. These tools can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

4. Get enough sleep.

Getting enough sleep is essential for healthy hair growth. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep every night.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about reducing hair fall problem naturally:

1. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause hair fall.

2. Can washing hair too often cause hair fall?

Washing hair too often can strip the natural oils from your scalp and cause hair fall. Aim to wash your hair 2-3 times a week.

3. Can hair fall be reversed?

Yes, hair fall can be reversed by following a healthy and balanced diet, using natural remedies, and avoiding harsh chemicals.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

1. Use a mild shampoo.

Use a mild shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals. Look for shampoos that are specifically formulated for hair fall.

2. Use a conditioner.

Use a conditioner after shampooing to nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Comb your hair gently.

Use a wide-toothed comb to comb your hair gently. Avoid pulling or tugging your hair.

4. Avoid tying your hair tightly.

Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause hair fall. Use a loose hairband or scrunchie instead.

In conclusion, reducing hair fall problem naturally is possible by following a healthy and balanced diet, using natural remedies, and avoiding harsh chemicals. With consistent effort and patience, you can achieve healthy and strong hair.
