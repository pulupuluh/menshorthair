How To Cut Black Men s Hair With Clippers Short Back And Sides

Written by Aprilia Apr 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking to give your black male clients a sleek and stylish haircut? Look no further than the short back and sides cut with clippers. This classic look is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieve this look and provide some tips for hair care along the way.

Description

The short back and sides cut is exactly what it sounds like. The hair is cut short on the back and sides, while leaving a bit more length on top. This creates a clean and polished look that is suitable for both formal and casual occasions. Clippers are used to achieve the short length, and different guard sizes can be used to create different lengths.

How to

Before getting started, make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. It's also a good idea to have a few different guard sizes on hand, depending on the desired length. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry the hair

Start by washing the hair to remove any product buildup. Dry the hair completely before beginning the cut.

Step 2: Determine the desired length

Consult with your client to determine the desired length for the cut. Use a longer guard size for more length on top, or a shorter guard size for a closer cut.

Step 3: Start with the sides

Start cutting the sides with the clippers, using the desired guard size. Work from the bottom up, using an upward motion to create a clean line. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Move to the back

Next, move to the back of the head and repeat the same process, starting at the nape of the neck and working upward.

Step 5: Blend the top

Using a longer guard size, blend the hair on top with the sides and back. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a seamless transition.

Step 6: Clean up the edges

Use scissors or trimmers to clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline. Check for any uneven spots and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a spray bottle to wet the hair if it becomes too dry or difficult to work with.
  • Be mindful of the direction of the hair growth to avoid any uneven patches.
  • Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

FAQ

How often should black men get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Is it okay to use clippers on wet hair?

It's best to use clippers on dry hair to avoid any snagging or pulling.

Hair Care

After the cut, recommend hair care products to your clients to keep their hair healthy and looking great. Products like leave-in conditioner and argan oil can help keep the hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Encourage your clients to avoid excessive heat styling and to use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while sleeping.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to give your black male clients a sharp and polished short back and sides cut with ease. Happy cutting!
