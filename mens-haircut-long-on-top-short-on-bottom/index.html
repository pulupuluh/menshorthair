Mens Haircut Long On Top Short On Bottom A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then a mens haircut long on top short on bottom might be just what you need. This haircut is a great option for men who want to keep their hair looking neat and tidy while still maintaining a modern and fashionable appearance. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect mens haircut long on top short on bottom, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Get a Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Bottom

Description

A mens haircut long on top short on bottom is a versatile and stylish hairstyle. As the name suggests, the hair on the top of the head is kept long, while the hair on the sides and back is cut short. This haircut can be customized to suit your personal style and can be made edgy, sleek, or even casual.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Bottom

If you are interested in getting a mens haircut long on top short on bottom, then follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Decide on the length of hair you want on the top of your head.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist or barber.
  3. Communicate your desired style and length to the stylist.
  4. Sit back and enjoy the process!

It's important to note that getting a mens haircut long on top short on bottom requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp and stylish.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Bottom

Here are some tips for keeping your mens haircut long on top short on bottom looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for touch-ups.
  • Consider using styling products such as pomade or hair gel to create different looks.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

What face shapes does a mens haircut long on top short on bottom suit?

This haircut suits most face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair type.

Can I still get a mens haircut long on top short on bottom if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still get this haircut if you have thinning hair. Your hairstylist can customize the style to suit your hair type and create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

Hair Care Advice for Mens Haircut Long on Top Short on Bottom

Here are some hair care tips specifically for mens haircut long on top short on bottom:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as curling irons or straighteners, as this can cause heat damage.

By following these tips and maintaining your mens haircut long on top short on bottom, you can keep your hair looking stylish and trendy for months to come.
