How to Get Natural Fluffy Hair: Tips, Step by Step Guide and Hair Care

Are you tired of your flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve bouncy and fluffy hair without using harsh chemicals? Look no further because we have gathered the best tips and tricks on how to get natural fluffy hair. Say goodbye to dull and limp hair, and hello to voluminous and healthy locks!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Natural Fluffy Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Fluffy Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

How to Get Natural Fluffy Hair

There are several ways to achieve natural fluffy hair without using harsh chemicals or heat. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heavy products that weigh down your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add volume.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

1. Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that contains ingredients that add volume to your hair, such as keratin, biotin, and panthenol. These ingredients help to strengthen your hair and add volume without weighing it down. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to prevent any product buildup that can weigh down your hair.

2. Blow-dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down helps to create volume at the roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair as you blow-dry, and focus the nozzle of the hairdryer at the roots. Once your hair is dry, flip it back up and use a cool shot of air to set the style.

3. Use a Wide-tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently helps to prevent breakage and damage, which can lead to flat and lifeless hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to tug or pull on your hair.

4. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils, serums, and creams. Instead, opt for lightweight products, such as mousses and sprays, that add volume and texture without weighing down your hair.

5. Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great way to refresh your hair and add volume without washing it. Spray dry shampoo at the roots of your hair and massage it in with your fingers. This helps to absorb any excess oil and add volume to your hair.

6. Regular Trims

Getting regular trims helps to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look flat and dull. Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your hair healthy and voluminous.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get natural fluffy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  3. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  4. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots.
  5. Once your hair is dry, flip it back up and use a cool shot of air to set the style.
  6. Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add volume.
  7. Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Tips for Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving fluffy hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as much as possible.
  • Try sleeping on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a volumizing powder to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Try a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can I achieve fluffy hair with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve fluffy hair with naturally curly hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair without disrupting your natural curls. Apply a curl-enhancing mousse or cream to define your curls and add volume.

What if I have fine hair?

If you have fine hair, use lightweight products that add volume without weighing down your hair. Avoid heavy oils and creams that can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Can I achieve fluffy hair without blow-drying?

Yes, you can achieve fluffy hair without blow-drying. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair, and then let your hair air dry. Once your hair is dry, use a volumizing powder to add texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

Here are some tips on how to care for your fluffy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as much as possible.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve natural fluffy hair without using harsh chemicals or heat. Remember to take care of your hair and give it the love and attention it deserves. Happy hair days!
