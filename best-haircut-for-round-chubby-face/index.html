Best Haircut For Round Chubby Face Flattering And Stylish

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that will flatter your round chubby face? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for round chubby face, how to achieve it, tips, and hair care. Let's get started!

Description

A round chubby face can be a bit of a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect haircut. The key is to go for styles that will elongate your face and create an illusion of a slimmer face. The goal is to create volume on the top and keep the sides and back shorter. This will create a more oval-shaped face which is considered the most flattering for round faces.

How to

The best haircut for round chubby face is the layered bob. This style works wonders for round faces as it elongates the face and creates a slimmer look. The layers create volume on the top, and the shorter length on the sides and back creates an illusion of a slimmer face. A side-swept fringe is also a great addition to this style as it softens the face and adds a touch of elegance.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect layered bob:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Cut your hair to the desired length, keeping the sides and back shorter.
  5. Start creating layers on the top by taking small sections and cutting them at an angle.
  6. Blend the layers by using a razor or thinning shears.
  7. Add a side-swept fringe by cutting it at an angle.
  8. Style your hair using a round brush to add volume on the top.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for round chubby face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they tend to make the face look rounder.
  • Avoid short hairstyles as they tend to make the face look wider.
  • Go for styles with layers and volume on the top.
  • Avoid center parts and go for side parts instead.
  • Opt for soft, side-swept fringes instead of blunt bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for round chubby face:

Q. Can I go for a pixie cut if I have a round chubby face?

A. While pixie cuts can look great on some round faces, they tend to make the face look wider. It's best to go for layered styles that create volume on the top and keep the sides and back shorter.

Q. Can I go for a bob if I have a double chin?

A. Absolutely! A layered bob can be a great option for those with a double chin as it creates volume on the top and elongates the face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look of your new haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the best haircut for round chubby face is the layered bob. It's important to go for styles that elongate the face and create an illusion of a slimmer face. With the right cut and styling, you can have a flattering and stylish look that suits your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the look of your new haircut.
