How To Make Your Hair Waves Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're a guy looking to add some texture to your hair, waves are a great option. Not only do they add some dimension and style to your look, but they can also be a low-maintenance way to switch up your hair routine. Here's a step-by-step guide for how to make your hair waves guys.

Description:

In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to create waves in your hair as a guy. We'll cover everything from the tools and products you'll need to the technique for achieving the perfect wave. We'll also include tips for caring for your hair once you've achieved your desired look.

How to:

To make your hair waves guys, you'll need a few tools and products:

Tools:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips to section hair
  • Blow dryer with diffuser attachment
  • Curling iron or wand

Products:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair mousse or sea salt spray
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of your styling tools.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Sea Salt Spray

Apply a hair mousse or sea salt spray to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. These products will help create texture and hold in your waves.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to section your hair into manageable sections. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your hair. Diffusing your hair will help create volume and texture. Make sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl each section of your hair away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 6: Finger Comb Your Hair

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through your hair. This will help create a more natural, relaxed wave.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your waves in place. Be sure to hold the can at least 10 inches away from your hair to avoid creating a stiff, crunchy look.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different types of curling irons and wands to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Use a lower heat setting on your curling iron or wand to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't over-apply products to your hair, as this can weigh it down and create a greasy look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it to avoid breakage.

FAQ:

Q: How long will my hair waves last?

A: Your hair waves should last for a few days with proper care and maintenance. Use a silk pillowcase to avoid creating frizz and try not to over-style your hair with heat tools.

Q: Can I make my hair waves without using heat tools?

A: Yes! You can create waves in your hair by braiding it before you go to bed. Simply section your hair into two or three braids and secure them with elastics. When you wake up in the morning, undo the braids and finger-comb your hair to create waves.

Hair Care:

Keep your hair healthy and strong by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Additionally, try not to over-style your hair with heat tools and give it a break from time to time by wearing it in a low-maintenance style.

Conclusion:

With a few tools and the right technique, you can easily make your hair waves guys. Experiment with different products and styling tools to find the look that works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair by using moisturizing products and avoiding excessive heat styling.
