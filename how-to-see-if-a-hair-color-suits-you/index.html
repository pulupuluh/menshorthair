How To See If A Hair Color Suits You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 30, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be exciting, but it can also be nerve-wracking. How do you know if the color you’re considering will look good on you? Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you figure out if a hair color suits you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 4: Try On Wigs or Extensions
  • Step 5: Consult with a Professional
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in figuring out if a hair color suits you is to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have yellow or golden undertones. If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have pink or blue undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have a cool skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you best. If you have blue or gray eyes, cool tones like ash blonde or platinum blonde may work well. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm tones like caramel or honey may be more flattering.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Looking at your natural hair color can give you a clue as to which shades will work well with your skin tone and eye color. For example, if you have naturally dark hair, going too light may not be the best option. If you have naturally light hair, going too dark may not be the best option.

Step 4: Try On Wigs or Extensions

If you’re still unsure about which hair color will suit you, try on some wigs or extensions in the color you’re considering. This will give you a better idea of how the color will look against your skin tone and with your eye color.

Step 5: Consult with a Professional

Finally, if you’re still unsure about which hair color will suit you, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a color that will complement your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

  • Don’t go too drastic. If you’re making a big change, consider going in stages.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a color that won’t require a lot of upkeep.
  • Think about the season. Warmer tones may be more flattering in the summer, while cooler tones may be more flattering in the winter.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it’s important to choose a dye that is formulated for sensitive skin. Look for products that are free from harsh chemicals and fragrances.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on the color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Some colors may require touch-ups every 4-6 weeks, while others may only need to be touched up every 8-12 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Color

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these steps and tips, you can figure out if a hair color suits you and choose a color that will flatter your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Remember to consult with a professional stylist if you’re still unsure.
