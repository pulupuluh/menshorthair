How To Cut My Own Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 14, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right haircut. But what if you could cut your own hair at home? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-quality cut without leaving your house.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut My Own Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you have thick curly hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut in the comfort of your own home. This guide will show you how to cut your own hair step-by-step, including tips and tricks for achieving the perfect style.

How to Cut My Own Thick Curly Hair

The first step in cutting your own hair is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips or elastics to section off your hair. It’s also a good idea to have a mirror or two on hand so you can see the back of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Comb out any tangles and divide your hair into sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Secure each section with a hair clip or elastic.

3. Begin cutting small sections of hair, starting with the bottom layer. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a straight line, using the comb as a guide. Continue cutting small sections, working your way up to the top layer.

4. For more layers, take smaller sections of hair and cut at an angle. This will create a softer, more textured look.

5. Once you’ve finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

- Start with a small amount of hair and cut slowly, making sure to avoid cutting too much at once.

- Use the comb as a guide to ensure a straight line.

- For more volume, cut your hair while it’s dry.

- If you’re unsure about cutting your own hair, start with small trims and work your way up to a full haircut.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair while it’s wet?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair while it’s wet. However, keep in mind that wet hair is longer and may shrink as it dries.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain a healthy, shiny appearance. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Finally, protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can cut your own thick curly hair at home and achieve a salon-quality look. Remember to take it slow, use the right tools, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
