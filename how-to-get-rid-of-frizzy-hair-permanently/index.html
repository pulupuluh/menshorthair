How To Get Rid Of Frizzy Hair Permanently A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're tired of dealing with frizzy hair every day, you're not alone. Frizz can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, heat damage, and genetics. But the good news is that there are ways to get rid of frizzy hair permanently. In this guide, we'll explore the best methods for achieving smooth, sleek locks that will last.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair Permanently
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Frizz-Free Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Products for Frizzy Hair

Description

Frizzy hair can be frustrating to deal with, especially if you have thick, curly or wavy hair. It can make your hair look unkempt and unmanageable, and can be difficult to style. While there are many temporary solutions for dealing with frizz, such as anti-frizz serums and hair masks, these products only provide short-term relief. To achieve permanent results, you need to address the root cause of your frizz.

How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair Permanently

There are several methods for getting rid of frizzy hair permanently, including keratin treatments, chemical straightening, and hair relaxing.

Keratin Treatments

Keratin treatments are a popular method for achieving smooth, frizz-free hair. This treatment involves applying a keratin solution to your hair, which is then sealed in with heat. The keratin helps to smooth the hair cuticle, resulting in smoother, sleeker hair.

Chemical Straightening

Chemical straightening, also known as a perm, involves using a chemical solution to break down the bonds in your hair, making it straight. This process can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to choose a reputable salon and stylist.

Hair Relaxing

Hair relaxing is a less harsh alternative to chemical straightening, and is ideal for those with curly or wavy hair. This process involves applying a relaxer to your hair, which loosens the curl pattern and makes your hair easier to manage.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're considering one of these methods for getting rid of frizzy hair permanently, here's what you can expect:

Keratin Treatment

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply the keratin solution according to the instructions.
  3. Blow dry your hair straight, then seal in the solution with a flat iron.
  4. Avoid washing your hair for at least 72 hours to allow the keratin to fully bond with your hair.

Chemical Straightening

  1. Choose a reputable salon and stylist.
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  3. Apply the chemical solution to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  4. Rinse out the solution and apply a neutralizer to stop the chemical process.
  5. Blow dry and flat iron your hair to seal in the straightness.

Hair Relaxing

  1. Choose a reputable salon and stylist.
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  3. Apply the relaxer to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  4. Rinse out the relaxer and apply a neutralizer to stop the process.
  5. Deep condition your hair to restore moisture.

Tips for Maintaining Frizz-Free Hair

Once you've achieved smooth, frizz-free hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, or use a heat protectant spray if you must.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about getting rid of frizzy hair permanently:

Is it safe to get a keratin treatment?

Yes, keratin treatments are generally safe when applied by a professional. However, they do contain formaldehyde, which can be irritating to some people. If you have sensitive skin or allergies, it's important to consult with your stylist before getting a keratin treatment.

How long does a keratin treatment last?

A keratin treatment can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain it.

Can hair relaxing damage my hair?

Hair relaxing can be damaging to your hair if not done properly. It's important to choose a reputable salon and stylist, and to follow the instructions carefully.

Hair Care Products for Frizzy Hair

If you're not ready to commit to a permanent solution for frizzy hair, there are plenty of hair care products that can help. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Moroccan Oil Treatment
  • Living Proof No Frizz Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Aveda Smooth Infusion Style-Prep Smoother
  • Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil

With the right products and techniques, you can say goodbye to frizzy hair for good. Whether you choose a permanent solution like a keratin treatment or hair relaxing, or opt for temporary relief with anti-frizz products, there's a solution for everyone.
