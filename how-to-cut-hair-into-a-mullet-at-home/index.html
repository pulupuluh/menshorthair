How To Cut Hair Into A Mullet At Home

Written by Frank Feb 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're feeling adventurous and want to try out a new hairstyle, why not go for a mullet? This iconic haircut is making a comeback and you can easily achieve this look at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your hair into a mullet step by step. We'll also provide you with some tips, answer some frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair into a Mullet at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Mullet
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for a Mullet

Step by Step Guide

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a clip to hold sections of hair in place.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair using a towel or a hair dryer until it's completely dry. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into three sections: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Starting with one side of your head, take a section of hair from the top of your ear to the crown of your head. Comb the hair straight up and cut it straight across. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the back

Unclip the back section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut the hair to the desired length, keeping the hair longest in the middle and shortest at the sides.

Step 5: Cut the top

Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight up. Cut the hair straight across, leaving it longer in the middle and shorter towards the sides. Make sure to blend the top section with the sides and back.

Step 6: Finishing touches

Use your scissors to trim any uneven pieces and make sure the mullet is symmetrical. You can also use thinning shears to add texture to the ends.

Tips for a Perfect Mullet

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to make it easier to cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Work in a well-lit area so you can see what you're doing.
  • Keep checking your progress in the mirror to make sure the mullet is balanced and symmetrical.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair into a mullet?

Yes, you can cut your own hair into a mullet. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair a little shorter to even it out. If you're really unhappy with the result, you can always go to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

Can I style my mullet in different ways?

Yes, you can style your mullet in different ways. You can use hair gel or wax to create spikes or slick it back for a more classic look.

Hair Care for a Mullet

To keep your mullet looking its best, you'll need to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Cutting your hair into a mullet at home can be a fun and exciting experience. With just a few tools and some patience, you can achieve a trendy new look. Just remember to follow the steps carefully, take your time, and have fun!
