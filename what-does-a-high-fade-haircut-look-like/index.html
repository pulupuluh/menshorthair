What Does A High Fade Haircut Look Like

Written by Petter Apr 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, a high fade haircut might be what you need. This type of haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially among men. In this article, we'll discuss what a high fade haircut looks like, how to get one, some tips to keep in mind, and how to take care of your hair after the haircut.

Description

A high fade haircut is a style where the hair on the sides and back of the head is gradually cut shorter and shorter, while the hair on top is kept longer. The hair gradually fades from long to short, hence the name "fade." The fade can start at different heights, but a high fade usually starts around the temples or above the ears. The hair on top can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference and hair type.

How to Get a High Fade Haircut

If you want to get a high fade haircut, you should go to a professional barber or hairstylist. They will know how to create the fade and blend the hair seamlessly. You can bring a picture of the style you want to show your hairstylist or ask for their recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. Make sure to communicate clearly with your hairstylist to avoid any misunderstandings.

Step by Step

Here are the steps involved in getting a high fade haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Choose the starting point of the fade with your hairstylist.
  3. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head, gradually decreasing the length.
  4. Blend the hair using scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  5. Style the hair on top as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a high fade haircut:

  • Choose a reputable hairstylist or barber with experience in creating fades.
  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the style you want.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing the length of the fade and the style on top.
  • Maintain the haircut by getting regular trims to keep the fade looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about high fade haircuts:

  • What is the difference between a high fade and a low fade?
  • Can I get a high fade with curly hair?
  • How often should I get a high fade haircut?

Hair Care

After getting a high fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the fade.

In conclusion, a high fade haircut is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that is popular among men. It involves gradually cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head shorter, while keeping the hair on top longer. To get a high fade haircut, you should go to a professional hairstylist or barber and communicate clearly about the style you want. After getting the haircut, maintain it by getting regular trims and taking care of your hair.
