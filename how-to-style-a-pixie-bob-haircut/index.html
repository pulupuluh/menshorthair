How To Style A Pixie Bob Haircut In 2023

If you're looking for a chic and trendy haircut that's low-maintenance, a pixie bob might be just what you need. This short, layered style is perfect for those who want to add some edge to their look without sacrificing femininity. In this article, we'll show you how to style a pixie bob haircut step by step, along with some tips and hair care advice.

How to Style a Pixie Bob Haircut

The key to styling a pixie bob is to keep it textured and tousled. You want to avoid a flat, lifeless look and instead aim for a messy, carefree vibe. Here are the basic steps to styling your pixie bob:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then blot it dry with a towel. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your palms and rub them together. You can use a texturizing spray, a volumizing mousse, or a pomade â it all depends on the look you're going for. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way outwards.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Pull the brush through your hair while blowing hot air onto it, lifting it up and away from your scalp. This will create volume and texture.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling wand

If you want to add more texture to your hair, use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or curls. Don't worry about making them look perfect â the more tousled, the better.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To set your style in place, spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair one more time for a perfectly imperfect look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of how to style your pixie bob:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Blot it dry with a towel, then comb it out to detangle any knots.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Choose a styling product that's right for your hair type and the look you want to achieve. For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to use a volumizing foam. If you have thick hair, a texturizing spray might work better. Apply a small amount of product to your palms and rub them together.

Step 3: Work the product through your hair

Starting at the roots, work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you go, creating volume and texture.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, pulling the brush through your hair while blowing hot air onto it. Lift your hair up and away from your scalp to create volume.

Step 5: Use a flat iron or curling wand

If you want to add more texture to your hair, use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or curls. Start at the bottom layer of your hair and work your way up, taking small sections at a time.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

To set your style in place, spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair one more time for a perfectly imperfect look.

Tips for Styling a Pixie Bob

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your pixie bob haircut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different textures and finishes â a pixie bob can be edgy, elegant, or casual depending on how you style it.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your look.

FAQ

How often should I get my pixie bob trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your pixie bob.

Can I style my pixie bob without heat tools?

Absolutely! You can create volume and texture by using a texturizing spray or mousse and scrunching your hair with your fingers. You can also air-dry your hair and use a little hairspray to set your style.

Hair Care for Pixie Bob Haircuts

Here are some tips to keep your pixie bob looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these styling tips and hair care advice, you'll be able to rock your pixie bob with confidence and style.
