How to Cut Your Own Short Wavy Hair - Step by Step Guide

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want more control over your hairstyle? Cutting your own short wavy hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and efficiently. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to cut your own short wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You'll Need

The first step to cutting your own hair is to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • Spray bottle with water

Preparing Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to wash it and let it dry naturally. This will allow you to see your natural waves and texture. Once your hair is dry, comb it out so that it's tangle-free. Then, part your hair where you normally do and clip away the sections you won't be cutting.


Cutting Your Hair

Start by cutting the back of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and pull it straight up. Use your scissors to snip off the ends, cutting vertically. Work your way down to the nape of your neck, then move on to the sides.

If you want to cut your hair shorter, use clippers with a guard to blend the shorter hair into the longer hair. Be sure to use a light touch and go slowly to avoid any mistakes.


Styling Your Hair

After you've finished cutting your hair, style it as you normally do. Use a curling iron or straightener to define your waves or straighten your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.


Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger ones.
  • Always cut vertically to avoid any blunt lines.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.
  • Don't cut too much at once, you can always go back and trim more later.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How often should I cut my own hair?

    • It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

  • Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

    • It's best to cut your hair when it's dry so that you can see the natural texture and shape.

  • What if I make a mistake?

    • If you accidentally cut too much, don't panic. You can always go back and trim more or style your hair in a way that hides the mistake.


Hair Care Tips

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the health of your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hair.

Cutting your own short wavy hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect style without breaking the bank. Remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and have fun!
