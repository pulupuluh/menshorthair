How To Put A Wig On With Glue A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you new to wearing wigs and want to know how to put on a wig with glue? This comprehensive guide will walk you through the steps of gluing down your wig for a secure and natural-looking hold. Whether you're wearing a wig for a special event or as a daily hair solution, this guide will help you achieve a flawless look.

How to Put on a Wig With Glue

Before you begin, gather your materials:

  • Wig cap
  • Adhesive glue
  • Wig
  • Cotton swabs
  • Alcohol
  • Scissors
  • Blow dryer

Step 1: Prepare Your Scalp

First, prepare your scalp by cleaning it with alcohol. This will help remove any excess oil or dirt that could prevent the glue from adhering properly. Let your scalp dry completely before applying the glue.

Step 2: Put on a Wig Cap

Next, put on a wig cap. This will help keep your hair secure and prevent any stray hairs from poking out from under the wig. Make sure the wig cap fits snugly on your head.

Step 3: Trim the Lace

If your wig has lace around the hairline, use scissors to carefully trim the lace to match your hairline. Be cautious not to cut any of the wig hair.

Step 4: Apply Glue to Scalp

Using a cotton swab, apply a thin layer of glue along your hairline. Be sure to spread the glue evenly and avoid applying too much, as this can cause the glue to seep through the lace and become visible.

Step 5: Wait for the Glue to Dry

Wait for the glue to dry until it becomes tacky. This usually takes about 5-10 minutes, depending on the type of glue you are using.

Step 6: Place the Wig

Carefully place the wig onto your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Gently press the wig into the glue, making sure it adheres securely to your scalp.

Step 7: Style the Wig

Once your wig is in place, use a blow dryer on a cool setting to style the wig to your liking. Be cautious not to use too much heat, as this can damage the wig fibers.

Step 8: Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the quality and longevity of your wig. Be sure to wash and condition your wig regularly, and avoid using harsh chemicals or hot styling tools.

Tips for Putting on a Wig with Glue

  • Use a high-quality adhesive glue specifically made for wigs.
  • Apply the glue sparingly to avoid seeping through the lace.
  • Wait until the glue is tacky before placing the wig.
  • Use a cool setting on your blow dryer to avoid damaging the wig fibers.

FAQs

How long will a wig stay on with glue?

A wig can stay on for several days with glue, but it's recommended to remove the wig every few days to allow your scalp to breathe and prevent any irritation.

Can you swim with a wig on with glue?

No, it's not recommended to swim with a wig on with glue, as water can cause the glue to loosen and the wig to come off.

How do I remove the wig glue?

To remove the wig glue, use a cotton swab dipped in alcohol and gently rub the glue until it dissolves. You can also use a wig adhesive remover to make the process easier.

Hair Care for Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the quality and longevity of your wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your wig regularly.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools.
  • Store your wig on a wig head or in a wig bag to prevent tangling and damage.

Now that you know how to put on a wig with glue, you can confidently wear your wig with a secure and natural-looking hold. Remember to practice proper hair care to keep your wig looking its best.
