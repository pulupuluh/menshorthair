Can You Do A Perm With Thick Hair

Written by Robby Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick hair, you may be wondering if you can get a perm. The answer is yes, you can! However, there are some important things to keep in mind before you decide to go ahead with the process. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about getting a perm with thick hair, including how to prepare for it, what to expect during the process, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

How to Prepare for a Perm

Before you get a perm, it is important to prepare your hair properly. This will help ensure that your hair is healthy and strong, which will make it easier for the perm to take hold. Here are some tips for preparing your hair for a perm:

1. Get a consultation

Before you get a perm, it is important to talk to a hair stylist who has experience working with thick hair. They can assess your hair and help you decide whether a perm is the right choice for you.

2. Strengthen your hair

Thick hair can often be prone to breakage, so it is important to strengthen your hair before getting a perm. You can do this by using a protein treatment or a deep conditioning treatment.

3. Avoid chemical treatments

If you have recently had a chemical treatment, such as coloring or relaxing, it is important to wait at least two weeks before getting a perm. This will give your hair time to recover and will help ensure that the perm takes hold properly.

What to Expect During the Perm Process

The perm process typically takes around two hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair. Here is what you can expect during the process:

1. Your hair will be washed

Your hair will be washed to remove any dirt, oil, or styling products.

2. Your hair will be rolled

Your hair will be rolled onto perm rods, which will determine the size and shape of the curls.

3. The perm solution will be applied

The perm solution will be applied to your hair, which will break down the bonds in your hair and allow it to take on the shape of the perm rods.

4. The neutralizer will be applied

The neutralizer will be applied to your hair to stop the perming process and lock in the curls.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Permed Hair

Once you have a perm, it is important to take care of your hair properly to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of your permed hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for permed hair. Avoid using hot water, which can damage your hair and cause the curls to loosen.

2. Condition regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment on a regular basis to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible. These tools can damage your hair and cause your curls to loosen.

4. Get regular trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

1. Can you get a perm if you have thick hair?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have thick hair. However, it is important to prepare your hair properly and work with a stylist who has experience working with thick hair.

2. How long does a perm last?

A perm typically lasts around six months, although this can vary depending on the individual and the type of perm.

3. Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

It is generally recommended to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm.

Conclusion

If you have thick hair, you can definitely get a perm! Just be sure to prepare your hair properly, work with a stylist who has experience working with thick hair, and take care of your hair afterwards. With the right preparation and care, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls for months to come.
