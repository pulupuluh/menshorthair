How To Wear Hair When Growing Out Pixie Cut

Written by Luffy Jul 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you currently in the process of growing out your pixie cut and struggling with how to wear your hair during the awkward stages? Fear not, because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style your hair during the growing out process.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to Wear Hair When Growing Out Pixie Cut

When growing out a pixie cut, it’s important to be patient and experiment with different styles. The key is to find a style that works for you and that you feel comfortable with. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Accessorize

Accessorizing is an easy way to add some flair to your hair. Try wearing a headband or a clip to keep your hair out of your face. Alternatively, you can opt for a cute scarf or bandana to add some color to your outfit.

2. Embrace the Messy Look

When growing out a pixie cut, your hair will likely be at an awkward length where it’s too short to tie back but too long to style. Embrace the messy look by tousling your hair and adding some texturizing spray. This will give your hair some volume and make it look intentional.

3. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair off your face and add some interest to your style. Try a simple french braid or a fishtail braid to add some texture and dimension to your hair.

4. Try a Half-Up, Half-Down Style

A half-up, half-down style is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off some length. Try pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a clip or elastic band.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair when growing out a pixie cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a texturizing spray.
  2. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create some volume.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add some waves or curls to your hair.
  4. Try a half-up, half-down style by pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a clip or elastic band.
  5. Accessorize with a headband or clip to add some interest to your style.

Tips for Styling

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair when growing out a pixie cut:

  • Invest in some quality hair accessories to add some interest to your style.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works for you.
  • Be patient and embrace the awkward stages of growth.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and promote growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out a pixie cut:

Q: How long does it take to grow out a pixie cut?

A: The length of time it takes to grow out a pixie cut will vary depending on the individual’s hair growth rate, but it typically takes around 6-12 months to reach a bob-length style.

Q: Should I cut my hair during the growing out process?

A: It’s a good idea to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and promote growth, but avoid cutting too much length off at once.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: While there’s no magic solution for faster hair growth, maintaining a healthy diet and lifestyle can help promote hair growth. Additionally, using hair care products specifically formulated for growth can help.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for growing out a pixie cut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make the growing out process much more manageable and even enjoyable. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles until you find what works for you. Good luck!
