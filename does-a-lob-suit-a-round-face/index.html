Does A Lob Suit A Round Face

Written by Eveline Aug 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to haircuts, we all want something that complements our face shape and enhances our features. For those with round faces, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. A lob, or long bob, is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a few years now. But does a lob suit a round face?

Table of Contents

  • How To Get A Lob Haircut
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips To Keep Your Lob Looking Fresh
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips For Lobs

How To Get A Lob Haircut

Before we dive into whether a lob suits a round face, let's talk about how to get a lob haircut. A lob is essentially a longer version of a bob, and it typically falls just above the shoulders. Here's how to get a lob:

1. Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your lob to be. A true lob falls just above the shoulders, but you can also opt for a slightly longer or shorter version depending on your preference.

2. Decide on Layers

Next, decide whether you want layers or not. Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, but they can also make your hair appear thinner if you have fine hair.

3. Book An Appointment

Once you've decided on the length and layers, it's time to book an appointment with a hairstylist. Be sure to bring pictures of the lob you want to ensure that you and your stylist are on the same page.

Step By Step Guide

Now that you know how to get a lob haircut, let's talk about whether it suits a round face. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Identify Your Face Shape

The first step is to identify your face shape. A round face is characterized by a rounded chin and a width that is roughly the same as the length.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, a lob can help to slim down your face. If you have fine hair, however, a lob may not be the best choice as it can make your hair appear thinner.

3. Opt for Face-Framing Layers

If you have a round face, opt for face-framing layers. These layers will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

4. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for a more textured cut with layers.

Tips To Keep Your Lob Looking Fresh

Now that you know how to get a lob that suits your face shape, here are some tips to keep it looking fresh:

1. Use Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools. This will help to prevent damage and breakage.

2. Invest in Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

3. Schedule Regular Trims

Schedule regular trims to keep your lob looking fresh and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about lobs and round faces:

1. Can a lob suit a round face?

Yes, a lob can suit a round face. However, it's important to choose the right length and layers to complement your face shape.

2. What is the best length for a lob on a round face?

The best length for a lob on a round face is just above the shoulders.

3. Should I opt for layers if I have a round face?

Yes, opt for face-framing layers to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

Hair Care Tips For Lobs

Here are some hair care tips for lobs:

1. Use A Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't overwash your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Protect Your Hair From The Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, a lob can suit a round face if you choose the right length and layers. Opt for face-framing layers and avoid blunt cuts. With the right hair care and styling, you can keep your lob looking fresh and healthy.
