How To Put Dreads In A Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have dreadlocks and want to sport a man bun, you might be wondering how to put dreads in a man bun. Don't worry, it's easier than you think! In this post, we'll show you how to put dreads in a man bun in a step-by-step guide. We'll also give you tips on how to maintain your dreadlocks and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Dreads in a Man Bun
  • Tips for Putting Dreads in a Man Bun
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Dreadlocks

How to Put Dreads in a Man Bun

Here are the steps to put dreads in a man bun:

Step 1: Gather your dreadlocks

Gather your dreadlocks into a high ponytail using a hair tie. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 2: Twist the dreadlocks

Twist your dreadlocks clockwise until they start to coil on themselves. Keep twisting until the dreadlocks start to form a bun shape.

Step 3: Secure the bun

Once you have twisted the dreadlocks into a bun shape, use another hair tie to secure the bun. Make sure the hair tie is tight and secure.

Step 4: Tuck in loose ends

If you have loose ends sticking out of the bun, tuck them in using bobby pins. Make sure the bobby pins are hidden so that they don't ruin the look of your man bun.

Tips for Putting Dreads in a Man Bun

Here are some tips to help you put dreads in a man bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Don't twist your dreadlocks too tightly, as this can cause them to break.
  • Use bobby pins to secure loose ends, but make sure they are hidden.
  • Experiment with different styles of man buns to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Can I put my dreadlocks in a man bun if they are not long enough?

If your dreadlocks are not long enough to put in a man bun, you can try using hair extensions to add length. Alternatively, you can try a different hairstyle that doesn't require as much length.

How do I maintain my dreadlocks?

To maintain your dreadlocks, make sure to wash them regularly and use a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a locking gel or wax to help your dreadlocks stay in place. Avoid using conditioner, as this can make your dreadlocks loose and frizzy.

Hair Care Tips for Dreadlocks

Here are some hair care tips for dreadlocks:

  • Wash your dreadlocks regularly to keep them clean.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol or silicone, as these can cause build-up and damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your dreadlocks from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know how to put dreads in a man bun, you can rock this stylish look with confidence. Just remember to take care of your hair by following our hair care tips for dreadlocks. Happy styling!
