What Hairstyles Suit Long Oval Faces

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you someone who has long oval-shaped faces and is struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features? Worry no more because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the hairstyles that will suit your face shape and make you look stunning.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long oval faces are considered to be one of the most versatile face shapes in the world of hairstyles. With a face shape that is longer than it is wide, you have the freedom to experiment with various hairstyles without worrying about it overpowering your features. However, it’s important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles that suit long oval faces, it’s important to understand your face shape. To determine whether you have an oval face shape, measure the widest part of your face, which is usually across your cheekbones. If the measurement is less than the length of your face, then you have an oval face shape.

Step by Step

Now that you know your face shape, let’s take a look at the hairstyles that will suit you:

1. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is a great option for those with long oval faces. The layers will add volume and texture to your hair and help to balance out your face shape.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for long oval faces as they help to shorten the length of your face. This hairstyle will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that will suit anyone with a long oval face. It’s important to choose a bob that is chin-length or shorter to help balance out your face shape.

4. Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling daring, a pixie cut is a great option for those with long oval faces. This hairstyle will help to elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for long oval faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this will only elongate your face further.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides of your face, as this will help to balance out your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long oval face. However, it’s best to avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as this will only elongate your face further. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a long oval face. However, it’s best to avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as this will only elongate your face further. Instead, opt for a loose and messy ponytail.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyles suit long oval faces, it’s time to experiment and find the perfect one for you. Remember to keep these tips in mind and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
