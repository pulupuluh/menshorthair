How To Style Emo Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Emo hair is a popular style among guys, known for its edgy and rebellious look. This hairstyle features long bangs that usually cover one eye, with shorter hair on the sides and back. In this guide, we will show you how to style emo hair for guys in easy steps, so you can rock this look with confidence.

How to Style Emo Hair for Guys

Before you start styling your emo hair, you need to have the right haircut. Ask your hairstylist for a layered haircut with long bangs that can be swept to the side. Once you have the right cut, follow these steps to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Use a towel to dry your hair, but don't rub it too hard, as this can cause damage.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel to your hair, starting from the roots and moving towards the tips. Use your fingers to work the gel through your hair, making sure it is evenly distributed.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using your fingers to style it as you go. Blow-dry your hair in a downward direction, so that it falls naturally. Make sure to focus on your bangs, as these are the most important part of an emo hairstyle.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs. This will help them to stay in place and prevent them from getting too frizzy. Use a hairspray to hold your bangs in place, and then use your fingers to sweep them to the side. You can also use a comb to create a more defined look.

Tips for Styling Emo Hair for Guys

Here are some tips to help you style your emo hair:

  • Use a good quality hair gel and hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't overdo it with the hair products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, especially your bangs, to create a sleek and polished look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: Can I style emo hair without using hair gel?

A: Yes, you can use a hair wax or pomade instead of hair gel to style your hair. These products will give your hair a more natural look and feel.

Q: How often should I wash my emo hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you style it. Generally, you should wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Emo Hair

To keep your emo hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much hair products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

With these tips and steps, you can easily style your emo hair and rock this edgy look with confidence. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
