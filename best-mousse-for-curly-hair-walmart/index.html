Best Mousse For Curly Hair Walmart Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy and unmanageable curly hair? Look no further than Walmart’s selection of the best mousses for curly hair. In this article, we’ll explore the top options available and provide tips on how to use them for optimal results.

Description

Mousse is a styling product that is perfect for curly hair. It adds volume, hold, and definition to curls without weighing them down or making them crunchy. Walmart offers a variety of mousses with different formulas, hold levels, and price points. Here are some of the best options:

How to Use Mousse on Curly Hair

Using mousse on curly hair is easy, but there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with damp hair: Mousse works best on damp hair, so either apply it right after washing your hair or wet your hair with a spray bottle before using it.
  • Section your hair: To ensure that you apply the mousse evenly, divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  • Apply the mousse: Shake the can of mousse and dispense a golf ball-sized amount onto your hand. Rub your hands together and distribute the mousse evenly through each section of your hair.
  • Scrunch your curls: To enhance your curls, scrunch your hair gently from the bottom up.
  • Let your hair dry naturally or diffuse it: Once you’ve applied the mousse, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use mousse on curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Shake the can of mousse and dispense a golf ball-sized amount onto your hand.
  5. Rub your hands together and distribute the mousse evenly through each section of your hair.
  6. Scrunch your hair gently from the bottom up to enhance your curls.
  7. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls.
  8. Once your hair is dry, scrunch your curls again to soften any crunchiness.
  9. Spray with a curl-friendly hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Using Mousse on Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your mousse:

  • Use a mousse that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Experiment with different amounts of mousse to find the right balance for your hair.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying to prevent frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can dry out your curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I use mousse on dry hair?

A: Mousse works best on damp hair, but you can use it on dry hair to revive your curls. Simply wet your hair with a spray bottle and apply the mousse as usual.

Q: Can I use mousse on straight hair?

A: While mousse is designed for curly hair, it can also add volume and hold to straight hair. However, it may not provide the same level of definition as it does on curly hair.

Q: How often should I use mousse on my hair?

A: It’s best to use mousse on an as-needed basis. Overuse can cause buildup and make your hair look greasy.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With the right mousse and care, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that last all day. Try out Walmart’s selection of mousses for curly hair to find the perfect one for your hair type and needs.
