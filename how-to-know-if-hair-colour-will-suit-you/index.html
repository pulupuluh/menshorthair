How To Know If Hair Colour Will Suit You

Written by Robby Aug 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're like most people, you've probably wondered what you would look like with a different hair color. Maybe you've even gone as far as to try a new shade or two, only to find that it didn't quite work out the way you had hoped. In this article, we'll be discussing how to know if hair color will suit you, so you can avoid any hair disasters and feel confident in your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know if Hair Colour Will Suit You
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Know if Hair Colour Will Suit You

There are a few key factors to consider when deciding on a new hair color:

1. Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to go for golden, honey, or copper shades. If you have cool undertones, look for ash, platinum, or burgundy colors.

2. Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you. For example, if you have blue eyes, you may want to try a warm, golden color to make your eyes pop. If you have green eyes, a cool, ash tone can complement your eye color nicely.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a new hair color. If you're constantly on the go and don't have time for frequent touch-ups, a low-maintenance color may be the best option for you.

4. Personality

Your personality can also come into play when choosing a hair color. If you're outgoing and love to stand out, a bold, bright color may be the perfect fit for you. If you prefer a more low-key look, a natural shade may be a better option.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a new hair color:

1. Determine your skin tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

2. Consider your eye color

Think about which hair colors will complement your eye color.

3. Assess your lifestyle

Determine how much time and money you're willing to invest in maintaining your new color.

4. Think about your personality

Consider what type of look you're going for.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair color:

1. Start with a subtle change

If you're unsure about a drastic change, start with a subtle shift in color.

2. Use technology

Many salons offer virtual hair color try-ons, or you can use apps to see what you would look like with different shades.

3. Consider your wardrobe

Think about which colors you wear most often and which hair shades will complement them.

FAQ

What if I don't know my skin tone?

You can always ask a professional stylist to help you determine your skin tone.

What if I have multiple undertones?

You may have a combination of warm and cool undertones. In this case, look for colors that have a balance of both warm and cool tones.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hair color, it's important to take care of your locks to keep the color looking its best. Use color-safe shampoos and conditioners, avoid excessive heat styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and steps, you can feel confident in choosing a new hair color that will suit you perfectly. Remember to take your skin tone, eye color, lifestyle, and personality into account, and don't be afraid to try something new!
