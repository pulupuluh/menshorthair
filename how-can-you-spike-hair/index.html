How Can You Spike Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, spiking your hair may be just what you need. Spiked hair is a popular trend that can be achieved on both short and long hair lengths. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create spiked hair that will get you noticed. From how to prepare your hair to the best products to use, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

How to Prepare Your Hair

The first step in spiking your hair is to make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair thoroughly with a shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it gently, making sure not to rub too hard or damage your hair. If you have curly hair, straighten it with a flat iron before you begin styling. This will make it easier to create the spikes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Spiking Your Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Product
There are many hair products that can be used to create spikes, including hair gel, pomade, and wax. Choose a product that's appropriate for your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. Gel is best for short hair, while pomade and wax work well on longer hair.

Step 2: Apply the Product
Take a small amount of the product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply the product evenly to your hair, making sure to distribute it from root to tip. Use more product if necessary.

Step 3: Create the Spikes
Using your fingers, create spikes by pulling your hair up and twisting it slightly. Use a comb to define the spikes and make them more precise. Continue creating spikes until you achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray
To make sure your spikes stay in place all day, finish with a strong hold hairspray. Spray the hairspray evenly over your hair, holding the can about 6 inches away from your head.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

- Start with a small amount of product and add more if necessary.
- Use a comb to create precise spikes.
- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
- Don't touch your hair too much once you've created the spikes, as this can cause them to fall flat.
- Don't be afraid to try different styles and variations of spiked hair.

FAQs about Spiking Your Hair

Q: Can I spike my hair if it's naturally curly?
A: Yes, but you may need to straighten your hair with a flat iron before you begin styling to make it easier to create the spikes.

Q: Can I create spikes on long hair?
A: Yes, but you may need to use a stronger hold product like wax or pomade to keep the spikes in place.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

To keep your spiked hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair. And always use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
