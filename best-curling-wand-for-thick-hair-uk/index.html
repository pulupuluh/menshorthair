Best Curling Wand For Thick Hair Uk Perfect For Gorgeous Curls

Written by Robby Aug 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of using a curling wand that doesn't work for your thick hair? Say goodbye to bad hair days and hello to beautiful curls with the best curling wand for thick hair UK. It can be a challenge to find a curling wand that can tame thick hair, but with the right wand, you can achieve effortless curls that last all day.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous, it can also be difficult to style. The best curling wand for thick hair UK is designed to create curls that last all day without damaging your hair. It has a larger barrel size that can handle more hair at once, which is perfect for thick hair. Additionally, it has a higher heat setting that can handle the density of your hair without causing any damage.

How to Choose the Best Curling Wand for Thick Hair UK?

When choosing the best curling wand for thick hair UK, there are a few things to consider. Here are some factors that you should keep in mind:

1. Barrel size

The barrel size of your curling wand is crucial in creating the perfect curls for your thick hair. A larger barrel size can handle more hair at once, which will save you time when curling your hair. It's recommended to choose a barrel size between 1.5 to 2 inches for thick hair.

2. Heat setting

The heat setting of your curling wand is also important. Thick hair requires a higher heat setting to create curls that last all day. It's recommended to choose a curling wand with a heat setting between 350°F to 450°F.

3. Material

The material of your curling wand can affect the durability and performance of your curls. Ceramic and tourmaline are excellent materials for curling wands as they distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Step by Step Guide to Use Best Curling Wand for Thick Hair UK

Here's a step by step guide on how to use the best curling wand for thick hair UK:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Prep your hair by washing and blow-drying it. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.

Step 3: Heat up your curling wand

Heat up your curling wand to the desired temperature setting.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Set your curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, set your curls with a hairspray to make them last longer.

Tips for Using the Best Curling Wand for Thick Hair UK

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls with the best curling wand for thick hair UK:

1. Use a heat protectant spray

Using a heat protectant spray will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

2. Use a larger barrel size

Using a larger barrel size can help you achieve bigger and looser curls that look effortless.

3. Section your hair

Sectioning your hair will make it easier to curl and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

FAQs

1. Can I use the best curling wand for thick hair UK on other hair types?

Yes, you can use the best curling wand for thick hair UK on other hair types. However, you may need to adjust the temperature setting and barrel size to suit your hair type.

2. Can I use the best curling wand for thick hair UK on wet hair?

No, you should never use a curling wand on wet hair as it can cause damage to your hair.

3. How often should I clean my curling wand?

You should clean your curling wand after every use to prevent product buildup and ensure that it works properly.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips to help you take care of your thick hair:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week will help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb will help prevent breakage and damage to your hair when detangling.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair. When you do use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips and using the best curling wand for thick hair UK, you can achieve gorgeous curls that last all day without damaging your hair.
