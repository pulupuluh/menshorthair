How To Curl Front Of Hair With Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of straightening your hair every day and want to switch things up? Why not try curling the front of your hair using a straightener? This technique can give you beachy waves or tight curls, depending on your preference. In this article, we will show you how to curl the front of your hair with a straightener in relaxed English language.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Curl Front of Hair with Straightener

To curl the front of your hair with a straightener, you will need a few things:

  • A hair straightener
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Hairspray (optional)

Follow these steps to curl the front of your hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the straightener.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section off the front portion of your hair that you want to curl. Use clips or hair ties to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener as close to the roots as possible. Twist the straightener away from your face and glide it down the length of the hair. Repeat this process until you have curled all the sections of hair in the front.

Step 4: Set the Curls

If you want your curls to last longer, spray them with hairspray. Otherwise, you can leave them as is.

Tips for Curling Hair with Straightener

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair with a straightener:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start curling.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Take small sections of hair to curl for more defined curls.
  • Twist the straightener away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Don't hold the straightener on your hair for too long to avoid burning your hair.
  • Use hairspray to set your curls and make them last longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I curl my hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a straightener. It's a versatile tool that can be used for both straightening and curling hair.

How do I curl my hair with a straightener?

Section off the hair you want to curl, twist the straightener away from your face, and glide it down the length of the hair. Repeat until you have curled all the sections of hair.

Will curling my hair with a straightener damage it?

Using a straightener too often or at high heat settings can damage your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't apply too much heat to your hair.

Hair Care After Curling

After curling your hair with a straightener, make sure to take good care of it. Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Now that you know how to curl the front of your hair with a straightener, you can switch up your hairstyle and create different looks. Remember to take care of your hair and use heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Happy styling!
