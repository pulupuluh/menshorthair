Best Hairstyles For Small Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a male with a small face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle to complement your features? Look no further than this comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for small face male. We will cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the different styles that will enhance your features. Read on to discover the perfect hairstyle for you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for small face male, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

Using a tape measure, measure the distance between your eyebrows and your hairline's highest point. Record this measurement.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Measure the distance between your two cheekbones' widest points. Record this measurement.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Measure the distance between the tip of your chin and the point below your ear where your jawbone angles upward. Record this measurement.

Step 4: Determine Your Face Shape

Using the measurements you recorded, determine your face shape:

  • Oval: The length of your face is one and a half times greater than its width.
  • Square: The length and width of your face are roughly equal.
  • Round: The length and width of your face are roughly equal, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Heart: Your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your face tapers to a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and chin are narrower.

Best Hairstyles for Small Face Male

Now that you've determined your face shape let's take a look at the best hairstyles for small face male:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, consider yourself lucky as most hairstyles suit your face shape. However, a side-parted, slicked-back undercut or a messy fringe can enhance your features.

Square Face

If you have a square face, opt for hairstyles that soften your features. A short, textured crop or a side-parted quiff can work wonders for you.

Round Face

If you have a round face, choose hairstyles that elongate your face. A side-parted, slicked-back undercut or a pompadour can give you the illusion of a longer face.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, go for hairstyles that balance your forehead's width and your narrow chin. A messy fringe or a side-swept crew cut can complement your features.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, choose hairstyles that widen your forehead and jawline while minimizing your cheekbones' width. An angular fringe or a textured crop can work wonders for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your small face, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in styling products like pomade or wax to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.

FAQ

What if I have a small face and a receding hairline?

If you have a small face and a receding hairline, opt for hairstyles that create volume at the top like a messy fringe or a textured crop.

Can I have long hair if I have a small face?

Yes, you can have long hair if you have a small face, but make sure to keep it well-maintained and styled to avoid it overwhelming your features.

What if I'm balding?

If you're balding, opt for a buzz cut or a shaved head. Embrace your baldness and rock it with confidence.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial to maintaining a healthy and stylish mane. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Invest in a good brush or comb and use it regularly to detangle your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With this comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for small face male, you can now confidently choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Remember to maintain your hairstyle and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
