How To Style Short Wavy Hair For Guys

Written by Oliver Aug 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a guy with short wavy hair and wondering how to style it? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this blog post, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style short wavy hair for guys.

Description

Short wavy hair is a popular hairstyle among men. However, it can be challenging to style it correctly. Wavy hair has natural waves and curls that can make it difficult to manage. But don't worry, we have some simple tips and tricks that can help you style your short wavy hair effortlessly.

How to Style Short Wavy Hair for Guys

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for wavy hair. It will help to keep your hair hydrated and manageable.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair, but don't rub it vigorously. Gently pat your hair to remove excess water.

Step 3: Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. You can use a pomade or wax to create a natural look or a gel for a more defined style.

Step 4: Use your fingers to create waves or curls in your hair. You can scrunch your hair to add texture or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural waves.

Step 5: Blow-dry your hair on a low setting to set your style in place.

Step by Step Guide to Style Short Wavy Hair for Guys

Step 1: Wash your Hair

Start with washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for wavy hair. It will help to keep your hair hydrated and manageable.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Step 5: Blow-dry your Hair

Blow-dry your hair on a low setting to set your style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Wavy Hair for Guys

Here are some additional tips that can help you style your short wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using a brush as it can make your hair frizzy.
  • Apply styling product to damp hair to avoid making it greasy.
  • Don't use too much product as it can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.
  • If you have long hair, consider getting a haircut to remove split ends and make it easier to manage.

FAQs

Q. Can I straighten my short wavy hair?

A. Yes, you can straighten your short wavy hair using a straightener. However, it is recommended to avoid using it frequently as it can damage your hair.

Q. Can I use hair spray to style my short wavy hair?

A. Yes, you can use hair spray to hold your style in place. However, make sure to choose a hair spray that is suitable for wavy hair and doesn't make it stiff or sticky.

Q. How often should I wash my short wavy hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day, while if you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Short Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips that can help you keep your short wavy hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can make it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and promote healthy growth.

Styling short wavy hair for guys can be a breeze with the right products and techniques. Follow these tips, and you will have a stylish and effortless look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
