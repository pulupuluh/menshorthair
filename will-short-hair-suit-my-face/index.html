Will Short Hair Suit My Face

Written by Frank May 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're considering chopping off your locks and going for a short hairstyle, you may be wondering if it will suit your face shape. It's important to choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore how to determine if short hair will suit your face and provide tips for styling and caring for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if Short Hair Will Suit Your Face
  • Different Face Shapes and Short Hair

How to Determine if Short Hair Will Suit Your Face

There are a few factors to consider when deciding if short hair will suit your face shape. Here are some guidelines:

Consider Your Face Shape

The shape of your face is the most important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features and proportions, which can be enhanced or minimized with the right haircut.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect how a short hairstyle will look on you. If you have fine, straight hair, a short bob or pixie cut may be a good choice. If you have thick, curly hair, a shorter cut may require more maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your daily routine and lifestyle can also be a factor in choosing a short hairstyle. If you're constantly on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance short haircut may be a good choice.

Different Face Shapes and Short Hair

Here are some guidelines for choosing a short hairstyle based on your face shape:

Round Face

If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with volume on top can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid chin-length bobs, which can make your face look wider.

Oval Face

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off many different short hairstyles. A pixie cut, bob, or asymmetrical cut can all look great on an oval face.

Square Face

If you have a square face, a short bob or layered cut can soften your features and make your face appear more oval-shaped. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your jawline look even more pronounced.

Heart-Shaped Face

A chin-length bob or layered cut can complement a heart-shaped face by drawing attention to the cheekbones and balancing the proportions of the face.

Diamond-Shaped Face

A pixie cut or short bob with volume on top can accentuate the cheekbones and balance the proportions of a diamond-shaped face.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Short hair can be versatile and easy to style, but it does require some maintenance. Here are some tips for styling short hair:

  • Invest in quality styling products, such as a lightweight hairspray, texturizing spray, and a smoothing serum.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find what works best for your face shape.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.

FAQs About Short Hair and Face Shape

Will a pixie cut suit my face?

A pixie cut can look great on many different face shapes, but it's important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a short hairstyle.

Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and sophisticated to fun and playful.

Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

Depending on your face shape and the style of your haircut, a short hairstyle can either elongate or widen your face. It's important to choose a haircut that complements your features and proportions.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair may require less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs some TLC. Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to add moisture to your hair.

In conclusion, short hair can be a stylish and flattering choice for many different face shapes. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose a short hairstyle that enhances your natural beauty and complements your features. With the right styling products and hair care routine, your short haircut can look healthy and vibrant for months to come.
