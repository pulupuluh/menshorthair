Haircuts For A Round Face Female How To Choose The Perfect Cut

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As a round-faced female, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that complements your features. Your face shape is characterized by fullness in the cheeks and a rounded jawline, so you need a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and angles. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for a round face female, how to choose the perfect cut, step-by-step guides, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.

How to Choose the Perfect Haircut for a Round Face Female

When choosing a haircut for a round face female, you need to consider the following factors:

  • Length: The length of your hair can affect how your face appears. Long hair can elongate your face, while short hair can make it appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, but they need to be strategically placed to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help to balance out the proportions of your face, but they need to be cut in a way that flatters your face shape.
  • Volume: Adding too much volume to the sides of your face can make your face appear wider. Opt for hairstyles that create volume at the crown of your head instead.

The Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that can flatter a round face female. The key to pulling off a bob with a round face is to keep the length just above or below your chin and add volume at the crown of your head. This will create the illusion of length and angles.

Another way to add texture and movement to a bob is to add layers. However, the layers need to be kept long and subtle to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and low-maintenance hairstyle that can flatter a round face female. The key to pulling off a pixie cut with a round face is to keep the length short and add volume at the crown of your head. This will create the illusion of length and angles.

Another way to add texture and movement to a pixie cut is to add layers. However, the layers need to be kept long and subtle to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

Layers

Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, but they need to be strategically placed to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face. Opt for long, subtle layers that frame your face and add volume at the crown of your head.

Bangs

Bangs can help to balance out the proportions of your face, but they need to be cut in a way that flatters your face shape. Opt for long, side-swept bangs that elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Updos

Updos can be a great way to elongate your face and create the illusion of length and angles. Opt for updos that create volume at the crown of your head and keep the sides of your hair sleek and smooth.

Hair Care Tips for a Round Face Female

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is essential to pulling off any haircut. Here are some hair care tips for a round face female:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying, curling, or straightening your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to avoid damaging your hair while detangling.

FAQs

What haircut should I get if I have a round face?

The best haircuts for a round face female are those that create the illusion of length and angles. Opt for haircuts that keep the length just above or below your chin and add volume at the crown of your head. The bob haircut, pixie cut, and updos are great options.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt cuts, short curly hair, and heavy bangs. These hairstyles can make your face appear wider.

How do I style my hair for a round face?

When styling your hair for a round face, focus on creating volume at the crown of your head and keeping the sides of your hair sleek and smooth. Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying, and finish with a shine spray or serum to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. If you exercise regularly or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Can I color my hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a round face. However, choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color, and avoid colors that are too drastic or harsh. Always use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking vibrant and healthy.
