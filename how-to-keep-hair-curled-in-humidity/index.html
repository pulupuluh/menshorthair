How To Keep Hair Curled In Humidity Tips And Tricks

How to Keep Hair Curled in Humidity: Tips and Tricks
  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Humidity can be a curly girl’s worst nightmare. It can cause frizz, poof, and limp curls. But fear not! There are ways to keep your curls intact even in the most humid of climates. In this article, we’ll go over tips and tricks for how to keep hair curled in humidity.


How to

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to keeping your curls intact in humidity. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and that contain ingredients that help fight frizz, such as glycerin. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as alcohol can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.


2. Apply Products Correctly

Applying products correctly is just as important as using the right products. Apply your styling products to soaking wet hair, as this will help lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and defined. Scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to help enhance your curls.


3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a must-have tool for curly girls. It helps evenly distribute heat and prevent frizz. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair with the diffuser to help enhance your curls.


4. Avoid Touching Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more frizz you’ll create. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible. If you need to adjust your curls, use a bit of leave-in conditioner or water to help smooth them out.


5. Sleep with a Satin Pillowcase

Satin pillowcases help prevent friction, which can cause frizz and breakage. Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help keep your curls intact and prevent them from getting tangled overnight.


6. Refresh Your Curls

If your curls start to fall flat throughout the day, try refreshing them with a bit of water and leave-in conditioner. Spray your curls lightly with the mixture and scrunch them to help reshape them.


Step by step

Here’s a step-by-step guide for how to keep hair curled in humidity:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing styling product to your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible.
  6. Sleep on a satin pillowcase.
  7. Refresh your curls throughout the day with a water and leave-in conditioner mixture.

Tips

  • Avoid using heavy oils in humid weather, as they can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your curls, as this can disrupt their shape and cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Consider getting a haircut that’s specifically designed for curly hair, as this can help enhance your curls and make them easier to manage in humid weather.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hairspray to keep my curls intact in humidity?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to help keep your curls intact in humidity. Look for a hairspray that’s specifically designed for curly hair and that contains ingredients that help fight frizz.


Q: How often should I refresh my curls throughout the day?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it tends to lose its shape. Try refreshing your curls every 4-6 hours to help keep them looking their best.


Q: Can I use a hair oil to help keep my curls hydrated in humidity?

A: Yes, you can use a hair oil to help keep your curls hydrated in humidity. Look for a lightweight oil that won’t weigh down your curls or make them look greasy.


Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to keeping your curls intact in humidity. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a regular basis.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or relaxers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
