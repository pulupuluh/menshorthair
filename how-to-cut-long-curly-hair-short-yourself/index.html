How To Cut Long Curly Hair Short Yourself Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your long curly hair and ready for a drastic change? Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish short haircut. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting long curly hair short yourself. Keep reading for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Long Curly Hair Short Yourself

Cutting your own hair can seem like a daunting task, but it’s doable with the right preparation and tools. Follow these steps to cut your long curly hair short yourself:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

Step 2: Choose Your Short Haircut

There are many short haircuts to choose from, so decide on the style that suits you best. Look for inspiration online or consult with a hairstylist for advice.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to prepare it for cutting. Dry your hair with a towel or diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to maintain your curls.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the back section, working your way up to the top and sides. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut small sections at a time.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

As you cut your hair, use the mirror to check your progress. Make sure the length and style are even on both sides.

Step 7: Refine Your Haircut

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, refine your haircut by trimming any uneven strands or adjusting the length. Take your time and be patient.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Style your hair by using a curling iron, diffuser attachment, or styling cream to define your curls and create volume.

Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair Short Yourself

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your long curly hair short yourself:

  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. Start with small sections and trim gradually.
  • Invest in a good pair of haircutting scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your own hair requires focus and attention to detail.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you’re unsure about the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long curly hair short yourself:

Can I cut my own curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair if you have the right tools and techniques. Be patient and start with small sections to achieve the best results.

What’s the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair depends on your face shape and hair type. Consult with a hairstylist for advice on the best short haircut for you.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

You should cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short curly hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

Now that you know how to cut long curly hair short yourself, you can take the plunge and transform your look. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for the best results. Happy cutting!
