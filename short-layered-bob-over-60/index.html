Short Layered Bob Over 60 A Perfect Haircut For Older Women

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, you might want to consider a short layered bob. This haircut is perfect for older women who want to look stylish and sophisticated. In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about getting a short layered bob over 60, from its description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description of Short Layered Bob Over 60

A short layered bob over 60 is a haircut that features layers of different lengths, starting from the crown of the head and tapering down to the nape of the neck. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, giving it a youthful and vibrant look. The length of the bob can vary, but it is usually cut just above the shoulders or chin. This haircut is perfect for older women who want to look chic and modern without sacrificing their comfort.

How to Get a Short Layered Bob Over 60

Getting a short layered bob over 60 is relatively easy. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose a Bob Style

There are many types of bob styles to choose from, such as asymmetrical bob, graduated bob, blunt bob, and choppy bob. Choose one that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bob style for you. They will also advise you on the appropriate length and layers for your hair.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before going to the salon. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut and style your hair.

Step 4: Get Your Haircut

Sit back and relax while your hairstylist cuts your hair. They will create layers to add volume and texture to your hair. They may also add bangs if you like.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Your hairstylist will style your hair using a blow dryer and styling products. They will also give you tips on how to maintain your hairstyle at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Layered Bob Over 60

Here are some tips on how to maintain your short layered bob over 60:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ About Short Layered Bob Over 60

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layered bob over 60:

Q: Is a short layered bob suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a short layered bob can be tailored to suit any face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bob style for you.

Q: Can I add highlights to my short layered bob?

A: Yes, highlights can add dimension and depth to your short layered bob. Consult with your hairstylist for the best color that suits you.

Q: Can I wear a short layered bob if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, a short layered bob can add volume and texture to thin hair. Consult with your hairstylist for the best layering technique that suits your hair.

Hair Care for Short Layered Bob Over 60

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a short layered bob over 60. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, preferably sulfate-free.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it. If you need to use a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Overall, a short layered bob over 60 is a perfect haircut for older women who want to look chic and stylish. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bob style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Follow our tips and hair care advice to maintain your short layered bob and keep it healthy and shiny.
