How To Know If Short Hair Suits You

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short hair can be a bold and daring look that can make you feel confident and empowered. However, not everyone can pull off a short hairstyle. If you're considering going short but are unsure if it will suit you, then this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover the essential factors to consider to help you determine if short hair is the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Face Shape
  2. Hair Texture
  3. Lifestyle
  4. Personal Style
  5. Hair Care
  6. FAQ

Face Shape

The first factor to consider when determining if short hair suits you is your face shape. Certain short hairstyles will complement your facial features, while others may not. The general rule of thumb is to choose a hairstyle that balances your face shape.

For example, if you have a round face, then a short pixie cut with lots of volume on top can create the illusion of a longer face. On the other hand, if you have an angular or square face, then a short bob with soft layers can soften your features.

It's essential to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short hairstyle for your face shape.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another crucial factor to consider when determining if short hair suits you. Some short hairstyles require a specific hair texture to look their best. For example, if you have fine hair, then a short bob with blunt ends can create the illusion of thicker hair.

On the other hand, if you have curly hair, then a short pixie cut can be challenging to maintain and style. It's essential to consider your hair texture before going short since certain hairstyles may require more maintenance and styling.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another essential factor to consider when determining if short hair suits you. Short hair can be a low-maintenance and practical option for those who lead an active lifestyle or don't have a lot of time to style their hair.

However, if you're used to having long hair and enjoy styling it, then going short may not be the best option for you. It's essential to consider your daily routine and how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling and maintaining your hair.

Personal Style

Your personal style is another crucial factor to consider when determining if short hair suits you. Short hair can be a bold and daring look that can complement certain styles, such as edgy or minimalist fashion.

However, if you have a more classic or feminine style, then a short hairstyle may not be the best choice for you. It's essential to consider your personal style and how a short hairstyle will fit in with your overall aesthetic.

Hair Care

Short hair requires different hair care than long hair. You may need to change your hair care routine and products to maintain healthy and vibrant short hair. It's essential to consider the maintenance and care required for a short hairstyle before making the chop.

Consult with a hairstylist or do some research to determine the best hair care routine and products for your short hair.
