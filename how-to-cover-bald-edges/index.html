How To Cover Bald Edges A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Feb 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're struggling with bald edges, you're not alone. Many people, particularly women of color, experience hair loss around the hairline due to tight hairstyles, chemical damage, and other factors. But don't worry, there are several ways to cover bald edges and promote hair growth. In this article, we'll share some effective tips and tricks to help you achieve a fuller, healthier hairline.

How to Cover Bald Edges

There are several options for covering bald edges, including hairstyles, hair products, and accessories. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Hairstyles

One of the easiest ways to cover bald edges is to opt for hairstyles that conceal the hairline. Here are some styles that work well:

  • Braids: Box braids, cornrows, and other braided styles can cover bald edges and protect your hair.
  • Buns: A high or low bun can hide a receding hairline and add volume to your hair.
  • Weaves: A weave or wig can cover bald spots and give you a full, glamorous look.

Hair Products

Using the right hair products can also help cover bald edges and promote hair growth. Here are some products to consider:

  • Edge control: A good edge control product can smooth and hold down your edges, making them less noticeable.
  • Scalp treatments: Massaging your scalp with oils or serums can stimulate hair growth and reduce hair loss.
  • Hair fibers: Hair fibers are tiny particles that cling to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

Accessories

Finally, accessories can be a great way to cover bald edges and add some flair to your hairstyle. Here are some options:

  • Headbands: A wide headband can cover your hairline and add some style to your look.
  • Hats: A cute hat or beanie can conceal bald spots and protect your hair from the elements.
  • Scarves: A colorful scarf can be tied around your head to cover bald spots and add a pop of color to your outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide to Covering Bald Edges

Now that you know your options, here's a step-by-step guide to covering bald edges:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that conceals your hairline, such as braids or a bun.
  2. Apply edge control or hair fibers to smooth and thicken your edges.
  3. Massage your scalp with a scalp treatment to promote hair growth.
  4. Accessorize with a headband, hat, or scarf to cover any remaining bald spots.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to covering bald edges, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage and promote growth. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hairline.
  • Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with oils or leave-in conditioners.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair health.

FAQs

How long does it take for hair to grow back?

It depends on the cause of your hair loss, but it can take several months to a year for hair to fully grow back.

Can bald edges be reversed?

Yes, with the right care and treatment, bald edges can be reversed and new hair can grow back.

Are there any natural remedies for bald edges?

Yes, some natural remedies for bald edges include massaging your scalp with essential oils, using castor oil as a hair mask, and taking hair growth supplements.

Hair Care: Wrapping Up

Covering bald edges can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a fuller, healthier hairline. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent further damage and promote growth. With patience and persistence, you can achieve the hair of your dreams.
