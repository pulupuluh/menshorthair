How Do You Cut Curtain Bangs Step By Step

Written by Oliver May 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try? Curtain bangs might be the perfect choice for you! This trendy hairstyle features long, wispy bangs that frame the face and create a soft, feminine look. But how do you cut curtain bangs step by step? In this article, we’ll guide you through the process and give you some tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curtain Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

How to Cut Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curtain bangs:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be difficult to cut accurately.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Consider your face shape when deciding how long to cut your bangs. Longer bangs can elongate a round face, while shorter bangs can balance out a long face.
  • Be conservative when cutting your bangs. You can always trim more later, but it’s difficult to fix a mistake if you cut too much.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own curtain bangs:

  1. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be at the center of your forehead, and the base should be at your temples.
  2. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers in the center of your forehead.
  3. Use the scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the center of your forehead and working outward toward your temples.
  4. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure to keep the length and angle consistent with the first side.
  5. Trim any stray hairs or uneven sections until you’re satisfied with the length and shape of your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curtain Bangs

Now that you’ve perfected your curtain bangs, here are some tips for keeping them looking great:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your bangs to keep them smooth and shiny.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.
  • If you’re in a hurry or don’t have time to style your bangs, pin them back with bobby pins or a headband.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes! With a little practice and patience, you can cut your own curtain bangs at home.

What face shape is best suited for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can work on any face shape, but longer bangs can elongate a round face, while shorter bangs can balance out a long face.

How do I style my curtain bangs?

Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to keep them smooth and shiny.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs require a little extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your bangs to keep them hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Protect your bangs from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair spray.

Now that you know how to cut curtain bangs step by step, you can try out this trendy hairstyle for yourself. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a soft, feminine look that frames your face beautifully.
