What Hair Colours Would Suit Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Match

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and wondering what other shades would look good on you? Choosing a new hair colour can be daunting, especially with so many options available. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this guide, we'll walk you through how to choose the right hair colour for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Matching Your Eye Colour
  • Considering Your Personal Style
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour
  • FAQs About Choosing a New Hair Colour

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right hair colour for you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. A simple way to figure out your skin tone is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start to narrow down your hair colour options. If you have a cool skin tone, you'll look best in hair colours with cool undertones, like ash blonde or cool brown. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll look best in hair colours with warm undertones, like golden blonde or chestnut brown. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off a wide range of hair colours.

Matching Your Eye Colour

Another factor to consider when choosing a new hair colour is your eye colour. Certain hair colours can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural beauty. For example, if you have blue eyes, you might want to try a cool blonde or a rich brown to make them stand out. If you have green eyes, a warm red or copper hair colour can bring out the green in your eyes.

However, keep in mind that you don't have to match your hair colour to your eye colour. Sometimes, contrasting colours can create a striking and unique look. So, don't be afraid to experiment!

Considering Your Personal Style

Of course, your personal style should also be taken into account when choosing a new hair colour. Do you prefer a natural, low-maintenance look? Or do you like to stand out with bold and vibrant colours? Your hair colour should reflect your personal style and make you feel confident and beautiful.

If you're not sure what hair colour would suit your style, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preferences.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you've found your perfect hair colour, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair colour:

  • Use colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Protect your hair from UV rays with a hat or hair product
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your colour looking fresh

FAQs About Choosing a New Hair Colour

Q. How do I know if a hair colour will look good on me?

A. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style, you can narrow down your options and find a hair colour that will flatter your features and suit your preferences.

Q. Can I dye my hair at home, or should I go to a salon?

A. While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend going to a salon for the best results. A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right hair colour and ensure that the dye is applied evenly and safely.

Q. How often do I need to touch up my hair colour?

A. This depends on the type of hair colour you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Some hair colours, like highlights or balayage, can go several months between touch-ups. Other colours, like bright fashion colours, may fade quickly and require more frequent touch-ups.

We hope this guide has helped you determine what hair colours would suit you best. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy hair colouring!
