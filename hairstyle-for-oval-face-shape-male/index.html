Hairstyles For Oval Face Shape Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. In this guide, we'll take you through the best hairstyles for oval face shape male, along with tips and tricks for maintaining your hair.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the jawline. Men with oval face shape can experiment with a range of hairstyles, from short to long and everything in between. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for oval face shape male is all about balancing the proportions of your face. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight, such as buzz cuts or crew cuts, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider a side-parted hairstyle, as it can help to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find what works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape male:

1. Short Sides, Long Top

This hairstyle features short sides and a longer top, which can be styled in different ways, such as a slick back or messy fringe. It adds volume to the top of your head and creates a balanced look.

2. Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that features short sides and a longer top. It can be styled in different ways, such as a side part or slick back. This hairstyle adds volume to the top of your head and creates a sharp look.

3. Medium Length

Medium length hairstyles, such as a textured crop or messy fringe, are great for oval face shapes. They add volume to the top of your head and create a balanced look.

4. Long Hair

Men with oval face shape can pull off long hair, whether it's a messy bun or shoulder-length locks. Long hair adds volume to the top of your head and creates a laid-back look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as pomade or wax, to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

What hairstyles should oval face shape male avoid?

Oval face shape male should avoid hairstyles that are too tight, such as buzz cuts or crew cuts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Can oval face shape male pull off long hair?

Yes, oval face shape male can pull off long hair, whether it's a messy bun or shoulder-length locks. Long hair adds volume to the top of your head and creates a laid-back look.

What hair products should oval face shape male use?

Oval face shape male should experiment with different hair products, such as pomade or wax, to find what works best for their hair type.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals, as they are essential for healthy hair.

With these tips, you can find the perfect hairstyle for oval face shape male and keep your hair looking its best. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
