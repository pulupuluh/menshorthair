How Does Diamond Shape Look Like

Physical Characteristics of Diamond Shape

Diamonds are one of the most precious and sought-after gemstones in the world. They are known for their exceptional hardness, brilliance, and fire. The physical characteristics of diamond shape are:

  • Four sides
  • Pointed corners
  • Shallow depth
  • Facets on the top and bottom

The facets on a diamond are the small flat surfaces that reflect light and create the diamond's sparkle. The number of facets on a diamond can vary depending on the cut of the diamond. The most common cut for diamonds is the round brilliant cut, which has 58 facets. Other popular cuts include the princess cut, emerald cut, and pear cut.

How to Identify Diamond Shape

The easiest way to identify diamond shape is by looking at its physical characteristics. If you see a gemstone that has four sides, pointed corners, and a shallow depth, it is likely a diamond. You can also use a jeweler's loupe or a microscope to examine the facets on the diamond. If the facets are symmetrical and evenly spaced, it is a well-cut diamond.

Another way to identify diamond shape is by looking at the diamond's certificate. A diamond certificate is a document that provides information about the diamond's characteristics, including its shape, cut, clarity, and color. If you are buying a diamond, make sure to ask for its certificate.

Tips for Diamond Care

Diamonds are durable, but they still require proper care to maintain their beauty and value. Here are some tips for diamond care:

  • Store your diamond jewelry in a soft pouch or a separate compartment in your jewelry box to prevent scratches.
  • Clean your diamond jewelry regularly with a mild detergent and a soft brush.
  • Avoid wearing your diamond jewelry while doing household chores or engaging in physical activities.
  • Take your diamond jewelry to a professional jeweler for maintenance and repairs.

FAQ About Diamonds

What is the hardest substance on earth?

Diamonds are the hardest substance on earth, with a rating of 10 on the Mohs scale of mineral hardness.

What is the most expensive diamond in the world?

The most expensive diamond in the world is the Pink Star, which sold for $71.2 million at a Sotheby's auction in 2017.

What is the best cut for a diamond?

The best cut for a diamond depends on personal preference, but the round brilliant cut is the most popular and has the most sparkle.

How are diamonds formed?

Diamonds are formed deep within the earth's mantle under high pressure and high temperature. They are brought to the surface through volcanic eruptions.

What is the 4 Cs of diamond quality?

The 4 Cs of diamond quality are cut, clarity, color, and carat weight. These factors determine a diamond's value and beauty.

Can diamonds break?

Diamonds are very hard and durable, but they can still break or chip if subjected to a strong impact or force.
