Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair Round Face A Guide To A Stylish And Flattering Look

Written by Robby Jul 20, 2023 Â· 12 min read

If you have wavy hair and a round face, finding a short haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural texture can be a challenge. However, with the right guidance and inspiration, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that complements your face shape and hair type. In this guide, we will explore the best short haircuts for wavy hair round face, along with tips on how to style and care for your new hairdo.

Why Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Round Face?

Short haircuts are an excellent option for wavy hair and round face as they help to balance and elongate your face shape while enhancing your natural texture. Short haircuts also require less maintenance and styling time, making them a practical and stylish choice for busy women who want to look their best without sacrificing their valuable time.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Wavy Hair Round Face?

When choosing a short haircut for wavy hair round face, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. The following are some tips to guide you:

Consider Your Face Shape

Round faces typically have soft and curved features, such as a round chin and full cheeks. The goal of a short haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. Therefore, choose a haircut that adds height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks and chin. A layered and asymmetrical bob, pixie cut, or angled bob are excellent options for a round face.

Take Your Hair Type into Account

Wavy hair tends to have a mind of its own and can be challenging to tame. Therefore, opt for a short haircut that enhances your natural texture and adds movement and volume. A layered or choppy haircut is ideal for wavy hair as it helps to create a more defined and structured look while making styling more manageable.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a short haircut that fits your lifestyle and personality. If you're always on the go, opt for a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling time. If you're more daring and love to experiment with different looks, go for a bold and edgy cut that reflects your personality.

1. Layered Bob

2. Pixie Cut

3. Angled Bob

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Round Face

Styling short haircuts for wavy hair round face can be a breeze with the right techniques and products. Here are some tips to help:

Use the Right Products

Use products that enhance your natural texture and add volume and movement to your hair. A volumizing mousse, sea salt spray, or texturizing spray are excellent options for wavy hair.

Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume and movement to your hair while creating a more defined and structured look. Use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture and create bouncy and playful curls.

Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural texture and work with it instead of against it. Wavy hair looks best when it's left to air dry or styled with minimal heat. Use your fingers to create loose and natural curls or waves that enhance your features.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Round Face

Short haircuts for wavy hair round face require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished. Use a hair mask or oil treatment to restore moisture and shine.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible to prevent damage and breakage. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

In conclusion, short haircuts for wavy hair round face can be a stylish and flattering option for women who want a low-maintenance and practical hairstyle. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, enhances your natural texture, and fits your lifestyle and personality. With the right styling techniques and products, you can achieve a chic and effortless look that brings out the best in you.
