How To Make Flat Hair Fluffy

Written by Bardi Mar 31, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make flat hair fluffy. From hair care to styling techniques, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we get into styling techniques, it's important to take care of your hair. Healthy hair is more voluminous and easier to style. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo: Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen. These ingredients can help strengthen your hair and add volume.

2. Condition your hair: Use a lightweight conditioner that won't weigh your hair down. Apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

3. Don't over-wash: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and flatness. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every third day.

4. Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair and make it look flat. Try to air-dry your hair as much as possible and use heat styling tools sparingly.

How To

Now that you've taken care of your hair, it's time to add some volume. Here's how to make flat hair fluffy:

1. Blow-dry upside down: Flip your hair upside down and blow-dry it using a round brush. This technique will help lift your roots and add volume.

2. Use a volumizing mousse: Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your roots, then blow-dry your hair as usual. The mousse will help add lift and hold.

3. Tease your hair: Take small sections of hair and tease them at the roots using a fine-tooth comb. This technique will create volume and texture.

4. Use a dry shampoo: If your hair is looking flat and oily, spray some dry shampoo at the roots. The dry shampoo will absorb excess oil and add volume.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make flat hair fluffy:

1. Wash your hair: Use a volumizing shampoo and lightweight conditioner to clean your hair.

2. Apply volumizing mousse: Apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your roots, then comb it through your hair.

3. Blow-dry upside down: Flip your hair upside down and blow-dry it using a round brush. Focus on lifting your roots and adding volume.

4. Tease your hair: Take small sections of hair and tease them at the roots using a fine-tooth comb. Continue until you've teased all of your hair.

5. Finish with hairspray: Spray some hairspray all over your hair to hold the volume in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your hair look even more voluminous:

1. Sleep with a bun: Before going to bed, put your hair in a loose bun on the top of your head. This technique will create natural volume and waves.

2. Use a diffuser: If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume.

3. Get regular trims: Split ends can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and voluminous.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a volumizing shampoo every day?

A: It's best to use a volumizing shampoo every other day or every third day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Will teasing my hair damage it?

A: Teasing your hair can cause some damage, but if you do it sparingly and use a fine-tooth comb, the damage will be minimal.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting flat throughout the day?

A: Use a lightweight hairspray or dry shampoo throughout the day to refresh your hair and add volume.

Hair Care

By following these tips and techniques, you can make flat hair fluffy and voluminous. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid damaging it with excessive heat styling. With a little bit of effort, you can have the bouncy, voluminous hair of your dreams!
