How To Cut The Back Of Women s Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to cut the back of your short hair, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your short hair, along with some tips and FAQ's to ensure that you get the best results possible. So, whether you're a beginner or an experienced hairdresser, read on to learn more.

Description

Cutting the back of women's short hair can be a daunting task for beginners. The hair at the back of the head is hard to see, and it can be difficult to get the right angle and length. However, with the right tools, technique, and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking cut that you'll be proud of. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to cut the back of your short hair, including how to section your hair, the tools you'll need, and the steps to take to achieve the perfect cut.

How to Cut the Back of Women's Short Hair

Step 1: Section Your Hair

The first step to cutting the back of your short hair is to section it off. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting at the back of your head, just above the nape of your neck. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way, so it doesn't get in the way while you're cutting the back.

Step 2: Choose Your Tools

Next, you'll need to choose the right tools for the job. A pair of sharp hairdressing scissors is essential, as is a comb to help you guide your scissors. You may also want to use a mirror, so you can see the back of your head while you're cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Once you've sectioned your hair and chosen your tools, it's time to start cutting. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head, and comb it straight down. Using your scissors, make small, snipping motions to cut the hair to the desired length. Be sure to cut the hair evenly, and don't cut too much at once, as it's easier to take more off than to add it back.

Step 4: Move On to the Next Section

After you've cut the first section, move on to the next one, using your comb and scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Continue working your way up the back of your head, section by section, until you've cut all the hair at the back.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting the back of your short hair, it's time to check your work. Use a mirror to check the length and angle of the cut, and make any adjustments as necessary. Don't be afraid to take a little more off if needed, but be careful not to overdo it.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Women's Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when cutting the back of your short hair:

  • Use small, snipping motions with your scissors to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Take your time, and don't rush the process.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional hairdresser.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut the back of my short hair myself?

A: Yes, you can cut the back of your short hair yourself, but it can be challenging for beginners. It's essential to take your time and follow the steps outlined in this guide to achieve the best results.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting the back of my short hair?

A: If you make a mistake while cutting the back of your short hair, don't panic. You can always make adjustments by cutting a little more hair or using styling products to cover up any unevenness.

Hair Care After Cutting the Back of Women's Short Hair

After cutting the back of your short hair, it's essential to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

By following these tips and steps, you can cut the back of your short hair with confidence and achieve the perfect look. Whether you're cutting your hair for the first time or are an experienced hairdresser, remember to take your time, be patient, and enjoy the process!
