How To Relax Curly Hair For Black Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Apr 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to relax curly hair for black guys. If you're tired of your natural curls, then it's time to switch things up and try a new look. Relaxing your hair is a great way to achieve a straight, sleek style that is easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about relaxing your curly hair as a black man.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Relax Curly Hair for Black Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

Description

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially for black guys. Relaxing your hair is an option that many black men choose to straighten their hair. Hair relaxing involves using a chemical treatment to break down the protein bonds in the hair, making it straight. However, relaxing your hair requires a lot of maintenance and upkeep to keep it looking healthy and strong.

How to Relax Curly Hair for Black Guys

Before you start the relaxing process, it is important to understand that hair relaxing can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. Therefore, it is recommended to seek professional help from a hair stylist who has experience with hair relaxing. However, if you decide to do it yourself, follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather Materials

You will need to purchase a hair relaxer kit, gloves, a comb, a timer, a neutralizing shampoo, and a deep conditioner. Make sure to read the instructions on the kit before you start the process.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before applying the relaxer, make sure your hair is clean and free of any oils or products. Part your hair into sections and apply petroleum jelly to your scalp to protect it from the relaxer.

Step 3: Apply the Relaxer

Follow the instructions on the kit and apply the relaxer to each section of your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Apply the relaxer evenly, making sure not to overlap the product onto previously relaxed hair.

Step 4: Time the Relaxer

Set a timer for the recommended time on the kit. It is important not to leave the relaxer on longer than the recommended time as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Step 5: Rinse and Neutralize

After the recommended time, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Apply the neutralizing shampoo to your hair and massage it in for a few minutes. Rinse out the shampoo and repeat the process until the water runs clear.

Step 6: Deep Condition

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and let it sit for at least 15 minutes. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water to close the cuticles and seal in the moisture.

Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when relaxing your curly hair:

  • Do not relax your hair too often as this can cause damage
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as this can cause further damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip the natural oils from your hair

FAQs

Q: How often should I relax my hair?

A: It is recommended to relax your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Can I relax my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can relax your hair at home, but it is recommended to seek professional help to prevent damage to your hair and scalp.

Q: Will relaxing my hair make it grow faster?

A: No, relaxing your hair will not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by genetics and other factors such as diet and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

After relaxing your hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few hair care tips for relaxed hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Trim your hair regularly
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly

Conclusion

Relaxing your curly hair as a black guy can be a great way to switch up your style. However, it is important to understand that hair relaxing can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. Follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to keep your relaxed hair healthy and strong. Remember to seek professional help if you are unsure about the process.
