How To Make Party Hairstyles For Long Hair At Home

Written by Oliver Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle for every party? Do you want to make a statement with your long hair? Look no further, because we have got you covered! In this blog post, we will teach you how to make party hairstyles for long hair at home. Whether you want to keep it simple or go all out with an elaborate updo, we have got something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: How to
  • Section 2: Step by Step
  • Section 3: Tips
  • Section 4: FAQ
  • Section 5: Hair Care

Section 1: How to

Before we get into the hairstyles, it is important to know how to prep your hair for styling. Start with freshly washed and blow-dried hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Then, section your hair and start styling!

Section 2: Step by Step

1. Braided Crown

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it. Do the same on the other side. Then, cross the braids over each other at the back of your head and pin them in place. Finish with hairspray.

2. Twisted Bun

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around its base to create a bun. Pin it in place and finish with hairspray.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

Take the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Tease the ponytail to add volume. Finish with hairspray.

4. Messy Updo

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around its base to create a messy bun. Pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face. Finish with hairspray.

Section 3: Tips

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them.
  • Create texture in your hair by using a curling iron or a straightener.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with hairpins, headbands, or flowers.

Section 4: FAQ

1. Can I do these hairstyles on shorter hair?

Some of these hairstyles might not work on shorter hair. However, you can always modify them to suit your hair length.

2. How long will these hairstyles last?

With the right products and techniques, these hairstyles can last for hours.

3. How can I make my hair look fuller?

You can use volumizing products like mousse or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.

Section 5: Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends. And lastly, eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to make party hairstyles for long hair at home, it's time to try them out! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles. Happy styling!
