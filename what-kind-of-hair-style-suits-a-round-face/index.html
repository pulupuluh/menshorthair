What Kind Of Hair Style Suits A Round Face

Written by Frank Feb 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, it can be difficult to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, there are plenty of options to choose from that will complement your face shape. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for round faces, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Description: Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to understand what makes a round face. Round faces typically have a wider forehead, rounded cheekbones, and a rounded chin. The goal of a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face and create more definition in the cheekbones and jawline.

How To: Choosing the Right Hairstyle

There are a few key things to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head
  • Avoid styles that add width to the sides of the face
  • Choose styles that create angles and definition in the cheekbones and jawline

Step by Step: Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best styles for round faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces because they create movement and texture without adding width to the sides of the face. Opt for layers that start below the chin to create angles and definition in the jawline.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round faces because they create a diagonal line across the forehead, which helps to elongate the face. Avoid blunt bangs, which can make the face appear rounder.

Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great option for round faces because it adds height to the crown of the head, which helps to elongate the face. To create a messy bun, simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and wrap it around the base, securing with bobby pins.

Tips: Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it nourished and hydrated
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of haircut makes your face look thinner?

Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as long layers or a messy bun, can help to elongate the face and create a thinner appearance.

Can I wear my hair in a bob if I have a round face?

A bob can be a great option for round faces, as long as it is cut at the right length. Opt for a longer bob that falls just below the chin to create angles and definition in the jawline.

What is the best way to style curly hair for a round face?

Curly hair can be tricky to style for round faces, as it tends to add width to the sides of the face. Opt for long layers that add movement and texture, and avoid styles that are too voluminous.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right guidance, it's easy to find a style that flatters your features. Remember to maintain healthy hair by washing and conditioning regularly, avoiding heat tools, and getting regular trims. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to have healthy, beautiful hair that complements your face shape.
