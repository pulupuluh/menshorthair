Best Short Hair For Oval Face Flattering Cuts For 2023

Written by Petter Mar 03, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to finding the best short hair for oval face shapes, the options are endless. With an oval face, you have the freedom to experiment with different styles, whether you prefer a pixie cut or a bob. To help you find the perfect cut, we’ve put together a guide to the most flattering short hairstyles for oval face shapes. From choppy layers to blunt bobs, here are our top picks for 2023.

Description

An oval face shape is defined by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. This balanced shape means that you can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, from short and choppy to long and sleek. The key is to choose a cut that highlights your best features and flatters your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair for Oval Face Shapes

When choosing a short hairstyle for oval face shapes, consider your hair texture and personal style. Are you looking for a low-maintenance cut that you can style quickly in the morning? Or do you prefer a more polished look that requires styling tools and products? Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture: If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob can help tame unruly strands.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you’re always on the go, a wash-and-go style may be best. If you have more time to style your hair, a sleek, polished look may be more your style.
  • Play up your best features: If you have a prominent jawline, a chin-length bob can draw attention to this area. If you have beautiful eyes, consider a pixie cut that highlights your face.

Step by Step Guide: How to Style the Best Short Hair for Oval Face Shapes

Once you’ve chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face shape, it’s important to know how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving some of our favorite looks:

Short Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair in small sections.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax to the ends of your hair to create texture and definition.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair to add texture and definition.
  3. Using a blow dryer and a round brush, style your hair away from your face.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax to the ends of your hair to create a piecey look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some upkeep to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best short hair for oval face shapes?

The best short hair for oval face shapes depends on your personal style and hair texture. Some of our favorite cuts include a short bob, pixie cut, and lob.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Absolutely! Oval faces can pull off a wide range of bang styles, from blunt bangs to side-swept fringe. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

Do I need to use styling products on short hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. If you have fine hair, a volumizing mousse can help add body and texture. If you have thick hair, a styling cream can help tame unruly strands. Talk to your stylist about which products are best for your hair.

Conclusion

With so many short hairstyles to choose from, finding the perfect cut for your oval face shape can feel overwhelming. But with a little experimentation and some expert styling tips, you can find a style that flatters your face and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you prefer a sleek bob or a playful pixie cut, the key is to choose a style that highlights your best features and fits your personal style.
