How To Get Curly Bangs To Lay Flat A Step By Step Guide

Are you struggling to get your curly bangs to lay flat? We know the struggle all too well. Curly bangs can be unpredictable, but with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve the sleek, smooth look you desire. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of getting curly bangs to lay flat, step by step. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Curly Bangs to Lay Flat
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Keeping Curly Bangs Flat

How to Get Curly Bangs to Lay Flat

There are a few things you can do to get your curly bangs to lay flat:

  • Use a flat iron
  • Blow dry your bangs
  • Use a hair straightening cream
  • Wrap your bangs while you sleep

Of course, the method you choose will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Let's take a closer look at each of these methods.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Use a Flat Iron

The first method to try is using a flat iron. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of your bangs and clamp the flat iron at the roots.
  4. Gently pull the flat iron down the length of your bangs, being careful not to burn yourself.
  5. Repeat until all of your bangs are straightened.
  6. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Using a flat iron is a quick and easy way to get your curly bangs to lay flat. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Bangs

Another method to try is blow drying your bangs. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs, pulling them straight as you go.
  4. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Blow drying your bangs is a great option if you don't want to use a flat iron. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and be careful not to burn yourself with the blow dryer.

3. Use a Hair Straightening Cream

If you don't want to use heat on your hair, you can try using a hair straightening cream. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Apply a hair straightening cream to your bangs.
  4. Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs, pulling them straight as you go.
  5. Apply a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Hair straightening creams can be a great option for those who want to avoid heat styling. Just be sure to choose a cream that is designed for your hair type and follow the instructions carefully.

4. Wrap Your Bangs While You Sleep

If you want to avoid heat styling altogether, you can try wrapping your bangs while you sleep. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Apply a light hold styling cream to your bangs.
  4. Wrap your bangs around your head, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. Sleep with your hair wrapped overnight.
  6. Remove the bobby pins and gently comb through your bangs in the morning.

Wrapping your hair while you sleep can take some getting used to, but it's a great option if you want to avoid heat styling. Just be sure to use a light hold styling cream to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Keeping Curly Bangs Flat

Once you've gotten your curly bangs to lay flat, here are a few tips to help keep them that way:

  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.
  • Avoid humidity and rain, which can cause your hair to frizz.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to avoid friction while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

With a little bit of effort, you can keep your curly bangs looking sleek and smooth all day long.

FAQ

Can I get my curly bangs to lay flat without using heat?

Yes, you can try using a hair straightening cream or wrapping your hair while you sleep.

How often should I trim my bangs?

We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

What kind of hairspray should I use?

Use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Bangs

Finally, here are a few tips for caring for your curly bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

With a little bit of care, your curly bangs can look great every day.

Now that you know how to get curly bangs to lay flat, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to be patient and experiment with different methods until you find what works best for your hair type. Good luck!
