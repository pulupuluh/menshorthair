Is Rose Gold Hair Still In Style 2020 A Guide To The Trendiest Hair Color Of The Year

Introduction

When it comes to hair color trends, it seems like there's always something new and exciting to try. One of the most popular trends of recent years has been rose gold hair, a stunning shade that combines soft pink and warm gold tones. But with so many new trends emerging all the time, you may be wondering: is rose gold hair still in style in 2020?

What is Rose Gold Hair?

Rose gold hair is a beautiful blend of pink and gold tones that creates a warm, flattering hair color. It's a versatile shade that can be customized to suit different skin tones and hair types. Some people opt for a more subtle rose gold look, with just a hint of pink, while others go for a bolder, more vibrant shade.

How to Achieve Rose Gold Hair

If you're interested in trying out rose gold hair for yourself, there are a few different ways to achieve this trendy look. One option is to visit a professional hair stylist, who can help you find the perfect shade of rose gold and ensure that your hair stays healthy throughout the coloring process.

Another option is to try a DIY approach. There are a variety of at-home hair coloring kits available that can help you achieve a rose gold hue. However, it's important to note that coloring your hair at home can be tricky, and it's always best to consult with a professional if you're unsure about the process.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Rose Gold Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving rose gold hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Mix your hair color according to the instructions on the box or as directed by your stylist.
  4. Start applying the color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Leave the color on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  6. Apply a conditioning treatment to help nourish and protect your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Rose Gold Hair

Once you've achieved your perfect shade of rose gold hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-protecting shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sunlight, as this can also cause your color to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions about Rose Gold Hair

Here are some common questions people have about rose gold hair:

Q: Is rose gold hair still in style in 2020?

A: Yes! Rose gold hair is still a popular trend in 2020 and shows no signs of slowing down.

Q: Is rose gold hair suitable for every skin tone?

A: Yes, rose gold hair can be customized to suit different skin tones.

Q: How long does rose gold hair color last?

A: The longevity of rose gold hair color depends on a variety of factors, including your hair type, how well you take care of your hair, and how often you wash it. Generally, rose gold hair color can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months.

Conclusion

So, is rose gold hair still in style in 2020? The answer is a resounding yes! Whether you opt for a subtle, barely-there shade or a bold, vibrant hue, rose gold hair is a beautiful and trendy choice that's sure to turn heads. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant for as long as possible.
