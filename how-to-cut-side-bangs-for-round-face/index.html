How To Cut Side Bangs For Round Face

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be looking for a way to add some dimension and style to your look. One way to do this is by cutting side bangs. Not only do side bangs add interest to your hairstyle, but they can also help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of cutting side bangs for round face and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks.

Description

Side bangs are a great way to add some style and dimension to your hair. They can help to frame your face and accentuate your best features. However, if you have a round face, it's important to cut your side bangs in a way that flatters your face shape. By following a few simple steps, you can create side bangs that complement your round face and enhance your overall look.

How to Cut Side Bangs for Round Face

The process of cutting side bangs for round face is relatively simple. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length of your side bangs. For a round face, it's best to cut them just below the eyebrows. This will help to elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section off the hair that you want to cut into side bangs. Use the comb to create a triangular section of hair that starts at the top of your head and ends at the outer corner of your eye.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, keeping the hair taut. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, just below the eyebrows. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully, making small cuts to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Check your work in the mirror to make sure your side bangs are even and at the right length. If they are too long, you can always go back and trim them a bit more.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting side bangs for round face:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any jagged or uneven cuts.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry, as wet hair can shrink and alter the length.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time, and then gradually cut more if needed.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own side bangs, consider visiting a professional hair stylist.

FAQ

How do I know if side bangs will look good on me?

Side bangs can look good on anyone, but it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, cutting side bangs just below the eyebrows can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my side bangs?

If you make a mistake while cutting your side bangs, don't panic. You can always go back and trim them a bit more. If you're really unhappy with how they turned out, you can always visit a professional hair stylist to fix them.

Hair Care

After cutting your side bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes washing your hair regularly, using conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a hair serum or leave-in conditioner to add shine and moisture to your hair.

Overall, cutting side bangs for round face can be a great way to add some style and dimension to your hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can create side bangs that flatter your face shape and enhance your overall look.
