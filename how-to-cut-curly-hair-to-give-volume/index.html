How To Cut Curly Hair To Give Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair falling flat and lacking volume? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to cut curly hair to give volume. By following these simple steps, you can transform your flat curls into voluminous, bouncy locks. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

How to Cut Curly Hair to Give Volume

The key to cutting curly hair for volume is to create layers. Layers create movement and volume, which will give your curls the bounce they need. When cutting curly hair, it's important to start with dry hair. This will allow you to see the natural shape of your curls and ensure that you don't cut too much off. Follow these steps to cut curly hair to give volume:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start by dividing your hair into four sections: front right, front left, back right, and back left. Secure each section with a clip or hair tie.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start with the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this step for the entire bottom layer of your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the next layer of hair and repeat the same process. Comb the hair straight down, hold it between your fingers, and cut it straight across. This layer should be shorter than the bottom layer, creating a graduated effect.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Release the top layer of your hair and repeat the same process as the previous steps. This layer should be the shortest and create the most volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting curly hair to give volume:

  1. Section your hair into four sections: front right, front left, back right, and back left.
  2. Cut the bottom layer of your hair straight across.
  3. Release the middle layer of your hair and cut it shorter than the bottom layer.
  4. Release the top layer of your hair and cut it the shortest to create the most volume.
  5. Blend the layers by cutting into them with scissors at a 45-degree angle.
  6. Trim any uneven or split ends.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Start with dry hair to see the natural shape of your curls.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. You can always go back and trim more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to ensure that your layers are even.
  • Experiment with different cutting techniques to find what works best for your curls.

FAQs About Cutting Curly Hair

Can I cut my curly hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair yourself. However, it's important to be cautious and start with small cuts. It's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist if you're unsure.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends and promote growth.

Do I need to use special products for curly hair?

Yes, curly hair requires special products to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter and avoid products with harsh chemicals like sulfates.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to prevent dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your curly hair to give volume and achieve those bouncy, voluminous curls you've always wanted. Don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for your curls. Happy cutting!
