Short Spiky Hairstyles For Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Short spiky hairstyles for guys have been in vogue for years, and they’re not going anywhere anytime soon. They’re easy to maintain, versatile, and can be styled in a variety of ways. If you’re looking for a new hairstyle, or just want to switch things up, then this guide is for you. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about short spiky hairstyles for guys, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and more.

What are short spiky hairstyles for guys?

Short spiky hairstyles for guys are characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, and longer hair on top that’s styled into spikes. The length of the hair on top can vary, depending on the look you’re going for. Some guys prefer shorter spikes, while others opt for longer, more dramatic spikes.

How to Style Short Spiky Hairstyles for Guys

Styling short spiky hairstyles for guys is relatively easy. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in styling short spiky hairstyles for guys is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. A hair gel or pomade works well for creating spikes. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve applied the product, it’s time to style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes. Start at the crown of your head and work your way to the front. Use a blow dryer on low heat to set the spikes in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your spikes stay in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Short Spiky Hairstyles for Guys

Maintaining short spiky hairstyles for guys is relatively easy. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Trim Your Hair Regularly: To keep your hair looking neat and tidy, it’s important to get regular haircuts. We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use Quality Hair Products: Using quality hair products can make a big difference in the appearance and health of your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid Heat Damage: Excessive heat styling can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of blow dryers, flat irons, and other heat styling tools.
  • Protect Your Hair from the Sun: UV rays can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with UV protection when you’re outside.

FAQ

1. What face shape is best for short spiky hairstyles for guys?

Short spiky hairstyles for guys can work well for a variety of face shapes, but they’re particularly flattering for guys with square or oval faces.

2. How long should my hair be for a short spiky hairstyle?

The length of your hair on top can vary, depending on the look you’re going for. Some guys prefer shorter spikes, while others opt for longer, more dramatic spikes. We recommend experimenting with different lengths to find the one that works best for you.

3. Can I style short spiky hairstyles for guys with curly hair?

Yes, you can style short spiky hairstyles for guys with curly hair. Use a styling product that’s designed for curly hair, and work the product through your hair with your fingers. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help set the curls in place.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash Your Hair: Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type.
  • Eat a Healthy Diet: A healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid Overstyling: Excessive heat styling, chemical treatments, and tight hairstyles can damage your hair over time.
  • Protect Your Hair: Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with UV protection when you’re outside to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, short spiky hairstyles for guys are a classic look that’s easy to maintain and style. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect short spiky hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong, and experiment with different lengths and styles to find the one that works best for you.
