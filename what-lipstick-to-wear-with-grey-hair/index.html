What Lipstick To Wear With Grey Hair

Written by Petter Apr 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you wondering what lipstick to wear with grey hair? As we age, our hair color changes, and so does our skin tone. It can be challenging to find the right lipstick shade to complement your grey hair. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll share some tips on what lipstick shades work best with grey hair.

Description

Grey hair can be beautiful and elegant, but it also requires a different approach when it comes to makeup. As we age, our skin loses its natural glow, and our lips become thinner. That's why it's essential to choose a lipstick shade that enhances your natural beauty and complements your grey hair color.

How to Choose the Right Lipstick Shade

Choosing the right lipstick shade for grey hair can be tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect lipstick shade:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining the right lipstick shade for you. If you have cool undertones, choose lipstick shades with blue or purple undertones. If you have warm undertones, opt for lipstick shades with orange or yellow undertones.

Think About Your Hair Color

Your hair color can also influence the lipstick shade you choose. If you have silver or white hair, opt for shades with blue or purple undertones. If you have salt and pepper hair, choose shades with brown or pink undertones.

Choose a Matte Formula

Matte lipstick formulas are perfect for mature skin. They don't bleed or feather, and they provide long-lasting coverage. Matte lipsticks also help to make your lips look fuller and more defined.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Lipstick Shade

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right lipstick shade:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they look greenish, you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Color

Look at the color of your hair. If it's silver or white, choose shades with blue or purple undertones. If it's salt and pepper, opt for shades with brown or pink undertones.

Step 3: Choose a Matte Formula

Choose a matte lipstick formula that provides long-lasting coverage without bleeding or feathering.

Tips for Wearing Lipstick with Grey Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you wear lipstick with grey hair:

Keep it Simple

Avoid bold or bright colors that can overpower your grey hair. Instead, opt for neutral shades that enhance your natural beauty.

Use a Lip Liner

Use a lip liner to define your lips and prevent your lipstick from bleeding or feathering.

Moisturize Your Lips

As we age, our lips become thinner and drier. That's why it's essential to moisturize your lips regularly to keep them looking plump and youthful.

FAQs

What Lipstick Shades Should I Avoid with Grey Hair?

Avoid shades that are too bright or bold, as they can overpower your grey hair. Also, avoid shades with orange or yellow undertones if you have cool undertones.

Can I Wear Glossy Lipstick with Grey Hair?

Glossy lipstick can be challenging to wear with mature skin, as it can highlight fine lines and wrinkles. However, if you want to wear glossy lipstick, choose a sheer formula that provides a subtle shine.

How Can I Make My Lips Look Fuller?

Use a lip liner to define your lips and create the illusion of fullness. You can also apply a lip plumper that contains hyaluronic acid or collagen to give your lips a boost.

Hair Care for Grey Hair

Finally, it's essential to take care of your grey hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a gentle shampoo that's free of sulfates and parabens to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat-styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what lipstick to wear with grey hair, you can confidently choose the perfect shade to complement your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair and skin to keep them looking radiant and youthful.
