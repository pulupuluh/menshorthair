What Is The Best Clip In Human Hair Extensions Brand In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of waiting for your hair to grow out? Do you want to add length and volume to your hair instantly? The answer is simple – clip in human hair extensions. But with so many brands out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one. That’s why we’ve done the research for you and found the top clip in human hair extensions brand for 2023.

Description

The best clip in human hair extensions brand for 2023 is Bellami Hair. Bellami Hair offers a wide range of clip in human hair extensions in various lengths, colors, and textures. Their extensions are made from high-quality, 100% Remy human hair, which means that the hair cuticles are aligned in the same direction, making the hair look natural and silky.

Bellami Hair also offers a unique feature called the "seamless" weft. This means that the weft is specially designed to lay flat against your head, making it virtually undetectable. This is a game-changer for those who want a natural-looking hairstyle without anyone knowing that they are wearing extensions.

How to Choose the Best Clip in Human Hair Extensions Brand

When choosing the best clip in human hair extensions brand, there are a few things to consider:

  • Quality of hair: Look for extensions made from 100% Remy human hair. This ensures that the extensions will look natural and blend seamlessly with your hair.
  • Length and color: Choose extensions that match your hair length and color. Bellami Hair offers a wide range of lengths and colors to choose from.
  • Weft design: Look for a weft that is thin and flexible, so that it lays flat against your head and is comfortable to wear.
  • Customer reviews: Read customer reviews to see what others have to say about the brand and their experience with the extensions.

Step by Step Guide to Applying Clip in Human Hair Extensions

Here is a step by step guide to applying clip in human hair extensions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off a small amount of hair at the nape of your neck and clip the rest of your hair up and out of the way.
  3. Take the smallest extension piece and clip it into the root of your hair at the back of your head.
  4. Release another section of your hair and clip the next extension piece in.
  5. Continue this process, working your way up the back of your head and then on the sides, until all the extension pieces are clipped in.

Tips for Maintaining Your Clip in Human Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for maintaining your clip in human hair extensions:

  • Brush your extensions: Brush your extensions daily to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Wash your extensions: Wash your extensions every 6-8 wears, using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat: Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store your extensions properly: Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use, preferably in their original packaging.

FAQs

Q: How long do clip in human hair extensions last?

A: Clip in human hair extensions can last up to a year or longer with proper care and maintenance.

Q: Can I color my clip in human hair extensions?

A: Bellami Hair's clip in human hair extensions are made from 100% Remy human hair, which means that they can be colored just like your natural hair. However, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist before coloring your extensions to ensure the best results.

Q: Can I swim with clip in human hair extensions?

A: We recommend avoiding swimming with clip in human hair extensions, as chlorine and salt water can damage the hair.

Hair Care for Clip in Human Hair Extensions

Here are some additional hair care tips for clip in human hair extensions:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals or products containing alcohol on your extensions, as this can damage the hair.
  • Avoid excessive brushing: Brush your extensions gently and avoid excessive brushing, as this can cause shedding.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair: Make sure your hair is completely dry before going to bed to avoid tangling and matting.

With proper care and maintenance, clip in human hair extensions can give you the length and volume you desire without damaging your natural hair. Choose Bellami Hair for the best quality and natural-looking clip in human hair extensions in 2023.
