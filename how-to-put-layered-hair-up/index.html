How To Put Layered Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that adds texture and volume to your hair. However, putting layered hair up can be challenging, especially if you have layers of different lengths. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to put layered hair up. We will also give you some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about layered hair.

Table of Content

  1. How to Put Layered Hair Up
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

How to Put Layered Hair Up

Putting layered hair up can be a bit tricky, but it's not impossible. The key is to find the right technique that works for your hair type and the length of your layers. Here are some popular ways to put layered hair up:

  • Bun
  • Ponytail
  • Braid
  • Half-Up, Half-Down

Each of these styles can be modified to suit your preferences and the occasion. For example, a messy bun is perfect for a casual day out, while a sleek ponytail is great for a formal event.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to put layered hair up:

  1. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Decide on the style you want to create, such as a bun or ponytail.
  3. If you're doing a bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. If you're doing a ponytail, gather your hair into a high or low ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. You can leave some layers loose for a more relaxed look.
  5. If you're doing a braid, start by separating your hair into three sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair and secure the braid with an elastic band.
  6. If you're doing a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with an elastic band. You can leave the bottom section loose or style it in a bun or ponytail.
  7. Once you've created your desired style, use hairspray or hair gel to keep it in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make putting layered hair up easier:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • If you have short layers, use a small amount of hair gel or mousse to keep them in place.
  • If you have long layers, try using a hair donut to create a fuller bun.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

1. Can I put layered hair up if I have short layers?

Yes, you can still put layered hair up even if you have short layers. Use hair gel or mousse to keep the layers in place and use bobby pins to secure the style.

2. Can I put layered hair up if I have long layers?

Yes, you can put layered hair up if you have long layers. Consider using a hair donut to create a fuller bun or braid your hair to keep the layers in place.

3. How do I make sure my layers stay in place?

Use hairspray or hair gel to keep your layers in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose layers.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

Here are some hair care tips for layered hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to put layered hair up, you can experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
