Medium Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick, wavy hair? Do you struggle to find a haircut that works for your hair type? Look no further, because we have compiled a list of the best medium haircuts for thick wavy hair. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

Medium haircuts are a great option for those with thick, wavy hair. They offer versatility and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you prefer a layered look or a blunt cut, there is a medium haircut that will work for you. In this article, we will go over some of the best medium haircuts for thick wavy hair and how to style them.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect medium haircut for your thick wavy hair is to consider your face shape. This will help you determine what type of haircut will be the most flattering for your features. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircut inspiration.

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

It's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist before getting a new haircut. They can offer advice on what type of haircut will work best for your hair type and face shape. They can also give you tips on how to style your new haircut.

Step 2: Choose a Haircut

Once you have consulted with a professional, it's time to choose a haircut. Some of the best medium haircuts for thick wavy hair include long layers, blunt cuts, and shaggy cuts. These haircuts offer versatility and can be styled in many different ways.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to style your hair. There are many different styling products and techniques that can be used to enhance your thick wavy hair. Some popular styling products include mousse, hair gel, and hair spray. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get one of the best medium haircuts for thick wavy hair: the long layered haircut.

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if the long layered haircut is right for you. They can also offer advice on what type of layers will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for thick, wavy hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and make them easier to style.

Step 3: Towel Dry Your Hair

Towel dry your hair gently. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hair gel, to your damp hair. This will help to enhance your waves and keep them in place.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your waves and add volume to your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves to your hair. Finish with a spritz of hair spray to keep your waves in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium haircut for thick wavy hair:

  • Choose a haircut that works with your natural waves.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a haircut.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist before getting a new haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best medium haircut for thick wavy hair?

Some of the best medium haircuts for thick wavy hair include long layers, blunt cuts, and shaggy cuts.

How do I style my thick wavy hair?

You can style your thick wavy hair by using styling products, such as mousse or hair gel, and using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.

Hair Care

When caring for your thick wavy hair, it's important to use products that are designed for your hair type. Look for products that are formulated to enhance your natural waves and add moisture to your hair. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

In conclusion, there are many great medium haircuts for thick wavy hair. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type. Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your thick wavy hair with confidence!
