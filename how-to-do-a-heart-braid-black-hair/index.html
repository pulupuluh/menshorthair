How To Do A Heart Braid Black Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Heart braids are an intricate and stunning hairstyle that can be done on black hair. It's a perfect style for a romantic date, prom, or any special occasion. In this article, we will take you through a step-by-step guide on how to do a heart braid on black hair. We will also provide some tips, FAQ, and hair care for maintaining your heart braid.

Description

A heart braid is a unique hairstyle that forms a heart shape at the back of the head. It's a combination of a French braid and a fishtail braid. The style requires some skill and patience, but the result is worth it. It's a perfect way to show off your creativity and uniqueness.

How To Do a Heart Braid Black Hair

Before you begin, ensure that your hair is clean, dry, and tangle-free. You will need a comb, hair tie, hairpins, and hair spray. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Create a Middle Part

Divide your hair into two sections by creating a middle part. Use a comb to create a clean and straight part. Ensure that the part is in the center of your head.

Step 2: Create a Heart Shape

Start by creating a heart shape at the back of your head. Take a small section of hair from the right side of your part and create a small loop. Pin the loop in place with a hairpin. Repeat the same process on the left side of your part. The two loops should form a heart shape.

Step 3: French Braid the Hair

Start French braiding your hair from the top of your head. Take a small section of hair from the right side of your part and divide it into three equal sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. As you braid, add more hair from the right side of your head to the right section and cross it over the middle section. Repeat the process on the left side of your head. Continue braiding until you reach the base of your head.

Step 4: Fishtail Braid the Hair

Once you reach the base of your head, start fishtail braiding your hair. Divide the hair into two equal sections. Take a small section of hair from the right side of the right section and cross it over to the left section. Then, take a small section of hair from the left side of the left section and cross it over to the right section. Repeat the process until you reach the end of your hair. Secure the end with a hair tie.

Step 5: Pin the Braid in Place

Take the fishtail braid and pin it in place by tucking it under the French braid. Use hairpins to secure the braid in place. Gently pull the braid to make it look fuller and more voluminous. Finish off the style with some hair spray.

Tips

  • Practice the style on a mannequin head before trying it on your hair.
  • Use hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Use a hair net to protect your hair while sleeping.

FAQ

How long does it take to do a heart braid?

The style can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on your skill level.

Can I do a heart braid on short hair?

It's best to have medium to long hair to achieve the heart braid style.

Hair Care

To maintain your heart braid, ensure that you keep it clean and moisturized. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling. Avoid using too much hair spray as it can dry out your hair.

With this step-by-step guide, you can now achieve a stunning heart braid on black hair. The style is unique, creative, and perfect for any special occasion. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the style's longevity.
