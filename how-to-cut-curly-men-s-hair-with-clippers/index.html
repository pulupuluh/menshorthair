How To Cut Curly Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair can be challenging to cut, especially with clippers. But with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a clean and stylish cut that will make you look your best. In this guide, we will show you how to cut curly men's hair with clippers, step-by-step.

Description

Cutting curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it's important to cut it when it's dry to avoid any surprises. Clippers are a great tool for cutting curly hair because they can help you achieve an even cut quickly.

How to Cut Curly Men's Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step is to wash and dry your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be misleading, and you might end up cutting more than you intended.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose a clipper guard that is suitable for your hair length. For shorter hair, use a smaller clipper guard, and for longer hair, use a larger clipper guard. It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually.

Step 3: Start at the Nape of Your Neck

Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the clipper guard to create an even cut. Move the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Make sure you don't go too high up on the back of your head.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Next, move to the sides of your head. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Use the clipper guard to create an even cut, and make sure you don't cut too close to your scalp. Use the same upward motion, against the direction of hair growth.

Step 5: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Finally, it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use the clipper guard to create an even cut, and make sure you don't cut too much off. Start from the front and work your way back.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head
- Use a comb to help you guide the clippers
- Don't rush the process, take your time
- Clean your clippers after each use
- If you're not sure about cutting your own hair, seek professional help

FAQ

Can I use clippers on wet hair?

No, it's best to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises. Wet hair can be misleading, and you might end up cutting more than you intended.

What clipper guard should I use for curly hair?

Choose a clipper guard that is suitable for your hair length. For shorter hair, use a smaller clipper guard, and for longer hair, use a larger clipper guard. It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools on a regular basis. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Cutting curly men's hair with clippers can be easy and fun. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a clean and stylish cut that will make you look your best. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and follow these steps for a successful haircut every time.
