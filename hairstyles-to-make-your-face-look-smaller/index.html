Hairstyles To Make Your Face Look Smaller Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your round face and looking for ways to make it look smaller? Well, you're in luck! There are plenty of hairstyles that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we will discuss hairstyles to make your face look smaller, step by step guide, tips, FAQ and hair care. Let's dive in!

Bob Cut

The bob cut is one of the most popular hairstyles that can make your face look smaller. It is a short haircut that falls just above the shoulders and can be styled in many ways. The bob cut can help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a smaller face. It is also a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for busy women.

To achieve the perfect bob cut, you should ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to the length that falls just above your shoulders. You can also add layers to your hair to give it more volume and texture. To style your bob cut, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair or use a curling iron to add some waves. You can also add some bangs to your bob cut to frame your face and make it look smaller.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is another hairstyle that can make your face look smaller. It is a short haircut that is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style. The pixie cut can help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a smaller face. It is also a great hairstyle for women who want to show off their facial features.

To achieve the perfect pixie cut, you should ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave it longer on the top. You can also add some layers to your hair to give it more volume and texture. To style your pixie cut, you can use a hair gel to create a messy look or use a hair wax to add some texture. You can also add some bangs to your pixie cut to frame your face and make it look smaller.

Bangs

Bangs are a great way to make your face look smaller. They can help to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. There are many different types of bangs that you can choose from, including side-swept bangs, blunt bangs, and wispy bangs.

To achieve the perfect bangs, you should ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to the length that falls just above your eyebrows. You can also ask for side-swept bangs that will frame your face and make it look smaller. To style your bangs, you can use a flat iron to straighten them or use a curling iron to add some waves. You can also pin your bangs back with a bobby pin or a hair clip to create a different look.

Updo

An updo is a great hairstyle that can make your face look smaller. It is a perfect hairstyle for special occasions like weddings, proms, and parties. An updo can help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a smaller face. It is also a great way to show off your neck and shoulders.

To achieve the perfect updo, you should start by washing and drying your hair. You can then use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or straighten your hair. You can then pull your hair back into a ponytail or a bun and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins. You can also add some accessories like a headband or a hair clip to create a different look.

Curls

Curls are a great way to make your face look smaller. They can help to add volume and texture to your hair and create a different look. There are many different types of curls that you can choose from, including loose curls, tight curls, and spiral curls.

To achieve the perfect curls, you should start by washing and drying your hair. You can then use a curling iron or a wand to create your desired curls. You can also use a hair mousse or a hair spray to hold your curls in place. You can also add some accessories like a headband or a hair clip to create a different look.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you know about the different hairstyles that can make your face look smaller, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some hair care tips that you should follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that can help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a smaller face. Hairstyles like the bob cut, the pixie cut, and bangs are great for women with round faces.

How can I make my face look smaller without cutting my hair?

You can make your face look smaller without cutting your hair by using makeup to contour your face. You can use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour your cheeks and a lighter shade of foundation or highlighter to highlight your cheekbones.

Do bangs make your face look smaller?

Yes, bangs can make your face look smaller. They can help to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

In conclusion, there are many different hairstyles that can make your face look smaller. Whether you prefer a short haircut like the bob cut or the pixie cut, or you want to add some bangs to your hair, there is a hairstyle to suit your needs. By following the hair care tips mentioned in this article, you can ensure that your hair looks its best and complements your face shape. So, go ahead and try out these hairstyles to achieve the look you want!
