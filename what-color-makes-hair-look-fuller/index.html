What Color Makes Hair Look Fuller Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you struggling with thin and lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and depth to your locks without resorting to expensive treatments or extensions? One simple solution is to change your hair color. By choosing the right shade, you can create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore the best colors for making hair look fuller and share some expert tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Color for Your Hair Type
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair for Maximum Volume
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Full, Healthy Hair
  • FAQs About Hair Color and Volume

Why Does Hair Color Affect Volume?

Before we dive into the best colors for making hair look fuller, let's first understand why hair color can affect volume. Hair color works by changing the pigment in your hair shaft, which can alter the way light reflects off your strands. When you choose a lighter shade, for example, it can create the illusion of more depth and dimension, making your hair look thicker and fuller.

Similarly, darker shades can make your hair look flatter and more weighed down, which can accentuate thinness and lifelessness. However, this isn't always the case, and the right shade of dark hair can still add volume and texture to your locks.

How to Choose the Right Color for Your Hair Type

When it comes to choosing the right hair color for volume, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider your natural hair color and skin tone. While you don't have to stick to your natural color, choosing a shade that complements your skin tone can help enhance your overall look.

Secondly, think about your hair type and texture. If you have fine or thin hair, lighter shades can help create the illusion of more volume and thickness. However, if you have curly or coarse hair, darker shades can add depth and dimension, making your curls look fuller and more defined.

Finally, consider your lifestyle and maintenance preferences. If you're not keen on frequent touch-ups or salon visits, choose a color that will grow out gracefully and won't require too much upkeep. Similarly, if you're hesitant about drastic changes, start with subtle highlights or lowlights that can add depth and dimension without completely changing your look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair for Maximum Volume

Once you've chosen the right color for your hair type and skin tone, it's time to color your hair for maximum volume. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Dry your hair thoroughly and comb out any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Protect Your Skin and Clothes

Apply a thin layer of petroleum jelly or conditioner around your hairline and ears to protect your skin from staining. Wear an old shirt or towel to avoid getting color on your clothes.

Step 3: Mix and Apply the Color

Follow the instructions on your hair color kit to mix the color and developer. Apply the color evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the color and ensure even coverage.

Step 4: Wait and Rinse

Leave the color on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly with warm water. Apply the conditioner that comes with your kit and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Dry and style your hair as usual, using volumizing products and techniques to enhance your new color and create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Full, Healthy Hair

To keep your hair looking full and healthy, follow these expert tips and tricks:

  • Choose a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQs About Hair Color and Volume

Q: Can any hair color make hair look fuller?

A: While some colors are better than others for creating the illusion of volume, any color can work if it's applied correctly and complements your skin tone and hair type.

Q: Is it better to go lighter or darker for fullness?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have fine or thin hair, lighter shades can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. If you have curly or coarse hair, darker shades can add depth and dimension, making your curls look fuller and more defined.

Q: How often should I color my hair for volume?

A: It's best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between colorings to give your hair time to recover and prevent damage. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and the specific color you're using.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve fuller, thicker hair with the right hair color. Whether you opt for a lighter shade or a darker hue, the key is to choose a color that complements your natural beauty and enhances your overall look. So go ahead and experiment with different shades and techniques, and enjoy your new voluminous locks!
