How To Make Curls Look Good For Guys A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Aug 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a guy with curly hair struggling to find the right style? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing how to make curls look good for guys. From the basics of hair care to step-by-step instructions on how to style your curls, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

How to:

Step 1: Hair Care

The first and most important step in styling your curls is taking care of them. This means using the right products for your hair type, washing and conditioning regularly, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. When selecting products, opt for those specifically designed for curly hair, as they will provide the moisture and definition your curls need.

Step 2: Cut and Shape

The right haircut can make all the difference in how your curls look. Ask your stylist for a cut that works with your natural curl pattern and face shape. A layered cut can add volume and movement to your curls, while a shorter cut can give you a more polished look. Don’t be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you!

Step 3: Styling

Now that you’ve got the basics down, it’s time to style your curls. Start by applying a curl-defining product, such as a mousse or gel, to damp hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair, encouraging your curls to form. If you have longer hair, you may want to use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls without disrupting their shape. Once your hair is dry, use a bit of styling wax or pomade to define individual curls and add texture.

Tips:

- Don’t over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be dryer than straight hair, so washing too often can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash every 2-3 days, or as needed.

- Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curls. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle while your hair is damp.

- Experiment with different products until you find what works best for your hair. Some guys prefer a lightweight mousse, while others swear by a heavier gel.

FAQ:

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: While it is possible to straighten curly hair, we recommend embracing your natural texture instead of fighting it. If you do decide to straighten your hair, be sure to use heat protectant products and a quality flat iron.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: Frizz is a common concern for guys with curly hair. To prevent it, avoid touching your hair too much, choose products with anti-frizz properties, and sleep on a silk pillowcase.

Hair Care:

Remember, the key to making your curls look good is taking care of them. Be gentle when washing and styling, use products designed for curly hair, and don’t be afraid to experiment until you find what works for you. With a little effort, you can rock those curls and feel confident in your style.

We hope this guide on how to make curls look good for guys has been helpful. If you have any other tips or tricks, feel free to share them with us in the comments below. Happy styling!
