How To Grow Out Wavy Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with wavy hair, you might be wondering how to grow it out and achieve that coveted surfer look. Growing out your hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect wave. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to grow out wavy hair guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Grow Out Wavy Hair Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Growing Out Wavy Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Grow Out Wavy Hair Guys

Growing out wavy hair requires patience and dedication. Here are a few tips to help you achieve your desired look:

  • Start with a shorter cut: If you're currently sporting a shorter haircut, it's easier to grow out your hair and achieve a more defined wave.
  • Use the right products: Invest in high-quality products that are designed for wavy hair, such as a sea salt spray or a curl-enhancing cream.
  • Be patient: Growing out your hair takes time, so don't expect overnight results. It's important to stick with it and be consistent with your hair care routine.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to grow out wavy hair guys:

  1. Start with a shorter cut: As mentioned earlier, it's easier to grow out your hair if you start with a shorter cut. This will also help you achieve a more defined wave.
  2. Invest in the right products: Choose products that are designed for wavy hair, such as a sea salt spray or a curl-enhancing cream. These products will help define your waves and keep them looking great.
  3. Be consistent with your hair care routine: This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using the right products, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.
  4. Avoid over-washing your hair: Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type.
  5. Get regular trims: Even if you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tips for Growing Out Wavy Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you grow out your wavy hair:

  • Avoid using too much product: While using the right products can help define your waves, using too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting a diffuser: A diffuser attachment for your hair dryer can help enhance your waves and give them more volume.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair: Brushing your hair can cause frizz and disrupt your wave pattern. Instead, use your fingers to gently detangle your hair.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about growing out wavy hair:

How long does it take to grow out wavy hair?

It can take several months to a year or more to grow out your wavy hair, depending on your starting length and desired length.

Can I still use heat styling tools?

While it's best to avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, you can still use them occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are a few tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause dryness and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Instead, opt for a scrunchie or hair clip.

Growing out wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect wave. Be patient, consistent, and take good care of your hair, and you'll be on your way to surfer-chic locks in no time.
