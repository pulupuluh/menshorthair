Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Face Asian

Written by Luffy Aug 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As an Asian with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be quite challenging. You want a hairstyle that complements your round face shape and enhances your features. Luckily, medium length hairstyles can work wonders for round face Asians. In this article, we'll guide you through the best medium length hairstyles for round face Asians, tips, and hair care to keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hairstyles are versatile and easy to maintain, making them ideal for round face Asians who want to switch up their look without committing to a short or long hairstyle. These hairstyles typically fall between the chin and shoulders, framing the face and bringing attention to the cheekbones, jawline, and eyes. The key to finding the perfect medium length hairstyle for round face Asians is to select a cut that adds height and volume to the crown while elongating the face's shape.

How To

To achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle for round face Asians, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before selecting a medium length hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar or close, you have a round face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture will determine how your medium length hairstyle will look. If you have straight hair, you can opt for blunt, choppy, or layered cuts that add volume and dimension. If you have curly or wavy hair, you can choose soft layers or long bangs that frame your face and add movement.

3. Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also influence your choice of medium length hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic, elegant look, or do you like to experiment with edgier styles? Consider your lifestyle, career, and overall aesthetic when selecting your hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best medium length hairstyles for round face Asians:

1. Lob with Bangs

A lob (long bob) with bangs is a popular choice for round face Asians. The length of the lob elongates the face, while the bangs add dimension and draw attention to the eyes. You can opt for side-swept bangs or full bangs depending on your preference.

2. Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are perfect for round face Asians with wavy or curly hair. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, creating a soft, romantic look. You can also add wispy bangs to frame your face and add dimension.

3. Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a chic and sophisticated hairstyle that works well for round face Asians with straight hair. The blunt cut adds height and volume to the crown, elongating the face shape. You can also add blunt bangs for a more dramatic look.

4. Shaggy Layers

If you prefer a more edgy look, shaggy layers are a great option. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, creating a messy, effortless look. You can also add choppy bangs or side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. If you have straight hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and body. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural texture.

2. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your medium length hairstyle looking fresh and healthy. Trimming the ends of your hair will prevent split ends and breakage, making your hair look thicker and fuller.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener. Heat can damage your hair, causing breakage and split ends.

FAQ

1. Can round face Asians wear short hairstyles?

Yes, round face Asians can wear short hairstyles. However, it's essential to select a cut that adds height and volume to the crown while elongating the face's shape. A pixie cut or a short bob with choppy layers can work well for round face Asians.

2. What are the best haircuts for round face Asians with thick hair?

Round face Asians with thick hair can opt for a layered haircut that thins out the hair and reduces bulkiness. A long layered haircut or a shaggy bob with choppy layers can work well.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hairstyle looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair after washing to keep it moisturized and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and repair your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair, causing dryness, breakage, and split ends. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can find the perfect medium length hairstyle for your round face Asian features. Experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
