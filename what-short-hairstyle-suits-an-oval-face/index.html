What Short Hairstyle Suits An Oval Face

Written by Aprilia Mar 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face, you are lucky because it is considered to be the most versatile face shape. Almost every hairstyle suits this face shape, including short hairstyles. However, it is important to choose the right short hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we will guide you on what short hairstyle suits an oval face.

Description

An oval face is characterized by balanced features, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. This face shape has no prominent angles and has a rounded hairline. Since an oval face is well-proportioned, almost every hairstyle suits it. However, choosing the right short hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look more attractive.

How to

Follow these steps to choose the right short hairstyle for your oval face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before choosing a short hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. An oval face is characterized by balanced features, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture is an important factor when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have thick hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut will look great on you. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a cropped pixie will add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Look for inspiration

Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or even celebrities who have an oval face. Take note of the hairstyles that you like and bring them to your hairstylist for reference.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead, as it can make your face look shorter.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as it can make your face look longer.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What short hairstyles suit an oval face?

Almost every short hairstyle suits an oval face. However, some of the most popular short hairstyles for an oval face include a layered bob, a blunt bob, a cropped pixie, and a side-swept pixie.

How do I know if I have an oval face?

An oval face is characterized by balanced features, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. This face shape has no prominent angles and has a rounded hairline.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially when you have a short hairstyle that requires regular maintenance. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your short hairstyle.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, an oval face is the most versatile face shape, and almost every short hairstyle suits it. However, choosing the right short hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look more attractive. Follow our guide on what short hairstyle suits an oval face, and don't be afraid to experiment until you find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine.
