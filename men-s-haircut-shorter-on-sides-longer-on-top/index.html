Men s Haircut Shorter On Sides Longer On Top A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Frank Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and low-maintenance? Look no further than the men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top! This hairstyle has been a popular choice for men in recent years and continues to be a trending style in 2023.

Description

The men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top is characterized by having shorter hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's also easy to maintain and style, making it a popular choice for men who prefer a fuss-free hairstyle.

How to

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step is to choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut that will work for you. The most common haircuts for this style are the undercut, fade, and taper.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

The next step is to trim the sides and back of your head. Use clippers to achieve the desired length, starting with a longer guard and gradually working your way down until you reach your desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Once you're done with the sides, it's time to cut the top. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Remember to leave enough length so that you can style it later.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Finally, blend the top and sides by using scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top:

  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the best one for your hair type.
  • Consider adding a fade or an undercut to add more dimension to your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top:

What face shape is best suited for this hairstyle?

This hairstyle works well for most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or square-shaped faces.

Do I need to use styling products?

While styling products are not necessary, they can help add texture and hold to your hair. Experiment with different products to find the right one for your hair type.

Can I achieve this hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still achieve this hairstyle if you have curly hair. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to cut and style your curls to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right cut and care, the men's haircut shorter on sides longer on top can be a stylish and low-maintenance choice for men in 2023. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
