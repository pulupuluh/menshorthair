How To Do A Really Good High Ponytail

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old low ponytail and looking for a fresh, new hairstyle? Look no further than the high ponytail! Not only is it practical and easy to achieve, but it's also versatile and can be dressed up or down. Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve a really good high ponytail.

Description

A high ponytail is a hairstyle where the hair is pulled back and secured at the crown of the head. It's a classic look that has been popular for decades and is still in fashion today. The high ponytail is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. It's also a great option for those with long hair who want to keep their hair out of their face.

How to

1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.

2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.

3. Hold your ponytail in place with one hand and use the other hand to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

4. Secure your ponytail with a hair tie. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

5. Use a comb or your fingers to tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume.

6. Use hairspray to keep your ponytail in place and prevent flyaways.

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Use a paddle brush or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair from the ends to the roots.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Secure Your Ponytail

Hold your ponytail in place with one hand and use the other hand to secure it with a hair tie. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume. This will prevent your ponytail from looking flat.

Step 5: Use Hairspray

Use hairspray to keep your ponytail in place and prevent flyaways. Spray lightly and evenly over your hair, holding the can about 10 inches away from your head.

Tips

- Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.

- Add a hair accessory such as a bow or scrunchie to dress up your ponytail.

- Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

FAQ

Q: Can I do a high ponytail with short hair?

A: It's possible, but it may require some hair extensions to achieve the desired length.

Q: How can I prevent my hair tie from slipping?

A: Use a hair tie that has a textured surface or wrap it around your hair multiple times to create more friction.

Q: Can I wear a high ponytail every day?

A: Yes! The high ponytail is a timeless hairstyle that can be worn every day without damaging your hair.

Hair Care

When wearing a high ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

- Avoid wearing a high ponytail every day. Switch it up with other hairstyles to prevent breakage.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

- Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a really good high ponytail that will elevate any outfit. Give it a try and see how it transforms your look!
