How To Make Guys Hair Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve a fluffy and voluminous look? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make guys hair fluffy. Whether you have short or long hair, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the desired look.

Description

Fluffy hair is the latest trend in men's hairstyles. It gives a youthful and playful vibe that is perfect for any occasion. However, achieving this look can be challenging, especially if you have naturally straight and flat hair. That is why we have compiled a list of tips and tricks to help you make your hair fluffy and voluminous.

How to Make Guys Hair Fluffy

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in making your hair fluffy is to start with clean hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help remove any dirt and oils that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Volumizing Spray

After washing your hair, apply a generous amount of mousse or volumizing spray. This will help add texture and volume to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair to achieve a consistent look.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots. This will help create volume and add texture to your hair. Be sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall to achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

If you want even more volume, you can tease your hair. To do this, take a small section of hair and backcomb it using a fine-toothed comb. Repeat this process on different sections of your hair until you achieve the desired volume.

Step 5: Use Hairspray

To lock in the volume and texture, use a good quality hairspray. Be sure to hold the can at least 10 inches away from your hair and spray evenly. This will help your hair stay fluffy and voluminous for longer.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall for a more natural look
  • Tease your hair in small sections to avoid damaging your hair
  • Use a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh down your hair

FAQ

Can I make my hair fluffy if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a fluffy look even if you have thin hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner and apply a generous amount of mousse or volumizing spray. Blow dry your hair while using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots. You can also tease your hair to add more volume.

How do I maintain my fluffy hair?

To maintain your fluffy hair, avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair. Use a lightweight hairspray to lock in the volume and texture. Be sure to also wash your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. You can also incorporate hair masks and serums into your routine to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a fluffy and voluminous look that is perfect for any occasion. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Try out these tips and see the difference for yourself!
