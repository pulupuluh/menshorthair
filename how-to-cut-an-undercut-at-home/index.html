How To Cut An Undercut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read
If you're feeling adventurous and want to try a new hairstyle, an undercut may be just what you need. An undercut is a trendy hairstyle that involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the top long. It's a bold look that can be customized to suit your personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut an undercut at home.

Description

An undercut is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back of your head while leaving the top long. This style can be customized to your liking, with variations such as a disconnected undercut, a fade undercut, or a shaved undercut. Cutting an undercut at home can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it.

How to

Before you begin, gather the necessary tools: clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and hair clips. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. Step 1: Section your hair Separate the hair on top of your head from the sides and back using hair clips. This will help you focus on one section at a time. Step 2: Choose your clipper guard Decide on the length you want for the sides and back of your head. Choose a clipper guard that matches that length. Step 3: Start cutting Using your clippers and chosen guard, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up to the top, making sure to keep the clipper level and even. Step 4: Blend the sides and top Once you've finished cutting the sides and back, use scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the longer hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths. Step 5: Style your hair Now that your undercut is complete, style your hair as desired. Use a hair product to add texture, volume, or hold.

Step-by-Step

1. Section your hair 2. Choose your clipper guard 3. Start cutting 4. Blend the sides and top 5. Style your hair

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut. - Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head while cutting. - Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring and stressful.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut an undercut with scissors only? A: While it's possible to cut an undercut with scissors only, it's easier and more precise to use clippers. Q: Can I cut my own hair? A: Yes, you can cut your own hair. Just make sure to use the right tools and techniques. Q: How often should I trim my undercut? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how neat you want your undercut to look. Generally, it's recommended to trim it every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

Maintaining an undercut requires regular washing, conditioning, and styling. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Apply hair product to damp hair and style as desired. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Cutting an undercut at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
