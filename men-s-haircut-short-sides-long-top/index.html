Men s Haircut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can work for any occasion? Look no further than the men's haircut short sides long top. This style has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for maintenance, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Short Sides Long Top

The men's haircut short sides long top is a versatile style that can be tailored to your preferences. To get this style, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting men's hair. When you arrive at the salon, show them a picture of the style you want to achieve. This will help them understand your vision and give you the best results.

Communication is key when getting a men's haircut short sides long top. Be sure to tell your stylist how short you want the sides and how long you want the top. You can also ask for a fade or undercut on the sides to add some edge to the style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Men's Haircut Short Sides Long Top

If you're feeling adventurous and want to cut your own men's haircut short sides long top, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair with a clip or hair tie.
  3. Choose your desired length for the sides and use clippers to trim the hair.
  4. Blend the sides into the top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Remove the clip or hair tie and trim the top to your desired length.
  6. Style the hair using your favorite styling product.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Haircut Short Sides Long Top

To keep your men's haircut short sides long top looking fresh, follow these tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of the style.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Style your hair with a matte or low-shine product for a natural look.
  • Experiment with different partings and styling techniques to keep the style fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions about Men's Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Can a men's haircut short sides long top work for any hair type?

Yes, this style can work for any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist can tailor the cut to your hair type to ensure the best results.

What face shape is best suited for a men's haircut short sides long top?

This style can work for any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or square faces.

Can I still style my hair if I have a men's haircut short sides long top?

Yes, you can still style your hair in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Haircut Short Sides Long Top

To keep your men's haircut short sides long top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right cut and care, the men's haircut short sides long top can be a stylish and versatile choice for any man. Whether you get it done at the salon or cut it yourself, this style is sure to turn heads.
