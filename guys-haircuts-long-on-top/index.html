Guys Haircuts Long On Top A Trending Style In 2023

Are you looking for a new and trendy haircut? Look no further than guys haircuts long on top. This style has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down in 2023. In this article, we'll take a look at how to get the perfect guys haircut long on top, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get The Perfect Guys Haircut Long On Top

Description

Guys haircuts long on top are exactly as they sound. The hair on top is longer than the hair on the sides and back. This style can be versatile and worn in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get The Perfect Guys Haircut Long On Top

The first step in getting the perfect guys haircut long on top is to find a good barber or hair stylist who specializes in men's hair. It's important to communicate your desired style and length, as well as any specific details or preferences you may have.

Step by Step Instructions

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a comb to section off the hair on top of your head.

3. Use scissors or clippers to trim the sides and back, leaving the hair on top untouched.

4. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length.

5. Style your hair using your preferred products, such as pomade, gel, or wax.

Tips for Maintaining Your Guys Haircut Long On Top

1. Schedule regular haircuts to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

2. Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and styled.

3. Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.

4. Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can guys haircuts long on top work for any hair type?

A: Yes, guys haircuts long on top can work for any hair type, from straight to curly.

Q: How often should I get a haircut to maintain my guys haircut long on top?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care for Guys Haircuts Long On Top

1. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

2. Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

4. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Overall, guys haircuts long on top are a versatile and trendy style that can work for any hair type. By finding the right barber or stylist, maintaining your haircut regularly, and using high-quality hair products, you can achieve the perfect guys haircut long on top.
