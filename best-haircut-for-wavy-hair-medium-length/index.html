Best Haircut For Wavy Hair Medium Length A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have wavy hair of medium length, you're in luck! This hair type is versatile and can be styled in many ways. However, finding the right haircut for wavy hair can be a challenge. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircut for wavy hair medium length and provide tips on how to care for your waves.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • Step by Step Guide to Getting the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • Tips for Styling Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Description

Medium-length wavy hair is a popular choice for many women. It falls between the chin and shoulders, making it easy to style and maintain. The right haircut can enhance the natural waves in your hair and make it look more voluminous.

The best haircut for wavy hair medium length is one that complements the natural texture of your hair. Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to wavy hair. A blunt cut can make your hair look heavy and flat. A layered cut, on the other hand, will create a more textured look.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Medium Length

When choosing a haircut for wavy hair medium length, keep in mind the shape of your face. The right cut can enhance your best features and balance out your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, go for a layered cut that falls below the chin. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  • If you have a square face, go for a long bob with layers. This will soften the angles of your face.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any haircut. Try a blunt cut with layers for a chic look.

Another factor to consider when choosing the best haircut for wavy hair medium length is your hair density. If you have thick hair, a layered cut will help to thin it out and make it more manageable. If you have thin hair, a blunt cut with layers can add volume and texture to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Medium Length

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut for wavy hair medium length, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will give your stylist a better idea of your natural texture.
  2. Communicate your desired look to your stylist. Show them pictures of the haircut you want and be clear about how much length you want to take off.
  3. Ask for layers. Layers will add movement and volume to your hair.
  4. Consider getting bangs. Bangs can frame your face and enhance your natural features.
  5. Ask your stylist to use a razor instead of scissors to create a softer, more textured look.
  6. Style your hair using a curling iron or a diffuser to enhance your natural waves.

Tips for Styling Wavy Hair

Styling wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, natural look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl cream to damp hair to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your natural waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the Best Haircut for Thick Wavy Hair Medium Length?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair medium length is a layered cut with long layers. This will thin out your hair and make it more manageable. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face.

What is the Best Haircut for Thin Wavy Hair Medium Length?

The best haircut for thin wavy hair medium length is a blunt cut with layers. This will add volume and texture to your hair. You can also add bangs to create the illusion of fuller hair.

Can I Straighten My Wavy Hair?

Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair. You can also use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

To keep your wavy hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair. Instead, opt for air-drying or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know the best haircut for wavy hair medium length and how to care for your waves, you can rock your natural texture with confidence. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.
