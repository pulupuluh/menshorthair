How To Cut Mullet Bangs

Written by Bardi Aug 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, mullet bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This trendy cut is a modern twist on the classic mullet, featuring shorter layers on top and longer layers in the back. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut mullet bangs step by step, as well as provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Mullet Bangs

Cutting mullet bangs is a relatively simple process, but it does require some precision to ensure that the layers blend seamlessly. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a few basic tools to cut your mullet bangs:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into three parts: the top section, the sides, and the back. Clip the side and back sections out of the way for now.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Take the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Use your scissors to cut straight across, making sure that the length is even. You can either cut your bangs at eyebrow level or slightly shorter, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Release one side section of your hair and comb it forward. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the top of your head and moving toward the front. The length of the hair should gradually get shorter as you move toward the front, blending seamlessly with the bangs. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the Back

Finally, release the back section of your hair and comb it forward. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the top of your head and moving toward the nape of your neck. The length of the hair should gradually get longer as you move toward the back, blending seamlessly with the longer layers.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting mullet bangs:

  • Start with longer layers and gradually work your way to shorter layers to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short, as they will shrink up when they dry.
  • Take your time and work carefully to avoid making any mistakes.

FAQ

Can I cut my own mullet bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own mullet bangs as long as you have the right tools and take your time. However, if you're not confident in your skills, it's always better to go to a professional stylist.

How often should I trim my mullet bangs?

You should trim your mullet bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Can I style my mullet bangs in different ways?

Yes, you can style your mullet bangs in a variety of ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your mullet bangs looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut mullet bangs, it's time to give this trendy style a try. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a bold new look, mullet bangs are sure to turn heads.
