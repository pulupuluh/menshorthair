Does Layer Cut Suit Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Mar 22, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you someone with a round face and wondering if a layer cut would suit you? Layer cuts are a popular hairstyle choice as they can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair. But, when it comes to round faces, it's essential to choose a haircut that can complement your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss whether a layer cut suits a round face and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right layer cut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Layer Cut for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Get a Layer Cut
  • Tips to Maintain Layer Cut on Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layer Cut on Round Face

Description

A round face is typically characterized by a rounded jawline, full cheeks, and a wide forehead. If you have a round face, you can opt for a layer cut to create an illusion of length and slimness. A layer cut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

However, not all layer cuts are suitable for round faces. You need to choose a haircut that can balance out the roundness of your face and create an elongated appearance. In the next section, we'll discuss how to choose the right layer cut for a round face.

How to Choose the Right Layer Cut for a Round Face

When choosing a layer cut for a round face, you should consider the following factors:

  • Length: Opt for a layer cut that falls below your chin or at your shoulders. A longer length can create an illusion of length and make your face look slimmer.
  • Layers: Choose a layer cut with long layers that start from your chin and go down. Avoid layers that start from your cheekbones or higher as they can make your face look wider.
  • Bangs: Consider getting side-swept bangs that can create an angle and make your face look longer. Avoid blunt bangs or center-parted hairstyles that can make your face look rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get a Layer Cut

If you have decided to get a layer cut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of layer cuts that you like.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before the appointment.
  3. Sit down and let the hairstylist section your hair into parts.
  4. Cut the hair according to the desired length and layers.
  5. Blow-dry and style your hair to get the desired look.

Tips to Maintain Layer Cut on Round Face

Here are some tips to maintain your layer cut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your layer cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about layer cuts on round faces:

  • Can a layer cut make a round face look slimmer? Yes, a layer cut can create an illusion of length and slimness on a round face.
  • What type of layer cut is suitable for a round face? A layer cut with long layers that start from your chin and go down is suitable for a round face.
  • Should I get bangs with a layer cut on a round face? Yes, side-swept bangs can create an angle and make your face look longer.

Hair Care for Layer Cut on Round Face

Here are some hair care tips for layer cut on round face:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, a layer cut can suit a round face if you choose the right length, layers, and bangs. Consult with your hairstylist to choose a layer cut that can complement your face shape and create an elongated appearance. With proper hair care and maintenance, you can rock a stylish and trendy layer cut on a round face.
