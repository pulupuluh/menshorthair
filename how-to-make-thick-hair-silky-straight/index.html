How To Make Thick Hair Silky Straight Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to achieve a silky straight appearance. But don't worry! With the right tools, techniques and a little bit of patience, you can get the smooth, shiny and straight hair of your dreams. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make thick hair silky straight.

How to Straighten Thick Hair: Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make thick hair silky straight:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly and remove all the product buildup.

Step 2: Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help protect your hair from damage and breakage caused by heat styling.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. To avoid frizz, use a diffuser attachment and dry your hair in sections.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Use a flat iron to straighten each section of your hair. Start at the roots and slowly glide the flat iron down to the ends. Make sure to use a low to medium heat setting and avoid going over the same section of hair multiple times.

Step 6: Apply a Serum

Once you've straightened your hair, apply a serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Tips for Making Thick Hair Silky Straight

Here are some additional tips that can help you achieve silky straight hair:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Look for products specifically designed for thick, curly or frizzy hair. These products will help tame your hair and make it easier to straighten.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It

Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to straighten.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing it. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to straighten.

Tip 4: Wrap Your Hair at Night

To prevent your hair from getting tangled or frizzy while you sleep, wrap it in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I straighten my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat by using a hair straightening brush or a hair straightening cream.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to avoid straightening your hair too often. Try to limit it to once or twice a week to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair. This can cause severe damage to your hair and even burn your scalp.

Hair Care for Silky Straight Hair

To maintain your silky straight hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

Tip 4: Avoid Using Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to straighten.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve silky straight hair that looks and feels amazing. Remember to take good care of your hair and avoid over-styling it, and you'll have beautiful, healthy locks that are the envy of everyone around you.
