The Best Haircuts For Oval Faces In 2023

Are you looking for the best haircuts for oval faces in 2023? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts, styles, and hair care tips for people with oval faces. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval faces. So, let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing forward with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the photo.
  3. Compare your face shape to the following shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

What is an Oval Face Shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, a slightly curved jawline, and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. People with oval faces can pull off a variety of haircuts and styles.

Best Haircuts for Oval Faces

Here are some of the best haircuts for oval faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for people with oval faces. They add movement and volume to the hair without overwhelming the face.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic haircut that looks great on oval faces. Opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above the shoulders.

3. Pixie

A pixie cut is a bold choice, but it can look great on people with oval faces. Keep it short and textured for a modern look.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some dimension to an oval face. They can also help balance out a long forehead.

5. Beach Waves

Beach waves are a great option for people with oval faces. They add texture and movement to the hair without overwhelming the face.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips for people with oval faces:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can overwhelm an oval face. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

2. Embrace Layers

Layers add movement and volume to the hair, which can help balance out an oval face.

3. Keep Hair Off the Face

Avoid hairstyles that cover the face, as they can make an oval face appear longer.

FAQ

Q: Can people with oval faces pull off short haircuts?

A: Yes, people with oval faces can pull off short haircuts like pixie cuts and bobs.

Q: What haircuts should people with oval faces avoid?

A: People with oval faces should avoid hairstyles that cover the face, as well as heavy bangs that can overwhelm the face.

Q: What are some hair care tips for people with oval faces?

A: Embrace layers, avoid heavy bangs, and keep hair off the face to help balance out an oval face.

Now that you know the best haircuts and hair care tips for oval faces, it's time to head to the salon and try out a new look!
