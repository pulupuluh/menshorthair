Description

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Do you want to know what haircut will fit you best? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to determine your face shape and what haircut will complement it. Say goodbye to bad hair days and hello to a new and improved you!


How To Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out what haircut will fit you best is determining your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, look in a mirror and do the following:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with a slightly wider forehead than the chin and prominent cheekbones. If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off most haircuts and styles, so feel free to experiment!

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. To elongate your face, go for a haircut with layers and volume at the crown.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with a squared-off chin. To soften your features, go for a haircut with layers around the face and wispy bangs.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is characterized by a wider forehead and temples, with a pointed chin. To balance out your features, go for a haircut with layers and volume below the chin.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. To soften your features, go for a haircut with layers and volume at the crown.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a pointed chin. To add width to your face, go for a haircut with layers and volume around the sides.


Step By Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, here is a step by step guide on how to choose the perfect haircut:

  1. Choose a haircut that complements your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to get a better idea of what the haircut will look like on you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Consider your hair color and skin tone
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist
  • Keep your hair healthy with regular trims and conditioning treatments

FAQ

Q: What if I have multiple face shapes?

A: It's possible to have a combination of face shapes. In that case, choose a haircut that complements the dominant face shape.

Q: Can I pull off short hair?

A: Short haircuts can be flattering on any face shape, as long as it's the right cut for you.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.


Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
