How To Treat Thyroid Hair Loss Naturally

Written by William Mar 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Thyroid hair loss can be a frustrating and distressing experience. Thyroid disorders, such as hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism, can cause hair loss due to the disruption of the hair growth cycle. However, there are natural remedies that can help treat thyroid hair loss. In this article, we will discuss how to treat thyroid hair loss naturally.

  • How to Treat Thyroid Hair Loss Naturally
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

How to Treat Thyroid Hair Loss Naturally

The following natural remedies can help treat thyroid hair loss:

1. Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth. Foods rich in vitamin B, vitamin C, iron, zinc, and omega-3 fatty acids can help improve hair health. Some examples of these foods include:

  • Leafy green vegetables
  • Fruits such as berries, citrus fruits, and melons
  • Lean proteins such as chicken, fish, and beans
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Whole grains
  • Eggs and dairy products

2. Scalp Massage

Massaging the scalp can help improve blood circulation to the hair follicles, promoting hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.

3. Essential Oils

Essential oils can help improve hair health and promote hair growth. Some essential oils that are beneficial for hair loss include:

  • Lavender oil
  • Rosemary oil
  • Peppermint oil
  • Cedarwood oil

You can mix a few drops of essential oil with a carrier oil, such as coconut oil or olive oil, and massage it into your scalp.

4. Yoga and Meditation

Stress can worsen thyroid hair loss, so practicing yoga and meditation can help reduce stress levels. Yoga poses such as downward-facing dog and child's pose can help increase blood flow to the scalp.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to treat thyroid hair loss naturally:

  1. Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  2. Massage your scalp daily
  3. Use essential oils to promote hair growth
  4. Practice yoga and meditation to reduce stress levels

Tips

  • Avoid harsh hair treatments such as bleaching and coloring
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly

FAQ

What causes thyroid hair loss?

Thyroid disorders, such as hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism, can cause hair loss due to the disruption of the hair growth cycle.

Can thyroid hair loss be reversed?

Yes, thyroid hair loss can be reversed with proper treatment and care.

How long does it take for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment?

It can take several months for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment, depending on the severity of the hair loss.

Hair Care

In addition to natural remedies, taking care of your hair can help promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair
  • Avoid brushing wet hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction

By following these natural remedies and tips for hair care, you can help treat thyroid hair loss naturally and promote hair growth.
