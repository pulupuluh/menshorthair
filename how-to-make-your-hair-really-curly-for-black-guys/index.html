How To Make Your Hair Really Curly For Black Guys

Written by Oliver Apr 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a black guy looking to achieve really curly hair? Well, you’ve come to the right place! Curly hair is a great style that can add a lot of personality to your look. In this article, we’ll be going through the steps on how to make your hair really curly and provide some tips and hair care advice along the way. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Your Hair Really Curly
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Curly Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Really Curly

The first step to achieving curly hair is to understand your hair type. Black hair is often classified as Type 4 hair, which can be further divided into subcategories such as 4A, 4B, and 4C. Knowing your hair type can help you determine the best methods to achieve curly hair.

One of the most popular methods to achieve curly hair is by using a perm. Perms involve the use of chemicals to break down the hair’s natural structure and then reforming it into a curly pattern. However, perms can be damaging to the hair and can lead to breakage if not done properly.

Another option is to use a curling iron or rollers to create curls. This method is less damaging to the hair, but can be time-consuming and may not result in long-lasting curls.

The final method is to use natural techniques such as twist-outs or braid-outs. These methods involve twisting or braiding the hair while wet and allowing it to dry before unraveling the twists or braids to reveal curly hair. This method may take longer to achieve but is less damaging to the hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve curly hair using the natural twist-out method:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel.
  3. Section the hair into small sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Allow the hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  5. Unravel the twists and separate the curls with your fingers.
  6. Apply a small amount of oil or serum to the hair to add shine and reduce frizz.
  7. Style as desired.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair to avoid breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve curly hair without using chemicals or heat?

A: Yes, you can achieve curly hair using natural techniques such as twist-outs or braid-outs.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but washing once or twice a week is usually sufficient. Over-washing can cause the hair to become dry and brittle.

Q: Can I use a regular hair conditioner instead of a leave-in conditioner?

A: While regular conditioner can help with detangling and adding moisture to the hair, leave-in conditioner is specifically formulated to provide additional moisture and help define curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort, but the results are worth it. Here are some additional tips for keeping your curly hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out the hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause breakage. Instead, detangle with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb while the hair is wet and coated with conditioner.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve really curly hair as a black guy. Remember to take care of your hair and it will reward you with healthy, bouncy curls!
