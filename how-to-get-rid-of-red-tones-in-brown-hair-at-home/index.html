How To Get Rid Of Red Tones In Brown Hair At Home

Written by Luffy Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have brown hair, you may have experienced red tones appearing over time. These can be caused by various factors such as sun exposure, hair dye, or even the natural aging process. Whatever the cause, getting rid of red tones in brown hair at home is possible with a few simple steps. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of red tones in brown hair at home, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Red Tones in Brown Hair at Home
  • Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Products
  • Step 2: Wash Your Hair with Cold Water
  • Step 3: Use a Color-Correcting Shampoo
  • Step 4: Try a DIY Hair Mask
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Brown Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Brown Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Products

The first step in getting rid of red tones in brown hair is to choose the right hair products. Look for hair products that are specifically designed for brown hair, such as color-safe shampoos and conditioners. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause further damage.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair with Cold Water

Washing your hair with hot water can cause the cuticle to open up, which can lead to color fading and red tones becoming more prominent. To prevent this, wash your hair with cold water instead. Cold water helps to seal the cuticle, which can help to keep your hair color looking vibrant and healthy.

Step 3: Use a Color-Correcting Shampoo

If you have red tones in your brown hair, using a color-correcting shampoo can help to neutralize them. Look for a purple or blue shampoo, as these tones can counteract red tones in brown hair. Use the shampoo once a week to help maintain the color balance in your hair.

Step 4: Try a DIY Hair Mask

If you want to try a more natural approach to getting rid of red tones in brown hair, you can make a DIY hair mask using ingredients that you may already have at home. Here's a simple recipe:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Mash the avocado in a bowl and mix in the honey and coconut oil. Apply the mixture to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it out with cold water. This hair mask can help to nourish your hair while also reducing red tones.

Tips for Maintaining Your Brown Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining your brown hair:

  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf

FAQs

Can I use apple cider vinegar to get rid of red tones in brown hair?

Yes, apple cider vinegar can help to remove red tones from brown hair. Mix equal parts apple cider vinegar and water and apply it to your hair after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cold water.

How often should I use a color-correcting shampoo?

You should use a color-correcting shampoo once a week to maintain the color balance in your hair.

Hair Care for Brown Hair

Taking care of your brown hair is important to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and steps, you can get rid of red tones in brown hair at home and keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.
