Best Unnatural Hair Color For Skin Tone Chart

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're opting for an unnatural shade. With so many colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will look best on you. Fortunately, there's a solution to your problem: the best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart. In this article, we'll dive into everything you need to know about this chart and how to use it to find your perfect hair color.

Description

The best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart is a tool that helps you find the perfect hair color based on your skin tone. It provides a range of colors that will complement your skin tone and make you look your best. This chart takes into account factors such as your skin's undertones, natural hair color, and eye color to recommend the most flattering shades.

How to Use the Chart

Before using the chart, you need to determine your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear greenish, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can use the chart to find your perfect hair color. The chart typically categorizes skin tones into three categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Each category has a range of recommended hair colors that will complement your skin tone. Some of the most popular unnatural hair colors include blue, green, purple, pink, and red.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to using the best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist.
  2. Consult the chart to find your skin tone category (cool, warm, or neutral).
  3. Look at the recommended hair colors for your skin tone category.
  4. Choose a hair color that you like and that falls within the recommended colors for your skin tone category.
  5. Visit a salon or purchase the hair dye and follow the instructions to dye your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using the best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart:

  • Consider your natural hair color when choosing an unnatural shade. For example, if you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before dyeing it a bright color.
  • Take into account your eye color when choosing a hair color. Some colors may make your eyes pop more than others.
  • If you're hesitant to dye your entire head, consider trying highlights or ombre instead.
  • Be sure to do a patch test before dyeing your hair to avoid an allergic reaction.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart:

Q: Will any unnatural hair color work for my skin tone?

A: No, not all hair colors will look good on all skin tones. The best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart provides recommendations based on your skin undertones, natural hair color, and eye color.

Q: Can I dye my hair an unnatural color at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair an unnatural color at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my unnatural hair color?

A: This depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Some colors may fade faster than others, and you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

When dyeing your hair an unnatural color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping the color.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

Now that you know everything about the best unnatural hair color for skin tone chart, you can confidently choose your next hair color. Remember to take into account your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color to find the most flattering shade. With the right hair color, you'll feel confident and beautiful.
