How To Cut Shirts Into Muscle Tees

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your plain shirts and want to try something new? Cutting your shirt into a muscle tee is a great way to add some edge to your wardrobe. In this article, we'll show you how to cut shirts into muscle tees in easy steps. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

Description

A muscle tee is a sleeveless shirt with oversized armholes, giving you a relaxed and comfortable fit. It's perfect for hot summer days or when you want to show off your toned arms. Cutting your shirt into a muscle tee is a quick and easy way to transform your old shirts into something new.

How to Cut Shirts into Muscle Tees

Step 1: Choose the Right Shirt

Choose a shirt that you want to transform into a muscle tee. It's best to choose a shirt that is a size bigger than your normal size, so you can have more room to work with.

Step 2: Mark the Armholes

Put on the shirt and mark where you want the armholes to be. You can use a fabric chalk or a pencil to make the marks. Make sure the armholes are big enough to show off your arms but not too big that it exposes too much skin.

Step 3: Cut the Sleeves

Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut along the marked lines to remove the sleeves. It's important to cut slowly and carefully to avoid making mistakes. If you're not confident with your cutting skills, you can use a ruler or a template to guide you.

Step 4: Cut the Neckline

You can leave the neckline as is or cut it into a V-neck or scoop neck. If you're cutting the neckline, make sure to cut slowly and gently, so you don't cut too much off.

Step 5: Cut the Bottom Hem

You can leave the bottom hem as is or cut it into a curved hem or a high-low hem. If you're cutting the hem, make sure to cut it evenly and smoothly.

Step 6: Wash and Dry

Wash the shirt to remove any excess fabric and to soften the edges. Dry the shirt in the dryer or air dry it.

Tips

  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. You can always cut more if needed.
  • Practice on an old shirt or a scrap fabric before cutting your favorite shirt.

FAQ

Can I cut any shirt into a muscle tee?

Yes, you can cut any shirt into a muscle tee as long as it's made of a fabric that won't fray easily.

Do I need to sew the edges after cutting?

No, you don't need to sew the edges after cutting. Muscle tees are meant to have raw edges for a relaxed look. However, if you want to prevent fraying, you can use a fabric glue or a fray check on the edges.

Hair Care

If you're cutting your shirt into a muscle tee, you might want to show off your hair too. Keep your hair healthy and strong by washing it regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

Cutting your shirt into a muscle tee is a fun and easy way to add some personality to your wardrobe. Follow these steps and tips to create your own unique muscle tee. Happy cutting!
