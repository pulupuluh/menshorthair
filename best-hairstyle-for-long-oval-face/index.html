Description

Having a long oval face shape is a blessing as it is considered the most versatile face shape. You can wear any hairstyle you like, but finding the perfect one can be overwhelming. It is essential to choose a hairstyle that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is crucial in finding the perfect hairstyle. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Analyze the shape of your face and compare it to the following face shapes:

  • Oval Face - Longer than it is wide with a rounded jawline and forehead
  • Round Face - Width and length are almost equal with a round chin and cheeks
  • Square Face - Width and length are almost equal with a square jawline and forehead
  • Heart Face - Narrow at the jawline and wider at the forehead
  • Diamond Face - Narrow at the forehead and jawline with wider cheekbones

Best Hairstyles for Long Oval Face

The best hairstyles for long oval face shape are those that add width to the forehead and jawline while complementing the length of the face. Here are some of the most flattering hairstyles for long oval face shape:

  • Long Layers
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Bob Cut
  • Wavy Lob
  • Low Ponytail
  • Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle
  • Top Knot

Step-by-Step Guide on Styling Your Hair

To achieve the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face shape, follow these simple steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  4. Curl your hair using a curling wand or hot rollers.
  5. Style your hair according to the hairstyle you prefer.
  6. Set your hairstyle using a hairspray.

Useful Tips to Consider

Here are some tips to consider when choosing the best hairstyle for your long oval face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much height at the crown of your head.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a center part with a long oval face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear a center part with a long oval face shape as it creates symmetry and balances out the length of the face.

Q: Will a short haircut suit a long oval face shape?

A: Yes, a short haircut can suit a long oval face shape as long as it adds volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

Maintaining your hairstyle is crucial to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Wash your hair regularly using products suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid overusing heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Choosing the best hairstyle for your long oval face shape can be overwhelming, but with these tips, you can find the perfect one that accentuates your features and complements your face shape. Remember to maintain your hairstyle with proper hair care to keep it looking its best.
