How To Pick The Best Sunglasses For Your Face Shape

Written by William Mar 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect pair of sunglasses to complement your face shape? Choosing the right pair of sunglasses can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll show you how to pick the best sunglasses for your face shape, so you can look stylish and protect your eyes from the sun's harmful rays.

Face Shapes

Before we dive into how to pick the best sunglasses for your face shape, let's first identify your face shape. There are six common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Knowing your face shape will help you narrow down your options and find the perfect pair of sunglasses for you.

How to Pick the Best Sunglasses for Your Face Shape

The key to picking the best sunglasses for your face shape is to find a pair that complements your features. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Round faces look best in angular or rectangular shapes.
  • Square faces look best in round or oval shapes.
  • Oval faces look great in almost any shape, but oversized frames can overpower smaller features.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best in round or square shapes with curved edges.
  • Diamond-shaped faces look best in oval or cat-eye shapes.
  • Triangle-shaped faces look best in aviator or cat-eye shapes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to find the perfect pair of sunglasses for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Determine which styles look best on your face shape.
  3. Consider the size and proportion of the frames.
  4. Choose a color that complements your skin tone.
  5. Try on multiple pairs to find the perfect fit.
  6. Make sure the sunglasses provide adequate UV protection.

Tips for Choosing the Best Sunglasses

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best sunglasses:

  • Consider the shape and size of your nose when choosing frames.
  • Choose a frame that is proportional to your face size.
  • Consider the shape and size of your eyebrows when choosing frames.
  • Choose a color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Invest in high-quality sunglasses that will last for years.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best sunglasses:

What color lenses are best for sunglasses?

Gray and brown lenses are the most popular and offer the most natural color perception. Green lenses are also a good option for bright conditions. Avoid yellow or rose-colored lenses, as they can distort color perception.

What size sunglasses should I wear?

Choose a frame that is proportional to your face size. The sunglasses should cover your eyes and eyebrows without overwhelming your face.

What is the best brand of sunglasses?

There are many great brands of sunglasses, but some of the most popular include Ray-Ban, Oakley, Maui Jim, and Costa Del Mar.

Hair Care Tips for Sunglasses Wearers

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great while wearing sunglasses:

  • Choose sunglasses with wide arms to prevent hair from getting caught.
  • Opt for a low ponytail or bun to prevent hair from getting tangled.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to prevent hair from getting dry and damaged by the sun.

Now that you know how to pick the best sunglasses for your face shape, you can shop with confidence and find the perfect pair to complete your look. Remember to choose high-quality sunglasses that provide adequate UV protection to keep your eyes safe and healthy.
