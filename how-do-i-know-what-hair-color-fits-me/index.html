How Do I Know What Hair Color Fits Me

Written by Luffy Aug 29, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're looking to change your hair color, it can be overwhelming to decide what shade will best suit you. With so many different colors and tones to choose from, it's important to consider factors such as your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide to choosing the perfect hair color for you.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty, boost your confidence, and even make you feel younger! But with so many options to choose from, it's easy to get lost in the sea of hair dyes at your local drugstore. That's why it's important to take the time to figure out what hair color will work best for you.

How To

There are several factors to consider when deciding what hair color will fit you best:

1. Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll want to go for warmer hair colors like honey blonde, copper, or rich brown. If you have a cool skin tone, you'll want to stick with cooler hair colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, or black.

2. Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a big role in determining what hair color will look best on you. If you have light hair, you'll have more options when it comes to coloring your hair. However, if you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before coloring it in order to achieve the desired shade.

3. Personal Style

Your personal style is another factor to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have a more edgy style, you may want to go for bold colors like bright red or purple. If you have a more classic style, you may want to stick with more natural hair colors.

Step by Step

Now that you know the factors to consider, here are the steps to follow when choosing a hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they're blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they're green, you have a warm skin tone. If they're a mix of both, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Think about your current hair color and how it will impact your desired hair color. If you have dark hair and want to go blonde, you may need to bleach your hair first. If you have light hair and want to go darker, you may need to add some warmth to avoid looking washed out.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Search for hair color inspiration online or in magazines. Look for people with similar skin tones and natural hair colors to get an idea of what might work for you.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure about what hair color will work best for you, consider scheduling a consultation with a professional stylist. They can help you determine what colors will look best on you and provide advice on how to maintain your new hair color.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider the season. In general, warmer hair colors work well in the fall and winter, while cooler hair colors are better for the spring and summer.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment! Hair color is temporary, so if you don't like your new color, you can always change it.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, choose a color that will require minimal upkeep.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to make sure you're not allergic to the dye.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause damage, especially if you bleach it or use harsh chemicals. However, there are products available that can help minimize damage and keep your hair healthy.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. In general, most people touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've dyed your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and fade your color.

By following these tips and considering the factors we've outlined, you'll be able to choose a hair color that will make you look and feel your best!
