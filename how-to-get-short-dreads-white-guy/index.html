How To Get Short Dreads White Guy A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a white guy looking to get short dreads, you're in the right place! Dreads are a popular hairstyle among men of all races, and they can be a great way to express yourself and show off your individual style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get short dreads as a white guy, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. So, without further ado, let's dive in!

How to Get Short Dreads White Guy - Step by Step

Getting short dreads as a white guy is a process that requires some patience and dedication. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

The first step in getting short dreads is to grow your hair out to a suitable length. Your hair should be at least 2-3 inches long to start the dreadlocking process.

Step 2: Decide on Your Dreadlock Size

The next step is to decide on the size of your dreadlocks. Short dreads can be anywhere from pencil-thin to chunky, depending on your preference. Keep in mind that thicker dreads will take longer to form than thinner ones.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Once you've decided on your dreadlock size, it's time to section your hair. Use a rat-tail comb to divide your hair into equal sections. The size of the sections will depend on the size of your dreads.

Step 4: Backcomb Your Hair

The next step is to backcomb your hair. This involves using a comb to tease your hair in the opposite direction of its natural growth. This creates tangles in your hair, which will eventually form into dreadlocks.

Step 5: Twist Your Hair

After backcombing, twist each section of hair in one direction until it begins to lock. This process can take several weeks or even months, depending on the thickness of your dreads.

Step 6: Maintain Your Dreads

Once your dreads have formed, it's important to maintain them properly. This means washing them regularly and using a dreadlock-specific shampoo. You'll also need to palm roll your dreads to keep them tight and neat.

Tips for Getting Short Dreads White Guy

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when getting short dreads as a white guy:

  • Use a natural hair oil to keep your scalp and hair moisturized
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat treatments on your hair
  • Be patient - dreadlocks take time to form and mature
  • Consider using a crochet hook to help lock your dreads faster
  • Cover your hair at night to prevent frizz

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about getting short dreads as a white guy:

Can white guys get dreadlocks?

Yes, white guys can get dreadlocks just like anyone else!

How long does it take to get short dreads?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to form short dreads, depending on the thickness of your hair and how well you maintain your dreads.

Do you need to have curly or kinky hair to get dreads?

No, you don't need to have curly or kinky hair to get dreads. However, if your hair is straight, it may take longer for your dreads to form and mature.

Hair Care for Short Dreads White Guy

Proper hair care is essential when it comes to maintaining short dreads as a white guy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair at least once a week with a dreadlock-specific shampoo
  • Avoid using conditioner, as it can loosen your dreads
  • Keep your scalp and hair moisturized with a natural hair oil
  • Palm roll your dreads regularly to keep them tight and neat
  • Cover your hair at night to prevent frizz

Conclusion

Getting short dreads as a white guy is a process that requires patience and dedication, but the results can be well worth it. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to get the best results and enjoy your new hairstyle!
