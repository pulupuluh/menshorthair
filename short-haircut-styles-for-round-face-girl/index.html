Short Haircut Styles For Round Face Girl

Written by Oliver Jul 20, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. However, short haircuts can be an excellent option for round face girls as they can help create the illusion of length and slim down the face. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircut styles for round face girls.

Description

When it comes to short haircut styles for round face girls, the goal is to create a look that elongates the face and adds volume to the crown. The right haircut can help to balance out the proportions of the face, making it appear slimmer and more angular.

How to

Here are some steps to help you choose the right short haircut style for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine the shape of your face. Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved lines and lack of sharp angles. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all about the same width, you have a round face.

Step 2: Look for Length

When choosing a short haircut, look for styles that add length to the face. This can be achieved through layers or by having the hair cut shorter on the sides and longer on top.

Step 3: Add Volume

Adding volume to the crown can also help to elongate the face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, or by using volumizing products.

Step 4: Consider Your Hair Type

When choosing a short haircut style, it’s important to consider your hair type. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume, as they may be difficult to achieve. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a layered or textured style to help reduce bulk.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut style for round face girls:

  • Consider the length of your neck – a shorter neck may require a shorter haircut
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or bobs that end at the jawline
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircut styles for round face girls:

What short haircuts are best for round faces?

Short haircuts that add length to the face and create volume at the crown are best for round faces. Some popular styles include pixie cuts, bobs with layers, and asymmetrical cuts.

Can I still have bangs with a round face?

Yes, but it’s important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept or tapered bangs can help to create a more angular look, while blunt bangs should be avoided as they can add width to the face.

How do I style my short haircut?

The best way to style your short haircut will depend on the style you choose and your hair type. Your stylist can provide you with tips and advice on how to style your hair at home.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Be sure to schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep your haircut looking fresh. You may also want to invest in quality styling products to help you achieve your desired look.

With the right short haircut style, round face girls can achieve a stylish and flattering look. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a haircut, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
