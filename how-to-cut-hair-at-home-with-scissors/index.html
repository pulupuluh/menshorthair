How To Cut Hair At Home With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the hair salon? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair at home with scissors? It's easier than you think. In this guide, we'll go over the steps to cut your hair at home with scissors, tips for a successful cut, and how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

How to Cut Hair at Home with Scissors

Cutting your own hair at home can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Here are the steps to cut your hair at home with scissors:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section hair

Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more accurate result.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use clips to section your hair. Start by dividing your hair into four sections: top, back, and two sides. Clip each section out of the way, leaving one section loose to work on.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair in small sections. Hold a section of hair between your fingers and cut the ends off at an angle. Be sure to cut a little bit at a time, as it's easier to take more off than to add it back.

Continue cutting each section of hair until you've trimmed all the way around your head.

Step 5: Check for Balance

Once you've finished cutting, check for balance. Make sure both sides and the back are even.

Step 6: Clean Up the Ends

After you've checked for balance, clean up the ends by making small snips where necessary.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. If you're unsure how to style your hair, look up tutorials online or consult with a stylist.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a successful cut:

  • Start by cutting less than you think you need to. It's easier to take more off than to add it back.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't hesitate to take a break if you need one.
  • Don't be too hard on yourself if it doesn't turn out perfectly. It's just hair, and it will grow back!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair at home with scissors:

Is it easy to cut your own hair at home?

It can be easy if you have the right tools and techniques. Take your time and start with small cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most people need a trim every 6-8 weeks.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, it's okay! You can always go to a salon to have it fixed, or wait for it to grow out.

Hair Care

After you cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like a straightener or curling iron, too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great!

Now that you know how to cut your hair at home with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be too hard on yourself if it doesn't turn out perfectly. Happy cutting!
