How To Get Different Hairstyles In Animal Crossing New Leaf

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to spice up your character's style in Animal Crossing New Leaf? Whether you want to try out a new hair color or experiment with a different hairstyle, there are many options available in the game. Here's a guide on how to get different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf.

How to

There are a few ways to get different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

1. Visit Harriet's Beauty Salon

Harriet's Beauty Salon is located in Shampoodle, which is on the second floor of the Able Sisters building. Once you've unlocked the Able Sisters, you can visit Harriet's Beauty Salon and talk to Harriet. She will ask you a series of questions to determine your character's personality and recommend a new hairstyle. You can also choose to customize your hair color.

2. Use Hair Color Spray

You can purchase Hair Color Spray from the Able Sisters for 3,000 Bells. This spray allows you to change the color of your character's hair without changing the style.

3. Use a Mii Mask

If you have a Mii character saved on your Nintendo 3DS, you can purchase a Mii Mask from the Able Sisters for 3,000 Bells. This mask will change your character's appearance to look like your Mii character, including the hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

1. Unlock the Able Sisters

To access Harriet's Beauty Salon, you must first unlock the Able Sisters. To do this, you need to spend 10,000 Bells at the Able Sisters' shop and wait for them to build a permanent store in your town.

2. Visit Harriet's Beauty Salon

Once the Able Sisters have built their permanent store, you can visit Harriet's Beauty Salon on the second floor. Talk to Harriet to get started.

3. Answer Harriet's Questions

Harriet will ask you a series of questions to determine your character's personality. Your answers will determine which hairstyles are available to you.

4. Choose Your New Hairstyle

Harriet will show you a selection of hairstyles that are available to you based on your answers. Choose the one you like best.

5. Customize Your Hair Color (Optional)

If you want to change your character's hair color, you can do so at this point. Harriet will show you a selection of colors to choose from.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

1. Save Your Bells

Getting a new hairstyle can be expensive, especially if you want to customize your hair color. Make sure you have enough Bells saved up before you visit Harriet's Beauty Salon.

2. Experiment

Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles and colors. You can always change your hair again later.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

1. Can I change my hairstyle after I've chosen one?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle at any time by visiting Harriet's Beauty Salon again.

2. Can I change my hair color without changing my hairstyle?

Yes, you can use Hair Color Spray to change your hair color without changing your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Just like in real life, taking care of your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf is important. Here are a few tips:

1. Visit the Salon Regularly

Make sure to visit Harriet's Beauty Salon regularly to keep your hair looking fresh.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Adding hair accessories like hats, headbands, and bows can add some extra flair to your character's style.

3. Take Care of Your Hair Color

If you've used Hair Color Spray to change your hair color, make sure to take care of it. Avoid swimming or getting caught in the rain, as this can cause the color to fade.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find a hairstyle that suits your character's style in Animal Crossing New Leaf. Happy styling!
