What Haircuts Suit Heart Shaped Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts that don't seem to suit your heart-shaped face? Look no further, as we have compiled a comprehensive guide for the best haircuts that complement your facial features. From short pixie cuts to long layers, we have got you covered.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal is to balance out the features by creating width at the jawline and adding volume to the top of the head. With the right haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin.

How to

The first step is to determine your face shape. If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead will be the widest part of your face, and your chin will be the narrowest. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start exploring different haircut options that flatter your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and creates width at the jawline.

Step 3: Consider adding layers to your haircut to soften your features.

Step 4: Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect fit.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear unbalanced.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as layered cuts or bobs.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut to draw attention to your eyes and balance out your features.
  • If you prefer long hair, consider adding layers to create movement and dimension.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face?

The best haircut for a heart-shaped face is one that adds volume to the top of your head and creates width at the jawline. Some popular options include layered cuts, bobs, and long layers.

Should I get bangs if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes, bangs can be a great option for heart-shaped faces as they draw attention to your eyes and balance out your features. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes, a middle part can work well for heart-shaped faces, as it can help to balance out your features. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to avoid making your face appear too long.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is tailored to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips in mind, you can rock any haircut that suits your heart-shaped face and feel confident in your own skin. So go ahead, book that appointment with your hairstylist, and get ready to turn heads.
