Is Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum Good For Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum is a popular hair care product that promises to smooth and tame unruly hair. But is it suitable for those with curly hair? In this blog post, we will take a closer look at the benefits of using Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on curly hair and answer some of the most frequently asked questions about this product.

Description

Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum is a lightweight, silicone-based serum designed to smooth and tame frizzy hair. It is infused with various natural ingredients, including wheat germ oil and jojoba seed oil, that help to nourish and protect the hair while providing a smooth, glossy finish.

How to Use Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on Curly Hair

If you have curly hair and are considering using Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Curly hair tends to be naturally dry, so it is essential to use a moisturizing shampoo that will not strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply the serum

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently to remove excess water. Take a small amount of Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum and apply it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Style your hair

You can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to style your curls. If you choose to blow-dry your hair, use a low heat setting and avoid using a brush, as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Using Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on Curly Hair

Here are some tips to get the most out of using Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on your curly hair:

  • Start with a small amount of serum and gradually add more if needed
  • Focus on the ends of your hair, as this is where frizz tends to be most noticeable
  • Avoid applying the serum to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they are wet
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it is drying, as this can cause frizz
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls moisturized

FAQ

Is Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum suitable for all types of curly hair?

Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum is suitable for most types of curly hair, including wavy, curly, and coily hair. However, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a smaller amount of serum to avoid weighing your hair down.

Can I use Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on dry hair?

Yes, you can use Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on dry hair to tame frizz and add shine. Simply take a small amount of serum and apply it to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

How often should I use Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on my curly hair?

You can use Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum on your curly hair every time you wash it. However, if you find that your hair is getting weighed down or greasy, you may want to use it every other wash or reduce the amount you are using.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your curls
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized throughout the day

Conclusion

Overall, Paul Mitchell Super Skinny Serum can be a great addition to your curly hair care routine. It can help to tame frizz, smooth your hair, and add shine, leaving you with gorgeous, healthy-looking curls. Just remember to use it sparingly, focus on the ends of your hair, and avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals on your curls. With the right care and attention, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls that are the envy of everyone around you!
