Shoulder Length Haircuts With Long Bangs A Chic And Versatile Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, versatile, and low maintenance? Look no further than shoulder length haircuts with long bangs. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a chic look without committing to a dramatic change. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs
  2. Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs
  4. FAQs About Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

How to Get Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

The first step in getting shoulder length haircuts with long bangs is to find a good stylist. Look for someone who has experience with this hairstyle and who can give you the look you want. Once you’ve found a stylist, schedule a consultation appointment to discuss your hair goals and to get their recommendations.

During the consultation, your stylist will examine your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best length and style for your hair. They may suggest adding layers or texture to your hair to give it more volume and movement.

Once you and your stylist have agreed on the length and style, they will begin cutting your hair. Your stylist will likely start by cutting the back of your hair to the desired length, and then move on to the sides and bangs.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

Shoulder length haircuts with long bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the look that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling shoulder length haircuts with long bangs:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair.
  4. Part your hair in the middle or to one side.
  5. Tuck the bangs behind your ears or sweep them to one side.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  7. Spray with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

What face shapes work best with shoulder length haircuts with long bangs?

Shoulder length haircuts with long bangs work well with most face shapes, but they are particularly flattering for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

What hair types work best with shoulder length haircuts with long bangs?

Shoulder length haircuts with long bangs work well with most hair types, but they are particularly flattering for those with fine or medium hair. If you have thick or curly hair, your stylist may recommend adding layers to your hair to help it lay flat.

How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

It’s recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to avoid split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Haircuts with Long Bangs

To keep your shoulder length haircuts with long bangs looking their best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid tying your hair up in tight ponytails or buns, as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

In conclusion, shoulder length haircuts with long bangs are a chic and versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. Follow these tips for styling and hair care to keep your hair looking its best.
