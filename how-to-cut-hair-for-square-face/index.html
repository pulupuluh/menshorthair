How To Cut Hair For Square Face A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read
If you have a square face shape, you need to choose the right hairstyle that will flatter your features. While it can be challenging to find the perfect cut, it's not impossible. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair for square face shapes and provide tips on how to maintain your new look.

Description

A square face shape has a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The goal of a good haircut is to soften the sharpness of these features and create a more balanced look. A good haircut will also highlight your best features, such as your cheekbones and eyes.

How to Cut Hair for Square Face

When cutting hair for square face shapes, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create softness around the jawline. Second, you want to add volume to the top of the head to balance out the wide forehead. Finally, you want to create layers to add movement and texture to the hair.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. 2. Cut the top section first, creating layers that fall around the face. This will add volume to the top of the head and balance out the wide forehead. 3. Move on to the sides, creating soft layers that frame the face. Avoid cutting the hair too short around the jawline, as this can make the face appear more angular. 4. Cut the back section, adding layers for movement and texture. 5. Blend the layers together, creating a seamless look.

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear more angular. - Opt for soft layers and textured cuts. - Add volume to the top of the head to balance out the wide forehead. - Avoid cutting the hair too short around the jawline. - Use a round brush when blow-drying the hair to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face shape? A: Avoid blunt cuts, straight bangs, and hairstyles that accentuate the jawline. Q: What hairstyles are best for square face shapes? A: Soft layers, textured cuts, and hairstyles with volume at the top of the head are all great options for square face shapes. Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a square face shape? A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools, and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. In conclusion, cutting hair for square face shapes can be challenging, but with the right techniques and styles, it's possible to create a flattering and balanced look. Remember to choose soft layers, add volume to the top of the head, and avoid cutting the hair too short around the jawline. With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your square face shape.
