How To Get Volume In Thin Hair Naturally At Home

Written by Luffy Sep 05, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thin hair, you know how difficult it can be to get volume. You've probably tried countless products that promise to give you fuller, thicker hair, only to be disappointed. But don't give up hope just yet! There are natural ways to add volume to your hair without spending a fortune on expensive products. In this article, we'll show you how to get volume in thin hair naturally at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Volume in Thin Hair Naturally at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Thin Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

How to Get Volume in Thin Hair Naturally at Home

There are a few natural methods to add volume to thin hair. Here are some great ways to achieve fuller, thicker hair:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can create volume at the roots. Start by flipping your hair over and blow-dry your hair from the roots to the tips. This will create lift and fullness at the crown of your head.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add thickness and volume to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and amino acids, which can help strengthen and thicken hair strands.

3. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair. Apply dry shampoo to your roots, then use your fingers to massage it into your scalp. This will help create lift and fullness at the crown of your head.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can create volume and texture. Take a section of hair and backcomb it using a fine-toothed comb. Repeat this process around the crown of your head to create lift and fullness.

5. Use Hair Rollers

Hair rollers can add volume and curl to your hair. Roll your hair in sections and leave the rollers in for at least 30 minutes. Remove the rollers and gently brush out your hair to create fullness and volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get volume in thin hair naturally at home:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Dry Shampoo

Apply dry shampoo to your roots to absorb excess oil and add volume. Massage the dry shampoo into your scalp using your fingers.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your hair over and blow-dry your hair from the roots to the tips. Use your fingers to lift and separate your hair as you blow dry to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take a section of hair and backcomb it using a fine-toothed comb. Repeat this process around the crown of your head to create lift and fullness.

Step 5: Use Hair Rollers

Roll your hair in sections using hair rollers. Leave the rollers in for at least 30 minutes, then remove them and gently brush out your hair to create fullness and volume.

Tips for Adding Volume to Thin Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to thin hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Try a volumizing mousse or root lift spray to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

A: Yes! You can add volume to your hair by using hair rollers or by teasing your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. However, washing your hair every other day can help prevent your hair from becoming too oily or dry.

Q: Is it bad to tease your hair?

A: Teasing your hair can cause damage and breakage, so it's important to do it gently and sparingly. Use a fine-toothed comb and be gentle when backcombing your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular exercise to improve circulation and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these natural methods and hair care tips, you can achieve fuller, thicker hair without spending a fortune on expensive products. So go ahead and give them a try!
