How Do I Know If I ll Look Good With Bangs

Written by Petter May 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting bangs, you may be wondering how to know if they'll look good on you. Bangs can completely transform your look, but it's important to know what style will work for your face shape and hair texture. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks to help you figure out if bangs are right for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Know Your Face Shape
  2. Consider Your Hair Texture
  3. Try It Out
  4. Consult a Stylist
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Know Your Face Shape

The key to determining whether bangs will suit you is knowing your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each shape has different features and proportions, which means that certain styles of bangs will complement them better than others.

For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, you may want to go for softer, wispy bangs that will soften your features.

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is another important factor to consider when deciding on bangs. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid thick, heavy bangs that can weigh your hair down. Instead, opt for lighter, wispy bangs that won't overwhelm your hair. If you have thick hair, you may be able to pull off heavier bangs that will add some structure to your look.

Try It Out

If you're still unsure whether bangs will suit you, why not try them out before committing to a full cut? You can create faux bangs by sectioning off the front of your hair and pinning it back to create a fringe. This will give you an idea of how you'll look with bangs without making a permanent change.

Consult a Stylist

If you're still unsure about whether bangs will suit you, it's always best to consult a stylist. They will be able to give you advice on what style of bangs will work for your face shape and hair texture. They can also help you make any adjustments to your existing hairstyle to make sure that your new bangs will complement it.

FAQs

Can anyone pull off bangs?

While bangs can look great on many people, they may not be the best choice for everyone. It's important to consider your face shape and hair texture before deciding to get bangs.

What kind of bangs should I get?

The type of bangs that will suit you will depend on your face shape and hair texture. A stylist will be able to give you advice on what style of bangs will work best for you.

Will bangs make my hair greasy?

Bangs can be more prone to becoming greasy, as they come into contact with your forehead more than the rest of your hair. It's important to keep your bangs clean and dry to prevent them from becoming greasy.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

If you decide to get bangs, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to sit to set them in place.
  • Use a dry shampoo or baby powder to absorb any excess oil in your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a straightener or curling iron to style your bangs depending on the look you're going for.

Now that you know how to determine whether bangs will suit you, it's time to go out and try them for yourself. Whether you opt for a wispy fringe or a full-on blunt cut, you're sure to find a style that suits you and your unique look.
