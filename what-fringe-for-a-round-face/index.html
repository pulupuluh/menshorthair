What Fringe For A Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what kind of fringe (or bangs) would suit you best. The right fringe can help balance out your features and make your face look slimmer, so it's important to choose the right one. In this article, we'll take a look at what fringe for a round face and provide some tips on how to style it.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what fringe for a round face, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is wider at the cheeks and tapers towards the chin, you have a round face shape.

Types of Fringe for a Round Face

When it comes to what fringe for a round face, there are several options to choose from:

Side-Swept Fringe

A side-swept fringe is a great choice for a round face. This type of fringe is longer on one side and gradually gets shorter towards the other side. It helps create the illusion of a slimmer face and draws attention to your eyes.

Long, Wispy Fringe

If you want to keep your forehead covered but don't want a heavy fringe, a long, wispy fringe could be a good option. This type of fringe is longer in the middle and gradually gets shorter towards the sides. It helps create the illusion of length and slims down your face.

Curly Fringe

If you have curly hair, a curly fringe could work well for you. This type of fringe is cut in a way that allows your curls to fall naturally, creating a soft and romantic look.

Blunt Fringe

A blunt fringe is cut straight across and can be a bold choice for a round face. This type of fringe can help create the illusion of a longer face, but it's important to make sure it's not too heavy or thick, as this can make your face look even rounder.

How to Style Your Fringe

Once you've chosen what fringe for a round face, it's important to style it properly. Here are some tips:

Blow Dry Your Fringe

Blow-drying your fringe can help give it shape and keep it in place. Use a round brush to create volume and curl the ends under for a polished look.

Straighten Your Fringe

If you have a curly fringe, you might want to consider straightening it. This can help give it a sleek and polished look.

Use Products

Using the right products can help keep your fringe in place and prevent it from getting oily or greasy. Try using a light-hold hairspray or a dry shampoo to keep your fringe looking fresh.

FAQ

Can I have a fringe if I have a round face?

Yes, you can definitely have a fringe if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose the right type of fringe that will flatter your features.

What type of fringe should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid heavy or thick fringes that will make your face look even rounder. Also, avoid fringes that are cut straight across, as this can create a boxy look.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Try to avoid using heated tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

In conclusion, what fringe for a round face? There are several options to choose from, including side-swept, long and wispy, curly, and blunt fringes. Choose the one that flatters your face shape and style it properly using the tips we've provided. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
