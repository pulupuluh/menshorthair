Short Hairstyles For Round Face Girl How To Look Your Best

Written by Christine Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a round face girl looking for the perfect short hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the top short hairstyles that flatter round faces. From bobs to pixie cuts, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Round face girls have a face shape that is wider at the cheeks and forehead, with a rounded chin. The goal with any hairstyle for a round face is to create a slimming effect and to elongate the face. Short hairstyles can be a great option for round face girls, as they can create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to other features such as the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face girl, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, consider the length of the cut. A shorter cut can help to elongate the face, but be careful not to go too short as this can make the face appear wider. Second, think about the shape of the cut. A layered cut can add texture and movement to the hair, while a blunt cut can create a sleek and sophisticated look. Finally, consider the placement of the part. A side part can help to create the illusion of a longer face, while a center part can make the face appear wider.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the top short hairstyles for round face girls:

Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part the hair to the side.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry the hair straight.
  4. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a deep side part.
  3. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Use a razor to add texture and movement to the hair.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect short hairstyle for a round face girl:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or curls.
  • Consider adding layers to the hair to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different part placements to find what works best for your face shape.
  • Use a volumizing mousse to add body to the roots of the hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round face girls:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that creates the illusion of a longer face. This can be achieved with a layered cut, a side part, and a length that falls just below the jawline.

Can round face girls wear bangs?

While blunt bangs are not recommended for round face girls, side-swept bangs can be a great option. They can create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Should round face girls avoid short haircuts?

No, round face girls can definitely rock a short haircut! The key is to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

Short hairstyles can be a great option for round face girls, as long as the right cut is chosen. Consider the length, shape, and part placement of the cut to create a slimming effect and draw attention to your best features. With the right care and maintenance, your short hairstyle can look great for weeks to come!
