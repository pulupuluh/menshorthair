Is Feathered Hair Back In Style 2020

Written by Aprilia Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Feathered hair was a popular hairstyle back in the 1970s, and it is making a comeback in 2020. If you are wondering whether feathered hair is back in style, the answer is yes! Many celebrities and fashion icons have been spotted with feathered hair recently, and it has become one of the hottest hair trends of 2020.

Description

Feathered hair is a hairstyle that involves layers of hair that are flipped outwards. It creates a feather-like effect and adds volume to the hair. The hairstyle was popularized by Farrah Fawcett in the 1970s, and it became a trend among women of all ages.

How to Get Feathered Hair in 2020

If you want to get the feathered hair look in 2020, here's how you can achieve it:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to get rid of any dirt or oil. This will make your hair easier to style and give it a clean and fresh look.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create the feathered effect. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 3: Create Layers

Create layers in your hair by cutting it in a feathered style. You can do this yourself or go to a professional hairstylist.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outwards to create the feathered effect. Be careful not to burn your hair or scalp.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a good quality hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Feathered Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining feathered hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair

FAQ About Feathered Hair

Q: Is feathered hair suitable for all hair types?

A: Feathered hair can work on all hair types, but it looks best on medium to long hair.

Q: Can I achieve the feathered hair look without cutting my hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the feathered hair look without cutting your hair. You can use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outwards to create the feathered effect.

Q: Can I style my feathered hair differently?

A: Yes, you can style your feathered hair in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy, depending on your preference.

Hair Care for Feathered Hair

To keep your feathered hair healthy and looking great, you need to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.

With these tips, you can keep your feathered hair looking great and stay on top of the latest hair trends in 2020.
