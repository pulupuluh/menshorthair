Shoulder Length Straight Hair Long Layers A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you thinking about getting a new haircut? Shoulder length straight hair with long layers is a great option for those who want a fresh and modern look without sacrificing too much length. In this guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about this style, from how to achieve it to hair care tips to FAQs.

Description

Shoulder length straight hair long layers is a haircut that features layers that are longer than the typical layers you’d find in a layered haircut. The layers start at the chin or just below it, and they add movement and dimension to the hair without taking away too much length. This style is perfect for those who want to switch up their look without going too short.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Straight Hair Long Layers

If you’re thinking about getting shoulder length straight hair long layers, here’s how to achieve the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  4. Starting at the back, take a one-inch section of hair and hold it straight up.
  5. Cut the hair at a 45-degree angle, making sure to keep the length of the hair intact.
  6. Repeat this process on the remaining sections of hair.
  7. Once you’ve cut all sections of hair, shake your hair out to see how the layers fall.
  8. If necessary, go back in and make additional cuts to perfect the look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step-by-step guide to achieving shoulder length straight hair long layers:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It’s best to start with hair that hasn’t been washed in a day or two, as it will be easier to work with.
  2. Part your hair down the middle. Use a comb to create a straight and even part down the middle of your head.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips to separate each section of hair.
  4. Starting at the back, take a one-inch section of hair and hold it straight up. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and hold it straight up so that it’s perpendicular to your head.
  5. Cut the hair at a 45-degree angle, making sure to keep the length of the hair intact. Use sharp haircutting scissors to make your cut. Make sure to keep the length of the hair intact, as you don’t want to cut too much off.
  6. Repeat this process on the remaining sections of hair. Work your way through each section of hair, making sure to maintain an even length throughout.
  7. Once you’ve cut all sections of hair, shake your hair out to see how the layers fall. This will give you a better idea of how the layers will look once your hair is styled.
  8. If necessary, go back in and make additional cuts to perfect the look. Take your time and make sure you’re happy with the length of each layer before moving on.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Straight Hair Long Layers

Once you’ve achieved the perfect shoulder length straight hair long layers, it’s important to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your layers.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length straight hair long layers:

Q. Will shoulder length straight hair long layers work on all hair types?

A. Shoulder length straight hair long layers can work on most hair types, but it’s best to consult with your stylist to see if it will work for your specific hair type.

Q. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A. It’s recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your layers.

Q. Can I still wear my hair up with shoulder length straight hair long layers?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair up with shoulder length straight hair long layers. However, the layers may make your updo look a bit messier than usual.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Straight Hair Long Layers

Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length straight hair long layers:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.

Shoulder length straight hair long layers is a great option for those who want a fresh and modern look without sacrificing too much length. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain the perfect shoulder length straight hair long layers.
