Best Braid Hair Style For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Feb 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that suits your round face shape? Braids are a perfect solution for you! In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on the best braid hair style for round face. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips. Let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Round faces have a width and height that are the same. If you have a round face, you need a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds volume to your hair. Braids are a great option for a round face because they add height and shape to your hair. They also help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

How to Style Your Hair

The first step in styling your hair is to choose the right type of braid. There are many different types of braids, including French braids, Dutch braids, fishtail braids, and more. Choose a braid that you like and that suits your hair length and texture.

Next, decide where you want to start the braid. You can start at the top of your head, the side of your head, or the back of your head. Starting at the top of your head will create a crown braid, while starting at the side will create a side braid.

Once you have decided on the type of braid and where to start, it's time to start braiding. Follow the steps below for a step by step guide on how to braid your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Divide your hair into three sections.

Step 3: Take the right section and cross it over the middle section.

Step 4: Take the left section and cross it over the middle section.

Step 5: Continue braiding your hair by crossing the right and left sections over the middle section.

Step 6: Secure the braid with a hair tie at the end.

Tips for Maintaining Your Braids

Once you have braided your hair, it's important to maintain it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Follow these tips for maintaining your braids:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your braids from frizzing.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least two weeks after braiding to allow your hair to settle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Apply oil to your scalp to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids.

FAQs

Q: How long do braids last?

A: Braids can last up to two months if maintained properly.

Q: Can I wash my hair with braids?

A: Yes, you can wash your hair with braids, but avoid using hot water.

Q: How do I remove braids?

A: To remove braids, cut the hair tie at the end of the braid and gently unravel the braid from the bottom up.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to brush your hair.

Now that you have a complete guide on the best braid hair style for round face, it's time to try it out for yourself. Braids are a great way to add volume and shape to your hair while elongating your face and making it look slimmer. Follow our tips and tricks for maintaining your braids and taking care of your hair, and you'll have a beautiful hairstyle that lasts for weeks.
