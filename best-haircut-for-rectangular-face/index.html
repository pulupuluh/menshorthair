Best Haircut For Rectangular Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Mar 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a rectangular face shape, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. But with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your facial structure and bring out your best features.

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a square jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately the same width, and the face is longer than it is wide.

How to Identify a Rectangular Face Shape

To identify if you have a rectangular face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Measure the length and width of your face with a tape measure.
  2. If your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately the same width, you have a rectangular face shape.

Best Haircuts for Rectangular Face

Here are some of the best haircuts that flatter a rectangular face:

Long Layers

Long layers add movement and softness to a rectangular face shape. The layers should start at the cheekbones to create a rounder and softer face shape.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for rectangular face shapes as they soften the forehead and add width to the face. The bangs should be swept to the side to create a diagonal line that breaks up the length of the face.

Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is perfect for rectangular face shapes as it creates the illusion of a wider face. The bob should be chin-length and layered to add volume and movement to the hair.

Wavy Lob

A wavy lob is a great option for rectangular face shapes as it adds volume and movement to the hair. The lob should be collarbone length and styled with loose waves to create a rounder and softer face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect haircut for a rectangular face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face.
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, a bob with volume, or a wavy lob.
  3. Show your hairstylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  4. Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on the best haircut for your face shape.
  5. Discuss the length and style of the haircut with your hairstylist.
  6. Ask your hairstylist to add layers or volume to the haircut to create a rounder and softer face shape.
  7. Ask your hairstylist to show you how to style your hair at home.
  8. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a rectangular face shape:

  • Avoid straight and blunt haircuts that accentuate the square jawline.
  • Try hairstyles with volume and movement to create a rounder and softer face shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, and hair clips to add dimension and interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for a rectangular face shape:

What is the most flattering haircut for a rectangular face?

The most flattering haircut for a rectangular face shape is one that adds volume and movement to the hair. Long layers, side-swept bangs, a bob with volume, and a wavy lob are all great options.

Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a rectangular face shape?

Short haircuts can be flattering for a rectangular face shape as long as they add volume and movement to the hair. A pixie cut or a short bob with layers can be a great option.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a rectangular face shape?

Straight hair can be flattering for a rectangular face shape as long as it is styled with volume and movement. A sleek straight hairstyle can accentuate the square jawline, so try adding curls or waves to soften the face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your rectangular face shape, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your hairstyle.
