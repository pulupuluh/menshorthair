Shoulder Length Hair For Oblong Face A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you might find it difficult to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about shoulder length hair for oblong face. From the best styles to care tips, we've got it all. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hair for Oblong Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and prominent chin. The key to choosing the right hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of width and volume. Shoulder length hair is an excellent choice for oblong face as it adds fullness and balance to the face. You can opt for various styles like layers, waves, and curls to enhance your features.

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hair for Oblong Face

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, you need to keep in mind the following tips:

1. Add Volume

As an oblong face tends to be long and narrow, you need to add volume to your hair to create the illusion of width. Shoulder length hair with layers or curls can help you achieve this.

2. Opt for Side Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break the length of the face and make it appear shorter. They also add volume to the hair and frame the face beautifully.

3. Avoid Center Parting

Avoid center parting as it can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a side parting to add volume and balance to your features.

4. Go for Soft Waves

Soft waves add volume to the hair and create a flattering frame for the face. You can achieve this style with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

5. Consider Layers

Layers add volume and movement to the hair, making it look fuller and more balanced. They also create a soft frame for the face, enhancing your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Hair

Here's a simple guide to styling your shoulder length hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Rinse well and towel dry.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product like mousse or hair serum to your hair. This will help add volume and hold your hairstyle in place.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to add volume and create soft waves or curls. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction of your face to create a flattering frame.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as per your preference. You can opt for loose waves, soft curls, or straight hair with layers.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your shoulder length hair:

1. Trim Regularly

Regular trims are essential to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use Heat Protectant

When using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

4. Avoid Over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs

1. Can I style my shoulder length hair if I have an oblong face?

Yes, shoulder length hair is an excellent choice for oblong face. You can opt for various styles like layers, waves, and curls to enhance your features.

2. How often should I trim my shoulder length hair?

Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Can I use heat tools on my shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can use heat tools like curling irons or straighteners on your shoulder length hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length hair:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brush your hair regularly to distribute the natural oils and prevent tangles.

3. Protect Your Hair from Sun and Wind

Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from sun and wind damage.

4. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage while you sleep.

So, there you have it - everything you need to know about shoulder length hair for oblong face. Remember to choose a style that adds volume and balance to your features, and maintain your hair with regular trims and proper care. With these tips, you'll be sure to rock your shoulder length hair like a pro!
