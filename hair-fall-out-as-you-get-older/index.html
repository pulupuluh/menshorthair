Hair Fall Out As You Get Older Causes Prevention And Treatment

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're reading this article, chances are you have noticed an increase in hair fall out as you get older. This is a common problem for both men and women, and it can be caused by a variety of factors. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why your hair might be falling out, as well as some tips on how to prevent and treat it.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older
  • Prevention of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older
  • Treatment of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older
  • Tips for Healthy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Causes of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older

As you age, your hair naturally starts to thin and fall out. This is because the hair follicles become less active, and the hair that does grow is thinner and weaker. However, there are other factors that can contribute to hair fall out, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Mental and physical stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications

If you are experiencing hair fall out, it is important to talk to your doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions or medication side effects.

Prevention of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older

While you can't stop the natural aging process, there are steps you can take to help prevent hair loss:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein and vitamins
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments and styling tools
  • Manage stress through exercise, meditation, or therapy
  • Get enough sleep and stay hydrated
  • Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners

It's also important to avoid tight hairstyles, as these can pull on the hair and cause damage to the follicles.

Treatment of Hair Fall Out as You Get Older

If you are experiencing significant hair loss, there are several treatment options available:

  • Over-the-counter hair growth products, such as minoxidil
  • Prescription medications, such as finasteride
  • Hair transplant surgery
  • Laser therapy
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy

It's important to talk to your doctor about which treatment option is right for you, as some may have side effects or be more effective for certain types of hair loss.

Tips for Healthy Hair

Whether you are experiencing hair fall out or just want to maintain healthy hair as you age, there are a few things you can do:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to avoid damaging the hair
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat from styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray
  • Consider taking a multivitamin or biotin supplement to support healthy hair growth

Frequently Asked Questions

Is hair fall out normal as you get older?

Yes, it is normal for hair to thin and fall out as you age. However, if you are experiencing significant hair loss, it is important to talk to your doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions.

Can stress cause hair fall out?

Yes, mental and physical stress can cause hair to fall out. This is because stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause hair follicles to enter a resting phase.

What can I do to prevent hair fall out?

Eating a balanced diet, avoiding harsh chemical treatments and styling tools, managing stress, and using gentle hair products can all help prevent hair fall out.

What treatments are available for hair fall out?

Treatment options for hair fall out include over-the-counter and prescription medications, hair transplant surgery, laser therapy, and PRP therapy.

How can I maintain healthy hair as I age?

To maintain healthy hair as you age, avoid over-washing your hair, use a wide-toothed comb or brush, avoid excessive heat from styling tools, protect your hair from the sun, and consider taking a multivitamin or biotin supplement.
