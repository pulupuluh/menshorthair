Hairstyles For Long Face Older Woman A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Mar 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin and lose volume. This can be especially challenging for women with long faces, as their features can appear even longer and more prominent. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter and enhance a long face. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for long face older women, as well as tips for hair care and styling.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  2. Step-by-Step: Styling Tips
  3. Expert Tips for Long Face Hairstyles
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Face Older Women

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to selecting a hairstyle for a long face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: Long hair can elongate the face even more, so shorter haircuts are often more flattering. However, if you prefer longer hair, adding layers or waves can help break up the length.
  • Volume: Adding volume to the sides of the face can balance out a long face shape. This can be achieved through layering, curls, or a volumizing product.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great option for long face shapes, as they can shorten the forehead and balance out the face. However, be careful not to choose heavy, blunt bangs that can make the face appear even longer.

Some of the best hairstyles for long face older women include:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for older women, as they can provide a more youthful and low-maintenance look. For long face shapes, a pixie cut or bob with layers can add volume and balance out the face. Adding side-swept bangs can also help shorten the forehead.

Medium-Length Haircuts

Medium-length haircuts can be a versatile option for older women with long faces. Adding layers or waves can help create volume and movement, while side-swept bangs or a center part can balance out the face. A lob (long bob) can also be a flattering option.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer longer hair, adding layers or waves can help prevent the hair from dragging down the face. A center part or side-swept bangs can also help balance out the face. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs or straight, one-length haircuts that can elongate the face even more.

Step-by-Step: Styling Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that works for your long face shape, it's important to know how to style it to enhance your features. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing products: Adding volume to the sides of the face can balance out a long face shape. Use a volumizing mousse or spray on damp hair, and blow dry with a round brush to create lift.
  • Add waves: Waves can add movement and texture to the hair, as well as create the illusion of width. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add loose waves to the ends of the hair.
  • Try a side part: A center part can elongate the face even more, so try a side part instead. This can help balance out the face and create the illusion of width.

Expert Tips for Long Face Hairstyles

Here are some additional tips from hair experts:

  • Experiment with layers: Layers can create volume and movement in the hair, as well as help balance out a long face shape. Try different lengths and angles to see what works best for you.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs: Heavy, blunt bangs can make the face appear even longer. Instead, opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that can shorten the forehead and balance out the face.
  • Don't be afraid of color: Adding highlights or lowlights can create depth and dimension in the hair, as well as draw attention away from a long face shape.

FAQs

Can older women with long faces wear short haircuts?

Yes! Short haircuts can be a great option for older women, especially those with long faces. A pixie cut or bob with layers can add volume and balance out the face.

What are some styling tips for long hair on a long face?

If you prefer longer hair, adding layers or waves can help prevent the hair from dragging down the face. A center part or side-swept bangs can also help balance out the face.

Hair Care for Long Face Older Women

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling products, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo: Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Switch to a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.
  • Deep condition once a week: Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish and moisturize the hair, preventing breakage and split ends.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage the hair and cause breakage. Try air-drying your hair or using heat-free styling techniques instead.

By choosing the right hairstyle and taking care of your hair, you can enhance your features and look and feel your best as an older woman with a long face.
