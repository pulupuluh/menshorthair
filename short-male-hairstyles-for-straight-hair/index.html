Short Male Hairstyles For Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to men's hairstyles, short hair is always in style. If you have straight hair and are looking for a new hairstyle to try, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover some of the best short male hairstyles for straight hair in 2023. From classic crew cuts to modern fades, we've got you covered.

Description

Straight hair is a great texture to work with when it comes to short hairstyles. It's easy to manage, and many styles require little to no styling. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance cut or something that requires a little more upkeep, there's a short hairstyle out there for you.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Before we get into specific hairstyles, it's important to consider your face shape. Different face shapes look better with different styles. Here's a quick guide:

  • Round face: short hairstyles with volume on top, such as a textured crop or side-swept fringe
  • Square face: short hairstyles with texture, such as a messy crop or a side-parted quiff
  • Oval face: almost any short hairstyle will work, but a textured crop or slicked back undercut are particularly flattering
  • Heart-shaped face: short hairstyles with volume on top, such as a messy crop or a pompadour

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

If you're planning on cutting your own hair, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. Here's a basic guide to cutting short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use the clippers to create a fade on the sides and back of the head.
  3. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length.
  4. Blend the two lengths together with the clippers.
  5. Use the scissors to add texture and shape to the hair.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair is easy, but there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a lightweight product, such as a matte clay or paste, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for added volume and control.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair, rather than a brush or comb, for a more natural look.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for straight hair?

Some of the best short hairstyles for straight hair include the crew cut, textured crop, side-swept fringe, messy crop, and slicked back undercut.

How often should I get my short hair cut?

Short haircuts typically require more frequent maintenance than longer styles. You should plan on getting your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair is easy to care for, but it still requires some maintenance. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and ideas for short male hairstyles for straight hair, you're sure to find a style that works for you. Whether you're looking for something classic or modern, there's a short hairstyle out there that will make you look and feel your best.
