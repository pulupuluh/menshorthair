How Should Your Hair Be For Box Braids

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Box braids are a great protective hairstyle for any hair type. They are popular because they can be styled in many different ways and are low maintenance. However, before you get box braids, you need to prepare your hair. In this article, we will discuss how your hair should be for box braids.

  • How to Prepare Your Hair for Box Braids
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Preparing Your Hair for Box Braids
  • Tips for Preparing Your Hair for Box Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

Description of Box Braids

Box braids are a protective hairstyle that involves braiding hair into small, square-shaped sections. The braids can be made with natural hair or extensions and can last up to 2-3 months with proper care. Box braids are popular because they are versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Prepare Your Hair for Box Braids

Before getting box braids, it is important to prepare your hair to ensure that the braids last as long as possible and do not damage your hair. Here are some steps you can take to prepare your hair for box braids:

Step-by-Step Guide to Preparing Your Hair for Box Braids

1. Wash your hair: Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or product residue. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

2. Deep condition: Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 15-30 minutes. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

3. Detangle: Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

4. Trim: If your hair has split ends or damage, trim it before getting box braids.

5. Moisturize: Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.

6. Stretch: If you have naturally kinky or curly hair, stretch it using a blow dryer or a stretching method to make it easier to braid.

Tips for Preparing Your Hair for Box Braids

- Avoid using heavy products before getting box braids as they can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to braid.

- Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage.

- Make sure your hair is completely dry before getting box braids to prevent mildew or bacteria growth.

FAQs

Q: Can I get box braids on relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can get box braids on relaxed hair. However, it is important to ensure that your hair is healthy and strong enough to withstand the tension of the braids.

Q: How long should I leave box braids in?

A: Box braids can last up to 2-3 months with proper care. However, it is recommended to remove them after 8 weeks to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

- Keep your scalp clean by washing it every 1-2 weeks with a diluted shampoo.

- Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.

- Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause breakage.

- Wrap your hair with a satin scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, preparing your hair for box braids is essential to ensure that the style looks great and lasts as long as possible. By following these steps and tips, you can ensure that your hair is healthy and strong enough to handle box braids.
