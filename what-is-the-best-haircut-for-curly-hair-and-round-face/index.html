What Is The Best Haircut For Curly Hair And Round Face

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair and round face can be a challenging combination when it comes to choosing the best haircut. The wrong haircut can make your face look rounder or your curls look frizzy and unkempt. But don't worry, we have got you covered. In this blog post, we will guide you on what is the best haircut for curly hair and round face.

Description

A curly hair and round face combination is a unique challenge because the curl pattern can make your face appear wider, and the roundness can make your curls look less defined. A good haircut can help balance out your features and enhance your natural curls.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Curly Hair and Round Face

Choosing the right haircut can be overwhelming, but here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Look for haircuts that add height to your hair, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for layers that frame your face, as this can soften your features.
  • Consider getting bangs that are swept to the side, as this can create the illusion of a longer face.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Haircut for Curly Hair and Round Face

Follow these steps to choose the best haircut for your curly hair and round face:

  1. Find inspiration: Look for pictures of haircuts that you like and think would suit your face shape and hair type.
  2. Consult a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Consider your lifestyle: Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle and maintenance routine.
  4. Try it out: Before committing to a new haircut, try it out by using virtual hairstyling apps or wearing a wig.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair can be a challenge, but these tips can help:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush and instead use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and let your curls air dry.
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for curly hair and round face?

The best haircut for curly hair and round face is one that adds height to your hair, has layers that frame your face, and avoids haircuts that are too short.

Can bangs work for curly hair and round face?

Yes, bangs can work for curly hair and round face. Opt for bangs that are swept to the side to create the illusion of a longer face.

How do I maintain my curly hair?

Maintain your curly hair by using sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding hairbrushes, applying leave-in conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims.

Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care products that can help you maintain your curly hair:

  • DevaCurl No-Poo Original Zero Lather Conditioning Cleanser
  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel
  • Moroccanoil Treatment

Now that you know what is the best haircut for curly hair and round face, you can confidently choose a haircut that suits your unique features and enhances your natural curls. Remember to maintain your curls with the right hair care products and techniques to keep them healthy and defined.
