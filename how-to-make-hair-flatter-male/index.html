How To Make Hair Flatter Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, voluminous hair? Do you want to achieve a sleek, flat hairstyle? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make hair flatter male. From choosing the right haircut to using the right products, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

  9. Use Hair Spray

Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in making your hair flatter is to choose the right haircut. A shorter haircut with layered sides and longer hair on top is a great option. This will allow you to style your hair back and create a flatter look. You can also opt for a buzz cut or crew cut if you prefer a shorter hairstyle.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in achieving a flat hairstyle. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture, such as sea salt spray or texturizing paste. Apply these products to your hair while it's still damp and use a comb to distribute them evenly.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is an important step in making it flatter. Use a hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle attachment to direct the airflow down the hair shaft. This will help to smooth out the hair and prevent it from becoming too poofy. Use a brush to help guide your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Flat Iron Your Hair

If you have curly or wavy hair, using a flat iron can help to create a flatter look. Use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to prevent damage to your hair. Use the flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Use a Heat Protectant

Using heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray before using them. This will help to prevent breakage and split ends, and keep your hair looking healthy.

Avoid Oily Products

Avoid using products that contain oils or silicones, as these can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Look for products that are lightweight and won't leave any residue on your hair.

Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great way to refresh your hair and absorb excess oils. Use it in between washes to keep your hair looking clean and flatter. Simply spray it onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

Style Your Hair Backwards

Styling your hair backwards can help to create a flatter look. Use a comb to slick your hair back and use a hair dryer to set it in place. You can also use a pomade or wax to add some texture and hold.

Use Hair Spray

Using a hair spray can help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from becoming too poofy. Look for a lightweight hair spray that won't weigh your hair down or leave any residue.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, taking care of your hair is important to achieve a flatter look. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle. Instead, wash it every other day or every few days. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush on wet hair, as this can cause breakage.

FAQs

Q. Can I make my hair flatter without using heat styling tools?

A. Yes, you can achieve a flatter look by using the right haircut and products. Look for products that are designed to add texture and volume, and style your hair backwards using a comb and hair dryer.

Q. Can I use a hair straightener instead of a flat iron?

A. Yes, a hair straightener can be used to create a flatter look. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and straighten your hair in small sections to avoid damaging it.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It's best to wash your hair every other day or every few days to avoid drying it out. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and personal preference.

Final Thoughts

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a sleek, flat hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Remember to choose the right haircut, use the right products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. With a little bit of effort, you can make your hair flatter and rock any look with confidence.
