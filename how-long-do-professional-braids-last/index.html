How Long Do Professional Braids Last

Written by Eveline Mar 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Description

Professional braids are a popular hairstyle for many people. They are often worn for special occasions, such as weddings or proms. However, many people also wear them as an everyday hairstyle. Professional braids are made by a hairstylist who has experience creating intricate designs with braids. But how long do professional braids last? In this article, we will explore the lifespan of professional braids and give tips on how to make them last longer.

How To

Professional braids are created using a variety of techniques, depending on the style and the client's hair type. The hairstylist will first section the hair and then create the braids using a weaving technique. The braids can be made using synthetic hair or natural hair, depending on the client's preference. The hairstylist will then secure the braids using either hair ties or pins.

Step by Step

1. Section the hair. 2. Create the braids using a weaving technique. 3. Use either hair ties or pins to secure the braids.

Tips

If you want your professional braids to last longer, there are a few things you can do. First, avoid washing your hair for the first few days after getting the braids. This will help the braids to set and stay in place. Second, avoid using heavy hair products, such as gels or oils, as these can weigh down the braids and cause them to unravel. Third, avoid sleeping on your braids or pulling them too tight, as this can cause them to come loose.

FAQ

How long do professional braids last?

Professional braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on the individual's hair type and the care they take of the braids.

Can you wash professional braids?

Yes, you can wash professional braids. However, it is best to wait a few days after getting the braids before washing them, to allow them to set and stay in place.

How do you take care of professional braids?

To take care of professional braids, avoid using heavy hair products, avoid sleeping on them or pulling them too tight, and avoid washing them for the first few days after getting them.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while wearing professional braids, it is important to moisturize your scalp and hair. You can use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your hair hydrated. It is also important to protect your hair from the sun and heat, as this can cause damage to the hair. Finally, when you take out the braids, be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair, as this can cause breakage. In conclusion, professional braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on the individual's hair type and the care they take of the braids. To make them last longer, avoid washing your hair for the first few days after getting the braids, avoid using heavy hair products, and avoid sleeping on them or pulling them too tight. To keep your hair healthy while wearing professional braids, moisturize your scalp and hair, protect your hair from the sun and heat, and be gentle when taking out the braids.
