How To Braid Your Own Hair Guys Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 02, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a guy with short hair and want to experiment with different hairstyles, braids are a great option. Braids are not only trendy, but they also add texture and volume to your hair. However, braiding your own hair can be tricky, especially if you're not used to it. In this article, we'll show you how to braid your own hair guys short, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Braid Your Own Hair Guys Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Braiding Your Own Hair Guys Short
  • FAQs

How to Braid Your Own Hair Guys Short

Braiding your own hair can seem intimidating, but with the right techniques and some practice, it can be done. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Hair wax or gel

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first for a smoother braid. Now, let's get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle using a comb. This will create two equal sections of hair.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline on one side. Divide it into three equal sections.

Step 3: Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair from the outer edge of the right section, and add it to the right section. Cross the right section over the middle section.

Step 5: Take a small section of hair from the outer edge of the left section, and add it to the left section. Cross the left section over the middle section.

Step 6: Repeat steps 4 and 5, alternating sides, until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 7: Secure the end of your braid with a hair tie.

Tips for Braiding Your Own Hair Guys Short

Now that you know the basics of braiding your own hair guys short, here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be discouraged if your first few attempts don't turn out the way you want.
  • Use hair wax or gel to help keep your hair in place while braiding.
  • Try different braid styles, such as a French braid or a fishtail braid.
  • Experiment with different parts and angles to create unique looks.

FAQs

Q: Can I braid my hair if it's too short?

A: It may be difficult to braid very short hair, but you can try using smaller sections and braiding them tightly.

Q: How long does it take to braid your own hair?

A: The time it takes to braid your own hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the style you're going for. It could take anywhere from a few minutes to an hour.

Hair Care for Braided Hair

Once you've braided your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids, as they can cause damage.
  • Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Cleanse your scalp and hair with a mild shampoo to remove buildup and keep your braids fresh.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or using a silk or satin pillowcase.

Now that you know how to braid your own hair guys short, you can experiment with different styles and looks. Have fun and don't be afraid to try something new!
