How To Cut Thick Bangs Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your bangs trimmed at the salon? Well, you can easily cut your thick bangs yourself with just a few tools and some patience. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut thick bangs yourself in a relaxed manner. Here’s what you need to know.

How to Cut Thick Bangs Yourself

Cutting your thick bangs yourself can seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are some steps you need to follow to cut your thick bangs yourself:

Step 1: Gather Tools

The first thing you need to do is gather the necessary tools. You will need sharp scissors, a fine-tooth comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Once you have gathered all the tools, wet your hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair by parting it down the middle. Take the section of hair you want to cut and clip the rest of your hair away.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using the fine-tooth comb, comb your hair straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut your hair straight across with the scissors. Remember to cut your hair a little longer than you want it to be as you can always go back and trim it later.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After cutting your hair, take a step back and check your work. If you need to make any adjustments, do so carefully.

Tips for Cutting Thick Bangs Yourself

Cutting your thick bangs can be challenging, but with a few tips, you can achieve a perfect look. Here are some tips to help you cut your thick bangs yourself:

  • Start by cutting your hair a little longer than you want it to be.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Go slow and cut a little at a time.
  • Always check your work before making any adjustments.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQs

How often should I cut my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should cut your bangs every four to six weeks.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon and get it fixed.

Can I cut my bangs when they are dry?

It is not recommended to cut your bangs when they are dry as it can cause uneven cuts.

Hair Care

After cutting your bangs, it’s essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. If you want to style your hair, use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

Cutting your thick bangs yourself can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll have beautiful, thick bangs in no time!
