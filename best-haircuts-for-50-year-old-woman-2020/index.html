Best Haircuts For 50 Year Old Woman 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're a 50-year-old woman looking for a new haircut, you're at the right place. We've rounded up some of the best haircuts for 50-year-old women that are trending in 2020. Whether you prefer long or short hair, straight or curly, there's a haircut that will suit your style and personality. In this article, we'll show you how to choose the best haircut, how to take care of your hair, and tips to keep your hair looking fabulous. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Best Haircut for 50-Year-Old Women

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best haircut is to consider your face shape. Oval faces can pull off almost any haircut, while round faces look best with longer hair. Square faces look great with short hair, while heart-shaped faces look best with a side-swept bang.

2. Think About Your Hair Texture

The next step is to think about your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered haircut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help to control it. If you have curly hair, consider a medium-length cut with layers to enhance your curls.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

The final step is to consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance haircut. If you have a professional job, you may want a more polished look. If you're a busy mom, you may want a style that's easy to maintain.

Best Haircuts for 50-Year-Old Women

Now that you know how to choose the best haircut, let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for 50-year-old women:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic that never goes out of style. It's a great choice for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that's easy to style. The bob can be worn straight or curled, and it's a great option for women with thin or fine hair.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, edgy haircut that's perfect for women who want a bold look. It's a great choice for women with a heart-shaped face or strong facial features. The pixie cut can be styled with a side-swept bang or a textured look.

3. Shoulder-Length Haircut

The shoulder-length haircut is a versatile option that can be worn straight or curled. It's a great choice for women who want a longer haircut without the maintenance of long hair. The shoulder-length haircut can be layered or blunt, depending on your hair texture and face shape.

4. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women who want to keep their length but add movement and volume to their hair. Long layers are great for women with thick hair, and they can be styled with beachy waves or a straight look.

Step by Step: How to Get the Best Haircut for 50-Year-Old Women

Now that you've chosen the best haircut, it's time to get it. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

1. Find a Good Stylist

The first step is to find a good stylist who understands your hair type and face shape. Look for reviews online or ask for recommendations from friends.

2. Bring Pictures

Before your appointment, bring pictures of the haircut you want. This will help your stylist understand the look you're going for.

3. Discuss Your Hair Type and Face Shape

When you meet with your stylist, discuss your hair type and face shape. This will help them determine the best haircut for you.

4. Trust Your Stylist

Finally, trust your stylist. They're experts in their field and will give you a haircut that suits your style and personality.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Fabulous

Now that you have the perfect haircut, here are some tips to keep your hair looking fabulous:

1. Use the Right Products

Use the right products for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't overwash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims. Trimming your hair every 6 to 8 weeks will help to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for fine hair?

A layered haircut is a great choice for fine hair as it can add volume and movement.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

A blunt cut is a great choice for thick hair as it can help to control it.

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

A medium-length cut with layers is a great choice for curly hair as it can enhance your curls.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is important to keep it looking fabulous. Here are some tips:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet. Foods rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Drink plenty of water. Staying hydrated can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent your hair from becoming damaged by the sun.

4. Don't Use Too Many Products

Don't use too many products. Overloading your hair with products can cause it to become weighed down and greasy.

With these tips and haircuts, you're sure to find a look that suits you perfectly. Remember to choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, and take care of your hair to keep it looking fabulous. Happy styling!
