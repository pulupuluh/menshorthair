Which Hairstyle Best Suits My Face

Written by Oliver May 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Welcome to our blog post on which hairstyle best suits your face. This post is designed to help you determine the best hairstyle for your unique facial features. We’ll cover everything from the shape of your face to your hair type and texture. Whether you have long, short, curly, or straight hair, we’ve got you covered. So, without further ado, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type and Texture
  • Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step to determining which hairstyle is best for you is to identify your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a hairstyle with volume at the top.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck because almost any hairstyle will suit you. You can opt for a short or long cut, and you can experiment with different styles and textures.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that softens your features. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a side-swept bang.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a hairstyle with volume at the bottom.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and the bottom of your chin. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a hairstyle with volume at the top and bottom.

Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

In addition to your face shape, you’ll also want to consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle. Some hairstyles work better with certain hair types and textures than others. For example, a short pixie cut may not work well with thick, curly hair, while a long, layered cut may not work well with fine, straight hair.

When choosing a hairstyle, consider the following:

  • Hair type (straight, wavy, curly, or kinky)
  • Hair texture (fine, medium, or thick)
  • Hair length
  • Personal style

Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Now that you’ve determined your face shape and considered your hair type and texture, it’s time to choose a hairstyle. Here are some hairstyle ideas for different face shapes:

Round Face

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • High ponytail
  • Messy bun

Oval Face

  • Short pixie cut
  • Long layers
  • Bob
  • Top knot

Square Face

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob
  • Messy bun
  • Loose waves

Heart Face

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Updo
  • Half-up, half-down

Diamond Face

  • Short bob
  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Top knot

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape and hair type
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures
  • Consult with a hairstylist for expert advice

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length to your face. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a hairstyle with volume at the top.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

Almost any hairstyle will suit an oval face. You can opt for a short or long cut, and you can experiment with different styles and textures.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyle for a square face is one that softens your features. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a side-swept bang.

What is the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

What is the best hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face?

The best hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face is one that adds volume to the top of your head and the bottom of your chin. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a hairstyle with volume at the top and bottom.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

We hope this post has helped you determine which hairstyle best suits your face. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures. Happy styling!
