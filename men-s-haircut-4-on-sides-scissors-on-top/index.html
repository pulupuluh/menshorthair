Men s Haircut 4 On Sides Scissors On Top How To Achieve The Look

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're in search of a trendy and versatile haircut, then the men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top might just be the perfect one for you. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's a classic style that can be easily customized to suit your personal style and preferences. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve this look, step by step.

Description

The men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top is a simple yet stylish haircut that features a short length on the sides and back of the head, while leaving a longer length on top. The 4 on the sides indicates the clipper guard length used, which is equivalent to 1/2 inch. The top is cut with scissors and can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, textured, or with a side part.

How to

Here are the steps to achieve the men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting the haircut, it's important to have a consultation with your barber or stylist. Discuss the length and style you want, and ask for their professional opinion. They can also recommend the best products to maintain the look.

Step 2: Clipper Cut

The first step is to clipper cut the sides and back of the head with a 4 guard length. This will create a uniform length and provide a clean canvas for the top.

Step 3: Scissor Cut

Next, the top is cut with scissors. The length can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually left longer than the sides. The stylist can also add texture or layers to the top to create movement and volume.

Step 4: Style

Once the haircut is done, it's time to style the hair. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the top and comb through to create your desired look. You can slick it back, create a side part, or add texture for a more casual look.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain the men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and nourished.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider adding a beard or mustache to complement the haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top:

What face shape does this haircut suit?

This haircut can suit various face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, it's best to consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Can this haircut be styled for formal occasions?

Yes, this haircut can be easily styled for formal occasions. You can slick it back or add a side part for a polished and sophisticated look.

Can this haircut be worn by older men?

Absolutely! This haircut is versatile and can suit men of all ages. It's a classic style that can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences.

Hair Care

To maintain a healthy and stylish men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

Overall, the men's haircut 4 on sides scissors on top is a great option for those who want a stylish and versatile haircut. With the right styling products and maintenance routine, you can achieve a look that suits your personal style and preferences.
