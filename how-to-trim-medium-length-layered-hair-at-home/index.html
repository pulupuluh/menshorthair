How To Trim Medium Length Layered Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're one of the many people who have medium length layered hair, you know how important it is to keep your locks looking fresh and healthy. While it's always a good idea to visit a professional stylist for a cut, there are times when you may want to trim your hair at home. Doing so can save you time and money, and it's also a great way to experiment with different looks. In this guide, we'll show you how to trim medium length layered hair at home in just a few easy steps.

Description

The first step to trimming your medium length layered hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water on hand, as this will help you to see any uneven areas that need to be trimmed.

How to Trim Medium Length Layered Hair at Home

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to see the different layers.

2. Use your comb to separate your hair into sections. Begin by separating your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head. Then, separate each of these sections into two more sections, one on top and one on bottom.

3. Starting with the bottom section on one side of your head, comb through your hair to remove any tangles. Then, use your scissors to trim the ends of your hair, being careful not to cut too much off at once.

4. Continue working your way up the section, trimming small amounts of hair at a time, until you reach the top. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.

5. Once you've finished trimming the bottom sections, move on to the top sections. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles, then use your scissors to trim the ends of your hair, working your way from the back of your head to the front.

6. Once you've finished trimming all the sections, use your comb to blend the layers together. This will help to create a seamless, natural look.

Tips

- It's always a good idea to start with small snips and work your way up. This will help you to avoid cutting off too much hair at once.

- If you're not sure where to start, it's always a good idea to consult a professional stylist. They can give you tips on how to trim your hair at home and help you to achieve the look you want.

- Be sure to use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and make your hair look frizzy and damaged.

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my medium length layered hair?

A: It's generally recommended that you trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and fresh.

Q: Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

A: It's generally easier to trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to work with.

Hair Care

Trimming your medium length layered hair at home is just one part of maintaining healthy locks. To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness. If you're going to be using heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage.

By following these simple tips and tricks, you can keep your medium length layered hair looking healthy and stylish, without ever having to leave your home!
