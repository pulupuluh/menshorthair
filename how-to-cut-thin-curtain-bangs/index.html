How To Cut Thin Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and add some trendy curtain bangs to your look? Cutting your own bangs at home can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve the perfect thin curtain bangs. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps to achieve this look, and provide tips and hair care advice to keep your bangs looking their best.

How to Cut Thin Curtain Bangs

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle, and separate the front section that you want to cut into bangs. Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. For thin curtain bangs, we recommend cutting them just above the eyebrows.

Step 3: Cut the Middle Section

Take the middle section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger, and cut straight across.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Take the hair on one side of your middle section and comb it down at a diagonal angle towards your nose. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger, and cut at a slight angle. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Check the Length

Comb your bangs straight down and check the length. Make any necessary adjustments by trimming the ends.

Step 6: Add Texture

If you want to add some texture to your bangs, point cut into the ends with your scissors. This will give your bangs a softer, more natural look.

Step 7: Style Your Bangs

Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs. Brush them to the side and use your fingers to create a natural, tousled look.

Tips for Cutting Thin Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your thin curtain bangs:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and cut slowly. You can always cut more, but you canât put hair back once itâs been cut.
  • Donât cut your bangs when theyâre wet. Hair shrinks when it dries, so you may end up cutting them too short.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Take breaks. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take breaks if you need to.

FAQ

How often should I trim my thin curtain bangs?

We recommend trimming your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair?

Cutting bangs on curly hair can be challenging, so we recommend going to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Thin Curtain Bangs

To keep your thin curtain bangs looking their best, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and prevent them from looking greasy. Avoid using too much heat when styling, as this can damage your hair.
