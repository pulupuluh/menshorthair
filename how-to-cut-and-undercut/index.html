How To Cut And Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's edgy and easy to maintain, an undercut may be just what you need. This style involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the top longer. It's a great way to add some personality to your look without committing to a full buzz cut.

Description

Before you start cutting, it's important to understand what an undercut is and how it works. Essentially, an undercut involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head very short, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a dramatic contrast between the two sections of hair, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to Cut an Undercut

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting an undercut:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into three sections: the top, the left side, and the right side. Use clips to keep each section separate.

Step 2: Choose the Length

Decide how short you want the sides and back to be. You can use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Starting at the bottom, use your clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Be sure to blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once the sides and back are cut, it's time to move on to the top. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and top, creating a seamless transition between the two sections.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, it's time to style the hair. You can use a variety of products to achieve different looks, from a sleek and polished style to a messy, textured look.

Tips for Cutting an Undercut

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an undercut last?

An undercut can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Can I cut an undercut at home?

Yes, you can cut an undercut at home, but it's important to have the right tools and take your time to avoid mistakes.

What products should I use to style my undercut?

There are a variety of products you can use to style an undercut, including pomade, wax, and gel. It's important to choose a product that works well with your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

Hair Care for an Undercut

To keep your undercut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style

With these tips and a little practice, you can achieve a stylish and edgy undercut that's sure to turn heads.
