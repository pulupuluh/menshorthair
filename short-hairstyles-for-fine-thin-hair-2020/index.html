Short Hairstyles For Fine Thin Hair 2020 A Guide To The Best Styles

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have fine, thin hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a hairstyle that works for you. From limp locks to lackluster volume, it can be a challenge to achieve a look that makes you feel confident and stylish. Fortunately, in 2020, there are many trendy and chic short hairstyles that are perfect for fine, thin hair. In this guide, we'll explore the best short hairstyles for fine thin hair 2020 and provide tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Description

Short hair is a great option for those with fine thin hair. It can help to create the illusion of thickness and volume, while also being easy to maintain and style. There are many different short hairstyles to choose from, ranging from pixie cuts to bobs, so you're sure to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle for fine thin hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A good stylist will be able to recommend a style that will flatter your features and enhance the natural texture of your hair. Short hairstyles that work well for fine thin hair include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that's perfect for fine thin hair. It can help to create the illusion of volume and thickness, while also being easy to style and maintain. For a modern twist on the classic pixie cut, try adding choppy layers or a side swept fringe.

Bob Cut

The bob is a classic style that works well for many hair types, including fine thin hair. A chin-length bob can help to add volume and body to your hair, while also framing your face and creating a sleek and polished look. For a more modern take on the bob, try adding layers or opting for an asymmetrical cut.

Short Shag

A short shag is a layered hairstyle that's perfect for fine thin hair. It can help to add volume and texture to your hair, while also creating a chic and edgy look. The key to a great short shag is to have your stylist create layers that are tailored to your hair texture and face shape.

Step by Step

When styling your short hair, it's important to use the right products and techniques to achieve the best possible results. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for fine thin hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and condition your hair using products that are designed for fine thin hair. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray.

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair to add volume and texture. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Step 3: Use a round brush to style your hair.

Use a round brush to style your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use the brush to lift and add volume to your hair, creating a full and textured look.

Step 4: Finish with a lightweight hairspray.

Finish your style with a lightweight hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down. Be sure to choose a hairspray that's designed for fine thin hair, as heavy products can make your hair look limp and lifeless.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your fine thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look limp.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add volume and texture.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have fine thin hair?

A: Yes, you can wear long hair if you have fine thin hair. However, it's important to choose the right style and care for your hair properly to avoid making it look limp and lifeless. Consider adding layers and texture to your hair, and use products that are designed for fine thin hair.

Q: How often should I wash my fine thin hair?

A: It's best to wash your fine thin hair every other day, as washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull and lifeless. Be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for fine thin hair.

Q: Can I color my fine thin hair?

A: Yes, you can color your fine thin hair. However, it's important to choose the right color and care for your hair properly to avoid damaging it. Consider getting your hair colored by a professional stylist, and use products that are designed for color-treated hair.

Hair Care

Caring for fine thin hair requires a gentle touch and the right products. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for fine thin hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can have beautiful and healthy hair, even if you have fine thin hair. Whether you opt for a chic pixie cut or a classic bob, be sure to work with a stylist who understands your hair type and can recommend the best style for you. With a little bit of care and attention, you can have the hair you've always wanted.
