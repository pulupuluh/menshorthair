Square Face Long Or Short Hair How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by William May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what hairstyle will flatter your strong jawline and angular features. Long or short hair? Layers or no layers? Bangs or no bangs? In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape.

Description: What is a Square Face Shape?

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a wide forehead, with a face length that is roughly equal to the width. Some famous examples of square face shapes include Angelina Jolie, Olivia Wilde, and Demi Moore.

How to: Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle for a Square Face Shape

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape, you need to make sure that you actually have a square face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you have a square face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair type and texture

The next step is to consider your hair type and texture. Do you have thick or thin hair? Is it straight, wavy, or curly? These factors will play a role in determining the best hairstyle for your square face shape.

Step 3: Choose a hairstyle that creates balance

The key to choosing the perfect hairstyle for a square face shape is to create balance. You want to soften the angular features of your face and create some curves. Here are some tips:

  • Long hair with soft, face-framing layers can help to soften your features.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to create a more oval-shaped face.
  • A bob or a lob (long bob) can be flattering for a square face shape, especially if it has some layers.
  • A pixie cut can also work for a square face shape, as long as it has some volume on top.

Step 4: Consider your lifestyle and personality

Finally, you should consider your lifestyle and personality when choosing a hairstyle. If you are a busy mom who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance bob might be a better choice than a high-maintenance pixie cut. If you are a creative type who likes to experiment with different looks, a funky asymmetrical bob might be perfect for you.

Tips: How to Style Your Square Face Long or Short Hair

Once you have chosen the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape, you will need to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • If you have long hair, try curling the ends to create some softness.
  • If you have a bob, try adding some texture with a sea salt spray.
  • If you have a pixie cut, try using a volumizing mousse to add some height on top.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Square Face Long or Short Hair

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a square face shape?

A: A middle part can actually emphasize the squareness of your face shape. Instead, try a side part or a deep side part.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a square face shape?

A: Bangs can be flattering for a square face shape, as long as they are side-swept and not too blunt.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a square face shape?

A: A high ponytail can actually make your face shape look even more angular. Instead, try a low ponytail or a messy bun.

Hair Care: How to Keep Your Square Face Long or Short Hair Looking Great

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape can seem daunting, but with these tips, you can find a style that flatters your features and suits your personality. Remember to consider your hair type, think about creating balance, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
