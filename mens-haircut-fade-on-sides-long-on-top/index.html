How to Get a Mens Haircut Fade on Sides Long on Top

The mens haircut fade on sides long on top is a combination of short sides and longer hair on the top. The length of the top hair can vary depending on your preference, but it's usually around 2-4 inches. The fade on the sides can be high, medium, or low, depending on your face shape and personal style. You can ask your barber or stylist for a mens haircut fade on sides long on top, and they will know exactly what to do.

It's important to bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your barber or stylist to ensure that you get the look you want. You can also ask for their recommendations on the best type of fade for your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Mens Haircut Fade on Sides Long on Top

If you want to try cutting your own hair, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens haircut fade on sides long on top:

  1. Start by trimming the sides with a clipper, using a longer guard at the top and shorter guard at the bottom.
  2. Blend the sides with the top using scissors or a clipper over comb technique.
  3. Cut the top hair to your desired length, either with scissors or clippers.
  4. Style your hair using a pomade or wax, and comb it to the side for a classic look or messy for a more modern look.

It's important to take your time and be patient when cutting your own hair. If you're unsure about anything, it's best to consult with a professional barber or stylist.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Fade on Sides Long on Top

Maintaining your mens haircut fade on sides long on top is relatively easy, but here are some tips to help you keep your hairstyle looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the length in check and maintain the shape of the fade.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles and partings to keep your mens haircut fade on sides long on top looking fresh and modern.

FAQs About Mens Haircut Fade on Sides Long on Top

1. Is the mens haircut fade on sides long on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens haircut fade on sides long on top can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

2. How long does it take to style a mens haircut fade on sides long on top?

The time it takes to style a mens haircut fade on sides long on top depends on your hair type and the products you use. On average, it takes around 5-10 minutes to style your hair in the morning.

3. How often should I wash my hair with a mens haircut fade on sides long on top?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. However, if you have an active lifestyle or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Fade on Sides Long on Top

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your mens haircut fade on sides long on top looking healthy and stylish:

  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.
  • Avoid wearing tight hats or caps, as they can cause friction and breakage on your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools on your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and promote hair growth.

Overall, the mens haircut fade on sides long on top is a stylish and modern hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual preferences and face shape. Whether you choose to go to a professional barber or try cutting your own hair, this versatile style is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
