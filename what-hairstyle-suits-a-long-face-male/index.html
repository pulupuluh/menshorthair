What Hairstyle Suits A Long Face Male

Written by Christine Feb 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that suits your face shape. But worry not, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle suits a long face male, along with some tips and tricks to help you look your best.

Description

A long face is characterized by having more length than width, with a high forehead and a narrow chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a long face male is to create balance and proportion, enhancing the natural features of the face while minimizing any elongation.

How to

The best hairstyle for a long face male is one that adds width and volume to the sides while keeping the top relatively short. Here are some hairstyles that work well:

1. Side-Parted Hair

A side-parted hairstyle is perfect for a long face male, as it adds volume to the sides and creates an illusion of width. You can keep the top short or medium length, and style it to the side.

2. Messy Textured Hair

Messy textured hair is another great option for a long face male, as it adds volume and texture to the sides while keeping the top short. You can use hair products like wax or pomade to create a messy and edgy look.

3. Fringe Haircut

A fringe haircut is a stylish and modern hairstyle that works well for a long face male. It creates a horizontal line across the forehead, adding width to the face. You can keep the sides short or medium length, and style the fringe to the side or straight down.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a side-parted hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.
  3. Apply a hair styling product like wax or pomade to your hair, focusing on the sides.
  4. Use a comb to create a side parting on one side of your head.
  5. Style the top of your hair to the side, using your fingers or a comb to create a messy and textured look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face male:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as it will only elongate the face further.
  • Keep the sides short or medium length, and add volume and texture to create width.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slick or flat, as it will only emphasize the length of the face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle suits a long face male:

1. Can I have long hair if I have a long face?

While long hair can elongate the face further, you can still have long hair if you prefer. Just make sure to add volume and texture to the sides to create balance.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a long face?

Bangs can work well for a long face male, as long as they are styled to the side or straight down to create width.

3. Can I have a bald head if I have a long face?

A bald head can work well for a long face male, as it creates a clean and streamlined look. Just make sure to keep the beard or mustache to add some width to the face.

Hair Care

It's essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use hair styling products in moderation, and make sure to wash them off before going to bed.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can find a hairstyle that suits your long face and enhances your natural features. Experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to try something new.
