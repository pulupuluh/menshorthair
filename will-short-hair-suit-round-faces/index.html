Will Short Hair Suit Round Faces A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Oliver Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a popular choice for many women, but those with round faces may wonder if a shorter cut will flatter their features. The answer is a resounding yes! With the right style and cut, short hair can accentuate your best features and bring out the beauty of your round face. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the best short haircuts for round faces, tips for styling and maintenance, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircuts for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length and height to balance out the roundness of your face. This can be achieved with layers, volume, and asymmetrical cuts. Second, you want to avoid styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.

Step by Step Guide: Choosing the Perfect Short Haircut for a Round Face

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Look for short hairstyles that create height and length, such as pixie cuts, layered bobs, and asymmetrical styles.

3. Consider adding volume to the crown of your head with a layered cut or volumizing product.

4. Avoid blunt bobs, straight-across bangs, and styles that add width to your face.

5. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Once you've chosen your perfect short haircut, it's important to know how to style and maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Invest in quality hair products, such as a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair while blow-drying.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks.

FAQ: Will Short Hair Suit Round Faces?

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for round faces:

Q: Will a pixie cut suit a round face?

A: Yes! A pixie cut can be a great option for a round face, especially if it is layered and styled with volume at the crown.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or angled bangs can help to elongate your face, while straight-across bangs should be avoided.

Q: What is the best way to style a short haircut for a round face?

A: The best way to style a short haircut for a round face is to add volume and height at the crown of your head. Use a round brush while blow-drying and add a volumizing product to create lift and shape.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require a bit more maintenance than longer styles, but with the right care, your hair will look healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a weekly deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a short haircut that flatters your round face and showcases your unique style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for you, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
