Should I Get Curtain Bangs With Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 15, 2023 · 7 min read
Should I Get Curtain Bangs with Straight Hair? | Hair Care Tips and FAQ

If you're thinking of getting a new hairstyle, you might be wondering if you should get curtain bangs with straight hair. Curtain bangs have been in trend for a while now, and they can be a great way to change up your look without making a drastic change to your hair. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about curtain bangs with straight hair, including how to style them and how to take care of them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs with Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted down the middle and frame the face on both sides. They're called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the drapes or curtains that hang on either side of a window. This type of bangs works well with straight hair because it gives the hair some movement and texture. Curtain bangs can also help to soften the face and make it look more youthful.

How to Get Curtain Bangs with Straight Hair

If you're thinking of getting curtain bangs with straight hair, it's important to find a stylist who has experience cutting this type of bangs. The stylist should be able to assess your hair type and face shape to determine if curtain bangs will work for you. They should also be able to give you advice on how to style and maintain your new look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to get curtain bangs with straight hair:

  1. Choose a stylist who has experience cutting curtain bangs.
  2. Show them a picture of the type of bangs you want.
  3. Discuss your hair type and face shape with the stylist.
  4. Sit back and let the stylist work their magic.
  5. Style your new bangs as desired.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for styling curtain bangs:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a straightener to smooth out any kinks or flyaways.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and smoothness.
  • Try different parting styles to change up the look of your bangs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs with straight hair:

Are curtain bangs suitable for all face shapes?

Curtain bangs can work for most face shapes, but it's important to consult with a stylist to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. You may need to pin your bangs back or style them to the side to keep them out of your face.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs require regular trims to maintain their shape, but they don't require any additional maintenance beyond that. You can style them as desired with a blow dryer, straightener, or other styling tools.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and free of oil.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your bangs with heat tools.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

Overall, curtain bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some texture and movement to your straight hair. If you're thinking of getting curtain bangs, be sure to find a stylist who has experience cutting this type of bangs and follow our tips for styling and hair care.
