Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Eveline Apr 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but not sure which one will suit your heart-shaped face? Look no further, as we have gathered some of the best hairstyles that will complement your face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to style your hair to accentuate your features and bring out the best in your heart-shaped face.

Description

Heart-shaped faces typically have a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the wider forehead and create volume around the jawline. It's important to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make the forehead look even wider.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a heart-shaped face, it's important to consider the length of your hair. Short haircuts that end just above the chin or shoulder-length haircuts with layers are great options. These styles add volume around the jawline and create a balanced look.

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers or soft waves. This will create movement around the face and draw attention away from the forehead. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that add volume to the top of the head.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Choose a hairstyle that balances out the wider forehead and adds volume around the jawline.

3. Consider the length of your hair and choose a style that complements your face shape.

4. If you have short hair, consider a chin-length bob or a layered pixie cut.

5. If you have medium-length hair, consider shoulder-length haircuts with layers or soft waves.

6. If you have long hair, consider adding layers or soft waves to create movement around the face.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head.

- Consider adding bangs to balance out a wider forehead.

- Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off long hair?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off long hair. However, it's important to add layers or soft waves to create movement around the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

Q: What are the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces with curly hair?

A: Chin-length bobs or shoulder-length cuts with layers are great options for heart-shaped faces with curly hair. These styles add volume around the jawline and balance out a wider forehead.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air-dry as much as possible and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

Regular trims are also important to maintain the shape of your hairstyle and prevent split ends. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim, or as needed.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for a heart-shaped face can be a challenge, but with these tips and ideas, you can find a style that complements your features and brings out the best in your face shape. Remember to consider the length of your hair, add volume around the jawline, and avoid adding too much volume to the top of the head. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
