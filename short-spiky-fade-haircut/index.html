Short Spiky Fade Haircut A Trendy And Edgy Look

Written by Daniel Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's trendy and edgy, then the short spiky fade haircut might be the one for you. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. It's versatile, easy to maintain and style, and it works well on a variety of hair types and face shapes. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get a short spiky fade haircut, as well as some tips on how to care for it.

Description: What is a Short Spiky Fade Haircut?

A short spiky fade haircut is a hairstyle that features short, spiky hair on top and a fade on the sides and back. The fade is a gradual taper that starts at the temples and fades down to the neckline. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and works well for those with straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How To Get a Short Spiky Fade Haircut

Getting a short spiky fade haircut is easy and can be done at a barbershop or salon. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Find a Good Hairstylist or Barber

The first step in getting a short spiky fade haircut is to find a good hairstylist or barber. Look for someone who has experience with this type of haircut and can give you the look you want.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Before getting a short spiky fade haircut, you need to decide on the style you want. Do you want a messy, textured look or a more defined, sleek look? Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist or barber.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on Top

Once you've chosen your style, your stylist or barber will start by cutting the hair on top of your head. They'll use scissors or clippers to trim the hair to the desired length and create the spiky texture.

Step 4: Fade the Sides and Back

Next, your stylist or barber will start fading the sides and back of your head. They'll use clippers to create a gradual taper from the temples to the neckline. The fade can be high, mid, or low, depending on your preference.

Step 5: Blend and Style

Finally, your stylist or barber will blend the hair on top with the faded sides and back. They'll use scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the two sections. Afterward, they'll style your hair with a pomade, wax, or gel to create the spiky look.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Spiky Fade Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short spiky fade haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you

FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a short spiky fade haircut last?

A: A short spiky fade haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Q: Is a short spiky fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a short spiky fade haircut works well on a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: Can I style my short spiky fade haircut differently?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks with your short spiky fade haircut.

Hair Care for a Short Spiky Fade Haircut

Caring for a short spiky fade haircut is relatively easy. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish.

A short spiky fade haircut is a trendy and edgy look that's easy to maintain and style. With the right hairstylist or barber, you can get the perfect short spiky fade haircut for your face shape and hair type. Follow our tips for maintaining your haircut, and you'll have a stylish and fashionable look that will turn heads wherever you go.
