How To Cut A Man s Hair Short With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Cutting a man's hair short with clippers is a simple and straightforward process that can be done at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut that is both stylish and easy to maintain. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps of cutting a man's hair short with clippers, including tips and tricks for achieving the perfect cut.

Description

Cutting a man's hair short with clippers is a great way to save time and money. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut at home. Clippers are a popular choice for cutting men's hair because they are easy to use and can be adjusted to different lengths. Whether you want a buzz cut or a short, layered style, clippers can help you achieve the look you want.

How to Cut a Man's Hair Short with Clippers

Before you start cutting, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You will need:

  • A set of hair clippers
  • Clipper guards in various lengths
  • Scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle with water
  • A mirror
  • A cape or old towel to catch hair clippings

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, wash and dry the man's hair. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of the hair, and it will also make the hair easier to cut. Use a towel to dry the hair thoroughly, and make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose a clipper guard that is the right length for the style you want. The longer the guard, the longer the hair will be. If you are not sure which guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start at the Bottom

Start cutting at the bottom of the hairline, using the clipper guard to guide the clippers. Move the clippers in an upward motion, following the natural curve of the head. Repeat this process around the sides and back of the head, making sure to blend the hair evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once you have cut the sides and back, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to cut the hair on top of the head, blending it with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use the comb to lift the hair up and cut it at an angle, creating a layered look. Take your time and make sure to blend the hair evenly.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers to clean up the edges around the hairline and ears. Use a shorter guard for a more defined look, or a longer guard for a softer look. Make sure to check both sides of the head in the mirror to ensure they are even.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting, check the hair for evenness. Use the comb to lift the hair up and check for any uneven areas. Use the scissors to trim any areas that need it.

Tips for Cutting a Man's Hair Short with Clippers

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect cut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length
  • Take your time and make sure to blend the hair evenly
  • Check both sides of the head in the mirror to ensure they are even
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on top of the head
  • Clean up the edges around the hairline and ears for a polished look

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. However, it can be difficult to achieve an even cut without help. It's best to have someone else cut your hair for you, or to practice cutting on a mannequin head before attempting to cut your own hair.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want. For a short, buzzed style, you may need to cut your hair every two weeks. For a longer, layered style, you may only need to cut your hair every four to six weeks.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish the hair

Cutting a man's hair short with clippers is a simple and easy process that can be done at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut that is both stylish and easy to maintain. Follow these steps, tips and tricks for the perfect cut, and take care of your hair to keep it looking great!
