How To Trim Guys Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Trimming your hair at home can be a daunting task, especially for guys who are used to going to the barbershop. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim guys hair with scissors.

How to Trim Guys Hair with Scissors

Before we get started, here are some tools you will need:

  • Hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Hair clippers (optional)

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean and free of any oils or product buildup.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is clean, comb it to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want your hair to be. You can use hair clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to a specific length, or you can use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to a desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair on top of your head by using the comb to lift a section of hair and then trimming the ends with the scissors. Work your way around your head, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

If you are using clippers, use the different guard sizes to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head. If you are using scissors, use the comb to blend the hair by cutting at an angle.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting, use a mirror to check your work from different angles. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Trimming Guys Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when trimming your hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take your time and work in small sections
  • Comb your hair often to ensure an even cut
  • Use the tip of the scissors to create texture and layers
  • Trim less hair than you think you need to avoid cutting too much

FAQ

Can I Trim My Hair with Regular Scissors?

We recommend using hair cutting scissors, as regular scissors may not be sharp enough and can damage the hair.

Do I Need to Wet My Hair Before Cutting it?

It is best to cut your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly. However, if you prefer to cut your hair when it is wet, make sure to comb it thoroughly and avoid cutting too much.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

With these tips and techniques, you can trim your hair at home with confidence. Remember to take your time and work in small sections, and don’t be afraid to make adjustments as you go.
