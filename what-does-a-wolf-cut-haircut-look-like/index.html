What Does A Wolf Cut Haircut Look Like

Written by Christine Jun 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

The wolf cut haircut is one of the trendiest hairstyles right now. This haircut is a mix between a shaggy mullet and a layered bob. It has a lot of texture and volume, and it's perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look. If you're considering getting a wolf cut, here's everything you need to know about what it looks like, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A wolf cut is a messy, asymmetrical haircut that features short layers at the top and longer layers at the bottom. The top layers are usually cut in a shaggy style, while the bottom layers are more structured. This creates a lot of volume and texture, which makes the haircut look wild and untamed. The length of the haircut can vary, but it's usually shoulder-length or shorter.

How to Get a Wolf Cut

To get a wolf cut, you'll need to find a hairstylist who specializes in this type of haircut. They'll start by cutting your hair into a bob shape, with the length falling just above your shoulders. Then, they'll add layers to the top of your hair, cutting it in a shaggy style. The bottom layers will be left longer and more structured. Finally, they'll add texture and volume to the haircut by using thinning shears or a razor.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Find a hairstylist who specializes in wolf cuts.

2. Discuss the length and style you want for your haircut.

3. The hairstylist will start by cutting your hair into a bob shape.

4. They'll add layers to the top of your hair in a shaggy style.

5. The bottom layers will be left longer and more structured.

6. The hairstylist will add texture and volume to the haircut by using thinning shears or a razor.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wolf Cut

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking full and thick.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume and texture.

3. Use a texturizing spray to enhance the messy, wild look of your haircut.

4. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your wolf cut.

FAQ About the Wolf Cut

Q: Is the wolf cut suitable for all hair types?

A: The wolf cut works best for those with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the textured, messy look that the wolf cut is known for.

Q: How long does it take to get a wolf cut?

A: The length of time it takes to get a wolf cut depends on the length and thickness of your hair. However, it usually takes around 1-2 hours to complete the haircut.

Q: Is the wolf cut easy to style?

A: Yes, the wolf cut is relatively easy to style. You can use a texturizing spray to enhance the messy, wild look of your haircut, or blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume and texture.

Hair Care Tips for the Wolf Cut

1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Conclusion

The wolf cut is a bold and edgy haircut that's perfect for those who want a wild, untamed look. It's a mix between a shaggy mullet and a layered bob, with a lot of texture and volume. If you're considering getting a wolf cut, make sure to find a hairstylist who specializes in this type of haircut. And don't forget to follow our hair care tips to keep your wolf cut looking fresh and stylish!
