How To Spike My Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to add some edge to your hairstyle? Spiking your hair is a great way to achieve that look. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to create that perfect spiked hairdo.

Description

Spiking your hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It’s a versatile look that can be achieved with different hair lengths, textures and colors. The style involves lifting the hair and pointing it upwards, creating a spiky effect. It’s a great way to add some personality and edge to your look.

How to Spike Your Hair

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a hair dryer, a hairbrush, and hair gel or wax. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, and product buildup that may be on your hair. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Gel or Wax

Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your hair. The amount you use will depend on your hair length and thickness. Rub the product between your palms and apply evenly to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a hairdryer to dry your hair while using a hairbrush to lift and point the hair upwards. This will create volume and help to set the spikes in place. Continue until your hair is completely dry.

Step 4: Add More Product

If you want a more dramatic look, add more hair gel or wax to your hair. This will help to hold the spikes in place and create a more defined look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve added enough product, use your fingers to style your hair. Lift and point the hair upwards to create the spikes. You can also use a hairbrush to create a more polished look.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiked hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. This will help the product to hold better.
  • Use a small amount of product at first and add more as needed.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and textures to find the right style for you.
  • For a more natural look, use a hair wax instead of gel.
  • Use a hair spray to set your style in place and keep it looking great all day.

FAQ

How long will my spiked hairstyle last?

The longevity of your spiked hairstyle will depend on the product you use and your hair type. Generally, hair gel will hold the spikes in place for longer than wax. However, both products can be reapplied throughout the day to maintain the look.

Can I spike my hair if it’s thin or fine?

Yes, you can still spike your hair even if it’s thin or fine. Use a hair wax instead of gel and apply it sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.

How often should I wash my hair when I have a spiked hairstyle?

You can wash your hair as often as you normally would. Just make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair when you have a spiked hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much product.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and steps, you’ll be able to create the perfect spiked hairstyle in no time. Whether you’re going for a casual or edgy look, spiking your hair is a great way to add some personality to your style.
