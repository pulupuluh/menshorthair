Does Beard Grow Thicker If You Shave

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Beards have been a symbol of masculinity for ages. They have been worn by men all over the world for various reasons - to show authority, to express style, to keep warm, or simply to look good. However, not all men are blessed with a thick, luscious beard. Some may have patchy, uneven growth, while others may have none at all. This leads to the question - does beard grow thicker if you shave?

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The myth that shaving makes your beard grow thicker is a common one. It's been around for decades, and many men believe it to be true. The idea is that shaving stimulates hair growth, making your beard thicker and fuller. However, this is not true.

Shaving does not make your beard grow thicker. In fact, it has no effect on the thickness or density of your beard at all. Your beard growth is determined by genetics and hormones, not by how often you shave.

How to

If you want a thicker, fuller beard, there are a few things you can do to help promote healthy hair growth:

1. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein and vitamins, especially vitamin B7 (biotin), vitamin C, and vitamin E.

2. Exercise regularly

Regular exercise can help increase blood flow to your hair follicles, which can promote healthy hair growth.

3. Take care of your skin

Healthy skin is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you're using a good moisturizer and exfoliating regularly to promote healthy skin.

Step by step

If you're looking to grow a thicker beard, here are some steps you can take:

1. Let it grow

Give your beard time to grow. It may take a few weeks or even months to see significant growth. Be patient and resist the urge to trim or shave it prematurely.

2. Keep it clean

Wash your beard regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a beard shampoo and conditioner to keep it soft and manageable.

3. Use beard oil

Beard oil can help keep your beard soft and healthy. It can also help promote healthy hair growth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for growing a thicker beard:

1. Don't overwash

Washing your beard too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your beard every 2-3 days.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools can damage your beard and lead to breakage. Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners on your beard.

3. Trim regularly

While shaving does not make your beard grow thicker, trimming can actually help promote healthy hair growth. Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to thicker, healthier hair.

FAQ

Q: Does shaving make your beard grow thicker?

A: No, shaving does not make your beard grow thicker. Your beard growth is determined by genetics and hormones, not by how often you shave.

Q: Can I use minoxidil to grow a thicker beard?

A: Yes, minoxidil can help promote beard growth. However, it should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy beard growth. Make sure you're washing and conditioning your beard regularly, using beard oil to keep it soft and healthy, and trimming it regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Remember, while shaving does not make your beard grow thicker, a healthy lifestyle and proper hair care can help promote healthy hair growth.
