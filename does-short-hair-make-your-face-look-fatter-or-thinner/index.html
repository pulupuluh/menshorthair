Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter Or Thinner

Written by Luffy Jun 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair is a popular trend that many people opt for, but some individuals are concerned that it might make their face look fatter or thinner. This article will answer that question and provide tips on how to style short hair to complement your facial features.

Description

Short hair is a versatile hairstyle that can work for all face shapes. But, when it comes to choosing a short haircut, individuals with round faces often wonder if it will make their face look fatter or thinner. The answer is that it depends on the hairstyle you choose.

How to

The key to choosing the right short haircut is to select a style that complements your facial features. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides. Instead, opt for a haircut that adds volume on the top, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with side-swept bangs. This will lengthen your face and make it look thinner.

If you have a square face, you should choose a short haircut that softens your features, such as a layered bob or a shaggy cut. This will create a more round and feminine appearance. Individuals with oval faces can choose any short haircut as their face shape is considered the most versatile.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Research short haircuts that complement your facial features

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for you

Step 4: Style your hair according to your face shape

Step 5: Experiment with different short haircuts until you find the one that works best for you

Tips

- Use hair products that add volume to the top of your head

- Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides

- Consider adding side-swept bangs to lengthen your face

- Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair according to your face shape

- Experiment with different short haircuts until you find the one that works best for you

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look fatter?

A: It depends on the hairstyle you choose. If you choose a short haircut that adds volume on the sides, it can make your face look fatter. On the other hand, if you choose a haircut that adds volume on the top, it can make your face look thinner.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look thinner?

A: It depends on the hairstyle you choose. If you choose a short haircut that adds volume on the top, it can make your face look thinner. On the other hand, if you choose a haircut that adds volume on the sides, it can make your face look fatter.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for taking care of short hair:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner

- Use a leave-in conditioner to prevent dryness

- Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools

- Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair

Conclusion

Short hair can make your face look either fatter or thinner depending on the hairstyle you choose. The key is to select a cut that complements your facial features. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can find the perfect short haircut that flatters your face shape.
