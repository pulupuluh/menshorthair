How To Cut Toddler Hair With Clippers Only

Written by Oliver Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money at the hair salon for your toddler? Do you want to learn how to cut your toddler's hair at home? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut toddler hair with clippers only.

Description

Cutting your toddler's hair at home can save you a lot of money and time. However, it can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. With the right tools and techniques, cutting your toddler's hair can be a fun and easy experience.

How to Cut Toddler Hair with Clippers Only

Here are the steps to follow when cutting your toddler's hair with clippers:

Step 1: Prepare the Area

Choose a well-lit and comfortable area to cut your toddler's hair. Make sure you have all the necessary tools, including a cape or towel to cover your toddler's clothes, a comb, and clippers.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

When it comes to choosing clippers for your toddler's hair, it is essential to choose a clipper that is specifically designed for children's hair. Make sure the clipper has adjustable blades to allow you to cut different lengths of hair.

Step 3: Choose the Right Length of Hair

Before you start cutting your toddler's hair, decide on the length you want to cut. You can start with a longer length and gradually cut the hair shorter until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting the Hair

Start by combing your toddler's hair to remove any tangles. Then, turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair. Start at the back of the head, and work your way to the sides and front. Be gentle and take your time, especially around the ears and neck.

Step 5: Clean up

After you have finished cutting your toddler's hair, use a brush and comb to clean up any loose hairs. Then, remove the cape or towel and clean up the area.

Tips

Here are some tips to make cutting your toddler's hair easier:

  • Choose a time when your toddler is well-rested and fed to avoid any tantrums.
  • Use distractions like toys or videos to keep your toddler entertained during the haircut.
  • Take breaks if your toddler becomes fussy or restless.
  • Always use safety guards on your clippers to avoid any accidents.

FAQs

Is it safe to cut my toddler's hair with clippers?

Yes, it is safe to cut your toddler's hair with clippers as long as you follow the right safety procedures and use safety guards on your clippers.

How often should I cut my toddler's hair?

It depends on how fast your toddler's hair grows. On average, toddlers' hair grows about half an inch per month. You can cut their hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a neat look.

Hair Care

After cutting your toddler's hair, it is essential to take care of their hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your toddler's hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for children's hair.
  • Use a soft and absorbent towel to dry your toddler's hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your toddler's hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your toddler's hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your toddler's hair to prevent damage.

Cutting your toddler's hair with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can cut your toddler's hair at home and save money. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to achieve a neat and stylish haircut for your toddler.
