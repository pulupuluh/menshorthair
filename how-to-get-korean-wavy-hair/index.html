In this article, we will guide you on how to get Korean wavy hair step by step, including some tips and hair care for maintaining that gorgeous hairstyle. Let's get started!

How to Get Korean Wavy Hair

Korean wavy hair is a popular trend among women and men in Korea, and it has become a global trend. The good news is that you can achieve that look without going to a salon. Here are some ways on how to get Korean wavy hair.

1. Use a curling iron

One of the easiest ways to get Korean wavy hair is by using a curling iron. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your hair length and desired wave size. Start curling your hair from the middle to the ends and hold it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until you reach your desired wave look.

2. Try a perm

If you want to have long-lasting Korean wavy hair, you can try a perm. A perm is a chemical process that can make your hair curly or wavy. However, perm can damage your hair, so make sure to consult with a professional hairstylist before doing this process.

3. Use a hair straightener

Another way to get Korean wavy hair is by using a hair straightener. This method is also called the "twist and pull" technique. Start by twisting a section of your hair and pulling it down with a hair straightener. Repeat this process until you achieve your desired wave look.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the ways on how to get Korean wavy hair, here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve that look.

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply hair protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a hair protectant to avoid hair damage.

Step 3: Choose your styling method

Decide on which method you want to use in getting your Korean wavy hair. You can choose between a curling iron, perm, or hair straightener.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into smaller sections before styling to achieve a more defined wave look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Start styling your hair depending on your chosen method. For curling iron and hair straightener, start from the middle to the ends and hold it for a few seconds before releasing. For perm, follow the instructions of the hairstylist.

Step 6: Apply hair serum

After styling your hair, apply a hair serum to add shine and to keep your hair frizz-free.

Tips for Maintaining Korean Wavy Hair

Here are some tips on how to maintain your Korean wavy hair.

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb when combing your hair to avoid breakage and damage.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Excessive use of heat styling tools can damage your hair. Avoid using it every day and give your hair a break.

3. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask at least once a week to moisturize your hair and avoid dryness.

FAQ

1. Can I get Korean wavy hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve Korean wavy hair even if you have short hair. You can use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size or try the hair straightener method.

2. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last up to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care for Korean Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your Korean wavy hair.

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils.

2. Avoid hot showers

Hot showers can dry out your hair and scalp, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day.

There you have it, a complete guide on how to get Korean wavy hair, including some tips and hair care for maintaining that gorgeous hairstyle. Try these methods and see which one works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to achieve healthy and beautiful locks.
