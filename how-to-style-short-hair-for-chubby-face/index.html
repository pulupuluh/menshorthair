How To Style Short Hair For Chubby Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair can be a great option for those with a chubby face, but styling it can be a challenge. In this guide, we'll show you how to style short hair for a chubby face to make you look and feel your best. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right haircut to tips for maintaining your style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Chubby Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a chubby face, the goal is to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Opt for a haircut that frames your face and draws attention away from your cheeks.
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider a bob or lob, as these haircuts can elongate your face.
  • Try a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Remember, the most important thing is to choose a haircut that you feel confident and comfortable in.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Chubby Face

Now that you've chosen the right haircut, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for a chubby face:

  1. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or hair gel.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  3. Use a straightening or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember to play around with different styles until you find one that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Once you've styled your short hair, it's important to maintain your look. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking its best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair for a chubby face:

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still wear a pixie cut if you have a chubby face. Just make sure to choose a pixie cut that adds volume to the top of your head and avoids adding volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a chubby face?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a chubby face. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape, such as side-swept bangs.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It's best to wash your short hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your short hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your short hair healthy and looking great.

Conclusion

Styling short hair for a chubby face can be a challenge, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to choose a haircut that frames your face and draws attention away from your cheeks, and to use styling products and tools to create volume and texture.
