How To Stop Hair Falling Out Due To Thyroid

Written by William Feb 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thyroid problems, you may experience hair loss or thinning. This can be a frustrating and discouraging experience, but there are things you can do to stop hair falling out due to thyroid. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair falling out due to thyroid, including causes, symptoms, and treatments.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Loss Due to Thyroid
  • Symptoms of Hair Loss Due to Thyroid
  • Treatments for Hair Loss Due to Thyroid
  • Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

Causes of Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

The thyroid gland plays an important role in regulating metabolism, which affects hair growth. When the thyroid gland produces too much or too little hormone, it can cause hair loss or thinning. The most common cause of hair loss due to thyroid is hypothyroidism, or an underactive thyroid. This condition can cause hair to become brittle and break easily.

Other factors that can contribute to hair loss due to thyroid include stress, poor nutrition, and hormonal imbalances. Women may also experience hair loss due to changes in hormone levels during menopause or pregnancy.

Symptoms of Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

The most common symptom of hair loss due to thyroid is thinning hair or bald patches. Hair may also become dry, brittle, or coarse. Other symptoms of thyroid problems may include fatigue, weight gain or loss, and changes in mood or mental function.

Treatments for Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

If you are experiencing hair loss due to thyroid, there are several treatments that can help. The most effective treatment will depend on the underlying cause of your thyroid problems.

One common treatment for hypothyroidism is hormone replacement therapy. This involves taking synthetic thyroid hormone to replace the hormones your body is not producing. This can help to regulate metabolism and promote hair growth.

If stress or poor nutrition is contributing to your hair loss, you may need to make lifestyle changes to address these factors. This may include getting more exercise, eating a balanced diet, and getting enough sleep.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Loss Due to Thyroid

In addition to medical treatments, there are several things you can do to prevent hair loss due to thyroid:

  • Be gentle with your hair. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, and use a soft, wide-toothed comb to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals. Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals that can damage hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet. Make sure you are getting enough protein, iron, and other nutrients that are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Manage stress. Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga to reduce stress and promote hair growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can hair loss due to thyroid be reversed?

Yes, hair loss due to thyroid can be reversed with the right treatment. This may include hormone replacement therapy, lifestyle changes, and hair care tips.

How long does it take for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment?

It can take several months for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment. The rate of hair growth will depend on the severity of the hair loss and the effectiveness of the treatment.

What is the best shampoo for hair loss due to thyroid?

The best shampoo for hair loss due to thyroid is one that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients such as biotin, keratin, and vitamin E to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and seeking the right treatment, you can stop hair falling out due to thyroid and promote healthy hair growth.
