How To Give Yourself A High Top Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the barbershop for your high top fade? Do you want to save money and try doing it yourself? If you answered yes to these questions, then you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll show you how to give yourself a high top fade in relaxed English. We’ll cover everything from the tools you need to the steps you need to take, so let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You’ll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Blending Your Hair
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for a Perfect High Top Fade
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start giving yourself a high top fade, you’ll need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Brush
  • Clipper guards
  • Hand mirror
  • Towel

Make sure your clippers are sharp and your guards are clean. You don’t want to accidentally cut yourself or have an uneven cut!

Prepping Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it and comb it out. It’s important to have your hair completely detangled before you start cutting.

Cutting Your Hair

Start by using a #2 guard on your clippers. This will be the shortest part of your high top fade. Use the clippers to cut around the sides and back of your head. Make sure to follow the natural curve of your head.

Once you’ve cut the sides and back, switch to a #3 guard and cut the top of your hair. The top should be longer than the sides and back. You can use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to blend the top and sides together.

Blending Your Hair

Blending is the most important part of giving yourself a high top fade. You want to make sure there are no harsh lines between the different lengths of your hair. To blend, use a #1.5 guard on your clippers and cut the hair around your temples and sideburns. Gradually work your way up to the longer parts of your hair.

Use a comb to lift the hair as you cut. This will help you blend the different lengths together. You can also use scissors to trim any areas that need extra blending. Take your time and make sure everything looks even.

Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting and blending, it’s time to style your high top fade. Use a brush to smooth down the sides and back. Use your fingers to style the top. You can add a little bit of hair gel or pomade to help hold the style in place.

Tips for a Perfect High Top Fade

  • Take your time. It’s better to go slow and get it right than to rush and make mistakes.
  • Use a hand mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Start with a longer length guard and work your way down. You can always go shorter, but you can’t go longer.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask someone for help if you need it.

FAQs

How long does a high top fade last?

A high top fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows. You’ll need to trim the sides and back regularly to maintain the look.

Can I use a razor to give myself a high top fade?

We don’t recommend using a razor to cut your own hair. Razors are very sharp and can easily cause cuts and nicks. Stick to clippers and scissors for the best results.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve given yourself a high top fade, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and try to air dry it whenever possible. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to give yourself a high top fade in no time. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
