How Do I Get Less Volume In My Hair Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Petter Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you've been struggling with voluminous hair, you might be wondering how to tame it down. While some people love the fullness and body that comes with voluminous hair, others prefer a sleeker, more manageable look. Whether you have naturally voluminous hair or simply want to reduce the volume for a specific hairstyle, there are several ways to achieve a less voluminous look.

Table of Contents

  • How To Get Less Volume in Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Reducing Hair Volume
  • Tips for Maintaining Less Volume in Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Reducing Hair Volume
  • Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

How To Get Less Volume in Your Hair

The first step to reducing hair volume is to choose the right haircut. Layers can add volume to hair, so opt for a blunt cut or a cut with longer layers. You can also consider a shorter haircut, as it can make hair appear less voluminous. Another way to reduce volume is to avoid using certain hair products, such as mousses and volumizing sprays, which can add volume to hair.

Another way to reduce hair volume is to use a conditioner specifically formulated to add moisture and smoothness to hair. Look for a conditioner that contains ingredients like keratin, which can help to smooth hair and reduce frizz. You can also use a hair serum or oil to help smooth hair and reduce volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Reducing Hair Volume

Here's a step-by-step guide to reducing hair volume:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently and avoid rubbing it vigorously.
  3. Apply a smoothing conditioner to your hair, paying special attention to the ends.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, pulling the hair taut as you go.
  6. Use a flat iron to smooth out any remaining frizz or volume.
  7. Finish with a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce volume.

Tips for Maintaining Less Volume in Your Hair

To maintain less volume in your hair, try these tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage hair and make it more prone to frizz and volume.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with natural bristles to detangle hair and smooth it out.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it more prone to frizz and volume.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions About Reducing Hair Volume

Q: Can I use hair gel to reduce hair volume?

A: No, hair gel is designed to hold hair in place and can actually make hair appear more voluminous.

Q: Will using a straightening iron reduce hair volume?

A: Yes, using a straightening iron can help to smooth out hair and reduce volume.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to reduce hair volume?

A: Yes, using a hair dryer can help to smooth out hair and reduce volume. However, be careful not to use too much heat, as this can damage hair.

Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

Voluminous hair can be beautiful, but it also requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for voluminous hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for voluminous hair to help maintain body and fullness.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can break it and make it more prone to frizz and volume.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help prevent damage.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb excess oil and add volume to hair.

Reducing hair volume can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleeker, more manageable look. Whether you have naturally voluminous hair or simply want to reduce volume for a specific hairstyle, these tips and tricks can help you achieve your desired look.
