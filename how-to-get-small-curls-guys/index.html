How To Get Small Curls Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 06, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a guy looking to add some texture to your hair, you might be wondering how to get small curls. Luckily, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this stylish look at home. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about getting small curls for guys, including how to do it, tips for achieving the best results, and how to care for your curls once you’ve got them.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Small Curls Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Best Results
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Your Curls

How to Get Small Curls Guys

Before we get into the specifics of how to get small curls, let’s talk about what you’ll need. Here are the key tools and products you’ll need to achieve this look:

  • A curling iron with a small barrel (around 0.5 to 1 inch in diameter)
  • A heat protectant spray
  • A styling product, such as mousse or gel
  • A blow dryer

With these tools in hand, you’re ready to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Protect Your Hair

Before you start curling, it’s important to protect your hair from heat damage. Spray a heat protectant spray throughout your hair, paying special attention to the ends.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections, securing each one with a hair clip. This will make it easier to curl each section evenly.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Once all of your hair is curled, apply a styling product to help hold the curls in place. Mousse or gel are good options for creating small curls that will last all day.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair and set the curls. Be sure to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Achieving the Best Results

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when trying to get small curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight, defined curls.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that each curl is even and well-defined.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a styling product to help hold your curls in place.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I get small curls without using a curling iron?

A: While a curling iron is the easiest way to get small curls, you can also achieve this look by braiding your hair while it’s damp and then letting it air dry.

Q: How long will my small curls last?

A: With the right styling product and technique, your small curls should last all day.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It’s best to avoid curling your hair every day, as this can cause damage. Instead, try curling your hair every other day or every few days.

Hair Care for Your Curls

Once you’ve achieved your small curls, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz and tangles.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated throughout the day.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to achieve small curls that look great and last all day. Happy styling!
