How To Style Your Medium Length Hair For Guys

Written by Eveline Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length hair for guys is a great way to add some style and personality to your look. With the right styling techniques, you can create a variety of different looks that are perfect for both casual and formal occasions. In this article, we will be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style your medium length hair for guys.

Description

Medium length hair for guys is hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a versatile length that allows you to experiment with different styles and looks. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there's a medium length hairstyle that's perfect for you.

How to Style Your Medium Length Hair for Guys

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in styling your medium length hair is to choose the right haircut. A good haircut will provide the foundation for your hairstyle and make it easier to style. Some popular haircuts for medium length hair include layered cuts, textured cuts, and shaggy cuts. Talk to your hairstylist about which haircut would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to wash it with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to apply some styling products. The type of product you use will depend on the style you want to achieve. For a sleek and polished look, use a styling gel or pomade. For a more textured and tousled look, use a styling cream or wax.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to style your hair. Use a brush or your fingers to create the desired shape and texture. If you want a more natural look, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your hair's natural waves or curls.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your style with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and add some extra shine and volume.

Tips for Styling Your Medium Length Hair for Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to use different styling products to achieve different looks.
  • Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for best results.
  • Consider investing in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools to make styling your hair easier.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length hair for guys:

Can I style my medium length hair if it's curly?

Absolutely! In fact, medium length hair is perfect for guys with curly hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the best way to style your curls.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. If your hair gets particularly oily, you can wash it more frequently.

What should I do if my hair won't stay in place?

If your hair won't stay in place, try using a stronger hold styling product or consider getting a haircut that's better suited to your hair type.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

To keep your medium length hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your medium length hair for guys like a pro. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a more textured and tousled style, there's a medium length hairstyle that's perfect for you.
