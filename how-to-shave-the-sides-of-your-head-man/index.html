How To Shave The Sides Of Your Head Man A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Shaving the sides of your head is a bold and stylish move that can add a touch of edge to your look. Whether you're looking to shave the sides completely or just trim them down, it's important to do it right to avoid any mishaps. In this article, we'll be providing you with a step-by-step guide on how to shave the sides of your head man, along with some tips and hair care advice.

  • The Tools You'll Need

How to Prepare for Shaving

Before you start shaving the sides of your head, it's important to prepare your scalp and hair for the process. Start by taking a shower and washing your hair to remove any dirt or excess oils. This will make it easier for the razor to glide over your scalp without any resistance.

If you have long hair on top of your head, consider tying it up or clipping it back to keep it out of the way while you shave the sides. This will also help you to see what you're doing more clearly.

The Tools You'll Need

To shave the sides of your head, you'll need a few essential tools. These include:

  • Clippers or trimmers
  • A razor with a fresh blade
  • Shaving cream or gel
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cloth
  • After-shave lotion or balm

How to Shave the Sides of Your Head

Now that you're prepared and have all the necessary tools, it's time to start shaving the sides of your head. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

If you have long hair on the sides of your head, start by trimming it down with clippers or trimmers. Use the longest guard to avoid cutting your hair too short.

Step 2: Apply Shaving Cream

Apply a generous amount of shaving cream or gel to the sides of your head. Make sure to spread it evenly to create a smooth surface for your razor to glide over.

Step 3: Shave the Sides of Your Head

Using a fresh razor with a sharp blade, start shaving the sides of your head. Use short, gentle strokes and be sure to rinse the razor frequently to remove any excess hair or shaving cream.

If you're shaving the sides completely, start at the top and work your way down. If you're just trimming the sides, start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 4: Rinse and Repeat

Once you've finished shaving one side of your head, rinse it with warm water and check for any missed spots. If you notice any, apply more shaving cream and go over them again with your razor. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.

Tips for a Clean Shave

Shaving the sides of your head can be tricky, but these tips will help you achieve a clean and smooth shave:

  • Use a fresh razor with a sharp blade to avoid irritation and cuts
  • Shave in the direction of hair growth to avoid ingrown hairs
  • Rinse your razor frequently to remove excess hair and shaving cream
  • Take your time and be patient

Aftercare for Your Shaved Head

After you've finished shaving the sides of your head, it's important to take care of your scalp to avoid any irritation or discomfort. Rinse your head with cool water and apply a soothing after-shave lotion or balm to help calm any redness or inflammation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to shave the sides of your head at home?

Yes, it's safe to shave the sides of your head at home as long as you follow the proper steps and use the right tools. Just be sure to take your time and be careful to avoid any mishaps.

How often should I shave the sides of my head?

How often you shave the sides of your head is up to you and your personal preference. Some people prefer to do it once a week, while others may do it every few days. Just be sure to give your scalp time to rest and recover between shaves.

Hair Care Tips for Shaved Heads

Shaving the sides of your head is a bold move, but it also requires some extra hair care. Here are some tips to keep your shaved head looking and feeling great:

  • Apply sunscreen to your scalp to protect it from the sun
  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and flakiness
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair

Shaving the sides of your head can be a daring and stylish move, but it's important to do it right to avoid any mishaps. With this step-by-step guide and some helpful tips, you'll be able to achieve a clean and smooth shave that will make you look and feel your best.
