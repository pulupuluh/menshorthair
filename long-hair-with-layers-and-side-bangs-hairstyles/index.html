Long Hair With Layers And Side Bangs Hairstyles A Guide To Rocking The Look

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyles have been a popular trend for years, and it's not going away anytime soon. This look is perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their long locks while still keeping it low-maintenance. In this blog post, we'll go over everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling, and hair care practices to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ

Description

The long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyle features long, flowing locks with shorter layers throughout the hair. The side bangs add a touch of femininity to the look, framing the face and drawing attention to the eyes. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some dimension and movement to their hair without sacrificing length.

How to Achieve the Look

The long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyle can be achieved in a few different ways, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Here are some options:

  • Visit a hair salon and ask for a long layered cut with side bangs. The stylist will be able to tailor the cut to your face shape and hair type.
  • Use hair cutting shears to cut your own hair at home. This option is best for those who have experience cutting hair, as it can be difficult to achieve the desired look without professional help.
  • Use hair extensions to add length and volume to your hair. Extensions can be cut and styled to match the rest of your hair, creating a seamless look.

Step by Step Guide

If you're cutting your own hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into layers, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  3. Hold the section of hair you want to cut straight up with your fingers, and cut off the desired length.
  4. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut the layers at different lengths for added dimension.
  5. Section off the hair you want to use for the side bangs.
  6. Hold the hair straight down in front of your face, and cut at an angle to create the desired length and shape.
  7. Repeat this process on the other side of your face.
  8. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your long hair with layers and side bangs:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves to your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to the ends of your hair to create a beachy, tousled look.
  • Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame any flyaways or frizz.

FAQ

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

To maintain the long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyle, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

What face shapes does this hairstyle work best on?

This hairstyle works well on a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or updo?

Absolutely! This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, including in a ponytail or updo.

Hair Care Practices

Here are some hair care practices to keep your long hair with layers and side bangs looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the long hair with layers and side bangs hairstyle with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a casual, beachy look or a more polished, sophisticated style, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
