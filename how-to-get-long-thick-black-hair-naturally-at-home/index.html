Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Step 2: Apply Natural Oils

Apply natural oils such as coconut oil, castor oil, or olive oil to your hair and scalp. Massage your scalp for a few minutes to promote blood circulation.

Step 3: Cover Your Hair

Cover your hair with a shower cap and leave the oils on for a few hours or overnight. This will allow the oils to penetrate your hair and scalp, providing nourishment and moisture.

Step 4: Rinse Your Hair

Step 5: Air Dry
