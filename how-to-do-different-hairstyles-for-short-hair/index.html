How To Do Different Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Robby Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair can be versatile and chic, and there are many different hairstyles you can try to change up your look. Whether you have a pixie cut, bob, or lob, there are options for you. In this article, we will discuss how to do different hairstyles for short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before diving into different hairstyles for short hair, it is important to have the right tools on hand. You will need a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and possibly hairspray or other styling products. Start with clean, dry hair, and consider using a heat protectant if you plan on using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.

Curling

One way to add some texture and volume to short hair is by curling it. To do this, start by sectioning off your hair and using a curling iron or wand to curl each section away from your face. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Once you have curled all sections of your hair, use your fingers to gently tousle the curls and add some volume. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Straightening

If you prefer a sleeker look, straightening your short hair is a great option. Start by sectioning off your hair and using a straightener to smooth out each section. Be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Once you have straightened all sections, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness.

Braiding

Braids are not just for long hair! You can create braids on short hair by starting with a small section of hair and dividing it into three pieces. Cross the right piece over the middle piece, then cross the left piece over the middle piece. Continue this pattern, adding more hair to each section as you go, until you reach the end of the braid. Secure the end with a hair tie or bobby pin.

Step by Step

Crown Braid

To create a crown braid on short hair:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and divide it into two sections.
  2. Starting on one side, braid a small section of hair from the front of your head to the back.
  3. Secure the end of the braid with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Take one of the braids and wrap it around the back of your head, securing it with bobby pins as you go.
  6. Repeat with the other braid.
  7. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Messy Bun

To create a messy bun on short hair:

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun shape, securing it with bobby pins as you go.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  4. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or clips, to add some extra flair to your short hair.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • If you are using hot tools on your short hair, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to smooth your hair and distribute natural oils.

FAQ

What if my short hair is too short for these hairstyles?

If your hair is too short for the hairstyles mentioned in this article, consider using hair extensions or trying out some hair accessories to change up your look.

Can I still curl my short hair if it is naturally curly?

Absolutely! You can use a curling iron to enhance your natural curls or create looser, more defined curls.

Hair Care

Short hair still requires proper care to stay healthy and looking its best. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. If you have colored or treated hair, use products specifically designed for your hair type to maintain your color and prevent damage.
