How To Style Hair With Layers And Side Bangs

Written by Christine Mar 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Layered hairstyles and side bangs are popular hair trends that can add a lot of volume, texture, and dimension to your hair. If you have these features, you may be wondering how to style them to get the most out of your hair. In this blog post, we’ll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style hair with layers and side bangs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Hair with Layers and Side Bangs
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Hair with Layers and Side Bangs
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Layered Hair and Side Bangs

How to Style Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

The first step in styling hair with layers and side bangs is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair. After washing, blow dry your hair and use a round brush to lift and add volume to your roots.

Next, section your hair into layers and use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. Make sure to curl your hair away from your face to create a natural-looking wave. Once you’ve curled your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create more volume.

To style your side bangs, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Then, use a small amount of styling wax or pomade to create a piecey, textured look. You can also use a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to style hair with layers and side bangs:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair and use a round brush to add volume to your roots.
  3. Section your hair into layers and use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  4. Separate the curls with your fingers to create more volume.
  5. Use a flat iron to smooth out your side bangs.
  6. Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your side bangs to create a textured look.
  7. Use a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Styling Hair with Layers and Side Bangs

Here are some tips for styling hair with layers and side bangs:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to your roots.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  • Curl your hair away from your face to create a natural-looking wave.
  • Use your fingers to separate the curls and create more volume.
  • Smooth out your side bangs with a flat iron.
  • Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your side bangs for a textured look.
  • Use a hair spray to keep your bangs in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair with layers and side bangs:

What products should I use to style my hair with layers and side bangs?

You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a round brush, a curling iron, a flat iron, styling wax or pomade, and hair spray.

How often should I wash my hair if I have layers and side bangs?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Can I style my hair with layers and side bangs if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can style your hair with layers and side bangs if you have thin hair. Just make sure to use volumizing products and avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care for Layered Hair and Side Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair if you have layers and side bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layers and side bangs.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your hair with layers and side bangs and achieve a voluminous, textured look. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you, and don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
