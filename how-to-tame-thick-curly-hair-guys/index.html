How To Tame Thick Curly Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with thick, curly hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame it. It can be frustrating to deal with, but with the right techniques and products, you can manage your hair and make it look great. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to tame thick curly hair guys.

How to

There are a few steps you can take to tame your thick curly hair.

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo

The first step in taming your thick curly hair is to use the right shampoo. Look for a shampoo that is specifically designed for curly hair. These shampoos will help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Condition, Condition, Condition

Conditioning your hair is important, especially if you have thick, curly hair. Use a conditioner that is designed for curly hair, and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it easier to manage.

Step 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After you've washed and conditioned your hair, use a leave-in conditioner. This will help to moisturize your hair throughout the day and reduce frizz. Apply the leave-in conditioner evenly throughout your hair, and avoid putting too much on the roots.

Step 4: Use the Right Styling Products

To tame your thick curly hair, you need to use the right styling products. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl creams or gels. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the styling product evenly throughout your hair, and scrunch your curls to help them take shape.

Step 5: Use a Diffuser

If you're using a hair dryer to dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Start by drying your hair on a low heat setting, and increase the heat as needed.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you tame your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about taming thick curly hair for guys:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type, but generally you should aim to wash your hair no more than every other day. Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it dry and frizzy.

What kind of brush should I use?

Avoid using a brush on your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron. However, be aware that using heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant and not straighten your hair too often.

Hair Care

Taking care of your thick curly hair is important if you want to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and techniques, you can tame your thick curly hair and keep it looking great. Remember to use the right products, avoid over-washing your hair, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
