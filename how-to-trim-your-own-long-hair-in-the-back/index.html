How To Trim Your Own Long Hair In The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to get your long hair trimmed? With a little bit of patience and practice, you can learn how to trim your own long hair in the back. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the convenience of doing it in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go over the steps and tips to help you achieve a professional-looking trim.

How to Trim Your Own Long Hair in the Back

Trimming the back of your hair can be a daunting task, but with these simple steps, you'll be able to do it with ease:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from any loose hairs.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles. Make sure your hair is dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the handheld mirror, section off the back of your hair into two or three parts, depending on the thickness of your hair. You can use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 4: Begin Trimming

Starting with the bottom section, use your comb to hold the hair straight and trim off any split ends or damaged hair. It's important to only trim off a small amount at a time to avoid cutting off too much.

Step 5: Move to the Next Section

Once you've finished trimming the bottom section, move on to the middle section and repeat the process. Then, move to the top section, taking care to blend the layers together.

Step 6: Check Your Work

After you've finished trimming, use the handheld mirror to check the back of your hair to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them carefully with your scissors.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Long Hair in the Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve a professional-looking trim:

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and only trim off a small amount at a time.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check your work and make sure it's even.
  • Don't hesitate to seek help from a friend or family member if needed.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming your own long hair in the back:

Can I use any scissors to trim my hair?

No, it's important to use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Using dull or improper scissors can damage your hair.

How often should I trim my long hair?

It's recommended to trim your long hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends.

Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

No, it's best to trim your hair when it's dry to avoid any uneven cutting.

Hair Care After Trimming

After trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain healthy and shiny locks. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun's UV rays by wearing a hat or using a protective spray
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to trim your own long hair in the back like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't hesitate to seek help if needed. Happy trimming!
