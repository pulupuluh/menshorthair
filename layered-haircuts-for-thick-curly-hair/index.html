Layered Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. One of the best ways to make your hair more manageable is by getting a layered haircut. Layered haircuts for thick curly hair can help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to style and maintain. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks for getting the perfect layered haircut for your thick curly hair.

How to Get a Layered Haircut for Thick Curly Hair

Getting a layered haircut for thick curly hair can be a bit tricky. Here are some steps you can follow to get the perfect layered haircut:

Step 1: Find a Good Stylist

Before you do anything else, you need to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting thick curly hair. Look for someone who has good reviews and who has worked with curly hair before.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide how long you want your hair to be. Do you want a short bob, or do you want your hair to be shoulder-length? Your stylist can help you decide on the right length for your hair.

Step 3: Choose the Layers

Decide on the type of layers you want. Do you want long layers or short layers? Do you want your hair to be layered all over, or just in certain areas? Your stylist can help you decide on the right layers for your hair.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Once you’ve decided on the length and layers, it’s time to get the cut. Your stylist will start by washing your hair and then cutting it according to your specifications. They may use a razor or scissors to create the layers.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut

Once you’ve got your layered haircut, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for curly hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and that will help to define your curls.

Tip 2: Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Don’t wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a layered haircut is right for my thick curly hair?

A: A layered haircut is a good option for thick curly hair because it can help to reduce the weight of your hair and make it more manageable. Talk to your stylist to see if a layered haircut is right for your hair type.

Q: How often should I get a layered haircut?

A: You should get a layered haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Layered Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your layered haircut looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Tip 3: Air Dry Your Hair

Air dry your hair instead of using a blow dryer. This will help to prevent frizz and keep your curls looking great.

With these tips and tricks, you can get the perfect layered haircut for your thick curly hair and keep it looking great. Remember to find a good stylist, choose the right length and layers, and maintain your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
