Will I Look Good With Ash Blonde Hair

Written by Christine May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Ash blonde hair is a popular hair color trend that has been dominating the beauty industry for a while now. It's a gorgeous, cool-toned blonde that looks great on a variety of skin tones. But the question is, will you look good with ash blonde hair?

Description

Ash blonde hair is a mixture of blonde and gray tones, creating a cool-toned, almost silver look. It's a popular hair color trend that has been embraced by many celebrities and influencers. This hair color is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look, as the cool tones can help to neutralize any brassy or yellow tones in the hair.

How to

If you're considering going ash blonde, there are a few things you should consider before making the switch. Here are a few steps to help you achieve your desired look:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

The first step in achieving ash blonde hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type, skin tone, and natural hair color to determine if ash blonde is a good choice for you.

Step 2: Choose the Right Shade of Ash Blonde

There are many shades of ash blonde, ranging from light ash blonde to dark ash blonde. Your stylist will be able to help you choose the right shade that will complement your skin tone and natural hair color.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you go ash blonde, your stylist may need to lighten your hair. This will involve a bleaching process, which can be damaging to your hair. It's important to prepare your hair by using a deep conditioning treatment in the weeks leading up to your appointment.

Step 4: Maintain Your Ash Blonde Hair

Once you've achieved your desired ash blonde hair color, it's important to maintain it properly. This means using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for colored hair, as well as avoiding excessive heat styling and exposure to the sun.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you rock your new ash blonde hair:

  • Wear makeup that complements your new hair color.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to extend the life of your color.
  • Consider getting a trim to remove any damaged ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles that showcase your new color.

FAQ

Will ash blonde hair look good on me?

It depends on your skin tone and natural hair color. It's best to consult with a professional stylist to determine if ash blonde is a good choice for you.

Is ash blonde high-maintenance?

Ash blonde hair can be low-maintenance, as the cool tones can help to neutralize any brassy or yellow tones in the hair. However, it's important to use products specifically designed for colored hair to maintain your color.

Can I go ash blonde if I have dark hair?

It's possible to go ash blonde if you have dark hair, but it may require a few rounds of bleaching to achieve the desired color. This can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to prepare your hair properly and maintain it with the right products.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after going ash blonde. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid exposure to the sun
  • Get regular trims to remove any damaged ends

Overall, ash blonde hair is a beautiful and trendy choice for those who want a cool-toned, low-maintenance look. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if ash blonde is a good choice for you, and be sure to take care of your hair properly to maintain your new color.
