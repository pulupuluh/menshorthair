How To Cut Sides Of Hair Shorter Than Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle and want to try something different? One popular trend is to have the sides of your hair cut shorter than the top. This style is versatile and can be worn by both men and women. In this post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the sides of your hair shorter than the top. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Sides of Hair Shorter Than Top
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair
  • FAQs

How to Cut Sides of Hair Shorter Than Top

The first step in cutting the sides of your hair shorter than the top is to decide on the length you want. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Once you have decided on the length, you will need to gather the necessary tools such as scissors, clippers, and combs.

Tools Required:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Spray bottle

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect cut:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and allowing it to dry naturally or blow-drying it. This will make it easier to work with. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to part your hair into sections. You can use hair ties or clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 2: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the front. Be sure to cut in a straight line and take small sections at a time.

Step 3: Cut the Sides of Your Hair

Use clippers to cut the sides of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a gradual fade. Be sure to blend the top and sides of your hair together so that there are no visible lines.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and top of your head. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths. Be sure to take small sections at a time and cut at an angle to create a natural look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a comb to style it as desired. You can use a hairdryer and pomade to create a more polished look or leave it natural for a more casual style.

Tips for Cutting Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Invest in high-quality tools such as scissors and clippers.
  • Take your time and work in small sections.
  • Be sure to blend the hair to create a natural look.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair if it becomes too dry.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or go to a professional if you are unsure.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair by myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair by yourself, but it may be easier to have someone help you with the back and harder to reach areas.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but on average, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't worry if you make a mistake, hair grows back. If the mistake is significant, you may need to go to a professional to fix it.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you have your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as a hairdryer or straightener.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips, you can maintain your new hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Cutting the sides of your hair shorter than the top is a trendy and versatile style that can be worn by anyone. By following our step-by-step guide and using our tips and hair care advice, you can achieve the perfect cut and maintain it for weeks to come. So go ahead and try something new with your hair today!
