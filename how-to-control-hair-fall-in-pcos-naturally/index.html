How To Control Hair Fall In Pcos Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Feb 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects millions of women worldwide. One of the most common symptoms of PCOS is hair fall. Hair fall can be distressing and can significantly impact a woman's self-esteem. The good news is that there are natural ways to control hair fall in PCOS. In this article, we will explore how to control hair fall in PCOS naturally and provide tips and tricks to help you achieve healthy and luscious locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Hair Fall in PCOS Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Controlling Hair Fall in PCOS
  • Tips and Tricks to Prevent Hair Fall in PCOS
  • FAQs about Controlling Hair Fall in PCOS
  • Hair Care Routine for Women with PCOS

How to Control Hair Fall in PCOS Naturally

PCOS causes a hormonal imbalance that can lead to hair fall. The good news is that there are natural ways to control hair fall in PCOS. Here are some tips:

Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet is essential for overall health and can help prevent hair fall. Make sure you eat foods that are rich in protein, iron, and vitamins. Include leafy greens, nuts, seeds, and fruits in your diet as they are rich in essential nutrients.

Exercise Regularly

Regular exercise can help regulate hormones and improve blood flow to the scalp, which can help prevent hair fall. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise every day to keep your body and mind healthy.

Reduce Stress

Stress can cause hormonal imbalances, leading to hair fall. Try to reduce stress by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Avoid using curling irons, straighteners, and hair dryers as much as possible. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Use Natural Hair Care Products

Switch to natural hair care products that contain fewer chemicals. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like aloe vera, coconut oil, and argan oil.

Step-by-Step Guide to Controlling Hair Fall in PCOS

If you have PCOS and are experiencing hair fall, here is a step-by-step guide to help you control it:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

If you are experiencing hair fall, consult a doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions. Your doctor may recommend tests to check your hormone levels and suggest treatment options.

Step 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in nutrients to promote hair growth. Include foods that are rich in protein, iron, and vitamins in your diet.

Step 3: Exercise Regularly

Exercise regularly to regulate hormones and improve blood flow to the scalp. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise every day.

Step 4: Reduce Stress

Reduce stress by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

Step 5: Use Natural Hair Care Products

Switch to natural hair care products that contain fewer chemicals. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like aloe vera, coconut oil, and argan oil.

Step 6: Take Supplements

Taking supplements can help promote hair growth. Consult your doctor before taking any supplements.

Tips and Tricks to Prevent Hair Fall in PCOS

Here are some additional tips and tricks to prevent hair fall in PCOS:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Avoid chemical treatments such as hair straightening and coloring
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption

FAQs about Controlling Hair Fall in PCOS

Q. Can PCOS cause permanent hair loss?

A. PCOS can cause permanent hair loss if left untreated. It is essential to consult a doctor and get treatment to prevent permanent hair loss.

Q. Can hair fall be reversed in PCOS?

A. Yes, hair fall can be reversed in PCOS with proper treatment and lifestyle changes.

Q. How long does it take to see results?

A. It can take several months to see results. Be patient and consistent with your treatment and lifestyle changes.

Hair Care Routine for Women with PCOS

Here is a hair care routine for women with PCOS:

Morning

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Apply a natural conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes
  • Rinse your hair with cold water

Afternoon/Evening

  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-toothed comb
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Avoid chemical treatments such as hair straightening and coloring
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption

Controlling hair fall in PCOS can be challenging, but with proper treatment and lifestyle changes, it is possible to achieve healthy and luscious locks. Be patient and consistent with your treatment and lifestyle changes, and you will see results.
