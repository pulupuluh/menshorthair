Does Straight Hair Suit Oval Faces

Written by Frank Apr 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Description

Do you have an oval face and are contemplating whether straight hair suits your face shape? The truth is, straight hair can definitely suit oval faces. Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, and straight hair can complement this shape beautifully. In this article, we will discuss how to determine if straight hair suits oval faces, a step-by-step guide in choosing straight hair for oval faces, tips on maintaining straight hair, FAQs, and hair care for straight hair and oval faces.

How to Determine if Straight Hair Suits Oval Faces

Before deciding whether straight hair suits oval faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Oval faces have the following characteristics:

  • Forehead is slightly wider than the chin
  • Cheekbones are high and prominent
  • Face length is about one and a half times the width

If you have these characteristics, then you have an oval face shape. Once you've established your face shape, you can decide if straight hair suits you based on your hair texture and personal preference.

Step by Step Guide in Choosing Straight Hair for Oval Faces

If you've decided that straight hair is for you, here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the best straight hair for oval faces:

  1. Consider the length of your hair. Oval faces suit all hair lengths, so choose the length that you are most comfortable with.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that frames your face. This can be achieved by adding layers or bangs.
  3. Consider your hair texture. Straight hair suits oval faces, but if your hair is naturally curly, you can still achieve straight hair with the use of a straightening iron or a keratin treatment.
  4. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your features. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade for you.

Tips on Maintaining Straight Hair for Oval Faces

Maintaining straight hair for oval faces requires some effort. Here are some tips to keep your straight hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals or excessive heat
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQs

1. Can oval faces pull off short straight hair?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short straight hair. In fact, oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape and can suit any hair length.

2. Does straight hair make oval faces look longer?

Straight hair can elongate the face, but it's not necessarily a bad thing. Oval faces are already longer than they are wide, so straight hair can complement this shape beautifully.

3. Can I still wear my hair straight if I have an oval face and curly hair?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have an oval face and curly hair. You can achieve straight hair with the use of a straightening iron or a keratin treatment.

Hair Care for Straight Hair and Oval Faces

Proper hair care is essential in maintaining healthy and beautiful straight hair for oval faces. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair

Now that you know that straight hair can suit oval faces, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and hair colors. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With these tips, you can rock a beautiful straight hair look that complements your oval face shape.
