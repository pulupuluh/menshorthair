How to Change Your Hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf

To change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf, you'll need to visit Harriet at Shampoodle. Shampoodle is a hair salon that you can unlock on Main Street by spending 10,000 Bells at the Able Sisters' store. Once you've unlocked Shampoodle, Harriet will be waiting inside to give you a new hairdo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

  1. Visit Harriet at Shampoodle on Main Street
  2. Sit in the chair and choose "Makeover" from the menu
  3. Choose "Haircut" from the next menu
  4. Choose your new hairstyle from the options available
  5. Confirm your choice and pay 3,000 Bells
  6. Enjoy your new hairdo!

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

When choosing a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Leaf, keep in mind that your character's face shape and skin tone can affect how the hairstyle looks. You can also try on wigs at Shampoodle to see how different hairstyles will look before you make a final decision. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your character's face shape and skin tone
  • Try on wigs to see how different hairstyles look
  • Choose a hairstyle that you like and that suits your personality

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

  • How much does it cost to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf?
  • It costs 3,000 Bells to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf.
  • Can you change your hair color in Animal Crossing New Leaf?
  • Yes, you can change your hair color at Shampoodle in Animal Crossing New Leaf.
  • Can you undo a haircut in Animal Crossing New Leaf?
  • No, once you've paid for a haircut at Shampoodle, you can't undo it. You'll have to pay 3,000 Bells again to change your hair again.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you've got a new hairdo in Animal Crossing New Leaf, it's important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips to keep your new hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your hair tangle-free
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends

Now that you know how to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Leaf, it's time to visit Shampoodle and get a new hairdo. Don't forget to take care of your new hair and show it off to your friends and neighbors in your Animal Crossing town. Happy styling!
