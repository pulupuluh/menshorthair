How To Keep Wet Hair Curly Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep your curls looking great, especially when your hair is wet. Wet hair can be heavy, and it can weigh your curls down, causing them to lose their shape and definition. But don't worry! With the right techniques and products, you can keep your wet hair curly and fabulous. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve that perfect curly look.

How to Keep Your Hair Curly When It's Wet

Keeping your hair curly when it's wet can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips to help you keep your curls looking great:

1. Use the Right Products

One of the most important things you can do to keep your curls looking great when your hair is wet is to use the right products. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking great.

2. Apply Styling Products to Wet Hair

Another key to keeping your curls looking great when your hair is wet is to apply styling products to wet hair. This will help define your curls and keep them in place as your hair dries. Apply your styling products evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair

When your hair is wet, it's more fragile and prone to damage. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape and definition. Instead, let your hair air dry as much as possible, and avoid brushing or combing it until it's completely dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you keep your hair curly when it's wet:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly, and gently squeeze out any excess water.

2. Apply Styling Products

Apply your styling products to wet hair, using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair, encouraging your curls to form. Continue scrunching your hair until it's approximately 80% dry.

4. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry as much as possible, avoiding touching it as much as possible. Once your hair is completely dry, you can gently fluff your curls with your fingers.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your wet hair curly:

1. Use a Microfiber Towel

Instead of using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel. These towels are gentler on your hair and will help prevent frizz.

2. Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help prevent your curls from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.

3. Use a Diffuser

If you're using a blow dryer to dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment. This will help distribute the heat more evenly and prevent your curls from getting frizzy.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: How can I refresh my curls between washes?

A: To refresh your curls between washes, you can mist your hair with water and apply a small amount of curl-enhancing product.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Keeping your curly hair looking great takes more than just knowing how to keep it curly when it's wet. Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape. Instead, try air drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your wet hair curly and fabulous. Remember to use the right products, apply styling products to wet hair, and avoid touching your hair too much. With a little bit of effort, you can keep your curls looking great all day long!
