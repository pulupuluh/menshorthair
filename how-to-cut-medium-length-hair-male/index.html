How To Cut Medium Length Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the barbershop every few weeks to get your hair trimmed? Learning how to cut medium length hair male is not as difficult as you might think. With a little bit of practice, you can save yourself time and money by giving yourself a trim at home. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut medium length hair male, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Medium Length Hair Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Medium Length Hair Male
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting Medium Length Hair Male

How to Cut Medium Length Hair Male

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s go over some basic tools you’ll need to have on hand:

  • Hair clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle
  • Barber cape or towel

Once you have these tools, you’re ready to get started with your haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel and let it air dry until it’s damp but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section to keep it in place.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Start with the sides. Use your clippers or scissors to trim your hair to your desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to hold the hair in place. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Next, move onto the back. Use the same technique as you did with the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to hold the hair in place.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move onto the top. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and trim to your desired length.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use your clippers or scissors to blend the hair between the sides and the top. This will create a seamless transition between the two sections.

Step 7: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them to your desired length. Use the comb to hold the hair in place while you trim.

Step 8: Clean Up the Edges

Use your clippers or scissors to clean up the edges around your hairline and ears. This will give your haircut a polished look.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, style it using your favorite products.

Tips for Cutting Medium Length Hair Male

  • Invest in a good pair of clippers or scissors. Cheap tools can make it difficult to achieve the look you want.
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down. You can always trim more, but you can’t add length back.
  • Use the comb to hold the hair in place while you trim. This will give you more control and prevent uneven cuts.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes and an uneven cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home with the right tools and some practice.

What is the best length for medium length hair?

Medium length hair is typically between 2 and 4 inches long.

Can I cut my hair with just scissors?

Yes, you can cut your hair with just scissors. However, clippers can make the process quicker and easier.

Hair Care After Cutting Medium Length Hair Male

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain your new look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Visit a professional barber every few months to maintain your haircut.

Learning how to cut medium length hair male is a great way to save time and money. With our step-by-step guide and tips, you’ll be able to give yourself a haircut in no time.
