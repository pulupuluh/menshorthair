Best Styling Tools For Thick Coarse Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Thick, coarse hair can be challenging to style. It requires styling tools that can effectively manage the unruly texture and provide a polished finish. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best styling tools for thick coarse hair that can help you achieve the perfect hairstyle. From flat irons to hair dryers, we've got you covered.

Flat Irons

Flat irons are a must-have styling tool for anyone with thick coarse hair. They can straighten even the most unruly hair and provide a sleek, polished finish. When choosing a flat iron for thick coarse hair, look for one with wider plates as they can cover more hair and save you time. Also, consider the heat settings. You'll need a flat iron that can get hot enough to straighten your hair effectively.

Recommended flat irons for thick coarse hair:

  • BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron
  • CHI G2 Ceramic and Titanium Hairstyling Iron
  • Remington S9500PP Pearl Pro Ceramic Flat Iron

Hair Dryers

Hair dryers are another essential styling tool for thick coarse hair. They can help you dry your hair quickly and efficiently. When choosing a hair dryer for thick coarse hair, look for one with higher wattage as it can provide more heat and airflow. You'll also want to consider the weight of the hair dryer as you'll be holding it for an extended period.

Recommended hair dryers for thick coarse hair:

  • Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer
  • BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Hair Dryer
  • Conair InfinitiPRO 1875 Watt AC Motor Hair Dryer

Curling Irons

Curling irons are perfect for adding volume and texture to thick coarse hair. When choosing a curling iron for thick coarse hair, look for one with a larger barrel size as it can create bigger and looser curls. Also, consider the heat settings as you'll need a curling iron that can get hot enough to curl your hair effectively.

Recommended curling irons for thick coarse hair:

  • Hot Tools Professional 24k Gold Curling Iron
  • BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Spring Curling Iron
  • Remington CI9538 Pro 1"-1.5" Pearl Ceramic Conical Curling Wand

Hot Brushes

Hot brushes are a great styling tool for thick coarse hair as they can straighten and add volume at the same time. When choosing a hot brush for thick coarse hair, look for one with a larger barrel size as it can cover more hair and save you time. Also, consider the heat settings as you'll need a hot brush that can get hot enough to style your hair effectively.

Recommended hot brushes for thick coarse hair:

  • Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer
  • Hot Tools Professional 24k Gold Charcoal Infused One-Step Blowout
  • John Frieda Hot Air Brush

Hair Care Tips

Styling tools alone can't do the trick. You need to take care of your hair to reduce the damage caused by heat styling. Here are some hair care tips for thick coarse hair:

1. Use Heat Protectant: Always use a heat protectant before using any styling tool to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Deep Condition Regularly: Thick coarse hair can be dry and prone to breakage. Deep conditioning regularly can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid Over-washing: Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to limit washing to once or twice a week.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb: When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to minimize breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I use any flat iron for thick coarse hair?

A: No, not all flat irons are suitable for thick coarse hair. Look for one with wider plates and high heat settings.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to straighten my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer with a nozzle attachment to straighten your hair. However, using a flat iron can provide a more polished finish.

Q: How often should I deep condition my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and condition. For thick coarse hair, once a week should be sufficient.

Q: How can I prevent heat damage to my hair?

A: Always use a heat protectant and avoid using styling tools on high heat settings. Also, limit the use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

In conclusion, styling thick coarse hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle. Remember to always use a heat protectant and deep condition regularly to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Happy styling!
