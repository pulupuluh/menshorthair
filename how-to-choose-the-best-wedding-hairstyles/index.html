How To Choose The Best Wedding Hairstyles

Written by Robby Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Getting married is one of the most important moments in anyone's life. And with all the preparations that need to be done, choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of choosing the best wedding hairstyles that will make you look and feel your best on your special day.

Table of Contents

  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Match Your Hairstyle to Your Dress Style
  • Determine Your Face Shape
  • Accessorize Your Hairstyle
  • Do a Trial Run
  • Hair Care Leading Up to the Wedding Day
  • FAQs

Consider Your Hair Type

The first thing to consider when choosing the best wedding hairstyle is your hair type. Whether you have curly, straight, thick, or thin hair, there are countless options available to you. If you have curly hair, consider wearing your hair down with a few loose curls or an updo with loose curls around your face. For straight hair, try a sleek and chic updo or a half-up, half-down style. Those with thick hair can try voluminous updos or braids, while those with thin hair can add volume with extensions or textured styles.

Match Your Hairstyle to Your Dress Style

Your wedding hairstyle should complement your dress style. If you have a strapless dress, consider wearing your hair in an updo to show off your shoulders. For a high-neck dress, try a simple and elegant bun or a low ponytail. If your dress has intricate detailing or a high back, a low bun or loose waves can be the perfect complement.

Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in choosing the best wedding hairstyle. If you have a round face, consider a side-swept bang or a high updo to elongate your face. For a square face, soft curls or a low, loose bun can soften the angles. If you have an oval face, you're in luck â almost any hairstyle will look great on you! And for those with a heart-shaped face, a side part with loose waves or a half-up, half-down style can balance out the face shape.

Accessorize Your Hairstyle

Accessories can add the perfect finishing touch to your wedding hairstyle. From tiaras and headbands to flowers and combs, there are countless options available. When choosing an accessory, make sure it complements your dress style and doesn't overpower your hairstyle. If you're wearing a simple dress, a bold accessory can add a pop of color and personality. And if your dress has lots of embellishments, a simple accessory can help balance out the look.

Do a Trial Run

It's important to do a trial run of your wedding hairstyle before the big day. This will give you a chance to see how the hairstyle looks with your dress and accessories, and to make any necessary adjustments. You can also test how well the hairstyle holds up throughout the day and make any necessary changes to your hair care routine leading up to the wedding.

Hair Care Leading Up to the Wedding Day

Leading up to your wedding day, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends. You should also avoid any drastic changes to your hair, such as a new haircut or color, in the weeks leading up to the wedding.

FAQs

What if I have short hair?

Short hair can be just as beautiful and elegant as long hair. Consider adding extensions or trying a sleek bob or pixie cut for a chic and modern look.

What if I'm getting married outside?

If you're getting married outside, consider a hairstyle that won't be affected by wind or humidity, such as an updo or braided style.

What if I have a veil or headpiece?

Your veil or headpiece should be incorporated into your hairstyle. Work with your hairstylist to find a style that allows your veil or headpiece to sit comfortably and securely.

Choosing the best wedding hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect style to make you feel confident and beautiful on your special day.
