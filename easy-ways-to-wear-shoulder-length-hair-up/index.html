Easy Ways To Wear Shoulder Length Hair Up

Description

Shoulder length hair is versatile and easy to style. However, sometimes you may want to switch things up and wear your hair up for a change. There are many easy ways to wear shoulder length hair up, and in this post, we will share some of our favorite styles.

How to

Before we get into the specific styles, there are a few general tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use hair ties that won't damage your hair
  • Don't pull your hair too tight, as this can cause headaches and damage
  • Add accessories like clips or headbands for extra flair

Step by step

1. Low Ponytail

Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure with a hair tie and wrap a small section of hair around the base of the ponytail to hide the elastic.

Pro tip: For a more polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair before pulling it back.

2. Half Up Half Down

Section off the top half of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail. Secure with a hair tie and fluff up the ponytail for volume.

Pro tip: Add a hair accessory like a clip or bow to the top of the ponytail for extra style.

3. Messy Bun

Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure with bobby pins and pull out a few pieces of hair for a messy, effortless look.

Pro tip: Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to create more volume and texture in your bun.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like clips, bows, and headbands.
  • Try braiding sections of your hair before pulling it back to add extra texture.
  • Don't be afraid to add a little hairspray or gel to keep your style in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my shoulder length hair up if it's layered?

A: Yes, layering can actually add texture and interest to your updo.

Q: How can I make my ponytail look more stylish?

A: Try wrapping a small section of hair around the base of the ponytail to hide the elastic, or add a hair accessory like a clip or bow.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh your hair down

With these easy ways to wear shoulder length hair up, you can switch up your style and try something new. Whether you're heading to work, running errands, or going out with friends, these styles are versatile and easy to achieve.
