What Color Goes Best With Medium Skin Tone

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a medium skin tone, you may have wondered what colors would look best on you. Choosing the right color can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we will discuss the colors that complement medium skin tones and how to wear them.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Color
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Wearing Colors with Medium Skin Tone
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Medium Skin Tone

Description

Medium skin tone is a skin color that falls between light and dark. People with medium skin tone have a warm, golden undertone that makes them look great in certain colors. The right colors for medium skin tone can enhance your natural beauty, while the wrong ones can make you look dull or washed out.

How to Choose the Right Color

When it comes to choosing the right color for medium skin tone, there are a few things to consider:

  • Undertones: Medium skin tone has warm undertones, so you should choose colors that complement those undertones. Colors with warm undertones, such as yellow, orange, and brown, will look great on you.
  • Intensity: The intensity of the color can also affect how it looks on you. Bright, bold colors can be overwhelming, while muted or pastel colors can look washed out. You should choose colors that are neither too bright nor too dull.
  • Occasion: You should also consider the occasion when choosing colors. Bright colors may be too bold for a formal event, while muted colors may not be appropriate for a fun night out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right color for medium skin tone:

  1. Determine your undertones: Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones.
  2. Choose warm colors: Colors with warm undertones, such as yellow, orange, and brown, will look great on you.
  3. Avoid cool colors: Colors with cool undertones, such as blue, green, and purple, may not be the best choice for you.
  4. Experiment with different shades: Try on different shades of the same color to see which one looks best on you. For example, if you like green, try on different shades of green, such as olive, emerald, and forest green.
  5. Consider the occasion: Choose colors that are appropriate for the occasion. Bright colors may be too bold for a formal event, while muted colors may not be appropriate for a fun night out.

Tips for Wearing Colors with Medium Skin Tone

Here are some tips for wearing colors with medium skin tone:

  • Wear bright colors sparingly: Bright colors can be overwhelming, so it's best to wear them sparingly. For example, if you're wearing a bright red dress, pair it with neutral accessories.
  • Mix and match: Don't be afraid to mix and match colors. For example, you can pair a yellow top with blue jeans.
  • Try prints: Prints can add interest to your outfit. Choose prints with warm colors, such as floral prints or animal prints.
  • Accessorize with jewelry: Jewelry can add color to your outfit. Choose jewelry with warm colors, such as gold or bronze.
  • Experiment with makeup: Makeup can also add color to your look. Try warm-toned eyeshadows and lipsticks, such as gold or bronze eyeshadow and coral or peach lipstick.

FAQs

What colors should I avoid if I have medium skin tone?

You should avoid colors with cool undertones, such as blue, green, and purple. These colors may not complement your warm undertones.

Can I wear black if I have medium skin tone?

Yes, you can wear black if you have medium skin tone. However, you should pair it with warm-colored accessories, such as gold or bronze jewelry, to avoid looking washed out.

What are some colors that look great on medium skin tone?

Colors with warm undertones, such as yellow, orange, and brown, look great on medium skin tone. You can also try warm shades of green, blue, and purple.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Skin Tone

When it comes to hair color, there are several shades that complement medium skin tone:

  • Warm browns: Warm shades of brown, such as chestnut and caramel, complement medium skin tone.
  • Honey blonde: Honey blonde is a warm shade that looks great on medium skin tone.
  • Copper: Copper is a warm shade that can add depth and dimension to medium skin tone.

It's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you're unsure, consult with a professional stylist.

In conclusion, choosing the right color for medium skin tone can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident. Remember to consider your undertones, the intensity of the color, and the occasion when choosing colors. Experiment with different shades and don't be afraid to mix and match. Follow these tips, and you'll be sure to find colors that look great on you.
