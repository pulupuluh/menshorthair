How To Change Long Face Shape Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Petter May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a long face shape, you may be wondering how to change it. While you can't physically change the shape of your face, there are ways to create the illusion of a different shape. This article will provide tips, a step-by-step guide, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Changing Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Face Shapes

Tips for Changing Face Shape

There are several things you can do to create the illusion of a different face shape:

  • Contour your face with makeup, focusing on the areas you want to appear smaller
  • Experiment with hairstyles that add width to your face, such as bangs or layers
  • Wear accessories that draw attention away from your face shape, such as statement earrings or necklaces
  • Choose clothing that balances out your proportions and creates a more symmetrical look

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create the illusion of a different face shape:

  1. Use a foundation that matches your skin tone. Apply it evenly all over your face.
  2. Use a concealer that's one shade lighter than your skin tone. Apply it under your eyes, on the bridge of your nose, and on your forehead.
  3. Use a contour powder or cream that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and on the sides of your nose.
  4. Blend the contour into your skin using a brush or sponge.
  5. Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to add color and dimension to your face.
  6. Experiment with hairstyles that add width to your face, such as bangs or layers. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly.
  7. Wear statement accessories that draw attention away from your face shape, such as bold earrings or necklaces.
  8. Choose clothing that balances out your proportions and creates a more symmetrical look. Avoid clothing that emphasizes your long face shape, such as turtlenecks or high collars.

FAQ

Can I physically change my face shape?

No, you cannot physically change the shape of your face. However, you can create the illusion of a different shape through makeup, hairstyles, and accessories.

What hairstyles should I avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly or emphasize your long face shape, such as turtlenecks or high collars.

What accessories should I wear?

Wear statement accessories that draw attention away from your face shape, such as bold earrings or necklaces.

Hair Care for Long Face Shapes

In addition to experimenting with hairstyles, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage
  • Consider using hair products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray

By following these tips and experimenting with different makeup, hairstyles, and accessories, you can create the illusion of a different face shape and feel more confident in your appearance.
