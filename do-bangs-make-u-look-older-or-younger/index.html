Do Bangs Make You Look Older Or Younger

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades, and they can be worn by people of all ages. However, there is a common question that many people ask themselves when considering getting bangs: do bangs make you look older or younger? The answer to this question is not simple, as it depends on a variety of factors, such as the shape of your face, the type of bangs you get, and how you style them. In this article, we will explore the different types of bangs and how they can either make you look younger or older.


How to

Before we dive into the different types of bangs, it's important to understand that bangs can be styled in many different ways. You can have them straight across, side-swept, curtain, choppy, or layered. The way you style your bangs can also affect how old or young you look. For example, if you have straight across bangs that are cut too short, it can make you look older. On the other hand, side-swept bangs can give you a youthful, playful look. The key to finding the right type of bangs for you is to consider your face shape and personal style.


Step by step

To determine which type of bangs will work best for you, follow these steps:

  1. Determine your face shape. This can be done by looking in the mirror and examining the shape of your face. Is it round, oval, heart-shaped, or square?
  2. Consider your personal style. Do you prefer a more classic, sophisticated look, or do you like to be more playful and trendy?
  3. Look at pictures of different types of bangs and hairstyles that you like. This will give you an idea of what you want and what will work best for you.
  4. Talk to your hairstylist. They can give you advice on what type of bangs will work best for your face shape and personal style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when considering getting bangs:

  • If you have a round face, avoid straight across bangs, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides.
  • If you have a square face, go for soft, layered bangs that will soften your features.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, try curtain bangs that are slightly shorter in the middle and longer on the sides.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pretty much pull off any type of bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs:

Do bangs make your face look wider?

It depends on the type of bangs you get. Straight across bangs can make your face look wider, while side-swept bangs can make your face appear narrower.

Can bangs make you look younger?

Yes, they can! Side-swept bangs can give you a youthful, playful look, while layered bangs can soften your features and make you appear younger.

Can bangs make you look older?

Yes, they can. If you have straight across bangs that are cut too short, it can make you look older.


Hair Care

Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, it's important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.
  • Wash your bangs often to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to give them volume and shape.
  • Use a small amount of product, such as hairspray or pomade, to keep your bangs in place.

In conclusion, the answer to the question "do bangs make you look older or younger?" is not a simple one. It depends on a variety of factors, such as your face shape, personal style, and the type of bangs you get. However, by following the tips and advice in this article, you can find the perfect type of bangs that will make you look and feel your best.
