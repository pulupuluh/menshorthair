What Size Guard Is A Number 3

Written by William Mar 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you confused about what size guard is a number 3? If you’re trying to cut your own hair or someone else’s, it’s important to understand the different guard sizes and what they mean. In this article, we’ll explain what size guard is a number 3 and share some tips on how to use it.

Table of Contents

  2. What Size is a Number 3 Guard?
  3. How to Use a Number 3 Guard
  4. Tips for Using a Number 3 Guard

What is a Guard?

A guard is an attachment that you use with a hair clipper to control the length of hair you’re cutting. Guards come in a range of sizes, from 0.5 to 8, with each size corresponding to a specific length of hair. The smaller the size, the shorter the hair will be.

Guards are usually made of plastic and clip onto the blades of your clipper. They’re easy to attach and remove, so you can switch between sizes quickly and easily.

What Size is a Number 3 Guard?

A number 3 guard is 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. This means that when you attach a number 3 guard to your clipper, it will leave 3/8 of an inch of hair on your scalp. A number 3 guard is a popular choice for people who want a short, neat haircut.

How to Use a Number 3 Guard

If you’re new to cutting hair with clippers, it’s important to take your time and practice before you start. Here’s how to use a number 3 guard:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a comb to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Attach the number 3 guard to your clipper. Make sure it’s securely attached and won’t come loose while you’re cutting.
  3. Turn on your clipper and start at the back of your head, near your neck. Hold the clipper so the blades are flat against your scalp.
  4. Slowly move the clipper up your head, against the direction of hair growth. Use long, even strokes to ensure a neat and even cut.
  5. When you reach the top of your head, move to the sides and front. Use the same technique to cut the hair evenly.
  6. Check your work in a mirror to make sure the hair is the same length all over.

Tips for Using a Number 3 Guard

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using a number 3 guard:

  • Go slowly and use even strokes to ensure an even cut.
  • Check your work frequently in a mirror to make sure the hair is the same length all over.
  • Use a higher guard size if you want a longer haircut.
  • Change the direction of the clipper to get different effects, such as a faded look.
  • Clean your clipper blades regularly to keep them sharp and prevent them from pulling on the hair.

FAQs

What other guard sizes are there?

Guard sizes range from 0.5 to 8, with each size corresponding to a specific length of hair. The smaller the size, the shorter the hair will be.

Can I use a number 3 guard on any type of hair?

Yes, you can use a number 3 guard on any type of hair. However, if you have very thick or curly hair, you may need to go over the same area several times to ensure an even cut.

Can I cut my own hair with a number 3 guard?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with a number 3 guard. However, it’s important to take your time and practice before you start. You may also need to use a mirror to check your work.

Hair Care After Using a Number 3 Guard

After using a number 3 guard, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat.

Now that you know what size guard is a number 3, you can confidently use this popular guard size for your next haircut. Remember to take your time, use even strokes, and check your work frequently to ensure an even cut. With practice, you’ll be a pro at using a number 3 guard in no time!
