How To Cut Long Hair Short With Clippers

Written by William Apr 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and ready to make a change? Cutting your hair short can be a refreshing and exciting new look. But how do you do it yourself without spending a fortune at a salon? The answer is simple: clippers! In this article, we will guide you through how to cut long hair short with clippers.

Description

Cutting your hair short with clippers can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Clippers are a great option for cutting hair short because they are easy to use and allow for precision cutting. Whether you are looking for a buzz cut or a short, textured style, clippers can help you achieve your desired look.

How to

To cut long hair short with clippers, you will need:

  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting scissors
  • Clipper guards
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape

Before you begin cutting, it's important to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any products. Once your hair is dry, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, ranging from 1/16 inch to 1 inch. The size of the guard will determine how short your hair will be. Choose a guard based on how short you want your hair to be.

Step 2: Start at the sides

Begin by placing the clipper guard on the clippers and starting at the sides of your head. Hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and move the clippers upwards, against the direction of hair growth. Continue this motion until you have cut all the hair on the sides of your head.

Step 3: Move to the back

Next, move to the back of your head and use the same motion to cut the hair. Make sure to hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and move them upwards.

Step 4: Cut the top

Using the scissors, cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. You can use the clippers without a guard to create texture on the top of your head.

Step 5: Blend the sides

Using the clippers without a guard, blend the sides of your head into the top. Hold the clippers at a 90-degree angle and move them upwards, blending the sides into the top of your head.

Step 6: Trim the edges

Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven edges.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair short with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want your hair shorter.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Use a towel or cape to catch any loose hair.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair short with clippers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair short with clippers if you have curly hair. However, it's important to choose the right guard size and to cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even.

Do I need to oil my clippers?

Yes, it's important to oil your clippers regularly to keep them functioning properly. You can purchase clipper oil at most beauty supply stores.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair short with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your new style.

Cutting long hair short with clippers can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a great new style without breaking the bank.
