What Does 2 On The Sides Mean A Relaxed English Guide

If you're new to the world of haircuts or simply haven't kept up with the latest trends, hearing the phrase "2 on the sides" might be a bit confusing. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this guide, we'll break down what this phrase means, how to ask for it, and some tips for taking care of your new haircut. So, let's get started!

What Does 2 on the Sides Mean?

When someone says they want a "2 on the sides," they are referring to the length of the hair on the sides of their head. Specifically, a "2" refers to a clipper guard size. Clippers are used to cut hair to a specific length, and the guard determines how short or long the hair will be cut.

A "2" guard is typically around 1/4 inch in length, which is quite short. This style is often paired with a longer length on top, allowing for a bit of versatility in styling.

How to Ask for a 2 on the Sides Haircut

If you're headed to the barber or hair salon and want to ask for a 2 on the sides haircut, it's important to use the right terminology. Here's what to say:

"I'd like a 2 on the sides and longer on top, please."

This will let the stylist know exactly what you're looking for and ensure that you walk out with the right haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide for a 2 on the Sides Haircut

While it's always best to leave haircutting to the professionals, it can be helpful to understand the steps involved in a 2 on the sides haircut. Here's a basic guide:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Select a 2 guard for your clippers.
  4. Starting at the bottom of your hairline, use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides of your head to a 2 length.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides into the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers with longer guards.
  6. Style as desired.

Tips for Taking Care of Your 2 on the Sides Haircut

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups - this style tends to grow out quickly.
  • Invest in a good quality clipper set for at-home maintenance.
  • Wash and condition regularly to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a pomade or styling product to keep your hair in place and add some shine.

FAQ

Q: Is a 2 on the sides a good haircut for me?

A: This style can look great on a variety of face shapes and hair types, but it's always best to consult with a stylist to see if it's right for you.

Q: How often do I need to get a touch-up?

A: It's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for keeping your hair healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition regularly with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know what a 2 on the sides means, how to ask for it, and how to take care of it, you're ready to rock this classic haircut style. Good luck!
