How To Have A Nice Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter May 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to improve the shape and overall appearance of your face? There are a variety of techniques and tips you can utilize to achieve a more defined, attractive face shape. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best practices and methods for achieving your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Contouring and Highlighting
  • Step 3: Facial Exercises
  • Step 4: Hair Care
  • FAQ

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in achieving a nice face shape is to determine your current face shape. Generally, faces can be categorized into six different shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. Each shape has its own unique features and characteristics, and certain techniques work better for different shapes.

One way to determine your face shape is to take a selfie and trace the outline of your face onto the photo. You can also stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can move on to the next steps.

Step 2: Contouring and Highlighting

Contouring and highlighting are two techniques that can help to create the illusion of a more defined face shape. Contouring involves using a darker shade of makeup to create shadows on the face, while highlighting involves using a lighter shade of makeup to draw attention to certain areas.

For example, if you have a round face shape, you can use contouring to create a more defined jawline and cheekbones. You can also use highlighting to draw attention to your eyes or lips. There are many tutorials available online that can help you to master these techniques.

Step 3: Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are another way to improve the shape of your face. These exercises involve using your facial muscles to tone and tighten the skin. Some popular exercises include puckering your lips and holding for 10 seconds, smiling as wide as possible and holding for 10 seconds, and raising your eyebrows as high as possible and holding for 10 seconds.

Facial exercises can also help to improve circulation and reduce puffiness, which can contribute to a more defined face shape. You can find many tutorials and videos online that demonstrate different facial exercises.

Step 4: Hair Care

Believe it or not, your hair can also play a role in the shape of your face. Certain hairstyles can help to frame your face and create the illusion of a more defined shape. For example, if you have a round face shape, you can try a long, layered haircut with side-swept bangs.

On the other hand, if you have a square face shape, you can try a chin-length bob or a layered cut with soft, wispy bangs. Experiment with different hairstyles and see what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can facial exercises really improve my face shape?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, which can lead to a more defined shape over time.

Q: Do I need to contour and highlight every day?

A: It’s up to you! Contouring and highlighting can be time-consuming, so you can choose to do it every day or only on special occasions.

Q: Can I change my face shape permanently?

A: While you can’t change your bone structure, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises to create the illusion of a more defined face shape.

Q: What’s the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

A: A side-swept bang and long, layered haircut can help to balance out a heart-shaped face.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more defined, attractive face shape. Whether you choose to contour and highlight, practice facial exercises, or experiment with different hairstyles, there are many ways to enhance your natural beauty.
