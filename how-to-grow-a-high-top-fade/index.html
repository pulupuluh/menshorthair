How To Grow A High Top Fade A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, a high top fade might be just what you need. This iconic hairstyle has been popular since the 80s and has made a comeback in recent years. However, growing and maintaining a high top fade can be tricky, especially for those with relaxed hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of growing a high top fade and provide some tips on how to keep it looking sharp.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow a High Top Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade
  • Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair
  • FAQ

How to Grow a High Top Fade

A high top fade is a type of haircut that features short sides and a longer, flat top. To grow a high top fade, you'll need to start with some length on top of your head. If you have short hair or a buzz cut, you'll need to let it grow out for several months before you can start shaping your high top fade.

It's important to note that growing a high top fade takes time and patience. You may experience some awkward phases along the way, but don't give up! With the right approach, you can achieve the perfect high top fade that suits your style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps you need to follow to grow a high top fade:

  1. Start with at least 2-3 inches of hair on top of your head.
  2. Trim the sides and back of your hair regularly to maintain a clean look.
  3. Use a comb and hair clippers to shape the flat top of your hair. The length of the top should be longer than the sides.
  4. Gradually increase the length of the top as you shape your high top fade.
  5. Use a hair pick or brush to lift the hair on top and create more volume.
  6. Apply a styling product to keep your high top fade in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Top Fade

Once you've grown your high top fade, it's important to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips for maintaining your high top fade:

  • Trim the sides and back of your hair regularly to keep it clean and neat.
  • Use a hair pick or brush to lift the hair on top and create more volume.
  • Apply a styling product to keep your high top fade in place and add shine.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

If you have relaxed hair, it's important to take extra care when growing a high top fade. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy relaxed hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or relaxers too often, as this can weaken your hair and cause breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing a high top fade:

How long does it take to grow a high top fade?

It can take several months to grow a high top fade, depending on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. Be patient and don't rush the process.

What products should I use to style my high top fade?

You can use a variety of styling products to keep your high top fade in place, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Can I still get a high top fade if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can still get a high top fade if you have thin hair. However, you may need to add some volume with a hair pick or brush, and use a styling product to keep it in place.

Do I need to see a professional barber to get a high top fade?

While it's possible to cut your own hair and shape a high top fade, it's recommended to see a professional barber for best results. They can help you achieve the perfect shape and style for your high top fade.

How often do I need to trim my high top fade?

You should trim the sides and back of your hair every 2-4 weeks to maintain a clean look. You may need to trim the top less frequently, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Now that you know how to grow a high top fade, it's time to start growing your own! Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you'll soon have a stylish and iconic hairstyle that turns heads wherever you go.
