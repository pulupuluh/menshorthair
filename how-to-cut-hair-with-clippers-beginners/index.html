How To Cut Hair With Clippers For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting your own or someone else's hair with clippers can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to it. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to cut hair with clippers for beginners.

Description

Cutting hair with clippers is a popular and convenient way to achieve a variety of hairstyles. Clippers are easy to use, and with practice, you can become an expert in no time. Whether you're looking to trim your own hair or someone else's, it's important to follow the right steps.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers for Beginners

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair with clippers for beginners:

Step 1: Get the Right Tools

Before you start cutting hair with clippers, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a set of clippers, clipper guards, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Make sure your clippers are sharp and well-maintained to achieve the best results.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is free of tangles and knots. Use a towel to dry the hair and comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard Size

Clipper guards come in different sizes, and each size corresponds to a specific length of hair. Choose the right guard size based on the length of hair you want to achieve. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Once you have the right guard size, it's time to start cutting. Begin at the back of the head and work your way towards the front. Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion, following the natural curve of the head. Make sure to go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you've cut the top of the hair, it's time to move on to the sides and back. Use a shorter guard to trim the sides and back, and use the clippers to create a clean line around the ears and neckline.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

To achieve a natural-looking haircut, it's important to blend the hair. Use a longer guard to blend the top of the hair with the sides and back, creating a smooth transition between the different lengths.

Step 7: Use Scissors to Trim the Top

Once you've achieved the desired length with the clippers, use a pair of scissors to trim the top of the hair. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any uneven or longer pieces.

Step 8: Clean up the Neckline and Ears

Use the clippers and a clean razor to clean up the neckline and around the ears. This will create a clean and polished look.

Step 9: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, check for evenness. Use the comb and scissors to trim any uneven or longer pieces of hair.

Step 10: Style the Hair

Once you're happy with the length and shape of the haircut, it's time to style the hair. Use a styling product to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut when cutting hair with clippers:

  • Use a steady hand and take your time.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.
  • Use the clippers to create a clean line around the ears and neckline.
  • Blend the hair for a natural-looking haircut.
  • Use scissors to trim any uneven or longer pieces of hair.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. However, it's important to follow the right steps and use the right tools to achieve a great haircut.

What are the best clippers to use for cutting hair?

The best clippers for cutting hair are those that are sharp, well-maintained, and easy to use. Some popular brands include Wahl, Andis, and Oster.

Hair Care After Cutting with Clippers

After cutting hair with clippers, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too frequently. Regular trims can also help to keep the hair healthy and prevent split ends.
