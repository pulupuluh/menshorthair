How To Curl Long Hair With Sponge Rollers

Written by William Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Table of Content: Description | How to | Step by Step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

If you're looking for a way to add volume and curls to your long hair without using heat, then sponge rollers are a great option. They are easy to use and can give you a variety of curl sizes depending on the size of the rollers you choose. In this article, we will walk you through how to curl long hair with sponge rollers step by step.

How to

The first step in using sponge rollers is to make sure that your hair is clean and completely dry. If your hair is wet, the curls won't hold as well. You can use a blow dryer to dry your hair if needed, but make sure it's completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Next, section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be. Take one section of hair and place the sponge roller at the end of the hair. Roll the hair up towards your scalp, making sure that the ends are tucked in. Secure the roller in place with the clip that comes with the roller.

Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in rollers. Make sure that the rollers are secure and not loose, or they won't hold the curl.

Leave the rollers in your hair for at least a few hours, or overnight if possible. The longer you leave them in, the tighter the curl will be. When you're ready to take them out, carefully unroll each roller and gently shake out your curls.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be.

Step 3: Roll Your Hair

Take one section of hair and place the sponge roller at the end of the hair. Roll the hair up towards your scalp, making sure that the ends are tucked in. Secure the roller in place with the clip that comes with the roller.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in rollers. Make sure that the rollers are secure and not loose, or they won't hold the curl.

Step 5: Leave In

Leave the rollers in your hair for at least a few hours, or overnight if possible.

Step 6: Take Out Rollers

When you're ready to take them out, carefully unroll each roller and gently shake out your curls.

Tips

  • Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and larger rollers for looser curls.
  • Experiment with different placement of the rollers for different curl patterns.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before rolling to ensure the curls hold.
  • Apply a styling product before rolling to help hold the curls.
  • Use a hairspray to set the curls and help them last longer.

FAQ

How long should I leave the sponge rollers in?

You should leave the sponge rollers in your hair for at least a few hours, or overnight if possible, for the best results.

How do I make sure the rollers don't fall out?

Make sure the rollers are secure and not loose, or they won't hold the curl. Use the clips that come with the rollers to secure them in place.

Can I use sponge rollers on wet hair?

No, you should not use sponge rollers on wet hair. Your hair should be completely dry before rolling to ensure the curls hold.

Hair Care

Using sponge rollers can be a great way to add volume and curls to your hair without using heat. However, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated. Also, be gentle when brushing and styling your hair to avoid breakage.
