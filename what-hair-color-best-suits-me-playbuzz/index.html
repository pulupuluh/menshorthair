What Hair Color Best Suits Me Playbuzz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and want to switch things up? Or maybe you're considering dyeing your hair for the first time but don't know where to start. Playbuzz has a quiz that can help you determine the best hair color for you, but we've put together a comprehensive guide to help you make the best decision for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. Read on for tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice!

Description

The Playbuzz quiz "What Hair Color Best Suits Me" has been a popular tool for those looking to switch up their hair color. By answering a few questions about your skin tone, eye color, and style preferences, the quiz generates a recommended hair color for you. However, it's important to keep in mind that the quiz is just a suggestion and there are many factors to consider before dyeing your hair. This guide will help you determine the best hair color for you based on your individual features and preferences.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining the best hair color for you is to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories: warm and cool. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden hue. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue hue. An easy way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color that complements your golden or yellow complexion. Shades of red, copper, and honey blonde are great options. Avoid cool tones like ash blonde or platinum, which can wash out your complexion.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color that complements your pink or blue complexion. Shades of ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown are great options. Avoid warm tones like copper or gold, which can clash with your complexion.

How to Determine Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the best hair color for you. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural color.

Blue Eyes

If you have blue eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and strawberry blonde can make your eyes stand out. Cool tones like ash blonde or platinum can also complement your eye color.

Brown Eyes

If you have brown eyes, warm hair colors like caramel, chestnut, and golden brown can make your eyes pop. Cool tones like ash brown can also be a great option.

Green Eyes

If you have green eyes, warm hair colors like copper or auburn can bring out the green tones in your eyes. Cool tones like ash blonde or platinum can also complement your eye color.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair

Once you've determined the best hair color for you based on your skin tone and eye color, it's time to dye your hair! Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Dye

Choose a hair dye that fits your budget and is specifically formulated for your hair type and color. If you have dark hair and want to go lighter, you may need to use a bleach or lightening kit.

Step 2: Do a Patch Test

Before applying the dye to your whole head, do a patch test to make sure you're not allergic to the ingredients. Apply a small amount of dye to the inside of your elbow and wait 24 hours to see if you have a reaction.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and make sure your hair is clean and dry before applying the dye. Protect your skin and clothing by wearing gloves and an old shirt.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to apply the dye evenly to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time and rinse thoroughly.

Step 5: Maintain Your Hair Color

After dyeing your hair, it's important to maintain your new color. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and keep your hair healthy. Avoid hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Color

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair color for you:

  • Consider your personal style and the look you're going for. Do you want a dramatic change or a subtle update?
  • Take into account your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do. Certain hair colors require more upkeep than others.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure. They can help you determine the best hair color for your individual features and provide guidance on how to achieve your desired look.

FAQ

Is it safe to dye your hair?

Dyeing your hair is generally safe, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the dye to your whole head.

How often should I dye my hair?

This depends on the individual and the type of dye used. Semi-permanent dyes can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, while permanent dyes can last up to 8 weeks. It's best to wait at least 4-6 weeks between dye jobs to prevent damage to your hair.

Can I dye my hair at home or should I go to a salon?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's recommended to go to a salon for more dramatic changes or if you're unsure about the process. A professional hairstylist can help you achieve your desired look and provide guidance on hair care and maintenance.

Hair Care Tips

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage and maintain your new color. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.

Following these tips and using the information provided in this guide can help you determine the best hair color for you and achieve your desired look. Whether you're going for a bold new shade or a subtle update, choosing the right hair color can boost your confidence and make you feel like a whole new person!
