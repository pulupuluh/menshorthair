What Hairstyles Make Your Face Look Smaller

Written by Christine May 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of having a round or chubby face? Do you want to make your face appear slimmer and more defined? If so, then you're in luck! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best hairstyles that can help make your face look smaller.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a game-changer, especially when it comes to making your face look smaller. The right hairstyle can help create an illusion of a slimmer face by framing your facial features in a way that balances out the roundness or chubbiness. There are many different hairstyles that can help you achieve this, from short bobs to long layers, but it all depends on your face shape and hair type.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle that can make your face look smaller, the key is to create volume at the crown of your head and add layers around your face. This will help draw attention away from the roundness and create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Here are some hairstyles that you can try:

1. Short Bob

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that can help make your face look smaller. It works well for all face shapes, but especially for round faces. The length of the bob should be at your chin or just above it, and it should be cut in layers to create volume and movement. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a more elongated face shape.

2. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can also help make your face look smaller. This short hairstyle is perfect for those with oval or heart-shaped faces. The key is to add volume at the crown of your head and keep the sides and back short. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a more balanced look.

3. Long Layers

For those with long hair, adding layers can help make your face look smaller. The layers should start at your chin or just below it and frame your face. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a more elongated face shape.

4. Side Part

A side part is a simple but effective way to make your face look smaller. It works well for all hair lengths and face shapes. The key is to create volume at the crown of your head and keep the sides sleek and smooth.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of these hairstyles:

1. Short Bob

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Starting from the back, use a straightener to create loose curls or waves.
  4. Once you've curled all your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create volume.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

2. Pixie Cut

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Using a clipper, cut the back and sides of your hair short.
  4. Leave the top longer and use scissors to create layers and volume.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

3. Long Layers

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Starting from the back, use a curling iron to create loose curls or waves.
  4. Once you've curled all your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create volume.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

4. Side Part

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Use a comb to create a deep side part.
  3. Starting from the back, use a straightener to create sleek, smooth hair.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle that can make your face look smaller:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is a short bob with layers or a pixie cut with volume at the crown of your head.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

The best hairstyle for an oval face is long layers that frame your face and create volume.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know what hairstyles make your face look smaller, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out some of these styles for yourself. Remember, the key is to create volume at the crown of your head and add layers around your face to create an illusion of a slimmer face. With the right hairstyle, you can feel more confident and beautiful than ever before!
