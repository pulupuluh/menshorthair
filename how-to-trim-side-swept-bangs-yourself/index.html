How To Trim Side Swept Bangs Yourself

Written by William Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glamor and sophistication to your look. However, keeping them looking great requires regular trimming. While it can be tempting to visit the salon every time you need a trim, it's also possible to trim side swept bangs yourself at home. Here's how to do it.

How to Trim Side Swept Bangs Yourself

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Tools

Before you start trimming, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also need a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section off the hair you want to trim. If you have long hair, you may want to tie the rest of your hair back to make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. A good rule of thumb is to start with longer bangs and trim them gradually until you reach your desired length.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Hold the scissors vertically and start cutting small sections of hair at a time. Make sure to cut at an angle to create a natural-looking curve. Take breaks to check your progress in the mirror and adjust as needed.

Step 5: Blend and Style

Once you've trimmed your bangs to the desired length, use the scissors to blend any uneven spots. Style your bangs as desired using a blow dryer or straightening iron.

Tips for Trimming Side Swept Bangs Yourself

  • Use sharp, high-quality scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Start with small snips and work gradually. You can always trim more if needed.
  • Make sure your bangs are dry before you start trimming. Wet hair can look longer than it really is, which can result in uneven cuts.
  • When in doubt, go shorter. You can always grow your bangs out again if you don't like the length.
  • If you're nervous about trimming your bangs yourself, consider watching a tutorial video or practicing on a wig first.

FAQs

What if I mess up?

If you accidentally cut your bangs too short or unevenly, don't panic. You can always visit a salon to have them fixed or styled differently until they grow out.

How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look uneven. As a general rule, you may want to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

Can I trim my bangs when they're wet?

No, it's best to trim your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair can look longer than it really is, which can result in uneven cuts.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Keeping your side swept bangs looking great requires regular maintenance and care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your bangs.
  • Use a blow dryer or straightening iron to style your bangs as desired.
  • Visit a salon regularly to have your bangs trimmed and styled by a professional.
