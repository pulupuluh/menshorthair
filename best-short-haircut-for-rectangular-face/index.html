Best Short Haircut For Rectangular Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 08, 2023 · 10 min read
If you have a rectangular face shape, finding the perfect short haircut can be tricky. You want a style that will compliment your features and make your face appear more balanced. In this article, we’ll go over the best short haircuts for rectangular faces, as well as tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a longer face length and a narrower width. The jawline is typically square, and the forehead and cheekbones are of similar width. To balance out the face shape, it’s best to choose a short haircut that adds width to the sides of the face.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Rectangular Face

When choosing a short haircut for a rectangular face, it’s important to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

As mentioned, rectangular face shapes have a longer face length and narrower width. To balance this out, choose a haircut that adds width to the sides of the face.

Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture when choosing a short haircut. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to add movement and texture. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob may be more suitable.

Lifestyle

Choose a short haircut that fits your lifestyle. If you’re someone who prefers low-maintenance styles, a pixie cut may be a great option. If you’re looking for something more versatile, a layered bob may be the way to go.

Best Short Haircuts for Rectangular Face

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short haircut for a rectangular face, let’s take a look at some of the best styles:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that works well for rectangular face shapes. It adds width to the sides of the face and draws attention to the eyes. This low-maintenance style is perfect for those who want to save time on styling.

Layered Bob

The layered bob is a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape. For rectangular faces, ask your stylist to add layers to the sides of the hair to create the illusion of width. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance cut that can be dressed up or down.

Blunt Bob

The blunt bob is a chic and sophisticated style that works well for rectangular faces. This cut adds width to the sides of the face and frames the jawline, creating a balanced look. This style is perfect for those who want a polished and professional look.

Step by Step: Getting the Perfect Short Haircut for Rectangular Face

Now that you know which short haircuts work best for rectangular faces, it’s time to book your appointment with a trusted stylist. Follow these steps to ensure you get the perfect cut:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before heading to the salon, find inspiration for your new haircut. Browse Pinterest or Instagram for photos of short haircuts that you like. This will give your stylist an idea of what you’re looking for.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

During your consultation, discuss your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle with your stylist. They’ll be able to recommend the best short haircut for you.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you’ve decided on a style, it’s time to get the cut. Relax and trust your stylist to create the perfect look for you.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After the cut, ask your stylist for tips on styling your new haircut. They’ll be able to recommend products and techniques that will help you achieve the perfect look.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Now that you have the perfect short haircut for your rectangular face, it’s important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often. This can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQ

What’s the best short haircut for a rectangular face?

The best short haircuts for rectangular faces are the pixie cut, layered bob, and blunt bob. These styles add width to the sides of the face and create a balanced look.

How do I choose a short haircut for my face shape?

When choosing a short haircut, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. For rectangular faces, choose a style that adds width to the sides of the face.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day or every two days, depending on your hair type. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any haircut, short or long. Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair looking and feeling great:

Tip 1: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage. Look for a mask that is appropriate for your hair type.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. This can prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as tight braids or ponytails, that can damage your hair and cause breakage. Opt for looser styles instead. In conclusion, finding the perfect short haircut for a rectangular face doesn’t have to be difficult. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose a style that compliments your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to maintain healthy hair by washing regularly, avoiding heat styling, and getting regular trims. With these tips, you’ll have a stylish and healthy short haircut in no time.
