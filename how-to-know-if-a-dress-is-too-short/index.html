How To Know If A Dress Is Too Short A Guide For Fashionable Women

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As a fashion-conscious woman, you want to look your best when you step out in public. One of the most important things to consider when choosing an outfit is the length of your dress. Wearing a dress that's too short can be uncomfortable and embarrassing. In this article, we'll show you how to know if a dress is too short, so you can avoid any fashion mishaps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know if a Dress is Too Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Avoid Wearing a Dress that's Too Short
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Look

How to Know if a Dress is Too Short

There are a few things to consider when determining if a dress is too short or not. Here are some indicators:

  • The dress doesn't cover your buttocks. If the hemline of your dress is above your buttocks, it's too short.
  • The dress is shorter than your fingertips when you stand up straight with your arms at your sides. This is a general rule of thumb to follow when choosing a dress length.
  • The dress feels uncomfortable when you sit down. If you're constantly pulling your dress down to cover your thighs, it's too short.
  • The dress shows your underwear when you sit down. This is a clear sign that your dress is too short.

Keep these things in mind when trying on dresses, and you'll be able to avoid any wardrobe malfunctions.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure if your dress is too short, follow these steps:

  1. Put on the dress and stand in front of a full-length mirror.
  2. Stand up straight with your arms at your sides.
  3. Bend over as if you're picking something up off the ground. If your dress doesn't cover your buttocks, it's too short.
  4. Sit down in a chair. If your dress rides up and shows your underwear, it's too short.

By following these steps, you'll be able to determine if your dress is an appropriate length.

Tips to Avoid Wearing a Dress that's Too Short

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a dress:

  • Measure the length of your dress before purchasing it. This will give you an idea of how long it will be on you.
  • Try on dresses before buying them. This will allow you to see how the dress fits and determine if it's an appropriate length.
  • Consider the occasion. If you're going to a formal event, a shorter dress may not be appropriate.
  • Wear appropriate underwear. If you're wearing a short dress, make sure your underwear isn't visible.

By following these tips, you'll be able to choose a dress that's both stylish and appropriate for the occasion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about dress length:

Q: Can I wear a short dress if I have long legs?

A: Yes, you can wear a short dress if you have long legs. Just make sure the dress isn't too short and follows the general rule of thumb (the dress should be longer than your fingertips when your arms are at your sides).

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code of the wedding. If it's a formal wedding, a shorter dress may not be appropriate. If it's a casual wedding, a shorter dress may be acceptable.

Q: How can I make a short dress look more modest?

A: You can pair a short dress with tights or leggings to make it look more modest. You can also wear a cardigan or blazer to cover your shoulders.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Look

Your hair is an important part of your overall look. Here are some hair care tips to complement your dress:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress. If you're wearing a formal dress, an updo may be appropriate. If you're wearing a casual dress, a messy bun or loose waves may be more appropriate.
  • Use hair products to keep your hair in place. If you're wearing a short dress, you may want to use hairspray or gel to keep your hair from falling in your face.
  • Accessorize with hair accessories. A headband or barrette can add a touch of elegance to your look.

By following these hair care tips, you'll be able to create a complete look that's both stylish and comfortable.

Conclusion

Knowing how to determine if a dress is too short is an important skill for any fashion-conscious woman. By following the guidelines in this article, you'll be able to choose a dress that's both stylish and appropriate for any occasion. Remember to measure the length of your dress, try it on before buying it, and consider the occasion when choosing a dress. And don't forget to complement your dress with a hairstyle that completes your look!
