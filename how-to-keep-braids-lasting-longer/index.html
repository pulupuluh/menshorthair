How To Keep Braids Lasting Longer Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Braids are a popular hairstyle for many women, but they can be time-consuming and expensive to maintain. If you want to keep your braids looking great for as long as possible, there are a few things you can do to make sure they last longer. In this article, we'll share some helpful tips and tricks to help you keep your braids looking fresh and fabulous for weeks on end.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Keep Braids Lasting Longer

Keeping your braids looking great for longer is all about taking care of your hair and your scalp. Here are some tips to help you keep your braids looking fresh and fabulous:

1. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your braids too often can cause them to unravel and lose their shape. Instead, try to wash your hair no more than once a week. Use a gentle shampoo and a conditioner to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

2. Keep Your Scalp Moisturized

Dry scalp can cause itching and discomfort, which can lead to hair breakage and damage. Apply a moisturizer to your scalp every few days to keep it hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your hair while sleeping. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and damage to your braids, causing them to unravel or become frizzy.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and stress on your braids. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that will not pull on your braids or cause them to unravel.

5. Keep Your Hair Dry

Avoid getting your braids wet, as this can cause them to unravel and lose their shape. If you need to wash your hair, use a shower cap to keep your braids dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you keep your braids looking great for longer:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair no more than once a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply Moisturizer

Apply a moisturizer to your scalp every few days to keep it hydrated and healthy. Use a light, non-greasy formula that won't weigh your hair down.

Step 3: Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your hair while sleeping. This will prevent friction and damage to your braids.

Step 4: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause stress and tension on your scalp. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that will not pull on your braids.

Step 5: Keep Your Hair Dry

Avoid getting your braids wet, as this can cause them to unravel and lose their shape. Use a shower cap to keep your braids dry when washing your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your braids looking great for longer:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your braids to keep them moisturized and healthy. This will also help to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Don't Scratch Your Scalp

Avoid scratching your scalp, as this can cause irritation and damage to your braids. If you have an itchy scalp, try using a scalp spray or oil to soothe it.

3. Trim Your Ends

Trim your ends every few weeks to prevent split ends and damage. This will help to keep your braids looking neat and tidy.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids, as this can cause them to unravel and become frizzy. Instead, opt for natural styles that don't require heat.

FAQ

Q: How long do braids typically last?

A: Braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well they are cared for and how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I wash my braids?

A: Yes, you can wash your braids, but it's important to avoid getting them wet. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and try to wash your hair no more than once a week.

Q: How do I prevent my braids from frizzing?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid over-washing your hair and use a leave-in conditioner. You can also apply a small amount of oil to your braids to help keep them smooth and shiny.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy and strong is important for maintaining your braids. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair in top condition:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help to keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, opt for natural styles that don't require heat.

3. Trim Your Ends

Trim your ends every few weeks to prevent split ends and damage. This will help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. This will prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

5. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and strong. Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Following these tips and tricks can help you keep your braids looking great for longer. By taking care of your hair and your scalp, you can enjoy your braids for weeks on end.
