How To Get Rid Of Bangs On A Wig

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Wearing a wig is a great way to change up your look without having to commit to a new haircut or color. However, sometimes the wig comes with bangs that just don't work for your face shape or personal style. The good news is that it's easy to get rid of bangs on a wig and create a look that suits you better. In this article, we'll go over how to do just that.

How to

The easiest and most straightforward way to get rid of bangs on a wig is to cut them off. Here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a wig stand or mannequin head to hold your wig in place while you work on it.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Use the comb to section off the hair you want to remove. If you're not sure how much to cut off, start with a small amount and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back on.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using the scissors, cut the hair off in a straight line. Be careful not to cut too much off at once, and make sure the line is even. If you're having trouble keeping the hair in place, use hair clips to hold it down.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use the comb to blend the hair you just cut into the rest of the wig. This will help create a seamless look and prevent any choppy lines or unevenness.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Now that your bangs are gone, you can style your wig however you like. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or sleekness, or leave it as is for a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your wig:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent jagged edges
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Blend the hair to create a seamless look
  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of bangs on a wig:

Can I get rid of bangs on a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can cut the bangs off a synthetic wig just like you would with a human hair wig. Just be sure to use sharp scissors and take your time to prevent any damage to the fibers.

Can I add bangs to a wig?

Yes, you can add bangs to a wig using clip-in bangs or by cutting hair from another wig and attaching it to the front of your wig. However, this can be a more complicated process and may require the help of a professional.

How often should I cut my wig?

It depends on how often you wear your wig and how quickly your hair grows. If you wear your wig frequently, you may need to trim it every few weeks to keep it looking fresh. If you only wear it occasionally, you can wait longer between cuts.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your wig, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to prevent tangles and maintain its shape
  • Wash your wig regularly with wig shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat on synthetic wigs, as it can damage the fibers
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to prevent tangles and breakage
  • Protect your wig from sunlight and heat sources, as they can cause fading and damage

With these tips and tricks, you can easily get rid of bangs on a wig and create a look that suits your personal style. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and blend the hair for a seamless look. Happy wig styling!
