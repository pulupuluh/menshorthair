How To Cut Men s Curly Hair With Scissors At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or a loved one a haircut at home, it can be daunting to know where to start. With curly hair, it can be even more challenging. But don't worry, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to cut men's curly hair with scissors at home.

Description

Curly hair requires a different approach to cutting than straight hair. The curls need to be cut in a way that allows them to fall naturally and blend seamlessly. With the right technique, you can achieve a great-looking cut that enhances the natural texture of the hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this with a pair of scissors.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also a good idea to have a mirror set up so you can see the back of your head.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural shape of your curls and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair out. This will help to separate the curls and make them easier to cut. Make sure you comb your hair in the direction you want it to fall.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section off your hair. Start by dividing your hair into four sections: two on top and two on the sides. Clip each section up with a hair clip.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the hair on the sides. Use the comb to lift a section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair on the sides.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Next, move on to the top of your head. Use the same technique as before, lifting a section of hair and cutting it to the desired length. Make sure you blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top so it looks seamless.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the hair, use the scissors to blend the hair. This will help to make the haircut look more natural. Use the scissors to take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to blend the hair together.

Tips

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. You can always cut more later.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. It will look shorter when it dries.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair with clippers?

You can cut your curly hair with clippers, but it's not recommended. Clippers can be difficult to use on curly hair and can leave the hair looking uneven.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After you've cut your curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair to keep it looking healthy and hydrated. It's also a good idea to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help define your curls.

Now that you know how to cut men's curly hair with scissors at home, you can give it a try yourself. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut that enhances the natural texture of your hair.
