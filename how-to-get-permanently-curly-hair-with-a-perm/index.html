How To Get Permanently Curly Hair With A Perm

Written by Aprilia Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of your straight hair and want to add some volume and bounce to your locks, getting a perm might be the solution you're looking for. A perm is a chemical treatment that permanently curls your hair. In this article, we'll discuss how to get permanently curly hair with a perm, step by step. We'll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Permanently Curly Hair with a Perm
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

Step by Step

The first step in getting a perm is to find a reputable hair salon that specializes in perms. You can ask your friends or family members for recommendations, or you can search online for salons in your area.

Once you've found a salon, schedule a consultation with a stylist to discuss your hair type and the type of curls you want. Your stylist will also perform a strand test to determine the best perm solution for your hair.

On the day of your appointment, your stylist will wash your hair and wrap it around perm rods of various sizes, depending on the type of curls you want. The perm solution will then be applied to your hair and left on for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and the desired level of curl.

After the perm solution is rinsed out, a neutralizing solution will be applied to your hair to stop the chemical process. Your stylist will then remove the perm rods and rinse your hair thoroughly.

Finally, your stylist will apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage. You can then style your new curls as desired.

Tips

  • Make sure to follow your stylist's instructions for aftercare, which may include avoiding heat styling tools and using a specific shampoo and conditioner.
  • Be prepared for some maintenance, as perms require regular trims to keep the curls looking fresh.
  • If you have color-treated hair, be sure to inform your stylist, as certain types of perms may not be suitable for colored hair.

FAQ

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your curls.

Can I get a perm if my hair is damaged?

If your hair is severely damaged, it's best to wait until it's healthier before getting a perm. Your stylist can advise you on the best course of action.

Can I wash my hair after getting a perm?

Your stylist will advise you on when it's safe to wash your hair after getting a perm. It's important to avoid washing your hair too soon, as this can affect the curl formation.

Hair Care

After getting a perm, it's important to take good care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls looking fresh.

Getting a perm can be a great way to add some volume and bounce to your hair. By following these tips and taking good care of your curls, you can enjoy your new look for months to come.
