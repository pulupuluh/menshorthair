How To Straighten Short Curly Hair For Guys

Written by Robby Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short curly hair can be difficult to manage, especially for guys who want a straighter, sleeker look. Fortunately, with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to straighten short curly hair without damaging it. In this article, we'll go over how to straighten short curly hair for guys step by step.

Description

Straightening short curly hair requires a combination of heat, products, and tools. It's important to use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair. Additionally, using the right tools, such as a flat iron, can help you achieve a straighter look without excessive heat exposure.

How to Straighten Short Curly Hair for Guys

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to soften your curls and make them easier to straighten. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it, gently patting it dry without rubbing.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Products

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant serum, spray, or cream to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the heat and keep your hair looking healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Use a round brush to help straighten your hair as you blow dry it. Be sure to keep the blow dryer moving and avoid concentrating the heat in one area for too long.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is mostly dry, divide it into sections using hair clips. This will help you to work on one section at a time and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Clamp the flat iron around a small section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process on each section of your hair until you've straightened it all.

Step 6: Apply Finishing Products

Once you've straightened your hair, apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to the ends to prevent frizz and add shine. You can also use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant product to prevent damage to your hair
  • Don't use excessive heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates for best results
  • Work on one section of your hair at a time to ensure that you don't miss any areas
  • Apply a small amount of finishing product to the ends of your hair for added shine and frizz control

FAQ

Can I straighten short curly hair without using heat?

While heat styling tools are the most effective way to straighten short curly hair, you can also try using hair rollers or braids to achieve a straighter look without heat.

How often should I straighten my hair?

Straightening your hair too often can cause damage, so it's best to limit it to once or twice a week. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to use the right products and techniques. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated. Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant product when using heat styling tools. Additionally, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
