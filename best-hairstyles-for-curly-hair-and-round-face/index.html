The Best Hairstyles For Curly Hair And Round Face How To Rock Your Natural Curls

Written by Oliver Aug 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of straightening your curly hair to fit traditional beauty standards? Embrace your natural curls and round face shape with these hairstyles that flatter and enhance your features. From short cuts to long layers, we’ve got you covered with tips, tricks, and product recommendations for your curly hair journey.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair and round faces can be a winning combination if you know how to style them right. The key is to create balance and elongate your face shape with volume and texture. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face or flatten your curls, and opt for styles that enhance your natural curls and frame your features.

How to

The best hairstyles for curly hair and round face include:

Short Cuts

A short cut can be a great option for curly hair and round face as it adds height and volume to your hair, creating a more elongated face shape. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can be a stylish and easy-to-maintain option for busy girls who want to rock their natural curls.

Medium Length Cuts

A medium length cut with layers can also be a great option for curly hair and round face as it adds movement and texture to your hair without adding width. A long bob or a shaggy cut with side-swept bangs can be a flattering option that frames your face and highlights your best features.

Long Layers

Long layers can be a great option for curly hair and round face as they add volume and texture to your hair without adding width. A layered cut with a side part can be a flattering option that elongates your face and enhances your natural curls.

Step by step

To style your curly hair and round face, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without flattening your curls.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls or add more texture to your hair.
  5. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or serum to lock in your style and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your curly hair and round face:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent dryness and frizz. Try washing your hair every other day or using a co-wash instead of shampoo.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they’re wet to prevent breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Try a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different partings and accessories to create different looks with your curly hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyles for curly hair and round face:

What are the best products for curly hair?

The best products for curly hair include leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and styling gels that enhance your natural curls and provide moisture and hold. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you want to change up your look. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using a flat iron on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Also, remember to embrace your natural curls and avoid heat styling too often to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair.

How do I know if a hairstyle is right for my face shape?

The best way to determine if a hairstyle is right for your face shape is to consult with a professional stylist. They can recommend styles that flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. You can also experiment with different styles and see what works best for you.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful curly hair and round face, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they’re wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your natural curls and round face with confidence and style. Embrace your unique features and experiment with different looks to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
