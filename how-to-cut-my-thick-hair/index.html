How To Cut My Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you have thick hair and want to give yourself a haircut? Don’t worry, you’re not alone! Many people with thick hair prefer to cut their own hair at home, either to save money or because they prefer the convenience of not having to go to a salon. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your thick hair at home, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the best results. Let’s get started!

How to Cut My Thick Hair

Cutting thick hair requires some extra attention, as it’s often harder to manage and style than other hair types. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to cut your thick hair:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. You may also want to have a mirror, towel, and spray bottle of water on hand.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and towel dry it before you start cutting. It’s easier to cut your hair when it’s clean and dry. Don’t style it with any products or heat tools, as this can make it harder to cut evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you to cut your hair evenly and avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Start by cutting a small amount of hair at a time. It’s better to cut less than more, as you can always go back and cut more if needed. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way up to the top layer.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any necessary adjustments or touch-ups as needed.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you’re happy with your haircut, style your hair as desired. You can use hair products, heat tools, or accessories to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your thick hair:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. This will make the process easier and less damaging to your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time. This will help you to avoid any mistakes and achieve an even cut.
  • Use a comb to help guide your cutting and ensure that your hair is even.
  • If you’re not sure how short to cut your hair, start with a longer length and work your way down gradually.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as this can make it harder to see how much you’re cutting.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting thick hair:

Is it okay to cut my own hair?

Yes, it’s okay to cut your own hair. However, it’s important to be careful and take your time to avoid any mistakes or damage to your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s generally recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its health and shape.

How can I avoid cutting too much hair?

Start by cutting small sections at a time, and use a comb to guide your cutting. It’s better to cut less than more, as you can always go back and cut more if needed.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain the health of your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide provided in this article, you can successfully cut your thick hair at home and achieve great results!
