How To Naturally Straighten Curly Hair Without Heat

Written by Christine Jun 06, 2023 Â· 12 min read

If you have curly hair, you might have tried many different methods to straighten your hair, including heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers. However, using heat on your hair can cause damage and breakage over time. If you're looking for more natural ways to straighten your curly hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through some simple steps to straighten your hair without using heat.

How to Naturally Straighten Curly Hair Without Heat

If you're looking to straighten your curly hair without heat, you can use some simple methods that won't damage your hair. Here are some natural ways to straighten curly hair:

  • Stretching your hair
  • Banding your hair
  • Twisting your hair
  • Braiding your hair
  • Roller-setting your hair

Each of these methods uses different techniques to help straighten your hair without heat. Let's take a closer look at each one.

Stretching Your Hair

Stretching your hair is a simple technique that can help straighten curly hair without heat. To stretch your hair, wash and condition it as you normally would, then gently comb it out. Next, divide your hair into sections and secure each section with a hair tie or clip. Finally, gently pull each section of hair taut, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the hair tied until it's dry. Once it's dry, you can remove the ties and style your hair as desired.

Banding Your Hair

Banding your hair is another effective way to straighten curly hair without heat. To band your hair, wash and condition it as you normally would, then gently comb it out. Next, divide your hair into sections and secure each section with a hair tie or clip. Finally, wrap a hair tie or elastic band around each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the hair tied until it's dry. Once it's dry, you can remove the bands and style your hair as desired.

Twisting Your Hair

Twisting your hair is a simple technique that can help straighten curly hair without heat. To twist your hair, wash and condition it as you normally would, then gently comb it out. Next, divide your hair into sections and secure each section with a hair tie or clip. Finally, twist each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the hair twisted until it's dry. Once it's dry, you can untwist the hair and style it as desired.

Braiding Your Hair

Braiding your hair is another effective way to straighten curly hair without heat. To braid your hair, wash and condition it as you normally would, then gently comb it out. Next, divide your hair into sections and secure each section with a hair tie or clip. Finally, braid each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Leave the hair braided until it's dry. Once it's dry, you can un-braid the hair and style it as desired.

Roller-Setting Your Hair

Roller-setting your hair is a classic technique that can help straighten curly hair without heat. To roller-set your hair, wash and condition it as you normally would, then gently comb it out. Next, divide your hair into sections and secure each section with a hair tie or clip. Finally, wrap each section of hair around a roller and secure it with a hair clip. Leave the rollers in until your hair is dry. Once it's dry, you can remove the rollers and style your hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide to Naturally Straighten Curly Hair Without Heat

Now that you know some different methods for naturally straightening curly hair, let's walk through the process step by step.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Use products that are designed for your hair type, whether that's dry, oily, or somewhere in between. Rinse your hair thoroughly to make sure all the product is out.

Step 2: Comb Out Your Hair

Next, gently comb out your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair ties, clips, or hair bands. This will make it easier to work with and help ensure that each section is straightened evenly.

Step 4: Choose Your Straightening Method

Choose the straightening method that works best for your hair type and the look you're trying to achieve. You can try stretching, banding, twisting, braiding, or roller-setting your hair.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Use your chosen straightening method to straighten each section of hair. Follow the instructions for the method you've chosen, and be sure to work carefully and gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 6: Leave Your Hair to Dry

Leave your hair tied, banded, twisted, braided, or roller-set until it's completely dry. This may take several hours or even overnight.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, remove the ties, bands, twists, braids, or rollers and style your hair as desired. You can use a brush or comb to smooth out any remaining kinks or bumps.

Tips to Keep Your Straightened Hair Healthy and Smooth

Here are some tips to help keep your straightened hair healthy and smooth:

  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools
  • Don't brush or comb your hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

FAQs About Naturally Straightening Curly Hair Without Heat

Is it possible to straighten curly hair without heat?

Yes, it is possible to straighten curly hair without heat. You can use methods like stretching, banding, twisting, braiding, or roller-setting to achieve a straighter look without damaging your hair.

How long will the straightening last?

The length of time that your straightened hair will last depends on your hair type, the straightening method you use, and how well you care for your hair. Generally, the straightening will last until your next wash day.

Can I straighten my hair without using any products?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without using any products. However, using a heat protectant spray or a deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Will straightening my curly hair damage it?

Straightening your hair can damage it over time, especially if you use heat styling tools like flat irons or blow dryers. However, using natural methods like stretching, banding, twisting, braiding, or roller-setting can help minimize damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Naturally Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for naturally curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once or twice a week to prevent dryness
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair
