How To Do An Undercut Pixie Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you ready to try out a bold and edgy new look? Consider getting an undercut pixie haircut! This trendy hairstyle has been popular for several years now and continues to be a favorite among fashion-forward individuals. In this article, we'll be giving you a step-by-step guide on how to do an undercut pixie haircut.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

An undercut pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that features shaved or closely cropped sides and back, with longer hair on top. This style is great for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and bold. It's also a perfect choice for those with fine hair, as the short layers can add volume and movement.

How to

The first step in doing an undercut pixie haircut is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair styling products such as pomade or styling wax.

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to achieve an undercut pixie haircut:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections. Use a comb to draw a horizontal line from temple to temple, and clip the top section of hair away from the bottom section.

Step 2: Clip the Sides and Back

Use the hair clippers to shave or closely crop the sides and back of the head. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the temples. Use a comb to blend the hair from the sides into the top section.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Unclip the top section of hair and use scissors to cut it to the desired length. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create layers and texture.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Apply hair styling products such as pomade or styling wax to the hair to add texture and hold. Use your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut pixie haircut:

  • Choose a length that works well with your face shape and hair texture.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the back of your head if needed.
  • Experiment with different hair styling products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercut pixie haircuts:

How often do I need to get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Can I still style my hair in different ways?

Yes, even though the haircut is short, there are still many ways to style it. You can try adding waves, curls, or braids to the top section of hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your undercut pixie haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Overall, an undercut pixie haircut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can suit many different face shapes and hair textures. With this step-by-step guide, you can achieve the perfect undercut pixie haircut in no time!
