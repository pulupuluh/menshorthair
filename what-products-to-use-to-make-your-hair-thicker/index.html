What Products To Use To Make Your Hair Thicker A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 14, 2023 · 10 min read

If you are one of those who have fine or thin hair, you know how challenging it is to achieve the voluminous look you desire. Fortunately, there are products that can help you achieve that thicker and fuller hair you’ve been dreaming of. In this article, we will guide you through the best products and methods to make your hair thicker.

Shampoo for Thicker Hair

The right shampoo can make a huge difference in the appearance of your hair. Look for a shampoo with ingredients that are known to add volume and thickness to hair, such as biotin, keratin, and collagen. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as they can strip away natural oils and make hair look flat and lifeless.

One of our top picks for a thickening shampoo is the OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo. It contains biotin and hydrolyzed wheat protein, which work together to strengthen and thicken hair strands. It’s also sulfate-free, so it won’t damage your hair.

Conditioner for Thicker Hair

Just like shampoo, the right conditioner can make a big difference in how thick and full your hair looks. Look for a conditioner that contains ingredients like keratin, collagen, and panthenol, which can help to strengthen and thicken hair strands. Avoid using heavy conditioners that can weigh your hair down.

The L’Oreal Elvive Fibrology Thickening Conditioner is an excellent choice for those looking to add volume and thickness to their hair. It contains filloxane, which penetrates the hair fiber and expands it from within, resulting in thicker-looking hair.

Hair Oil for Thicker Hair

Using a hair oil can help to nourish and strengthen your hair, making it look thicker and healthier. Look for a hair oil that contains ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, and castor oil, which are known to promote hair growth and thickness.

The Moroccanoil Treatment is a cult favorite among hair enthusiasts, and for good reason. It contains argan oil, which helps to nourish and strengthen hair, making it look thicker and healthier.

Hair Serum for Thicker Hair

A hair serum can help to coat your hair strands, making them appear thicker and fuller. Look for a serum that contains ingredients like keratin, collagen, and biotin, which can help to strengthen and thicken hair strands.

The John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse is an excellent choice for those looking to add volume and thickness to their hair. It contains a blend of keratin and biotin, which work together to strengthen and thicken hair strands, making hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Hair Mask for Thicker Hair

A hair mask can help to deeply nourish and strengthen your hair, making it look thicker and healthier. Look for a hair mask that contains ingredients like keratin, collagen, and biotin, which can help to strengthen and thicken hair strands.

The Briogeo Don’t Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask is an excellent choice for those looking to add volume and thickness to their hair. It contains a blend of biotin, keratin, and collagen, which work together to strengthen and thicken hair strands, making hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Scalp Treatment for Thicker Hair

A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth. Look for a scalp treatment that contains ingredients like tea tree oil and peppermint oil, which can help to stimulate hair growth and promote a healthy scalp.

The Paul Mitchell Tea Tree Hair and Scalp Treatment is an excellent choice for those looking to promote hair growth and a healthy scalp. It contains a blend of tea tree oil, peppermint oil, and lavender oil, which work together to soothe the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

Supplements for Thicker Hair

In addition to using hair care products, taking supplements can also help to promote thicker and healthier hair. Look for supplements that contain ingredients like biotin, collagen, and vitamin E, which are known to promote healthy hair growth.

The HairAnew Hair Growth Vitamins are an excellent choice for those looking to promote thicker and healthier hair. They contain a blend of biotin, collagen, and other vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth.

Diet for Thicker Hair

Your diet can also play a role in the health of your hair. Eating a diet that is rich in protein, healthy fats, and vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.

Some of the best foods for promoting healthy hair growth include salmon, avocado, nuts and seeds, eggs, and leafy greens.

Lifestyle Changes for Thicker Hair

In addition to using hair care products and taking supplements, making lifestyle changes can also help to promote thicker and healthier hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look thin and lifeless.

Try to reduce your stress levels, as stress can lead to hair loss and thinning. Getting enough sleep and exercise can also help to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

What causes thinning hair?

Thinning hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, age, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions.

Will using hair care products make my hair thicker immediately?

No, using hair care products alone will not make your hair thicker immediately. It takes time for hair to grow and become thicker and healthier.

How often should I use hair care products to make my hair thicker?

It depends on the specific product and your hair type. Generally, it’s best to follow the instructions on the packaging and use the product as directed.

In conclusion, achieving thicker and fuller hair takes a combination of using the right products, taking supplements, and making lifestyle changes. By following the tips and recommendations in this article, you can achieve the voluminous, healthy hair you’ve been dreaming of.
