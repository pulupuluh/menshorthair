How Long Does It Take To Slick Your Hair Back

Written by Petter Aug 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to rock a sleek, sophisticated look, slicking your hair back is a great option. But how long does it actually take to achieve this style? In this article, we'll break down the steps and provide some tips to help you get the perfect slick back in no time.

Description

Slicking your hair back involves combing your hair away from your face and towards the back of your head. This style works best for those with longer hair, but can be achieved with shorter hair as well. The key to a successful slick back is to use the right products and tools, and to take your time to ensure a smooth, sleek finish.

How To Slick Your Hair Back

Here are the steps to follow to achieve a slicked-back hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry. You don't want to slick back wet hair as it won't hold its shape.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Once your hair is mostly dry, apply a styling product such as pomade, gel, or wax. Start with a small amount and work it into your hair evenly, making sure to coat all of your strands. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair straight back from your forehead. Start at the hairline and work your way towards the back of your head. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay, and don't be afraid to use a little force to get it to stay in place.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once you've combed your hair back, use a light hold hairspray to set the style in place. Hold the can about 10-12 inches away from your head and spray all over your hair. This will help your hair stay in place throughout the day.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

To make the slicked-back look work for you, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a quality styling product that works well for your hair type.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to get a smooth, even finish.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to set the style in place.
  • If you have shorter hair, consider using a hair clay or paste for a more natural, textured look.

FAQ About Slicking Your Hair Back

Q: How long does it take to slick your hair back?

A: It typically takes about 5-10 minutes to slick your hair back, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: What products should I use to slick my hair back?

A: Pomade, gel, and wax are all good options for slicking your hair back. Choose a product that works well for your hair type and provides the level of hold you need.

Q: Can I slick back short hair?

A: Yes, you can slick back short hair, but you may need to use a different product such as a hair clay or paste to achieve the look.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

To keep your hair looking its best when slicked back, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a hair dryer or other heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to achieve a sleek, sophisticated look in no time. Whether you're heading to the office or out on the town, a slicked-back hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
