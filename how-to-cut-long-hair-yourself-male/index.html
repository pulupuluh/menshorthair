How To Cut Long Hair Yourself Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Feb 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can be a great look for men, but it can also be difficult to maintain. With barbershops closed due to the pandemic, many men are wondering how they can cut their own hair at home. This guide will help you learn how to cut long hair yourself male, without having to leave the house.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Long Hair Yourself Male

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have someone else nearby to help you.

The first step is to decide on the length you want your hair to be. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and then gradually cut more if you need to. It's easier to cut more off than it is to put hair back on!

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your long hair yourself:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see any uneven areas.
  2. Using your comb, separate your hair into sections. Start at the back and work your way forward.
  3. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to trim the ends of the hair, cutting off about 1/4 inch at a time.
  4. Continue this process with each section of hair, working your way around your head.
  5. When you get to the front of your head, be careful not to cut your bangs too short. Start by cutting a small amount and then gradually cut more if you need to.
  6. Once you've cut all of your hair, use your scissors to trim any uneven areas or stray hairs.
  7. Finally, comb your hair out and look in the mirror to make sure everything is even.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with dry hair. Wet hair can be harder to cut and can also lead to uneven results.
  • Take your time. Cutting your own hair can be a slow process, but it's better to take your time and get it right.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Have someone else help you. It can be difficult to cut the back of your head, so having someone else nearby to help can make the process easier.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's easier to cut more off later if you need to, but it's harder to put hair back on.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if you have curly hair. However, it can be more difficult to cut curly hair evenly, so be sure to take your time and use the right tools.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always cut more off later or visit a professional hair stylist to fix the mistake.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you cut your hair will depend on how fast it grows and how long you want it to be. Generally, it's a good idea to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, be sure to take care of it properly. This means washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut long hair yourself male, you can maintain your hairstyle from the comfort of your own home. With a little patience and practice, you'll be able to get the look you want without having to leave the house.
