How To Grow Your Hair Out For A Man Bun

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're looking to sport a trendy man bun, you'll need to grow your hair out long enough to achieve the look. While growing your hair out can be a challenge, it's not impossible. With a little patience and the right tips, you can start rocking a stylish man bun in no time. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about growing your hair out for a man bun.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Your Hair Out for a Man Bun
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Your Hair Out
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Grow Your Hair Out for a Man Bun

Growing your hair out for a man bun may seem daunting, but it's a fairly straightforward process. Here are the basic steps:

1. Decide on the Length

The first step in growing your hair out for a man bun is to decide on the length you want to achieve. Keep in mind that the longer your hair, the more maintenance it will require. If you're not used to having long hair, you may want to start with a shorter style and work your way up.

2. Be Patient

Growing your hair out takes time, so be patient. It's not uncommon for it to take several months or even a year to grow out your hair to the desired length. During this time, you may experience some awkward stages as your hair grows, but don't give up!

3. Avoid Haircuts

Avoid getting regular haircuts during the growing-out phase. While it may be tempting to trim your hair to get rid of split ends or to maintain a certain style, doing so will only delay the growing-out process.

4. Stay Healthy

Eating a healthy diet and getting enough sleep can help promote hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals, and stay hydrated.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to start growing out your hair for a man bun, follow these steps:

1. Stop Getting Regular Haircuts

As mentioned earlier, avoid getting haircuts during the growing-out phase. This will allow your hair to grow as long as possible.

2. Use Hair Products

Use hair products such as shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed to promote hair growth. Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, and other hair-healthy ingredients.

3. Experiment with Hairstyles

As your hair grows, experiment with different hairstyles to help you get through the awkward stages. Consider using hair ties, headbands, and other hair accessories to keep your hair out of your face.

4. Trim Your Hair When Necessary

While you should avoid getting regular haircuts, you may need to trim your hair occasionally to get rid of split ends or to maintain a certain style. Only trim your hair when absolutely necessary, and try to do so sparingly.

Tips for Growing Your Hair Out

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your hair for a man bun:

1. Be Patient

It can be frustrating waiting for your hair to grow, but be patient. The end result will be worth it.

2. Protect Your Hair

Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat, such as from blow dryers and flat irons. This can cause damage and slow down the growing-out process.

3. Get Regular Trims

While you should avoid getting regular haircuts, getting occasional trims can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

4. Use Hair Oil

Applying hair oil to your hair can help keep it moisturized and healthy. Look for natural oils such as coconut oil, jojoba oil, and argan oil.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow out hair for a man bun?

The amount of time it takes to grow out hair for a man bun can vary depending on the individual's hair type and length. On average, it can take anywhere from six months to a year or more.

Do I need to use special hair products to grow out my hair?

While you don't necessarily need to use special hair products to grow out your hair, using products that are designed to promote hair growth can help speed up the process. Look for products that contain biotin, keratin, and other hair-healthy ingredients.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Once you've grown out your hair for a man bun, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and cause damage.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

3. Don't Overstyle Your Hair

Avoid using excessive heat or styling products on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

4. Protect Your Hair

If you're going to be out in the sun, wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from damage.

Now that you know how to grow your hair out for a man bun, it's time to get started. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair properly, and you'll be sporting a stylish man bun in no time!
