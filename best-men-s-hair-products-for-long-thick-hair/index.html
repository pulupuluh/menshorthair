Best Men s Hair Products For Long Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Long and thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a challenge to manage. Finding the right hair products can make all the difference in keeping your mane healthy, shiny, and easy to style. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best men's hair products for long thick hair, including shampoos, conditioners, styling products, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Shampoos
  • Conditioners
  • Styling Products
  • Combs
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Shampoos

When it comes to long thick hair, using the right shampoo can make all the difference. Look for shampoos that are designed for your hair type, and avoid those that contain harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Some of the best shampoos for long thick hair include:

  • Redken High Rise Volume Lifting Shampoo
  • Aveda Invati Advanced Thickening Shampoo
  • SheaMoisture Jamaican Black Castor Oil Strengthen & Restore Shampoo
  • Briogeo Blossom & Bloom Ginseng + Biotin Volumizing Shampoo

Conditioners

Conditioners are essential for keeping long thick hair healthy, hydrated, and easy to manage. Look for conditioners that contain nourishing ingredients like argan oil, keratin, and vitamin E. Some of the best conditioners for long thick hair include:

  • It's a 10 Miracle Deep Conditioner Plus Keratin
  • TRESemmé Moisture Rich Conditioner
  • Paul Mitchell The Detangler
  • Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil Conditioner

Styling Products

Styling long thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right products, it can be a breeze. Look for styling products that provide hold and control without weighing your hair down. Some of the best styling products for long thick hair include:

  • Redken Rough Paste 12 Texturizing Hair Paste
  • American Crew Fiber
  • Oribe Matte Waves Texture Lotion
  • Schwarzkopf Osis+ Dust It Mattifying Powder

Combs

Using the right comb can also make a big difference in managing long thick hair. Look for combs with wide teeth that won't snag or pull your hair. Some of the best combs for long thick hair include:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Paddle brush
  • Detangling brush
  • Round brush

Hair Care Tips

Using the right hair products is just one part of keeping your long thick hair healthy and manageable. Here are some additional tips for taking care of your mane:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to prevent tangles.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as omega-3 fatty acids, biotin, and vitamin E.

FAQs

Q: Can I use the same shampoo and conditioner for my long thick hair?

A: It's best to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that are formulated to provide hydration and nourishment for long thick hair.

Q: How often should I wash my long thick hair?

A: It's recommended that you wash your hair no more than twice a week, as frequent washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Q: How can I prevent my long thick hair from getting tangled?

A: Brush your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up. You can also use a detangling spray or conditioner to help prevent tangles.

With the right hair products and care, you can keep your long thick hair looking healthy, shiny, and stylish. Try out some of the products and tips in this guide to find what works best for you!
