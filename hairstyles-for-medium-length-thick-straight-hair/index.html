Hairstyles For Medium Length Thick Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have medium length thick straight hair, you know that it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that works for you. Luckily, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your hair type and make you look fabulous. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for medium length thick straight hair that you can try.

Description

Medium length thick straight hair is a versatile hair type that can be styled in many different ways. It has a lot of natural volume, which can make it difficult to manage at times. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can make your hair look beautiful and stylish.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your medium length thick straight hair is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes require different hairstyles to flatter them. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will work for you.

One of the best hairstyles for medium length thick straight hair is a layered haircut. Layers can help to add movement and texture to your hair, making it easier to manage. You can also add some side-swept bangs to soften your look and frame your face.

Step by Step

If you want to create a layered haircut for your medium length thick straight hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  3. Part your hair in the middle or to one side.
  4. Use scissors or a razor to create layers throughout your hair. Start at the back and work your way forward. Make sure to blend the layers into each other for a natural look.
  5. Trim your bangs to the desired length and shape.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length thick straight hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and lift.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or straightener to help you achieve a sleek and polished look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find the ones that work best for you.

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for medium length thick straight hair?

Some of the best haircuts for medium length thick straight hair include layered haircuts, blunt cuts, and long bobs.

How can I add volume to my medium length thick straight hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using a root-lifting spray.

Can I wear my medium length thick straight hair in an updo?

Absolutely! You can wear your hair in a variety of updos, including ponytails, buns, and braids.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length thick straight hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and hairstyles for medium length thick straight hair, you can rock your hair and feel confident every day.
