How To Cut Men s Hair Bald On Top With Scissors Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are looking for a new hairstyle or want to save money by cutting your own hair, cutting men's hair bald on top with scissors is a great option. This style is trendy and easy to maintain. In this guide, we will show you how to achieve this look step-by-step, including tips on how to care for your hair afterward.

Description

Bald on top hairstyles have been popular for many years, and they continue to be a trendy option for men. This style involves shaving the top of your head, leaving the sides and back of your head longer. The result is a bold, edgy look that is easy to maintain. Cutting your own hair bald on top with scissors is a great way to save money and get the look you want.

How to Cut Men's Hair Bald on Top with Scissors

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, hair clippers, and a comb. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Use your hair clippers to cut the sides and back of your head to the desired length. You can use different clipper guards to achieve different lengths. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you get the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. If you want a shorter look, cut the hair to about 1/4 inch. If you want a longer look, cut the hair to about 1/2 inch.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair on top of your head with the sides and back using the scissors. This will create a seamless, natural look.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

Trim the edges of your hairline and sideburns with your scissors. This will give your haircut a clean, polished look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a great bald on top hairstyle:

  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut your hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut a little at a time than to cut too much at once.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you need it.
  • Use hair products to style your hair after you have cut it.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair bald on top with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair bald on top with scissors. It may take some practice, but it is doable.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair bald on top with scissors?

You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, hair clippers, and a comb.

How often do I need to cut my hair bald on top?

You should aim to cut your hair bald on top every 2-3 weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care

After you have cut your hair bald on top with scissors, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a moisturizing hair product to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your bald on top hairstyle for longer.
