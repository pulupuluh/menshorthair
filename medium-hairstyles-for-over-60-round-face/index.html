Medium Hairstyles For Over 60 Round Face A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Luffy May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a hairstyle that flatters your round face shape? Look no further than medium length haircuts! From layered bobs to shoulder-length waves, there are plenty of options to choose from that will enhance your natural beauty. Read on to discover our top picks for medium hairstyles for over 60 round face.

Description

As we age, our faces tend to lose volume and become more rounded. This can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements our features without adding unnecessary bulk. Medium length haircuts are an excellent choice for women over 60 with round faces, as they provide a balance between length and volume.

How to

To achieve the perfect medium hairstyle for your round face, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will flatter your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a length that falls between your chin and shoulders to create the illusion of a longer, more slender face.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear wider, and opt for layers instead.
  4. Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.
  5. Use volumizing products to add body to your hair without weighing it down.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving one of our favorite medium hairstyles for over 60 round face:

Layered Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off a small portion of hair at the nape of your neck.
  3. Using a razor, create long, wispy layers throughout your hair, working from the bottom up.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium hairstyle for over 60 round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate a round face shape.
  • Choose a cut that complements your hair texture, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.
  • Experiment with different partings to see which one flatters your face shape the most.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium hairstyles for over 60 round face:

What is the best haircut for a round face over 60?

The best haircut for a round face over 60 is a shoulder-length cut with layers. This style adds volume and movement to the hair without overwhelming the face.

Can I wear bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a round face! Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy, textured bangs that graze the eyebrows.

Should I choose a center or side part for my hairstyle?

It depends on your face shape and personal preference. A center part can elongate a round face, while a side part can add asymmetry and balance.

Hair Care

To maintain your medium hairstyle for over 60 round face, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you'll be able to find the perfect medium hairstyle for your round face shape and feel confident and beautiful every day.
