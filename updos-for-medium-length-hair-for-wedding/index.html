How to Create Updos for Medium Length Hair for Wedding

Before we get into the details of creating the perfect updo for your wedding day, let's take a look at some of the best styles for medium length hair:

  • Low Bun
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Braided Updo
  • Chignon
  • Side Swept Updo

Now, let's dive into the steps for creating these styles.

Low Bun

The low bun is a classic and elegant style that works well for medium length hair. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and parting it in the middle.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. For a more polished look, you can wrap a strand of hair around the base of the bun to cover the bobby pins.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a great way to show off your hair while keeping it out of your face. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and parting it in the middle.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  3. You can leave the bottom half of your hair straight or add curls for a more romantic look.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braided Updo

The braided updo is a beautiful and intricate style that works well for medium length hair. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and parting it in the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and begin braiding it.
  3. Continue braiding until you reach the other side of your head, then secure the braid with a hair tie.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Gather the two braids at the back of your head and twist them into a bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Chignon

The chignon is a classic French style that is perfect for a formal wedding. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and gathering it at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. For a more polished look, you can wrap a strand of hair around the base of the bun to cover the bobby pins.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side Swept Updo

The side swept updo is a romantic and feminine style that works well for medium length hair. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and parting it on one side.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Sweep the front section of your hair to the side and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. For a more polished look, you can wrap a strand of hair around the base of the bun to cover the bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know the best styles for medium length hair, let's take a look at the step-by-step instructions for creating an updo:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and parting it in the desired style.
  2. Gather your hair and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add curls or straighten your hair, depending on the style you want to achieve.
  4. Begin twisting or braiding your hair, depending on the style you want to achieve.
  5. Secure your hair with bobby pins and hairspray as needed.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Updo

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect updo:

  • Start with clean, dry hair that is free of tangles.
  • Use a high-quality hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Consider using hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles before your wedding day to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a stylist or friend.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedding hairstyles:

What is the best hairstyle for a wedding?

The best hairstyle for a wedding depends on your personal style and the formality of the event. Updos are a popular choice for weddings, as they are elegant and timeless.

How do I choose the right wedding hairstyle?

When choosing a wedding hairstyle, consider your dress, your face shape, and your personal style. It's also important to choose a style that will hold up throughout the day and night.

Should I do my own hair for my wedding?

While it is possible to do your own hair for your wedding, it can be a stressful and time-consuming process. Consider hiring a professional stylist to ensure that your hair looks perfect on your special day.

Hair Care Advice for Your Wedding Day

Here are some hair care tips to help you prepare for your wedding day:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask to nourish your hair before your wedding day.
  • On the day of your wedding, use a light hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much to keep your style in place.

Congratulations on your upcoming wedding! We hope this article has provided you with the information you need to create the perfect updo for your medium length hair. Remember to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day, and don't be afraid to ask for help from a professional stylist or friend. Best wishes for a beautiful wedding day!
