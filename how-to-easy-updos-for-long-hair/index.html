How To Easy Updos For Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long hair down and want to try something new? Updos are a great option for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or just a casual day out. But the thought of styling your hair into an updo can be overwhelming, especially if you're not a hair expert. In this article, we'll show you how to easy updos for long hair, step-by-step. So, grab your bobby pins and let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Easy Updos for Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Easy Updos
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Updos

How to Easy Updos for Long Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the different types of updos for long hair:

  • Bun
  • Braid
  • Twist
  • Ponytail

Each of these updos can be customized to fit your personal style and the occasion. Now, let's get into the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Prep your hair

Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a heat protectant if you plan on using heat tools. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

2. Choose your updo

Decide which updo you want to create. For a bun, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it into a bun. For a braid, divide your hair into three sections and braid it. For a twist, divide your hair into two sections and twist them together. For a ponytail, gather your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.

3. Secure your updo

Use bobby pins to secure your updo in place. For a bun, twist the hair around the base of the ponytail and pin it in place. For a braid or twist, use bobby pins to secure the ends of the hair. For a ponytail, wrap a small section of hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it with a bobby pin.

4. Finish with hairspray

Spritz your updo with hairspray to hold it in place. Use a light hold hairspray to avoid a stiff look.

Tips for Easy Updos:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different styles
  • Use hair accessories, such as headbands or hairpins, to add interest to your updo
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't get discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect

FAQs:

1. Can I create an updo with dirty hair?

Absolutely! In fact, dirty hair can be easier to work with because it has more texture and grip. Just make sure to apply dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

2. How long does it take to create an updo?

It depends on the style and your skill level. Some updos can be created in just a few minutes, while others may take longer. Give yourself plenty of time to practice before the event.

3. Can I create an updo with short hair?

Yes, there are plenty of updos that work for short hair. Look for styles that incorporate braids or twists, or try a messy bun.

Hair Care for Updos:

Updos can put stress on your hair, so it's important to take care of your locks before and after styling. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant if you plan on using heat tools
  • Avoid excessive brushing or teasing, which can cause damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight, which can cause breakage

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create easy updos for long hair in no time!
