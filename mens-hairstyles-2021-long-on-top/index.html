Mens Hairstyles 2021 Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hairstyles have been trending for the past few years, and it looks like they are here to stay for 2021. In this article, we will be discussing mens hairstyles 2021 long on top. These hairstyles are versatile, trendy, and suitable for all face shapes and hair types. Whether you have thick, thin, curly, or straight hair, there is a long hairstyle that will suit you.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The long on top hairstyle for men is a versatile and trendy look that can be customized to suit individual preferences. It typically involves having longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style can be worn with a variety of hair textures and can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to

To achieve the long on top hairstyle, you will need to have at least 4-6 inches of hair on top of your head. The sides and back can be shorter, depending on your preference. You can either go to a professional hairstylist or attempt to cut your hair at home.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve the long on top hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry.
  2. Blow-dry your hair on the top of your head, using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to the top of your hair, and work it in with your fingers.
  4. Style your hair, using a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long on top hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that will hold your hair in place without making it look greasy or weighed down.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy look or a slicked-back style, to find what suits you best.
  • Consider getting regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Be patient with your hair as it grows out, and use hair accessories such as headbands or hair ties to keep it out of your face during the awkward in-between stages.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people can go several days without washing their hair, while others need to wash it daily. Experiment to find what works best for you.

What styling product should I use?

It depends on your hair type and the desired look. Pomade is good for a slicked-back style, while wax is better for a messy, textured look. Gel is great for holding hair in place, but it can make hair look stiff and unnatural.

Can I still wear a hat with a long on top hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this hairstyle. However, you may need to adjust the placement of the hat to avoid flattening your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long on top hairstyle looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can rock the long on top hairstyle with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
