How To Change Your Curl Pattern Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy May 29, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’ve ever wanted to change your natural curl pattern, you’re not alone. Many people are looking for ways to switch up their style and try something new. Changing your curl pattern can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it’s definitely achievable. In this post, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for changing your curl pattern.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Change Your Curl Pattern
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Changing Your Curl Pattern
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Change Your Curl Pattern

Before we dive into the techniques for changing your curl pattern, it’s important to understand what exactly curl pattern is. Your curl pattern is determined by the shape of your hair follicle. People with straight hair have round hair follicles, while those with curly hair have oval-shaped follicles. There are three main types of curl patterns: type 2 (wavy), type 3 (curly), and type 4 (coily/kinky).

If you’re looking to change your curl pattern, there are a few methods you can try:

Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments, such as relaxers and texturizers, are the most common way to change your curl pattern. Relaxers work by breaking the bonds in your hair that give it its curl pattern. Texturizers, on the other hand, are milder versions of relaxers that loosen your curl pattern without completely straightening it.

Heat Styling

Heat styling, such as using a flat iron or curling iron, can also temporarily change your curl pattern. However, excessive heat can damage your hair, so it’s important to use heat protectant products and not overdo it.

Braids and Twists

Braids and twists can also change your curl pattern. When you take them out, your hair may have a new, textured look. However, this method can be time-consuming and may not work for all hair types.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’ve decided to try a chemical treatment to change your curl pattern, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the right product for your hair type. If you’re not sure, consult a professional stylist.
  2. Do a patch test to make sure you’re not allergic to the product.
  3. Follow the instructions on the product carefully. Make sure to wear gloves, as the chemicals can be harsh on your skin.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly and follow up with a deep conditioner.
  5. Be gentle with your hair for the next few days. Avoid heat styling and tight hairstyles.

Tips for Changing Your Curl Pattern

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your curl pattern:

  • Consult a professional stylist before trying any chemical treatments.
  • Use products specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair.
  • Be patient – it may take a few tries to achieve your desired curl pattern.
  • Be gentle with your hair, especially after a chemical treatment.

FAQs

Can I change my curl pattern without chemicals?

While chemical treatments are the most common way to change your curl pattern, you can also try heat styling or braids/twists. However, these methods may not be as effective as chemical treatments.

Will a relaxer completely straighten my hair?

A relaxer will break the bonds in your hair that give it its curl pattern, but it may not completely straighten your hair. The amount of straightening will depend on the strength of the relaxer and the texture of your hair.

How often should I get a chemical treatment?

It’s best to consult a professional stylist for this question, as it will depend on your hair type and the strength of the product. Generally, chemical treatments should be done no more than every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

After changing your curl pattern, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather.

Changing your curl pattern can be a fun way to switch up your style. Whether you choose to try a chemical treatment or use heat styling, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
