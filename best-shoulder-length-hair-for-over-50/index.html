Best Shoulder Length Hair For Over 50 A Guide

Written by Aprilia May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair changes just like everything else. Our once luscious locks may become thinner and more fragile, making it harder to style. However, that doesn't mean you can't still look fabulous with the right hairstyle! If you're over 50 and looking for a new 'do, consider a shoulder length haircut. Here's everything you need to know about the best shoulder length hair for over 50.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile option that can suit almost any face shape and hair type. It's long enough to create different styles, but not so long that it becomes difficult to manage. For women over 50, shoulder length hair is perfect for maintaining a youthful appearance without looking like they're trying too hard.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a shoulder length haircut, the first step is to find a style that suits you. Consider your face shape and hair type, as well as your personal style. Do you prefer a sleek and polished look, or something more relaxed and tousled? Once you've found a style you like, it's time to visit the salon.

When you're at the salon, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist. Let them know what you like and don't like, and ask for their professional opinion. They can help you choose the best length and style for your hair, and can also recommend products to help you maintain your new 'do.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the best shoulder length hair for over 50:

  1. Find a style that suits you
  2. Visit the salon and communicate clearly with your stylist
  3. Consider using products to help maintain your new hairstyle
  4. Enjoy your new, fabulous look!

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new shoulder length hairstyle:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Consider using a volumizing product to help add body to thinning hair
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair for women over 50:

What face shapes suit shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair can suit almost any face shape, but it's especially flattering on those with heart-shaped or oval faces.

Can shoulder length hair be styled in different ways?

Yes, shoulder length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curled, in an updo, or with bangs.

Will shoulder length hair make me look younger?

Shoulder length hair can help to create a more youthful appearance, especially if you choose a style that's soft and feminine.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important no matter what your age. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week

With the right care and attention, you can have beautiful, healthy hair at any age.
