Tapering Hair Meaning What You Need To Know

Written by Eveline Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's stylish and low-maintenance, you might be interested in tapering your hair. Tapering is a popular hairstyle for men and women that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. In this article, we'll explain what tapering hair means, how to get a taper haircut, and provide some tips on how to care for your tapered hair.

What is Tapering Hair?

Tapering hair is a hairstyle that gradually changes in length from the top of the head to the nape of the neck. This style is achieved by cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. Tapering is often used to create a clean, polished look that's suitable for any occasion.

There are several different types of taper haircuts, including the high taper, low taper, and skin fade. The high taper involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The low taper is similar to the high taper, but the hair is cut shorter towards the nape of the neck. The skin fade is a more extreme version of the taper, where the hair is cut very short on the sides and back, gradually fading into the skin.

How to Get a Taper Haircut

If you're interested in getting a taper haircut, the first step is to find a good hairstylist or barber. Look for someone who has experience with tapering hair and can help you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type.

During your appointment, your hairstylist will start by washing and drying your hair. They will then use clippers to cut the hair short on the sides and back, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. The length of the taper will depend on your personal preference, but most people opt for a gradual fade from short to long.

Once the taper is complete, your hairstylist will trim the hair on top to the desired length and style it according to your preferences. They may also use a razor to clean up the edges and create a more polished look.

Tips for Tapered Hair

If you're considering a taper haircut, there are a few things you should keep in mind to ensure your hair looks its best:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your taper and keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good hair styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help you create different looks with your taper.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a taper haircut last?

A taper haircut should last between 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Can women get a taper haircut?

Absolutely! Taper haircuts are suitable for both men and women, and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type.

Is a taper haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, taper haircuts are relatively low-maintenance and easy to style. However, you will need to visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your taper looking fresh.

Hair Care for Tapered Hair

If you want to keep your tapered hair looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and product buildup.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it too vigorously.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance taper haircut that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're a man or a woman, tapering your hair is a great way to switch up your look and show off your personal style.
