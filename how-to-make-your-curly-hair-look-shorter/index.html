How To Make Your Curly Hair Look Shorter

Written by William Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have curly hair that always seems to look longer than you want it to? Whether you're looking for a shorter look or just want to try something new, there are plenty of ways to make your curls appear shorter without actually cutting them off. Here's how to make your curly hair look shorter, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Shorter

How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Shorter

There are a few different techniques you can try to make your curly hair look shorter:

  • Layering
  • Diffusing
  • Pulling the curls apart
  • Using the right products

Let's take a closer look at each of these techniques.

Layering

One of the easiest ways to make your curly hair look shorter is to get it layered. This will add some texture and volume to your curls, which will make them appear shorter. Talk to your hairstylist about the best type of layering for your hair type and face shape.

Diffusing

Diffusing your hair is another great way to make your curls appear shorter. After washing your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls too much. This will help your curls keep their shape and appear shorter.

Pulling the Curls Apart

If you want to make your curls look even shorter, try pulling them apart. After diffusing your hair, use your fingers to gently pull the curls apart. This will make them look more voluminous and less long.

Using the Right Products

The right products can also help make your curly hair look shorter. Look for products that add volume and texture, like mousse or curl cream. Apply these products to your hair before diffusing or pulling your curls apart.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your curly hair look shorter:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing or texturizing product to your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls too much.
  4. Use your fingers to gently pull your curls apart, making them look more voluminous.
  5. If desired, get your hair layered by a professional hairstylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making your curly hair look shorter:

  • Be gentle with your curls. Curly hair is delicate and can easily become damaged if handled too roughly.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often. Heat can also damage curly hair, so try to limit your use of blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner. This will help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Consider using a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Regular towels can cause frizz and damage to curly hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly hair look shorter:

Can I make my curly hair look shorter without cutting it?

Yes! You can try layering, diffusing, pulling your curls apart, and using the right products to make your curly hair appear shorter without actually cutting it.

Will layering my curly hair make it frizzy?

Not necessarily. If you get your hair layered by a professional hairstylist who knows how to work with curly hair, your curls should still look smooth and defined.

Hair Care

Here are some general tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your curls
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy

With these tips and techniques, you can make your curly hair look shorter and try out a new hairstyle without actually cutting your hair. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products to see what works best for you!
