How To Look Good With Round Glasses

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are someone who wears glasses, you know how important it is to find the right pair that not only corrects your vision but also complements your face shape and style. In recent years, round glasses have become a popular choice for both men and women, but not everyone knows how to wear them to look their best. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to look good with round glasses.

Description

Round glasses are a classic and timeless style that can suit any face shape. They are characterized by a circular frame that is equal in width and height. Round glasses are often associated with intellectuals, artists, and free spirits. They can make a statement and add a touch of quirkiness to your look.

How to Wear Round Glasses

Here are some tips on how to wear round glasses:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing round glasses, it is essential to consider your face shape. Round glasses can look good on any face shape, but you need to find the right size and proportion to balance your features. If you have a round face, choose a smaller frame to avoid making your face look rounder. If you have a square or angular face, choose a larger frame to soften your features.

Choose the Right Color

The color of your glasses can make a big difference in how they look on you. If you have a warm skin tone, choose glasses with warm colors like brown, gold, or tortoise. If you have a cool skin tone, choose glasses with cool colors like black, silver, or blue.

Match Your Style

Your glasses should match your personal style and the clothes you wear. If you have a classic style, choose round glasses with a thin metal frame. If you have a more eclectic style, choose round glasses with a thicker plastic frame or a unique shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to look good with round glasses:

Step 1: Choose the Right Size

Choose glasses that are proportional to your face. If you have a smaller face, choose a smaller frame, and if you have a larger face, choose a larger frame.

Step 2: Choose the Right Color

Choose glasses that complement your skin tone and hair color. If you have warm undertones, choose warm colors like brown or gold. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colors like black or silver.

Step 3: Match Your Style

Choose glasses that match your personal style and the clothes you wear. If you have a classic style, choose round glasses with a thin metal frame. If you have a more eclectic style, choose round glasses with a thicker plastic frame or a unique shape.

Step 4: Consider Your Hairstyle

Choose glasses that complement your hairstyle. If you have short hair, choose glasses that are small and lightweight. If you have long hair, choose glasses that are larger and more substantial.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to look good with round glasses:

Tip 1: Don't Be Afraid to Make a Statement

Round glasses can make a statement and add a touch of quirkiness to your look. Don't be afraid to choose glasses with a unique shape or color.

Tip 2: Keep Your Makeup Simple

When wearing glasses, keep your makeup simple and natural. Focus on your eyes and choose neutral shades that complement your glasses.

Tip 3: Clean Your Glasses Regularly

Keep your glasses clean and free of smudges. Use a microfiber cloth and cleaning solution to remove dirt and oil from your lenses.

FAQ

What Face Shape Looks Best with Round Glasses?

Round glasses can look good on any face shape, but it is essential to find the right size and proportion to balance your features. If you have a round face, choose a smaller frame to avoid making your face look rounder. If you have a square or angular face, choose a larger frame to soften your features.

Can I Wear Round Glasses with Any Outfit?

Yes, you can wear round glasses with any outfit, but it is essential to choose glasses that match your personal style and the clothes you wear. If you have a classic style, choose round glasses with a thin metal frame. If you have a more eclectic style, choose round glasses with a thicker plastic frame or a unique shape.

Hair Care

When wearing glasses, it is essential to keep your hair clean and styled. If you have long hair, try wearing it in a ponytail or bun to keep it out of the way. If you have short hair, use a texturizing product to add volume and dimension.

By following these tips and tricks, you can look good with round glasses and make a statement with your eyewear. Remember to choose glasses that complement your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. With the right pair of glasses, you can enhance your look and feel confident in your appearance.
