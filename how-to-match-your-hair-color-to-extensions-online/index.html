How To Match Your Hair Color To Extensions Online

Written by Robby May 12, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to hair extensions, one of the most important things to consider is your hair color. You want your extensions to blend seamlessly with your natural hair color, so it’s important to know how to match your hair color to extensions online. Here, we’ll walk you through the process step by step and provide some helpful tips along the way.

How to

Step 1: Determine your hair color

The first step in matching your hair color to extensions online is to determine your current hair color. This may seem obvious, but it’s important to get an accurate assessment of your hair color before selecting extensions. You can do this by looking at your hair in natural lighting and comparing it to color swatches online.

Step 2: Choose the right shade

Once you’ve determined your hair color, it’s time to select the right shade of extensions. Look for extensions that are labeled with your hair color or a shade that is close to your natural hair color. It’s also important to consider the undertones of your hair color. For example, if your hair has warm undertones, look for extensions with warm undertones as well.

Step 3: Consider the texture

In addition to color, you’ll also want to consider the texture of your hair and the extensions. If your hair is straight, look for straight extensions. If your hair is curly, look for curly extensions. This will help your extensions blend seamlessly with your natural hair.

Step 4: Order a swatch

If you’re still unsure about which shade of extensions to choose, consider ordering a swatch. Many online retailers offer swatches that you can order for a small fee. This will give you a better idea of what the extensions will look like against your natural hair color.

Step 5: Consult with a professional

If you’re still having trouble matching your hair color to extensions online, consider consulting with a professional. A hair stylist or colorist can help you find the perfect shade of extensions and provide tips on how to care for them.

Tips

- When in doubt, go slightly darker rather than lighter. It’s easier to darken extensions than it is to lighten them.

- Don’t be afraid to mix and match shades to create a custom color.

- Consider the length of the extensions when selecting a shade. If you’re only adding a few inches of length, you may be able to get away with a slightly different shade than your natural hair color.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if the extensions will match my hair color?

A: Look for extensions that are labeled with your hair color or a shade that is close to your natural hair color. You can also order a swatch to get a better idea of what the extensions will look like against your natural hair color.

Q: What if I can’t find a shade that matches my hair color?

A: Consider mixing and matching shades to create a custom color. You can also consult with a professional for advice.

Q: Can I dye my extensions to match my hair color?

A: It’s generally not recommended to dye extensions, as they can become damaged and lose their quality. If you’re having trouble finding a shade that matches your hair color, consider consulting with a professional for advice.

Hair Care

Once you’ve found the perfect shade of extensions, it’s important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are a few tips for caring for your extensions:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling.

- Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions.

- Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

By following these tips and selecting the right shade of extensions, you can achieve a seamless, natural look that will have everyone wondering how you got such amazing hair!
