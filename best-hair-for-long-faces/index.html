Best Hair for Long Faces in 2023 - Tips, Styles, and Care

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your long face shape? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best hair for long faces, how to style it, and tips for hair care to keep your locks looking fabulous. Read on to discover your next signature style!

Table of Contents

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, elongated chin, and narrow cheekbones. The best haircuts for long faces are those that add width to the face and create an illusion of balance. Hairstyles that fall below the shoulders, with layers, waves, or curls, are great for adding volume and texture.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Long Faces

When choosing the best hair for long faces, consider the following:

  • Length: Haircuts that fall below the shoulders work best for long faces.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, creating the illusion of width on the face.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can also help to balance out a long face shape.
  • Curls and Waves: Adding curls or waves to long hair can also create volume and width, making it a great option for long faces.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Your Long Face Hair

To style your long face hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.
  4. If you have naturally straight hair, use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls to your hair.
  5. If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, enhance your natural texture using a curl-defining product.
  6. Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Face Hair

To keep your long face hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing: Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair for long faces:

  • What is the best haircut for a long face? Haircuts that fall below the shoulders with layers, waves, or curls are great for long faces.
  • What kind of bangs are best for a long face? Side-swept or curtain bangs are great for balancing out a long face shape.
  • Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face? Yes, but adding waves or curls can create volume and width, which can be flattering for a long face shape.

Hair Care for Long Face Hair

To keep your long face hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner: Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often: Overusing hot tools can damage your hair, so use them sparingly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Use a hat or a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Now that you know the best hair for long faces, how to style it, and how to care for it, you can rock your long face shape with confidence. Whether you prefer long, wavy locks or a cute bob, there's a style out there that's perfect for you. So go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles until you find your perfect match!
