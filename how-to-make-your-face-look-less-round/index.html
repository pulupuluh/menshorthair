How To Make Your Face Look Less Round

Written by Eveline Apr 06, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may feel self-conscious about your appearance. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your face look less round that don't involve surgery or expensive treatments. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve a slimmer, more contoured look through simple changes to your hair and makeup routine. Read on for our step-by-step guide.

Description

Having a round face is a common concern for many people. A round face can make your features look less defined and can even make you look heavier than you actually are. However, there are several ways to make your face look less round without resorting to drastic measures. By making changes to your hairstyle, makeup, and overall style, you can achieve a more slim and contoured look that will boost your confidence and help you feel great about your appearance.

How to Make Your Face Look Less Round

There are several ways to make your face look less round, including:

  • Choosing the right hairstyle
  • Contouring with makeup
  • Wearing the right accessories
  • Choosing the right clothing
  • Maintaining a healthy weight

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in the appearance of your face. When choosing a hairstyle, look for styles that elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin
  • Choose styles with layers that start at your cheekbones or below
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or side part
  • Avoid volume at the sides of your face
  • Consider a long bob or shoulder-length cut

Step 2: Contour with Makeup

Contouring your face with makeup can help create the illusion of a slimmer, more defined face shape. Here are some tips for contouring:

  • Choose a contour shade that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone
  • Apply the contour shade to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline
  • Blend well with a brush or sponge
  • Use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone

Step 3: Wear the Right Accessories

The right accessories can help draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Here are some tips:

  • Wear earrings that are long and dangly
  • Avoid round or hoop-shaped earrings
  • Wear necklaces that are long and slimming
  • Avoid chokers or necklaces that sit at your collarbone

Step 4: Choose the Right Clothing

The right clothing can help create a more slim and contoured look. Here are some tips:

  • Choose tops that are fitted at the waist and flare out at the bottom
  • Avoid tops that are too loose or boxy
  • Wear high-waisted pants or skirts
  • Avoid low-rise pants or skirts that sit at your hips

Step 5: Maintain a Healthy Weight

Maintaining a healthy weight can help prevent excess fat from accumulating in your face. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid sugary, processed foods

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your face look less round:

  • Choose glasses with rectangular or square frames
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your face, such as pigtails or buns
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Use a bright lipstick color to draw attention to your lips
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too slicked back

FAQ

Q: Can I make my face look less round without makeup?

A: Yes! Choosing the right hairstyle, accessories, and clothing can all help make your face look less round. Maintaining a healthy weight is also important.

Q: Will losing weight make my face look less round?

A: Yes, losing weight can help prevent excess fat from accumulating in your face, which can make it look less round.

Q: What kind of makeup should I use to contour my face?

A: Use a contour shade that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone. You can use a powder or cream contour product.

Hair Care

Choosing the right hairstyle is an important part of making your face look less round. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight when styling
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can make your face look less round and feel more confident about your appearance. Remember, there's no need to resort to drastic measures – simple changes to your hair and makeup routine can make a big difference.
