How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair Comb Over A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A comb over hairstyle has been popular among men for decades. It is a classic style that never goes out of fashion. However, to achieve the perfect comb over, you need to know how to cut the top of men's hair. In this article, we will discuss step-by-step instructions on how to cut the top of men's hair comb over.

Description

A comb over hairstyle requires short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. The hair on top is combed over to one side to create a sleek and elegant look. To cut the top of men's hair comb over, you need to have the right tools and techniques.

How to Cut Top of Men's Hair Comb Over

Step 1: Gather the Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clippers. The scissors should be sharp and have a pointed tip for precision cutting. The hair clippers should have adjustable blades to create different lengths of hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash and dry the hair before you start cutting. Wet hair is easier to work with, and it will give you a better idea of the final look. Make sure to towel dry the hair and comb it straight before cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair on top with scissors. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut it to the desired length. Start at the front and work your way back. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as it can ruin the final look.

Step 4: Use Clippers

Once you have cut the top of the hair with scissors, use the clippers to trim the sides and the back. Use a longer guard for the top and a shorter guard for the sides and the back. Blend the hair on top with the sides and the back for a seamless look.

Step 5: Create the Part

Create the part by combing the hair to one side. Use the comb to create a clean and sharp part. Make sure the part is straight and aligned with the face.

Step 6: Final Touches

Once you have cut the hair, make sure to clean up any stray hairs. Use the clippers to trim any hair around the ears and the neckline. Use the scissors to trim any hair that is longer than the rest.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to check the final look from all angles.
  • Be patient and take your time while cutting the hair.
  • Ask for help if you are not confident in cutting your hair.

FAQ

1. How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

2. Can I cut my hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your hair yourself, but it is recommended to ask for help if you are not confident.

3. How do I maintain the comb over hairstyle?

You can maintain the comb over hairstyle by using a styling product, such as hair gel or pomade. Comb the hair to one side and apply the product evenly.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure to wash and condition it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage the hair. Protect your hair from the sun and the wind by wearing a hat or a cap.

Cutting the top of men's hair comb over requires patience and precision. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect comb over hairstyle. Follow the step-by-step instructions in this article to get the perfect look.
