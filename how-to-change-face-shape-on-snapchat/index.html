How To Change Face Shape On Snapchat

Are you tired of your natural face shape and want to experiment with a different look? Snapchat has a feature that allows you to change your face shape in just a few clicks. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to change face shape on Snapchat.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Face Shape on Snapchat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Face Shape on Snapchat
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Snapchat Selfies

How to Change Face Shape on Snapchat

To get started, open Snapchat and make sure the front-facing camera is on. Swipe left or right until you see the filters option. Look for the icon that resembles a smiley face with stars around it. This is the face shape filter.

Tap on the filter and it will automatically change your face shape. If you don't like the first filter you see, swipe left or right to choose from other options. Once you find a filter you like, take a photo or video and save it to your memories or send it to your friends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to change your face shape on Snapchat:

  1. Open Snapchat and ensure the front-facing camera is on.
  2. Swipe left or right until you see the filters option.
  3. Look for the smiley face with stars around it.
  4. Tap on the filter to activate it.
  5. Swipe left or right to choose from other face shape filters.
  6. Take a photo or video.
  7. Save it to your memories or send it to your friends.

Tips for Changing Face Shape on Snapchat

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your face shape on Snapchat:

  • Experiment with different filters until you find one that you like.
  • Make sure the lighting is good to get the best results.
  • Try different angles and poses to see which works best with the filter.
  • Consider using makeup or other accessories to enhance the effect of the filter.
  • Remember that the effect is temporary and will only last as long as the filter is active.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing face shape on Snapchat:

Can I make permanent changes to my face shape on Snapchat?

No, the face shape filters on Snapchat are temporary and can only be used while the filter is active.

Do I need to have a Snapchat account to use the face shape filters?

Yes, you need to have a Snapchat account to access the filters. You can download the app for free on the App Store or Google Play.

Are the face shape filters available for both iOS and Android?

Yes, the face shape filters are available on both iOS and Android versions of Snapchat.

Hair Care Tips for Snapchat Selfies

Now that you know how to change your face shape on Snapchat, it's time to take your selfies to the next level. Here are some hair care tips to help you look your best in your Snapchat photos:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Consider using hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scrunchies to add some extra flair to your Snapchat selfies.

Now that you know how to change your face shape on Snapchat and have some hair care tips in your arsenal, you're ready to take on the world of social media. Happy snapping!
