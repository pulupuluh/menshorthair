Mens Haircut Back of Head Fade - How to Get the Look

If you are looking for a stylish and modern haircut, then a back of head fade is the perfect option for you. This haircut involves tapering the hair from the back of the head to the neckline, creating a faded effect. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a mens haircut back of head fade, including how to style it and take care of it.

How to Get a Back of Head Fade Haircut

The easiest way to get a back of head fade haircut is to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will have the expertise and tools required to create the perfect fade. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, then follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers with guard sizes ranging from 0 to 4.

Step 2: Start with the Right Guard Size

Start with the highest guard size and work your way down. Begin by trimming the hair at the nape of the neck with a guard size of 4.

Step 3: Work Your Way Up

As you move up, use progressively smaller guard sizes. Use a guard size of 3 to trim the hair above the nape of the neck, a guard size of 2 for the middle section, and a guard size of 1 for the top section.

Step 4: Blend the Fade

Once you have finished trimming the hair with the clippers, use a pair of scissors to blend the fade.

Tips for Styling a Back of Head Fade Haircut

Now that you have the perfect back of head fade haircut, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a comb-over, slick-back, or messy look.
  • Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to maintain the fade.

FAQ

Q: How long does a back of head fade last?

A: A back of head fade can last anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can you get a back of head fade with long hair?

A: Yes, it is possible to get a back of head fade with long hair. However, it will require more time and effort to create the fade.

Hair Care Tips for a Back of Head Fade Haircut

Maintaining a back of head fade requires some effort, but it's worth it for the stylish look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as it can damage the hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a back of head fade haircut with confidence. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual style, a back of head fade is a versatile haircut that can be tailored to your individual preferences.
