Best Hairstyles For Grey Hair Over 60

As we age, our hair naturally turns grey or white. However, this doesn't mean that we need to give up on our hairstyles. There are plenty of hairstyles for grey hair over 60 that are both stylish and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for grey hair over 60, as well as some tips on how to care for grey hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Grey Hair over 60

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to consider a few factors when choosing a hairstyle for grey hair over 60:

  • Face shape: Different hairstyles flatter different face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape.
  • Hair texture: Grey hair can be coarser and drier than other hair types, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that works well with your hair texture.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you're constantly on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled in a few minutes.

Short Hairstyles for Grey Hair over 60

Short hairstyles are a great option for grey hair over 60, as they can be both stylish and easy to maintain. Here are a few options:

  • Pixie cut: A pixie cut is a short, chic hairstyle that can be easily styled with a bit of product. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style.
  • Bob: A bob is a classic hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or wavy.
  • Layered cut: A layered cut can add volume and texture to grey hair. It's a great option for those who want to add some dimension to their hair.

Medium Hairstyles for Grey Hair over 60

Medium hairstyles are a great option for those who want a bit more length than a short hairstyle. Here are a few options:

  • Shoulder-length cut: A shoulder-length cut is a versatile style that can be worn straight or wavy. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style with a bit more length.
  • Layered lob: A layered lob is a longer version of the bob. It's a great option for those who want a bit more length but still want a low-maintenance style.
  • Curly bob: A curly bob is a great option for those with naturally curly hair. It can be styled with a bit of product to define the curls and add volume.

Long Hairstyles for Grey Hair over 60

Long hairstyles can be a bit more high-maintenance than short or medium hairstyles, but they can be a great option for those who want a bit more length. Here are a few options:

  • Long layers: Long layers can add volume and texture to grey hair. It's a great option for those who want a bit more length but still want a low-maintenance style.
  • Straight and sleek: A straight and sleek hairstyle can be a great option for those who want a polished, sophisticated look.
  • Beachy waves: Beachy waves are a great option for those who want a more relaxed, casual look. It can be achieved with a bit of product and a curling iron.

Tips for Caring for Grey Hair

Grey hair can be a bit more delicate than other hair types, so it's important to care for it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner: Grey hair can be coarser and drier than other hair types, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner: Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep grey hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage grey hair, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible. If you do need to use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep grey hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for grey hair over 60?

The best hairstyle for grey hair over 60 depends on a few factors, such as face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Short hairstyles like a pixie cut or bob are a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style, while medium and long hairstyles can offer more versatility and length.

How do I care for grey hair?

To care for grey hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, deep condition once a week, avoid heat styling as much as possible, and get regular trims.

Can I dye my grey hair?

Yes, you can dye your grey hair if you want to. However, it's important to choose a hair dye that's specifically formulated for grey hair and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

What haircuts make you look younger?

Layered haircuts can add volume and texture to hair, which can make you look younger. A bob or lob can also be a great option, as it can help frame the face and give a youthful appearance.
