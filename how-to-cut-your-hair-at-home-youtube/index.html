How To Cut Your Hair At Home Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Cutting your hair at home can save you a lot of money and time, especially if you don't have time to go to a hair salon. With the help of YouTube tutorials, you can learn how to cut your hair at home like a pro. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair at home using YouTube videos.

How to Cut Your Hair at Home YouTube: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, clips to section off your hair, and a mirror.

Step 2: Choose the Right YouTube Tutorial

There are many YouTube tutorials on how to cut your hair at home, but not all of them are suitable for your hair type and desired style. Choose a tutorial that matches your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Use clips to section off your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair in layers.

Step 5: Follow the Tutorial

Follow the tutorial step by step, making sure to cut your hair slowly and carefully. Don't rush the process to avoid making mistakes.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

After cutting each section, check your progress in the mirror. Make sure the length and style are consistent with your desired look.

Step 7: Trim the Ends

Once you have cut all the sections, trim the ends to finish the look.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a hair straightener, curling iron, or hair products.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home

- Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid making drastic changes.
- Use a comb to straighten your hair before cutting.
- Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it can shrink when it dries.
- Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair at home if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair at home if you have curly hair. Choose a tutorial that matches your hair type and style.

How often should I cut my hair at home?

It depends on your hair growth and desired length. On average, you should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair at Home

After cutting your hair at home, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Cutting your hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience, especially if you follow the right tutorial and take care of your hair properly. Use this step-by-step guide to cut your hair at home using YouTube videos and achieve the perfect look.
