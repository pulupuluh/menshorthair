Guy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Feb 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men, the shaved sides long top look is an excellent choice. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a favorite among celebrities and fashion-forward men. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to get the perfect guy haircut shaved sides long top, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Advice

Description

The guy haircut shaved sides long top is characterized by closely shaved or faded sides and a longer top. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. You can choose to style the top in various ways, such as slicking it back, spiking it up, or leaving it messy and textured. This look is perfect if you want a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd.

How to Get the Look

To get the guy haircut shaved sides long top, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist or barber. They will have the skills and expertise to create the perfect look for you. Before your appointment, it's a good idea to gather some inspiration photos to show your stylist. This will help them understand the exact look you're going for.

Choosing the Right Haircut

The first step to getting the perfect guy haircut shaved sides long top is to choose the right haircut. There are a few different options to consider:

  • The undercut: This is a classic option for the shaved sides long top look. The hair on the sides and back is shaved or faded, and the top is left longer.
  • The fade: This is a more gradual version of the undercut, where the hair gradually fades from short to long.
  • The tapered cut: This option features a gradual taper from the top of the head to the sides and back.

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's time to move on to the styling stage.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for getting the perfect guy haircut shaved sides long top:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.
  2. Visit your hairstylist or barber and show them the inspiration photos you've gathered.
  3. Choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  4. Ask your hairstylist or barber to shave or fade the sides and back of your head.
  5. Instruct your hairstylist or barber on how you want the top of your hair styled.
  6. Choose a hair product that suits your hair type and desired style.
  7. Apply the hair product to your hair and style it according to your preferences.
  8. Use a hairdryer or let your hair air dry.
  9. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

It's important to keep in mind that the styling process may vary depending on your hair type and desired look. Your hairstylist or barber will be able to provide you with personalized advice and recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your guy haircut shaved sides long top:

  • Book regular appointments with your hairstylist or barber to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that suit your hair type and desired style.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair and style it as desired.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guy haircut shaved sides long top:

1. Is the guy haircut shaved sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist or barber will be able to provide you with personalized advice on the best style for your face shape.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

You should book regular appointments with your hairstylist or barber to maintain the length and shape of your haircut. The frequency of these appointments will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your look.

3. What hair products should I use for the guy haircut shaved sides long top?

You should choose hair products that suit your hair type and desired style. For example, if you want a slicked-back look, you may want to use a pomade or gel. If you prefer a more natural, textured look, you may want to use a texturizing spray or wax.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get enough rest and exercise to promote overall health and wellness, which can also benefit your hair.

With these tips and instructions, you'll be able to get the perfect guy haircut shaved sides long top and keep it looking great for weeks to come. So, go ahead and try out this stylish and trendy look today!
