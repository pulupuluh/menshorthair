Unlocking Hairstyles In Animal Crossing New Horizons

If you're an Animal Crossing New Horizons player, you might want to change up your hairstyle from time to time. Luckily, there are several ways to unlock new hairstyles in the game. Keep reading to find out how!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Unlock Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Unlocking Hairstyles
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care in Animal Crossing New Horizons

How to Unlock Hairstyles:

There are several ways to unlock new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Visit Harriet's salon on Harv's Island
  • Buy a mirror from Nook's Cranny and change your hairstyle at home
  • Use a scallop to trade with Pascal for a mermaid hairstyle
  • Get a new hairstyle as a reward for completing a challenge or event

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here's a step-by-step guide for unlocking new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  1. Visit Harriet's salon on Harv's Island. Harriet will offer to change your hairstyle for 3,000 bells or 6 Harv's Island medals.
  2. Buy a mirror from Nook's Cranny for 1,200 bells. Use the mirror to change your hairstyle at home.
  3. Use a scallop to trade with Pascal for a mermaid hairstyle. Pascal will appear randomly when you find a scallop while diving.
  4. Complete challenges or events to unlock new hairstyles as rewards. Some examples of challenges/events include the Fishing Tourney, Bug-Off, and the Wedding Season event.

Tips for Unlocking Hairstyles:

Here are some tips to help you unlock new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Make sure to participate in events and challenges to have a chance at unlocking new hairstyles.
  • Visit Harriet's salon on Harv's Island regularly to see if there are any new hairstyles available.
  • Keep a scallop in your inventory while diving so you're prepared to trade with Pascal if he appears.
  • Check Nook's Cranny regularly to see if they have a mirror in stock.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about unlocking hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

Can I unlock all hairstyles?

Yes, you can unlock all hairstyles in the game.

How often does Harriet change her hairstyles?

Harriet changes her hairstyles every day.

Can I change my hair color?

Yes, you can change your hair color at Harriet's salon or with a mirror at home.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

Just like in real life, it's important to take care of your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly by taking a shower or using a bathtub.
  • Use a towel to dry your hair after washing.
  • Don't wear hats or headwear for too long, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your hairstyle and make sure it looks good.

Now that you know how to unlock new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons, go out there and try some new looks!
