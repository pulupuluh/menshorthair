How To Make Your Wig Curly Again Bring Life Back To Your Curls

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 · 9 min read

Wigs are a great way to switch up your look without making any drastic changes to your natural hair. However, after some time, your wig’s curls can start to fall flat, leaving you with a lifeless mane. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you on how to make your wig curly again so that you can bring back the life and bounce to your curls.

Description

When you first purchase a wig, it may have beautiful and bouncy curls. However, over time, the curls may start to fall flat, leaving you with a wig that looks dull and lifeless. This can be frustrating, especially if you love the look of curly hair. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your wig curly again, allowing you to restore your wig's beautiful curls and keep it looking fabulous.

How To

Here are some ways to make your wig curly again:

1. Use Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great way to create curls without using heat. Simply wrap small sections of your wig hair around the flexi rods and leave them in overnight. In the morning, carefully remove the rods and fluff your curls with your fingers for a bouncy and curly look.

2. Use a Curling Iron or Wand

If you prefer to use heat to style your wig, you can use a curling iron or wand to create beautiful curls. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling to prevent damage to your wig.

3. Use Perm Rods

Perm rods are similar to flexi rods but are smaller and create tighter curls. Wrap small sections of your wig hair around the perm rods and leave them in overnight. In the morning, gently remove the rods and fluff your curls with your fingers for a beautiful and defined look.

4. Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you have a wig that is made of synthetic hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create bouncy and curly locks. Simply place the wig on a wig stand and use the diffuser attachment on a low heat setting to dry your hair while adding volume and curls.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your wig curly again:

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

The first step in making your wig curly again is to wash it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to clean your wig, making sure to rinse it thoroughly with cool water.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your wig, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and protect it from damage.

Step 3: Choose Your Curling Method

Decide which method you want to use to create curls on your wig. You can use flexi rods, curling iron, perm rods, or a diffuser attachment, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Once you have chosen your curling method, begin to style your wig. Follow the instructions for your chosen method to create beautiful and bouncy curls.

Step 5: Set Your Curls

Once you have styled your wig, it's time to set your curls. Use a hairspray or holding spray to keep your curls in place and prevent them from falling flat.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making your wig curly again:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray if you are using heat to style your wig.
  • Be gentle when handling your wig to prevent any damage or breakage.
  • Use a wig stand to style your wig, making it easier to work with.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray or holding spray, as this can make your wig look stiff and unnatural.
  • Try different methods to find the one that works best for your wig and your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your wig curly again:

Can I use a hairdryer to make my wig curly again?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer to make your wig curly again. Use a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting to dry your hair while adding volume and curls.

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on the method you use and how well you set your curls. Typically, curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I use the same curling method on synthetic and human hair wigs?

No, you should use different methods for synthetic and human hair wigs. Synthetic hair wigs cannot handle heat, so you should use flexi rods or a diffuser attachment to create curls. Human hair wigs can handle heat, so you can use a curling iron or wand to create curls.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your wig to ensure that it lasts for a long time. Here are some tips for wig care:

  • Wash your wig regularly to keep it clean and fresh.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to keep its shape and prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using hot tools on synthetic hair wigs.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and keep your wig in place.

With these tips and methods, you can make your wig curly again and keep it looking fabulous. Remember to take good care of your wig to ensure it lasts for many wears to come.
