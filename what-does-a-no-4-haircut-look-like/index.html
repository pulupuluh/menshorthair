What Does A No 4 Haircut Look Like

Written by Oliver May 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're unfamiliar with haircut numbers, you're not alone. It can be confusing to navigate the different clipper guard sizes and know what each one means. In this article, we'll focus on the no 4 haircut and explore what it looks like, how to achieve it, and how to care for your hair afterwards.

Description

The no 4 haircut is a medium-length buzz cut that leaves approximately 1/2 inch of hair on the scalp. It's a versatile style that works well for both men and women, and it's often used as a starting point for longer hairstyles.

The no 4 guard is commonly used for fades, tapered cuts, and blending shorter hair into longer hair. It's also a popular choice for military haircuts and athletic hairstyles that require minimal maintenance.

How to Achieve a No 4 Haircut

To achieve a no 4 haircut, you'll need a set of clippers with a no 4 guard attached. Start by washing and drying your hair, then comb it in the direction of the growth. Begin at the crown of your head and work your way towards the hairline.

Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head, moving the clipper in an upward motion. Be sure to keep the blade flat against your scalp to ensure an even cut. Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top of your head, working from front to back.

Check your work periodically by looking in a mirror or using a handheld mirror to view the back of your head. If you notice any uneven spots, go back over them with the clippers until you achieve the desired length.

Tips for Maintaining a No 4 Haircut

Once you've achieved your no 4 haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims. Depending on how quickly your hair grows, you may need to trim it every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you want to change up your style, consider adding some layers or texture to your hair.

FAQ

Can I achieve a no 4 haircut with scissors?

While it's technically possible to achieve a no 4 haircut with scissors, it's much easier and more efficient to use clippers with a guard. Scissors are better suited for creating texture and layers in the hair.

Is a no 4 haircut appropriate for all hair types?

Yes, a no 4 haircut can work well for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. However, if you have particularly thick or coarse hair, you may need to use a lower guard size to achieve the desired length.

Can I achieve a no 4 haircut at home?

Yes, it's possible to achieve a no 4 haircut at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's always best to visit a professional stylist to ensure a clean, even cut.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy scalp and preventing damage to your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to ensure that your hair gets the nutrients it needs to grow strong and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With proper care and maintenance, a no 4 haircut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for anyone looking for a clean, classic look.
