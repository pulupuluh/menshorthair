Short Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 Curly Hair How To Rock Your Look

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle to flatter your round face and curly hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for round faces over 50 curly hair. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and lose its volume, which can make it challenging to find a style that flatters our face shape. If you have a round face and curly hair, you may feel like your options are limited. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can complement your features and give you a fresh, youthful look.

How to

The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and curly hair is to choose a cut that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face and balance out your features. Here are some popular short hairstyles for round faces over 50 curly hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for women with round faces and curly hair. This cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. To add volume to your pixie cut, use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow-dry your hair with a round brush. You can also add texture with a texturizing spray.

Short Bob

A short bob is another great option for women with round faces and curly hair. This cut is shorter in the back and longer in the front, with layers that frame your face. To style your short bob, use a curl-enhancing cream and scrunch your hair to enhance your natural curls. You can also add volume by blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment.

Curly Shag

The curly shag is a layered cut that works well for women with round faces and curly hair. This cut features choppy layers that add texture and volume to your hair. To style your curly shag, use a curl-enhancing cream and scrunch your hair to enhance your natural curls. You can also add volume by blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment.

Step by step

If you're unsure how to style your new short haircut, here are some step-by-step instructions:

Pixie Cut

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown of your head to add height.
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold.

Short Bob

  1. Apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair.
  2. Scrunch your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to add volume.

Curly Shag

  1. Apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair.
  2. Scrunch your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to add volume.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces over 50 curly hair:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my face look rounder?

A: No, as long as you choose a cut that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, it can actually elongate your face and balance out your features.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's thinning?

A: Yes, you can use volumizing products and styling techniques to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring agents, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these short hairstyles for round faces over 50 curly hair and tips for maintaining your new look, you can rock your new hairstyle with confidence and style. Whether you choose a pixie cut, short bob, or curly shag, you're sure to turn heads and feel fabulous!
