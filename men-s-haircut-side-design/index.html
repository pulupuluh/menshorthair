Men s Haircut Side Design A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Aprilia Feb 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to add some edge and personality to your hairstyle? Consider getting a side design! This trendy haircut style is popular among men of all ages and is a great way to show off your style and creativity. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about men's haircut side design, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Side Design

Getting a side design is a relatively simple process, but it's important to choose the right barber or stylist who has experience with this style. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a men's haircut side design:

Step 1: Choose Your Design

The first step in getting a side design is choosing the design you want. There are countless options to choose from, including geometric shapes, tribal patterns, and even words or phrases. Take some time to browse through pictures online or talk to your barber to get inspiration for your design.

Step 2: Decide on the Placement

Once you've chosen your design, it's time to decide on the placement. Most men choose to get their side design on one or both sides of their head, but it's also possible to get it on the back of your head or even around your ear. Your barber can help you decide on the best placement for your design based on the shape of your head and the look you're going for.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

Now comes the fun part! Your barber will start by trimming your hair to the desired length and then use clippers or a razor to create the design. This process can take anywhere from a few minutes to an hour depending on the complexity of your design.

Step 4: Maintenance

Now that you have your side design, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh. Be sure to wash your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. You may also need to visit your barber every few weeks for touch-ups to keep your design looking sharp.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Haircut Side Design

Now that you have your side design, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Visit your barber every few weeks for touch-ups to keep your design looking fresh
  • Consider using a styling product like gel or pomade to help hold your design in place

FAQ

How long does a men's haircut side design last?

A men's haircut side design can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Is a men's haircut side design suitable for all hair types?

Yes! A men's haircut side design can be adapted to suit any hair type, whether you have curly hair, straight hair, or anything in between.

How much does a men's haircut side design cost?

The cost of a men's haircut side design can vary depending on the complexity of the design and the experience of the barber or stylist. Expect to pay anywhere from $30 to $100 or more for a side design.

Hair Care

In order to keep your men's haircut side design looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often
  • Use a styling product like gel or pomade to help hold your design in place
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your men's haircut side design with confidence and style.
