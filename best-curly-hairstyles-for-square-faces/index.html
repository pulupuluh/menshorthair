Best Curly Hairstyles For Square Faces In 2023

Written by Frank Apr 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a square face, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. But with curly hair, you have more options than you might think! In this blog post, we'll explore the best curly hairstyles for square faces in 2023. From short and sassy to long and luscious, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead, and the goal with any hairstyle is to soften these features. Curly hairstyles work well with square faces because they add volume and texture, which can help balance out the strong lines of the face. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape without overwhelming it.

How to

The first step in choosing a curly hairstyle for a square face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length of your face and the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features.

Step by Step

1. Start by considering a shoulder-length bob with loose, beachy waves. This style draws attention away from the jawline and towards the hair, which can help balance out the face. 2. If you prefer a shorter style, consider a pixie cut with lots of volume on top. This can help elongate the face and add height to the forehead. 3. For a longer style, try long layers with big, loose curls. This style adds volume and movement to the hair, which can help soften the angles of the face. 4. Another option is to go for a curly shag with layers around the face. This style can help add texture and depth to the hair, which can help balance out the strong lines of the face. 5. If you want to keep your hair up, try a messy bun with curly tendrils around the face. This style can help soften the jawline and add a touch of romance to your look.

Tips

When choosing a curly hairstyle for a square face, it's important to consider the texture of your hair. If you have naturally curly hair, you may want to opt for a style that embraces your curls rather than fighting against them. If you have straight hair, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to add volume and texture to your locks.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a square face?
A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Soft, side-swept bangs can help balance out the strong lines of the face, while blunt bangs can make the face look more angular.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for curly hair, it's important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your curls. You should also use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.

With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect curly hairstyle for your square face in 2023. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
