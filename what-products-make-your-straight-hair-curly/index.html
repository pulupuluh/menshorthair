What Products Make Your Straight Hair Curly

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to try out a new curly look? Well, you're in luck because there are plenty of hair products that can help you achieve those bouncy curls you've been dreaming of. In this post, we'll be discussing some of the best products that can make your straight hair curly. So, let's get started!

  2. Products That Can Make Your Straight Hair Curly
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Your Straight Hair Curly

Before we dive into the products that can make your straight hair curly, let's talk about the different methods you can use to achieve curly hair. Here are some popular methods:

  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Using rollers or flexi rods
  • Getting a perm
  • Using hair products that enhance curls

While all of these methods can be effective, using hair products is often the easiest and most affordable option. That's why we'll be focusing on products in this post.

Products That Can Make Your Straight Hair Curly

Here are some of the best products that can make your straight hair curly:

Hair Mousse

Hair mousse is a lightweight foam that can add volume and texture to your hair. It's perfect for creating soft, bouncy curls. Simply apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. Then, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help enhance your curls and give you a natural-looking style.

Curl-Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

If you want to enhance your natural curls, using a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner can be a great option. These products are specifically designed to define and enhance curls while also providing moisture and nourishment to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and glycerin.

Curl Cream

Curl cream is a styling product that can define and enhance your curls. It's typically a thicker, creamier product that you can apply to your damp hair. Simply work the product through your hair, scrunching it with your hands as you go. Then, either let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help create soft, defined curls.

Hair Serum

Hair serum is a lightweight, silicone-based product that can add shine and smoothness to your hair. It's perfect for taming frizz and flyaways, which can be especially helpful when trying to create curly hair. Simply apply a pea-sized amount of serum to your wet or dry hair, focusing on the ends. This will help give your curls a polished, finished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know about some of the best products for making your straight hair curly, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a golf ball-sized amount of hair mousse to your hair.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your curls.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.
  5. If desired, apply a curl cream to define and enhance your curls.
  6. Finish with a pea-sized amount of hair serum to tame frizz and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your desired curly hair look, it's important to take care of your curls to maintain them. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I make my straight hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use products like hair mousse, curl cream, and curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your natural curls without using heat styling tools.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer while scrunching your hair with your hands. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Can I use these products on color-treated hair?

Yes, most of these products are safe to use on color-treated hair. However, be sure to check the label or consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a slightly different hair care routine than straight hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can cause frizz and dryness.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to provide extra moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

With the right products and hair care routine, you can easily make your straight hair curly. So, why not give it a try and switch up your look?
