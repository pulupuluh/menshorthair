What Haircuts Suit Rectangle Face Shapes

Written by Petter Mar 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your rectangle face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts that flatter your facial features and enhance your natural beauty.

Description

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a long, straight jawline and forehead. The length and width of the face are almost equal, creating a box-like appearance. The goal of choosing the right haircut is to soften the angles and create balance between the upper and lower parts of the face.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a rectangle face shape, it's important to consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • The texture of your hair
  • Your personal style
  • Your face shape

By taking these factors into consideration, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut that suits your rectangle face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the right haircut for your rectangle face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face.
  2. Look for hairstyles that soften the angles of your face, such as side-swept bangs or layers around the face.
  3. Avoid hairstyles that add length to your face, such as long, straight hair.
  4. Consider short haircuts, such as a pixie cut or bob, to create balance between the upper and lower parts of the face.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your personal style and enhances your natural beauty.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a rectangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers or waves to create movement and soften the angles of your face.
  • Choose a haircut that emphasizes your best features, such as your eyes or cheekbones.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what haircut would suit your face shape and personal style.

FAQ

Q. Can rectangle face shapes have long hair?

A. Yes, rectangle face shapes can have long hair, but it's important to avoid hairstyles that add length to the face. Consider adding layers or waves to create movement and soften the angles of the face.

Q. What is the best haircut for a rectangle face shape?

A. The best haircut for a rectangle face shape is one that softens the angles and creates balance between the upper and lower parts of the face. Consider short haircuts, such as a pixie cut or bob, or hairstyles with side-swept bangs or layers around the face.

Q. Can rectangle face shapes have bangs?

A. Yes, rectangle face shapes can have bangs, but it's important to choose a style that softens the angles of the face. Side-swept bangs are a great option, as they create movement and balance.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for a rectangle face shape can be challenging, but by considering your personal style and face shape, you can find the perfect haircut that enhances your natural beauty. Remember to experiment with different styles and consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion. With the right haircut, you can look and feel your best!
