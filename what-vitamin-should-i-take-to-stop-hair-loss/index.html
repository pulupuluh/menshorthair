What Vitamin Should I Take To Stop Hair Loss

Written by Daniel May 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're experiencing hair loss, you're not alone. It's estimated that up to 50% of men and women will experience some degree of hair loss in their lifetime. While there are many factors that can contribute to hair loss, including genetics, stress, and hormonal changes, one factor that is often overlooked is nutrition.

In this article, we'll explore what vitamin you should take to stop hair loss, as well as other vitamins and nutrients that can help support healthy hair growth.

Table of Contents:

  • Biotin
  • Iron
  • Vitamin C
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is an essential nutrient that plays a role in many bodily functions, including hair growth. Studies have shown that vitamin D deficiency is associated with hair loss, and supplementing with vitamin D may help promote healthy hair growth.

The easiest way to get vitamin D is through sun exposure, but many people don't get enough sun exposure to meet their daily needs. In these cases, supplementing with a vitamin D supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of vitamin D is 600-800 IU for adults.

Biotin

Biotin, also known as vitamin B7, is another nutrient that is crucial for healthy hair growth. Biotin helps produce keratin, a protein that makes up the structure of hair, skin, and nails. Studies have shown that supplementing with biotin may help improve hair growth in people with biotin deficiency.

Biotin is found in a variety of foods, including eggs, nuts, and whole grains. However, if you're not getting enough biotin in your diet, supplementing with a biotin supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of biotin is 30-100 mcg for adults.

Iron

Iron is an essential mineral that is needed for many bodily functions, including hair growth. Iron helps transport oxygen to the hair follicles, which is necessary for healthy hair growth. Studies have shown that iron deficiency is associated with hair loss, and supplementing with iron may help improve hair growth in people with iron deficiency.

Iron is found in a variety of foods, including red meat, poultry, and leafy green vegetables. However, if you're not getting enough iron in your diet, supplementing with an iron supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of iron is 8-18 mg for adults.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is an antioxidant that helps protect the hair follicles from damage by free radicals. Free radicals can damage the hair follicles, which can lead to hair loss. Studies have shown that vitamin C deficiency is associated with hair loss, and supplementing with vitamin C may help improve hair growth in people with vitamin C deficiency.

Vitamin C is found in a variety of foods, including citrus fruits, berries, and leafy green vegetables. However, if you're not getting enough vitamin C in your diet, supplementing with a vitamin C supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of vitamin C is 75-90 mg for adults.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fatty acids that are important for many bodily functions, including hair growth. Omega-3 fatty acids help nourish the hair follicles, which can help promote healthy hair growth. Studies have shown that supplementing with omega-3 fatty acids may help improve hair growth in people with hair loss.

Omega-3 fatty acids are found in a variety of foods, including fatty fish, nuts, and seeds. However, if you're not getting enough omega-3 fatty acids in your diet, supplementing with an omega-3 fatty acid supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of omega-3 fatty acids is 250-500 mg for adults.

Zinc

Zinc is an essential mineral that is needed for many bodily functions, including hair growth. Zinc helps maintain the structure of the hair follicles, which is necessary for healthy hair growth. Studies have shown that zinc deficiency is associated with hair loss, and supplementing with zinc may help improve hair growth in people with zinc deficiency.

Zinc is found in a variety of foods, including red meat, poultry, and shellfish. However, if you're not getting enough zinc in your diet, supplementing with a zinc supplement may be beneficial. The recommended daily intake of zinc is 8-11 mg for adults.

Tips for Healthy Hair

In addition to getting enough of the vitamins and nutrients listed above, there are other things you can do to support healthy hair growth:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on high heat
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins
  • Manage stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques

FAQs

What is the most important vitamin for hair growth?

While there is no one "most important" vitamin for hair growth, some of the most important vitamins and nutrients include vitamin D, biotin, iron, vitamin C, omega-3 fatty acids, and zinc.

Can vitamins help stop hair loss?

Yes, vitamins can help stop hair loss in some cases. If hair loss is caused by a vitamin deficiency, supplementing with the deficient vitamin may help improve hair growth.

What is the recommended daily intake of vitamin D?

The recommended daily intake of vitamin D is 600-800 IU for adults.

What foods are high in biotin?

Foods that are high in biotin include eggs, nuts, and whole grains.

What is the recommended daily intake of iron?

The recommended daily intake of iron is 8-18 mg for adults.

What foods are high in vitamin C?

Foods that are high in vitamin C include citrus fruits, berries, and leafy green vegetables.

What is the recommended daily intake of omega-3 fatty acids?

The recommended daily intake of omega-3 fatty acids is 250-500 mg for adults.

What foods are high in zinc?

Foods that are high in zinc include red meat, poultry, and shellfish.

In conclusion, if you're experiencing hair loss, it's important to consider your nutrition as a possible factor. Supplementing with the vitamins and nutrients listed above, as well as following the tips for healthy hair, may help support healthy hair growth.
