How Do You Find The Right Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Daniel May 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features. In this article, we will walk you through the steps of finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Identify Your Face Shape
  2. Consider Your Hair Type and Texture
  3. Look for Inspiration
  4. Consult with a Professional
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face is to identify your face shape. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oval face shape. If your face is round and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your chin is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face. Lastly, if your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your chin is pointed, you have a diamond-shaped face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type and Texture

Once you've identified your face shape, the next step is to consider your hair type and texture. Your hair type and texture will play a significant role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to consider a shorter hairstyle to add volume. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to go for a longer hairstyle that will help control the volume.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Once you have identified your face shape and hair type, it's time to start looking for inspiration. Browse through magazines, online galleries, and social media platforms to find hairstyles that you like. Create a collection of images that you can take with you to your stylist. This will help your stylist understand exactly what you're looking for and make recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

Now that you have a good idea of what you're looking for, it's time to consult with a professional stylist. A professional stylist can help you determine the best hairstyle based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. They can also make recommendations for haircuts and styles that will work well with your daily routine and maintenance needs.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

For a round face, it's best to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face. Long, layered haircuts or side-swept bangs are great options. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, which can make your face appear rounder.

What is the best hairstyle for an oval face?

With an oval face shape, you have a lot of flexibility when it comes to hairstyles. You can pull off just about any haircut, from short pixie cuts to long, flowing locks. Consider a cut that highlights your best features, such as your cheekbones or jawline.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

For a square face shape, it's best to choose a hairstyle that softens your features. Consider a layered cut or a style with side-swept bangs. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts, which can accentuate your angular features.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray. Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips in mind, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and keep your hair looking its best. Remember, the key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
