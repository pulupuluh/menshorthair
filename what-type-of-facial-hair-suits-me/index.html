What Type Of Facial Hair Suits Me A Guide For Men

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old look and thinking about growing out some facial hair? Not sure what type of facial hair will suit you best? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore the different types of facial hair and help you find the perfect style that suits your face shape and personal style. Let's dive in!

What is Your Face Shape?

The first step in determining what type of facial hair suits you is to identify your face shape. Your face shape will play a significant role in determining the style that will work best for you. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you have identified your face shape, you can start exploring the different types of facial hair that will work best for you.

How to Choose the Right Facial Hair Style?

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right facial hair style that will suit you best. Here are some tips to help you out:

  1. Consider your lifestyle - Do you have a job that requires a clean-shaven look? If so, then a full beard may not be the best option for you. Consider a shorter beard or mustache instead.
  2. Think about your personal style - Do you prefer a rugged, masculine look or a more polished, refined style? Choose a facial hair style that reflects your personality and personal style.
  3. Look at your hair - If you have thick, coarse hair, then a full beard may be a great option for you. If your hair is thin or patchy, then a shorter beard or mustache may work better.
  4. Consider your age - If you're younger, then a more trendy or edgy facial hair style may work well for you. If you're older, then a more classic or timeless style may be more appropriate.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Facial Hair

If you're new to growing facial hair, then here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Stop shaving - Let your facial hair grow out for at least 2-4 weeks.
  2. Trim your beard - Once your facial hair has grown out, use a trimmer to shape your beard and remove any stray hairs.
  3. Clean and moisturize - Use a beard shampoo and conditioner to keep your facial hair clean and moisturized.
  4. Style your beard - Use a beard balm or oil to keep your facial hair looking neat and styled.
  5. Maintain your beard - Keep your beard trimmed and groomed regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your facial hair and keep it looking great:

  • Use a beard brush or comb to keep your facial hair neat and tidy.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and style.
  • Keep your facial hair clean and moisturized with a beard shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your facial hair.
  • Use a beard balm or oil to keep your facial hair soft and conditioned.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about facial hair:

  • Q: What type of facial hair suits me if I have a round face?
  • A: If you have a round face, then a beard that is shorter on the sides and longer on the chin can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Q: What type of facial hair suits me if I have an oval face?
  • A: If you have an oval face, then a full beard or a short beard with a mustache can work well for you.
  • Q: What type of facial hair suits me if I have a square face?
  • A: If you have a square face, then a beard that is longer on the chin and shorter on the sides can help soften your jawline.

Conclusion

Choosing the right facial hair style can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect style that suits your face shape and personal style. Remember to maintain your facial hair regularly to keep it looking great, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you. Happy grooming!
