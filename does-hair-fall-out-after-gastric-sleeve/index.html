Does Hair Fall Out After Gastric Sleeve

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering getting a gastric sleeve surgery but worried about potential hair loss? You're not alone. One of the common questions people ask is "does hair fall out after gastric sleeve?" In this article, we will address this concern and provide tips on how to manage hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Gastric sleeve surgery, also known as sleeve gastrectomy, is a weight loss surgery that involves removing a portion of the stomach to reduce its size. This results in a smaller stomach capacity, which helps people eat less and lose weight. While gastric sleeve surgery can be an effective weight loss tool, it may also have some side effects, including hair loss.

How to

If you're experiencing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, there are several things you can do to manage it:

1. Talk to your doctor

If you notice excessive hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, it's important to talk to your doctor. They may recommend supplements or other treatments to help manage your hair loss.

2. Take vitamins

Vitamins and minerals are essential for healthy hair growth. After gastric sleeve surgery, you may need to take supplements to ensure you're getting enough nutrients. Your doctor may recommend taking biotin, iron, or other vitamins to support hair growth.

3. Eat a balanced diet

A balanced diet is important for overall health, including hair growth. Make sure you're eating a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats. Avoid crash diets or extreme calorie restriction, which can contribute to hair loss.

4. Avoid harsh treatments

Avoid harsh hair treatments, such as chemical relaxers, perms, or hair dyes. These can damage your hair and make hair loss worse. Stick to gentle hair care products and treatments.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to manage hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery:

Step 1: Talk to your doctor

If you're experiencing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, the first step is to talk to your doctor. They can help determine the underlying cause of your hair loss and recommend appropriate treatments.

Step 2: Take vitamins

Your doctor may recommend taking vitamins or supplements to support healthy hair growth. These may include biotin, iron, or other nutrients.

Step 3: Eat a balanced diet

Eating a balanced diet can help support healthy hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Avoid crash diets or extreme calorie restriction, which can contribute to hair loss.

Step 4: Be gentle with your hair

Avoid harsh hair treatments and products that can damage your hair. Stick to gentle hair care products and treatments, and be gentle when brushing or styling your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery:

1. Be patient

It may take several months for your hair to start regrowing after gastric sleeve surgery. Be patient and stick to your doctor's recommendations for managing hair loss.

2. Wear a wig or hairpiece

If you're self-conscious about your hair loss, consider wearing a wig or hairpiece. This can help boost your confidence while you wait for your hair to regrow.

3. Practice stress-reducing techniques

Stress can contribute to hair loss. Practice stress-reducing techniques, such as meditation or yoga, to help manage your stress levels.

FAQ

Q: Does hair loss always occur after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: No, hair loss doesn't always occur after gastric sleeve surgery. However, it's a common side effect that affects many people.

Q: How long does hair loss last after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is usually temporary and may last for several months. Hair typically starts regrowing within 6-12 months after surgery.

Q: Can taking vitamins prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Taking vitamins and supplements can help support healthy hair growth after gastric sleeve surgery. However, they may not completely prevent hair loss.

Hair Care

After gastric sleeve surgery, it's important to take care of your hair to minimize hair loss. Here are some tips for healthy hair care:

1. Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair

Avoid harsh brushing or styling that can damage your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb and be gentle when brushing out tangles.

2. Use gentle hair care products

Avoid harsh shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Stick to gentle, natural products that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

Avoid using heated styling tools, such as flat irons or curling wands, which can damage your hair. If you must use these tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

In conclusion, hair loss is a common side effect after gastric sleeve surgery. However, it's usually temporary and can be managed with proper care and treatment. If you're experiencing excessive hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, talk to your doctor for guidance and support.
