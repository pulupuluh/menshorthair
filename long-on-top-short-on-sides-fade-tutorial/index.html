Long On Top Short On Sides Fade Tutorial A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then the long on top short on sides fade might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of achieving a perfect long on top short on sides fade haircut.

Description

The long on top short on sides fade is a modern haircut that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides, which gradually fades into the skin. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to fit any face shape and hair type. The long on top short on sides fade is low maintenance and easy to style, making it a great choice for busy men who want to look their best.

How to Achieve the Long on Top Short on Sides Fade Haircut

Follow these easy steps to achieve the long on top short on sides fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length and Style

The first step is to choose the right length and style for your hair. Decide how long you want the hair on top to be and how short you want the hair on the sides to be. You can choose to have a classic undercut, a modern comb-over, or a textured crop, depending on your personal preference.

Step 2: Cut the Hair on the Sides

Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a smooth fade. Use a comb to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head with the longer hair on top.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head. Start by sectioning off the hair on top and trimming it to the desired length. Use a comb to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Apply a styling product to the hair on top and style it as desired. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to achieve a slicked-back look, a messy textured look, or a classic side part.

Tips for Maintaining the Long on Top Short on Sides Fade Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your long on top short on sides fade haircut:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair and avoid using your fingers to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

What face shape is best for the long on top short on sides fade haircut?

The long on top short on sides fade haircut can be customized to suit any face shape, but it is particularly flattering for men with square, oval, or heart-shaped faces.

Can I style the long on top short on sides fade haircut differently?

Yes, the long on top short on sides fade haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can try a slicked-back look, a messy textured look, or a classic side part, depending on your personal style.

Is the long on top short on sides fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long on top short on sides fade haircut can be customized to suit any hair type, whether you have thick, thin, curly, or straight hair.

Hair Care for the Long on Top Short on Sides Fade Haircut

To maintain the health and appearance of your hair, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally or use a low-heat setting on your hair dryer. Use a comb or brush to style your hair and avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you have learned how to achieve the long on top short on sides fade haircut, it's time to go to your barber and get a fresh new look. With the right length and style, this haircut can transform your appearance and boost your confidence. Follow our tips for maintaining your haircut and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
