How To Elongate A Short Dress Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Mar 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of feeling uncomfortable in your favorite short dress? Do you want to elongate it to look more elegant and sophisticated? You're not alone! Many women face this issue and struggle with finding the right solution to elongate their short dresses. But worry no more! In this article, we will walk you through some simple and easy tips and tricks to elongate a short dress and make you feel confident and comfortable.

How to Elongate a Short Dress: Step by Step

Step 1: Wear high heels or wedges. The easiest and most obvious way to elongate a short dress is to wear high heels or wedges. Not only will they make you look taller and slimmer, but they also give a more sophisticated look to your outfit.

Step 2: Add a belt. Adding a belt to your short dress can help elongate your torso and make your legs look longer. A wide belt placed at your natural waistline will create the illusion of longer legs.

Step 3: Layer with a long jacket or cardigan. Wearing a long jacket or cardigan over your short dress can create a more elongated look. The jacket or cardigan will cover your hips and thighs and make your legs look longer.

Step 4: Accessorize with a long necklace. A long necklace can help elongate your upper body and make your short dress look more proportional. Avoid wearing short necklaces as they will make your upper body look shorter.

Tips for Elongating a Short Dress

Tip 1: Choose the right fabric. Choosing the right fabric for your short dress can make all the difference. Look for fabrics that drape well and don't cling to your body. Avoid bulky fabrics that can make you look shorter and wider.

Tip 2: Avoid horizontal stripes. Horizontal stripes can make you look wider and shorter. Opt for vertical stripes or solid colors instead.

Tip 3: Hemline matters. The length of your short dress can affect how elongated you look. Choose a hemline that hits just above the knee or slightly below. Avoid hemlines that hit mid-thigh as they can make you look shorter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear flats with a short dress?

A: While high heels or wedges are the best option for elongating a short dress, you can still wear flats. Opt for pointed-toe flats or strappy sandals that show more skin to create a more elongated look.

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can wear a short dress to a formal event. Just make sure to choose a dress made from a formal fabric, such as silk or chiffon, and accessorize with elegant jewelry and high heels.

Hair Care Tips to Complete Your Look

Don't forget about your hair! A polished hairstyle can complete your elongated look. Here are some hair care tips to try:

- Straighten or curl your hair to add length and volume.

- Add a hair accessory, such as a headband or barrette, to elongate your face shape.

- Create a sleek ponytail or bun to elongate your neck.

With these simple tips and tricks, you can elongate a short dress and feel confident and comfortable in any situation. Remember to choose the right fabric, accessorize appropriately, and style your hair to complete your look.
