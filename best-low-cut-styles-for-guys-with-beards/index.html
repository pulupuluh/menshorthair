Best Low Cut Styles For Guys With Beards

Written by William Aug 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a guy with a beard looking for a low cut style that will complement your facial hair? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss the best low cut styles for guys with beards. From the buzz cut to the crew cut, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

A low cut hairstyle is a short haircut that is typically no longer than two inches in length. This type of hairstyle is perfect for guys with beards because it allows the beard to stand out and become the focal point of the look. Low cut styles are also low maintenance and easy to maintain, making them perfect for guys who are always on the go.

How to

Getting a low cut style is easy and can be done at home or at a barbershop. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect low cut:

  1. Decide on the length of your hair - a low cut is typically no longer than two inches.
  2. Use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
  3. Trim your beard to complement your low cut style.
  4. Use a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect low cut style:

  1. Wash your hair and beard with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  2. Decide on the length of your hair - a low cut is typically no longer than two inches.
  3. Use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
  4. Trim your beard to complement your low cut style. Use scissors or clippers to trim your beard to the desired length.
  5. Use a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and style as desired.
  6. Use a beard oil or balm to moisturize and condition your beard.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a low cut style:

  • Choose a style that complements the shape of your face.
  • Consult with your barber to determine the best length and style for your hair and beard.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Maintain your beard by trimming it regularly and using a beard oil or balm to keep it moisturized.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about low cut styles for guys with beards:

What is a low cut hairstyle?

A low cut hairstyle is a short haircut that is typically no longer than two inches in length.

What is the best low cut style for guys with beards?

The best low cut style for guys with beards depends on the shape of their face and the length of their beard. Some popular styles include the buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop.

Can I maintain a low cut style at home?

Yes, you can maintain a low cut style at home with clippers and a high-quality hair product.

Hair Care

To keep your hair and beard looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair and beard regularly with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  • Use a high-quality hair product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to moisturize and condition your beard.

Now that you know the best low cut styles for guys with beards, it's time to hit the barbershop and try out a new look. Whether you opt for a buzz cut or a crew cut, you're sure to turn heads with your stylish new look.
