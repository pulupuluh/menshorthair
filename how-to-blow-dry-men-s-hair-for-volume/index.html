How To Blow Dry Men s Hair For Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with fine or thinning hair, you may be looking for ways to add volume to your locks. One of the easiest and most effective ways to do this is by blow drying your hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to blow dry your hair for maximum volume, using techniques that are easy to follow even if you're new to styling your hair at home.

How to Blow Dry Men's Hair for Volume

Step 1: Start with Clean, Damp Hair

Before you begin blow drying your hair, make sure it's clean and damp. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry until it's no longer dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

To protect your hair from heat damage, apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair. This will help prevent split ends, breakage, and other damage that can be caused by blow drying.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

To make it easier to blow dry your hair, section it into manageable pieces. Start by dividing your hair into three sections: one at the crown of your head, one at the back, and one on each side.

Step 4: Begin Blow Drying

Starting at the back of your head, use a round brush to lift the hair and direct the airflow from your blow dryer down the hair shaft. Aim the nozzle of your blow dryer at the roots of your hair, then work your way down to the ends of your hair. Repeat this process on each section of your hair, working from the back to the front.

Step 5: Add Volume

To add even more volume to your hair, use your fingers to lift your hair away from your scalp as you blow dry. This will create more lift and body, giving your hair a fuller, more voluminous look.

Step 6: Finish with a Blast of Cool Air

Once you've finished blow drying your hair, switch your blow dryer to the cool setting and give your hair a blast of cool air. This will help set your style and add shine to your hair.

Tips for Blow Drying Men's Hair for Volume

- Use a round brush to lift your hair and direct the airflow from your blow dryer down the hair shaft.

- Section your hair into manageable pieces to make it easier to blow dry.

- Use your fingers to lift your hair away from your scalp as you blow dry to create more lift and body.

- Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style and add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can blow drying damage my hair?

A: Yes, blow drying can damage your hair if you use too much heat or don't use a heat protectant. To prevent damage, use a heat protectant spray or cream and don't use the highest heat setting on your blow dryer.

Q: How often should I blow dry my hair?

A: It's best to limit how often you blow dry your hair to prevent damage. Try to only blow dry your hair a few times a week and let it air dry the rest of the time.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

To keep your hair looking voluminous and healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant.

- Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can blow dry your hair for maximum volume and create a stylish, voluminous look that's perfect for any occasion.
