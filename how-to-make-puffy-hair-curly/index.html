How To Make Puffy Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 ¬∑ 8 min read

If you're someone with naturally puffy hair, you may feel like you're doomed to a life of frizzy locks. But fear not! With the right techniques and tools, you can transform your puffy hair into beautiful, curly locks. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to make puffy hair curly.

Description

Puffy hair can be caused by a number of factors, including humidity, genetics, and damage from heat styling or chemical treatments. While some people embrace their puffy locks, others prefer a more polished look. If you're in the latter camp, don't worry - there are ways to tame your hair and turn it into gorgeous, bouncy curls.

How to Make Puffy Hair Curly: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

Clean, moisturized hair is the foundation of any good hairstyle. To prepare your hair for curling, start by washing it with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using products with sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and contribute to puffiness. After washing, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your hair hydrated.

Step 2: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're going to be using heat tools to curl your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant spray or serum first. This will help minimize damage from the heat and keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Before you start curling, separate your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Choose Your Curling Tool

There are a variety of tools you can use to curl your hair, including curling irons, wands, and rollers. Experiment with different tools to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired curl pattern.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Start by curling the bottom section of your hair, working your way up to the top. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling tool, holding for a few seconds before releasing. To create a more natural look, alternate the direction of your curls and vary the size of the sections you curl.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled all of your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place. Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and contribute to puffiness.

Tips for Making Puffy Hair Curly

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve beautiful, curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls and minimize frizz.
  • Try using foam rollers or flexi rods to create curls overnight, without using heat.
  • Invest in a high-quality curling tool, which will help you achieve better results and minimize damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or curl-enhancing creams, to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQ

Can I make puffy hair curly without using heat?

Yes! There are a variety of techniques you can use to create curls without using heat, including foam rollers, flexi rods, and braids. Experiment with different methods to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

How often should I curl my hair?

It's best to limit heat styling as much as possible to minimize damage to your hair. Try to curl your hair no more than once or twice a week, and be sure to use a heat protectant product each time.

Hair Care for Puffy Hair

Lastly, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and minimize puffiness. Here are a few hair care tips for puffy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can contribute to damage and puffiness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products with sulfates or alcohol, which can dry out your hair and contribute to puffiness.
  • Consider getting a trim every few months to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and bouncy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to transform your puffy hair into beautiful, curly locks. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or just want to switch up your everyday look, curly hair is a fun and versatile option that anyone can rock.
