How To Cut And Style Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will transform your look? Short hair is a great option for anyone who wants to change things up. But, cutting and styling short hair can be tricky. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut and style short hair like a pro.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut and Style Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting and Styling Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Cut and Style Short Hair

Before you start cutting or styling your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. If you're planning to use styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, make sure you have those on hand as well.

Next, decide on the length and style you want. Short hair can be anything from a pixie cut to a bob, so choose a style that flatters your face shape and suits your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to cut and style short hair in a few simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will give you a clean canvas to work with.
  2. Section off your hair. Use hair clips to separate your hair into sections. Start by clipping the top section of your hair out of the way.
  3. Cut the sides and back. Use your scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time.
  4. Cut the top. Unclip the top section of your hair and use your scissors to cut it to the desired length. You can use a comb to help guide your scissors and create a more even cut.
  5. Style your hair. Once you've cut your hair, you can style it using a blow dryer, flat iron, or other styling tools. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Tips for Cutting and Styling Short Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your short hair:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape. If you're not sure what style will look best on you, ask your hairdresser for advice.
  • Use the right products. Short hair often requires different products than long hair. Look for products that are designed for short hair, like texturizing sprays and pomades.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Try out different looks to find one that works for you.

FAQs

How often should I cut my short hair?

It's recommended that you get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Can I still style my short hair if I have thin hair?

Absolutely! There are many styling techniques that can add volume and texture to fine or thin hair. Talk to your hairdresser about the best products and tools to use.

What if I don't like my short hair?

Don't worry! Hair grows back, and there are many ways to style short hair while you're waiting for it to grow out. Talk to your hairdresser about options like extensions or wigs.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different kind of maintenance than long hair. Here are a few tips for keeping your short hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly. Short hair can look greasy or oily more quickly than long hair, so it's important to keep it clean.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Short hair can be more prone to breakage, so it's important to use products that won't damage your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling. Using too much heat or product can damage your hair and make it look dull or lifeless. Try to limit your use of styling tools and products.

Now that you know how to cut and style short hair, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice and some patience, you can create a stylish and flattering look that will turn heads.
