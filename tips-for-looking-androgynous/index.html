Tips For Looking Androgynous How To Embrace Gender Neutral Style

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to embrace a gender-neutral style, you're not alone. Androgynous fashion has been on the rise in recent years, with more and more people embracing clothing and accessories that blur the lines between masculine and feminine. Whether you're non-binary, gender-nonconforming, or simply looking to experiment with your style, there are plenty of ways to look androgynous and feel confident in your skin. In this article, we'll share some tips for looking androgynous, including how to dress, accessorize, and style your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dress Androgynously
  • Accessorizing for a Gender-Neutral Look
  • Hair Care for an Androgynous Look
  • FAQ: Common Questions About Androgynous Fashion

How to Dress Androgynously

Dressing androgynously means wearing clothing that doesn't conform to traditional gender expectations. This can include items like oversized button-up shirts, tailored blazers, and loose-fitting pants. Here are some tips for achieving an androgynous look:

1. Embrace Neutral Colors

Neutral colors like black, white, gray, and beige are great for achieving a gender-neutral look. These colors are timeless and versatile, and they can be paired with a variety of different clothing items.

2. Experiment with Tailoring

Tailoring can be a great way to achieve a more masculine or feminine silhouette, depending on your preferences. For example, wearing a tailored blazer with a pair of loose-fitting pants can create a more masculine look, while wearing a fitted dress can create a more feminine look.

3. Mix and Match Clothing Items

Androgynous fashion is all about blurring the lines between masculine and feminine clothing items. Try mixing traditionally masculine items like button-up shirts and blazers with traditionally feminine items like skirts and dresses.

4. Pay Attention to Fit

One key element of androgynous fashion is paying attention to fit. Clothing items that are too tight or too loose can detract from the overall look. Aim for a fit that's comfortable and flattering.

Accessorizing for a Gender-Neutral Look

Accessories can also play a big role in achieving an androgynous look. Here are some tips for accessorizing in a gender-neutral way:

1. Choose Minimalist Jewelry

Minimalist jewelry like stud earrings, simple necklaces, and thin bracelets can add a nice touch to an androgynous outfit without detracting from the overall look.

2. Invest in a Gender-Neutral Watch

A gender-neutral watch can be a great accessory for an androgynous outfit. Look for watches with neutral colors and simple designs.

3. Experiment with Hats

Hats can be a fun way to add a touch of masculinity or femininity to an outfit. Try pairing a baseball cap with a dress, or a fedora with a button-up shirt.

Hair Care for an Androgynous Look

Your hair can also play a big role in achieving an androgynous look. Here are some tips for styling your hair in a gender-neutral way:

1. Experiment with Short Haircuts

Short haircuts like pixie cuts and buzz cuts can be a great way to achieve a more masculine look. If you're not ready to commit to a short haircut, try styling your hair in a way that mimics the look of short hair.

2. Try a Half-Up, Half-Down Hairstyle

A half-up, half-down hairstyle can be a great way to achieve a more gender-neutral look. This hairstyle involves pulling half of your hair up into a bun or ponytail, while leaving the other half down.

3. Experiment with Hair Accessories

Adding hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bows can be a fun way to add a touch of femininity to an androgynous outfit. Try experimenting with different styles and colors.

FAQ: Common Questions About Androgynous Fashion

Q: How can I make an androgynous outfit look more feminine?

A: Adding a touch of femininity to an androgynous outfit can be as simple as accessorizing with jewelry or adding a pop of color. Try pairing a neutral outfit with a bright scarf or a statement necklace.

Q: Can I wear makeup with an androgynous outfit?

A: Absolutely! Makeup can be a great way to express your personal style and add a touch of femininity to an androgynous outfit. Try experimenting with different makeup looks to find what works for you.

Q: Can I wear heels with an androgynous outfit?

A: Yes! Heels can be a great way to add a touch of femininity to an androgynous outfit. Try pairing a blazer and pants with a pair of heels for a more feminine look.

Conclusion

Looking androgynous is all about embracing a gender-neutral style and expressing yourself in a way that feels authentic. Whether you're experimenting with your style or fully embracing a non-binary identity, there are plenty of ways to look androgynous and feel confident in your skin. Use these tips as a starting point, and remember to have fun with your fashion choices!
