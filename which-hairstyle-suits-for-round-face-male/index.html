Which Hairstyle Suits for Round Face Male

If you have a round face, you may struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore the different hairstyles that suit round face males. Here's a table of content for your convenience:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by fullness and width, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. The jawline is usually soft and less defined. The goal when selecting a hairstyle for round face males is to create length and definition, and to avoid styles that add width to the face. Let's take a look at some hairstyles that suit round face males.

How to

When deciding which hairstyle suits for round face male, consider the length, texture, and style of the hair. The ideal length for round face males is medium to long, as shorter hairstyles can make the face appear wider. The hair texture should be thick and full, as this adds volume and definition to the hair. As for the style, it's best to avoid styles that add width to the face, such as a center part or a wide fringe.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for round face males:

  • The Side Part: Apply a styling product to wet hair, then create a side part with a comb. Blow-dry the hair, then use a straightening iron to create a smooth finish.
  • The Pompadour: Apply a volumizing product to wet hair, then blow-dry the hair upwards with a round brush. Use a comb to create a pompadour shape, then finish with a strong hold hairspray.
  • The Messy Quiff: Apply a texturizing product to dry hair, then use your fingers to create a messy quiff shape. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a hairstyle for round face males:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds length and definition to the face.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect one for you.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round face males:

  • What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face? You should avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as a center part or a wide fringe.
  • What is the ideal hair length for round face males? The ideal length is medium to long, as shorter hairstyles can make the face appear wider.
  • How can I create definition in my hair? Use a volumizing or texturizing product to add volume and definition to the hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
