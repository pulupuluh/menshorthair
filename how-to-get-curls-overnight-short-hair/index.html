How To Get Curls Overnight Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Having short hair is great, but sometimes you might want to switch up your look and add some curls. The good news is that you can achieve curls overnight without damaging your hair. In this article, we will show you how to get curls overnight short hair using simple methods.

Table of Content

  • How to Get Curls Overnight Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

There are different ways to get curls overnight short hair. Here are some methods to try:

1. Twist Curls

Twist curls are the easiest way to get curls overnight short hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a hair serum or oil to your hair to protect it from damage.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section tightly and secure it with a hair tie or bobby pin.
  5. Sleep with the twists in your hair.
  6. Unravel the twists in the morning and use your fingers to separate the curls.

Twist curls work best on damp or wet hair. You can also use a curling cream or mousse to enhance the curls.

2. Braided Curls

Braided curls are another way to get curls overnight short hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a hair serum or oil to your hair to protect it from damage.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Braid each section tightly and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. Sleep with the braids in your hair.
  6. Unravel the braids in the morning and use your fingers to separate the curls.

Braided curls work best on dry hair. You can also use a curling cream or mousse to enhance the curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your curls last longer:

  • Use a hair spray to hold the curls in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Can I use hot rollers to get curls overnight short hair?

No, it is not recommended to use hot rollers to get curls overnight short hair as it can damage your hair. It's best to use heatless methods like twist curls or braided curls.

How long do I have to leave the twists or braids in my hair?

You can leave the twists or braids in your hair overnight or for at least 4-6 hours to get the best results.

Can I use a diffuser to dry my hair before twisting or braiding it?

Yes, you can use a diffuser to dry your hair before twisting or braiding it. Just make sure your hair is not completely dry as it can cause frizz.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like hair dyes and relaxers.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to get curls overnight short hair, it's time to try it out and see which method works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage. Happy curling!
