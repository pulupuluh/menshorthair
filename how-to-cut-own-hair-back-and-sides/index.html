How To Cut Your Own Hair Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own hair back and sides in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. With this guide, you can save money and have the satisfaction of cutting your own hair.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Back and Sides

Cutting your own hair back and sides can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. The first thing you need to do is gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. Once you have these tools ready, it's time to start cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own hair back and sides:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely.
  2. Use the handheld mirror to view the back of your head.
  3. Take the hair on the sides of your head and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut it at the desired length. Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  5. Repeat step 4 on the other side of your head.
  6. For the back of your head, comb the hair straight down and use the handheld mirror to view it.
  7. Hold the hair with your fingers and cut it at the desired length. Again, start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  8. Continue to cut the hair in small sections until you have achieved the desired length.
  9. Use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back for a more natural look.
  10. Check your hair in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your own hair back and sides:

  • Start with longer lengths and gradually cut more if needed. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. It's better to spend more time cutting your hair than to make a mistake and have to fix it later.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections at a time for more control.
  • When cutting the back of your head, use the handheld mirror to view it from different angles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair back and sides:

  • How often should I cut my own hair? It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.
  • Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair? No, it's not recommended. Regular scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends. Use hair cutting scissors for the best results.
  • What should I do if I make a mistake? Don't panic! It's easy to fix minor mistakes. You can try blending the hair with the scissors or using clippers to create a shorter cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair back and sides, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons for a few days to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and style.
  • Use hair products like oils and serums to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair back and sides, you can save money and have the satisfaction of doing it yourself. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
