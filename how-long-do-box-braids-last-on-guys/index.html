How Long Do Box Braids Last On Guys

Written by William Aug 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're considering getting box braids, you're probably wondering how long they'll last. It's a common question, and one that's important to answer before you go through the trouble of getting them done. In this article, we'll explore how long box braids last on guys, and provide some tips for taking care of them so they last as long as possible.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Box Braids
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Box Braids
  • Tips for Maintaining Box Braids
  • FAQs

Description

Box braids are a protective hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped sections and braiding them from the roots to the ends. This style is popular among both men and women, and can be worn in a variety of lengths and thicknesses. The length of time that box braids last on guys can vary depending on a number of factors, including the length and thickness of the braids, the type of hair used, and the level of care taken to maintain them.

How to Get Box Braids

If you're interested in getting box braids, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in this style. You'll want to choose someone who has a good reputation and who uses high-quality hair extensions. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you'll need to decide on the length and thickness of the braids you want. Your stylist can help you make this decision based on your hair type and the look you're going for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Box Braids

1. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly before getting braided. This will help to remove any buildup or oils that could interfere with the braiding process.
2. Section your hair into small, square-shaped sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of the braids you want.
3. Attach hair extensions to your natural hair using a braiding technique. Your stylist will likely use a crochet hook or a latch hook to do this.
4. Braid the extensions from the roots to the ends of your hair. Your stylist will use a specific braiding technique to create the square-shaped sections.
5. Once all of the braids are complete, you can style them in a variety of ways. You can leave them down, pull them back into a ponytail, or create other styles using hair accessories.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids

If you want your box braids to last as long as possible, it's important to take good care of them. Here are some tips for maintaining your braids:

  • Wrap your hair at night with a silk scarf or bonnet to prevent friction and tangling
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your braids, as this can cause damage and frizzing
  • Keep your scalp moisturized with a light oil or leave-in conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils on your braids, as this can cause buildup and make them heavy
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long; most experts recommend taking them out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to the hair and scalp

FAQs

Q: How long do box braids last on guys?
A: The length of time that box braids last on guys can vary depending on a number of factors, but most experts recommend taking them out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to the hair and scalp.

Q: Can box braids be done on short hair?
A: Yes, box braids can be done on short hair, but the length of the braids will be limited by the length of the natural hair.

Q: Can I swim with box braids?
A: Yes, you can swim with box braids, but it's important to protect them from chlorine and saltwater. You can do this by wearing a swim cap or keeping your head above water.

Hair Care for Box Braids

When it comes to hair care for box braids, it's important to keep your scalp and hair moisturized. You can do this by using a light oil or leave-in conditioner, and by drinking plenty of water to hydrate your body from the inside out. You should also avoid using heavy products or oils on your braids, as this can cause buildup and make them heavy. Finally, be sure to take your braids out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to the hair and scalp.

So, how long do box braids last on guys? The answer is that it depends on a number of factors, but most experts recommend taking them out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to the hair and scalp. By following the tips in this article, you can help to ensure that your box braids last as long as possible and look great while doing it.
