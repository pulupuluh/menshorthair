How To Cut Short Pixie Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Feb 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to a short pixie cut. However, with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short pixie hairstyles.

Description

A short pixie cut is a trendy hairstyle that has been around for decades. It’s a low-maintenance haircut that can complement any face shape and hair texture. It’s also a great option for those who want to try something new or for those who want to get rid of damaged hair. The best part about this haircut is that it can be customized to fit your personal style.

How to Cut Short Pixie Hairstyles

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also important to have a clear idea of what you want your hair to look like. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure you dry your hair completely before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. Start at the back of your head and work your way towards the front. Clip each section of hair in place using hair clips.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start cutting your hair at the back of your head. Use your fingers to guide the scissors and cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure you don’t cut too much hair at once. Continue cutting until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you’ve finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Use your fingers to guide the scissors and cut the hair in a diagonal line. Follow the shape of your head and make sure the sides are symmetrical.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top of your head. Use your fingers to guide the scissors and cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure the length is even across the top of your head.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting all the sections, use a comb to blend the hair together. Check for any uneven spots and trim them accordingly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect short pixie cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up. It’s easier to cut more hair than to fix a mistake.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head. It’s important to make sure the hair is even all around.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, so it’s always good to have a second opinion.
  • Experiment with different styles. The beauty of a short pixie cut is that it can be styled in many different ways.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short pixie hairstyles:

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I cut my hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it’s curly. However, it’s important to keep in mind that the length will shrink when the hair dries.

Can I cut my hair if it’s thin?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it’s thin. A short pixie cut can actually make thin hair appear fuller and thicker.

Hair Care

After you’ve cut your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect short pixie cut. Remember to take your time and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles.
