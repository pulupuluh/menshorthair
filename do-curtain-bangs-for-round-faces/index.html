Do Curtain Bangs For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle that just doesn't seem to flatter your round face? Look no further than curtain bangs! This trendy and versatile haircut can work wonders for those with round faces, framing the face and accentuating features. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to do curtain bangs for round faces, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Do Curtain Bangs for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a versatile and face-framing haircut that can work for a variety of face shapes, including round faces. These bangs are called "curtain" because they are parted down the middle and hang on either side of the face, like curtains.

For those with round faces, curtain bangs can help elongate and slim the face by drawing attention to the cheekbones and jawline. They can also soften the features of a round face and add texture and movement to the hair.

How to Do Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

If you're ready to try out curtain bangs for your round face, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Using a comb, create a middle part down the center of your head.
  4. Divide the hair on each side of the part into two sections, creating a total of four sections.
  5. Starting with one section, hold the hair between your index and middle fingers at the point where you want to cut it.
  6. Cut the hair straight across using sharp scissors.
  7. Repeat on the other three sections.
  8. Using your fingers, separate the bangs and style them as desired.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Now that you've cut your curtain bangs, it's time to style them! Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new haircut:

  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and shape to your bangs.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that best flatters your face shape.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and movement to your bangs.
  • Consider adding layers or feathering to your bangs to soften the look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs for round faces:

Do curtain bangs work for all face shapes?

While curtain bangs can work for a variety of face shapes, they may not be the best choice for those with very round faces or very angular faces. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if curtain bangs will flatter your face shape.

What if I don't like my curtain bangs?

If you're not happy with your curtain bangs, don't worry! Bangs grow out relatively quickly, and there are plenty of ways to style them as they grow. Consider pinning them back or wearing a headband until they reach a length you're comfortable with.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and free of oil.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your bangs with hot tools.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent oil buildup.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your new curtain bangs in no time! Remember to consult with a hairstylist before making any major changes to your hair, and have fun experimenting with different styles and textures.
