How To Diffuse Wavy Hair To Make It Curly

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have wavy hair and want to achieve curly hair, you may have tried a lot of methods with disappointing results. However, using a diffuser can help transform your wavy hair into beautiful, defined curls. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to diffuse wavy hair to make it curly.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Diffuse Wavy Hair to Make it Curly
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Diffusing Wavy Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

The following steps will guide you on how to diffuse wavy hair to make it curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Wavy hair tends to be more prone to frizz, so using a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and smooth.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Defining Product

After washing your hair, apply a curl-defining product to your damp hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream that is specifically formulated to enhance curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer. A diffuser is a special attachment that helps to distribute heat more evenly, which can help to enhance your curls without causing frizz. Make sure the diffuser is securely attached to your hair dryer.

Step 4: Start Diffusing Your Hair

With your hair dryer on a low heat setting, start diffusing your hair. Place your hair into the diffuser bowl and gently scrunch your hair towards your scalp. Hold the diffuser in place for a few seconds before releasing. Continue this process until all of your hair has been diffused.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

Once your hair is fully diffused, finish with a hairspray to help hold your curls in place. Choose a lightweight, flexible hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your curls.

Tips for Diffusing Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you diffuse your wavy hair to make it curly:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to avoid frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before applying the curl-defining product to help keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a diffuser with a larger bowl to help enhance your curls.
  • Start diffusing your hair at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

FAQs

Can I diffuse my hair without a curl-defining product?

While it is possible to diffuse your hair without a curl-defining product, using a product specifically designed for curly hair can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Can I diffuse my hair every day?

It is not recommended to diffuse your hair every day as it can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit diffusing to once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls with your wavy hair. Remember to use a curl-defining product and a diffuser to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. With the right care and styling, your curly hair can look its best every day.
