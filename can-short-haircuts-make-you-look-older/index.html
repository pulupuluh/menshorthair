Can Short Haircuts Make You Look Older

Written by Robby Jul 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts have been in trend for decades, and there’s no denying that they can make you look chic, elegant, and even sexy. However, some people believe that short haircuts can make you look older than you actually are. In this article, we’ll explore whether or not this is true.

How to

The answer to the question, “Can short haircuts make you look older?” is both yes and no. It depends on various factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and the type of short haircut you opt for. Here are some tips on how to choose the right short haircut for your face:

Consider Your Face Shape

Short haircuts can accentuate certain features of your face. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob haircut can make your face look fuller. On the other hand, if you have a square face, a pixie cut can soften your jawline.

Think About Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also affect how your short haircut looks on you. If you have thin hair, a short bob haircut can make your hair look flat and lifeless. In contrast, if you have thick hair, a pixie cut can make your hair look voluminous and bouncy.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your short haircut can also make a difference in how old or young you look. If you opt for a super-short pixie cut, you may look edgy but also older. However, if you choose a chin-length bob, it can make you look youthful and fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair
  • Consult with your hairstylist to get their professional opinion
  • Experiment with different short haircuts to find the one that suits you best
  • Accessorize your short haircut with earrings, headbands, or scarves to add some personality

FAQ

Q. Can short haircuts make you look older if you’re over a certain age?

A. No, there’s no specific age where short haircuts automatically make you look older. It all depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Q. What’s the best short haircut for a round face?

A. A long bob or a lob is the best short haircut for a round face as it elongates the face and makes it look slimmer.

Q. Will a pixie cut make me look too masculine?

A. No, a pixie cut can look feminine and elegant if styled correctly. Ask your hairstylist to add some layers and texture to your pixie cut to soften your features.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf

In conclusion, short haircuts can make you look older or younger depending on various factors. The key is to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
