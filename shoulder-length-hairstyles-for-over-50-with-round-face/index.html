Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Over 50 With Round Face

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 50 and have a round face? Do you want to switch up your hairstyle to something more flattering? Look no further than shoulder length hairstyles! In this article, we will discuss the best shoulder length hairstyles for those over 50 with a round face, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Shoulder Length Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Shoulder Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for those who want a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle. It's long enough to style in a variety of ways, but not so long that it requires a lot of upkeep. For those over 50 with a round face, shoulder length hairstyles can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness.

How to Choose a Shoulder Length Hairstyle

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. For those with a round face, it's best to choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides sleek and straight. Some great shoulder length hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Long Bob
  • Layered Cut
  • Asymmetrical Bob
  • Side-Swept Bangs

It's also important to consider your hair type and texture when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help remove some of the weight and make styling easier. If you have fine hair, a blunt cut can help create the illusion of more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here's a simple guide to styling your shoulder length hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a shoulder length hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can! A low ponytail or a messy bun are both great options for a shoulder length hairstyle.

Q: Will a layered cut work for my fine hair?

A: Yes, a layered cut can work for fine hair. Just make sure to ask your stylist to remove weight from the bottom layers, which can make your hair appear even thinner.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a shoulder length hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can! Side-swept or blunt bangs can add dimension and frame your face.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too greasy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before blow drying to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, which can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and hairstyles, you're sure to find a shoulder length hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
