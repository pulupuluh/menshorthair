How to Have Oblong Face: Tips and Tricks in 2023

If you have an oblong face and want to enhance your features, you're in luck! In this blog post, we'll share tips and tricks on how to have an oblong face. From haircuts to makeup, we've got you covered.

How to Have Oblong Face

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and a narrow chin. If you want to enhance this shape, there are several ways to do so.

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to enhancing an oblong face. Opt for a cut that adds width to the sides of your face, such as layers, curls or waves. Avoid styles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate your face even further.

2. Experiment with Makeup

Makeup can also help enhance your oblong face. Use contouring to create shadows and add depth to your cheekbones. Highlight the center of your face to bring attention to your features. A bold lip can also draw attention away from the length of your face.

3. Wear the Right Accessories

Accessories can also help enhance your oblong face. Opt for earrings that add width to your face, such as hoops or studs. Avoid long, dangling earrings that can elongate your face even further.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to have an oblong face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can enhance your oblong face, you need to determine if you have this face shape. Take a selfie or stand in front of a mirror and observe the length and width of your face.

2. Choose the Right Haircut

Once you've determined your face shape, choose a haircut that adds width to your face. Layers, curls, and waves are great options.

3. Experiment with Makeup

Try contouring and highlighting to enhance your cheekbones and draw attention to the center of your face.

4. Wear the Right Accessories

Choose earrings that add width to your face, such as hoops or studs.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to enhance your oblong face:

1. Embrace Your Features

Don't try to hide your oblong face. Instead, embrace your features and use makeup and hairstyles to enhance them.

2. Avoid Styles that Elongate Your Face

Avoid hairstyles and accessories that elongate your face, such as long, straight hair and dangling earrings.

3. Choose Glasses Carefully

If you wear glasses, choose frames that add width to your face. Avoid frames that are too narrow or elongated.

FAQs

1. Can I still have long hair with an oblong face?

Yes, you can still have long hair with an oblong face. Just make sure to add layers or waves to add width to your face.

2. Can makeup really make a difference?

Yes, makeup can make a big difference in enhancing your features. Just be sure to use the right techniques and products.

Hair Care for Oblong Face

Here are some additional hair care tips for oblong face:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Use a volumizing shampoo to add fullness and body to your hair.

2. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Use hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add width to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can enhance your oblong face and feel confident in your features. Remember to embrace your natural beauty and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and makeup looks.
