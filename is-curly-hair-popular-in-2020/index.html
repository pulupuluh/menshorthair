Is Curly Hair Popular In 2020

Written by Robby May 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair has always been a popular trend among women, but is it still in style in 2020? In this article, we will explore the popularity of curly hair in recent years, how to achieve curly hair, and some tips for taking care of your curly locks.

Description:

Curly hair has been a popular trend for generations, but it has gained more popularity in recent years. With social media and the internet, there is an abundance of curly hair inspiration and tutorials available to anyone who wants to embrace their natural curls.

How to Achieve Curly Hair:

If you have naturally curly hair, then you are already ahead of the game. However, if you have straight hair and want to achieve curly hair, there are a few ways to do so.

1. Use a Curling Iron:

A curling iron is one of the easiest ways to achieve curly hair. Simply section your hair and curl each section with a curling iron. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Use a Perm:

A perm is a chemical treatment that can give you permanent curls. This method is not recommended for everyone, as it can be damaging to your hair if not done properly.

Step by Step:

If you have naturally curly hair, then there are a few steps you can take to enhance your curls.

1. Use a Curl Enhancing Product:

There are many curl enhancing products available, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. These products can help define your curls and reduce frizz.

2. Use a Diffuser:

A diffuser is an attachment for your hair dryer that can help enhance your curls. Simply attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and dry your hair using a low heat setting.

Tips:

Here are a few tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb:

A wide-toothed comb is less damaging to your hair than a brush. Use it to detangle your hair when it is wet to avoid breakage.

2. Avoid Heat:

Heat can be damaging to your curly hair. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

FAQ:

Q: Is curly hair popular in 2020?

A: Yes, curly hair is still very popular in 2020.

Q: Can I achieve curly hair if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve curly hair using a curling iron or a perm.

Hair Care:

It is important to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo:

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it is important to use a moisturizing shampoo to keep it hydrated.

2. Deep Condition:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, curly hair is still very popular in 2020. Whether you have naturally curly hair or want to achieve curly hair, there are many ways to embrace this trend. Remember to take care of your curly locks to keep them healthy and looking their best.
