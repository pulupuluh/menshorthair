How To Tone Your Hair At Home After Bleaching

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to tone your hair after bleaching it? Toning your hair is a crucial step in achieving the perfect blonde shade. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of toning your hair at home after bleaching. We'll cover everything from how to do it, step-by-step, to tips for maintaining your new color. Let's get started!

Description

Toning your hair after bleaching is essential to achieve the perfect blonde shade. When you bleach your hair, it can often turn brassy or yellow. Toning helps neutralize those unwanted tones and leaves you with a beautiful, bright blonde color.

How to Tone Your Hair at Home After Bleaching

The process of toning your hair at home after bleaching can be daunting, but with the right tools and knowledge, it's easy to do. Here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin the toning process, you'll need to gather your supplies. You'll need:

  • Purple shampoo or toner
  • Bowl
  • Brush or comb
  • Gloves
  • Towel

Step 2: Prepping Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Towel dry your hair until it's damp but not too wet. Put on your gloves to protect your hands.

Step 3: Mixing Your Toner

Follow the instructions on the toner or purple shampoo to mix it with developer or water. Mix the toner in a bowl until it's smooth and creamy.

Step 4: Applying the Toner

Section your hair and apply the toner to each section, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate each section evenly. Leave the toner on for the recommended time on the packaging.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Rinse out the toner with cool water until the water runs clear. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage.

Tips

To maintain your newly toned hair, follow these tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo once a week to keep your blonde color from turning brassy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent further damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQ

Can I tone my hair at home?

Yes, you can tone your hair at home. Just make sure to follow the instructions carefully and use the right products.

How often should I tone my hair?

It depends on your hair and how often you wash it. Generally, you should tone your hair once a month to maintain your blonde color.

Can I use purple shampoo instead of toner?

Yes, purple shampoo can be used as a toner. Just make sure to leave it on for the recommended time and rinse it out thoroughly.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after bleaching and toning it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools and harsh chemicals on your hair to prevent further damage. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Toning your hair at home after bleaching is easy with the right tools and knowledge. Follow these steps, tips, and FAQs to achieve the perfect blonde shade. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
