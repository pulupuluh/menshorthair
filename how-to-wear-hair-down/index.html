How To Wear Hair Down A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Aug 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Wearing your hair down is a great way to show off your natural beauty. Whether you have long, flowing locks or a short, sassy bob, there are plenty of ways to wear your hair down that can make you look and feel amazing. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to create a range of different hairstyles that will keep you looking stylish and chic no matter what the occasion.

How to Wear Hair Down: Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you create some of the most popular ways to wear your hair down:

The Loose Waves

If you want to create a relaxed, bohemian look, loose waves are the perfect choice. Here's how to create them:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry it gently.
  2. Apply a heat protection spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair, working in sections.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your waves in place.

This style is perfect for a casual day out, a music festival, or a romantic date night.

The Sleek and Straight

If you want a more sophisticated look, sleek and straight hair is the way to go. Here's how to create it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry it gently.
  2. Apply a heat protection spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, working in sections.
  4. Finish with a spritz of shine spray to give your hair a glossy finish.

This style is perfect for a formal event, a job interview, or a night out on the town.

The Messy Bun

If you want to keep your hair out of your face but still want a relaxed, casual look, the messy bun is perfect. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and securing it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist your ponytail around the elastic band to create a messy bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some loose strands around your face.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your bun in place.

This style is perfect for a lazy day at home, a day at the beach, or a yoga class.

Tips for Wearing Your Hair Down

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your hair when you wear it down:

  • Choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  • Invest in good quality styling tools and products.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and in good condition.

FAQ: How to Wear Hair Down

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair down:

  • Q: Can I wear my hair down if it's thin or fine?
  • A: Yes, but you may need to use volumizing products to give your hair more body and fullness.
  • Q: How often should I wash my hair if I'm wearing it down?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people may need to wash their hair daily, while others can go several days between washes.
  • Q: Can I wear my hair down if it's curly?
  • A: Absolutely! Just make sure you use products that help define and control your curls.

Hair Care for Wearing Your Hair Down

When you wear your hair down, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protection spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can wear your hair down with confidence and style. Whether you prefer loose waves, sleek and straight locks, or a messy bun, there's a perfect way to wear your hair down for every occasion.
