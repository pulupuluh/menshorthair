Does Your Arm Hair Grow Back Thicker If You Wax It

Description

Many people have concerns about waxing their arm hair. One of the most common concerns is whether the hair will grow back thicker after waxing. This is a valid concern, as nobody wants to have thicker arm hair than they started with! In this article, we'll explore the question of whether your arm hair grows back thicker if you wax it.

How To

Before we dive into the question of whether your arm hair grows back thicker after waxing, let's briefly discuss how to wax your arm hair in the first place.

The easiest way to wax your arm hair is to use a pre-made wax strip. These can be found at most drugstores and are relatively inexpensive. Simply warm the wax strip between your hands, apply it to your arm where you want to remove hair, and then quickly rip it off in the opposite direction of hair growth. Repeat this process until you've removed all the hair you want to.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to waxing your arm hair:

  1. Warm the wax strip between your hands.
  2. Apply the wax strip to your arm where you want to remove hair.
  3. Quickly rip the wax strip off in the opposite direction of hair growth.
  4. Repeat until all desired hair is removed.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your arm hair waxing experience as smooth as possible:

  • Exfoliate your skin before waxing to remove any dead skin cells.
  • Apply a soothing lotion or aloe vera gel after waxing to calm your skin.
  • Avoid waxing if you have sunburned skin or any open cuts or wounds on your arm.

FAQ

Now, let's get to the question at hand: does your arm hair grow back thicker if you wax it?

The answer is no, your arm hair does not grow back thicker if you wax it. This is actually a common myth. When you wax your arm hair, you're removing the hair from the root. This can make the hair appear thinner and less noticeable when it grows back. However, the hair is still growing back at the same rate and thickness as it was before you waxed it.

Hair Care

While waxing your arm hair won't make it grow back thicker, it's still important to take care of your hair after waxing. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid using any harsh chemicals on your arm hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Moisturize your arm hair regularly to keep it soft and healthy.
  • If you notice any irritation or redness after waxing, apply a soothing lotion or aloe vera gel.

So, there you have it. Waxing your arm hair won't make it grow back thicker, so feel free to wax away without any worries!
