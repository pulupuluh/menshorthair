Men s Hair Short On Sides Long On Top Curly A Complete Guide

If you're a man who loves to experiment with different hairstyles, then you may have heard of the popular trend of men's hair short on sides long on top curly. This style is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair while keeping it manageable.

Description

The men's hair short on sides long on top curly style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, and long hair on the top. The hair on top is usually curly or wavy, and can be styled in different ways to create volume and texture. This style can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types, and can be worn in both formal and casual settings.

How to Get the Look

The men's hair short on sides long on top curly style requires a bit of maintenance, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the look you want. Here are some tips on how to get the look:

1. Choose the Right Cut

The first step to getting the men's hair short on sides long on top curly style is to choose the right cut. You'll need to have your hair cut short on the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer. Talk to your stylist about the best cut for your face shape and hair type.

2. Invest in the Right Products

To keep your hair looking its best, you'll need to invest in the right products. Look for a good quality shampoo and conditioner for curly hair, as well as a styling product that will help define your curls and add volume.

3. Learn to Style Your Hair

Learning to style your hair is key to achieving the men's hair short on sides long on top curly look. Experiment with different techniques, such as scrunching your hair with your hands or using a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance your curls. You can also try using a curling iron or hot rollers to add more defined curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's hair short on sides long on top curly look:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends and the areas where you want to add volume.

3. Style Your Hair

Using a diffuser on your hair dryer, dry your hair, scrunching it with your hands to enhance your curls. You can also use a curling iron or hot rollers to add more defined curls to your hair.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hair short on sides long on top curly style:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair looking its best, you'll need to trim it regularly. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to maintaining your hairstyle. Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner for curly hair, as well as a styling product that will help define your curls and add volume.

3. Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Instead, focus on using gentle styling techniques that enhance your curls without causing damage.

FAQ

1. Is the men's hair short on sides long on top curly style suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can be customized to suit different face shapes. Talk to your stylist about the best cut for your face shape.

2. How often should I trim my hair to maintain the style?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use heat protectant products when using hot styling tools, such as hair dryers, curling irons, and flat irons. This will help prevent heat damage to your hair.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is important for maintaining healthy hair. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein-rich foods.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the men's hair short on sides long on top curly style and keep your hair looking its best.
