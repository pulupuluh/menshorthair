How To Curl Men s Hair With Blow Dryer A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man looking to add some texture and volume to your hair, then you might want to consider curling your hair with a blow dryer. Not only is it an easy and fast way to change up your look, but it's also a great way to achieve a natural-looking curl without using any heat tools. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to curl men's hair with a blow dryer step by step.

How to Curl Men's Hair with Blow Dryer

Before we start, you'll need the following tools:

  • A high-quality blow dryer
  • A brush (preferably a round brush)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair spray

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp, but not completely dry.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant spray

Before you start blow drying, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from any potential heat damage.

Step 3: Section your hair

Use a comb to section your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the easier it will be to curl your hair.

Step 4: Start blow drying

Take your round brush and start blow drying your hair. Use the brush to lift your hair up and away from your scalp, and aim the blow dryer downwards.

Step 5: Curl your hair

Once you've blow dried a section of hair, wrap it around the brush and hold it for a few seconds. Then, release the hair and move on to the next section.

Step 6: Repeat

Continue blow drying and curling your hair until you've done your entire head.

Step 7: Apply hairspray

Once you've finished, apply a light mist of hairspray to your hair to help hold the curl.

Tips for Curling Men's Hair with Blow Dryer

  • Don't use too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a high-quality blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to help distribute heat evenly.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can make your hair look unnatural.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you practice, the easier it will be to curl your hair with a blow dryer.

FAQs

Can men use a curling iron to curl their hair?

Yes, men can use a curling iron to curl their hair, but it's not as easy as using a blow dryer. A curling iron requires more skill and practice to use properly.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last for several hours, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

How often should I curl my hair?

You can curl your hair as often as you like, but it's important to use heat protectant spray and not apply too much heat to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to curl your hair with a blow dryer like a pro! Remember to take your time, use the right tools and products, and practice regularly to achieve the perfect look.
