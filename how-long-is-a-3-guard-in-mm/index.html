How Long Is A 3 Guard In Mm

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to trim your hair using a 3 guard but you're not sure how long it is in millimeters? Fear not, as we break down everything you need to know about the length of a 3 guard and how to use it for your hair care routine.

Description

A 3 guard is a clipper guard that is commonly used by barbers and hair stylists to achieve a short to medium length haircut. This guard leaves approximately 3/8 of an inch of hair on the scalp when used with a hair clipper. In millimeters, a 3 guard is equivalent to 9.5mm.

How to Use a 3 Guard

If you're planning to use a 3 guard to trim your hair, you will need to have a hair clipper that is compatible with the guard. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Attach the 3 guard to your hair clipper.
  2. Start at the bottom of your head and work upwards, trimming small sections of hair at a time.
  3. Make sure that you move the clipper against the natural direction of hair growth to achieve an even cut.
  4. Trim the sides of your head to match the length of the top.
  5. Use a mirror to check your work and make any necessary adjustments.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step by step guide on how to use a 3 guard:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting.
  2. Attach the 3 guard to your hair clipper, making sure that it is securely in place.
  3. Turn on the hair clipper and start at the bottom of your head, working your way upwards.
  4. Move the clipper against the natural direction of hair growth to achieve an even cut.
  5. Trim the sides of your head to match the length of the top.
  6. Use a mirror to check your work and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Once you're satisfied with the length, remove the guard from your hair clipper and clean it thoroughly.
  8. Apply a hair serum or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a 3 guard:

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a comb to guide the clipper and ensure an even cut.
  • Don't forget to clean your hair clipper and guard after use to maintain its longevity.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 3 guard:

What is the difference between a 2 and 3 guard?

A 2 guard leaves approximately 1/4 inch of hair on the scalp, while a 3 guard leaves around 3/8 of an inch of hair.

Can I use a 3 guard on long hair?

A 3 guard is generally used for short to medium length hair. If you have long hair, it is better to use a higher guard or scissors to trim your hair.

How often should I clean my hair clipper and guard?

You should clean your hair clipper and guard after every use to prevent bacteria buildup and maintain its longevity.

Hair Care

Using a 3 guard is a great way to achieve a short to medium length haircut at home. However, it's important to take care of your hair by using a quality shampoo, conditioner, and hair serum or oil. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and trim it regularly to maintain its health and vitality.

Now that you know how long is a 3 guard in mm and how to use it for your hair care routine, you can confidently achieve the desired length for your hair. Happy trimming!
