How To Angle Cut The Front Of Your Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Want to switch things up and add some edge to your look? One way to achieve this is by angle cutting the front of your hair. This technique gives a stylish, modern look that can complement any face shape. In this article, we'll teach you how to angle cut the front of your hair and provide tips and tricks to make sure you get the desired result.

  • How to Angle Cut the Front of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Angle Cut the Front of Your Hair

Angle cutting the front of your hair is a simple process that can be done at home with the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an accurate cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, create a vertical part in the center of your head. Then, take a section of hair from the front and clip it away from the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Hold the section of hair you clipped in front of your face and determine the length you want to cut it. Using your fingers as a guide, create a diagonal line from the length you want to the bottom of your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Using your scissors, cut along the diagonal line you created. Make sure to cut in a straight line and not at an angle. Repeat this step with the other side of your hair.

Step 5: Blend the Cut

Using your comb, blend the cut sections of your hair with the rest of your hair. This will ensure that the cut looks natural and not choppy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to angle cut the front of your hair:

  1. Wash your hair.
  2. Section your hair.
  3. Determine the length.
  4. Cut your hair.
  5. Blend the cut.

Tips for Angle Cutting Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when angle cutting the front of your hair:

  • Start with a small section of hair and cut a little at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't undo a cut that's too short.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Angle the cut slightly towards your face to create a flattering frame for your face.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair appears longer, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

FAQs

How often should I angle cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your look. On average, you can angle cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I angle cut my own hair?

Yes, you can angle cut your own hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

Can I angle cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can angle cut curly hair. However, it's best to get it done by a professional stylist who can work with the natural texture of your hair.

Hair Care After Angle Cutting

After angle cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair and damage the cut.

By following these tips and steps, you can angle cut the front of your hair and achieve a sleek, modern look that's perfect for any occasion.
