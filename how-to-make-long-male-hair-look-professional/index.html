How To Make Long Male Hair Look Professional A Guide For Men

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Long hair can be a great look for men, but it can also be difficult to maintain and style in a way that looks professional. If you're struggling with how to make your long hair look appropriate for the workplace or other formal settings, this guide is for you. Here, we'll cover everything from basic hair care to styling tips and tricks.

Description

Many men with long hair struggle with finding a professional look that fits their personal style. There are a lot of factors to consider, including the texture and thickness of your hair, your face shape, and the type of work you do. This guide will help you achieve a look that is both professional and true to your personal style.

How to Make Long Male Hair Look Professional: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

If you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep it looking neat and healthy. A good stylist can help you choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, while also keeping it professional. Be sure to communicate your goals and preferences clearly to your stylist.

Step 2: Keep Your Hair Clean and Healthy

Long hair can be more prone to damage and breakage than short hair, so it's important to take good care of it. This means washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week. You should also avoid using harsh styling products or tools that can damage your hair.

Step 3: Choose a Style that Fits Your Face Shape

There are many different styles you can choose from when it comes to long hair, but not all of them will look good on every face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head. If you have a square jawline, you may want to avoid styles that are too long and straight.

Step 4: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try out different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. You can try braiding your hair, pulling it back into a ponytail or bun, or letting it hang loose. You can also experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or clips, to add some variety to your look.

Tips for Making Long Male Hair Look Professional

Tip 1: Keep it Neat and Tidy

One of the keys to making long hair look professional is keeping it neat and tidy. This means avoiding messy styles that look unkempt or disheveled. You can use a comb or brush to smooth out any tangles or knots, and use a bit of styling product to keep it in place.

Tip 2: Avoid Extreme Colors or Cuts

If you're trying to maintain a professional look, it's generally best to avoid extreme colors or cuts. Stick with natural shades that complement your skin tone, and avoid any styles that are too edgy or trendy. This will help you maintain a timeless and classic look that works in any setting.

Tip 3: Keep Your Facial Hair in Check

If you have facial hair, it's important to keep it neat and tidy as well. This means trimming it regularly, and avoiding any styles that look too wild or unkempt. You should also make sure that your facial hair complements your hairstyle, rather than competing with it.

FAQs About Making Long Male Hair Look Professional

Q: Can I wear my hair down to work?

A: It depends on the type of work you do and the dress code at your workplace. If you work in a more formal setting, you may want to avoid wearing your hair down, or at least make sure it's styled in a way that looks neat and professional.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's generally recommended that you get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and how quickly it grows.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my hair?

A: Yes, you can use heat styling tools on your hair, but it's important to use them carefully to avoid damaging your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray, and avoid using the highest heat setting.

Hair Care Tips for Long Male Hair

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Long hair can be more prone to dryness and damage than short hair, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Use a Hair Mask or Deep Conditioner Once a Week

A hair mask or deep conditioner can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, preventing damage and breakage. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil.

Tip 3: Avoid Using Harsh Styling Products or Tools

Harsh styling products or tools can damage your hair over time, so it's important to use them sparingly. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.

Conclusion

By following these tips and tricks, you can make long male hair look professional and polished, whether you're heading to the office or a formal event. Remember to keep your hair clean and healthy, experiment with different styles, and choose a look that complements your personal style and face shape. With a little bit of effort and care, you can achieve a look that is both professional and true to yourself.
