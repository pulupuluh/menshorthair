How To Give Yourself A Haircut Male Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking of giving yourself a haircut but don't know where to start? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men are opting for DIY haircuts these days, and with the help of YouTube, it's easier than ever to learn how to do it yourself. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to give yourself a haircut male YouTube-style, from start to finish.

Description

With the rise of YouTube, it's now possible to learn just about anything online, including how to give yourself a haircut. This guide will take you through the process step-by-step, from choosing the right tools to cleaning up the mess afterwards.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have clippers, you can use them instead of scissors, but they're not necessary.

Once you have your tools ready, it's time to decide on the length and style of your haircut. You can find inspiration on YouTube or by browsing photos online. Make sure to choose a style that's achievable with your hair type and length.

Next, it's time to get started. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and will ensure a more even cut.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots. This will make it easier to see where you need to cut.

Step 3: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair from the back and work your way forward. Use the comb to lift sections of hair and cut them to the desired length. Be sure to cut small sections at a time, as it's easier to control the length this way.

Step 4: Use the mirror

Use the mirror to check your progress as you go. This will help you see any areas that need more attention.

Step 5: Clean up

Once you're finished cutting your hair, it's time to clean up. Use a vacuum or broom to sweep up any hair clippings, and wipe down the surfaces with a damp cloth.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great DIY haircut:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use the comb to lift sections of hair and cut them at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Be patient - cutting your own hair takes time and practice.
  • If you're not happy with the results, don't worry - hair grows back!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving yourself a haircut:

Is it easy to give yourself a haircut?

It can be challenging to cut your own hair, especially if you're new to it. However, with practice and patience, it's definitely achievable.

What tools do I need to give myself a haircut?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Clippers are optional.

How do I choose a style for my haircut?

You can find inspiration on YouTube or by browsing photos online. Choose a style that's achievable with your hair type and length.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after you've given yourself a haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a hairbrush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Consider using hair products, such as styling gel or hairspray, to keep your hair looking great.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you should be able to give yourself a great DIY haircut in no time. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
