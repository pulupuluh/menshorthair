How To Prevent Hair Thinning On Accutane

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are currently taking Accutane, you may have noticed that your hair is becoming thinner. This is a common side effect of the medication, but there are steps you can take to prevent it. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to prevent hair thinning on Accutane, including a step-by-step guide and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo
  • Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools
  • Step 3: Eat a Balanced Diet
  • Step 4: Take Supplements
  • Step 5: Be Gentle with Your Hair
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Thinning on Accutane
  • FAQs
  • Conclusion

Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

The first step to preventing hair thinning on Accutane is to use a gentle shampoo. Look for a shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals and sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause further damage. Instead, opt for a shampoo that contains natural ingredients, such as aloe vera and coconut oil.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. To prevent hair thinning on Accutane, it's best to avoid these tools altogether. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat at a low setting.

Step 3: Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you are consuming enough protein, as this is the building block of hair. Additionally, be sure to eat plenty of fruits and vegetables, as these provide your body with essential vitamins and minerals that are necessary for healthy hair.

Step 4: Take Supplements

There are certain supplements that can help prevent hair thinning on Accutane. Biotin is a popular supplement that is known to promote healthy hair growth. Additionally, omega-3 fatty acids and vitamin D are also essential for healthy hair.

Step 5: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Finally, it's important to be gentle with your hair. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, and be sure to use a wide-toothed comb when detangling. Additionally, avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, as these can cause damage to your hair follicles.

Tips for Preventing Hair Thinning on Accutane

  • Wash your hair every other day instead of every day to allow your hair's natural oils to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause further damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Can Accutane cause hair loss?

Yes, Accutane can cause hair loss as a side effect. However, not everyone who takes Accutane will experience hair loss.

Will my hair grow back after I stop taking Accutane?

Yes, your hair should grow back after you stop taking Accutane. However, it may take several months for your hair to fully recover.

Can I dye my hair while taking Accutane?

It's best to avoid dyeing your hair while taking Accutane, as the medication can make your hair more fragile and prone to damage.

Conclusion

Preventing hair thinning on Accutane is possible with the right steps. By using a gentle shampoo, avoiding heat styling tools, eating a balanced diet, taking supplements, and being gentle with your hair, you can keep your hair healthy and prevent thinning. Additionally, following the tips provided in this article can help you maintain healthy hair while taking Accutane.
