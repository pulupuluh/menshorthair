How To Do A High Ponytail With Long Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it looking neat and tidy. One of the easiest ways to achieve a polished look is by putting your hair up in a high ponytail. This classic style is perfect for any occasion, from a day at the office to a night out with friends. In this article, we'll show you how to do a high ponytail with long hair step by step, as well as provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Ponytail with Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for the Perfect High Ponytail
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Do a High Ponytail with Long Hair

The first step to achieving a perfect high ponytail is to make sure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Next, gather all of your hair up towards the crown of your head. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways, and then secure your hair with an elastic band. Make sure that the elastic is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Once your ponytail is secured, take a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band. Use bobby pins to secure the end of the wrapped hair to the base of your ponytail, making sure that the pins are hidden from view.

Finally, use a hairspray to set your style in place and smooth down any remaining flyaways. Voila! You now have a perfect high ponytail.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair up towards the crown of your head.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure the end of the wrapped hair to the base of your ponytail.
  6. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  7. Use a hairspray to set your style in place and smooth down any remaining flyaways.

Tips for the Perfect High Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a good quality elastic band that won't break or damage your hair.
  • Wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it and add a polished look.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Use hairspray to set your style in place and prevent frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a high ponytail with long hair:

Can I do a high ponytail with curly hair?

Yes, you can. Just make sure that you use a good quality elastic band that won't damage your hair, and wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to add a polished look. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

What if my hair is too thick for a high ponytail?

If your hair is too thick for a high ponytail, you can try dividing your hair into two sections and creating two smaller ponytails. Secure each ponytail with an elastic band, and then wrap a small section of hair around each elastic band to add a polished look.

How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

The best way to prevent tangles is to brush your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a detangling spray or leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair smooth and tangle-free.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your long hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or wearing your hair in tight styles for extended periods of time, as this can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect high ponytail with your long hair in no time. Whether you're headed to work, a night out with friends, or just running errands, this classic style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
