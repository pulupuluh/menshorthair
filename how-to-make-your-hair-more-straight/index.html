How To Make Your Hair More Straight Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Oliver May 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you want to know how to make your hair more straight? Whether you have naturally curly hair or just want to switch up your style, there are plenty of ways to get straight hair. In this article, we’ll go through some tips, step by step guide, and hair care to help you achieve sleek, straight locks.

Table of Contents

  • Tips
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Tips

Before we dive into the step by step guide, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before applying heat to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or hair straightener.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to make your hair more manageable.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils and promote shine.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know some tips, let’s go through the step by step guide to straightening your hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a straightening shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to make your hair more manageable and easier to straighten.

2. Apply heat protectant spray

Before applying heat to your hair, use a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and smoothness. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

4. Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces using hair clips. This will make it easier to straighten each section and ensure that you don’t miss any areas.

5. Use a flat iron

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, use a flat iron to straighten each piece. Clamp the flat iron down on a small section of hair near the roots, then slowly slide it down to the ends. Repeat until all sections are straightened.

6. Finish with a serum

To add shine and tame any flyaways, finish with a small amount of hair serum. Rub it between your palms and smooth it over the surface of your hair.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to straighten your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It’s best to avoid straightening your hair on a daily basis, as this can cause damage. Instead, try to limit it to once or twice a week.

Q: Can I straighten my hair without using heat?

A: Yes, there are non-heat methods for straightening your hair, such as using a hair straightening brush or using a hair wrap method. However, these methods may not be as effective as using a flat iron.

Q: Will straightening my hair make it grow faster?

A: No, straightening your hair will not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by genetics and other factors, such as diet and lifestyle.

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it’s damaged?

A: It’s best to avoid straightening your hair if it’s damaged, as this can cause further damage. Instead, focus on repairing your hair with deep conditioning treatments and avoiding heat styling until it’s healthy again.

By following these tips and step by step guide, you can achieve sleek, straight locks. Just remember to take care of your hair properly to maintain its health and shine.
