Description

The men's hair longer on top shorter on sides hairstyle has been a popular trend for years. It's a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. The longer top allows for more styling options, while the shorter sides keep the hair looking neat and tidy. This style is perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, yet stylish look.

How to Achieve the Look

While this style can be achieved with any hair type, it works best for those with thick, straight hair. To achieve the look, you'll need to visit a skilled barber or stylist who can create the right cut for you. The cut should involve leaving the top of your hair longer than the rest, with the sides and back tapered or faded. The length and style of the top can vary based on your preference, from a classic slicked-back look to a more modern, textured style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the men's hair longer on top shorter on sides look:

  1. Visit a skilled barber or stylist who can create the right cut for you.
  2. Decide on the length and style of the top of your hair.
  3. Ask for a taper or fade on the sides and back.
  4. Style your hair using a product that works well for your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To maintain this style, you'll need to visit the barber or stylist regularly to keep the sides and back short. You'll also need to use a product that works well for your hair type to keep the top looking neat and styled. Here are some tips to help you maintain the look:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hairdryer and a brush or comb to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Consider getting a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep the sides and back looking neat.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hair longer on top shorter on sides style:

Can this style be worn by anyone?

Yes, this style can be worn by anyone, but it's best for those with thick, straight hair.

How often should I visit the barber or stylist?

You should visit the barber or stylist every 2-4 weeks for a touch-up.

What type of product should I use?

The type of product you should use depends on your hair type. A matte pomade works well for thick hair, while a lightweight cream is better for finer hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking and feeling healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and the steps outlined above, you can achieve and maintain the men's hair longer on top shorter on sides style. It's a classic yet modern look that's perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, yet stylish hairstyle.
