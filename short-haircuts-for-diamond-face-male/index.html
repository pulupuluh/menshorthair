Short Haircuts For Diamond Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a man with a diamond-shaped face and looking for the perfect short haircut that complements your features? Look no further, as we have compiled a complete guide to short haircuts for diamond face male. In this article, we will provide you with tips, steps, and hair care advice to ensure you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Diamond face shapes are characterized by narrow foreheads and jawlines, with the cheekbones being the widest part of the face. Short haircuts that add width to the forehead and minimize the width of the cheekbones are perfect for diamond face male. These haircuts should also accentuate the jawline, making it appear more defined.

How to

When it comes to short haircuts for diamond face male, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. This will help balance out the width of the cheekbones.
  • Shorter hairstyles that are tapered on the sides and back are also great for diamond face shapes.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair, such as with layers or choppy ends, to create a more interesting look.

Step by step

Here are some short haircuts for diamond face male to consider:

1. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a simple, low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for diamond face male. It adds volume to the top of the head and minimizes the width of the cheekbones. This haircut also accentuates the jawline, making it appear more defined.

2. Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short haircut that is tapered on the sides and back. This style adds volume to the top of the head and creates a more balanced look for diamond face male. It also accentuates the jawline and adds definition to the face.

3. French Crop

The French crop is a short, textured haircut that is perfect for diamond face male. It adds volume to the top of the head and minimizes the width of the cheekbones. This haircut also accentuates the jawline, making it appear more defined. The textured top adds interest to the style and creates a more modern look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for diamond face male:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut that complements your features.
  • Consider adding color to your hair to create a more interesting look.
  • Use hair products to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims.

FAQ

What other hairstyles are good for diamond face male?

Other hairstyles that are good for diamond face male include the side part, textured quiff, and messy fringe.

Can I still style my short haircut?

Yes, you can still style your short haircut. Use hair products, such as wax or pomade, to add texture and volume to your hair.

How often should I get my haircut?

You should get your haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and use hair products sparingly to avoid buildup. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and protect your hair from the sun and chlorine. Regular trims are also important to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you have a complete guide to short haircuts for diamond face male, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut that complements your features and use the tips and advice provided to maintain your new look.
