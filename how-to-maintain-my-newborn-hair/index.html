How To Maintain My Newborn Hair Tips And Faq

Written by William Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a new parent, you may be wondering how to maintain your newborn's hair. While it may seem daunting, it's important to establish a hair care routine early on to promote healthy growth and prevent any discomfort for your little one. In this guide, we'll provide tips and answer common questions on how to maintain your newborn's hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Maintain My Newborn Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Newborn Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Products for Newborns

How to Maintain My Newborn Hair

Before diving into specific tips and products, it's important to understand the basics of how to maintain your newborn's hair. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Gently wash your newborn's hair once or twice a week
  • Use a mild shampoo specifically designed for infants
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your baby's hair, using a soft-bristled brush or wide-toothed comb
  • Don't use any hair styling products, as they can irritate your baby's delicate scalp

By following these basic steps, you'll be able to keep your baby's hair clean and healthy.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to maintain your newborn's hair:

  1. Fill a basin or sink with lukewarm water
  2. Wet your baby's hair with the water, being careful not to get any water in their eyes
  3. Apply a small amount of infant shampoo to your hand and lather it up
  4. Gently massage the shampoo into your baby's scalp with your fingertips
  5. Rinse the shampoo out of your baby's hair with clean water
  6. Wrap your baby's head in a soft towel and gently pat their hair dry
  7. Once their hair is dry, comb through it with a soft-bristled brush or wide-toothed comb to remove any tangles

Repeat this process once or twice a week, depending on how dirty your baby's hair gets.

Tips for Maintaining Newborn Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your newborn's hair:

  • Use a baby hat or hood to protect your baby's head from the sun and wind
  • Don't use adult hair care products on your baby's hair, as they can be too harsh and irritating
  • If your baby develops cradle cap (a type of dandruff), gently massage their scalp with a baby brush and use a special shampoo formulated for cradle cap
  • Don't put your baby's hair in tight ponytails or braids, as they can be uncomfortable for your little one

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about maintaining newborn hair:

When should I start washing my baby's hair?

You can start washing your baby's hair once or twice a week from birth, using a mild infant shampoo.

What kind of shampoo should I use on my baby's hair?

Use a shampoo specifically designed for infants, which will be mild and gentle on your baby's delicate scalp.

How often should I brush my baby's hair?

You can brush your baby's hair once a day with a soft-bristled brush or wide-toothed comb to remove any tangles.

Hair Care Products for Newborns

Here are some recommended hair care products for newborns:

  • Johnson's Baby Shampoo
  • Mustela Foam Shampoo for Newborns
  • Aveeno Baby Wash and Shampoo
  • BabyBum Brush for Cradle Cap

Remember to always check the ingredients of any product you use on your baby's hair, and avoid any that contain harsh chemicals or fragrances.

Conclusion

Maintaining your newborn's hair doesn't have to be complicated. By following a few simple steps and using gentle, baby-specific products, you can keep your little one's hair healthy and clean. Remember to be gentle when washing and brushing, and don't hesitate to reach out to your pediatrician if you have any concerns.
