How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly Permanently A Guide For Black Girls

Written by Daniel Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Description | How to | Step by Step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

If you're a black girl with relaxed hair, you might be wondering how to make your hair naturally curly permanently. While relaxing your hair can make it easier to manage, it can also damage your hair and make it harder to achieve the curls you want. Luckily, there are a few things you can do to make your hair naturally curly permanently, without causing damage.

How to

The first step to making your hair naturally curly permanently is to stop relaxing it. Relaxing your hair chemically straightens it, which can damage your hair follicles and make it harder for your hair to grow back curly. Instead, focus on natural hair care techniques that encourage your hair to grow back curly.

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Dry hair is more prone to breakage and damage, which can make it harder to achieve the curls you want. To keep your hair healthy and moisturized, use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week. You can also use natural oils like coconut oil or argan oil to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Detangle Your Hair Gently

When you're trying to grow your hair back curly, it's important to detangle it gently to avoid breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to separate your hair, and start at the ends, working your way up to the roots.

3. Use Curl-Enhancing Products

To help your hair grow back curly, use curl-enhancing products like curling creams, gels, and mousses. These products help define your natural curls and make them more visible. Look for products that are specifically designed for black hair to get the best results.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it harder to grow back curly. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

Step by Step

If you're not sure where to start, here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair naturally curly permanently:

  1. Stop relaxing your hair.
  2. Moisturize your hair regularly.
  3. Detangle your hair gently.
  4. Use curl-enhancing products.
  5. Avoid heat styling tools.
  6. Be patient! It can take several months for your hair to grow back curly.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your hair naturally curly permanently:

  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a satin pillowcase or using a satin scarf or bonnet.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to help your hair grow strong and healthy.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about making your hair naturally curly permanently:

Q: Can I still straighten my hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you want to. However, it's important to avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.

Q: How long will it take for my hair to grow back curly?

A: It can take several months for your hair to grow back curly, so be patient and stick to your natural hair care routine.

Q: Can I use hair extensions or wigs while my hair is growing back curly?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions or wigs while your hair is growing back curly. Just be sure to take good care of your natural hair underneath.

Hair Care

Here are a few additional tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but don't over-wash it. Too much washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors that can dry it out.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can make your hair naturally curly permanently without causing damage. Remember to be patient and stick to your natural hair care routine, and you'll soon have the curly hair of your dreams!
