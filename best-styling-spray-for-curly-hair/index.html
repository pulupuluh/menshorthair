Best Styling Spray For Curly Hair Top Picks For 2023

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're blessed with curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. It's not easy to find products that work without leaving your hair feeling heavy or crunchy. That's where styling sprays come in. A good styling spray can help define your curls, tame frizz, and add shine without weighing your hair down. In this post, we'll be sharing our top picks for the best styling spray for curly hair in 2023.

  • How to Use Styling Spray for Curly Hair

Description

Styling sprays for curly hair typically fall into two categories: those that enhance your curls and those that tame frizz. Some sprays do both. The best styling sprays for curly hair will enhance your curls without making them feel heavy, crunchy, or greasy. They'll also help keep frizz under control, leaving you with defined, bouncy curls that last all day.

How to Use Styling Spray for Curly Hair

To use styling spray for curly hair, start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. Towel dry your hair so that it's damp but not dripping wet. Then, spray the styling spray onto your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Styling Spray for Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to using styling spray for curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair so that it's damp but not dripping wet.
  3. Shake the styling spray well and hold it about 6 inches away from your head.
  4. Spray the product onto your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  5. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to speed up the process.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently scrunch your curls to add volume and definition.

Tips for Using Styling Spray on Curly Hair

Here are some tips for using styling spray on curly hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make your curls look limp.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt your curls and make them frizzy.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about using styling spray on curly hair:

Q: Can I use styling spray on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can use styling spray on dry hair, but it's best to use it on damp hair for the best results.

Q: Will styling spray make my hair crunchy?

A: Not if you use the right product. Look for styling sprays that are designed for curly hair and don't contain alcohol, which can make your hair feel crunchy.

Q: How often should I use styling spray on my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have dry hair, you may only need to use styling spray once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to use it more often.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use products that are designed for curly hair, as these are formulated to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt your curls and make them frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.

In conclusion, the best styling spray for curly hair will help enhance your curls and tame frizz without leaving your hair feeling heavy or crunchy. Use our top picks and tips to help you achieve defined, bouncy curls that last all day. Happy styling!
