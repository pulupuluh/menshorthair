How Do I Find A Good Hair Colorist

Written by Bardi Mar 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, finding a good hair colorist is key. But with so many salons and stylists out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding a hair colorist that will give you the look you want, and provide tips on how to maintain your new color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find a Good Hair Colorist
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing a Hair Colorist
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Find a Good Hair Colorist

Before you start your search, it's important to determine what you're looking for. Do you want a natural look, a bold statement color, or something in between? Once you've identified your desired outcome, you can begin your search.

Here are a few ways to find a good hair colorist:

1. Ask for Recommendations

Ask friends, family, and coworkers who have great hair color who they go to. If you see someone with a color you love while you're out and about, don't be afraid to ask them where they got it done. Word of mouth is a powerful tool.

2. Research Online

Check out online reviews and ratings for salons and stylists in your area. Look for pictures of their work to get an idea of their style and expertise. Don't forget to check out their social media pages, as many stylists showcase their work there.

3. Check Out Local Salons

Take a walk or drive around your neighborhood and visit local salons. Check out their prices and services, and ask to see pictures of their work. Most salons have portfolios of their stylists' work, so don't be afraid to ask.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to find a good hair colorist, here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

1. Research Stylists in Your Area

Start by researching stylists in your area. Look at their websites and social media pages to get an idea of their style and expertise. Make a list of your top choices.

2. Check Reviews

Read reviews from previous clients to see what they have to say about the stylist's work.

3. Schedule a Consultation

Once you've narrowed down your list, schedule a consultation with each stylist. This will give you a chance to meet them in person, discuss your desired outcome, and get an estimate for the cost.

4. Look at Their Portfolio

Ask to see their portfolio of previous work. This will give you an idea of their style and whether it matches what you're looking for.

5. Ask Questions

Don't be afraid to ask questions. Ask about their experience with your hair type and desired color, as well as any concerns you may have.

6. Make Your Decision

After meeting with each stylist, make your decision based on their expertise, experience, and overall vibe. Choose the stylist that you feel most comfortable with, and who you believe can give you the look you want.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Colorist

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colorist:

1. Look for Experience

Choose a stylist who has experience with your hair type and desired color. Look for someone who has been in the business for a while, and who has a portfolio of previous work to show you.

2. Consider Price

Price is an important factor to consider when choosing a hair colorist. Look for someone who fits within your budget, but don't sacrifice quality for cost.

3. Check Their Availability

Make sure the stylist you choose is available when you need them. Some stylists may have long waiting lists, so make sure to book your appointment well in advance.

4. Ask About Maintenance

Ask your stylist about maintenance for your new color. Will you need touch-ups? How often should you come in for a trim?

FAQs

1. How often should I get my hair colored?

It depends on the type of color and your hair type. Generally, you should get your hair colored every 4-6 weeks.

2. How can I maintain my new color?

Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoid excessive heat styling, and protect your hair from the sun. Your stylist can provide additional tips for maintaining your new color.

3. How much should I expect to pay for hair color?

The cost of hair color varies depending on the stylist and the type of color you choose. Expect to pay anywhere from $50-$200 or more.

Hair Care Tips

After you've found a good hair colorist and have your new color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

1. Use Color-Safe Products

Use shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair to prevent fading.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and choosing a good hair colorist, you can achieve the look you want and keep your hair looking great.
