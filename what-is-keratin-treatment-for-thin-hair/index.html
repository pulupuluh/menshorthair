What Is Keratin Treatment For Thin Hair

Written by Luffy May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Keratin treatment has become one of the most popular hair treatments for women who want to improve the texture and strength of their hair. This treatment is especially beneficial for women with thin hair who want to add volume and thickness to their hair. In this article, we will discuss what keratin treatment is and how it can benefit thin hair.

Description

Keratin treatment is a hair treatment that uses keratin, a protein that is naturally present in hair, skin, and nails. This treatment involves applying a keratin solution to the hair and then sealing it in with heat. The heat helps the keratin to penetrate the hair shaft, which helps to strengthen and smooth the hair. This treatment can last up to six months, depending on the hair type and how often the hair is washed.

How to

Before getting a keratin treatment, it is important to consult with a stylist who is experienced with this treatment. The stylist will evaluate your hair to determine if this treatment is right for you. If it is, the stylist will wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and then apply the keratin solution to your hair. The keratin solution will be left on your hair for a certain amount of time, depending on your hair type. After the solution is rinsed out, the stylist will blow-dry your hair and then seal in the keratin with a flat iron.

Step by Step

The following are the steps involved in getting a keratin treatment:

Step 1: Consultation

Consult with a stylist who is experienced with keratin treatments to determine if this treatment is right for you.

Step 2: Shampoo

The stylist will wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.

Step 3: Apply Keratin Solution

The stylist will apply the keratin solution to your hair and leave it on for a certain amount of time, depending on your hair type.

Step 4: Rinse

The stylist will rinse the keratin solution out of your hair.

Step 5: Blow Dry

The stylist will blow-dry your hair.

Step 6: Seal in Keratin

The stylist will seal in the keratin with a flat iron.

Tips

The following are some tips to keep in mind when getting a keratin treatment:

  • Consult with a stylist who is experienced with keratin treatments to determine if this treatment is right for you.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner after getting a keratin treatment.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a keratin treatment.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair.

FAQ

The following are some frequently asked questions about keratin treatment:

Is keratin treatment safe for thin hair?

Yes, keratin treatment is safe for thin hair. In fact, it can help to add volume and thickness to thin hair.

How long does keratin treatment last?

Keratin treatment can last up to six months, depending on the hair type and how often the hair is washed.

Can I color my hair after getting a keratin treatment?

It is best to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after getting a keratin treatment.

Hair Care

After getting a keratin treatment, it is important to take good care of your hair to maintain the results. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and avoid using hair products that contain alcohol. It is also important to avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a keratin treatment. With proper care, your hair can remain smooth, shiny, and healthy-looking for up to six months.
