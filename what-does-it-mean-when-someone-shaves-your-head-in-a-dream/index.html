Interpreting your dream is a personal process. Here are some steps that you can take to interpret your dream:

Step 1: Write Down Your Dream

Write down your dream as soon as you wake up. Try to remember as many details as possible.

Tips for Dealing with the Meaning of Your Dream

Here are some tips for dealing with the meaning of your dream:

Tip 1: Don't Panic

Tip 2: Journal
