How To Make Wavy Hair Curly Without Products

Written by Aprilia Feb 16, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair and want to switch up your look, you may be wondering how to make your hair curly without using products. Fortunately, there are several techniques you can use to achieve curly locks without relying on styling products. In this article, we’ll cover how to make wavy hair curly without products, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Wavy Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Making Wavy Hair Curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Wavy Hair Curly

Before we get started, it’s important to note that wavy hair and curly hair are not the same thing. While wavy hair has a slight curl pattern and may have some loose spirals, curly hair has a more defined and tighter curl pattern. That being said, there are several techniques you can use to make your wavy hair curlier without using products:

1. Twist your hair

One of the easiest ways to make wavy hair curlier is to twist your hair while it’s damp. Start by dividing your hair into small sections and twisting each section tightly. Once you’ve twisted all of your hair, let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the twists to reveal bouncy, curly hair.

2. Braid your hair

Braiding your hair is another great technique for creating curls without using products. Start by dividing your hair into small sections and braiding each section tightly. Once you’ve braided all of your hair, let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the braids to reveal soft, curly hair.

3. Use a curling iron

If you have a curling iron, you can use it to create curls in your wavy hair. Start by dividing your hair into small sections and wrapping each section around the curling iron barrel. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until you’ve curled all of your hair. Once your hair is curled, you can use your fingers to gently separate the curls for a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know how to make wavy hair curly without products, here are step-by-step instructions for each technique:

Twist Method

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section tightly.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the twists to reveal bouncy, curly hair.

Braid Method

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Braid each section tightly.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently unravel the braids to reveal soft, curly hair.

Curling Iron Method

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Wrap each section around the curling iron barrel.
  5. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl.
  6. Repeat this process until you’ve curled all of your hair.
  7. Once your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls for a more natural look.

Tips for Making Wavy Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to make your wavy hair curly:

  • Start with clean, damp hair to help the curls hold their shape.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help speed up the drying process and create more defined curls.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Be gentle when unraveling twists or braids to avoid frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate curls for a more natural look.

FAQs

Can you make wavy hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can make wavy hair curly without heat by using techniques like twisting or braiding your hair while it’s damp. These methods allow your hair to air dry, which is a gentler alternative to using heat styling tools.

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type, the technique you used to create the curls, and how well you maintain your curls. Generally, curls created without heat or products may not last as long as those created with heat or styling products.

How can I maintain my curly hair?

To maintain your curly hair, be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancer to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, it’s important to take care of your locks to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancer to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
