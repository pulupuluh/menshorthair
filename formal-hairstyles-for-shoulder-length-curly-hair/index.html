Formal Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair that falls just below your shoulders, you may find it challenging to style your hair for formal events. However, there are plenty of gorgeous formal hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair that you can try. In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step tutorial on how to create some stunning formal hairstyles for your curly hair. We will also provide you with some tips on how to take care of your curly hair, so it looks its best for any formal event.

Description

Shoulder length curly hair can be both bouncy and elegant, but it can be tricky to style for formal events. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can create stunning hairstyles that will turn heads. Whether you are attending a wedding, a formal dinner, or a prom, these hairstyles will add a touch of glamour to your look.

How to

Hairstyle 1: Half-Up Half-Down Curly Updo

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their curls while still keeping their hair out of their face. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail, leaving some curls loose around your face.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the bun, securing it with a bobby pin.
  5. Use a curling wand to add some more curls to the loose hair around your face.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

This half-up half-down curly updo is effortless but chic, making it perfect for any formal event.

Hairstyle 2: Curly Side Bun

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to create an elegant and sophisticated look. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair to one side.
  2. Gather your hair to one side and create a low ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use a curling wand to add some more curls to the loose hair around your face.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and twist it around your finger to create a small curl.
  6. Secure the curl to the side of your head with a bobby pin.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

This curly side bun is timeless and elegant, making it perfect for any formal event.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create the half-up half-down curly updo:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail, leaving some curls loose around your face.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the bun, securing it with a bobby pin.
  5. Use a curling wand to add some more curls to the loose hair around your face.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to help define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Invest in a good quality diffuser for your hair dryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a curling wand to add some more curls to your hair if needed.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my curly hair down for a formal event?

A: Absolutely! You can wear your curly hair down for a formal event, but make sure to define your curls with a styling product and use a curling wand to add some more curls if needed.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in your curly hair, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and invest in a good quality diffuser for your hair dryer.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires some extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips on how to take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

With these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking its best for any formal event.
