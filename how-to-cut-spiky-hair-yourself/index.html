How To Cut Spiky Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to try something new with your hair? Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. In this article, we will show you how to cut spiky hair yourself. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced haircutter, this guide will help you achieve the spiky hairstyle you desire.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Spiky hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. Spiky hair is achieved by cutting the hair short and then styling it with gel or wax to create spikes. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look.

How To

To cut spiky hair yourself, you will need the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or wax

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to cut spiky hair yourself:

  1. Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly.
  2. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to a short length. Leave the hair on top of your head longer.
  3. Use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  4. Apply hair gel or wax to your hair and use your fingers to create spikes. Start at the front of your head and work your way back.
  5. Use the comb to refine the spikes and make them look more polished.
  6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting spiky hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to get a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut.
  • Experiment with different hair gels or waxes to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting spiky hair:

Can I cut spiky hair with just scissors?

While it is possible to cut spiky hair with just scissors, clippers are recommended to get an even cut on the sides and back of your head.

What length should I cut my hair for spiky hair?

The length you cut your hair for spiky hair depends on your personal preference. Typically, the hair on the sides and back of your head should be cut short, while the hair on top of your head should be left longer.

How often should I cut my spiky hair?

You should cut your spiky hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your spiky hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your spiky hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair gel or wax, as this can cause buildup on your scalp.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Visit a professional stylist every few months for a trim or to try a new style.

Cutting spiky hair yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the spiky hairstyle you desire. Follow the steps in this guide and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you. Happy cutting!
