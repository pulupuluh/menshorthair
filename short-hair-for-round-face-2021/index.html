Short Hair For Round Face 2021 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Feb 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what kind of short hairstyle would suit you best. Fortunately, there are plenty of options to choose from, ranging from pixie cuts to bobs. In this post, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces in 2021. We'll also share some tips and tricks for maintaining your new hairstyle and keeping your hair healthy.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the right cut for your face shape. As a general rule, you'll want to look for styles that add length to your face and create the illusion of more angular features. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in determining which short hairstyle will look best on you. For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to avoid very short cuts that require a lot of styling to look good. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut could be a great choice.

Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling. If you're always on the go and don't have a lot of time to fuss with your hair, you might want to opt for a low-maintenance cut that requires minimal styling.

Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist can be a great resource when it comes to choosing the right short haircut for your face shape. They can help you identify your face shape and recommend styles that will flatter your features. Be sure to bring in pictures of styles you like, and don't be afraid to ask for advice.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces in 2021

Now that you have a better idea of what to look for in a short haircut, let's explore some of the best styles for round faces in 2021.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can look great on round faces. To make this style work, opt for a cut that's longer on top and shorter on the sides. This will help elongate your face and create more angular features. You can also add layers or bangs to add texture and depth to the style.

Bob

A bob is another great option for round faces. To make this style work, go for a cut that's chin-length or longer. This will help elongate your face and create more balance. You can also add layers or bangs to add texture and movement to the style.

Short Shag

The short shag is a trendy and versatile style that can work well on round faces. To make this style work, ask your stylist to cut choppy layers throughout your hair. This will help create texture and movement, while also adding volume to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've chosen a short haircut that works for your face shape, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

Invest in Quality Hair Products

Using high-quality hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and be sure to use a heat protectant if you style your hair with hot tools.

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape and style. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Experiment with Styling

Short haircuts offer plenty of styling options, so don't be afraid to experiment with different looks. You can try adding volume with a round brush, creating texture with a texturizing spray, or adding waves with a curling iron.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! While you might not be able to pull your hair back into a ponytail, there are plenty of ways to style short hair in an updo. You can try a half-up style, a messy bun, or even a braided updo.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right short haircut for your round face can take some trial and error, but with a little experimentation and the right styling techniques, you can find a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to consult with your stylist, take good care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different looks!
