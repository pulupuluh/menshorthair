Description

Layering shoulder length curly hair is an excellent way to add texture and movement to your hair. The layers will give your hair a more defined shape and make it easier to manage. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to layer your shoulder length curly hair. You will also find tips and hair care advice to keep your curly locks healthy and vibrant.


How to Layer Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Layering shoulder length curly hair is not as difficult as it may seem. You can either cut your hair yourself or go to a professional stylist. Here are the steps to follow:


Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. You can use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.


Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.


Step 3: Cut your hair

Start cutting your hair from the back section. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut your hair at an angle. The angle will help to create layers. Cut each section of your hair, making sure the layers are even.


Step 4: Blend the layers

Use a pair of thinning shears to blend the layers. This will create a more natural look and remove any harsh lines.


Step 5: Style your hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance your curls or use a curling iron to create loose waves.


Tips

  • Do not cut your hair when it is wet. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it is best to cut it when it is dry.
  • Use hair products specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I layer my curly hair at home?

Yes, you can layer your curly hair at home. However, it is recommended that you seek the help of a professional stylist, especially if you have never cut your hair before.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

What type of scissors should I use to cut my curly hair?

Use a pair of sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Do not use regular scissors as they can damage your hair.


Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. Do not rub your hair with a regular towel as it can cause frizz.
