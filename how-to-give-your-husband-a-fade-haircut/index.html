How To Give Your Husband A Fade Haircut

Written by Frank May 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for your husband's haircut? Well, why not give him a fade haircut at home? It's easier than you think and with a little practice, you'll be able to give him a professional-looking haircut. In this article, we'll show you how to give your husband a fade haircut step-by-step.

Description

A fade haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back and gradually blending it into longer hair on the top. This creates a "fading" effect and gives a stylish and modern look to the haircut.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to give your husband a fade haircut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the fade. You can choose a high, mid, or low fade depending on your preference. A high fade starts at the temples, a mid fade starts at the ears, and a low fade starts at the neckline.

Step 2: Start with the Clippers

Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a lower guard to create the fading effect. Use the comb to blend the hair between the different guard lengths.

Step 3: Blend with Scissors

Once you've finished with the clippers, use the scissors to blend the hair between the top and the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths of hair.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top to the desired length. You can cut it straight across or at an angle for a more modern look.

Step 5: Clean Up

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up any stray hairs and make sure the haircut is even all around.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a spray bottle to wet the hair before cutting to make it easier to work with.
  • Use the mirror to check the back of the head and make sure the haircut is even.
  • Take your time and be patient. It may take a few tries to get it right.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving a fade haircut:

How often should you give a fade haircut?

It depends on how fast your husband's hair grows and how he likes the length. Generally, a fade haircut should be given every 2-4 weeks.

What if I mess up?

Don't worry! Hair grows back and mistakes can be fixed. If you're not confident, start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually.

Can I use regular scissors?

No, it's best to use hair cutting scissors as they are sharper and designed for cutting hair.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of your husband's hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim hair regularly to maintain the style.

Now that you know how to give your husband a fade haircut, you can save time and money by doing it at home. With practice, you'll be able to give him a professional-looking haircut that he'll love.
