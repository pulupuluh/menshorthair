Best Haircut For Round Face Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't suit your round face? Finding the perfect haircut for round face male can be tricky, but not impossible. With the right tips and tricks, you can easily find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the perfect haircut for round face male, it's essential to understand how to determine your face shape. Take a measuring tape and measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the widest part of your forehead.
  • Cheekbones: Measure the widest part of your cheekbones.
  • Jawline: Measure the widest part of your jawline.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape. If your cheekbones and face length are equal and your jawline is rounded, you have a round face.

Perfect Haircut for Round Face Male

The perfect haircut for round face male is one that adds length to your face and creates the illusion of a more angular jawline. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round face men:

1. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that suits almost all face shapes. It adds length to your face and creates a more defined jawline. To achieve this style, part your hair to one side and use a comb to create a sleek look.

2. High Fade

The high fade is a modern hairstyle that looks great on round face men. It creates a more angular jawline by tapering the hair on the sides and back while leaving the hair on top longer. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

3. Pompadour

The pompadour is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape. For round face men, a pompadour with a tapered back and sides and a longer top can add length to your face and create a more defined jawline. Use a hair product to create a sleek and stylish look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Once you have found the perfect haircut for round face male, it's essential to maintain your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a hair product to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQ

What hairstyles should round face men avoid?

Round face men should avoid hairstyles that add width to their face, such as a full fringe or a buzz cut. They should also avoid hairstyles that have too much volume on top or too much hair on the sides.

Can round face men have long hair?

Yes, round face men can have long hair, but it's essential to keep it well-maintained and styled. Long hair can add length to your face and create a more defined jawline.

What hair products should round face men use?

Round face men should use hair products that add volume and texture to their hair, such as a styling mousse or a hair wax. These products can help create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect haircut for round face male can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can easily find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your features. Remember to maintain your hair regularly and use the right hair products to keep it looking great.
