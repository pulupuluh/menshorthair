Best Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Daniel Feb 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't suit your heart-shaped face? Well, worry no more! We have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces that will make you look stunningly beautiful. Keep reading to find out which hairstyles suit you best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces are those that balance out the features and give the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Short haircuts that frame the face and add volume to the crown area are perfect for heart-shaped faces.

How To

Before you decide on a short haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. Heart-shaped faces look great with short haircuts that add volume to the crown area and create width around the jawline. Layered bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical haircuts are some of the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces.

Step By Step

1. Layered Bob: A layered bob with side-swept bangs is perfect for heart-shaped faces. The layers add volume to the crown area and create width around the jawline, while the side-swept bangs soften the forehead.

2. Pixie Cut: A pixie cut with side-swept bangs is another great short haircut for heart-shaped faces. The short hair draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while the bangs soften the forehead.

3. Asymmetrical Haircut: An asymmetrical haircut with a deep side part is perfect for heart-shaped faces. The deep side part creates volume on one side of the face, balancing out the features.

Tips

When getting a short haircut, it's crucial to communicate with your hairstylist and let them know what you want. They will be able to recommend the best short haircut for your heart-shaped face and hair texture.

Always use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. Also, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown area.

FAQ

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off a bob haircut?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces look great with layered bob haircuts that add volume to the crown area and create width around the jawline.

Q: What is the best hair color for heart-shaped faces?

A: The best hair color for heart-shaped faces is one that complements your skin tone. Warm tones like golden blonde and copper look great on heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Also, use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces are those that balance out the features and create width around the jawline. Layered bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical haircuts are some of the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces. Remember to communicate with your hairstylist and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
