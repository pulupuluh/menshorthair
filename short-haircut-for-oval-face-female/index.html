Short Haircut For Oval Face Female Best Hairstyles In 2023

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want a change? Why not opt for a short haircut that flatters your oval face shape? Short haircuts are versatile and easy to maintain, making them popular among women of all ages. In this article, we will guide you through the best short haircuts for oval face females in 2023.

Table of Contents:

Description

Description:

Oval face shape is considered the most balanced and proportional shape among all face shapes. It has a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Short haircuts for oval face females should accentuate their features and add volume to the crown area. The best short haircuts for this face shape are bob, pixie, and lob.

How to:

Before going for a short haircut, consider the texture of your hair and the thickness of your strands. Short haircuts work best for fine or medium hair. If you have thick hair, you can opt for a layered bob or pixie haircut to reduce the bulk. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the best short haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step:

Step 1: Choose the right short haircut for your face shape.

Step 2: Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it.

Step 3: Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling wand to style your hair according to your desired look.

Step 5: Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips:

1. Experiment with different short haircuts to find the best one that suits you.

2. Use a dry shampoo to add volume to your hair after washing it.

3. Opt for a side parting to create an illusion of a narrower forehead.

4. Add layers to your short haircut to create movement and texture.

FAQ:

Q: Can oval face females wear bangs with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can opt for side-swept or blunt bangs that flatter your face shape.

Q: Is it easy to maintain a short haircut?

A: Yes, short haircuts are easy to maintain and require minimal styling.

Hair Care:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent hair damage. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your short haircut. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for oval face females in 2023, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and transform your look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
