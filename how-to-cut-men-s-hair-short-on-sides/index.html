How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a simple and stylish haircut, cutting your hair short on the sides is a great option. This classic style is easy to maintain and looks great on men of all ages. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this look at home.

Description

Cutting men's hair short on the sides involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head to a shorter length than the hair on top. This creates a neat and tidy appearance that's easy to style and maintain.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short on Sides

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools for the job. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you're not confident in your cutting abilities, consider asking a friend or family member to help you.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a clean cut.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide how short you want your hair to be on the sides. A good rule of thumb is to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if needed.

Step 4: Begin Cutting

Start by using the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Use a longer guard for a less dramatic cut or a shorter guard for a more dramatic cut.

Once you've trimmed the hair with clippers, use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the cut is even. Make any necessary adjustments until you're happy with the results.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut on the sides:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you go.
  • Blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides for a natural look.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member for help if you're not confident in your cutting abilities.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair short on the sides:

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how neat you want it to look. As a general rule, aim to cut your hair at least once a month.

Q: Can I cut my hair short on the sides with scissors?

A: While it's possible to cut your hair short on the sides with scissors, it's easier and more precise to use clippers.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking neat and tidy, consider using a styling product like pomade or wax. These products can help you achieve a sleek and polished look. Additionally, be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly to promote healthy growth and prevent dryness or breakage.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short on the sides, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for any occasion.
