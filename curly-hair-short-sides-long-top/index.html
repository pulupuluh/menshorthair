Curly Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and low-maintenance, then the curly hair short sides long top look might just be what you need. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. With its short sides and long, curly top, it's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Description

The curly hair short sides long top hairstyle is a modern take on the classic undercut. It features shaved or short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer, curly hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it generally falls between 2 to 6 inches. This hairstyle is perfect for those with natural curls, but it can also be achieved with the help of a curling iron or styling products.

How To

The curly hair short sides long top hairstyle can be achieved in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally curly, skip to step 2. If not, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls. A mousse or curl-enhancing cream works best.
  3. Use a hair clipper to trim the sides and back of your head. You can choose the length you prefer, but a fade or undercut is recommended.
  4. Leave the hair on top of your head longer and style it with your fingers or a comb to create volume and texture.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally curly, skip to step 2. If not, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using any hot tools.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls. A mousse or curl-enhancing cream works best. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, paying extra attention to the ends.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Use a hair clipper to trim the sides and back of your head. You can choose the length you prefer, but a fade or undercut is recommended. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create a gradual fade.

Step 4: Style the Top

Leave the hair on top of your head longer and style it with your fingers or a comb to create volume and texture. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls if needed.

Step 5: Finish with Product

Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold the style in place. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls without causing frizz.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ

How often should I trim my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your style loses its shape. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your look.

Can I achieve this style with straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve this style with straight hair. Use a curling iron or styling products to create waves or curls, and follow the same steps as you would with curly hair.

What kind of face shape does this style suit?

This style is versatile and can suit a variety of face shapes, but it looks particularly good on those with oval or diamond-shaped faces.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

With the right styling products and techniques, the curly hair short sides long top look can be a stylish and low-maintenance choice for any occasion. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look for your hair type and face shape.
