Why Is My Hair Thinning Out On The Sides

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 · 8 min read

If you've noticed that your hair is thinning out on the sides, don't worry, you're not alone. Thinning hair is a common problem that affects both men and women. There are many reasons why your hair might be thinning out on the sides, and it's important to understand the causes so you can take the necessary steps to combat it.

Description

Thinning hair on the sides can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and lifestyle choices. Genetics can play a huge role in hair loss, and if your family has a history of baldness, you may be more likely to experience thinning hair on the sides. Hormonal changes, such as those that occur during pregnancy or menopause, can also contribute to hair loss. Lifestyle choices, such as a poor diet, stress, and certain medications, can also impact hair health.

How to Combat Thinning Hair on the Sides

If you're experiencing thinning hair on the sides, there are several steps you can take to combat it. Here are some tips:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair. Make sure you're eating plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can have a negative impact on hair health, so it's important to manage your stress levels. Try practicing relaxation techniques, such as meditation or yoga, or find a hobby that helps you unwind.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh hair products can damage hair and contribute to hair loss. Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are designed to promote healthy hair.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage hair and contribute to hair loss. Try air-drying your hair or using heat styling tools sparingly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Combat Thinning Hair on the Sides

Step 1: Assess Your Hair Health

The first step to combat thinning hair on the sides is to assess your hair health. Look for any signs of damage, such as split ends or breakage, and make note of any changes in hair texture or thickness.

Step 2: Make Lifestyle Changes

If you're not already living a healthy lifestyle, now is the time to make changes. Eat a healthy diet, manage stress, and avoid harsh hair products and heat styling tools.

Step 3: Use Hair Growth Products

There are many hair growth products on the market that can help combat thinning hair on the sides. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen.

Step 4: Consider Hair Restoration

If your hair loss is severe, you may want to consider hair restoration options, such as hair transplants or scalp micropigmentation.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to the steps outlined above, there are several tips you can follow to maintain healthy hair:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can contribute to hair loss.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage hair and contribute to hair loss. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

3. Don't Brush Wet Hair

Wet hair is more susceptible to breakage and damage, so it's important to be gentle when brushing wet hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle hair instead of a brush.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can hair loss be reversed?

A: In some cases, hair loss can be reversed with the right treatment. However, it's important to address hair loss as soon as possible to increase the chances of successful treatment.

Q: Are there any home remedies for hair loss?

A: There are several home remedies that may help promote hair growth, such as using onion juice or coconut oil on the scalp. However, it's important to consult with a healthcare professional before trying any home remedies.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss. When you're under stress, your body produces cortisol, which can affect hair growth and cause hair loss.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on the Sides

If you're experiencing thinning hair on the sides, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Use gentle hair products, avoid heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun. With the right care and treatment, you can combat thinning hair on the sides and promote healthy hair growth.
