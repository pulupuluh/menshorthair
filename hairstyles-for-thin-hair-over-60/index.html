Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Apr 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and more delicate. This can make it difficult to find hairstyles that flatter us and make us feel confident. However, there are plenty of hairstyles for thin hair over 60 that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for thin hair over 60, as well as some tips for caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Thin Hair Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Hair Over 60
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Thick-Looking Hair

  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair Over 60

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Thin Hair Over 60

Choosing the right hairstyle for thin hair over 60 can be challenging, especially if you're not sure what to look for. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Keep It Short

Short haircuts are great for thin hair because they create the illusion of more volume. A bob, pixie cut, or layered haircut can all work well for thin hair over 60.

2. Add Layers

Layering your hair can also help create the illusion of more volume. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face and at the crown of your head to give your hair more body.

3. Consider Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to add volume to thin hair over 60. Side-swept bangs, in particular, can help create the illusion of more volume and make your hair look fuller.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Hair Over 60

Styling thin hair over 60 doesn't have to be complicated. Here's a simple step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. This will help give it more volume and make it easier to work with.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product, such as mousse or root lifter, to your roots. This will help give your hair more body and lift.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to blow dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift your hair and create volume.

4. Style as Desired

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair, or simply use your hands to tousle it for a more casual look.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Thick-Looking Hair

Keeping your hair healthy is key to making it look thick and full. Here are some tips to help you care for your thin hair over 60:

1. Use Gentle Hair Products

Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are gentle and designed for thin hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it look even thinner. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and try to limit your use of heat styling tools.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 60

1. Can I still wear my hair long if it's thin?

While shorter haircuts are generally better for thin hair, you can still wear your hair long if you prefer. Just be sure to add layers to create more volume.

2. What are the best hairstyles for thin hair over 60?

Some of the best hairstyles for thin hair over 60 include bobs, pixie cuts, and layered haircuts. Bangs can also help create the illusion of more volume.

3. Will coloring my hair make it look thinner?

Coloring your hair can make it look thinner if it's not done properly. Avoid using harsh chemicals and opt for a lighter color to create the illusion of more volume.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair Over 60

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling your hair properly, there are some hair care tips you can follow to keep your thin hair looking its best:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, can pull on your hair and make it look thinner. Avoid wearing your hair in tight styles for extended periods of time.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair looking its best. Be sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein in your diet.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health, including the health of your hair. Aim for seven to eight hours of sleep per night to help keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can keep your thin hair over 60 looking healthy and full. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you!
