Why Does My Hair Fall Out After Gastric Sleeve

Written by Luffy Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have undergone gastric sleeve surgery, it is common to experience hair loss. This is a common side effect of the procedure and is known as telogen effluvium. This article will discuss why hair loss occurs after gastric sleeve surgery, how to prevent it, and tips on how to care for your hair after surgery.

Table of Contents

  • Why Does Hair Loss Occur After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?
  • How to Prevent Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Why Does Hair Loss Occur After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

During gastric sleeve surgery, the stomach is reduced to a small pouch, and the body's ability to absorb nutrients is reduced. This can lead to a deficiency in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth.

Additionally, the stress of surgery and the rapid weight loss that follows can cause telogen effluvium. This is a condition where hair enters the resting phase of the growth cycle prematurely, causing hair to fall out.

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

To prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, it is essential to maintain a healthy diet and take supplements to ensure you are getting the necessary vitamins and minerals. Your doctor may recommend taking biotin supplements to promote hair growth.

It is also important to avoid crash diets and rapid weight loss, which can cause stress on the body and lead to hair loss. Gradual weight loss is the best way to prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery.

Tips for Hair Care After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

After gastric sleeve surgery, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is sulfate-free to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils.
  2. Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners as they can damage the hair.
  3. Limit hair treatments such as coloring, highlighting, and chemical straightening.
  4. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  5. Take biotin supplements as recommended by your doctor.
  6. Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids and ponytails, which can cause hair breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long does hair loss last after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is temporary and usually lasts for a few months. It typically resolves on its own, and new hair growth will begin to appear.

Q: Is hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery permanent?

A: No, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is not permanent. It is a temporary side effect of the procedure and typically resolves on its own.

Q: Can I use hair growth products to prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Hair growth products such as shampoos, conditioners, and serums may be helpful in promoting hair growth, but it is essential to consult with your doctor before using them. They may recommend specific products or supplements to help promote hair growth.

Q: How much hair loss is normal after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: It is normal to lose up to 100 hairs a day. However, after gastric sleeve surgery, you may experience more significant hair loss, which can be alarming. It is essential to consult with your doctor if you are experiencing excessive hair loss.

In conclusion, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is a common side effect and is temporary. It is essential to maintain a healthy diet, take supplements, and care for your hair to prevent further hair loss. If you are experiencing excessive hair loss, consult with your doctor for recommendations on how to promote hair growth.
