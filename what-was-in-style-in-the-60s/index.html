What Was In Style In The 60S

Welcome to our blog post about what was in style in the 60s! The 60s was a decade that revolutionized fashion and style. It was a time of liberation, experimentation, and breaking free from traditional norms. In this article, we will explore the fashion trends that defined the 60s and see how they continue to inspire fashion today.

Table of Contents

Trendy Clothing

The 60s was a decade of change, and this was reflected in the fashion of the time. Women's clothing became less formal and more practical. The mini skirt was introduced, and it quickly became the must-have item of the decade. It was a symbol of liberation and freedom, and it was worn by women of all ages.

Aside from the mini skirt, other trendy clothing included shift dresses, bell-bottoms, and hot pants. These styles were often made from bold and colorful prints, such as paisley and tie-dye. Mod fashion was also popular, which featured clean lines, bold graphics, and geometric shapes.

Bold Colors

The 60s was all about vibrant, eye-catching colors. Bright pinks, oranges, yellows, and greens were popular, as were bold patterns like polka dots and stripes. The color blocking trend was also introduced, which involved combining contrasting colors in one outfit.

Black and white was also a popular color combination. The 'little black dress' was still a staple in every woman's wardrobe, but it was often paired with white accessories, such as shoes and handbags.

Accessories

Accessories were an essential part of 60s fashion. Oversized sunglasses, headbands, and hoop earrings were all the rage. Women also wore chunky jewelry made from plastic or wood.

Handbags were also an important accessory. The shoulder bag was introduced, which allowed women to carry all their essentials while keeping their hands free. The clutch bag was also popular, and it was often adorned with sequins or beading.

Hairstyles

Hairstyles in the 60s were big and bold. The beehive was a popular style, which involved teasing the hair into a high, conical shape. The bouffant was also popular, which involved teasing the hair to create a full, rounded look.

Long, straight hair was also in style, often with a center parting. The afro was introduced, which was a symbol of black pride and identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What was the most popular clothing item in the 60s?

The mini skirt was the most popular clothing item in the 60s. It was a symbol of liberation and freedom, and it was worn by women of all ages.

What colors were popular in the 60s?

Bright pinks, oranges, yellows, and greens were popular in the 60s, as were bold patterns like polka dots and stripes.

What kind of hairstyles were popular in the 60s?

The beehive, bouffant, and long, straight hair with a center parting were all popular hairstyles in the 60s.

Hair Care Tips

If you want to recreate a 60s hairstyle, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Tease your hair gently with a comb to create volume.
  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, embrace your curls and use a diffuser to dry your hair.

That's all for our blog post about what was in style in the 60s. We hope this article has inspired you to try out some of these trends and incorporate them into your own style.
