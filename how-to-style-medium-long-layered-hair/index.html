How To Style Medium Long Layered Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium long layered hair is a popular hairstyle that suits a range of face shapes and hair types. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, making it look more dynamic and interesting. However, styling medium long layered hair can be a challenge for some people. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to style medium long layered hair, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Long Layered Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Medium Long Layered Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Long Layered Hair

How to Style Medium Long Layered Hair

There are many ways to style medium long layered hair, depending on your personal preferences and the occasion. Here are some popular styles:

  • Beachy waves
  • Sleek and straight
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Messy bun
  • Braids

Before styling your hair, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body and texture to your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can start styling it.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for styling medium long layered hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or on one side, depending on your preference.
  2. If you want to create beachy waves, use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves. You can also use a salt spray to add more texture to your hair.
  3. If you want a sleek and straight look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. You can apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.
  4. If you want to create a half-up, half-down style, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or a clip. You can leave the rest of your hair down or create loose waves or curls.
  5. If you want to create a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  6. If you want to create braids, start by parting your hair in the middle or on one side. You can create a French braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid.

Tips for Styling Medium Long Layered Hair

Here are some tips for styling medium long layered hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or a flat iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't apply too much product to your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more body to your hair.
  • If you have thick hair, use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz and add shine.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my medium long layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry or curly hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Q: What products should I use to style my medium long layered hair?

A: It depends on the style you want to create. For beachy waves, you can use a salt spray. For a sleek and straight look, you can use a hair serum or oil. For a messy bun, you can use a texturizing spray. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Long Layered Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium long layered hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to style medium long layered hair, you can experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and following a good hair care routine.
