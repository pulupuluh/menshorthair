What Is The Best Serum For Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that finding the right product can be a challenge. There are so many different serums on the market, each claiming to be the best for curly hair. So, how do you know which one to choose? In this article, we'll take a look at what makes a serum great for curly hair and which products are worth considering.

What Makes a Serum Great for Curly Hair?

First and foremost, a good serum for curly hair should be moisturizing. Curly hair tends to be dry and brittle, so it's important to use products that will help hydrate and nourish your locks. Look for serums that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.

Another important factor to consider is frizz control. Curly hair is prone to frizz, so a good serum should help keep your curls smooth and defined. Look for products that contain anti-frizz ingredients like silicone, which can help smooth the hair cuticle and prevent moisture from penetrating the hair shaft.

Finally, a good serum for curly hair should provide some hold. While you don't want your curls to be stiff or crunchy, a little bit of hold can help keep your curls in place and prevent them from falling flat. Look for serums that contain natural hold ingredients like flaxseed or aloe vera.

Top Serums for Curly Hair

1. Moroccanoil Curl Defining Cream

Moroccanoil Curl Defining Cream is a popular choice among curly-haired individuals. This cream is infused with argan oil, which helps moisturize and nourish your curls. It also contains coconut oil, which helps prevent frizz and adds shine to your hair. This cream provides a medium hold, so your curls will stay defined without feeling stiff or crunchy.

2. DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler

The DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler is another great option for those with curly hair. This cream is infused with coconut oil, which helps hydrate and nourish your curls. It also contains jojoba oil, which helps add shine and prevent frizz. This cream provides a strong hold, so your curls will stay defined all day.

3. Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel

If you live in a humid climate, the Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel may be the perfect serum for you. This gel is infused with anti-frizz ingredients like silicone and is designed to protect your curls from humidity. It provides a strong hold, so your curls will stay in place even on the most humid of days.

How to Use Serum on Curly Hair

Using serum on curly hair is easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a small amount of serum to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  4. Gently scrunch your hair to help define your curls.
  5. Let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser to dry your curls.

Remember, a little bit of serum goes a long way, so start with a small amount and add more if necessary.

Tips for Using Serum on Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your serum:

  • Don't apply serum to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying serum.
  • Try using a leave-in conditioner before applying serum for extra hydration.
  • Experiment with different amounts of serum to find what works best for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use serum on wet hair?

Yes, you can use serum on wet hair. In fact, it's recommended to apply serum to damp hair to help seal in moisture.

How often should I use serum on my curly hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have very dry hair, you may want to use serum every day. If your hair is less dry, you may only need to use serum a few times a week.

Can serum make my hair look greasy?

If you use too much serum or apply it to your roots, it can make your hair look greasy. Be sure to start with a small amount of serum and focus on applying it to the ends and mid-lengths of your hair.

Final Thoughts

When it comes to finding the best serum for curly hair, it's important to look for products that are moisturizing, provide frizz control, and offer some hold. The three serums we've highlighted in this article are all great options to consider. Be sure to experiment with different amounts of serum to find what works best for your hair, and don't be afraid to try different products until you find the perfect one for you.
