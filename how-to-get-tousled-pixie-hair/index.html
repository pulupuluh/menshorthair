Get Tousled Pixie Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Feb 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, a tousled pixie cut is a great option. This low-maintenance look is perfect for those who want something edgy and stylish without having to spend hours styling their hair each morning.

Description

A tousled pixie cut is a short, choppy hairstyle with layers that give it a messy, textured look. This style is perfect for those with fine hair as it creates the illusion of volume and thickness. It's a versatile cut that can be worn straight or with waves and is easy to style with just a few products.

How to

Getting a tousled pixie cut is easy if you follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Find a Good Stylist

The first step in getting a tousled pixie cut is to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Look for someone who has experience cutting short hair and who can give you tips on how to style your new cut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a tousled pixie cut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair type. A good stylist will be able to recommend a cut that flatters your features and works with your hair's texture.

Step 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and conditioned. This will help you achieve the best results and avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

To create a tousled look, you need to apply the right styling products. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to your hair, then work in a texturizing spray to add texture and hold.

Step 5: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, lifting the roots to create volume. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to tousle it and create a messy, textured look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your tousled pixie cut in place, finish with a light hairspray. Spray lightly, holding the can about 12 inches away from your head.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect tousled pixie cut:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume
  • Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look
  • Finish with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place

FAQ

How often should I get my tousled pixie cut trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your cut and keep it looking fresh.

What if I have thick hair?

If you have thick hair, a tousled pixie cut can still work for you. Talk to your stylist about adjusting the cut to work with your hair's texture.

Can I still style my hair if I have a tousled pixie cut?

A tousled pixie cut is easy to style and can be worn straight or with waves. Simply apply the right styling products and use your fingers to create the look you want.

Hair Care

To keep your tousled pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf, and get regular trims to keep your cut looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a tousled pixie cut like a pro. So go ahead, chop off those locks and embrace your new edgy, low-maintenance style!
