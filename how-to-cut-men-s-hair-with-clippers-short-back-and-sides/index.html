How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barber shop every month just to get a simple haircut? Learning how to cut men's hair with clippers short back and sides is easier than you think. With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a professional-looking cut in the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will take you through the step-by-step process of cutting men's hair with clippers short back and sides.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a simple, low-maintenance cut that looks great on almost everyone. The style involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides of the head while leaving the top longer. This creates a neat, clean-cut look that is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Short Back and Sides

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's hair with clippers short back and sides is to gather your tools. You will need a good quality pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in and that the blades are sharp.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, then pat it dry with a towel. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Create a Guide Line

Section the hair by creating a guide line. This is the line that separates the longer hair on top from the shorter hair on the back and sides. Use your comb to create a straight line from the front hairline to the back of the head.

Step 4: Use the Clippers

Start cutting the hair using the clippers. Begin at the base of the neck and work your way up to the guide line. Use a comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over the comb. This will help you achieve an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair on the back and sides, it's time to blend it with the longer hair on top. Use scissors to blend the hair by cutting small sections at a time. Blend the hair by cutting at an angle, starting from the hairline and working your way up.

Step 6: Taper the Hairline

Taper the hairline by using the clippers to create a gradual fade. Start with a higher guard on the clippers and work your way down to a lower guard. This will create a natural-looking fade that blends the hairline with the rest of the hair.

Step 7: Clean Up the Neckline

Clean up the neckline by using the clippers to create a clean, straight line. Start at the center of the neckline and work your way out to the sides. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 8: Trim the Sideburns

Trim the sideburns by using scissors to cut them to the desired length. Use the ear as a guide and cut the sideburns in a straight line.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head as you cut.
  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Practice on a friend or family member before trying to cut your own hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut men's hair with clippers if I have no experience?

A: Yes, you can. With some basic knowledge and the right tools, anyone can cut men's hair with clippers short back and sides.

Q: What type of clippers should I use?

A: Look for clippers that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Choose a brand that is known for its quality and durability.

Q: How often should I cut men's hair with clippers short back and sides?

A: It depends on how fast the hair grows. Most men need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting men's hair with clippers short back and sides, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much styling products as they can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy. Trim the hair regularly to maintain the style.
