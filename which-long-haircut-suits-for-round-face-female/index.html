Which Long Haircut Suits For Round Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Mar 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts but still can't find the perfect one for your round face? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss which long haircut suits for round face female and provide you with some tips and tricks to maintain your new hairstyle.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Long Haircut for Round Face Female
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Maintain Your Long Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Description

Round face females have a circular shape face, and the length and width of their face are almost the same. The best way to balance the roundness of the face is by adding some length to the hair. A long haircut can elongate the face and create an illusion of an oval face shape. The right long haircut can be a game-changer and enhance your overall look.

How to Choose the Right Long Haircut for Round Face Female

Choosing the right long haircut for a round face female can be tricky. Here are some tips to find the perfect one:

1. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can soften the facial features and make the face appear slimmer. The layers should start from the cheekbones and blend into the hair length. Avoid layers that start from the chin level as they can make the face appear wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create an illusion of a longer face and add dimension to the hair. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face appear shorter and wider.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can create a natural-looking texture and add volume to the hair. Waves that start from the mid-length can elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to get the perfect long haircut for a round face female:

1. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist and discuss the long haircut options that would suit your face shape. The hairstylist can suggest a few hairstyles that would enhance your features.

2. Choose the Right Length

Long haircuts can vary from mid-length to waist-length. Choose the length that would complement your face shape and hair texture. Avoid haircuts that end at the chin level as they can make the face appear wider.

3. Add Layers

Add face-framing layers to soften the facial features and create a slimming effect. The layers should start from the cheekbones and blend into the hair length.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair with beachy waves or side-swept bangs to create an illusion of a longer face. Use a curling iron or a flat iron to create the texture and apply a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips to Maintain Your Long Haircut

Here are some tips to maintain your long haircut:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to the hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish the hair and restore its shine.

FAQ

1. Can a Long Haircut Make the Face Appear Slimmer?

Yes, a long haircut can elongate the face and create an illusion of a slimmer face.

2. What Type of Bangs Should I Avoid if I Have a Round Face?

Avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face appear shorter and wider.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to remove the dirt and oil buildup.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the harmful UV rays of the sun.

In conclusion, a long haircut can be a perfect choice for a round face female. Follow the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to find the perfect long haircut and maintain it for a trendy and stylish look.
