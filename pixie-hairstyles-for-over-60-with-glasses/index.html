Pixie Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Frank Mar 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 60 and wearing glasses? Do you want a stylish and easy-to-maintain hairstyle to complement your look? Look no further than a pixie cut! In this guide, we will go over everything you need to know about pixie hairstyles for over 60 with glasses, including how to style them, maintenance tips, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the head and features short layers that frame the face. This hairstyle is perfect for women over 60 who want to look stylish and modern while also being low-maintenance. Pixie cuts are also great for women who wear glasses because they do not interfere with the frames and can actually accentuate them.

How to Style a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. To style your pixie cut, you will need a few basic tools:

  • A hairdryer
  • A round brush
  • A styling product (e.g. mousse, gel, wax)

To style your pixie cut, start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Apply a styling product to your hair, then use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. Once your hair is dry, you can use your fingers or a small amount of product to shape and style your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

If you are new to styling a pixie cut, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers or a small amount of product to shape and style your hair.
  5. If you want to add more volume, use a volumizing product and blow-dry your hair upside down.
  6. To finish your look, apply a small amount of hairspray or other styling product.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie hairstyles for over 60 with glasses:

Q: Will a pixie cut look good on me if I have a round face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can look great on women with round faces. Just make sure to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and features.

Q: How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It is recommended that you get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you have a pixie cut. Just make sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair if you have a pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance pixie cut at any age. So go ahead and try out this trendy hairstyle today!
