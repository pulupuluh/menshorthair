Best Wedding Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the perfect wedding hairstyle that will complement your heart-shaped face? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best wedding hairstyles that suit heart-shaped faces. Whether you have long or short hair, we have got something for everyone. Read on to find out more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The best wedding hairstyles for this face shape are those that balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin. These hairstyles should also accentuate the cheekbones and draw attention to the eyes.

How to

Before we dive into the best wedding hairstyles for heart-shaped faces, here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the crown as they can make the forehead appear wider.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek and straight as they can make the chin appear more pointed.
  • Consider adding some volume to the lower half of your hair to balance out the width of the forehead.
  • Consider hairstyles that frame the face, such as side-swept bangs or loose waves.

Step by step

Here are some of the best wedding hairstyles for heart-shaped faces:

1. Loose Waves

Loose waves are perfect for heart-shaped faces as they add volume to the lower half of the hair, balancing out the width of the forehead. To achieve loose waves, use a curling iron or a straightener to create loose curls, then run your fingers through your hair for a more natural look.

2. Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame the face and draw attention to the eyes. They also help balance out the width of the forehead. To create side-swept bangs, part your hair on one side and sweep the hair across your forehead.

3. Half-up Half-down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for heart-shaped faces as it adds volume to the lower half of the hair while keeping the forehead open. To create this hairstyle, simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or a hair tie.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a wedding hairstyle for a heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back as they can accentuate the narrowness of the chin.
  • Consider adding volume to the crown of your head to balance out the width of the forehead.
  • Consider a hairstyle that creates the illusion of width at the jawline, such as a bob or a layered cut.

FAQ

What wedding hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart-shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the crown or too sleek and straight. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back.

What hairstyles are best for heart-shaped faces?

The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces are those that balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin. Consider hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of the hair, such as loose waves or a half-up half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know the best wedding hairstyles for heart-shaped faces, you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your big day. Remember to keep in mind the general tips and tricks we mentioned earlier and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
