Are High Waisted Swim Bottoms Flattering

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy, stylish and comfortable swimwear option, high waisted swim bottoms are a great choice. However, you may be wondering if they are actually flattering. The answer is yes! High waisted swim bottoms can be incredibly flattering, and here's why.

Description

High waisted swim bottoms are a style of swimwear that sit higher on the waist than traditional bikini bottoms. They often have a retro-inspired look and can come in a variety of styles, including full coverage, high leg, and skirted options. High waisted swim bottoms can be paired with a variety of swim tops and are a popular choice for women of all ages and body types.

How to Wear High Waisted Swim Bottoms

Wearing high waisted swim bottoms is easy and can be done in a variety of ways. Here's how to wear them:

Step 1: Choose the Right Size

It's important to choose the right size high waisted swim bottoms to ensure a comfortable and flattering fit. Make sure to measure your waist and hips and refer to the size chart when selecting your size.

Step 2: Choose the Right Style

High waisted swim bottoms come in a variety of styles, including full coverage, high leg, and skirted options. Choose a style that you feel comfortable in and that flatters your body type.

Step 3: Mix and Match

High waisted swim bottoms can be paired with a variety of swim tops, including crop tops, bandeaus, and halter tops. Mix and match to find a combination that you love.

Tips for Flattering High Waisted Swim Bottoms

Here are some tips for making high waisted swim bottoms even more flattering:

1. Look for High-Quality Material

Investing in high-quality material will ensure that your high waisted swim bottoms flatter your body and last for many seasons to come.

2. Choose a Dark Color

Dark colors, like black or navy, can be incredibly slimming and will help to create a streamlined silhouette.

3. Play with Patterns

Patterns can be fun and playful, but be sure to choose a pattern that flatters your body type.

4. Pair with a Crop Top

Pairing high waisted swim bottoms with a crop top can help to elongate your torso and create a more balanced look.

FAQ

Q: Are high waisted swim bottoms only for certain body types?

A: High waisted swim bottoms can be flattering on a variety of body types, including those with a curvier figure or those who want more coverage on their midsection.

Q: Can high waisted swim bottoms be worn by older women?

A: Absolutely! High waisted swim bottoms are a great option for women of all ages and can provide both comfort and style.

Q: Are high waisted swim bottoms comfortable?

A: Yes! High waisted swim bottoms can be incredibly comfortable, especially for those who prefer more coverage on their midsection.

Hair Care Tips for Swimming

When swimming, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and dryness. Here are some tips:

1. Wet Your Hair Before Swimming

Wetting your hair before swimming can help to prevent chlorine and salt water from penetrating the hair shaft.

2. Use a Swim Cap

Using a swim cap can help to protect your hair from the damaging effects of chlorine and salt water.

3. Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

Rinsing your hair after swimming can help to remove any chlorine or salt water that may have gotten on your hair.

4. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, high waisted swim bottoms are a stylish and flattering option for women of all ages and body types. With the right fit and style, you can feel confident and comfortable while enjoying a day at the beach or pool. And remember to take care of your hair while swimming to prevent damage and dryness.
