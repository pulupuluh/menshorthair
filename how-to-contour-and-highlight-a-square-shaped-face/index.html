How To Contour And Highlight A Square Shaped Face

Written by Frank Feb 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square shaped face, you may feel like your jawline is too prominent or that your face lacks dimension. However, with the right makeup techniques, you can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. In this article, we will guide you through how to contour and highlight a square shaped face to enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour and Highlight a Square Shaped Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring and Highlighting
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Square Shaped Faces

Description

Square shaped faces have a strong jawline and a wide forehead. The goal of contouring and highlighting is to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face by sculpting the jawline and softening the forehead. Contouring involves using a darker shade of makeup to create shadows, while highlighting involves using a lighter shade to bring out the high points of the face.

How to Contour and Highlight a Square Shaped Face

Before you begin contouring and highlighting, it is important to prep your skin with a moisturizer and primer. This will ensure that your makeup goes on smoothly and lasts all day.

Choose the Right Products

For contouring, choose a shade that is two to three shades darker than your skin tone. For highlighting, choose a shade that is two to three shades lighter. You can use either cream or powder products, depending on your preference.

Identify Your Face Shape

To contour and highlight effectively, it is important to know your face shape. Use a mirror to identify the widest points of your face, which will be the forehead, cheeks, and jawline.

Contour Your Jawline

To contour your jawline, apply the darker shade of makeup along the jawline, blending downwards towards your neck. This will create a shadow that will make your jawline appear more sculpted.

Contour Your Cheeks

To contour your cheeks, suck in your cheeks and apply the darker shade of makeup in a diagonal line towards your ear. Blend the makeup upwards towards your temples to create a shadow that will make your cheeks appear more defined.

Contour Your Forehead

To contour your forehead, apply the darker shade of makeup along the hairline, blending downwards towards your temples. This will make your forehead appear smaller and more oval-shaped.

Highlight Your Cheekbones

To highlight your cheekbones, apply the lighter shade of makeup along the tops of your cheekbones. This will bring out the high points of your face and make your cheeks appear more lifted.

Highlight Your Forehead

To highlight your forehead, apply the lighter shade of makeup along the center of your forehead. This will make your forehead appear more prominent and balanced with the rest of your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to contour and highlight a square shaped face:

  1. Prep your skin with moisturizer and primer.
  2. Identify your face shape.
  3. Choose the right contouring and highlighting products.
  4. Contour your jawline.
  5. Contour your cheeks.
  6. Contour your forehead.
  7. Highlight your cheekbones.
  8. Highlight your forehead.
  9. Blend everything together for a seamless finish.
  10. Set your makeup with setting spray or powder.

Tips for Contouring and Highlighting

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when contouring and highlighting:

  • Blend, blend, blend! Blending is key to achieving a natural-looking finish.
  • Use a light hand when applying makeup. You can always add more, but it's difficult to remove too much.
  • Choose the right products for your skin type. If you have oily skin, opt for powder products. If you have dry skin, choose cream products.
  • Use a fluffy brush to apply and blend your makeup for a soft, diffused look.
  • Don't forget to contour your neck if you're wearing your hair up.

FAQ

Can contouring and highlighting damage my skin?

No, contouring and highlighting should not damage your skin as long as you use high-quality products and remove your makeup at the end of the day.

How often should I contour and highlight?

You can contour and highlight as often as you like, but it's important to give your skin a break from makeup every now and then to let it breathe.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry if you make a mistake - it's easy to fix! Simply use a clean brush to blend out any harsh lines or remove any excess product with a makeup wipe.

Hair Care for Square Shaped Faces

If you have a square shaped face, you may want to consider a hairstyle that softens your features. Soft, layered hairstyles work well for square shaped faces, as they create movement and add dimension to the hair. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as they can make your face appear even more square.

With these tips and techniques, you can contour and highlight your square shaped face to enhance your natural beauty. Remember to have fun and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
