Best Hairstyle For Overweight Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your round face? Do you feel like your weight is getting in the way of finding a hairstyle that suits your features? Look no further as we have curated a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyle for overweight round face. In this article, we will cover everything from how to choose the right hairstyle to hair care tips to maintain your locks. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Best Hairstyles for Overweight Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for overweight round face, it is essential to understand how to choose the right hairstyle that complements your features. Here are a few tips:

Consider Your Face Shape

As someone with a round face, it is best to avoid hairstyles that add more volume to your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate your face shape. Hairstyles that add height to your crown or have layers that frame your face are great options.

Avoid Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that add dimension to your face.

Consider Your Hair Texture

It is important to choose a hairstyle that suits the texture of your hair. For example, if you have thick hair, a layered haircut can help add volume and movement to your locks. On the other hand, if you have thin hair, a short bob can help make your hair appear fuller.

Best Hairstyles for Overweight Round Face

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for overweight round face:

Long Layers

Long layers that frame your face can help elongate your face shape. Opt for layers that start at your chin and taper towards the bottom.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs that graze your cheekbones can add dimension to your face. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear rounder.

Messy Updo

A messy updo can help create an illusion of height, making your face appear longer. Keep some strands loose around your face to frame it.

Bob with Side Parting

A bob with a side parting can help elongate your face shape. Opt for a shorter bob that ends just above your chin.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have found the perfect hairstyle, it is crucial to maintain your locks. Here are a few hair care tips:

Use the Right Products

Choose hair care products that suit your hair type. For example, if you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Avoid Overstyling

Avoid overstyling your hair with heating tools like curling irons and straighteners. These tools can damage your hair and make it appear dull.

Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face like blunt bangs and short bobs that end at your chin.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Long layers that frame your face can help elongate your face shape.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, wash it every other day. For dry hair, aim to wash it once or twice a week.

We hope this guide has helped you find the best hairstyle for overweight round face. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture, and maintain your locks with proper hair care. Happy styling!
