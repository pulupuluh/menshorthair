Best Hairspray For Fine Hair Australia Keep Your Locks Looking Luscious

Written by Christine May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have fine hair, you know the struggles of making it look voluminous and full. Hairspray can be a lifesaver when it comes to giving your locks that extra lift and hold. However, not all hairsprays are created equal, especially when it comes to fine hair. In this blog post, we'll dive into the best hairspray for fine hair in Australia and how to use it to achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Hairspray on Fine Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Hairspray for Fine Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fine Hair

Description

When it comes to choosing the best hairspray for fine hair in Australia, you want to look for a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down or leave a sticky residue. Look for hairsprays that are specifically designed for fine hair and offer a flexible hold. This will allow you to add volume and hold to your hair without making it look stiff or unnatural.

How to Use Hairspray on Fine Hair

Before using hairspray on fine hair, it's important to prep your hair properly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for fine hair. This will help to add volume and texture to your locks. After washing, towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or root lift spray to your roots. Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

Once your hair is dry, you can begin to apply hairspray. Hold the can about 10-12 inches away from your hair and spray in short bursts. Be sure to focus on the roots and areas where you want to add volume. Avoid spraying too much hairspray, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for fine hair.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or root lift spray to your roots.

3. Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.

4. Hold the can of hairspray 10-12 inches away from your hair.

5. Spray in short bursts, focusing on the roots and areas where you want to add volume.

6. Avoid spraying too much hairspray.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairspray for Fine Hair

When choosing a hairspray for fine hair, look for a lightweight formula that offers a flexible hold. Avoid hairsprays that contain alcohol or other drying ingredients, as these can damage your hair and make it look dull. Consider a hairspray that also offers UV protection, as this can help to prevent sun damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Some of the best hairsprays for fine hair in Australia include:

  • Kevin Murphy Doo.Over
  • Oribe Superfine Hairspray
  • Aveda Air Control Hairspray
  • L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray
  • TIGI Bed Head Masterpiece Hairspray

FAQs

Q: Can hairspray damage fine hair?

A: Yes, hairspray can damage fine hair if you use too much or use a formula that contains drying ingredients like alcohol. Be sure to choose a hairspray that is designed for fine hair and use it sparingly.

Q: How often should I use hairspray on fine hair?

A: It's best to use hairspray on fine hair sparingly, as overuse can cause damage and make your hair look greasy. Use hairspray only when necessary to add volume and hold to your locks.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

When it comes to caring for fine hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. Avoid using heavy conditioners or hair masks, as these can make your hair look greasy and flat.

Be sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products. Finally, avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

In conclusion, choosing the best hairspray for fine hair in Australia is all about finding a lightweight formula that offers a flexible hold. Use hairspray sparingly and be sure to prep your hair properly before applying. With the right care and styling products, you can keep your fine hair looking voluminous and full of life.
