Which Haircut Suits For Round Face Female

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a soft jawline. The right haircut can help to elongate your face and create a more angular look. In this article, we will discuss which haircut suits for round face female.

Description

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, it's important to understand the unique features of this face shape. A round face is typically wider than it is long, and it lacks angular features. The goal of a great haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and add some angles to the face.

How To

The best haircuts for round faces are those that add height to the crown of the head and create the illusion of length. Here are some options to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers can be very flattering for a round face, particularly if they start below the chin. This haircut creates movement and volume, which helps to elongate the face. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face look wider.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great addition to any haircut for a round face. They help to create angles and draw attention to the eyes. Avoid heavy, straight-across bangs, as they can make the face look wider.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob can be a great choice for a round face. This haircut is longer on one side than the other, which creates an angled look. Avoid bobs that are chin-length, as they can make the face look wider.

Step by Step

If you're considering one of these haircuts, here's what you can expect from the process:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.
  2. Bring photos of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  3. During the haircut, your stylist will work to create movement and volume.
  4. If you're getting bangs, your stylist will determine the best length and angle for your face shape.
  5. After the haircut, your stylist may use a styling product to create volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make the face look wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked-back, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Remember that everyone's face shape is unique, so it's important to work with a stylist who understands your individual features.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

Q: Are there any haircuts that I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid blunt cuts, chin-length bobs, and heavy, straight-across bangs.

Hair Care

To keep your haircut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a styling product to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

With the right haircut and some care, you can look and feel your best with a round face.
