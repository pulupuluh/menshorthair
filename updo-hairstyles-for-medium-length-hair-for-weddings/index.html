Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair For Weddings

Written by William May 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to weddings, the bride's hairstyle is just as important as her dress. Updo hairstyles for medium length hair are perfect for weddings as they are elegant, timeless and versatile. In this article, we will provide you with the ultimate guide to updo hairstyles for medium length hair for weddings, including descriptions, how-to's, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs and hair care advice.

Description

Updo hairstyles for medium length hair are the perfect balance between a glamorous updo and a more casual, relaxed look. They can be achieved with a variety of different techniques, including braids, twists, buns, and curls. These hairstyles add an extra touch of elegance to any wedding look, and can be customized to suit any bride's personal style.

How To

Creating an updo hairstyle for medium length hair is easier than you might think. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. If you have natural curls, you can skip the blow-drying step.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair if you plan to use hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener.
  3. Select the updo style you want to create and gather the necessary tools, such as bobby pins, hair ties, and a comb.
  4. Section your hair and create the base of the updo.
  5. Add in any braids, twists or curls as desired.
  6. Secure the updo with bobby pins or hair ties.
  7. Finish with hairspray or other hair products as desired.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for two popular updo hairstyles for medium length hair:

The Braided Updo

  1. Section your hair into two sections, one top and one bottom.
  2. Braid the top section of your hair into a French braid, starting at the crown of your head.
  3. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  4. Take the bottom section of your hair and twist it into a bun.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  6. Take the end of the braid and wrap it around the bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  7. Finish with hairspray.

The Half-Up Half-Down Updo

  1. Section the top half of your hair and create a small bun at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  3. Curl the bottom half of your hair using a curling iron.
  4. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.
  5. Take two small sections of hair from the front of your head and twist them towards the back.
  6. Secure the twists with bobby pins, tucking them behind the bun.
  7. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting and creating updo hairstyles for medium length hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and wedding dress.
  • Use hair products, such as mousse or gel, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to add in hair accessories, such as flowers or jeweled pins, to enhance your updo.
  • Consider booking a trial appointment with a hairstylist to perfect your chosen updo before the wedding day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for medium length hair for weddings:

Can updo hairstyles for medium length hair work for bridesmaids?

Yes, updo hairstyles for medium length hair are perfect for bridesmaids as well. They add an extra touch of elegance to any wedding party.

What kind of hair accessories should I use with my updo?

You can use a variety of hair accessories with your updo, such as flowers, jeweled pins, or hair combs. Choose accessories that complement your wedding dress and personal style.

How can I make my updo last all day?

Use hairspray or other hair products to hold your updo in place. You can also consider using bobby pins or hair ties for added security.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Conclusion

Updo hairstyles for medium length hair are the perfect way to add an extra touch of elegance to any wedding look. With these tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions, you'll be able to create a stunning updo hairstyle for your wedding day. Remember to take care of your hair leading up to the big day to ensure it looks its best.
