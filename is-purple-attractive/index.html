Is Purple Attractive A Comprehensive Guide To Rocking This Color

Written by Petter Jun 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you considering adding some purple to your wardrobe or hairstyle but not sure if it's attractive or not? Well, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll explore the question "is purple attractive?" and share tips on how to wear this color confidently. So without further ado, let's dive in!

Description

Purple is a color that has been associated with royalty, luxury, and creativity throughout history. It's a mix of red and blue, which makes it a unique and eye-catching color. However, some people might be hesitant to wear purple because they think it's too bold or difficult to style. But the truth is, purple can be a very attractive color if you know how to wear it.

How to Wear Purple

There are many ways to incorporate purple into your wardrobe or hairstyle. Here are some ideas:

Wear Purple Clothing

One of the most straightforward ways to wear purple is to add it to your outfit. You can start with small accessories like a purple scarf or purse and work your way up to bolder pieces like a purple dress or jumpsuit. If you're not comfortable wearing a lot of purple at once, you can pair it with neutral colors like black, white, or beige to tone it down.

Try Purple Makeup

If you're not ready to commit to purple clothing, you can experiment with purple makeup instead. Purple eyeshadow, lipstick, or nail polish can add a pop of color to your look without being too overwhelming. Plus, purple makeup looks great on all skin tones.

Add Purple to Your Hair

If you're feeling adventurous, you can also try adding purple to your hair. There are many ways to do this, from dyeing your entire head of hair to adding purple highlights or ombre. Just make sure to consult a professional hairstylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Wearing Purple

Still not sure how to wear purple? Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Shade

There are many shades of purple to choose from, so pick one that complements your skin tone and personal style. For example, if you have fair skin, you might want to opt for a lighter shade of purple, while darker skin tones can pull off deeper shades like plum or eggplant.

Step 2: Mix and Match

If you're new to wearing purple, start by mixing it with neutral colors like black, white, or beige. For example, you can pair a purple blouse with black pants or a beige skirt. As you get more comfortable, you can start experimenting with bolder color combinations.

Step 3: Accessorize

If you're not ready to wear a lot of purple, you can start by adding small accessories like a purple scarf, earrings, or purse. This is a great way to add a pop of color to your outfit without going overboard.

Step 4: Experiment with Makeup

If you're not ready to wear purple clothing, you can experiment with purple makeup instead. Try purple eyeshadow, lipstick, or nail polish to add a pop of color to your look.

Step 5: Go Bold

Once you're comfortable wearing purple, don't be afraid to go bold. You can try wearing a purple dress, jumpsuit, or pantsuit for a statement-making look.

Tips for Wearing Purple

Here are some additional tips for wearing purple:

Choose the Right Accessories

If you're wearing a lot of purple, make sure to choose accessories that complement the color. Silver, gold, or black accessories work well with purple.

Pair with Neutral Colors

If you're not sure how to style purple, pair it with neutral colors like black, white, or beige. This will help balance the boldness of the purple.

Wear Confidence

The most important thing to remember when wearing purple is to wear it with confidence. If you feel good in your outfit or hairstyle, it will show.

FAQ

Is purple attractive on everyone?

Yes, purple can be attractive on everyone. The key is to find the right shade that complements your skin tone and personal style.

What colors go well with purple?

Neutral colors like black, white, and beige go well with purple. Silver, gold, and black accessories also complement the color.

Can I wear purple to work?

Yes, you can wear purple to work. Just make sure to pair it with professional clothing items like a blazer, skirt, or pantsuit.

Hair Care for Purple Hair

If you decide to dye your hair purple, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner designed for dyed hair to prevent the color from fading.

Avoid Hot Water

Avoid washing your hair with hot water, as it can strip the color from your hair. Use lukewarm water instead.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can cause your hair color to fade, so wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when spending time outside.

Conclusion

So, is purple attractive? The answer is yes, but it's all about how you wear it. Whether you choose to wear purple clothing, makeup, or hair, make sure to wear it with confidence and pair it with complementary colors and accessories. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking purple in no time!
