How To Get Your Hair Curly For Black Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a black guy who wants to achieve curly hair? It can seem like a daunting task, especially if you have relaxed hair. But fear not, there are ways to get curly hair that look natural and effortless. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting your hair curly, step by step. From hair care tips to FAQs, we've got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care Tips
  • How to Get Your Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Useful Tips
  • FAQs

Hair Care Tips

Before we dive into how to get your hair curly, let's first talk about hair care tips. Proper hair care is essential to achieve healthy and curly hair. Here are some tips:

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Black hair is naturally dry, so it's essential to keep it moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using brushes on your curly hair, as they can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

4. Trim Your Ends

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. Aim to trim your ends every 6-8 weeks.

How to Get Your Hair Curly

Now that we've covered hair care tips, let's move on to how to get your hair curly. Here are some methods:

1. Twist Outs

Twist outs involve twisting small sections of hair around your finger and letting them dry. Once dry, unravel the twists, and you'll have defined curls. To make the curls last longer, use a hair gel or mousse.

2. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another method to achieve curly hair. Divide your hair into small sections and twist them into knots. Let them dry, and then unravel the knots. You'll have bouncy, defined curls.

3. Curlformers

Curlformers are plastic curlers that come in different sizes. They work by stretching your hair and creating defined curls. To use them, apply a styling product to your hair, then place the curlers and let them dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get your hair curly:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a leave-in conditioner, hair oil, or curling cream.

3. Choose Your Method

Choose your preferred method to achieve curly hair, such as twist outs, Bantu knots, or Curlformers.

4. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, and apply the method to each section.

5. Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair dry completely before unraveling the twists or knots.

6. Apply a Holding Product

Apply a holding product, such as a hair gel or mousse, to make the curls last longer.

Useful Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve curly hair:

1. Experiment with Different Products

Not all products work for everyone, so it's essential to experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

2. Don't Over-Manipulate Your Hair

Over-manipulating your hair can cause breakage and damage. Be gentle when styling your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wear a satin or silk bonnet or use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your curls while you sleep.

FAQs

1. Can I get my relaxed hair curly?

Yes, you can get your relaxed hair curly with the right techniques and products.

2. Is it possible to get curly hair without heat?

Yes, there are many methods to achieve curly hair without heat, such as twist outs, Bantu knots, and Curlformers.

3. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people wash their hair once a week, while others wash it every two weeks. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care is Key

Getting your hair curly as a black guy is possible, but it requires proper hair care and the right techniques. Remember to moisturize your hair, avoid heat styling tools, and trim your ends regularly. Experiment with different products and methods to find what works best for your hair. With patience and dedication, you'll achieve the curly hair of your dreams!
