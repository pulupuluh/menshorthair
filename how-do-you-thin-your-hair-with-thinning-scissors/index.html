How To Thin Your Hair With Thinning Scissors In 2023

Written by Frank Mar 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning your hair using thinning scissors can help you achieve a more manageable and stylish look. If you have thick hair and want to reduce the volume, or if you have a hair style that requires a less bulky look, thinning scissors can be your best friend. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how do you thin your hair with thinning scissors, and provide you with tips and tricks to get the best results.

How to Use Thinning Scissors

Thinning scissors are a type of hair cutting scissors that have teeth on one of the blades, which are designed to remove some hair strands and reduce the volume of your hair. Here is a step by step guide for how to thin your hair with thinning scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before starting to cut your hair, you should wash it thoroughly and dry it with a towel. Wet hair is more difficult to cut, and it can make the thinning process more complicated.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections that are easy to handle, and clip them using hair clips. A good way to divide your hair is to start from the bottom of your head and work your way up.

Step 3: Start Cutting with Thinning Scissors

Hold the thinning scissors vertically, and start cutting your hair from the bottom. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Then, use the thinning scissors to cut the hair in a zigzag or back-and-forth motion, rather than a straight line.

Step 4: Continue Cutting

Continue cutting your hair using the thinning scissors, and move to the next section. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as it can make your hair look uneven.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After you have finished thinning your hair, blend it by using regular hair cutting scissors. This will help you achieve a more natural look, and avoid any noticeable differences between the thinned and non-thinned parts of your hair.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when using thinning scissors:

  • Use sharp thinning scissors for a clean and even cut.
  • Start with a small amount of hair, and cut gradually to avoid mistakes.
  • Use the thinning scissors only on the ends of your hair, and avoid cutting close to the scalp.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as it can make your hair look uneven.
  • Always blend the thinned parts of your hair with regular scissors to achieve a natural look.

FAQ

Can I use thinning scissors on any hair type?

Thinning scissors are most suitable for thick hair, and they may not work well on fine or thin hair. If you have fine hair, it is better to use regular hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

Can thinning scissors damage my hair?

Thinning scissors can damage your hair if they are not used properly. To avoid damaging your hair, use sharp scissors, and cut gradually. Also, avoid using thinning scissors too often, as it can make your hair look too thin and frizzy.

Hair Care After Thinning

After thinning your hair, you should take good care of it to maintain its healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips for hair care after thinning:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Thinning your hair with thinning scissors can be an easy and effective way to achieve a more manageable and stylish look. By following the steps and tips provided in this article, you can get the best results and maintain the health and shine of your hair.
