How To Cut Your Fringe Yourself Diy Guide

Written by Petter Mar 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your overgrown fringe and don't want to go to the salon? Cutting your own fringe can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be a simple task that you can do at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own fringe, giving you tips and tricks along the way.

How to Cut Your Fringe Yourself

To start with, it's essential to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror to see the back of your head. Additionally, make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting to cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own fringe:

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle, creating two equal sections.

Step 2: Comb the section of hair that you want to cut forward and secure the rest of your hair with clips or hair ties.

Step 3: Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and slide your fingers down to the length you want to cut. Make sure to keep your fingers parallel to your forehead.

Step 4: Use the scissors to cut straight across the hair. Do this slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Repeat this process on the other side, making sure the length is the same on both sides.

Step 6: Comb your fringe and check for any unevenness. If you notice any, carefully trim the ends until they are even.

Tips for Cutting Your Fringe Yourself

Here are some tips to make sure your fringe cutting goes well:

  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to avoid any unwanted split ends.
  • Try not to cut your fringe too short, as it will take time to grow back.
  • Make sure to cut your fringe when you have plenty of time and won't be rushed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your fringe:

Q: How often should I cut my fringe?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your fringe becomes overgrown. Typically, it's best to cut your fringe every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Is it possible to cut my fringe too short?

A: Yes, it's possible to cut your fringe too short. To avoid this, cut your hair in small sections and take your time. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

Q: Can I cut my fringe if I have curly or wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can still cut your fringe if you have curly or wavy hair. However, it's best to cut your hair when it's dry to ensure you don't cut too much off.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Fringe

After cutting your fringe, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Now you know how to cut your fringe yourself! With the right tools and technique, it's a simple task that anyone can do. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and tricks, and you'll have a great-looking fringe in no time.
