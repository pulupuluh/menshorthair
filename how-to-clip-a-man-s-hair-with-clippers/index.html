How To Clip A Man s Hair With Clippers Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Clipping a man's hair with clippers is a simple and straightforward process that can be done at home. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, this step by step guide will help you achieve a clean and stylish haircut. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to prepare for the haircut, to tips for achieving the perfect cut, and how to care for your hair after the haircut.

How to Prepare for the Haircut

Before you start clipping, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of clippers, a comb, hair clips, a mirror, and a cape or towel to protect your clothes. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help you wet the hair if necessary.

Next, decide on the length of the haircut. You can start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length. It's always better to start with a longer guard and go shorter if needed, rather than starting too short and having to wait for the hair to grow back.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start clipping, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can cause the clippers to snag. Use a towel to dry the hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Use a comb to remove any tangles or knots in the hair. Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls. If the hair is particularly thick, you may need to use hair clips to section it off.

Step 3: Choose the Guard

Choose the guard that corresponds to the desired length of the haircut. Attach the guard to the clippers and make sure it is securely in place.

Step 4: Start Clipping

Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the clippers to cut the hair in even strokes, moving against the direction of hair growth. As you move up the head, use the comb to lift the hair and clip it at the desired length. Work your way around the head, making sure to keep the clippers level and even.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've clipped the hair to the desired length, use a shorter guard to blend the hair around the edges. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until the hair is blended evenly.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use a trimmer to clean up the neckline and around the ears. This will give the haircut a clean and polished look.

Step 7: Check for Any Stray Hairs

Once you've finished clipping, use a mirror to check for any stray hairs or uneven spots. Make any necessary adjustments to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Cut

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

- Use the comb to lift the hair and clip it at the desired length.

- Keep the clippers level and even to avoid uneven spots.

- Use a shorter guard to blend the hair around the edges.

- Clean up the neckline and around the ears for a polished look.

FAQ

Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your own hair. However, it can be difficult to achieve a professional-looking cut on your own. It's always better to have someone else do it for you if possible.

What is the best way to clean clippers?

The best way to clean clippers is to remove any hair from the blades with a brush. Then, use a disinfectant spray or wipe to sanitize the blades. Make sure to oil the blades regularly to keep them in good condition.

How often should I clip my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to clip your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair. Regular trims can also help maintain the shape of the haircut.

Clipping a man's hair with clippers is a simple process that can be done at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and stylish haircut. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect cut every time.
