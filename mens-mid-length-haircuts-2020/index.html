Mens Mid Length Haircuts 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's trendy and low maintenance, then a mid-length haircut is perfect for you. In 2020, mens mid length haircuts were all the rage, and they're still popular today. In this post, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect mid-length haircut, as well as tips and tricks for taking care of your hair.

Description

A mid-length haircut is a versatile hairstyle that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a great option for men who want a stylish haircut that doesn't require a lot of maintenance. You can wear your hair down for a casual look or style it up for a more formal occasion. There are many different types of mid-length haircuts to choose from, including shaggy, layered, and textured styles.

How to Get a Mid-Length Haircut

Getting a mid-length haircut is easy. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you go to the salon, decide on the type of mid-length haircut you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines and bring pictures with you to show your stylist.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to determine if the hairstyle you want will work with your hair type and face shape. They can also make suggestions on how to customize the look to suit your individual style.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you and your stylist have decided on a style, they will begin cutting your hair. Mid-length haircuts typically involve layering and texturizing to create movement and volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, your stylist will show you how to style your hair. They may recommend using a styling product to enhance your hair's texture and hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mid-Length Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your mid-length haircut looking fresh:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. Avoid using hot water, which can dry out your hair and scalp.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy. This will also help to prevent split ends.

Tip 3: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. This will help to keep it looking shiny and healthy.

Tip 4: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

FAQ

What face shapes suit mid-length haircuts?

Mid-length haircuts can work for many different face shapes. If you have a round face, opt for a layered style that adds volume to the top of your head. If you have a square face, a shaggy style with messy layers can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any mid-length style.

What hair types work best for mid-length haircuts?

Mid-length haircuts can work for almost any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, certain styles may work better for certain hair types. For example, a textured shaggy style can work well for thin or fine hair, while a layered style can add volume to thick hair.

Can I still wear my hair up with a mid-length haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a mid-length haircut. You can try a messy bun, a half-up style, or a slicked-back look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish mid-length haircut. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products. Trim your hair regularly, and use a hair mask once a week to keep it looking shiny and healthy. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
