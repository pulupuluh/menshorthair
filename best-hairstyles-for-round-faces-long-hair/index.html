Best Hairstyles For Round Faces Long Hair In 2023

Written by Christine Feb 23, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don’t seem to work for your round face and long hair? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best hairstyles for round faces and long hair that are trendy and stylish in 2023. Here’s a table of contents to help you navigate:

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face and long hair, it’s all about creating balance. You want to avoid hairstyles that add more volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create height, length, and volume at the crown of your head.

How To

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face and long hair is to first determine your face shape. To do this, measure the widest point of your face (usually across your cheekbones) and compare it to the length of your face (from your hairline to your chin). If your face width and length are about the same, you likely have a round face shape.

Step-by-Step

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces and long hair:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round faces and long hair, as they help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer towards the ends of your hair.

2. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a trendy and stylish option for round faces and long hair. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Then, use your fingers to tousle and separate the waves for a more relaxed look.

3. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a great way to create height and length at the crown of your head, which helps to balance out a round face shape. To achieve this look, pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic. Then, use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head for added volume.

4. Side Part

A side part is a simple yet effective way to create balance for a round face shape. To achieve this look, part your hair on the side and sweep it over to one side of your face. This helps to create the illusion of length and draws attention away from the roundness of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face and long hair:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and length at the crown of your head.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth in your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces and long hair:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face and long hair?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or wide curls. These can make your face look rounder.

What hairstyles are best for adding height and length to a round face?

Hairstyles that create height and length at the crown of your head, such as high ponytails or long layers, are great options for adding balance to a round face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining healthy hair, it’s important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have some ideas for the best hairstyles for round faces and long hair in 2023, it’s time to book an appointment with your stylist and try them out for yourself. Remember, the key is to find a hairstyle that creates balance and works well with your unique face shape and hair type. Good luck!
