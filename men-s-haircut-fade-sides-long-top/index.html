Men s Haircut Fade Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that can fit any occasion? Look no further than the men's haircut fade sides long top. This haircut has been gaining popularity over the years and for a good reason. It's versatile, low maintenance, and looks great on all face shapes. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the men's haircut fade sides long top, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Long Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The men's haircut fade sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and a longer length on top. The sides are gradually tapered, and the length increases as it goes towards the top of the head. This style is perfect for men who want a low maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and put together. It's also easy to style and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Long Top

The men's haircut fade sides long top can be achieved by going to a professional hairstylist or barber. It's essential to find a stylist who has experience creating this type of haircut to ensure that it looks as good as possible. When getting the haircut, it's crucial to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want. Bring in pictures or examples of the haircut to help your stylist understand what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's haircut fade sides long top:

  1. Start by cutting the sides of the hair short using clippers.
  2. Gradually increase the length of the hair as you move towards the top of the head.
  3. Blend the sides and the top using scissors or clippers.
  4. Style the hair on top using your preferred product.

Styling Tips

To style the men's haircut fade sides long top, use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips, then work through the hair on top. Use your fingers to create texture and add volume. For a more polished look, use a comb to style the hair.

FAQ

Who can get the men's haircut fade sides long top?

The men's haircut fade sides long top can be worn by anyone, regardless of face shape or hair type. It's a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit individual preferences.

How often should I get the haircut?

It's recommended to get the men's haircut fade sides long top every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that works for you.

Hair Care

To keep the men's haircut fade sides long top looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage the hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.

In conclusion, the men's haircut fade sides long top is a trendy and versatile haircut that's perfect for any occasion. With the right styling products and hair care, you can keep this haircut looking great for weeks to come.
