How To Style Baby Boy Hair Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a parent of a baby boy, you know how important it is to keep him looking cute and tidy. However, many parents are hesitant to use hair gel or other styling products on their little one's delicate hair. The good news is that there are plenty of ways to style baby boy hair without gel. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling your baby boy's hair without using gel.

Table of Contents

  • How To Style Baby Boy Hair Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Baby Boy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Baby Boys

How To Style Baby Boy Hair Without Gel

There are many ways to style baby boy hair without using gel. Some popular methods include:

1. Natural Texture

One of the easiest ways to style baby boy hair is to work with his natural texture. If your baby boy has curly hair, you can simply use a wide-tooth comb to detangle and shape his curls. If your baby boy has straight hair, you can use a fine-tooth comb to create a neat part and comb his hair to the side.

2. Water

Another way to style baby boy hair without gel is to use water. Wetting your baby boy's hair can help you shape and style it. You can either wet his hair by misting it with a spray bottle or by giving him a bath.

3. Hair Wax

If you're looking for a product to help you style your baby boy's hair without using gel, hair wax is a great option. Hair wax is a styling product that provides hold and texture without making hair look greasy or shiny. Simply apply a small amount of hair wax to your baby boy's hair and style as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling baby boy hair without gel:

Step 1: Wet hair

Wet your baby boy's hair by misting it with a spray bottle or giving him a bath. Wetting his hair will make it easier to shape and style.

Step 2: Apply hair wax

Apply a small amount of hair wax to your baby boy's hair. Be sure to use a gentle, baby-friendly hair wax that won't irritate his delicate scalp.

Step 3: Style hair

Use a comb or your fingers to shape and style your baby boy's hair. You can create a neat part, comb his hair to the side, or tousle his hair for a more casual look.

Step 4: Let hair dry

Allow your baby boy's hair to air dry or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting. Be sure to use a diffuser attachment to protect his delicate hair and scalp from heat damage.

Tips for Styling Baby Boy Hair

Here are some tips for styling your baby boy's hair without using gel:

1. Use gentle products

Be sure to use gentle, baby-friendly hair products that won't irritate your baby boy's delicate scalp.

2. Don't over-style

Remember that your baby boy's hair is delicate and fragile. Don't over-style it or use too much product, as this can damage his hair and scalp.

3. Work with natural texture

Try to work with your baby boy's natural hair texture rather than fighting against it. This will help you achieve a natural and effortless look.

FAQ

Can I use regular hair gel on my baby boy?

While it's technically possible to use regular hair gel on your baby boy, we don't recommend it. Regular hair gel can be too harsh for your baby boy's delicate scalp and can cause irritation or even hair loss.

What if my baby boy doesn't have much hair?

If your baby boy doesn't have much hair, you can still style it by using a soft brush or comb to create a neat part or shape his hair into a cute little fauxhawk.

Hair Care for Baby Boys

When it comes to caring for your baby boy's hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Be gentle

Remember that your baby boy's hair is delicate and fragile. Be gentle when brushing, combing, or styling his hair to avoid damaging it.

2. Wash hair regularly

Wash your baby boy's hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Be sure to use a gentle, baby-friendly shampoo that won't irritate his scalp.

3. Protect hair from the sun

When you're outside with your baby boy, be sure to protect his hair from the sun by using a hat or sunscreen.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily style your baby boy's hair without using gel. Remember to be gentle and use baby-friendly products, and your little one's hair will look cute and tidy in no time!
