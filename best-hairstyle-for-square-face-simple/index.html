Best Hairstyle For Square Face Simple A Guide To Flattering Haircuts

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a cut that flatters your features and accentuates your jawline, without making your face look too angular or boxy. Fortunately, there are plenty of simple, stylish haircuts that can work well with a square face shape.

Description

As the name suggests, a square face shape is characterized by strong, angular jawline and a broad forehead. The width and length of the face are roughly equal, and the cheekbones are usually quite prominent. When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a square face shape, the goal is to soften the angles of the face and create a more balanced, harmonious look.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Simple

When selecting a haircut for a square face shape, the key is to create the illusion of length and softness. You want to choose styles that add volume and movement to the hair, without adding too much width to the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, which can accentuate the squareness of the face.
  • Consider long, layered styles that frame the face and add movement to the hair.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to soften a square jawline.
  • Avoid styles that are too short, as they can make the face look boxy.
  • Consider adding layers to the hair to create volume and movement.
  • Soft waves or curls can add dimension to the hair and create a more feminine look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Cut

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut that flatters a square face shape:

  1. Start by consulting with a stylist who has experience working with square face shapes.
  2. Discuss your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style preferences with your stylist.
  3. Consider bringing in photos of hairstyles that you like to help your stylist understand your vision.
  4. Your stylist will likely recommend a cut that is longer in the front and shorter in the back, with layers to add movement and volume.
  5. If you want bangs, consider side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle.
  6. Be sure to discuss styling options with your stylist, and ask for tips on how to recreate the look at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking fresh and flattering:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Consider using a curling iron or hot rollers to add soft waves or curls to your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can make it look stiff and unnatural.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your haircut looking its best.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for a square face shape?

The best hairstyles for a square face shape are those that add volume and movement to the hair, while softening the angles of the face. Consider long, layered styles with side-swept bangs, or soft waves and curls.

Should I avoid blunt, straight-across bangs if I have a square face shape?

Yes, blunt, straight-across bangs can accentuate the squareness of the face and make it look boxy. Consider side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides and shorter in the middle, for a more flattering look.

How often should I visit my stylist for trims and touch-ups?

It is generally recommended that you visit your stylist every six to eight weeks for trims and touch-ups, to keep your haircut looking fresh and flattering.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Shapes

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, there are a few hair care tips that can help to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using heavy, greasy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or flat irons, to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage to the hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your square face shape, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
