Easy Bun Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have shoulder-length hair and are looking for quick and easy hairstyles, then buns are the perfect option for you. Not only are they versatile, but they also add a touch of elegance to your overall look. In this blog post, we will share some easy bun hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can try out at home. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Buns are hairstyles that involve twisting or wrapping your hair into a circular shape and securing it with pins, clips, or hair ties. They can be worn in different ways, such as high or low, messy or sleek, and are suitable for various occasions, from casual to formal events. With shoulder-length hair, you have plenty of options to create different bun hairstyles that suit your taste and face shape.

How To

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first go over some basic tips to prepare your hair for bun hairstyles:

  • Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.
  • Apply some hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Use a hair elastic or pins that match your hair color to secure the bun.

Step by Step

1. Low Bun

To create a low bun hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a coil.
  2. Tuck the ends under the coil and secure them with bobby pins or an elastic band.
  3. Adjust the bun as desired, and use hairspray to hold it in place.

2. High Bun

To create a high bun hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or an elastic band, and use hairspray to hold it in place.

3. Braided Bun

To create a braided bun hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair in the middle and create two braids on each side.
  2. Twist the braids around each other and create a bun at the back of your head.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or an elastic band, and use hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your bun hairstyles look even better:

  • Add hair accessories, such as a headband, scarf, or flowers, to enhance the look.
  • Create a messy bun by pulling out some strands and loosening the bun for a relaxed vibe.
  • Create a sleek bun by using a comb and some hair gel to smoothen the hair.
  • Create a half-up bun by tying half of your hair in a bun and leaving the rest down.

FAQ

Q: Can I create a bun with short hair?

A: Yes, you can create a bun with short hair by using a bun maker or hair extensions to add length and volume.

Q: How long does it take to create a bun hairstyle?

A: It depends on the complexity of the bun hairstyle and your hair type. Simple buns can take a few minutes, while intricate ones may take up to 30 minutes.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

In conclusion, bun hairstyles are easy, versatile, and perfect for shoulder length hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can create different bun hairstyles that suit your style and occasion. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its beauty and health. Have fun experimenting with different bun hairstyles!
