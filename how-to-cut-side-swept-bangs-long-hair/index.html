How To Cut Side Swept Bangs For Long Hair

Written by Daniel May 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to change up your look without making a drastic change? One way to do that is by adding side swept bangs to your long hair. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to cut side swept bangs for long hair.

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can suit any face shape. They are cut diagonally across the forehead and swept to one side. This style can add volume to your hair and give you a fresh, new look. However, cutting your own bangs can be daunting, especially if you don't have any experience. But don't worry, we'll guide you through the process.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs for Long Hair

Before you start cutting your bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also need a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Section your hair

Part your hair in the middle and bring the front section forward. This will be the section you'll be cutting.

Step 2: Decide on the length

Decide on the length of your bangs. It's always better to start longer and take off more if needed.

Step 3: Cut the bangs

Hold the hair between your forefinger and middle finger and cut diagonally. Cut small sections at a time to ensure that the length is even. Remember to cut at an angle, not straight across.

Step 4: Check the length

Check the length of your bangs by letting them fall naturally. If they're too long, you can trim them again. If they're too short, there's not much you can do except wait for them to grow out.

Step 5: Style your bangs

Style your bangs by sweeping them to the side. Use a round brush to blow dry them if needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your side swept bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs and cut them gradually to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don't cut your bangs when you're in a hurry or feeling stressed. Take your time and be patient.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across as this can create a blunt, unflattering look.
  • Ask someone for help if you're not confident in cutting your own bangs.

FAQ

Can I cut my bangs wet or dry?

It's best to cut your bangs when your hair is dry as wet hair can shrink when it dries, making your bangs shorter than intended.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. As a general rule, trim them every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Bangs grow back quickly, and there are ways to style them to hide any imperfections. You can also go to a salon to get them fixed.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your side swept bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid any build-up of oil and dirt.
  • Use a nourishing hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends.

Cutting your own side swept bangs for long hair can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. Just remember to take your time, start with longer bangs, and use sharp scissors. Follow these steps, and you'll have perfect side swept bangs in no time!
