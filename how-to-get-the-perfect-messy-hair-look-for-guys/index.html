How To Get The Perfect Messy Hair Look For Guys

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet trendy look, the perfect messy hair look for guys is the way to go. This style has been in trend for years now and is still a popular choice among men. Plus, it doesn't require much effort, making it perfect for those who don't have much time to style their hair. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect messy hair look for guys.

Description

The messy hair look is all about creating a natural and effortless hairstyle that looks slightly unkempt, yet stylish. This style is perfect for guys who have medium to long hair and want to look trendy without putting in too much effort.

How to

To achieve the perfect messy hair look, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type. Use a conditioner to soften your hair and make it easier to style. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of hair styling product like hair wax, pomade, or clay to your hair. Rub the product between your palms and run your fingers through your hair to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Create texture

Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a natural-looking messy style.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

To hold your hairstyle in place, spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair. This will keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect messy hair look:

  • Use a hair styling product that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the perfect messy hair look for you.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair without ruining the messy look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the perfect messy hair look for guys:

Q: Can I get the messy hair look with short hair?

A: The messy hair look works best with medium to long hair, but you can still create a messy style with short hair. Use a hair styling product with a matte finish to create texture and volume.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain the messy hair look?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to maintain the natural oils in your hair and prevent it from drying out. However, if your hair gets too oily, you can wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I create the messy hair look without using hair styling products?

A: While hair styling products help create and hold the messy hair look, you can still create a messy style without using any products. Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair and tousle it with your fingers to create texture.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Use a conditioner to soften your hair and make it easier to style.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve the perfect messy hair look for guys. So, go ahead and give it a try!
