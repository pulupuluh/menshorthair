How To Get Soft Curls On Natural Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Mar 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools and harsh chemicals to achieve the perfect curls? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing how to get soft curls on natural hair without damaging your locks. From prep to styling, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description

Natural hair is beautiful and versatile, but sometimes it can be difficult to achieve the desired curl pattern without causing damage. Soft curls are a great way to add dimension and bounce to your natural hair without compromising its health. This guide will provide you with tips and tricks to achieve soft, defined curls without using heat or harmful chemicals.

How To

Before we dive into the step-by-step process of achieving soft curls, let’s go over some basics. First, it’s important to determine your hair type and porosity. This will help you choose the right products and techniques for your hair. Second, make sure you have the right tools on hand, including a wide-tooth comb, hair clips, a spray bottle, and styling products.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, detangled hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them hydrated throughout the day.

3. Section your hair into small sections, using hair clips to keep them in place.

4. Take one section at a time and apply a curl defining gel or mousse from roots to ends.

5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the section and distribute the product evenly.

6. Twirl the section around your finger to create a spiral curl. You can also use a curling wand to create more defined curls.

7. Repeat steps 4-6 for each section of hair, making sure to apply product and detangle each section before curling.

8. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process.

9. Once your hair is completely dry, use a pick or wide-tooth comb to separate the curls and create volume.

10. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

- Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

- Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

- Avoid touching your hair too much once it’s dry to prevent frizz.

- Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve soft curls on short hair?

A: Yes, you can! The process is the same, just adjust the size of your sections and curls accordingly.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain soft curls?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing your hair once or twice a week is sufficient.

Q: Can I use heat tools to achieve soft curls?

A: While it is possible, we recommend avoiding heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after styling. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, deep condition regularly, and avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve soft, healthy curls on your natural hair.
