How To Cut Wispy Front Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Feb 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking for a new hairstyle that’s both trendy and easy to maintain, wispy front bangs might be the perfect choice for you. These soft, face-framing bangs are a popular choice among celebrities and fashion icons, and they’re surprisingly simple to cut at home. In this guide, we’ll show you how to cut wispy front bangs in just a few easy steps.

How to Cut Wispy Front Bangs: Step by Step

Before we get started, here’s a list of the tools you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step 1: Decide on the Length

First, decide how long you want your bangs to be. It’s best to start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter, so take it slow and be patient.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the fine-toothed comb to section off the hair you want to cut. For wispy front bangs, you’ll want to section off a triangular shape in the front of your hairline.

Step 3: Spray Your Hair

Spray your hair with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

With your scissors, cut the hair in a straight line across your forehead. Take it slow and make sure you’re cutting evenly.

Step 5: Start Texturizing

Now it’s time to start texturizing your bangs. Hold your scissors vertically and make small snips in the hair. This will create the wispy, feathered effect.

Step 6: Check the Length

Check the length of your bangs and make any necessary adjustments. Remember, it’s better to start longer and gradually cut shorter.

Step 7: Style Your Bangs

Style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush or simply air-drying them.

Tips for Cutting Wispy Front Bangs

Here are a few extra tips to help you achieve the perfect wispy front bangs:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Take it slow and be patient.
  • When in doubt, cut less. You can always cut more later.
  • Don’t cut your bangs when they’re wet. They’ll look shorter when they dry.

FAQs

How often should I trim my wispy front bangs?

It’s best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut wispy front bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to cut them while your hair is dry so you can see exactly how they’ll look.

What if I mess up?

Don’t worry! If you’re not happy with your bangs, you can always go to a salon to have them fixed.

Hair Care Tips for Wispy Front Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep your wispy front bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying your hair.
  • Consider using dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.

That’s it! Now you know how to cut wispy front bangs like a pro. With a little practice, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect look every time.
