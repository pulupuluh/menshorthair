How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to save some money and cut your own hair at home, using scissors is a great option. Not only is it cost-effective, but it also gives you more control over the final result. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting men's hair with scissors.

Description

Cutting men's hair with scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. All you need is a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and some patience. By following the steps below, you can achieve a professional-looking cut without having to leave your house.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an accurate cut.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use a comb to section the hair into manageable parts. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Use clips to hold the sections in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut with the scissors pointing upwards. This will give you a layered look and prevent the hair from looking too blunt.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you've finished the back, move on to the sides. Use the same technique as before, holding the hair between your fingers and cutting upwards. Be careful not to cut too much off at once.

Step 5: Cut the Top

For the top of the head, use a comb to lift the hair up and cut with the scissors pointing downwards. This will help to blend the layers together and give a more natural look.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If your hair has bangs, use the same technique as before to cut them. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut upwards, being careful not to cut too much off at once.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They'll make the process much easier and give you a more professional-looking cut.
  • Take your time. Cutting hair can be a slow process, especially if you're doing it for the first time.
  • Start with small snips. It's easier to cut more off than it is to add it back on!
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go. This will help you see if you're cutting evenly on both sides.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with scissors:

Is it hard to cut men's hair with scissors?

It can be tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. However, with practice and patience, you can achieve great results.

Do I need any special equipment?

All you need is a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and some clips to hold the hair in place.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to take your time and be patient. It's also a good idea to have someone else check your progress as you go.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut without having to leave your house. So grab your scissors and get started!
