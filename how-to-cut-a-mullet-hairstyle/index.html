How To Cut A Mullet Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and classic, the mullet might just be the perfect choice for you. This iconic cut has been around for decades and continues to be a popular choice among both men and women. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut a mullet hairstyle, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

The mullet is a hairstyle that features short hair on the top and sides and long hair in the back. It's often associated with the 1980s, but it's still a popular choice for many people today. The mullet can be styled in a variety of ways, from a sleek and polished look to a more edgy and grungy style.

How to Cut a Mullet Hairstyle

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and clippers if you plan on cutting the sides and back of your hair short.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use the comb to create a part down the middle of your head, separating the top and sides from the back. Clip the top and sides out of the way so you can focus on the back.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Using your scissors, start cutting the back of your hair. Begin by cutting a straight line across the bottom, making sure it's even. Then, start cutting the length of your hair in the back, working from the bottom up. Make sure to leave enough length for the mullet effect.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

If you want to keep the sides short, use your clippers to trim them down. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Make sure to blend the sides into the back for a seamless look.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Once you're done with the back and sides, unclip the top of your hair and start cutting. The length of the top can vary depending on your preference, but make sure it's short enough to create a contrast with the longer back.

Tips

  • Work in small sections to ensure even cutting
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair
  • Don't cut too much at once – it's better to take off a little at a time
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I cut a mullet hairstyle at home?

A: Yes, you can cut a mullet hairstyle at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you're not confident in your cutting abilities, it's always best to visit a professional stylist.

Q: What face shape is best for a mullet?

A: The mullet can work on a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with square or oval faces.

Q: How often should I cut my mullet?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the length. Generally, you should aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your mullet looking fresh.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized, and avoid using too much heat when styling. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Cutting a mullet hairstyle can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create the perfect mullet in no time.
