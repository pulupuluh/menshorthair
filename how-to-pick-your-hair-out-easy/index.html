How To Pick Your Hair Out Easy

Written by Christine Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read
Are you tired of struggling to pick your hair out? It can be frustrating and time-consuming, but with the right techniques, it can be easy and stress-free. In this article, we will show you how to pick your hair out easy in just a few simple steps.

Description

Picking your hair out is the process of separating your curls to create a fuller and more voluminous look. It is a popular styling method for those with natural hair, but it can also be done by those with relaxed hair. However, picking out relaxed hair can be a bit more challenging. In this article, we will focus on how to pick out relaxed hair easily.

How to

Here are the steps to follow for easy hair picking:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start picking your hair out, you need to wash and condition it. This will make your hair softer and easier to manage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to remove any knots or tangles in your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage and make it easier to pick out.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry your hair. Make sure to use a comb attachment to help straighten your hair while drying it.

Step 5: Apply Oil

Apply a small amount of oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Step 6: Pick Your Hair Out

Use a pick comb to pick out your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Tips

- Use a pick comb with wide teeth to prevent breakage - Don't pick your hair out when it is wet - Avoid using too much heat on your hair - Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction on your hair

FAQ

Can I pick my hair out every day?

Yes, you can pick your hair out every day, but it is not necessary. Picking your hair out once or twice a week is enough to maintain a full and voluminous look.

Can I use a regular comb instead of a pick comb?

No, you should not use a regular comb to pick out your hair. Regular combs have tightly spaced teeth that can damage your hair and cause breakage.

How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled while picking it out?

Apply a leave-in conditioner and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before picking it out. This will make the process easier and prevent your hair from getting tangled.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner - Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair - Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools - Avoid using too much heat on your hair - Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction on your hair - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends In conclusion, picking your hair out can be easy and stress-free if you follow the right techniques. By washing, conditioning, blow-drying, and picking your hair out gently, you can achieve a full and voluminous look without damaging your hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
