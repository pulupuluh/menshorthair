How To Make Hair Stop Falling Out After Pregnancy

Written by Oliver Mar 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a new mom, you may have noticed that your hair is thinning or falling out after pregnancy. This is a common occurrence due to hormonal changes during pregnancy and postpartum. However, there are ways to prevent or minimize hair loss. In this article, we'll share tips and steps to make your hair stop falling out after pregnancy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Stop Falling Out After Pregnancy
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Loss
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Pregnancy

How to Make Hair Stop Falling Out After Pregnancy

The first step to preventing hair loss is to maintain a healthy lifestyle. This includes eating a well-balanced diet, staying hydrated, and getting plenty of sleep. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair, such as hair dyes or straighteners. Here are some other steps you can take:

1. Take Supplements

Supplements such as biotin, iron, and vitamin D can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. You can find these supplements at your local drugstore or online. Make sure to consult with your doctor before taking any supplements, especially if you're breastfeeding.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can stimulate hair growth and increase blood flow to the hair follicles. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day. You can also use a scalp massager, which can be found at most drugstores or online.

3. Use Natural Oils

Natural oils such as coconut oil, castor oil, and argan oil can help moisturize your hair and prevent breakage. Apply a small amount of oil to your hair and scalp, then massage it in. Leave the oil on for a few hours or overnight before washing it out.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and buns can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Instead, opt for looser hairstyles that don't put as much tension on your hair.

Step by Step Guide

To make your hair stop falling out after pregnancy, follow these steps:

Step 1: Maintain a healthy lifestyle

Eat a well-balanced diet, stay hydrated, and get plenty of sleep.

Step 2: Take supplements

Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Step 3: Massage your scalp

Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Step 4: Use natural oils

Apply a small amount of oil to your hair and scalp, then massage it in. Leave the oil on for a few hours or overnight before washing it out.

Step 5: Avoid tight hairstyles

Opt for looser hairstyles that don't put as much tension on your hair.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling on your hair
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Don't tie your hair up when it's wet
  • Avoid stress as much as possible
  • Don't smoke

FAQs

Q: Is hair loss after pregnancy normal?

A: Yes, hair loss after pregnancy is common due to hormonal changes.

Q: How long does postpartum hair loss last?

A: Postpartum hair loss usually lasts for a few months, but can last up to a year.

Q: Can breastfeeding cause hair loss?

A: Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair loss due to hormonal changes.

Hair Care After Pregnancy

After pregnancy, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage and promote growth. Here are some tips:

  • Continue taking supplements
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Don't tie your hair up too tightly
  • Get regular trims to prevent breakage

By following these tips and steps, you can make your hair stop falling out after pregnancy and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time for your hair to fully recover.
