How to Straighten the Back of Your Hair by Yourself

Straightening the back of your hair can be difficult, but with the right tools and technique, it's definitely doable. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it until it's completely dry. This will ensure that you have a smooth base to work with.

Step 2: Section your hair into manageable parts. Clip the top half of your hair up and out of the way so that you can focus on the back.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 4: Using a flat iron, clamp down on the section of hair as close to the roots as possible. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of your hair, being careful not to stop and start as this can leave a crease. Repeat this process on each section of hair until you've straightened the entire back of your head.

Step 5: Once you've finished straightening the back of your hair, unclip the top half of your hair and repeat the process until you've straightened all of your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Invest in a good quality flat iron. Cheap flat irons can damage your hair and won't straighten it as effectively.

Tip 2: Use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair to prevent damage.

Tip 3: Comb through each section of hair before straightening it to remove any tangles.

Tip 4: Take your time and work in small sections to ensure that you get every strand of hair straight.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten the back of my hair without a mirror?

A: It's definitely possible, but it will be more difficult. If you can, try to position yourself in front of a mirror so that you can see what you're doing.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage. Aim to straighten your hair no more than once a week.

Hair Care

Straightening your hair can cause damage if you don't take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Tip 2: Avoid using hot styling tools every day.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, as this will cause less damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to straighten the back of your hair by yourself in no time. Just remember to take your time and be gentle with your hair to avoid damage.
