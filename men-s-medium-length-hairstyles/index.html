Men s Medium Length Hairstyles A Guide To Stylish Trendy Haircuts In 2023

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's not too long or too short, then men's medium length hairstyles are the way to go. In this guide, we'll show you some of the best medium length hairstyles for men in 2023. We'll cover everything from how to get the perfect cut, to styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Get the Perfect Medium Length Haircut

Before you can start styling your medium length hair, you need to get the right haircut. Here's how to get the perfect medium length haircut:

  1. Find a good hairstylist who has experience cutting men's hair.
  2. Show them a picture of the hairstyle you want, or describe it in detail.
  3. Discuss your hair type and face shape with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  4. Ask for a medium length cut that's not too short or too long.
  5. Choose a style that will work with your natural hair texture and thickness.

Remember, communication is key when it comes to getting the perfect haircut. Be clear and specific about what you want, and don't be afraid to ask questions if you're unsure about anything.

Trendy Medium Length Hairstyles for Men

Now that you've got your medium length haircut, it's time to start styling your hair. Here are some of the best medium length hairstyles for men in 2023:

The Messy Textured Look

This hairstyle is all about creating a messy, textured look. To achieve this look, apply a small amount of texturizing cream to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled style.

The Slicked Back Look

The slicked back look is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair and comb it back away from your face.

The Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that's perfect for medium length hair. To achieve this look, create a deep side part and comb your hair to one side. You can use a small amount of pomade or gel to keep your hair in place.

Styling Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hair styling product, such as pomade or texturizing cream.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a polished, refined look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add some color or highlights to your hair to give it some extra dimension.

FAQs About Medium Length Hairstyles for Men

Q: Is medium length hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, medium length hair is relatively easy to maintain. However, you'll need to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a good quality styling product to keep it looking its best.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with medium length hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with medium length hair. Just make sure to style your hair in a way that won't get flattened or messed up by your hat.

Q: Can I style my medium length hair for a formal occasion?

A: Yes, medium length hair can be styled for a formal occasion. Just make sure to choose a polished, refined hairstyle that complements your outfit.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair looking healthy and strong:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often. These can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and trendy medium length hairstyle in 2023. So go ahead and experiment with different looks, and don't be afraid to step out of your comfort zone!
