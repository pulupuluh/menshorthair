How To Control Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame your locks. It can be frustrating to deal with frizz, knots, and unmanageable curls on a daily basis. However, with the right techniques and products, you can control your hair and achieve the look you want. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to control thick curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Control Thick Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair
  4. FAQ on Controlling Thick Curly Hair
  5. Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

How to Control Thick Curly Hair

The first step in controlling thick curly hair is to find the right products for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for curly hair, as these will help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz. Avoid products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and keep them moisturized. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

When styling your hair, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause breakage and make your hair frizzy. You can also try diffusing your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting to help set your curls and reduce frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to control thick curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Detangle your hair with a wide-toothed comb or your fingers.
  4. Part your hair and style it as desired.
  5. Use a diffuser on a low heat setting to help set your curls.
  6. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ on Controlling Thick Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling thick curly hair:

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally 2-3 times a week is sufficient. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Q: What products should I use to control frizz?

A: Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter, as these can help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out and make it frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from chlorine and salt water by wearing a swim cap or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like relaxers or perms, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control your thick curly hair and achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
