How To Get Thick Curly Hair Naturally In 2023

Written by Frank Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for ways to get thick curly hair naturally, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will be discussing how to achieve thick, bouncy, and voluminous curls using natural methods. We will be covering everything from how to care for your hair to how to style it. So, without further ado, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Get Thick Curly Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Hair Care

The first step in achieving thick curly hair naturally is to take care of your hair. This means using gentle products that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Avoid using shampoo with sulfates and opt for a conditioner that contains natural oils and humectants like coconut oil, shea butter, and honey. These ingredients help to nourish and moisturize your hair, leaving it soft and manageable.

Another important aspect of hair care is to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. These can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Instead, try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create soft, bouncy curls. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

How to Get Thick Curly Hair Naturally

Now that you know how to care for your hair, let's talk about how to get thick curly hair naturally. One of the best ways to achieve this is by using natural hair masks. These can be made at home using ingredients like avocado, banana, olive oil, and honey. Simply mix the ingredients together and apply to your hair, leaving it on for 30 minutes before rinsing thoroughly.

Another way to get thick curly hair naturally is to eat a healthy diet. Foods like salmon, avocado, nuts, and leafy greens are all rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth. Additionally, drinking plenty of water can help to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving thick curly hair naturally:

  1. Cleanse your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  2. Apply a natural hair mask and leave on for 30 minutes
  3. Rinse thoroughly and apply a natural conditioner
  4. Gently detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help nourish and protect your hair
  6. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create soft, bouncy curls
  7. Avoid using heat styling tools whenever possible
  8. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  9. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  10. Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Tips for Maintaining Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining thick curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as this can cause frizz
  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a rough towel
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as this can dry out your hair

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use a hair mask every day?

A: No, it is not recommended to use a hair mask every day as this can lead to product buildup and weigh down your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, and apply a leave-in conditioner to help tame flyaways.

By following these tips and using natural methods, you can achieve thick, bouncy, and healthy curls naturally. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you will see results in no time!
