How To Make Natural Nappy Hair Curly

Written by William Aug 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have natural nappy hair, you may be looking for ways to make your hair curly. While there are many ways to achieve curly hair, not all methods are suitable for natural nappy hair. In this article, we will show you how to make your natural nappy hair curly using natural methods.

Description

Natural nappy hair is hair that has not been chemically treated to alter its texture. It is characterized by tight coils and curls that can be difficult to manage. Adding curls to natural nappy hair can be a challenge, but it is possible with the right methods.

How to

There are several methods you can use to make your natural nappy hair curly. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Method 1: Twist-Out

To create a twist-out, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a curling cream to your hair. Take small sections of your hair and twist them tightly. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer. Once your hair is dry, unravel the twists and separate the curls with your fingers.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a great way to create defined curls. To create Bantu knots, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a curling cream to your hair. Take small sections of your hair and twist them tightly. Coil the twisted sections into small knots and secure them with bobby pins. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer. Once your hair is dry, remove the bobby pins and unravel the knots.

Method 3: Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a great way to create bouncy curls. To create curls with flexi rods, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a curling cream to your hair. Take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the flexi rods. Secure the rods with the clips that come with them. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer. Once your hair is dry, remove the clips and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions for creating natural curls in your nappy hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your hair.

Step 3: Apply Curling Cream

Apply a curling cream to your hair to help define and hold your curls.

Step 4: Choose Your Curling Method

Choose the curling method that you prefer. You can choose from twist-outs, Bantu knots, or flexi rods.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Follow the instructions for your chosen curling method to create curls in your hair.

Step 6: Allow Your Hair to Dry

Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until it is completely dry.

Step 7: Separate Your Curls

Once your hair is dry, separate your curls gently with your fingers.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your natural nappy hair curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use a satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making natural nappy hair curly:

Can I use a curling iron to create curls in my natural nappy hair?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to create curls in your natural nappy hair, but it is not recommended. Curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

How long do the curls last?

The length of time your curls last depends on several factors, including the curling method you use, your hair type, and your hair care routine. Generally, curls created with twist-outs or Bantu knots can last up to a week, while curls created with flexi rods can last up to two weeks.

How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended that you wash your hair once a week to keep it clean and moisturized.

Hair Care

Taking care of your natural nappy hair is important to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin scarf or bonnet.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and methods, you can easily make your natural nappy hair curly. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.
