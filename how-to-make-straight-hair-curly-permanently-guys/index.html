How To Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently Guys

Written by Luffy Sep 05, 2023 · 9 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to make straight hair curly permanently for guys. Having curly hair is a great way to change up your look and it’s a style that has become increasingly popular in recent years. If you’re tired of constantly using heat styling tools or getting regular perms, then this guide is for you. We’ll be discussing various methods that can help you achieve curly hair permanently.

How to Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently

There are a few different methods that you can use to make your straight hair curly permanently. These methods include:

  • Chemical treatments
  • Hot rollers
  • Curling irons
  • Curling wands
  • Braids or twists

Each of these methods has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the best one for you will depend on your hair type and personal preferences.

Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments are the most popular way to permanently curl straight hair. There are two main types of chemical treatments:

  • Perms
  • Relaxers

Perms use chemicals to break and reform the bonds in your hair, creating a permanent curl pattern. Relaxers, on the other hand, use chemicals to straighten curly or kinky hair. However, if you leave a relaxer on for a shorter amount of time, it can create a looser curl pattern.

It’s important to note that chemical treatments can be damaging to your hair and can cause breakage, so it’s important to use a deep conditioning treatment before and after the process.

Hot Rollers

Hot rollers are a great way to create curls without using heat styling tools. They work by heating up and curling your hair while it’s wrapped around the roller. Hot rollers come in different sizes, so you can choose the size that best suits your desired curl pattern.

Hot rollers are easy to use and can be done at home, but they can take longer to set than other methods.

Curling Irons and Wands

Curling irons and wands are a popular way to create curls quickly and easily. Curling irons have a clamp that holds your hair in place while you wrap it around the iron. Curling wands, on the other hand, don’t have a clamp and require you to wrap your hair around the wand manually.

Curling irons and wands come in different sizes, so you can create different sized curls. They’re also easy to use and can be done at home, but it’s important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Braids or Twists

Braids or twists are a great way to create curls without using heat or chemicals. Simply braid or twist your hair while it’s wet and leave it overnight. When you take out the braids or twists in the morning, you’ll have natural-looking curls.

Braids or twists can take longer to set than other methods, and the size of the curls will depend on the size of the braids or twists.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make straight hair curly permanently:

  1. Choose your preferred method for creating curls.
  2. If using chemical treatments, consult a professional stylist to ensure that the process is done correctly.
  3. If using hot rollers, curling irons or wands, use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  4. If using braids or twists, ensure that your hair is wet before braiding or twisting.
  5. Leave the curls to set according to the instructions for your chosen method.
  6. Use a deep conditioning treatment after chemical treatments or heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the best possible curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot rollers, curling irons or wands.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment before and after chemical treatments or heat styling.
  • Don’t wash your hair for 24-48 hours after a chemical treatment.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry curly hair without causing frizz.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to enhance and define your curls.

FAQs

Can I make my straight hair curly permanently without using chemicals?

Yes, you can use hot rollers, curling irons or wands, or braids or twists to create curls without using chemicals.

Can I get a perm if I have already relaxed hair?

It’s not recommended to get a perm if you have already relaxed hair, as it can cause damage and breakage. If you want to create a curly look, try using hot rollers, curling irons or wands, or braids or twists instead.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair should be washed 1-2 times per week, as frequent washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires specific care to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it’s wet.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to enhance and define your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve curly hair permanently and keep it looking healthy and defined. Remember to choose the method that works best for you and to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage.
