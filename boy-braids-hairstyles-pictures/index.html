Boy Braids Hairstyles Pictures - How to, Step by Step, Tips, FAQ and Hair Care

Are you looking for some inspiration for your son's next hairstyle? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be sharing some boy braids hairstyles pictures along with tips, step by step guides, and hair care information.

Inspiration

When it comes to boy braids hairstyles, there are many different styles to choose from. Some popular options include:

  • Cornrows
  • Box braids
  • Twists
  • Bantu knots
  • Dreadlocks
  • Fishtail braids
  • And more!

Take a look at some of these boy braids hairstyles pictures for inspiration:

How to

If you're new to braiding or are trying out a new style, it can be helpful to watch some tutorials. YouTube is a great resource for finding step by step guides. Here are some general tips:

  • Start with clean, detangled hair.
  • Use a comb to create even sections of hair.
  • Keep the braids tight, but not too tight to avoid discomfort.
  • Use hair ties or rubber bands to secure the ends of the braids.
  • Be patient and take breaks if needed.

Step by Step

Here's a basic step by step guide for creating cornrows:

  1. Divide the hair into sections using a comb.
  2. Starting at the front of the head, take a small section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Add a small section of hair from the right side to the right section.
  6. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  7. Add a small section of hair from the left side to the left section.
  8. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  9. Repeat steps 5-8, adding more hair to each section as you go.
  10. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.
  11. Repeat on the remaining sections of hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when creating boy braids hairstyles:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much tension on the hair to prevent breakage.
  • Try different parting techniques for a unique look.
  • Experiment with different braid sizes and shapes.
  • Accessorize with beads, rings, or hair jewelry.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about boy braids hairstyles:

How long do braids last?

Braids can typically last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on the style and how well they are maintained.

Are braids damaging to the hair?

While braids can be a protective style, they can also cause damage if not done properly. It's important to use the proper tension and avoid leaving braids in for too long.

Can boys with short hair get braids?

Yes, boys with short hair can still get braids. Depending on the length of the hair, different styles may be more suitable.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy hair, especially when wearing braids. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the hair.
  • Cover the hair with a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent breakage.

With these boy braids hairstyles pictures, tips, and step by step guides, you can create a variety of stylish looks for your son. Just remember to prioritize hair care and proper technique to keep his hair healthy and strong.
