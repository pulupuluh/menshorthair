Best Products For Aging Hair 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 · 7 min read

As we age, our hair changes too. It becomes thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. But don't worry, there are plenty of hair care products that can help you maintain healthy and vibrant hair at any age. In this article, we'll discuss the best products for aging hair in 2021. Whether you have fine, thick, curly, or straight hair, we've got you covered. Let's dive in.

What Causes Aging Hair?

Before we get into the best products for aging hair, let's take a look at what causes our hair to age. As we get older, our bodies produce less of the hormones that stimulate hair growth, which can lead to thinning hair. Our hair also produces less oil, which can make it dry and brittle. Additionally, our hair follicles shrink, which can cause hair to become finer and more fragile. Finally, exposure to environmental factors, such as the sun and pollution, can also cause our hair to age more quickly.

How to Choose the Best Products for Aging Hair

When choosing hair care products for aging hair, it's important to look for ingredients that will nourish and strengthen your hair. Here are some key ingredients to look for:

  • Collagen: This protein helps to strengthen hair and improve elasticity.
  • Keratin: This protein helps to smooth and strengthen hair.
  • Vitamin E: This antioxidant helps to protect hair from environmental damage.
  • Biotin: This B vitamin helps to promote hair growth and strengthen hair.
  • Argan oil: This oil is rich in fatty acids and antioxidants, which help to nourish and moisturize hair.

When shopping for hair care products, look for ones that contain these ingredients, as well as other nourishing oils and proteins.

Best Products for Aging Hair

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the most important steps in a hair care routine is shampooing and conditioning. For aging hair, look for products that are specifically designed to nourish and strengthen hair. Some of our top picks include:

  • Redken Extreme Shampoo and Conditioner
  • L'Oreal Paris Elvive Total Repair 5 Repairing Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Briogeo Farewell Frizz Smoothing Shampoo and Conditioner

2. Hair Masks

Hair masks are a great way to give your hair an extra boost of hydration and nourishment. Here are some of our favorite hair masks for aging hair:

  • Shea Moisture Manuka Honey & Mafura Oil Intensive Hydration Hair Masque
  • Olaplex Hair Perfector No. 3
  • Moroccanoil Intense Hydrating Mask

3. Hair Oils

Hair oils are a great way to add moisture and shine to aging hair. Here are some of our favorite hair oils:

  • Argan oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Jojoba oil

You can use these oils on their own or look for hair care products that contain them.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to using the right hair care products, there are some other things you can do to maintain healthy hair as you age:

  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your body and hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

FAQ

1. How often should I wash my aging hair?

It's best to wash aging hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Can I still color my aging hair?

Yes, you can still color your aging hair. However, it's important to use hair color that is gentle and nourishing to prevent further damage.

3. What should I do if my aging hair is thinning?

If your aging hair is thinning, try using hair care products that are specifically designed to promote hair growth, such as those that contain biotin or caffeine. You may also want to speak to your doctor about other options, such as medication or supplements.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of hair care products that can help you maintain healthy and vibrant hair as you age. By using the right shampoo and conditioner, hair masks, and hair oils, as well as following some simple tips for maintaining healthy hair, you can keep your locks looking beautiful at any age.
