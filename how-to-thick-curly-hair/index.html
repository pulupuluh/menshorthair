How To Thick Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of thin, lifeless curls? Do you want to know how to make your curls thicker and fuller? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to thicken curly hair and provide you with tips and tricks for achieving the thick, luscious curls you've always wanted.

Description

Thick curly hair is highly coveted for its fullness and body. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally thick curls. This article will provide you with steps on how to thicken your curls and make them look fuller.

How to

The first step in thickening your curly hair is to start with a good haircut. A layered cut can add volume and movement to your hair. Avoid getting a blunt cut, as this can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Next, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin and keratin, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

When styling your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls' natural shape and volume. Make sure to dry your hair on a low heat setting to prevent damage and frizz.

You can also use styling products like mousse or gel to add volume to your curls. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching as you go to enhance your curls' natural shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Get a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your curls.

Step 2: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to strengthen and thicken your hair.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls' natural shape and volume.

Step 4: Apply styling products like mousse or gel to add volume to your curls.

Tips

- Avoid using heavy oils or serums on your hair, as these can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.

- Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt your curls' natural shape and cause frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I want to?

A: Of course! You can still straighten your hair if you want to switch up your look. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick curls. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, as sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Deep conditioning treatments can also help nourish and strengthen your hair.

Lastly, avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as these can cause damage and breakage. Embrace your natural curls and use these tips to enhance their thickness and fullness.
