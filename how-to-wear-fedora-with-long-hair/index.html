How To Wear Fedora With Long Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by William May 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Fedora hats have been around for ages, and they never go out of style. They can add a touch of sophistication and mystery to any outfit. However, if you have long hair, you might be hesitant to wear a fedora. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a fedora with long hair.

Description

Fedora hats have a unique style that can add a touch of elegance to any outfit. They have a wide brim and a creased crown, and they are made from materials such as wool, felt, or straw. They come in a variety of colors and styles, making them the perfect accessory for any occasion. However, if you have long hair, you might be unsure how to wear a fedora without looking messy.

How to Wear a Fedora with Long Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Fedora

The first step is to choose the right fedora. When selecting a fedora, consider the color, material, and style. You want to choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color. If you have warm undertones, consider a brown or beige fedora. If you have cool undertones, consider a black or navy fedora.

When it comes to material, wool and felt fedoras are perfect for winter, while straw fedoras are perfect for summer. You also want to choose a style that complements your face shape. If you have a round face, consider a fedora with a narrower brim. If you have a square face, consider a fedora with a wider brim.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before putting on your fedora, you want to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry. If you have curly hair, you can use a diffuser attachment to define your curls. If you have straight hair, you can use a round brush to add volume.

Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz. Then, brush your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 3: Put on Your Fedora

Now it's time to put on your fedora. Start by tilting the fedora slightly forward so that it sits on the top of your head. Make sure it's not too far back or too far forward. The brim should be parallel to the ground.

If you have long hair, you can either tuck it into the fedora or let it hang loose. If you choose to tuck it in, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and tuck it into the fedora.

If you choose to let your hair hang loose, make sure it's not covering your face. You can also add a scarf or headband to keep your hair in place.

Tips

  • Choose a fedora that complements your outfit
  • Don't wear a fedora that's too big or too small
  • Experiment with different hairstyles
  • Add accessories such as scarves or headbands
  • Don't be afraid to make a statement

FAQ

Can I wear a fedora with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a fedora with curly hair. Make sure to define your curls with a diffuser attachment and apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to reduce frizz.

How do I choose the right size fedora?

To choose the right size fedora, measure the circumference of your head at the widest point. Then, consult the sizing chart for the fedora you're interested in.

Can I wear a fedora with a dress?

Yes, you can wear a fedora with a dress. A fedora adds a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to follow a hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling, and trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

You can also use hair masks and oils to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

Conclusion

Wearing a fedora with long hair can be a stylish and elegant look. By choosing the right fedora, prepping your hair, and following our tips, you can rock this look with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
