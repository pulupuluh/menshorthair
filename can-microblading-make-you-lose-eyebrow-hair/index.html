Can Microblading Make You Lose Eyebrow Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Microblading has become a popular cosmetic procedure in recent years, with more and more people turning to it for fuller, more defined eyebrows. However, with any cosmetic procedure, concerns about potential side effects arise. One common question is, can microblading make you lose eyebrow hair? In this blog post, we will explore this question and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision about microblading.

Description

Microblading is a semi-permanent tattooing technique that involves creating small, hair-like strokes on the eyebrow area using a special tool and pigment. The result is a natural-looking eyebrow shape that lasts for up to two years. However, some people have reported experiencing hair loss after getting microblading done.

How to

If you are considering getting microblading done, it is important to do your research and find a reputable and experienced technician. Before the procedure, the technician will discuss your desired eyebrow shape and color with you and draw on a template to ensure you are happy with the result. The procedure itself takes around two hours and involves numbing cream to minimize any discomfort or pain.

Step by Step

The following are the steps involved in getting microblading done:

  1. Cleanse the eyebrow area
  2. Apply numbing cream
  3. Create the eyebrow template
  4. Make hair-like strokes with the microblading tool
  5. Apply pigment to the strokes
  6. Wipe off excess pigment
  7. Repeat the process on the other eyebrow
  8. Apply aftercare ointment

Tips

After getting microblading done, it is important to follow the aftercare instructions provided by the technician to ensure proper healing and to avoid any potential complications. These instructions may include avoiding water and sweat for a certain period, avoiding certain skincare products, and applying aftercare ointment regularly.

FAQ

Can microblading cause hair loss?

While microblading itself does not cause hair loss, some people have reported experiencing hair loss after getting the procedure done. This can be due to a number of factors, such as improper aftercare, infection, or an allergic reaction to the pigment.

How long does microblading last?

Microblading can last for up to two years, with touch-ups recommended every 6-12 months to maintain the desired shape and color.

Is microblading safe?

When done by a reputable and experienced technician, microblading is generally considered safe. However, as with any cosmetic procedure, there are potential risks and side effects, such as infection or an allergic reaction to the pigment.

Hair Care

If you do experience hair loss after getting microblading done, it is important to take care of your eyebrow hair to encourage regrowth. This can include using eyebrow serums or oils, avoiding over-plucking or waxing, and eating a nutritious diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

Conclusion

In conclusion, while microblading itself does not cause hair loss, it is important to choose a reputable and experienced technician and follow proper aftercare instructions to minimize the risk of any potential complications. If you do experience hair loss after getting microblading done, taking care of your eyebrow hair can help encourage regrowth.
