Best Hair Style For Oval Face Female

Written by Robby Feb 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that just don't look right on your oval-shaped face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hair styles for oval face females. From long locks to short bobs, we've got a style that will flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

The oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. This is because the oval face has balanced proportions and can pull off almost any hair style. However, there are some styles that flatter the face shape more than others. By choosing the right hair style, you can accentuate your best features and create a balanced and beautiful look.

How to Choose the Right Hair Style

When choosing a hair style, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For oval face females, the goal is to create balance and symmetry. This can be achieved by choosing a hair style that adds volume to the top of the head and narrows at the bottom.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are about the same, you have an oval face shape.

2. Consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a short, layered cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a long, layered cut can help to thin out your hair and create movement.

3. Find inspiration. Look for pictures of hair styles that you like and that flatter your face shape. Consider bringing these pictures to your stylist for reference.

4. Consult with a stylist. A stylist can help you choose the right hair style for your face shape and hair texture. They can also advise you on how to maintain and style your new look.

Tips

1. Avoid hair styles that add too much volume to the sides of the head.

2. Bangs can be a great way to add balance to an oval face shape.

3. If you have a long face, avoid hair styles that are too long and straight.

4. Experiment with different hair styles until you find one that you love.

FAQ

What is the most flattering hair length for oval face females?

The most flattering hair length for oval face females is shoulder length or longer. This length helps to balance out the face and create symmetry.

What are some popular hair styles for oval face females?

Some popular hair styles for oval face females include long layers, side-swept bangs, and a short bob.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools on your hair.

2. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair style, you can accentuate your best features and create a balanced and beautiful look. Remember to consult with a stylist and experiment with different styles until you find one that you love.
