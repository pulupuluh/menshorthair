How To Make Wavy Hair Look Professional A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s easy to achieve and gives your hair a unique texture, it can be difficult to style and make it look professional. If you’re wondering how to make wavy hair look professional, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a sleek, sophisticated look with wavy hair. We’ll also provide you with some tips and tricks for maintaining and caring for your wavy locks. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Wavy Hair Look Professional
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Make Wavy Hair Look Professional

Making wavy hair look professional starts with the right cut. A layered haircut is ideal for wavy hair as it adds structure and definition to your waves. If you have long hair, consider adding face-framing layers to balance out your face shape. If you have short hair, opt for a layered bob or a pixie cut to give your hair some movement.

Once you have the right cut, it’s time to style your hair. Here are some tips on how to make wavy hair look professional:

1. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-drying your hair is essential to achieving a sleek, professional look. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage. Then, use a round brush to blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the cool setting on your hairdryer to set your style.

2. Use a flat iron

If your waves are too unruly, use a flat iron to tame them. Start by sectioning your hair and using a heat protectant spray. Then, use your flat iron to straighten your hair in sections. Don’t forget to use the cool setting to set your style.

3. Add some texture

Wavy hair already has texture, but you can enhance it by using a texturizing spray. This will give your hair more definition and make it look more polished.

4. Finish with a hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a hairspray to keep your hair in place. Opt for a light-hold hairspray to avoid making your hair look stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make wavy hair look professional:

  1. Start with a layered haircut.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair in sections using a round brush.
  4. Straighten your hair with a flat iron if necessary.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to enhance your waves.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making wavy hair look professional:

  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute your hair’s natural oils and add shine.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Don’t wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and flat iron. Cheap tools can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

FAQs

Q: Can I make wavy hair look professional without straightening it?

A: Yes, you can. Simply blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add structure and definition to your waves. You can also use a texturizing spray to enhance your waves further.

Q: How do I prevent my wavy hair from getting frizzy?

A: Frizz is a common problem for wavy hair. To prevent it, use a smoothing serum or oil to tame your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Caring for wavy hair requires a little extra effort. Here are some tips on how to keep your wavy locks healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make wavy hair look professional and polished. Remember to invest in good quality tools and products, and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
