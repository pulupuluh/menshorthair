How To Keep The Wet Hair Look A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 13, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairdo? Do you want to try something new? The wet hair look is a great option for those who want to achieve a sleek and stylish look. But how do you keep the wet hair look all day long? In this article, we’ll show you how to achieve and maintain this trendy hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Wet Hair Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Wet Hair Look
  • FAQs

How to Achieve the Wet Hair Look

The wet hair look is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a while now. It’s a simple style that involves slicking back your hair to create a wet, glossy look. Here’s how you can achieve the wet hair look:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and damp. You can either wash your hair or dampen it with a spray bottle. Wet hair is easier to mold and shape, making it perfect for the wet hair look.

2. Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. You can use a gel, mousse, or pomade. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.

3. Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back starting from the front of your head. Make sure to comb your hair evenly, so it lays flat against your head.

4. Use more product if needed

If you feel like your hair isn’t slicked back enough, you can apply more product. Just make sure to apply it evenly and not use too much, as it can weigh your hair down.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to achieve the wet hair look, let’s go through a step-by-step guide to help you maintain the style:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

As mentioned earlier, wet hair is easier to mold and shape, making it perfect for the wet hair look. Start by washing your hair or dampening it with a spray bottle.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair. You can use a gel, mousse, or pomade. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back starting from the front of your head. Make sure to comb your hair evenly, so it lays flat against your head.

Step 4: Use more product if needed

If you feel like your hair isn’t slicked back enough, you can apply more product. Just make sure to apply it evenly and not use too much, as it can weigh your hair down.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To help maintain the wet hair look, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from frizzing up throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining the Wet Hair Look

Now that you know how to achieve and maintain the wet hair look, here are some tips to help you keep the style all day long:

  • Don’t touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can disrupt the style.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to help keep your hair in place without weighing it down.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can cause your hair to look greasy and limp.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to help distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as this can disrupt the wet hair look.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the wet hair look with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the wet hair look with curly hair. Just make sure to use a styling product that works well with curly hair, and use a diffuser when drying your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair when trying to maintain the wet hair look?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every other day when trying to maintain the wet hair look. Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils, making it harder to style.

Q: Can I achieve the wet hair look without using any styling products?

A: It’s possible to achieve the wet hair look without using any styling products, but it may not last as long. If you want to maintain the style all day, it’s best to use a styling product.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to achieve and maintain the wet hair look, it’s time to give it a try! With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence.
