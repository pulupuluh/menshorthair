What Hairstyle Suits A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Robby Mar 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that suits your heart shaped face? Look no further. In this article, we’ll explore the different hairstyles that complement your face shape, tips on how to achieve them, and hair care advice.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The objective of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to create balance by softening the forehead and creating volume around the chin area.

How to

Here are some hairstyles that suit heart shaped faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers that frame the face create an illusion of width around the chin area. This balances out the forehead, creating a more oval face shape.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs draw attention away from the forehead and towards the eyes, creating balance. They can be paired with any hairstyle, from long hair to a bob.

3. Bob

A bob that grazes the jawline creates volume around the chin area, balancing out the forehead. This hairstyle can be worn straight or with loose waves.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with volume around the crown creates balance by adding width to the forehead. It’s a bold and edgy hairstyle that works well for heart shaped faces.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

1. Consult with your hairstylist

Your hairstylist can recommend the best hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to maintain and style your hair.

2. Determine your hair texture

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your hair texture is important. If you have straight hair, a bob or a pixie cut would work well. If you have curly hair, long layers or a side swept bangs would be a great choice.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles

Try out different hairstyles to see which one suits you best. Don’t be afraid to try something new and bold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

1. Avoid hairstyles that add volume around the forehead

Hairstyles that add volume around the forehead can make your face appear top-heavy. This includes blunt bangs and high ponytails.

2. Opt for hairstyles that add volume around the chin area

Creating volume around the chin area balances out the forehead, creating a more oval face shape. This includes long layers, bobs, and pixie cuts.

3. Use hair products that add volume

Hair products such as volumizing mousse and dry shampoo can add volume to your hair, creating the illusion of a wider chin area. Apply these products at the roots of your hair for maximum effect.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a middle part with a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a heart shaped face. However, it’s best to pair it with long layers or side swept bangs to create balance.

2. Can I wear a short bob with a heart shaped face?

Yes, a short bob that grazes the jawline can work well for a heart shaped face. It creates volume around the chin area, balancing out the forehead.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week

A hair mask can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair. Avoid using them too often and use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your heart shaped face can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you’ll find the perfect hairstyle in no time. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
