What Is The Best Haircut For Round Face Male In 2023

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a guy with a round face? Finding the right haircut can be a task, especially when you want to make your face appear slimmer and more angular. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircut for round face male in 2023, along with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best haircut for round face male in 2023 is one that compliments your face shape by adding height and volume to the crown and temples, while keeping the sides and back short. This helps create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. The key is to avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear rounder.

How to

The best way to get the right haircut for your round face is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best length and style for your face shape, as well as give you tips on how to maintain your new look. You can also try experimenting with different hairstyles and lengths to find what works best for you.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a round face male:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist
  2. Determine the best length and style for your face shape
  3. Keep the sides and back short
  4. Add height and volume to the crown and temples
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles and lengths to find what works best for you

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face male:

  • Avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your head
  • Choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown and temples
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create more angles and movement
  • Use hair products like pomade, wax, or hairspray to help maintain your new style
  • Don't be afraid to try new things and experiment with different styles

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round face males:

Q: What length should I keep my hair?

A: It's best to keep the sides and back short, while adding height and volume to the crown and temples. The length of your hair will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear rounder. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can make your face look wider.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use hair products like pomade, wax, or hairspray to help maintain your new style
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

Now you know what the best haircut for round face male in 2023 is, along with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new style. Remember, the key is to choose a style that compliments your face shape by adding height and volume to the crown and temples, while keeping the sides and back short. So go out there and rock your new look with confidence!
