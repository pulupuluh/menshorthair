How To Do A Messy Half Up Half Down Ponytail

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that still looks stylish, a messy half up half down ponytail is the perfect option. This hairstyle is versatile, can be dressed up or down, and is perfect for a variety of hair types and lengths. In this article, we'll show you how to create a messy half up half down ponytail in just a few simple steps.

Description

A messy half up half down ponytail is a hairstyle that combines the classic look of a ponytail with a more relaxed, messy style. This hairstyle involves pulling half of your hair up into a ponytail while leaving the other half down. The result is a chic, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.

How to

Follow these simple steps to create a messy half up half down ponytail:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Begin by washing and drying your hair. This hairstyle works best with clean, dry hair, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle. Then, section off the top half of your hair from your temples to the back of your head. Clip the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Create a ponytail

Take the bottom section of your hair and pull it up into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 4: Tease the crown

Using a comb or your fingers, tease the crown of your hair to create volume and texture. This will help give your messy half up half down ponytail a more relaxed, casual look.

Step 5: Pull back the top section

Take the top section of your hair and pull it back over the ponytail. Use your fingers to gently pull the hair back, creating a messy, relaxed look.

Step 6: Secure the top section

Use bobby pins to secure the top section of your hair to the base of the ponytail. Make sure the pins are hidden under the hair for a seamless look.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Once your messy half up half down ponytail is in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating a messy half up half down ponytail:

  • For a more relaxed, messy look, use your fingers to gently pull at the hair once it's in place.
  • If you have shorter hair, try using a hairband to create the ponytail instead of a hair tie.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like hair clips or ribbons, to add some extra flair to your messy half up half down ponytail.

FAQ

Can I create a messy half up half down ponytail with curly hair?

Absolutely! This hairstyle works well with all hair types, including curly hair. Just be sure to use a hair tie that won't pull or damage your curls.

Is this hairstyle appropriate for formal events?

Yes! While a messy half up half down ponytail is definitely a more casual hairstyle, it can be dressed up with the right accessories and styling techniques. Try adding a sparkly hair clip or smoothing down any flyaways with a bit of hairspray to create a more formal look.

Hair Care

When creating a messy half up half down ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create a stylish, relaxed messy half up half down ponytail in no time. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is the perfect choice for any occasion.
