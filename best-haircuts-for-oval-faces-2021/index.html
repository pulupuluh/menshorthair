Best Haircuts For Oval Faces 2021

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you blessed with an oval face? Then, you are lucky! Oval faces are versatile and can pull off any hairstyle. However, choosing the right haircut that accentuates your best features can be a daunting task. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for oval faces in 2021, and some tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oval Faces
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  5. FAQs About Haircuts for Oval Faces
  6. Hair Care for Oval Faces

Description

Oval faces are characterized by their balanced proportions, with the forehead and jawline being about the same width. The face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and chin. The advantage of having an oval face is that you can rock any haircut, from long to short, without worrying about it overwhelming your face shape. However, to enhance your features, you need to choose a haircut that complements your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oval Faces

The best haircut for an oval face is one that highlights your best features. Here are some tips:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a haircut.
  • Choose haircuts that add volume to the top of your head if you have thin hair.
  • Stay away from haircuts that add too much volume to the sides if you have thick hair.
  • Choose haircuts that balance the proportions of your face.
  • Avoid haircuts that hide your forehead if you have a high forehead.
  • Choose haircuts that accentuate your jawline if you have a round face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know how to choose the best haircut for your oval face, here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.
  2. Show your hairstylist pictures of haircuts you like and discuss the features you want to highlight.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to recommend hair products that will help you maintain your haircut.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to show you how to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Use hair products recommended by your hairstylist.
  • Follow a regular hair care routine.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

FAQs About Haircuts for Oval Faces

What is the best haircut for oval faces?

The best haircut for oval faces is one that highlights your best features. Consider your hair type, texture, and face shape when choosing a haircut.

Can oval faces pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval faces can pull off any hairstyle. However, the best haircut for oval faces is one that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

What hairstyles should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides if they have thick hair. They should also avoid haircuts that hide their forehead if they have a high forehead.

Hair Care for Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips for oval faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use hair products recommended by your hairstylist.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, oval faces are versatile and can pull off any haircut. However, to enhance your features, you need to choose a haircut that complements your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and follow a regular hair care routine to maintain your haircut. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look your best every day!
