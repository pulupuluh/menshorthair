Medium Length Hairstyles For Square Face Shapes

Description

Are you tired of trying out hairstyles that do not complement your square face shape? Look no further because medium length hairstyles are perfect for square face shapes. They soften the angles of your face and highlight your best features. Medium length hairstyles also give you the flexibility to style your hair in different ways. In this article, we will guide you through the best medium length hairstyles for square face shapes.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to determine whether you have a square face shape. A square face shape has a strong jawline, a square forehead, and a wide face. To check if you have a square face shape, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and then measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are almost equal, then you have a square face shape.

Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair and Square Face Shapes

1. Layered Bob - A layered bob with side-swept bangs works great for square face shapes. The layers soften the angles of your face while the side-swept bangs highlight your eyes.

2. Shoulder-Length Waves - Soft waves that fall on your shoulders are perfect for square face shapes. This hairstyle adds volume to the sides of your face, making it appear less angular.

3. Long Bob - A long bob, also known as a lob, is a versatile hairstyle that looks great on square face shapes. It elongates your face and the length of the hair draws attention away from your jawline.

4. Layered Cut with Side Part - A layered cut with a side part is a classic hairstyle that flatters square face shapes. The layers add texture and movement to your hair while the side part accentuates your cheekbones.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a curling iron or a straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.

3. Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

4. Use a brush to create a side part or a center part, depending on your preference.

5. Style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. You can add volume to the sides of your face or create layers to soften your angles.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long as they can make your face appear wider.

- Bangs are a great way to highlight your eyes and soften your forehead.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or a bun if I have a square face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail or a bun. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out your jawline.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a square face shape?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Also, avoid straight bangs as they can make your face appear more angular.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray before using them. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
