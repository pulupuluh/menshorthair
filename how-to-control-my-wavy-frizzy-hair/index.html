How To Control My Wavy Frizzy Hair A Complete Guide For 2023

Are you tired of dealing with unmanageable, frizzy hair? If you have wavy hair, you might find it difficult to tame those waves and keep them under control. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve smooth, sleek, and shiny hair without sacrificing your natural waves. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to control your wavy frizzy hair for a stunning look.

How to Control My Wavy Frizzy Hair

The first step in controlling wavy frizzy hair is to understand its texture and type. Wavy hair has a natural S-shaped pattern and tends to be more prone to frizz. Here are some steps to help you control your wavy frizzy hair:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by choosing a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil. These ingredients will help to nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and adding shine.

2. Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair. This will make it easier to style and prevent breakage. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair while the conditioner is still in.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel to Dry Your Hair

Instead of rubbing your hair with a regular towel, use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out the excess water. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage. Avoid using a hairdryer, as the heat can damage your hair and make it more frizzy.

4. Apply a Hair Serum

Once your hair is damp, apply a hair serum to help control frizz and add shine. Look for a serum that contains argan oil or jojoba oil, as these ingredients are great for wavy hair. Apply the serum from mid-length to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

5. Style Your Hair with a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to style your hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and reduce frizz. Start by using the diffuser on low heat and speed, and work your way up as needed. You can also use your fingers to scrunch your hair while drying it to create more defined waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to control your wavy frizzy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair while the conditioner is still in.

Step 3: Squeeze Out Excess Water

Squeeze out the excess water using a microfiber towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

Step 4: Apply Hair Serum

Apply a hair serum containing argan oil or jojoba oil to control frizz and add shine. Apply from mid-length to the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Style with a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to style your hair. Start on low heat and speed, and work your way up as needed. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while drying it to create more defined waves.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you control your wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to control my wavy hair?

A: While you can use a hair straightener to straighten your hair, it's not recommended for controlling wavy hair. Straightening your hair can damage it and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, try using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your natural waves.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your wavy hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wavy hair?

A: While you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on your wavy hair, it's better to use products that are specifically designed for wavy hair. These products contain ingredients that will help to nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and adding shine.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a hair care routine to help you control your wavy frizzy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to detangle and moisturize your hair
  • Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out the excess water
  • Apply a hair serum containing argan oil or jojoba oil to control frizz and add shine
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to style your hair, enhancing your natural waves

By following these steps and incorporating these tips into your hair care routine, you can achieve smooth, sleek, and shiny wavy hair without the frizz. Remember, consistency is key, so be patient and stick to your routine to see the best results.
