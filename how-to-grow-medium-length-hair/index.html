How To Grow Medium Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Mar 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having short hair? Do you want to grow your hair out to medium length? Growing your hair out can be a challenging experience, but with the right tips and tricks, it can be a breeze. In this article, we'll show you how to grow medium length hair and provide you with some helpful hair care tips along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Medium Length Hair

How to Grow Medium Length Hair

Growing medium length hair requires patience and consistency. Here are some tips to help you grow your hair out to the desired length:

1. Get Regular Trims

It may seem counterintuitive, but getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster. Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks will prevent split ends from forming and breaking off your hair.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your hair needs proper nutrition to grow. Make sure you're eating a well-balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and slow down growth. Try to avoid using hot tools like flat irons, curling irons, and hair dryers as much as possible.

4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to your hair follicles, which can promote growth. Use your fingers to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some tips to help grow your hair out, here's a step-by-step guide to follow:

1. Set a Goal Length

Determine the length you want your hair to be and use that as your goal. This will give you something to work towards and help you stay motivated.

2. Get Regular Trims

As mentioned earlier, getting regular trims will prevent split ends from forming and breaking off your hair. Make sure you're trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks.

3. Be Patient

Growing hair takes time, so be patient. It may take several months or even a year to reach your goal length.

4. Use Hair Masks

Using hair masks once a week can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, and shea butter.

Tips for Growing Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow your hair out:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can slow down growth. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to prevent tangles.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf to protect it while you sleep.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about growing medium length hair:

1. How long does it take to grow medium length hair?

It can take several months or even a year to grow medium length hair, depending on your starting length and how fast your hair grows.

2. Can I still use hair products while growing out my hair?

Yes, you can still use hair products while growing out your hair. Just make sure to choose products that are gentle and nourishing.

3. Does brushing my hair help it grow?

Brushing your hair can help distribute natural oils and stimulate blood flow to your scalp, but be careful not to overbrush or brush too aggressively, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you grow your hair out:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you do have to use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can pull on your hair and cause damage. Opt for looser styles instead.

3. Don't Brush Wet Hair

Wet hair is more susceptible to damage, so avoid brushing it while it's wet. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle instead.

Growing medium length hair takes time and patience, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving your goal length. Remember to take care of your hair along the way, and you'll have healthy, beautiful locks in no time.
