Hairstyles For Oval Face Female Indian Wedding

Written by Aprilia Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you an Indian bride with an oval face shape and confused about which hairstyle to choose for your big day? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyles for oval face female Indian weddings, along with tips and tricks to make your hair look stunning on your wedding day.

Description

Indian weddings are grand affairs, and every bride wants to look her best on her special day. Your hairstyle plays a significant role in completing your bridal look, and it's crucial to choose a hairstyle that compliments your face shape. If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky, as you can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's essential to understand the basics of choosing a hairstyle for an oval face shape. An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are high and prominent. The length of the face is longer than the width, making it an ideal face shape for most hairstyles.

When selecting a hairstyle, keep in mind that it should balance your features and not make your face appear too long or too round. A hairstyle that adds volume to the crown area or has layers around the face will work best for oval face shapes.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to create some of the best hairstyles for oval face female Indian weddings:

1. Loose Waves

To create loose waves, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Use a curling iron to curl your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Once done, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create soft waves. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side Swept Updo

To create a side-swept updo, start by parting your hair to the side. Gather your hair to one side and create a loose braid or twist. Pin the braid or twist to the back of your head, leaving some hair loose to frame your face. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Half Up Half Down

To create a half up half down hairstyle, start by sectioning your hair from the crown area. Tease the crown area to create volume and pin it back. Leave the rest of your hair loose and create loose waves or curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the crown area, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead, as this can make your face appear shorter.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers around the face to create the illusion of width.
  • Consider adding accessories like flowers or hairpins to your hairstyle to make it more bridal.

FAQ

What are some hairstyles to avoid for an oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the crown area or cover your forehead, as this can make your face appear longer or shorter, respectively.

Can oval face shapes pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face shapes can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease.

What accessories can I add to my hairstyle?

You can add flowers, hairpins, or hair jewelry to your hairstyle to make it more bridal.

Hair Care

To ensure your hair looks its best on your wedding day, it's essential to take care of it in the months leading up to the big day. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can weaken and damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face female Indian wedding. Remember to take care of your hair in the months leading up to your big day to ensure it looks its best. Good luck!
