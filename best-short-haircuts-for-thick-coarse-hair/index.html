Best Short Haircuts For Thick Coarse Hair

Written by Luffy Mar 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick and coarse hair? Don't worry, there are plenty of great short haircuts that can help you manage and style your hair with ease. In this article, we'll be discussing the best short haircuts for thick coarse hair, along with tips on how to care for and style your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick and coarse hair can be a challenge to manage, especially if you prefer shorter haircuts. However, there are a variety of styles that can help you achieve a chic and sophisticated look without sacrificing manageability. The key is to choose a cut that works with your hair's natural texture and thickness, rather than trying to force it into a style that doesn't suit your hair type.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect short haircut for your thick, coarse hair is to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with this hair type. They can help you choose a cut that will flatter your face shape and complement your hair's texture and thickness.

When choosing a short haircut for thick, coarse hair, it's important to consider the overall shape of the cut. A layered bob, for example, can help to soften the look of thick, coarse hair and make it more manageable. A pixie cut, on the other hand, can be a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks polished and put-together.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best short haircuts for thick, coarse hair:

Layered Bob

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for thick, coarse hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight, using a round brush to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  3. Section your hair into layers, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, trim each layer to the desired length, making sure to blend the layers together for a seamless look.
  5. Finish by applying a smoothing serum or cream to your hair to help tame any flyaways and add shine.

Pixie Cut

  1. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for thick, coarse hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight, using a flat brush to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  3. Section your hair into small sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, trim each section to the desired length, making sure to blend the sections together for a seamless look.
  5. Finish by applying a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair to help create a tousled, beachy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling a short haircut for thick, coarse hair:

  • Choose a cut that works with your natural hair texture and thickness, rather than trying to fight against it.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed specifically for thick, coarse hair, such as a smoothing serum or texturizing spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow-drying, flat ironing, or curling, to find the best way to manage your hair.

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for thick, coarse hair?

Some of the best short haircuts for thick, coarse hair include a layered bob, a pixie cut, and a shaggy bob.

How can I style my thick, coarse hair?

You can style your thick, coarse hair by blow-drying it straight, using a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz, or adding texture with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

What products should I use on my thick, coarse hair?

You should use high-quality products that are designed specifically for thick, coarse hair, such as a smoothing serum, texturizing spray, or deep conditioning treatment.

Hair Care

Caring for thick, coarse hair requires a bit of extra effort, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your thick, coarse hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for thick, coarse hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut for your thick, coarse hair, you can achieve a chic and sophisticated look that's easy to manage and style.
