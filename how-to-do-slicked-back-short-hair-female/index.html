How To Do Slicked Back Short Hair Female A Step By Step Guide

Short hair is a popular hairstyle for women, and many women choose to slick back their short hair for a sleek and sophisticated look. In this article, we will be discussing how to do slicked back short hair female.

Description

Slicked back short hair is a hairstyle that involves pulling the hair back and styling it with a product to create a sleek and polished look. This hairstyle is popular for women who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and put together.

How to Slick Back Short Hair Female

The first step to achieving a slicked back short hair look is to choose the right product. You will need a strong hold hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place. You can also use a hairspray to set the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a slicked back short hair look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is damp, blow dry it before you begin styling.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips and rub it between your hands to warm it up.
  3. Starting at the front of your hairline, use your fingertips to smooth the product back through your hair.
  4. Continue working the product through your hair, using your fingertips to ensure that every strand is coated.
  5. Use a comb to slick your hair straight back, starting at the front and working your way to the back of your head. Make sure that your hair is laying flat against your head.
  6. If you have any flyaways or loose hairs, use a small amount of product to smooth them down.
  7. Finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Slicked Back Short Hair Female

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back short hair look:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You can always add more, but itâs hard to take it away once itâs applied.
  • Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you begin styling. Wet hair will not hold the style.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a clean, smooth part before you start slicking your hair back.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked back short hair female:

Q: Can I slick back my short hair if itâs curly?

A: Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep the curls in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a slicked back short hair style?

A: Itâs best to wash your hair every other day if you have a slicked back short hair style. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Short Hair Female

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, itâs important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care for slicked back short hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from hair products.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful slicked back short hair female look thatâs both chic and low-maintenance. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you!
