How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Lion Mane

Written by Frank Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some wildness to your hair, why not try making it look like a lion's mane? This look is perfect for those who want to stand out and make a statement. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look using some simple techniques and products.

Description

A lion's mane is known for being thick, voluminous, and full of texture. To achieve this look, you'll need to use products that add volume and texture to your hair. You'll also need to know how to style your hair in a way that mimics the wild, untamed look of a lion's mane.

How to

To make your hair look like a lion's mane, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Next, apply a volumizing mousse to your hair. This will help to add even more volume and texture. Use a generous amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use your fingers to comb through your hair as you blow dry it. This will help to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Use a teasing comb to tease your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down. This will help to create even more volume and texture.

Step 5: Use a Texturizing Spray

Use a texturizing spray to add even more texture to your hair. Spray it throughout your hair and use your fingers to work it in.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers to style your hair in a way that mimics the wild, untamed look of a lion's mane. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to add more texture and volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your hair look even more like a lion's mane:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to comb through your hair instead of a brush.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use a lot of product. The more product you use, the more volume and texture you'll have.

FAQ

How long will this style last?

This style can last for several days if you use the right products and techniques.

Can I achieve this look on short hair?

Yes, you can achieve this look on short hair. You may need to use more product and tease your hair more to create volume and texture.

Will this style damage my hair?

This style shouldn't damage your hair as long as you use the right products and techniques.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent damage. You should also avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your hair look like a lion's mane and stand out from the crowd. Give it a try and see how wild and untamed your hair can look!
