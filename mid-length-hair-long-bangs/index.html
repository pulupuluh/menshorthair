Mid Length Hair Long Bangs A Hairstyle You Should Try In 2023

Written by Frank Mar 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's versatile, trendy, and easy to maintain, mid length hair with long bangs might just be the perfect fit for you. This chic and effortless hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out with friends to a fancy evening event. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know about mid length hair long bangs, from how to style it to hair care tips.

Description

Mid length hair with long bangs is a hairstyle that's characterized by hair that falls just below the shoulders and long, side-swept bangs that cover the forehead. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long but still want a manageable length that's easy to style.

How To

Styling mid length hair with long bangs is easy and can be done in just a few steps. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect mid length hair with long bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it using a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  2. Part your hair on one side and section the front part of your hair for your bangs.
  3. Blow dry your bangs to the side to create a side-swept fringe.
  4. Apply some hair wax or styling gel to your hair to add texture and hold.
  5. Use a curling iron to add some loose waves to your hair for a more relaxed and effortless look.
  6. Finish off your hairstyle with some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Use lukewarm water to wash your hair and rinse it thoroughly to avoid any residue.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by parting it on one side and separating the front part of your hair for your bangs. Use a comb to create a straight parting.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow dry your bangs to the side using a round brush to create a side-swept fringe. Use a hair clip to hold the rest of your hair in place while you blow dry your bangs.

Step 4: Apply Hair Wax or Styling Gel

Apply some hair wax or styling gel to your hair to add texture and hold. Make sure to apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to add some loose waves to your hair for a more relaxed and effortless look. Take small sections of your hair and curl them away from your face.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with some hairspray to keep everything in place. Spray it lightly all over your hair to avoid making it stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling mid length hair with long bangs:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to your hair and create a fuller look.
  • Use a curling iron or a straightener to add some texture to your hair and create different looks.
  • Try different hairstyles and experiment with different products to find what works best for you and your hair.

FAQ

What face shape is best for mid length hair with long bangs?

Mid length hair with long bangs is suitable for almost any face shape, but it works best for oval and heart-shaped faces.

Can I still wear mid length hair with long bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still wear mid length hair with long bangs if you have curly hair. In fact, it can look even better with curly hair as it adds more volume and texture to your hair.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long and covering your eyes.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for mid length hair with long bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair by blow drying it on a low setting or letting it air dry.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy and neat.

Mid length hair with long bangs is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mid length hair with long bangs and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
