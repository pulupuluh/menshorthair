How To Cut Curly Hair For Maximum Volume

Written by Aprilia Mar 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous when styled properly, it can be difficult to manage and style on a daily basis. One of the biggest challenges with curly hair is achieving maximum volume. In this blog post, we will discuss how to cut curly hair for maximum volume.

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, especially if you want to achieve maximum volume. Cutting curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. You need to consider the texture, curl pattern, and density of the hair to ensure that you get the best results. In this blog post, we will discuss how to cut curly hair for maximum volume, step by step.

How to

If you want to achieve maximum volume with your curly hair, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Look for someone who specializes in curly hair and has a portfolio of clients with similar hair types to yours.

When you go for your appointment, make sure to communicate your goals and concerns with your stylist. Let them know that you want to achieve maximum volume with your curly hair, and ask them to recommend a cut that will work best for your hair type.

Once you have discussed your goals with your stylist, they will begin the cutting process. Here are the steps they will likely follow:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle

The first step in cutting curly hair is to wash and detangle it. Your stylist will use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse your hair and remove any tangles or knots. They may also use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, your stylist will section your hair using clips or hair ties. This will help them to work on one section at a time and ensure that they don't miss any areas.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using scissors, your stylist will begin cutting your hair. They may use a variety of cutting techniques, such as the DevaCurl method, to achieve maximum volume. The DevaCurl method involves cutting each curl individually to create a more defined and voluminous look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it using products that are designed for curly hair. They may use a leave-in conditioner, curl enhancing cream, or gel to help define your curls and add volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve maximum volume with your curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help boost volume
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a curly bob or half-up half-down style, to see what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my curly hair myself?

A: It is not recommended to cut your own curly hair, especially if you are trying to achieve maximum volume. Cutting curly hair requires a specific skill set and technique, and it's best to leave it to a professional stylist.

Q: How often should I get my curly hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your curly hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of your haircuts may vary depending on your hair type and personal preferences.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving maximum volume with curly hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy
  • Limit the use of hair products, as too much product can weigh down your curls

In conclusion, cutting curly hair for maximum volume requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. It's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair and to communicate your goals and concerns with them. With the right cut and proper hair care, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy curls that will turn heads.
