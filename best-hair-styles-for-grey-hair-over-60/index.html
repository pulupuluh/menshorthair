Best Hair Styles For Grey Hair Over 60 In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair can lose its pigment and turn grey. However, grey hair can be beautiful and chic, and there are many hairstyles that can enhance its natural beauty. In this article, we will discuss the best hair styles for grey hair over 60. Whether you have short or long hair, fine or thick hair, we have something for everyone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Grey hair can be thin, coarse, curly, or straight. Some women experience hair loss or thinning as they age, and this can affect the hairstyle that they choose. However, there are many hair styles that can work well for grey hair over 60. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

How To

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape. A hairstyle that looks great on a round face may not work as well on a square face. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Then, compare your shape to the following:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheeks and a rounded chin, with the width and length of the face being almost equal.
  • Square: Forehead, cheeks, and jawline are almost equal in width, with a squared jaw.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheeks and chin, which come to a point.
  • Long: Forehead, cheeks, and jawline are almost equal in width, with a longer length than width.

Step by Step

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some of the best hair styles for grey hair over 60:

Short Hair

Short hair can be low maintenance and stylish. A pixie cut or a bob can work well for grey hair over 60. These styles can add volume and texture to thinning hair, and they can also highlight your facial features.

Medium Hair

Medium hair can be versatile and flattering. A layered cut or a shaggy bob can work well for grey hair over 60. These styles can add movement and dimension to your hair, and they can also be styled in different ways for different occasions.

Long Hair

Long hair can be elegant and timeless. A layered cut or a blunt cut can work well for grey hair over 60. These styles can add volume and length to thinning hair, and they can also be styled in different ways for different occasions.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for grey hair over 60, it is important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that thins and layers your hair.

You may also want to consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you are active and on-the-go, you may want a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled. If you have a formal or professional job, you may want a style that is elegant and polished.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my grey hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your grey hair. However, it is important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and moisturizing, as grey hair can be fragile and prone to breakage.

Q: How often should I wash my grey hair?

A: It is recommended to wash grey hair every two to three days. This allows the natural oils in your hair to nourish and protect your strands.

Q: How can I protect my grey hair from sun damage?

A: You can protect your grey hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF. You can also avoid excessive heat styling, as this can dry out and damage your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your grey hair looking healthy and shiny, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This may include washing your hair with a gentle shampoo, conditioning your hair with a moisturizing conditioner, and using a hair mask or treatment once a week. You may also want to use a heat protectant before styling your hair with heat tools, and a leave-in conditioner to protect your strands from environmental damage.

Overall, there are many hair styles that can work well for grey hair over 60. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, there is a style that can flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. By choosing a style that complements your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can feel confident and stylish at any age.
