How To Tell If A Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Frank Mar 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many different styles to choose from, it can be difficult to determine which one will look best on you. Fortunately, there are a few simple steps you can take to help you figure out which hairstyle will suit you. In this article, we will walk you through the process of determining which hairstyle is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Think About Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle
  • Step 4: Consult with a Professional Stylist
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in determining which hairstyle will suit you is to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles flatter different face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Here are some general guidelines:

Oval Face: Oval faces are well-proportioned and can wear most hairstyles.

Round Face: Round faces benefit from hairstyles that add height and length. Avoid styles that are too short or too curly.

Square Face: Square faces look best with hairstyles that soften sharp angles. Soft waves or layers work well.

Heart-Shaped Face: Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that balance the face. Try a chin-length bob or long layers.

Long Face: Long faces benefit from hairstyles that add width. Try a side-swept bang or a chin-length bob.

Step 2: Think About Your Hair Type

Once you've considered your face shape, the next step is to think about your hair type. Different hairstyles work better with certain hair types, so it's important to choose a style that will work with your natural texture. Here are some general guidelines:

Straight Hair: Straight hair looks great in sleek, smooth styles. Consider a blunt bob or a long, straight cut.

Curly Hair: Curly hair looks best with hairstyles that enhance natural curls. Try a layered cut or a curly bob.

Wavy Hair: Wavy hair looks great in styles that enhance natural texture. Consider a shoulder-length cut with layers.

Thick Hair: Thick hair looks best with hairstyles that add layers and texture. Try a long, layered cut or a pixie cut.

Thin Hair: Thin hair benefits from hairstyles that add volume and texture. Consider a layered cut or a bob with side-swept bangs.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. A low-maintenance style may be best if you're short on time, while a more elaborate style may be better suited for a special occasion. Here are some general guidelines:

Low-Maintenance: If you're short on time, consider a style that requires minimal styling, such as a bob or a pixie cut.

Active Lifestyle: If you're active, consider a style that can be pulled back, such as a ponytail or a braid.

Special Occasion: If you have a special occasion coming up, consider a more elaborate style, such as an updo or a half-up, half-down style.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional Stylist

If you're still unsure which hairstyle is right for you, consider consulting with a professional stylist. A stylist can help you determine which styles will flatter your face shape and hair type, and can also offer advice on how to maintain your new style. Be sure to bring pictures of styles you like to your appointment.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your face shape when choosing a style.
  • Think about your hair type when choosing a style.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a style.
  • Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure.
  • Bring pictures of styles you like to your appointment.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a style. You can also consult with a professional stylist.

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

A: Short hair can be flattering on many different face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that works with your hair type and lifestyle.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's generally recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can achieve a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
