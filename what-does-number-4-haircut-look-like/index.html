What Does Number 4 Haircut Look Like

Written by Bardi Apr 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut, you may have heard of the number 4 haircut. But what does it actually look like? In this article, we'll explain what a number 4 haircut is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A number 4 haircut is a medium-length cut that leaves about 1/2 inch of hair on top of your head. The sides and back are usually tapered or faded, but the length can vary depending on your preference. The result is a clean, classic look that's easy to maintain.

How To Get It

If you're interested in getting a number 4 haircut, the first step is to find a stylist who's familiar with the style. You can ask for a number 4 at any barbershop or salon, but it's important to communicate exactly what you want to your stylist. Bring a picture or explain the length and style you're looking for to ensure you get the desired result.

The stylist will start by using clippers to cut the sides and back of your head to the desired length. They may use different guard sizes to achieve a tapered or faded look. The top of your head will then be trimmed with scissors to the desired length.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a number 4 haircut:

  1. Find a stylist who's familiar with the style.
  2. Communicate exactly what you want, either with a picture or by explaining the length and style.
  3. Sit back and relax as the stylist uses clippers to cut the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  4. The top of your head will then be trimmed with scissors to the desired length.
  5. When finished, check your hair in the mirror to ensure you're happy with the result.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a number 4 haircut:

  • Be specific about what you want. Use pictures or be clear in your explanation to ensure you get the desired result.
  • Regular maintenance is key. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Consider using hair products to style your hair after your cut. A little bit of product can go a long way in achieving your desired look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 4 haircuts:

Is a number 4 haircut good for all hair types?

Yes, a number 4 haircut can work for all hair types. However, it's important to communicate with your stylist to ensure they can adjust the cut to your specific hair type and texture.

Can I style my hair after getting a number 4 haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair after a number 4 haircut. Consider using a small amount of hair product to achieve your desired look.

How often should I get a number 4 haircut?

It's recommended to get a number 4 haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and well-maintained.

Hair Care

After getting a number 4 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and avoid over-washing. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Overall, a number 4 haircut is a classic and versatile style that can work for many hair types and face shapes. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, you can keep your number 4 haircut looking fresh and stylish.
