How To Style Your Hair To Give It Volume

Written by Petter May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to style your hair to give it volume.

Description

Voluminous hair is a timeless trend that never goes out of style. Whether you have long hair or short hair, adding volume can make a world of difference. Voluminous hair is not only fashionable, but it also adds depth and dimension to your tresses. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect amount of volume for your hair type and texture.

How to

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

The foundation of voluminous hair is a good haircut. Layers and textured ends can add dimension and movement to your hair. Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to achieving voluminous hair. Look for products specifically designed to add volume, such as volumizing shampoo and conditioner, root lifter, and volumizing mousse or spray. Apply these products to damp hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is crucial for adding volume. Start by blow drying your hair upside down to lift the roots. Use a round brush to further lift and add volume to your hair. Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can add instant volume. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them with a teasing comb. Be careful not to overdo it, as too much teasing can damage your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your voluminous hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your style in place and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it appear flat.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

What if I have thin or fine hair?

If you have thin or fine hair, you can still achieve voluminous hair with the right products and techniques. Look for lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair, and use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and lift.

How often should I blow dry my hair?

Blow drying your hair too often can damage it, so it's best to limit it to no more than twice a week. On other days, try air-drying your hair or using a dry shampoo to refresh your style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Get regular trims to prevent split ends, and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve voluminous hair that will turn heads wherever you go. So go ahead, let your hair down, and enjoy your new voluminous locks!
