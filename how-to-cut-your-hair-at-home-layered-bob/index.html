How To Cut Your Hair At Home Layered Bob

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? With a little bit of practice and patience, you can easily learn how to cut your hair at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a layered bob. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Hair at Home Layered Bob
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Your Hair at Home Layered Bob

Before we begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It is also recommended to have a mirror in front of you and a second mirror behind you.

Follow these simple steps to cut your hair at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture and thickness of your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into three sections: the top, middle, and bottom. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Start with the bottom section. Comb your hair straight down and cut the ends straight across. This will be the longest layer of your bob.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Section

Release the middle section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut the ends at a slight angle, starting from the back of your head to the front. This layer should be shorter than the bottom layer.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Cut the ends at a steeper angle, starting from the back of your head to the front. This layer should be the shortest of the three layers.

Tips for a Perfect Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect layered bob:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t put it back.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet. Wet hair is longer and will shrink when it dries.
  • Take breaks during the cutting process to avoid fatigue and mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a layered bob:

Can I cut my own hair without any experience?

Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to start with simple cuts and gradually work your way up to more complex styles.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it’s not recommended. Regular scissors are not sharp enough and can cause split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bob.

Now that you know how to cut your hair at home layered bob, give it a try and let us know how it turned out!
