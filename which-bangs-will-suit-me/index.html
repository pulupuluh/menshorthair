Which Bangs Will Suit Me A Guide To Finding The Perfect Bangs

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting bangs but not sure which style would suit you? Bangs can transform your look and add a touch of chic to your hairstyle. However, choosing the right style can be a daunting task. In this guide, we will help you find out which bangs will suit you.

Description: Different Types of Bangs

Before we get into which bangs will suit you, let's first go through the different types of bangs:

  • Straight bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Blunt bangs
  • Wispy bangs
  • Curly bangs

Each type of bangs has its own unique characteristics and can suit different face shapes and hair types. Keep reading to find out which bangs will suit you.

How to: Find the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right bangs for you is to determine your face shape. Here are the common face shapes and the bangs that suit them:

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides. This will create the illusion of a longer face and make your features appear slimmer.

Square Face

For a square face, go for soft, wispy bangs that are longer at the sides. This will soften your features and add a touch of femininity to your look.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, go for side-swept bangs that are shorter at the sides and longer in the middle. This will balance out your face shape and draw attention to your eyes.

Oval Face

For an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any type of bangs. However, blunt bangs or side-swept bangs that hit just above the eyebrows will complement your face shape the most.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face shape, go for blunt bangs that are straight across. This will soften your features and bring attention to your eyes.

Step-by-Step: How to Style Your Bangs

Once you have found the right bangs for you, it's important to know how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair where you want your bangs to start.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to add volume.
  4. If you have straight bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them out.
  5. If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to curl them towards your face.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair serum to smooth out any frizz.

Tips: Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips on how to maintain your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs to prevent damage.

FAQ: Common Questions About Bangs

Here are some common questions people have about bangs:

Do bangs make you look younger?

Yes, bangs can make you look younger as they soften your features and add a touch of playfulness to your look.

Can you pull off bangs with curly hair?

Yes, curly bangs can look great on some people. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Can I cut my own bangs?

We recommend going to a professional to get your bangs cut. However, if you're confident in your skills, you can cut your own bangs at home. Just make sure to use sharp scissors and take your time.

Hair Care: Products for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some products that can help you maintain your bangs:

  • Dry shampoo
  • Hair serum
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Round brush
  • Flat iron

Using the right products can help keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the right type of bangs can be a game-changer for your hairstyle. By following our guide, you can find out which bangs will suit you and how to style and maintain them. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks!
