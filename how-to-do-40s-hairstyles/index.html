How To Do 40S Hairstyles Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

The 1940s was an era of glamour and sophistication, and the hairstyles of that time reflected that. From victory rolls to pin curls, there are many iconic 40s hairstyles that continue to inspire us today. If you're looking to add a touch of vintage charm to your hair, read on for our ultimate guide on how to do 40s hairstyles.

Description

In this article, we'll cover some of the most popular 40s hairstyles, including victory rolls, pin curls, and the classic updo. We'll provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve these looks, as well as tips for maintaining them. Additionally, we'll answer some frequently asked questions about 40s hairstyles and offer advice on hair care.

How to Do 40s Hairstyles

Victory Rolls

Victory rolls are perhaps the most iconic 40s hairstyle. To achieve this look, start by separating your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head. Take a small section of hair from one side and roll it upwards towards your scalp, securing it with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side. For added volume, backcomb the hair at the crown of your head before starting your rolls.

Pin Curls

Pin curls were another popular 40s hairstyle. To create this look, start by washing your hair and applying a styling product. Take a small section of hair and wrap it tightly around your finger. Remove your finger and continue to roll the hair until it reaches your scalp. Secure the curl with a bobby pin. Repeat this process all over your head, then allow your hair to dry completely. Remove the pins and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

Classic Updo

The classic updo was a staple of 40s fashion. To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail upwards towards the crown of your head, then secure it with bobby pins. Use additional pins to tuck in any loose ends. For added elegance, accessorize your updo with a ribbon or flower.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Decide on the hairstyle you want to create.

2. Gather the necessary tools, including bobby pins, a comb, and styling products.

3. Start by preparing your hair with a styling product or by washing it.

4. Follow the specific instructions for your chosen hairstyle.

5. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

6. Finish with hairspray or other finishing products to ensure your style lasts all day.

Tips

- Backcombing can add volume and texture to your hair.

- Use hairspray or other finishing products to keep your style in place.

- Accessories like ribbons or flowers can add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle.

- Be patient! It may take a few tries to get your desired look just right.

FAQ

What products do I need to create 40s hairstyles?

You'll need bobby pins, a comb, and styling products like hairspray or mousse.

Can I create 40s hairstyles on short hair?

Yes! Victory rolls and pin curls can be adapted for shorter hair lengths.

How do I maintain my 40s hairstyle?

Use hairspray or other finishing products to keep your style in place. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to lose its shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for creating and maintaining 40s hairstyles. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use styling products that won't damage your hair. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create and maintain a variety of 40s hairstyles. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more modern, these vintage-inspired hairstyles are sure to turn heads.
