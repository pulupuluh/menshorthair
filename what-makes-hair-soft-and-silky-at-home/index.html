What Makes Hair Soft And Silky At Home

Written by Aprilia Jul 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Having soft and silky hair is something that most of us desire. However, achieving it can sometimes feel like an impossible task, especially if you don’t know what to do. Fortunately, there are a few things that you can do at home to make your hair soft and silky. In this article, we’ll discuss what makes hair soft and silky at home, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks that you can use to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • What Makes Hair Soft and Silky?
  • How to Achieve Soft and Silky Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

What Makes Hair Soft and Silky?

There are a few things that contribute to soft and silky hair:

  • Deep conditioning
  • Protein treatments
  • Using the right shampoo and conditioner
  • Using natural oils
  • Avoiding heat styling

By incorporating these things into your hair care routine, you can achieve softer and silkier hair.

How to Achieve Soft and Silky Hair

Now that we know what makes hair soft and silky, let’s discuss how to achieve it:

  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Use a protein treatment once a month
  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Use natural oils such as coconut oil, argan oil, or jojoba oil
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

By following these steps, you can achieve soft and silky hair in no time.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve soft and silky hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes
  3. Rinse out the conditioner and towel dry your hair
  4. Apply a natural oil to your hair and comb through it
  5. Avoid heat styling and let your hair air dry
  6. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you’re going to be outside

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve and maintain soft and silky hair:

  • Don’t wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage while you sleep

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about achieving soft and silky hair:

  • Q: Can I achieve soft and silky hair if I have curly hair?
  • A: Yes, you can. Follow the same steps, but use products that are suitable for curly hair.
  • Q: How often should I deep condition my hair?
  • A: It’s recommended to deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Q: Can I use natural oils on my scalp?
  • A: Yes, you can. Just be careful not to use too much, as it can make your hair look greasy.

Hair Care

Having soft and silky hair is all about taking care of your hair. By deep conditioning, using natural oils, and avoiding heat styling, you can achieve the hair of your dreams. Just remember to choose products that are suitable for your hair type and to protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
