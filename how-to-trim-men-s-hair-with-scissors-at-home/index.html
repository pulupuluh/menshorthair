How To Trim Men s Hair With Scissors At Home

Written by Oliver Mar 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Trimming men's hair with scissors at home is a great way to save money and time. It can be done easily with the right tools and technique. In this article, we will discuss how to trim men's hair with scissors at home, step by step. We will also provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also recommended to have a hair clipper to trim the sides and back of the head.

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to fall. This will make it easier to see the length and shape you want to achieve.

Next, divide your hair into sections. Use the comb to create a parting in the middle of your head, and then part your hair from the front to the back. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.

Step by Step

1. Take a small section of hair from the top section and comb it straight up. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process on the rest of the top section, working from front to back.

2. Unclip the bottom section of your hair. Comb it straight down and trim the ends using the same technique as the top section. Work from front to back.

3. Use the hair clipper to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard, depending on the length you want to achieve. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and trim gradually. You can always go shorter, but you can't go back once you've cut too much.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

FAQ

How often should I trim my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to achieve. On average, it is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I trim my own hair?

Yes, you can trim your own hair with scissors at home. However, it is recommended to have someone help you with the back of your head or use a mirror to check your work.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back quickly. You can always go to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

Hair Care

After trimming your hair, it is important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners. If you have split ends, it is recommended to have them trimmed by a professional hairdresser.

Trimming men's hair with scissors at home is an easy and cost-effective way to maintain your hair. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut without leaving your home.
