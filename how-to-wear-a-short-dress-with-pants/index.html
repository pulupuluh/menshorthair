How To Wear A Short Dress With Pants A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short dresses are a great way to show off your legs and stay cool in warm weather, but sometimes you might feel a little exposed. That's where pants come in! By pairing your short dress with pants, you can create a stylish and unique look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a short dress with pants and give you some tips for styling this trendy look.

How to Wear a Short Dress with Pants

Before we get started, it's important to note that not all short dresses will work with pants. You'll want to choose a dress that's not too tight or too short, as this can make it difficult to find pants that fit well. A loose-fitting, knee-length dress is a great option for this look.

Step 1: Choose Your Pants

The first step in wearing a short dress with pants is to choose the right pants. Skinny jeans or leggings are a great option, as they'll help to balance out the loose-fitting dress. Alternatively, you can opt for wide-leg pants or culottes for a more flowy look.

When it comes to color, you can either match your pants to your dress or go for a contrasting color. For example, if your dress is a bright color, you can pair it with black or white pants for a chic look.

Step 2: Layer Your Pieces

Once you've chosen your pants, it's time to layer your pieces. Start by putting on your pants and then slip your dress over the top. Make sure the dress is pulled down enough so that it covers the waistband of your pants.

If you're worried about your dress riding up, you can add a belt to cinch in your waist and keep everything in place. Alternatively, you can also tuck your dress into your pants for a more streamlined look.

Step 3: Add Accessories

Accessories are key to pulling off this look. A statement necklace or earrings can add some personality to your outfit, while a scarf or hat can help to tie everything together. Just make sure to keep your accessories simple, as you don't want to overwhelm your outfit.

Tips for Wearing a Short Dress with Pants

Now that you know how to wear a short dress with pants, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a dress with a loose, flowy fit
  • Opt for skinny jeans or leggings for a more streamlined look
  • Add a belt to cinch in your waist and keep your dress in place
  • Keep your accessories simple
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns

FAQs

Q: Can I wear boots with a short dress and pants?

A: Yes, boots can be a great addition to this look. Ankle boots or knee-high boots can help to elongate your legs and add some edge to your outfit.

Q: What should I do if my dress keeps riding up?

A: If your dress keeps riding up, try adding a belt to cinch in your waist and keep everything in place. Alternatively, you can tuck your dress into your pants for a more streamlined look.

Hair Care

When wearing a short dress with pants, it's important to keep your hair looking polished. A sleek ponytail or messy bun can be the perfect complement to this look. Alternatively, you can leave your hair down and add some waves for a more bohemian vibe. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your locks.

Conclusion

Wearing a short dress with pants is a fun and trendy way to switch up your style. By following these tips and tricks, you can create a unique and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion. Just remember to choose the right pants, layer your pieces, and add some accessories to make your outfit pop. Happy styling!
