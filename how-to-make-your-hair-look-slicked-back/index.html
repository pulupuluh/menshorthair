How To Make Your Hair Look Slicked Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic, sophisticated hairstyle, you can't go wrong with a slicked-back look. Whether you're going for a formal event or just want to look your best for a night out, slicking back your hair is a timeless style that's easy to achieve. In this guide, we'll show you how to make your hair look slicked back in just a few simple steps.

Description

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic look that has been around for decades. It is a simple, yet sophisticated hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. This style is achieved by combing the hair back and adding a product to keep it in place. It works well on most hair types and lengths, making it a versatile option for anyone looking to switch up their look.

How to

Here's how to make your hair look slicked back in a few easy steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that work well with your hair type to ensure that your hair is clean and healthy. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. Use a pomade or gel to achieve a strong hold, or a mousse or cream for a lighter hold. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to coat every strand.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back. Start at the hairline and work your way back, making sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles. Continue combing until your hair is completely slicked back.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can choose to leave it as is or add some extra style. You can create a part, slick the sides down, or add some volume at the top. Play around with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Use a fine-tooth comb for a smooth, polished look
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you
  • Use a hair spray to set your style in place

FAQ

Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still achieve a slicked-back look even if you have short hair. Use a styling product with a strong hold to keep your hair in place.

How often should I wash my hair if I'm wearing a slicked-back style?

You should wash your hair as often as you normally would. However, if you're using a lot of styling product, you may want to wash your hair more frequently to avoid buildup.

Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can still achieve a slicked-back look even if you have curly hair. Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and use a comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Hair Care

While the slicked-back hairstyle is a great look, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that work well with your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling great, whether you're going for a slicked-back look or trying out a new style.
