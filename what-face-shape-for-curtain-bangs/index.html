What Face Shape Is Best For Curtain Bangs

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle for years now, and it's no wonder why. They're versatile, low-maintenance, and can suit a wide range of face shapes. However, not all curtain bangs are created equal, and the style that looks great on your friend may not be the best fit for you. That's why it's important to consider your face shape when deciding on the perfect curtain bangs for you.

What Are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They are longer than traditional bangs, often reaching past the eyebrows, and are usually cut in a soft, feathered style. Curtain bangs can be worn straight or styled with a bit of texture, making them a versatile choice for any hair type or length.

What Face Shapes Are Best for Curtain Bangs?

While curtain bangs can flatter many face shapes, they tend to look best on those with oval, heart, or square-shaped faces. Here's a breakdown of how curtain bangs can accentuate each of these face shapes:

Oval-Shaped Faces

If you have an oval-shaped face, congratulations! You have the most versatile face shape of all, and can pull off just about any hairstyle, including curtain bangs. The soft, feathered style of curtain bangs can help balance out the length of an oval face, creating a flattering, face-framing effect.

Heart-Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces tend to have a wider forehead and a narrower jawline, making curtain bangs an excellent choice for adding balance to the face. The middle part of the curtain bangs can draw attention to the center of the face, while the longer pieces on the sides help soften the angles of the jawline.

Square-Shaped Faces

If you have a square-shaped face, curtain bangs can help soften the strong angles of your face shape. The middle part of the curtain bangs can create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face, while the longer pieces on the sides help to soften the jawline.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you've decided that curtain bangs are the right choice for you, here's how to get them:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut into bangs. This can be the entire front section of your hair, or just a small section.
  2. Comb the hair forward and hold it between your index and middle fingers at the desired length.
  3. Cut the hair straight across, making sure to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be. You can always go back and trim more later.
  4. Use a razor or scissors to create a soft, feathered texture along the edges of the bangs.
  5. Finish off the look by parting the bangs down the middle and styling them as desired.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Once you have your new curtain bangs, here are a few tips for keeping them looking great:

  • Use a small amount of styling cream or mousse to add texture and hold to your bangs.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create a soft, bouncy effect.
  • If your bangs start to look greasy, use a dry shampoo to refresh them without having to wash your hair.
  • Trim your bangs every six to eight weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ About Curtain Bangs

What's the difference between curtain bangs and regular bangs?

Curtain bangs are longer than traditional bangs and are parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They are usually cut in a soft, feathered style, while traditional bangs are cut straight across the forehead.

Can I wear curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes! Curtain bangs can look great on curly hair, but you may need to style them differently than someone with straight hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curl pattern, or use a curling iron to add definition to your bangs.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs are a relatively low-maintenance hairstyle, but you will need to trim them every six to eight weeks to keep them looking fresh. You may also need to touch them up with a straightener or curling iron between washes.

How to Take Care of Your Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips for keeping your curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and free of oil.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • If you're going to be outside for an extended period of time, wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent your bangs from fading or becoming damaged by the sun.

Overall, curtain bangs can be a flattering and versatile hairstyle for a wide range of face shapes. By considering your face shape and following these tips for styling and care, you can achieve the perfect curtain bangs for you.
