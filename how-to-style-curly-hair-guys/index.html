How To Style Curly Hair Guys Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine May 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Caring for and styling curly hair can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. If you're a guy with curly hair, you're in luck. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a variety of hairstyles that will make you look and feel great. In this article, we'll take a look at how to style curly hair guys by providing you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Hair Care

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of styling your curls, let's talk about hair care. Proper hair care is essential for healthy, manageable curls. Here are some tips:

1. Shampoo Less Often

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important not to strip it of its natural oils. Shampooing less often (once or twice a week) can help maintain moisture and prevent frizz.

2. Condition, Condition, Condition

Conditioner is your friend when it comes to curly hair. Use a moisturizing conditioner every time you wash your hair, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using brushes on your curly hair, as they can break up your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

4. Don't Overuse Heat Tools

Heat tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your curls and cause frizz. If you must use heat tools, use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

5. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and breakage, which is especially problematic for curly hair. Switch to a silk or satin pillowcase to help preserve your curls while you sleep.

How to Style Curly Hair Guys

Now that you know how to take care of your curly hair, let's talk about how to style it. Here are some popular hairstyles for curly-haired guys:

The Classic Taper

The classic taper is a timeless and versatile haircut that works well with curly hair. The sides and back are cut short, while the top is left longer and styled with a bit of product. This style is perfect for guys who want a clean, low-maintenance look.

The Curly Quiff

The curly quiff is a modern take on the classic quiff. The sides are cut short, while the top is left longer and styled up and back. This style is perfect for guys who want a trendy, edgy look.

The Curly Afro

The curly afro is a bold and iconic hairstyle that works well with natural curls. This style requires a bit of length and volume, so make sure to let your curls grow out a bit before trying it. To style, simply pick out your curls and fluff them up with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your curly hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. Choose a product that's designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

3. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it into the shape you want. You can use a bit of pomade or wax to add definition and hold.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling your curly hair:

1. Experiment with Products

Not all products work for all curly hair types. Experiment with different types of styling products to find what works best for your hair.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don't try to fight your natural texture. Embrace your curls and work with them to achieve a style that looks great and feels comfortable.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair:

Q: Should I brush my curly hair?

A: No, brushing curly hair can break up your curls and cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash curly hair once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: What's the best way to dry curly hair?

A: The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help maintain your curls' shape and prevent frizz.

Congratulations! You now know how to style curly hair guys. With the right hair care and styling techniques, you can achieve a range of hairstyles that will make you look and feel great.
