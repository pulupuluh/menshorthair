How To Curl Hair Away From Your Face A Step By Step Guide

Curling your hair can be a great way to switch up your look, but it can be tricky to get those curls to go in the right direction. If you're trying to curl your hair away from your face, it can be even more challenging to get the curls to stay put. In this post, we'll go over how to curl your hair away from your face in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Curl Hair Away from Your Face

Curling your hair away from your face is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. Here's how to do it:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron.

2. Choose the Right Curling Iron

Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want. For looser curls, use a larger barrel, and for tighter curls, use a smaller barrel.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping the top sections of your hair out of the way.

4. Curl Your Hair Away from Your Face

Take a small section of hair and clamp it with the curling iron. Hold the curling iron vertically and twist the barrel away from your face. Hold for a few seconds and release. Continue curling the rest of your hair, always twisting the barrel away from your face.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Hair Away from Your Face

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair away from your face:

  • Start at the bottom and work your way up.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want.
  • Section your hair using clips.
  • Twist the barrel away from your face when curling.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray.

FAQs

Q: How do I make my curls last longer?

A: Use hairspray to set your curls in place and avoid touching your hair too much.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener or even bobby pins to create curls.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It's best to avoid curling your hair every day to prevent damage. Aim for once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Curling your hair away from your face can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can create beautiful curls that last all day. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
