Do You Look Younger With Or Without Bangs

Description

Many women wonder whether they look younger with or without bangs. Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some style to your hair, but they can also make you look older or emphasize certain features. This article will explore the pros and cons of having bangs and help you figure out whether they're right for you.

How to

The first step in deciding whether to get bangs is to figure out your face shape. Bangs can work for any face shape, but they may need to be styled differently depending on your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for longer, side-swept bangs to create a more elongated look. If you have a square face, you may want to opt for blunt, straight-across bangs to soften your features.

Once you've figured out your face shape, you'll want to consider the thickness and texture of your hair. Bangs can be a great way to add some volume and texture to thin or fine hair, but they can also be a nightmare to style if you have thick or curly hair. Make sure to discuss your hair type with your stylist before getting bangs.

Step by step

If you've decided to take the plunge and get bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Consult with your stylist and bring pictures of the type of bangs you want.
  3. Make sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and thickness of your bangs.
  4. Be prepared to style your bangs every day and invest in some good hair products, such as hairspray or dry shampoo.
  5. Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your bangs.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your bangs work for you:

  • If you're not sure whether you want to commit to bangs, try out clip-in bangs or a wig first.
  • If you're nervous about getting bangs, start with longer, side-swept bangs that can be easily grown out.
  • If you have oily skin or tend to sweat a lot, be prepared for your bangs to get greasy or stick to your forehead.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs:

Do bangs make you look younger?

Not necessarily. Bangs can make you look younger or older, depending on the style and how they're cut. If you have a youthful face shape or features, bangs can emphasize those and make you look even younger. However, if you have mature features, such as wrinkles or a sagging jawline, bangs may not be the best choice.

Do bangs make your forehead look smaller?

Yes, bangs can make your forehead look smaller or shorter. If you have a large or high forehead, blunt bangs or side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a smaller forehead.

How often do you need to trim your bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length. If you have longer, side-swept bangs, you may be able to go longer between trims.

Hair Care

If you decide to get bangs, you'll need to invest in some hair care products to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs, but be careful not to burn them.
  • Use a hairspray or dry shampoo to keep your bangs in place and prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, whether you look younger with or without bangs depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some edge to your hair, but they can also be high-maintenance and difficult to style. Make sure to consult with your stylist before getting bangs and consider trying out clip-in bangs or a wig first.
