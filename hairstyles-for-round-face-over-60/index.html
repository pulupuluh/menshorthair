Hairstyles For Round Face Over 60

Written by Petter Aug 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are over 60 and have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you look younger and more vibrant. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles for round faces over 60, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to maintain your hair's health.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Round faces have equal width and length, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. You want a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and slims down your face.

For round faces over 60, hairstyles that add volume on top and have layers can elongate the face. Avoid straight and blunt cuts that make your face appear wider. Opt for soft and wispy bangs that cover your forehead and create an angle on your face.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces over 60 as they can create the illusion of height and length. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs and textured layers can add volume on top and slim down your face. A bob with layers and a side part can also elongate your face and create a flattering look.

How to Achieve a Pixie Cut

  1. Start with dry hair and section it into four parts.
  2. Begin at the nape of the neck and use clippers to cut the hair to your desired length.
  3. Move to the sides and top of the head and use scissors to cut the hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Use texturizing shears to add layers and texture to the hair.
  5. Finish with a styling product to add volume and texture to the hair.

Medium Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Medium hairstyles are versatile and can work for any face shape, including round faces over 60. A layered shag with side-swept bangs can frame your face and add volume on top. A lob with layers and a side part can elongate your face and create a youthful look.

How to Achieve a Layered Shag

  1. Start with dry hair and section it into four parts.
  2. Begin at the nape of the neck and use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.
  3. Move to the sides and top of the head and use scissors to cut the hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Add layers to the hair by cutting diagonal sections and texturizing the ends.
  5. Finish with a styling product to add volume and texture to the hair.

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Long hairstyles can work for round faces over 60 if they have layers and volume on top. A layered lob with side bangs can elongate your face and create a youthful look. A long bob with layers and a center part can slim down your face and add texture to your hair.

How to Achieve a Layered Lob

  1. Start with dry hair and section it into four parts.
  2. Begin at the nape of the neck and use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.
  3. Move to the sides and top of the head and use scissors to cut the hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Add layers to the hair by cutting diagonal sections and texturizing the ends.
  5. Finish with a styling product to add volume and texture to the hair.

Tips to Maintain Your Hairstyle

After getting your ideal hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently and always use a heat protectant.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for round faces over 60?

The best hairstyles for round faces over 60 are hairstyles that create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Short, medium, and long hairstyles with layers and volume on top can be flattering for round faces.

Can round faces over 60 wear bangs?

Yes, round faces over 60 can wear bangs. Soft and wispy bangs that cover the forehead and create an angle on the face can be flattering for round faces. Avoid blunt and heavy bangs that make your face appear wider.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle's shape.


In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for round faces over 60 requires consideration of face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Short, medium, and long hairstyles with layers and volume on top can be flattering for round faces. Maintaining your hairstyle with proper hair care can keep it looking fresh and healthy.
