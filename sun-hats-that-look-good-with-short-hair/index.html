Sun Hats That Look Good With Short Hair A Guide To Choosing The Perfect Accessory

Written by Bardi Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to start thinking about protecting your skin from the sun's harmful rays. But if you have short hair, finding a sun hat that looks good can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of options out there that will complement your style and keep you safe from the sun. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best sun hats for short hair and offer tips on how to wear them.

Description

Short hair can be a great look for summer, but it can also leave your scalp and face exposed to the sun. That's where sun hats come in. Not only do they provide protection from the sun, but they can also be a stylish accessory that complements your outfit. There are many different styles of sun hats to choose from, so it's important to find one that works with your hair type and personal style.

How to Choose the Right Sun Hat

When shopping for a sun hat, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the size and shape of your head. Some hats are better suited to round faces, while others look better on longer faces. You'll also want to think about the material of the hat. Straw hats are a popular choice for summer, but they may not be the best option if you have sensitive skin. Look for hats made from breathable fabrics like cotton or linen.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a sun hat that looks good with short hair:

  1. Measure your head to determine your hat size
  2. Consider your face shape and hair type
  3. Choose a hat made from a breathable material
  4. Try on different styles to see what looks best
  5. Accessorize with sunglasses and other summer essentials

Tips for Wearing a Sun Hat with Short Hair

Once you've found the perfect sun hat, it's important to know how to wear it. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hat that fits snugly on your head, but not too tightly
  • Consider wearing your hair in a low bun or ponytail to keep it off your face
  • Pick a hat that complements your outfit and personal style
  • Accessorize with sunglasses and other summer essentials

FAQ

Q: What is the best material for a sun hat?

A: Breathable fabrics like cotton and linen are ideal for sun hats, as they allow air to flow through and keep you cool.

Q: How do I know what size hat to buy?

A: Measure the circumference of your head at its widest point, just above your eyebrows. Most hats come in sizes ranging from small to extra-large.

Q: Can I wear a sun hat with short hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many styles of sun hats that look great with short hair, including fedoras, bucket hats, and wide-brimmed hats.

Hair Care Tips for Summer

Summer can be tough on your hair, especially if you're spending a lot of time outdoors. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners
  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With the right sun hat and a few hair care tips, you can enjoy the summer sun without damaging your hair or skin. Whether you're spending the day at the beach or running errands around town, a stylish sun hat is the perfect accessory for any short hair look. So go ahead and embrace your short hair this summer, and don't forget to protect it with a chic sun hat!
