What Is The Best Volumizing Hairspray For Fine Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how difficult it can be to add volume and body to your locks. That's where volumizing hairspray comes in. But with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your hair. In this article, we'll discuss the best volumizing hairspray for fine hair and provide tips on how to use it for the best results.

What to Look for in a Volumizing Hairspray

Before we dive into the best volumizing hairsprays for fine hair, it's essential to understand what to look for in a product. Here are some factors to consider:

Hold Level

The hold level of a hairspray determines how well it will keep your hair in place. If you have fine hair, you don't want a hairspray that's too heavy or sticky, as it can weigh your hair down.

Ingredients

Look for a hairspray that contains ingredients that add volume and body, such as hydrolyzed wheat protein and panthenol. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair and make it look flat.

Application

Consider the application method of the hairspray. Some sprays are aerosol, while others are pump sprays. Choose the application method that works best for you.

The Best Volumizing Hairsprays for Fine Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a volumizing hairspray, let's dive into our top picks:

1. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Hairspray

This lightweight spray adds volume and body without weighing down your hair. It contains panthenol and hydrolyzed wheat protein to strengthen and nourish your locks. Plus, it has a pleasant scent that's not overpowering.

2. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

This spray provides long-lasting hold and volume, thanks to its unique formula that includes keratin and hydrolyzed vegetable protein. It also contains a UV protectant to shield your hair from sun damage.

3. TRESemmÃ© Tres Two Extra Hold Hairspray

This affordable hairspray is a great option for those on a budget. It provides a strong hold without making your hair feel sticky or crunchy. It's also humidity-resistant, making it perfect for humid climates.

How to Use Volumizing Hairspray

Now that you have your volumizing hairspray, it's essential to know how to use it correctly for the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before applying hairspray.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to apply the hairspray evenly.

3. Apply Hairspray

Hold the can of hairspray about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly on each section of hair. Avoid spraying too much in one area, as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

4. Finish Your Style

Once you've applied the hairspray, finish your style as desired. You can use a brush or your fingers to tease your hair for added volume.

Tips for Using Volumizing Hairspray

Here are some additional tips for using volumizing hairspray:

Avoid Overloading

Don't overload your hair with too much product, as this can make it look greasy and weighed down.

Use on Dry Hair

Only use hairspray on dry hair. Applying it to wet hair can make it look crunchy and stiff.

Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you've applied hairspray, avoid touching your hair too much. This can cause the product to break down and make your hair look flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about volumizing hairspray:

Can I Use Hairspray Every Day?

Yes, you can use hairspray every day. Just be sure to choose a product that's lightweight and doesn't contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Can Hairspray Damage My Hair?

Hairspray itself doesn't damage your hair, but using it too much can cause build-up and make your hair look dull and lifeless. Be sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid this.

How Do I Remove Hairspray Build-Up?

If you have hairspray build-up in your hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove it. You can also use a mixture of baking soda and water to create a DIY clarifying treatment.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

In addition to using volumizing hairspray, there are other things you can do to care for your fine hair:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain nourishing ingredients like argan oil.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look flat. Try to limit your use of heat tools and use a heat protectant when you do style your hair.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Be sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the best volumizing hairspray for fine hair is all about finding a product that provides hold and volume without weighing your hair down. Consider factors like hold level, ingredients, and application method when choosing a hairspray. And remember, proper hair care is crucial for keeping your locks looking healthy and voluminous.
