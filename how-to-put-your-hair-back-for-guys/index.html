How To Put Your Hair Back For Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're a guy with long hair, you know that sometimes it can be a hassle to keep it out of your face. Whether you're working out, studying, or just trying to look your best, putting your hair back can be a great solution. In this guide, we'll show you how to put your hair back for guys in a few simple steps.

How to Put Your Hair Back for Guys

Putting your hair back can be done in a few different ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Here are some popular methods:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Braid

Each of these methods can be adapted to suit your personal style and preference. Let's take a look at how to do each one.

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic way to put your hair back. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, near the top of your neck.
  3. Hold your hair in one hand and use the other hand to wrap a hair tie around the base of the ponytail.
  4. Adjust the ponytail so it sits where you want it.

For a sleek look, you can use a comb to smooth down any flyaways before putting your hair back in a ponytail.

Bun

A bun is a more formal way to put your hair back. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, near the crown.
  3. Twist your hair into a tight spiral.
  4. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to form a bun.
  5. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.

A bun can be worn high or low, depending on your preference.

Braid

A braid is a unique way to put your hair back. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, near the crown.
  3. Divide your hair into three sections.
  4. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Continue crossing the sections over each other until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

A braid can be worn tight or loose, depending on your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the three main ways to put your hair back, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Decide which method you want to use (ponytail, bun, or braid).
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Gather your hair at the back of your head in the position you want it (near the neck for a ponytail, near the crown for a bun or braid).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen method (see above).
  5. Adjust the position of your hair as needed.

And that's it! You now know how to put your hair back for guys.

Tips for Putting Your Hair Back

Here are some additional tips to help you put your hair back:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • For a more polished look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways before putting your hair back.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • If you're working out or doing an activity that may cause your hair to come loose, use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can guys with short hair put their hair back?

Yes! Guys with short hair can use a hair tie or headband to keep their hair out of their face.

Is it bad for guys to put their hair back?

No, it's not bad for guys to put their hair back. In fact, it can be a practical and stylish choice.

How often should guys wash their hair?

It depends on the individual's hair type and lifestyle. Some guys may need to wash their hair daily, while others can go a few days between washes. It's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.

Hair Care for Guys with Long Hair

If you have long hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like blow dryers and straighteners) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking great.

Putting your hair back can be a great solution for guys with long hair. Whether you choose a ponytail, bun, or braid, there are plenty of ways to keep your hair out of your face. With this step-by-step guide and some helpful tips, you'll be able to put your hair back with ease.
