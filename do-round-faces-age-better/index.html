Do Round Faces Age Better

Written by Bardi May 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our face changes in shape and structure. Many people believe that those with round faces age better than those with other face shapes. But is this really true? In this article, we will explore the question, "Do round faces age better?" and provide tips on how to maintain a youthful appearance regardless of your face shape.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

People with round faces often have fuller cheeks and a softer jawline, which can create a youthful appearance. However, as we age, the skin loses elasticity, and wrinkles, fine lines, and sagging skin can develop regardless of face shape.

Round faces can also be prone to showing age-related changes such as jowls, which are pockets of fat that develop along the jawline. These changes can make the face appear heavier and less defined, which can make a person look older.

How to

1. Protect Your Skin From the Sun

The sun's UV rays can cause damage to the skin, including wrinkles and age spots. Protect your skin by wearing sunscreen with an SPF of at least 30 every day, even on cloudy days. Wear a hat and sunglasses to protect your face from the sun.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin hydrated, which can reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines. Aim for at least eight glasses of water a day.

3. Use Anti-Aging Products

Anti-aging products can help reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines. Look for products that contain retinol, vitamin C, and hyaluronic acid.

4. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is essential for healthy skin. Lack of sleep can cause bags under the eyes and make the skin appear dull and tired.

5. Exercise Regularly

Exercise can help improve circulation, which can promote healthy skin. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise a day.

Step by Step

1. Cleanse Your Skin

Cleanse your skin twice a day to remove dirt and oil. Use a gentle cleanser that is appropriate for your skin type.

2. Exfoliate

Exfoliating can help remove dead skin cells and promote healthy skin. Use a gentle exfoliant once or twice a week.

3. Moisturize

Moisturizing can help keep your skin hydrated and reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines. Use a moisturizer that is appropriate for your skin type.

Tips

  • Avoid smoking, which can cause wrinkles and fine lines.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption, which can dehydrate the skin.
  • Get regular facials to promote healthy skin.

FAQ

Do round faces age better than other face shapes?

While people with round faces may appear more youthful when they are younger, all face shapes can show signs of aging as they get older.

What causes wrinkles?

Wrinkles are caused by a combination of factors, including genetics, sun exposure, and natural aging processes.

Can anti-aging products really reduce the appearance of wrinkles?

Anti-aging products can help reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines, but they cannot completely reverse the signs of aging.

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can also play a role in how youthful you appear. Consider a shorter haircut or layers to add volume and definition to your face. Bangs can also help create a more youthful appearance. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, which can drag down the face and make you look older.

So, do round faces age better? While they may appear more youthful when we are younger, all faces can show signs of aging as we get older. However, by following these tips and taking care of your skin, you can maintain a youthful appearance regardless of your face shape.
