How Do You Get Tiktok Boy Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 17, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a fan of TikTok, you’ve probably seen a lot of guys with cool, edgy hairstyles. The TikTok boy hair trend is all about embracing your natural texture and adding some personality to your look. In this article, we’ll show you how to get that TikTok boy hair style that you’ve been admiring.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get TikTok Boy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling TikTok Boy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for TikTok Boy Hair

How to Get TikTok Boy Hair

TikTok boy hair is all about embracing your natural texture, whether that’s curly, wavy, or straight. The key is to keep your hair looking messy and effortless, while still maintaining a cool and edgy vibe. Here are some tips for achieving that TikTok boy hair style:

1. Start with the Right Haircut

The first step in getting TikTok boy hair is to get the right haircut. This usually involves keeping the sides and back short, while leaving more length on top. Some guys opt for a fade, while others prefer a more classic look with longer layers all around. Talk to your barber or stylist about what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

TikTok boy hair is all about embracing your natural texture. If you have curly hair, let those curls shine! If your hair is straight, add some texture with a sea salt spray or texturizing paste. Wavy hair can benefit from a light mousse or cream. The key is to avoid using too much product and to keep things looking effortless.

3. Experiment with Length and Layers

The great thing about TikTok boy hair is that it’s versatile. You can play around with different lengths and layers to find the look that works for you. Some guys prefer a shorter, more structured cut, while others like to keep things longer and more messy. Layers can add volume and texture, while a shorter cut can give you a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to get that TikTok boy hair style? Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it until it’s damp but not dripping wet.

2. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, depending on your hair type and the look you’re going for. A sea salt spray can add texture to straight hair, while a light mousse can define curls. Work the product through your hair with your fingers.

3. Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. Don’t worry about making it perfect – the key is to keep things looking effortless and messy. TikTok boy hair is all about embracing imperfection!

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve got your hair styled the way you want it, finish with a light misting of hairspray to hold the look in place. You don’t want your hair to look stiff or crunchy – just a light, flexible hold will do.

Tips for Styling TikTok Boy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling TikTok boy hair:

  • Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for your hair type
  • Don’t be afraid to try out new hairstyles – TikTok boy hair is all about being creative and expressive
  • Avoid using too much product, which can make your hair look greasy or weighed down
  • Pay attention to your hairline – make sure it looks neat and tidy

FAQs

What if I have short hair?

Even if your hair is short, you can still achieve the TikTok boy hair look. Keep the sides and back short, and add some texture to the top with a product like texturizing paste.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair is perfect for TikTok boy hair! Embrace your natural texture and use a light mousse or curl defining cream to keep your curls looking defined.

Hair Care for TikTok Boy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when you’re rocking the TikTok boy hair look:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using products with harsh chemicals
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

Now that you know how to get that TikTok boy hair style, it’s time to get creative and have some fun with your hair! Remember, the key is to embrace your natural texture and let your personality shine through.
