Shoulder Length Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle that doesn't flatter your round face? Look no further as we bring you the latest shoulder length haircuts for round faces in 2021. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get that perfect haircut, tips on how to style it, and a few FAQs to help you choose the best style for you.

Description

Shoulder length haircuts have been trending for a few years now, and they're perfect for women with round faces. The latest styles in 2021 include layers, bangs, and curls, which can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous and less round.

How to

Getting a shoulder length haircut for a round face is easy. You can either visit a salon or do it yourself at home. If you're going to a salon, make sure to bring a picture of the haircut you want and consult with your stylist about the best style for your face shape. If you're doing it yourself, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure to wash your hair and towel dry it. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the front, the back, and two sides.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back section, and work your way towards the front. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut straight across. Remember to cut small sections at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting, style your hair using a hairdryer and a round brush. You can also add some curls or waves using a curling iron to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a shoulder length haircut for round faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Opt for layers, as they can add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider getting bangs, as they can help balance out your face shape.
  • If you have thicker hair, avoid blunt cuts and opt for layers instead.

FAQs

1. What is the best shoulder length haircut for round faces?

The best shoulder length haircut for round faces is one that adds volume and texture to your hair, such as layers or curls. Bangs can also help balance out your face shape.

2. Can I get a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get a bob if you have a round face, but make sure it's a longer bob that ends at your collarbone. Avoid shorter bobs, as they can make your face look rounder.

3. How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the latest shoulder length haircuts for round faces in 2021 with confidence. Remember to consult with your stylist before making any drastic changes to your hair, and always take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
