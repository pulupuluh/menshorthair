How To Cut Your Own Hair In Layers With Bangs

Written by Christine Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts? Do you want to try something new and cut your own hair in layers with bangs? It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own hair in layers with bangs step by step.

How to Cut Your Own Hair in Layers with Bangs

Step 1: Gather the Necessary Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair completely before you start cutting. It's important to have clean and dry hair so you can see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference. Use the comb to create a straight and clean parting.

Step 4: Clip the Top Section of Your Hair

Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section. This will help you work on one section at a time and prevent any accidental cuts.

Step 5: Cut the Bottom Section in Layers

Take a small section of your hair from the bottom layer and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut the ends of the hair in a straight line. Repeat this process for the rest of the sections in the bottom layer.

Step 6: Release the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight. Use the bottom layer as a guide and cut the top layer in the same way. Make sure to blend the top and bottom layers together to create a seamless look.

Step 7: Cut Your Bangs

Comb your bangs straight down and cut them to your desired length. If you want side-swept bangs, comb them to the side and cut them at an angle.

Step 8: Check Your Hair

Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even and balanced. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair in Layers with Bangs

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it's difficult to manage.
  • Watch tutorial videos for additional guidance and inspiration.

FAQs

Is it easy to cut your own hair in layers with bangs?

It may take some practice to get it right, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can cut their own hair in layers with bangs.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and shape.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Own Hair in Layers with Bangs

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and shape. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim any split ends regularly and visit a professional stylist for more complex cuts.
