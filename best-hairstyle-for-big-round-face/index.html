Best Hairstyle For Big Round Face Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your big round face? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide you with the best hairstyles that will complement your facial features and enhance your overall appearance.

Description

Having a big, round face can be both a blessing and a curse. While it gives you a youthful and charming look, it can also make it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and slimness. A good hairstyle can help to elongate your face and make it look more oval, which is the ideal shape for a face.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to determine your face shape accurately. To do this, pull your hair back and study the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is round, your cheeks are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is soft and rounded. Once you have identified your face shape, it's time to choose the best hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Search for hairstyles that complement your features
Step 3: Choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and slimness
Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist for further advice and recommendations

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's essential to avoid styles that add width and volume to your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head
  • Choose hairstyles with layers that frame your face
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin level
  • Consider side-swept bangs that create angles and add definition to your face
  • Experiment with different parts to create the illusion of length

FAQ

Q: Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?
A: Yes, you can wear short hair if you have a round face. However, it's essential to choose a style that adds height and volume on top, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.

Q: Can I wear a middle part if I have a round face?
A: It's best to avoid a middle part if you have a round face as it can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part that creates angles and adds definition to your face.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it's crucial to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and shiny locks. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and tangle-free
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth

Now that you know the best hairstyle for a big round face, it's time to experiment and find the perfect look that suits your personality and style. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful, regardless of your face shape.
