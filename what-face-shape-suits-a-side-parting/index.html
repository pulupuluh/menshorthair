What Face Shape Suits A Side Parting

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be tricky, especially if you have a specific face shape. One hairstyle that has stood the test of time is the side parting. In this article, we will discuss what face shape suits a side parting and how you can achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Side Part Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Side Part
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description:

The side parting is a classic hairstyle that can look great on anyone, but it is particularly flattering on certain face shapes. The side parting involves separating your hair to one side of your head, creating a sleek and sophisticated look. This hairstyle can be achieved with any hair length, from short to long, and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Side Part Your Hair:

The first step in achieving the perfect side part is to choose which side of your head you want your hair to fall on. This will depend on your face shape, which we will discuss in more detail below. Once you have decided which side you want your hair to fall on, use a comb to create a straight line from the front of your hairline to the back of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.
  3. Blow-dry your hair until it is about 80% dry.
  4. Using a comb, create a straight line from the front of your hairline to the back of your head.
  5. Use a hair clip to hold the hair on the side you are not parting in place.
  6. Comb the hair on the side you are parting over to the other side of your head.
  7. Use a hairdryer and a round brush to style your hair as desired.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Part:

Maintaining a side part can be easy with a few simple tips. First, make sure to use a comb or your fingers to keep your hair in place throughout the day. Second, use a hairdryer and a round brush to style your hair when it is wet to create a sleek and polished look. Lastly, use a hairspray that provides a strong hold to keep your style in place all day long.

FAQs:

Q: What face shape suits a side parting?

A: A side parting is particularly flattering on those with oval, round, or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I achieve a side part with short hair?

A: Yes, a side part can be achieved with any hair length.

Q: How do I know which side to part my hair on?

A: The side you choose to part your hair on will depend on your face shape. See the section below for more information.

Hair Care Tips:

Keeping your hair healthy is essential when it comes to achieving the perfect side part. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what face shape suits a side parting, you can confidently rock this classic hairstyle. With the right styling tools and a few simple tips, you can achieve a sleek and sophisticated look that will turn heads wherever you go.
