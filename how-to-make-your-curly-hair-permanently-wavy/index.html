How To Make Your Curly Hair Permanently Wavy

Written by Robby Jul 26, 2023 · 8 min read

Do you have curly hair that you wish was wavy instead? You’re not alone! Many people with curly hair want to achieve a more relaxed, wavy look. In this article, we’ll go over how to make your curly hair permanently wavy. We’ll cover everything from the basics to advanced techniques, so you can find the best method for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into how to make your curly hair permanently wavy, let’s first understand the difference between curly and wavy hair. Curly hair has a tight, spiral shape, while wavy hair has a looser, more relaxed shape. So, to make your curly hair wavy, we need to loosen the curls and create a more relaxed pattern.

How to

There are a few different methods you can use to make your curly hair permanently wavy. We’ll go over some of the most popular ones below:

Chemical Treatments

One of the most popular methods for making curly hair permanently wavy is through chemical treatments. These treatments typically use a chemical relaxer or a perm solution to break down the protein bonds in your hair and create a new, wavy pattern. It’s important to note that these treatments can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly, so it’s best to seek out a professional stylist.

Curling Irons or Flat Irons

Another method for making curly hair wavy is by using a curling iron or flat iron. This method involves using heat to reshape your curls into waves. It’s important to use a heat protectant spray and to not use the iron at too high of a temperature, as this can damage your hair.

Braids or Twists

A more natural and gentle way to make your curly hair wavy is by braiding or twisting it while it’s damp. This method involves dividing your hair into sections and braiding or twisting each section. You can then leave the braids or twists in overnight and unravel them in the morning to reveal your new wavy texture.

Step by Step

If you want to try the braids or twists method, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it’s damp, but not soaking wet.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many waves you want.
  4. Braid or twist each section, starting at the root and working your way down to the end.
  5. Secure the end of each braid or twist with a hair tie or clip.
  6. Leave the braids or twists in overnight.
  7. Unravel the braids or twists in the morning and finger-comb your hair to separate the waves.
  8. Apply a lightweight hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to make your curly hair permanently wavy:

  • Be gentle with your hair, as curly hair is more fragile than straight hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair mask or deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I make my curly hair wavy without using heat or chemicals?

Yes! The braids or twists method is a natural and gentle way to make your curly hair wavy without using heat or chemicals. You can also try using a styling mousse or sea salt spray to enhance your natural waves.

Will making my curly hair wavy damage it?

It depends on the method you use and how often you do it. Chemical treatments and excessive heat can damage your hair, so it’s best to use these methods sparingly and to seek out a professional stylist. The braids or twists method is a more natural and gentle way to make your hair wavy without causing damage.

Hair Care

No matter which method you choose to make your curly hair permanently wavy, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and moisturized. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment.
  • Avoid using hot tools or excessive heat on your hair.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid tangles and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your curly hair permanently wavy and achieve the relaxed, effortless look you’ve always wanted. Remember to be gentle with your hair and to take care of it to keep it healthy and beautiful.
