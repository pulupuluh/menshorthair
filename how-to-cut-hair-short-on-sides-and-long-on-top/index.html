How To Cut Hair Short On Sides And Long On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, then cutting your hair short on the sides and long on the top might be the perfect solution for you. This style is trendy, versatile, and it works well with various face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut hair short on sides and long on top step by step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Cut Hair Short on Sides and Long on Top

Description

The short on sides and long on top haircut is also known as the undercut or disconnected haircut. This style features a shorter length on the sides and a longer length on the top, creating a contrast between the two sections. The hair on top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, depending on your preference and hair type.

How to Cut Hair Short on Sides and Long on Top

To achieve a short on sides and long on top haircut, you'll need to follow these steps:

1. Gather your tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the necessary tools, such as scissors, clippers, comb, and mirror. You may also need a spray bottle with water to dampen your hair.

2. Determine the length

Decide how short you want the sides to be and how long you want the top to be. You can use a clipper guard to achieve a uniform length on the sides or use scissors for a more textured look.

3. Create a guideline

Use your clippers or scissors to create a guideline around the sides and back of your head. This will help you maintain a consistent length and avoid any unevenness.

4. Cut the sides

Start cutting the hair on the sides, following the guideline you created earlier. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. You can use different clipper guards or scissors to create a fade or taper, depending on your preference.

5. Blend the sides and top

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top. This will create a smooth transition between the two sections and avoid any harsh lines.

6. Cut the top

Trim the hair on the top to the desired length and style it as you like. You can use scissors to create layers or texture, or use a razor for a more edgy look.

7. Final touches

Check your hair in the mirror and make any final adjustments. You can also use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to enhance the texture and hold of your hair.

Step by Step

If you're still unsure about how to cut hair short on sides and long on top, here's a detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any products. You can use a shampoo and conditioner to ensure your hair is soft and manageable.

Step 2: Dampen your hair

Spray your hair with water to dampen it. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 3: Create a parting

Use a comb to create a parting on your hair. You can choose to have a side part, middle part, or no part at all.

Step 4: Clip the sides

Use a clipper with a guard to cut the hair on the sides. Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create a fade or taper.

Step 5: Cut the back

Use the same clipper and guard to cut the hair on the back of your head. Make sure you follow the natural curve of your head to avoid any unevenness.

Step 6: Blend the sides and back

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, creating a gradual transition between the two sections.

Step 7: Cut the top

Use scissors to cut the hair on the top to the desired length. You can choose to leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference.

Step 8: Create texture

Use scissors or a razor to create texture on the hair on top. This will give it a more natural and relaxed look.

Step 9: Style your hair

Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair and style it as you like. You can use a comb, your fingers, or a brush to create different looks.

Step 10: Finish with hairspray

Use a hairspray to set your hair in place and give it extra hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short on sides and long on top haircut:

  • Start with a longer length on the top and gradually cut it shorter until you reach the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a more textured look.
  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • Consider getting your hair cut by a professional stylist for a more precise and polished finish.

FAQ

Q: Will a short on sides and long on top haircut work for me?

A: This style works well with various face shapes and hair types, so it's worth trying if you're looking for a modern and versatile haircut. However, it's always best to consult with a stylist to determine the best haircut for your specific features and preferences.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut?

A: The frequency of haircuts depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Q: How do I maintain my short on sides and long on top haircut?

A: To maintain your haircut, you should regularly wash and condition your hair, use a styling product to enhance the texture and hold, and get regular trims to avoid any split ends or breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, you should follow a regular hair care routine, including:

  • Washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoiding heat styling tools and excessive brushing to prevent damage.
  • Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy a stylish and trendy short on sides and long on top haircut that looks and feels great.
