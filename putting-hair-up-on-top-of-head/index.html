Putting Hair Up On Top Of Head A How To Guide

Written by Petter Jun 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a simple and stylish way to keep your hair out of your face, putting it up on top of your head is a great option. Not only does it create a sleek and polished look, but it's also practical and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of putting your hair up on top of your head, as well as provide tips and tricks for making it look its best.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Put Hair Up on Top of Head

Putting your hair up on top of your head is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Here's a quick overview of what you'll need to do:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  5. Smooth down any flyaways or loose strands with hairspray or gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to try putting your hair up on top of your head? Follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start by brushing your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to gently remove any tangles or knots, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail. Use your hands or a brush to pull your hair up to the crown of your head. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself. Hold the end of the ponytail with one hand and use the other hand to wrap the hair around itself in a tight spiral. Keep twisting until the hair forms a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie. Use bobby pins to hold the bun in place, or use a hair tie to secure it if you prefer a looser style.
  5. Smooth down any flyaways or loose strands. Use a small amount of hairspray or gel to tame any frizz or stray hairs. Smooth down the hair around your face to create a sleek, polished look.

Tips for Putting Hair Up on Top of Head

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of putting your hair up on top of your head:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Add volume to your bun by teasing your hair at the crown of your head before putting it up.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a braided bun, to keep things interesting.
  • Use hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.

FAQs

Here are a few common questions and answers about putting hair up on top of your head:

Q: Will putting my hair up on top of my head damage it?

A: No, putting your hair up in a bun or ponytail is a safe and gentle way to style it. Just be sure not to pull your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage or damage over time.

Q: How long will my hair stay up on top of my head?

A: This depends on a few factors, such as how thick your hair is and how you've secured the bun or ponytail. Using bobby pins or a hair tie can help keep your hair up for longer periods of time.

Q: Can I put my hair up on top of my head if I have bangs?

A: Yes, you can still put your hair up even if you have bangs. Simply use bobby pins to secure your bangs back away from your face.

Hair Care for Putting Hair Up on Top of Head

Putting your hair up on top of your head can be a great way to keep it out of your face and off your neck, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for hair care when putting your hair up:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair nourished and moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when putting it up, as this can cause breakage or damage.
  • Give your hair a break from being up every once in a while to prevent stress on your scalp and hair follicles.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Now that you know how to put your hair up on top of your head, you can enjoy a stylish and practical hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're heading to work, running errands, or going out with friends, this simple and easy style will keep your hair out of your face and looking great.
