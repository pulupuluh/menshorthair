Haircuts for Long Oval Faces Female

If you have a long oval face, you know how important it is to choose the right haircut that flatters your facial features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long oval faces female, how to style them, and hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a longer length and a narrower width. The cheekbones are usually high, and the forehead is slightly wider than the chin. The goal of a haircut for a long oval face is to add width to the face by creating volume and fullness around the cheeks and jawline.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a long oval face, it's essential to consider the length and texture of your hair. Here are some haircut options to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add volume and movement to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. The layers should start at the chin or below to create width around the face.

2. Bob

A bob haircut is a versatile option for a long oval face. The length of the bob should be at or above the chin to create width around the face. Adding layers to a bob can give it more texture and fullness.

3. Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can break up the length of a long oval face and add width to the forehead. The bangs should be long and tapered, starting at the hairline and blending into the rest of the hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to create a long-layered haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide the hair into sections.
  3. Cut the hair in long, vertical sections, starting at the back of the head.
  4. Create layers by cutting the hair at an angle, starting at the chin or below.
  5. Blend the layers by using a razor or scissors.
  6. Style the hair using a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and movement.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a long oval face:

  • Avoid center parts as they can elongate the face.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and fullness around the cheeks and jawline.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.
  • Use volumizing products to give the hair more body and thickness.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut with a long oval face. Opt for a pixie cut with longer layers on top to add volume and fullness to the hair.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a long oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a long oval face. Use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair or add volume to the roots to create a more textured look.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain healthy locks. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
