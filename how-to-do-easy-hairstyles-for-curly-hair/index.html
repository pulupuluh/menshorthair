How To Do Easy Hairstyles For Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch things up but don't know where to start? If you have curly hair, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to do easy hairstyles for curly hair that you can do at home without the help of a stylist. Whether you have long or short curly hair, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Let's get started!

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's beautiful and unique. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. That's why we've put together this guide to help you achieve easy hairstyles for curly hair. These hairstyles are perfect for those who want to switch things up without spending hours in front of the mirror.

How to

Before we get started, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls. Once your hair is dry, you can begin styling.

1. The Half-Up, Half-Down Style

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still show off their curls. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back.
  2. Secure the section with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Fluff out the curls to create volume.

You can leave the rest of your hair down or pull it back into a low ponytail for a more polished look.

2. The Messy Bun

If you're in a rush and don't have time to style your hair, the messy bun is the perfect solution. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a casual, effortless look.

3. The Side Braid

The side braid is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and braid it.
  2. Secure the braid with a hair tie.
  3. Pull the braid to the side and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Fluff out the curls to create volume.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add a little bit of flair to their look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving easy hairstyles for curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose which hairstyle you want to try.
  3. Follow the steps outlined above for your chosen hairstyle.
  4. Use bobby pins and hair ties to secure your hair in place.
  5. Fluff out your curls to create volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.
  • Use bobby pins and hair ties to secure your hair in place.
  • Fluff out your curls to create volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for curly hair:

1. Can I achieve these hairstyles with short curly hair?

Yes! These hairstyles work for both long and short curly hair.

2. How do I prevent frizz?

Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.

3. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair.

With these tips and easy hairstyles for curly hair, you can achieve a beautiful, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. Give them a try and see which one works best for you!
