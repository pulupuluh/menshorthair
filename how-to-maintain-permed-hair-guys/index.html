How To Maintain Permed Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Perming hair is a popular way for men to add texture, volume, and style to their hair. However, many men struggle with maintaining their permed hair, as it can be prone to damage and breakage. In this guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about how to maintain permed hair guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

How to Maintain Permed Hair Guys

Maintaining permed hair for guys requires a bit of effort and care. Here are some essential steps that you can follow to maintain your perm and keep it looking fresh:

1. Use the Right Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is specifically designed for permed hair. These shampoos contain gentle ingredients that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Avoid using shampoos that contain sulfates or alcohol, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your permed hair is essential to keep it healthy and moisturized. Use a conditioner that is formulated for permed hair, and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Avoid Heat

Permed hair is already prone to damage, so avoid using heat styling tools such as blow dryers or flat irons. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Don't Overwash

Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to damage. Try to limit your hair washing to two or three times a week, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

5. Protect Your Hair

Protect your permed hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can cause damage. Wear a hat or use a scarf when you're outside, and avoid swimming in chlorinated water without wearing a swim cap.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to maintain permed hair guys:

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Wet your hair and apply a small amount of shampoo. Massage your scalp gently, and rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a small amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it in for a few minutes, and rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Air Dry

Avoid using heat styling tools to dry your hair. Instead, let it air dry naturally, or use a diffuser on your blow dryer.

Tips for Maintaining Permed Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips for maintaining permed hair guys:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can damage your permed hair.

2. Use Hair Masks

Hair masks can provide deep conditioning and nourishment to your permed hair. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles can put a strain on your permed hair and cause breakage. Avoid tight braids, ponytails, or other hairstyles that pull on your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my permed hair?

A: It's best to wash your permed hair two or three times a week. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Q: Can I color my permed hair?

A: Yes, but it's best to wait at least two weeks after perming your hair before coloring it. Be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your color.

Q: Can I use hair gel or other styling products on my permed hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to choose products that are specifically designed for permed hair. Avoid products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as they can damage your hair.

Hair Care for Permed Hair Guys

In addition to following the steps and tips above, here are some other things you can do to take care of your permed hair:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is essential for overall hair health. Aim for seven to eight hours of sleep per night.

3. Drink Plenty of Water

Staying hydrated can help keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Aim for at least eight glasses of water per day.

Following these steps and tips can help you maintain your permed hair and keep it looking fresh and healthy. With a bit of effort and care, you can enjoy your permed hair for months to come.
