Number 2 Haircut Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a classic and low-maintenance haircut, the number 2 haircut short back and sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a timeless style that never goes out of fashion and is suitable for all hair types and face shapes. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to get a number 2 haircut short back and sides, and everything else you need to know about this style.

Description

The number 2 haircut short back and sides is a simple and clean-cut style that involves leaving two-eighths of an inch of hair on the top, and trimming the sides and back with a number 2 clipper guard. This creates a short and neat look that is easy to maintain and style.

How to Get a Number 2 Haircut Short Back and Sides

Getting a number 2 haircut short back and sides is easy and can be done at home or by a professional stylist. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting a haircut, it's important to wash your hair to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Use a shampoo that suits your hair type and rinse thoroughly with warm water. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to create a parting on the top of your head, from the front to the back. This will help you to section your hair and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Attach a number 2 clipper guard to your clippers and start trimming the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a mirror to check that the length is even on both sides. Remember to trim behind your ears, and take your time to ensure a clean and neat finish.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using a pair of scissors, trim the top of your hair to two-eighths of an inch. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting small sections at a time. You can also use clippers with a number 2 guard to trim the top, but be careful not to cut too much off.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair on the sides and top using scissors or clippers with a shorter guard. This will create a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired using a hair product that suits your hair type. You can opt for a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect number 2 haircut short back and sides:

  • Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head while trimming.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter cut.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your hair, seek the help of a professional stylist.
  • Use a comb to comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay while cutting.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a number 2 haircut short back and sides?

A number 2 haircut short back and sides is a low-maintenance style that can last for several weeks. However, the frequency of haircuts depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length.

Can I style my number 2 haircut short back and sides differently?

Yes, you can style your number 2 haircut short back and sides in different ways using different hair products. Experiment with different textures and finishes to find a look that suits you.

Hair Care

To keep your number 2 haircut short back and sides looking sharp, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance.

Getting a number 2 haircut short back and sides is a simple and classic style that is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a clean and neat look that will last for weeks.
