How To Relax Coarse Grey Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Coarse grey hair can be difficult to manage, but there are ways to relax it and make it more manageable. In this article, we’ll show you how to relax coarse grey hair using a step-by-step approach. We’ll also provide tips and advice for maintaining your hair after it’s been relaxed.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Relax Coarse Grey Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Relaxed Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Products for Relaxed Hair

How to Relax Coarse Grey Hair

Relaxing grey hair requires the use of a chemical relaxer that breaks down the protein bonds in the hair. This process softens the hair and makes it more manageable. It’s important to note that relaxing your hair can be damaging and should only be done by a professional stylist.

Before relaxing your hair, it’s important to prepare it by washing and conditioning it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt and oils that may interfere with the relaxing process. It’s also important to protect your scalp and skin by applying petroleum jelly or a protective cream around your hairline and ears.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Section your hair: Divide your hair into four sections using hair clips.

2. Apply the relaxer: Starting at the back of your head, apply the relaxer to each section of hair using a brush or applicator. Be sure to follow the instructions on the relaxer carefully and avoid getting it on your scalp or skin.

3. Wait: Leave the relaxer on your hair for the amount of time specified in the instructions. This typically ranges from 10-20 minutes.

4. Rinse: Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer.

5. Neutralize: Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the chemical process. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes, then rinse it out.

6. Condition: Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse it out thoroughly.

7. Style: Blow-dry your hair and style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Relaxed Hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to relaxed hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and promote healthy growth.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQ:

Q: How often should I relax my hair?

A: It’s recommended to wait at least 8-10 weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I relax my hair at home?

A: It’s not recommended to relax your hair at home, as it can be dangerous and result in damage to your hair and scalp. Always have a professional stylist perform this service.

Hair Care Products for Relaxed Hair:

- Mizani Butter Blend Relaxer

- Design Essentials Strengthening Therapy System

- Moroccanoil Moisture Repair Shampoo and Conditioner

Relaxing your coarse grey hair can be a great way to make it more manageable and easy to style. By following these steps and using the right products, you can achieve smooth, healthy-looking hair that you’ll love.
