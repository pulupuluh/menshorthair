What Are The Best Hair Accessories For 2023

Written by Petter Mar 19, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re looking to elevate your hairstyle and add some personality to your look, hair accessories are the way to go. From barrettes to headbands, there are so many different types of hair accessories to choose from. In this article, we’ll go over the best hair accessories for 2023.

How to Choose the Right Hair Accessories

When it comes to choosing the right hair accessories, it’s important to consider your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Here are some tips:

  • If you have thick hair, opt for larger accessories that can hold your hair in place.
  • If you have fine hair, choose smaller accessories that won’t weigh your hair down.
  • If you have a round face, avoid headbands that sit too high on your forehead.
  • If you have a square face, opt for softer, more rounded accessories.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, choose accessories that sit at the crown of your head.
  • If you have an oval face, you can wear just about any type of accessory.

Headbands

Headbands are a classic hair accessory that never go out of style. They’re perfect for keeping your hair out of your face and adding some flair to your look. There are so many different types of headbands to choose from, including:

  • Thin headbands
  • Thick headbands
  • Braided headbands
  • Knotted headbands
  • Printed headbands

Headbands are great for all hair types, but they’re especially useful for those with bangs or shorter hair.

Scrunchies

Scrunchies are making a comeback, and for good reason. They’re a great way to add some volume and texture to your hair, and they’re gentle on your strands. Scrunchies come in all different colors and patterns, so you’re sure to find one that matches your style.

Scrunchies are perfect for those with curly or wavy hair, as they won’t flatten your curls like traditional hair ties can.

Barrettes

Barrettes are a versatile hair accessory that can be used to hold back your bangs, add some sparkle to your updo, or create a half-up, half-down style. There are so many different types of barrettes to choose from, including:

  • Classic barrettes
  • Statement barrettes
  • Minimalist barrettes
  • Crystal barrettes
  • Pearl barrettes

Barrettes work well for all hair types, but they’re especially useful for those with thin hair who want to add some volume to their hairstyles.

Hair Pins

Hair pins are a great way to add some detail to your updo or half-up, half-down style. They come in all different shapes and sizes, from simple bobby pins to intricate jeweled pins. Hair pins are perfect for those with thick or long hair, as they can hold a lot of hair in place.

Claw Clips

Claw clips are a quick and easy way to create an updo or half-up, half-down style. They come in all different sizes and colors, so you’re sure to find one that matches your style. Claw clips work well for all hair types, but they’re especially useful for those with thick or curly hair.

Tiaras

Tiaras aren’t just for brides and princesses. They’re a fun and playful way to add some sparkle to your look. Tiaras come in all different shapes and sizes, from dainty and delicate to bold and statement-making. Tiaras work well for all hair types, but they’re especially useful for those with long or curly hair.

Hats

Hats are a great way to accessorize your hair while also keeping your head warm. There are so many different types of hats to choose from, including:

  • Beanies
  • Fedoras
  • Berets
  • Caps

Hats are perfect for those with any hair type, but they’re especially useful for those with short hair who want to add some height to their hairstyles.

Scarves

Scarves are a versatile hair accessory that can be used in a variety of ways. You can tie a scarf around your head like a headband, use it to tie your hair back in a ponytail or braid, or wrap it around your bun for a chic look. Scarves come in all different colors and patterns, so you’re sure to find one that matches your style.

FAQs

Q: Can hair accessories damage my hair?

A: Some hair accessories, like tight headbands or elastics, can cause damage to your hair if worn too often. It’s important to choose accessories that are gentle on your hair and avoid wearing them for long periods of time.

Q: Can I wear hair accessories with any hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can wear hair accessories with any hairstyle. However, some accessories work better with certain hair types or lengths. For example, headbands are great for those with bangs or shorter hair, while barrettes work well for those with thin hair.

Hair Care Tips

Using hair accessories is a fun and easy way to elevate your look, but it’s important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and the right hair accessories, you’ll be able to create a variety of stylish and fun hairstyles. Happy accessorizing!
