Best Face Shape for Undercut

The undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can suit almost any face shape, but some face shapes look more flattering than others. The best face shape for an undercut is a diamond, oval, or square face shape.

Diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and a pointed chin. It is characterized by high cheekbones, which make the face look angular. The undercut can help accentuate the cheekbones and create a more defined jawline.

Oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off any hairstyle. The oval face shape has a balanced proportion, with a slightly wider forehead than the jawline. The undercut can add more texture and volume to the hair, making it look more stylish.

Square face shape has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The undercut can help soften the angular features and create a more balanced look.

However, if you have a round or heart-shaped face, an undercut may not be the best choice for you. A round face has a circular shape, and the undercut can make the face look more round. On the other hand, a heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin, and the undercut can make the forehead look more prominent.

How to Style an Undercut

Styling an undercut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are the steps to style an undercut:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a hair styling product of your choice, such as a hair gel, wax, or pomade.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a hairbrush or a comb to create the desired style.
  4. If you want a slicked-back look, use a hair gel or pomade and comb your hair back. If you want a messy look, use a hair wax and tousle your hair with your fingers.
  5. Finish off the look with a hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get an Undercut

If you have decided to get an undercut, here is a step-by-step guide on how to get it:

  1. Choose the right undercut style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Show a picture of the undercut style to your hairstylist for reference.
  3. Section your hair and clip the top part of your hair away from the bottom part.
  4. Use a hair clipper to shave the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Blend the shaved part with the top part of your hair using scissors or a razor.
  6. Style your hair using a hair styling product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut

Maintaining an undercut is easy, but it requires regular trimming to keep the style fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim the sides and back of your hair.
  • Use a hair styling product that suits your hair type to achieve the desired style.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent dandruff and maintain healthy hair.
  • If you have long hair on top, use a hair tie or a hair clip to keep it away from your face.
  • Avoid using too much hair styling product as it can make your hair look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I get an undercut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get an undercut if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a hair straightener to achieve the desired style.

Q: Is an undercut suitable for men or women?

A: An undercut is a unisex hairstyle and can be worn by both men and women.

Q: How long does an undercut take to grow out?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, it takes 3-6 months for an undercut to grow out.

Hair Care for Undercut

Having an undercut requires proper hair care to maintain healthy hair. Here are some hair care tips for an undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair straighteners or curling irons.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best face shape for an undercut and how to style it, you can confidently rock this versatile hairstyle. Remember to maintain your undercut regularly and follow proper hair care to keep your hair healthy and stylish.
