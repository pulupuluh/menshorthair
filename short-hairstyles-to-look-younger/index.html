Short Hairstyles To Look Younger

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of feeling like your hair makes you look older than you are? Short hairstyles can be a great way to take years off your appearance. In this article, we will give you tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle to make you look younger.

Description

Short hairstyles have been popular for years, and for good reason. Not only are they versatile and easy to maintain, but they can also make you look younger. By choosing the right short hairstyle, you can highlight your best features and minimize the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

Choosing the right short hairstyle can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you choose the best style for you:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a big role in determining which short hairstyle will look best on you. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to choose a style that adds height to your crown and length to your face.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in which short hairstyle you choose. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, on the other hand, you may want to choose a style that thins out your hair.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in which short hairstyle you choose. If you are constantly on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style. If you have more time to style your hair, you may want to choose a more intricate style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Short Hairstyles

Here are some step-by-step guides on how to achieve some popular short hairstyles:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for those looking to make a bold statement. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  2. Use a styling gel to create texture and hold.
  3. Using a comb and scissors, cut your hair into short, choppy layers.
  4. Finish the look by using a hair wax to add definition and shine.

The Bob

The bob is a versatile short hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  2. Using a comb and scissors, cut your hair into a chin-length bob.
  3. Use a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Finish the look by using a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still requires some upkeep. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your short hair looking fresh and stylish. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. To protect your hair from heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short hairstyles that add height to your crown and length to your face can be flattering for round faces. Examples include the pixie cut and the asymmetrical bob.

What short hairstyles are best for fine hair?

Short hairstyles that add volume and texture can be flattering for fine hair. Examples include the layered bob and the shaggy pixie cut.

Can short hair make you look younger?

Yes, short hair can make you look younger by highlighting your best features and minimizing the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use products that are designed for your hair type and texture. Here are some general tips for keeping your hair healthy and shiny:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep your scalp healthy and your hair shiny. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. To avoid heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

A deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Aim to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF.

Short hairstyles can be a great way to take years off your appearance. By choosing the right short hairstyle and taking care of your hair, you can look younger and more vibrant than ever before.
