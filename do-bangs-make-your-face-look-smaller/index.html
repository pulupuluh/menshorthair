Do Bangs Make Your Face Look Smaller

Description

Many people wonder whether bangs make their faces look smaller or not. The answer is yes, they can make your face look smaller, but it depends on the type of bangs you choose. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. They can also help to balance out your face shape and emphasize your best features. However, the wrong type of bangs can actually make your face look larger or unbalanced.

How To

If you want to make your face look smaller with bangs, you need to choose the right style for your face shape. Here are some tips:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should choose bangs that are longer at the sides than in the middle. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs, as these can make your face look wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should choose bangs that are soft and wispy. These will help to soften the angles of your face and make it look less boxy. Avoid thick, heavy bangs that can make your face look even more angular.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should choose bangs that are longer at the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to balance out your forehead and make your face look more proportional. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can overwhelm your face.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pretty much pull off any type of bangs. However, if you want to make your face look smaller, you should choose bangs that are shorter in the middle and longer at the sides. This will help to balance out your face and make it appear more symmetrical.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for getting bangs:

  1. Decide on the type of bangs you want based on your face shape.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the type of bangs you want.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs when your hair is dry.
  4. Make sure your hairstylist cuts your bangs slowly and gradually, so you don't end up with bangs that are too short.
  5. Style your bangs using a round brush and a blow dryer, or flat iron them for a sleek look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your face look smaller with bangs:

  • Choose bangs that are slightly off-center to create a diagonal line that slims your face.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face look wider.
  • Consider getting side-swept bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair.
  • Experiment with different types of bangs to find the style that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can bangs make my face look larger?

A: Yes, if you choose the wrong type of bangs for your face shape, they can actually make your face look larger or unbalanced.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with curly hair, but you'll need to choose a style that works with your natural texture. Talk to your hairstylist about the best type of bangs for your curly hair.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your bangs looking great:

  • Wash your bangs every day or every other day to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place.

In conclusion, bangs can make your face look smaller if you choose the right style for your face shape. Follow the tips and guidelines outlined above to get the perfect bangs for your face. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and flattering.
