How To Relax Your Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Relaxing your hair can give you a sleeker, more manageable look. While traditionally associated with women, men can also enjoy the benefits of relaxed hair. If you're interested in learning how to relax your hair for guys, keep reading for a step-by-step guide.

Description

Relaxing your hair involves using chemicals to break down the bonds that give your hair its natural texture. This process can make your hair more manageable and easier to style. However, it's important to note that relaxing can also damage your hair if it's not done correctly or if you don't take care of your hair afterwards.

How to Relax Your Hair for Guys

The process of relaxing your hair involves applying a chemical relaxer to your hair. This is best done by a professional stylist, but it's possible to do it at home if you're comfortable with the process. Here's what you'll need:

  • Relaxer kit
  • Gloves
  • Applicator brush
  • Plastic cap
  • Neutralizing shampoo
  • Conditioner

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.


2. Put on your gloves and mix the relaxer according to the instructions on the kit. It's important to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.


3. Divide your hair into four sections using clips. Begin at the nape of your neck and apply the relaxer to each section, making sure to cover all of your hair.


4. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended amount of time, usually between 10 and 20 minutes. Do not exceed the recommended time.


5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until all of the relaxer is removed. Apply the neutralizing shampoo and massage it into your hair for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly.


6. Apply conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 5 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.


Tips for Relaxing Your Hair

- Always follow the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully.

- Avoid relaxing your hair too frequently, as this can damage your hair.

- If you're not comfortable relaxing your hair at home, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

- Be sure to use a neutralizing shampoo after relaxing to prevent any damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I relax my hair if it's already damaged?

A: It's best to avoid relaxing your hair if it's already damaged. Wait until your hair is healthy before attempting to relax it.


Q: How often should I relax my hair?

A: It's best to wait at least 8 weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid damaging your hair.


Q: Can I color my hair after relaxing it?

A: It's best to wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair after relaxing it.

Hair Care After Relaxing

After relaxing your hair, it's important to take extra care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to relax your hair for guys, you can enjoy a sleeker, more manageable hairstyle. Just be sure to take care of your hair properly to avoid damage and maintain healthy hair.
