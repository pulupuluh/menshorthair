Does Shoulder Length Hair Suit Oval Faces

Are you considering getting a shoulder length haircut but unsure if it will suit your oval face? Look no further as we explore whether this hairstyle is a match made in heaven for oval faces.

Description

Oval-shaped faces are considered one of the most versatile face shapes and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for many women as it frames the face and adds volume and texture without being too short or too long. However, not every hairstyle suits every face shape, and it's important to know what works best for you.

How to

The key to finding the perfect shoulder length hairstyle is to look for one that complements your face shape. Oval faces are symmetrical and have a slightly rounded chin and forehead with high cheekbones. This means that most hairstyles will suit this face shape, but some are more flattering than others.

Step by step

To determine whether shoulder length hair suits your oval face, follow these steps:

  1. Measure your face length and width.
  2. Divide your face length by your face width.
  3. If the result is 1.5 or lower, you have an oval face.
  4. Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle.
  5. Look for styles that add volume and texture to your hair.
  6. Avoid styles that elongate your face or make it appear too narrow.

Tips

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle for an oval face, consider the following tips:

  • Experiment with layers to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider a side-parted style to balance out your features.
  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face appear too long.
  • Try out different hairstyles with virtual makeover tools before committing to a cut.

FAQ

Q: Can oval faces pull off any hairstyle?

A: Oval faces are versatile and can pull off most hairstyles, but some are more flattering than others.

Q: What shoulder length hairstyles suit oval faces?

A: Shoulder length hairstyles with layers, side-parted styles, and textured cuts are flattering for oval faces.

Q: Should I consider my hair texture when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle?

A: Yes, your hair texture and thickness should be considered when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle as it can affect the overall look of the cut.

Hair Care

Now that you've found the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and polished. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and protecting your hair from heat damage are all important steps in maintaining your new haircut.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair can suit oval faces with the right cut and styling. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle that works best for you.
