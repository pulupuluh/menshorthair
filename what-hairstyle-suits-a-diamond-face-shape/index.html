What Hairstyle Suits A Diamond Face Shape

Written by Robby May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our hair care blog where we discuss everything about hair care, styling, and trends. Today, we are going to talk about what hairstyle suits a diamond face shape. If you have a diamond face shape, you have high cheekbones, a narrow forehead, and a pointed chin. It is a unique face shape that requires a specific hairstyle to accentuate your features.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we mentioned earlier, a diamond face shape is unique and requires a specific hairstyle to bring out the best in you. The ideal hairstyle for a diamond face shape is one that adds width to your forehead, softens your cheekbones, and narrows your chin. In this article, we will discuss different hairstyles that suit a diamond face shape.

How to

The first thing you need to do is determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and analyzing your face shape in the mirror. Once you have confirmed that you have a diamond face shape, you can choose from the following hairstyles.

Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is an excellent hairstyle for a diamond face shape. It adds volume to your hair and makes your forehead look wider. This hairstyle also draws attention away from your pointed chin, making it look less prominent.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is another great hairstyle for a diamond face shape. It adds volume to your hair and softens your cheekbones. This hairstyle also narrows your chin, creating a balanced look.

Side-Swept Bangs

If you have a diamond face shape, you can also consider side-swept bangs. They add width to your forehead and draw attention away from your narrow chin. This hairstyle also adds softness to your face, making you look more feminine and elegant.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve a short pixie cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hair.
  5. Use your fingers to style your hair into a pixie cut.

Here are the steps to achieve a layered bob:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hair.
  5. Use a razor to create layers in your hair.

Here are the steps to achieve side-swept bangs:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  4. Part your hair on the side.
  5. Use a flat iron to create a swoop towards one side of your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheekbones.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your chin look more prominent.
  • Choose hairstyles that add width to your forehead.
  • Choose hairstyles that soften your cheekbones.
  • Choose hairstyles that narrow your chin.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle suits a diamond face shape:

What celebrity has a diamond face shape?

Rihanna has a diamond face shape.

Can I have long hair with a diamond face shape?

Yes, you can have long hair with a diamond face shape. However, make sure to add layers to your hair to add volume and soften your cheekbones.

Do bangs suit a diamond face shape?

Yes, side-swept bangs suit a diamond face shape. They add width to your forehead and draw attention away from your narrow chin.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine to your hair.

We hope this article has helped you understand what hairstyle suits a diamond face shape. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead, softens your cheekbones, and narrows your chin. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one for you.
