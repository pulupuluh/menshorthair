Best Hair For Small Oval Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your small oval face? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best hair for small oval face shapes, as well as providing tips and tricks on how to style your hair to suit your face shape. Let’s dive in!

Description

A small oval face is a rare and beautiful face shape that can be quite challenging to find the perfect hairstyle for. While oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, small oval faces require a bit more attention to detail to ensure your hair enhances your features rather than overwhelms them.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair for small oval face shapes, let’s quickly go over how to determine your face shape. To do this, you’ll need a tape measure and a mirror.

  1. Measure the width of your forehead at its widest point.
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones at their widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline at its widest point.
  4. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  5. Using these measurements, determine which face shape you have based on the following chart:

Best Hair for Small Oval Face

Now that you’ve determined you have a small oval face shape, let’s discuss the best hair for small oval face shapes. Here are some styles to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for small oval faces because they add volume and movement to your hair without overwhelming your features. They also help to elongate your face, making it appear less round.

Bob

A bob is another great choice for small oval faces. It’s a classic style that’s easy to maintain and can be customized to suit your preferences. Opt for a chin-length or shoulder-length bob to enhance your features.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your face without overwhelming your features. They also help to elongate your face, making it appear less round.

Wavy Lob

A wavy lob is a great choice for small oval faces. It’s an effortless and chic style that can be dressed up or down. Opt for a subtle wave to enhance your features.

Step-by-Step Guide on Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hair for small oval face shapes, let’s go over how to style your hair to suit your face shape.

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear rounder.
  • Opt for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a wavy lob.
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for small oval faces?

The best haircut for small oval faces is one that adds volume and movement to your hair without overwhelming your features. Long layers, a bob, side-swept bangs, and a wavy lob are all great choices to consider.

Can small oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes, small oval faces can pull off short hair. However, it’s important to choose a style that suits your face shape and enhances your features.

Hair Care for Small Oval Faces

Now that you know the best hair for small oval face shapes and how to style your hair to suit your face shape, let’s discuss hair care for small oval faces. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use products that suit your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your small oval face and keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous. Happy styling!
