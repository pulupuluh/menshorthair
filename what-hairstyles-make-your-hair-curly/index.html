What Hairstyles Make Your Hair Curly

Written by Christine May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is the dream of many people, but not everyone is blessed with natural curls. Fortunately, there are ways to achieve curly hair without using heat or chemicals. One of the easiest ways is to style your hair in certain hairstyles that can make your hair curly. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyles make your hair curly, how to style your hair, and some useful tips and tricks to maintain your curly hair.

  2. Hairstyles That Make Your Hair Curly

How to Make Your Hair Curly

Before we dive into the hairstyles that can make your hair curly, let's first discuss how to make your hair curly. There are several ways to achieve curly hair without using heat or chemicals:

  • Braiding your hair
  • Twisting your hair
  • Using curling rods
  • Using flexi rods
  • Using perm rods

Each of these methods can create different types of curls, so it's important to choose the method that will give you the type of curls you want. For example, braiding your hair will create loose waves, while using perm rods will create tighter curls.

Hairstyles That Make Your Hair Curly

Now that you know how to create curly hair, let's discuss some hairstyles that can make your hair curly:

  • Bantu knots
  • Twist outs
  • Braid outs
  • Finger coils
  • Pineapple updo

These hairstyles work by creating twists or braids in your hair, which will then create curls when you take them out. Finger coils and pineapple updos work by twisting small sections of hair around your fingers or a hair pick, and then letting them dry to create curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating curly hair using the braid out method:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Braid each section of hair tightly
  5. Let your hair dry completely
  6. Take out the braids
  7. Separate the curls with your fingers
  8. Use a hair pick to lift the roots
  9. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the best curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid using heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Use a satin pillowcase or sleep cap to prevent frizz
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using heat or chemicals?

A: Yes, there are several methods you can use to create curly hair without using heat or chemicals, such as braiding, twisting, and using curling rods.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair once a week or every other week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, use a satin pillowcase or sleep cap, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, and apply a hair oil or serum to add shine.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid using heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Use a diffuser when drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz

Now that you know what hairstyles make your hair curly, it's time to try them out and see which ones work best for you. Remember to take care of your curly hair by using the right products and following a proper hair care routine.
