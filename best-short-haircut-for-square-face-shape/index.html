Best Short Haircut For Square Face Shape

Written by Robby Apr 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may feel like finding the perfect haircut is difficult. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircut for square face shape and provide you with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a square forehead. The length and width of the face are almost equal, and the cheekbones are usually prominent. The goal of a haircut for square face shape is to soften the angles and create a more oval-shaped face.

How to

The best short haircut for square face shape is a layered bob. The layers create movement and volume around the face, which softens the angles of the jawline and forehead. The bob should be just above or below the chin, and the layers should start at the cheekbones.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a comb to create a center part.

3. Section off the hair from the ears down.

4. Cut the hair to the desired length, just above or below the chin.

5. Cut the layers starting at the cheekbones.

6. Blend the layers into the rest of the hair.

7. Style the hair with a blow dryer and round brush.

8. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, as they will emphasize the squareness of your face.

- Add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

- Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I have a pixie cut with a square face shape?

A: Yes, but make sure to add volume to the crown of your head and avoid straight bangs.

Q: What about a bob with side-swept bangs?

A: This can work, but make sure the bangs are long enough to blend with the rest of the hair and soften the forehead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish haircut. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid over-washing. Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and don't forget to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for square face shape is a layered bob. Remember to soften the angles of your face, add volume to the crown of your head, and experiment with different parting styles. With proper hair care, you can rock any haircut with confidence!
