Best Hairstyle For Long Oval Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 06, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a long oval face male looking for the best hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further because we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll share the best hairstyle ideas for long oval face male that will make you look your best. Read on to find out more.

Description

Long oval face males have a face shape that is longer than it is wide. This face shape is characterized by a high forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best hairstyles for long oval face males are those that add width to the face, balance out the proportions, and draw attention away from the length of the face.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyle ideas for long oval face male, let's first determine if you have a long oval face shape. Here's how:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, typically just above your eyebrows.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Compare your measurements using the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Your face length is larger than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is larger than your jawline.
  • Round: Your face length and cheekbones are roughly equal, and your forehead and jawline are also roughly equal.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal in width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones.

Best Hairstyle Ideas for Long Oval Face Male

Now that you know your face shape, let's take a look at the best hairstyle ideas for long oval face male:

1. Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is a great option for long oval face males because it adds width to the face and draws attention away from the length. Keep the sides and back short and leave a little more length on top to create some volume.

2. Side Parted Hairstyle

A side-parted hairstyle is another great option for long oval face males. The side part creates a diagonal line that breaks up the length of the face and adds width. Keep the sides and back short and leave more length on top to create volume.

3. Textured Quiff

A textured quiff is a stylish and modern hairstyle that works well for long oval face males. The quiff adds volume to the top of the head, which balances out the proportions of the face. Use a texturizing product to add some texture and hold to the hair.

4. Slicked Back Hair

Slicked back hair is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle that can work well for long oval face males. The slicked back style adds width to the face and creates a sleek and polished look. Use a strong hold gel or pomade to keep the hair in place.

5. Messy Crop

A messy crop is a low-maintenance and stylish hairstyle that works well for long oval face males. The messy texture adds volume and creates a relaxed and casual look. Use a texturizing product to create the messy texture.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a hair product that works well for your hair type and the hairstyle you want to achieve.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to create volume and hold.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Maintain your hairstyle by getting regular haircuts and trims.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for long oval face male:

What is the best haircut for a long oval face male?

The best haircut for a long oval face male is one that adds width to the face and balances out the proportions. Short buzz cuts, side-parted hairstyles, textured quiffs, slicked back hair, and messy crops are all great options.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat, as they can make the face appear even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as they can make the face appear even narrower.

Should I consult a hairstylist before choosing a hairstyle?

Yes, it's always a good idea to consult a hairstylist before choosing a hairstyle. A hairstylist can help you determine your face shape, recommend hairstyles that flatter your face shape and hair type, and help you achieve your desired look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts and trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

So, there you have it, the best hairstyle ideas for long oval face male. Try out these hairstyles and see which one works best for you. Remember to consult a hairstylist if you're not sure which hairstyle to choose, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
