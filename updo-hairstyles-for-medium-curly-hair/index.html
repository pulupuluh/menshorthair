Updo Hairstyles For Medium Curly Hair Perfect For Any Occasion

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be tricky to style, especially when you have medium length hair. It can be challenging to find the perfect updo hairstyle that works well with your curls. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll be sharing some of the best updo hairstyles for medium curly hair that are perfect for any occasion. From weddings to proms, these hairstyles will take your curly locks to the next level.

How to Style Updo Hairstyles for Medium Curly Hair

Before we dive into the different updo hairstyles, it's essential to know how to style your curly hair. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Apply a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls or add more definition.
  • Tease the crown of your hair for added volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your updo.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Updo Hairstyles for Medium Curly Hair

1. Low Curly Bun

To achieve this hairstyle, gather your hair into a low ponytail, leaving out some strands at the front. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins. Take the front strands and twist them away from your face, securing them at the back of your head with bobby pins. Finish with hairspray.

2. Half-Up Half-Down Curly Updo

Section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. Let the bottom half of your hair hang loose. Take the top section of your hair and twist it, securing it with bobby pins at the back of your head. Use a curling iron to touch up any loose curls. Finish with hairspray.

3. Messy Curly Updo

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Tease the crown of your hair for added volume. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out some strands of hair to frame your face. Finish with hairspray.

Tips for Perfecting Updo Hairstyles for Medium Curly Hair

  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls.
  • Tease the crown of your hair for added volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your updo.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different updo hairstyles to find one that works best for you.

FAQs About Updo Hairstyles for Medium Curly Hair

1. Can I wear an updo hairstyle with medium curly hair?

Yes, you can! There are many updo hairstyles that work well with medium curly hair.

2. Do I need to use a curling iron to achieve an updo hairstyle?

It's not necessary, but it can help touch up any loose curls or add more definition.

3. How do I keep my updo hairstyle in place?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to secure your updo and hold it in place.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Curly Hair

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to style updo hairstyles for medium curly hair, it's time to experiment with different looks. Don't be afraid to try something new and find a hairstyle that works best for you. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock any updo hairstyle with confidence!
