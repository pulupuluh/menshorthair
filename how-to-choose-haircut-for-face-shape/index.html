How To Choose The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially when you have no idea where to start. The key to getting the perfect haircut lies in knowing your face shape. The right haircut can make all the difference and enhance your features, while the wrong one can do the opposite. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Before we dive into the details of how to choose a haircut for your face shape, let's briefly discuss the different face shapes. There are six main face shapes - oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and the length of your face.

How to Choose a Haircut for Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the perfect haircut. Here are some tips:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off any haircut as long as it flatters your facial features. You can try a long layered haircut or a short pixie cut.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should opt for a haircut that adds height and length to your face. A long layered cut or a side-swept bangs will look great on you.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should try a haircut that softens your jawline. A layered bob or a long wavy hairstyle will look great on you.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you should try a haircut that balances your chin and forehead. A long bob or a side-swept bangs will look great on you.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you should try a haircut that emphasizes your cheekbones. A layered pixie cut or a medium-length bob will look great on you.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you should try a haircut that adds width to your face. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a voluminous bob will look great on you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose a haircut for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research different haircuts that flatter your face shape
  3. Bring pictures to your hairstylist to show them the exact look you're going for
  4. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair
  5. Ask your hairstylist for their professional opinion

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Choose a haircut that complements your personality
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts
  • Consider your face shape and hairline when choosing a haircut
  • Take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can pull off a short haircut if you have a round face shape. Opt for a short pixie cut or a layered bob.

Q: What haircut is best for a heart face shape?

A: A long bob or a side-swept bangs will look great on a heart face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a square face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a square face shape. Just make sure to soften your jawline with layers or waves.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've chosen the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

Choosing the right haircut can be a game-changer. By following these tips, you can find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
