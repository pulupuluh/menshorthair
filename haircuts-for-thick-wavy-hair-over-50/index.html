Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Daniel May 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on haircuts for thick wavy hair over 50! If you have thick, wavy hair and are over 50, you may be wondering which haircuts are most suitable for you. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and inspiration to help you achieve a stylish and manageable haircut that complements your hair type and face shape.

Description

Thick, wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it has plenty of volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. That's why it's essential to choose a haircut that suits your hair type and lifestyle. For women over 50, finding a flattering haircut that enhances their natural beauty while reducing maintenance is a top priority.

How to

The first step in choosing a haircut is to determine your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Once you've identified your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features and balances your proportions.

For round faces, long layered haircuts that frame the face and add volume at the crown are ideal. Oval faces can pull off almost any haircut, but shoulder-length bobs and shaggy cuts are particularly flattering. Square faces can benefit from soft layers that soften the jawline, while heart-shaped faces look great with side-swept bangs that draw attention away from the forehead.

Once you've chosen a haircut that suits your face shape, it's time to consider your hair texture. Thick, wavy hair benefits from haircuts that reduce bulk and add movement. Layered haircuts, angled bobs, and shaggy cuts are all excellent choices for thick, wavy hair over 50.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Choose a haircut that flatters your features and balances your proportions

Step 3: Consider your hair texture and choose a haircut that reduces bulk and adds movement

Step 4: Consult with your hairstylist to ensure the haircut suits your lifestyle and maintenance needs

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for thick wavy hair over 50:

  • Opt for haircuts that are easy to maintain and style
  • Consider adding layers to reduce bulk and add movement
  • Avoid haircuts that require daily heat styling or excessive product use
  • Choose a haircut that complements your natural hair color and skin tone

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a short haircut if I have thick wavy hair over 50?

A: Yes! Short haircuts, such as pixie cuts and bobs, can look great on women over 50 with thick wavy hair. Just be sure to choose a length and shape that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed if I have thick wavy hair?

A: It's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on your hair length and growth rate.

Hair Care

Thick, wavy hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some hair care tips for thick wavy hair over 50:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on a daily basis
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to reduce frizz and add shine
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling and damaging your hair

We hope this guide has helped you choose a haircut that flatters your thick wavy hair over 50. Remember, the key to a great haircut is choosing a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and maintenance needs. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you.
