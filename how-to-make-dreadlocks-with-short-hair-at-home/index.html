How To Make Dreadlocks With Short Hair At Home

Written by Bardi Apr 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair and want to make dreadlocks at home, you're in the right place. Dreadlocks are a popular hairstyle that can be achieved with hair of any length. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of making dreadlocks with short hair at home. We'll cover everything from how to start your dreadlocks to how to care for them once they're done. So, let's get started!

Description

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that involves twisting, matting, and braiding hair to form long, rope-like strands. This style has been around for centuries and has been worn by people from many different cultures. Dreadlocks can be made with any hair type, but the process is slightly different for those with short hair. In this post, we'll show you how to make dreadlocks with short hair at home.

How to Make Dreadlocks with Short Hair at Home

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start making your dreadlocks, it's important to wash your hair thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt, oils, or product buildup that may be in your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo and rinse your hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll need to section your hair. Use a comb to create small, even sections of hair all over your head. The size of each section will depend on how thick you want your dreadlocks to be. The smaller the section, the thinner the dreadlock.

Step 3: Backcomb Each Section

Now it's time to start making your dreadlocks. Take one section of hair and backcomb it using a comb. Hold the section of hair taut and comb it backwards, towards your scalp. This will create tangles and knots in the hair, which will eventually form the dreadlock. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Twist Each Section

Once you've backcombed each section, it's time to twist them. Take a section of hair and twist it tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the end. Continue twisting until the hair starts to form a tight coil. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Secure the Ends

Once you've twisted each section of hair, you'll need to secure the ends. You can use a rubber band or a piece of string to tie off the end of each dreadlock. Make sure the tie is tight enough to hold the dreadlock in place, but not so tight that it cuts off circulation to the hair.

Tips

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a comb to create even sections of hair.
  • Backcomb each section of hair to create tangles and knots.
  • Twist each section tightly to form a coil.
  • Use a rubber band or string to secure the end of each dreadlock.
  • Be patient - it can take several months for your dreadlocks to fully mature.

FAQ

How long does it take to make dreadlocks with short hair?

The length of time it takes to make dreadlocks with short hair depends on the thickness of your hair and how many dreadlocks you want. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days to complete the process.

Do I need to use any products to make dreadlocks with short hair?

No, you don't need to use any products to make dreadlocks with short hair. However, you may want to use a dreadlock wax or gel to help your dreadlocks hold their shape and stay in place.

Hair Care

Once you've made your dreadlocks, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or oils, as they can make your hair too slippery to form dreadlocks.
  • Be gentle when washing and drying your hair - avoid rubbing or pulling your dreadlocks too hard.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and make your dreadlocks unravel.

With these tips and techniques, you can make dreadlocks with short hair at home. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and your dreadlocks will look great for years to come!
