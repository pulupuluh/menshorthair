How To Get Men s Hair Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for ways to make your hair look fuller and more voluminous, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to get men's hair fluffy, so you can achieve your desired hairstyle without having to visit a salon.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Men's Hair Fluffy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get Men's Hair Fluffy

Getting your hair to look fluffy requires a bit of effort and some hair products. Here's what you'll need to do:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and body to your hair. Use a towel to pat your hair dry and avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Hair Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of hair mousse to your palm and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. This will help create volume and hold your hairstyle in place.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while running your fingers through it to add lift and volume. Make sure to direct the air flow from your roots to your ends for the best results.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish off your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and maintain the volume throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to getting men's hair fluffy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add texture and body to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to pat your hair dry, avoiding rubbing your hair to prevent frizz. Leave your hair slightly damp for the next step.

Step 3: Apply Hair Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of hair mousse to your palm and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Make sure to work the mousse into your roots to create lift and volume.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while running your fingers through it to add lift and volume. Make sure to direct the air flow from your roots to your ends for the best results. You can also use a round brush to add more volume to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish off your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and maintain the volume throughout the day.

Tips for Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your fluffy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Don't apply too much hair product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Try using a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and add volume.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to flatten and lose its volume.

FAQs

Can I get fluffy hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve fluffy hair with short hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, apply hair mousse, blow-dry your hair, and finish with hairspray for added volume and hold.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still get fluffy hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, applying hair mousse, and blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume. You can also try using a hair fiber product to add more texture and thickness to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve fluffy and voluminous hair that will turn heads wherever you go. Remember to use high-quality hair products and take good care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality.
