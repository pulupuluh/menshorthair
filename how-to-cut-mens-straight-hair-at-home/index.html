How To Cut Men s Straight Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? With a little practice and the right tools, you can easily learn how to cut men's straight hair at home. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the satisfaction of knowing you did it yourself. Follow these steps and tips for a professional-looking cut.

How to Cut Men's Straight Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a clipper with guards. You may also want to invest in a handheld mirror to check the back of the head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, then towel-dry it. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Use the clipper guards to determine the length you want. Start with the longest guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Use the clipper with the chosen guard to cut the sides of the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of the hair to the desired length. Start at the back and work your way forward, taking small sections at a time.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the sides and top of the hair together. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut the ends at an angle.

Step 7: Trim the Neckline

Use the clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the neckline. Start at the center and work your way outwards, following the natural curve of the hairline.

Step 8: Check for Unevenness

Use the handheld mirror to check the back of the head for any unevenness. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 9: Clean Up

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hairline around the ears and neckline. Use a brush or towel to clean up any loose hair.

Step 10: Style and Finish

Style the hair as desired using gel, pomade, or hairspray.

Tips for Cutting Men's Straight Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and scissors
  • Check for evenness throughout the cutting process
  • Watch online tutorials or ask a friend for help if you're unsure

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's straight hair at home:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, with practice and the right tools, you can cut your own hair at home.

What tools do I need?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a clipper with guards.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good timeframe for a trim.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep it moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to confidently cut men's straight hair at home. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the satisfaction of knowing you did it yourself. Happy cutting!
