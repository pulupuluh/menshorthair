How To Cut Your Hair Short Straight By Yourself

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new and fresh hairstyle, why not try cutting your hair short and straight? Not only is this look easy to maintain, but it also provides a stylish and chic appearance. In this article, we'll guide you on how to cut your hair short straight by yourself, without having to visit a salon or barbershop.

Description

Cutting your hair short straight by yourself can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. This guide will take you through the steps of cutting your hair short straight, from preparing your hair to trimming and styling it.

How to Cut Your Hair Short Straight by Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. These include:

  • Sharp scissors or hair clippers
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Make sure your scissors or clippers are sharp and clean, as this will ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting it. It's easier to cut clean, dry hair, as wet hair can be difficult to handle and may result in an uneven cut. Once your hair is dry, comb it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair ties or clips. Start by creating a part down the middle of your head, and then divide each side into three sections: the top, middle, and bottom. This will help you to cut your hair evenly and prevent any mistakes or unevenness.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Start by trimming the sides and back of your hair. Use your scissors or clippers to cut your hair in a straight line, following the shape of your head. Trim the hair gradually, taking small sections at a time. Remember to check your progress in the mirror to ensure that both sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Next, move on to the top of your hair. Take small sections of hair and trim them in a straight line. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, taking care to cut the hair evenly. Remember to check your progress in the mirror to ensure that the length is even across the top of your head.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished trimming your hair, style it as desired. You can use a styling product such as gel or mousse to give your hair some texture and hold. Alternatively, you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Tips

  • Take your time when cutting your hair, as rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors or clippers, as this will help you to cut the hair evenly.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Be patient and don't get discouraged if you make a mistake. You can always trim the hair again or visit a salon for a professional fix.

FAQ

Is it easy to cut your hair short straight by yourself?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, cutting your hair short straight by yourself can be easy and straightforward.

What tools do I need to cut my hair short straight?

You'll need sharp scissors or hair clippers, a comb, a mirror, and hair ties or clips.

Can I style my hair after cutting it short straight?

Yes, you can style your hair as desired after cutting it short straight. You can use a styling product such as gel or mousse to give your hair some texture and hold, or leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair short straight, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair short straight by yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a stylish and chic haircut in no time!
