How To Get Heart Shaped Face Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking for ways to naturally achieve a heart shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to get heart shaped face naturally through a combination of exercises, makeup techniques, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Exercises for a Heart Shaped Face
  • Makeup Techniques for a Heart Shaped Face
  • Hair Care for a Heart Shaped Face

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the steps for achieving a heart shaped face, it's important to determine if your face shape is indeed heart shaped. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and observe the following:

  • Is your forehead wider than your jawline?
  • Do you have a pointed chin?
  • Is the width of your face at the cheekbones narrower than the forehead?

If you answered yes to these questions, then you likely have a heart shaped face.

Exercises for a Heart Shaped Face

One way to naturally achieve a heart shaped face is through facial exercises. These exercises help to tone and define the muscles in your face, leading to a more defined and sculpted look. Here are a few exercises to try:

  1. Jaw Release: Open your mouth as wide as you can and hold for 5-10 seconds. Then, slowly close your mouth and repeat 5-10 times.
  2. Cheek Puff: Puff out your cheeks with air and hold for 5-10 seconds. Then, release the air and repeat 5-10 times.
  3. Tongue Stretch: Stick out your tongue and stretch it down towards your chin. Hold for 5-10 seconds and repeat 5-10 times.

Incorporating these exercises into your daily routine can help to achieve a more defined and sculpted heart shaped face.

Makeup Techniques for a Heart Shaped Face

Makeup can also be used to enhance and define a heart shaped face. Here are a few techniques to try:

  • Contouring: Use a contour shade to create shadows under your cheekbones and along your jawline. This will help to define and sculpt your face.
  • Highlighting: Apply highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose to draw attention to these areas.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add a pop of color and enhance your natural cheekbones.

These makeup techniques can help to enhance and define your heart shaped face, leading to a more sculpted and balanced look.

Hair Care for a Heart Shaped Face

Lastly, hair care can also play a role in achieving a heart shaped face. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Side Part: A side part can help to balance out a heart shaped face and draw attention away from the forehead.
  • Layers: Layers can help to add volume and texture to the hair, creating a more balanced and proportionate look.
  • Bangs: Soft, side-swept bangs can help to shorten the forehead and balance out a heart shaped face.

By keeping these hair care tips in mind, you can create a more balanced and proportionate look that complements your heart shaped face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I naturally change my face shape?

While you can't necessarily change your natural face shape, you can use techniques such as exercises, makeup, and hair care to enhance and define your features.

How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

Results from facial exercises will vary depending on the individual and how consistently the exercises are performed. However, with regular practice, you can expect to see results within a few weeks.

What if I don't want to wear makeup?

That's perfectly fine! While makeup can be used to enhance and define your natural features, it's not necessary to achieve a heart shaped face. The exercises and hair care tips outlined in this article can still help to create a more defined and sculpted look.

Conclusion

Achieving a heart shaped face naturally is possible with a combination of exercises, makeup techniques, and hair care. By incorporating these tips and techniques into your daily routine, you can enhance and define your natural features, leading to a more sculpted and balanced look.
