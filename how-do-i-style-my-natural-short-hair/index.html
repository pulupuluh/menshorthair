How Do I Style My Natural Short Hair

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is amazing, but it can be a bit tricky to style. If you have natural short hair, then you know how hard it can be to find the perfect style that suits you. In this post, we will take you through some tips and tricks to help you style your natural short hair effortlessly. Whether you have a TWA (teeny weeny afro) or a slightly longer length, these tips will work for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Natural Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Natural Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Natural Short Hair

How to Style Natural Short Hair

The first thing you need to do is determine your hair type. This will help you identify the best style for you. There are different hair types, including curly, coily, and kinky. Once you know your hair type, you can then choose the best style that will suit you.

Another thing to consider is your face shape. Different face shapes suit different styles, so you need to choose the style that works best for your face shape.

Styles for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, then you can rock a TWA, a curly fro, or a tapered cut. These styles are easy to maintain and will keep your curls looking amazing.

Styles for Coily Hair

For coily hair, you can go for a twist-out or a braid-out. These styles will give you beautiful curls and will help you to maintain your hair's moisture.

Styles for Kinky Hair

If you have kinky hair, then you can try a wash and go or a finger coil. These styles will keep your hair looking amazing, and they are easy to maintain.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style your natural short hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  2. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner.
  3. Dry your hair with a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  6. Apply your preferred styling product to your hair.
  7. Style your hair using your fingers, a brush, or a comb.
  8. Use a hairdryer or let your hair air dry.
  9. Finish off with a hair oil or a hair sheen spray.

Tips for Styling Natural Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your natural short hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness.
  • Use a protein treatment to strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair when sleeping by wearing a bonnet or a scarf.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my natural short hair?

A: You can wash your hair once a week or every two weeks, depending on your hair type and your lifestyle.

Q: Can I wear a wig or a weave with natural short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a wig or a weave with natural short hair. Just make sure to keep your hair moisturized and protected underneath.

Q: Can I color my natural short hair?

A: Yes, you can color your natural short hair. However, make sure to use a gentle hair dye and to moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness.

Hair Care for Natural Short Hair

To take care of your natural short hair, you need to:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Protect your hair from heat and harsh weather conditions.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet to nourish your hair from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your natural short hair effortlessly. Remember to take care of your hair and to choose the style that suits you best.
