Hairstyles That Suit Round Fat Faces

Written by Bardi Mar 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round, fat face, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. However, there are many hairstyles that can flatter your features and make you look amazing. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for round, fat faces and provide you with tips and advice on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Round, fat faces have softer angles and fuller cheeks, which can make them look younger and more approachable. However, the wrong hairstyle can also make your face look rounder and fuller. The key is to choose a hairstyle that balances your features and creates the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your round, fat face is to identify your face shape. This will help you determine which hairstyles will flatter your features and which ones to avoid. Some of the best hairstyles for round, fat faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Messy buns
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Asymmetrical cuts

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair texture and thickness, as well as your lifestyle and personal style. You should also consult with a professional hairstylist who can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your overall look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle for your round, fat face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling some of the best hairstyles for round, fat faces:

Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Curl the ends of your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on your bangs.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth and straighten your bangs.
  5. Sweep your bangs to the side and secure with bobby pins.
  6. Apply a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Maintaining healthy, beautiful hair is essential for any hairstyle, but it's especially important for round, fat faces. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round, fat face?

You should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and blunt cuts. These styles can make your face look rounder and fuller.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round, fat face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round, fat face. However, it's best to keep the ponytail low and loose to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling your hair properly, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

With these tips and advice, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round, fat face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized recommendations and advice.
