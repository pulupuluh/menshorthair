How To Perm Your Hair At Home For Guys Without Chemicals

Written by William Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some texture and volume to your locks? Perming your hair at home is a great solution without having to use harsh chemicals. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to perm your hair at home for guys without chemicals.

How to Perm Your Hair at Home for Guys Without Chemicals

What You Will Need:

Before you start perming your hair at home, you will need to gather the necessary tools and products. Here is a list of the things you will need:

  • Perm kit (without chemicals)
  • Plastic gloves
  • Shower cap
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips
  • Towel
  • Neutralizing shampoo
  • Conditioner

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you have everything you need, follow these steps to perm your hair at home:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb through it with a wide-tooth comb to remove any knots.
  3. Section your hair into four parts and clip them up.
  4. Put on plastic gloves and mix the perm kit according to the instructions.
  5. Apply the perm solution to each section of your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front.
  6. Cover your hair with a shower cap and let it sit for the recommended time in the instructions.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water for at least 5 minutes.
  8. Apply the neutralizing shampoo to your hair and massage it into your scalp for a few minutes.
  9. Rinse your hair with warm water again and towel dry it.
  10. Apply the conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off with cool water.

Tips:

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when perming your hair at home:

  • Do not wash your hair for at least 24 hours after perming it.
  • Avoid using any heat styling tools for at least 48 hours after perming your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about perming hair at home:

1. Can I perm my hair if it is already chemically treated?

No, it is not recommended to perm your hair if it has already been chemically treated as it may cause damage to your hair.

2. How often can I perm my hair?

You should wait at least 6-8 weeks between perms to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner after perming my hair?

It is recommended to use a neutralizing shampoo and a conditioner specifically designed for permed hair to keep it healthy and moisturized.

Hair Care:

Perming your hair can cause some damage, so it is important to take care of your hair afterwards. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Now that you know how to perm your hair at home for guys without chemicals, you can add some texture and volume to your locks without having to go to a salon.
