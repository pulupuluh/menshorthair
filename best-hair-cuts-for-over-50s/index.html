Best Hair Cuts For Over 50S A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Oliver Jun 26, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes changes in texture, thickness, and color. Finding the right hairstyle can make all the difference in looking and feeling our best. In this guide, we'll explore the best hair cuts for over 50s. From short and sleek to long and layered, there's a style to suit every face shape and hair type. Let's dive in!

Description

Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, especially if you're used to the same style you've had for years. As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. It's important to find a style that complements your face shape and enhances your natural features. In this guide, we'll share some of our favorite haircuts for over 50s.

How to

The first step in choosing a new hairstyle is to consider your face shape. The right cut can balance out your features and create a more youthful appearance. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • If you have a round face, opt for a style that adds height and volume at the crown
  • If you have a square face, soften your features with layers and waves
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any style, but steer clear of styles that add too much volume at the sides
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, balance out your features with a chin-length bob or lob

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for styles that suit your hair type and lifestyle. Some of the best hair cuts for over 50s include:

Short and Sleek

A short, sleek haircut can be a chic and low-maintenance option for over 50s. This style works well for those with fine or thinning hair, as it can create the illusion of thickness and volume. A side-swept fringe can also add some softness to the look.

Long and Layered

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create movement and texture. This style works well for medium to thick hair, and can be worn straight or wavy. Avoid adding too much volume at the sides, as this can make the face appear wider.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and daring choice for over 50s. This short, cropped style can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. Opt for a longer fringe or side-swept bangs to soften the look.

Step by Step

When visiting your stylist, be sure to bring in photos of styles you like, as well as any concerns you have about your hair. Here are some steps to follow when getting a new haircut:

  1. Consult with your stylist about your desired look
  2. Wash and condition your hair the night before your appointment
  3. Arrive with clean, dry hair
  4. During the cut, communicate with your stylist about any changes you'd like to make
  5. After the cut, ask for styling tips and product recommendations

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new haircut:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with color, but keep it natural-looking
  • Avoid styles that require a lot of upkeep or styling time
  • Invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for over 50s:

What is the best haircut for thinning hair?

A short, layered haircut can be a great option for those with thinning hair, as it can add volume and texture. A side-swept fringe can also help to conceal any balding or thinning areas.

Can I still wear long hair over 50?

Absolutely! Long hair can be flattering at any age, as long as it's well-maintained and styled to suit your face shape and hair type. Consider adding layers or waves to create movement and volume.

What is the most popular haircut for over 50s?

There are many popular haircuts for over 50s, including the pixie cut, bob, and layered lob. Ultimately, the best haircut for you will depend on your personal style and hair type.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, vibrant hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too frequently
  • Invest in a high-quality hair brush to minimize breakage
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best at any age. Whether you opt for a short and chic pixie cut or a long and layered lob, there's a style to suit every over 50s woman.
