How To Maintain Dreads With Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have straight hair, you might think that dreadlocks are not an option for you. However, with the right technique and maintenance, you can achieve and maintain beautiful, healthy dreadlocks with straight hair. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about how to maintain dreads with straight hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to achieving dreadlocks with straight hair is to section your hair into small, even sections. Use hair elastics to hold each section in place. You can also use a rat-tail comb to help create clean, even sections.

Next, use a dread wax or locking gel to help hold the hair in place. You can also use a locking accelerator to help speed up the locking process. Work the product into each section of hair, making sure to cover the entire length of the hair.

Once you have applied the product, use a comb or your fingers to twist each section of hair. Start at the root and twist the hair in a clockwise direction, working your way down to the tips. Make sure to twist each section tightly to help the hair lock in place.

Step by Step

1. Section your hair into small, even sections using hair elastics or a rat-tail comb.

2. Apply a dread wax or locking gel to each section of hair, making sure to cover the entire length of the hair.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to twist each section of hair, starting at the root and working your way down to the tips.

4. Twist each section tightly to help the hair lock in place.

5. Repeat the process for each section of hair.

Tips

- Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help speed up the locking process.

- Be patient, as it can take several months for your hair to fully lock.

- Avoid using conditioner or other products that can make your hair slippery, as this can make it difficult for the hair to lock.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your locks while you sleep.

FAQ

How often should I wash my dreadlocks?

You should wash your dreadlocks once a week to keep them clean and healthy. Use a residue-free shampoo and be sure to rinse thoroughly.

Can I swim with dreadlocks?

Yes, you can swim with dreadlocks, but be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly afterwards to remove any chlorine or salt water.

Can I color my dreadlocks?

Yes, you can color your dreadlocks. However, be sure to use a hair dye that is safe for dreadlocks and avoid using bleach.

Hair Care

Once you have achieved your dreadlocks, it is important to maintain them properly to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks:

- Wash your dreadlocks once a week with a residue-free shampoo.

- Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help speed up the drying process.

- Avoid using products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your locks while you sleep.

- Trim any loose or frayed hairs regularly to keep your locks looking neat and tidy.

With the right technique and maintenance, you can achieve and maintain beautiful, healthy dreadlocks with straight hair. Follow these tips and techniques to keep your locks looking their best.
