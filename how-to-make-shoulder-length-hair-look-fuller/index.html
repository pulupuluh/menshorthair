How To Make Shoulder Length Hair Look Fuller

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you wish your shoulder length hair had more volume and body? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make your shoulder length hair look fuller and more voluminous. We'll cover everything from how to style your hair to the best products to use. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Shoulder Length Hair Look Fuller
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fuller Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fuller Hair

How to Make Shoulder Length Hair Look Fuller

There are a few tricks you can use to make your shoulder length hair look fuller:

  • Use volumizing products
  • Blow dry your hair upside down
  • Use a round brush when blow drying
  • Add layers to your hair
  • Tease your roots
  • Use dry shampoo

By using these tips and tricks, you can achieve fuller, more voluminous hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your shoulder length hair look fuller:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing product, such as mousse or root lifter, to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush.
  4. Add layers to your hair to create more body.
  5. Tease your roots for added volume.
  6. Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add texture.
  7. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold your style in place.

With these simple steps, you can achieve fuller, more voluminous hair.

Tips for Fuller Hair

Here are some additional tips for making your shoulder length hair look fuller:

  • Avoid heavy, oily products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type.

By following these tips, you can achieve fuller, more voluminous hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making shoulder length hair look fuller:

What products should I use to make my hair look fuller?

You should use volumizing products, such as mousse or root lifter, to add volume to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

Hair Care for Fuller Hair

In addition to styling your hair for more volume, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and full. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair healthy and full.

In conclusion, there are many ways to make shoulder length hair look fuller. By using volumizing products, blow drying your hair upside down, adding layers, and teasing your roots, you can achieve the full, voluminous hair you've always wanted. And by taking care of your hair with gentle products and regular trims, you can keep your hair healthy and full for years to come.
