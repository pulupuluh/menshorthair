Would I Look Good With Bangs Curly Hair

Written by William Apr 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting bangs with your curly hair, you might be wondering if it's a good choice for you. Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some personality to your curly hair. In this blog post, we'll explore whether or not you would look good with bangs and give you some tips on how to care for them.

Description

Bangs are a trendy and stylish way to update your look, but they can be a bit tricky to pull off with curly hair. Curly hair has its own unique set of challenges, and adding bangs to the mix requires a bit of extra care and attention. The good news is that with the right cut and some styling tips, you can rock bangs with your curly hair.

How to Get Bangs with Curly Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and get bangs with your curly hair, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Find the Right Stylist

Not all stylists are created equal when it comes to cutting curly hair. Look for a stylist who specializes in curly hair and has experience working with bangs. They'll be able to give you a cut that works with your natural curl pattern and flatters your face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Bangs

There are a few different types of bangs to choose from, and some work better with curly hair than others. Side-swept bangs are a great choice for curly hair because they blend seamlessly into your curls and don't require as much styling. Blunt bangs can also work with curly hair, but they require more maintenance to keep them looking sleek and smooth.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Styling your bangs with curly hair requires a bit of finesse. You'll need to use a diffuser to dry your hair and create defined curls, and you may need to use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs. Use a lightweight styling product to keep your curls looking bouncy and voluminous.

Tips for Caring for Bangs with Curly Hair

Once you have your bangs cut and styled, you'll need to take some extra care to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls and bangs.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make your bangs look frizzy.
  • Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your curls and bangs moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy and weighed down.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bangs with curly hair:

Q: Will bangs make my curly hair look shorter?

A: Yes, bangs can make your curly hair look shorter, especially if you get blunt bangs. However, side-swept bangs can actually make your hair look longer by adding some extra length to your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with bangs. Just make sure to secure your bangs with bobby pins or a headband so they don't get in your face.

Hair Care for Bangs with Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and bouncy, and adding bangs to the mix requires even more attention. Make sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Use a diffuser to dry your hair and create defined curls, and use a lightweight styling product to keep your curls looking voluminous. And don't forget to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and stylish.

So, would you look good with bangs curly hair? The answer is yes, as long as you choose the right cut and take good care of your hair. Follow our tips and advice, and you'll be rocking your new curly bangs in no time!
