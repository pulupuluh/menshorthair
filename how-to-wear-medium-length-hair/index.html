How To Wear Medium Length Hair Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Luffy May 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium length hair, you have a lot of styling options that can make you look fabulous. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, you can rock a variety of hairstyles for any occasion. In this article, we'll share tips, a step-by-step guide, and hair care advice on how to wear medium length hair.

Table of Contents

  • Tips
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Tips

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing medium length hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use the right hair products for your hair type and style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some extra flair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips in mind, let's take a look at a step-by-step guide on how to wear medium length hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in styling your medium length hair is to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. After washing, gently towel dry your hair and detangle any knots with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan on using any heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, it's important to apply a heat protectant first. This will help prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. If you have wavy or curly hair, you can use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.

Step 4: Choose Your Hairstyle

Now it's time to choose your hairstyle. Here are some popular options for medium length hair:

  • Straight and sleek
  • Beachy waves
  • Curls
  • Braids
  • Ponytail

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen your hairstyle, it's time to style your hair. Use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls, or simply apply some hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz. If you're going for a braided look, use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure the braids in place.

Step 6: Add Accessories

To complete your look, add some accessories to your hair. Headbands, clips, and scarves are all great options for adding some extra flair to your hairstyle. Just make sure they complement your outfit and don't overpower your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my medium length hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Q: What's the best way to add volume to medium length hair?

A: Blow drying your hair with a round brush can add volume and shape to your hair. You can also use volumizing hair products, such as mousse or dry shampoo, to add some extra oomph.

Q: Can I wear my medium length hair up?

A: Yes, you can wear your medium length hair up in a ponytail, bun, or braid. Just make sure to use hair ties and bobby pins that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair looking healthy and beautiful, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can wear your medium length hair with confidence and style.
