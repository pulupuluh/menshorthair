How To Spike Your Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to add some edge and personality to your hair? Spiky hair is a classic style that has been around for decades and can give you a bold and confident look. In this article, we will provide a step by step guide on how to spike your hair and offer some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect style. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Spike Your Hair
  2. Tips for Spiking Your Hair
  3. FAQs About Spiky Hair
  4. Hair Care Tips for Spiky Hair

How to Spike Your Hair

Before you start spiking your hair, it's important to have the right tools and products. You will need a good quality hair gel, a comb or brush, and a blow dryer. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Begin by washing your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any excess oils and provide a clean base for styling.
  2. Towel dry your hair, but don't rub it too hard as this can damage the hair follicles. Instead, pat it dry gently.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel to your palms and rub it together to warm it up. Then, run your fingers through your hair to distribute the gel evenly.
  4. Using a comb or brush, create the desired shape of your spikes. You can use a side part or slick back the sides for a more polished look.
  5. Blow dry your hair on low heat, using your fingers to lift and separate the hair as you go. This will help to set the spikes in place.
  6. Once your hair is dry, add a small amount of hair gel to your fingertips and apply it to the tips of your spikes to define them further.

Congratulations, you've successfully spiked your hair!

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with a small amount of hair gel and add more if needed. Too much gel can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different shapes and heights of spikes to find the style that suits you best.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and thickness before applying the gel.
  • Use a hairspray to set the spikes in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day.

FAQs About Spiky Hair

Here are some common questions about spiky hair:

How long does spiky hair last?

Spiky hair can last for up to 8 hours, depending on the thickness and length of your hair, as well as the quality of the products you use.

Can you spike short hair?

Yes, you can spike short hair. In fact, short hair can be easier to style and maintain than long hair. Just use a small amount of hair gel and create short, pointed spikes with your fingers.

Can you spike curly hair?

Yes, you can spike curly hair. However, it may require more product and effort to achieve the desired shape and hold. Use a strong-hold gel and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the curls.

Hair Care Tips for Spiky Hair

Spiky hair requires some extra care and attention to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle.
  • Use a quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a bold and stylish spiky hairdo that will turn heads and make you feel confident and cool. Experiment with different shapes, heights, and textures to find the style that suits you best, and don't be afraid to have fun with your hair!
