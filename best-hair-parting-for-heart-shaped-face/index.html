If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. One of the most important factors that can make or break your look is the hair parting. In this article, we will discuss the best hair parting for heart-shaped face, including how to choose the right one and some tips and tricks to make the most out of it.

How to Choose the Right Hair Parting for Heart-Shaped Face

The first step in choosing the right hair parting for your heart-shaped face is to determine the widest part of your face, which is usually the forehead. You want to balance out your forehead with the rest of your face, so the goal is to create an illusion of a wider face at the bottom and a narrower one at the top. Here are some things to keep in mind when choosing the right hair parting:

  • Opt for a deep side parting or a center parting to elongate the face and draw attention away from the forehead.
  • Avoid a straight, blunt fringe as it can make your face look even narrower.
  • Experiment with different hair partings to find what suits you best.

Center Parting

A center parting is a classic hairstyle that suits heart-shaped faces perfectly. It creates an illusion of symmetry and draws attention away from the forehead, making it appear narrower. It also adds length to the face, which is ideal for heart-shaped faces that tend to be wider at the forehead.

One of the best things about a center parting is that it works with almost any hair length or texture, from short to long, straight to curly. To create a center parting, use a tail comb to create a straight line down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, use your fingers or a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Side Parting

A side parting is another great option for heart-shaped faces. It creates an asymmetrical look that balances out the forehead and chin, making the face appear more oval-shaped. A deep side parting is particularly effective as it adds volume to the hair, which can help to balance out the narrowness of the forehead.

To create a side parting, use a tail comb to create a diagonal line from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head. Then, use your fingers or a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also experiment with different angles and positions to find what suits you best.

Deep Side Parting

A deep side parting is a more dramatic version of a side parting that can add a touch of glamour to any look. It's particularly effective for heart-shaped faces as it draws attention away from the forehead and towards the cheekbones, creating a more balanced look.

To create a deep side parting, use a tail comb to create a diagonal line from the outer corner of your eyebrow to the crown of your head. Then, use your fingers or a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also add some volume to the hair on the opposite side of the parting to balance out the look.

How to Style Hair with Your Chosen Parting

Once you've chosen the right hair parting for your heart-shaped face, it's time to style your hair to make the most out of it. Here are some tips and tricks:

  • For center parting, you can wear your hair down, in a bun, or in a ponytail. If you have long hair, you can also try a half-up, half-down style.
  • For side parting, you can wear your hair down, in a low ponytail or a messy bun. You can also add some curls or waves to create volume and texture.
  • For deep side parting, you can wear your hair down, in a side-swept style, or in a low ponytail. You can also add some volume to the hair on the opposite side of the parting to balance out the look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make the most out of your hair parting:

  • Use a tail comb to create a precise parting.
  • Apply some volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the hair on the opposite side of the parting.
  • Experiment with different angles and positions to find what suits you best.
  • Try adding some curls or waves to create volume and texture.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a fringe with a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, but avoid a straight, blunt fringe as it can make your face look even narrower. Opt for a side-swept or wispy fringe instead.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle parting if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, a middle parting is a great option for heart-shaped faces as it creates an illusion of symmetry and draws attention away from the forehead.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best with your chosen parting, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair parting for your heart-shaped face, you can create a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
