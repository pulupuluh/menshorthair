Is Grey Hair Trendy In 2021

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering going grey but unsure if it's currently in style? Look no further, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll discuss if grey hair is trendy in 2021 and provide tips on how to rock the look.

Description

Grey hair has been gaining popularity over recent years, with many celebrities and influencers opting for the natural look. However, with the pandemic causing many to skip regular salon appointments, more people have been embracing their greys. But is it still considered fashionable? Let's find out.

How to Go Grey

If you're interested in going grey, the first step is to stop dyeing your hair. This can be a lengthy process, depending on how often you dye your hair and the length. It's essential to be patient and let your hair grow out naturally. If you're struggling with the transition, consider getting a shorter haircut to make the process easier.

Step by Step Process

1. Stop dyeing your hair
2. Be patient and let your hair grow out naturally
3. Consider getting a shorter haircut to make the transition easier

Tips on Rocking Grey Hair

1. Keep your hair healthy by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
2. Add volume to your hair by using a volumizing mousse.
3. Play around with different haircuts to find the perfect style for you.
4. Embrace your natural texture and consider using hair oils to add shine.
5. Experiment with different makeup looks to complement your new hair color.
6. Don't forget to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
7. Finally, own your look with confidence.

FAQs

1. Will going grey damage my hair?

No, going grey naturally does not damage your hair. However, if you've been dyeing your hair for years, the process of growing out your natural color may cause some damage. It's essential to keep your hair healthy with regular trims and nourishing treatments.

2. Can I dye my hair grey?

Yes, you can dye your hair grey. However, it's best to visit a professional stylist to achieve the desired shade and avoid damaging your hair.

3. What if I don't like my grey hair?

If you're not happy with your grey hair, you can always dye it back to your desired color. However, it's essential to wait until your hair is healthy enough to handle the process to avoid further damage.

Hair Care for Grey Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
2. Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
3. Nourish your hair with regular hair masks and oils.
4. Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or UV-protectant spray.
5. Visit a professional stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.
6. Embrace your natural texture and consider air-drying your hair.
7. Finally, don't forget to have fun with your new look!

Conclusion

So, is grey hair trendy in 2021? The answer is yes. Embracing your natural hair color has been gaining popularity over the years, and the pandemic has only accelerated the trend. By following our tips and embracing your new look with confidence, you can rock your grey hair with style.
