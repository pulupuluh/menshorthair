How To Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Products

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have straight hair and want to give it some curls, you don't need to rely on products like curling irons and hair rollers. In fact, there are natural ways to get curly hair without using any products. In this post, we'll show you how to make your hair curly without using products. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Products
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Products

If you're looking for a natural way to make your hair curly, try these tips:

1. Braid Your Hair

One of the easiest ways to get curly hair without using any products is by braiding your hair. Start by dividing your hair into sections and braiding each section tightly. Leave the braids in overnight, then undo them in the morning. You should have curly hair that lasts all day.

2. Twist Your Hair

Another way to get curly hair without products is by twisting your hair. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. Leave the twists in overnight, then undo them in the morning. You'll have curly hair that looks natural and effortless.

3. Use a Headband

You can also use a headband to get curly hair without products. Start by putting a headband on your head. Take a section of hair and tuck it over the headband. Continue doing this until all your hair is tucked into the headband. Leave the headband in overnight, then take it out in the morning. You'll have curly hair that looks like you used a curling iron.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly without using products:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly with water.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to braid or twist your hair.

Step 4: Braid or Twist Your Hair

Braid or twist each section of hair tightly. Make sure the braids or twists are secure and won't come undone while you sleep.

Step 5: Leave Your Hair Overnight

Leave the braids or twists in overnight. This will give your hair time to set and form curls.

Step 6: Undo the Braids or Twists

In the morning, undo the braids or twists. Gently run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly Without Products

Here are some tips to help you make your hair curly without using any products:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact. Silk is gentle on your hair and won't cause damage like cotton or polyester.

2. Don't Brush Your Hair

Brushing your curly hair can cause frizz and ruin your curls. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your curls hydrated and defined. Apply the conditioner to your hair after washing it and leave it in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without using heat. Try braiding, twisting, or using a headband to get natural curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last all day, but it depends on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Can I use these methods on short hair?

Yes, you can use these methods on short hair. Just make sure to divide your hair into smaller sections.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

2. Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause frizz and damage. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel

Microfiber towels are gentle on your hair and can help prevent frizz. Use a microfiber towel to gently dry your hair after washing it.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and ruin your curls. Try to avoid using curling irons, straighteners, and hair dryers on your curly hair.

By following these tips, you can make your hair curly without using any products. Try these natural methods and see how they work for you!
