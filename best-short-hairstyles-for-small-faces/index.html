Best Short Hairstyles For Small Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Mar 20, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a small face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and make your face look proportionate. Luckily, there are plenty of great short hairstyles for small faces that can achieve just that. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best options available, along with tips for maintenance and care.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyles for Small Faces

Before we dive into specific styles, it's important to understand what features to look for when choosing a short hairstyle for a small face. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape. For small faces, this often means choosing styles with layers and volume to add dimension.
  • Avoid styles that are too heavy or blunt, as these can make your face appear smaller.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some styles work better with certain types of hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling and maintenance.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can work well for small faces. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. The pixie cut is low maintenance and can be a great way to show off delicate features.

The Bob Hairstyle

The bob is another timeless style that can be adapted to suit small faces. This style features hair cut to chin length or just above, with layers that can add volume and movement. The bob can be styled straight or with waves for a more textured look.

The Angled Bob

The angled bob is a variation of the classic bob that features longer hair in the front and shorter hair in the back. This style can help elongate the face and create a more balanced look. The angled bob can be worn straight or with curls for added texture.

The Layered Bob

The layered bob is another variation of the classic bob that features layers throughout the hair. This style can add volume and movement to thin or fine hair, and can be styled in a variety of ways. The layered bob can be worn straight or with waves for a more textured look.

The Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a modern take on the classic bob that features longer hair on one side and shorter hair on the other. This style can be a great way to add visual interest to a small face, and can be styled in a variety of ways. The asymmetrical bob can be worn straight or with curls for added texture.

The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a bold and edgy style that can work well for small faces. This style features hair that is longer in the center and shorter on the sides, creating a mohawk-like effect. The faux hawk can be worn straight or with textured waves for added volume and movement.

The Short Shag

The short shag is a layered style that can add volume and texture to thin or fine hair. This style features layers throughout the hair, with shorter layers on top and longer layers on the bottom. The short shag can be worn straight or with waves for added texture.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a versatile addition to any short hairstyle. This style features hair cut to chin length or just above, with bangs that are swept to one side. Side-swept bangs can help balance out a small face and can be styled in a variety of ways.

FAQ about Short Hairstyles for Small Faces

What should I consider when choosing a short hairstyle for a small face?

When choosing a short hairstyle for a small face, consider your face shape, hair texture and thickness, and lifestyle. Look for styles that add volume and dimension, and avoid styles that are too heavy or blunt.

What are some good short hairstyles for small faces?

Some good short hairstyles for small faces include the pixie cut, bob hairstyle, angled bob, layered bob, asymmetrical bob, faux hawk, short shag, and side-swept bangs.

How can I style my short hairstyle for a special occasion?

There are many ways to style a short hairstyle for a special occasion, including adding accessories like hair clips and headbands, using texturizing products to add volume and movement, and curling or straightening the hair for a more polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles for Small Faces

How often should I get my hair cut?

For short hairstyles, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the cut.

What products should I use to style my short hair?

For short hairstyles, it's important to use lightweight products that won't weigh down the hair. Look for texturizing sprays, mousses, and pomades that can add volume and movement.

How can I protect my hair from heat damage?

When using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. It's also a good idea to limit heat styling and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

With these tips and styles, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your small face. Whether you opt for a classic bob or a daring faux hawk, you can rock any look with confidence and style.
