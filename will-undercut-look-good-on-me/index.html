Will Undercut Look Good On Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting an undercut, you're probably wondering if it will suit you. The good news is that undercuts are versatile and can work for most people. However, there are a few factors to consider before taking the plunge. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about whether an undercut will look good on you.

Description: What is an Undercut?

An undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top remains longer. This creates a sharp contrast between the two lengths and emphasizes the hair on top. Undercuts can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

How to: Determine if an Undercut will Suit You

Here are some factors to consider before getting an undercut:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in whether an undercut will look good on you. If you have a round face, an undercut can help create a more angular look. On the other hand, if you have a square face, an undercut can make your face appear too boxy. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle, including an undercut.

Hair Texture

Undercuts work well with thick, straight, and wavy hair. If you have thin or curly hair, an undercut may not be the best option for you.

Lifestyle

Undercuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. If you have a busy lifestyle or prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, an undercut may not be the best choice for you.

Step by Step: How to Get an Undercut

If you've decided that an undercut is the right hairstyle for you, here's how to get one:

Step 1: Choose a Style

There are many undercut styles to choose from, so find one that suits your face shape and hair texture. Bring a picture to your stylist for reference.

Step 2: Shave the Sides and Back

Your stylist will use clippers to shave the hair on the sides and back of your head. The length can vary, but typically, it's anywhere from a #1 to a #4 guard.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Your stylist will cut the hair on top to your desired length and style it accordingly.

Step 4: Maintain Regularly

Undercuts require regular maintenance to keep their shape. Plan on getting a touch-up every 4-6 weeks.

Tips: How to Style an Undercut

Here are some tips for styling your undercut:

Use the Right Products

Use a styling product that works well with your hair texture and desired look. For a sleek look, use pomade or gel. For a messy or textured look, use wax or clay.

Experiment with Styling

Undercuts are versatile and can be styled in various ways. Try different partings, directions, and textures to find a style that suits you.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will an undercut look good on me if I have curly hair?

A: Undercuts work best with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, an undercut may not be the best option for you.

Q: Can I still tie my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes! Although the sides and back of your head are shaved or cut short, the hair on top is long enough to tie up.

Hair Care: How to Maintain Your Undercut

Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut:

Wash Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Trim Regularly

Plan on getting a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to keep your undercut looking sharp.

Protect from the Sun

If you're spending time outdoors, protect your hair and scalp from the sun's harmful rays with a hat or sunscreen.

Conclusion

An undercut can be a bold and stylish hairstyle that works for most people. However, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before getting one. If you do decide to get an undercut, follow the steps for styling and maintenance, and experiment with different looks to find one that suits you.
