Medium Length Hairstyles For Oval Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Mar 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide on the perfect medium length hairstyle that complements your oval face. In this article, we will explore the best medium length hairstyles for oval faces and how to achieve them.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are a great option for those who want to add some versatility to their hair without committing to a short or long length. They are also perfect for oval faces, as they can help balance out the proportions of the face. Medium length hairstyles typically fall between the shoulders and collarbone and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for your oval face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. If you have straight hair, a blunt bob or lob can be a great option. If you have curly hair, consider a shoulder-length layered cut to enhance your natural texture.

To achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle for your oval face, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine which medium length hairstyle will best suit your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend products and styling techniques to help you achieve your desired look.

Step 2: Choose Your Medium Length Hairstyle

Once you've consulted with a hairstylist, it's time to choose your medium length hairstyle. Some popular options for oval faces include:

  • Blunt Bob or Lob
  • Shoulder-Length Layers
  • Side-Swept Bangs with Layers
  • Messy Updo

Step 3: Maintain Your Medium Length Hairstyle

To keep your medium length hairstyle looking its best, it's important to maintain it regularly. This includes getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks, using the right hair products for your hair type, and protecting your hair from heat damage.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium length hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Consider your lifestyle and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Use hair accessories to add some flair to your medium length hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that best suits your face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for oval faces:

What is the best medium length hairstyle for oval faces?

The best medium length hairstyle for oval faces is one that complements your personal style and hair texture. Some popular options include a blunt bob or lob, shoulder-length layers, and side-swept bangs with layers.

Can oval faces pull off short hairstyles?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short hairstyles. However, it's important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type.

How do I maintain my medium length hairstyle?

To maintain your medium length hairstyle, it's important to get regular trims, use the right hair products for your hair type, and protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes:

  • Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Limiting heat styling
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle for your oval face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
