Short Layered Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair A Guide

Written by Petter May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. Luckily, short layered hairstyles are a great option for those with thick curly hair. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best short layered hairstyles for thick curly hair, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. How to Style Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Description

Short layered hairstyles are perfect for those with thick curly hair because they provide volume and shape to your curls. Layers also help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to manage and style. Short hairstyles are also a great option for those who want to save time styling their hair in the morning or those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. There are many different short layered hairstyles to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How to Style Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

Styling short layered hairstyles for thick curly hair can be easy if you know what products to use and how to properly style your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short layered hairstyles for thick curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your damp hair, making sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hair type, but especially for those with thick curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Will short layered hairstyles work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, short layered hairstyles can work for all face shapes. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features and complements your face shape.

Q: Will short layered hairstyles work for all hair types?

A: Short layered hairstyles can work for most hair types, but they're especially great for those with thick curly hair.

Q: Can I style short layered hairstyles without heat?

A: Yes, you can style short layered hairstyles without heat by using curl-enhancing products and air-drying your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock a short layered hairstyle that suits your thick curly hair. Remember to always take good care of your hair to keep it healthy, hydrated, and full of life!
