How To Trim Long Boy Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of taking your son to the hair salon every month to get his hair trimmed? Why not try doing it yourself at home? With the right tools and techniques, trimming long boy hair with scissors can be a breeze. In this guide, we’ll show you how to trim long boy hair with scissors in a relaxed and easy-to-follow language. Let’s get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Trim Long Boy Hair with Scissors

Trimming long boy hair with scissors requires a bit of practice and patience, but it’s definitely doable. Before you start, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Wash and Comb the Hair

Before you start trimming, make sure your son’s hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut and can result in an uneven trim. Use a comb to detangle any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Sectioning the hair will make it easier to trim and ensure that you don’t miss any areas. Use a comb to divide the hair into four sections: top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Use the first section as a guide for the rest of the hair. Move on to the side sections and then the top section.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After you’ve trimmed all the sections, use the scissors to blend the hair. This will help create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 5: Check for Uneven Spots

Once you’re done trimming and blending, check for any uneven spots. Use the scissors to trim any areas that may need a little more attention.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basic steps, let’s dive into a more detailed guide:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start trimming, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You’ll need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Make sure your son’s hair is clean and dry before you start trimming. Wet hair is harder to cut and can result in an uneven trim. Use a comb to detangle any knots or tangles.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into four sections: top, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Start with the Back

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Use the first section as a guide for the rest of the hair.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Move on to the side sections and trim them to the desired length. Use the back section as a guide.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Trim the top section to the desired length, using the side sections as a guide.

Step 7: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair, creating a more natural look and preventing any harsh lines.

Step 8: Check for Uneven Spots

Check for any uneven spots and trim them with the scissors.

Step 9: Style the Hair

Once you’ve finished trimming, style the hair as desired.

Tips for Trimming Long Boy Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use the comb to hold the hair in place while you trim.
  • Check the hair length frequently to avoid cutting too much.
  • Trim the hair when it’s dry, as wet hair is harder to cut.
  • Use a spray bottle to mist the hair if it becomes too dry.

FAQs

How often should I trim my son’s hair?

It depends on how fast his hair grows and how long you want it to be. Generally, a trim every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the length and shape of the hair.

Can I use regular scissors to trim my son’s hair?

No, it’s best to use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can be too dull and cause split ends.

What if I make a mistake while trimming?

If you accidentally cut too much hair or create an uneven spot, don’t worry. You can always trim a little more or blend the hair to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care Tips for Boys

Here are some tips to help keep your son’s hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the length and shape.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Trimming long boy hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can give your son a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy trimming!
