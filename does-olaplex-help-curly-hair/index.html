Does Olaplex Help Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right products, it can be easier to maintain. Olaplex is a popular hair treatment that claims to repair and protect hair from damage. But does Olaplex help curly hair? In this article, we'll explore the benefits of using Olaplex on curly hair and provide tips on how to use it effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Olaplex on Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using Olaplex on Curly Hair
  • Tips for Using Olaplex on Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Description

Olaplex is a hair treatment that was developed to protect and repair hair from damage caused by chemical treatments such as coloring, bleaching, and relaxing. Olaplex works by repairing the broken bonds in the hair that are caused by these types of treatments. This results in stronger, healthier hair that is less prone to breakage and damage.

Curly hair is often more prone to damage because of its structure. The curls and waves create bends in the hair that can weaken the strands, making them more prone to breakage. Additionally, curly hair can be dry and brittle, which makes it more susceptible to damage.

Olaplex can help repair and protect curly hair by strengthening the hair strands and preventing further damage. By using Olaplex regularly, curly hair can become stronger, healthier, and more resilient.

How to Use Olaplex on Curly Hair

When using Olaplex on curly hair, it is important to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips for using Olaplex on curly hair:

  • Use Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector as a weekly treatment
  • Use Olaplex No. 4 Bond Maintenance Shampoo
  • Follow up with Olaplex No. 5 Bond Maintenance Conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair while conditioner is in
  • Avoid using heat styling tools

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Olaplex on Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to using Olaplex on curly hair:

  1. Wet hair thoroughly
  2. Apply Olaplex No. 4 Bond Maintenance Shampoo to hair and scalp
  3. Massage shampoo into hair and scalp, focusing on the roots
  4. Rinse hair thoroughly with warm water
  5. Apply Olaplex No. 5 Bond Maintenance Conditioner to hair, focusing on the ends
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair while conditioner is in
  7. Leave conditioner in for 3-5 minutes
  8. Rinse hair thoroughly with cool water
  9. Squeeze excess water out of hair with a towel
  10. Apply Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector to damp hair
  11. Comb through hair to distribute product evenly
  12. Leave product in for at least 10 minutes (or longer for more damaged hair)
  13. Rinse hair thoroughly with cool water

Tips for Using Olaplex on Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for using Olaplex on curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause further damage to curly hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying curly hair to avoid causing frizz
  • Protect hair from the sun and other environmental stressors
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates and other harsh chemicals
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage while sleeping

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using Olaplex on curly hair:

1. Is Olaplex safe for curly hair?

Yes, Olaplex is safe for all hair types, including curly hair. It is designed to repair and protect hair from damage, making it a great choice for those with curly hair that is prone to breakage and damage.

2. How often should I use Olaplex on my curly hair?

It is recommended to use Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector as a weekly treatment for best results. However, you can use Olaplex No. 4 Bond Maintenance Shampoo and Olaplex No. 5 Bond Maintenance Conditioner as often as needed to maintain healthy hair.

3. Can Olaplex make curly hair straight?

No, Olaplex is not a straightening treatment. It is designed to repair and protect hair from damage, but it will not change the texture of curly hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

In addition to using Olaplex, there are other steps you can take to care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can dry out curly hair
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and define curls
  • Avoid brushing curly hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage

Overall, Olaplex can be a great addition to your curly hair care routine. By using Olaplex regularly and following the tips above, you can help strengthen and protect your curly hair, resulting in healthier, more manageable curls.
