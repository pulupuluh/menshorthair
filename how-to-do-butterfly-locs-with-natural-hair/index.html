How To Do Butterfly Locs With Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Butterfly locs have become a popular protective hairstyle in recent years. This unique style is characterized by its loose, wavy texture and butterfly-like appearance. If you have natural hair and want to try out butterfly locs, read on for our step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: Description
  • Section 2: How to
  • Section 3: Step-by-Step
  • Section 4: Tips
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care

Description

Butterfly locs are a type of crochet locs that use wavy or curly hair for a textured, bohemian look. They get their name from their unique shape, which resembles the wings of a butterfly. Butterfly locs are a great protective style for natural hair, as they allow your hair to rest and grow while still looking stylish.

How to

To achieve butterfly locs with natural hair, you will need to use crochet braiding techniques. This involves weaving pre-made locs into your hair using a crochet needle. You can purchase butterfly locs from most beauty supply stores or online retailers.

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to achieve butterfly locs with natural hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair into small, even parts using a comb.
  3. Take a crochet needle and insert it into the first section of hair.
  4. Loop the pre-made butterfly locs onto the crochet needle and pull it through the section of hair.
  5. Repeat this process until the entire section of hair is covered in butterfly locs.
  6. Move onto the next section of hair and repeat the process until your entire head is covered in butterfly locs.
  7. Trim any excess hair or stray strands with a pair of scissors.
  8. Style your butterfly locs as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing butterfly locs:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before starting the crochet process.
  • Use a crochet needle with a small hook to make the process easier.
  • Be gentle when pulling the crochet needle through your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Apply oil or leave-in conditioner to your scalp to keep it moisturized.
  • Try using different colors of butterfly locs for a unique look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about butterfly locs:

Q: How long do butterfly locs last?

A: Butterfly locs can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks with proper maintenance.

Q: Can I swim with butterfly locs?

A: It is not recommended to swim with butterfly locs, as the chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair.

Q: How do I take care of my butterfly locs?

A: To take care of your butterfly locs, make sure to keep your scalp moisturized and avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down the hair. You can also wrap your hair at night to prevent frizz.

Hair Care

To keep your natural hair healthy while wearing butterfly locs, make sure to:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your ends every 6-8 weeks.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful butterfly locs with natural hair. Remember to take care of your hair and enjoy your new style!
