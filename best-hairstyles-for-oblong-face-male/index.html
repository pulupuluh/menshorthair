Best Hairstyles For Oblong Face Male A Complete Guide

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to suit your oblong face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! From short to long haircuts, we have curated a list of hairstyles that will suit every personality and lifestyle. So, let's get started!

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and forehead. The aim of choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face male is to create an illusion of width and balance out the features. The right hairstyle can help enhance your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead, making your face appear more proportionate.

How to

Before jumping into the hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape accurately. Once you have identified that you have an oblong face shape, you can start exploring the hairstyles that would suit you the best. You can consult your hairstylist or use virtual makeover tools to visualize the hairstyle on your face before committing to a particular look.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Explore the hairstyles that suit an oblong face shape

3. Consult a hairstylist or use virtual makeover tools to visualize the hairstyle

4. Choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face male, keep the following tips in mind:

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short

2. Opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides of your face

3. Use hair products such as mousse or pomade to add volume and texture

4. Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or have a center parting

5. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best

FAQ

Q. What are the best hairstyles for oblong face male?

A. The best hairstyles for oblong face male are short haircuts, medium-length hairstyles with texture, and hairstyles with side-swept bangs.

Q. Can I wear a beard with an oblong face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a beard with an oblong face shape. However, make sure to keep it well-groomed and avoid styles that add length to your face, such as long goatees.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid over-styling or using excessive heat on your hair. Regular trims and haircuts will help maintain the shape and texture of your hairstyle.

So, there you have it - our complete guide on the best hairstyles for oblong face male. Follow these tips and tricks and choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
