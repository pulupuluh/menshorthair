How Can I Change My Face Shape Naturally

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for ways to change your face shape without undergoing any cosmetic surgery? Well, you’re in luck! In this article, we will discuss how you can change your face shape naturally. Whether you want to add more definition to your jawline, make your cheeks look plumper, or slim down your face, these tips will help you achieve the desired result.

Description

Changing your face shape can boost your confidence and enhance your overall appearance. Although it’s not possible to change your bone structure, there are several natural ways to alter your face shape. These include facial exercises, makeup techniques, and haircuts that flatter your face shape.

How To Change Your Face Shape Naturally?

There are several ways to change your face shape without undergoing any surgery. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and add more definition to your jawline and cheekbones. Some of the most popular facial exercises include smiling fish, cheek puff, and jaw release. These exercises can be done anywhere and require no equipment.

2. Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool to alter your face shape. By using contouring and highlighting techniques, you can make your cheeks appear plumper, your jawline more defined, and your forehead smaller. There are several tutorials available online that can teach you how to contour and highlight your face.

3. Haircuts

The right haircut can flatter your face shape and make it appear more balanced. For example, if you have a round face, a long bob or a pixie cut can make your face appear slimmer. If you have a square face, a side-swept bang or a layered cut can soften your features.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to change your face shape naturally:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in changing your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right haircut and makeup techniques to flatter your features.

Step 2: Try Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and add more definition to your jawline and cheekbones. Try doing these exercises for a few minutes each day to see results.

Step 3: Use Contouring and Highlighting Techniques

Contouring and highlighting can help alter your face shape. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows on your face and make your features appear slimmer. Use a highlighting powder or cream to make your features appear more prominent.

Step 4: Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can flatter your face shape and make it appear more balanced. Consult your hairstylist to find the right haircut for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you change your face shape naturally:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to nourish your skin and body.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your skin and make it appear dull.
  • Get enough sleep to allow your body to rest and regenerate.
  • Wear sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage.

FAQ

Can facial exercises really change your face shape?

Yes, facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and add more definition to your jawline and cheekbones. However, they won’t alter your bone structure.

Are there any risks associated with changing your face shape naturally?

No, there are no risks associated with changing your face shape naturally. However, it’s important to consult your doctor before starting any new exercise or diet regimen.

How long does it take to see results?

The time it takes to see results varies from person to person. However, with consistent effort, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Hair Care

Changing your face shape isn’t just about facial exercises and makeup techniques. Your hair can also play a role in altering your face shape. Here are some hair care tips to help you achieve the desired result:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape.
  • Use hair products that add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see which ones flatter your features.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Changing your face shape naturally is possible with the right techniques and consistent effort. By trying these methods, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel more confident in your own skin.
