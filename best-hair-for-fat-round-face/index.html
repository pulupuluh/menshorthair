Best Hair For Fat Round Face Flattering Styles For 2023

Written by Bardi Apr 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we enter a new year, it's time to refresh your hairstyle and find the best one that suits your face shape. For those with a round and chubby face, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and make you look your best. In this article, we'll be discussing the best hair for fat round face, tips on how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hair for Fat Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Styling Tips
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair for fat round face, it's crucial to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

1. Pull your hair back and take a photo of your face.

2. Examine the photo and look at the proportions of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

3. Determine which shape your face resembles the most. Common face shapes include oval, square, heart, and round.

Best Hair for Fat Round Face

Now that you've determined your face shape let's take a look at the best hair for fat round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid blunt cuts and opt for soft, face-framing layers that will add movement and dimension to your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the roundness of your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear wider.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and flattering option for those with a round face. It can help create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to your cheekbones.

4. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Opt for a bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, and add some volume to the crown of your head.

5. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob can be a great option for those with a round face. The waves can add texture and dimension to your hair, and the length can help elongate your face.

Step-by-Step Styling Tips

Here are some step-by-step styling tips for the best hair for fat round face:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.

3. If you have straight hair, use a curling iron to add some waves or curls to your hair.

4. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser to enhance your curls.

5. Finish off with some hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

4. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a bob with a fat round face?

Yes, you can wear a bob with a fat round face. Opt for a bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, and add some volume to the crown of your head.

2. Can I wear my hair straight with a fat round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a fat round face. However, it's essential to add some volume to your hair to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

3. Should I avoid bangs with a fat round face?

No, you don't have to avoid bangs with a fat round face. Side-swept bangs can help break up the roundness of your face and make it appear slimmer.

In conclusion, finding the best hair for fat round face can be challenging, but with the right hairstyle and styling tips, you can enhance your features and look your best. Remember to take care of your hair and get regular trims to keep it healthy.
