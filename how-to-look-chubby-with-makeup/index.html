How To Look Chubby With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of being called thin or skinny and want to look a bit more plump? Well, makeup can do wonders for you. With the right techniques and products, you can create the illusion of a fuller face. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to look chubby with makeup.

How to Look Chubby with Makeup

Looking chubby with makeup might seem like a daunting task, but it is not. The key is to create the illusion of roundness and fullness. Here are some tips:

  • Use a foundation that is a shade darker than your skin tone for a shadow effect.
  • Apply a cream blush on the apples of your cheeks and blend it well.
  • Use a highlighter on the high points of your face to create contrast.
  • Apply a nude or light-colored lipstick to make your lips appear fuller.
  • Use a volumizing mascara to make your lashes look thicker.

By following these steps, you can achieve a chubby look with makeup. But if you want to take it up a notch, read on.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to look chubby with makeup:

Step 1: Prep your Skin

Achieving a chubby look with makeup starts with prepping your skin. Cleanse your face and apply a moisturizer. If you have dry skin, use a hydrating primer before applying makeup.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Use a foundation that is a shade darker than your skin tone. Apply it on your cheeks, temples, and jawline to create a shadow effect. Blend it well with a beauty blender or a brush.

Step 3: Apply Blush

Apply a cream blush on the apples of your cheeks and blend it well. Use a circular motion to blend it outwards, towards your temples.

Step 4: Highlight

Use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the tip of your nose. This will create contrast and make your face appear fuller.

Step 5: Contour

Use a contour powder or cream on the hollows of your cheeks to create the illusion of roundness. Blend it well with a brush.

Step 6: Fill in your Brows

Fill in your brows with a brow pencil or powder to make them appear fuller.

Step 7: Apply Mascara

Use a volumizing mascara to make your lashes look thicker. Apply it on your top and bottom lashes.

Step 8: Apply Lipstick

Use a nude or light-colored lipstick to make your lips appear fuller. Apply it using a lip brush for precision.

Makeup Tips for a Chubby Look

Here are some additional makeup tips for a chubby look:

  • Use a shimmery eyeshadow to make your eyes appear bigger.
  • Apply a white or nude eyeliner on your waterline to make your eyes look wider.
  • Use a lip plumper for an extra boost of volume.
  • Do not overdo the contouring. Use a light hand and blend well.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve a chubby look with makeup if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can. The key is to enhance your features and create the illusion of fullness. Follow the tips mentioned in this blog post.

Q: What kind of blush should I use to look chubby?

A: Use a cream blush instead of a powder blush. It blends well and gives a natural finish.

Q: Can I use bronzer instead of a foundation for a shadow effect?

A: Yes, you can. But make sure to blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Hair Care for a Chubby Look

Your hairstyle can also make a difference in how chubby you look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Get a haircut with layers to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to make your hair appear fuller.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down for added volume.
  • Avoid sleek hairstyles and go for messy, textured looks.

With these makeup and hair care tips, you can achieve a chubby look effortlessly. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
