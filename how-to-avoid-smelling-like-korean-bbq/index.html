How To Avoid Smelling Like Korean Bbq

Written by Oliver Feb 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of Korean BBQ, then you know how delicious it can be. But the downside of enjoying this mouth-watering cuisine is that it can leave you smelling like a walking BBQ pit. If you're tired of the lingering scent of Korean BBQ on your clothes and hair, then keep reading to find out how you can avoid smelling like Korean BBQ.

How to Avoid Smelling Like Korean BBQ

Step 1: Wear the Right Clothes

When going to a Korean BBQ restaurant, it's important to wear the right clothes. Avoid wearing clothes that are made of heavy fabrics or materials that tend to absorb smells easily. Instead, opt for clothes made of breathable fabrics, such as cotton or linen, that will allow your skin to breathe and prevent the smells from sticking to your clothes.

Step 2: Keep Your Hair Up

One of the easiest ways to avoid smelling like Korean BBQ is to keep your hair up. If you have long hair, tie it up in a bun or ponytail. This will prevent the smell from getting into your hair and lingering there. You can also wear a hat or headband to keep your hair away from your face.

Step 3: Wash Your Hands and Face Frequently

When you're eating Korean BBQ, it's easy for the smell to get on your hands and face. To prevent this, wash your hands and face frequently. Use soap and water to wash your hands and face thoroughly, paying special attention to areas where the smell tends to linger, such as your fingertips and around your mouth.

Step 4: Use a Breath Mint

Korean BBQ can leave a strong smell in your mouth, so it's important to freshen your breath after eating. Use a breath mint or chew some gum to get rid of any lingering smells.

Step 5: Use a Deodorant

If you're prone to sweating, then use a deodorant before going to a Korean BBQ restaurant. This will help prevent the smell from sticking to your clothes and skin.

Tips for Avoiding Smelling Like Korean BBQ

Here are some additional tips for avoiding smelling like Korean BBQ:

  • Choose a restaurant with good ventilation.
  • Sit near a window or door.
  • Bring a change of clothes to change into after eating.
  • Avoid wearing perfume or cologne, as it can mix with the smell of Korean BBQ and create an unpleasant scent.

FAQs About Avoiding Smelling Like Korean BBQ

Can I still enjoy Korean BBQ if I'm worried about the smell?

Yes, you can still enjoy Korean BBQ even if you're worried about the smell. Just follow the tips above to minimize the smell and enjoy your meal.

How long does the smell of Korean BBQ usually last?

The smell of Korean BBQ can last for a few hours to a few days, depending on how much you ate and how strong the smell was.

Is there anything I can do to get rid of the smell of Korean BBQ?

To get rid of the smell of Korean BBQ, try taking a shower or bath, washing your clothes, and airing out your home.

Hair Care Tips for Avoiding Smelling Like Korean BBQ

If you have long hair, then you know how easily it can absorb smells. Here are some hair care tips for avoiding smelling like Korean BBQ:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any lingering smells from your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain strong fragrances.
  • Wash your hair frequently to prevent the smell from lingering.
  • Consider tying your hair up or wearing a hat to keep it away from your face.

Now that you know how to avoid smelling like Korean BBQ, you can enjoy this delicious cuisine without worrying about the lingering smells. Follow these tips and you'll be able to enjoy your meal without any unpleasant after-effects.
