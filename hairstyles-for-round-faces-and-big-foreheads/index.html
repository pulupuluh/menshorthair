Hairstyles For Round Faces And Big Foreheads Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Feb 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of always struggling to find the right hairstyle that will suit your round face and big forehead? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will be sharing some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features. Keep on reading to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to determine your face shape and forehead size
  • Hairstyles for round faces and big foreheads
  • Tips for styling your hair
  • Frequently asked questions
  • Hair care tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape and Forehead Size

Before we get into the hairstyles, it’s essential to determine your face shape and forehead size. Knowing your face shape and forehead size will help you narrow down the hairstyle options that will suit you best.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. The four main face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. If your forehead is wide and prominent, then you have a big forehead. If your forehead is small and narrow, then you have a small forehead.

Hairstyles for Round Faces and Big Foreheads

Now that you have determined your face shape and forehead size, let’s dive into the hairstyles that will complement your features.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length to balance out your features. Here are some hairstyles that will do just that:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • High ponytail
  • Long and straight

Hairstyles for Big Foreheads

If you have a big forehead, you want to create the illusion of a smaller forehead. Here are some hairstyles that will do just that:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wispy bangs
  • Curly hair
  • Updos with face-framing pieces
  • Short haircuts with volume on top

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you have some hairstyle options let’s talk about tips for styling your hair.

  • Use hair products that will add volume and texture to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  • Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair
  • Don’t be afraid to add layers to your hair to create movement
  • Use a hair straightener to create sleek and straight hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions when it comes to hairstyles for round faces and big foreheads:

Can I wear a center part with a round face and big forehead?

Yes, but it’s best to add some volume to the crown of your head to create balance.

Can I wear a short haircut with a round face and big forehead?

Yes, but it’s best to add volume to the top of your head to create balance.

Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail with a round face and big forehead?

Yes, but it’s best to add volume to the crown of your head to create balance.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, let’s talk about some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen

Now that you have some tips and tricks for hairstyles for round faces and big foreheads, it’s time to experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for you. Remember to have fun and be confident in your hairstyle choices!
