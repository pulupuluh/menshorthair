How To Make Your Face Look Androgynous A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to make your face look more androgynous, there are a few things you can do to achieve this look. Androgyny is all about playing with gender expectations and blurring the lines between masculinity and femininity. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to make your face look more androgynous, including makeup techniques, hair care, and more.

How to Make Your Face Look Androgynous

The key to achieving an androgynous look is to balance masculine and feminine features on your face. This can be done through makeup, hair styling, and even clothing choices. Here are some things you can do to make your face look more androgynous:

1. Contouring

Contouring is the process of using makeup to create the illusion of shadows and highlights on your face. By strategically placing darker shades in certain areas and lighter shades in others, you can make your face appear more angular and defined. To create a more androgynous look, focus on creating sharper lines and more defined angles.

2. Eyebrows

Your eyebrows can have a big impact on the overall shape of your face. For a more androgynous look, consider shaping them in a more angular, less curved shape. This will help to create a more masculine appearance.

3. Lips

For a more androgynous look, try using lip balm or a nude lipstick instead of a bold, bright color. This will create a more neutral look and help to balance out any more feminine features on your face.

4. Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in how androgynous you look. Consider a shorter, more masculine cut or a longer, more feminine style that can be easily styled in a more masculine way. Play around with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look more androgynous:

Step 1: Start with a clean, moisturized face.

Before applying any makeup, make sure your face is clean and moisturized. This will help your makeup go on more smoothly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a light foundation.

Start by applying a light foundation that matches your skin tone. This will create a smooth base for the rest of your makeup.

Step 3: Contour your face.

Using a contouring powder or cream, create shadows and highlights on your face to create a more angular, defined look.

Step 4: Shape your eyebrows.

Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to shape your eyebrows in a more angular, less curved shape.

Step 5: Apply a neutral lip color.

Instead of a bold, bright lip color, opt for a neutral lip balm or nude lipstick to create a more neutral look.

Step 6: Style your hair.

Choose a hairstyle that works for you and can be easily styled in a more masculine or feminine way, depending on the look you're going for.

Tips for Achieving an Androgynous Look

Here are some additional tips for achieving an androgynous look:

  • Choose clothing that blurs the lines between masculine and feminine styles.
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques to find what works for you.
  • Practice good skincare to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant.
  • Consider getting a more androgynous haircut that can be styled in different ways.

FAQs

Q: Can anyone achieve an androgynous look?

A: Yes, anyone can achieve an androgynous look. It's all about playing with gender expectations and finding a balance between masculine and feminine features on your face.

Q: Do I have to wear makeup to look androgynous?

A: No, you don't have to wear makeup to look androgynous. However, makeup can help to create a more defined, angular look.

Hair Care for an Androgynous Look

When it comes to hair care for an androgynous look, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a haircut that can be styled in more masculine or feminine ways.
  • Experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to create a more tousled, messy look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your face look more androgynous and play with gender expectations in a fun and creative way. Remember, androgyny is all about finding a balance between masculine and feminine features, so don't be afraid to experiment and find what works for you.
