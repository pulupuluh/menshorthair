Products To Make Straight African American Hair Curly

Written by Christine May 08, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have naturally straight African American hair and want to switch up your look, there are plenty of products out there that can help you achieve bouncy, curly locks. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best products to make straight African American hair curly, as well as how to use them and other helpful tips to get the most out of your new curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Straight African American Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Straight African American Hair Curly

There are a variety of methods for making straight African American hair curly, including using heat styling tools, chemical treatments, and even hair accessories. However, the easiest and most affordable way to get curly hair is by using the right products. Here are some of the best products to make straight African American hair curly:

Curl-Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start your curly hair journey in the shower with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, and shea butter, which can help define and shape curls while also adding moisture to your hair.

Curl-Defining Cream

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a curl-defining cream to damp hair. This will help enhance your natural curl pattern and create more defined curls. Apply the cream evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends where your hair tends to be driest.

Curl-Enhancing Mousse

If you want extra volume and hold, consider using a curl-enhancing mousse in addition to your curl-defining cream. Apply the mousse to your hair and scrunch your hair upwards to encourage curls to form. This will help create bouncy, defined curls that last all day.

Curl-Defining Gel

If you want even more hold and definition, you can also use a curl-defining gel. Apply the gel to your hair after your curl-defining cream and mousse, and then scrunch your hair upwards. This will help create more defined curls that last even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Curly Hair

Now that you know the best products to use, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-defining cream to damp hair, making sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. If you want extra volume and hold, apply a curl-enhancing mousse to your hair and scrunch your hair upwards.
  4. If you want even more hold and definition, apply a curl-defining gel to your hair and scrunch your hair upwards.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, you can use a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your curls looking bouncy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as this can disrupt your curl pattern and create frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Try to avoid using heat styling tools on your curly hair, as this can cause damage and frizz. If you must use a heat styling tool, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Refresh your curls in between washes by spritzing your hair with a mixture of water and leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What if I have relaxed hair?

If you have relaxed hair, you can still use these products to create curly hair. However, keep in mind that chemical treatments can weaken your hair and make it more prone to damage, so be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment regularly.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

How often you wash your curly hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair wash their hair every day, while others only wash their hair once a week. Experiment to find the washing schedule that works best for your hair.

Can I use these products on natural hair?

Absolutely! These products are great for all hair types, including natural hair. Just be sure to adjust the amount of product you use based on your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

When it comes to hair care for curly hair, moisture is key. Be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use the right products to enhance and define them.

With these products and tips, you’ll be able to achieve beautiful, bouncy curls in no time. Whether you have naturally straight hair or relaxed hair, these products are sure to help you achieve the curly look you’ve been dreaming of.
