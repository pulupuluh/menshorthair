Can You Use Thinning Scissors On Long Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who likes to experiment with different hairstyles, then you may have considered using thinning scissors on your long hair. Thinning scissors are a popular tool used by hairstylists to help create a textured look by removing some of the bulk from the hair. But can you use thinning scissors on long hair? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide you with some tips on how to use them properly.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of scissors that have teeth on one blade and a straight edge on the other. These teeth help to remove some of the hair, creating a softer and more textured look. Thinning scissors are often used on thick hair to remove some of the bulk, but they can also be used on long hair.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Long Hair

If you're going to use thinning scissors on your long hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you should make sure that it's clean and dry. This will help you to get a more accurate cut and will prevent the scissors from getting stuck in your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you should section your hair into small sections. This will help you to focus on one section at a time and will prevent you from accidentally cutting too much hair at once.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Correctly

When using thinning scissors, you should hold them with the teeth facing downwards. This will help to create a more natural look and will prevent you from cutting too much hair at once.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair by taking small sections and using the thinning scissors to remove some of the bulk. Be sure to only cut a little bit at a time and to blend the sections together as you go.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting your hair, you should check your progress in a mirror. If you're happy with the way it looks, then you're done. If not, you can always go back and make some more adjustments.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors on Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when using thinning scissors on long hair:

  • Use a comb to help guide the scissors as you cut
  • Work slowly and be patient
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Blend the sections together as you go
  • Check your progress regularly

FAQ

Can thinning scissors damage your hair?

Thinning scissors are generally safe to use and won't damage your hair if used correctly. However, if you use them too aggressively or cut too much hair at once, you could end up damaging your hair.

Can thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

Thinning scissors can be used on most hair types, but they're generally best suited for thick or coarse hair. If your hair is thin or fine, you should be careful when using thinning scissors as they could end up making your hair look too thin.

How often should you use thinning scissors on your hair?

How often you use thinning scissors on your hair will depend on your personal preference and your hair type. Some people may only use them once or twice a year, while others may use them more frequently. It's important to be careful not to overdo it, as too much thinning could end up damaging your hair.

Hair Care

After using thinning scissors on your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

By following these tips, you can help keep your hair healthy and looking great after using thinning scissors.
