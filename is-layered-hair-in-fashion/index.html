Is Layered Hair In Fashion

Written by Christine Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Layered hair has been in fashion since the 70s, but is it still in trend in 2023? The answer is a resounding yes! Layered hair is still one of the most popular hairstyles, and it’s not going anywhere anytime soon. In fact, it’s become more versatile than ever with new styling techniques and variations.

Description

Layered hair is a style where the hair is cut into different lengths, creating layers. This gives the hair a more textured and voluminous look. Layered hair can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type. It can be long or short, straight or curly, and can be styled in many different ways.

How to

If you’re thinking of getting a layered haircut, here’s what you need to know:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The length of your hair will determine how many layers you can have. If you have long hair, you can have more layers, while if you have short hair, you’ll need fewer layers. Talk to your hairstylist about what length would suit you best.

Step 2: Decide on the type of layers

There are different types of layers you can have, such as face-framing layers, choppy layers or feathered layers. Think about the look you want to achieve and discuss it with your hairstylist.

Step 3: Consider your hair type

Your hair type will determine the type of layers that will work best for you. For example, if you have thick hair, you may need more layers to reduce the weight, while if you have fine hair, you may need fewer layers to avoid making your hair look too thin.

Step 4: Style your hair

Layered hair can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, wavy, or in an updo. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your layered hair looking great:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. - Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools. - Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh. - Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type. - Don’t be afraid to change up your hairstyle and try something new.

FAQ

Q: Is layered hair suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, layered hair can be adapted to suit any hair type. Talk to your hairstylist about what type of layers would work best for your hair.

Q: How often should I get my layered hair trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your layers start to lose their shape. As a general rule, you should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with layers?

A: Yes, layered hair can be styled in an updo. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to style your hair for an updo.

Hair Care

Caring for your layered hair is important to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. - Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair. - Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling on your hair and damaging your layers. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With the right care and styling, layered hair can be a timeless and versatile look. So why not give it a try?
