Images Of Mens Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Mar 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then a long on top short on sides haircut might be just what you need. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. In this article, we'll show you some images of mens haircuts long on top short on sides, and provide some tips on how to achieve the look.

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, which gradually gets shorter towards the sides and back. This creates an asymmetrical look that adds dimension and texture to the hair. The length of the hair on top can vary, depending on personal preference and face shape. The sides and back are usually trimmed with clippers, using different guard sizes to achieve the desired length.

How To

If you're interested in getting a long on top short on sides haircut, here's how you can achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose your length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal style and face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the hair on top shorter, while those with a longer face can opt for longer hair on top.

Step 2: Trim the sides

Next, it's time to trim the sides and back of your hair. Use clippers with different guard sizes to achieve the desired length. Start with a longer guard size and gradually work your way down until you reach the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides and back.

Step 3: Cut the top

Once the sides and back are trimmed, it's time to cut the hair on top. Use scissors to create layers and texture, and to remove any bulk. You can also use a razor to create a more textured look. Make sure to leave enough length on top to create the desired style.

Step 4: Style your hair

Finally, it's time to style your hair. There are many ways to style a long on top short on sides haircut, from slicked back to messy and textured. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and personal style, and experiment with different looks until you find one that works for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long on top short on sides haircut:

- Visit a barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut. - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. - Experiment with different hair products to find one that works for your hair type and desired style. - Consider getting a fade or undercut on the sides and back for a more dramatic look. - Don't be afraid to try different styles and looks until you find one that suits you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long on top short on sides haircuts:

What face shapes work best with this style?

This style works well with most face shapes, but those with round or square faces may want to keep the hair on top shorter to avoid making the face look wider.

Can this style be worn curly?

Absolutely! This style looks great with curly or wavy hair. Just make sure to keep the sides and back trimmed to avoid creating a triangular shape.

Is this style easy to maintain?

This style can be easy to maintain, as long as you visit a barber or stylist regularly to keep the length and shape of your haircut. Using high-quality hair products can also help keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your long on top short on sides haircut:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. - Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers, flat irons, and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. - Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized. - Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut. - Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Conclusion

A long on top short on sides haircut is a versatile and trendy style that can be customized to suit different face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. With the right length, shape, and styling products, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and easy to maintain. So why not give it a try?
