How To Cut Boy Straight Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a young boy with straight hair, you may want to save money and time by learning how to cut his hair at home. Cutting boy straight hair is not as difficult as it may seem, and with a little practice, you can achieve great results. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to cut boy straight hair, including some tips and hair care advice. Let’s get started!

Description

Cutting boy straight hair involves trimming the hair to a desired length and style. It’s important to have the right tools and technique to ensure a clean and even cut. The process can take anywhere from 15 to 45 minutes, depending on the length and thickness of the hair, as well as the style you want to achieve.

How to Cut Boy Straight Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you will need to gather the necessary tools. This includes a pair of scissors, clippers, a comb, and hair ties. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to help dampen the hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will remove any dirt or oil and make it easier to cut. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry the hair gently. You can also use a blow dryer to dry the hair completely.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Using the comb, section the hair into smaller sections. Start at the top of the head and work your way down. Use hair ties to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair using the scissors or clippers. For a shorter cut, use the clippers with a guard to achieve an even length. For a longer cut, use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Start at the back of the head and work your way to the front, being careful to keep the hair even.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to blend the hair. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines. Hold the scissors vertically and snip at the ends of the hair to create texture.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If your boy has bangs, use the scissors to trim them to the desired length. Cut the bangs in a straight line or at an angle, depending on the style you want to achieve.

Step 7: Clean Up the Hairline

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up the hairline around the ears and neck. This will create a clean look and prevent any stray hairs from sticking out.

Step 8: Style the Hair

Once you have finished cutting the hair, you can style it as desired. Use a styling product such as gel or pomade to create a defined look, or leave it natural for a more relaxed style.

Tips for Cutting Boy Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting boy straight hair:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to create a guide when cutting with scissors.
  • Hold the scissors vertically when blending the hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head and make sure the hair is even.
  • Take your time and don’t rush the process.

FAQ

Is it easy to cut boy straight hair?

Yes, cutting boy straight hair is relatively easy with the right tools and technique. Practice makes perfect, so don’t be afraid to try it out.

Do I need to go to a salon to cut boy straight hair?

No, you can easily cut boy straight hair at home with the proper tools and technique. This can save you time and money in the long run.

Hair Care for Boy Straight Hair

After cutting the hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and avoid using too much heat when styling. Brush the hair regularly to prevent tangles and breakage.

Conclusion

Cutting boy straight hair is not as daunting as it may seem. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve great results in the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, follow the steps, and practice to perfect your technique. Happy cutting!
