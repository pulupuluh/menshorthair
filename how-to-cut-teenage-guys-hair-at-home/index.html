How To Cut Teenage Guys Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts for your teenage son? Do you want to learn how to cut his hair at home? In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut teenage guys hair at home in a relaxed English language. By following these tips, you can save money and learn a new skill!

Description

Cutting teenage guys hair at home can be a daunting task, especially if you have no experience. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Not only will you save money, but you will also have the satisfaction of knowing that you did it yourself.

How to

Before you start cutting your teenage son's hair, you will need to gather the necessary tools. These include:

  • Clippers with different guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Towel or cape

Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your teenage son's hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even result. Use a towel or cape to protect their clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Start by selecting the right guard for the clippers. Use a longer guard for the top of the head and a shorter one for the sides and back. You can always switch guards if you need to go shorter.

Step 3: Start with the Top

Begin by cutting the top of the hair using the longer guard. Start at the front and work your way to the back, making sure to cut in a straight line. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it. Repeat until you have the desired length.

Step 4: Move to the Sides and Back

Next, switch to the shorter guard and trim the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it. Be careful not to cut too short.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the top and sides, use the scissors to blend the hair. Start by cutting the longer hair on top and gradually blend it into the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use the comb to lift the hair and the scissors to cut it.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up the neckline. Start at the center and work your way out, making sure to create a clean, straight line.

Step 7: Trim the Bangs

If your teenage son has bangs, use the scissors to trim them. Start at the center and work your way out, cutting the hair at an angle to create a natural-looking shape.

Step 8: Final Touches

Once you have finished cutting the hair, use the comb and scissors to make any final touches. Check for any uneven spots or missed hairs and trim as necessary.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and switch to a shorter one if necessary
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it
  • Blend the hair with scissors for a more natural look
  • Use the scissors to trim the bangs at an angle for a natural-looking shape
  • Check for any uneven spots or missed hairs and trim as necessary

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my teenage son's hair with just scissors?

A: While it is possible to cut hair with just scissors, it is recommended to use clippers with guards for a more even result.

Q: How often should I cut my teenage son's hair?

A: It depends on how fast their hair grows and the desired length. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so aim to cut it every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back, and mistakes can be fixed. Use the scissors to trim any uneven spots or consult with a professional stylist if necessary.

Hair Care

After cutting your teenage son's hair, make sure to clean up any hair clippings and wash their hair to remove any loose hairs. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep their hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to cut teenage guys hair at home and save money on haircuts. Remember to use the right tools, follow the steps carefully, and make any necessary adjustments to achieve the desired result. Happy cutting!
