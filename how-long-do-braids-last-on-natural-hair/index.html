How Long Do Braids Last On Natural Hair

Written by Luffy Mar 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Braids have been a popular hairstyle for centuries because they are convenient, low maintenance, and versatile. They are also great for protecting natural hair from damage and promoting growth. However, one question that many people have is, how long do braids last on natural hair? In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide helpful tips on how to make your braids last longer.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Braids are a protective hairstyle that involves weaving strands of hair together. They can be done in many different styles and sizes, from microbraids to jumbo braids. Braids are a great way to protect natural hair from damage caused by heat styling, chemicals, and environmental factors. They can also help to promote hair growth by reducing breakage and tangling.

How to

To get the most out of your braids, it is important to follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you get your braids done, make sure your hair is clean, moisturized, and detangled. This will make the braiding process easier and help your braids last longer.

Step 2: Choose the right braiding hair

The type of braiding hair you choose can make a big difference in how long your braids last. Synthetic hair is cheaper, but it may not last as long as human hair. Human hair is more expensive, but it is also more durable and can be reused for future braiding styles.

Step 3: Choose a braiding style that suits your hair

There are many different braiding styles to choose from, so it is important to choose one that suits your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine or thin hair, smaller braids may be better for you. If you have thick or coarse hair, jumbo braids may be a better option.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow to make your braids last longer:

Step 1: Keep your scalp clean

It is important to keep your scalp clean and free of buildup to prevent itching and flaking. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.

Step 2: Moisturize your hair

Moisturizing your hair is important to prevent dryness and breakage. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

Step 3: Protect your braids

Wear a satin scarf or bonnet at night to protect your braids while you sleep. Avoid wearing hats or helmets that may cause friction and damage to your braids.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your braids last longer:

  • Avoid swimming or getting your braids wet, as this can cause them to unravel and frizz.
  • Don't pull or tug on your braids, as this can cause them to loosen and fall out.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your scalp and absorb any excess oil.
  • Trim any loose or frayed ends to prevent your braids from unraveling.

FAQ

How long do braids last on natural hair?

Braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well they are maintained and how fast your hair grows.

Can you wash your hair with braids?

It is not recommended to wash your hair with braids, as this can cause them to unravel and frizz. Instead, use a dry shampoo or scalp refresher to keep your hair clean and fresh.

Hair Care

Taking care of your natural hair while it is in braids is important to prevent damage and promote growth. Here are some tips for caring for your hair while it is in braids:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Trim any split ends to prevent further damage

In conclusion, braids are a great way to protect natural hair and promote growth. With proper care and maintenance, they can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks. Follow these tips to make your braids last longer and keep your hair healthy and happy.
