Best Hairstyle For Oblong Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape? Look no further! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyle for oblong face shape, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Description

An oblong face shape is defined by a long, narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. This face shape can be challenging to style, as it can make the face appear even longer. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and create balance in your overall appearance.

How To

The best hairstyle for oblong face shape is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face while minimizing the length. Here are some hairstyles that work well for oblong face shape:

1. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for oblong face shape. This hairstyle adds volume to the sides of the face, making it appear wider, and can be customized to suit your personal style. Whether you prefer a sleek, straight bob or a layered, textured cut, a bob is a great option for oblong face shape.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for oblong face shape. Bangs can help to balance out the length of the face while adding volume and dimension to the front. Opt for side-swept bangs that start at the eyebrow and angle down towards the cheekbone to create a flattering frame for your face.

3. Layered Hairstyle

A layered hairstyle is a versatile option that works well for oblong face shape. Layers can help to add volume and texture to the hair, creating a more balanced appearance. Whether you opt for long, loose layers or shorter, choppy layers, a layered hairstyle can help to enhance your features and flatter your face shape.

Step by Step

When styling your hair for oblong face shape, it's important to keep a few key tips in mind:

1. Add Volume

Adding volume to the sides of the face can help to create balance and make the face appear wider. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a styling product such as mousse or volumizing spray to give your hair extra lift.

2. Avoid Length

While long hair can be beautiful, it can also make an oblong face shape appear even longer. Opt for a shorter hairstyle that adds volume and dimension to the sides of the face.

3. Frame Your Face

When styling your hair, consider framing your face with layers or bangs. This can help to draw attention to your features and create a more balanced appearance.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for oblong face shape:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Playing up your natural texture can help to create a more effortless, flattering hairstyle. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, work with your natural texture to create a style that complements your face shape.

2. Experiment with Parting

Changing up your part can help to create a new look and flatter your face shape. Try a side part or a deep side part to add volume and dimension to your hair.

3. Consider a Bold Color

A bold hair color can help to draw attention to your features and create a more striking overall appearance. Consider opting for a vibrant shade such as red or purple to add some personality to your hairstyle.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer, such as long, straight hair or center parts. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of the face.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with oblong face shape?

A high ponytail can actually elongate an oblong face shape even further. Instead, consider a low ponytail or a half-up, half-down style that adds volume to the sides of the face.

Do I need to use special products when styling my hair for oblong face shape?

While you don't necessarily need to use special products, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner and a styling product such as mousse or volumizing spray can help to add volume and lift to your hair.

Hair Care

When styling your hair for oblong face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for healthy, beautiful hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle on your hair and scalp to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using heat styling tools such as a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for oblong face shape, it's time to start experimenting with different styles and finding the perfect look for you. Remember to keep these tips in mind and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
