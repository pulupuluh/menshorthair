Does Cutting Hair Make It Curlier

Written by Robby May 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have naturally straight hair and you’re looking for ways to add some curl or wave to your locks, you may have heard that cutting your hair can make it curlier. But is this actually true? In short, no, cutting your hair does not make it curlier. However, there are some ways you can change the appearance of your hair to look curlier after a haircut.

How to

If you want to make your hair look curlier after a haircut, there are a few things you can do:

  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance any natural waves or curls
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair

Step by Step

If you want to use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or hot roller
  3. Hold for a few seconds, then release the hair
  4. Repeat with the rest of your hair
  5. Once all your hair is curled, use your fingers to break up the curls for a more natural look
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to make your hair look curlier:

  • Start with a good haircut – a haircut that is well-suited to your hair type and face shape can help enhance your natural texture and make your hair look curlier
  • Avoid using too much heat – excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look frizzy and dull
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether cutting hair makes it curlier:

Does cutting hair make it curlier?

No, cutting your hair does not make it curlier. The texture of your hair is determined by your genetics and cannot be changed by a haircut.

What can I do to make my hair look curlier?

You can use a curling iron, hot rollers, or a diffuser to add curls or waves to your hair. You can also use texturizing sprays or mousses to add volume and texture.

What kind of haircut is best for making hair look curlier?

A haircut that is well-suited to your hair type and face shape can help enhance your natural texture and make your hair look curlier. Talk to your stylist about what kind of haircut would work best for your hair.

Hair Care

When trying to make your hair look curlier, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy

In conclusion, cutting your hair does not make it curlier. However, there are plenty of ways you can add curls or waves to your hair after a haircut. By using heat styling tools, texturizing products, and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the look you want.
