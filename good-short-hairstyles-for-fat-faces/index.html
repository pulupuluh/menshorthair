Good Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces Flattering Cuts For Every Occasion

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry – there are plenty of good short hairstyles for fat faces that can help you look your best. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for choosing the perfect cut, as well as some of our favorite styles for every occasion. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Before we dive into specific styles, it's important to understand how to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. If you have a round or chubby face, you'll want to look for styles that add height and length to your face, while minimizing width. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid styles that add volume at the sides, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Look for cuts that have layers, which can add movement and texture to your hair.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs, which can help to elongate your face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get a Short Haircut for a Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a flattering haircut, let's talk about some specific styles to consider. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut for a round face:

  1. Start by booking a consultation with a stylist who has experience working with round faces.
  2. Bring in some inspiration photos of short hairstyles that you like, but be open to your stylist's suggestions as well.
  3. Discuss your face shape and any concerns you have about certain styles.
  4. Work with your stylist to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture.
  5. If you're nervous about going too short, start with a longer bob and gradually go shorter as you get more comfortable.
  6. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your new cut at home.
  7. Enjoy your fresh new look!

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Once you've got your new cut, it's important to know how to style it to flatter your face shape. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid parting your hair down the middle, as this can emphasize the roundness of your face. Instead, try a side part.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height at the crown of your head.
  • Consider adding some texture to your hair with a curling iron or texturizing spray.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy updo or a sleek bob.

FAQ: Short Hairstyles for Fat Faces

Here are some common questions people have about finding the right short hairstyle for a round face:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height and length to your face, while minimizing width. Consider a layered cut with a side-swept fringe or bangs.

Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

A pixie cut can be a great option for a round face, as long as it's styled in a way that adds height and length to your face. Consider a layered pixie with a side-swept fringe.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides, as this can make your face look wider. Also, avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles

Once you've found the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Our Favorite Short Hairstyles for Fat Faces

Now that you have some tips and tricks for finding the perfect short haircut for your face shape, let's take a look at some of our favorite styles for every occasion:

The Classic Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on almost everyone. For a round face, opt for a longer bob that hits just below the chin. Add some layers for movement and texture.

The Textured Pixie

A textured pixie cut can be a great option for a round face, as long as it's styled in a way that adds height and length. Add some choppy layers and a side-swept fringe for a modern look.

The Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut can help to elongate a round face, as it draws the eye downward. Try a short cut on one side and a longer cut on the other.

The Wavy Lob

A wavy lob (long bob) can be a great option for a round face, as long as it's styled with loose, beachy waves. Add some layers to keep it from looking too heavy.

The Side-Swept Bob

A side-swept bob can help to elongate a round face, as it draws the eye downward. Add some layers for movement and texture.

We hope this article has helped you find some inspiration for your next short haircut. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
