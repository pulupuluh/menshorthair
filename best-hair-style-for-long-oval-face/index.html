Best Hair Style For Long Oval Face

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't complement your long oval face shape? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will share with you the best hair styles for long oval face shape, tips, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking fabulous. So, let's dive in!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

Long oval face shape is one of the most versatile face shapes, and you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, some hairstyles can enhance your facial features and make you look stunning. The best hairstyles for long oval face shape are those that add width to the forehead or cheeks and balance out the length of the face.

How to

Choosing the right hairstyle for your long oval face shape can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair style:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions to create the best hair style for your long oval face shape:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face
  3. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or sleek straight hair
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best hair style for your long oval face shape:

  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and movement
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best
  • Ask your stylist for advice on the best hair style for your face shape and hair texture

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair style for long oval face shape:

  • Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut if I have a long oval face shape?
  • A: Yes, you can, but make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length.
  • Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a long oval face shape?
  • A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs, but make sure to choose a style that adds width to your forehead.
  • Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a long oval face shape?
  • A: Yes, you can, but make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is crucial for any hair style. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know the best hair style for long oval face shape, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain it regularly to keep it looking fabulous.
