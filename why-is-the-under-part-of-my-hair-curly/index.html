Why is the Under Part of My Hair Curly? | FAQ & Tips

Are you tired of dealing with your curly hair? Do you ever wonder why the under part of your hair is always curly, no matter what you do? You're not alone. This is a common question that many people ask. In this article, we will discuss the reasons behind the curly under part of your hair and provide some tips and tricks to help you manage it.

Why is the Under Part of My Hair Curly?

The under part of your hair is curly because of genetics. Your hair follicles determine the shape and texture of your hair, and this is something that cannot be changed. The shape of the hair follicles on the under part of your head is different from the hair follicles on the top of your head, which is what causes the difference in texture.

Another reason why the under part of your hair may be curly is due to the way you style it. If you constantly put your hair up in a bun or ponytail, it can cause the hair on the top of your head to be straighter, while the hair on the under part of your head will be curly due to the constant tugging and pulling.

How to Manage Curly Hair?

Managing curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques, it can be done. Here are some tips to help you manage curly hair:

Step-by-Step Guide to Managing Curly Hair

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it hydrated.

3. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help maintain the shape of your curls.

4. Avoid using hair brushes, as they can cause frizz and breakage.

5. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and breakage.

Tips and Tricks to Keep Your Hair Healthy

1. Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

2. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated.

3. Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it healthy and shiny.

4. Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten the under part of my hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten the under part of your hair, but keep in mind that constantly using heat styling tools can cause damage and breakage.

Q: Can I use regular hair products on my curly hair?

A: It is best to use hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they contain ingredients that can help keep your curls defined and hydrated.
