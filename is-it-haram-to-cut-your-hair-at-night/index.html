Is It Haram To Cut Your Hair At Night

Written by Aprilia Mar 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a Muslim, you may have heard that it's haram to cut your hair at night. But is this true? In this article, we'll explore this topic and answer some common questions related to hair cutting in Islam.

Description

Many Muslims believe that cutting your hair at night is haram. This belief is based on a hadith narrated by Abu Hurayrah, in which the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Whoever cuts his hair during the night, the devil will come to him and make him bald." However, some scholars argue that this hadith is weak and cannot be used as a basis for making a ruling.

How to Cut Your Hair

If you decide to cut your hair, it's important to do it in a way that is in line with Islamic teachings. Here are some tips:

Step 1: Make an intention

Before cutting your hair, make an intention to do it for the sake of Allah. This will turn a mundane act into a spiritual one.

Step 2: Say Bismillah

Start by saying Bismillah (in the name of Allah). This will remind you that you're cutting your hair for the sake of Allah.

Step 3: Use clean scissors

Make sure your scissors are clean and free from any impurities. This is important because impurities can nullify your wudu (ablution).

Step 4: Cut a little at a time

When cutting your hair, cut a little at a time. This will help you avoid making any mistakes and will ensure that your hair is cut evenly.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair:

  • Don't cut your hair too short. According to some scholars, it's haram to shave your head completely.
  • Don't cut your hair in a way that imitates the hairstyle of non-Muslims.
  • Don't cut your hair during the first ten days of Dhul-Hijjah (the month of Hajj).

FAQ

Is it haram to cut your hair at night?

There is no clear ruling on this issue. Some scholars say it's haram, while others say it's permissible. It's up to you to decide based on your own beliefs and the evidence you have.

Can I cut my hair during my period?

Yes, you can. There is no prohibition on cutting your hair during your period.

Can I cut my hair during Ramadan?

Yes, you can. There is no prohibition on cutting your hair during Ramadan.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair is just one aspect of hair care in Islam. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use natural oils like olive oil or coconut oil to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid breakage.

Conclusion

While there is no clear ruling on whether cutting your hair at night is haram or not, it's important to approach hair cutting in a way that is in line with Islamic teachings. Make an intention, say Bismillah, use clean scissors, and cut a little at a time. And remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
