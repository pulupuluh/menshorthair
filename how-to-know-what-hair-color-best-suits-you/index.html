How To Know What Hair Color Best Suits You

Written by Christine May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair color? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially when there are so many options available. The last thing you want is to end up with a hair color that does not suit you. But how do you know what hair color best suits you? In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect hair color for your skin tone, eye color, and personality.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in choosing the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone falls into one of two categories: warm or cool. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins are blue, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins are green, you have a warm skin tone.

Next, you need to determine your eye color. Your eye color can also play a role in which hair color suits you best. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors such as golden blonde and copper can complement your eye color. On the other hand, cool hair colors such as platinum blonde and ash brown can complement green or hazel eyes.

Once you have determined your skin tone and eye color, it's time to consider your personality. Are you someone who likes to stand out in a crowd? Or do you prefer a more natural look? If you like to stand out, you may want to consider a bold hair color such as red or purple. If you prefer a more natural look, you may want to consider a hair color that is close to your natural hair color.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.

2. Determine your eye color.

3. Consider your personality.

4. Research different hair colors that complement your skin tone and eye color.

5. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair color for you.

Tips

- If you're unsure about a hair color, try a temporary color first.

- Consider the maintenance required for different hair colors.

- Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a hair dye that is specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Always do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's already been dyed?

A: Yes, but it's important to consider the condition of your hair. If your hair is damaged from previous dye jobs, it may not be able to handle another round of coloring.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your perfect hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it healthy. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and dryness. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color from your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and personality, you can find a hair color that suits you best. Don't be afraid to consult with a professional stylist to get their expert opinion. With the right hair color, you can feel confident and beautiful.
