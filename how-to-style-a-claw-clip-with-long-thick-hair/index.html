How To Style A Claw Clip With Long Thick Hair

Claw clips have been in fashion for decades, and they are still a very popular hair accessory today. They are perfect for those who have long, thick hair and want to keep it out of their face. If you have long, thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it properly. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

How to Style a Claw Clip

Styling a claw clip with long thick hair can be a bit tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips that will help you style your hair with a claw clip:

1. Choose the Right Size

The first thing you need to do is choose the right size claw clip. If you have long, thick hair, you'll need a larger clip. A small clip won't be able to hold all of your hair properly. So, choose a clip that is big enough to hold all of your hair comfortably.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair with a claw clip, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, it will be difficult to style and won't hold properly. So, make sure your hair is dry and tangle-free.

3. Choose the Right Style

There are many different styles you can create with a claw clip. You can create a messy bun, a half-up-half-down style, or even a simple ponytail. Choose the style that suits your hair and the occasion.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your hair with a claw clip:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to style your hair with a claw clip.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather all of your hair and pull it back as if you were going to create a ponytail. But instead of securing it with an elastic band, hold it with one hand.

Step 3: Position the Claw Clip

With your other hand, position the claw clip at the base of your head, where you want to secure your hair.

Step 4: Secure the Claw Clip

Clamp the claw clip down on your hair, making sure that it holds all of your hair securely. You may need to adjust the clip a few times to get it just right.

Step 5: Adjust Your Hair

Once the claw clip is secure, adjust your hair to create the style you want. You can pull some hair out at the front to create a more relaxed look or leave it all up for a more formal style.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips that will help you style your hair with a claw clip:

1. Use Hairspray

If you want your style to last all day, use some hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling out of the clip.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can create a messy bun, a half-up-half-down style, or even a simple ponytail. Try different styles and see what works best for you.

3. Use Bobby Pins

If you have very thick hair, you can use bobby pins to help secure your hair in place. Just insert the bobby pins into the hair that is not held by the claw clip.

FAQs

1. Can I use a claw clip on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a claw clip on wet hair. Wet hair is more fragile and can break easily, so it's best to wait until your hair is dry before styling it with a claw clip.

2. How long can I leave a claw clip in my hair?

You can leave a claw clip in your hair for as long as you like. However, it's recommended to remove it before going to bed to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your long thick hair healthy, follow these hair care tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often. These tools can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

3. Trim Your Ends Regularly

Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Styling your long thick hair with a claw clip is easy once you know how to do it. Just follow the tips and steps outlined in this article, and you'll have a stylish and comfortable hairstyle in no time!
