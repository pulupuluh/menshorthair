Short Layered Cuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unmanageable thick, wavy hair? A short layered haircut might be the solution you are looking for. Short layered cuts are perfect for thick wavy hair as they add movement and texture to your hair, making it easier to style and maintain. In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about short layered cuts for thick wavy hair, including how to get the perfect cut, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

Short layered cuts for thick wavy hair are a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair to different lengths, creating layers. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, making it easier to manage and style. This style is perfect for those who have thick wavy hair, as the layers help to reduce the weight of the hair, making it more manageable.

How to

Getting the perfect short layered cut for thick wavy hair requires a few steps. The first step is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting thick wavy hair. Once you have found the right hairstylist, the next step is to communicate the style you want. It is important to be specific about the length and layers you want.

During the haircut, your hairstylist will cut your hair into different lengths, creating layers. The layers will be shorter at the top and gradually get longer as they move towards the bottom. This creates a textured and choppy look that is perfect for thick wavy hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a short layered cut for thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Find the right hairstylist

Look for a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting thick wavy hair. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or do some research online to find a hairstylist near you.

Step 2: Communicate the style you want

Be specific about the length and layers you want. Show your hairstylist pictures of the style you want to achieve.

Step 3: Cut the hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair into different lengths, creating layers. The layers will be shorter at the top and gradually get longer as they move towards the bottom.

Step 4: Style the hair

You can style your short layered cut for thick wavy hair in various ways. You can use a curling iron to create loose waves or use a straightener for a sleeker look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short layered cut for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layered cuts for thick wavy hair:

Q: Will a short layered cut work for all face shapes?

A: Short layered cuts can work for all face shapes, but it is important to find the right length and layers to flatter your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short layered cut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short layered cut. You can use bobby pins and hair ties to create different hairstyles.

Hair Care

Maintaining a short layered cut for thick wavy hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

In conclusion, short layered cuts for thick wavy hair are a great way to add movement and texture to your hair. With the right hairstylist, communication, and hair care, you can achieve the perfect short layered cut for your hair. Remember to maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using proper hair care techniques.
