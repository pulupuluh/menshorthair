How To Refresh Curly Hair In The Morning A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of waking up to a tangled mess of curly hair every morning? Do you dread the thought of having to style your hair from scratch every day? If so, you're not alone. Many women with curly hair struggle with maintaining their curls throughout the day. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to refresh curly hair in the morning so that you can start your day with beautiful, defined curls.

Why Do Curly Hair Need Refreshing?

Curly hair tends to lose its shape and definition overnight due to friction from pillowcases, hats, and even our own hands. This can leave your curls looking frizzy, flat, and lifeless in the morning. Refreshing your curls in the morning is a quick and easy way to bring them back to life and restore their natural bounce and definition.

How to Refresh Curly Hair in the Morning

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to refresh your curly hair in the morning:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair with a spray bottle filled with water. Be sure to spray your hair evenly, focusing on the areas that need the most attention.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your wet hair. This will help to moisturize your curls and prevent frizz. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to reform. Be sure to scrunch gently so that you don't disrupt the curl pattern.

Step 4: Apply a Curl Defining Product

Apply a curl defining product to your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Be sure to use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 6: Finish with a Hair Oil

Finish by applying a hair oil to your ends to help seal in moisture and prevent frizz. Be sure to apply the oil sparingly so that your hair doesn't look greasy.

Tips for Refreshing Curly Hair in the Morning

Here are some additional tips to help you refresh your curly hair in the morning:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent frizz
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls
  • Apply a curl refreshing spray throughout the day to revive your curls

FAQ

How often should I refresh my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some women refresh their hair every day, while others do it every other day or every few days. Experiment with different routines to find what works best for you.

Can I refresh my curly hair without getting it wet?

Yes, you can use a curl refreshing spray to revive your curls without getting your hair wet. Simply spray the product onto your curls and scrunch with your hands to reform your curls.

What if I don't have a diffuser attachment for my hair dryer?

You can use a t-shirt or microfiber towel to scrunch your hair dry instead. Simply wrap your hair in the towel and scrunch gently to absorb excess moisture.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your curly hair by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, deep conditioning once a week, and protecting your hair from heat damage. With the right care and maintenance, you can enjoy beautiful, defined curls every day.

Now that you know how to refresh curly hair in the morning, you can say goodbye to bad hair days and hello to gorgeous, defined curls. Follow these steps, experiment with different products and routines, and find what works best for you. Happy styling!
