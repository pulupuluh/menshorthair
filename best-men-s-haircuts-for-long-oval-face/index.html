Best Men s Haircuts For Long Oval Face

Written by Eveline Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a long oval face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. It's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and highlights your best features. In this article, we'll explore some of the best men's haircuts for long oval faces, as well as tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a narrow chin and forehead, with a longer length in between. The best haircuts for this face shape are those that add width and volume to the sides, while keeping the length in check. This can be achieved through layering, texture, and strategic styling.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long oval face, it's important to consider your hair type and lifestyle. If you have thick, curly hair, a longer style with layers can help to tame the curls and add volume. If you have fine hair, a shorter style with texture can create the appearance of thickness.

Some popular haircuts for long oval faces include:

  • The classic crew cut
  • The textured crop
  • The side-swept fringe
  • The messy quiff
  • The slicked-back undercut

Step by step

If you're looking to get a haircut for your long oval face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Take some time to research different hairstyles and find inspiration.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Bring photos of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  4. Ask your hairstylist for styling tips and product recommendations.
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and at-home styling.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Invest in quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair, rather than your fingers.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for best results.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long oval face?

The best haircut for a long oval face is one that adds width and volume to the sides. This can be achieved through layering, texture, and strategic styling.

Can I pull off a short haircut with a long oval face?

Yes, a short haircut can work well with a long oval face. Just be sure to choose a style that adds volume and texture to the sides, rather than elongating the face further.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, great-looking hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too frequently.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best no matter what your face shape. Whether you prefer a classic crew cut or a trendy textured crop, there is a style out there that will suit your needs and enhance your natural features.
