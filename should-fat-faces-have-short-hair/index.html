Should Fat Faces Have Short Hair

Written by Oliver May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a popular trend among women, but many wonder if it is suitable for those with a round or fat face. In this article, we will explore whether or not fat faces should have short hair, how to choose the right short hairstyle, and provide tips for hair care. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • FAQ

Description

When it comes to hairstyles, there are many factors to consider, such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For those with a round or fat face, the question of whether or not to have short hair can be daunting. However, the answer is not a simple yes or no. While some short hairstyles may not complement a round face, there are many options that can look great.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

The key to choosing the right short hairstyle for a round or fat face is to find one that creates the illusion of length and slims down the face. Some popular options include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that is perfect for those with fine hair. It creates the illusion of height and elongates the face. This style is also low maintenance and easy to style.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape. For those with a round face, a long bob that hits just below the chin can create the illusion of length.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a trendy hairstyle that can be tailored to suit any face shape. For those with a round face, an asymmetrical cut that is longer on one side can create the illusion of length and slim down the face.

Step by Step Guide

If you decide to go for a short hairstyle, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Choose the right hairstyle for your face shape
  2. Consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for you
  3. Maintain regular haircuts to keep the style looking fresh
  4. Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair
  5. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you

Tips for Hair Care

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep the style looking fresh

FAQ

Can short hair make a round face look even rounder?

Yes, some short hairstyles can make a round face look even rounder. However, with the right cut and styling, short hair can create the illusion of length and slim down the face.

Is a pixie cut a good option for those with a round face?

Yes, a pixie cut can be a great option for those with a round face. It creates the illusion of height and elongates the face. However, it is important to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for you.

How often should I get my hair cut?

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking sharp.

What styling products should I use for short hair?

For short hair, it is best to use lightweight styling products that add volume and texture without weighing down your hair. Some good options include mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo.

In conclusion, whether or not fat faces should have short hair is a matter of personal preference and finding the right style that complements your face shape. With the right cut and styling, short hair can be a great option for those with a round or fat face. Just remember to maintain regular haircuts and use the right products for healthy, stylish hair.
