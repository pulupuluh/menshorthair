How To Wear A Wide Headband A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair accessories? Want to add some flair to your hairstyle? One great option is the wide headband. Not only is it stylish, but it can also keep your hair out of your face. However, if you’re not sure how to wear a wide headband, don’t worry! This guide will give you all the tips and tricks you need.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Wide Headband
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Wide Headband
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Wide Headband

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s talk about the different ways you can wear a wide headband.

First, you can wear it across your forehead, creating a bohemian look. Second, you can wear it further back on your head, like a traditional headband. Finally, you can wear it over your hair, like a hat.

No matter how you choose to wear it, a wide headband is a versatile accessory that can add some fun to any outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways to wear a wide headband, let’s go through the steps for each.

Across your forehead

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Place the headband on your forehead, with the widest part at the front.
  3. Secure the headband at the back of your head with bobby pins.
  4. If you have any flyaways, use hairspray or gel to keep them in place.

Further back on your head

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Place the headband on top of your head, just behind your hairline.
  3. Secure the headband at the back of your head with bobby pins.
  4. If you have any flyaways, use hairspray or gel to keep them in place.

Over your hair

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Place the headband over your hair, with the widest part at the front.
  3. Adjust the headband to your liking.

Tips for Wearing a Wide Headband

Now that you know how to wear a wide headband, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a headband that matches your outfit.
  • Experiment with different styles and colors.
  • Use hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Here are some common questions about wearing a wide headband:

Can I wear a wide headband with short hair?

Yes! A wide headband can add some fun to a short hairstyle.

Can I wear a wide headband to work?

It depends on your workplace’s dress code. If you’re not sure, it’s best to stick with a more traditional accessory.

Will a wide headband damage my hair?

No, as long as you’re not pulling your hair too tight.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some tips to keep your hair healthy while wearing a wide headband:

  • Don’t wear a headband for too long, as it can lead to breakage.
  • Take breaks between wearing headbands to let your hair breathe.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to wear a wide headband, it’s time to try it out for yourself! With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to add some fun and flair to any hairstyle.
