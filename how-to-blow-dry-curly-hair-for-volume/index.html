How To Blow Dry Curly Hair For Volume

Written by Robby Aug 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this blog post, we will discuss how to blow dry curly hair for volume. If you have curly hair, you may have noticed that it can be challenging to add volume to your hair. Blow drying your hair properly can help to add volume and make your curls look bouncier. Follow these steps to achieve voluminous curls.

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair for Volume

Step 1: Start with Clean and Moisturized Hair

Before blow drying your hair, make sure that your hair is clean and moisturized. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Use a Diffuser Attachment

A diffuser attachment is a must-have when blow drying curly hair. It helps to distribute the heat evenly and prevents the curls from getting frizzy. Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to low or medium heat.

Step 3: Flip Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your hair upside down and start diffusing your hair from the roots to the tips. This will help to add volume to your curls. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you diffuse it.

Step 4: Finish with a Blast of Cool Air

Once you have diffused your hair, finish with a blast of cool air to set your curls. This will help to lock in the volume and give your curls a bouncy look.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair for Volume

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before blow drying.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before blow drying to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair before blow drying. Avoid using a regular towel as it can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair while blow drying to prevent frizz.

FAQ about Blow Drying Curly Hair for Volume

Can I blow dry my curly hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause heat damage and make your curls dry and brittle. It is best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week and let your hair air dry on other days.

What kind of blow dryer should I use for curly hair?

Look for a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment and adjustable heat settings. Choose a blow dryer with a ceramic or tourmaline coating as it helps to reduce frizz and static.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause breakage and frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Blow drying your curly hair for volume can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve bouncy and voluminous curls. Follow these tips and tricks to get the most out of your blow drying routine and keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.
