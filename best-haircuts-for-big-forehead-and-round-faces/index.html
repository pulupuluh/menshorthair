Best Haircuts For Big Forehead And Round Faces In 2023

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a big forehead and a round face, you might be wondering what the best haircut is for you. Fortunately, there are plenty of options that can flatter your features and make you feel confident. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for big forehead and round faces in 2023, including how to style them and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Big Forehead and Round Faces

Description

The best haircuts for big forehead and round faces are those that create balance and proportion. A good haircut can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the forehead, while also accentuating the cheekbones and jawline. There are a variety of haircuts that can achieve this effect, from short pixie cuts to long layers. The key is to choose a style that complements your unique features and personality.

How to Choose the Best Haircut

When choosing a haircut for big forehead and round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the shape of your face. Round faces benefit from hairstyles that create angles and lengthen the face, while big foreheads benefit from hairstyles that cover or minimize the forehead. Second, think about your hair texture and thickness. Some haircuts work better with straight, fine hair, while others are better suited for thick, curly hair. Finally, consider your personal style and lifestyle. Choose a haircut that you feel comfortable with and that fits your daily routine.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

For round faces, the best haircuts are those that add height and length to the face. A short pixie cut with volume on top can create the illusion of a longer face, as can a long bob with layers. Bangs can also help to elongate the face, especially if they are side-swept or asymmetrical. Avoid haircuts that end at the chin or are too blunt, as they can make the face appear wider.

Best Haircuts for Big Foreheads

If you have a big forehead, consider a haircut with bangs or layers that can cover or minimize the forehead. A side-swept bang or long, wispy bang can create a soft, feminine look, while a blunt bang can create a bold, edgy look. Layers can also help to frame the face and draw attention away from the forehead. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too slicked back, as they can accentuate the forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the best haircut for your big forehead and round face, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your haircut looking great, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your haircut.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a big forehead and round face?

The best haircut for a big forehead and round face is one that creates balance and proportion. Consider a haircut with bangs or layers that can cover or minimize the forehead, and a style that adds height and length to the face.

Can I wear a short haircut with a big forehead and round face?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut with a big forehead and round face. Consider a pixie cut with volume on top, or a short bob with layers.

What hair care products should I use for my big forehead and round face?

Choose hair care products that are suited to your hair type, such as a shampoo and conditioner for fine or curly hair. Invest in a heat protectant spray, as well as styling products like hairspray and mousse.

Hair Care for Big Forehead and Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling products, there are a few hair care tips that can help to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut and styling products, you can create a look that flatters your big forehead and round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
