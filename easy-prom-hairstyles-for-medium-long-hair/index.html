Easy Prom Hairstyles For Medium Long Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for some easy prom hairstyles for your medium long hair? If so, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best hairstyles that are not only easy to do but also perfect for prom night. So, whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Prom night is one of the most important events in every high school student's life. It's a night where you get to dress up, dance, and have a good time with your friends. And of course, you want to look your best. That's why choosing the right hairstyle is crucial. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide on the perfect one. That's why we've put together a list of easy prom hairstyles that will make you look stunning on your special night.

How to

Before we get started with the hairstyles, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Use hair accessories to add some glam to your hairstyle.
  • Practice your hairstyle before prom night to avoid any last-minute disasters.

Loose Waves

Loose waves are a classic and easy prom hairstyle for medium long hair. To achieve this look, you'll need a curling iron or wand. Start by dividing your hair into sections and curl each section away from your face. Once you've curled all sections, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a natural wave. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their long hair without having it all in their face. To achieve this look, start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Tie the top section into a ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. You can also add some volume to the crown by teasing the hair before tying it up. Leave the bottom section of your hair down and add some curls or waves for a more romantic look.

Braided Updo

If you're looking for a more elegant and sophisticated hairstyle, then a braided updo is the way to go. Start by creating a low ponytail and tying it with a hair tie. Divide the ponytail into three sections and create a simple braid. Once you've braided the entire length of your hair, wrap it around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. You can also add some flowers or other hair accessories to make the look more romantic.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for creating an easy prom hairstyle for medium long hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before starting with any hairstyle. This will make it easier to work with and hold the style better.

Step 2: Choose your hairstyle.

Decide on the hairstyle you want to create and gather all the necessary tools and accessories.

Step 3: Prep your hair.

Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any hot tools. This will protect your hair from damage and breakage.

Step 4: Create your hairstyle.

Follow the instructions for your chosen hairstyle and create the look you want.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray.

Once you've created your hairstyle, finish off with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating easy prom hairstyles for medium long hair:

  • Use hair accessories to add some sparkle and glam to your hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles before prom night.
  • Practice your hairstyle at least a few times before prom night to avoid any last-minute disasters.
  • Take care of your hair by using a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

FAQ

What are some easy prom hairstyles for medium long hair?

Some easy prom hairstyles for medium long hair include loose waves, half-up half-down, and braided updos.

What should I do to prepare my hair before creating a prom hairstyle?

You should start with clean, dry hair and apply a heat protectant before using any hot tools.

What are some tips for maintaining healthy hair?

To maintain healthy hair, use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week, avoid using too much heat on your hair, and eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients.

With these easy prom hairstyles, you'll be sure to turn heads on your special night. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Good luck and have fun!
