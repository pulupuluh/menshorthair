Does Blonde Hair Make You Look Fatter The Truth Behind The Myth

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Blonde hair has always been a popular choice for women around the world. It is a versatile hair color that can be styled in a variety of ways and looks great on almost any skin tone. However, there has been a long-standing myth that blonde hair can make you look fatter. In this article, we will explore the truth behind this myth and give you tips on how to style your blonde hair to look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The myth that blonde hair makes you look fatter is based on the idea that lighter colors tend to make things look larger. This is true to an extent, but it is not the whole story. The truth is that your hair color does not make you look fatter or thinner. Your body shape and size are determined by your genetics, diet, and exercise habits.

However, the color of your clothing and hair can affect the way you look. Wearing darker colors can create a slimming effect, while lighter colors can make you look larger. This is why the myth that blonde hair makes you look fatter has persisted for so long.

How To

If you are concerned about whether your blonde hair is making you look larger, there are a few things you can do to create a more slimming effect. Here are some tips:

1. Choose the Right Shade

The shade of blonde you choose can make a big difference in how you look. If you have a fair complexion, a lighter shade of blonde may wash you out and make you look paler. On the other hand, if you have a darker complexion, a lighter shade of blonde can create a striking contrast and make you stand out.

2. Get a Flattering Cut

The right haircut can also make a big difference in how you look. If you have a round face or carry weight in your chin or neck area, a longer hairstyle with layers can help to slim your face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or shorter, bob-style cuts that can make your face look wider.

3. Use Highlights and Lowlights

Adding highlights and lowlights to your blonde hair can create depth and dimension, which can help to create a more slimming effect. Darker lowlights can create a shadow effect that can make your face look slimmer, while lighter highlights can create a brightening effect that can make your face look more vibrant.

Step By Step

If you are unsure how to achieve a slimming effect with your blonde hair, here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose a Flattering Shade

Start by choosing a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone. If you have a fair complexion, a golden or honey blonde shade can add warmth to your skin. If you have a darker complexion, a platinum or ashy blonde shade can create a striking contrast.

Step 2: Get a Flattering Cut

Next, choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, a longer hairstyle with layers can help to slim your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, a bob with side-swept bangs can help to balance your features.

Step 3: Add Highlights and Lowlights

Finally, add highlights and lowlights to create depth and dimension. Darker lowlights can create a shadow effect that can make your face look slimmer, while lighter highlights can create a brightening effect that can make your face look more vibrant.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your blonde hair:

1. Wear Darker Colors

As we mentioned earlier, wearing darker colors can create a slimming effect. Try pairing your blonde hair with darker clothing to create a more flattering look.

2. Use Hair Products

Using hair products like volumizing mousse or texturizing spray can help to create a more voluminous, slimming effect. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

3. Consider Your Makeup

Makeup can also help to create a slimming effect. Try using contouring techniques to create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

FAQ

Q: Does blonde hair make you look fatter?

A: No, your hair color does not make you look fatter or thinner. Your body shape and size are determined by your genetics, diet, and exercise habits.

Q: What shade of blonde is most slimming?

A: The most slimming shade of blonde depends on your skin tone. If you have a fair complexion, a golden or honey blonde shade can add warmth to your skin. If you have a darker complexion, a platinum or ashy blonde shade can create a striking contrast.

Q: What haircut is most slimming?

A: The most slimming haircut depends on your face shape. If you have a round face, a longer hairstyle with layers can help to slim your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, a bob with side-swept bangs can help to balance your features.

Hair Care

Finally, it is important to take good care of your blonde hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Blonde hair can sometimes turn brassy or yellow over time. Using a purple shampoo can help to neutralize these tones and keep your hair looking fresh and bright.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Blonde hair can be more prone to damage than other hair colors. Deep conditioning regularly can help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

3. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

In conclusion, the myth that blonde hair makes you look fatter is just that â a myth. Your hair color does not determine your body shape or size. However, the way you style your hair can affect the way you look. By choosing a flattering shade, haircut, and using highlights and lowlights, you can create a more slimming effect. Remember to take good care of your blonde hair to keep it looking its best.
