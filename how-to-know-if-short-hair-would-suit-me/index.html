How To Know If Short Hair Would Suit Me

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hairstyle but are hesitant to try a short hairdo? Short hair is a bold and stylish choice, but it's understandable to feel unsure if it would suit you. In this article, we will discuss how to know if short hair would suit you, so you can make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • Determining your face shape
  • Considering your hair texture
  • Assessing your lifestyle
  • Tips for maintaining short hair
  • Frequently asked questions

Determining your face shape

The first step in determining if short hair would suit you is to consider your face shape. Short hair can be flattering on any face shape, but certain styles may be more complementary than others. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: A short bob or pixie cut can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Square face: Soft, layered cuts can help soften the angles of your face.
  • Heart-shaped face: Chin-length bobs or side-swept bangs can balance out a wider forehead.
  • Oval face: Lucky you! Almost any short hairstyle will suit your face shape.

Of course, these are just guidelines, and you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Considering your hair texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair would suit you. Some textures may be more challenging to style, while others may work better with certain cuts. Here are some tips:

  • Straight hair: Short bobs, pixie cuts, and blunt cuts can work well with straight hair.
  • Curly hair: Short layered cuts can help enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Thick hair: Short, layered cuts can help thin out your hair and make it more manageable.
  • Fine hair: A pixie cut or bob can help add volume and texture to fine hair.

Remember, you can always ask your stylist for recommendations based on your hair texture.

Assessing your lifestyle

Before committing to a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Short hair requires more frequent trims to maintain its shape, and styling can take more time and effort. Here are some questions to ask yourself:

  • How much time do I have to style my hair each day?
  • Am I willing to commit to more frequent trims?
  • Will a short hairstyle suit my profession or personal style?

Ultimately, you want a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle and makes you feel confident.

Tips for maintaining short hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Invest in a good styling product to help add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.

With proper care, short hair can be a low-maintenance and stylish choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will short hair make me look older?

Short hair can actually make you look younger and more vibrant, especially if you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

2. Can I still style short hair?

Absolutely! There are many styling options for short hair, from tousled waves to sleek bobs. Talk to your stylist about which products and tools will work best for your hair type.

3. Will short hair suit my body type?

Short hair can complement any body type, as long as you choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. You can also experiment with different lengths and cuts to find what works best for you.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can determine if short hair would suit you and make a confident decision about your hairstyle. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel comfortable and confident in your own skin.
