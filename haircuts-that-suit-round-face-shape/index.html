Haircuts That Suit Round Face Shape A Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face shape, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features and accentuates your best qualities. Luckily, there are plenty of haircuts that suit round face shapes, and we're here to help you find the perfect one for you. Read on for our guide to the best haircuts for round faces in 2023.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of more defined cheekbones and a slimmer jawline. The right haircut can do wonders for your face shape and make you feel more confident and beautiful.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a round face shape, you want to look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, while keeping the sides of your hair closer to your face. This helps to elongate your face and create a more angular look. You also want to avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are all relatively similar, you have a round face shape.

2. Look for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, while keeping the sides of your hair closer to your face.

3. Avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder.

4. Consider adding layers to your haircut to create movement and texture.

5. Experiment with bangs. Side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face, while blunt bangs can make your face look rounder.

Tips

- Consider trying an asymmetrical haircut, with one side longer than the other, to create a more angular look.

- If you have curly hair, experiment with shorter layers to create volume at the crown of your head.

- Don't be afraid to try new things! The right haircut can make all the difference.

FAQ

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a round face. However, you may want to consider adding layers or a side-swept bang to create a more angular look.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut for your round face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Limit your use of hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can feel confident and beautiful with a round face shape. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
