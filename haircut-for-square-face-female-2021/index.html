Haircut For Square Face Female 2021 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Aprilia Apr 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a female with a square face shape and looking for a new haircut? Fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this guide, we’ll provide you with tips, tricks, and inspiration for the best haircuts for square face females in 2021. Let’s get started!

Description

First, it’s important to understand what a square face shape looks like. If you have a square face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all of a similar width. Your face shape can be accentuated or softened with the right haircut.

How To

The key to finding the perfect haircut for a square face is to opt for styles that soften the angles of your face. Hairstyles with layers and texture help to create movement and soften harsh lines. Bangs can also be a great option, as they can help to shorten a long forehead and balance out a square jawline.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape. If you’re not sure whether you have a square face, take a photo of yourself facing the camera head-on and draw a line across your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the lines are of a similar width, you have a square face.

2. Consider your hair texture. Different hair textures may require different haircuts. For example, those with curly hair may want to avoid blunt cuts and opt for layers instead.

3. Look for inspiration. Browse online for haircuts for square face females in 2021, or ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

4. Book an appointment with a skilled hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you achieve the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for square face females in 2021:

  • Opt for layered haircuts to soften harsh lines.
  • Consider side-swept bangs to balance out a square jawline.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make a square face shape appear more severe.
  • Experiment with different lengths to find what flatters your face shape the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for square face females in 2021:

What is the best haircut for a square face female?

The best haircut for a square face female is one that softens harsh lines and creates movement. Layered haircuts and side-swept bangs are great options.

Can a square face female have short hair?

Yes, a square face female can have short hair! Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

What haircuts should square face females avoid?

Square face females should avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face shape appear more severe. Additionally, hairstyles that emphasize the jawline, such as chin-length bobs, may not be the most flattering.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. Additionally, regular trims can help to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut’s shape.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for a square face female in 2021 can seem daunting, but with the right tips and inspiration, it’s easy to find a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Remember to opt for layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and to experiment with different lengths to find what works best for you. Happy hairstyling!
