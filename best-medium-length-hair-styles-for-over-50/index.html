Best Medium Length Hair Styles For Over 50 In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair changes and so do our styling preferences. Medium length hair is a great option for women over 50 as it is versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit different face shapes and hair textures. In this article, we will explore the best medium length hair styles for over 50, how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hair is typically considered to be hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length is popular among women over 50 as it is not too short or too long, and can be styled in various ways. Some of the best medium length hair styles for over 50 include:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can work for different face shapes and hair textures. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, while the length is flattering and easy to style. To achieve a layered bob, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair and to cut it to your desired length.

2. Shoulder-Length Shag

A shoulder-length shag is another great option for women over 50. This hairstyle features layers that start from the crown of the head and continue down to the ends, creating a textured and voluminous look. To achieve a shoulder-length shag, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair and to cut it to your shoulder length.

3. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, then long layers might be the best medium length hair style for you. Long layers add movement and dimension to the hair, while the length can be styled in various ways. To achieve long layers, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair starting from the jawline and continuing downwards.

How to

The best way to achieve these medium length hair styles for over 50 is to visit a professional hairstylist. They have the skills and experience to cut and style your hair to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. However, if you prefer to style your hair at home, here are some tips:

1. Layered Bob

  1. Start with clean and dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and movement in your hair
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create sleekness
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

2. Shoulder-Length Shag

  1. Start with clean and dry hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and texture in your hair
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your hair

3. Long Layers

  1. Start with clean and dry hair
  2. Apply a smoothing serum to your hair
  3. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and movement in your hair
  4. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or sleekness in your hair
  5. Finish with a hairspray or a shine spray to hold the style in place and add shine

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a layered bob:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair into four parts
  3. Start at the back and work your way up, cutting the hair in small sections
  4. Use a razor or shears to create layers in the hair
  5. Texturize the ends of the hair with a razor or shears
  6. Blend the layers into the rest of the hair
  7. Style your hair using a round brush and a blow dryer
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style and maintain your medium length hair:

  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using hot tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes

FAQ

1. Will medium length hair suit my face shape?

Medium length hair can suit different face shapes such as oval, round, heart, and square. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best medium length hair style for your face shape.

2. Can I color my medium length hair?

Yes, you can color your medium length hair. However, it is important to use hair dye that is gentle and nourishing to prevent damage and dryness.

3. How often should I wash my medium length hair?

You should wash your medium length hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair texture and lifestyle. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, while under-washing can cause buildup and scalp irritation.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as it can cause breakage and damage
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids as they can cause tension and breakage
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health

In conclusion, medium length hair is a great option for women over 50 as it is versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit different face shapes and hair textures. By choosing one of the best medium length hair styles for over 50, and following the tips and hair care advice provided in this article, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy-looking hair that reflects your personal style and enhances your natural beauty.
