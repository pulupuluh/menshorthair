How To Make Oval Shape Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to enhance your facial features, one of the best ways to do it is to make your face look more oval. Having an oval face shape is considered to be the most ideal and proportionate, and it can make you look more attractive and youthful. In this guide, we'll be discussing how to make oval shape face in relaxed English language. Here's what you can expect:

  • How to Make Oval Shape Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

Description

Oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. The jawline is gently curved, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. If you have a different face shape, don't worry - there are ways to make your face look more oval.

How to Make Oval Shape Face

There are a few ways to make your face look more oval:

  • Hairstyle
  • Makeup
  • Fashion

In this guide, we'll be focusing on hairstyle, as it's the most effective way to make your face look more oval.

Step by Step Guide

Here's how you can make your face look more oval:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can make your face look more oval, you need to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Different face shapes require different hairstyles to make them look more oval. If you're not sure about your face shape, ask your hairstylist or use an online face shape calculator.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right hairstyle to make your face look more oval. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval face shape: You're lucky - you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top of your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Square face shape: Soften your strong jawline with a layered haircut that falls around your face.
  • Round face shape: Add height to your crown with a voluminous hairstyle, or go for a long, layered cut that frames your face.
  • Heart face shape: Balance out your wider forehead with a chin-length bob or a layered cut that falls around your shoulders.
  • Diamond face shape: Soften your angular cheekbones with a choppy, layered cut that falls around your chin.
  • Triangle face shape: Add volume to your crown with a layered haircut that falls around your shoulders.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut, you can style your hair to make your face look more oval. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top of your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Try hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head, as this can elongate your face.
  • Try hairstyles that frame your face, as this can make your face look more oval.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to make your face look more oval:

  • Don't forget about makeup and fashion - they can also help enhance your facial features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too harsh or severe, as this can make you look older.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're not sure about the right hairstyle for your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your face look more oval:

Q: Can I make my face look more oval with makeup?

A: Yes, you can use makeup to contour your face and make it look more oval. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour your cheekbones and jawline, and a lighter shade to highlight your forehead and chin.

Q: Can I make my face look more oval with fashion?

A: Yes, you can use fashion to create the illusion of an oval face shape. Wear tops and dresses that create a V-neckline, as this can elongate your face. Avoid turtlenecks and high-necked tops, as they can make your face look wider.

Q: Can I make my face look more oval without changing my hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can use accessories like earrings and glasses to create the illusion of an oval face shape. Choose earrings that are longer than they are wider, and glasses that have a slight curve to them.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

Once you've made your face look more oval, you want to make sure you're taking care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips, you can make your face look more oval and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your look!
