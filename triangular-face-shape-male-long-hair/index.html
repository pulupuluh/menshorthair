Triangular Face Shape Male Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a triangular face shape and long hair, you might be wondering how to style it in a way that accentuates your features. In this guide, we'll share tips, tricks, and advice to help you make the most of your hair and face shape.

Description

A triangular face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and narrow chin. This can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right cut and styling, you can make your triangular face shape work to your advantage.

How to

Step 1: Find the Right Cut

The first step to styling your long hair is finding the right cut. For a triangular face shape, we recommend a cut that adds volume and width to the chin area. This can be achieved with layers, a side parting, or a fringe.

Step 2: Choose the Right Products

Once you have the right cut, it's time to choose the right products. For a triangular face shape, we recommend products that add volume and texture to the hair. This can include mousse, hair spray, and dry shampoo.

Step 3: Experiment with Styles

With the right cut and products, you can start experimenting with different styles. For a triangular face shape, we recommend styles that add volume to the chin area, such as loose waves or a messy bun. You can also try a slicked-back look to highlight your forehead.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long hair with a triangular face shape:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the forehead, such as a high ponytail or slicked-back hair
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb through your hair, as a brush can create static and frizz
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair in a bun with a triangular face shape?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a bun with a triangular face shape. Just make sure to add volume to the chin area to balance out your features.

Should I avoid bangs with a triangular face shape?

Not necessarily. Bangs can work with a triangular face shape, as long as they are side-swept or layered to add volume to the chin area.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still make the most of your long hair with a triangular face shape. Consider adding layers or using volumizing products to add texture and volume.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you can make the most of your triangular face shape and long hair. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.
