How To Do Cool Hairstyles For Short Hair In 2023

Description

Short hair can be versatile and stylish with the right hairstyles. If you have short hair, you don't have to stick to one boring style. You can do cool and trendy hairstyles that can be suitable for different occasions. This article will guide you on how to do cool hairstyles for short hair in 2023.

How To

Before we dive into the cool hairstyles for short hair, you need to have the right tools. You will need a comb, hairpins, hair ties, styling gel, and a hairdryer. Once you have these tools, you can follow the steps below to create cool hairstyles for short hair:

1. Messy Pixie Cut

The messy pixie cut is perfect for those who want a playful and edgy style. To create this hairstyle:

  1. Apply styling gel to your hair to make it easier to manage.
  2. Use a comb to create a side parting.
  3. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  4. Use hairpins to create a messy look by pinning sections of hair in different directions.

2. Sleek Bob

The sleek bob is a classic hairstyle that can be suitable for different occasions. To create this hairstyle:

  1. Apply styling gel to your hair to make it easier to manage.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Use a straightener to make your hair sleek and shiny.
  4. Use hairpins to tuck the hair behind your ears.

3. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a romantic and elegant hairstyle that can be suitable for weddings and other formal events. To create this hairstyle:

  1. Part your hair in the middle.
  2. French braid the hair on each side of your head, starting from the hairline towards the back of your head.
  3. Secure the braids with hairpins at the back of your head.
  4. Tuck the ends of the braids under the hairpins to hide them.

Step by Step

Creating cool hairstyles for short hair requires attention to detail. Follow these step-by-step instructions to get the best results:

1. Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair and apply conditioner to make it easier to manage. Towel-dry your hair and apply styling gel to keep it in place.

2. Choose Your Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. You can get inspiration from magazines, social media, or celebrity hairstyles.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair according to the hairstyle you want to create. Use hairpins or hair ties to keep the hair in place.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a hairdryer, comb, or straightener to style your hair. Follow the instructions for each hairstyle above to get the best results.

5. Finish with Hair Spray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep it in place. Use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create cool hairstyles for short hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and scarves.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightener or curling iron to protect your hair from damage.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.

FAQ

What are some cool hairstyles for short hair?

Some cool hairstyles for short hair include the messy pixie cut, sleek bob, braided crown, and messy bun.

Can I use a curling iron on short hair?

Yes, you can use a curling iron on short hair. Choose a small barrel size to create tight curls, or a larger barrel size to create loose waves.

How do I make my short hair look fuller?

You can make your short hair look fuller by blow drying it with a round brush, using a volumizing mousse, or adding hair extensions.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to avoid damage.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can create cool hairstyles for short hair in 2023. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, short hair can be versatile and stylish.
