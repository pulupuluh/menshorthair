How To Get Perfect Messy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Description

Messy hair is all the rage these days, and for good reason. It's effortless, stylish, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going to work or out with friends, messy hair can make you look put together without trying too hard. In this article, we'll show you how to get perfect messy hair in just a few easy steps.

How to Get Perfect Messy Hair

The first step to getting perfect messy hair is to start with the right haircut. Layers are key for creating texture and movement in your hair. If you don't have layers, talk to your hairdresser about getting some added to your cut.

Next, it's important to use the right hair products. You'll need a texturizing spray or cream, a volumizing mousse, and a light hold hairspray. These products will help create the perfect messy look without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots, working it through your hair with your fingers. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.

Step 2: Add Texture

Once your hair is dry, spray a texturizing spray or cream throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create more texture and movement.

Step 3: Create Volume

Flip your hair upside down and backcomb the roots with a fine-tooth comb. This will create volume and lift at the roots. Flip your hair back over and gently tease the crown of your head for more volume.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your messy hair look with a light hold hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining Perfect Messy Hair

To maintain your perfect messy hair, it's important to avoid washing your hair too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Instead, try washing your hair every other day or use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair. Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it look frizzy and dull. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on your hairdryer to create more natural-looking waves and curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I get messy hair with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a messy hair look with short hair. Talk to your hairdresser about adding layers and texture to your cut to create movement and volume.

Q: Is messy hair appropriate for work?

A: Yes, messy hair can be appropriate for work as long as it's not too messy. Keep your hair styled and under control, and avoid looking like you just rolled out of bed.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

To keep your messy hair looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. You should also avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish, messy hair is the perfect choice. With a little bit of effort and the right products, you can achieve the perfect messy hair look in just a few easy steps.
