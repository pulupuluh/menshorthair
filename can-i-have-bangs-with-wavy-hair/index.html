Can I Have Bangs With Wavy Hair

Written by William Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you someone with wavy hair who's been contemplating getting bangs, but you're not sure if it'll suit your hair type? Worry not, because we're here to help you out. In this blog post, we'll be answering the question, "Can I have bangs with wavy hair?" and giving you some tips on how to style them. So, let's dive in!

Description

Bangs have been a popular hairstyle for decades, and for good reason. They can completely change the way you look and add an extra dimension to your hairstyle. However, they may not be suitable for all hair types, especially wavy hair. Wavy hair can be unpredictable and have a mind of its own, so getting bangs can be a daunting prospect. But with the right technique and styling, anyone can rock bangs with wavy hair.

How to Get Bangs with Wavy Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and get bangs with your wavy hair, here's what you should do:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

The first step in getting bangs with wavy hair is to consult with your stylist. They'll be able to advise you on the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type. They may also recommend a specific styling technique to help you manage your bangs.

Step 2: Choose the Right Type of Bangs

When it comes to bangs, there are many different types to choose from. Some of the most popular types of bangs include blunt, side-swept, and curtain bangs. For wavy hair, side-swept or curtain bangs are the best options, as they blend in well with the natural texture of your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Styling your bangs is key to making them look good with wavy hair. You can blow dry them straight or use a curling iron to add some waves or curls. Alternatively, you can let them air dry and embrace their natural texture. Just make sure to use a good styling product to keep them in place.

Tips for Styling Bangs with Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your bangs with wavy hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it frizzy.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to help tame any frizz or flyaways.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.

FAQs

1. Can I get bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get bangs with curly hair, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs and style them carefully. Side-swept or curtain bangs are the best options for curly hair, as they blend in well with the natural texture of your hair.

2. Will bangs make my wavy hair look frizzy?

If you style your bangs properly and use the right products, they shouldn't make your wavy hair look frizzy. However, if you use too much heat or don't use the right products, your hair may become frizzy.

3. Can I style my bangs differently every day?

Yes, you can style your bangs differently every day depending on your mood and the occasion. Just make sure to use the right products and tools to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair with Bangs

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make your hair frizzy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to avoid tugging at your hair and causing breakage.

So, there you have it, folks! You can definitely have bangs with wavy hair, and now you know how to style them. Just remember to choose the right type of bangs, consult with your stylist, and take good care of your hair. Happy hairstyling!
