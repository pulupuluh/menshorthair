What Hair Color Do Guys Find More Attractive

Description

As much as we want to look good for ourselves, we can't deny that we also want to look good for the opposite sex. One of the things that can change our appearance is our hair color. But what hair color do guys find more attractive? Is it blonde, brunette, red, or something else? Let's find out!

How to

The answer to this question is not straightforward because every guy has his own preference. But there are some hair colors that are generally considered more attractive by the majority of men. If you want to know what hair color do guys find more attractive, here are some tips:

1. Look at your skin tone

The first thing you need to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, you will look better with warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey brown, and copper red. If you have a cool skin tone, you will look better with cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and burgundy.

2. Consider your eye color

Your eye color can also affect what hair color looks good on you. If you have blue or green eyes, blonde hair will bring out the color of your eyes. If you have brown eyes, warm hair colors like honey brown and copper red will make your eyes pop.

3. Look at your natural hair color

Your natural hair color can also give you some clues as to what hair color will suit you best. If you have warm undertones in your hair, warm hair colors will look good on you. If you have cool undertones in your hair, cool hair colors will look good on you.

Step by step

If you're still not sure what hair color to choose, here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Determine your skin tone

You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

2. Determine your eye color

If you're not sure what your eye color is, stand in natural light and look at your eyes in a mirror. If they appear blue or green, you have cool undertones. If they appear brown, you have warm undertones.

3. Look at your natural hair color

If you're not sure what undertones your hair has, ask your hairstylist. They will be able to tell you if your hair has warm or cool undertones.

4. Choose a hair color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, choose a hair color that will complement your features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

1. Consider your lifestyle

If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a hair color that is easy to maintain. If you have a lot of free time, you can choose a hair color that requires more upkeep.

2. Experiment with temporary hair color

If you're not sure if a hair color will suit you, try a temporary hair color first. This will give you an idea of what the color will look like on you without committing to it.

3. Consult with a hairstylist

A hairstylist will be able to give you personalized advice on what hair color will suit you best.

FAQ

What hair color do guys find most attractive?

There is no one hair color that all guys find attractive. It really depends on the guy's personal preference.

What hair color do guys like on blondes?

Guys tend to like lighter shades of blonde on blondes, like platinum blonde or honey blonde.

What hair color do guys like on brunettes?

Guys tend to like darker shades of brown on brunettes, like chocolate brown or espresso brown.

Hair Care

No matter what hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Get regular trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

So, what hair color do guys find more attractive? It really depends on the guy's personal preference, but by considering your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can choose a hair color that will complement your features. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
