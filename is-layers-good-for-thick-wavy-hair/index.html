Is Layers Good For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you might be wondering if layers are a good option for your hair type. Layers can add volume, movement, and texture to your hair, but they may not be suitable for everyone. In this article, we'll explore whether layers are good for thick wavy hair and provide you with some tips on how to style and care for your hair.

  • Step By Step Guide

How To Get Layers for Thick Wavy Hair

If you're thinking about getting layers for your thick wavy hair, it's best to consult with your hair stylist first. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best layering technique for you. Some popular layering techniques for thick wavy hair include:

  • Long layers: These layers start at the chin and gradually blend into the rest of your hair. They're great for adding volume and movement to your hair.
  • Short layers: These layers start at the cheekbones and create more texture and bounce in your hair.
  • Face-framing layers: These layers are shorter around the face and gradually get longer towards the back. They're great for accentuating your features and creating a more youthful look.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your thick wavy hair with layers:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and curl to your hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Section your hair into layers and use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Start from the middle of your hair and work your way down to the ends.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your waves in place and add more volume.

Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Hair with Layers

Here are some tips for styling your thick wavy hair with layers:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too dry or frizzy.
  • Invest in a good hair serum or oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much as this can lead to frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Experiment with different layering techniques to find the one that suits you best.
  • Try not to over-style your hair as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

FAQs about Layers for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about layers for thick wavy hair:

1. Will layers make my hair look thinner?

No, layers can actually make your hair look thicker and more voluminous as they create movement and texture in your hair.

2. Can I get layers if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get layers if you have a round face. Just make sure to consult with your hair stylist to determine the best layering technique for your face shape.

3. Can I still straighten my hair if I have layers?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have layers. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair with Layers

Here are some hair care tips for thick wavy hair with layers:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layers.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy and lifeless.

In conclusion, layers can be a great option for thick wavy hair as they add volume, movement, and texture to your hair. However, it's important to consult with your hair stylist to determine the best layering technique for your hair type and face shape. With the right styling and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, healthy, and voluminous hair with layers.
