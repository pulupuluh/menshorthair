How To Cut A Mullet With Shaved Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 30, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting a mullet with shaved sides is a bold and trendy haircut that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This edgy style is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut a mullet with shaved sides step by step, including tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Mullet with Shaved Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting a Mullet with Shaved Sides
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for a Mullet with Shaved Sides

How to Cut a Mullet with Shaved Sides

The first thing you need to do when cutting a mullet with shaved sides is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to consider investing in some hair cutting shears if you plan to maintain this style long-term.

Once you have your tools ready, it’s time to prep your hair. Wash and condition your hair to ensure it's clean and tangle-free. Make sure to dry it completely before you start cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning off the top of your hair with a clip. This will help you focus on the sides and back of your head.

2. Use your clippers to shave the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers. Use a shorter guard for a closer shave or a longer one if you want to keep some length.

3. Once you’ve shaved the sides, it’s time to work on the back. Use your clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck, leaving some length at the top.

4. Release the top section of your hair and use your scissors to cut it to the desired length. You can opt for a classic mullet shape, with longer hair at the back and shorter layers at the top, or a more modern variation.

5. Blend the top section with the shorter sides by using your scissors to create layers that gradually get longer towards the back.

6. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the edges around your ears and neckline.

Tips for Cutting a Mullet with Shaved Sides

- Start with longer hair if you’re not sure how short you want to go. You can always go back and shave more later.

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut. This will help you avoid any uneven or choppy areas.

- Experiment with different guard sizes to find the perfect length for your shaved sides.

FAQs

Q: Will a mullet with shaved sides suit my face shape?

A: This style can work for most face shapes, but it’s best suited for those with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you’re not sure, ask your stylist for their professional opinion.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the shaved sides lose their shape. It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your mullet looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for a Mullet with Shaved Sides

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

- Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent further damage to your hair.

- Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to cut a mullet with shaved sides, you can experiment with this bold and trendy style. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Have fun and enjoy your new look!
