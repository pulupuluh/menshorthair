How To Make Hair Look Dirty A Relaxed Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve a messy, undone, and effortlessly cool look, then you might want to try making your hair look dirty. This trend has been around for a while, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll show you how to make hair look dirty in a relaxed and easy-to-follow way. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Look Dirty
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Look Dirty
  • FAQs about Making Hair Look Dirty
  • Hair Care Tips for Dirty Hair

How to Make Hair Look Dirty

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about what we mean by "dirty" hair. Dirty hair refers to hair that looks tousled, textured, and a bit greasy. The goal is to make your hair look like you haven't washed it in a few days. Here are some ways to achieve this look:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and absorb excess oil
  • Apply a sea salt spray to create beachy waves
  • Tease your hair at the roots for added volume
  • Use a matte hair product to create a messy, undone look

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide on how to make hair look dirty:

  1. Start with second-day hair. If your hair is freshly washed, it might be too clean and slippery to hold any texture.
  2. Apply dry shampoo to your roots. This will absorb any excess oil and add texture to your hair. Use your fingers to massage the dry shampoo into your roots.
  3. Apply a sea salt spray to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair. This will create beachy waves and add more texture to your hair.
  4. Tease your hair at the crown. Use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair at the roots. This will add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Use a matte hair product. Apply a small amount of matte hair product to your hands and run it through your hair. This will create a messy, undone look.
  6. Finish with hairspray. Spray a light hold hairspray all over your hair to set the style.

Tips for Making Your Hair Look Dirty

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair look dirty:

  • Experiment with different dry shampoos and sea salt sprays to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to use your hands to scrunch and tousle your hair. This will help create a more natural look.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  • Try sleeping with braids in your hair to create natural waves.

FAQs about Making Hair Look Dirty

Is it bad to make your hair look dirty?

No, it's not bad to make your hair look dirty. In fact, this trend has become popular because it looks effortless and cool. Just make sure to still take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using hair care products that work for you.

Can I make my hair look dirty if it's short?

Absolutely! Even if your hair is short, you can still add texture and volume to create a messy, undone look.

How often should I wash my hair if I want it to look dirty?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go a few days without washing their hair, while others need to wash it every day. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Dirty Hair

Just because you're making your hair look dirty doesn't mean you should neglect your hair care routine. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair while it looks dirty:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup of dry shampoo or hair products.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo to deep clean your hair and scalp.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.

And there you have it – a relaxed guide on how to make hair look dirty. Remember to have fun with it and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
