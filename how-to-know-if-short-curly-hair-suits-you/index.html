How To Know If Short Curly Hair Suits You

Written by Petter Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short curly hair is a popular hairstyle that has been embraced by many women around the world. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways, from messy curls to sleek and sophisticated styles. However, not everyone can pull off a short curly hairstyle. In this article, we will share with you some tips on how to know if short curly hair suits you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Know if Short Curly Hair Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short curly hair is a hairstyle that can be worn by people of all ages, face shapes, and hair types. It's a great way to show off your natural curls and create a fun and playful look. However, not everyone can pull off this hairstyle. Before you decide to cut your hair short and embrace your curls, it's important to consider a few things.

How to Know if Short Curly Hair Suits You

The first step in determining if short curly hair suits you is to consider your face shape. Short curly hair tends to look best on oval, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid this hairstyle as it can make your face look wider.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Short curly hair works best on people with naturally curly hair. If you have straight hair, you may need to use a curling iron or perm your hair to achieve this look. However, keep in mind that perming your hair can cause damage and breakage.

The length of your hair is also important. If you have long hair, you may want to consider gradually cutting it shorter to see how you look with shorter hair. This will give you an idea of how you will look with short curly hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Consider your face shape

Short curly hair looks best on oval, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid this hairstyle.

Step 2: Consider your hair type

Short curly hair works best on people with naturally curly hair. If you have straight hair, you may need to use a curling iron or perm your hair to achieve this look.

Step 3: Consider the length of your hair

Gradually cut your hair shorter to see how you look with shorter hair. This will give you an idea of how you will look with short curly hair.

Tips

  • Consult with a hairstylist before cutting your hair.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how easy it will be to maintain short curly hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.
  • Keep your hair healthy by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Add some hair accessories like hairbands or clips to add more style and variety to your hair.

FAQ

Can I pull off short curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve short curly hair if you have straight hair by using a curling iron or getting a perm. However, keep in mind that perming your hair can cause damage and breakage.

Will short curly hair work for me if I have a round face?

Short curly hair tends to look best on oval, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid this hairstyle as it can make your face look wider.

Hair Care

Short curly hair needs proper care and maintenance to keep it healthy and looking good. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often as they can cause damage to your hair. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use hair accessories like hairbands or clips to add more style and variety to your hair.

Short curly hair can be a fun and playful hairstyle that can make you look younger and more energetic. However, before you decide to cut your hair short, it's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and the length of your hair. By following these tips, you can determine if short curly hair suits you and create a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
