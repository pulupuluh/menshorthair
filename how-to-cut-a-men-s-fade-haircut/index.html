How To Cut A Men s Fade Haircut

Written by Daniel Apr 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A men's fade haircut is a popular hairstyle that features a gradual transition from short hair on the sides to longer hair on the top. This style can be achieved with clippers and scissors, and is a great option for anyone looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. In this article, we'll go over step-by-step instructions on how to cut a men's fade haircut, as well as some tips and FAQs about hair care.

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Hair Care for Men's Fade Haircuts

How to Cut a Men's Fade Haircut

To cut a men's fade haircut, you'll need a few tools:

  • Clippers with guard attachments
  • Scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Before you start cutting, it's important to have a clear idea of what kind of fade you want. There are many different types of fades, including high fades, low fades, and skin fades. You should also decide how long you want the hair on top to be.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to cut a men's fade haircut:

  1. Start by wetting the hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut.
  2. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter attachment as you move up the head.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides into the hair on top using scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and make small cuts to blend the two sections together.
  4. Cut the hair on top to the desired length using scissors. You can use the comb to lift the hair and make sure it's all even.
  5. Blend the hair on top into the hair on the sides using scissors. Make small cuts to blend the two sections together.
  6. Clean up any stray hairs and make sure the fade is even on both sides.
  7. Style the hair on top using your preferred product.

Tips for Cutting a Men's Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter one. This will help you achieve a gradual fade.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides into the hair on top. This will create a more natural look.
  • Take your time and make small cuts. It's easier to take more hair off than to put it back on.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.

FAQs About Men's Fade Haircuts

How often should I get a men's fade haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, a men's fade haircut should be touched up every 2-4 weeks.

Can I cut a men's fade haircut myself?

It's possible to cut a men's fade haircut yourself, but it can be difficult to achieve a seamless blend between the different lengths of hair. We recommend going to a professional stylist for the best results.

What products should I use to style a men's fade haircut?

There are many different products that can be used to style a men's fade haircut, including pomade, wax, and gel. Choose a product that works well for your hair type and desired style.

Hair Care for Men's Fade Haircuts

To maintain a men's fade haircut, it's important to keep the hair clean and hydrated. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often. Regular trims will also help keep the style looking fresh.

With these tips and instructions, you should feel confident in your ability to cut a men's fade haircut. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll end up with a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that you'll love.
