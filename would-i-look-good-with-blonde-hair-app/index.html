Would I Look Good With Blonde Hair App The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair blonde but unsure if it will suit you? Well, there's an app for that! The "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair App" can help you decide if blonde hair is the way to go. In this article, we'll guide you through how to use the app, provide tips on hair care, and answer frequently asked questions about going blonde.

Description

The "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair App" is a free app available on both iOS and Android devices. It uses facial recognition technology to analyze your face and simulate what you would look like with blonde hair. It's a fun and easy way to experiment with your hair color before making the decision to dye it.

How to Use the App

Using the app is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Download the App

Search for "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair App" on the App Store or Google Play Store and download it for free.

Step 2: Take a Photo

Take a clear photo of your face with your hair pulled back, away from your face. Ensure that your face is well-lit and visible.

Step 3: Choose Your Blonde Shade

Select the shade of blonde you want to try on your hair. The app offers a range of blonde shades, from platinum to honey blonde.

Step 4: Apply the Blonde Hair Filter

Apply the blonde hair filter to your photo and wait for it to generate the result. The app will show you what you would look like with blonde hair.

Tips

If you're considering going blonde, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip 1: Consult with a Professional

Before dyeing your hair, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist. They can advise you on the best shade of blonde to suit your skin tone and hair type.

Tip 2: Use Hair Masks

Bleaching your hair can cause damage and dryness. To keep your hair healthy, use hair masks and deep conditioners regularly.

Tip 3: Use Heat Protectant Products

If you plan on using hot styling tools on your hair, such as a flat iron or curling wand, use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about going blonde:

Q: Will blonde hair suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone and facial features. The "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair App" can help you visualize what you would look like with blonde hair.

Q: Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

A: It's possible to dye your hair blonde at home, but it's best to leave it to the professionals. Bleaching your hair can cause damage and uneven color if not done correctly.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my roots?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Typically, root touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to care for your blonde hair:

Tip 1: Use Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo can help maintain the tone of your blonde hair and prevent it from turning brassy. Use it once a week or as needed.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage and dryness to your hair, which can be more noticeable with blonde hair. Air-dry your hair as much as possible and limit the use of hot styling tools.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause damage and fading to blonde hair. Wear a hat or use hair products with UV protection when spending time outdoors.

With these tips and the "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair App," you can confidently decide if blonde hair is right for you. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.
