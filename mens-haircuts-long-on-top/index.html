Mens Haircuts Long On Top A Complete Guide For 2023

Description

Mens haircuts long on top are becoming increasingly popular. This style is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. There are many variations of this style, including the undercut, the side part, and the pompadour.

The long top mens haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. It can be slicked back, styled with a messy fringe, or worn in a quiff. This style is perfect for men who want to look stylish and trendy.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Long on Top

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top, there are a few things to consider. First, find a reputable barber or hairdresser who specializes in mens haircuts. They will be able to help you choose the best style for your face shape and hair type.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in some pictures of the style you want. This will give your barber or hairdresser a better idea of what you're looking for.

Choose the Right Style

There are many different styles of mens haircuts long on top. Some popular options include:

  • The undercut
  • The side part
  • The pompadour
  • The messy fringe
  • The quiff

Each of these styles has its own unique look and feel. Choose the one that best suits your personal style and preferences.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to getting a mens haircut long on top:

  1. Choose the right style for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a reputable barber or hairdresser who specializes in mens haircuts.
  3. Bring in pictures of the style you want to show your barber or hairdresser.
  4. Discuss your preferences with your barber or hairdresser.
  5. Sit back and relax while your barber or hairdresser works their magic.
  6. Ask your barber or hairdresser for styling tips and product recommendations.

Tips to Maintain your Long Top Mens Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long top mens haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a hair wax or pomade.
  • Visit your barber or hairdresser regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

How long should the hair on top be for a mens haircut long on top?

The length of the hair on top can vary depending on the style you choose. Generally, it should be at least 2-3 inches long.

What face shapes suit a mens haircut long on top?

A mens haircut long on top can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond.

Hair Care for Mens Haircuts Long on Top

To keep your mens haircut long on top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair gently.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish mens haircut long on top with confidence.
