Gacha Club is a popular game where you can create and customize your own anime-style characters. One of the many features of this game is the ability to change your character's face shape. This can give your character a whole new look and make them stand out from the rest.

How to

The process of changing your character's face shape in Gacha Club is very simple. Here's how:

  1. Open the Gacha Club app on your device.
  2. Select the character you want to edit.
  3. Click on the "Customize" button located at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click on the "Face" icon.
  5. Select the "Shape" option.
  6. Choose a new face shape from the options provided.
  7. Adjust the size and position of the face shape to fit your character's features.
  8. Click on the "Save" button to apply the changes.
