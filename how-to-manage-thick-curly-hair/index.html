How To Manage Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look gorgeous, it can also be difficult to manage. If you have thick curly hair, you know the struggle of finding the right products and techniques to keep your locks looking healthy and defined. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for managing thick curly hair, so you can rock your natural texture with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Thick Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQ About Thick Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Hair

How to Manage Thick Curly Hair

The first step in managing thick curly hair is finding the right products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for curly hair. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls. Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz. If you need to brush your hair, use a boar bristle brush or a brush with soft, flexible bristles.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them moisturized. You can also use a gel or mousse to add hold and reduce frizz.

When drying your hair, avoid using a towel to rub your hair dry. Instead, gently scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. You can also diffuse your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the drying process.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for managing thick curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair
  4. Use a gel or mousse to add hold and reduce frizz
  5. Dry your hair by scrunching with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt
  6. Diffuse your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting if needed

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing thick curly hair:

  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

FAQ About Thick Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?
A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it moisturized. Q: What’s the best way to detangle thick curly hair?
A: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair while it’s wet. Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick curly hair?
A: Use a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or gel to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Hair

Here’s a sample hair care routine for managing thick curly hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair
  • Use a gel or mousse to add hold and reduce frizz
  • Dry your hair by scrunching with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt
  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you can easily manage your thick curly hair and rock your natural texture with confidence. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
