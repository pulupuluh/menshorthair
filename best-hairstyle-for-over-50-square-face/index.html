Best Hairstyle For Over 50 Square Face

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our faces tend to lose their youthful contours and become more angular, especially for those with square face shapes. If you are over 50 and have a square face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. But fret not! Here, we have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for over 50 square faces that will help you look your best and feel confident.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and angular cheekbones. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create a more balanced look. The best hairstyles for square faces over 50 are those that add volume and softness to the hair.

How To

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a square face is to determine the length that suits you best. Most women with square faces over 50 look great with medium to long hair. Short hair can be too severe and accentuate the angles of the face.

Once you have determined the length, consider adding layers to your hair. Layers help to soften the face and create movement. Soft, side-swept bangs are also a great option for square faces over 50 as they help to balance out the forehead and jawline.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the length that suits you best

Step 2: Consider adding layers to your hair

Step 3: Opt for soft, side-swept bangs to balance out the forehead and jawline

Step 4: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type

Tips

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

- Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make the face appear more angular

- Experiment with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to try something new

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair short if I have a square face over 50?

A: While short hair can be too severe for square faces over 50, a pixie cut with soft, side-swept bangs can be a great option.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face over 50?

A: Avoid straight, blunt cuts and center parts that can make the face appear more angular.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the best hairstyle for your square face over 50. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your new look with pride!
