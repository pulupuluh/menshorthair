How To Slick Back Wet Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Slicked back hair is a timeless and sophisticated look that can be achieved with natural hair, even when wet. If you're wondering how to slick back wet natural hair, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve this classic style, from how to prepare your hair to the best products to use.

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before slicking back your wet natural hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and leave your hair clean and moisturized.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your wet hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, from the roots to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide to Slicking Back Wet Natural Hair

Step 1: Comb Your Hair

Starting at the front of your hairline, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Work your way back, combing through each section of your hair to ensure that it's smooth and tangle-free.

Step 2: Apply Your Product

Next, apply your preferred styling product to your hair. A gel or pomade works best for slicked back hair. Be sure to use a product that is formulated for your hair type and texture.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a boar bristle brush, brush your hair back, starting at the front of your hairline. Brush your hair in the direction that you want it to lay, ensuring that it's smooth and sleek.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Once your hair is brushed back, use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure it in place. Be sure to hide the hair tie or bobby pins so that they're not visible.

Tips for Slicking Back Wet Natural Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a dime-sized amount of product to start, adding more as needed.
  • Use a satin or silk scarf to wrap your hair overnight. This will help to maintain the style and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Can I slick back my natural hair when it's dry?

Yes, you can slick back your natural hair when it's dry. However, it may be easier to achieve the style when your hair is wet.

Will slicking back my hair damage it?

Slicking back your hair should not damage it, as long as you're using the proper products and not pulling your hair too tightly.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil, and avoiding heat styling whenever possible.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and stylish look that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're headed to work or out for a night on the town, slicked back hair is a classic and sophisticated choice.
