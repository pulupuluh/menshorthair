How To Not Ruin Curly Hair While Sleeping

Written by Frank May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful when styled properly, it can be difficult to maintain its shape and texture, especially while sleeping. If you're tired of waking up with flattened, frizzy curls, then keep reading for some tips on how to not ruin curly hair while sleeping.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in keeping your curly hair intact while sleeping is to prepare it properly before bed. This means avoiding any heavy products or oils that can weigh down your curls and cause them to flatten out. Instead, opt for a lightweight leave-in conditioner or curl cream that will help define your curls and keep them bouncy.

Choose the Right Pillowcase

The type of pillowcase you use can also make a big difference in how your curls look in the morning. Avoid rough fabrics like cotton and opt for a silk or satin pillowcase instead. These materials are much gentler on your hair and will help prevent frizz and breakage.

Use a Silk Scarf

If you're worried about your curls getting flattened while you sleep, try wrapping them up in a silk scarf. This will help keep them in place and protect them from rubbing against your pillowcase. Simply wrap the scarf around your head, making sure to tuck in any loose ends, and you're good to go.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to not ruin curly hair while sleeping:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a lightweight leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  4. Wrap your curls in a silk scarf or bonnet.
  5. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase.
  6. In the morning, remove the scarf or bonnet and shake out your curls.
  7. Touch up any frizzy areas with a lightweight curl cream or serum.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your curly hair looking great while you sleep:

  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • If you prefer to sleep with your hair down, try placing it in a loose pineapple on the top of your head to help preserve your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin scrunchie instead of an elastic hair tie, as these can cause breakage and damage to your curls.
  • Invest in a silk or satin sleep cap, which will help protect your curls and keep them in place while you sleep.

FAQ

Can I sleep with my curls loose?

While it's possible to sleep with your curls loose, it's not recommended. This can cause your curls to become flattened and frizzy, especially if you toss and turn during the night. It's better to wrap your curls in a silk scarf or bonnet to help keep them in place.

Do I need to wash my hair every night?

No, you don't need to wash your hair every night. In fact, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and damage. Instead, try washing your hair every 2-3 days and using a dry shampoo in between washes to help absorb any excess oil.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your curly hair to keep it looking its best. This means using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools whenever possible, and deep conditioning your hair regularly. With the right care and attention, you can keep your curls looking beautiful and healthy for years to come.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your curly hair looking great even while you sleep. With a little bit of effort and the right products, you can wake up with bouncy, defined curls every morning.
