How To Curl Short Hair For Older Women A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you an older woman with short hair looking to add some curls to your hairstyle? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve perfect curls in your short hair. From choosing the right curling iron to tips for maintaining your curls, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Curling Iron
  • Prepping Your Hair for Curling
  • Curling Your Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Older Women

How to Choose the Right Curling Iron

The first step to curling your short hair is choosing the right curling iron. You’ll want to choose a curling iron with a barrel size that’s appropriate for your hair length. If you have very short hair, a 1/2 inch or 3/4 inch barrel will work best. If your hair is a bit longer, a 1 inch barrel may be better.

Another thing to consider is the type of curling iron you want. Ceramic curling irons are great for reducing frizz and adding shine to your hair. Tourmaline curling irons emit negative ions that help to smooth your hair cuticle and reduce static. Titanium curling irons are great for heat distribution and durability.

Prepping Your Hair for Curling

Before you start curling, it’s important to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Blow dry your hair until it’s completely dry. If you want extra volume, you can flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in that position.

Next, section your hair into small sections. Depending on how short your hair is, you may only need to section it into two or three sections.

Curling Your Short Hair

Now it’s time to start curling! Take a small section of hair and clamp it in your curling iron. If you want a looser curl, hold the curling iron horizontally. If you want a tighter curl, hold the curling iron vertically.

Hold the curling iron in place for about 10 seconds, and then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Once you’ve curled your hair, you’ll want to make sure your curls last as long as possible. One way to do this is to apply a hairspray with a light hold. You can also use a texturizing spray to give your curls some extra volume.

Another tip is to avoid touching your curls too much. The more you touch your curls, the more likely they are to fall out.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The length of time your curls last will depend on a variety of factors, including the type of curling iron you used, the products you used, and how well you maintained your curls. In general, you can expect your curls to last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Can I curl my short hair with a straightener?

A: Yes, you can curl your short hair with a straightener. Simply clamp a small section of hair in your straightener, twist the straightener 180 degrees, and pull it down to the end of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Older Women

As an older woman, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Some hair care tips to keep in mind include:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to curl your short hair like a pro in no time. Happy styling!
