How To Make Wavy Curls Without Heat

Written by Oliver May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Want to achieve those beautiful beachy waves without damaging your hair with heat? Look no further! In this blog post, we'll show you how to make wavy curls without heat.

How to

There are a few different methods you can use to create wavy curls without heat. Here are some of our favorites:

Method 1: Braids

One of the easiest ways to create wavy curls without heat is by braiding your hair. Here's how:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Take one section and braid it all the way down to the end.
  4. Repeat with the other section.
  5. Leave your braids in overnight.
  6. Take out your braids in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose, beachy waves.

Method 2: Twists

Another method for creating wavy curls without heat is by twisting your hair. Here's how:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Take one section and twist it all the way down to the end.
  4. Repeat with the other sections.
  5. Leave your twists in overnight.
  6. Take out your twists in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose waves.

Method 3: Buns

You can also create wavy curls without heat by putting your hair up in buns. Here's how:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Twist each section into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Leave your buns in overnight.
  5. Take out your buns in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose waves.

Step by Step

For each of the methods above, here are the step-by-step instructions:

Braids

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Take one section and braid it all the way down to the end.
  4. Repeat with the other section.
  5. Leave your braids in overnight.
  6. Take out your braids in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose, beachy waves.

Twists

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Take one section and twist it all the way down to the end.
  4. Repeat with the other sections.
  5. Leave your twists in overnight.
  6. Take out your twists in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose waves.

Buns

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Twist each section into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Leave your buns in overnight.
  5. Take out your buns in the morning and gently finger comb your hair for loose waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with damp hair. This will help your waves set better.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for extra hold and texture.
  • Don't brush your hair after taking out your braids, twists or buns. This will help keep your waves intact.
  • If you have trouble getting your waves to hold, try using a little bit of hairspray.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wavy curls without heat:

How long do I need to leave my braids/twists/buns in?

You should leave your braids, twists or buns in overnight for best results.

Can I make wavy curls without heat on all hair types?

Yes! These methods work on all hair types, whether your hair is straight, wavy or curly.

Will my waves last all day?

Your waves should last most of the day, but if you have trouble getting them to hold, try using a little bit of hairspray.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using heat styling tools regularly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools to no more than once a week.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal parts, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to make wavy curls without heat, go ahead and try it out for yourself! With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve those beachy waves you've always wanted without damaging your hair.
