Top Men s Hairstyles 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our guide on the top men's hairstyles for 2021. In this article, we will be discussing the latest hairstyles that are trending in 2021. We'll cover everything from how to achieve these styles, tips on how to maintain them, and frequently asked questions about men's hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve These Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guides
  • Tips for Maintaining These Styles
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Achieve These Hairstyles

Before we dive into the specific hairstyles, it's important to know the basics of how to achieve them. A good haircut is the foundation of any great hairstyle. So, before you try any of these styles, make sure you have a good cut that works with your hair type and face shape.

Next, you'll need to invest in some styling products. The type of product you use will depend on the hairstyle you're going for. A good rule of thumb is to use a lightweight product for shorter styles and a heavier product for longer styles.

Finally, it's important to know how to properly style your hair. This will take some practice, but with the right techniques, achieving these hairstyles will be a breeze.

Step-by-Step Guides

Now that you know the basics of achieving these hairstyles, let's dive into the specific styles themselves.

1. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a short, textured haircut that has been popular for several years. To achieve this style, you'll need to have your hair cut short on the sides and back, leaving more length on top. Use a lightweight styling product to add texture and volume to the hair on top, and then use your fingers to style the hair into place.

2. The Side Part

The side part is a classic men's hairstyle that has been popular for decades. To achieve this style, you'll need to have your hair cut shorter on the sides and back, with more length on top. Use a comb to create a clean, defined part on one side of your head, and then use a heavy styling product to slick the hair back on the other side.

3. The French Crop

The French crop is a short, textured haircut that has become popular in recent years. To achieve this style, you'll need to have your hair cut short on the sides and back, leaving more length on top. Use a lightweight styling product to add texture and volume to the hair on top, and then use your fingers to style the hair forward.

4. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a super short haircut that has been popular for decades. To achieve this style, you'll need to have your hair cut very short all over your head. This style requires no styling products, making it a great option for low-maintenance guys.

5. The Quiff

The quiff is a classic men's hairstyle that has been popular for decades. To achieve this style, you'll need to have your hair cut shorter on the sides and back, with more length on top. Use a heavy styling product to slick the hair back on the sides, and then use a comb to create a voluminous quiff on top.

Tips for Maintaining These Styles

Now that you know how to achieve these hairstyles, it's important to know how to maintain them. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a styling product that works with your hair type.
  • Invest in a good quality comb or brush.
  • Visit your barber for regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for men in 2021?

The best hairstyle for men in 2021 will depend on your personal style, hair type, and face shape. Some popular styles include the textured crop, side part, French crop, buzz cut, and quiff.

What styling products should I use?

The type of styling product you should use will depend on your hair type and the style you're going for. A lightweight product is best for shorter styles, while a heavier product is better for longer styles.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and level of activity. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the top men's hairstyles of 2021 with ease. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and always consult with your barber if you're unsure about anything. Happy styling!
