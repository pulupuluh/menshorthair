Best Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 60

Written by William Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face and are over 60, you are in luck as there are several short haircuts that will flatter your face shape and age. It's important to choose a haircut that enhances your best features and makes you feel confident. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for oval faces over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its volume. However, short haircuts can help create the illusion of volume and thickness. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and enhances your best features. For oval faces, short haircuts with layers and texture work best as they add volume to the hair and make the face appear fuller.

How to

To start, you need to determine your face shape. Oval faces are characterized by a balanced shape with slightly wider cheekbones and a narrow forehead and jawline. Once you have identified your face shape, it's time to choose a short haircut that flatters it.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose a short haircut with layers and texture.

Step 2: Consider a bob or pixie cut.

Step 3: Opt for a side-swept bang or fringe.

Step 4: Keep the length above the shoulders.

Step 5: Don't be afraid to add highlights or lowlights for dimension.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture and density. If you have thin or fine hair, a layered haircut will add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut will help to control the volume. Additionally, consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Short haircuts can be low-maintenance, but some require regular trims to maintain their shape.

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for oval faces over 60?

The best short haircuts for oval faces over 60 are those that add volume and texture to the hair. Consider a layered bob or pixie cut with a side-swept bang or fringe.

Can I add highlights or lowlights to my short haircut?

Yes, highlights or lowlights can add dimension and depth to your short haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best options for your hair color and texture.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools frequently and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will help to maintain the shape of your short haircut.

In conclusion, there are several short haircuts that flatter oval faces over 60. The key is to choose a haircut that enhances your best features and makes you feel confident. Consider a layered bob or pixie cut with a side-swept bang or fringe, and don't be afraid to add highlights or lowlights for dimension. Remember to keep your hair healthy and hydrated with regular trims and a proper hair care routine.
