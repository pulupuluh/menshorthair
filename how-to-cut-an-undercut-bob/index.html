How To Cut An Undercut Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, the undercut bob is a great option. This hairstyle features a shorter length on the sides and back of the head, with longer layers on top. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your individual style. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to cut an undercut bob.

How to Cut an Undercut Bob

The undercut bob is a popular hairstyle that can be achieved with a few simple steps. Here's how to cut an undercut bob:

1. Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use clips to secure each section.

2. Determine the Length

Determine the length you want for the undercut by using a comb and scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. For a dramatic look, go shorter. For a more subtle look, leave more length.

3. Create the Undercut

Use clippers to create the undercut. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers. Be sure to blend the hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top.

4. Cut the Top Layer

Release the hair on the top of the head and use a comb to determine the length you want. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, and be sure to blend the top layer with the undercut.

5. Check for Balance

Check the length and balance of the haircut by comparing the sides and back to the top layer. Make any necessary adjustments.

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for cutting an undercut bob:

1. Gather Your Tools

You'll need a few basic tools to cut an undercut bob, including:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Cutting cape
  • Spray bottle
  • Hair dryer

3. Determine the Length

7. Style the Hair

Use a hair dryer and styling products to create the desired look.

Tips for Cutting an Undercut Bob

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting an undercut bob:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb and scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back before using clippers.
  • Be sure to blend the hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top.
  • Check the length and balance of the haircut by comparing the sides and back to the top layer.
  • Use a hair dryer and styling products to create the desired look.

FAQ

Q: How long does an undercut bob last?

A: An undercut bob can last anywhere from 4 to 6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Q: How do I maintain an undercut bob?

A: To maintain an undercut bob, you'll need to get it trimmed regularly. You can also use styling products to keep the hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Q: Can I dye my hair with an undercut bob?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair with an undercut bob. Just be sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye packaging and consult with a professional if necessary.

Hair Care for an Undercut Bob

To keep your undercut bob looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how to cut an undercut bob, it's time to give it a try. With a little practice, you can achieve a bold and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion.
