How To Stop Hair Flicking Out At The Bottom

Written by Christine May 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long hair, you may have experienced hair flicking out at the bottom. This can be frustrating and make your hair look unkempt. Luckily, there are ways to prevent this from happening. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair flicking out at the bottom.

Description

Hair flicking out at the bottom is a common problem for those with long hair. It occurs when the hair at the bottom of the hair shaft curls outwards, giving an untidy appearance. This can be caused by a variety of factors including humidity, damage, and split ends.

How to Stop Hair Flicking Out at the Bottom

Step 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

The first step to preventing hair flicking out at the bottom is to trim your hair regularly. This will help to get rid of any split ends and prevent further damage to the hair. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent it from flicking out at the bottom.

Step 2: Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a good quality shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from flicking out at the bottom. Look for products that contain nourishing ingredients like keratin, biotin, and argan oil to strengthen and moisturize your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends to keep them moisturized and prevent them from flicking out. Look for a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down or make it greasy.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair Straight

Blow drying your hair straight can help to prevent it from flicking out at the bottom. Use a round brush to smooth out the ends and direct the heat downwards. This will help to seal the hair cuticle and prevent it from curling outwards.

Step 5: Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Look for a product that contains ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter to nourish your hair while protecting it from heat.

Tips

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as this can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to flicking out at the bottom.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from flicking out at the bottom.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too vigorously, as this can cause damage and make it more prone to flicking out at the bottom.

FAQ

Why does hair flick out at the bottom?

Hair flicks out at the bottom when the hair cuticle is damaged or split. This can be caused by a variety of factors including humidity, heat styling, and brushing your hair too vigorously.

Can I prevent hair flicking out at the bottom?

Yes, you can prevent hair flicking out at the bottom by trimming your hair regularly, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, applying a leave-in conditioner, blow drying your hair straight, and using a heat protectant.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is the key to preventing hair flicking out at the bottom. Make sure to trim your hair regularly, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and protect your hair from heat styling tools. By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking smooth and sleek, without any unwanted flicking at the bottom.
