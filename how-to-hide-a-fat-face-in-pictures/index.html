How To Hide A Fat Face In Pictures

Written by Frank Apr 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Having a fat face can be a source of insecurity for many people. It's especially frustrating when you feel like you look great in person, but then you see a photo of yourself and your face looks larger than you remember. Fortunately, there are some simple tricks you can use to hide a fat face in pictures. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to look your best in photos.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs About Hiding a Fat Face in Pictures

How to Hide a Fat Face in Pictures

Before we get into the specifics of how to hide a fat face in pictures, it's important to understand what causes a face to look larger than it is. There are a few factors that can contribute to this:

  • Camera angle: If the camera is too low or too high, it can distort the proportions of your face.
  • Lighting: Harsh lighting can cast unflattering shadows on your face.
  • Pose: Certain poses can emphasize the fullness of your face.

Now that you understand what can make your face look larger in pictures, let's talk about how to hide it.

1. Use the Right Camera Angle

The most important thing you can do to hide a fat face in pictures is to use the right camera angle. Ideally, the camera should be at eye level or slightly above. This will help to avoid the distortion that can occur when the camera is too low or too high. If you're taking a selfie, try holding the camera slightly above your head and tilting it down.

2. Pay Attention to Lighting

Lighting can make a big difference in how your face looks in pictures. Harsh, overhead lighting can cast unflattering shadows on your face, making it look larger. Soft, diffused lighting is much more flattering. If you're taking pictures indoors, try to position yourself near a window or use a softbox to create a flattering light.

3. Choose the Right Pose

The right pose can also help to hide a fat face in pictures. Avoid poses that tilt your head back, as this can emphasize a double chin. Instead, try tilting your head slightly forward and angling your face to one side. This can help to create a more flattering angle for your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you understand the basics of how to hide a fat face in pictures, let's go over a step-by-step guide to help you get the best results:

  1. Choose a camera angle that is at eye level or slightly above.
  2. Pay attention to lighting and position yourself in a flattering light.
  3. Avoid poses that tilt your head back and instead tilt your head slightly forward and angle your face to one side.
  4. If you're taking a selfie, hold the camera slightly above your head and tilt it down.
  5. Consider using a filter or editing app to further enhance your pictures.

Tips for Hiding a Fat Face in Pictures

Here are a few additional tips that can help you to hide a fat face in pictures:

  • Wear makeup that flatters your face shape.
  • Choose clothing and accessories that draw attention away from your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face.
  • Practice your poses in front of a mirror to find the most flattering angles.

FAQs About Hiding a Fat Face in Pictures

What is the most flattering camera angle for taking pictures?

The most flattering camera angle is at eye level or slightly above. This helps to avoid distortion and can create a more natural-looking photo.

Can makeup help to hide a fat face in pictures?

Yes, wearing makeup that flatters your face shape can help to create a more flattering photo. Consider using contouring techniques to enhance your features and create a more sculpted look.

Hair Care Tips to Help Hide a Fat Face

Finally, here are a few hair care tips that can help to hide a fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as blunt bangs or a full, curly style.
  • Consider getting a haircut that frames your face and draws attention to your best features.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to hide a fat face in pictures and feel confident in every photo you take.
