What Color Looks Best On Olive Skin Tones

Written by Aprilia Mar 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have olive skin, you might find it challenging to pick the right colors for your outfits. But don't worry! In this article, we'll help you discover what color looks best on olive skin tones. We'll share some tips on how to choose the right colors, what shades to avoid, and how to take care of your hair to complement your skin tone. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Colors
  • Shades to Avoid
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Colors

The key to finding the right colors for olive skin tones is to focus on warm, earthy, and rich colors. These hues complement olive skin tones and enhance their natural warmth. Some of the best colors to wear include:

  • Warm Browns
  • Earthy Greens
  • Rich Reds
  • Deep Blues
  • Burnt Orange
  • Gold and Bronze

These shades can be worn in a variety of ways, from clothing to accessories to makeup. For example, you can wear a warm brown sweater with a deep blue scarf and gold earrings for a chic and effortless look.

Tip: Use Color to Enhance Your Features

When choosing colors, consider how they can enhance your features. For example, if you have warm brown eyes, wearing a rich red or burnt orange top can make your eyes pop. If you have olive skin with golden undertones, wearing gold or bronze jewelry can complement your skin tone and bring out the warmth in your complexion.

Shades to Avoid

While there are many colors that look great on olive skin tones, there are also some shades to avoid. These include:

  • Pastels
  • Neons
  • Light Greens
  • Light Blues
  • Grey

These colors can wash out olive skin tones and make them appear dull or ashy. Instead, opt for warm and rich colors that enhance your natural warmth and complement your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips

When it comes to hair care, there are a few tips to keep in mind for olive skin tones:

  • Avoid harsh hair colors, such as platinum blonde or jet black, which can clash with olive skin tones.
  • Opt for warm hair colors, such as honey blonde, caramel, or chestnut brown, which complement olive skin tones.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can dry it out and make it appear dull.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

What colors should I avoid if I have olive skin?

You should avoid pastels, neons, light greens, light blues, and grey if you have olive skin. These colors can wash out your complexion and make it appear dull or ashy.

What colors look best on olive skin?

Warm, earthy, and rich colors look best on olive skin tones. These include warm browns, earthy greens, rich reds, deep blues, burnt orange, and gold and bronze.

What hair colors complement olive skin?

Honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut brown are great hair colors for olive skin tones. These warm colors complement olive skin and enhance its natural warmth.


We hope this article has helped you discover what color looks best on olive skin tones. Remember to choose warm and rich colors that enhance your natural warmth and complement your skin tone. And, don't forget to take care of your hair with warm hair colors and deep conditioning treatments. Happy styling!
