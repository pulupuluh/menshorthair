Long Hair With Short Layers On Top The Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Robby Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair with short layers on top is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their long locks without sacrificing length. It is a versatile style that can be worn straight or curled, and it works well with all hair types. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Long hair with short layers on top is a hairstyle where the top layers of hair are cut shorter than the bottom layers. This creates a voluminous, textured look that is perfect for those with long hair. The short layers on top can be cut in a variety of lengths, depending on the desired look. This style is perfect for those who want to add some dimension to their long hair without losing length.

How to Achieve Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

Here are the steps to achieve long hair with short layers on top:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips to secure the sections.

Step 3: Cut the top layers

Using scissors or a razor, cut the top layers of your hair to the desired length. Keep in mind that the shorter the layers, the more volume you will have.

Step 4: Blend the layers

Blend the top layers with the bottom layers by using a razor or thinning shears. This will create a seamless transition between the layers.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style your hair using a round brush and a hair dryer. This will add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

Here are some tips for maintaining your long hair with short layers on top:

Tip 1: Use a volumizing spray

Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair. Apply it to your roots before blow drying your hair.

Tip 2: Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length of your layers.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 4: Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: Will this style work on all hair types?

A: Yes, this style works well on all hair types.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every two to three days to prevent damage and breakage.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. It will create a voluminous, textured look.

Hair Care

Long hair with short layers on top requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length of your layers. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Long hair with short layers on top is a classic hairstyle that will never go out of style. It is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their long locks without sacrificing length. Follow the steps and tips in this article to achieve this look and keep your hair looking its best.
