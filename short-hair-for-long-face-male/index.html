Short Hair For Long Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by William May 06, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a man with a long face searching for a new hairstyle that will flatter your face shape? Look no further than short haircuts! In this ultimate guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about short hair for long face male, including how to choose the right cut, step-by-step styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

First, let's define a long face. This face shape is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheekbones. Short haircuts work well for this face shape because they draw attention upwards, creating the illusion of a shorter face. Plus, short haircuts are low maintenance and easy to style!

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut for a long face, it's essential to consider the balance between the top and sides of the hair. The goal is to create width at the sides to balance the length of the face. Here are some of the best short haircuts for long face male:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that works well for long face male. It's easy to maintain and creates a balanced look by reducing the appearance of a high forehead.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another short haircut that creates a balanced look for long face male. It features short sides with slightly longer hair on top, creating width and volume at the sides of the face.

The Short Quiff

The short quiff is a stylish and modern short haircut that works well for long face male. It features longer hair on top styled upwards, creating height and volume at the crown of the head.

Step-by-Step Styling Tips

Once you've chosen the right short haircut, it's essential to know how to style it. Here are some step-by-step styling tips for short hair for long face male:

Step 1: Apply a styling product

Start with a small amount of styling product, like wax or pomade, and apply it evenly to your hair.

Step 2: Style the top of your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style the top of your hair upwards, creating volume and height at the crown of your head.

Step 3: Style the sides of your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style the sides of your hair downwards and slightly back, creating width at the sides of your face.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for short hair for long face male:

Tip 1: Avoid center parts

A center part will only elongate your face further. Instead, opt for a side part or tousled style.

Tip 2: Embrace texture

Add texture to your hair with a styling product like salt spray or texturizing paste to create volume and dimension.

Tip 3: Consider facial hair

Facial hair can also help balance a long face. A well-groomed beard or stubble can create width at the jawline.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for long face male:

What's the best short haircut for a long face?

The best short haircut for a long face is one that creates width at the sides of the face, like the crew cut or short quiff.

How often should I get a haircut?

Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles. Aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Can I still style my short hair if I'm balding?

Absolutely! There are plenty of short haircuts that work well for balding men, like the buzz cut or shaved head.

Hair Care

Finally, it's essential to take care of your short hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair for long face male:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo

Avoid harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, opt for a gentle shampoo that won't dry out your scalp.

Tip 2: Condition regularly

Conditioner helps keep your hair soft and manageable. Use a conditioner every time you shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

If you use hot tools like a blow dryer or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

In conclusion, short hair for long face male is a great option for those looking for a low maintenance, flattering haircut. Use this ultimate guide to choose the right cut, style your hair, and take care of it for a look that's both stylish and practical.
