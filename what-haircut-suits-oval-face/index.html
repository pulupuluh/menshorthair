What Haircut Suits Oval Face in 2023: Tips, FAQs, and Hair Care

If you have an oval face, you’re lucky because most haircuts will suit you. However, not all haircuts are created equal. Some will complement your features better than others. Here, we’ll discuss what haircut suits oval face in 2023. We’ll give you tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care tips as well. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face is characterized by balanced proportions. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width. This means you have a lot of versatility when it comes to haircuts. However, you’ll want to choose a haircut that highlights your best features and flatters your face shape.

How to

The first step in choosing a haircut for an oval face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they’re all the same width, you have an oval face.

Next, consider your hair texture and personal style. Do you prefer short or long hair? Do you like to wear your hair straight or curly? These factors will also play a role in determining the right haircut for you.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a haircut for an oval face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and personal style.
  3. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for an oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top as this can make your face look longer.
  • Consider adding layers to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and features.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval faces:

1. What’s the best haircut for an oval face?

There isn’t one “best” haircut for an oval face as everyone’s face shape and features are unique. However, some haircuts that tend to work well for oval faces include long layers, a lob (long bob), a pixie cut, and a side-swept fringe.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have an oval face?

No, you don’t need to avoid bangs if you have an oval face. However, you should avoid blunt bangs or heavy bangs that cover your forehead as they can make your face look shorter. Instead, opt for a side-swept fringe or wispy bangs that add softness to your face.

3. Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have an oval face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have an oval face. However, you may want to consider a side part as it can add more dimension to your face.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen a haircut for your oval face, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair masks or deep conditioning treatments once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know what haircut suits oval face in 2023, you’re ready to choose the perfect haircut for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when making your decision. And don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
