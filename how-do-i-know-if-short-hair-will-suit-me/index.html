How Do I Know If Short Hair Will Suit Me

Written by Christine Mar 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and considering getting a shorter haircut? Short haircuts are a great way to switch up your look and can be super low maintenance. However, making the decision to chop your locks can be daunting. How do you know if short hair will suit you? Read on to find out.

Description

Short hairstyles have become increasingly popular over the years. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are many different styles to choose from. However, not all short haircuts will suit everyone. Factors such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style preferences should all be taken into consideration before making the chop.

How To

So, how do you know if short hair will suit you? Here are some tips:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is an important factor to consider when deciding on a short haircut. Different face shapes suit different styles. For example, a round face may suit a longer bob, while a square face may suit a shorter pixie cut.

2. Look at Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also impact how a short haircut will look on you. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a layered bob to prevent your hair from looking bulky. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut may add volume and texture.

3. Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration. Do you prefer a more edgy or classic look? Are you willing to style your hair every day or do you prefer a low-maintenance style? These are all important factors to consider when deciding on a short haircut.

Step by Step

Once you have considered the above factors and have decided to go for a short haircut, here are the steps you should take:

1. Find Inspiration

Look for inspiration online or in magazines to find a style you like. Bring pictures of the style to your hairdresser to ensure they understand the look you are going for.

2. Consult with Your Hairdresser

Talk to your hairdresser about your hair texture, face shape, and personal style preferences. They can provide advice on what styles will suit you best.

3. Book Your Appointment

Book your appointment with your hairdresser and get ready for your new look!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when getting a short haircut:

1. Start with a Longer Cut

If you are unsure about getting a super short cut, start with a longer style and work your way up to a shorter cut.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Short haircuts offer a lot of versatility. Experiment with different styles to find the look that suits you best.

3. Invest in Quality Hair Products

Short haircuts require less maintenance, but investing in quality hair products can help keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

FAQ

What face shape suits a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it is especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Will a short haircut suit thick hair?

Yes, a short haircut can suit thick hair. However, it is important to choose a style that will prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Can I still style my short hair?

Yes, short hair can still be styled. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that suits you best.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require less maintenance, but it is still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

2. Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. Visit your hairdresser every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you style your hair with heat tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

In conclusion, deciding whether a short haircut will suit you comes down to various factors such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style preferences. Consider these factors when making your decision and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!
