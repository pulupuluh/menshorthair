How To Style A Too Short Pixie Cut

Written by Bardi Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling with a too short pixie cut? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to style your hair in a way that is both chic and easy to maintain. Whether you're aiming for a textured look or a sleek bob, we've got tips and tricks that will help you achieve the perfect style. So, let's get started!

How to Style a Too Short Pixie Cut

Styling a too short pixie cut can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of fashionable looks. Here are some popular styles to consider:

Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic. To achieve this look, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and definition to your hair. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or curls.

Sleek Bob

If you want a more polished look, consider a sleek bob. To achieve this style, use a straightening iron to smooth out your hair and create a sleek, straight look. You can also add a bit of shine serum or oil to give your hair a healthy, glossy finish.

Faux Hawk

If you're feeling bold, a faux hawk is a daring and stylish option. To create this look, use a gel or pomade to slick back the sides of your hair, leaving the top section standing up in a spiky, textured style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some style ideas in mind, let's go through the step-by-step process of styling a too short pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots and the ends.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair.
  4. For a sleek bob, use a straightening iron to smooth out your hair and create a sleek look.
  5. For a faux hawk, use a gel or pomade to slick back the sides of your hair, leaving the top section standing up in a spiky, textured style.
  6. Finish with a bit of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your too short pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different styles and looks until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a too short pixie cut:

How often should I cut my hair?

It's best to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

How can I make my hair grow faster?

Eating a healthy diet and taking good care of your hair can help promote healthy hair growth. You can also try taking biotin supplements or using hair growth products.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, stylish pixie cut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently style your too short pixie cut and maintain a fashionable look that suits your style and personality. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you, and don't be afraid to have fun with your hair!
