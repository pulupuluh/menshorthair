Discover The Hair Color That Suits You Best

Written by Oliver Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to discover the hair color that suits you best. Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which color will work best with your skin tone and natural hair color. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips, tricks, and FAQs to help you find the perfect hair color for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in your overall appearance. It can enhance your natural features and make you feel more confident. However, finding the perfect hair color can be a challenge. There are so many factors to consider, such as your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. In this guide, we'll help you discover the hair color that suits you best.

How To

There are several ways to determine which hair color will suit you best:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is an important factor to consider when choosing a hair color. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow, peach or gold, warm hair colors like honey blonde, copper, or chestnut shades will complement you best. On the other hand, if you have cool undertones, such as blue or pink, cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy will look great on you.

Look at Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine which hair color will look best on you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde or auburn will make your eyes pop. If you have brown or hazel eyes, cool hair colors like ash brown or black will create a striking contrast.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also help you decide which hair color will suit you best. If you have light hair, such as blonde or light brown, going a few shades darker or lighter can create a subtle change. If you have dark hair, such as black or dark brown, adding highlights or going for a warmer tone can add dimension to your hair.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to follow to discover the hair color that suits you best:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Look at your eye color
  3. Consider your natural hair color
  4. Research hair colors that complement your skin tone and eye color
  5. Consult with a hair stylist to find the right shade and technique

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance the color will require
  • Choose a color that complements your wardrobe and personal style
  • Start with a subtle change and work your way up to a more dramatic color
  • Use hair color products that are gentle and nourishing to maintain the health of your hair

FAQ

What if I'm not sure which hair color to choose?

If you're not sure which hair color to choose, consult with a hair stylist. They can help you determine the best shade and technique for your hair type and skin tone.

What if I want to try a bold hair color?

If you want to try a bold hair color, such as bright pink or blue, consider doing a temporary color first. This will allow you to try out the color without committing to a permanent change.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

How often you touch up your hair color depends on the type of color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, root touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after coloring it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help maintain the vibrancy of your hair color
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause the color to fade

By following these tips and consulting with a hair stylist, you can discover the hair color that suits you best and feel confident and beautiful.
