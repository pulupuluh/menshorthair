Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top and Back

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle, the mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back might just be what you need. This style has been around for a while, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this popular haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top and Back

Description

The mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back is a modern and edgy style that involves shaving the sides of the head while leaving the top and back longer. This style can be customized to fit your individual preferences, with the length of the top and back hair varying from person to person. It's also a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for a variety of occasions.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top and Back

The mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back can be achieved at a barbershop or salon. It's important to find a stylist who has experience with this style and can provide you with the look you want. Before your appointment, you should also have a clear idea of the length you want for the top and back of your hair. This will help your stylist to create a haircut that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry.
  2. Section off the top and back of your hair with hair clips.
  3. Shave the sides of your head with clippers, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Blend the shaved sides into the longer hair on top and back using scissors and/or clippers.
  5. Style the top and back of your hair using a comb or your fingers, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top and Back

To keep your mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Style your hair using a hair product that works well with your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage it and make it more difficult to maintain.

FAQs

How often should I get my mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back trimmed?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking its best.

Can the mens haircut with shaved sides, long top and back be customized?

Yes, this style can be customized to fit your individual preferences. You can adjust the length of the top and back hair to suit your face shape and personal style.

Hair Care for the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top and Back

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
