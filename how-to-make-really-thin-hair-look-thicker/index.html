How To Make Really Thin Hair Look Thicker

Written by Luffy Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thin hair, you might feel like you’re stuck with a certain hairstyle and can’t do much to change it. However, there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to make your hair look thicker and fuller. In this article, we’ll go over how to make really thin hair look thicker, step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Really Thin Hair Look Thicker
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make Really Thin Hair Look Thicker

Here are some tips and tricks to make your thin hair look thicker:

1. Get a Haircut

The first step to making your thin hair look thicker is to get a haircut. A layered haircut can add volume to your hair and make it look fuller. Talk to your hairstylist about what kind of haircut would work best for your hair type.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a huge difference in how your hair looks. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are designed to add volume to your hair. Use a root-lifting spray or mousse before blow-drying your hair to add even more volume.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume to your roots and make your hair look thicker. Use a round brush to lift your hair as you blow-dry it, concentrating on the roots.

4. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid heavy products like oils and serums, which can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Instead, opt for lightweight products that won’t weigh down your hair.

5. Add Some Color

Adding some color to your hair can make it look thicker and more voluminous. Highlights and lowlights can add depth and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your thin hair look thicker:

1. Start with a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Apply a Root-Lifting Spray or Mousse

Apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to your roots before blow-drying your hair.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to lift your hair as you blow-dry it.

4. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

5. Add some Color

Add some color to your hair to make it look thicker and more voluminous.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your thin hair look thicker:

  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can damage it and make it look even thinner.
  • Consider getting hair extensions to add volume and length to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my thin hair look thicker without using any products?

A: While using the right products can make a big difference in how your hair looks, there are some things you can do to make your hair look thicker without using any products. For example, getting a layered haircut can add volume to your hair, and blow-drying your hair upside down can also add volume.

Q: Can I make my thin hair look thicker by changing my diet?

A: While eating a healthy diet can promote overall hair health, there’s no one food or nutrient that can make your hair look thicker. However, if you’re deficient in certain vitamins or minerals, it can affect the health of your hair, so it’s important to eat a balanced diet.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote overall hair health.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thin hair look thicker and fuller, giving you more hairstyle options and boosting your confidence.
