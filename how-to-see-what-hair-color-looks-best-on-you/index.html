How To See What Hair Color Looks Best On You Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure which shade will look best on you. With different hair colors available, it's essential to know how to choose the right one that complements your skin tone and features. In this blog post, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to see what hair color looks best on you.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Before choosing a hair color, it's essential to determine your skin tone. Skin tone refers to the color of your skin, and it can be categorized into three groups: warm, cool, and neutral. Knowing your skin tone will help you choose the right hair color that complements your complexion.

To determine your skin tone, follow these steps:

  1. Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.
  2. Another way to determine your skin tone is to hold up a piece of white paper to your face. If your skin appears yellow or golden, you have warm undertones. If your skin appears pink or rosy, you have cool undertones. If your skin appears gray, you have neutral undertones.

Knowing your skin undertones will help you choose a hair color that complements your complexion.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • If you have warm undertones, choose hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey brown, or copper red.
  • If you have cool undertones, choose hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can choose hair colors with warm or cool undertones.
  • If you have fair skin, avoid hair colors that are too dark, as they can wash you out. Instead, opt for lighter shades like platinum blonde or light brown.
  • If you have dark skin, choose hair colors that provide a contrast, such as honey blonde or auburn.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your natural hair color. If you want a drastic change, choose a hair color that is two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.
  3. Take into account your eye color. Choose a hair color that enhances your eye color.
  4. Consider your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to maintain your hair, choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  5. Think about the season. In the summer, choose a lighter hair color, and in the winter, choose a darker hair color.
  6. Consult with a hairstylist. They can provide you with expert advice on which hair color will look best on you.

FAQs

What hair color looks best on me if I have warm undertones?

Hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey brown, or copper red, will look best on you if you have warm undertones.

What hair color looks best on me if I have cool undertones?

Hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy, will look best on you if you have cool undertones.

What hair color looks best on me if I have neutral undertones?

If you have neutral undertones, you can choose hair colors with warm or cool undertones.

How often should I color my hair?

It depends on the type of hair color you choose. If you choose a permanent hair color, you should color your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color. If you choose a semi-permanent hair color, you should color your hair every 2-3 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain the color.

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but by following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shade that complements your skin tone and features. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
