How To Grow Long And Thicken Hair Faster With Onion And Potato

Written by Daniel Mar 27, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a natural way to grow longer and thicker hair, look no further than your kitchen! Onion and potato are two ingredients that can work wonders for your hair growth, and this article will show you how to use them effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Onion and potato are both rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for hair growth. Onion contains sulfur, which helps to stimulate the production of collagen, a protein that is crucial for healthy hair growth. Potato, on the other hand, is rich in vitamin C, which helps to strengthen hair follicles and prevent hair breakage.

How to

There are several ways to use onion and potato for hair growth, including:

  • Onion juice and potato juice hair mask
  • Onion and potato hair rinse
  • Onion and potato hair oil

Step by Step

Here's how to make each of these remedies:

Onion juice and potato juice hair mask

  1. Peel and grate one onion and one potato
  2. Squeeze out the juice from the grated onion and potato
  3. Mix the juices together
  4. Apply the mixture to your hair and scalp
  5. Leave it on for 30 minutes
  6. Rinse your hair with water

Onion and potato hair rinse

  1. Peel and chop one onion and one potato
  2. Boil the chopped vegetables in water for 15-20 minutes
  3. Strain the mixture and let it cool
  4. Use the mixture as a hair rinse after shampooing

Onion and potato hair oil

  1. Peel and chop one onion and one potato
  2. Heat two tablespoons of coconut oil in a pan
  3. Add the chopped vegetables to the pan and sauté for 10-15 minutes
  4. Strain the mixture and let it cool
  5. Apply the mixture to your hair and scalp
  6. Leave it on for at least an hour
  7. Rinse your hair with water and shampoo as usual

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your onion and potato hair treatments:

  • Use fresh, organic onions and potatoes for best results
  • Apply the treatments regularly for at least a month to see results
  • Be patient - it takes time for hair to grow
  • Use a shower cap to help the mixture penetrate your hair better

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using onion and potato for hair growth:

  • Q: Will the smell of onion stay in my hair?
  • A: The smell of onion can be strong, but it should dissipate after a few washes.
  • Q: Can I use onion and potato remedies if I have dyed hair?
  • A: Yes, you can still use these remedies if you have dyed hair.
  • Q: Can onion and potato remedies cause hair loss?
  • A: No, these remedies are safe and should not cause hair loss.

Hair Care

In addition to using onion and potato remedies, it's important to take good care of your hair to promote healthy growth. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and remedies, you'll be on your way to long, thick, and healthy hair in no time!
