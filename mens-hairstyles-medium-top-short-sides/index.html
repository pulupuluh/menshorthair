Mens Hairstyles Medium Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a versatile and trendy haircut, then the mens hairstyles medium top short sides is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle features a longer top that can be styled in various ways, while the sides are kept short and neat. In this detailed guide, we’ll show you how to get the perfect medium top short sides haircut, what products to use and how to take care of it.

Description

The mens hairstyles medium top short sides is a modern and stylish haircut that suits most face shapes and hair types. The top is usually 2-4 inches long and can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, textured, or a side part. The sides and back are usually buzzed or faded to create a sharp contrast with the longer top. This creates a clean and polished look that’s perfect for any occasion.

How To

Getting the mens hairstyles medium top short sides is relatively easy, but it’s important to go to a skilled barber or hair stylist who knows how to create the perfect fade. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get the haircut:

Step 1: Choose the right length for the top

The top of the hair should be at least 2-4 inches long to create the medium length. Make sure to communicate the length you want with your barber or stylist.

Step 2: Clipper the sides and back

The sides and back should be buzzed or faded using clippers with different guard sizes. The fade should be gradual, with the hair getting shorter as it goes down towards the neck.

Step 3: Blend the top with the sides and back

Use scissors or a clipper-over-comb technique to blend the top with the sides and back. This will create a smooth transition between the longer top and the shorter sides.

Step 4: Style the top

Style the top using your preferred technique and product. You can use pomade, wax, clay or gel to create different looks such as slick back, messy or textured.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens hairstyles medium top short sides:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the haircut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a hairdryer to create volume and texture on the top.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

1. Is the mens hairstyles medium top short sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens hairstyles medium top short sides is a versatile haircut that suits most face shapes. Your barber or stylist can adjust the length and fade to complement your face shape.

2. Can I style the top in different ways?

Yes, the mens hairstyles medium top short sides allows for different styling options such as slick back, messy, textured, or a side part. Experiment with different looks to find your favorite.

3. What products should I use to style the top?

You can use pomade, wax, clay or gel to style the top. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mens hairstyles medium top short sides requires some hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers, flat irons or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp and well-groomed.

Now that you know all about the mens hairstyles medium top short sides, it’s time to book your appointment with your barber or stylist and get the perfect haircut that will make you look stylish and confident.
