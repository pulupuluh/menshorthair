How Do I Know What Type Of Hair I Have Male

Written by Frank Mar 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a male who's wondering what type of hair you have, you're not alone. Understanding your hair type is crucial when it comes to finding the right products and styling techniques. In this article, we will discuss how to determine your hair type, as well as some tips for taking care of your specific hair type.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Hair Type
  • Different Types of Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in determining your hair type is to take a look at your hair's texture. Is your hair fine, medium, or coarse? Fine hair is typically thin and delicate, while coarse hair is thick and sturdy. Medium hair falls somewhere in between.

The next thing to consider is your hair's curl pattern. Does your hair curl tightly or is it straight? If your hair curls, how tight are the curls? This will help determine whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair.

Finally, consider your hair's porosity. This refers to how easily your hair absorbs and retains moisture. If your hair soaks up water quickly and dries fast, it's likely to have high porosity. If it takes a while to absorb water and retains moisture well, it's likely to have low porosity.

Different Types of Hair

Based on the above factors, hair can be categorized into several types:

  • Straight hair: This hair type has no curl pattern and is often fine or medium in texture.
  • Wavy hair: This hair type has a slight curl pattern and is typically medium in texture.
  • Curly hair: This hair type has a tight curl pattern and can be fine, medium, or coarse in texture.
  • Kinky hair: This hair type has a very tight curl pattern and is typically coarse in texture.

Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types

Once you've determined your hair type, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for different hair types:

  • Straight hair: Use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair. Avoid heavy styling products and instead opt for light-hold gels or mousses.
  • Wavy hair: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your hair's natural wave pattern.
  • Curly hair: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining cream to enhance your curls.
  • Kinky hair: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every two to three days.

Q: What should I do if my hair is dry?
A: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your ends. Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair.

Q: Can I change my hair type?
A: No, your hair type is determined by genetics. However, you can change the appearance of your hair through styling techniques and products.

Knowing your hair type is the first step towards taking care of your hair properly. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.
